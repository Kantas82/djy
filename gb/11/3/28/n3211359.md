<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：达赖喇嘛欲辞去西藏政治领袖</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Dalai Lama Plans to Step Down as Tibetan Political Leader<br />达赖喇嘛计划辞去西藏政治领袖<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer"allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v/nu/1a5f867de26e42969d3304d53de18bb9001.xml&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /></p>
<p>本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻<br />http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/news_china/2011-03-10/dalai-lama-plans-to-step-down-as-tibetan-political-leader.md#1</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>step down</B> [ˋst?p?da?n]：ph.辞职；下台<br />2.	<B>announce</B> [?ˋna?ns]：v.宣布；声称<br />3.	<B>modernize</B> [ˋmɑd?n?a?z]：v.现代化<br />4.	<B>ratify</B> [ˋr?t??fa?]：v.批准；认可<br />5.	<B>parliament</B> [ˋpɑrl?m?nt]：n.议会；国会<br />6.	<B>Spiritual</B> [ˋsp?r?t???l]：adj.精神(上)的；心灵的<br />7.	<B>devolve</B> [d?ˋvɑlv]：v.转移；继承<br />8.	<B>forthcoming</B> [?forθˋk?m??]：adj.即将到来的<br />9.	<B>amendment</B> [?ˋm?ndm?nt]：n.修正案<br />10.	<B>uprising</B> [ˋ?p?ra?z??]：n.起义；暴动<br />11.	<B>deadlock</B> [ˋd?d?lɑk]：n.僵局；停顿</p>
<p>On Thursday, the Dalai Lama <B>announced</B> he plans to formally <B>step down</B> as a political leader.<br />在星期四达赖喇嘛宣布，他计划正式辞去政治领袖之职。</p>
<p>The move is seen as<B> modernizing</B> the Tibetan government-in-exile in the face of Chinese pressure.<br />这是一项被视为在面对中国的压力下、现代化西藏流亡政府的举动。</p>
<p>But it still needs to be<B>ratified </B>by the <B>parliament</B>-in-exile based in India on Monday.<br />但是此举仍需要由设立在印度的流亡国会在周一的批准。</p>
<p>[Dalai Lama, Tibetan<B> Spiritual </B>Leader]:<br />&#8220;As early as the 1960s, I have repeatedly stressed that Tibetans need a leader, <br />elected freely by the Tibetan people, to whom I can<B> devolve</B> power. <br />西藏的精神领袖达赖喇嘛说：“早在1960年代，我就已经多次强调，西藏需要一位我可以把权力移交、由藏人自由选出的领导者。</p>
<p>Now, we have clearly reached the time to put this into effect. <br />现在我们显然已经到了付诸实施的时侯了。</p>
<p>During the<B> forthcoming </B>eleventh session of the fourteenth Tibetan Parliament in Exile, which begins on 14th March, I will formally propose that the necessary<B> amendments</B> be made to the Charter for Tibetans in Exile, reflecting my decision to devolve my formal authority to the elected leader.&#8221;<br />在即将到来、从3月12开始的第14届西藏流亡议会的第11次开会期间，我将正式地提出这项必要的西藏流亡宪章修正案，反映我决定将移交正式职权给这位当选的领导人。”</p>
<p>The Dalai Lama, who fled Tibet in 1959 after a failed<B> uprising</B> against Chinese rule, now lives in exile in northern India.<br />在1959年一场对抗中国统治的起义失败后，达赖喇嘛逃离西藏，现今居住在印度北方的流亡地。</p>
<p>The prime minister of the Tibetan government-in-exile says it’s not clear if the <br />parliament would accept the resignation and warned of a constitutional <B>deadlock</B>.<br />西藏流亡政府的总理说，还不清楚是否议会愿意接受这项辞职，并警告会有宪法上的僵局。</p>
<p>The Dalai Lama&#8217;s announcement also comes as the three main contenders for the new prime minister to be elected later in March.<br />达赖喇嘛的宣布时刻，正当三位角逐新任总理的主要竞争者，将在3月下旬后被选出。</p>
<p>None of them are monks, adding to a sense of further modernization of the exiled movement.<br />他们之中没有一位是僧侣，将会增进流亡运动进一步现代化的理性。</p>
<p>***<br /><B>【英文说文解字】</B></p>
<p>各位读者好：</p>
<p>达赖喇嘛将“宣布”辞去作为政治领袖，这对西藏的政统与发言而言，当然是一件大事情。英文新闻记者表达“宣布”时，常会使用announce, release, issue, publish, release, declare, unveil, unpack, uncover, disclose等字，是很重要的六星级新闻结构字喔！</p>
<p>-nounce是“说”的意思，透过David老师所指出的“强调的造字法则”，重复字首+a，announce就造出来了。</p>
<p>辞职下台一般就是用resign, step down。Step down很传神，就是走下台来；那么resign为什么叫辞职呢？因为re-这里当back，达喇签署送回给流亡议会，说我不做政治领袖了，所以就是“辞职”啰！</p>
<p>另外“批准”也是David老师BOS英语新闻学习系统中，所归纳整理的结构字，一般就是approve, nod, give a green light, OK, ratify, sanction等。这里用的ratify与sanction就是用在议会、国家等级的批准。</p>
<p>那么approve为何叫批准呢？大家应该都会prove是证明，于是透过“强调的造字法则”，重复字首+a，approve就造出来啰！就是特别强调“证明通过”啦！<br />@*</p>
