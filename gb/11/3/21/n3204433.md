<p><B>Nuclear Radiation Concern for Many in Japan Following Earthquake </B><br />日本地震之后 许多人忧心核子辐射 </p> <p>本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/news_asia/2011-03-15/nuclear-radiation-concern-for-many-in-japan-following-earthquake.md#1</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B></p> <p>1. <B>debris </B>: [d?ˋbri] n.[U] 残骸<br />2. <B>prefecture</B>: [ˋprif?kt??]n.[C] 县<br />3. <B>diaster</B>: [d?ˋz?st?] n.[C][U]灾难,<br />4. <B>Great Hanshin Quake</B>: n.[U] 神户大地震（同 Great Kobe Quake）<br />5. <B>incredible</B>: [?nˋkr?d?b!] a. 难以置信的<br />6. <B>pressing matter-one</B>: n. 当务之急<br />7. <B>ailing</B>: [ˋel??] a.生病的<br />8. <B>explosions</B>: [?kˋsplo??n] n.[C] 爆炸<br />9. <B>evacuate</B>: [?ˋv?kj??et] vt. 撤离<br />10. <B>radiation</B>: [?red?ˋe??n] n.[C][U] 放射线<br />11. <B>temporary</B>: [ˋt?mp??r?r?] a.暂时的<br />12. <B>relieved</B>: [r?ˋlivd] a.放心的<br />13. <B>swarm </B>: [sw?rm] vi.被挤满; 群集[(+with)]</p> <p>Heavy machinery moving in… trying to get heavy <B>debris</B> out.<br />大型机具深入灾区，试图将沉重的残骸移除。</p> <p>In Miagyi <B>prefecture</B> in Japan, one of the worst hit regions from Friday&#8217;s earthquake and tsunami, residents continue searching for loved ones&#8230; rescuers just starting out on the long road to recovery.<br />日本宫城县为上周五遭受地震及海啸袭击最严重的区域之一，当地居民持续寻找他们所爱的人&#8230;救难人员开始进行漫长的复原之路。</p> <p>[Hanji Hasegawara, Kesennuma Firefighter]:<br />&#8220;I think it will get back to how it was, but it will take a few years. For instance, after <B>Great Hanshin Quake</B>, they were back on their feet in 2-3 years. But to get here, there&#8217;s a chain of <B>disasters</B> on the way from Tokyo up to the Tohoku region here.&#8221; <br />气仙沼市消防员 Hanji Hasegawara说：“我想我们可以回到过去的样子，但是会花上很多年。举例来说，在神户大地震后，他们在两三年内回复正常运作。但在这里，一连串的灾难一路从东京延伸到东北区。”</p> <p>Besides the<B> incredible</B> damage left by the earthquake and tsunami, Japan is also dealing with another<B> pressing matter-one</B> of it&#8217;s <B>ailing</B> nuclear plants.<br />除了由地震及海啸所造成难以想像的损失外，日本目前要处理另一个当务之急，也就是状况不断的核能电厂。</p> <p>Since Saturday, there have been four<B> explosions</B> at the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant.<br />从周六起，福岛核能电厂发生了四次大爆炸。</p> <p>Fears of a<B> radiation</B> leak are real and officials have been warning residents to <B>evacuate </B>or stay indoors.<br />辐射外泄的恐惧已成真，日本官员已警告当地居民撤离或是待在室内。</p> <p>The latest reports suggest the situation could be improving.<br />最新的报导暗示，状况可能正在改善。</p> <p>[Yukio Edano, Japanese Chief Cabinet Secretary]:<br />&#8220;Of course, I think I should take the radiation levels seriously, but it (the high levels) was <B>temporary</B> at that moment, and it is coming down, so I am<B> relieved</B>.&#8221; <br />日本官房长官枝野幸男说：“当然，我想我应该严肃看待辐射等级，但当时高幅射量只是暂时的，然后便开始下降，因此我松了一口气。”</p> <p>Still, residents are not taking any chances&#8230; one of Japan&#8217;s international airports was <B>swarming </B>with people trying to leave.<br />然而，当地居民仍然不敢冒险&#8230;一座日本国际机场挤满了试图离开日本的群众。<br />@*</p> <p>本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p> <p>***<br /><B>【David老师演讲讯息】</B></p> <p>线上公益讲座：看艺文?学英语<br />时间：03/23 19:30~21:00 （台湾时间）<br />地点：前卫英语线上演讲厅<br />报名：service@davidlee.url.tw</p> <p>台湾清华大学人社院英文讲座<br />03/24讲座系列3：如何看英文国际与社会新闻<br />03/31讲座系列4：如何看英文财经与科技新闻<br />地点：新竹清大人社院C310会议室<br />时间：18:30~20:45</p> <p>台湾台北医学大学英文讲座<br />03/25 英语新闻了没？心智绘图大解密<br />地点：北医大教学大楼一楼2107教室<br />	台北市吴兴街250号<br />时间：18:00~20:30</p> <p>演讲海报请见：www.davidlee.url.tw</p>
