<p><B>Australian Diver Killed by Great White Sharks</B><br />澳大利亚潜水员被大白鲨咬死</p>

<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>abalone</B>: [??b?ˋlon?] n. 鲍鱼<br />2.	<B>diver</B>: [ˋda?v?] n. 潜水者<br />3.	<B>off the coast</B>:phr. 外海<br />4.	<B>shark</B>: [ˋ?ɑrk] n. 鲨鱼<br />5.	<B>slim</B>: [sl?m] adj. 苗条的、渺茫的<br />6.	<B>nil</B>:n?l] n. 零<br />7.	<B>encounter</B>: [?nˋka?nt?] v. n. 遭遇</p>
<p>The search is on for the body of <B>abalone</B> diver Peter Clarkson.<br />搜寻鲍鱼潜水员彼得克拉克森尸体的行动，正在持续进行着。</p>
<p>Forty nine-year-old Clarkson was surfacing off the coast of South Australia on Thursday when two sharks attacked him.<br />49岁的克拉克森周四在南澳大利亚外海浮潜时，两条鲨鱼袭击他。</p>
<p>Authorities believe the sharks were great whites, and that Clarkson&#8217;s chances of having survived the attack are<B> slim</B> to <B>nil</B>.<br />有关单位相信，鲨鱼是大白鲨，克拉克森的生存机会渺茫地等于零。</p>
<p>[Senior Sergeant Mick Walsh, Port Lincoln, Australia]:<br />&#8220;He is presumed dead, however we will continue searching until proven otherwise.&#8221;<br />澳大利亚林肯港高级警长米克?瓦旭说：“他被推定死亡，然而除非被证实，不然的话我们将继续寻找。”<br />Local media reports say the <B>skipper</B> who saw Clarkson being attacked is being treated for shock.<br />地方媒体报导说，看到克拉克森被袭击的船长，非常惊吓。</p>
<p>Great white shark <B>encounters</B> are most common in the waters off South Australia.<br />在南澳外海遭遇大白鲨鱼，是最为普遍的。</p>
<p>Great whites can reach lengths of up to 20 feet, and can weigh as much as two tons.<br />大白鲨的长度可以达到20英尺，重量可以多达2吨。<br />@*</p>
