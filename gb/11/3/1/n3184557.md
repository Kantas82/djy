<p>An earthquake survivor, who was<B> rescued from</B> a collapsed building after being trapped for over six hours, <B>went ahead </B>with her wedding plans and married her fiancé on Friday.<br />一位在地震中，受困超过6小时的生还者，从倒塌的建筑物被救出后，照样进行她的婚礼计划，于周五，嫁给她的未婚夫。</p>
<p>Emma Howard, an accountant in the Pyne Gould Guinness building, <B>narrowly</B>escaped when a concrete roof crashed down on her workstation during Tuesday&#8217;s 6.3 <B>magnitude tremor</B>.<br />Emma Howard是在Pyne Gould Guinness 办公大楼上班的会计师，周二芮氏规模6.3级地震时，当时混凝土屋顶坠倒她的工作站，她险些逃出。</p>
<p>She was<B> huddled </B>in the <B>fetal position </B>for six and a half hours while she waited for help, but she could see and hear her colleagues who were also trapped near her.<br />在等待救援时，她蜷缩成胎姿状，达六个半小时，但她能看到并听到也困在她附近的同事。</p>
<p>She was finally rescued along with six of her colleagues.<br />她终于和六位同事一起获救。</p>
<p>Howard&#8217;s fiancé, Chris Greenslade, anxiously waited for his bride <B>ahead of</B> their wedding ceremony at the Christ the King Catholic Church in Burnside. The church was not affected by the quake.<br />Howard的未婚夫，Chris Greenslade，在伯恩赛德的Christ the King Catholic教堂，婚礼仪式前焦急地等待他的新娘，该教堂未受到地震影响。</p>
<p>[Chris Greensdale, Emma’s Fiancé]:<br />“It&#8217;s a big day here today, but it’s been a crazy week, crazy week and I&#8217;m just glad I&#8217;ve got a happy ending to it all.”<br />艾玛的未婚夫Chris Greensdale说：<br />“今天是个大日子，但这是很疯狂的一周，我很庆幸这一切有个快乐的结局。”</p>
<p>He rushed to the scene immediately after the quake struck. He dug among the rubble and helped to free others while searching for Howard.<br />地震袭击后，他随即赶到现场。在寻找Howard同时，他在瓦砾堆中挖掘，帮助拯救其它人。</p>
<p>After Emma was rescued, the couple decided to go ahead with their wedding as planned.<br />在Emma被救出后，这对夫妻决定按原计划，进行他们的婚礼。</p>
<p>Howard was unharmed except for some bruising when she was pulled from the rubble.<br />除了从瓦砾中被救出的一些擦伤外，Howard毫发未伤。</p>
<p>The disaster has <B>claimed</B> 113 lives so far, and the authorities warned the death toll is<B> set to </B>climb, with more than 200 still missing.<br />到目前为止，这场灾难已夺取 113条性命，有关当局预告，死亡人数会开始增加，仍有超过 200人下落不明。<br />@*</p>
