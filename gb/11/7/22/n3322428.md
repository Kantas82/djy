<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>mettle </B> :[ˋmetl] n. 勇气<br />2.	<B>mighty</B> : [ˋmaiti] a. 巨大的<br />3.	<B>coveted </B> : [ˋk?vitid] a. 梦寐以求的<br />4.	<B>subtle</B> : [ˋs?tl] a. 微妙的<br />5.	<B>mob</B> : [m?b] n. 民众<br />6.	<B>heroic</B> : [hiˋr?uik] a. 英雄的<br />7.	<B>pierce</B> : [pi?s] v. 穿过<br />8.	<B>separate</B> : [ˋsep?reit] v. 分隔<br />9.	<B>carnivore</B> : [ˋkɑ:niv?:] n. 食肉者<br />10.	<B>vegetarian</B> : [?ved?iˋt??ri?n] n. 素食者</p>
<p>July 4th, 2011! 235 years of American history has all lead to this moment. Nathan&#8217;s Annual Hot Dog Eating Contest.<br />2011年7月4日！235年的美国历史一路走到了这一刻：纳森年度吃热狗大赛。</p>
<p>The greatest eaters in the world have gathered to test their <B>mettle</B> against the <B>mighty</B> hot dog. Four-time champion Joey Chestnut returns to defend his title, but his competitors are hungry for their chance at the <B>coveted</B> Mustard Belt. <br />世界上的大胃王们聚集在一起，测试他们对大热狗的勇气。获得四届世界冠军的乔伊?切斯纳，再度回来悍卫他的冠军头衔，但他的竞争对手，也渴望赢得芥末腰带。</p>
<p>There&#8217;s a change in the air. A <B>subtle</B> stir. Something electric moves through the crowd. As <B>mobs</B> shout for their favorite <B>heroic</B> eater, flags <B>pierce </B>the sky in joyous celebration. It&#8217;s time to eat!<br />空气中发生变化：微妙的触动。某种像电一般的东西穿过人群。在快乐的庆祝活动中，旗帜飘扬在空中，民众高喊他们最喜爱的英雄大胃王。现在是吃的时候！</p>
<p>This is the moment that separates the men from the boys, the <B>carnivores from the <B>vegetarians</B>. <br />这是从男孩中分辨出男人的一刻，从素食者中分出食肉者的时刻。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />11.	<B>contemplate </B> :[ˋk?ntem?pleit] v. 注视<br />12.	<B>neck and neck</B> : phr. 平分秋色<br />13.	<B>victorious </B> : [vikˋt?:ri?s] a. 胜利的<br />14.	<B>emerge</B> : [iˋm?:d?] v. 出现<br />15.	<B>sustain</B> : [s?sˋtein] v. 支持, 鼓舞<br />16.	<B>contest</B> : [ˋk?ntest] n. 竞争<br />17.	<B>self-aggrandizement </B> : [ˋself?ˋgr?ndizm?nt] n. 自我扩张<br />18.	<B>farce </B> : [fɑ:s] n. 闹剧<br />19.	<B>delusionment</B> : [diˋlu:??nm?nt] n.妄想<br />20.	<B>crown</B> : [kraun] v. 为&#8230;加冕</p>
<p>Chestnut pauses, <B>contemplating</B>the hotdogs before him—and then it begins! But who&#8217;s that to Chestnut&#8217;s left? Does the champion face a new challenge in number-two man, Patrick &#8220;Deep Dish&#8221; Bertoletti?<br />切斯纳屏息止住，注视着在他面前的热狗，然后比赛开始了！但谁在切斯纳的左边呢？是不是冠军面临新的挑战，排名第二的人，派翠克“深盘”Bertoletti？</p>
<p>Chestnut and Bertoletti are<B> neck and neck</B>. This is no longer a competition about eating, but a test of will.<br />切斯纳和深盘Bertoletti并驾齐驱。 这不再是关于吃的竞争，而是意志的测试。</p>
<p>But it&#8217;s a hot-dog-eat-hot-dog world out there, and there can be only one champion. When the dust and hotdog bits settle, Chestnut <B>emerges victorious</B>, 62 hotdogs fallen.<br />但在热狗吃热狗的世界里，只能有一位冠军。当时间结束，切斯纳赢得了胜利，他吃下62个热狗。</p>
<p>[Bad Lands Brooker, Competitive Eater]:<br />What can I say about Joey George Chestnut? He&#8217;s just awesome. He was the one who pushed Kobayashi, who we thought was unbeatable, and bring it home to America and sustain it, and we&#8217;re all just trying to catch up to be like Joey.<br />竞争者 Bad Lands Brooker说：“关于乔伊?切斯纳，我可以说什么呢？他就是令人敬畏。是他赶走小林（译按：先前的日本人冠军），我们认为是打不败的，把奖牌带回美国的家，并且延续它，我们都想试者赶上像乔伊一样。”</p>
<p>[Frank, Audience Member]:<br />&#8220;He represents the USA. He took it from Kobayashi in &#8217;07. Kobayashi has been winning from 2001-2006. He brought it back here to the US.&#8221;<br />观众法兰克说：“他代表美国。07年他赢了小林。小林从2001年至2006年一直是赢家。切斯纳把奖牌带回到了美国。“</p>
<p>Kobayashi has been banned from Nathan&#8217;s competition, so he held his own match on the rooftop of a Manhattan bar during the<B> contest</B>, where he ate 69 hotdogs.<br />小林被禁止参加纳森的竞争，所以在比赛期间，他在曼哈顿酒吧屋顶上，举办个人比赛，在那里他吃了69个热狗。</p>
<p>But not everyone was impressed.<br />但不是每个人都留下了深刻印象。</p>
<p>[George Shea, Chairman of Major League Eating]:<br />&#8220;What Kobayashi did today was an exercise in <B>self-aggrandizement</B>, and it was a farce. He put a tin crown on his head and he calls himself king. You know what I mean, that&#8217;s <B>delusionment</B>.&#8221;<br />大胃王联盟主席乔志?谢伊说：“小林今天所做的是自我扩张，它是一场闹剧，他把锡冠带在头上，自称国王，你明白我的意思，那是狂想者。“</p>
<p>[Joey Chestnut, Hot Dog Eating Champion]:<br />&#8220;He did a little thing on his own. It&#8217;s no different from when I do a practice on my own and I&#8217;m able to beat a record in my house.&#8221;<br />吃热狗冠军乔伊切斯纳说：“他自己做了一点小事情，这和我自己练习，我可以在自己家里击败记录一样，没有什么不同。“</p>
<p>[George Shea, Chairman of Major League Eating]:<br />&#8220;The world champion is <B>crowned</B>in Coney Island since 1916 and forever, it will always be.&#8221;<br />大胃王联盟主席乔志?谢伊说：“在科尼岛加冕世界冠军是从1916年开始，并且永远，一直都会是。</p>
<p>Also crowned in Coney Island this year: the first champion of the new women&#8217;s category.<br />同时今年在科尼岛加冕的：是新的女性类别的冠军。</p>
<p>Sonya &#8220;Black Widow&#8221; Thomas, who enjoyed the sweet taste of victory. She ate a Women&#8217;s world record—40 hotdogs in just 10 minutes.<br />索尼娅“黑寡妇”托马斯，享受胜利的甜美。她在短短10分钟内吃了40个热狗。<br />打破了女子世界纪录。</p>
