<p><B>Six Wheeled Supercar on Tour Ahead of 2012 Launch</B><br />2012年六轮式超级跑车 提前巡回亮相<br /><embed src="http://english.ntdtv.com/p55.swf" width="600" b="450" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" flashvars="config=http://english.ntdtv.com/p55config.xml&#038;file=http://media5.ntdtv.com/ml/english/news/sn/2011-07-09/20110709-SN-03_Six-Wheeled-Supercar-on-Tour-Ahead-of-2012-Launch_v2.mp4&#038;abouttext=english.ntdtv.com&#038;aboutlink=http://english.ntdtv.com&#038;image=http://english.ntdtv.com/files/Content/20110709-SN-03_Six-Wheeled-Supercar-on-Tour-Ahead-of-2012-Launch.jpg&#038;autostart=false"></embed><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Eye-catching</B>: adj. 引人注目的<br />2.	<B>Labor of love</B>:ph. 乐意做的工作<br />3.	<B>Watchword </B>: n. [&#8216;wɑt??w?d] 标语；口号<br />4.	<B>Exclusivity</B>:n. [??ksklu&#8217;s?v?t?] 独特性；排它性<br />5.	<B>Commit to</B>:ph. 承诺<br />6.	<B>Outfit</B>:n. [&#8216;aut?f?t] 团队；小组<br />7.	<B>Know a thing or two </B>: ph. 明白事理<br />8.	<B>Foremost </B>: adj. [&#8216;f?r?most] 最重要的<br />9.	<B>Drawing board</B>:ph. 计划阶段<br />10.	<B>Handling</B>:n. [&#8216;h?ndl???] 操纵性能<br />11.	<B>Understeer</B>:v. [??nd?st?r] 转向不足<br />12.	<B>Gimmick</B>:n. [&#8216;g?m?k] 噱头</p>
<p>STORY: <br />The UK&#8217;s recent Festival of Speed at Goodwood saw plenty of supercars but one model was particularly <B>eye-catching</B>. The Covini C3A is a supercar with a difference &#8211; it has six wheels.<br />在英国最近的古德伍德速度节，可以看到很多超级跑车，其中有一款特别引人注目─ ─Covini C3A，一台很不一样、拥有六个轮子的超级跑车。</p>
<p>The Covini family has been designing this dream car for some five years and they plan to go into production in 2012. Meanwhile six prototypes are turning heads wherever they go.<br />科威尼家族花了大约五年的时间一直在设计这台梦幻汽车，他们并计划在2012年投入生产。与此同时，六轮原型跑车随着他们巡回展出。</p>
<p>Developing the C3A has been something of a<B> labor of love</B>.<br />C3A的开发是基于某种工作热情。</p>
<p>[Gianluca Covini, Covini Designer and Test Driver]:<br /> &#8221;This car was born by the mind of my father about 20 years ago. Then in about the late &#8217;90s I find some investors and I start to produce this prototype with which we made some testing and development. Now we find Carugati Eugenio who is helping us financially and we want to start serious production of this car.”<br />Covini设计师及试驾员吉安卢卡科威尼说：“这台车是大约20年前从我父亲的想法中诞生的。大约在90年代晚期，我找到了一些投资人，就开始生产这台原型，供我们测试和研发用。现在我们更找到了卡路佳提欧金尼奥支援我们财务，所以我们想开始正式生产这款汽车。”</p>
<p>As well as safety and drivability, a key<B> watchword</B> for the Covinis is &#8220;<B>exclusivity</B>.&#8221; At one million dollars a pop that&#8217;s probably a good thing, but however many people join the queue next year the Covinis have <B>committed to</B> producing only 20 vehicles per year.<br />而安全性和驾驶性能，Covini车系的关键标语是 “独家独有”。每台一百万美元似乎听起来还不错，但许多人加入排队等待的明年车款，Covini承诺每年只生产20台。</p>
<p>The exact price tag depends on the client&#8217;s custom specifications. But the relationship is two-way, the Covinis see their customers as partners in developing the design of the vehicles both inside and out. They hope to build on customer feedback to keep improving the design.<br />实际的价格会依照客户的客制化规格而定。但关系是双向的，Covini把他们的顾客视为一同开发设计汽车内、外部的伙伴。他们希望以顾客的意见为基础，来持续改善设计。</p>
<p>As a test driver for tire maker Pirelli, as well as for the family <B>outfit</B>, Gianluca Covini <B>knows a thing or two</B> about the quality of the driving experience. This is <B>foremost</B> in his mind when he is at the <B>drawing board</B>.<br />做为家族成员以及轮胎制造商帕瑞里的试驾员，吉安卢卡科威尼对于驾驶体验的品质好坏有所了解。这是他在计划阶段时，心中感到最重要的事。</p>
<p>[Gianluca Covini, Covini Designer and Test Driver]:<br /> &#8221;It has good <B>handling</B>. Amazing handling! I&#8217;m a test driver so I tried to make this car very enjoyable to drive. So very little <B>understeering</B>, controllability in the power oversteering and I tried to do my best to make this car very enjoyable to drive.&#8221; <br />Covini设计师及试驾员吉安卢卡科威尼说：“它有很好的，甚至惊人的操控性能！身为试驾人员，我得尝试让这台车开起来很舒服。马力控制上非常少有转向不足或转向过度的情况，我试图尽我最大努力使这台车开起来感觉非常愉快。”</p>
<p>The Audi 4.2 liter V8 engine produces nearly 500 horsepower, and the vehicle weighs in at 2500 pounds. This combination means it reaches speeds of 190 miles per hour.<br />能产生将近500马力的Audi 4.2 升 V8引擎，以及2500磅的车重。这组合意谓着它时速可达190英里。</p>
<p>The C3A has a growing fan base and once it hits the roads next year its popularity will surely grow. Maybe it&#8217;s the design of the future, maybe it&#8217;s a<B> gimmick</B>. Time will tell.<br />C3A 已有愈来愈多的爱好者基础。明年一旦上路，人气肯定会再成长。而它到底是未来的设计，还是或许只是个花招，时间会证明一切。<br />@*<br />本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p>
