<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：矿工仍受困山东煤矿地底</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>28 Workers Still Trapped Underground in Shandong Coal Mine Fire</B><br />28位矿工仍受困山东煤矿火灾地底</p>
<p><embed src="http://english.ntdtv.com/p55.swf" width="600" b="450" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" flashvars="config=http://english.ntdtv.com/p55config.xml&#038;file=http://media5.ntdtv.com/ml/english/news/cn/2011-07-08/20110708-CN-02_28-Workers-Still-Trapped-Underground-in-Shandong-Coal-Mine-Fire_v3.mp4&#038;abouttext=english.ntdtv.com&#038;aboutlink=http://english.ntdtv.com&#038;image=http://english.ntdtv.com/files/Content/20110708-CN-02_28%20Workers-Still-Trapped-Underground-in-Shandong-Coal-Mine-Fire.jpg&#038;autostart=false"></embed><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>新闻关键字</B><br />1.	<B>broke out </B>:v.phr. 爆发 <br />2.	<B>shift</B>: [??ft] n. 转向<br />3.	<B>Billow</B>: [ˋb?lo] v. 波浪般地前进<br />4.	<B>shaft</B>: [??ft] n. 矿井<br />5.	<B>alert</B>: [?ˋl?t] v. 使警觉<br />6.	<B>round-the-clock</B>: phr. 24小时地<br />7.	<B>retreat</B>: [r?ˋtrit] v. 撤退</p>
<p>According to local authorities, 28 miners still remain trapped underground after a fire<B> broke out</B> in a coal mine in Zaozhuang City, Shandong Province on Wednesday evening.<br />根据当地有关单位，周三傍晚，山东枣庄市煤矿爆发火灾，28位矿工仍然受困在地底。</p>
<p>Miner Qi Qu was lucky enough to escape the blaze with 62 other colleagues on Thursday.<br />周四，矿工戚曲很幸运与62位其他同事逃离火场。</p>
<p>Qi is worried about what’s happened 700 feet below the ground, as he tries to piece the story together.<br />当他试着把故事情节串在一起，戚曲很担心在地下700尺发生了什么事。</p>
<p>Qi was on his <B>shift</B> underground when a phone call<B> alerted</B> him to the blaze.<br />戚曲本来转向地下，但一通电话警示戚曲发生火警了。</p>
<p>[Qi Qu, Coal Miner]:<br />&#8220;I was told that there was smoke running out of the mine and a big fire had broken out underground.&#8221;<br />矿工戚曲说：“我被告知有烟从矿坑冒出，一场大火在地底下爆发。”</p>
<p>Heavy smoke <B>billowed</B> out of the <B>shaft</B> after Qi and others reached the surface. <br />在戚曲与其他人到达地表，浓烟从矿坑冒出。</p>
<p>[Qi Qu, Coal Miner]:<br />&#8220;I was very worried about those trapped underground, and of any risks threatening their lives.&#8221;<br />矿工戚曲说：“我非常担心那些受困在地下的人，还有任何会威胁他们生命的危险。”</p>
<p>According to local authorities, an underground air compression device caught fire on Wednesday evening at Zaozhuang Fangbei Coal Mine. Ninety-one miners were working underground at the time.<br />根据当地有关单位，周三晚上在枣庄矿坑，一具地下空气压缩机着火，91位矿工在那时正在地底工作。</p>
<p>The fast spreading fire blocked the exit of the mining area, preventing the first rescue efforts.<br />快速扩散的火封住了矿区的出口，阻止了最先的救援努力。</p>
<p>[Qi Qu, Coal Miner]:<br />&#8220;We tried to reach the fire scene. But the fire was so big that we couldn&#8217;t do anything and had to <B>retreat</B>.&#8221;<br />“我们试着到达火场，但火势太大了，我们什么也做不了，只有撤退。”</p>
<p>After the blaze was put out, Fangbei miners have been sent underground in three shifts, in a<B> round-the-clock</B> rescue operation.<br />在火灾被扑灭，矿工们被转送地底，进行24小时救援行动。</p>
<p>Qi stands waiting by the shaft exit for any information on the situation inside the mine.<br />戚曲站在矿井出口，等待矿坑内情况的任何讯息。</p>
<p>Rescue efforts are still underway in a race against time.<br />救援行动仍然是在与时间赛跑地进行中。<br />@*</p>
