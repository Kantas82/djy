<p>本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>don</B>: [dɑn]穿上、披上<br />2.	<B>spot </B>: [spɑt]现场<br />3.	<B>contestant </B>: [k?nˋt?st?nt] 竞赛者<br />4.	<B>glide</B>:[gla?d] 滑行<br />5.	<B>sled</B>:[sl?d] 雪橇<br />6.	<B>soil </B>: [s??l] 弄脏<br />7.	<B>pristine </B>: [ˋpr?stin] 原始的<br />8.	<B>beard</B>:[b?rd]胡须<br />9.	<B>reindeer</B>: [ˋren?dir] 驯鹿<br />10.	<B>lasso</B>: [ˋl?so]套索</p>
<p>These merry men and women in Hong Kong are being put through their paces to find who is fit to <B>don</B> the red suit and hat.<br />这些快乐的男生和女生正在香港比赛他们的步履，看谁最适合披上红衣服与帽子。</p>
<p>They&#8217;re competing for a <B>spot</B> in Sweden&#8217;s 2011 Santa Claus Winter Games.<br />他们在竞争瑞典2011冬季圣诞老人现场比赛。</p>
<p>And to prove their proficiency the eight <B>contestants</B> had to <B>glide </B>on <B>sleds</B>, deliver gifts &#8211; in slightly unconventional ways &#8211; and eat without <B>soiling</B> their <B>pristine beards</B>.<br />为了证明他们的优越性，这八位角逐者必须在雪橇上滑行，递送礼物─以有点非传统的方式─并且吃东西时不能弄脏他们的胡子。</p>
<p>2009 world champion, Jim Chan, has been playing the role of Santa for 18 years.<br />2009年世界冠军张吉姆，已经扮演圣诞老人的角色18年。</p>
<p>[Jim Chan World&#8217;s Best Santa Claus, 2009]: <br />&#8220;Heh heh, basically every Santa Claus has his own trademark trick. But, as everyone can see, he knows magic. He can show you his magic tricks.&#8221;<br />“呵呵，基本上每位诞老人有他自己的招牌花招，但如每个人所知道的，他知道神奇所在，他能够向你表现出他的神奇花招。”</p>
<p>Even though Hong Kong is no place for <B>reindeers</B>, these Santas had to also demonstrate their <B>lassoing</B> skills.<br />即使香港不是驯鹿场，这些圣诞老人必须展示他们的套索技术。</p>
<p>And full-time magician Mario Wong thinks it&#8217;s great fun. <br />全职魔术师Morio Wang认为，这是很有趣的。</p>
<p>[Mario Wong, Champion Santa Claus]: <br />&#8220;You never know if you would win or lose, it all depends on the judge. The most important thing is to have fun. Today I&#8217;m here not to win, but to have fun. If people can see, &#8216;oh, he&#8217;s having fun!&#8217; then it&#8217;s good enough.&#8221; <br />“你不会知道你是否会赢或输，这全看裁判。最重要的，是要有趣。今天我在这里，不是为了获胜，而是为了趣味。如果人们能看到，‘哇，他真有趣’，那就够了！”</p>
<p>Wong was crowned the winner and will represent Hong Kong in Sweden from November 17th to 19th. <br />最后王先生获得冠军，自11月17日到19日，将会在瑞典代表香港比赛。<br />@*</p>
