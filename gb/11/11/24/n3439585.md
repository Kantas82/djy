<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：地震幸存者在严寒中挣扎</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Quake Survivors Struggle in Bitter Cold<br />地震幸存者在严寒中挣扎</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed615709.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Bitter</B>: [ˋb?t?] adj. 严寒刺骨的<br />2.	<B>Magnitude</B>: [ˋm?gn??tjud] n. 巨大<br />3.	<B>Desperately</B>: [ˋd?sp?r?tl?] adv. 迫切地<br />4.	<B>Tremor</B>: [ˋtr?m?] n. 震动<br />5.	<B>Tent</B>: [t?nt] n. 帐篷<br />6.	<B>Erect</B>: [?ˋr?kt] v. 设立；搭建<br />7.	<B>Damp</B>: [d?mp] adj. 潮湿的<br />8.	<B>Collapse </B>: [k?ˋl?ps] v. 倒塌<br />9.	<B>Rescue effort</B>: ph. 救援工作；救援行动<br />10.<B> wind down </B>:v. phr. 放松下来</p>
<p>People in the eastern Turkish city of Ercis – the town hardest hit by last week&#8217;s 7.2 <B>magnitude</B> earthquake –<B>desperately</B> need proper shelter as temperatures continue to drop.<br />随着气温持续下降，在土耳其东部城市埃尔季什 ─ 上周被7.2级大地震重击的城镇，当地人们迫切需要适当的住处。</p>
<p>Many families made homeless by the deadly <B>tremor</B> have been living in so-called &#8220;<B>tent</B> cities&#8221;, <B>erected </B>by aid agencies.<br />致命的地震让许多家庭无家可归，一直生活在援助机构搭建的所谓 “帐篷城”。</p>
<p>A small amount of food is being distributed every day, but people say they are freezing in the tents.<br />虽然每天有分发少量食物，但人们说他们在帐篷里快冻僵了。</p>
<p>[Veysi Can, Resident]:<br />&#8220;We are dying of the cold. It&#8217;s unbelievably cold. The ovens are not enough (to keep warm). If we turn them up too high we can be poisoned. It is also very <B>damp</B>. A solution needs to be found. It can&#8217;t continue in the tents.&#8221;<br />居民菲希肯说：“我们快冷死了。天气冷到令人难以置信。烤炉不够（保暖）。如果我们把它调太高，我们又会被毒死。而且它也非常潮湿。我们必需找出一个解决方案。这个情况不能在帐篷里持续下去。”</p>
<p>[Murat Altinsoy, Resident]:<br />&#8220;The tents aren&#8217;t stable. They are being opened by the wind. We have to keep going out to tie them down. It is a dry cold. There is nothing warm. We can&#8217;t have hot food and tea at the time we want it.&#8221; <br />居民慕拉阿汀索说：“帐篷很不稳。他们都会被风吹开。我们必须一直走出去把它关紧。天气很干冷。没有东西是暖的。我们不能在想要的时间食用热食和热茶。”</p>
<p>Some people in Ercis have not yet been given tents, and have been forced to sleep outside, with small fires the only thing keeping them warm.<br />在埃尔季什有些人们尚未拿到账篷，被迫露宿，只有一点让他们取暖的小火。</p>
<p>Early Saturday morning, it was 4 degrees and a strong wind made conditions bitterly cold. Snow could be seen on nearby hills.<br />星期六一大早，气温4度，强风下更加严寒。附近山头上还可以看到雪。</p>
<p>Clean-up operations have begun in the city centre, where hundreds of buildings<B> collapsed</B> or were seriously damaged.<br />清理行动已经在市中心开始，那儿有数百栋建筑物倒塌或严重受损。</p>
<p>The streets of the city seemed emptier than before, as<B> rescue efforts wind down</B> and the cold weather keeps people off the streets.<br />当救援工作放松下来，寒冷的天气让街上人们远离，城市街道似乎较以往更显空虚。<br />@*</p>
