<p>Green Car Race Electrifies British Students<br />振奋英国学子的绿色赛车</p>
<p><a width="560" b="315" src=" http://www.youtube.com/embed/quIycBYfUEY<br /> &#8221; frameborder=&#8221;0&#8243; allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Electrify</B>: [?ˋl?ktr??fa?] v. 使充电；使激动<br />2.	<B>Endurance</B>: [?nˋdj?r?ns] n. 耐久<br />3.	<B>Identical</B>: [a?ˋd?nt?k!] adj. 同一的<br />4.	<B>Quarantine</B>: [ˋkw?r?n?tin] n. 隔离<br />5.	<B>Pit </B>: [p?t] n. （赛车中途的）维修站<br />6.	<B>Lap</B>: [l?p] n. 圈数<br />7.	<B>Springboard</B>: [ˋspr???bord] v. 成为跳板<br />8.	<B>Aerodynamics</B>: [??roda?ˋn?m?ks] n. 空气动力学<br />9.	<B>Aptly</B>: [ˋ?ptl?] adv. 适切地</p>
<p>Designed, built and raced by teenagers.<br />由青少年设计、建造、比赛。</p>
<p>This is the Greenpower Education Trust&#8217;s annual <B>endurance</B> race &#8211; a four hour test of engineering and teamwork &#8211; with all the cars using<B> identical</B> batteries and electric motors, says Greenpower&#8217;s Emma Tyler.<br />绿色能源的艾玛泰勒表示，这是绿色能源教育信托基金的年度耐久赛–一项四小时的工程技术和团队合作测验。所有车子都使用相同的电池和电子马达。</p>
<p>[Emma Tyler, Greenpower Education Trust]:<br />&#8220;They have two batteries on the car, they have four batteries in <B>quarantine</B> &#8212; they can swap them over in a series of <B>pit</B> stops. So they&#8217;ve also got to work out their pit strategy and their team work as well as all the engineering.&#8221;<br />绿色能源教育信托基金的艾玛泰勒说：“他们车上有两组电池，有四组电池在隔离区。他们可以在赛道上一系列的维修站更换。所以他们也得思考进站的策略、他们的团队合作，以及所有的工程面。”</p>
<p>Teams must have six drivers and six pit staff and cars must have a maximum speed of 25mph (40kmh) &#8211; the test is how many <B>laps </B>can you complete within the timescale.<br />团队必须要有六名驾驶和六名维修站工作人员，而且车子必须有每小时25英里的最高速度（每小时40公里）– 测验你能在时间表内完成多少圈。</p>
<p>80 teams from around the country won a place at the Goodwood race track final &#8211; racing creations made from wood to aluminium &#8211; inspiring the next generation of British engineers and their parents, according to Tom Bollard from the Malting School in Essex.<br />据雅息士郡麦町学校的汤姆波兰德表示，来自全国各地的80组队，最后赢得了古德伍德赛道的席位 – 从木制到铝制的赛车创作 – 激励了下一世代的英国工程师和他们的家长。</p>
<p>[Tom Bolland, Maltings Academy]: <br />&#8220;We started off two years ago with a kit car which we purchased through Greenpower, that allowed us to <B>springboard</B> into a competitive region so that we could teach the students about <B>aerodynamics</B>, about physics about the general design principle behind creating a race car.&#8221;<br />麦町学院的汤姆波兰德说：“我们从两年前跟绿色能源购买汽车工具包开始，这使我们成为一个迈向竞争区块的跳板，使我们能教导学生有关空气动力学，也就是制造一辆赛车背后一般设计原则的物理知识。”</p>
<p>This year&#8217;s race was won by Chipping Sodbury School&#8217;s Rotary Racer &#8211; <B>aptly </B>numbered No 1.<br />启平沙伯瑞学校的旋转赛车赢得了今年的比赛 – 很巧妙地，它的编号是1。<br />@*</p>
