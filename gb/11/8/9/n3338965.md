[ˋmid??et]调停解决、居中</p> <p>A suspected right-wing Christian gunman in police uniform killed at least 84 people in a <B>ferocious</B> attack on a youth summer camp of Norway&#8217;s ruling Labour party, hours after a bomb killed seven in Oslo.<br />在奥斯陆7人死于爆炸后数小时，一名疑似右翼基督教持枪嫌犯，身穿警察制服，凶残攻击挪威执政劳工党的青年夏令营，造成至少84人死亡。</p> <p>Witnesses said the gunman, identified by police as a 32-year-old Norwegian, moved across the small, wooded Utoeya holiday island on Friday, firing<B> at random</B> as young people<B> scattered</B> in fear.<br />目击者说，持枪者被警方认定为是32岁挪威人，周五，穿过遍布树林的Utoeya渡假小岛，随机扫射，年轻人们在恐惧中四散。</p> <p>Teenagers at the lakeside camp fled screaming in panic, many leaping into the water to save themselves, when the assailant began<B> spraying</B> them with gunfire, witnesses said.<br />目击者说，当攻击者开始用枪扫射他们，湖边营区的青少年，在恐慌中尖叫逃离，许多人跳水自救。</p> <p>Many sought shelter in buildings as shots <B>echoed </B>across the island that was hosting the annual camp for the youth wing of the Labour Party. Others fled into the woods or tried to swim to safety.<br />因为枪发出的回声遍及全岛，许多人寻找建筑物当避难所，劳工党正在岛上为青年翼举办的年度夏令营。其他人则逃进树林，或试着游泳到安全处。</p> <p>[Dana Barzingi, Survivor]:<br />&#8220;Afterwards there were many shot people, so I saved&#8230; I tried to save those I could, but there were people there I couldn&#8217;t do anything for.&#8221;<br />幸存者Dana Barzingi说：<br />“之后很多人被射杀，所以我救了&#8230; &#8230;我试图挽救那些我能力可及的人，但也有人在那里，我却无力可施。”</p> <p>Police have <B>detained </B>the gunman, and charged him for the island killing <B>spree</B> and the Oslo bomb blast.<br />警方拘捕了枪手，并控告他岛上狂妄杀人及与奥斯陆爆炸案有关。</p> <p>The bomb took out Oslo mid-afternoon, <B>blowing out</B> the windows of the prime minister&#8217;s building and damaging the finance and oil ministry buildings. <br />奥斯陆午后的这起爆炸，炸碎总理大楼窗户，也破坏财政部及石油部大楼。</p> <p>Streets were <B>strewn</B> with shattered <B>masonry</B>, glass and twisted steel. <br />街道都是破碎的砖石、玻璃及扭曲变形的钢筋。</p> <p>The Oslo district attacked is the very heart of power in Norway.
