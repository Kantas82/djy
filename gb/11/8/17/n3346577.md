<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：最快的婴儿在爬行比赛中夺冠</h2>

<h6></h6>

<p><B>Fastest Baby Crowned at Crawling Competition</B><br />最快的婴儿在爬行比赛中夺冠<br /><embed src="http://english.ntdtv.com/p55.swf" width="600" b="450" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" flashvars="config=http://english.ntdtv.com/p55config.xml&#038;file=http://media5.ntdtv.com/ml/english/news//sn/2011-08-06/20110806-SN-04_Fastest-Baby-Crowned-at-Crawling-Competition.mp4&#038;abouttext=english.ntdtv.com&#038;aboutlink=http://english.ntdtv.com&#038;image=http://english.ntdtv.com/files/Content/20110806-SN-04_Fastest-Baby-Crowned-at-Crawling-Competition.jpg&#038;autostart=false"></embed><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>crawl</B>: [kr?:l] v. 爬行<br />2.	<B>derby</B>: [ˋdɑ:bi] n. 圆顶窄边礼帽<br />3.	<B>toddler</B>: [ˋt?dl?] n. 学步的小孩<br />4.	<B>coach</B>: [kot?] v.训练、指导<br />5.	<B>strategy </B>: [ˋstr?tid?i] n. 策略 <br />6.	<B>coax</B>: [k?uks] v. 哄诱<br />7.	<B>snag</B>: [sn?g] n. 缺牙<br />8.	<B>trophy</B>: [ˋtr?ufi] n. 奖品、奖杯<br />9.	<B>Triathlon</B>: [traiˋ?θl?n] n. 三项运动</p>
<p>Thirty babies showed off their <B>crawling </B>skills during Nautica&#8217;s third annual diaper <B>derby</B>.<br />在第三届诺蒂卡的年度尿布德比比赛中，三十位婴儿展示了他们的爬行技能。</p>
<p>The<B> toddlers</B> crawled across a 10-foot long mat as excited adults <B>coached</B> from the sidelines.<br />刚学步的小孩们在一块10英尺长的垫子上爬行，而兴奋的父母们在一旁指导。</p>
<p>Some of the parent&#8217;s <B>strategies</B> included stuffed animals, food and toys. The winning baby, Trent Miele, was<B> coaxed</B> across the finish line with a baseball.<br />一些家长的战略包括毛绒动物、食品和玩具。赢家宝宝─ ─特伦特美诺─ ─是被棒球哄着跨越终点线。</p>
<p>[Mary, Mother of Winning Toddler]:<br />&#8220;He&#8217;s very interested in baseball so anything to do with them, he&#8217;ll go for them, run for them across the room.&#8221; <br />获奖婴儿的妈妈玛瑞说：“他对棒球非常感兴趣，所以任何和棒球有关的事，他都会去做，他会在房间里追着棒球跑。</p>
<p>Nine-month-old Trent started crawling when he was just 6 and a half months old.<br />九个月大的特伦特，在他只有六个半月的时候，就开始爬行。</p>
<p>While Trent <B>snagged</B> the largest prize, none of the babies went home empty handed–each received a smaller<B> trophy</B> for their efforts.<br />当特伦特出乎意外的拿到大奖，但没有一个婴儿两手空空，每一位婴儿为他们的努力付出都拿到了小小的奖杯。</p>
<p>The race was held to celebrate the Nautica New York City<B> Triathlon</B> which will be held on Sunday, August 7.<br />这次比赛是为庆祝八月七日，就是周日即将举行的诺蒂卡纽约市三项运动赛。<br />@*</p>
