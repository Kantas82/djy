<h6></h6>

<p>Stranded Baby Whale Rescued Back to Sea<br />搁浅幼鲸被救回大海</p>
<p><embed src="http://english.ntdtv.com/p55.swf" width="600" b="450" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" flashvars="config=http://english.ntdtv.com/p55config.xml&#038;file=http://media5.ntdtv.com/ml/english/news/wn/2011-08-09/20110809-WN-12_Stranded-Baby-Whale-Rescued-Back-to-Sea.mp4&#038;abouttext=english.ntdtv.com&#038;aboutlink=http://english.ntdtv.com?=http://english.ntdtv.com/files/Content/20110809-WN-12-Stranded-Baby-Whale-Rescued-Back-to-%20Sea.jpg&#038;autostart=false"></embed><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻 </p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>strand</B>: [str?nd] v. 搁浅、受困 <br />2.	<B>humpback</B>: [ˋh?mp?b?k] n. 座头鲸（又叫大翅鲸）<br />3.	<B>dug</B>: [d?g] pp. dig挖的过去分词<br />4.	<B>channel </B>: [ˋt??nl] n. 水道<br />5.	<B>excavation</B>: [??ksk?ˋve??n] n. 挖掘<br />6.	<B>ski</B>: [ski] n. 滑雪板<br />7.	<B>coax</B>: [koks] v. 哄骗<br />8.	<B>calf</B>: [k?f] n. 仔鲸<br />9.	<B>monitor </B>: [ˋmɑn?t?] v. 监看</p>
<p>A baby <B>humpback</B> whale that <B>stranded</B> itself on a Gold Coast beach on Monday was rescued and guided back out to sea.<br />周一，一只小座头鲸搁浅在黄金海岸获救，并被引导游回大海。</p>
<p>Rescuers <B>dug</B> a channel in the sand with <B>excavation</B> equipment and waited for the tide to come in. <br />救援人员用挖掘设备挖了一条水道，并等到潮水涌入。</p>
<p>Helped by people on two jet <B>skis</B>, the baby whale was gently <B>coaxed</B> back into the water. <br />在坐着二艘喷射滑水板的人们协助下，幼鲸温柔地被哄骗回到海水中。</p>
<p>According to local media the 3000 lb whale, believed to be only two weeks old, had become separated from its mother. <br />据当地媒体报导，这只3000磅的鲸鱼， 据信只有两周大，已与母亲分开。</p>
<p>Seaworld will <B>monitor</B> the <B>calf </B>for the rest of the day with hopes that it will be reunited with its mother.<br />海洋世界将会在其余的日子里，监看这只幼鲸，并希望它会与母亲团聚。<br />@*</p>
