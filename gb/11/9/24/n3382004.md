<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Meet the New Guinness Record Holders for 2012</B><br />2012金氏世界纪录新得主亮相</p>
<p><embed src="http://english.ntdtv.com/p55.swf" width="600" b="450" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" flashvars="config=http://english.ntdtv.com/p55config.xml&#038;file=http://media5.ntdtv.com/ml/english/news//sn/2011-09-17/20110917-SN-03_Meet-the-New-Guinness-Record-Holders-for-2012.mp4&#038;abouttext=english.ntdtv.com&#038;aboutlink=http://english.ntdtv.com&#038;image=http://english.ntdtv.com/files/Content/20110917-SN-03_Meet-the-New-Guinness-Record-Holders-for-2012.jpg&#038;autostart=false"></embed><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Guinness world record</B>: ph. 金氏世界纪录<br />2.	<B>Sift </B>:v. [s?ft] 筛选；过滤<br />3.	<B>Whittle</B>: v. [ˋhw?t!] 削；切<br />4.	<B>Elastic</B>: adj. [?ˋl?st?k] 有弹力的<br />5.	<B>Perspex</B>: n. [ˋp?sp?ks] 帕斯佩有机玻璃<br />6.	<B>Racquet</B>: n. [ˋr?k?t] 球拍</p>
<p>The weird and wonderful talents of new <B>Guinness world record </B>holders were displayed in London this week to mark the launch of the 2012 book.<br />奇特且非比寻常、金氏世界纪录新得主的天才们，本周在伦敦亮相，彰显2012年出版的书。</p>
<p>[Craig Glenday, Editor in Chief, Guinness World Records]:<br />&#8220;We&#8217;ve noticed an increase in applications, possibly inspired by the Olympics, who knows. We&#8217;ve had to <B>sift </B>through about 60,000 claims this year. It&#8217;s a huge job, we&#8217;ve<B> whittled</B> it down to maybe about 4,000 that appear in the book, 3,000 of which are new. So it&#8217;s been a very busy year, but it&#8217;s been worth it because we can guarantee that what is in the book is the best of the best.&#8221;<br />金氏世界纪录总编辑克雷格葛林戴说：“或许是受到奥运的启发也说不定，我们发现申请的数量上有增加。今年我们得筛选大约6000项案件，这是一件巨大的工作，我们已经删减到大约4000件收录书中，其中有3000件是新的。所以这是非常繁忙的一年，不过很值得，因为我们可以保证书中收录的都是最佳中的最佳。”</p>
<p>The world&#8217;s most <B>elastic </B>woman, Skye Broberg from Australia, climbed into a tiny square<B> perspex</B> box. She also earned the title by passing through a tennis <B>racquet</B> head the most times.<br />来自澳洲、世界上最有弹性的女人斯凯布洛柏，爬进了一个小方形有机玻璃盒。她还获得了用网球球拍头传球次数最多的称号。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />7.	<B>Sport</B>: v. [sport] 炫耀；夸示<br />8.	<B>Stud</B>: n. [st?d] 饰钉<br />9.	<B>Pierce</B>: v. [p?rs] 穿孔；穿洞<br />10.	<B>Bow</B>: n. [bo] 弓<br />11.	<B>Afro</B>: n. [ˋ?fro] 黑人头（圆蓬式发型）<br />12.	<B>Circumference</B>: n. [s?ˋk?mf?r?ns] 圆周；周长<br />13.	<B>Crowning glory</B>: ph. 至高无上的光荣<br />14.	<B>Contort</B>: v. [k?nˋt?rt] 扭曲<br />15.	<B>Rope skipping </B>:ph. 跳绳<br />16.	<B>Martial art</B>: ph. 武术</p>
<p>Rolf Buchholz, from Germany, <B> sported</B> 453<B> studs</B> and rings to win the title of the most <B>pierced</B> man.<br />来自德国的罗尔夫布赫霍尔茨，炫示了453个饰钉和饰环，赢得了穿最多孔的男子冠军。</p>
<p>The world&#8217;s largest playable violin is 14 feet long, with a maximum width of 4.5 feet, the <B>bow</B>, which took two people to hold, measures 17 feet. It was made in Germany.<br />世界上最大的可演奏小提琴有14英尺长, 最大宽度有4.5英尺. 弓有17英尺长, 必需要两个人扶着拉. 它制造于德国。</p>
<p>Aevin Dougas, from New Orleans, showed off her enormous <B>afrov. She&#8217;s now the world record holder for the largest natural afro. It measures 7.3 inches high, 7.7 inches wide and has a 4-foot 4-inch <B>circumference</B>.<br />艾芙因杜嘉斯从纽奥良来，展示了她巨大的黑人头（圆蓬式发型）。她现为是最大的自然圆蓬式发型的世界纪录保持者。它测量有7.3英寸高、7.7英寸宽，而且有一个 4英尺4英寸的周长。</p>
<p>Her<B> crowning glory</B> does come with problems though.<br />不过她这项至高荣耀也跟随着一些问题。</p>
<p>[Aevin Dougas, World Record Holder for Largest Natural Afro]:<br />&#8220;It tends to hook onto things when I am walking by, like trees and stuff and other people don&#8217;t have that problem, so it can be kind of problematic, but I wouldn&#8217;t change a thing about it.”<br />最大自然圆蓬式发型的世界纪录保持者艾芙因杜嘉斯说：“当我走路时它很容易会勾到东西，例如树枝或什么东西，其它人不会有这类麻烦，所以这可能会是一个问题，但我并不会为此而改变什么。”</p>
<p>Californian Chanel Tapper has the longest tongue, measuring 3.7 inches. She can <B>contort</B> it to be wide or long and thin and can easily touch her nose with it.<br />加州人香奈儿塔普儿拥有最长的舌头，测量3.7英寸。她可以扭曲它变宽或变长变薄，而且它可以很轻易地用舌头碰到她的鼻子。</p>
<p>13 dogs from Japan&#8217;s &#8220;Super Wan Wan Circus&#8221; now hold the record for the most dogs <B>skipping rope</B>. And Japan wins another title, Isao Machi is the fastest man to make 1,000 <B>martial arts</B> cuts in 36 minutes and 4 seconds.<br />13只来自日本的“超级旺旺马戏团”现在拥有最多只狗一起跳绳的纪录。日本还赢得另一个冠军，马池勇夫能在36分4秒内使武术削砍1000次，是最快的人。<br />@*</p>
