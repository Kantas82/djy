<p><B>【新闻关键字】 </B><br />1.	<B>nurse [n?s] </B>: v. 当&#8230;的褓姆<br />2.	<B>cub [k?b] </B>:n. (熊, 狮, 虎, 狼等的)幼兽<br />3.	<B>surrogate [ˋs??g?t ] </B>: n.代理人<br />4.	<B>furry [ˋf??] </B>: adj 覆有毛皮的<br />5.	<B>descendant [d?ˋs?nd?nt] </B>:n.子孙、后裔<br />6.	<B>mutant [ˋmjut?nt] </B>: adj.突变的<br />7.	<B>Bengal [b??ˋg?l] </B>:n. 孟加拉<br />8.	<B>Exotic [?gˋzɑt?k] </B>: adj.奇特的</p>
<p>Three one-month-old white tiger cubs in a wildlife park in China have found an unusual <B>surrogate </B> mother.<br />中国野生动物园区三个月大的白色幼虎，找到一位不寻常的奶妈。</p>
<p>This dog is nursing the three<B> furry </B> cubs in the northwestern Xinjiang region.<br />这只狗正在西北新彊地区照料三只毛绒绒的幼虎。</p>
<p>The dog adopted them after the cubs&#8217; biological mother rejected them after giving birth.<br />幼虎的生母生下它们后却拒绝它们，于是这只狗认养了这三只幼虎。</p>
<p>The park is now asking the public to name the cubs.<br />动物园正要求大众为幼虎们命名。</p>
<p>According to World Wildlife Fund, white tigers are<B> descendants </B> of the more common Indian <B>Bengal </B> tiger, but carry a<B> mutant </B> gene leading to their<B> exotic </B> coloring.<br />根据世界野生动物基金会说，白色老虎是较普遍的印度孟加拉虎的后裔，但是携带有变种基因，导至他们具有奇特的颜色。<br />@*<br />本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p>
