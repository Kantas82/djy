<p>It was a crisis watched by the world.<br />曾是全世界都在观看的一场危机。 </p>
<p>Now a reunion in Chile.<br />目前团聚在智利。 </p>
<p>A year after a mine collapsed and trapped 33 Chilean workers underground, the men reunite to remember their <B>ordeal</B> and give thanks.<br />矿坑崩塌造成33名智利工人受困地底下后一年，矿工们再度团聚，回忆他们的苦难并且给予感谢。</p>
<p>[Osman Araya, Chilean Miner]:<br />&#8220;I&#8217;m happy and content because although it&#8217;s a day to remember the accident, it&#8217;s also an opportunity to<B> embrace</B> the people who supported us nationally and globally.&#8221;<br />智利矿工Osman Araya： <br />“我很开心及满足，因为尽管这是记起那场意外的一天，也是个能拥抱全国及全世界支持我们的人的机会。”</p>
<p>The miners were<B> trapped</B> 2,000 feet below ground for 69 days.<br />矿工们在地底下2000英尺处，被困69天。 </p>
<p>Minister of Public Works Laurence Golborne, played a<B> pivotal</B> role in their rescue.<br />矿业部长Laurence Golborne，在救援中扮演关键的角色。</p>
<p>[Laurence Golborne, Minister of Public Works]:<br />&#8220;It&#8217;s very emotional to return to Copiapo after one year and to meet with a lot of people with whom we were working for so long and with so much effort for this rescue that made all of Chile feel proud.&#8221; <br />矿业部长Laurence Golborne说:：<br />“一年后再回到科皮亚波，与许多人见面，情绪非常激动。这些是在这次救援中，一起工作很久，付出很大努力，让全智利感到骄傲的人。”</p>
<p>The men were joined by President Sebastian Pinera.<br />总统皮涅拉加入矿工们的行列。</p>
<p>Many of the miners are still suffering from<B> psychological </B>problems, <B>insomnia</B> and nightmares. But on Friday, gratitude was the overwhelming emotion.<br />许多矿工仍遭受心理问题、失眠和梦靥之苦。但周五，感激之情充满心中。</p>
<p>The men and their families hope the government will announce a special lifetime <B>pension</B> for each of them that could amount to $430 dollars a month.<br />矿工们和他们的家属，希望政府宣布一项特殊的终身养老金，每月每位能获发430美元。</p>
<p>The miners have taken public <B>flack</B> after suing the state for <B>negligence </B>for allowing the<B> notoriously</B> dangerous mine–which had faced years of <B>sanctions</B> and warnings–to remain in operation.<br />在控告政府枉顾险恶及危险、让多年受制裁及警告的矿坑继续运作之后，旷工们采取公开宣传。</p>
<p>They are seeking $542,000 dollars each in damages.<br />他们正在寻求每位542,000美元的赔偿金。 </p>
<p>They are also<B> suing</B> the local private mining company.<br />他们也控告当地私人的矿业公司。 <br />@*<br />本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p>
