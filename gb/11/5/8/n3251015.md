<p><b>Londoners Throw Street Parties to Celebrate Royal Wedding</b><br />
伦敦人民举行街头派对庆祝王室婚礼<br />

<p><b>新闻关键字：</b><br />
1. <b>throw</b>: v. [θro]举行<br />
2. <b>gather</b>: v. [ˋg?e?] 聚集<br />

4. <b>struggle</b>: v. [ˋstr?g!]努力<br />
5. <b>step out</b>: ph. [st?p] [a?t]大步走<br />
6. <b>take something in one’s stride</b>: ph.不畏困难，从容应对<br />
7. <b>paparazzo</b>: n. [?pɑp?ˋrɑtso]狗仔队(复数:paparazzi)<br />
8. <b>downfall</b>: n. [ˋda?n?f?l]灭亡、坠落<br />

<p>Londoners <b>gather</b> at street parties today to celebrate Prince William and Catherine Middleton&#8217;s union in marriage. As the public watch together the celebrations on live TV, we asked them what they thought of the Royal Wedding.<br />
伦敦民众今天聚集在街头的派对，庆祝威廉王子和凯瑟琳．米德尔顿结为连理。当民众一起观看电视的现场直播庆祝活动，我们访问他们，对王家婚礼有什么看法。</p>
<p>[Kim Hau, London Resident]:<br />
“Probably the most <b>memorable</b> moment of the ceremony was when Prince William put the ring on Kate’s finger and it didn’t quite fit, so he was really <b>struggling</b> to make it fit. And everyone was ooh-ing and aah-ing in the audience and hoping that maybe it wouldn’t fit. He would have to get another one or something, but yes, it was quite tense.<br />
伦敦居民Kim Hau说：<br />
“最难忘的时刻也许是在威廉王子将戒指戴入凯特手指的仪式，戒指没有很合适，所以他很努力使它合适。观众惊叹声连连，希望或许它并不合适，那他就必须再找另一个或其他的。就是那时候，相当紧张。”</p>
<p>[Nina Ansah, London Resident]:<br />
“I think Kate will do really well in her new role as a Princess, &#8217;cause when she <b>stepped out</b> of her car she was so happy and she looked so relaxed, so I think she will <b>take it all in her stride</b>.”<br />
伦敦居民Nina Ansah说：<br />
“我认为凯特会是位相当称职的公主。因为当她步出车外，她是那么地开心，看起来是如此轻松自在，所以我想她能不畏困难，从容应对一切事情。”</p>
<p>[Rachel Law, London Resident]:<br />
“The only problem I see though – and it’s like a bit of a problem in the country’s celebrities – it’s the <b>paparazzi</b>. And also, that was the <b>downfall</b> for Diana. So, I really hope nothing like that will happen to her as well.”<br />
伦敦居民Rachel Law说：<br />
“我看到唯一的问题，是国家名人会遇到的问题，就是狗仔队。这也是黛安娜死亡的原因。所以，我真的希望像那样的事情，不会再发生在她身上。”</p>
<p>Catherine&#8217;s choice of wedding dress was greatly <b>anticipated</b>.<br />
凯瑟琳选择的婚纱礼服是众望所归。</p>
<p>[Jemma Sullivan, London Resident]:<br />
“I think it was really nice that it was a McQueen dress, I think it was Sarah Burton, who is from the House of McQueen. I think that was a really nice note to the late designer. And I think the dress was really classy, really elegant and I think that she<b> pulled</b> it <b>off</b>, and I just think, ‘Yes, perfect choice.’”<br />
伦敦居民Jemma Sullivan说：<br />
“我认为麦王后礼服真的非常好。我想这是出自麦王后之家Sarah Burton之手。我认为这对这位已故的设计师，那是非常好的注释。而且我觉得这条裙子真的很典雅，相当精致。她做对了，我认为，没错，是完美的选择。”</p>
<p>And as for William&#8217;s choice of wife-to-be, here is what they had to say.<br />
对于威廉对准妻子的选择，这里是他们说的话。</p>
<p>[Charlotte Louise Gould, London Resident]:<br />
“I know it’s been a lot of talk about her being a ‘<b>commoner</b>’, but I think he’s made a really good choice. They seem like they are very well matched. They’ve been together for almost ten years.”<br />
伦敦居民Charlotte Louise Gould说：<br />
“我知道已很多谈论说她是”平民”，但我认为他做了一个非常好的选择。他们看起来非常地匹配。他们已在一起将近十年。”</p>
<p>[Katie Bryan, London Resident]:<br />
“I think it’s important for kind of our Royal family to be moving forward and be shown to be a little bit more modern, and I think that Will marrying Kate, it’s kind of made that.”<br />
伦敦居民Katie Bryan说：<br />
“我想往前走及表现更现代一点，对我们这样的王室家族很重要。我认为威廉娶凯特，有做到这些的意味。”</p>
<p>The British public sent their best wishes to the young Royal couple.<br />
英国民众送给这对年轻的王家夫妇最棒的祝福。</p>
<p>“Kate and William, congratulations, you make an amazing couple! You are an <b>inspiration</b> to all of us young people.”<br />
“凯特和威廉，恭喜你们，你们是登对夫妻！你们鼓舞了我们所有的年轻人。”</p>
