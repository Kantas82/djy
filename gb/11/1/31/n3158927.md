<p>Homes are <B>inundated</B> and agricultural land is <B>submerged</B> as flooding in Sri Lanka continues on Wednesday. <br />周三，在斯里兰卡因持续的洪水，住宅被淹没，农田泡在水里。</p>
<p>At least 18 people have been killed and almost 200,000 forced from their homes due to the floods, <B>brought on</B>by heavy rain. <br />因豪大雨引起的洪水，至少造成18人死亡，约20万人被迫离开家园。 </p>
<p>The <B>Monsoon downpours</B> also <B>triggered</B> mudslides &#8211; authorities believe the death toll could rise as many are thought to have been buried. <br />季风夹带的倾盆大雨量也引发土石流 – 当局相信，死亡人数可能会再增加，因为许多人被认为已被活埋。 </p>
<p>Floodwaters are also threatening the country&#8217;s vital agricultural industry. <br />洪水也威胁该国重要的农产业。</p>
<p>Officials say 15,000 hectares of rice paddy fields have been damaged and tens of thousands more are under water. <br />官员说，1.5万公顷的稻田已被破坏，还有数万公倾泡在水里。 </p>
<p>The government has dispatched military helicopters to deliver supplies to villagers in remote areas. <br />政府派出军用直升机，运送物资给偏远地区的村民。 </p>
<p>The aircraft were also used to <B>evacuate</B> those who have been<B> isolated</B> by the floods.<br />飞机也被用来撤离因洪水而孤立无援的人们。</p>
<p>Sri Lanka is commonly<B> beset</B> by flooding as it<B> contends with </B>two monsoons per year.<br />斯里兰卡经常受洪水苦恼，因每年要应付两个季风。</p>
<p>The country&#8217;s <B>meteorology</B> department said they expect heavy rains to continue over the next two days.<br />该国气象局表示，他们预计未来两天仍会有豪大雨。<br />@*</p>
