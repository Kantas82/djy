[?mˋpr?zn] 监禁</p> <p>Chinese New Year is a time for celebration and family reunions. For Jin Zhaoyu, a Chinese woman living in Finland, that has been her greatest wish. <br />中国农历新年是庆祝及与家人团聚的时候。对居住芬兰的中国妇女金昭宇而言，这是她最大的心愿。</p> <p>[Jin Zhaoyu, Daughter of Persecuted Falun Gong Practitioner]:<br />“I miss my family the most. I miss my mother and my younger sister. This is the third year we have spent the New Year apart.”</p> <p>受迫害法轮功学员的女儿金昭宇说：“我最想念我的家人。我想念我的母亲和妹妹。 这是我们分开过年的第三个年头。”</p> <p>Jin’s family is just one many that have been <B>broken up</B>since the Chinese Communist Party began persecuting Falun Gong—a spiritual practice based on the principles of truth, compassion and tolerance. <br />自从中共开始迫害法轮功，金昭宇的家庭只是众多被迫分离的家庭之一。法轮功是基于真、善、忍原则的精神修炼。</p> <p>Originally from central Henan province, Jin last saw her mother Chen Zhenping and younger sister Jin Zhaoheng in 2008. Chen was<B> arrested</B> for practicing Falun Gong, just before the Beijing Olympics. She had been previously <B>detained</B> and<B> tortured</B> for speaking out against the persecution. <br />原本来自于河南省中部，金最后一次见到她的母亲陈真萍和妹妹金昭亨是在2008年。就在北京奥运会前，陈因修炼法轮功而被补。先前她因大声疾呼反对这场迫害，而遭到拘留及酷刑。</p> <p>[Jin Zhaoyu, Daughter of Persecuted Falun Gong Practitioner]:<br />“My mother is no longer able to chew, and can only take liquid food. Her teeth were all loosened after police tried to force feed her when she <B>went on</B> a hunger strike to protest the persecution in a labor camp.”<br />受迫害法轮功学员的女儿金昭宇说：“我母亲无法再咀嚼，只能喝流质食物。当她在劳教所持续绝食抗议迫害，在警方试图强行喂食之后，她的牙齿全部松动。”</p> <p>Henan authorities <B>sentenced</B> Chen to eight years in prison at a secret trial. Jin’s youngest sister escaped China and is now living in Thailand. <br />河南有关单位秘密审判陈真萍，判她八年牢狱。金最小的妹妹逃离中国，现居住在泰国。</p> <p>Jin visited Finland to see her husband’s family just before Chen’s arrest in 2008. She stayed in Finland and began a campaign to rescue her mother. Finnish media reported on her story, and<B> Amnesty International</B> issued an Urgent Action Appeal for her mother in early 2009. Last year, the rights group reported Chen was regularly beaten in prison and forcibly <B>injected with</B> drugs.<br />就在2008年陈真萍被逮捕前，金昭宇到访芬兰见她丈夫的家人。她留在芬兰，并开始拯救她母亲的运动。芬兰媒体报导她的故事，大赦国际在2009年初，为她母亲发布紧急呼吁行动。去年，人权组织报导陈真萍在狱中常遭殴打，并被强行注射药物。</p> <p>[Jin Zhaoyu, Daughter of Persecuted Falun Gong Practitioner]:<br />“My husband and I and Finnish Falun Gong practitioners will continue to <B>call on</B> western governments and community groups to rescue my mother. I plan to <B>sue</B> those responsible for my mother’s persecution at the international courts.”<br />受迫害法轮功学员的女儿金昭宇说：“我丈夫、我及芬兰法轮功学员，将继续呼吁西方政府和社会团体，拯救我的母亲。我打算在国际法庭上，控告那些迫害我母亲的人。”</p> <p>According to rights group, Falun Dafa Information Center, hundreds of thousands of Falun Gong practitioners are estimated to be<B> imprisoned</B> at any given time. Since the Chinese regime started the persecution in 1999, the center has documented more than 63-thousand accounts of torture and nearly 3,500 deaths.<br />根据人权团体法轮大法信息中心，估计有数十万法轮功学员随时会被监禁。自从中共政权于1999年开始迫害，该中心有文件证明超过 63,000名学员遭酷刑，近3,500人死亡。</p> <p>***<br /><B>【英文说文解字】</B></p> <p>各位读者新年好：</p> <p>今天英文说解字的部分，我们从中共迫害善良老百姓的手段中，常用强迫注射药物谈起（forcibly injected with drugs）。为什么 inject 这个字叫做注射呢？因为-ject这个动词字根意思是“throw,丢”的意思，因此inject就是to throw into 丢进去，“注射”就是把药物丢进身体里去。</p> <p>如果一个字根只能学会一个字，那这个字根也就不值得去学，就干脆学那个字就行了。但如果一个字根，可以让我们一下学会很多字，那就值得我们去认识它。 字根-ject如果配上不同的字首，就可以造出很多不同意义的字来喔，譬如配上pro-（之前）就成了project（计划），“计划”就是之前就要丢出来的东西。</p> <p>如果加上字首e-（out）就成了eject，to throw out，所以是弹出、射出的意思。字首re-是back之意，那配上-ject就成了reject（拒绝），to throw back，红包送出去又被丢回来，很明显地，就是被“拒绝”。</p> <p>还有object当动词是“反对”之意，因为ob-是against之意，to throw against所以是反对。<br />＠*</p> <p>本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p> <p>
