<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Egypt President Hosni Mubarak Steps Down<br />埃及总统穆巴拉克下台</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>reign</B>: [ren] n. 统治<br />2.	<B>grip </B>: [gr?p] n. 紧握<br />3.	<B>resolve </B>: [r?ˋzɑlv] v. 决心<br />4.	<B>outrage</B>: [ˋa?t?red?] v. 激怒<br />5.	<B>lift </B>: [l?ft] 解除<br />6.	<B>martial </B>: [ˋmɑr??l] adj. 军事的<br />7.	<B>grievance</B>: [ˋgriv?ns] n. 不满、抱怨<br />8.	<B>embattled</B>: [?mˋb?t!d] adj. 四面楚歌的<br />9.	<B>appear</B>: [?ˋp?r] v. 似乎<br />10.	<B>waive</B>: [wev] 放弃; 撤回</p> <p>Egypt&#8217;s President Hosni Mubarak stepped down on Friday. It comes after 18 days of protests in Cairo weakened his 30-year <B>grip</B> on power.<br />埃及总统胡斯尼?穆巴拉克周五下台，在开罗18天的抗议削弱了他30年的掌权。</p> <p>In a speech Thursday night, Mubarak had <B>resolved </B>to stay until the end of his term this year. Protestors were<B> outraged</B>; they had been expecting his resignation in that speech. That&#8217;s why it came to many as a surprise that Mubarak, in a brief televised appearance, suddenly stepped down today. Protestors cheered in the streets.<br />在周四晚上的演说中，穆巴拉克决定待到今年任期结束。抗议者被激怒了，他们本来预期在那场演讲中他会辞职。那就是为什么许多人非常惊讶，穆巴拉克今天短暂地出现在电视下台。抗议者在街上欢呼！</p> <p>Mubarak is the second Arab leader to resign this year. Tunisia&#8217;s president Zine al-Abidine Ben Ali resigned last month after protests rocked that country.<br />穆巴拉克是今年第二位阿拉伯领袖辞职。上个月，突尼西亚总统班阿里，在抗议行动蹂躙这个国家后辞职下台。</p> <p>In Egypt, Hossam Badrawi also stepped down. Just days ago Badrawi had been appointed to head the<B> embattled</B> ruling party, and had mistakenly predicted on television that Mubarak would resign Thursday night.<br />在埃及巴达维也下台了。就在几天前，巴达维被任命主掌四面楚歌的执政党，并错误地在电视上预测：穆巴拉克将会在周四晚辞职。</p> <p>Also today, Vice President Omar Suleiman<B> appeared </B>to waive</B> his right to take over the presidency, handing over power to the Supreme Council for the Armed Forces.<br />今天还有，副总统欧玛?苏雷曼似乎放弃接掌总统职位的权力，而将权力交接给军方的最高委员会。</p> <p>The Armed Forces then announced that they would<B> lift martial law </B>when the security allows. Martial law—one of protestors&#8217; biggest <B>grievances</B>—has been in place since 1981.<br />军方之后宣布，当安全情况允许时，他们将会废除戒严法。戒严法自1981年就颁布了，是抗议者最大不满中的一项。<br />@*<br />本则新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻<br />http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/ns_me/2011-02-11/635797531931.md#1</p> <p>***<br />本专栏由前卫英语李德良老师主编 www.davidlee.url.tw</p>
