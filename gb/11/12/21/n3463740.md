<p>Swedish Teen Wins Shanghai Model Competition<br />瑞典少女赢得上海模特儿竞赛<br /><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed634766.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Hustle </B> :[ˋh?s!] n. 喧嚣；奔忙<br />2.	<B>Win out</B> : ph. 获胜<br />3.	<B>Vie</B> : [va?] v. 竞争<br />4.	<B>Sought-after</B> : [ˋs?t??ft?] adj. 受欢迎的<br />5.	<B>Crown</B> : [kra?n] v. 荣登；为…加冕<br />6.	<B>Turn into </B> :ph. 使变成<br />7.	<B>Aspiring</B> : [?ˋspa?r??] adj. 有抱负的</p>
<p>15-year-old Julia Schneider is from a place far removed from the <B>hustle </B>of 21st century Shanghai: the small Swedish town of Gavle.<br />15岁的朱莉亚施耐德来自一个和21世纪喧嚣的上海相去甚远的地方：瑞典的耶夫勒小镇。</p>
<p>Yet she <B>won out </B>over competitors from 63 different countries, all gathered in China&#8217;s business hub to <B>vie</B> for one of modeling&#8217;s most <B>sought-after</B> prizes.<br />不过，她却能从63个不同国家的竞争对手中获胜。所有人聚集在这个中国的商业枢纽，争夺所有模特儿最向往的奖项之一。</p>
<p>[Julia Schneider, Model]:<br />&#8220;About winning today, it has been amazing. I am in shock and we will see what happens next.&#8221;<br />模特儿朱莉亚施耐德说：“赢得今天的比赛很吃惊。我还在震惊，而且期待接下来会发生的事。”</p>
<p>On Tuesday, Schneider was <B>crowned </B>the winner of the 2011 Elite Model Look World Final.<br />周二，施耐德荣登2011年精英模特大赛世界总决赛冠军。</p>
<p>The contest aims at discovering young talents who can be <B>turned into </B>international supermodels. Schneider gets a modeling contract as part of her prize.<br />这项比赛旨在发现可以成为国际超级名模的年轻人才。施耐德获奖的一部分还包含一个模特儿经纪合约。</p>
<p>Some major supermodels have gotten their start with Elite Model Look, including Claudia Schiffer, Naomi Campbell and Gisele Bundchen. It&#8217;s a list any <B>aspiring</B> model would be eager to join.<br />一些知名的超级名模都是从精英模特大赛开始他们的模特生涯，包括克劳迪娅希弗，纳奥米坎贝尔和吉赛尔邦辰。任何有抱负的模特儿都渴望名列其中。<br />@*</p>
