<p>China Ranks High for Bribery: Survey<br />调查显示：中国行赂排名居高</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed623817.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>rank</B>: [r??k] v.把…分等(评级)<br />2.	<B>bribery</B>: [ˋbra?b?r?] n.行贿、受贿<br />3.	<B>survey</B>: [s?ˋve]n.v.调查、研究<br />4.	<B>practice</B>: [ˋpr?kt?s] n.习惯、常规<br />5.	<B>transparency</B>: [tr?nsˋp?r?ns?]n.透明度<br />6.	<B>release</B>: [r?ˋlis]v发行、发表<br />7.	<B>perceive</B>: [p?ˋsiv] v.察觉、意识到<br />8.	<B>likelihood</B>: [ˋla?kl??h?d] n.可能性<br />9.	<B>offender</B>: [?ˋf?nd?] n.违法者、犯规者<br />10.	<B>parallel</B>: [ˋp?r??l?l]n.相似处、比较<br />11.	<B>measure</B>: [ˋm???] v.测量、计量<br />12.	<B>trustworthy</B>: [ˋtr?st?w?e?] adj.可信的、可靠的</p>
<p>According to a recent survey, a common business<B> practice</B> in China is bribery. The Berlin-based anticorruption group <B>Transparency</B> International <B>released</B> its fifth Bribe Payer’s Index. It ranks 28 of the world’s largest economies and ranks them according to the <B>perceived likelihood</B> of bribery.<br />根据最近一项调查显示，中国常见的商业运作是行贿。以柏林为总部的反腐败透明国际组织，公布第五次行贿指数。它评等世界最大的28个经济体，根据他们行贿可能性来做排名。</p>
<p>This year, the top<B> offenders</B> were Russia, followed closely by China. The results match a similar survey taken in 2008.<br />今年，行贿第一名的是俄罗斯，中国紧跟在后。结果与2008年所作的一项类似调查吻合。</p>
<p>The findings were based on a survey of 3,000 business executives worldwide on how likely they felt the use of bribery was in a given country.<br />这项结果是以全球3,000名企业主管作为调查基础，看他们感觉对特定国家行贿的可能性有多大。</p>
<p>Transparency International has drawn <B>parallels</B> between this survey and one from 2010 <B>measuring</B> perceived corruption. The same countries where the use of bribery is perceived to be high, also tends to have a less <B>trustworthy</B> view of officials.<br />国际透明组织已从这项调查及2010年衡量可能贪污的调查中，得到相似的结果。同样被觉得行贿可能性高的国家，其官员可信度也倾向较低。<br />@*</p>
