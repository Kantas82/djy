<p>Hong Kong’s Disneyland<B> unveils</B> the newly completed Toy Story Land, based off of the hit Toy Story trilogy. It’s the first of three<B> expansion</B> projects the<B> amusement </B>park is set to<B> release </B>by 2013. <br />香港迪士尼乐园推出新落成的“玩具总动员”的园区，基于正热门的“玩具总动员”三部曲。它是游乐园三项扩建案的第一项，将于2013启用。</p>
<p>The idea behind Toy Story land is to make visitors feel as if they’ve<B> shrunk </B>down to the size of a toy.<br />玩具总动员乐园的背后点子，是要让旅客觉得，他们仿佛缩小到与玩具同样大小的尺寸。</p>
<p>[Joe Lanzisero, Creative Sr. VP, Walt Disney Imagineering]:<br />“I think it&#8217;s really fun, really cool—the idea of being shrunk down to the size of a toy. You know I think what we do best is to create these environments that completely <B>immerse</B> you. When you walk into our parks and you&#8217;re no longer in the real world, you&#8217;re transported to a completely different place.”<br />华特迪士尼幻想工程创意部资深副总裁Joe Lanzisero说：<br />“我认为缩小到玩具尺寸的点子，真的很有趣，非常地酷。你知道我觉得我们做的最好的是，创造这些可以让你完全沈浸在其中的环境。当你走进我们的乐园，你不再处于现实世界中，你被转到另一个完全不同的地方。”</p>
<p>A visitor who tried the 82-feet &#8216;Toy Soldier<B> Parachute</B> Drop&#8217; enjoyed it.<br />一位尝试82英尺的“玩具士兵降落伞”的访客，非常喜爱这样子。</p>
<p>[Mandy Petty, Visitor]:<br />&#8220;It was fabulous, great fun! Great view of the park when you get up the top and it was just great dropping down. It wasn&#8217;t too scary, just within the<B> comfort </B>zone.&#8221; <br />游客Mandy Petty说：<br />“这真是太棒了，非常有趣！当你在顶端时，可以看到乐园很美的景观，降落时也很棒的。而且也不会太可怕，仍在舒适感的范围内。”</p>
<p>Hong Kong’s Disneyland is the smallest of the Disney parks. It<B> underperformed </B>in the years since its establishment. The Hong Kong government, that has a 52%<B> stake</B> in the park, is<B> eager</B> to<B> improve</B> its financial returns.<br />香港迪士尼乐园是迪斯尼乐园中最小的。它自成立以来，表现不尽理想。香港政府拥有乐园52％的股权，很渴望增加它的财务收益。</p>
<p>The park is hoping the expansion project, which will increase the parks size by 23%, will bring in more visitors.<br />迪士尼乐园希望这项增加23%面积的扩建项目，将会带来更多的游客。<br />@*</p>
