That’s one of the<B> ancient</B> codes of conduct of Korean archery which has been practiced for thousands of years. <br />行动力来自平静和善，这是韩国数千年来练习射箭人古老的行为准则。</p> <p>[Donghyung Cho, Secretary, Gyeryeong Archery Dao Club]: <br />“When I prepare to shoot I have to calm down and control my breathing. Good archery is not just shooting an arrow &#8211; it comes from focusing the mind. The correct physical action <B>originates</B> from internal stillness.” <br />也勇射箭之道俱乐部秘书周东永说：“当我准备射箭时，我必须把心静下来、控制呼吸，良好的箭术不只是把弓箭射出，必须专注心灵，正确的肢体动作源自于内心的平静。”</p> <p>When Donghyung Cho was a boy he lived near an archery field where he would often watch people training. <br />周东永孩童时期住在射箭场附近，常观看人们练习射箭。</p> <p>[Donghyung Cho, Secretary, Gyeryeong Archery Dao Club]: <br />“When I was 9 years old, I hoped to do archery like them (archers) so I tried to make a bow and arrow using bamboo and rubber bands.” <br />也勇射箭之道俱乐部秘书周东永说：“当我九岁的时候，希望能像那些人一样的射箭，所以用竹子及橡皮圈做了弓和箭。”</p> <p>40 years on, he’s now fulfilling his <B>childhood </B>dream. <br />四十年了，现在他实现他童年时期的梦想。</p> <p>[Donghyung Cho, Secretary, Gyeryeong Archery Dao Club]: <br />“I was really happy when I <B>rediscovered </B>archery. Now I’m playing with the real thing, I have a real bow and arrow like the soldiers of ancient days.” <br />周东永说：“当我再次发现射箭，真的很高兴，如今我是玩真的，我拥有真的弓和箭，就像古代士兵的弓箭一样。”</p> <p>He’s been playing for six years now. Cho was so<B> inspired</B> by archery; he convinced his wife to start as well. <br />至今他已经射箭六年了，周受到射箭的启发，也说服他的太太开始练习。</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B> recommend</B>: v. 推荐<br />2. <B>hobby</B>: n. 嗜好<br />3.<B> dominate</B>: v. 以…为主<br />4.<B> to get along with</B>: v.phr. 与～相处<br />5.<B> require</B>: v. 需要<br />6. <B>boom</B>: n. 景气、荣景</p> <p>[Donghyung Cho, Secretary, Gyeryeong Archery Dao Club]: <br />“I <B>recommended</B> it to my wife. Now we enjoy playing archery together and this<B> hobby</B> has given us more time to talk with each other, and to love each other more.” <br />周东永说：“我把它推荐给我太太，现在我们喜欢一起练习射箭，这个嗜好给了我们更多时间互相交谈，也更爱对方。”</p> <p>And their marriage was in need of a bit more love.<br />而他们的婚姻的确也需要多一点的爱。</p> <p>[Hyejeon Min, Archer, Dohyung Cho’s Wife]: <br />“I was unhappy with our marriage before, because my husband would stay out late and I was alone. After I started archery with him I became very happy in with marriage and there are no more problems.” <br />周东永的太太钟明烨说：“我以前觉得我们的婚姻不幸福，因为丈夫在外留到很晚，我很孤单，我和他开始一起射箭之后，我们的婚姻变得很快乐，问题都没有了。”</p> <p>But still, archery is a very a male<B> dominated</B> sport. <br />但是射箭仍然是一种以男性为主的运动。</p> <p>[Hyejeon Min, Female Archer]: <br />“There are only a few women archery players in Korea. I was told by my husband that there are women players. I’m very proud of myself being a woman and playing archery which is a male dominated sport.” <br />钟明烨说：“在韩国女的射箭选手很少，我丈夫说有女选手，我以身为女人自豪，且会玩以男性为主的射箭运动。”</p> <p>According to the president of the Gyeryeong Archery Dao Club, there are 50,000 members of the sport, and it’s growing each year. <br />根据也勇射箭之道俱乐部主席说，这项运动有五万名会员，而且每年持续增加中。</p> <p>[Pilhwa Kim, President, Gyeryeong Archery Club]:<br />“It’s good for health and a good tool <B>to get along with</B> others. This sport<B> requires</B> virtue.”<br />也勇射箭之道俱乐部主席金丕桦说：“射箭对健康有益，而且是和人相处的好运动，这项运动需要德性。”</p> <p>It’s not so surprising why there’s a recent <B>boom</B> in Korean archery here.<br />韩国兴起射箭热的原因一点也不令人讶异！</p> <p>Emma Hall, NTD, Gyeryeong, South Korea.<br />南韩新唐人电视台艾玛?霍尔</p> <p>***<br /><B>【听力加强训练】声音词组烙印</B></p> <p>1.
