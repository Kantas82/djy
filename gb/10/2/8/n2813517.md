<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>World’s Most Expensive Chicken</B><br />世上最昂贵的鸡<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D1371ee78d0e64fb4bef36a3319768e7f001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> <br />本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B> possession</B>: n. 资产<br />2. <B>criteria</B>: n. 标准<br />3. <B>intonation</B>: n. 音调<br />4. <B>vibration</B> : n. 振动<br />5.<B> bond</B> : n. 连结<br />6. <B>crow</B> : v. 啼叫<br />7. <B>hybrid</B> : a. 混血的<br />8.<B> offspring</B> : n. 后代<br />9.<B> domestic</B> : a. 家庭的、国内的<br />10. <B> mascot</B> : n. 吉祥物</p>
<p>It is the annual National Bekisar Chicken Competition in Jakarta, and dozens of Bekisar owners are waiting for the judges to take a look at their prized <B>possession</B> . <br />这是在雅加达的国家年度贝其沙鸡比赛，数十位贝其沙饲主正等着裁判见见他们引以为傲的资产。</p>
<p>How is the Bekisar judged? Although it looks like an ordinary chicken, the Bekisar has a distinct voice. So a Bekisar is mainly judged by its voice, which also determines its monetary value.<br />贝其沙如何评判呢？虽然看起来像普通的鸡，贝其沙的声音很好辨认。因此贝其沙主要是根居声音来做评判，也依此决定它的价格。</p>
<p>For ordinary people, all Bekisar chickens sound the same. But a professional can determine the quality of a Bekisar’s voice. Some of the <B>criteria </B> include<B> intonation</B>and <B>vibration</B>of the voice. <br />对一般人来说，所有贝其沙鸡听来都一样，但专家可以判断出贝其沙声音的品质。部分评判的标准包括根据音调及声音的振动。</p>
<p>Besides its beautiful voice, the Bekisar can form a special<B> bond </B> with its owner. It can sense what its owner is feeling.<br />除了美妙的声音，贝其沙与饲主间有着奇妙的连结。他能察觉到饲主的感觉。</p>
<p>[Joko – Bekisar Chicken Owner]:<br /> “He knew and he was familiar with anybody in my home. When I came home from my office and called his name, he always went looking for me. And if I tell him to crow</B> , he immediately sounds his beautiful crowing. So it looks like he has an ability to understand my heart.&#8221;<br />贝其沙鸡饲主悠可说：“他认识我家每个人而且和他们很亲近。当我从办公室回到家叫他的名字，他总是会跑过来看着我。如果我要他啼叫，马上就可以听到他美妙的声音。看起来就像他能了解我的心一样。”</p>
<p>The Bekisar is the first generation <B>hybrid offspring</B>of a male Green Jungle fowl and a <B>domestic</B>female game chicken. It is the<B> mascot</B>of East Java province.<br />贝其沙是雄绿野鸡和国内斗鸡的第一代混血。它是东爪哇省的吉祥物。@*<br /> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
