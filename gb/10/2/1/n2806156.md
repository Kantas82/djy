<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语(231)：神韵香港演出被迫取消</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Worldwide Protests Against Shen Yun Cancellation in Hong Kong</B><br />全球抗议神韵香港演出被迫取消</p>
<p><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D0dc23a6f197c4ae3979943db12cfe684001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> <br />本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语<br /><--ads--><br /><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B>Take place</B>: ph. 发生<br />2. <B> Embassy</B>:n. 大使馆<br />3. <B> Consulate</B>:n. 领事馆<br />4. <B> Upcoming </B>: adj. 即将来临的<br />5. <B> Dozens of </B>: ph. 许多<br />6.<B>Visa</B>:n. 签证<br />7. <B> Legal appeal</B>:ph. 提起上诉<br />8. <B> Press conference </B>: ph. 记者招待会<br />9.<B>Tactic</B>:n. 战术<br />10.<B>Despicable </B>: adj. 卑劣的<br />11.<B>Crucial </B>: adj. 关键的</p>
<p>STORY:<br />Protests are<B> taking place </B>outside Chinese <B>embassies</B> and consulates</B> around the world—after the Hong Kong government effectively forced Shen Yun Performing Arts to cancel its <B>upcoming</B> shows there.<br />抗议事件正在全世界的中领馆和中国大使馆外发生–因为香港政府突然强制取消即将在香港演出的神韵艺术团的表演。</p>
<p>Shen Yun is a Chinese dance and music company that has performed in <B>dozens of </B>cities around the world.<br />神韵是一个中国古典舞及音乐公司，曾在全世界数十个城市巡回表演。</p>
<p>But last week, the Hong Kong Immigration Department had denied entry <B>visas</B> to several of the company’s key production staff. The last-minute denial came less than a week before the sold-out shows were set to begin. And that left little time for<B> legal appeal</B>.<br />但就在上个星期，香港入境事务处拒绝核发入境签证给神韵公司的几位主要成员。这个最后一刻的拒绝通知，就发生在表演门票销售一空、剩不到一周就要开演前。几乎没有留下给人提出上诉的时间。</p>
<p>Shen Yun Stage Manager Gregory Xu was among those whose visas were denied. He spoke about it at a<B> press conference </B>in New York on Monday.<br />神韵舞台经理苏格瑞是被拒发签证的其中一位。星期一时他在纽约的记者会上发言。</p>
<p>[Gregory Xu, Shen Yun Performing Arts Stage Manager]:<br />&#8220;This is my first encounter with the Hong Kong government. They used delay<B> tactics</B>. We could say they used a very <B>despicable </B>method. At the last moment, in the last few days, they refused visas for our <B>crucial </B>technical staff, in order to reach their aim of not allowing us to enter Hong Kong.&#8221;<br />[苏格瑞，神韵艺术团舞台经理]：<br />“这是我第一次遇见香港政府。他们使用拖延战术。他们使用一种非常卑劣的方法：到最后一刻，在最后剩下没几天，才拒发签证给我们关键技术同仁，借此达到不让我们进入香港的目的。”</p>
<p>Protests over the visa refusal are now taking place around the world.<br />对于这件拒发签证的事件，全世界正掀起了抗议声浪。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />12. <B> Demonstration </B>: n. 示威<br />13.<B>Directive </B>: n. 指示; 命令<br />14.<B>Adhere to</B>:ph. 坚持<br />15.<B>Withstand</B>:v. 抵挡<br />16.<B>Revive</B>:v. 复苏<br />17.<B>Suppress</B>:v. 打压<br />18.<B>Box office </B>: ph. 售票处</p>
<p>On Saturday, protestors held a<B> demonstration </B>outside the Chinese Consulate in Munich, Germany.<br />在周六，抗议人士在德国慕尼黑的中领馆前举行示威。</p>
<p>And on Monday, there was a similar protest outside the Chinese Embassy in Canada.<br />而在周一，加拿大的中国大使馆外，也有类似的抗议活动。</p>
<p>In London, one woman at a protest there says her friends in mainland China had already purchased tickets to see the Hong Kong show.<br />在伦敦，抗议现场的一名女士说，他在中国大陆的朋友已经买好票准备要去香港看演出了。</p>
<p>[Ms. Gao]:<br />&#8220;The Chinese government must have put a lot of pressure on the Hong Kong government, who could only have done such a thing under their <B>directive</B>. I hope that the Hong Kong government can <B>adhere to </B>the principle of &#8216;one country two systems,&#8217; and <B>withstand </B>the pressures of the Chinese communist government.&#8221;<br />[高女士]：<br />“中国政府一定对港府施加了很多压力。在它们的指示下，它们只能做这样的事情。我希望香港政府能够坚持‘一国两制’的原则，不要屈服中共政府的压力。”</p>
<p>Protestors in London held a banner saying the Chinese Communist Party was behind the visa denials in Hong Kong.<br />在伦敦的抗议人士举着横幅写着，中共才是香港拒发签证背后的主谋。</p>
<p>Shen Yun&#8217;s mission is to <B>revive </B>traditional Chinese culture, which has been <B>suppressed</B> under the Chinese Communist Party. Some of its performances also touch on the Communist Party&#8217;s persecution of the Falun Gong spiritual movement. And that could be why the Communist Party may want to suppress the show in Hong Kong.<br />神韵的任务，是要复苏过去一直被中共打压的传统中华文化。神韵当中有一些触及中共迫害法轮功精神运动的表演，这可能是中共想要阻挠香港演出的原因。</p>
<p>Shen Yun Performing Arts was set to perform in Hong Kong from January 27th through the 31st. All seven shows had sold out within a week after the <B>box office</B> opened.<br />神韵艺术团原定在1月27至31日在香港演出。所有的七场表演，在售票处开张后的一个星期之内全数售完。</p>
