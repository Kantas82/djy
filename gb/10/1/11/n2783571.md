<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语(224)：氢气自行车减少碳排放</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Japan: Hydrogen Bicycles to Reduce Carbon Emissions </B><br />日本：氢气自行车将可减少碳排放<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D89ae6688d1964648852c5a51c1efeaa4001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /><br />本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻<br /><--ads--><br /><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B> carbon </B>：n. 化学元素 &#8212; 碳<br />2. <B>hydrogen</B> ：n. 化学元素 &#8212; 氢<br />3. <B>replace </B>：v. 取代、替代<br />4. <B>pedal</B>： v. 踩踏板，骑(脚踏车)<br />5.<B> fuel </B>：n. 燃料<br />6.<B> onboard</B>： adj. 附载在车上的</p> <p>In an effort to reduce <B>carbon </B>emissions, airport staff is testing <B>hydrogen </B>powered bicycles to<B> replace</B> cars to get around the airport facility.<br />为了减少碳的排放，机场的工作人员正在测试，用氢动力自行车来代替汽车在机场里穿梭。</p> <p>[Yuko Tsubone, Airport Employee]:<br />“Because (the airport) is really large, it makes it easier to get around. I also think it&#8217;s really nice since you can<B> pedal </B>without having to put in a lot of effort, and so you don&#8217;t get tired.”</p> <p>[Yuko Tsubone, 机场员工]:<br />“因为（机场）实在是太大了，所以这样穿梭在机场里变得更轻松。我也认为这是非常好的，因为你可以踩踏板而不必耗费许多力气，所以你不会感觉到很累。”</p> <p>The bicycle uses a hydrogen<B> fuel</B> cell to charge the <B>onboard </B>battery, similar to that used in an electric assisted bicycle. The system allows users to quickly fill up on hydrogen instead of waiting for a battery to charge. <br />自行车采用氢气燃料电池，来充车上电池的电，类似用在电动自行车上的电池。这种系统可让使用者快速填补氢气，而不用再等待电池充电。</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />7. <B>oxygen</B> ：n. 化学元素 &#8212; 氧<br />8. <B>convert </B>：v. 转换<br />9. <B>cartridge</B> ：n. 在此为盛装氢气的器具<br />10. <B>emit</B>： v. 排放<br />11.<B> steam </B>：n. 水蒸气<br />12. <B>carbon dioxide</B>：n. 二氧化碳</p> <p>The fuel cell generates electricity through an electrochemical reaction. Hydrogen is broken down and then combined with<B> oxygen converting </B>chemical energy into electrical energy.<br />燃料电池是经由电化学反应来产生电力。氢气先被分解，然后与氧气结合，这样的反应可使化学能转换为电能。</p> <p>[Yusuke Nagai, Hydrogen Energy Dept., Iwatani Corp.]:<br />“The bicycle has a hydrogen<B> cartridge </B>and fuel cell inside. The hydrogen is sent to the fuel cell, and the electricity that is produced is then used to provide the power to assist pedaling.”<br />[Yusuke Nagai, 氢气能源部门, Iwatani 公司]:<br />自行车内部有着装载氢气的器具和燃料电池。氢气被传送到燃料电池里，并且产生电力，然后所产生的能量可协助骑自行车。</p> <p>The main benefit of using hydrogen as the energy source for the bicycles is that it is 100 percent environmentally friendly. The only waste product is water which <B>emits </B>only <B>steam</B> rather than <B>carbon dioxide</B>.<br />使用氢气作为自行车动力的来源，主要的好处是，它是百分之百环保的。唯一会产生的废物是水，它排放的只有水蒸气，而不是二氧化碳。<br />@*</p> <p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
