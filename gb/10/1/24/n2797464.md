<p>Director James Cameron has only himself to beat at the box office. Avatar, the sci-fi fantasy epic is smashing records&#8211;opening number one at the box office for four straight weekends.<br />导演詹姆斯柯麦隆所创的票房纪录无人能及。阿凡达这部科幻动作史诗电影正刷新纪录&#8212;开创了蝉联四周的票房冠-x。</p>
<p>Avatar has already railed in 1.3 billion dollars worldwide so far, making it the second highest grossing movie ever made.<br />阿凡达目前在全球已赚进了13亿美元，使本片成为史上第二卖座的电影。</p>
<p>And at that top spot is James Cameron’s other epic, Titanic, which sits at 1.8 billion.<br />稳居第一名宝座的是詹姆斯柯麦隆的另一部史诗电影&#8212;铁达尼号，票房高达18亿美元。</p>
<p>As lead actor Sam Worthington explains it, a former marine gets to drive a remotely controlled body on a far away planet and in the process falls in love with a beautiful 10 foot tall blue alien.<br />正如男主角山姆．沃辛顿所言，一位退役的海军陆战队队员必须操控远方的一具躯体，它远在一个遥远的行星，并且在这过程中，他和一个美丽的10呎高的蓝色外星人坠入爱河。</p>
<p>The film was four and a half years in the making, including creating the groundbreaking technology that captures the actors performance and animates it into the avatars. <br />这部电影费时四年半拍摄，包括开创了独步全球的科技，能够撷取演-莾怐荦t出，并绘制成为阿凡达的动画。</p>
<p>The movie’s now gathering Oscar buzz, already nominated for best picture and best director. Now we’re wondering what’s next for this director? <br />现今这部电影的奥斯卡呼声很高，已经获得最佳影片和最佳导演的提名。现在我们感到好奇的是这位导演的下一部片子是什么?</p>
