<p><b>Sotheby&#8217;s Hong Kong Auction Brings Record Sales</b><br /> 苏富比香港拍卖创新高<br /> <embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="450" b="358"></embed></p> <p>本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻:<br /> http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/ns_life/2010-04-15/009880807166.md#1</p> <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br /> 1. <b> auction</b>（n）：拍卖<br /> 2. <b> indication</b>（n）：指示<br /> 3. <b> buoy</b>（v）：鼓励<br /> 4. <b> bronze</b>（adj.）：青铜制的<br /> 5. <b> fetch</b>（v）：卖得<br /> 6. <b> residual</b>（adj.）：剩余的<br /> 7. <b> dominate</b>（v）：支配<br /> 8. <b> shrink</b>（v）：减少<br /> 9. <b> competitor</b>（n）：竞争者<br /> 10. <b> imperial</b>（adj.）：王室的<br /> The two major art <b>auctions</b> in Hong Kong are a good<b> indication </b>on how the art market in Asia is performing.<br /> 在香港的两次重要的艺术拍卖，为亚洲艺术市场的经营方向，提供了很好的指标。</p> <p>Christie&#8217;s auction house is preparing for its May auction <b>buoyed </b>by Sotheby’s recent success.<br /> 佳士得拍卖公司，受到苏富比最近拍卖成功的鼓舞，正为五月的拍卖做准备。</p> <p>Sotheby’s spring auction was a world record, with sales of $256 million.<br /> 苏富比春季拍卖创下了2亿5千6百万美元的世界纪录。</p> <p>Christie’s will put up around two and a half thousand items for auction.<br /> 佳士得将会投入大约2500件艺品来拍卖。</p> <p>An early Ming Dynasty<b> bronze</b> figure is expected to <b>fetch</b> over $4.5 million dollars, while a pair of Beijing imperial 18th century vases could reach millions of dollars.<br /> 一件清初的青铜像预计可以卖得超过450万美元，而一对18世纪的北京王室花瓶可能高达数百万美元。</p> <p>[Jonathan Stone, Christie’s Asia Managing Director]:<br /> &#8220;There is really <b>residual </b>demand worldwide for Asian works of art. And I think as Asia becomes to<b> dominate</b> the world stage more, politically and economically and financially, there will also be much more interest from the West and a growing interest in Asian art.&#8221;<br /> 佳士得的亚洲总经理Jonathan Stone说：“全球对亚洲艺品的需求过剩，同时我认为不管在政治上、经济上、财政上，当亚洲更加掌握世界的舞台时，西方也将对亚洲的艺术产生更浓厚的兴趣。”</p> <p>As the supply of top artwork <b>shrinks</b> and demand remains strong for rare items, there is a real possibility that Christie’s auction could go one better than Sotheby’s.<br /> 由于顶级艺术品供应减少，同时对于稀有艺品的需求依然强烈，所以佳士得的拍卖是真的有可能较苏富比略胜一筹。</p> <p>[Jonathan Stone, Christie’s Asia Managing Director]:<br /> “The art market, particularly in Asia, has rebounded very strongly. And indeed the results of our<b> competitors </b>a week ago is a very strong indicator of that and really gives us great expectations for out forthcoming sale at the end of May.&#8221;<br /> 佳士得的亚洲总经理Jonathan Stone又说：“艺品市场，尤其是在亚洲，反弹回升非常强烈。而事实上，我们的竞争对手一个星期前的拍卖结果，便是一个非常强而有力的指标，并且让我们为五月底即将进行的拍卖寄予厚望。”</p> <p>But whether Christie’s can beat the imperial pearl necklace that Sotheby&#8217;s sold for $8.75 million or the white jade imperial seal for article content begin -->2.3 million remains to seen.<br /> 但是，佳士得是否能击败苏富比以875万美元成交的王室珍珠项链，或是1230万美元售出的白色玉玺，且拭目以待。<br /> @*</p> <p><span style="color: #ffffff;">(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</span></p>
