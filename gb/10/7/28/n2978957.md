<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自己动手做西式餐点-炸洋葱圈</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>今天，做了炸洋葱圈，炸的过程中，女儿就开始吃了，一直说好吃，我心里也很高兴。有时得到别人的认可也是一种幸福哦。</p>
<p>原料：<br />洋葱、面粉、鸡蛋、面包糠。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280420581469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280420571469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12">2</span></div>
<p><!-- --><br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280420561469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />做法：<br />1、洋葱一个，将洋葱洗净沥干，切成圆片，再剥开成洋葱圈。一圈圈洋葱之间会有一层薄薄的膜，也要去掉。哈哈，大圈小圈落案板哦。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280420591469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12">4</span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />2、配料有：面粉、鸡蛋、面包糠（可以自己用面包制作，我不想费事了，昨天在超市买了一袋。）面粉3大杓，鸡蛋1个，水2适量调成糊。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280421001469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --><br />3、先将洋葱圈沾上面粉，抖掉多余的，再沾上面糊，最后在面包糠里打个滚，然后放入预热好的油锅里，用筷子小心的翻面，至洋葱圈浮起，外表金黄，捞出，油沥干后装盘。配番茄沙司和其他果酱都不错，我们蘸的是超市买的番茄沙司。</p>
<p>洋葱圈做好了。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/07/1007280421311469-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
