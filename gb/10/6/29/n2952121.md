<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B> debut</B> : n. 亮相；登台<br />2.	<B> queue </B> :v. 排队<br />3.	<B> snap up</B> : ph. 抢购<br />4.	<B> coveted</B> : adj. 梦寐以求的<br />5.	<B> suspend</B> : v. 暂停<br />6.	<B> straight</B> : adv. 连续不断地<br />7.	<B> boost</B> : v. 使提高<br />8.	<B> margin</B> : n. 利润率<br />9.	<B> a slew of </B> :ph. 许多<br />10.	<B> advantage over</B> : ph. 优于<br />11.	<B> pixel</B> : n. 像素<br />12.	<B> shipment </B> :n. 出货</p>
<p>STORY:<br />Fans <B>queued</B> outside Apple&#8217;s store in Japan Thursday, as well local telecom Softbank, to <B>snap up</B> the latest version of its wildly popular iPhone.<br />周四日本的粉丝们在苹果专门店外排队，和当地电信软银，准备抢购广受欢迎的iphone最新版。</p>
<p>Japanese customers were the first globally to get hold of the <B>coveted </B>iPhone 4, which debuts in the U.S., France, Germany and the UK later on Thursday.<br />日本是全世界第一批可以拥有梦寐以求的iphone 4的地区，iPhone 4在周四随后也在美国、法国、德国、英文亮相。</p>
<p>Sales blew past expectations, with over 600,000 sold in just 24 hours of pre-orders, which Softbank <B>suspended</B> after three days due to overwhelming demand.<br />销售业绩超出市场预期，24小时前预订就有超过60万支售出，其中由于太过庞大的下单量，还导致软银暂停3天。</p>
<p>High school student Akira Nakazawa had waited in Tokyo&#8217;s Ginza district through heavy rains for more than a day.<br />高中生彰泽在东京银座的大雨中等候了超过一天。</p>
<p>[Akira Nakazawa, High School Student male]:<br />&#8220;I&#8217;ve been waiting for almost 33 hours <B>straight</B> since two days ago from 10 p.m. on the 22nd. The hardest part was a hard rain during yesterday. I had two umbrellas to protect my stuff and myself.&#8221; <br />[彰泽，男高中生]：<br />“我已经连续等了将近33个小时，因为我从前两天22日晚上10点就开始等了。最辛苦的部分是昨天有一场大雨。不过我有准备两支雨伞来保护我的东西，以及帮自己挡雨。”</p>
<p>The iPhone, launched in 2007, has <B>boosted </B>Apple&#8217;s <B>margins </B>but faces <B>a slew of</B> new smartphone competitors.<br />iPhone自2007年问世以来，大大提高了苹果公司的获利率，但也使苹果开始面临一系列新智能手机的竞争对手。</p>
<p>The device comes less than a month after Apple&#8217;s iPad began sales outside the U.S., which has already sold 3 million units worldwide.<br />这支iPhone 4在苹果的另一项产品iPad、开始在美国地区以外销售不到一个月的时间后推出。而iPad已经在全球售出三百万套了。</p>
<p>This customer says the new iPhone has<B> advantages over</B> the Apple tablet.<br />这位顾客表示，新版的iPhone有许多比苹果平板 (iPad) 好的优点。</p>
<p>[Roy Hoshino, iPhone Customer male]:<br />&#8220;Well, I can feel that<B> pixel</B> is much more than 3GS &#8212; beautiful, much more beautiful than iPad. No complaint about it.&#8221; <br />[罗伊星野，iPhone男顾客]：<br />“嗯，我可以感受画面解析度比3GS好多了。很漂亮，比iPad漂亮的多。无可挑剔。”<br />@*</p>
