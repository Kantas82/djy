it is the manufacturing base for millions of high tech goods &#8211; and home to 300 hundred thousand workers who live on one of the biggest production sites in the world. <br />一半工厂，一半城市&#8211;这里是数百万高科技产品的制造基地，也是世界上最大的生产地点之一，有3万劳工在这儿居住。</p> <p>But the electronics firm has been rocked this year by the suicides of twelveemployees jumping to their deaths from buildings with two further falls causing workers serious injuries. <br />但这家电子公司今年因为员工跳楼自杀事件，造成有12人死亡及两人受重伤。</p> <p>All were aged under 25 and had been with the firm for less than a year. <br />他们全都在25岁以下，且到职都未满一年。</p> <p>Now the spot light is falling on the working conditions here as the world&#8217;s <B>insatiable </B>demand for high tech electronic goods at low price clashes with the methods used by companies like this to produce them. <br />现在镁光灯都聚焦到这里的工作状况，因为世界的对低价高科技产品永不满足的需求，与像这样的制造公司所使用的方法显然有冲突。</p> <p>Workers have criticised the company&#8217;s management following the most recent suicide.<br />在最近一次的自杀事件后，员工们就一直批评公司的管理。</p> <p>[Unidentified Foxconn Worker]:<br />&#8220;Foxconn&#8217;s management is totally inhumane. The personal quality of lower management are very low.&#8221; <br />“不具名的富士康员工”：<br />“富士康的管理很没有人性。基层的管理人员非常没有素质。”</p> <p>[Unidentified Foxconn Worker]: <br />&#8220;To put it a bit more<B> bluntly</B>, it feels like<B> mistreatment</B>. They don&#8217;t regard the workers as humans. That&#8217;s how they <B>cultivate </B>new employees in this company.&#8221;<br />“不具名的富士康员工”：<br />“坦白说，那感觉像虐待。他们不把员工当人看。这就是他们培育新进员工的方式。”</p> <p>Following the most recent death, Foxconn has organized <B>recreational </B>activities, opened a counselling center and set up a hotline for workers to call with their problems. <br />在最近死亡事件之后，富士康已组织了娱乐活动、开设心理咨询中心、及设置员工热线表达他们的问题。</p> <p>Analysts have warned that clients may reconsider their contracts with the company if it doesn&#8217;t address their workers&#8217; issues.<br />分析家警告：如果不能解决员工的问题，当事人可以重新考虑他们与公司的合约。<br />@*</p> <p><p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
