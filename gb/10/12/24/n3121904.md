<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>penguin</B>: [ˋp?ngw?n]n. 企鹅<br />2.	<B>reindeer</B>: [ˋren?dir] n. 驯鹿<br />3.	<B>costume</B>: [ˋkɑstjum] n. 服装<br />4.	<B>outfit</B>: [ˋa?t?f?t] n. 全套服装<br />5.	<B>thrill</B>: [θr?l] v. 使兴奋, 使激动<br />6.	<B>amusement</B>: [?ˋmjuzm?nt] 娱乐<br />7.	<B>waddle</B>: [ˋwɑdl] v. 摇摇摆摆地走<br />8.	<B>parade</B>:[p?ˋred]n. 游行</p>
<p>Christmas is coming soon, and in South Korea a family of penguins dressed in Santa and <B>reindeer costumes</B> bring holiday cheer and spirit to the children.<br />圣诞节快要到了，南韩一个企鹅家族穿上圣诞老人和驯鹿的服装，带给孩子们假日的欢笑与精神。</p>
<p>On Tuesday, at an <B>amusement </B>park near Seoul, children welcomed the penguins as they <B>waddled</B> down the park’s decorated holiday avenue.<br />周二，在首尔附近的娱乐公园，孩童们在公园内，欢迎企鹅们摇摇摆摆地走在有着节日装饰的大道上。</p>
<p>[Park Mee-Hwa, Zoo Trainer]:<br />“We&#8217;re here with children to welcome Christmas with penguin friends in Santa and Rudolph<B> outfits</B>.”<br />动物园的训练师说：“我们在此和孩子们，与穿上全套圣诞老人与驯鹿鲁道夫服装的企鹅们，一块欢迎耶诞节。”</p>
<p>Kids were <B>thrilled</B> to see the penguins.<br />孩子们看到企鹅都很兴奋。</p>
<p>[Lim Yoo-Jin]:<br />“It reminds me of Christmas and they are very pretty and cute.”<br />企鹅提醒我耶诞节，而且它们非常漂亮且可爱。</p>
<p>[Lee Do-Eun]:<br />“It&#8217;s so exciting to see penguins in Santa outfits.”<br />看到穿着圣诞老人装扮的企鹅，好兴奋！</p>
<p>The penguins are set to welcome park visitors everyday until Christmas with a <B>mini-parade</B>.<br />企鹅们预定会每天以小游行的方式，来欢迎动物园的访客，一直到耶诞节。</p>
<p>Christmas is a major holiday in South Korea, home to one of Asia&#8217;s largest Christian populations.<br />耶诞节在南韩是主要假日，南韩有亚洲最多的基督徒人口。</p>

<p>【佳句精选】<br />It&#8217;s so exciting to see penguins in Santa outfits.<br />看到穿着圣诞老人装扮的企鹅，好兴奋！<br />@*</p>
