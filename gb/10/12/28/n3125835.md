<p>Journalists and cameras waited to capture Julian Assange as he arrived at a London court, but missed him as he<B> snuck</B> in a side entrance.<br />记者和录影机在伦敦法庭外等着亚桑杰的到来，但没见到他，因他悄悄从侧门进入。</p>
<p>The WikiLeaks founder handed himself in to British police on Tuesday in response to a <B>warrant </B>for his arrest issued in Sweden over <B>alleged</B> sex crimes.<br />周二，维基解密创办人向英国警方自首，回应瑞典指控他涉嫌性犯罪所发出的通辑令。</p>
<p>He denies all allegations.<br />他否认了所有指控。</p>
<p>The City of Westminster Magistrate&#8217;s court is likely to set a date for an <B>extradition</B> hearing which must take place within 21 days of his arrest.<br />威斯敏斯特市裁判法院可能会设一个引渡听证会日期，这必须在被他逮捕后21天内举行。</p>
<p>Assange&#8217;s lawyer believes the legal pressure on him has been <B>disproportionate</B>.<br />亚桑杰的律师认为，法律施加给他的压力已不成比例。</p>
<p>[Jennifer Robinson, Lawyer for Julian Assange]:<br />&#8220;Now instead of even seeking a formal <B>summons</B> for his <B>interrogation</B> she has gone direct for an<B> arrest warrant</B> so we absolutely think it is disproportionate and we don&#8217;t even have the full evidence. Mr Assange still has not seen the full allegations against him or the potential charges he faces in a language which he understands which is English and this in clear <B>breach</B> of his human rights under the European Convention of Human Rights.&#8221;<br />[Jennifer Robinson, Lawyer for Julian Assange] [Jennifer Robinson，亚桑杰律师说]：<br />“现在甚至已不寻求正式传唤审问他，她（法官）已直接签拘票，所以我们绝对认为这是不相称的，我们甚至没有充分的证据。亚桑杰先生尚未看到对他的完整指控，或他可能面临的指控，他理解的语言是英语，这显然违反欧洲人权公约。“</p>
<p>Assange&#8217;s WikiLeaks website is at the center of a <B>row</B> over the release of 250,000 secret U.S. diplomatic cables and has <B>provoked </B>fury in Washington with its publications.<br />亚桑杰的维基解密网站正处于争论之中，因泄露美国25万笔的秘密外交电讯，并将之公布，而引起华盛顿的愤怒。</p>
<p>His legal team has said it will mount a challenge in the British courts to any Swedish attempt to have the Australian citizen extradited.<br />他的律师团表示，对于任何瑞典企图引渡这位澳大利亚公民，这将会是英国法庭的一大挑战。<br />@*</p>
