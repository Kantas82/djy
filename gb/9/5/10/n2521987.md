<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>被真理唤醒的心(9)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>在中国大陆，法轮大法修炼者们每天清晨在住家附近的公园、校园、体育中心、河畔……集体炼功。</p>
<p>炼完功后孩子上学，大人上班，老人回家或到菜市场买菜。</p>
<p>学了一段时间《转法轮》后，我到我租住公寓附近的中山大学法轮大法炼功点学炼功。这所大学的很多教职工和学生每天早晨都在这里炼功。</p>
<p>炼功点上的辅导员，一位中年男士，免费教我炼功。</p>
<p>我动作笨拙，但辅导员一直很友善，每个动作都耐心的辅导到位。</p>
<p>我学了没炼、很快忘了、又回到炼功点请辅导员教。他友善耐心的从头又教了我一遍。</p>
<p>结果我又学了没炼、又忘了、又回到炼功点请他教。他仍然友善耐心的教我，一句埋怨的话都没有。</p>
<p>我感动的流下眼泪，在心里对自己说：“我找到了一块净土。”（待续）</p>
<p>(英文对照)</p>
<p>In mainland China, Falun Dafa practitioners practiced the exercises together every morning in the places near their homes, such as parks, campuses, sports centers, the banks of the rivers…</p>
<p>Upon finishing the practice, children practitioners went to school, adult practitioners went to work, and elderly practitioners went home or went shopping for the family.</p>
<p>After reading Zhuan Falun for some time, I went to one of the Falun Dafa practice sites on the campus of Zhongshan University, where was near my rental flat, to learn the exercises. Numerous teachers and students from the university practiced together every morning. </p>
<p>The volunteer assistant of the practice site, a middle-aged gentleman, taught me the exercises free of charge.</p>
<p>My movements were awkward, but the assistant was very nice and taught me every movement patiently. </p>
<p>I didn’t practice the exercises upon learning it, I soon forgot it and went to the practice site to request the assistant to teach me again. He taught me the two hours of exercises all over again kindly and patiently.</p>
<p>Again, I didn’t practice upon learning and forgot and went to the practice site to request the assistant to teach me. He taught me with the same kindness and patience without a word of complaint.</p>
<p>I was moved to tears. I whispered to myself, “I’ve found a pure land.”<br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
