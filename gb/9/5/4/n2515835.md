<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语(161)：墨西哥面临致命流感</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B> Mexico Confronts Deadly Flu Virus</B><br />墨西哥面临致命流感病毒</p> <p>本则英语影音新闻及出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻 <ahref= >http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/ns_na/2009-04-25/752524565846.md#1<br /></a>（可线上观看）</p> <p><B> 【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B> confront</B>： v. 面临; 遭遇<br />2.<B> strain</B>： n. 品种<br />3. <B>bid </B>：n. 企图; 努力<br />4.<B> halt</B>： v. 使停止<br />5.<B> commuter</B>： n. 通勤者<br />6.<B> suspend</B>： v. 中止<br />7.<B> at once </B>：ph. 立刻<br />8.<B> severity</B>： n. 严重<br />9. <B>decided </B>：adj. 明确的<br />10.<B> genetically </B>：adv. 基因上; 遗传学上<br />11.<B> sanitary</B>：adj.卫生的<br />12.<B> swine flu</B>： ph. 猪流感<br />13.<B> suspected </B>：adj. 有嫌疑的</p> <p>A deadly new flu <B>strain</B> has killed at least 68 people in Mexico.<br />一种致命的新型流感品种，已在墨西哥致至少六十八人丧生。</p> <p>In a <B>bid</B> to <B>halt</B> the virus from spreading the government has closed schools, museums and other public buildings.<br />为了防止病毒散播，政府关闭了学校、博物馆，以及各公共大楼。</p> <p>Health officials have warned the virus can spread in crowded and enclosed spaces.<br />健保官员已警告，病毒会在群众聚集且封闭的空间当中散播。</p> <p><B>Commuters</B> on Mexico&#8217;s busy subway wore masks given to them by local officials.<br />墨西哥忙碌的地下铁通勤族，都戴上了由当地官员发给的口罩。</p> <p>Some here are concerned about the situation.<br />有些人相当忧虑这样的情况。</p> <p>[Jose Lui Huerta, Resident]: <br />&#8220;I consider it dangerous; the situation must be very serious, they <B>suspended</B> work and classes. We are worried because they are not telling us the truth and they should tell us <B>at once</B> what is really happening.&#8221;<br />[居民琼斯卢荷塔]：<br />“我觉得很危险，这个情况一定非常严重，大家都停班停课了。我们很担心，因为相关单位并不告诉我们真相，它们应该要立刻告诉我们现在真正发生了什么事。”</p> <p>The government have also called in the army to help give out more masks.<br />政府也已经要求军方来协助发放更多的口罩。</p> <p>Mexican President Felipe Calderon said the issue will be resolved.<br />墨西哥总统卡德隆说，这个问题将得到解决。</p> <p>[Felipe Caldero, Mexican President]:<br />&#8220;We are aware of the <B>severity</B> of the problem. However, I am sure that we will be able to resolve it, as is our duty, with the <B>decided</B> support of the population and with the contributions and decisions taken by all of the country&#8217;s sanitary and health institutions.&#8221;<br />[墨西哥总统费利佩卡尔德龙]：<br />“我们察觉到问题的严重性。但，我确定我们能够解决，这是我们的责任，我们有来自民众明确的支援，以及全国所有的公共卫生单位及健保机构的决策和奉献。”</p> <p>The World Health Organisation said the virus is the same <B>genetically</B> as a new strain of <B>swine flu</B>.<br />世界卫生组织表示，这个病毒在基因上是一种新品种的猪流感。</p> <p>At least eight people have been infected with it in the United States and more than 1,000 <B>suspected</B> cases have been reported in Mexico.<br />在美国至少有八人已被感染，而在墨西哥据报有超过一千个疑似感染案例。<br />@*</p> <p><p><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
