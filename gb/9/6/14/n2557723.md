<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>被真理唤醒的心(41)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>一天晚上，北京弟子小曹突然打电话给我说有急事。</p>
<p>我坐上一辆出租车，一上车就和司机聊天。</p>
<p>“北京交通真拥挤。”</p>
<p>“嗯”中年司机面无表情。</p>
<p>“我在北京读大学时没这么多车。”</p>
<p>“你在哪个大学？”</p>
<p>“北二外。”</p>
<p>“……”</p>
<p>“您知道我现在为什么来北京吗？”</p>
<p>“为什么？”</p>
<p>“我是炼法轮功的，来北京为法轮功请愿，因为现在政府在迫害法轮功。”</p>
<p>“你再说我把你拉派出所去。”</p>
<p>“如果您想拉我到派出所，随便。但我现在对您讲的是真事，正在发生在这个国家的真事。我并不是在诽谤政府，我只是在告诉您从官方媒体无法知道的真相。我是在发自内心的和您说话，先生。”</p>
<p>司机突然看着我笑了，“我觉的你们炼法轮功的人真神！我觉的你也挺神！”</p>
<p>我开始给他讲大法如何拯救了我。</p>
<p>我真诚的和他分享我的人生故事，毫不理会他是否正开往派出所。</p>
<p>他听着突然憨憨一笑，“很感人！”然后突然叫了起来，“哎呀糟了！我听你讲的太入神，走错道儿了！真对不起！真对不起！”</p>
<p>他一个劲儿道歉，态度比二十分钟前我刚上车时好多了。<br />（待续）<br />(英文对照)</p>
<p><p>One night, a Beijing fellow practitioner, Cao, suddenly called me and said he needed my help urgently.</p>
<p>Upon getting in a taxi, I struck up a conversation with the driver. </p>
<p>“The traffic in Beijing is really heavy.”</p>
<p>“mm…” The middle-aged driver was expressionless.</p>
<p>“There weren’t so many vehicles when I was a university student in Beijing.”<br />“Which university did you attend?”<br />“Beijing International Studies University.”</p>
<p>“…” </p>
<p>“Could you guess why I came to Beijing now?”<br />“Why?”<br />“I’m a Falun Gong practitioner. I’ve come to Beijing to petition for Falun Gong, because the government is persecuting Falun Gong.”</p>
<p>“If you go on talking, I’ll drive you to the police station.”<br /> “If you want to drive me to the police station, go ahead. But what I’m telling you is true – true stories taking place in this country. I’m not slandering the CCP; I’m just telling you the truth you can’t know through the state-owned media. I’m talking to you from the bottom of my heart, sir.”<br />The driver took a look at me and burst into a smile, “I feel you Falun Gong practitioners are really miraculous! I feel you are pretty miraculous too! ”<br />I began telling him how Dafa had saved me.</p>
<p>I genuinely shared with him my life story without taking any notice of whether he was driving me to a police station.<br />While listening, he suddenly smiled genuinely, “A touching story!” And then he exclaimed, “Gosh! I was so attracted by your talking I just turned onto a wrong highway! I’m so sorry! I’m really sorry!”<br />He apologized repeatedly, his attitude having become much better than when I got in the taxi twenty minutes earlier. <br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
