<p><B> Rare Blue Diamond Breaks the Record</B> <br />稀有蓝钻破纪录</p>

<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B>auction</B>: n. 拍卖<br />2.<B>gemstone</B>: n. 宝石<br />3.<B>mounte</B>: v. 镶嵌<br />4. <B> platinum</B>: n.白金<br />5.<B>flaw</B>: n.瑕疵<br />6.<B>desperate</B>:adj. 极度渴望的<br />7.<B>perk</B>: n.奖励<br />8.<B>anonymous</B>: adj. 匿名的</p>
<p>This blue diamond reached a record 9.5 million dollars at a Sotheby&#8217;s <B>auction</B> in Geneva.<br />这块蓝钻石在日内瓦拍卖会中价格高达95万元。</p>
<p>That makes it the world&#8217;s most expensive gemstone per carat.<br />这使得它成为世界上每克拉最昂贵的宝石。</p>
<p>The stone is <B>mounted</B> in a <B>platinum</B> ring. It doesn&#8217;t have any internal <B>flaws</B> and weighs just over 7 carats.<br />这颗宝石镶嵌在白金戒指上，内部毫无瑕疵，而且重达7克拉。</p>
<p>It was the rarest to enter the market last year &#8211; which pushed the price up, says David Bennett who led the auction.<br />主持拍卖的大卫?班奈特说他是去年进入市场中最稀有的，价格因此攀升。</p>
<p>[David Bennett, Sotheby&#8217;s Jewelry Chairman]: <br />&#8220;I don&#8217;t know how long it took to sell the blue diamond, a long time, and the bids were basically of fifty thousand francs, which if you are at eight or nine million, going out for fifty thousand francs takes quite a lot of time. It was very, very exciting, I mean, there were several bidders towards the end who were <B>desperate</B> to have the stone.&#8221; <br />苏士比拍卖会主席大卫?班奈特说：“我不知道卖出这颗蓝宝石花多少时间，很久，喊价基本是5万法郎，8、9百万到5万法郎要很长时间，非常、非常刺激，我是指好几个出价人喊价到最后，他们极度渴望拥有这颗宝石。”</p>
<p>Sotheby&#8217;s say the sale of this gemstone and others shows there&#8217;s still demand for fine jewels.<br />苏士比说这颗宝石及其他宝石的卖出显示珠宝的需求仍然存在。</p>
<p>Like many other blue diamonds, the rock comes from the Cullinan mine in South Africa.<br />就像其他蓝钻石，这颗石头来自南非的库里南矿。</p>
<p>And there&#8217;s another <B>perk</B> for its <B>anonymous</B> new owner &#8211; they can name the diamond too.<br />匿名新主人的另一个奖励是他们可以为钻石命名。<br />@*</p>
