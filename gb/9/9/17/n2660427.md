<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>被真理唤醒的心(85)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>槎头女子劳教所不允许大法弟子阅读任何东西，除了中共诽谤法轮功的宣传材料；也不准大法弟子写任何东西，除了每月一封的家信和看守布置的“作业”。</p>
<p>那些“作业”包括：抄写中共诽谤法轮功的宣传材料和回答看守的问题，例如：你对被关押在劳教所怎么看？你现在想什么？</p>
<p>没有大法弟子做这些“作业”。但我们利用这个可以拿笔的机会写了很多文章，把大法的真相告诉看守，劝她们择善路、莫为邪恶的迫害做帮凶。</p>
<p>后来我们发现，很少看守真正读我们写的文章，她们只是想歪曲我们写的东西、反过来诽谤大法。</p>
<p>我曾和广州市劳教局教育处的处长有过这样的对话：</p>
<p>“你读过法轮大法的书吗？”</p>
<p>“没有。我只读政府发给我们的材料。”</p>
<p>“既然你对法轮大法一无所知，那你为什么迫害大法弟子、逼我们放弃修炼大法呢？”</p>
<p>“因为共产党命令我这样做。”</p>
<p>“如果共产党的命令是错的呢？”</p>
<p>“这我不管，反正共产党指东我走东，共产党指西我走西。”</p>
<p>“你没有自己的头脑和独立意志吗？”</p>
<p>“反正共产党指东我不走西。”</p>
<p>“如果有一天政府突然宣布：法轮大法是好的，共产党错了。你怎么办？”</p>
<p>他两眼空空的看着我，一句话说不出来。</p>
<p>就我所知，一位年轻看守真正读了我们写的文章。大法弟子所写的和政府所宣传的如此不同，她感到非常困惑。</p>
<p>我建议她找一本《转法轮》看看，自己判断。她认为是好主意，于是向所长申请要一本《转法轮》。</p>
<p>那所长平时经常对我们大法弟子说：“我手上有一本《转法轮》！我看过《转法轮》！”但从她的话中我发现她根本没看过这本书，她拿着这本书只是想从中断章取义、找出点什么来诽谤大法。</p>
<p>她拒绝了那位年轻看守的申请。</p>
<p>但那位看守从大法弟子的言行中得出了自己的判断。</p>
<p>她私下对我说：“我从你们大法弟子身上学到了很多东西。我发现每次有汤喝时，别人都围着汤桶抢，只有你们大法弟子不抢，让别人优先。我现在在食堂排队买饭也不和别人抢了，以前我抢的！我也不再打骂犯人了，以前她们惹火我我就给她们一耳光！踢她们一脚！”</p>
<p>后来她家里发生了一件伤心事，她请了一段时间假。我得知后马上请求看守给我纸笔，写了一封信给她，托和她关系要好的一名看守把信带给了她。</p>
<p>回来上班后她对我说：“我真的很感动。你自己生命都这么危险、朝不保夕，还记的安慰我。在我那么伤心的时候，你是第一个来安慰我的人……”</p>
<p>她悄悄给我们教被关押者学大法开绿灯，“我希望她们跟您们学法轮功，这样她们都会变好。”</p>
<p>当她的上司命令她对大法弟子施酷刑时，她坚决拒绝，悄悄对我说：“我不敢迫害这么善良的人，我怕有报应。”（待续）</p>
<p>(英文对照)</p>
<p>Chatou let Dafa practitioners read nothing but the CCP propaganda material that slandered Dafa, and let Dafa practitioners write nothing but the family letters once a month and the guards’ “assignments”.</p>
<p>The “assignments” were copying the CCP propaganda material and answering the guards’ questions, such as: What do you think of being locked up in the forced labor camp? What are you thinking?</p>
<p>No Dafa practitioner did the “assignments”, but we made use of this opportunity to write many articles clarifying the truth of Dafa, advising the guards to take a good path and not to aid and abet evil.</p>
<p>Later we found out that few guards truly read our articles, they just wanted to distort what we wrote so as to slander Dafa.</p>
<p>I once had a conversation with the Education Division chief with the Guangzhou Labor Camp Bureau.</p>
<p>“Have you ever read any Falun Dafa books?”</p>
<p>“No. I only read the material the government told us to read.”</p>
<p>“Now that you know nothing about Falun Dafa, why do you persecute Dafa practitioners and force us to renounce Dafa?”</p>
<p>“Because the CCP ordered me to do so.”</p>
<p>“What if the CCP is wrong?”</p>
<p>“I don’t care. The CCP points to the east, I go to the east. The CCP points to the west, I go to the west.”</p>
<p>“You don’t have your own mind?”</p>
<p>“Anyway, the CCP points to the east, I don’t go to the west.”</p>
<p>“If one day, the government suddenly declares that Falun Dafa is good and the CCP is wrong, what will you do?”</p>
<p>He looked blankly at me without being able to say a word.</p>
<p>As far as I knew, there was a young guard who truly read our articles. What Dafa practitioners wrote was so different from the government propaganda, she felt extremely perplexed.</p>
<p>I advised her to get a copy of Zhuan Falun to read and make the judgment by herself. She thought it was a good idea and then applied to the Chatou chief for a copy of Zhuan Falun.</p>
<p>The chief had often said to Dafa practitioners, “I have a copy of Zhuan Falun! I’ve read Zhuan Falun!”But I could tell from her words that she had never read the book, and her keeping the book was just for quoting the book out of context so as to slander Dafa.</p>
<p>She rejected the young guard’s application.</p>
<p>Nevertheless the guard drew her own conclusion through observing Dafa practitioners’ conduct.</p>
<p>She quietly said to me, “I have been learning a lot from you Falun Gong practitioners. I’ve noticed that whenever there was soup, all the inmates surrounded the soup bucket scrambling for the soup, but Falun Gong practitioners never scrambled, giving the priority to others. Now I no longer scramble with other guards while queuing for food at the canteen. I used to scramble! Now I no longer hit or scold the inmates. I used to slap them in the face and kick them when they made me mad!” </p>
<p>Later, something sad happened to the guard and she asked for leave for a while. Upon knowing it, I requested a guard to give me pen and paper and wrote her a letter, and then requested a guard who was close to her to take the letter to her.</p>
<p>She said to me after coming back to work, “I was so moved. While your own life was in such a dangerous and precarious situation, you were still concerned about me and wrote consoling me; when I was so sad, you were the first person who comforted me…” </p>
<p>She quietly gave the green light to us teaching the inmates to learn Dafa. “I hope they learn Falun Gong. Then they will all become good.”</p>
<p>When her superior ordered her to torture Dafa practitioners, she refused. She quietly said to me, “I dare not persecute such good people. I’m afraid of meeting with retribution.” <br /> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
