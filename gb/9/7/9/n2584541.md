<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>被真理唤醒的心(53)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>翌日一大早街道派出所警察就打电话来要我去一趟。</p>
<p>我穿上淡兰色连衣裙，把头发梳齐整，步履轻快朝派出所走去。在派出所门口一见到那位警察，我就微笑着和他道早安。</p>
<p>他愣了一下，马上也微笑的和我道早安。</p>
<p>在一间小办公室里，他一边问我一边做笔录：</p>
<p>“在拘留所关了十五天，感觉如何？”</p>
<p>“感觉很好。”</p>
<p>“你在拘留所关了十五天后感觉很好？”</p>
<p>“是的。”</p>
<p>“为什么？”</p>
<p>“因为在这十五天里，我更感受到了法轮大法的伟大。”</p>
<p>他低着头记录。</p>
<p>“像你这样受过高等教育的人，不觉的被关在那样的地方很难受吗？”</p>
<p>“不觉的难受。因为心中有大法，我在那样艰苦的环境中精神反而升华了。世上的一切金钱和安逸都带不来我心灵的幸福。而当我的精神不断升华时，我清晰的感受到了心灵的幸福和人生真正美好的意义。”</p>
<p>我缓慢、平和、清晰的说着，以便他能记录下我说的每一个字。</p>
<p>“你以后还会去北京吗？”</p>
<p>“中国的法律或宪法禁止法轮大法弟子去首都吗？”</p>
<p>他尴尬的笑了笑。</p>
<p>我开始坦诚的向他讲述我如何被大法拯救和改变。</p>
<p>我没把他看作是正在盘问我的警察，而把他看作是需要从中共邪恶的宣传中唤醒的人。</p>
<p>他被我的故事吸引、忘了他的笔录工作、静静听我讲。</p>
<p>几名警察也一个接一个从隔壁办公室踱进来听。</p>
<p>我一边讲一边插进师父的诗，轻声背诵给他们听。</p>
<p>他们开始问我有关<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>的问题 ——不是出于恶意，而是出于想了解真相。</p>
<p>突然，从旁边办公室传来一声凶恶的大吼：“你们不要被她感化了！”（待续）</p>
<p>(英文对照)</p>
<p>A policeman from the neighborhood police station called early the next morning, telling me to go to the police station right away.</p>
<p>Putting on my pale blue dress, combing my long hair neatly, I walked toward the police station with light steps. When I saw the policeman in the doorway of the police station, I bade him good morning smilingly.</p>
<p>He appeared a bit shocked, and then bade me good morning too.</p>
<p>In a small office, he took notes while interrogating me:</p>
<p>“How do you feel after being detained in the detention center for fifteen days?”</p>
<p>“I feel great.”</p>
<p>“You feel great after fifteen days of detention?”</p>
<p>“Yes.”</p>
<p>“Why?”</p>
<p>“Because during the fifteen days I have more profoundly experienced the greatness of Falun Dafa.”</p>
<p>He lowered his head and took notes.</p>
<p>“As such a well-educated person like you, didn’t you feel hard to be detained in that kind of place?”</p>
<p>“No, I didn’t feel hard. Instead, having Dafa in my heart made my moral character uplifted in that harsh environment. All the money and comfort in the world couldn’t bring my heart happiness; but as my moral character keeps uplifting, I have clearly felt the happiness of my heart and the truly wonderful significance of life.”</p>
<p>I spoke slowly, peacefully, and clearly so that he could write down every word I said.<br />“Will you go to Beijing again?”</p>
<p>“Is there an item in China’s law or constitution stating that Falun Dafa practitioners are forbidden to go to the capital?”</p>
<p>He smiled an embarrassed smile.</p>
<p>I began telling him sincerely how I had been saved and changed by Dafa.</p>
<p>I didn’t view him as a policeman who was interrogating me, but viewed him as a human being who needed to be awakened from the CCP’s evil propaganda.</p>
<p>He was attracted by my story, forgot his interrogation and listened quietly to me.</p>
<p>Several policemen strolled into the office one by one from next door to listen to me. </p>
<p>I inserted Master Li’s poems into my talking, reciting the poems for them softly.</p>
<p>They started asking me questions about Falun Gong – not out of hostility, but out of the desire to know the truth.</p>
<p>Suddenly, a fiendish voice roared from the next door office, “Don’t you guys be converted by her!”<br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
