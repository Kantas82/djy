<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>被真理唤醒的心(58)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>五月十三日早上，我们在旅馆房间里演习怎样以最快速度把横幅从一个小塑料袋里拿出来打开。</p>
<p>中午退房后，我们到邮局把我们的《转法轮》寄回广州，然后到天安门附近的小餐馆吃午饭。</p>
<p>下午两点，我们背着背囊，其中一人手拿装着横幅的小塑料袋，向天安门广场走去。</p>
<p>我们吃着冰棍、一边走一边像游客一样眼睛到处望，以免引起警察注意。</p>
<p>中共在天安门一带设了几重秘密警戒线，警察、公安、便衣和打手遍布其中。他们会突然盘问行人：“你是炼法轮功的吗？”一些大法弟子因为如实回答而没到天安门广场就被抓上警车。</p>
<p>下午两点三十分，我们到达天安门广场，选好了一个游客多的地方。“开始吧！”手拿塑料袋的同修说。</p>
<p>我们立即以闪电般的速度从塑料袋里拉出横幅，一边站俩个人展开巨大的横幅：“法轮大法好！”</p>
<p>警察、便衣、打手立即向我们狂奔过来，围住我们打，抢我们的横幅。我们拚命用自己的身体护住横幅。</p>
<p>大批游客默默围观。</p>
<p>一辆警车快速驶过来，我们被警察、便衣、打手野蛮的往车上推。</p>
<p>我突然大声喊：“法轮大法好！ ——”</p>
<p>那是从我心灵最深处喊出的声音。</p>
<p>一群警察、便衣、打手马上围上来打我。站在我旁边的一位老太太焦急的小声叫我：“别喊了！别喊了！”</p>
<p>我仍不停的大喊：“法轮大法好！——” “共产党在撒谎！——”</p>
<p>我已完全忘记了害怕。</p>
<p>被推进警车后，我把头伸出窗外继续大声喊。</p>
<p>车上一个一脸凶相的警察粗暴的把我从车窗边拉回来，掐着我的脖子用拳头狠狠的砸向我。三位同修马上过来用她们的身体护着我。</p>
<p>车上除了我们还有一位男弟子，他大喊一声“不许打人！”警察立即把拳头转向他。</p>
<p>我们女弟子马上过去用身体挡住凶狠的拳头。（待续）</p>
<p>(英文对照)</p>
<p>On the morning of May 13, we practiced in the hotel room how to pull out our banner from a plastic bag and unfold it at the fastest speed.</p>
<p>Upon checking out at noon, we went to a post office to mail our Zhuan Falun back to Guangzhou, and then went to a small restaurant near Tiananmen Square to have lunch.</p>
<p>At 2 p.m., we set off for Tiananmen Square carrying our backpacks, one of us carrying a small plastic bag containing the banner.</p>
<p>We ate ice creams and looked around like a tourist while walking so as not to attract the police attention.</p>
<p>The CCP had set up a few layers of secret cordons around Tiananmen Square, with policemen, public security men, plain-clothes, and hired ruffians penetrating everywhere. They would suddenly block and interrogate a pedestrian, “Do you practice Falun Gong?” Some Dafa practitioners were shoved into the police cars before getting to Tiananmen Square as they honestly replied, “Yes”.</p>
<p>At 2:30p.m., we got to Tiananmen Square and decided on a spot where a lot of tourists gathered. “Let’s start!” the fellow practitioner carrying the plastic bag said.We instantly pulled out the large banner from the plastic bag like lightening and held it up with two of us standing at each side of it: “Falun Dafa is Good!”</p>
<p>The policemen, plain-clothes, and hired ruffians instantly dashed toward us. They surrounded us, beat us, and snatched our banner. We desperately protected the banner with our bodies.</p>
<p>A lot of tourists looked on the scene silently.</p>
<p>A police car swiftly ran up. The policemen, plain-clothes, and hired ruffians fiercely shoved us toward it. </p>
<p>I suddenly shouted out, “Falun Dafa is Good! – ”</p>
<p>It was the voice shouted out from the deepest of my heart.</p>
<p>The policemen, plain-clothes, and hired ruffians instantly surrounded me and beat me. An elderly lady standing by me gestured to me apprehensively in a low voice, “Don’t shout anymore! Don’t shout anymore!”</p>
<p>I still went on shouting out: “Falun Dafa is Good!—” “The CCP is lying! –-”</p>
<p>I had utterly forgotten fear.</p>
<p>Upon being shoved into the police car, I stuck my head out of the window and went on shouting out.</p>
<p>A fiendish-looking policeman in the car violently dragged me away from the window by clutching my neck and fiercely punched me. The three fellow practitioners immediately rushed up to protect me with their bodies. </p>
<p>In the car there was a male fellow practitioner. He shouted, “Stop beating people!” The policeman immediately turned to punch him.</p>
<p>We female practitioners immediately protected him against the fierce fists with our bodies.<br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
