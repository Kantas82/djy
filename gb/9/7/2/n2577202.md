<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(175)：微型雕塑</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D2f48e62c1c5f4f8baef1523b21409f00001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /><br />本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B> Microsopic Sculpture</B><br />微型雕塑</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B> microscopic</B>： adj. 微型的; 只能用显微镜看到的<br />2.<B>microscope</B>： n. 显微镜<br />3.<B>miniaturist</B>： n. 微图画家<br />4.<B>grain</B>： n. 粒(米)<br />5.<B>pulse</B>： n. 脉搏<br />6. <B> diligent</B>： adj. 费尽心血的</B></p>
<p>It&#8217;s art, but not as you know it.<br />它是艺术，但和你知道的不一样。</p>
<p>These sculptures are so tiny, you need a <B>microscope</B> to see them.<br />这些雕塑品如此微小，小到你需要用一台显微镜来看它们。</p>
<p>This exhibition shows the work of Russian artist Vladimir Aniskin.<br />这个展览展出的是俄罗斯艺术家雷地摩阿尼斯金的作品。</p>
<p>He calls himself a micro-<B>miniaturist</B> and makes the sculptures using a microscope and hand-made tools.<br />他称他自己叫作微雕艺术家，并且使用一台显微镜和手工制成的工具做雕塑品。</p>
<p>His art fits on a sliced grape seed, a <B>grain</B> of rice or even a human hair.<br />他的艺术品比一个切片的葡萄种子，一粒大米或者甚至一根人发还小。</p>
<p>It&#8217;s a kind of work that requires extreme self-control.<br />这种工法需要具备极端的克制力。</p>
<p>[Vladimir Aniskin, Micro-minaturist]:<br />&#8220;If you hold an instrument under a microscope and relax, you will see that the end of the instrument moves each time your heart beats. The heart beats in <B>pulses</B> and between those pulses you have around half a second to make a controlled movement. After much practice, I have learned to work between my heart beats. In those breaks, I do the most <B>diligent</B> work.&#8221;<br />[雷地摩阿尼斯金，超微型艺术家]：<br />“如果你在一台显微镜下拿着一个工具并且放松自己，你将看到每当你的心脏跳动的时候，工具的末端在移动。脉搏里的跳动，在那些脉搏之间你只有大约半秒钟可以做一次可控制的移动。经过多次练习之后，我学到了在心跳之间工作。在那些间断的过程中，我完成我最精心的作品。”</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />7.<B>draw</B>： v. 吸引<br />8.<B>caravan </B>：n. 旅队<br />9.<B>needle</B>： n. 针<br />10.<B>trophy</B>： n. 胜利纪念品<br />11. <B> miniature</B>： n. 微型画<br />12.<B>work out</B>： ph. 解决</p>
<p>Aniksin&#8217;s skill is <B>drawing</B> visitors to an exhibition of his work in Novosibirsk in Siberia.<br />阿尼斯金的专长，吸引了参观者到位于西伯利亚的新西伯利亚，展示他作品的展览场上。</p>
<p>Aniskin&#8217;s been making these sculptures since 1998.<br />阿尼斯金从一九九八年开始微雕工作。</p>
<p>One of his early works was this <B>caravan</B> of camels that fits in the eye of a <B>needle</B>.<br />他早期有一座骆驼旅行队穿进针孔的作品。</p>
<p>More recently, football club Zenit St Petersburg&#8217;s victory in the UEFA Cup inspired Aniskin to make the <B>trophy</B> in <B>miniature</B>. <br />近年来，足球队车尼特圣彼得堡赢得欧洲杯，启发阿尼斯金做出这个小奖杯。</p>
<p>But he says the cup&#8217;s complicated design caused him particular problems.<br />但是他说杯子错综复杂的设计引发一些特殊的问题。</p>
<p>It took him about two months to <B>work out</B> how to make the sculpture.<br />经过两个月的努力他解决了这件雕刻品的制作。</p>
<p>But the challenge wasn&#8217;t too great, and his completed creation now stands two millimetres high.<br />但是这个挑战不够大，他目前完成的创作有两毫米高。<br />@*</p>
