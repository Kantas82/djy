<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语(182)：功夫节纪念李小龙</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Kung Fu Festival Honors Bruce Lee</B><br />功夫节纪念李小龙<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D0325faa062394b2f88b6bd4f438f4a9b001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> <br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><--ads--><br /><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>kick off</B>: v. 开始<br />2.	<B>legendary</B>: adj. 传奇的<br />3.	<B>martial art</B>: n. 武术<br />4.	<B>Czech</B>: n. 捷克<br />5.	<B>warrior</B>: n. 战士<br />6.	<B>philosophical</B>: a. 哲思的</p>
<p>The first international Kung Fu festival in Hong Kong has <B> kicked off</B>, 36 years after the death of Bruce Lee, a <B> legendary</B> Kung Fu movie icon. More than a thousand <B> martial artists</B> from 19 different countries are expected to participate in the event.<br />首届国际功夫节在香港开幕，距传奇功夫电影偶像明星李小龙逝世已36年。预期会有超过一千名来自19个不同国家的武术家参与本活动。</p>
<p>Although Kung Fu has taken different forms over the past three decades, many people who practice the sport still claim Bruce Lee as the original and the best.<br />虽然在过去三十年中，功夫已采取各种不同的形式，许多习武者仍将李小龙奉为始祖及第一好手。</p>
<p>[Jana, Competitor]: (Female, <B> Czech</B>)<br />&#8220;Bruce is the first. He is number one. No one is as good as him. That&#8217;s my opinion. I have read his book. He is not just a <B> warrior</B>. He is very <B> intelligent</B>, very <B> philosophical</B>.&#8221; <br />来自捷克的参赛者佳娜说：“李小龙是始祖，是第一好手，没有人比得上他。这是我的看法。我读过他的书，他不只是个战士，他非常有才智，非常有哲思的。”</p>
<p>In mainland China, Lee’s old movies are a popular choice among martial arts fans and have a significant influence for most Chinese. <br />在中国大陆，李小龙拍过的电影广受武术迷喜爱，对多数中国人也有深远的影响。</p>
<p>[Xiao Qing, Competitor]: <br />&#8220;When we were practicing Kung Fu, a movie called ‘The Legend of Bruce Lee’ hit the TV. A lot of people watched it, and discussed about him.”<br />参赛者萧琴说：“以前我们在练习功夫的时候，电视正在播映一部电影叫《李小龙传奇》，好多人都在看，然后讨论他。”</p>
<p>Fans also push to preserve and protect Lee’s <B> legacy</B>. <br />热爱李小龙的粉丝（影迷）也在推动保存他的遗物。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />7.	<B>legacy</B>: n. 遗物<br />8.	<B>withstand</B>: v. 经受<br />9.	<B>significance</B>: n. 意义、重要性<br />10.	<B>demolish</B>: v. 毁坏<br />11.	<B>wax figure</B>: n. 蜡像<br />12.	<B>bare-handed</B>: a. 赤手空拳的<br />13.	<B>rare collections</B>: n. 珍藏</p>
<p>[Wong Yiu-keung, Hong Kong Bruce Lee Club]: <br />&#8220;I&#8217;m relieved that his house is still around more than 30 years after his death and we can still preserve it. Having <B> withstood</B> the test of time, Bruce Lee&#8217;s <B> significance</B> and honor have become clearer. Of course, if Bruce Lee&#8217;s house had been preserved earlier, that would be even better. But now it&#8217;s not too late. At least the house hasn&#8217;t been <B> demolished</B> and we can still restore it.&#8221; <br />香港李小龙会的翁游强说：“让我放心的是，在他死后30多年，他的房子仍在那儿，我们仍可保存它。经过时间的考验，李小龙的重要性与荣誉日益显见。当然如果李小龙的房子能早点被保存更好，但现在也不算太迟。至少房子尚末毁坏，我们还能修复。”</p>
<p>An exhibition for Bruce Lee also opened in a shopping center in Hong Kong. Lee’s life size <B> wax figure</B> and <B> rare collections</B> were on display.<br />在香港一处购物中心也有李小龙展登场，展出李小龙的真人尺寸蜡像，以及珍贵收藏。</p>
<p>The festival, which is scheduled to continue until early August, aims to promote and develop Chinese martial arts, through holding a number of <B> bare-handed</B> and armed martial arts competitions.<br />此项功夫节预计持续到八月初，透过举办数场徒手的与携武器的武术竞赛，推展中华武术。<br />@*</p>
