<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场英语：办公室英语会话(14)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><B> Meeting a new boss</B></p>
<p>A: Good morning, Mr. Montgomery. I’ve been sent over from purchasing to fill in for Clare Williams.</p>
<p>B: Oh, really? Nice to meet you. I thought they were going to leave me here on my own! How long have you been with the company?</p>
<p>A: Oh, about seven years. Time flies, you know!</p>
<p>B: Does it ever! Well, let’s get started. If you could begin typing this letter now, I’ll see what else needs to be done.</p>
<p><B> 见新老板 </B></p>
<p>A：早上好，蒙格玛利先生。我是从采购部调来接替克雷尔·威谦姆斯的。</p>
<p>B：喔，是吗？幸会。我以为他们要把我一个人扔在这儿呢。你来公司有几年了？</p>
<p>A：哦，得有七年了。光阴似箭啊。</p>
<p>B：永远如此。好啦，我们开始吧。请你先打这封信，我来看看还有什么要做的。</p>
<p><B> Introducing new people</B></p>
<p>A: Tom, this is Mary Bobbins. She’s just come over from sales.</p>
<p>B: It’s nice to meet you! Guess we’ll be working together.</p>
<p>A: Yes, she’ll be working closely with you on the marketing end of things.</p>
<p>B: Good. Well, welcome aboard, Ms, Robbins.</p>
<p><B> 介绍新人 </B></p>
<p>A：汤姆，这是玛丽·罗宾斯。刚从销售部来。</p>
<p>B：见到你很高兴。我想我们要在一起工作了。</p>
<p>A：是这样。她会在市场行销方面与你密切合作的。</p>
<p>B：好啊，欢迎你的到来，罗宾斯女士。</p>
