I thought he was my brother. To make mistakes is human.&#8221;<br />薇拉．露西亚．康卡瓦，阿德米．康卡瓦的妹妹说道：<br />“那具尸体(在太平间里)和在高速公路上发现的一样，他和我哥哥极为相似，所以我才会以为他是我哥哥。人非圣贤孰能无过嘛！”</p> <p>News of his death quickly spread after being reported on a local radio station. When Goncalves was <B> spotted</B> Monday morning, after spending the night out drinking with friends. He was rushed to his funeral to clear up the <B> confusion</B>.<br />整夜和朋友在外面喝酒之后，康卡瓦在周一早晨被找到，他赶紧冲到他的丧礼去澄清误会。</p> <p>The &#8220;Typhoon&#8221; said everyone was surprised by his sudden show. <br /> “台风”说他突然的出现让每个人都吃了一惊。</p> <p>[Ademir Jorge Goncalves, Brazilian Bricklayer]:<br />&#8220;They thought I was a ghost!.&#8221;<br />阿德米．乔治．康卡瓦，巴西砌砖工人说：<br />“我到达丧礼时，大家都吓坏了，他们把我当成鬼了！”</p> <p>The body was correctly identified later on Monday and has already been buried in another state.<br />遗体在周一稍晚被正确的指认，并且已经被安葬在另一州。<br />@*</p> <p> <p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
