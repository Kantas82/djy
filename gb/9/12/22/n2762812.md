(赛马运动中的)超越障碍比赛<br />4. <B>tent pegging </B>: ph. 印度热门的一种(拔营钉)马术竞赛<br />5.<B> novice</B>:n. 新手；初学者<br />6. <B>civilian</B>:n. 平民；百姓<br />7. <B>lakh</B>:n. 10万(卢比)<br />8. <B>rupee</B>: n. 卢比 (印度、斯里兰卡、尼泊尔等的货币单位)</p> <p>Many<B> equestrian</B> events like<B> show jumping</B> and<B> tent pegging </B>were held to popularize riding in the region.<br />许多的骑术项目例如超越障碍，或者拔营钉的竞赛，在此地举办以普及骑马活动。</p> <p>[Major General Kamal Mohe, Organizing Committee Member]:<br />“We have tried to showcase some of the major events of the show jumping for the seniors, for the juniors, <B>novices</B> as well as for children.”<br />[少将 Kamal Mohe，筹备委员会成员]：<br />我们尝试着不但让资深的、初级的、新手甚至是孩童，去表现一些超越障碍之类的主要项目。</p> <p>The winner of the show jumping says the sport offers bright prospects.<br />超越障碍比赛的优胜者说：这个运动提供一个光明的未来。</p> <p>[Major Krishan Yadav, Winner, Show Jumping Novice]:“In the recent years, a lot of<B> civilians</B> have become interested in the sport. People are spending a lot of money buying horses from abroad and horses costs 40 to 50 <B>lakhs </B> (four million to five million <B>rupees</B> ) but people are willing to spend that much money and invest in the sport because there is a bright future for the sport.”<br />[少校Krishan Yadav，新手障碍赛优胜者]：<br />最近这几年，很多的老百姓对这项运动产生兴趣。人们花费大笔的金钱从国外买入马匹，而且即使这些马匹费用高达约400到500万卢比，人们依旧有意愿花费这笔费用，并投资这项运动，就是因为这项运动有着光明的前景。</p> <p>The show also saw a huge participation by young children, both boys and girls.<br />为数相当众多的年轻男孩和女孩，也来参与这场演出。</p> <p>[Uday, Rider]:<br />&#8220;By participating in such events, our confidence level goes up and also we can improve our riding skills. I would like to take horse riding as a serious career option in the future.&#8221; <br />[Uday，骑师]：<br />经由参与这类的比赛提高了我们的自信心，而且我们也可以增进我们的骑术。我想要将骑马当成是未来一个严肃的职业选项之一。</p> <p>Meanwhile, the show also drew a large crowd who came to see the free show.<br />同时，这项表演也吸引了大批的群众，来观赏这场免费的演出。<br />@* </p> <p><p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
