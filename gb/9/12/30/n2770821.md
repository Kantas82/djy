I&#8217;m on Bondi Beach in an <B>elf outfit</B>, it&#8217;s incredible.&#8221;<br />[Georgie Knul, 英国籍旅客]:<br />“是啊，这真的是很神奇，很独特，我们大老远的从英国，英格兰和苏格兰来到这里，而且我们从来没有这样做过。我现在正在Bondi海滩穿着小精灵的服装，真的是很不可思议耶。”</p> <p>It seems that they were not alone.<br />看来他们是不孤单的。</p> <p>Even Santa boarded a plane from freezing Finland to visit China.<br />甚至连圣诞老人都从冰天雪地的芬兰坐飞机到中国来拜访。</p> <p>He was amazed by what he saw.<br />他对他所看到的非常惊奇</p> <p>[Santa Claus, Who Travelled from Finland]:<br />&#8220;At first it was a little bit surprising for me because I found so much Christmas decorations all over, and you could see Christmas trees and lights and everything, everywhere. I thought here you don&#8217;t see Christmas in so many places, but it was a surprise for me.&#8221;<br />[远从芬兰来的圣诞老公公 ]：<br />“起初对我来说有点惊讶，因为我发现到处充满了圣诞节的布置，你可以看到圣诞树和美丽的灯光以及一切的一切，皆无处不在。我还在这里想，在许多地方你是看不到圣诞节的，但这里真的让我很讶异。”</p> <p>But celebrations in<B> Iraq </B>were more <B>bittersweet</B>.<br />但在伊拉克的庆祝活动更加的苦乐参半。</p> <p>As U.S. soldiers attended Christmas mass in <B>Baghdad</B>, most wished they could be home.<br />正如美国士兵在巴格达参加圣诞弥撒，他们最希望的就是可以回家。</p> <p>[Alice Robin, U.S. Soldier]:<br />&#8220;My wish for the new year is to go home and see my family, I miss them a lot .. of course I want to go home.&#8221;<br />[Alice Robin, 美国大兵]:<br />“我希望在新的一年，可以回家探望我的家人，我非常的想念他们…当然我也非常想回家。”</p> <p>But it looks like there&#8217;s no room for Scrooge here as despite their situation, the soldiers still haven&#8217;t lost their <B>yuletide </B>spirit.<br />尽管他们目前所面临的情况，但看起来已经没有吝啬的余地，士兵们仍然没有失去圣诞季节的精神。<br />@*</p> <p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
