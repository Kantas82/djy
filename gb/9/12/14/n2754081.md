<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>商用英文：演说与报告(3)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>阐明会议主要议题(Staring a presentation gracefully)</B></p> <p>这时主持人可从独特的观点（unusual angle）简述演讲的内容。</p> <p>o Let&#8217;s start by looking at this agreement.<br />＝ We are here today to look at this agreement.<br />（咱们先看这份协议。）</p> <p>o Why don&#8217;t we first go over the contract?<br />（让我们先看看这份合约吧。）</p> <p>o Before I begin, I would just like to mention that our department has achieved our sales quota this month.<br />（在我开始之前，我想提一下本部门此月已达销售业绩。）</p> <p>o First I&#8217;d like to say a few words about this research proposal. <br />（首先我想说一下这份研究提案。）</p> <p>o Today, I shall be dealing with the final arrangement of this joint venture.<br />＝ Our main goal today is to deal with the final arrangement of this joint venture.<br />（今天我将处理这桩合资案的最后协议。）</p> <p>o I&#8217;m here to tell you about the full contents of marketing strategy.<br />＝ I have called this meeting in order to tell you about the full contents of marketing strategy.<br />（在此我要告诉你们有关市场策略的所有内涵。）</p> <p>o I plan to say a few words about this report. <br />（我计划要谈一点这份报告。）</p> <p>o I&#8217;m going to talk about the corporate social responsibility today.<br />（今天我将谈论有关企业社会责任的议题。）</p> <p>o The subject of my talk is the sustainable development of an enterprise.<br />= The theme of my presentation is the sustainable development of an enterprise.<br />= The topic of my speech is the sustainable development of an enterprise.<br />= The field of my lecture is the sustainable development of an enterprise.<br />（我的演讲题目是企业的永续经营。）</p> <p>o I would like to give you an overview of international business. <br />（我想让你们对国际企业有个概括的认识。）</p> <p>o I&#8217;ve divided my talk into three parts. <br />= My talk will be in three sections. <br />（我的演讲题目将分成三个部分。）</p> <p><B>单字解析</B></p> <p>agreement (n)：协议、同意<br />New Zealand&#8217;s prime minister will sign a groundbreaking free trade agreement with Vietnam.<br />（纽西兰总理将与越南签订一项开创性自由贸易协定。）</p> <p>joint venture (ph.)：合资企业、创投公司<br />A joint venture (often abbreviated JV) is an entity formed between two or more parties to undertake economic activity together.<br />（合资企业（常缩写成JV）是指由两个或以上的单位所组成的一个实体，以共同从事经济活动。）</p> <p>corporate social responsibility (ph.)：企业社会责任<br />Company should align corporate social responsibility strategy to business objectives.<br />（公司应该将企业社会责任策略与企业目标相结合。）</p> <p>talk ＝ presentation ＝ lecture ＝ speech：演讲、演说</p> <p>deal with (ph.) 处理、应付<br />This little girl has learnt to deal with all kinds of complicated situations.<br />（这位小女孩已经学会处理各种复杂的情况。）<br />The article deals with an important concept.<br />（这篇文章处理一个重要的概念。）</p> <p><B>演讲时间的说法如下</B></p> <p>o My talk will take about twenty minutes. <br />（我的演讲约20分钟。）</p> <p>o The presentation will take about two and half hours, but there will be a twenty minute coffee break in the middle. <br />（我的演讲是2个半小时，但中场会有20分钟的休息时间。）</p> <p>o We&#8217;ll stop for lunch at 12 o&#8217;clock.<br />（演讲12点结束，并可用餐。）</p>
