<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>商用英文：电话沟通技巧(3)</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>o 我能留言给他吗?<br />May I leave him a message?</p>
<p>o 我能帮您留言给他吗?<br />May I take a message?<br />＝ Could I tell him who is calling?<br />＝ Would you like to leave a message?</p>
<p>o 我今天晚一点再打来。<br />I will call again later today.</p>
<p>o 知道了！<br />I see. <br />＝ I got it!</p>
<p>o 好的！<br />Okay <br />＝ Sure <br />＝ Certainly <br />＝ 100% sure.</p>
<p>o 请问他几点回来?<br />What time do you expect his back?<br />＝ When will he come back?</p>
<p>o 你知道他何时会开完会吗?<br />Do you know when he will be out of his meeting?</p>
<p>o 让我查看一下他今天的行程表。<br />Let me check his schedule for the day.</p>
<p>o 你可以告诉他我有打电话来? <br />Could you tell him that I called?</p>
<p>o 他大约一小时后回办公室。<br />He will be back in office about an hour.</p>
<p>o 他没有专线，请拨总机。<br />He doesn&#8217;t have a direct line, please try our switchboard/operator.</p>
<p>o 我想如果您能留言或试着今天下午晚一点再打过来，会比较好。<br />I think that it would be better if you leave a message or try to call back this late afternoon.</p>
<p>o 他有你的行动电话号码吗?<br />Does he have your mobile number (＝cell phone number)?</p>
<p>o 让我重复你的讯息，确认无误。<br />Let me repeat your message, just to confirm.</p>
<p>o 您的意思是指贵公司可以接受付款条件，对吗？<br />What are you saying is that you cab accept the payment term. Is that right?</p>
<p>o 我最好将它写下。<br />I&#8217;d better write it down.</p>
<p>o 汤玛斯先生，我可以寄一封简讯给罗斯，但是很抱歉我无法保证他能够及时收到。<br />Mr. Thomas, I can send Ross a message, but I am sorry that there&#8217;s no guarantee that he&#8217;ll get it in time.</p>
<p>o 我一定会转告他的。<br />I will be sure to pass the message to him.</p>
<p>o 你听得到吗?<br />Hello, can you hear me?</p>
<p>o 电话通讯不良，我再重拨。<br />We have a bad connection. Let me call you back.</p>
<p>o 不好意思，电话有杂音，请再说一次好吗？<br />Excuse me, there is some noise on the line. Could you repeat that, please?</p>
<p>o 听起来似乎你很沮丧，能告诉我发生什么事吗？<br />You seem upset. Do you want to tell me what&#8217;s wrong?</p>
<p>o 陈先生，这听起来很急，是吗？<br />This sounds urgent, Mr. Chen. Is it?</p>
<p>o 艾尔比女士，让我为您解决问题。我将打电话给本公司的工程师确认一、两件事情，然后马上回电给您。<br />Mrs. Elbe, let me save you the trouble. I will give our engineer a call and check one or two things. I&#8217;ll call you right back.</p>
<p>o 假如你需要协助，请让我知道。<br />If you need any help, just let me know.</p>
<p>o 让我试着总结我们今天的谈话内容。<br />Let me just try and summarize where we&#8217;ve got to.</p>
<p>o 感谢您的协助！<br />Thank you very much for your kind assistance.</p>
<p>o 我希望能够协助您，但是这已经超乎我的专业领域，所以我已经安排专家与您联系。<br />I&#8217;d like to be able to help, but it&#8217;s outside my professional area, so I&#8217;ve arranged for expert to contact you.</p>
<p>o 很抱歉造成您的不便。<br />I do apologize for any inconvenience this might have caused you.</p>
