<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(155)北京鸟巢的风险</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B> Beijing&#8217;s Bird&#8217;s Nest Risks Becoming White Elephant</B><br />北京鸟巢有成为累赘的风险</p> <p>本则英语影音新闻及出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻 <ahref= >http://english.ntdtv.com/?c=145&#038;a=8389</a>（可线上观看）</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B> Bird&#8217;s Nest</B>: n. 鸟巢 (北京奥运体育馆)<br />2.<B>Olympic Games</B>: n. 奥林匹克运动会,世运会<br />3.<B>stadium</B>: n. 体育馆<br />4.<B>potential</B>: a. 可能的，潜在的<br />5.<B>white elephants</B>: phr. 累赘之物，无用物品</p> <p>STORY:<br />Half a year after 91,000 people packed the <B> Bird&#8217;s Nest</B> to watch <B> the Olympic Games</B>, the <B> stadium&#8217;s</B> owners are struggling to find a lasting role for the arena.<br />新闻内容:<br />北京鸟巢在奥运时聚集了91,000人。现在已过了半年，体育场业主仍然费劲的在找看鸟巢还能做什么用。</p> <p>There&#8217;s no shortage of examples of Olympic hosts building huge stadiums that are rarely used after the Games. With a price tag of over 500 million U.S. dollars, the National Stadium always had the <B> potential</B> to become the mother of all <B> white elephants</B>. </p> <p>6.<B>structure</B>: n. 结构<br />7.<B>interior</B>: n.a. 内部(的)<br />8. <B> discoloration</B>: n. 变色，污点<br />9.<B>due to</B>: phr. 由于<br />10.<B>operate</B>: v. 经营</p> <p>There&#8217;s no shortage of examples of Olympic hosts building huge stadiums that are rarely used after the Games. With a price tag of over 500 million U.S. dollars, the National Stadium always had the potential to become the mother of all white elephants.<br />奥运东道国家建立的巨大体育馆，在奥运会后就没什么机会使用了，这样的例子也不少。这座耗资超过5亿美元的国家体育场，有可能成为所有累赘物之母。</p> <p>For the last half year it has functioned mainly as a tourist site, playing host to visitors from around the country.<br />在这半年，它主要是作为观光景点，招待全国各地来玩的游客。</p> <p>The stadium&#8217;s <B> structure</B> and <B> interior</B> are showing a bit of wear and tear in places, with ripped posters left over from the Games and slight <B> discoloration</B> of the roof.<br />体育馆的结构和内部已经有些磨损的地方，像奥运海报撕下来的痕迹，和轻微变色的屋顶。</p> <p>And <B> due to</B> the <B> operating</B> cost, the owners don&#8217;t want to put it to full use as a regular stadium. <br />由于经营成本的考量，业主不想把它当作一般体育馆来充份利用。</p> <p>11.<B>performance</B>: n. 表演，成果<br />12.<B>event</B>: n. 事件，(比赛)项目<br />13.<B>match</B>: n. 比赛、对手<br />14. <B> opening ceremony</B>: n. 开幕式<br />15.<B>retractable</B>: a. 可缩回的，可取消的<br />16. <B> admission</B>: n. 门票，任用<br />17. <B> memory</B>: n. 记忆<br />18.<B>eventually</B>: adv. 最终<br />19.<B>concern</B>: v. 使关心，使担心</p> <p>[Zhang Hengli, CITIC Consortium Stadium Operation Company]:<br />&#8220;The next <B> performance</B> will be in May of this year, but for sports <B> events</B> like football <B> matches</B> and other large events it is more difficult. Because of the Olympic <B> opening ceremony</B> we have a <B> retractable</B>grass pitch which is very expensive to set and remove, so a single football match would not be worth the cost.&#8221;<br />张恒力(读音，中信联合体体育场营运公司)说：“今年五月会再使用鸟巢一次。但对于体育活动，像足球赛或其它大型活动，就很难使用它。因为在奥运开幕式上，我们做了一个可伸缩的草地球场，要架设和移开这个球场费用昂贵，所以单单一个足球赛不值得花那个成本。”</p> <p>While <B> admission</B> fees from tourists are covering costs for now, Zhang says, <B> memories</B> of the 2008 Games will <B> eventually</B> fade. <br />张先生说，虽然现在游客的门票到目前还能支付所需费用，但2008年奥运的记忆终将消退。</p> <p>Mother and travel agent from Shaanxi province Shi Aiping is <B> concerned</B>about the financial implications of an Olympic white elephant. <br />陕西省来的施爱萍，是母亲兼旅游代理商。她担心鸟巢可能成为奥运后的财务重担。</p> <p>[Shi Anping, Travel Agent from Shaanxi Province]:<br />&#8220;I think it would be a great waste, it really would. Because the Bird&#8217;s Nest really did cost a lot of money, and if it had no other use it would be a great waste.&#8221; <br />施爱萍(陕西省旅游代理商)说: “我觉得鸟巢可能成为巨大的浪费，确实是这样的。因为鸟巢的确耗费了很多钱，如果它没有其它用途的话，将是巨大的浪费。”</p> <p>20. <B> anniversary</B>: n.a. 周年纪念(的)<br />21.<B>cancel</B>: v. 取消，废除<br />22.<B>tender</B>: n. v. 投标<br />23.<B>athletics</B>: n. 体育(课)，竞技</p> <p>A performance of the opera Turandot is planned for the one-year <B> anniversary</B> of the opening of the Olympics on August 8.<br />歌剧杜兰朵公主计划在今年8月8日，奥运开幕周年庆时演出。</p> <p>But plans for the Beijing Guo&#8217;an soccer team to play their home matches at the stadium were <B> cancelled. </B> And authorities have yet to invite <B> tenders</B>to host the IAAF Diamond Grand Prix <B> athletics</B> meeting that will begin in 2010.<br />但原本计划在鸟巢举办的北京国安足球队主赛已被取消。当局将邀请投标国际田联钻石联赛体育会议的东道，这些会议将于2010年开始召开。<br />@*</p> <p> <p><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
