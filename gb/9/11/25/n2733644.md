sent to about a 100 people, I guess, and that day the people started coming, and they came, and they came, and they came&#8230;&#8221;<br />哈德逊谷大蒜节的发起人帕特．瑞波特说：“我决定在我们的前院和我的药草园举办一个小型的大蒜野餐，所以我就透过我的新讯通知信发了邀请函……给大约100个人，我猜，结果那天大家开始来了，一直来，一直来，一直来……”</p> <p>And since then, she has been honored with this title.<br />从此以后，她开始被冠以这个名号。</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;I would say in the second festival people started calling me the Goddess of Garlic, so it <B>stuck</B>, at least during the garlic festival.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“从第二届开始大家叫我大蒜女神，这称号一直跟着我，至少在大蒜节是如此。”</p> <p>With the help of the <B>Kiwanis Club</B>, the event has grown to be the biggest garlic festival in New York.<br />在同济会的协助下，这活动逐渐成为纽约最大的大蒜节。</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;So I went to the Kiwanis Club, and asked them if they would be interested in this event&#8230; and so that&#8217;s how this festival started.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“我到同济会去，问他们对这个活动有没有兴趣。这节庆就是这么来的。”</p> <p>[Richard Kappler,Chairman, Hudson Valley Garlic Festival]:<br />&#8220;We typically average between about 40 and 50 thousand visitors over the weekend each year.&#8221;<br />哈德逊谷大蒜节的主席理查．卡卜勒说：“我们每年在周末大概有4万到5万的民众参加。”</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;And this is their 20th festival, someone told me, I can&#8217;t believe it. I mean, it&#8217;s like&#8230; You know, you don&#8217;t realize how time passes when you&#8217;re having fun.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“有人告诉我这已经是第20届了，我简直不敢相信。我的意思是，那就像……你知道，当你乐在其中就不会察觉时间的流逝。”</p> <p>[Richard Kappler, Chairman, Hudson Valley Garlic Festival]:<br />&#8220;Well we have a couple of different groups of <B>vendors</B>, but all total we have about 220 vendors.&#8221;<br />哈德逊谷大蒜节的主席理查．卡卜勒说：“我们有各种不同的摊贩，总共大约有220摊。”</p> <p>And which garlic is the goddesses favorite?<br />哪种大蒜是女神的最爱呢？</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;I like both the hard neck and the soft neck garlic&#8230; It&#8217;s more of a <B>gourmet</B> garlic, you have to find the farmer who grows it.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“硬颈和软颈的大蒜我都喜欢，比高级大蒜更好，种植这种大蒜的农夫必须要找。”</p> <p>So what are some of the garlic varieties?<br />那么大蒜有哪些种类呢？</p> <p>[Garlic Grower]:<br />&#8220;[White] German stiff neck garlic.&#8221;<br />蒜农说：“（白）德国硬颈大蒜。”</p> <p>[Frank Rühstorfer, Spanish Roja Garlic Grower]:<br />&#8220;Spanish Roja&#8230; It&#8217;s a stiff neck garlic, so those tend to be more fuller flavored garlic.&#8221;<br />西班牙罗亚蒜农法兰克．鲁斯道夫说：“西班牙罗亚……是种硬颈大蒜，一般来讲味道比较重。”</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>lip balm :</B>n. 护唇膏<br />2.<B>mellowness:</B> n. 醇熟甜美<br />3.<B>vanilla:</B> n. 香草<br />4.<B>chunks:</B> n. 一大块<br />5. <B> bits:</B> n. 一小块<br />6.<B>yummy:</B> a. 可口的<br />7.<B>venue:</B> n. 地点<br />8. <B> recipe:</B> n. 烹饪法<br />9.<B>ornament:</B> n. 装饰<br />10. <B>earring</B>: n. 耳环</p> <p>[Jacob Hooper, Garlic Grower]:<br />&#8220;We grow 2 varietes, German Red and German White.&#8221;<br />蒜农贾考伯．琥波说：“我们有两种，德国红大蒜和德国白大蒜。”</p> <p>And of course, many many more&#8230; Here you can even find garlic <B>lip balm</B>, garlic soap, elephant garlic, and garlic ice cream&#8230;<br />当然，还有许许多多，在这里你可找到大蒜护唇膏、大蒜皂、象蒜和大蒜冰淇淋……</p> <p>[Gaby Baker, Visitor]:<br />&#8220;I waited a whole year for this ice cream, and it is as good as ever, I love it.&#8221;<br />参观者加比．贝克说：“我等这冰淇淋等了一整年，它一如往昔的美味，我爱死它了。”</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;The ice cream kind of puts <B>mellowness</B> to the garlic flavour, and I really like it.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“这冰淇淋的蒜味相当醇熟甜美，我非常喜欢。”</p> <p>[Gaby Baker, Visitor]:<br />&#8220;You get the nice sweet taste of <B>vanilla</B> ice cream and then you get little <B>chunks</B> and <B>bits</B> of the garlic, that&#8217;s just <B>yummy</B>.&#8221;<br />参观者加比．贝克说：“你会先尝到香草冰淇淋的甜味，然后会吃到大大小小的蒜块，真的很可口。”</p> <p>What else is going down at this yearly event?<br />在这年度活动中还有什么呢？</p> <p>[Frank Rühstorfer, Spanish Roja Garlic Grower]:<br />&#8220;It&#8217;s an incredible <B>venue</B> because we can come here and move a lot of garlic at retail prices.&#8221;<br />西班牙罗亚蒜农法兰克．鲁斯道夫说：“这个场地非常棒，我们来到这里，而且以零售价格搬了很多的大蒜。”</p> <p>[Jacob Hooper, Garlic Grower]:<br />&#8220;We got a lot of folks who come here, buy their garlic, take it home, eat a little, plant a little, come back the next year: &#8216;oh your garlic was great, we had terrific success growing it&#8217;.&#8221;<br />蒜农贾考伯．琥波说：“许多人来到这里，买些大蒜，带回家，吃掉一些，种下一些，下一年再度回到这里，说着‘哦，你的大蒜很棒，种的很成功’。”</p> <p>What is the goddess of garlic&#8217;s next move ?<br />大蒜女神的下一步是？</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;You know, I had collected so many garlic <B>recipes</B> and done my own thing with them, that I should write a cookbook.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“你知道，我蓃集了很多大蒜的烹饪法，然后自己烹调，我应该写本烹饪书。”</p> <p>This might even create a demand for garlic fashion.<br />这可能将创造一股大蒜时尚的需求。</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;You know these garlic christmas <B>ornaments</B>, and I thought: &#8216;wow, I could make earrings out of those&#8217;.&#8221;<br />大蒜女神帕特．瑞波特说：“你知道的，像这样的大蒜圣诞装饰，于是我就想：‘哇，我可以用来做耳环’。”</p> <p>[Pat Reppert, The Goddess of Garlic]:<br />&#8220;People who like garlic are usually crazy about garlic.&#8221;<br
