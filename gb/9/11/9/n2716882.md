<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语(208)：远离新流感的洗手舞</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B> Tokyo Kids Dance to Keep Away Swine Flu</B><br />东京幼童跳舞远离新流感<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3De61445e634aa479ca24aedbe6c883056001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> </p> <p>本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语<br /><--ads--></p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>swine flu</B>(n)猪流感（新流感的早期称呼）<br />2. <B>kindergarten</B>(n)幼稚园<br />3. <B>wiggle</B>(v)扭动<br />4. <B>foam</B>(v)使起泡沫<br />5. <B>routine</B>(n)一套固定舞步(或动作)<br />6. <B>choreograph</B>(v)为……编舞<br />7. <B>come up with</B>(v phr)(针对问题等)想出<br />8. <B>pandemic</B>(n)大流行病</p> <p>Over 50 children at a Japanese <B>kindergarten</B> sing and <B>wiggle</B> their hips, while making soap <B>foam</B> to clean their hands. They’re celebrating the United Nations&#8217; World Hand-Washing Day on Thursday, October 15. The dance <B>routine</B> is meant to keep bugs away and prevent swine flu.<br />一所日本幼稚园里，超过50位孩童边唱边扭动臀部，同时制造肥皂泡沫来清洁他们的双手，他们正在庆祝10月15日周四的联合国世界洗手日。这套例行的舞蹈动作，目的在远离病菌和预防新流感。</p> <p>A United Nations-led program in Japan <B>choreographed</B> the dance routines to teach kids the proper way to wash their hands.<br />在日本一项联合国主导的计划编了一套例行性舞蹈动作，来教导孩童正确的洗手方式。</p> <p>[Haruka Mimi, Kindergarten Student]: &#8220;It was really fun!&#8221;<br />[Haruka Mimi, 幼稚园学童]: “它真的很有趣！”</p> <p>Many children were already aware of the H1N1 virus or the swine flu.<br />许多孩童已经知道了H1N1病毒或新流感。</p> <p>[Moemi Tenma, Kindergarten Student]: &#8220;I&#8217;ve been washing hands to protect myself from the flu!&#8221;<br />[Moemi Tenma, 幼稚园学童]: “我总是洗手来防护自己远离流感”</p> <p>Others simply enjoy the dance, saying they’ll do it everyday. <br />其他人就只是喜欢这套舞蹈，说他们会天天跳。</p> <p>[Hidekazu Otsuka, Kindergarten Student]: &#8220;I&#8217;ll never forget to wash my hands from now on!&#8221; <br />[Hidekazu Otsuka, 幼稚园学童]:“此现在起，我永远不会忘记洗手！”</p> <p>The foamy dance routines were designed to cover all hand parts, including the palms, fingers, nails and wrists. It aims to make young children more aware of the details.<br />这套设计的泡沫舞蹈动作涵盖了手的所有部位，包括手掌、手指，指甲和手腕，目标在于让小朋友更了解细节的动作。</p> <p>[Ayako Uragami, Japan Committee UNICEF]: <br />&#8220;In general, we need at least 20 seconds to wash our hands thoroughly, which sounds like a long time for little children. We <B>came up with</B> this universal hand-washing dance so that children can have fun and be motivated to wash their hands instead of thinking it&#8217;s boring and tiresome.&#8221;<br />[Ayako Uragami, 联合国儿童基金日本委员会]:<br />“一般而言，我们至少需要20秒彻底地洗净双手，对小朋友来说，听起来像是一段满长的时间。我们想出这套世界共用的洗手舞，以便小朋友从中获得乐趣而且增加洗手的动机，而不会认为它是无聊、烦人的。”</p> <p>This class is fun, but H1N1 is actually quite serious, with the World Health Organization predicting the <B>pandemic</B> could potentially affect 2 billion people globally. <br />这套课程是趣味的，然而H1N1实际上相当严重，世界卫生组织预测流感的大流行可能影响全球20亿人。</p> <p>In Japan, kindergartens in five cities joined in the soapy dance routines. Various activities have been held across more than 80 countries for the UN World Hand-Washing Day.<br />在日本，有5座城市的幼稚园加入了这项例行肥皂舞活动。超过80个国家，在联合国世界洗手日，举办了各式各样的活动。<br />@*</p> <p><p> <font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
