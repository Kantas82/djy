<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语（145）北京大火是央视的错</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Beijing Blaze Was CCTV&#8217;s Fault</B><br />北京大火是中央电视台的错</p> <p>本则英语影音新闻及出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻 <ahref= >http://english.ntdtv.com/?c=145&#038;a=7646</a>（可线上观看） </p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B></p> <p>1.<B>complex </B>: n. 复合体</p> <p>2.<B>state-run </B>:adj. 国营的</p> <p>3.<B>to blame for </B>:ph. 为～受谴责</p> <p>4.<B>rooftop </B>:n.　屋顶</p> <p>Anchor: We told you yesterday about the building in Beijing that went up in flames on Monday during the Lantern Festival celebrations. It&#8217;s a hotel building that&#8217;s part of the CCTV <B>complex</B>.<br />主播：延续昨日的报导，周一在元宵灯节活动庆祝期间，北京的一栋大楼燃起熊熊大火。起火的是一栋旅馆建筑，属于央视大楼复合体的一部分。</p> <p>CCTV—which is China&#8217;s <B>state-run</B> TV station—has now confirmed that people it hired are <B>to blame for </B>starting the fire. They had launched dangerous fireworks from the <B>rooftop </B>without proper permits.<br />现在，中国国营的中央电视台台方已确认，这场大火是由电视台的雇员所引燃的。他们在未经允许的情况下就私自从屋顶燃放危险的烟火。</p> <p>Yesterday, CCTV apologized for the incident. It had killed one fireman, injured at least six others, and severely damaged the 500-million-dollar building.<br />昨日，中央电视台针对这场意外道歉。这场大火造成一个消防员死亡，至少六个消防员受伤，并且对这栋造价五亿元的建筑物造成严重损害。</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B></p> <p>5.<B>brigade</B>: n. 队</p> <p>6. <B> sprinkler</B>: n. 洒水器</p> <p>7.<B>fireproofing</B>: n. 防火设施</p> <p>8.<B>leaked</B>: adj. 泄漏的(秘密)</p> <p>9. <B> propaganda</B>: n. 宣传</p> <p>A spokesman for the Beijing Fire <B>Brigade</B> says flames spread quickly throughout the building—which was still under construction—because <B>sprinkler</B> systems and <B>fireproofing</B> were not yet in place.<br />北京消防队的发言人表示，大火延烧整栋大楼的速度很快，因为这栋大楼还在盖，里头的洒水系统和防火设施都还没完成。</p> <p>A <B>leaked</B> memo shows the Communist Party&#8217;s <B>Propaganda </B>Bureau ordered media to use only official reports on the incident, and prevent Internet users from posting comments and photos of the fire.<br />一份外流的资料显示，中共的宣传部下令媒体只能用官方材料报导这一起意外事件，并且禁止网路使用者转贴照片和发表关于这场大火的意见。</p> <p><B>【新闻关键字】</B></p> <p>10.<B>circulate</B>: v. 流通</p> <p>11.<B>footage</B>: n. 画面</p> <p>12.<B>omen</B>: n. 前兆</p> <p>But by Tuesday, photos and video of the fire&#8211;captured by ordinary citizens&#8211;were <B>circulating</B> the Internet, including the <B>footage</B> you are watching, which was posted to YouTube. <br />但是在星期二，由一般市民所捕捉的大火照片和录像，已传遍网路上，包括您现在正在收看的画面，也是网友上传到YouTube上的影片。</p> <p>Many on the Internet called the blaze a bad <B>omen</B> for the new year.<br />许多网友称这场大火是新的一年的厄兆。<br />@*</p> <p><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
