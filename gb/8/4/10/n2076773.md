<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语  第269课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元4月10日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa269.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center>	</p> <p>李华告诉Larry说，经常有学生上课时间用手机。李华今天要学两个常用语，texting和txt tlk.</p> <p>LL: So, Li Hua, your students won&#8217;t stop using their cell phones in class? That is so rude! It is really impolite to be texting when you professor is giving a lecture.</p> <p>LH: 你说的texting想必就是text messaging发短信的意思吧？</p> <p>LL: Yes, Li Hua, texting is short for text messaging. And have to say I think it is rude for students to text in class.</p> <p>LH: 我也觉得在课堂上发短信是对老师的不尊重，所以我今天宣布，上课时间手机一律要关机。</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s a good class policy, Li Hua. I hope your students show you some respect and stop texting in class.</p> <p>LH: 我也希望他们上课不要再发短信了。可是我那些学生好像随时随地都在发短信。</p> <p>LL: Yeah, a lot of young people do. I read a news report the other day that several states in the U.S. are thinking of banning teens from texting while driving a car.</p> <p>LH: 什么？有些高中生开车的时候都在发短信？太危险了！</p> <p>LL: I know. I can&#8217;t believe it myself. I can&#8217;t imagine holding on to the steering wheel with one hand and texting with the other.</p> <p>LH: 一手握着方向盘，一手发短信，还要注意周围的路况。这简直不可能。</p> <p>LL: I agree. Texting while driving is a bad idea. Actually, Li Hua, I don&#8217;t text very often, do you?</p> <p>LH: 我有时会跟中国的朋友发短信联络，因为他们觉得这样可以练习英语。</p> <p>LL: Texting to practice English is a great idea! But as for me, I&#8217;d rather call my friends and talk to them than text them.</p> <p>LH: 我觉得这跟年纪有关。我妈就从来不发短信，我虽然有时发，但跟我的学生比，可差远了。</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s true. Say, how have your students reacted to your new &#8220;No Texting&#8221; policy?</p> <p>LH: 他们有点不高兴，但也知道上课发短信是不对的。</p> <p>LL: So, Li Hua, what will you do if you catch someone texting in class now?</p> <p>LH: 这简单。被抓住上课发短信的，手机一律没收，下课来取。</p> <p>LL: Whoa! I bet no one will be texting in your class again, Li Hua.</p> <p>******</p> <p>LL: So, Li Hua, since you text to your friends in China, have you learned about text talk?</p> <p>LH: 我也是刚知道text talk是怎么回事的。简而言之，text talk是一种特殊的短信语言，为了提高打字速度，对一些词进行简化。</p> <p>LL: Right! Text talk is a language, and it has its own set of rules.</p> <p>LH: 我的学生都是这方面的专家，我从他们那儿学到了不少text talk的规则.</p> <p>LL: Really, Li Hua? Maybe you can teach me about text talk. As you know, I don&#8217;t text very often.</p> <p>LH: 首先，打字的时候尽可能把元音字母省略掉。</p> <p>LL: Like Text Talk is shortened to TXT TLK.</p> <p>LH: 没错。把 Text, t-e-x-t中间的e省掉，变成t-x-t; 再把 talk, t-a-l-k中间的a省掉，变成t-l-k.</p> <p>LL: But even with the missing letters, you can still understand text talk, because your brain fills them in.</p> <p>LH: 对啊，看习惯了就好了。我最喜欢的是用数字替代字母。</p> <p>LL: Can you give me an example, Li Hua?</p> <p>LH: 比如说，打see you later一会儿见的时候，later, l-a-t-e-r, 就可以打成L-8-R,发音非常相近。</p> <p>LL: L-8-R for &#8220;later&#8221; is very clever. I bet the &#8220;See You&#8221; part is also shortened in text talk.</p> <p>LH: 没错。一般发短信的时候，see you可以简写为字母c加上字母u, 发音完全一样，也是C-U.</p> <p>LL: Mm, see you later, C-U L-8-R. Text talk is so funny, Li Hua! It makes me want to laugh out loud.</p> <p>LH: 你提醒我了，在text talk里面，如果要写laugh out loud, 可以用三个首写字母L-O-L来表达。</p> <p>LL: So, if I text L-O-L, my friend will know that I find something so funny it makes me want to laugh out loud?</p> <p>LH: 没错。L-O-L在短信里，就代表laugh out loud, 意思是看到了非常好笑的事情。哎呀，说得高兴忘了时间，I&#8217;ve got to go. C-U L-8-R! See you later, Larry!</p> <p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是texting, 指发短信。另一个是txt tlk, 是短信语言中使用缩略语。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
