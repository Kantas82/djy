<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语  第270课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元4月17日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa270.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center>	</p> <p>李华告诉Larry说，情人节那天，她班上的一个男生收到了很多女孩子送的花。李华今天要学两个常用语，heartthrob和gaga.</p> <p>LL: So, this student walked into your classroom on Valentine&#8217;s Day and THREE different girls gave him flowers? He must be the campus heartthrob.</p> <p>LH: 如果你说的heartthrob, 是指让人迷恋的对象，那没错，他可以校园名人，学校里喜欢他的女孩子一大堆。</p> <p>LL: A heartthrob is a male that is adored by women. Someone who is a heartthrob, literally makes women&#8217;s hearts throb.</p> <p>LH: 这种说法真形象！让人心跳的男人就是heartthrob. 送花的那三个女生迷他迷得不行，我真担心会影响到她们的成绩。</p> <p>LL: What makes this heartthrob so attractive to these girls? Is he handsome? Is he friendly? Is he a good student? Is he an athlete?</p> <p>LH: 他确实各方面都很出众，不仅长得一表人才，而且很随和、学习成绩优秀，而且还是校队橄榄球队的队长。</p> <p>LL: Well, it is no wonder he is such a heart throb. He sounds like the perfect guy.</p> <p>LH: 差不多吧。不过还是不如布拉德.皮特完美。Brad Pitt is a real heart throb.</p> <p>LL: You are not the first woman who has told me Brad Pitt is the perfect man. I guess I don&#8217;t understand why he is such a heart throb.</p> <p>LH: 这有什么难理解的。(Dreamily)你看他那金发碧眼，他的微笑能让人溶化，还有他的身材和肌肉&#8230;.</p> <p>LL: Okay, okay. That&#8217;s enough, Li Hua. I get it! I understand why Brad Pitt is such a heartthrob.</p> <p>LH: 对不起，Larry，一提起Brad Pitt，我就无法控制自己。</p> <p>LL: So, Brad Pitt is a big heartthrob in America. How about in China? Who is the biggest Chinese heart throb?</p> <p>LH: 那可能要算香港的电影影星刘德华了。</p> <p>LL: I have heard of Andy Lau. Isn&#8217;t he too old to be a heart throb?</p> <p>LH: 年轻的也有，不过我已经赶不少潮流了。</p> <p>LL: Well, it must be hard to be a heartthrob with girls following you everywhere. I&#8217;m glad I don&#8217;t have that problem. I mean, I&#8217;m not much of a heartthrob.</p> <p>LH: 肯定有什么女孩子为你心动的，你不知道罢了。</p> <p>******</p> <p>LL: So, let&#8217;s talk about these girls in your class some more, Li Hua. When they go gaga over this heart throb in class, what do you do to get their attention?</p> <p>LH: Gaga是什么意思？听起来不像英文啊？</p> <p>LL: Gaga is, in fact, a French world borrowed into English, Li Hua. &#8220;Gaga&#8221; is a word that suggests some one who is mentally confused or silly.</p> <p>LH: 我班上那几个女孩子可都是好学生，她们的脑子可没问题。</p> <p>LL: &#8220;To *go* gaga&#8221; means to like something so much that you act like an idiot. It means you can&#8217;t think of anything else besides the thing you are &#8220;gaga&#8221; over.</p> <p>LH: 噢，to go gaga是被弄得晕头转向的意思。要是这么说，I guess the girls do go gaga over him.</p> <p>LL: What about the boy students in your class? Is there a girl in class that they go gaga over?</p> <p>LH: 我们班上倒是没有什么特别招人的女生，但是学校里所有男生都围着啦啦队的女孩子们转。</p> <p>LL: Well, cheerleaders are usually very attractive and physically fit. It&#8217;s no wonder guys go gaga for cheer leaders.</p> <p>LH: 那你呢，Larry? 有什么人让你晕头转向的吗？</p> <p>LL: Well, Li Hua, I&#8217;m not really dating any one right now, so there is no one I am really gaga over, but there is something I&#8217;m really gaga for.</p> <p>LH: 什么东西让你gaga for?</p> <p>LL: I&#8217;m really gaga for computers. I think working with computers is so interesting, and I love playing computer games.</p> <p>LH: 我对电脑可没兴趣。每天坐在电脑前面写论文，我恨不得离电脑远远的。</p> <p>LL: Well, what do you go gaga over, Li Hua?</p> <p>LH: 我呀，喜欢看八卦杂志，了解像Brad Pitt这些明星的最新动向。</p> <p>LL: We&#8217;re back to Brad Pitt again?! Fine. You can go gaga over Brad Pitt, Li Hua. I&#8217;ll stick with my computer.</p> <p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是heartthrob, 指让人心动的男人。另一个是gaga, 指被什么人或是什么事弄得晕头转向。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
