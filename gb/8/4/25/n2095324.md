<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第271课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元4月25日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa271.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center>	</p> <p>Larry跟李华一起吃中饭，他发现李华心情很糟糕。李华今天要学两个常用语，glum和chum.</p> <p>LL: Hey, Li Hua. You haven&#8217;t eaten any of your food. Why so glum?</p> <p>LH: 如果你说的glum, 是沮丧的意思，那没错，我这两天心情确实很糟糕。</p> <p>LL: Yes, glum means sad. So, what&#8217;s wrong?</p> <p>LH: 我上星期去面试，本来以为那家公司肯定会要我，结果他们却选择了别人，害得我这两天一点儿食欲也没有。 Glum可以用来形容我现在的情况吗？</p> <p>LL: Yes, but I&#8217;m sorry to hear that you didn&#8217;t get the job. We use the word &#8216;glum&#8217; when we are sad and disappointed about something, but not something extremely serious, such as a death in the family.</p> <p>LH: 我知道了，glum是用来形容情绪低落或是失望，但如果是特别严重的事情，就不能用glum了。这两天烦心事太多，老天爷也不帮忙，连着这么多天的阴雨天气。</p> <p>LL: Don&#8217;t worry &#8211; this will all pass eventually. Everybody gets glum at times.</p> <p>LH: 你呢Larry? 有什么事会让你心烦意乱吗？</p> <p>LL: Well, rainy days can definitely have an effect on me, but I&#8217;d have to say that beating the gloominess is more important than what makes me feel glum.</p> <p>LH: 要想换个好心情可没那么容易，有人吃东西会感觉好起来，可是食物对我不起作用。</p> <p>LL: Well, a lot of times, people resort to things or people they can count on when they&#8217;re glum. For instance, I take a walk and listen to my favorite music, but I do this on my own. Others prefer to confide in friends and family.</p> <p>LH: 我就是；我往往会向朋友倾诉，或是看电视转移一下注意力，不过你说的出去走走，听听音乐好像也不错，而且还可以锻练身体。</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s true! I never thought of that! Maybe I should run and listen to music the next time I&#8217;m glum.</p> <p>LH: (Laughing)只要能让心情好起来就行！跟你说说我感觉好多了，胃口也好起来了。不好，这饭菜都凉了，我得让他们帮我热热。(To waiter) Excuse me &#8211; do you mind heating this up for me? Thanks!</p> <p>******</p> <p>LH: 我以前听别人说，&#8221;Why so glum, chum.&#8221; 就是问别人为什么看上去心情很不好的意思吧？</p> <p>LL: Oh yes! We use that when we are asking a friend, &#8216;hey, what&#8217;s wrong.&#8217; &#8216;Chum&#8217; means &#8216;friend&#8217; or &#8216;buddy,&#8217; especially when one male is speaking to another male. It&#8217;s very informal.</p> <p>LH: 哦，一般是男孩子之间说的，chum就是哥们儿，兄弟的意思。</p> <p>LL: We say it this way because it rhymes, and it&#8217;s intended to cheer people up!</p> <p>LH: Why so glum, chum. 确实押韵。你知道吗Larry, 我真的很喜欢这次没有被录用的那份工作，我事先准备了好几个小时呢。</p> <p>LL: So tell me what went wrong. Maybe if we talk about it you won&#8217;t be so glum.</p> <p>LH: 我当时特别紧张，可能是话太多了。我到得很准时，还带去了自己的简历和推荐信，现在回想起来，可能是显得太想得到那份工作了。</p> <p>LL: Were you friendly with the people who were interviewing you? Were you all &#8216;chummy&#8217; with them?</p> <p>LH: 我跟他们很客气。你说的&#8221;chummy&#8221;是拉近乎的意思吗？</p> <p>LL: Yes. It depends. Some interviewers like that, and others don&#8217;t. A lot of it can depend on personality.</p> <p>LH: 那Larry, 我们一起吃午饭，你帮我排解郁闷的心情，那咱们俩儿算不算是&#8221;acting chummy&#8221;呢？</p> <p>LL: Good question. Since we are already friends, our friendliness comes naturally. Being &#8216;chummy&#8217; is somewhat artificial, because it&#8217;s between two people who don&#8217;t know each other that well. So, we&#8217;re not being &#8216;chummy.&#8217;</p> <p>LH: 所以说chummy是指本来不熟的人，好像显得关系很好，那用在咱们这种老朋友的身上，就不合适了。</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s right. So, did you act chummy with the interviewer?</p> <p>LH: 要这么说，我跟面试的人还真没有acting chummy. 我没有像老朋友那样，把自己的事情告诉他。Actually, he looked quite glum.</p> <p>LL: It&#8217;s a good thing you didn&#8217;t say, &#8216;why so glum, chum?&#8217; I don&#8217;t think that would have helped you get the job!</p> <p>LH: (Laughing)没错！你可真厉害，Larry, 被你这么一说，我的胃口马上就好起来了。我真得好好谢谢你！</p> <p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是glum, 指情绪低落。另一个是chum, 指好朋友。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
