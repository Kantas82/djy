<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(116)毒奶粉事件新消息</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20201003111-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>China Milk Powder Update</B><br />中国奶粉事件最新消息</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>crisis</B>: n. 危机<br />2. <B>flock</B>: v. 蜂拥而至<br />3. <B> infant</B>: n. 婴儿 <br />4. <B>check-up</B>: n. 检查<br />5. <B>dairy</B>: n. 乳品业<br />6. <B>melamine</B>: n. 三聚氰胺<br />7. <B>illegal</B>: a. 非法的</p>
<p>Anchor: An update on the Chinese milk powder<B> crisis</B> to tell you about. Crowds of Chinese parents are<B> flocking</B> to a Beijing hospital today, bringing their <B>infants</B> for free health <B>check-ups</B>. Here&#8217;s more on this developing story.<br /> 新闻主播：告诉您中国奶粉危机的最新消息：今天中国大批父母带着他们的婴儿，蜂涌至北京医院做免费健康检查。以下是更多的相关报导。</p>
<p>STORY: Nearly 10 percent of milk samples from the three top Chinese <B>dairies </B> contain <B>melamine</B>. The<B> illegal</B> chemical has killed four children and left thousands more ill in hospital.<br /> 新闻内容：从中国前三家重要乳制品业者提供的样品中，近百分之十的牛奶样品含有三聚氰胺。这种非法化学品已造成4名儿童死亡，数千名儿童住院。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />8. <B>passed away</B>: v.phr. 过世<br />9. <B>acute</B>: a. 急性的<br />10. <B>kidney</B>: n. 肾脏</p>
<p>So far 6,244 children have become ill. Four have<B> passed away</B> and 158 are suffering <B>acute kidney</B> failure. According to Chinese Communist Party websites 86 babies are now ill in the northwest Xinjiang region as well. All this has parents extremely worried.目前已有6244名儿童患病，4名去世，158名罹患急性肾功能衰竭。根据中共网站，西北新疆地区也有86名婴儿得病。凡此种种都让父母极们极为担心。</p>
<p>[Jin Yantoa, Son Fallen Ill]: &#8220;If he isn&#8217;t OK, they say that the treatment will be free. When he&#8217;s thoroughly checked we can leave.&#8221;<br />儿子患病的金先生说：“如果孩子有问题，他们说会免费治疗。孩子彻底检查过后，我们就可以离开了。”</p>
<p>【新闻关键字】<br />11. <B> trick</B>: v. 蒙骗<br />12. <B> nationwide</B>: a. 全国性的<br />13. <B>carry out</B>: v.phr. 进行<br />14. <B>contamination</B>: 污染<br />15. <B>brand</B>: n. 品牌 </p>
<p>Adding melamine to milk can <B>trick</B> quality tests, making the protein count appear higher than its actual level. A<B> nationwide </B>check <B>carried out</B> this week found that melamine<B> contamination</B> in dairy products is far wider than one <B>brand </B>of baby milk.<br /> 添加三聚氰胺可以蒙骗质量检测，让显示出的蛋白质指数高于实际指数。本周中国进行全国性检测，发现含三聚氰胺毒的乳制品，远远比婴儿奶粉的一种品牌来的广泛。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />16. <B>suspect</B>: v. 怀疑<br />17. <B>tainted</B>: adj. 污染的、有毒的<br />18. <B>recover</B>: v. 复原<br />19. <B>in danger</B>: phr. 处于危险中<br />20. <B>admit</B>: v. 承认</p>
<p>Shanxi Province mother Li Ligna, took her child to a local hospital for tests. She was advised to bring her child to Beijing for further tests after doctors <B>suspected</B> he&#8217;s suffering from an illness related to <B>tainted </B>milk powder.<br />山西省李姓母亲带她的孩子到当地医院检查。医生怀疑孩子患有毒奶粉相关疾病，建议她带孩子到北京做进一步检查。</p>
<p> [Li Ligna, Son Fallen Ill]: &#8220;Yes I am (worried). Because I&#8217;ve heard when they are ill it is really hard, and it takes a long time for them to<B> recover</B>. My child&#8217;s health is usually pretty good, but when I took him for a check-up in a local hospital we were told to come to Beijing the next day as his life was<B> in danger</B>.&#8221; 儿子患病的李妈妈说：“是啊，我很担心，因为我听说病患生病的时候很痛苦，而且要很久才会复原。我孩子一向很健康，但当我带他到当地医院做检查时，医生要我们第二天就到北京治疗，因为孩子已经有生命危险了。”</p>
<p>Chinese officials have<B> admitted</B> that city authorities in Shijiazhuang, where the Sanlu Group is based, delayed news of milk contamination for over a month.中国官员已坦承，三鹿集团所在地石家庄当局，延误了一个多月才公布毒牛奶消息。</p>
