<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(114)日本毒水饺事件</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20201003111-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Japan&#8217;s Poison Dumplings Made in China</B><br />日本毒水饺来自中国</p>
<p> <B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B> trace</B>：n. 踪迹<br />2. <B>frozen dumplings</B>：n. 冷冻水饺<br />3. <B> blame</B>：v. 归咎<br />4. <B>contamination</B>：n. 污染、有毒<br />5. <B>complaint</B>：n. 控诉<br />6. <B>strain</B>：n. 紧绷<br />7. <B>deliberately laced</B>：故意加上<br />8. <B>organic phosphorous insecticide</B>：n.有机磷杀虫剂<br />9. <B>disclose</B>：v. 公布</p>
<p>Anchor: Back in January, the Japanese Health Ministry found<B> traces</B> of poison inside <B>frozen dumplings</B> imported from China. At least 10 Japanese had become sick afte Anchor:<br />新闻主播：回到今年元月，日本卫生部在从中国进口的冷冻水饺中发现有毒物质，至少有十位日本人在食用后生病。</p>
<p>At the time, Chinese officials said that Japan was too hasty in <B>blaming</B> China for the <B>contamination</B>, and that Japan&#8217;s <B>complaints</B> put a <B>strain</B> on Sino-Japanese relations. <br />在那时侯，中国官员说日本太匆促将有毒物质归咎中国，日本的控诉使得中日关系紧绷。</p>
<p>But last week, Chinese officials admitted for the first time that there was poison in the exported dumplings—and that they may have been <B>deliberately laced</B>—according to a Japan Broadcasting Corporation report. 但根据一家日本广播公司报导，上周中国官员首次承认，在出口的水饺中有毒，而且可能是故意加进去的。</p>
<p>Traces of an <B>organic phosphorous insecticide</B>, banned in Japan but not in China, were found in a sampling of dumplings made by Tianyang Food Processing in Hebei Province.在由河北天洋食品公司所制造的水饺样品中，发现有机磷杀虫剂，这种杀虫剂在日本是被禁用的，但在中国则没有。</p>
<p>In July, the Japanese government was accused by the media of ignoring safety concerns because it avoided—at Beijing’s request—<B>disclosing</B> that people in China were also poisoned by the same product from the same company. 七月份日本政府被媒体控诉：在北京的要求下忽略安全考量，因为日政府避免公布：中国人民也因为来自同一家公司的相同产品而中毒。</p>
