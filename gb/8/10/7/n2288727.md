<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(113)人权圣火在中国</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20201003111-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Human Rights Torch Still Burns inside China</B><br />人权圣火仍在中国内部燃烧</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>extinguish</B>：v. 扑灭<br />2. <B>underground</B>：adj. 地下的<br />2. <B>torch</B>：n. 火炬、火把<br />3. <B>relay</B>：n. 接力赛<br />4. <B>grassroots campaign</B>：n. 草根性活动<br />5. <B>awareness</B>：n. 觉知<br />6. <B>human rights abuses</B>：n. 践踏人权<br />7. <B> petition</B>：v. 请愿</p>
<p>Anchor: Even after Beijing&#8217;s Olympic flame has <B>extinguished</B>, the Human Rights Torch Relay continues. It&#8217;s still being passed around <B>underground </B>inside China, and has gotten lots of support from Chinese who had been hoping the CCP would improve its human rights situation.<br />新闻主播：虽然北京奥运火炬已经熄灭，人权圣火依然在中国境内台面下持续传递延烧，并且获得许多希望中共能改善人权的中国民众支持。 </p>
<p>STORY：The Human Rights<B> Torch Relay </B>is a global <B>grassroots campaign</B> to raise <B>awareness</B> of and stop the Chinese regime&#8217;s <B>human rights abuses</B>. After the Olympics, many Chinese are still unhappy with what they see as the regime having made little or no progress on human rights, despite promises it made.<br />新闻内容：人权圣火传递是一项全球草根性活动，为了唤醒民众关注并制止中共当局继续践踏人权。尽管中共承诺要改善人权，但在奥运过后，许多中国人还是对于他们所看到的当局，在人权上几乎没有进展而感到不快。 </p>
<p>We interviewed some Shanghai residents who are using the Torch Relay to<B> petition</B>for greater rights and freedom.<br />我们采访了一些上海居民，他们正利用人权圣火传递诉请更大的权利和自由。</p>
<p>&#8220;What we need are human rights and freedom. We hope the government takes more action with less empty talk. Give the Chinese people a stable, peaceful and harmonious living environment.&#8221;<br />我们需要人权和自由。我们希望政府采取的更多行动，少说些空谈。给中国人民一个稳定，平安及和谐的生活环境。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>covenant</B>：n. 盟约<br />2. <B>approve</B>：v. 批准<br />3. <B>be supposed to</B>：v.phr. 应该<br />4. <B>corruption</B>：n. 贪污<br />5. <B>suppression</B>：n. 镇压<br />6. <B>demolish</B>：v. 破坏<br />7. <B>dignity</B>：n. 尊严<br />8. <B>nonetheless</B>：adv. 不过</p>
<p>&#8220;The UN<B> Covenant</B> on Civil and Political Rights was signed more than a decade ago. But it&#8217;s still not<B> approved </B>by the People&#8217;s Congress. We<B> are supposed to</B> be the owners of the nation. But since we don&#8217;t have human rights, our property can be taken away easily. For high level Chinese officials, <B>corruption</B> has become part of their culture. It&#8217;s terrifying.&#8221;<br />“联合国的公民和政治权利的盟约在十多年就签了字，但是一直没有通过中国人大。我们应该是国家的所有者。但是，由于我们没有人权，我们的财产很容易被拿走。对于高级中国官员，腐败已经成为他们的文化。真是太可怕了。”</p>
<p>&#8220;The government uses <B>suppression</B> rather than laws. They <B>demolish</B> people&#8217;s houses with force and violence. They don&#8217;t allow people to complain, either. If they do, they&#8217;ll be punished.&#8221;<br />“政府使用镇压手段而不是法律，他们以强迫和暴力拆毁人们的房子。他们也不允许人们抱怨，否则就将遭受惩罚。”</p>
<p>&#8220;We welcome the Human Rights Torch Relay to Shanghai! We want freedom! We want human rights! We want houses! We want to live with <B>dignity</B>! Down with the illegal prison system!&#8221;<br />“我们欢迎人权圣火传递到上海！我们要自由! 我们要人权! 我们要房子! 我们要活的有尊严！非法监狱制度下台！”</p>
<p>Inside China today, the Human Rights Torch takes the form of posters and petitions, since a physical torch could draw attention—and arrests—from Chinese police. <B>Nonetheless</B>, for many Chinese, this symbolic torch can still bring to light the abuses they&#8217;re suffering under the Communist Party.<br />在中国境内，人权火炬采取海报和请愿的形式，因为实物的火炬可能引起中国员警的注意和抓捕。尽管如此，对于许多中国人而言，这个象征性的火炬仍带给生活在共产党统治下受苦的人们一线光明。</p>
