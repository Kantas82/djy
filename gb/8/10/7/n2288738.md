<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(114)西班牙蕃茄节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/10/810070440491452.jpg" />
<h6>Getty Images
</h6>
<hr>
<p><B>Paint the Town Red</B><br />西班牙蕃茄节</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>tomatina</B>: n.= tomato fight蕃茄大战 <br />2.	<B>tomato sauce</B>: n. 蕃茄酱<br />3.	<B>turn up</B>: v.phr. 出现、到达<br />4.	<B>intention</B>: n. 目的<br />5.	<B>splatter</B>: v. 泼、洒<br />6.	<B>ripe</B>：adj. 成熟的<br />7.	<B>squishy</B>: adj. 压扁的<br />8.	<B>missile</B>: n. 飞弹</p>
<p>Anchor:<br />Tomato throwers painted one Spanish town red for an annual <B>tomatina</B> festival. In the end the whole town and everyone in it looks to be covered in <B>tomato sauce</B>. Here&#8217;s more.<br />新闻主播：<br />一年一度的番茄节把西班牙小镇染成一片红，最后整个城镇每个人看起来都覆盖上一层番茄酱。 我们继续收看更精彩的。</p>
<p>STORY:<br />About 40,000 people<B> turned up</B> for this event but they don&#8217;t wear their best clothes. Their<B> intention</B> is to<B> splatter</B> everyone else with <B>ripe</B> tomatoes. In fact they hurl more than 130 tons of <B>squishy missiles</B> at each other.<br />新闻内容：<br />约有四万人涌向这场盛会现场，但他们并未穿着最佳服装，因为他们要向每个人泼洒成熟的蕃茄酱汁，事实上，他们总共互丢了超过一百三十吨的挤烂番茄。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />9.	<B>patron</B>: n. 守护神<br />10.	<B>goggle</B>: n. 护目镜<br />11.	<B>onslaught</B>: n. 突击<br />12.	<B>hose</B>：v.用水管清洗 </p>
<p>This is being done to honor the town&#8217;s <B>patron</B> saint, St. Luis Bertran. The famous festival attracts thousands from around the world and leaves the eastern Spanish town in a pool of tomato sauce.<br />这么做是为了荣耀保护神圣徒St. Luis Bertran。这个远近驰名的节庆吸引了数千名各国人士，让西班牙东部的小镇变成了蕃茄酱池。</p>
<p>People even wear <B>goggles</B> and shopkeepers put up plastic to protect their property from the messy <B>onslaught</B>. Later, local residents helped cool down and clean the tomato throwers by<B> hosing</B> them off.<br />人们甚至戴上护目镜，商店老板也把商品铺上一层塑胶，以避免遭到四面八方的突击。稍后，当地居民会用水管帮丢番茄的那些人冲洗，帮助他们冷静下来。（translated by Wen-Chi Liao）</p>
