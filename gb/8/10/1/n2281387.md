<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(111)中国节日的“月饼热”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20201003111-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><B>&#8216;Mooncake Mania&#8217; on Chinese Holiday </B><br />中国节日的“月饼热”</p>
<p>【新闻关键字】<br />1.	<B>mania</B>: n. 疯狂；狂热<br />2.	<B>Moon Festival</B>: n. 中秋节(外国称月亮节)<br />3.	<B>swept up</B>: v. phr. 打扫、横扫<br />4.	<B>approach</B>: v. 接近<br />5.	<B>pastry</B>: n. 酥皮点心(如馅饼、水果派)<br />6.	<B>symbolism</B>: n. 象征性</p>
<p>Anchor: As Chinese around the globe celebrated the <B>Moon Festiva</B>l this weekend, many found themselves <B>swept up</B> in the search for the perfect mooncake.<br />新闻主播：当全球的中国人庆祝本周末的中秋节时，很多人全身投入寻找十全十美的月饼。</p>
<p>STORY: For Chinese people around the world it&#8217;s that time of year again&#8230; 新闻内容：对全球华人来说，真是一年容易又中秋…</p>
<p>Every year as the Chinese Moon Festival<B> approaches</B>, from Hong Kong to Shanghai to London&#8211;Chinese people around the world are buying boxes of sweet bean paste<B> pastries </B>as gifts for family and friends. 每当中秋节来临时，从香港到上海到伦敦，全球的中国人买了一盒又一盒的红豆泥酥饼给家人、朋友当礼物。</p>
<p>They&#8217;re called mooncakes. And, like the holiday fruitcake in Western culture, the mooncake in Chinese culture is more important for its <B>symbolism </B>and tradition than for its taste. In fact, many of these cakes are said to go uneaten. 它们称为月饼，而且，就像西方文化节日里的水果蛋糕一样，月饼在中国文化中，象征性及传统比口味要重要的多了，事实上，这些月饼很多都没吃。</p>
<p>【新闻关键字】<br />7.	<B>variety</B>: n. 多样化；变化<br />8.	<B>enthusiast</B>: n. 热心者<br />9.	<B>confection</B>: n. 西点<br />10.	<B>to come into fashion</B>: v. phr. 流行<br />11.	<B>sorbet</B>: n. 冰糕<br />12.	<B>cornucopia</B>: n. 丰富的</p>
<p>But that may be changing. As Chinese tastes become more &#8220;global,&#8221; there is an ever-greater <B>variety </B>of mooncake flavors on the market. It&#8217;s no longer just limited to the traditional lotus seed paste or red bean paste.但这情形可能在改变。如同中国人的口味全球化，市场上月饼口味的变化也越来越多样，不再限制于传统莲子酥或红豆馅。</p>
<p>Now mooncake<B> enthusiasts</B> can enjoy dainty custard-filled cakes, or delicate white sugar powder <B>confections</B>. Flavors like green tea and even shark&#8217;s fin have <B>come into fashion</B>. <br /> 现在热爱月饼人士喜欢可口奶冻月饼，或细致的白糖粉西点月饼，像绿茶口味，甚至鱼翅口味都风行一时。</p>
<p>And jumping on the<B> trendy </B>mooncake band wagon, ice cream giant H?agen Dazs has created a line of high-end, ice cream-filled mooncakes for those really seeking to make an impression. In these &#8220;cakes,&#8221; the traditional preserved duck egg yolk is replaced by a sphere of mango <B>sorbet</B>.<br />跟上流行的月饼风潮，冰淇淋龙头H?agen Dazs为寻找创新的人研发了冰淇淋月饼，这些创意“月饼”中，芒果冰糕完全取代传统的蛋黄酥。</p>
<p>Some mooncake purists are, of course, crying foul&#8211;commenting that these days anything round can be called a mooncake. But the <B>cornucopia</B> of new mooncake flavors and styles is sure to bring one welcome change: more mooncakes in the belly and less in the bin. <br />当然，有些月饼纯粹主义者叫骂，批评最近什么饼都算月饼，但是新式月饼丰富的口味及风味确实受到欢迎：因为月饼都吃进肚子里，而不是丢进垃圾桶。</p>
