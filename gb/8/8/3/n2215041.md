<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(91)中共加紧控制互联网</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/08/808030848471663.jpg" />
<h6>中共在京奥期间的严打严控，向世人显示了其貌似强大实际虚弱的一面。(AFP)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><B>CCP Tightens Internet Control</B><br />中共加紧控制互联网</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br /><B>1. shut down</B>：v.phr. 关闭<br /><B>2. forum</B>：n. 论坛<br /><B>3. plant</B>：v. 种植、秘密布置<br /><B>4. commentator</B>：n. 评论员</p>
<p>Anchor: According to Internet users in China, the Chinese Communist Party is<B> shutting down</B> more and more websites, <B>forums</B>, and chat rooms. All this is in the effort to prevent the spread of news about riots, protests, and human rights abuses throughout China. Let&#8217;s hear what mainland Chinese Internet users say about it.<br />新闻主播：根据中国的网民说，中共正在关闭愈来愈多的网站、论坛与聊天室。所有的举动都是为了努力避免暴动、抗议与违反人权的新闻，扩散到全中国。</p>
<p>STORY: One Internet user says the CCP<B> planted</B> agents on the Internet to influence public opinion.<br />新闻内容：一位网民说，中共在互联网秘密布置干员，以影响民意。</p>
<p>[Mr.Hu, Internet User]: &#8220;Chinese website operators say that there are fewer things allowed to be said. There are many official (CCP) <B>commentators</B> online.&#8221;<br />一位胡姓网民说：“中国网站的营运者们说，很少的事情可以被允许陈述。有许多中共官方的评论员在线上。”</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br /><B>5. engage in</B>：致力于<br /><B>6. tilt</B>：vt. 使偏斜<br /><B>7. favor</B>:n. 偏爱、偏坦<br /><B>8. puzzled</B>：adj. 困惑的、茫然的<br /><B>9. hypocrisy</B>：n. 伪善<br /><B>10. upcoming</B>：adj. 即将来到的<br /><B>11. obscenity</B>：n. 猥亵、淫秽</p>
<p>The CCP&#8217;s agents on the Internet are known as the “Wu Mao Party.” They actively <B>engage in</B> chat room discussions and web forums in efforts to <B>tilt</B> public opinion in the Party&#8217;s<B> favor</B>. Another Internet user is<B> puzzled</B> by the CCP&#8217;s<B> hypocrisy</B>.<br />在互联网的中共干员一般被称作“五毛党”。他们积极地在聊天室与网路论坛中、致力于影响民意倒向中共所偏爱的方面。另外一位网民则对中共的伪善感到困惑。</p>
<p>[Mr. Li, Internet User]:<br />“Before the <B>upcoming</B> Olympic Games, the CCP shut down websites and forums. They say it is to control<B> obscenity</B> on the Internet, but they are shutting down websites and chat groups related to human rights.”<br />一位李姓网民说：“在即将来到的奥运赛之前，中共关闭网站与论坛。他们说是为了管制网路色情，但是他们关闭的却是与人权有关的网站与聊天群组。”</p>
