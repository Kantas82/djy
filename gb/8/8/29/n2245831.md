<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(100) 舞蹈大赛揭晓</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/08/808242202571823-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>8月24日第二届全世界中国舞舞蹈大赛获奖者合影（摄影：戴兵/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><B>大家好：</B></p>
<p>《<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%8B%E6%96%B0%E9%97%BB.md#1">看新闻</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E8%8B%B1%E8%AF%AD.md#1">学英语</a>》专栏，自2005年3月17日刊出第一讲以来，历经三年半的时光，终于迈入100集，期间感谢众多读者的支持与喜爱，才使得这个专栏得以一直延续下来。</p>
<p>目前互联网上最适合、最方便用来<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%8B%E6%96%B0%E9%97%BB.md#1">看新闻</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E8%8B%B1%E8%AF%AD.md#1">学英语</a>的网站，仍然首推新唐人英语新闻。这三年半来，新唐人英语新闻更是呈现飞跃的成长，内容上更为丰富而多采多姿，水平上也不断在提升。</p>
<p>“看新闻”已是当前人们生活中不可或缺的一部分，如果还能够看英语新闻，不仅可以充实许多全球见闻、世界观也会开阔许多，同时英文还会进步神速，一举数得，何乐而不为呢？</p>
<p>如果读者对本专栏有任何心得、意见或建议，欢迎来信给David老师，来函请寄：davidlee@ms1.epochtimes.com.tw</p>
<p><B>Results Announced for NTD&#8217;s Dance Competition</B><br />新唐人舞蹈大赛结果揭晓</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>wrap up</B>：v.phr. 结束<br />2.	<B>sponsor</B>：n. 主办者<br />3.	<B>participant</B>：n. 参赛者<br />4.	<B>long for</B>：v.phr. 渴望<br />5.	<B>blade</B>：n. 叶片</p>
<p>Anchor: NTD&#8217;s Second Annual Chinese Classical Dance Competition <B>wrapped up</B> on Sunday. Here are the results.<br />新闻主播：新唐人第二届中国古典舞大赛，在周日结束，结果如下。</p>
<p>STORY: There&#8217;s nothing else quite like it. This event, which is <B>sponsored </B>by NTD, aims to bring back the inner beauty and pure goodness of Chinese classical dance. 新闻报导：没有其他任何东西可以比拟。这场由新唐人电视台所主办的比赛，目地在于找回中国古典舞的内在美与纯善。</p>
<p>The<B> participants </B>each did one dance covering required movements, as well as a free-form dance, for example&#8230;Pure Heart, Still Lotus&#8230; <B>Longing for</B> Su Wu&#8217;s Return&#8230;Honoring the Heavens&#8230;and The Fan&#8217;s<B> Blade</B>.每一位参赛者跳一只包含规定动作的舞蹈，以及一只自选舞蹈，例如…纯净的心、静莲、渴望苏武归来…崇敬上天…与扇子舞。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />6.	<B>fabulous</B>：adj. 好极了<br />7.	<B>recommend</B>：v. 推荐<br />8.	<B>cover</B>：v. 报导</p>
<p>[Arlyn Blake, Publisher]: &#8220;It&#8217;s so moving, it&#8217;s<B> fabulous</B>. I can&#8217;t <B>recommend</B> it highly enough. Everybody should come.&#8221;<br />一位出版界人士Arlyn Blake说：“太感人了，好极了，我12万分推荐，每个人都应该来。”</p>
<p>We&#8217;ve been <B>covering</B> this Dance Competition all weekend, and after the final round on Sunday, judges announced the winners.<br />我们整个周末都在报导舞蹈大赛，在周日的最后回合中，评审宣布优胜者。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />9.	<B>category</B>：n. 领域<br />10.	<B>trophy</B>：n. 奖杯<br />11.	<B>congratulate</B>：v. 祝贺<br />12.	<B>martial arts</B>：n. 武术</p>
<p>There were four <B>categories</B> covering juniors and adults from both genders. From the female junior division, the first prize&#8211;which includes a<B> trophy</B> and a check for article content begin -->0,000&#8211;went to Chelsea Cai of New York. In the male junior division, first prize went to Gordon Li of New York.<br />总共有包含了男女青少年组与成年组共四个组别，少年女子组冠军由纽约选手蔡翘楚获得，包括奖杯壹座与一张壹万美元的支票，少年男子组冠军由纽约的李博健获得。</p>
<p>In the female adult division, the judges didn&#8217;t award anyone first place, but the highest award&#8211;silver&#8211;went to Jennifer Su of New York. And finally the winner of the male adult division was&#8211;also from New York&#8211;Tim Wu. <br />青年女子组冠军空缺，最高奖项银牌是纽约选手苏仙姿获得。最后青年男子组冠军是由来自纽约的选手吴巡天获得。</p>
<p>We here at New Tang Dynasty<B> congratulate</B> all the participants in this year&#8217;s Chinese Classical Dance Competition. And, stay tuned next month for NTD&#8217;s Chinese Traditional <B>Martial Arts</B> Competition, September 27th and 28th. 新唐人电视台在此恭贺所有今年中国古典舞大赛的参赛者，并且请大家等待下个月的新唐人全球华人武术大术，时间是9月27日与28日。</p>
