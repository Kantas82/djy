<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【创意饰品DIY】 夏日小花</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><B>美丽随我来 生活姿多采!</B></p>
<p><p><B>DIY属于自己风格的串珠饰品，配戴它时时刻刻拥有快乐和多彩多姿的好心情哦!@* </B></p>
<p><p>材料工具: 长约 60公分 的3号透明鱼线、12颗 8m m紫水晶和4颗 8m m粉晶、4支 10m m长的管状玛瑙、约60颗 2m m水晶圆珠、小蚂蚁一个、挡珠、开关。( 也可变换不同的颜色及材料，例如: 人造水晶或塑胶珠!)</p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180026561917-600x429.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>1. 裁剪 60公分 的3号透明鱼线，先穿入2颗 8 m m紫水晶，再穿过1支 10m m长的管状玛瑙时，将另一端线也同时交叉穿过管状玛瑙。(如上图所示)</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180026571917-600x428.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>2. 作好上一步骤后，拉紧鱼线，从右端的线头陆续穿入2颗 8m m紫水晶、再拿起左线由左至右交叉穿入其中的1颗。(如上图所示)</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180026581917-600x468.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>3. 从右端的线头再穿入1颗紫水晶及管状玛瑙，拿起左线由左至右交叉穿入玛瑙。(如上图所示)</p>
<p><p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180026591917-600x489.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>4. 重复步骤2、3后，依上图所示，右线再穿入1颗紫水晶，拿起从管状玛瑙穿出的左线穿入上方的紫水晶，再交叉穿入右上方的交叉穿入。(如上图所示)</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180028551917-600x488.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>5. 原来的右线往下穿过1支管状玛瑙，将另一端线穿过 8m m粉晶。(如上图所示)</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180028561917-600x486.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>6. 拿起从管状玛瑙穿出的左线再交叉穿入右端 8m m粉晶。(如上图所示)</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180028571917-600x488.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>7. 以此图的蓝绿线为准，正中间4颗红色即代表粉晶可做出第2层的效果。有星号的球代表紫晶，A球为粉晶。 (依照上图所示完成)</p>
<p>
<p><p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180028581917-600x468.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>8. 完成粉晶后，再穿越最后一颗紫晶，再把线穿过管状玛瑙(如上图所示)；剩余的线陆续穿过其余的水晶并绕到背面，两条线连结套入挡珠并用尖嘴钳夹扁，剪掉多余的线即可。</p>
<p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/06/806180028591917-600x474.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12"></span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>9. 完成了！如果您要当成墬子套入项链即可。也可当皮包吊式或手机吊式喔！(如上图所示)<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
