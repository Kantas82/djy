<p><B>Ice-age Siberian Mammoths</B><br />冰河世纪西伯利亚的长毛象</p>
<p>Sun, 13 Jul 2008 08:55:00 NTD</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br /><B>1. mammoth</B>：n. 长毛象<br /><B>2. Siberia</B>：n. 西伯利亚<br /><B>3. frozen remains</B>：n. 冰冻的遗体<br /><B>4. woolly</B>：adj. 毛茸茸的<br /><B>5. extinct</B>：adj. 绝种的、灭绝的<br /><B>6. hail from</B>：v. 来自于</p>
<p>HEADLINE: <br />Ice-age Siberian <B>mammoths</B> on exhibition in Taiwan.<br />冰河世纪西伯利亚的长毛象在台湾展出。</p>
<p>STORY:<br />Up close and impressive, the <B>frozen remains</B> of two <B>woolly</B> mammoths pulling in the crowds at a museum in Taiwan.<br />两只毛茸茸的长毛象的冰冻的遗体，拉到台湾博物馆的人群中，让人近距离观看并且令人印象深刻。</p>
<p>The remains of the long-<B>extinct</B> Ice Age elephants <B>hail from</B> Russia&#8217;s far<br />north.<br />早就绝种很久的冰河世纪大象的遗体，来自于俄罗斯遥远的北方。</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br /><B>7. roam：</B>v. 漫步、漫游<br /><B>8. excavate</B>：v. 挖掘<br /><B>9. Sponsor</B>：n. 赞助者<br /><B>10. huge beast</B>：巨兽<br /><B>11. permafrost</B>：永久冻土层</p>
<p>They&#8217;re of a male and a female thought to have <B>roamed Siberia </B>between<br />18,000 BC and 38,000 BC.<br />他们中一只是公的，一只是母的，被认为是生活在公元前18,000年前到公元前38,000年前。</p>
<p>The remains of the male were <B>excavated</B> in 2002 and the female in 2004, both in Russia&#8217;s Sakha Republic.<br />公象的遗体是在2002年被挖掘出来，母象则是在2004年挖出，两只象都是在俄罗斯的萨哈共和国。</p>
<p>[Student]:<br />&#8220;It is very different from the ones you seen in the cartoons.&#8221;<br />学生说：“这是与在卡通上看到的非常不同。”</p>
<p>[Mother]:<br />&#8220;Everyday my child has been asking &#8216;when can we see the mammoth&#8217;,<br />so we came on the first day.&#8221;<br />母亲说：“每天我的孩子一直在问‘什么时侯我们能看到长毛象？’所以我们在第一天就来了。”</p>
<p><B>Sponsors</B> of the exhibition in Taipei are using the huge beasts</B> to raise<br />awareness about global warming.<br />这场在台北展出的赞助商，正在运用巨兽，来提起大众对全球暖化的警觉。</p>
<p>Some experts believe the mammoths were only found because of Siberia&#8217;s melting <B>permafrost</B>. <br />一些专家相信，长毛象是因为西伯利亚冰原融化，才能够被发现。</p>
