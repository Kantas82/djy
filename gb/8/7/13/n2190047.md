<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场英语 :面试篇—谢绝职务</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>由于某些原因，你可能不想接受这个职位，所以在接到被录用的通知时，一定要坦白告诉对方你的决定。谢绝职位时，可以诚恳地说出自己的想法，并要感谢对方录用自己。</p>
<p> BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p>
<p>1) I抎 like to tell you that you can come to work for us.<br />我想通知你你可以来我们公司工作了。</p>
<p>2) I am sorry but I just accepted another offer that I feel is more suitable to my needs.<br />很抱歉，我刚接受了一个我认为更符合我需要的工作。</p>
<p>3) I really appreciate your offer, but I must decline it.<br />非常感谢贵公司的录用，但我不得不拒绝这份工作。</p>
<p>4) Frankly speaking, I have been thinking that the position is not right for me after the interview.<br />坦白地说，面试后我一直认为这个职位不适合我。</p>
<p>5) I really think that position suits my education background better.<br />我真的觉得那个职位更适合我的教育背景。</p>
<p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1<br />I:Hello, is that Mr. Sun? This is Mrs Smith with ABC Corp.<br />A:Yes, this is Sunlin.<br />I:I抎 like to tell you that you can come to work for us.<br />A:Oh, I am sorry, but I just accepted another offer that I feel is more suitable to my needs.<br />I:I see.<br />A:I really appreciate your offer, but I must decline it.<br />I:You are welcome. Please call us if you change your mind.<br />A: I will. Thank you.<br />I: 你好，孙先生吗？我是ABC公司的史密斯女士。<br />A: 是的，我是孙林。<br />I: 我想告诉你你可以来我们公司工作了。<br />A: 哦，很抱歉，我刚刚接受了另一个我认为更符合我要求的工作。<br />I: 我知道了。<br />A: 非常感谢贵公司的录用，但我不得不拒绝它。<br />I: 不用客气，如果你改变了主意，可以打电话给我们。<br />A: 我会的，谢谢你。</p>
<p>Dialogue 2<br />I:Mr. Sun, this is Mrs Smith at ABC. I am calling to offer you the Sales Manager position. I am wondering if you are still interested in it.<br />A:Frankly speaking, I have been thinking that the position is not right forme.<br />I:Really, what changed your mind?<br />A:I think there is too much responsibility for me. I wonder if I can handle it.<br />I:I am sure you can.<br />A:But I have my doubts, and I don抰 want to accept a position that I don抰 think I will be happy in.<br />I:You are right.<br />A:But thank you for the offer. Good luck.</p>
<p>I: 孙先生，我是ABC公司的史密斯女士。我打电话是想告诉你销售经理一职你已被录用了。我不知道你还有没有兴趣。<br />A: 坦白地说，面试后我一直认为这个工作不适合我。<br />I: 是吗，是什么让你改变了主意？<br />A: 我认为这份工作要承担的责任太多了，我不知道能否应付得了。<br />I: 我相信你能应付的。<br />A: 但我怀疑我自己，我不想接受一份自己感到不快乐的工作。<br />I: 你说得对。<br />A: 但还是非常感谢你的录用。祝你好运。</p>
<p>Dialogue 3<br />A:Hello, this is Sunlin, may I speak to Mrs. Smith?<br />I:Yes, this is Mrs Smith. Mr. Sun, have you received our letter?<br />A:Yes, Mrs Smith. I am just calling to thank you for your offer. But I am sorry to tell you I have accepted another position.<br />I:May I ask why?<br />A:I really think that position suits my educational background better. What抯 more, that company will supply me with an apartment, you know I haven抰 my own house and I have to rent.<br />I:I see.<br />A: 你好，我是孙林，我可以和史密斯女士讲话吗？<br />I: 我就是史密斯。孙先生，你收到我们给你的信了吗？<br />A: 我收到了，我打电话就是感谢贵公司的录用，不过非常遗憾地告诉你，我已经接受了另一个职位。<br />I: 我可以问为什么吗？<br />A: 我觉得那个职位非常符合我的教育背景，而且那个公司会为我提供一套住房，我没有自己的房子，必须租房子住。<br />I: 我明白。</p>

You may be turning down this position, but you might want to be considered for future opportunities. Savvy [悟性] job seekers use every possible chance to thank each person with whom you interviewed and wish them and their company continued success.<br />1) I just accepted another offer that I feel is more suitable to my needs.<br /> more right for me<br /> more interesting to me<br /> 我刚接受了另一份我觉得更能满足我需要的工作。<br />更适合我<br />更感兴趣</p>
