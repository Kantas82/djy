<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场英语 :面试篇—工作经验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258f47b3d77ac2c3b447ced06a5eef5-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>招聘方往往希望知道应聘者的工作性质是否和招聘的职位有关，或者应聘者从以往的工作中吸取到了哪些有用的经验。即便过去的工作和现在应聘的职位无关，也不能说是什么都没学到，因为每份工作都有它可取之处。有时也许由于种种原因，雇员跳槽比较多，但这不一定是坏事，所以如果自己是属于这种情况，可以向雇主说明干过多种工作使自己经验丰富，能为雇主更好的服务。</p>
<p> BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达<br />1) Do you have any work experience in this field?<br />你有这个行业的工作经验吗？</p>
<p>2) What kind of jobs have you had?<br />你做过哪些工作？</p>
<p>3) What抯 your responsibility at your present work unit?<br />现在这个单位你负责什么工作？</p>
<p>4) I am responsible for product distribution.<br />我负责产品销售。</p>
<p>5) I have 4 years抏xperience in staff management.<br />我有四年管理员工的经验。</p>
<p>6) I have experience as a receptionist.<br />我做过接待员。</p>
<p>7) What have you learned from the jobs you have had?<br />你从过去的工作中学到了什么？</p>
<p>8) Would you like to tell me something about your outstanding achievements?<br />谈谈你的工作业绩好吗？</p>
<p>9) How would you evaluate the company you are with？<br />你将如何评价你现在的公司？</p>
<p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1</p>
<p>I:Do you have any work experience in this field?<br />A:Yes. After my graduation from university, I worked as a Customer Service Coordinator in a foreign representative office, and then I transferred to a joint venture as a Market Deve1opment Manager. So I am familiar with the market in China.<br />I:What have you learned from the jobs you have had?<br />A:I learned to be patient when dealing with customerscomplaints and try my best to solve them. In addition, I learned at my previous jobs how to cooperate with my colleagues.<br />I:Does your current employer know you are looking for another job?<br />A:No, I haven抰 discussed my leaving plans with my current employer, but I am sure he will release me.<br />I:What is your impression of your present company?<br />A:Very good.<br />I:What would your current colleague say about you?<br />A:They would say I抦 a dependable and hard worker.<br />I: 你有这个行业的工作经验吗？<br />A: 有，在我大学毕业后，我就到一家外企代理处做了一名顾客服务协调员，之后我转到一家合资企业做市场发展部经理，所以我对中国市场非常熟悉。<br />I: 那你从以前的工作中学到了些什么？<br />A: 我学会了在处理客户的抱怨时要有耐心而且要尽力为他们解决困难。而且，我还学会了如何与同事共同合作。<br />I: 你现在的老板知道你在找另一份工作吗？<br />A: 不知道，我还没有和他讨论我要离职的计划，但我坚信他会让我走的。<br />I: 你对你现在这个公司印象如何？<br />A: 非常好。<br />I: 你现在的同事会如何评价你呢？<br />A: 他们会说我是一个值得信赖并且工作刻苦的人。</p>
<p>Dialogue 2<br />I:What kind of jobs have you had?<br />A:I have been a Production Manager.<br />I:How many years have you worked as a Production Manager?<br />A:3 years.<br />I:Would you like to tell me something about your outstanding achievements?<br />A:I had introduced an advanced product line, which increased the output and lessened the cost.<br />I:Wonderful. Then how many employers have you worked for?<br />A: Frankly speaking, three. If your company employs me, it will be my fourth.<br />I:Then have you received any award at your present company?<br />A:Yes. I was awarded the title of 揂dvanced Workerlast year.<br /> I:How would you evaluate the company you are with？<br />A:Although I could not say that everything is perfect there，I still very much appreciate what the company has given me，especial many chances．<br />I:How would your colleagues evaluate you?<br />A:They would say I am a responsible friend and a capable colleague.<br />I:Can you get recommendations from your present employers?<br />A:Yes, I have brought them with me. Here they are.<br />I:Do you have any questions?<br />A:I think that you have covered all the important points already. But if I have any questions later I will contact you.<br />I: 你都做过什么工作？<br />A: 我做过生产经理。<br />I: 你做了几年的生产经理？<br />A: 三年。<br />I: 能给我谈谈你的突出业绩吗？<br />A: 我曾经引进了一条先进的生产线，产量得到了提高，而且成本也降了下来。<br />I: 非常不错，那你为几个雇主工作过？<br />A: 坦白地说，三个。如果我被贵公司录取，这将是我第四份工作。<br />I: 那你在目前这个公司获得过什么奖励吗？<br />A: 有，去年我获得了“先进工作者”的称号。<br />I: 你将如何评价你现在的公司？<br />A: 虽然我不能说它尽善尽美，但我仍然非常感谢它给了我很多，尤其是许多机会。<br />I: 你的同事会如何评价你呢？<br />A: 他们会说我是一个值得信赖的朋友和很有能力的同事。<br />I: 你目前这个雇主能给你写推荐信吗?<br />A: 可以，我已经把它带来了，给你。<br />I: 你有什么问题要问吗？<br />A: 我想你已经把所有重点都提到了，不过如果以后我有什么问题，我会与你联系的。</p>
<p>coordinator协调员 representative office 代表处，代理处<br />joint venture 合资企业 cooperate合作，协作<br />colleague同事，同僚dependable 可靠的<br />outstanding 突出的，显着的introduce 引进，传入，介绍<br />prize 奖励evaluate 评价<br />perfect完美的 capable 能干的，有能力的<br />appreciate 感激 recommendation 推荐（信），介绍（信） </p>
<p>谈论工作经验是表现自己是该职位合适人选的良机，可以运用事例说明自己的工作经验和成就，如曾经一手成立了新部门、新系统、找到新客户等。不过，切记答案要言简意赅，不要长篇大论。至于没有或工作经验很少的毕业生，可将一些在学校生活学到的东西联系到申请的工作上。例如，上学时曾经当过学生会主席便可以用实例证明自己有领导才能。<br />询问工作经验的句型还有：<br />1）Please tell me about your working experience.能告诉我你的工作经验吗？<br />2）Have you ever been employed?你被雇佣过吗？<br />3）What kind of job are you doing now? 你现在做什么工作？<br />1) Do you have any work experiencein this field?<br />work history<br />occupational history<br />specific experience</p>
<p> 你有没有这方面的工作经验？<br /> 你有没有做过这方面的工作？<br /> 你有没有做过这方面的工作？<br /> 你有没有具体做过这方面的工作？</p>
<p>2)Frankly speaking, three. <br /> To be frank with you<br /> To be plain with you<br /> To be honest with you</p>
<p> 坦率的说，是三个。</p>
<p>3) How would your present employerevaluateyou?<br />appraise<br />judge<br />describe<br /> 你现在的老板会怎样评价你呢?</p>
<p>4) I have been aproduction manager.<br />a customer service coordinator<br />an assistant to the general manager</p>
