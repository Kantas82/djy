<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场英语 :面试篇—面试结束</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>退出面试时要和刚进来时一样彬彬有礼，充满自信，这样的表现将给你赢得一个精彩的退场，为你的成功增添一份希望。</p> <p> BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p> <p>1) Do you have any questions you want to ask?<br />你有问题要问吗？</p> <p>2) When will I know your decision?<br />我何时能知道你们的决定？</p> <p>3) How can we get in touch with you?<br />我们怎样才能和你取得联系呢？</p> <p>4) I can be reached at my office during work hours and at home in the evening.<br />工作时间我在办公室，晚上我在家，你们都能找到我。</p> <p>5) Thank you for your interest in our company.<br />谢谢你来参加我们公司的面试。</p> <p>6) I抣l look forward to hearing from you.<br />我期待着你的消息。</p> <p>7) Well, that抯 all for the interview.<br />好了，面试到此结束。</p> <p>8) And do I need the second interview?<br />我还需要第二次面试吗？</p> <p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1<br />I:Do you have any questions you want to ask?<br />A:Yes, I抎 like to know if there would be any future opportunities for specific training.<br />I:If necessary there will be. Any other questions?<br />A:When will I know your decision?<br />I:We抣l give you our decision in a few days. How can we get in touch with you?<br />A:I can be reached at my office during work hours and at home in the evening. My office phone number and home phone number are in my resume.<br />I:Thank you for your interest in our company.<br />A:Thank you, sir. I expect to hear from you as soon as possible.<br />I:Would you please let the next applicant come in on your way out?<br />A:All right. Goodbye.<br />I: 你有什么问题要问吗？<br />A: 有，我想知道贵公司将来是否提供特殊培训的机会。<br />I: 如果需要的话，会有的，还有问题吗？<br />A: 我何时能知道最终结果呢？<br />I: 我们会在几天内通知你我们的最终决定，我们怎样才能和你取得联系呢？<br />A: 工作时间我在办公室，晚上我在家，你们都能找到我。我办公室和家里的电话号码都在我的简历里。<br />I: 非常感谢你来我们公司面试。<br />A: 谢谢你，先生。我期待着能尽快得到您的消息。<br />I: 你出去的时候能通知下一个应聘者进来吗？<br />A: 好的，再见。</p> <p>Dialogue 2<br />I:Well, that抯 all for the interview. Thank you for your interest in this job.<br />A:You are welcome. Thank you for taking time out of your busy schedule to interview me.<br />I:May I call you about our final decision?<br />A:Yes, please. My telephone number is 2974-5328, and you can call me at any time in the daytime.<br />I:We will get in touch with you by the end of next week.<br />A:Great. I will look forward to hearing from you. And do I need the second interview?<br />I:We抣l notify you if necessary. Goodbye.<br />A: Goodbye.<br />I: 好了，面试到此结束，非常感谢你来面试这份工作。<br />A: 不用谢。也非常感谢你在百忙中抽出时间来面试我。<br />I: 我可以打电话通知你我们的最终决定吗？<br />A: 当然可以了。我的电话号码是：2974-5328，白天任何时候都可以打电话给我。<br />I: 我们会在下周末之前联系你的。<br />A: 好的，我期待着你的消息。那我还需要第二次面试吗？<br />I: 如果有必要我们会通知你的，再见。<br />A: 再见。</p> <p>get in touch with 和……取得联系 reach 触及，联系<br />schedule日程安排表，时刻表take 需要，花费，占用<br />notify 通知hear from收到……的消息 </p> <p>招聘者往往会以以下这些话语结束面试：<br />I抦 grateful for your concern about the job at our company.<br />我很感激你对我们公司这项工作的关注。</p> <p>Thank you for your concern about our recruitment, We shall inform you as soon as we have made our decision.<br />谢谢你对我们招聘工作的关心。我们一做出决定就会立即通知你。<br />We have known something about you. You know, we have to interview several applicants before we make our final decision.<br />你的情况我们已经了解了。你知道，在做出最后决定之前我们还要面试几位申请人。</p> <p>面试结束时，应聘者应该礼貌告退。这时无论你心中有多么渴望这份工作，切记不要说“I really want this job. When can I contact you about your decision? Please consider it seriously.”之类的话，这样只会造成对方的反感。通常典型的面试结束用语是：<br />I look forward to hearing from you.<br />Thank you for your time.</p> <p>1) How can weget in touch withyou?<br /> reach<br /> contact<br /> notify</p> <p> 我们怎样才能 和你取得联系呢？<br /> 联系到你呢<br /> 联系上你呢<br /> 通知你呢</p> <p>2) We will notify you of our decision by mail.<br /> telephone<br />email<br />我们会把我们的决定通过寄信告诉你的。<br /> 我们会打电话告诉你最终结果的。<br /> 我们会发电子邮件告知你最终结果的。</p> <p>3) Thank you for yourinterest in our company.<br />interest in this job<br />time</p> <p> 非常感谢你对我们公司感兴趣。<br /> 谢谢你对这份工作有兴趣。<br /> 谢谢你的宝贵时间。</p> <p>4) Ihope to hear from you as soon as possible.<br /> look forward to hearing</p> <p> 我希望能尽快听到你的消息。</p> <p>文章来源 : 旺旺英语 www.wwenglish.com</p>
