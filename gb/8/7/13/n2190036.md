<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场英语 :面试篇—薪金期望</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>关于待遇问题，合理地进行“自我报价”有以下讲究：</p> <p>1.分析市场行情价格，可从亲朋好友那里或职业中间机构获取资讯。</p> <p>2.分析市场需求状况，了解目前就业市场对这类人才是供不应求还是供过于求。</p> <p>3.个人的经验和能力将是薪酬定位的决定性因素。</p> <p>4.分析用人单位的特点，包括福利保障制度和个人发展空间。</p> <p> BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p> <p>1) What are your salary expectations?<br />你期望的薪水是多少？</p> <p>2) I expect to be paid according to my abilities.<br />我希望能根据我的能力支付薪资。</p> <p>3) With my experience, I抎 like to start at RMB 4000 per month.<br />以我的经验，我希望起薪是每月4000元人民币。</p> <p>4) What is your salary now?<br />你现在的薪水是多少？</p> <p>5) What抯 your expected salary?<br />你期望的薪水是多少？</p> <p>6) How long is my probation?<br />我的试用期多长？</p> <p>7) I think salary is closely related to the responsibilities of the job.<br />我觉得工资是与工作的责任紧密相关的。</p> <p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1<br />I:What is your salary at present?<br />A:My present pay is 100,000 Yuan per year.<br />I:What are your salary expectations now?<br />A:I like to be paid more than that. Moreover, I am sure I will get a raise quickly depending on my background and experience.<br />I:That sounds very reasonable.<br />A:By the way, how long is my probation?<br />I:Generally it is 6 months and during this period you are half-paid.<br />I: 你希望的薪酬是多少？<br />A: 我希望根据我的能力支付薪酬。<br />I: 你认为每年10万元外加奖金如何？<br />A: 这比我期望的少一些，但对我来说，最重要的是这个工作以及将和我一起工作的人。而且，凭借我的背景和经验，我很快就能得到加薪的。<br />I: 这样听起来很合理。<br />A: 顺便问一下，我的试用期多长？<br />I: 一般来说是6个月，这段期间你只能拿到工资的一半。</p> <p>Dialogue 3<br />I:How much do you expect to earn?<br />A:I think salary is closely related to the responsibilities of the job. Shall we discuss my responsibilities with your company first?<br />I:If you are hired, you are in charge of the design of toys that can meet the needs of the American market.<br />A:I know it is a crucial position, but I can do it well.
