<p><B>Typhoon Lashes Taiwan</B><br />台风狂扫台湾</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B>lash</B>: v. 猛烈冲击<br />2.	<B>torrential rain</B>：n. 豪雨<br />3.	<B>high wind</B>：强风<br />4.	<B>gust</B>：阵风<br />5.	<B>havoc</B>：n. 损害<br />6.	<B>dike</B>：n. 堤防<br />7.	<B>households</B>：户数</p>
<p>Anchor：Typhoon Fung-Wong hit Taiwan with strong winds and <B>torrential rain</B> today. There is widespread flooding across the island. It&#8217;s expected to weaken as it heads towards eastern China. Here’s more from Taiwan.<br />新闻主播：台风凤凰今天夹带强风豪雨侵袭台湾。全台都有淹水。预期当凤凰台风朝向中国东部移动时，强度会减弱。</p>
<p>STORY: Typhoon Fung-Wong is slowly moving toward eastern China. But <B>high wind gusts</B> are causing <B>havoc</B>. <br />新闻内容：凤凰台风正缓慢朝向中国东部移动，但强风正引起损害。</p>
<p>A 69-year-old man died when he fell from the roof of his house. Another five people were reported injured in Taipei and Kaohsiung. Floodwaters are blocking major highways crossing central Taiwan. <br />一名69岁男子当他从住宅屋顶掉落时死亡。在台北与高雄有另五个人受伤。洪水造成台湾中部公路受阻。</p>
<p>[Taiwanese Resident]: &#8220;There is no <B>dike</B>. It is dangerous, but could be worse if it rains harder.&#8221; Electricity was cut to more than 140,000 <B>households</B> and another 10,000 are waiting for water.<br />一位居民说：“没有堤防，很危险，但如果雨势更大的话会更糟糕。”超过14万户停电，另有1万户正等待供水。</p>
<p>[Taiwanese Resident]: &#8220;This damage could cost hundreds of thousands. It&#8217;s bad.&#8221;<br />另一位台湾居民说：“损害成千上万，真是糟糕。”</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B></p>
<p>8.	<B>generate</B>：引起<br />9.	<B>rough sea warning</B>：海上大浪警报<br />10.	<B>disrupt</B>：v. 中断<br />11.	<B>domestic flight</B>：n. 国内航班<br />12.	<B>suspend</B>：暂停<br />13.	 <B>recover</B>：复原</p>
<p>The storm&#8217;s approach<B> generated rough sea warnings</B>. Markets, offices and schools also closed for the day. Transport was<B> disrupted </B>with most<B> domestic flights</B> and rail services<B> suspended</B>. <br />台风的路径促使发布海上强浪警报。当天市场休市，并停止上班上学。大部分国内航班运输中断、铁路停驶。</p>
<p>Meanwhile, eastern China is bracing itself for Fung-Wong. The storm is predicted to hit Zhejiang and Fujian provinces tonight. <br />同时，中国东部正严正以待凤凰台风，预测将在今晚会侵袭浙江与福建。</p>
<p>Most areas are still trying to<B> recover </B>from damage caused by Typhoon Kalmaegi, which struck just over a week ago.<br />大部分地区仍在从一周前的台风卡玫基侵袭所造成的损害中复原。</p>
