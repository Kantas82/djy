<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第263课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2月7日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa263.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center></p> <p>李华在学校租了一块花圃，让Larry来帮忙。李华今天要学两个常用语，get into和grubby.</p> <p>LL: I think it is great that you have rented a garden plot, Li Hua, but I don&#8217;t think I can really get into gardening.</p> <p>LH: You don&#8217;t think you can &#8220;get into&#8221; gardening, Larry? 相信我，一点都不难，你只要跟我一起去，进了花圃就行了。Then you will be in the garden.</p> <p>LL: Well, I may be in the garden, but that doesn&#8217;t mean I&#8217;ll get into gardening. To &#8220;get into&#8221; something means to really like something or to enjoy doing something.</p> <p>LH: 噢，原来你说的get into是喜欢的意思。那你说you don&#8217;t think you can get into gardening。意思就是说你不u会喜欢上园艺了？</p> <p>LL: Right. I am sorry, Li Hua, because I know how much you get into gardening.</p> <p>LH: 不过，你起码应该试试看啊！</p> <p>LL: Okay, Li Hua. I&#8217;ll try it. Maybe I&#8217;ll learn why you have gotten into gardening.</p> <p>LH: 我喜欢园艺完全是因为我妈。我们小时候，她总是在家里的院子里种很多菜。</p> <p>LL: That sounds nice. Fresh grown vegetables are so much better than the ones in the grocery store.</p> <p>LH: 那你呢，Larry? What do you get into?</p> <p>LL: Oh, you know me, Li Hua. I love computers. You know I really get into video games. I could play video games all day long.</p> <p>LH: 我永远搞不懂你为什么那么喜欢打电玩，我觉得打电玩很吵，而且打久了会很累。</p> <p>LL: Well, every body gets into different things. You get into gardening, and I get into video games.</p> <p>LH: 那也是。还有，我喜欢布鲁斯，你喜欢摇滚乐。</p> <p>LL: Yes, and while you can&#8217;t understand how I can get into video games, Li Hua, I have never understood why you get into blues music. How can you listen to blues music? It just makes me sad.</p> <p>LH: 我喜欢布鲁斯的节奏和歌手的嗓音。但是我从来无法欣赏摇滚乐。简直吵死了。</p> <p>LL: I wonder if there is something we can both get into.</p> <p>LH: 我有个主意。We can both &#8220;get into&#8221; the car, 到花圃去。</p> <p>LL: &#8220;Get into&#8221; the car. Very funny, Li Hua!</p> <p>******</p> <p>(Outdoor sounds, Larry and Li Hua are at the garden)</p> <p>LL: I should have brought some garden gloves. My hands are getting all grubby from digging in the dirt.</p> <p>LH: Grubby? 我猜grubby肯定是脏的意思。你的手还真是够脏的。</p> <p>LL: Grubby does mean dirty. Li Hua, my hands are so grubby, I&#8217;ve got dirt under my fingernails. Yuck!</p> <p>LH: 真没想到你还挺爱干净。别忘了，园艺一半的乐趣就在于浑身上下沾满泥土。</p> <p>LL: Well, maybe you think so, Li Hua. I&#8217;m just glad I wore some grubby, old clothes. Look how grubby the knees of my pants are!</p> <p>LH: 别抱怨了！在园圃里干活难免是要弄脏的。</p> <p>LL: Okay, then I&#8217;ll stop complaining about being grubby. But, doesn&#8217;t it bother you, Li Hua?</p> <p>LH: 不会啊，我喜欢这样。Why don&#8217;t you like getting grubby, Larry?</p> <p>LL: I don&#8217;t think it is that unusual that I don&#8217;t like getting grubby, Li Hua. But I haven&#8217;t always been this way.</p> <p>LH: 你以前不是这样？那你是什么样子呢？</p> <p>LL: Oh, when I was a kid, I used to play outside all the time. I would come home grubby from head to toe!</p> <p>LH: 从头到脚都是土? 那你妈妈肯定不高兴。</p> <p>LL: No, after I came home from a day of playing outside, she would take me by my grubby little hand and take me straight to the bath tub.</p> <p>LH: 好了，Larry. 今天就干到这吧，你看见那个工具房了吗？</p> <p>LL: Grubby old tool shed? Oh, yes, I see it.</p> <p>LH: 你能帮我把这些工具和这个桶放到那里收好吗？</p> <p>LL: Sure, not a problem, Li Hua!</p> <p>LH: 谢谢你，Larry. I hope you didn&#8217;t mind getting grubby too much.</p> <p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是get into, 是喜欢的意思。另一个是grubby, 是脏的意思。<br />	<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
