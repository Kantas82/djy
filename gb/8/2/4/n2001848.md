<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>红包袋创意─ ─年年庆有鱼</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>新年来到，年节的喜气在空中弥漫，祝福声温暖了这个寒冷的冬天。而孩子们最高兴的就是能给长辈拜年，捧回满载着长辈心意与祝福的红包。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/lbfmuj387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E5%8C%85%E8%A2%8B.md#1">红包袋</a>里的钱会用完，我们却可以将红包袋创意一下，让这份心意长远留存，又能增添节日的气氛，真的很妙喔！</p>
<p>今天要教大家做的是用<ahref="https://github.com/lbfmuj387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E5%8C%85%E8%A2%8B.md#1">红包袋</a>做纸鱼。它可单独装饰于灯笼下方，也可以成串做门帘或挂饰，也可以加个小磁铁成为孩子们钓鱼的目标物&#8230;&#8230;</p>
<p><B>一、材料：</B><br />红包袋一个、剪刀、钻子、中国结线或钓鱼线、打洞机（若没有，可用黑笔划出眼珠即可。）<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040559411452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">1</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p><B>二、做法：</B><br />（一）红包袋底部一边对折，像在长方形纸张取一个正方形纸张的做法一样。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040559371452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">2</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040559381452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">3</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>（三）在嘴巴上方打个洞当作是鱼眼，鱼尾的末端剪开至鱼身中间处。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040559391452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">4</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040559401452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">5</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>（四）鱼尾两面的二个三角形分别（原本是一个等腰三角形，从中间剪开后成为二个角较尖锐的三角形）重叠黏合，此时扁扁的鱼身就成为立体的鱼体。</p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040604201452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">图6</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
<div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">
	<ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/01/802040604211452-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a> <br /><span class="bn12">图7</span></div>
<p><!-- --></p>
<p>（五）用钻子穿小洞，穿上中国结线、流苏，完成。</p>
