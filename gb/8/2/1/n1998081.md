<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第262课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2月1日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/chinese/eng/pa/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa262.mp3 >(MP3下载)</a></center>	</p> <p>李华和Larry一起去附近一家新开的中国餐馆吃自助餐。李华今天要学两个常用语：yum和yuck.</p> <p>LL: There is so much good food at this buffet, Li Hua. I can&#8217;t wait to eat some of those spring rolls. Yum!</p> <p>LH: 你说yum，那你肯定很喜欢吃春卷！因为我知道，yum在英语里就是可口，好吃的意思。</p> <p>LL: Hey, Li Hua, you are right! Where did you learn about the word &#8220;Yum&#8221;?</p> <p>LH: 我前不久跟一帮美国朋友去吃pizza，其中一个人刚咬第一口，就大喊yum！他说自己最爱吃的东西就是pizza.</p> <p>LL: Well, I really enjoy eating spring rolls &#8211; especially when they are fresh. And these spring rolls look especially tasty. What makes you say &#8220;Yum!&#8221;, Li Hua?</p> <p>LH: 我最爱吃的菜是鱼香茄子，想想都会流口水。Yum!</p> <p>LL: Well, I am afraid eggplant is not one of my favorites, Li Hua. I don&#8217;t find eggplant dishes to be very yummy.</p> <p>LH: Yummy肯定是从yum来的吧！</p> <p>LL: Right! Yummy is used to describe a thing &#8211; Like yummy spring rolls or yummy eggplant.</p> <p>LH: 所以yummy是形容词，yum则是发感慨的时候说的。</p> <p>LL: Speaking of being about to eat, let&#8217;s take our plates over to the table and enjoy this yummy food.</p> <p>LH: 对啊，我们得赶紧吃，吃完第一盘，马上再回来拿，你看他们正在上龙虾呢！ Yum!</p> <p>LL: But don&#8217;t fill up too much, Li Hua. You&#8217;ll want to save room for dessert. The almond cookies look especially yummy.</p> <p>LH: 杏仁酥看起来确实比红豆面包好，那些红豆面包看起来好像不太新鲜的样子。</p> <p>LL: Well, I think most of the food here is yummy. What do you think, Li Hua?</p> <p>LH: 跟其他中国餐馆比，味道确实还不错。</p> <p>LL: But &#8211; is it as yummy as the food you used to eat back home in China?</p> <p>LH: 这里的饭菜当然没办法跟我妈做的饭比了！</p> <p>******</p> <p>LL: What&#8217;s that on your plate, Li Hua? Is it a snake? Yuck!</p> <p>LH: 我如果没有理解错的话，这个yuck一定是yum的反义词吧？你觉得吃蛇肉很恶心吗？</p> <p>LL: Right again, Li Hua. Yuck is an exclamation of disgust. And I am sorry, but I find the idea of eating a snake disgusting.</p> <p>LH: 我可不是故意要恶心你。不瞒你说，蛇肉可好吃了，而且在美国，你想吃到蛇肉可不容易。刚才你说yuck的样子，突然间让我想起了我第一次吃奶酪的感觉。</p> <p>LL: Cheese? Cheese makes you say &#8220;Yuck!&#8221;, Li Hua? What&#8217;s wrong with cheese anyway?</p> <p>LH: 中国人做饭不用奶酪，以前我在中国的时候也没吃过。 可是美国人都很能吃奶酪。我到美国后，第一次吃奶酪的时候，简直难以下咽。 Yuck! 那种感觉，现在还记忆犹新。</p> <p>LL: That is interesting. Actually, Li Hua, there are some kinds of cheese that I find pretty yucky, too.</p> <p>LH: Yucky? Yucky肯定又是从yuck来的！就好像yummy是从yum来的一样。</p> <p>LL: Yucky is used to describe disgusting things. In this case, I was talking about disgusting cheeses. I find blue cheese to be especially yucky.</p> <p>LH: 你也有不爱吃的奶酪？我是从来没有尝过blue cheese, 可是如果连你都觉得yucky的话，那我也就不用尝了。</p> <p>LL: I don&#8217;t blame you, Li Hua. No one should have to eat something that yucky.</p> <p>LH: Larry, 你拿的鱼香茄子怎么也没吃啊？不好吃吗？你不会觉得鱼香茄子也yucky吧！</p> <p>LL: I think I told you earlier, Li Hua, that I don&#8217;t find eggplant to be very yummy. In fact, I do think it is pretty yucky.</p> <p>LH: 既然觉得鱼香茄子难吃，那你干嘛还要拿呢？</p> <p>LL: You told me fish fragrant eggplant was your favorite dish, Li Hua. So even though I think eggplant is yucky, I thought I should still try it.</p> <p>LH: 这就证明，一个人觉得yummy的食物，换个人完全可能会觉得yucky. Larry, 我看你呀，还是去吃春卷吧！</p> <p>今天李华学到了两个常用语。一个是yum或yummy, 是指食物可口好吃的意思。另一个是yuck或yucky, 是指食物难吃，难以下咽。<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
