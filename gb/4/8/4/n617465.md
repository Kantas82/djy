<h6></h6>

/>形态：多年生草根，形肥厚，类人形，色白而微褐。<br />性味：微酸，味甘苦，略有香气。<br />功效：强壮剂，一切体力消耗所引起之病症皆可用。对于神经衰弱之头痛眩晕，尤有特效。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />一、有一次，一对猎人兄弟上山打猎。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />二、兄弟俩在山上捕获到许多猎物。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />三、一天，天气突然转坏，下起大雪来。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />四、大雪连续下了两天两夜，山路完全被雪封住，根本无法下山。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />六、晴天他们就出去打猎、挖草根当食物。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />七、有一天，挖出了手腕粗的根。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />八、根的模样活像一个人，伸出的须根就像手脚一般。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center<br </table> <p></center><br />十、两人便到处挖那种根。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span class=bn12></span></td> </tr> </table> <p></center><br />十一、漫漫寒冬就靠着这种根维持体力和元气。</p> <p><center></p> <table cellpadding=3 cellspacing=3 border=0> <tr> <td align=center>  </td> </tr> <tr> <td align=center><span
