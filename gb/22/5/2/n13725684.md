<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>凯蒂受欢迎的泰式餐厅 用心做出好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725686-Thaicoon-9455-Tofu-Panang-Curry-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Tofu Panang Curry
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月02日讯】（休斯顿讯）Thaicoon Restaurant &amp; Pub高级<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%BC%8F%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">泰式餐厅</a>和酒吧将地道的泰式菜带到了德州<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E8%92%82.md#1">凯蒂</a>，自营业以来，一直深受中国、韩国、美国等各族裔食客的喜爱。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泰国菜</a>以酸、辣闻名，营养丰富，美味健康，泰式绿咖喱更是以蔬菜为原料制作而成。Thaicoon餐厅一直秉持着烹饪原汁原味泰国料理的理念，力求为客户提供优质健康的佳肴。脆皮猪肉蛋卷、炸鱼饼、冬阴功汤、泰式咖喱饭、菠萝炒饭、传统炒粉、泰国醉面等经典泰式菜系任您选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13725687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725687-Thaicoon-9543-Shrimp-Pad-Thai-e1651504690924.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13725687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725687-Thaicoon-9543-Shrimp-Pad-Thai-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725687" class="wp-caption-text">Thaicoon- Shrimp Pad Thai</figcaption></figure>
<p>现代的装饰、机器人送餐服务和休闲放松的氛围也吸引了不少食客前来。另外，餐厅还有一个全方位服务的酒吧，调酒师可以手工制作特色鸡尾酒。您可以和家人朋友一起到这里举行派对。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13725688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725688-Thaicoon-8022-Bubble-Smoke-Martini-e1651504757539.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13725688" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725688-Thaicoon-8022-Bubble-Smoke-Martini-600x929.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="929" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725688" class="wp-caption-text">Thaicoon- Bubble Smoke Martini</figcaption></figure>
<p>Thaicoon餐厅和酒吧在母亲节当天推出：送花和葡萄酒（瓶）20% Off优惠，并祝愿所有的母亲健康快乐。</p>
<h2>顾客反馈：</h2>
<p>这家餐厅装饰得很漂亮，还有一个服务机器人。我们先点的开胃菜，每样东西都煎得入味，一点也不油腻。肉饺子饱满紧实，鱼糕有很浓的柠檬草香味。朋友吃的菠萝炒饭，看起来分量十足，胡萝卜、虾、贻贝和切块的菠萝果肉混在炒饭中，香气浓郁。我点了炸鸭腿，鸭肉炸得很脆，但仍保有一些水分，吃起来很嫩，外层还涂了淡淡的调味料。如果您想吃泰式菜，这里是一个不错的选择。——Philip Quan</p>
<figure id="attachment_13725691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725691-Thaicoon-9309-Money-Bag-e1651504822396.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13725691" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725691-Thaicoon-9309-Money-Bag-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725691" class="wp-caption-text">Thaicoon- Money Bag</figcaption></figure>
<p>很高兴在三周前发现了这家餐馆，已在这里吃过四、五次午餐，还点了三次晚餐外卖。食材新鲜，午餐很划算。堂食和外卖服务都非常好。——River H</p>
<figure id="attachment_13725692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725692-Pineapple-Fried-Rice-1-e1651504879334.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13725692" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725692-Pineapple-Fried-Rice-1-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725692" class="wp-caption-text">Pineapple Fried Rice</figcaption></figure>
