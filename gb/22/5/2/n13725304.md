<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【野炊料理】巧克力锅、千层白菜猪肉锅食谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725312-dde8ff7bf83fad9c37c26dd63c11a970-600x400.png" />
<h6>野炊也可以享用巧克力锅，准备材料有板巧克力块、牛奶，以及配料水果如香蕉和孩子喜欢的棉花糖。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：户外<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%82%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">野炊料理</a>要如何准备才会省时省力又美味？《Mess Tin煮饭神器露营╳野炊料理》一书作者介绍“煮饭神器”Mess Tin，一锅到底就完成一餐。下面介绍2种<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E7%89%A9.md#1">锅物</a>，“<strong><ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B%E9%94%85.md#1">巧克力锅</a>”</strong>和“千层白菜猪肉锅”的食谱，简单好上手，零厨艺都可以做到！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B%E9%94%85.md#1">巧克力锅</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1.在加大版煮饭神器装入高约2cm的水，排摆好2块14g 固体燃料，点火加热。</p>
<p>2.在钛杯内倒入200ml的牛奶与弄碎的2片板巧克力后，将钛杯放入步骤1中。</p>
<p>3.待水沸腾后，将步骤2搅拌至融化，随后便可沾着切块的香蕉、棉花糖等配料享用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">千层白菜猪肉锅</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13725329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725329-c8ab98b78b53b6200d331700d42b8bf8.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13725329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725329-c8ab98b78b53b6200d331700d42b8bf8-450x362.png" alt="" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725329" class="wp-caption-text">外观华丽的经典<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E7%89%A9.md#1">锅物</a>食谱！（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1.在加大版煮饭神器内放入交叠白菜与猪肉。<br />
2.均匀撒上和风高汤粉后，淋上300ml 的水。<br />
3.点燃2 块并排的14g 固体燃料，关上盖子并加热至火熄灭为止。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13724852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13724852" style="width: 190px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13724852-998867b7ee5c6a79ea8646b968c3be55.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13724852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13724852-998867b7ee5c6a79ea8646b968c3be55-450x355.jpg" alt="" width="190" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13724852" class="wp-caption-text">《Mess Tin煮饭神器露营╳<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%82%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">野炊料理</a>》书封。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
