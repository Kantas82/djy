<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【野炊料理】小孩的最爱 美味的手工披萨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725347-p114-2.jpg" />
<h6>一锅到底的手工披萨很容易制作。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：户外<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%82%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">野炊料理</a>要如何准备才省时省力又美味？为了让挑嘴的孩子玩得开心也吃得开心，《Mess Tin煮饭神器露营╳野炊料理》一书作者介绍运用“加大版煮饭神器”Mess Tin可以制作出全家人都可以享用的<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a>料理。不用任何厨艺，一锅到底，而且食材放多样一样会煮熟，让你吃饱又满足口感。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">双料简易<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%A5%BA%E7%9A%AE.md#1">水饺皮</a><ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%A5%BA%E7%9A%AE.md#1">水饺皮</a>?6 片／披萨酱?适量<br />
橄榄油?适量／洋葱丝?适量<br />
维也纳香肠?通量<br />
鲔鱼罐头?1 罐／玉米?适量<br />
披萨用起司?适量／意大利欧芹?适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1.于煮饭神器内倒入橄榄油后，以让饺子皮边缘重叠的方式铺上饺子皮。</p>
<p>2.将所有的皮都涂满披萨酱，左半边摆上洋葱丝与维也纳香肠，右半边则摆上鲔鱼和玉米。</p>
<p>3.于步骤2上撒满披萨用起司，点燃14g固体燃料后盖上盖子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13725346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13725346" style="width: 438px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725346-p114-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13725346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13725346-p114-1.jpg" alt="" width="438" b="235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13725346" class="wp-caption-text">撒满披萨用起司才盖上盖子烹煮。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 等火熄灭后开盖，用喷枪轻轻炙烧表面，最后摆上意大利欧芹。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13724852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13724852" style="width: 190px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13724852-998867b7ee5c6a79ea8646b968c3be55.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13724852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13724852-998867b7ee5c6a79ea8646b968c3be55-450x355.jpg" alt="" width="190" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13724852" class="wp-caption-text">《Mess Tin煮饭神器露营╳<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%82%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">野炊料理</a>》书封。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
