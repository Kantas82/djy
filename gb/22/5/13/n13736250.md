<h6>2022 Genesis G80 AWD 3.5T Sport Prestige。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月14日讯】属于Hyundai的豪华品牌Genesis，近年致力推出新车款，在测试了G70、GV70及GV80之后，这次试驾的是G80，也是继2019年款之后，再一次开上这款汽车，上一次是第一代，这次则是第二代车款，各种设计也跟其他Genesis汽车更接近。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d239x61949z6p.cloudfront.net/F1lQbtBur"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ugwazd396/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ugwazd396/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/iIB3O_ldlG4" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a><br />
如果G70的对手是BMW 3系的话，G80则是以5系为目标，尺寸上也跟5系接近，甚至更大，BMW 5系的长宽高为195.8/73.5/58.2吋，而G80的尺寸为196.7/75.8/57.7吋，外形集豪华、优雅与运动于一身。前脸设计是巨大钻石形进风口栏栅，与双线四头灯的整体设计就如Genesis翼徽一样，头灯的设计伸延到车侧的方向灯。标配19吋轮圈及245/45R19（前）及275/40R18（后）轮胎，而测试车配上245/40ZR20（前）及275/35ZR20（后）轮胎和20吋Sport轮圈。使用了更多的高强度钢和热冲压件，比上一代热冲压件多42%，所以G80的一体式结构抗拉强度比旧有G80高6%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13736256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736256-2022_Genesis_G80_05-e1652486374186.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736256-2022_Genesis_G80_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736256" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Genesis G80 AWD 3.5T Sport Prestige。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13736258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736258-2022_Genesis_G80_11-e1652486380630.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736258-2022_Genesis_G80_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736258" class="wp-caption-text">头灯的设计伸延到车侧的方向灯。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">G80有两款不同引擎配置，3.5升双涡轮增压汽油直喷式V6引擎，可发出375匹马力发及391磅呎扭矩，0-60英里/时的加速时间约为4.9秒，标配AWD四驱系统；另一款是2.5升直四涡轮增压引擎，马力输出300匹及311磅呎扭矩，0-60英里/时的加速时间约为5.7秒，2.5T标配只有后驱，但可升级至AWD。不论2.5T或3.5T都是会搭配8速自动变速箱，只是最后齿比有分别！G80将提供总共五种驾驶模式，包括Eco、Comfort、Sport、Sport+和Custom，以便应付各种路况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13736259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736259-2022_Genesis_G80_18-e1652486387856.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736259-2022_Genesis_G80_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736259" class="wp-caption-text">3.5升双涡轮增压汽油直喷式V6引擎。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">G80的内舱采用了柔软触感材料，白色皮革的座椅与内装非常清新，再配上一些黑色饰线作点缀，并有不少磨沙金属车内部件，提高整体格调。G80采用变速盘，中间是“P”按键，顺时针转动是“D”档，而逆时针转动是“R”档，并配有Paddle Shift。方向盘是三幅式设计，更为运动化，并有二段式加热功能及四方向电动调节。Nappa皮革座椅，并有加热和通风功能，驾驶席更有三种不同的按摩程序，称为“Ergo Motion Driver Seat”。G80装备了世界首创的12.3吋3D仪表板，可以识别驾驶员的眼睛位置，让驾驶者在任何角度也可有3D体验，并可据驾驶员的喜好可转回2D。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13736266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736266-2022_Genesis_G80_40-e1652486435726.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736266-2022_Genesis_G80_40-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736266" class="wp-caption-text">白色皮革的座椅与内装非常清新。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13736265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736265-2022_Genesis_G80_37-e1652486428935.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736265-2022_Genesis_G80_37-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736265" class="wp-caption-text">“Ergo Motion Driver Seat”。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">中控台是14.5吋高解像触屏，用作各种音响的控制，连接手机，导航系统及倒车摄像显示等，分辨率达到2,560&#215;720。信息娱乐系统可连接Apple CarPlay或Android Auto，Sirius/XM卫星、HD收音机、导航系统、无线充电板和环视摄像头系统也是G80 3.5T的标准配置，但平视显示器则是可选配项目。另外，可加装1050瓦的Lexicon音频系统，带有21个扬声器，有QuantumLogic立体声。G80配备了不少豪华设备，后挡风玻璃的电动太阳挡、后座车窗的手拉式太阳挡、两侧后视镜的投影厂徽、吸入式车门、全景天窗及电动太阳挡、前座三段加热及降温功能、方向盘加热、电动折叠侧镜、二个驾驶席记忆及电动车尾门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13736260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736260-2022_Genesis_G80_22-e1652486393913.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736260-2022_Genesis_G80_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736260" class="wp-caption-text">12.3吋3D仪表板。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13736261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736261-2022_Genesis_G80_23-e1652486399116.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736261-2022_Genesis_G80_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736261" class="wp-caption-text">盲点监察。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">与所有?Genesis车辆一样，G80提供了标准主动和被动的安全设备， 其中包括刹车辅助、盲点防止碰撞辅助、车道保持辅助和驾驶员注意警告等。G80提供最高标准的安全设备，就像G70和G90轿车一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13736262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13736262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736262-2022_Genesis_G80_24-e1652486405853.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13736262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13736262-2022_Genesis_G80_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13736262" class="wp-caption-text">控台是14.5吋高解像触屏。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">G80行车有力又舒适，外形设计豪华时尚，车内既豪华又精致，可媲美日系豪华车Lexus ES，但高科技设备不输欧洲车，而且车内空间宽阔，但测试车价格达到7万多美元，相对Lexus ES昂贵，而差数千元便可选择BMW M550i，价格定位不利！◇</p>
