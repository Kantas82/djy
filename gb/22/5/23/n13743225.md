<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美国摄影师用心捕捉飞行中的冠蓝鸦</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743421-Jessica-Kirste-bird-1-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国新泽西州一位摄影师兼鸟类爱好者拍摄了一系列令人难以置信的冠蓝鸦照片。（Jessica Kirste提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月23日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">美国新泽西州一位<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>兼<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>爱好者拍摄了一系列令人难以置信的<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E8%93%9D%E9%B8%A6.md#1">冠蓝鸦</a>（Blue Jay，又名蓝松鸦）照片，通过展示这些色彩斑斓的小鸟来证明大自然的神奇。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">而自去年春天以来，她发现自己对这个特定主题变得更加感兴趣。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">来自林登市的杰西卡?科斯特 （Jessica Kirste)）在十几岁时就爱上了摄影。起初，她使用母亲送给她的一台胶卷相机尝试给家人和朋友拍照，偶尔会去动物园或水族馆拍些动物照片。到了2010年，当她获得一台较好的数码单反相机后，她开始将<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>和动物园拍摄作为她的主要摄影题材。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743422-IMG_9532.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743422-IMG_9532.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743422" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13743425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743425-IMG_7703.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743425-IMG_7703.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743425" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">近几年来，她热衷于在自家后院拍摄<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E8%93%9D%E9%B8%A6.md#1">冠蓝鸦</a>的特写镜头。“这一切始于我楼上的窗户”，43岁的杰西卡告诉《大纪元时报》，“我坐在箱式凳上，将无盐去壳<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F.md#1">花生</a>扔出窗外，然后看着它们飞过来。当它们降落在电线上时，正好是我拍摄的最佳时机。后来我决定尝试捕捉飞行中的冠蓝鸦。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">随着她对拍摄这些美丽鸟类的热情越来越高，她开始投入更多的时间和精力坐在窗前观察。她花了几个月的时间跟踪它们的飞行速度和飞行模式，而且这些有灵性的鸟儿也越来越习惯于晚间来到杰西卡的窗前觅食。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743423" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743423-IMG_0933.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743423-IMG_0933.jpeg" alt="" width="449" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743423" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13743424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743424" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743424-IMG_1816.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743424-IMG_1816.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="551" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743424" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">到了2021年春季，4只冠蓝鸦拜访了杰西卡后，最终成为了她后院的常客。当这四只鸟经历冬季短暂迁徙并于2022年返回后，总数竟达到了10只。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“我相信其中有一些是去年那4只鸟的后代”，杰西卡说，“每一只冠蓝鸦的颈部都环绕着一个独特的黑色项圈，一直延伸到头部的两侧，通过这一特征就可以识别出每只鸟。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743426" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743426-IMG_8472.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743426-IMG_8472.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743426" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这位自然<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>更喜欢在日落前两到三个小时的时间段拍摄冠蓝鸦，因为西落的夕阳会“让它们的蓝色羽毛在金色的光芒中格外漂亮”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">迄今为止最令人惊叹的一个镜头捕捉到了一只冠蓝鸦在风暴中穿越一道亮丽的彩虹。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743427" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743427-IMG_9598.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743427-IMG_9598.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743427" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">事实上，冠蓝鸦不是杰西卡后院唯一的羽毛访客，但它们却是她的最爱。其中还包括一只雄性和一只雌性红腹啄木鸟，当它们与冠蓝鸦为争夺<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F.md#1">花生</a>而“激战”时，它们也会成为有趣的拍摄主题。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13743428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743428-IMG_2368.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743428-IMG_2368.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743428" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13743429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13743429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743429-IMG_9665-Blue-Jay-Red-Bellied-Woodpecker.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13743429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13743429-IMG_9665-Blue-Jay-Red-Bellied-Woodpecker.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="559" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13743429" class="wp-caption-text">（Jessica Kirste提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">杰西卡在她的<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/jessicakirste/">Instagram</a>、<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Jessica-Kirste-Photography-1895182017190111">脸书</a>和<ahref="http://www.jessicakirste.com/">网站</a>上都分享了她的精彩照片。她表示，她这些受欢迎的照片“成功激发了一些人也走进后院与<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>互动”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这位居住在新泽西的摄影师认为，即使你不远行，同样能拍出好照片。“手头时常准备一些无盐带壳或去壳的花生，远离屏幕，多花些时间走到户外，你永远不知道自己会看到什么。”她建议说。</span></p>
