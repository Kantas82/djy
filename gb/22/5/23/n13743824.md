<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>民调：安省省选 福特与保守党领先</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/09/1109080038351117-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>益普索称，福特和保守党在竞选期间保持着健康的支持率。图为省议会大楼。（穆枫/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月24日讯】<b></b><span class="s1">（大纪元记者季薇多伦多报导）距离<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E7%9C%81%E7%9C%81%E9%80%89.md#1">安省省选</a>投票日还有不到</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">天的时间，最新民调显示，寻求连任的福特与保守党继续领先竞争对手。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在安省四个主要政党的党领进行完激烈的电视辩论之后，</span><span class="s2">Global News</span><span class="s1">委托民调机构益普索在</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">17</span><span class="s1">日至</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">日进行的一项新民意调查发现，保守党在许多关键人口统计区和安省地区均处于领先地位。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">数据显示，</span><span class="s2">38%</span><span class="s1">的选民会投票给福特和保守党，</span><span class="s2">28%</span><span class="s1">支持邓德华（</span><span class="s2">Steven Del Duca</span><span class="s1">）及自由党，</span><span class="s2">23%</span><span class="s1">称要投票给新民主党和贺华丝（</span><span class="s2">Andrea Horwath</span><span class="s1">）；薛瑞勒（</span><span class="s2">Mike Schreiner</span><span class="s1">）领导的绿党能获得</span><span class="s2">6%</span><span class="s1">的支持，但其中</span><span class="s2">5%</span><span class="s1">表示他们将投票给另一个政党。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">不投票者占受访者的</span><span class="s2">6%</span><span class="s1">，有</span><span class="s2">13%</span><span class="s1">举棋未定。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">自竞选开始以来，保守党的支持率降了</span><span class="s2">1%</span><span class="s1">，自由党上升了</span><span class="s2">2%</span><span class="s1">，新民主党跌了</span><span class="s2">2%</span><span class="s1">，绿党增了</span><span class="s2"> 1%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">益普索称，福特和保守党在竞选期间保持着健康的支持率。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">52%</span><span class="s1">受访者认可福特与保守党政党的表现：</span><span class="s2">15%</span><span class="s1">非常认可，</span><span class="s2">37%</span><span class="s1">在某种程度上支持。</span><span class="s2">41%</span><span class="s1">认为保守党政府做得很好，应该连任；</span><span class="s2">56%</span><span class="s1">认为是时候做出改变了。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在接受调查的安省每个地区，福特和保守党都领先或位居第二。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在至关重要的大多伦多</span><span class="s2">905</span><span class="s1">地区，保守党领先排名第二的自由党</span><span class="s2">14%</span><span class="s1">。数据显示，保守党会获得</span><span class="s2">44%</span><span class="s1">的选票，自由党</span><span class="s2">30%</span><span class="s1">，新民主党</span><span class="s2">17%</span><span class="s1">，绿党</span><span class="s2">5%</span><span class="s1">，其他政党</span><span class="s2">4%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在多伦多</span><span class="s2">416</span><span class="s1">地区，自由党以</span><span class="s2">32%</span><span class="s1">的微弱优势领先保守党（</span><span class="s2">30%</span><span class="s1">），新民主党</span><span class="s2">25%</span><span class="s1">，绿党</span><span class="s2">8%</span><span class="s1">，其他政党</span><span class="s2">4%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">益普索还发现，保守党在农村和城市选民中均处于领先地位。</span><span class="s2">53%</span><span class="s1">农村选民将投票给保守党，自由党获</span><span class="s2">20%</span><span class="s1">支持，新民主党</span><span class="s2">16%</span><span class="s1">，绿党</span><span class="s2">4%</span><span class="s1">，</span><span class="s2">7%</span><span class="s1">会考虑投票给其他政党。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在安省市区和市郊地区，保守党也领先。</span><span class="s2">36%</span><span class="s1">的选民会支持保守党候选人，自由党</span><span class="s2">29%</span><span class="s1">，新民主党</span><span class="s2">24%</span><span class="s1">，绿党</span><span class="s2">6%</span><span class="s1">，其他政党</span><span class="s2">5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">地区来看，保守党处处领先。</span></p>
