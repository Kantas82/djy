<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宾州6月1日电费涨价 最高涨幅达45%</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月28日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">（大纪元费城讯）<span style="color: #353535;">宾州公用事业委员会</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">PUC<span lang="ZH-CN">提醒消费者，大多数公用事业公司将调整<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E8%B4%B9.md#1">电费</a>，最高增加</span>45%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></span></p>
<p style="background: white; margin: 12.0pt 0in .0001pt 0in;">PUC<span lang="ZH-CN">说，从</span>6 <span lang="ZH-CN">月</span> 1 <span lang="ZH-CN">日开始，许多非购买（</span>non-shopping<span lang="ZH-CN">）（基础设定服务）客户的<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E8%B4%B9.md#1">电费</a>将“大幅增加”，具体价格取决于他们的<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%9B%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8.md#1">电力公司</a>，将加价</span>6%<span lang="ZH-CN">到</span>45%<span lang="ZH-CN">不等。宾州受监管的<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%8A%9B%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8.md#1">电力公司</a>会调整其“比较价格”（</span>PTC<span lang="ZH-CN">），这是非购买客户每季度或每半年的默认价格。</span></p>
<p style="background: white; margin: 12.0pt 0in .0001pt 0in;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">以下是住户所属的电力公司的电价变化：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">公民电力（</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Citizens<span lang="ZH-CN">’</span> Electric<span lang="ZH-CN">），从每千瓦时</span> 7.3995 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 9.3667 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>26.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Met-Ed<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 6.832 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 7.936 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>16.1%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Penelec<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 6.232 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 8.443 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>35.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Penn Power<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 7.082 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 8.694 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>22.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">PPL<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 8.941 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 12.366 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>38.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">Wellsboro Electric<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 7.7569 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 9.592 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>23.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">West Penn Power<span lang="ZH-CN">，从每千瓦时</span> 5.667 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分升至</span> 8.198 <span lang="ZH-CN">美分（</span>44.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">监管机构表示，客户现在应开始比较价格，并通过</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> PAPowerSwitch.com <span lang="ZH-CN">购买具有竞争力公司的电。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">PUC <span lang="ZH-CN">在一份声明中表示：“即将到来的电价变化，再加上夏季漫长炎热的日子增加用电消费，消费者现在应开始选择其能源提供者。” “从现在到</span> 6 <span lang="ZH-CN">月</span> 1 <span lang="ZH-CN">日，</span>PUC <span lang="ZH-CN">鼓励消费者仔细查看他们的电费，了解未来需支付的费率。浏览</span> PUC <span lang="ZH-CN">的官方购物网站，了解竞争优惠的详细信息，以及节能的提示。”</span>PUC <span lang="ZH-CN">表示，受天然气供需变化推动的电力批发市场价格上涨，增加了电力公司</span> (EDC) <span lang="ZH-CN">的采购成本，从而推高了比较价格</span>PTC<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">根据法律，公用事业公司不能从发电中获利，因此发电成本只是转嫁给公用事业客户。</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;"> PTC <span lang="ZH-CN">平均占客户总水电气费的</span> 40% <span lang="ZH-CN">到</span> 60%<span lang="ZH-CN">。当然，此百分比因客户使用水平而异。</span></span></p>
