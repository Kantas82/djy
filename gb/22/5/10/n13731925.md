<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：顽皮幼虎在雪地玩耍 可爱似萌宠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732039-Lahja-tiger-photo-1-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德国一位摄影师在捷克拍到一只6个月大的幼虎在雪地玩耍的系列可爱照。（Ina Schieferdecker提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月10日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者E. S. Armstrong报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">德国一位<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>曾在<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%B7%E5%85%8B.md#1">捷克</a>拍到一只6个月大的<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E8%99%8E.md#1">幼虎</a>在雪地玩耍的系列照片，这只<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E8%99%8E.md#1">老虎</a>在雪地里自由地奔跑、跳跃，开心享受的它就像一只可爱的萌宠，但随着渐渐长大，王者气质尽显。<br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">来自柏林的伊娜?席弗德克<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/ina_schieferdecker/">Ina Schieferdecker</a>）非常喜爱动物，经常带着她的佳能相机四处寻找它们的身影，希望能够捕捉到它们在野外最自然、最率真的情感流露。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13732040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732040" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732040-Tiger-23-gross-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732040-Tiger-23-gross-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732040" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732041" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732041-e0190f055bc771bffb435a19aac7cca8-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732041-e0190f055bc771bffb435a19aac7cca8-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732041" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732042" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732042-Serie-1-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732042-Serie-1-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732042" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“开车环游世界对我来说很特别，永远不知道我会在哪里遇到什么，动物会如何展示自己，或者我忙碌一天是否会有收获”，49岁的伊娜告诉《大纪元时报》，“这需要足够的毅力和耐心，只拍到一张满意的照片就能成为最幸福的时刻。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">伊娜以前从事老年护理工作，现在经营披萨送货业务，她于2016年开始涉足摄影。当时，她因失去一只13岁的爱犬而伤心不已。为了减轻她的痛苦，她的家人给她准备了一台旧相机，并鼓励她在家里给其它狗狗拍照。不料，她真的很快从悲伤中走出来。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">从那时起，伊娜一直在研究摄影艺术，包括上培训班、参加研讨会以及向其他<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>学习等途径。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13732043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732043" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732043-Tiger-Dachs-1200x773.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732043-Tiger-Dachs-1200x773.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732043" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732044-toger7-1200x878.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13732044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732044-toger7-1200x878.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732044" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732045" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732045-Tiger-65-gross-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732045-Tiger-65-gross-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732045" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2018年，她参加了在<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%B7%E5%85%8B.md#1">捷克</a>举办的一个研讨会，在那里她遇到了一位驯兽师，这位驯兽师允许摄影师到野外为他的动物拍照。正是在这种因缘际会下，伊娜遇到了仅6个月大的拉哈贾（Lahja）。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在拍摄拉哈贾的过程中，伊娜对摄影的热情得到进一步提升。她每隔两到三个月就定期回到捷克拍照，目睹拉哈贾从一个喜欢扑向她、顽皮啃咬她大腿的精力充沛的幼崽成长为一头威风凛凛的母<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E8%99%8E.md#1">老虎</a>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">经过一整年的努力，伊娜用相机记录下拉哈贾在中欧的不同地点、不同季节的珍贵镜头，包括拉哈贾躲在白雪皑皑的树后玩捉迷藏，在夏季的水池里嬉戏，在草地上急速奔跑，以及敏捷地爬到树上等等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13732046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732046" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732046-Tiger-7-gross-1200x1694.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732046-Tiger-7-gross-1200x1694.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="635" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732046" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13732047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732047" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732047-serie-3-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732047-serie-3-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732047" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">到了2019年，伊娜不得不与拉哈贾告别，但这段经历却让她沉醉不已。从那时起，她开始环游世界去寻找各种野生动物，维也纳、瑞典以及波兰等地都留下了她的足迹。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">值得一提的是，2019年，伊娜因其拍摄的一张<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0.md#1">地松鼠</a>照片而在奥地利维也纳自然历史博物馆被授予<ahref="https://www.inawolfisblickwinkel.de/cewe2019.php">CEWE摄影奖</a>的总冠军头衔，她的照片在448,152份提交的作品中脱颖而出。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13732048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732048" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732048-Versteckspiel-mit-dem-Ziesel-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13732048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732048-Versteckspiel-mit-dem-Ziesel-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732048" class="wp-caption-text">（Ina Schieferdecker提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">伊娜说她将继续带着她的相机四处冒险，尝试捕捉肉眼不易识别的美丽瞬间。而且，她说即使她空手而归，她仍会继续坚持下去，因为这个过程本身就会给她带来快乐，为她的生活带来平衡，而且“真的很高兴能够安静地享受大自然”。</span></p>
