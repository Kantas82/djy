<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“五月天”庆典游行回归高贵林港</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13730081-12-DSC_7252-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月08日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者杨清清加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在经历</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年疫情后，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2022</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日中午，<ahref="https://github.com/ardkds3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E8%B4%B5%E6%9E%97%E6%B8%AF.md#1">高贵林港</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/ardkds3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E4%BA%94%E6%9C%88%E5%A4%A9%E2%80%9D%E5%BA%86%E5%85%B8%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">“五月天”庆典游行</a>再度以传统的形式回归这个拥有百年历史的城镇。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">今年游行的最大亮点无疑是疫情中首度重启，数十个团体应邀参与，当地居民涌向街头。人们载歌载舞，共同庆祝这一夏日前的盛会。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">经典的苏格兰风笛队演奏，少女们活力四射的舞蹈表演，驱散了人们心中的阴霾，赢得观众的阵阵喝采</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">掌声。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/ardkds3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%95.md#1">法轮大法</a>团体的<ahref="https://github.com/ardkds3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9B%BD%E4%B9%90%E9%98%9F.md#1">天国乐队</a>、花车、<ahref="https://github.com/ardkds3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E9%BC%93%E9%98%9F.md#1">腰鼓队</a>向观众们展示</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">浓郁的东方文化气息，尤为引人关注，一路上不停有观众们随着天国乐团的乐曲舞动并鼓掌欢呼。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
