<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义县增181人确诊 县长吁打疫苗正向心防疫</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731246-554297-600x400.png" />
<h6>2022年5月9日，嘉义县增181人确诊。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月09日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）嘉义县5月9日公布181人<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%AE%E8%AF%8A.md#1">确诊</a>，出现症状151人，无症状30人。今天解除隔离673人，目前居家隔离447人，轻症居家照护1071人。全县6-11岁幼童疫苗接种1771人，无不良反应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731247-554298.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731247-554298.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731247" class="wp-caption-text">嘉县陪伴抗疫五安心。<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>快报安排心理师安心上线，教导乡亲缓解心理压力的“安静能系望”五大招，有心理咨商需求，请电洽05-3621150关怀中心安排专业咨商协助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731248-554299.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731248-554299.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731248" class="wp-caption-text">寻求信仰上的慰藉。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长翁章梁表示，今天增加181人<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%AE%E8%AF%8A.md#1">确诊</a>，年龄介于10个月-85岁，10岁以下13人，70岁以上6人。近来幼童确诊数日增，全县累计10岁以下染疫幼童202人，70岁以上72人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731249" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731249-554300.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731249-554300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731249" class="wp-caption-text">在家运动很安心，很简单。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁说，国内疫情持续攀升，打乱民众的家庭生活作息和经济的稳定性，2年多下来，造成许多乡亲严重的心理负担，医疗、警消……等<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>一线人员更是承受莫大的身心负荷，“心”的防疫，也是面对疫情的我们，必须共同关注的议题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731250-554301.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731250" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731250-554301.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731250" class="wp-caption-text">宠物陪伴。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>翁章梁强调，疫情期间应保持正向态度，做好防疫措施，不要惊慌，打好疫苗，保护自己，保护家人；尤其面对疫情的医护与一线防疫人员，他们坚守岗位，毫不怠慢，承担极大的精神与体力负荷，请给所有的防疫伙伴加油打气，多一点体谅，是支持他们持续坚持的最大动力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731251-554302.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731251-554302.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731251" class="wp-caption-text">专注于感受五感。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天“防疫快报”邀请慈济医院陈可家心理师“安心”上线，提醒民众面对Omicron“安静能系望”五大招，分别是:</p>
<p><strong>安：</strong>勤洗手、戴口罩、保持社交距离，以策安全。</p>
<p><strong>静：</strong>适量阅读疫情新闻，保持平静，减低焦虑。</p>
<p><strong>能：</strong>多运动、充实防疫知识，提升防疫知能。</p>
<p><strong>系：</strong>善用网路或电话与亲友相互支持鼓励。</p>
<p><strong>望：</strong>保持希望与正向能量，疫情终会过去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731252-554303.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13731252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731252-554303.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="650" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731252" class="wp-caption-text">疫情让我学到三件事。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局局长赵纹华提醒，解隔新制5/8上路，适用5/8检验阳性的确诊者，但不回溯5/8以前筛检的阳性者，只要距离发病日或采检日达7天，无须采检即可直接解隔，并进行7天自主健康管理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731253" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731253-554304.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13731253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731253-554304-600x867.jpeg" alt="" width="451" b="652" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731253" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县陪伴抗疫我OK你OK。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局表示，面对疫情，除了落实戴口罩、勤洗手、<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E7%96%AB%E8%8B%97.md#1">打疫苗</a>等基本功，也要用“心”防疫，好好照顾自己的心情，关心自己，关心周遭的人。疫情期间联系亲友，互相鼓励 ，学习正确的防疫资讯，并养成规律作息和运动习惯，才能打造最佳防疫力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731254-554305.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13731254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731254-554305-600x450.png" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731254" class="wp-caption-text">秒懂居家照护、居家隔离、自主防疫。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府再次呼吁65岁以上长者，5月31日前接种疫苗，可获得500元等值礼券或卫教品，赶快<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E7%96%AB%E8%8B%97.md#1">打疫苗</a>，携手走过疫情，迎向健康新生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13731255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731255-554306.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13731255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731255-554306-600x415.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731255" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县防疫“心”关怀专线。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13731256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13731256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731256-554307.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13731256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13731256-554307-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13731256" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长翁章梁说，今天“防疫快报”邀请慈济医院陈可家心理师“安心”上线，提醒民众面对Omicron“安静能系望”五大招。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
