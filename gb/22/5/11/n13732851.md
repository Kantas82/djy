<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>审慎因应梅雨 北水局完成防汛整备</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732853-554525-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>审慎因应梅雨，北水局完成防汛整备。（北水局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月11日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）依中央气象局预报，今年首波<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E9%9B%A8.md#1">梅雨</a>锋面将于5月12日靠近台湾，为因应梅雨带来明显降雨，<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%B1%80.md#1">北水局</a>就所辖<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">水库</a>设施完成整备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13732852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732852-554524.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732852-554524-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732852" class="wp-caption-text">审慎因应<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E9%9B%A8.md#1">梅雨</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%B0%B4%E5%B1%80.md#1">北水局</a>完成<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B1%9B%E6%95%B4%E5%A4%87.md#1">防汛整备</a>。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>截至今（5/11）日上午7时资料，北水局所辖供应桃园及新竹之石门、宝山及宝山第二<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">水库</a>蓄水率，分别为97.4%、98.6%及100%，水情稳定。相关水库设施之<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B1%9B%E6%95%B4%E5%A4%87.md#1">防汛整备</a>，就事件通报、放水警报发布、供电与闸门机组检测、设施安全检查及演练等，已陆续实施完成，且将于汛期间全日守视水库集水区降雨及入流状况，以最严谨的态度因应，确保营运维持正常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13732854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13732854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732854-554526.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13732854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13732854-554526-600x449.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13732854" class="wp-caption-text">审慎因应梅雨，北水局完成防汛整备。（北水局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基于汛期气象变化大，呼吁民众随时注意降雨讯息，尤其尽量避免进入河道活动，俾保护生命财产安全。◇</p>
