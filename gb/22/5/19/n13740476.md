<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>土水资源宣导列车开进校园 环保从教育开始</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740477-555916-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>博爱国小大合照。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月19日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83.md#1">环境</a>保护局竭力办理“土水资源保育学校<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%B4%E6%95%85%E4%BA%8B.md#1">说故事</a>活动”，已迈入第5年，参与学童人数超过4,000名，透过浅显易懂的<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%98%E6%9C%AC.md#1">绘本</a>故事书，将土壤及<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E6%B0%B4.md#1">地下水</a>环境保育观念，以轻松、有趣的方式深植学童心中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13740478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13740478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740478-555917.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13740478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740478-555917-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13740478" class="wp-caption-text">环教讲师与博爱国小学生互动。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本年度活动邀请嘉义大学附设实验国小何梦青老师担任讲师，以《泥宝搬新家》故事书为主题，并透过环教讲师以活泼生动的方式，模拟故事情节，於崇文国小及博爱国小向学童传达土壤及<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E6%B0%B4.md#1">地下水</a>污染的可怕，影响范围及资源保育的重要性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13740479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13740479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740479-555918.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13740479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740479-555918-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13740479" class="wp-caption-text">崇文国小学生上课。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市政府<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83.md#1">环境</a>保护局指出，土壤及地下水议题对学童来说，既熟悉又陌生，了解其物却不知其作用、特性跟污染预防的重要，透过宣导活动，引导学童重新认识“土壤”及“地下水”，培养学童正确守护土壤及地下水观念，进而影响家长一同于生活中实践环境保护的精神。</p>
<p>《泥宝搬新家》<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%98%E6%9C%AC.md#1">绘本</a><ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%B4%E6%95%85%E4%BA%8B.md#1">说故事</a>影片，可至行政院环境保护署土污基管会YouTube频道观看（网址: <ahref="https://reurl.cc/M0y4oX" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://reurl.cc/M0y4oX</a>）。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_13740480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13740480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740480-555919.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13740480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13740480-555919-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13740480" class="wp-caption-text">环教讲师与崇文国小学生互动。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
