<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒲公英：烦心杂草 vs 养生良药</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746944-Fotolia_40713510_Subscription_L-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒲公英是什么样的良药？（Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年05月28日讯】在北美，家家户户的草坪都是非常漂亮的，这得益于人们精心的打理。可是有一种杂草非常地令人心烦，它从春天开始，一直持续到整个夏天，不断地开出小黄花，黄花谢了之后， 变成白白的绒球，风一吹，绒絮四散，落地生根，又生长出一片新的杂草。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746948-Fotolia_43182745_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746948-Fotolia_43182745_Subaion_L-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746948" class="wp-caption-text">生命力旺盛的<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B2%E5%85%AC%E8%8B%B1.md#1">蒲公英</a>。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>锄铲挖不尽，春风吹又生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746955" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746955-Fotolia_93221306_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746955-Fotolia_93221306_Subaion_L-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746955" class="wp-caption-text">生命力旺盛的<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B2%E5%85%AC%E8%8B%B1.md#1">蒲公英</a>。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这，就是蒲公英，又名耨草、金簪草、黄花地丁。</p>
<p>蒲公英已经成了花园里的第一杀手，很多除草剂的广告都用它作例子。田园里，山坡上，水渠旁，甚至于在房屋车道两边的裂缝，都可以看到它不屈不挠的身影。</p>
<p>但这个令人头疼的杂草，实际上是一味非常好用的<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8D%AF.md#1">草药</a>。</p>
<p>中医使用蒲公英已有一千多年的历史了，<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E6%9C%AC%E8%8D%89%E7%BA%B2%E7%9B%AE%E3%80%8B.md#1">《本草纲目》</a>阐述了它的药效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746957" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746957-shutterstock_367707542.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746957-shutterstock_367707542-450x288.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746957" class="wp-caption-text">花园里第一杀手的蒲公英实际上是一味非常好用的<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8D%AF.md#1">草药</a>。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>功效之一：清热解毒，杀菌抗病毒。</h2>
<p>感冒、咽炎、上呼吸道感染，都可以应用蒲公英治疗。现代药理学发现，蒲公英的提取物确实有抗病毒的作用。目前，病毒大流行还没有完全结束，秋冬到了以后，感冒和流感也会增多，如此一来，蒲公英抗病毒的作用就尤为珍贵。</p>
<p>【蒲公英饮】蒲公英15~30克，加水煎煮20-30分钟，取水饮用。注意，煎煮不要超过30分钟，此时药效最强。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746958" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746958-Fotolia_13845259_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746958-Fotolia_13845259_Subaion_L-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746958" class="wp-caption-text">蒲公英饮。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>功效之二：保肝，解肝毒。</h2>
<p>现代研究发现，蒲公英可以帮助肝脏分解脂肪，生成氨基酸；帮助胆汁分泌，促进消化功能，可以治疗便秘。现在有人用蒲公英，尤其是作用更强的根，做成保健品，具有天然保肝、清肝毒的功效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746959" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746959-Fotolia_138732012_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746959-Fotolia_138732012_Subaion_L-450x305.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746959" class="wp-caption-text">保肝良药。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【蒲公英茶】取带有花朵和根的鲜蒲公英放入杯中，直接开水冲泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13200548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13200548" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13200548-shutterstock_1715674399-e1630420607426.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13200548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13200548-shutterstock_1715674399-450x300.jpg" alt="蒲公英茶" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13200548" class="wp-caption-text">整株蒲公英都可以泡茶，包括根、叶和花朵三部分，风味迥异且独特。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>功效之三：消肿散结，治疗乳腺炎。</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E6%9C%AC%E8%8D%89%E7%BA%B2%E7%9B%AE%E3%80%8B.md#1">《本草纲目》</a>记载，妇女产后哺乳期乳房肿痛，排奶不畅，可以用鲜蒲公英30~60克，煎水内服，捣烂外敷。女性在哺乳期可以经常煮点蒲公英，用水定期清洗乳房，可以预防乳腺炎的发生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746960" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746960-Fotolia_88796393_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746960-Fotolia_88796393_Subaion_L-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746960" class="wp-caption-text">妇女哺乳期，用蒲公英煮水清洗乳房，可以预防乳腺炎。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>功效之四：明目，治疗目赤肿痛。</h2>
<p>蒲公英入肝经，而肝开窍于目，所以它对于眼睛的症状，如眼红、眼痛、眼干涩、视物不清等，都有很好的疗效。用蒲公英煎水，内服或外洗。</p>
<p>我遇到过一个病人，眼睛有慢性炎症，经常去家庭医生和眼科大夫那里诊疗。医生开了滴眼液，但效果不佳。我让他用蒲公英水治疗，结果炎症很快好转。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13746963" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13746963" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746963-Fotolia_110052242_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13746963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13746963-Fotolia_110052242_Subaion_L-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13746963" class="wp-caption-text">眼睛有炎症？可用蒲公英水治疗。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧洲还用蒲公英治疗夜盲症。夜盲症是一到黄昏，就看不清东西。由此可见，蒲公英对眼睛确实很有益处。</p>
<h2>功效之五：祛斑美容。</h2>
<p>蒲公英被誉为“美容万金油”，对老化皮肤、色素斑、日晒斑、雀斑、粉刺、皮炎、疣等，都有治疗作用。可以煎水内服，也可以用汁液涂抹患处。</p>
<p>常常长痘痘，建议用蒲公英面膜改善。把蒲公英的花、叶绞碎，或茎中的汁液滴出来，配蛋清制成面膜，很有帮助。</p>
<p>蒲公英虽然好用，但是提醒大家一下，它性寒，对于脾胃虚寒、腹泻患者要小心使用。</p>
