<h6>2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月04日讯】（大纪元记者李奥美国西雅图报导）踏入春季，由美国西北汽车报业协会（NWAPA）主办的一年一度Mudfest<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A5%E6%B3%9E%E8%8A%82.md#1">泥泞节</a>），在离西雅图市西南的Ridge Motosports Park进行。由美西北地区的各界车评人参与测试及评分，最后选出各类别最佳汽车及年度户外活动车（NW Outdoor Activity Vehicle of the Year）。</p>
<p>Mudfest因疫情关系停办了数年，终于在今年4月中，重新举行这一年度活动。大纪元记者也获邀参加，活动分为赛道及越野测试，今年测试的汽车共有20辆，分为“紧凑型和超紧凑型家庭车（Compact and Subcompact Family）”、“中型和全尺寸家庭车（Mid and Full-size Family）”、“紧凑型和中型豪华车（Compact and Midsize Luxury）”、“全尺寸豪华车（Full-size Luxury）”及“皮卡（Pickups）”5大类别分。</p>
<p><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d1fejrztdgsube.cloudfront.net/U2wLQRq4p"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rpVE_Gg5_LU" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p><center></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>“紧凑型和超紧凑型家庭车”类别中，测试车有Subaru Forester Wilderness、Toyota RAV4 TRD Off-Road及Mazda CX-50 2.5 Turbo Premium Plus。</p>
<p>“中型和全尺寸家庭车”则有GMC Yukon AT4、Honda Passport TrailSport、Hyundai Santa Fe XRT、Jeep Grand Cherokee 4xe及Kia Sorento PHEV。</p>
<p>“紧凑型和中型豪华车”类别包括Mercedes-AMG GLB 35、Acura MDX Type S、Genesis GV70 AWD及Volvo V90 B6 Cross Country。</p>
<p>“全尺寸豪华车”有Genesis GV80 AWD、Wagoneer Series III 4&#215;4、Lexus LX 600 Ultra Lux及Rolls-Royce Cullinan。</p>
<p>“皮卡”类别的测试车包括Ford F-150 Raptor 4X4、Hyundai Santa Cruz AWD、Nissan Frontier Pro-4X及Toyota Tundra Platinum CrewMax。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726676-2022_Mudfest1-e1651639134989.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726676-2022_Mudfest1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726676" class="wp-caption-text">2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中最突出的是Rolls-Royce Cullinan，不仅因为其有着鲜橙色的外表，它的车价更是其他汽车的数倍到十倍，价格接近43万美元，驾着它去泥泞中打滚，份外开心！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726678-2022_Mudfest3-e1651639185771.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726678-2022_Mudfest3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726678" class="wp-caption-text">2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一日测试在柏油路面上进行，每一个车评人均需驾驶每一辆汽车，在场内设置的加速区、刹车区测试汽车的加速及刹车，并在设置的路障间行驶，测试汽车的过弯表现。继而可驶入赛道上走一圈，试验汽车的实际行驶表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726679-2022_Mudfest4-e1651639196771.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726679-2022_Mudfest4-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726679" class="wp-caption-text">2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13726677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726677-2022_Mudfest2-e1651639151443.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726677-2022_Mudfest2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726677" class="wp-caption-text">2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天在设置的越野路段行驶，有碎石路段、水坑、凹凸不平路段、不同倾斜度的山圾等，务求测试出汽车的越野能力及涉水能力。其中有些路段为极端（Extreme）路况，只会让部分经强化的车辆测试。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13726680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13726680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726680-2022_Mudfest5-e1651639204741.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13726680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13726680-2022_Mudfest5-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13726680" class="wp-caption-text">2022 NWAPA MudFest。（李奥／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过两天的道路及越野测试，最后经过各车评人投票选出各类别冠亚军，以及年度户外活动车：</p>
<p>最佳紧凑型和超紧凑型家庭车 &#8211; Mazda CX-50 Turbo Premium Plus; 亚军 – Subaru Forester Wilderness<br />

最佳电动化多用途车 -Jeep Grand Cherokee 4xe； 亚军 &#8211;?Kia Sorento PHEV<br />
