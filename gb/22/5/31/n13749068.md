<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中华民俗艺术工作坊2022公演“传奇再现”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749059-DSC08386-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊以压轴节目“涅槃重生”，为观众带来惊喜与希望。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月31日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨威斯顿镇报导）5月29日，<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%B7%A5%E4%BD%9C%E5%9D%8A.md#1">中华民俗艺术工作坊</a>（CFAW）在威斯顿镇（Weston）的瑞吉斯学院（Regis College）举办2022年度公演。两场<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%94%E5%87%BA.md#1">演出</a>各吸引数百观众到场欣赏，赢得热烈掌声和欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13749052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749052" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749052-DSC08198.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749052-DSC08198-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749052" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，“齐鼓震天”为<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%B7%A5%E4%BD%9C%E5%9D%8A.md#1">中华民俗艺术工作坊</a>2022公演拉开序幕。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749053-DSC08218.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749053-DSC08218-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749053" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊2022公演的舞龙节目令观众热情欢呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749057-DSC08332.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749057-DSC08332-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749057" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊的高难度扯铃表演赢得观众的阵阵惊呼与掌声。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在瑞吉斯学院的表演厅，观众和工作人员都戴着口罩进场和观看<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%94%E5%87%BA.md#1">演出</a>。在上、下半场总共12个节目中，观众们热情高涨，不断地为演员们欢呼和鼓掌。经文处孙俭元处长观赏了下午场，而侨教中心潘昭荣主任则是观赏晚场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13749054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749054" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749054-DSC08253.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749054-DSC08253-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749054" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊2022公演的扇子舞。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749055-DSC08259.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749055-DSC08259-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749055" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊的公演节目描绘彩狮的梦境。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749056-DSC08303.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749056-DSC08303-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749056" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊为观众带来的唯美<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749058-DSC08356.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749058-DSC08356-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749058" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊带来优美的<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88.md#1">舞蹈</a>演出。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演出一如既往地以气势磅礴的击鼓“齐鼓震天”拉开序幕，随后还有各种古典舞蹈，以及<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A6%E6%9C%AF.md#1">武术</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md#1">舞狮</a>、舞龙、扯铃等民俗艺术表演。演出的压轴节目是凤凰舞蹈“涅槃重生”，据节目册介绍，这象征着新的惊喜与希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13749060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749060-DSC08408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749060-DSC08408-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749060" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊公演之后，团长张昆、经文处处长孙俭元，以及演员和指导老师们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13749061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13749061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749061-DSC08422.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13749061" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13749061-DSC08422-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13749061" class="wp-caption-text">5月29日，中华民俗艺术工作坊公演的下午场之后，观众及演员们聚集在剧场大厅。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午的第一场演出之后，中华民俗艺术工作坊团长张昆感谢了每一位演出参与者。他说，正是有了演员、家长们，以及后勤团队等每位成员的参与和奉献，大家才能一起为观众带来成功的演出。</p>
<p>“大家都很辛苦，但也看到了这样很好的成绩。”张昆说。◇</p>
