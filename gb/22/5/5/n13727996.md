<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—仙人掌（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13727998-9537020211546f56e0030fba05015cb2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—仙人掌（彩墨）。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E4%BA%BA%E6%8E%8C.md#1">仙人掌</a><ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</p>
<p>在校园内的一个小角落里种有一大丛<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E4%BA%BA%E6%8E%8C.md#1">仙人掌</a>，五六株杂乱的长在一起，长得很高很茂密。因为它有尖刺，少有人敢靠近它，学生们打扫校园时也都离它远远的。</p>
<p>不过，在这些畏人的针刺丛生的隐处，竟然有小雀儿在那里筑巢，既隐密又安全。</p>
<p>巢中刚孵出尚未开眼的三四只幼雏，感应到父母归巢的颤动，张开大口争相索讨食物，正是所谓的“青虫不易捕，黄口无饱期”啊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13727998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13727998" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13727998-9537020211546f56e0030fba05015cb2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13727998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13727998-9537020211546f56e0030fba05015cb2.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="481" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13727998" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%868.md#1">徐明义画集8</a>—仙人掌<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。66×59cm。（图片来源：<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Cactus/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>In a small corner of the campus there is a large cluster of cactus, five or six stems messy grow up together, very tall and dense. Because of its sharp spikes, few people dare to approach them and students also keep them away while they are cleaning the campus.</p>
<p>However, a hidden place somewhere in these scary sharp spikes cluster of cactus, a nest of small sparrow bird is there, secret and safe.</p>
<p>Just hatching three or four chicks, eyes have not been opened yet, knew their father and mom are home, open wide mouth for food. It is the so-called &#8220;worms not easy to catch, chicks are never full&#8221;.@</p>
