<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大费城泛亚裔协会举办颁奖晚宴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728033-DSC00696-b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大费城泛亚裔协会主席 Narasimha (Nick) Shenoy（中间）与获奖者合影。（李晓苏/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月05日讯】(大纪元记者李晓苏费城报导)5月3日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%B4%B9%E5%9F%8E%E6%B3%9B%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">大费城泛亚裔协会</a>（The Pan Asian Association of Greater Philadelphia）在费城中国城东方明珠大酒楼举办了泛亚奖<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A2%81%E5%A5%96.md#1">颁奖</a><ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">晚宴</a>。一百多位各亚裔社区的嘉宾应邀出席。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A2%81%E5%A5%96.md#1">颁奖</a>典礼<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">晚宴</a>由6ABC电视台著名主播Christie Ileto主持。<ahref="https://github.com/wzivwa3659/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%B4%B9%E5%9F%8E%E6%B3%9B%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">大费城泛亚裔协会</a>主席 Narasimha (Nick) Shenoy向来宾逐一介绍获奖者。今年的主旨演讲者为新闻业艾美奖得主、WPVI-TV的著名韩裔主播Nydia Han，她也获得了此次晚宴的最佳影响力奖。而今年的杰出非营利服务奖则归于美国菲律宾医生协会（The Association of Philippine Physicians in America）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13728035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728035-DSC00653-copy-e1651766267655.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728035-DSC00653-copy-e1651766267655.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728035" class="wp-caption-text">新闻业艾美奖得主、WPVI-TV的著名韩裔主播Nydia Han在颁奖典礼上发表主旨演讲，她也获得了此次晚宴的最佳影响力奖。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宾州印度裔州参议员Nikil Saval，宾州州长办公室亚太移民委员会执行主任孙一，以及费城市议员Mark Squilla和David Oh等政要都应邀出席了颁奖晚宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13728043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728043-DSC00631-copy-e1651768347215.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728043-DSC00631-copy-e1651768347215.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728043" class="wp-caption-text">宾州印度裔参议员Nikil Saval在在颁奖典礼上致辞。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13728040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728040-DSC00628-copy-e1651767866547.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728040-DSC00628-copy-e1651767866547.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728040" class="wp-caption-text">费城市议员Mark Squilla在颁奖典礼上致辞。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5月份是亚太裔传统月，费城市议员大卫·欧（David Oh）在大费城地区泛亚协会的颁奖宴会对大纪元表示：美籍亚裔对美国的贡献非常大，遗憾的是却没有得到应有的承认。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13728036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728036-DSC00607-copy-e1651767517346.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728036-DSC00607-copy-e1651767517346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728036" class="wp-caption-text">费城市议员大卫·欧（David Oh）（中）与国际中国艺术协会创办人及会长Sue Cheng在晚宴上合影。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧议员对美籍亚裔的历史很了解：“亚裔美国人在美国的记录，首先是15世纪末的菲律宾人定居美国。我们非常确定我们的亚裔美国人在革命战争中服役。但根据记录，第一批亚裔美国人是在1812年的战争中在安德鲁·杰克逊手下作战。在那之后，各美籍亚裔参加了第一次和第二次世界大战、朝鲜战争、越南战争，以及其它许多事情。”</p>
<p>欧议员说，虽然亚裔美国人只占美国人口的7%，但亚裔却占世界人口的65%。他很高兴能参加这样的活动，“来庆祝这个国家7%的人口。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13728046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728046" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728046-DSC00618-copy-e1651768718453.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728046" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728046-DSC00618-copy-e1651768718453.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728046" class="wp-caption-text">与会嘉宾在晚宴上合影留念。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13728049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13728049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728049-DSC00688-copy-e1651769003480.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13728049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13728049-DSC00688-copy-e1651769003480.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13728049" class="wp-caption-text">颁奖晚宴现场。（李晓苏/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
