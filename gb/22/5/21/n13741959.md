<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美老妇驾驶一辆1957年雪佛兰64年</h2>
<a href="https://djwddd4nnr0na.cloudfront.net/N5yVLTZAy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/qmlkem357/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/qmlkem357/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>美国威斯康星州一位老车主对她1957年购买的雪佛兰Bel Air轿车呵护备至。（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年05月21日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">美国一位老车主对她1957年购买的<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E4%BD%9B%E5%85%B0.md#1">雪佛兰</a>Bel Air<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%BF%E8%BD%A6.md#1">轿车</a>呵护备至，以至于这辆完美无瑕的<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E7%88%B7%E8%BD%A6.md#1">老爷车</a>已经与她的身份联系在一起。现如今，这位骄傲的老<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">司机</a>走到哪里都倍受瞩目。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“我知道如何照顾物件，这辆车就证明了这一点”，来自威斯康星州西本德市的格蕾丝?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">布雷格（Grace Braeger）在接受Barcroft Cars YouTube频道采访时说，“我从来没有想过能拥有这辆车那么长时间。但就这么发生了！”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13741964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741964-1-1200x829.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741964-1-1200x829.png" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741964" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13741965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741965" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741965-11-1200x678.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741965-11-1200x678.png" alt="" width="602" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741965" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">格蕾丝以2,250美元的价格买下了她的硬顶<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E4%BD%9B%E5%85%B0.md#1">雪佛兰</a>，这是1957年生产的大约700,000辆同类产品中的一辆。今天，这辆稀有的收藏车价值在35,000美元到85,000美元之间。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这辆珍贵汽车的长寿秘诀在于格蕾丝不可思议的常规护理，她远远超出了手册的建议，使汽车保持在令人难以置信的运转状态。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13741967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741967" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741967-4-1200x677.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741967-4-1200x677.png" alt="" width="601" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741967" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13741968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741968" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741968-9-1200x695.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741968-9-1200x695.png" alt="" width="601" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741968" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“这本手册说每2,000英里换一次机油，而我每1,000英里就更换一次”，格蕾丝说，“不管它需要与否，我都会去送修。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这辆车的个性化车牌上写着“57 Lady”，这是格蕾丝从那些喜欢看她开车的人那里为自己赢得的绰号。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13741970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741970-2-1200x718.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741970-2-1200x718.png" alt="" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741970" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这辆雪佛兰Bel Air<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%BF%E8%BD%A6.md#1">轿车</a>拥有动力制动和动力转向系统、四扇门、带有白色和镀铬装饰的造型优美的黑色车身和红色内饰，以及上世纪50年代典型设计的大型方向盘。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">作为这辆已有64年历史的<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E7%88%B7%E8%BD%A6.md#1">老爷车</a>的唯一车主，格雷斯充分利用了制造商的终身保修，让她的爱车始终保持在良好状态。在使用62年后，格蕾丝需要为其更换消声器时，它的原始收据就派上了用场。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13741966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13741966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741966-7-1200x804.png"><img class=" wp-image-13741966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/05/id13741966-7-1200x804.png" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13741966" class="wp-caption-text">（Barcroft Cars／Future Studios提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">截至目前，这辆雪佛兰的行驶里程已经超过122,000英里，其中多数是在格雷斯人生比较“活跃的年份”使用的。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现如今，格蕾丝主要利用她引人注目的爱车进行短途旅行，例如去教堂、去杂货店以及去西本德市内的所有约会地点，并且沿途深受其他<ahref="https://github.com/qmlkem357/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">司机</a>的喜爱。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">她强调，这辆车不是普通的汽车，而是一款非常“出类拔萃”的汽车。</span></p>
