<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宾州参议院褒奖神韵艺术团</h2>

<h6>宾州参议院褒奖神韵艺术团莅临费城。图为宾州第二选区参议员Christine M. Tartaglione及参议院褒奖。（大纪元合成）
</h6>

<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">宾州参议院褒奖全文翻译：</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext">2022<span lang="ZH-CN">年</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span lang="ZH-CN">和</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT48_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">巡回艺术团再度莅临</span><span id="OBJ_PREFIX_DWT49_com_zimbra_url" class="Object"><span lang="ZH-CN">音乐</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">学院剧院（</span>Academy of Music<span lang="ZH-CN">），展开本季首场演出。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">世界首屈一指的中国古典舞蹈和音乐公司神韵艺术团将于</span>2022<span lang="ZH-CN">年</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">日和</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">日</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">重返</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">费城音乐学院</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span>?&#8220;<span lang="ZH-CN">神韵</span>?&#8220;<span lang="ZH-CN">意为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">“</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神之舞蹈</span><span lang="ZH-CN">”</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，并承认该公司对推动宾州艺术发展的重要贡献。该公司于</span>2006<span lang="ZH-CN">年在纽约州成立，其使命是复兴五千年的中国传统文化，这些文化在中共统治下</span><span lang="ZH-CN">几近</span><span lang="ZH-CN">失传。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">?</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵在中国</span><span lang="ZH-CN">仍</span><span lang="ZH-CN">被禁</span><span lang="ZH-CN">演。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵每年在世界各地约</span>150<span lang="ZH-CN">个城市巡回演出</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">从东京到巴黎</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-CN">从悉尼到纽约。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">?</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵的现场演出获得了雷鸣般的掌声和满堂彩的赞誉。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">?</span><span lang="ZH-CN">有些观众驱车几百英里去看演出，有些观众则看</span><span lang="ZH-CN">过了</span><span lang="ZH-CN">五六次</span><span lang="ZH-CN">演出。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵的受欢迎程度反映了人们对善良、和谐和复兴的需求日益增长。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">?</span><span lang="ZH-CN">神韵代表了我们国家伟大民主的艺术自由，</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">将古老的传统</span></span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">展现在</span></span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">现代舞台</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">。古典舞、民族舞和民间舞与神韵现场乐队的原创</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">配乐完美搭配</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">数字天幕让</span><span lang="ZH-CN">观众沉浸在每</span><span lang="ZH-CN">一</span><span lang="ZH-CN">个场景中，使每场演出都美</span><span lang="ZH-CN">不胜收</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，感觉</span><span lang="ZH-CN">到跌宕起伏的情节变化</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">?</span><span lang="ZH-CN">今年是</span><span lang="ZH-CN">宾</span><span lang="ZH-CN">州居民第</span>15<span lang="ZH-CN">年体验神韵</span><span lang="ZH-CN">带给</span><span lang="ZH-CN">世界的精湛艺术和文化</span><span lang="ZH-CN">遗产。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">鉴于</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">在</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">展现了令人钦佩的领导力和品</span><span lang="ZH-CN">行</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，并</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">恪守</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">我们社会的原则，使我们的社区成为一个更好的</span><span class="q4iawc"><span lang="ZH-CN">生活场所</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN">，并为这一成就赢得了家人和朋友的钦佩，你</span><span lang="ZH-CN">们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为自己、</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">家人</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">我们的社区带来了荣誉</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">因此，宾夕法尼亚州参议院向神韵艺术团致敬，因为他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">出色</span><span lang="ZH-CN">的服务记录</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-CN">远远</span><span lang="ZH-CN">地</span><span lang="ZH-CN">超过了通常被</span><span lang="ZH-CN">认</span><span lang="ZH-CN">为</span><span lang="ZH-CN">是</span><span lang="ZH-CN">模范的标准；并祝愿他们</span><span lang="ZH-CN">拥</span><span lang="ZH-CN">有一个最有价值和</span><span lang="ZH-CN">最</span><span lang="ZH-CN">充实的未来</span><span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p class="yiv9434479253msobodytext"><span lang="ZH-CN">并指示将参议员</span>Christine M. Tartaglione<span lang="ZH-CN">提出的这份文件的副本转交给神韵</span><span lang="ZH-CN">在费城的</span><span lang="ZH-CN">主办方</span><span lang="ZH-CN">大</span><span lang="ZH-CN">费城法轮大法</span><span lang="ZH-CN">学</span><span lang="ZH-CN">会。</span></p>
