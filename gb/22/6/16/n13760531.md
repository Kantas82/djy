<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>华埠狮子会换届 李典儒任会长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760416-DSC08780-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月11日晚在帝苑酒楼，波士顿华埠狮子会卸任会长伍国光将“明灯”交给新届会长李典儒。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年06月16日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）6月11日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">波士顿华埠</a><ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">狮子会</a>在帝苑酒楼举办换届典礼，<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%85%B8%E5%84%92.md#1">李典儒</a>接过“明灯”就任会长，资深会员丁慧明获颁狮子会的高级荣誉“<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%82%E6%96%87%E9%92%9F%E5%A3%AB%E4%BC%9A%E5%91%98.md#1">茂文钟士会员</a>”（Melvin Jones Fellowship）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760408-DSC08744.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760408-DSC08744-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760408" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">波士顿华埠</a><ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">狮子会</a>职员与嘉宾在帝苑酒楼合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>华埠狮子会属于国际狮子会麻省33K分区。会长交接典礼依照狮子会的传统进行。随着司仪陈国航、周丽玲宣布典礼开始，来宾们首先在蔡倩婷的带领下朗读《美国第一章》（First Verse of America），随后在林淑明带领下向国旗宣读效忠誓词。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760409-DSC08750.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760409-DSC08750-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760409" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">波士顿华埠狮子会</a>新职员就任，蔡倩婷带读《美国第一章》（First Verse of America）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13760411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760411-DSC08756.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760411-DSC08756-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760411" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">波士顿华埠狮子会</a>新职员就任，林淑明带读效忠誓词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13760410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760410" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760410-DSC08754.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760410-DSC08754-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760410" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，波士顿华埠狮子会新职员就任，嘉宾们朗读《美国第一章》（First Verse of America）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，卸任会长伍国光带领大家进行祈祷，资深会员雷国辉朗读“狮子祝词”，区域主席李源沛介绍了参与晚宴的嘉宾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760412-DSC08757.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760412-DSC08757-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760412" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，波士顿华埠狮子会新职员就任，会员们朗读“狮子祝词”。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与当晚典礼的嘉宾有近200人，包括多个地区的狮子会会员，以及波士顿地区的侨团代表。伍国光上台热情地向大家表示欢迎，并祝福会务继续繁荣，大家快乐安康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760413" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760413-DSC08764.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760413-DSC08764-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760413" class="wp-caption-text">在6月11日的波士顿华埠狮子会晚宴中，卸任会长伍国光欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在国际狮子会33k分区总监Margaret “Peg” Needre的见证下，华埠狮子会的新一届职员宣誓就职。伍国光将代表会长职责的“明灯”和锤子交给<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%85%B8%E5%84%92.md#1">李典儒</a>，后者正式宣誓就职会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760414" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760414-DSC08771.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760414-DSC08771-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760414" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，波士顿华埠狮子会新届职员在帝苑酒楼宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13760415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760415" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760415-DSC08777.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760415-DSC08777-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760415" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚，波士顿华埠狮子会新届会长李典儒在帝苑酒楼宣誓就职。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李典儒来自香港，从美国摩顿市高中（Malden High School）和Babson学院毕业，现从事银行业工作。</p>
<p>他说，他参加狮子会已经4年了，看到会员们为华埠社区贡献良多。在过去一年，华埠狮子会继续提供免费的眼睛检查服务，去养老院服务长者，向中文学校和儿童玩具项目捐钱捐物，以及参加华埠街道清扫、青少年项目等活动。</p>
<p>李典儒最后祝贺华埠狮子会迈向35周年，并祝福来宾们健康、快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760419-DSC08791.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760419-DSC08791-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760419" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚在帝苑酒楼，波士顿华埠狮子会参与晚宴的会员合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会期间，华埠狮子会的资深会员丁慧明获颁“<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%82%E6%96%87%E9%92%9F%E5%A3%AB%E4%BC%9A%E5%91%98.md#1">茂文钟士会员</a>”奖牌。据国际狮子会网站介绍，这个以狮子会创始人Melvin Jones命名的奖项，被用于感谢为狮子会捐款或募款超过1000美元的公益人士。</p>
<p>在波士顿华埠，余丽媖、雷国辉、蔡倩婷、司徒月华、阮鸿灿、林淑明、谢如键、李源沛、Paul Lee等会员都是“茂文钟士会员”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13760418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13760418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760418-DSC08789.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13760418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13760418-DSC08789-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13760418" class="wp-caption-text">6月11日晚在帝苑酒楼，波士顿华埠狮子会的资深会员丁慧明获颁“茂文钟士会员”（Melvin Jones Fellowship）。</figcaption></figure>
