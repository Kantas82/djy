<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡尔加里丁香节 驱散疫情阴霾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753742-20220605-WangYing-Lilac-festival-00-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2022年卡尔加里丁香节，街道上人山人海，摩肩接踵。（王颖／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年06月07日讯】暌违两年之后，一年一度的<ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a><ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%81%E9%A6%99%E8%8A%82.md#1">丁香节</a>于6月5日再次登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13753746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753746-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753746-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-01-600x404.jpg" alt="卡尔加里丁香节" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753746" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a><ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%81%E9%A6%99%E8%8A%82.md#1">丁香节</a>。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自1989年5月举办以来，卡尔加里丁香节规模逐年增大，已成为卡城年度最大的街头节日，标志着夏季庆典的开启。它的命名来自此时满城盛放的丁香花，白如雪，紫如霞，花影婆娑，暗香浮动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13753750" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753750-cacde86f461859ae369bafac7d521e90.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753750-cacde86f461859ae369bafac7d521e90-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753750" class="wp-caption-text">白丁香花。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13753751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753751" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753751-20220605-LeeYun-Lilac-festival-purple-lilac.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753751-20220605-LeeYun-Lilac-festival-purple-lilac-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753751" class="wp-caption-text">紫丁香花。（李蕴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2020年3月，全球疫情爆发，丁香节被迫中断两年，如今全面回归。</p>
<p>6月5日周日，沿市中心南北走向的第四大道，长达15个街区，数百摊位林立。与往年不同的是，今年首次开放东西走向的17大街上7个街区。大大小小的帐篷，形形色色的艺术品与服饰，令人目不暇接。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13753753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753753-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-artwork-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753753-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-artwork-01-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753753" class="wp-caption-text">精美艺术品。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13753755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753755-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-artwork-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753755-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-artwork-02-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753755" class="wp-caption-text">可爱小商品。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13753760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13753760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753760-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-flower-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13753760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13753760-20220605-LinCaiFeng-Lilac-festival-flower-01-600x458.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13753760" class="wp-caption-text">琳琅满目的植物花草。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有各式各样的音乐和舞蹈。30多个团体在不同地段表演，吸引人流驻足。</p>
