<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：活泼松鼠与玩具恐龙玩耍的逗趣画面</h2>
<a href="https://d3malt9gyhsmb.cloudfront.net/P9ccC3bZu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/trdaqu3782/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>比利时一位摄影师拍摄了一组令人惊叹的系列照，展示松鼠从令人生畏的玩具恐龙口中偷走坚果的有趣画面。（Niki Colemont提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年06月08日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Louise Chambers报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">比利时一位<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>将他对<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0.md#1">松鼠</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%90%E9%BE%99.md#1">恐龙</a>的热爱结合在一个主题中，拍摄了一组令人惊叹的系列照，展示松鼠从令人生畏的玩具恐龙口中偷走坚果的有趣画面。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">现年36岁的尼基?科莱蒙（Niki Colemont）是位自学成才的<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>，在过去六年的时间里一直致力于拍摄红<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0.md#1">松鼠</a>，迄今为止在这个主题上已经花费了3,200小时。在他看来，拍到这些可爱照片的关键因素是时间、耐心和毅力。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">对于科莱蒙来说，这一切都始于他在2016年与松鼠的第一次相遇。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13754994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754994-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-4-1-1200x868.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754994-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-4-1-1200x868.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754994" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13754999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754999" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754999-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-13-1-1200x777.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754999-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-13-1-1200x777.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754999" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“当我在女友祖母家的花园里看到第一只松鼠后，我买来一个松鼠喂食器，里面放上去皮的<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83.md#1">核桃</a>，希望每天都能吸引它们来到同一棵树上玩耍。”他告诉《大纪元时报》，“我又在花园里放置了一个带有动态侦测功能的野外摄像机，两个月后终于有了成效。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他在摄像机上注意到第一只松鼠学会如何打开喂食器，五天后，他又看到两只新松鼠也被吸引过来。对此，他感到兴奋不已，于是又添置了一个便于观察的“掩体”帐篷以及一个远摄镜头，他分享说自己从此打开了一个“充满机遇的新世界”。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13754998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754998-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-10-1200x821.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754998-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-10-1200x821.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754998" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13754996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754996" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754996-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-6-1200x765.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754996-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-6-1200x765.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754996" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">之后，科莱蒙尝试为松鼠布置一些自然的布景和有趣的小道具，并将食物放在精心安排的位置，希望在不使用图像处理软件的情况下诱使它们摆出独特的姿势。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021年10月10日，他在自家附近的一片私有林地中，拍摄了他迄今为止最出色的系列作品。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他把<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83.md#1">核桃</a>放进玩具<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%90%E9%BE%99.md#1">恐龙</a>的嘴里，然后躲在三米远的地方耐心等待松鼠来取核桃。它们花了三天的时间才战胜了对这只恐龙的恐惧，陆续陆续竟有七只松鼠好奇地围着恐龙爬来爬去。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13754995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754995" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754995-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-5-1200x748.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754995-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-5-1200x748.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754995" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13754993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754993" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754993-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-2-1-1200x800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754993-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-2-1-1200x800.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754993" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>科莱蒙分享了他拍摄这些活泼好动的松鼠的更多经历，“它们抓起坚果，把它们埋在离我较远的地下。然后我再放上一个新的，它们会不断来来回回地取，我也可以整天重复这个动作。”</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这位有才华的摄影师认为松鼠是“完美的模特”，因为它们可爱、好奇、聪明、有毅力，并且能做很多人类可以做的事情。他希望他的照片能给观众带来欢乐，“即使只是一瞬”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">事实上，科莱蒙是一名卢旺达大屠杀的幸存者，小时候得以逃难至比利时，并被一对比利时夫妇收养。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13754997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754997-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-7-1200x734.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754997-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-7-1200x734.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754997" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13754992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13754992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754992-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-1-1200x1067.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13754992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/id13754992-Niki-Colemont-squirrel-dinosaur-1-1200x1067.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13754992" class="wp-caption-text">（Niki Colemont提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“小时候，我总是和恐龙一起玩，我和它们一起长大”，他说，“我有充气恐龙可以骑，还有一本恐龙百科全书。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">毋庸置疑，科莱蒙以壮观的恐龙和松鼠为主题的照片系列获得了公众的好评。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">他在<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/nikicolemont/">Instagram</a>上分享他的作品并在线销售<ahref="https://squirrelman.picfair.com/">印刷品</a>，他希望能激励其他人更多地走到户外，充分享受大自然的美景。</span></p>
