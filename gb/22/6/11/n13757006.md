<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>三百西澳人死于中共病毒</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年06月11日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者彭妮澳洲珀斯编译报导</span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">自<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>（</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）大流行开始以来，西澳死于</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的人数已达到</span></span>300<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">人，该统计结果公布后的一个夜晚，又有</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">人丧生。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，周二（</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日）<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E5%8D%AB%E7%94%9F.md#1">西澳卫生</a>厅收到的死亡病例报告中包括一名</span></span>50<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">多岁的女性、两名</span></span>80<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">多岁的男性和一名女性，以及一名</span></span>70<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">多岁的男性。重症监护病房的</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">患者已从住院的</span></span>293<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">人增加了</span></span>16<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">人。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">过去一周重症监护病房入院人数有所增加，自</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日以来一直徘徊在</span></span>15<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">人以上。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">珀斯都市区记录了</span></span>6320<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例新病例，西南地区</span></span>526<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例，中西部地区</span></span>242<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例，</span> </span>Wheatbelt<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">地区</span></span>183<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例和大南部地区</span></span>167<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">例。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">自大流行开始以来，西澳有</span></span>3.842<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万例活跃病例，并记录了</span></span>79.7041<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万例</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">感染病例。</span></span></p>
<p>78<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的西澳旅行家</span></span>James Kwan<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">于</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月从日本的钻石公主号游轮遣返后，成为首位死于这种疾病的澳洲人。</span></span></p>
