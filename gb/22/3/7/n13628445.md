<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>从游乐场偷走幼儿 珀斯两少女被起诉</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月07日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏墨竹澳洲珀斯编译报导</span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">上周日（</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>27<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日）珀斯的一位父亲在户外游乐场丢失了他</span></span>18<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个月大的女儿，所幸很快女儿被找到。两名少女被指控偷走幼儿并遭到起诉。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据“七号电视网“报导，当帕斯莫尔（</span></span>Matthew Passmore<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）的女儿奥黛丽（</span></span>Audrey<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）于中午时分在珀斯附近的</span></span>Kwinana Adventure Park<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">失踪后，一场绝望的搜寻开始了。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">令帕斯莫尔感到欣慰的是，大约</span></span>20<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">分钟后，奥黛丽回到了他身边，然而，她穿着不同的衣服。</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">其他帮助帕斯莫尔寻找女儿的当地人说，他们看到两个十几岁的女孩抱着一个小孩从公园里跑出来。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">警方称，这两个女孩从公园附近的人行道上抱走了孩子，并将孩子带到附近的一个购物中心，在那里她们买了一套新衣服，并给孩子穿上。<br />
Rockingham<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区的警探周一指控两名不能被透露姓名的</span></span>14<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁女孩。这两人周一在<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%80%E6%96%AF%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%B3%95%E5%BA%AD.md#1">珀斯儿童法庭</a>出庭，当时法庭被告知其中一名</span></span>14<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁女孩是这起绑架案的主谋。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">法庭被告知，在女童奥黛丽被带走的前一刻，那名</span></span>14<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁女孩和她的男友之间的短信显示，她说她在“偷一个婴儿”，她需要“保护它”。<br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">律师们还在努力确认这名少女去年从珀斯的一个滑板公园绑架了另一个孩子的说法。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">法庭还被告知，该女孩据称过去曾假装流产，在其社交媒体上分享别人的超声波照片。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">警方反对她的保释。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><br />
<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">这名女孩的同案被告在法庭上崩溃大哭，法庭被告知她几乎不认识这名女孩。她获准保释。两人将在下周再次出庭。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
