<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大2月通胀创30年新高</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13616833-GettyImages-1211262574-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2月份的食品零售价格年同比上升7.4%，高于1月份的6.5%。这是自2009年5月以来的最大年度增幅。(INA FASSBENDER/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月17日讯】<span class="s3">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）统计局新发布的数字显示，<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">加拿大</a>的消费者价格</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份年同比上涨</span><span class="s2">5.7%</span><span class="s3">，这是自</span><span class="s2">1991</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">8</span><span class="s3">月以来的最大涨幅。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份录得<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">加拿大</a>通胀率连续第二个月超过</span><span class="s2">5%</span><span class="s3">，</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份是</span><span class="s2">5.1%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">汽油价格的上涨领先所有其他商品，但是，食品杂货及居所的成本上升也超过了平均通胀率。统计局的报告说，住房成本的上升速度，是自</span><span class="s2">1983</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">8</span><span class="s3">月以来最快的。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">如果扣除汽油，</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份消费者价格指数</span><span class="s2">(CPI)</span><span class="s3">年同比上涨</span><span class="s2">4.7%</span><span class="s3">，也超过</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的涨幅</span><span class="s2">(+4.3%)</span><span class="s3">，这是该指数自</span><span class="s2">1999</span><span class="s3">年推出以来录得的最高值。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">按月比较的话，</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份的消费者价格指数上升了</span><span class="s2">1.0%</span><span class="s3">。全国所有省份当月的价格指数都高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">表：各省</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">、</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月消费者价格指数（加拿大统计局）</span></p>

<h2 class="p1"><span class="s3">油价高升</span> <span class="s3">食品通胀超均值</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">与</span><span class="s2">2021</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月相比，加拿大司机加油的成本增加了</span><span class="s2">32.3%</span><span class="s3">。统计局的报告说，</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s3">东欧和中东的地缘政治冲突</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s3">造成全球石油供应不确定，并导致</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份的汽油价格月环比上涨了</span><span class="s2">6.9%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">同样，随着国际能源价格上涨，燃料油和其他燃料的价格月环比上涨了</span><span class="s2">8.5%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份的食品零售价格年同比上升</span><span class="s2">7.4%</span><span class="s3">，高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的</span><span class="s2">6.5%</span><span class="s3">。这是自</span><span class="s2">2009</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s3">月以来的最大年度增幅。统计局认为，这是产地价格升高及高昂的运输成本造成的结果。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">肉类价格年同比上升</span><span class="s2">11.7%</span><span class="s3">，高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的</span><span class="s2">10.1%</span><span class="s3">。其中新鲜或冷冻牛肉升价</span><span class="s2">16.8%</span><span class="s3">，鸡肉升价</span><span class="s2">10.4%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">乳制品和鸡蛋价格年同比上涨</span><span class="s2">6.9%</span><span class="s3">，高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的</span><span class="s2">4.5%</span><span class="s3">。原因是</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">日，农场的牛奶价格因生产成本上升而加价。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">相比之下，</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份面包、面包卷和小圆面包的价格只增长了</span><span class="s2">3.7%</span><span class="s3">，低于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的</span><span class="s2">7.5%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<h2 class="p4"><span class="s4">居住成本升</span><span class="s3">6.6%</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份，居住成本年同比增长了</span><span class="s2">6.6%</span><span class="s3">，这是自</span><span class="s2">1983</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">8</span><span class="s3">月以来的最高增速。其中自有住房和租用住房的成本分别上涨了</span><span class="s2">6.2%</span><span class="s3">和</span><span class="s2">4.2%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">与新房价格相关的房主重置成本上升</span><span class="s2">13.2%</span><span class="s3">，其他自有住房的开支增加了</span><span class="s2">14.3%</span><span class="s3">（包括房地产销售佣金）。相比之下，抵押贷款的利息成本下降了</span><span class="s2">6.0%</span><span class="s3">，抵消了部分成本上升压力。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">家用电器的价格增长也超过平均水平，与</span><span class="s2">2021</span><span class="s3">年</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月相比，家用电器价格升了</span><span class="s2">7.8%</span><span class="s3">，其中烹饪用具价格升</span><span class="s2">9.4%</span><span class="s3">，冰箱和冰柜升</span><span class="s2">15.6%</span><span class="s3">，洗衣和洗碗电器升</span><span class="s2">9.1%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">此外，</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s3">月份非电动的厨房用具、餐具和炊具价格年同比上涨</span><span class="s2">10.4%</span><span class="s3">，高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s3">月份的</span><span class="s2">7.5%</span><span class="s3">。</span></p>
