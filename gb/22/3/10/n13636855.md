<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义市校外会 春晖志工团参访活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636857-544493-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>	春晖志工团大合照。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月10日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）为<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E6%99%96%E5%BF%97%E5%B7%A5.md#1">春晖志工</a>能从<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E6%AF%92.md#1">戒毒</a>者个案真实故事出发，透过沉浸式体验设计，搭配实境解谜游戏，让观展者亲身走过‘成瘾’历程，感受<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E7%98%BE%E8%80%85.md#1">戒瘾者</a>所面临的伤痛及困难，理解 ‘瘾’不是一件遥远的事情；另藉由导览相关景点，纾解志工团平日工作压力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13636858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13636858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636858-544494.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13636858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636858-544494-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13636858" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E6%99%96%E5%BF%97%E5%B7%A5.md#1">春晖志工</a>团合照。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育部嘉义市联络处暨嘉义市学生校外生活辅导会（以下简称校外会），由军训督导樊立平上校带领春晖志工团共计31人，假椬梧滞洪池及台南文化创意园区，规划1日的春晖志工团参访活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13636860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13636860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636860-544495.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13636860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636860-544495-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13636860" class="wp-caption-text">春晖志工团参访活动。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>樊立平表示，此次参访椬梧滞洪池，为纾解志工团平日工作压力，借此提升志工辅导工作能量及丰富生活视野，并藉由参访台南文化园区，所办理111年教育部“解瘾-解开毒品上瘾的真相”反毒教育特展，了解毒品上瘾的真相，以增进尔后转导春晖个案的辅导技能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13636863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13636863" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636863-544498.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13636863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636863-544498-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13636863" class="wp-caption-text">春晖志工团聆听导览解说。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>春晖志工林木明表示，在参访椬梧滞洪池时，体会到当地原本是台糖甘蔗地，因台风受海水侵蚀而荒弃，最后却在政府及地方的激荡下，做成媲美日月潭的景点，在尽情的畅游在优美的风景及丰富的生态中，身心灵都得到了洗涤；而在反毒教育特展时，理解到‘成瘾’的过程，使春晖志工们更能深入的去辅导个案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13636862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13636862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636862-544497.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13636862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636862-544497-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13636862" class="wp-caption-text">春晖志工团聆听导览解说。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>社会大众多数对‘瘾’不甚了解，郤又极其敏感，避之唯恐不及，而当我们在面对‘瘾’时，唯有去了解它认识它，才能更好了帮助需要的学生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13636861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13636861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636861-544496.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13636861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13636861-544496-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13636861" class="wp-caption-text">春晖志工团聆听导览解说。（教育部嘉义市联络处提供）</figcaption></figure>
