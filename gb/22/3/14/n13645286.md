<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳超市主要物价上涨 涨幅逾七成</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月14日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张小凡澳洲珀斯编译报导</span><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">在过去两个月里，受供应链影响的西澳两大超市的一些主要商品物价已经大幅跃升，有的涨幅甚至超过</span></span>70%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，这些商品包括软饮料、咖啡和橄榄油等。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据《西澳人报》报导，澳洲有</span></span>157<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年历史的软饮料品牌</span></span>Kirks<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">创下了一个较大的涨价幅度，</span></span>10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">罐</span></span>375<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">毫升的</span></span>lemonade<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在西澳</span></span>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">超市从</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份的略高于</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元上升到现在的</span></span>10.55<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，在</span></span>Coles<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的价格也是如此。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">根据《西澳人报》看到的内部价格数据，</span></span>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">超市的一瓶</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">升可乐价格自</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份以来已经上涨了</span></span>50%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以上，从</span></span>2.85<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元上涨到</span></span>4.35<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，而</span></span>1.5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">升健怡可乐（</span></span>Diet Coke<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）已经上涨了</span></span>12%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>3.55<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">涨幅最大的产品之一是</span></span>200<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">克装雀巢咖啡金装原味，去年</span></span>8<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的售价为</span></span>11.5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，但本月顾客将花费</span></span>20<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，上涨了</span></span>73%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。在</span></span>Coles<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，它的售价为</span></span>14<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元。</span></span></p>
<p>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">销售的</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">升装</span></span>Moro<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">特级初榨橄榄油本月上涨了</span></span>33%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>16<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，而</span></span>500<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">克装</span></span>Fountain<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">番茄酱在</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份上涨了</span></span>35%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>2.7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，</span></span>120<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">克装</span></span>Gravox Instant Gravy<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">现在是</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，比去年年底的价格增加了</span></span>42%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">特别是今年软饮料价格的大幅提升，使顾客们对超市巨头愤怒不已，他们纷纷涌入社交媒体要求解释。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">这是因为</span></span>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">集团在</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份宣布，其连锁超市的半年业绩利润下滑了</span></span>7.6%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">首席执行官</span></span>Brad Banducci<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">警告说，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E8%B4%A7%E8%86%A8%E8%83%80.md#1">通货膨胀</a>是“真实存在的”，物价预计继续呈上升趋势，他承认商品价格在前一个月增加了</span></span>2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">至</span></span>3%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">可口可乐公司的一位女发言人表示，供应链上的<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%9A%E8%B4%A7%E8%86%A8%E8%83%80.md#1">通货膨胀</a>压力已经传递给了主要零售商。</span></p>
<p>Woolworths<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">集团的一位发言人也指出供应商的通胀压力是关键问题。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">推高物价的其它因素还包括：更高的燃料成本、东海岸和西澳之间被洪水破坏的铁路线（目前已经修好）、严重的集装箱短缺和与</span></span>COVID<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">有关的港口延误、恐慌性购买、供应链工人的隔离和航空货运的减少。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲的年通胀率在</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年第四季度升至</span></span>3.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，高于前一季度的</span></span>3%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。但珀斯的通胀率上升最快，去年第四季度同比上升</span></span>5.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p>澳大利亚竞争和消费者委员会（Australian Competition and Consumer Commission，简称ACCC）表示，它正在监测这一情况，并警告企业不得对高物价的原因做出虚假或误导性的陈述。</p>
<p>“ACCC已经注意到了关于<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC.md#1">超市价格</a>上涨的报告。我们将继续监测这些报告，特别是考虑到对供应链问题的持续关注”，一位女发言人说。</p>
<p>ACCC最近批准了一项紧急临时授权，允许供应链上的公司进行合作和分享信息，以确保关键货物能够送达西澳和北领地的消费者与商家。</p>
