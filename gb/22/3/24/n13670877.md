<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南费城车祸? 两名宾州州警丧生</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月25日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; background: white;">（大纪元费城讯）<span style="color: #353535;">宾<ahref="https://github.com/mlulcb3960/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9E%E8%AD%A6.md#1">州警</a>方周一（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">3<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>21<span lang="ZH-CN">日）表示，当天凌晨</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">点</span>45<span lang="ZH-CN">分，在南费城体育场附近的</span>I-95<span lang="ZH-CN"><ahref="https://github.com/mlulcb3960/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF.md#1">高速公路</a>上发生一起<ahref="https://github.com/mlulcb3960/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E7%A5%B8.md#1">车祸</a>，两名宾州<ahref="https://github.com/mlulcb3960/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9E%E8%AD%A6.md#1">州警</a>和一名平民丧生。</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">在周一的新闻发布会上，宾州警方表示，</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">33 <span lang="ZH-CN">岁的警察马丁</span>.<span lang="ZH-CN">麦克（</span>Martin Mack<span lang="ZH-CN">）和</span> 29 <span lang="ZH-CN">岁的布兰登</span>.<span lang="ZH-CN">西斯卡（</span>Branden Sisca<span lang="ZH-CN">）当时正在帮助一名在<ahref="https://github.com/mlulcb3960/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF.md#1">高速公路</a>上行走的男子，试图将他放到警车后座，一名高速行驶的汽车撞翻了他们三人。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">两名州警被撞飞到了高速公路的对面车道上，他们在现场被宣布死亡。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">警方尚未确定那位遇难平民的身份。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">警方表示，肇事司机已被捕。她是居住在宾州蒙哥马利郡、</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">21<span lang="ZH-CN">岁的非裔女子贾亚娜·韦伯（</span>Jayana Webb<span lang="ZH-CN">）。她被控</span>18<span lang="ZH-CN">项罪名，包括三级谋杀、车辆杀人、过失杀死执法人员、过失杀人、鲁莽危害他人和酒后驾车。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">州长汤姆</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">.<span lang="ZH-CN">沃尔夫向遇难者家属表示哀悼，并说周一是“宾州悲伤的一天”。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">?</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0in; margin-bottom: .0001pt; background: white;"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">麦克于</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; color: #353535; background: white;">2014 <span lang="ZH-CN">年</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">月加入宾州警察局。西斯卡于去年</span>2<span lang="ZH-CN">月加入。两人都在巡逻队</span>K<span lang="ZH-CN">，服务于费城地区。</span></span></p>
