<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>杏鲍菇酱香入味一诀窍 5分钟脆嫩鲜甜上菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633252-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-original-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>掌握一个诀窍让杏鲍菇酱香入味，5分钟做出具有铁板烧风味、劲脆、鲜嫩、香甜的佳肴。图为酱烧奶油杏鲍菇玉米笋。（波波小姐的厨房提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>料理杏鲍菇，避免口感有点水水又不入味，个中诀窍是待其出水略为收汁后，才放入调味料。本食谱其它巧思之处，如先用大蒜奶油煸炒出香气，接着将杏鲍菇搭配玉米笋拌炒，做出具有铁板烧风味、劲脆、鲜嫩、香甜的佳肴。</p>
<p><strong>时间</strong><strong>：</strong>5分钟</p>
<p><strong>食材</strong><strong>（2人份）：</strong></p>
<p>杏鲍菇10小条<br />
玉米笋约8根<br />
大蒜3小瓣<br />
奶油1小块</p>
<p><strong>调味</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>酱油2小匙<br />
盐适量<br />
黑胡椒适量</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 将杏鲍菇切成2公分的块状，玉米笋每一根切成3～4段，蒜头切成细末。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13633255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13633255" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633255-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13633255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633255-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-1-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13633255" class="wp-caption-text">（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 起锅加入2小匙油和约2立方公分大小的奶油（也可以全用奶油），开小火让奶油充份融化后倒入蒜末，煸炒出香气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13633259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13633259" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633259-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13633259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633259-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-2-300x237.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="237" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13633259" class="wp-caption-text">菇类具有天然的香气，和奶油非常对味。图为用小火将大蒜奶油煸出香气。（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 倒入杏鲍菇及玉米笋，<span style="color: #800000;">杏鲍菇炒至变软出水，略收干水分后，由锅边倒入酱油翻炒均匀</span>，视个人口味酌量加盐调整咸淡，最后关火，洒上少许黑胡椒提味，即可盛盘享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13633262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13633262" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633262-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13633262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13633262-Creamy-King-Oyster-Mushrooms-and-Bamboo-Corn-with-Sauce-3-300x224.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13633262" class="wp-caption-text">要将杏鲍菇的鲜味发挥至极致，须等待其水分释出蒸发后，才下佐料。（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Q&amp;A</strong><strong>：</strong>（节录自《<span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://icook.tw/recipes/257990">酱烧奶油杏鲍菇玉米笋</a></span>》留言）：</p>
<p>Q：怎么判断玉米笋已经炒熟了？</p>
<p>A：玉米笋不难熟，切小段，通常以中小火炒3分钟左右就熟了，怕不熟也可以闷一下。</p>
<p>Q：要用哪一种奶油？</p>
<p>A：有盐、无盐奶油均可，调味上自行尝过斟酌用量即可。（网站专文）</p>
