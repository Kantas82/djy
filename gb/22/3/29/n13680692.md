<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：威武骏马与浪漫樱花的梦幻组合</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680990-93190077_2637343149840990_6799525645097237935_n-1200x845-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>雄壮威武的骏马在浪漫樱花的映衬下更显优雅。（Jenna Vainionp??提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年03月29日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Louise Bevan报导／张雨霏编译</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">芬兰一位从小和<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%84%BF.md#1">马儿</a>一起长大的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>喜欢拍摄雄壮威武的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%8F%E9%A9%AC.md#1">骏马</a>，但到了春季，在浪漫<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>映衬下的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%84%BF.md#1">马儿</a>更显优雅，也是这位摄影师最热衷捕捉的梦幻场景。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">24岁的珍娜</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">维尼翁佩（Jenna Vainionp??）来自芬兰，目前在<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E5%85%B8.md#1">瑞典</a>生活和工作。她于2009年开始涉足摄影。六年后，她以<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>的身份第一次参加马展，自此，马儿成为她最喜欢的摄影主题。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13680991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680991" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680991-JennaAstuto-1200x1804.png"><img class=" wp-image-13680991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680991-JennaAstuto-1200x1804.png" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680991" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“我在2019年春季开始拍摄《<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>下》（Under the Cherry Blossom）的系列照片”，珍娜告诉《大纪元时报》，“这个想法来自我从互联网上找到的灵感，主要是Instagram。第一天我们有六匹马儿模特，而且很高兴在同一个地方就捕捉到粉红色的花朵和一些独特的白色花朵。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“我喜欢拍摄马的眼睛，它们讲述了完整的故事”，她解释道，“周围的自然只是给故事增加一些效果。我最大的目标，也是希望人们能从我的照片中感受到的东西，就是展现出带有灵魂的马儿。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13680992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680992" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680992-DSC_8161-1200x1800.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680992-DSC_8161-1200x1800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680992" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13680993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680993" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680993-93195276_1271975763005010_8081872097266617954_n-1200x1500.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680993-93195276_1271975763005010_8081872097266617954_n-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680993" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">自2019年以来，珍娜已走访了四个不同地点的樱花树，共拍摄了约20张照片。她在 <ahref="https://www.instagram.com/photographerjenna/">Instagram</a>上分享了她的这些作品。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">然而，拍摄过程并没有想像的那么容易。带上这么大的一种动物，珍娜需要先获得城市或公园业主的许可。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“你需要确认附近的停车场能否停放运马车，并确保能将马匹从停车场安全地带到所选的拍摄位置”，她说，“在<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E5%85%B8.md#1">瑞典</a>，大多数樱花盛开的公园都靠近学校、工作场所或大马路，因此我总是选择在日落前或日出后这两个时间段拍摄。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13680994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680994" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680994-56994193_295456267998566_2386600631637673129_n-1200x1500.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680994-56994193_295456267998566_2386600631637673129_n-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680994" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13680995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680995" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680995-92831737_556100018219025_18551691487301438_n-1200x1500.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680995-92831737_556100018219025_18551691487301438_n-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680995" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">另一个重点围绕在如何将马儿“最好的一面”拍摄出来，珍娜说，她发现声音应用程序、马喜欢的糖果、马头长杆玩具和塑料袋在拍摄过程中是非常有用的道具。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">拍摄一张照片通常需要花费30到90分钟，编辑所拍摄的照片可能需要五分钟到几个小时不等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13680996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680996" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680996-92697889_227124148499482_5214941328836445069_n-1200x1500.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680996-92697889_227124148499482_5214941328836445069_n-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680996" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13680997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680997" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680997-94426486_106398820949093_5015191626298463241_n-1200x1499.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680997-94426486_106398820949093_5015191626298463241_n-1200x1499.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680997" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">珍娜是一名全职摄影师，同时兼职客户支持的工作，她和她家的马儿一起长大，从小就被马的优雅迷住了。她说，与马儿一起工作需要了解它们以及它们需要什么，这是她早期就培养出的一项技能。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">珍娜希望在2022年春天再次以她最喜欢的主题——马儿与盛开的樱花，拍摄出更多转瞬即逝的美丽照片。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13680998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13680998" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680998-212393790_508563893754008_9109420208144029041_n-1200x1498.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13680998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13680998-212393790_508563893754008_9109420208144029041_n-1200x1498.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13680998" class="wp-caption-text">（Jenna Vainionp??提供）</figcaption></figure>
