<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大央行基准利率升至0.5%</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13617708-bank-canada-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月03日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）</span><span class="s1">面对物价急升及未来的不确定性，<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%AE%E8%A1%8C.md#1">加拿大央行</a>周三宣布，将<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E5%87%86%E5%88%A9%E7%8E%87.md#1">基准利率</a>从</span><span class="s2">0.25%</span><span class="s1">调升至</span><span class="s2">0.5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">两年前，为应对</span><span class="s2">COVID-19</span><span class="s1">大流行给经济带来的冲击，央行将其<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E5%87%86%E5%88%A9%E7%8E%87.md#1">基准利率</a>下调至</span><span class="s2">0.25%</span><span class="s1">的的历史低位。现在，大流行还没结束，通胀率已达到过去</span><span class="s2">30</span><span class="s1">年的最高记录。这是央行自</span><span class="s2">2018</span><span class="s1">年以来首次上调政策利率。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">央行在周三发布的一份公告中表示，俄罗斯入侵乌克兰带来了新的不确定性，加上石油和其他商品的价格大幅上涨，对信心的负面影响和新的供应中断可能影响全球经济增长。</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">金融市场波动已经加剧。情况不稳定，我们正在密切关注事态发展。</span><span class="s2">”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">加拿大目前的通胀率是</span><span class="s2">5.1%</span><span class="s1">，远高于央行的目标控制范围。央行说，现在预计，近期的通胀将高于</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">月份的预期。</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">央行将利用其货币政策工具，将通胀率恢复至</span><span class="s2">2%</span><span class="s1">的目标水平。</span><span class="s2">”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">经济方面，加拿大去年第四季度的经济增长高达</span><span class="s2">6.7%</span><span class="s1">，比央行预测的更强，出口和进口均有所回升。央行说，今年</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">月份，由于</span><span class="s2">Omicron</span><span class="s1">变种肆虐，劳工市场的复苏遭遇挫折。但是，复苏已经开始，家庭支出已在增加，并且随着公共卫生限制的解除，应该会进一步加强。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">RBC</span><span class="s1">银行高级经济学家乔什</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">奈（</span><span class="s2">Josh Nye</span><span class="s1">）表示，虽然俄罗斯入侵乌克兰被认为是一个新的、重要的不确定因素，但加拿大在</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">月份因疫情恶化丢失</span><span class="s2">200,000</span><span class="s1">个工作岗位后，就业市场正在迅速反弹，而且疫情仍在改善，限制在不断放松。这些都增强了央行<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%81%AF.md#1">加息</a>的信心。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">未足以冷却房市</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%AE%E8%A1%8C.md#1">加拿大央行</a><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%81%AF.md#1">加息</a>的决定，预计不会使火热的房市降温，购房者仍能够获得廉价的抵押房贷。一些经济学家表示，需要多次加息，才能使房贷成本显着上升，利率升</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">个基点（</span><span class="s2">0.25</span><span class="s1">个百分点）远远不够。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">据《环球邮报》报导，加拿大丰业银行首席经济学家让</span><span class="s2">&#8211;</span><span class="s1">弗朗索瓦</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">佩罗（</span><span class="s2">Jean-Fran?ois Perrault</span><span class="s1">）说：</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">我认为仅</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">个基点不会对房地产市场产生太大影响。要实现这一目标（房市降温）需要一系列加息。</span><span class="s2">”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">满地可银行首席经济学家道格拉斯</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">波特（</span><span class="s2">Douglas Porter</span><span class="s1">）说，周三的加息不太可能对加拿大强劲的房市产生有意义的影响。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">自</span><span class="s2">COVID-19</span><span class="s1">大流行开始以来，加拿大的房价以创纪录的速度攀升，很多购房者被迫到房价较便宜的郊区和小城市寻找宽敞的房产。根据加拿大房地产协会（</span><span class="s2">CREA</span><span class="s1">）的房价指数，</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">月份全国房屋的平均价格上涨了</span><span class="s2">28%</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s2">836,300</span><span class="s1">元。在过去两年里，加拿大的房价上涨了</span><span class="s2">48%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">房地产公司</span><span class="s2">Royal LePage Atlantic</span><span class="s1">的经纪人凯莉</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">泰恩斯</span><span class="s2">&#8211;</span><span class="s1">哈灵顿（</span><span class="s2">Kelli Tynes-Harrington</span><span class="s1">）说，她在新斯科舍省的哈利法克斯做了近</span><span class="s2">20</span><span class="s1">年的地产经纪，当地的房产非常抢手。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">她认为，这次利率的调升不会阻止买家。她说，在病毒大流行的大部分时间里，每处房产都有多个买家竞价。在大流行开始之前，这种竞争是闻所未闻的。</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">有很多买家对房源不足感到沮丧。</span><span class="s2">”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">央行周三没说明何时会再次加息，在公告中也只是提到一次房市，称其</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">活动更加活跃，给房价上行进一步增加了压力</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">有专家认为，这次加息有可能加速买家入市。丰业银行首席经济学家佩罗说：</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">首次加息实际上可能会增加市场压力，这并不难想像，因为购房者会在未来更高利率出现前抢购。</span><span class="s2">”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">抵押贷款经纪人、</span><span class="s2">Tribe Financial Group</span><span class="s1">总裁弗朗西丝</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">伊诺霍萨（</span><span class="s2">Frances Hinojosa</span><span class="s1">）有类似看法</span><span class="s2">——</span><span class="s1">周三的加息可能会产生</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">心理冲击</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">并</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">导致买家涌入市场</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">至于央行未来会有多少次加息，行业有不同的看法。一些银行预计今年将加息</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">到</span><span class="s2">4</span><span class="s1">次，每次加息幅度为</span><span class="s2">0.25</span><span class="s1">个百分点。按加拿大丰业银行的预计，今年将加息</span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">次，然后在</span><span class="s2">2023</span><span class="s1">年再加息</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">次，使央行的基准利率提高至</span><span class="s2">2.5%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">加元升值</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">加元周三兑美元走强，升</span><span class="s2">0.84%</span><span class="s1">至每加元兑换</span><span class="s2">0.7913</span><span class="s1">美元。</span><span class="s2">WTI</span><span class="s1">原油价格飙升</span><span class="s2">7.77%</span><span class="s1">至每桶</span><span class="s2">111.45</span><span class="s1">美元，对加元上升也有推动作用。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Action Economics</span><span class="s1">的北美高级经济学家瑞安</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">布莱希特</span><span class="s2">(Ryan Brecht)</span><span class="s1">表示，加拿大央行这次加息，</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">为今年更多的加息奠定了基础</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">美国看起来也将加息。美联储主席杰罗姆</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">鲍威尔（</span><span class="s2">Jerome Powell</span><span class="s1">）周三表示，尽管俄罗斯与乌克兰的战争带来</span><span class="s2">“</span><span class="s1">不确定</span><span class="s2">”</span><span class="s1">的影响，美联储可能在本月按计划加息。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">鲍威尔表示，他支持本月加息</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">个基点。并称如果通胀没有减弱，美联储准备采取更加激进的加息行动。</span></p>
