<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>只要多1个技巧 让你吃到美味的焦糖烤布蕾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13667517-cc7a9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香草烤布蕾的酥脆焦糖搭配滑顺布蕾，让人吃了会想念。（C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>“香草<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E7%83%A4%E5%B8%83%E8%95%BE.md#1">焦糖烤布蕾</a>”是法式甜点的经典传奇，制作原料简单，入口即化的布丁与酥脆的焦糖顶盖，让人吃了会想念。制作焦糖烤布蕾的原料有蛋黄、鲜奶油、天然香草荚与砂糖等，<strong>成品完成前再使用火枪将炖蛋表面的砂糖焦糖化，这样布蕾就有焦脆口感的糖盖</strong>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>香草<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E7%83%A4%E5%B8%83%E8%95%BE.md#1">焦糖烤布蕾</a></strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>INGREDIENTS</strong></span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E5%B8%83%E8%95%BE.md#1">香草布蕾</a></strong></span></h4>
<p>鲜奶油 180g<br />
牛奶 45g<br />
香草荚 3cm<br />
鸡蛋 36g<br />
蛋黄 33g<br />
砂糖 33g</p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>其他</strong></span></h4>
<p>焦糖 适量 <span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>（推荐阅读：<a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/22/3/17/n13652304.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">熟习甜点焦糖技法 煮出浓郁香气不怕烧焦</a>）</strong></span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>HOW TO MAKE</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.锅中放入鲜奶油、牛奶、香草荚（用刀子刮出豆荚中的籽，把豆荚和籽一起放入）加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665996" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665996-9edf6747c8db8757d4a3eca3843364ce.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665996-9edf6747c8db8757d4a3eca3843364ce-450x312.png" alt="" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665996" class="wp-caption-text">锅中放入鲜奶油、牛奶、香草荚加热。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.另一个碗中放入鸡蛋、蛋黄、砂糖搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665997" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665997-ba50d692e21de04fd96f4bd0fafc2a53.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665997-ba50d692e21de04fd96f4bd0fafc2a53-450x317.png" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665997" class="wp-caption-text">碗中放入鸡蛋、蛋黄、砂糖搅拌。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.把步骤①分两次加入步骤②中搅拌均匀，再过筛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665998" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665998-4853e97069e30fa5edddee83adc01bd1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665998-4853e97069e30fa5edddee83adc01bd1-450x298.png" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665998" class="wp-caption-text">步骤①分两次加入步骤②中搅拌均匀。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13665999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665999" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665999-b78627939b5eb3a5d52b0577c48ab97b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665999-b78627939b5eb3a5d52b0577c48ab97b-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665999" class="wp-caption-text">搅拌均匀的样子。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.倒入直径12cm的圆形容器中，放入事先预热好的烤箱中，以150℃隔水加热烤20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13666000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13666000" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13666000-9d16babd772c299bdaa7335271b5c767.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13666000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13666000-9d16babd772c299bdaa7335271b5c767-450x296.png" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13666000" class="wp-caption-text">倒入直圆形容器，准备隔水加热。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.烤好后放入冰箱中冷藏降温。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13666002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13666002" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13666002-c13975829d0542840f8cfd57408395a1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13666002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13666002-c13975829d0542840f8cfd57408395a1-450x302.png" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13666002" class="wp-caption-text">烤好后放入冰箱中冷藏降温。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.在<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89%E5%B8%83%E8%95%BE.md#1">香草布蕾</a>上慢慢地均匀撒一些焦糖。以喷枪小心地烧到焦糖融化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665992" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665992-c3ba151eebf995b416d098dbc18aa299.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665992-c3ba151eebf995b416d098dbc18aa299-450x303.png" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665992" class="wp-caption-text">在香草布蕾上慢慢地均匀撒一些焦糖。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.反复多次步骤⑥的过程，呈现微焦感的琥珀色即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13665994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13665994" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665994-1d4fed3a5d378c2e79c60b87940c2d12.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13665994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13665994-1d4fed3a5d378c2e79c60b87940c2d12-450x309.png" alt="" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13665994" class="wp-caption-text">呈现微焦感的琥珀色即完成。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>TIP</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>6~7焦糖烤布蕾要搭配表面脆脆的焦糖脆片才美味，但是焦糖脆片做好后会逐渐变湿软，因此<span style="color: #800080;">建议在吃之前再制作上层焦糖即可</span>。</strong>⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664685" style="width: 122px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664685-cc568a9581635234233d6783f38ebcee.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13664685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664685-cc568a9581635234233d6783f38ebcee-450x573.jpg" alt="" width="122" b="155" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664685" class="wp-caption-text">《焦糖甜点全图鉴》书封（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
