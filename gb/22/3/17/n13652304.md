<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>熟习甜点焦糖技法 煮出浓郁香气不怕烧焦</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13656244-id13568574shutterstock--600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为西班牙的焦糖蛋奶冻。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong><ahref="https://github.com/pxryyb358/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>，不仅只有糖粒型态而已，从稀状的糖浆到浓稠的糖酱，都可以自己在家做。“<ahref="https://github.com/pxryyb358/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E9%85%B1.md#1">焦糖酱</a>”很适合跟奶油搭配；让人爱不释手的“焦糖坚果”当馅料或装饰都合拍。熟习基础焦糖技法后，就能实际运用在<ahref="https://github.com/pxryyb358/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%84%E5%BC%8F%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">各式甜点</a>的制作上。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/pxryyb358/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>?</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>INGREDIENTS</strong></span></h3>
<p>砂糖 200g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>HOW TO MAKE</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_13652463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652463-P40-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652463-P40-1-1-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652463" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13652464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652464-P40-1-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652464-P40-1-2-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652464" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 锅中先放入部分砂糖，以小火慢慢加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13652465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652465-P40-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652465-P40-2-450x319.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652465" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 边缘开始融化后，慢慢加入剩下的砂糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13652466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652466-P40-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652466-P40-3-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652466" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加热至砂糖粒子完全融化、渐渐变为褐色后，将整个锅子放入温水中，颜色便不会再加深。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13652467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652467-P40-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652467-P40-4-450x323.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652467" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 倒在烘焙垫上，放至完全冷却。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13652468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652468-P40-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652468-P40-5-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652468" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 打碎成适当大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13652469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13652469" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652469-P40-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13652469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13652469-P40-6-450x306.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13652469" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入食物调理机中，搅拌至如同砂糖粒大小，再放入密闭容器中，保管于阴凉处。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>TIP︰</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.请准备厚一点的锅子，避免砂糖容易烧焦。</p>
<p>2 .若将砂糖一次全部放入，会不容易融化，导致花费更多时间，因此务必分次慢慢倒入。</p>
<p>3.焦糖的颜色会愈煮愈深。焦糖颜色愈浅，甜度愈高，但焦糖特有的香味较弱；焦糖颜色愈深，则愈不甜，而焦糖本身苦中带甜的滋味和风味会更强烈。</p>
<p>4.冷却过程中若放置于常温中过久，糖会吸收湿气，导致表面变湿黏。⊙</p>
