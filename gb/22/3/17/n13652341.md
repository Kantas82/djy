<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>教你手作甜点 吃不腻的海盐焦糖费南雪</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664761-30f79ddd088a37bb63f7b6aeebf0812a-600x400.png" />
<h6>海盐焦糖费南雪。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>想尝试原味以外的<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%8D%97%E9%9B%AA.md#1">费南雪</a>吗？那就试试看最不会吃腻的“<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E7%9B%90.md#1">海盐</a><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%8D%97%E9%9B%AA.md#1">费南雪</a>”吧！焦糖，不仅只有糖粒型态而已，从稀状的糖浆到浓稠的酱，都可以自己在家做。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E7%9B%90.md#1">海盐</a><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>费南雪</strong><strong>?</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>INGREDIENTS</strong></span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E5%8C%96%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">焦化奶油</a></strong></span></h4>
<p>无盐奶油 90g<strong>?</strong></p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>焦糖费南雪面糊</strong></span></h4>
<p>蛋白 90g<br />
砂糖 31g<br />
蜂蜜 9g<br />
<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/22/3/17/n13652305.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">焦糖酱</a>104g （推荐文章：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/22/3/17/n13652305.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">手作焦糖酱 只要2种简单食材即可香甜出锅</a>）<br />
低筋面粉 58g<br />
杏仁粉 58g<br />
糖粉 68g<br />
无铝泡打粉 2.3g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E5%8C%96%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">焦化奶油</a>70g</p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>其他</strong></span></h4>
<p>无盐奶油 适量<br />
海盐 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>HOW TO MAKE</strong></span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>焦化奶油</strong></span></h4>
<p>1.将无盐奶油放入锅中，以小火慢慢加热，不要搅拌，直到呈现淡褐色、散发榛果香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664767" style="width: 447px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664767-9edfdd165b937e4e4807c8d8f120af8f.png"><img class=" wp-image-13664767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664767-9edfdd165b937e4e4807c8d8f120af8f-450x357.png" alt="" width="447" b="355" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664767" class="wp-caption-text">无盐奶油放锅中，以小火慢慢加热。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13664769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664769" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664769-8e8496888057921d6a4c56deaed20993.png"><img class=" wp-image-13664769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664769-8e8496888057921d6a4c56deaed20993-450x351.png" alt="" width="451" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664769" class="wp-caption-text">奶油加热至呈现淡褐色。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.关火后过筛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664770" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664770-6feaf0956fc5e73f83f66cf1c409fbf9.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664770-6feaf0956fc5e73f83f66cf1c409fbf9-450x431.png" alt="" width="450" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664770" class="wp-caption-text">过筛。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.放入冰块水中，降温至约50℃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664771-8b304ff72fb6bc2cbcc78108ec27d189.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664771-8b304ff72fb6bc2cbcc78108ec27d189-450x343.png" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664771" class="wp-caption-text">放入冰块中降温至约摄氏50度。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>焦糖费南雪面糊</strong></span></h4>
<p>4.碗中加入常温状态的蛋白和砂糖，轻轻搅拌至均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664773-2efde9edb40be99d37ddd1e178bca240.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664773-2efde9edb40be99d37ddd1e178bca240-450x344.png" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664773" class="wp-caption-text">碗中放入常温状态的蛋白和砂糖。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.加入蜂蜜和<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E9%85%B1%E6%8B%8C%E5%8C%80.md#1">焦糖酱拌匀</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664775" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664775-33303e50089e37219a3d0ef44d14b1bb.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664775-33303e50089e37219a3d0ef44d14b1bb-450x319.png" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664775" class="wp-caption-text">加入蜂蜜和<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E9%85%B1%E6%8B%8C%E5%8C%80.md#1">焦糖酱拌匀</a>。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.接着加入过筛的低筋面粉、杏仁粉、糖粉和无铝泡打粉，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664817" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664817-e6fe21769b4a12d2ba6d65815fccbfad.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664817-e6fe21769b4a12d2ba6d65815fccbfad-450x304.png" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664817" class="wp-caption-text">加入过筛低筋面粉、杏仁粉、糖粉和无铝泡打粉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13664818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664818" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664818-d85910fbc5147c2b0dc9ab72ecd6ee28.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664818-d85910fbc5147c2b0dc9ab72ecd6ee28-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664818" class="wp-caption-text">搅拌均匀。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.加入步骤③的焦化奶油搅拌均匀后，包覆保鲜膜，放入冰箱中冷藏松弛约1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664820" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664820-f5124407ab2b2b72a454d05c28fb9b67.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664820-f5124407ab2b2b72a454d05c28fb9b67-450x294.png" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664820" class="wp-caption-text">加入步骤3的焦化奶油搅拌。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13664821" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664821" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664821-a8d74db85e751b4d11e1b6ca4f3e4dc0.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664821-a8d74db85e751b4d11e1b6ca4f3e4dc0-450x302.png" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664821" class="wp-caption-text">加入步骤3的焦化奶油搅拌均匀。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将冷藏过的面糊装入挤花袋中，再挤入抹好奶油的费南雪模具中，大约挤至9分满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664836-76ec4a851c5ea389ec7e9ad9ea7517ed.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664836-76ec4a851c5ea389ec7e9ad9ea7517ed-450x335.png" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664836" class="wp-caption-text">冷藏过的面糊挤入抹好奶油的费南雪模具中，大约挤至9分满。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.均匀地撒一点海盐后，放入事先预热好的烤箱中，以200℃烤10分钟，取出后脱模即完成。<strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13664837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664837" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664837-523694d621dc3117f801b4292421ab9d.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664837-523694d621dc3117f801b4292421ab9d-450x342.png" alt="" width="450" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664837" class="wp-caption-text">均匀地撒一点海盐后，放入事先预热好的烤箱中。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>TIP</strong></span></h3>
<p>1、2 先将奶油加热至呈淡咖啡色，再过筛使用。如果喜欢浓厚的奶油香，也可以不过筛直接使用。</p>
<p>9.本书食谱使用海盐，也可依个人喜好使用其他不过咸的盐。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664685" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664685" style="width: 118px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664685-cc568a9581635234233d6783f38ebcee.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13664685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664685-cc568a9581635234233d6783f38ebcee-450x573.jpg" alt="" width="118" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664685" class="wp-caption-text">《焦糖甜点全图鉴》书封（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
