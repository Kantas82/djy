<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作焦糖酱 只要2种简单食材即可香甜出锅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13660236-2020-12-02_144533-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>自制焦糖淋上爆米花，超百搭。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>“<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E9%85%B1.md#1">焦糖酱</a>”很适合跟<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a>搭配做成风味酱，让人爱不释手的“焦糖坚果”当馅料或装饰都合拍。鲜奶油可以用牛奶或各种水果泥替代，做出不同的风味。喜欢什么样的焦糖风味呢？<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%B7%B1%E5%8A%A8%E6%89%8B%E5%81%9A.md#1">自己动手做</a>吧！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E9%85%B1.md#1">焦糖酱</a></strong><strong>?</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>INGREDIENTS</strong></span></h3>
<p>砂糖 200g<br />
鲜<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a> 345g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>HOW TO MAKE</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 锅中先放入部分砂糖，以小火慢慢加热。</p>
<p>2. 边缘开始融化后，慢慢加入剩下的砂糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664699" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664699-dcf8f05b43cb7d63cbf33e9cc302b757.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664699-dcf8f05b43cb7d63cbf33e9cc302b757-450x283.png" alt="" width="450" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664699" class="wp-caption-text">加入剩下的砂糖。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加热至砂糖粒子完全融化、变为褐色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664700" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664700-36dcb91a5ee551ea10c0df09a98c8ee7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664700-36dcb91a5ee551ea10c0df09a98c8ee7-450x354.png" alt="" width="450" b="354" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664700" class="wp-caption-text">加热至砂糖粒子完全融化。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着煮至表面开始冒小泡泡，并且冒烟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664701-6600f75c6512c6381b03dafcdb5ea68b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664701-6600f75c6512c6381b03dafcdb5ea68b-450x338.png" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664701" class="wp-caption-text">接着煮至表面开始冒小泡泡，并且冒烟。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 关火，缓缓倒入加热到微滚的鲜奶油，一边搅拌均匀即制作完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664703" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664703-5b4e8cd236fe523e727b5044671a415f.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664703-5b4e8cd236fe523e727b5044671a415f-450x333.png" alt="" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664703" class="wp-caption-text">关火，缓缓倒入加热到微滚的鲜奶油，（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13664705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664705" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664705-ea757794caa0dd920ecac631d2f61180.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664705-ea757794caa0dd920ecac631d2f61180-450x342.png" alt="" width="450" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664705" class="wp-caption-text">加入滚鲜奶油后的样子。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13664706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664706" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664706-159af168c369d5e5ab6474a0cccfda6c.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664706-159af168c369d5e5ab6474a0cccfda6c-450x322.png" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664706" class="wp-caption-text">搅拌均匀即制作完成。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放凉后放入密闭容器中冷藏保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13664707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13664707" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664707-e7ab53ae467376b3b8a8b5094da47176.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13664707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13664707-e7ab53ae467376b3b8a8b5094da47176-450x367.png" alt="" width="450" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13664707" class="wp-caption-text">放凉后放入密闭容器中冷藏保存。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>TIP</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 请准备厚一点的锅子，避免砂糖容易烧焦。大小也需要适中，避免煮滚时溢出。</p>
<p>2. 鲜奶油需要分多次加入，避免滚起来。小心被喷溅而烫伤。鲜奶油可以用牛奶或各种水果泥替代，做出不同的风味。此外，也可以在含水分的食材中加入香辛料，如香草、伯爵茶、肉桂、咖啡等。</p>
<p>1~5 砂糖和鲜奶油的比例不同，甜度和浓度会随之改变，请依需求调整分量。⊙</p>
