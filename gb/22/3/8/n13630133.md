<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悉尼暴雨成灾 逾20条主路被淹 好事围超市天花板坠落</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630140-GettyImages-1239019149-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>周二（3月8日），悉尼卡姆登（Camden）地区一辆车被洪水围困。（MUHAMMAD FAROOQ/AFP via Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年03月08日讯】（大纪元记者天睿澳洲悉尼综合报导）周二，暴雨导致悉尼各地爆发洪水，大约20多条主要公路被关闭。一些建筑的天花板被暴雨冲垮，例如悉尼<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%8C%BA.md#1">华人区</a>好事围（Hurstville）的Aldi超市。</p>
<p>在悉尼南区好事围，Westfild购物中心的Aldi超市大片天花板在周一晚坍塌，大量积水从屋顶涌入落入屋内，仿佛瀑布一般。这家超市在周二全天关闭，以进行维修。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630154" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630154-2022-03-08-19563-1C6A0BF8-C249-466C-B7A7-AE628F4D0F37.jpeg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630154-2022-03-08-19563-1C6A0BF8-C249-466C-B7A7-AE628F4D0F37-533x400.jpeg" alt="" width="533" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630154" class="wp-caption-text">天花板掉落后，悉尼好事围地区的Aldi超市周二停业整修。（天睿/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在悉尼东区的邦岱交界处（Bondi Junction），Westfield购物中心的天花板突然掉落，一些购物者表示差点被砸中。</p>
<p>一名当时正在购物的作家萨克维尔（Kerry Sackville）在推特上发文说：“我差点就被邦岱交界处的掉下来的天花板砸死。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630149-GettyImages-1239018513.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630149-GettyImages-1239018513-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630149" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼西南区卡姆登的洪水暴涨，水位甚至接近一些房屋的屋檐。（MUHAMMAD FAROOQ/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瓢泼大雨下了一天后，悉尼许多公路被淹。截止到周二下午，已经有大约20条主要公路被关闭。救援机构警告驾车出行的人不要冒险穿越洪水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630174-GettyImages-1382688294.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630174-GettyImages-1382688294-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630174" class="wp-caption-text">暴雨引发的洪水切断了乔治河（Georges River）附近的公路。（James D. Morgan/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周二下午，新州各地有59个撤离令生效，大约4万人需要立即撤离，另外还有14个撤离警报生效，大约2万人被告知为撤离做准备。新州紧急救援服务局在24小时内接到2400多个求助电话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630145-GettyImages-1382759737.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630145-GettyImages-1382759737-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630145" class="wp-caption-text">经过周一、周二两天的暴雨后，悉尼<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%BC%E4%B8%BD%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">曼丽水库</a>出现溢流。（James D. Morgan/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曼利（Manly）水库已经出现溢流，当地800个家庭面临风险。随后，悉尼北海滩（Northern Beach）收到撤离警报。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630187" style="width: 646px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630187-GettyImages-1382759727.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630187-GettyImages-1382759727-646x400.jpg" alt="" width="646" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630187" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼西南区接到撤离令后，一条通往悉尼北区方向的路上出现交通拥堵。（James D. Morgan/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曼利地区的皮特沃特路（Pittwater Road）已经被淹没，洪水甚至漫过了罗斯维尔桥（Roseville Bridge），一些桥上的汽车甚至都漂了起来，当地巴士停止运行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13630215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13630215" style="width: 606px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630215-GettyImages-1382759764-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13630215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/03/id13630215-GettyImages-1382759764-1-606x400.jpg" alt="" width="606" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13630215" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼北区北巴尔高拉（North Balgowlah）的一棵大树在狂风暴雨中倒在路边，差点砸中一旁的汽车。（James D. Morgan/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新州州长佩罗泰特（Dominic Perrottet）表示，更多撤离令会发布，<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%BC%E4%B8%BD%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">曼丽水库</a>溢流会威胁到800个家庭，大约2000人。◇</p>
