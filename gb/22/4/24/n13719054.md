<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄金海岸百万元房产销售翻倍 逾5600套</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13719063-GettyImages-683950826-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>昆州黄金海岸以100万澳元以上价格出售的房产数量在一年中几乎翻了一番，超过了5,600套。(Bradley Kanaris/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月24日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（大纪元记者陈光澳洲悉尼编译报导）最新<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房产销售</a>数据显示，昆州<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%B8.md#1">黄金海岸</a>以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元以上价格出售的房产数量在一年中几乎翻了一番，超过了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">5,600</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">据澳洲房地产网消息，一项对<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%B8.md#1">黄金海岸</a>已结算<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房产销售</a>情况的分析显示，在截至今年</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">月的一年中，共有</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">5,660</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套房产以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万元以上的价格售出，而去年同期为</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">3,378</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">在最近的销售中，当地有</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">4,336</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">200</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的价格成交，有</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">863</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">200</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的价格成交，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">268</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">400</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的价格成交，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">193</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">400</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元或以上的价格成交。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">其中</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Surfers Paradise</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">200</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元之间价格售出的房产数量最多，有</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">285</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">处房产易手。其次是</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Palm Beach</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">209</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套）、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Hope Island</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">197</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套）、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Robina</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">184</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套）和</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Mermaid Waters</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">177</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套）。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">总的来说，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Surfers Paradise</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">几乎在从</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">1,000</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的每个<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7.md#1">房价</a>区间中，销售数量都是最多的，除了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">200</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7.md#1">房价</a>区间。在这个价格区间，售房数量最多的是</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Hope Island</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，共售出</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">84</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套；而在</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">700</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">800</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的价格区间，售出房产数量最多的是</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Mermaid Beach</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，有三套。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">在这期间，黄金海岸有多达</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">23</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">套房产是以</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">1,000</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元以上的价格成交的。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Adcock Prestige</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">房地产公司的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Jason Adcock</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">表示，这些数据反映出当地的房价是如何全面上涨的。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“过去，房地产市场有点像过山车，有很多高峰和低谷，但在过去两年里，价格已经稳定下来了”，他说。“许多房产销售额达数百万澳元的区并不在海滨，但它们为买家，特别是来自悉尼和墨尔本的买家，提供了更多的物有所值的房屋。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Adcock</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">说，随着预计到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">2041</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">年，黄金海岸的人口将从目前的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">57.7</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万人增加到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万人以上，对住房的需求将会更加强烈。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">该公司的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Dan Moloney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">和他的妻子</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Essie</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">负责黄金海岸的南端地区。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Essie Moloney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">说，在过去一年中，他们所负责的地区供需严重失衡，预计未来三年这种状况仍将持续，这将导致当地房地产价格进一步上涨。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">金融机构</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Custodian</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">的房产投资专家</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">James Fitzgerald</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">表示，尽管黄金海岸的房价增长惊人，但仍有一些负担得起的地区可以投资。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“低端市场（目前低于</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">150</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元的房产）经历了稳固的增长，但没有高端市场那么迅速，这是我认为目前对购房者和投资者来说是有价值的地方”，他说。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Coomera</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Pimpama</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Nerang</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Robina</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">和</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Merrimac</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">是我的选择。这些地区正在经历大量的基础设施投资，这意味着大量的就业机会和便利设施。”</span></p>
