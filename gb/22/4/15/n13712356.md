<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悉尼房产市场下滑 卖家降低要价</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13712926-2010170620472124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>尽管悉尼卖家的售房要价比去年同期仍高出16%，但每周的综合要价却比上个月下降了1.3%，季度下降了1.4%。（简沐/大纪元）  
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月24日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">（大纪元记者陈光澳洲悉尼编译报导）随着<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">房地产市场</a>不断传来下行的消息，在过去一年半的大部分时间中经历了<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7.md#1">房价</a>大幅上涨的悉尼卖家开始降低其房产要价，回应市场，以期成功售出。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">据澳洲房地产网消息，房地产研究与咨询</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">SQM</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">研究所的最新数据显示，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">悉尼房产</a><ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%8D%E5%8D%96%E6%88%90%E4%BA%A4%E7%8E%87.md#1">拍卖成交率</a>已连续六周下降，在截至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">27</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">日的六周内，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%8D%E5%8D%96%E6%88%90%E4%BA%A4%E7%8E%87.md#1">拍卖成交率</a>从</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">61.3%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">逐步下降到了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">51.2%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">尽管悉尼卖家的售房要价比去年同期仍高出</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">16%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，但每周的综合要价却比上个月下降了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">1.3%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，季度下降了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">1.4%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">SQM</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">研究所所长</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Louis Christopher</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">告诉澳洲</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">realestate.com.au</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，虽然卖家要价的下降幅度不大，但它标志着悉尼“市场疲软”的关键时刻。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“卖家讨厌降低他们的要价”，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Christopher</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">说，“通常要经过三到四个月的价格下跌后，卖家才会降低他们的预期。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“理论上说，正如我们在数据中看到的那样，卖家会继续试图抬高他们的要价。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“然后，他们终于明白市场已经放缓，而且他们已经从不止一个渠道听到了这样的消息</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">……</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">他们最终会愿意少要价。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Christopher</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">认为，新的数据表明，悉尼市场正在“软着陆”，而不是低迷。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">他说：“我认为有一些证据表明，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7.md#1">房价</a>正在逐渐下降，但它们没有大幅下跌。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“很明显，自今年年初以来，卖家已开始接受市场现状，市场已经放缓，他们正在进行相应的调整。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“目前没有恐慌，还有相当多的买家在那里，这种疲软会达到怎样的程度，还有待观察。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">在</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">2021</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">年的最后三个月里，悉尼有几个区的房价录得巨大的上涨，人们的情绪也随之发生了变化。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">澳洲热点房地产网站</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">hotspotting.com.au</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">的报告显示，在这三个月中，</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Fairlight</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">的独立房房价上涨了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">多万澳元，从</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">286.5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元上涨到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">340</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元；</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Bellevue Hill</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">的独立房房价从</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">670</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元飙升到</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">735</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">万澳元。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">在悉尼西北的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Rouse Hill</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">、</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Box Hill</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">和</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Bella Vista</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">等区，房价的年度上涨达到了</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">31%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">至</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">40%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Lumby Hampson Dural</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">房地产公司的</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Will Hampson</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">表示，在去年获得了巨额资本收益之后，管理卖家预期变得至关重要。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">虽然</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Hills</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">地区的某些房产售价仍然高于底价，但其它房产现在却在售价方面苦苦挣扎。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">Hampson</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">说，人们对价格已变得越来越敏感。“在过去的两年里，我们已经有了如此大幅度的增长，（现在）你不能只在价格上再加</span><span lang="EN-AU" style="font-family: NSimSun;">10%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">，并希望买家会出钱（购买）。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: NSimSun;">“那些切合实际并能迎合市场的卖家正在售出房产。而那些依据去年的销售情况，在他们的拍卖中提出一个夸张的底价，或者对价格预期过高，这样的卖家可能就无法适应市场了。”他说。</span></p>
