<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>春游采梅季节限定 台东一日农游启动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705996-549201-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东县农会和池上乡农会合作，办理采梅体验一日农游活动。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月09日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）每年4、5月正是青梅成熟采收季，台东县农会、池上乡农会与阿吉旅行社合作，配合农委会推广的一日<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E6%B8%B8.md#1">农游</a>，推出新年度的台东第一场<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E5%AD%90.md#1">梅子</a>体验活动。首发团将于4月12日起跑，带领民众春游踏青，到农家<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%87%E6%A2%85.md#1">采梅</a>子、摘野菜，并实际体验亲手制作<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E9%86%8B.md#1">梅醋</a>的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13705991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705991-549196.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705991-549196-600x478.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705991" class="wp-caption-text">每年4、5月正是青梅成熟季节。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13705992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705992-549197.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705992-549197-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705992" class="wp-caption-text">民众在菜园中采摘各种养生野菜。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位日前首邀各界尝新体验一日游行程，参加的民众在池上“三代三花梅园”感受亲手从梅树上采下<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E5%AD%90.md#1">梅子</a>的新奇，并在山坡菜园中认识各种养生野菜，同时现摘现煮现吃，还现场制作梅子豆腐乳带回家，许多人大呼好玩。</p>
<p>行程中还安排用青梅DIY做<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E9%86%8B.md#1">梅醋</a>的课程，同时手作Q梅米格子茶点，并到池上乡农会自营的“豆之间”品味梅子豆花。午餐则由池上乡农会供应特制的梅丽餐盒，吃完后还可把竹制汤匙筷子整套带回家。整套梅子相关活动大受民众好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13705993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705993-549198.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705993-549198-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705993" class="wp-caption-text">现场制作梅子豆腐乳带回家。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13705994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705994-549199.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705994-549199-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705994" class="wp-caption-text">民众用青梅DIY做梅醋，每人都有成品带回家。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13705995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13705995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705995-549200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13705995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13705995-549200-600x443.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13705995" class="wp-caption-text">池上乡农会为<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E6%B8%B8.md#1">农游</a>客人特制的梅丽餐盒。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民众陈小姐说，本只想出来散散心，结果收获满满，有吃有玩有伴手礼还轻松饱览池上田野风光。苏姓教师则强调，活动物超所值，花费不到千元就能享受体验丰盛优质的梅之旅，深感主办单位和农家的用心。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县农会农游督导杨素幸表示，台东推动农游体验数年来广受喜爱，来自各地的观光客，到农家进行具知识性又有趣的农业体验，品尝朴实农民认真栽培的在地农产品，很多人甚至和农家成为好朋友，带动农家与外界互动及成长，是很有意义的事。</p>
<p>旅行社负责人阿吉强调，配合梅子产季，本期活动只到5月15日。为了提供更多游客方便，“梅丽一日游”除了周三、周五固定出团外，其他时间只要6人即可成行。阿吉说，体验价每人990元，谈不上利润，只希望能鼓励更多人走出户外，多接触大自然清新空气，且配合防疫政策，小团体也很轻松自在。报名可洽询阿吉旅行社或池上乡农会。</p>
