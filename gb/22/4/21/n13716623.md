<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美中官员就新疆人权互怼 当事人戳穿中共谎言</h2>
<a href="https://d1fejrztdgsube.cloudfront.net/X0xxYtFvx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yahntu3246/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/yahntu3246/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>

</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年04月21日讯】（大纪元记者林燕报导）<span class="s1">美中联合国（UN）官员周三（4月20日）就中国新疆的人权问题再次针锋相对，不过，中共的谎言很快被当事人揭穿。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s2">美国驻</span><span class="s3">联合国</span><span class="s2">大使</span><span class="s4">琳达</span><span class="s5">·</span><span class="s4">托马斯</span><span class="s5">&#8211;</span><span class="s4">格林菲尔德</span><span class="s6">（</span><span class="s7">Linda Thomas-Greenfield</span><span class="s6">）周三在社交媒体推特上发文说，她</span><span class="s3">刚会见了</span><span class="s2">被中共当局“不公正拘捕”的</span><span class="s3"><ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%90%BE%E5%B0%94%E6%97%8F.md#1">维吾尔族</a>医生古</span><span class="s2">丽仙</span><span class="s8">·</span><span class="s3">阿巴斯（</span><span class="s8">Gulshan Abbas</span><span class="s3">）的家人。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s9">阿巴斯是一名提前退休的医生，</span><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s10">2019</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s10">3</span><span class="s1">月被中共政府以恐怖活动相关罪名秘密判刑</span><span class="s10">20</span><span class="s1">年。她的家人直到两年后才辗转得知这一消息。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">“美国将</span><span class="s2">为阿巴斯</span><span class="s1">的安全和释放继续施压</span><span class="s8">——</span><span class="s1">并</span><span class="s2">公开</span><span class="s1">反对中国（</span><span class="s2">中共</span><span class="s1">）对维吾尔人和其他少数民族和宗教团体成员的暴行。”</span><span class="s11">格林菲尔德写道。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s2">在另一条推文中，</span><span class="s11">格林菲尔德表示，</span><span class="s2">美国</span><span class="s1">敦促</span><span class="s2">联合国的人权</span><span class="s1">高级专员</span><span class="s2">发布</span><span class="s1">关于<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%96%86%E4%BA%BA%E6%9D%83.md#1">新疆人权</a>问题的报告，该报告</span><span class="s2">已经在</span><span class="s8">2021</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s8">9</span><span class="s1">月定稿。</span></p>
<p class="p6"><span class="s1">“</span><span class="s2">让</span><span class="s1">我</span><span class="s2">说</span><span class="s1">清楚：（</span><span class="s2"><ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E6%9D%83.md#1">联合国人权</a></span><span class="s1">）高级专员对中国的任何访问都必须不受阻碍，不受约束。”</span><span class="s2">她写道。</span></p>
