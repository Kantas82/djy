<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作奶茶牛奶糖 入口甜蜜 送礼自用两相宜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716460-776e97669754739ca3e3b971e1a043b0-600x400.png" />
<h6>浓郁奶香的手工奶茶牛奶糖，让贪嘴的孩子也吃得健康。（橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>“美丽的食物可以疗愈人心。”《糖果X<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E4%BD%9C.md#1">手作</a>日常》一书的作者Even烘培老师这么说道。她因为有丰富的“煮糖”经验，深知外在环境会影响甜点生成，又因世界各国文化不同导致各国的“糖”不同，自然有不同的甜点，所以标准化的传授各种“美丽的滋味”，让初学者就可以轻易入门，为自己内心珍视的人做甜点（马卡龙、软糖、棉花糖、米果）。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">奶茶<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">牛奶糖</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>① 红茶粉a &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.2g<br />
② 糖霜（剥小块） &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;50g<br />
③ 饮用水 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;75cc<br />
④ 水麦芽 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;250g<br />
⑤ 炼奶 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;100cc<br />
⑥ 细砂糖 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..200g<br />
⑦ 红茶粉b &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;4g<br />
⑧ 无盐奶油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.50g<br />
⑨ 盐巴 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.5g<br />
⑩ 小苏打粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..1g</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716462" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716462-486d220d048fce14e18b6259daa5b2e7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716462-486d220d048fce14e18b6259daa5b2e7-450x277.png" alt="" width="450" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716462" class="wp-caption-text">左上至右上依序为细砂糖、饮用水、无盐奶油、炼奶、红茶粉a。左下至右下依序为红茶粉b、小苏打粉、糖霜、盐巴、水麦芽。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">保存方式：</span>常温密封保存1个月。</h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"> 裁切时机：</span>冷却后裁切。</h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #800080;">??前置作业</span></h4>
<p>1. 将烤箱预热至上火60度、下火60度。<br />
2. 将红茶粉a盖上保鲜膜，并放入已预热好的烤箱中保温，备用。<br />
3 .用手将糖霜剥成小块，备用。</p>
<h4><strong><span style="color: #800080;">??糖浆制作</span></strong></h4>
<p>4.准备一空锅，依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、炼奶、细砂糖、红茶粉b、无盐奶油、小苏打粉、盐巴，再开火。<span style="color: #ff6600;">先加入液态材料，再加入固态材料。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716463-868fb5e8fce13b4af156006a89032d06.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716463-868fb5e8fce13b4af156006a89032d06-450x292.png" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716463" class="wp-caption-text">准备一空锅，依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、炼奶、细砂糖、红茶粉b、无盐奶油、小苏打粉、盐巴，再开火。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.在锅中放入探针以测量温度，待温度升至119度时关火，为糖浆。<span style="color: #ff6600;">勿搅拌糖浆，以免反砂</span>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716465-2b8364224578d6aab2b8e626fe83dcd8.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716465-2b8364224578d6aab2b8e626fe83dcd8-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716465" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中放入探针以测量温度，待温度升至119度时关火，为糖浆。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.将糖浆倒入钢盆中，取桌上型电动搅拌机以中速打3分钟。<br />
7.取出保温的红茶粉a，并加入钢盆中，以中速打至均匀。<br />
8.沿着钢盆边缘加入?糖霜，使糖霜平均分布，以中速打至均匀。<span style="color: #ff6600;">分次加入糖霜，成品口感更佳。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716466-4309476b649c0c4aee56ea50f634d1db.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716466-4309476b649c0c4aee56ea50f634d1db-450x304.png" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716466" class="wp-caption-text">沿着钢盆边缘加入 1?2 糖霜，使糖霜平均 分布，以中速打至均匀。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.加入剩下的?糖霜，并以中速打至均匀，即完成奶茶糖浆。<br />
10.将不沾黏硅胶垫平放在整形盘上后，以烤盘油在整形盘、擀面棍、刮板上喷油，以防止沾黏。<br />
11.取下桌上型电动搅拌机的桨状搅拌器，并以刮板将搅拌器上的奶茶糖浆刮下。<br />
12.以无盐奶油涂抹双手及钢盆盆壁，以防止倒出时奶茶糖浆沾黏。<br />
只须涂抹奶茶糖浆倒出时与盆壁的接触面。<br />
13.以刮板将奶茶糖浆刮入整形盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716503" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716503-0106b1531dc2f579984a91c6bc4c667e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716503-0106b1531dc2f579984a91c6bc4c667e-450x303.png" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716503" class="wp-caption-text">以刮板将奶茶糖浆刮入整形盘中。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14.以刮板、擀面棍、不沾黏硅胶垫为辅助，将奶茶糖浆整平，静置凝固。</p>
<h4><span style="color: #800080;">??脱模及裁切</span></h4>
<p>15.在切糖刀表面抹上无盐奶油（或烤盘油）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716500" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716500-6d9e0c3584d7bbcc3414bec8de6bc82d.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716500-6d9e0c3584d7bbcc3414bec8de6bc82d-450x308.png" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716500" class="wp-caption-text">在切糖刀表面抹上无盐奶油（或烤盘油）。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16.将静置后的奶茶<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">牛奶糖</a>取出。<br />
17.以切糖刀将奶茶牛奶糖切成块状，即可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716492" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716492-a2fc782b079544b7602ca5f6fb7ad116.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716492-a2fc782b079544b7602ca5f6fb7ad116-450x296.png" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716492" class="wp-caption-text">以切糖刀将奶茶牛奶糖切成块状，即可享用。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">糖霜制作</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>① 饮用水 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 适量<br />
② 水麦芽 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 适量<br />
③ 细砂糖 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716493" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716493-7c312e54b18bb5d2eb3da2a66eed48df.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716493-7c312e54b18bb5d2eb3da2a66eed48df-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716493" class="wp-caption-text">由左至右依序为细砂糖、饮用水、水麦芽。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<p>1.准备一空锅，依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、细砂糖后，开火。<span style="color: #ff6600;">先加入液态材料，再加入固态材料。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716494" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716494-3e9860b9844bcab7edc29942b9eb7f41.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716494-3e9860b9844bcab7edc29942b9eb7f41-450x305.png" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716494" class="wp-caption-text">准备一空锅，依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、细砂糖后，开火。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.在锅中放入探针以测量温度，待温度升至118度时关火。<span style="color: #ff6600;">无须搅拌糖浆，以免反砂。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716495-ca5520aba859c996b791b24e86be3ea9.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716495-ca5520aba859c996b791b24e86be3ea9-450x303.png" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716495" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中放入探针以测量温度，待温度升至118度时关火。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将糖浆倒入钢盆中，以电动搅拌机以中速打约3分钟。</p>
