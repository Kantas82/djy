<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>平底锅也能爆米香 “芝麻花生米香”创意食谱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716538-b85ae31208791da486fc51e39e1d9eea-600x400.png" />
<h6>不用有爆米香机器，在家也可以做黑白相间的花生芝麻米香。（橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>咬一口<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E9%A6%99.md#1">米香</a>，在嘴里散出稻香、芝麻香和花生香，幸福感立马跃上。充满小时候味道的米香做法大多要仰赖<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%86%E7%B1%B3%E9%A6%99.md#1">爆米香</a>机器，《糖果X甜点的手作日常》一书中米香粒只要有平底锅就可以做出来，简单方便不伤脑筋，让喜欢手作的人有收纳了一个甜点食谱。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F%E7%B1%B3%E9%A6%99.md#1">芝麻花生米香</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>① 米干 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 270g<strong>（作法如下）</strong><br />
② 糙米粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;27g<br />
③ 烤熟花生 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;100g<br />
④ 烤熟芝麻 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.6g<br />
⑤ 饮用水 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..80cc<br />
⑥ 水麦芽 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;130g<br />
⑦ 细砂糖 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;130g<br />
⑧ 盐巴 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.3g</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716539-d05eaf43798caff1b5f0e30a46bf5b1c.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716539-d05eaf43798caff1b5f0e30a46bf5b1c-450x282.png" alt="" width="450" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716539" class="wp-caption-text">左至右依序为水麦芽、细砂糖、盐巴、烤熟芝麻、饮用水、米干、烤熟花生、糙米粉。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">保存方式</span>：常温密封保存14天。</h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">裁切时机</span>：冷却后裁切。</h3>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #800080;">?? 前置作业</span></h4>
<p>1.将烤箱预热至上火100度、下火100度。</p>
<p>2.取一容器，加入米干，烤熟花生、烤熟芝麻、糙米粉后，放入已预热好的烤箱中保温，备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716542-1930cf33254222a37c240e5118714f5b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716542-1930cf33254222a37c240e5118714f5b-450x294.png" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716542" class="wp-caption-text">取一容器，加入米干，烤熟花生、烤 熟芝麻、糙米粉后，放入已预热好的 烤箱中保温，备用。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #800080;">?? <ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F%E7%B1%B3%E9%A6%99.md#1">芝麻花生米香</a>糊制作</span></h4>
<p>3.准备一空锅，并依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、细砂糖、盐巴，再开火。<span style="color: #ff6600;">先加入液态材料，再加入固态材料。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716543-0c9165a613c13bb833725b91fdc9a63e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716543-0c9165a613c13bb833725b91fdc9a63e-450x291.png" alt="" width="450" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716543" class="wp-caption-text">准备一空锅，并依序倒入饮用水、水麦芽、细砂糖、盐巴，再开火。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.在锅中放入探针以测量温度，待温度升至113度时关火。<span style="color: #ff6600;">温度越高，<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E9%A6%99.md#1">米香</a>成品口感越硬。</span></p>
<p>5.取出保温的米干、烤熟花生、烤熟芝麻、糙米粉，并加入锅中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716544" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716544-d4dee92176a01f6bd0dd617c7940381b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716544-d4dee92176a01f6bd0dd617c7940381b-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716544" class="wp-caption-text">取出保温的米干、烤熟花生、烤熟芝麻、糙米粉，并加入锅中。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.以刮刀切拌芝麻花生米香糊后，将边缘的米香往中间刮，以加强拌匀。</p>
<p>7.将耐热塑胶袋平放在整形盘上后，以烤盘油在耐热塑胶袋、刮刀、刮板、双手上喷油，以防止沾黏。</p>
<p>8.以刮刀为辅助，将锅中的芝麻花生米香糊刮入整形盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716545-3ecd6f25367a81451f0e544fc864d40b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716545-3ecd6f25367a81451f0e544fc864d40b-450x288.png" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716545" class="wp-caption-text">以刮刀为辅助，将锅中的芝麻花生米香糊刮入整形盘中。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.以刮板为辅助，整糖，将芝麻花生米香糊推开至整形盘四角。</p>
<p>10.将刮板平放在芝麻花生米香糊上，并用双手按压，以整平表面后，静置冷却，即完成芝麻花生米香制作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716546" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716546-4f196ae42a1496f503a1cd77f8f9430a.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716546-4f196ae42a1496f503a1cd77f8f9430a-450x288.png" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716546" class="wp-caption-text">将刮板平放在芝麻花生米香糊上，并 用双手按压，以整平表面后，静置冷却，即完成芝麻花生米香制作。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4><span style="color: #800080;">?? 脱模及裁切</span></h4>
<p>11.在切糖刀表面抹上无盐奶油（烤盘油）后，以烤盘油在砧板、双手上喷油，以防止芝麻花生米香沾黏。</p>
<p>12.将芝麻花生米香取出。</p>
<p>13.以切糖刀将芝麻花生米香切成块状，即可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716547-eac9c39fc27c8a49212ee502b510e5b2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716547-eac9c39fc27c8a49212ee502b510e5b2-450x298.png" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716547" class="wp-caption-text">以切糖刀将芝麻花生米香切成块状，即可享用。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">米干制作</span></h2>
<p><strong>米干请于三日内用完，以防止硬化。</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>① 食用油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.适量<br />
② 米&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716517" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716517-5afbda812bea066773fce526c93b4a26.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716517-5afbda812bea066773fce526c93b4a26-450x296.png" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716517" class="wp-caption-text">由左至右依序为米、食用油。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<p>1.准备一空锅，倒入食用油后，开火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13716518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716518" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716518-c9d3cc66795511d3d0ffe2906e001405.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716518-c9d3cc66795511d3d0ffe2906e001405-450x305.png" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716518" class="wp-caption-text">准备一空锅，倒入食用油后，开火。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将油加热至放入筷子时出现细小泡沫即可。<span style="color: #ff6600;">加热至放入米粒后浮起即可。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716519" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716519-71b8b537161c1735ce0d1455d0421d7e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716519-71b8b537161c1735ce0d1455d0421d7e-450x300.png" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716519" class="wp-caption-text">将油加热至放入筷子时出现细小泡沫即可。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.以滤网将少许米放入锅中后，持续画圈以散开米粒。<span style="color: #ff6600;">米、滤网须干燥无水分，以防止油爆。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13716521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716521-8d43793994a1ddec66b2286820fa3aa1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716521-8d43793994a1ddec66b2286820fa3aa1-450x296.png" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716521" class="wp-caption-text">以滤网将少许米放入锅中后。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13716522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13716522" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716522-16322b5f7d85d2d5178360c604a87ca4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13716522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13716522-16322b5f7d85d2d5178360c604a87ca4-450x294.png" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13716522" class="wp-caption-text">持续画圈以散开米粒。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
