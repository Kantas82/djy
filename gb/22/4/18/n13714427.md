<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>3式简易瑜伽练腰大肌 消除腰酸背痛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714428-0349798ea8184c73c26f0c24a7a8725a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>做几个简单瑜伽动作，即可强化你的腰大肌，缓解酸痛。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>很多人觉得，要放松<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E5%A4%A7%E8%82%8C.md#1">腰大肌</a>太难了。其实不难。</p>
<p>你可能会问，<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E5%A4%A7%E8%82%8C.md#1">腰大肌</a>是什么？这么说吧，就算给你一张最好的办公椅，老实讲，坐太久腰大肌也会受伤的。</p>
<p>这组肌群位于人体的深层，是极其重要、极其复杂的一组肌肉。每个人都有两条腰大肌，左右两侧各一条。腰大肌的头端附着在第12节胸椎及整个腰椎上，中段与骨盆内缘的髂肌相连，一起往下延伸，末端连在股骨上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13714654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13714654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714654-970d7211cd0ee6bc80b51d518816ff86.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13714654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714654-970d7211cd0ee6bc80b51d518816ff86-600x456.png" alt="" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13714654" class="wp-caption-text">腰大肌的构造（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这组核心肌群若太衰弱或扭伤，就会造成背痛、颈痛与许多其它问题（包括肠胃不舒服）。还有人认为，腰大肌跟精神、情绪健康有直接关系。</p>
<p>练这几式<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9C%E4%BC%BD.md#1">瑜伽</a>的好处是，当你站着、坐着、走路，或者做其它动作时，会比较自如，不容易造成疼痛。强化腰大肌还能有效缓解运动导致的各种疼痛与不适，有助于提升你的运动比赛成绩。</p>
<p>试试以下三个<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9C%E4%BC%BD.md#1">瑜伽</a>动作，每天练习，当你的腰大肌变强后，或许你的背痛或身体其它不适就会减轻甚至消失，全身上下都变舒服了。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1. <ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF%E5%BC%8F.md#1">婴儿式</a></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13714435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13714435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714435-237d0966d7329906f0ef40eefc9c25b9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13714435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714435-237d0966d7329906f0ef40eefc9c25b9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13714435" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF%E5%BC%8F.md#1">婴儿式</a>两种变化（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>◎ 跪坐，上半身向前弯，腹部贴近大腿，前额着地。肩膀、下巴、眼睛放松。</p>
<p>◎ 手臂姿势有两种变化：可将手臂往前伸，掌心朝下；也可将手臂往后伸，掌心朝上。选你觉得舒服的姿势来做。</p>
<p>◎ 稳定吸气、吐气，想维持多久都可以。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2. 仰卧膝碰胸式</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13714436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13714436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714436-e67a2a8ba0984387e7ef279e0938883d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13714436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714436-e67a2a8ba0984387e7ef279e0938883d-600x263.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13714436" class="wp-caption-text">膝碰胸式（Apanasana yoga pose）(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>◎ 抬腿时，骨盆不要弯曲。</p>
<p>◎ 也可以一次只抬一条腿，两条腿轮流做。</p>
<p>◎ 呼吸、放松。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3. <ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%BD%E5%BC%8F.md#1">鸽式</a></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13714438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13714438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714438-7e8e1e1a97b1471a8381514cbc012cad.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13714438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714438-7e8e1e1a97b1471a8381514cbc012cad-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13714438" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%BD%E5%BC%8F.md#1">鸽式</a>（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>◎ 髋部转动时要注意，若感觉太紧，就放松一点。别忘了呼吸。</p>
<p>◎ 若你觉得鸽式太简单，可以练习高阶鸽式。同样， 呼吸、放松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13714439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13714439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714439-0a773ea8b35f60f53ff41cd3f7664b00.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13714439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13714439-0a773ea8b35f60f53ff41cd3f7664b00-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13714439" class="wp-caption-text">高阶鸽式（GingerKitten/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
