<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>侨委会90周年庆典揭幕 “跨乐”作序曲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708807-157383-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>跨乐音乐协会四周年音乐会结束，音乐家谢幕并与所有贵宾合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年04月11日讯】（大纪元记者黄小堂<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）为庆祝中华民国<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%8A%A1%E5%A7%94%E5%91%98%E4%BC%9A.md#1">侨务委员会</a>成立90周年，侨委会、纽约侨教中心9日携手“跨乐音乐协会”举办“侨委会九十周年‘光辉九十 侨见未来’暨跨乐音乐协会四周年<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">音乐会</a>”。活动嘉宾云集，无不仔细聆听“跨乐”音乐家带来的音乐飨宴，一饱耳福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13708810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708810-157387.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708810-157387-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708810" class="wp-caption-text">跨乐音乐协会四周年<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">音乐会</a>结束，音乐家谢幕并与所有贵宾合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13708809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708809-157386.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708809-157386-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708809" class="wp-caption-text">音乐会结束后，嘉宾在法拉盛文艺中心展厅合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9日晚间的音乐会是庆祝侨委会成立90周年系列活动的首场，侨委会委员长童振源也特地录制影片祝贺，<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>经文处处长李光章，纽约侨教中心主任陈永丰、市议员黄敏仪、皇后区区长办公室文化事务和旅游部主管鲍尔曼（Phil Ballman），以及众多社会精英及纽约知名音乐家皆出席音乐会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13708812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708812-157389.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708812-157389-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708812" class="wp-caption-text">侨委会委员长童振源特地录制影片祝贺“侨委会九十周年‘光辉九十 侨见未来’暨跨乐音乐协会四周年音乐会”。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侨教中心主任陈永丰介绍，侨委会成立于1932年4月16日，过去90年一直与海外侨胞合作，推广台湾文化，同时积极结合侨界力量服务各地侨胞和侨界。</p>
<p>“音乐会是要现场听才能感受到那种活力和感动感觉。”跨乐音乐协会总监杨岚茵表示，当晚音乐会荣幸邀请到著名的中提琴家Samuel Rhodes教授，钢琴家David Witten教授、台裔小提琴家辛明峰（Ming Feng Hsin）教授，以及多位杰出台裔亚裔青年音乐家，为听众带来音乐飨宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13708808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708808-157385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708808-157385-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708808" class="wp-caption-text">跨乐音乐协会四周年音乐会9日晚间在法拉盛文艺中心演出。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13708806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708806-157381.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708806-157381-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708806" class="wp-caption-text">音乐家演奏。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13708805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708805-157382.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708805-157382-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708805" class="wp-caption-text">音乐家演奏。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>跨乐音乐协会（Muse Connect）董事长黄百龄介绍，此次音乐会很荣幸与侨委会一同举办活动、庆祝侨委会成立90周年，现场二百一十多个座位几乎坐满。当晚市议员黄敏仪也特地颁发褒奖给黄百龄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13708811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13708811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708811-157388.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13708811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13708811-157388-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13708811" class="wp-caption-text">市议员黄敏仪（右）颁发褒奖给跨乐音乐协会董事长黄百龄（左）。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
