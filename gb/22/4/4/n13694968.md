<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林县美术协会会员联展 首次下乡土库展出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694970-548372-600x400.jpg" />
<h6> 云林县美术协会理事长黄新弼（右三）总干事包怡倩(中)和与会来宾合影。（云林美术学会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%9C%AF%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美术协会</a>即日起至4月30日在<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%BA%93.md#1">土库</a>镇中山堂二楼举办<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%9A%E5%91%98%E8%81%94%E5%B1%95.md#1">会员联展</a>，3日举行开幕式，虽然下着细雨依然贵宾云集，来自县内各书画协会理事长及艺文界人士近百人参加。共展出学员近50幅精心之作，理事长黄新弼感谢各界送来之花篮及祝贺墨宝，更感谢冒雨前来的贵宾及<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%BA%93.md#1">土库</a>镇公所提供优雅之展出场所。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694977-548388.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694977-548388-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694977" class="wp-caption-text">各界送来之祝贺墨宝。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13694972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694972-548381.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694972-548381-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694972" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>县<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%9C%AF%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美术协会</a><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%9A%E5%91%98%E8%81%94%E5%B1%95.md#1">会员联展</a>开幕式，来自县内各书画协会理事长及艺文界人士近百人参加。（云林县美术协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与会贵宾包括县府文观处副处长苏建苍、土库镇公所机要秘书吴明哲、资深美术老师陈诚等多人分别致词，对云林县美术协会创立至今30余年，一直秉持着艺术生活化的目标，耕耘云林艺文界表示肯定，今年特别选在成立不久之土库镇中山堂二楼艺文展馆举办会员联展，别具意义，宽广之场地加上琳琅满目近50幅之作品，包括山水花鸟、书法、人物、还有雕塑，精湛的作品，让返乡省亲之游子或在地爱好艺文之人士，在疫情不断升温的时刻，大家的身心灵都能够得到舒缓，同时提升在地的艺术气息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694973" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694973-548382.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13694973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694973-548382-600x927.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="697" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694973" class="wp-caption-text">云林县美术协会理事长的作品~天香。（云林县美术协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13694974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694974-548383.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694974-548383-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694974" class="wp-caption-text">展出作品缩图！（云林县美术协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13694971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694971-548373.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694971-548373-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694971" class="wp-caption-text">资深美术老师陈诚（中）对于这次联展能在土库镇举办，表示特别的感动！（云林县美术协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>资深美术老师陈诚表示，土库镇的艺术水准很高，也出了不少人才，但这些人才都在外地发展成名，却从未在土库镇举办过个展，一方面是没有展览的场地，这次云林县美术协会能在土库举办联展，而且能有这么好的场地，是件非常让人感动的事！</p>
<p>云林县美术协会总干事包怡倩表示，这次会员联展有别以往，是第一次走出文化中心而选择在乡镇的展出，而且以在文化中心展出的高规格来举行，展出的作品水准都很高，目的是希望艺术能走入社区贴近民众生活，让云林未来能成为艺术之都！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694978" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694978-548389.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694978-548389-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694978" class="wp-caption-text">这次展出的作品水准相当高，吸引很多人观赏。（云林县美术协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13694976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694976-548386.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694976-548386-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694976" class="wp-caption-text">观众专注观赏作品。（云林美术学会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13694975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694975-548384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694975-548384-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694975" class="wp-caption-text">展出作品缩图！（云林县美术学会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云林县美术协会理事长全体理监事会员诚挚邀请乡亲莅临参观，该会邱垂智荣誉理事长的大作{林声系列-秋林}当场就以5位数之价码被慧眼人收藏！百闻不如一见，欢迎乡亲利用假期走一趟土库，欣赏艺文展品、尝附近有名鸭肉面及救国团精神领袖骆云从医师之故居，会不虚此行。</p>
<p>展期至4/30，开放时间：上午9：00~12：00，下午2：00~5：00，周一休馆，4/2、4/4、4/5休馆。</p>
