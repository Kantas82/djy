<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市府携手嘉义市家长协会  资助弱势学生</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695033-548399-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>大合照。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E9%95%BF%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">家长协会</a>理事长陈国庆在林森国小荣誉会长赖再兴的牵线下，成功引进高雄爱心书桌团的资源，在4日儿童节当日捐赠嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B1%E5%8A%BF.md#1">弱势</a>学生144张爱心桌椅。</p>
<figure id="13695035" aria-describedby="caption-13695035" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695035-548401-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695035-548401-450x300.jpeg" alt="合照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13695035" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在教育处长林立生及协会理事长陈国庆号召下，一大早40多位热心的<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E9%95%BF%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">家长协会</a>伙伴、志工，大家同心协力地一起将桌椅的搬进港坪国小风雨球场，要送给孩子们一个最难忘的儿童节礼物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13695034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695034-548400.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695034-548400-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695034" class="wp-caption-text">合照。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市家长协会一向为嘉义市政府教育政策合作与执行者，陈国庆有感于市长“嘉义市超越嘉义市”勇于挑战自我的核心思想，时时求好心切、时时保持进步，所以上任以来即四处寻求资源充实嘉义市教育现场。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_13695038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695038-548404.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695038-548404-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695038" class="wp-caption-text">家长协会及志工伙伴合力搬运桌椅。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林立生表示，非常感谢嘉义市家长协会，一路走来始终为学生营造更优质的读书环境，创造更好的学习效益而努力，如同黄敏惠市长所言：苦不能苦孩子；孩子是我们的未来，只要对学习有所助益，嘉义市政府随时携手家长协会共同努力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13695037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695037" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695037-548403.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695037-548403-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695037" class="wp-caption-text">家长协会及志工伙伴合力搬运桌椅。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈国庆说，社会经济态势不断改变，家庭收入正随着M型化发展，大部分家庭固然丰衣足食，但仍有少数<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B1%E5%8A%BF.md#1">弱势</a>家庭需要投入社会资源改善其就学及学习环境，希望这次爱心书桌捐赠会引起抛砖引玉的效益，让嘉义市弱势学生除了嘉义市的补助外，更有源源不断的爱心暖流照护。感谢市长黄敏惠尽心打造全台湾最幸福的城市，而改善基层的教育，成就更美的未来，由我们大家一同做起，打造更优的教育环境，更幸福的未来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13695039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695039" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695039-548405.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695039-548405-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695039" class="wp-caption-text">家长协会及志工伙伴合力搬运桌椅。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13695036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695036" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695036-548402.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695036-548402-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695036" class="wp-caption-text">家长协会及志工伙伴合力搬运桌椅。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13695038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13695038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695038-548404.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13695038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13695038-548404-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13695038" class="wp-caption-text">家长协会及志工伙伴合力搬运桌椅。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
