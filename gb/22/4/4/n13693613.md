<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BTS二度与葛莱美奖项擦肩 JIN负伤仍卖力演出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694197-220403213723100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）出席第64届葛莱美奖红毯照。(ANGELA  WEISS/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）第64届<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">葛莱美奖</a>（The Recording Academy）颁奖典礼今日（美国当地时间3日）于美国拉斯维加斯的美高梅大花园体育馆（MGM Grand Garden Arena）举行，韩国人气男团<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>（BTS）在典礼中带来了《Butter》演出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>以去年5月推出的英文数位单曲《Butter》入围“Best Pop Duo / Group Performance（最佳流行团体／组合）”奖。虽然最终该奖由蜜桃猫朵佳（DojaCat）与SZA的《Kiss Me More》拿下，但防弹少年团仍在本次<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">葛莱美奖</a>颁奖典礼中带来了特别准备的演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694335-220403213750100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694335" title="BTS perform onstage during the 64th Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694335-220403213750100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS perform onstage during the 64th Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694335" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）于第64届葛莱美奖演出照。(Matt Winkelmeyer/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在典礼开始前的红毯访谈时间，在被问及“获得葛莱美奖对你们及你们的粉丝有何种意义？”时，防弹少年团队长RM（金南俊）表示，葛莱美奖是一个由音乐界投票选出的奖，别具意义，而他们（因为中共病毒疫情）历经了艰辛的两年，若能获得葛莱美奖，会像是辛苦获得回报。</p>
<p>去年11月，防弹少年团以英文歌曲《Dynamite》首度入围“Best Pop Duo / Group Performance（最佳流行团体／组合）”奖，他们是第一个入围葛莱美奖的韩国歌手。今年以《Butter》入围的他们是连续两年入围该奖，连续三年于葛莱美奖带来演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694364-210315004527100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694364" title="BTS perform during 63rd Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694364-210315004527100707-600x338.jpg" alt="BTS perform during 63rd Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694364" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）于第63届葛莱美奖演出照。(Theo Wargo/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团今年以黑色西装造型带来了《Butter》的特别舞台演出。演出由在控制中心的场景下指令的JIN（金硕珍）开始，接着转换到乘着高空绳索自空中登场的柾国，再到j-hope、JIMIN、 RM、SUGA及V在台下的个人镜头，最终成员们齐聚主舞台带来了《Butter》演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694313" style="width: 407px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694313-220403213746100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694313" title="Jungkook of BTS" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694313-220403213746100707.jpg" alt="Jungkook of BTS" width="407" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694313" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）成员田柾国（Jungkook）于第64届葛莱美奖演出照。(Rich Fury/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管JIN（金硕珍）上月18日因左手食指受伤而接受肌腱缝合手术、j-hope（郑号锡）及柾国都于3月底确诊染疫，七位成员们仍齐聚于葛莱美奖并全力带来演出，特别是手指负伤的JIN在不宜剧烈运动、带着护具的情况下仍努力完成演出，令全球粉丝感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13694332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13694332" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694332-220403213753100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13694332" title="BTS perform onstage during the 64th Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/04/id13694332-220403213753100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS perform onstage during the 64th Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13694332" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）于第64届葛莱美奖演出照。中央为手指负伤仍带着护具演出的JIN（金硕珍）。(Rich Fury/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团的官方SNS也于本日公开了成员们在葛莱美奖的合照，并写下“We always love you, ?ARMY ?”为这天留念。（<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://twitter.com/bts_bighit/status/1510823560838717440" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点看</a></span>）</p>
<p>另外，防弹少年团还将于4月8日、9日、15日、16日在当地举行“BTS PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE－LAS VEGAS”演唱会。</p>
