<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清爽版三杯料理 这道蔬食媲美肉食 美味又饱足</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773821-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>减油清爽版的三杯杏鲍菇食谱，美味不逊于热炒店料理，有助于摄取优质蛋白质、丰富的膳食纤维、维生素和矿物质。（波波小姐的厨房提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>三杯料理是无敌下饭菜，但油脂含量常令人却步。清爽版三杯料理，采用有肉感的杏鲍菇，搭配豆腐，麻油以1大匙代替1杯的传统作法，Q弹鲜香、咸甜微辣的口感不输热炒店料理，还能摄取优质蛋白质、<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%86%B3%E9%A3%9F%E7%BA%A4%E7%BB%B4.md#1">膳食纤维</a>和维生素。</p>
<p><strong>时间</strong><strong>：</strong>10分钟</p>
<p><strong>食材</strong><strong>（</strong><strong>2</strong><strong>人份）：</strong></p>
<p>鸡蛋豆腐1盒<br />
杏鲍菇3条<br />
蒜头6瓣<br />
姜5～6片<br />
九层塔1小碗<br />
辣椒（配色用可略）5片<br />
麻油1大匙（3小匙）</p>
<p><strong>调味</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>三杯酱汁：<br />
酱油2大匙<br />
米酒2大匙<br />
糖1大匙<br />
醋1小匙</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 把蒜头拍扁，姜片、辣椒片跟九层塔洗净，杏鲍菇切成粗条，豆腐切成厚片状；酱汁调匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773825" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773825-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13773825" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773825-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-1-300x222.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773825" class="wp-caption-text">蒜头、姜片、九层塔是本食谱香气的关键材料。（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 先下1小匙麻油，将豆腐煎至双面金黄后，移出锅子备用。（或是锅子够大可以移到旁边即可）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773827" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773827" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773827-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13773827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773827-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-2-300x224.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773827" class="wp-caption-text">（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再下2小匙麻油，先煸香姜片至周围有干缩的感觉，然后下蒜头爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773828" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773828-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13773828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773828-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-3-300x224.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773828" class="wp-caption-text">把姜片爆到干缩的样子，以便炒出香气。（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 加入杏鲍菇翻炒到稍微熟软，倒入豆腐，然后均匀淋上酱汁煮滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773829" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773829-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13773829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773829-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-4-300x221.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="221" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773829" class="wp-caption-text">翻炒杏鲍菇时，先逼出内含的水分，炒出菇类的香气后，再倒入豆腐，然后才淋上酱汁煮滚，口感才会香甜、Q弹。（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 中火让酱汁收干一些，起锅前，加入九层塔及辣椒，拌炒一下即完成，盛盘享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13773831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13773831" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773831-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13773831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13773831-3-cups-tofu-king-oyster-mushrooms-5-300x222.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13773831" class="wp-caption-text">（波波小姐的厨房提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Q&amp;A</strong>：（节录自《<ahref="https://icook.tw/recipes/195854"><span style="color: #0000ff;">三杯豆腐杏鲍菇 减油清爽版</span></a>》留言）：</p>
<p>Q: 加入杏鲍菇翻炒是不是需要点技巧，很怕把豆腐弄碎了？</p>
<p>A: 步骤有说明，豆腐煎好了先移出，之后杏鲍菇炒软了，再加入豆腐，调味汁均匀淋上，不会有需要翻炒豆腐的情况。</p>
<p>Q: 可以不加醋吗？</p>
<p>A: 可以的，加醋是提升鲜味。</p>
<p>Q: 用酱油，还酱油膏呢？</p>
<p>A: 食谱是用酱油，也可以用酱油膏，糖改1小匙即可。</p>
<p>Q: 醋是白醋，还是乌醋呢？</p>
<p>A: 高粱酒醋或白醋比较合适。</p>
<p>Q: 一定要加米酒吗？</p>
<p>A: 三杯调味若没有米酒香气，会感觉少一味。米酒是提鲜、去腥、增加香气的好物。</p>
<p>Q: 可以使用板豆腐代替吗？</p>
<p>A: 当然没问题。（网站专文）</p>
