<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>菁市集品牌的优质商品 展现苗栗软实力</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779946-564637-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>青年店家齐聚共创特色行销平台。（许享富 ／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏苗栗报导）（111)年度记者会以“菁漾苗栗”为主题，由县长徐耀昌揭开<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%81%E5%B8%82.md#1">菁市</a>集品牌序幕，宣布推岀三场次菁市集活动，并带领青年朋友一同巡回全台各地。</p>
<p>本次实体市集布展承袭以往木箱、白伞营造出的独特清新气息，并依“漾漾青栗亮（苗栗尚顺育乐世界）”、“轻轻慢森活（台中草悟道）”及“恋恋好时光（新竹远东巨城购物中心）”营造各场次主题活动氛围，属于<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%81%E5%B8%82.md#1">菁市</a>集系列活动的独有风格，必能让游客们愉悦地享受不同氛围下的感官体验，吸?漾漾好物购！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779947-564638-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779947-564638-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779947" class="wp-caption-text">菁市集职人精神的优美产品 一。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“菁市集”自108年推出以来好评不断，由县府团队用心经营的特色青年品牌市集，不仅是青年店家的年度亮点，更是地方民众引颈期盼的盛事，藉由青年店家齐聚，成功为苗栗青年共创极富特色的行销推广平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779948-564639-600x459.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779948-564639-600x459.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="459" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779948" class="wp-caption-text">墨湘咖啡以创新LOGO结合地方特色行销。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>劳青处创办的青年品牌市集，今年即将迈入第四个年头，为跟随时下电商网购主流消费型态，并鼓励及辅导“菁”市集品牌相关商品转型投入电商及增加销售通路，111年度县府首创线上市集活动，特与Yahoo奇摩超级商城合作，预计招募15家摊商业者上架电商平台，展售苗栗青创、青农好物商品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779949-564640-600x417.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779949-564640-600x417.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779949" class="wp-caption-text">泥研制所创意作品。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>记者会现场以“菁元素”为背景呈现，并推出市集专属10台特色摊车搭配本次活动主轴展示苗栗在地优秀青年业者自营品牌，丰富多元的意趣，展现今年度菁市集品牌的职人精神，为本次菁市集活动拉开序幕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779950-564641-600x453.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779950-564641-600x453.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779950" class="wp-caption-text">高品质纯净天然蜂蜜产品 。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，三场实体市集活动现场将与青年摊商合作推出体验型活动，同时邀请劳动部劳动力发展署桃竹苗分署贾桃乐学习主题馆共襄盛举，让市集活动内容更加丰富并具吸引力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779951-564642-600x420.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779951-564642-600x420.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779951" class="wp-caption-text">铜锣好响 菊花系列产品 。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长徐耀昌表示，县府一直以来着重厚植青创场域并扩大行销青年商品，近年来苗栗青年逐渐洄流、投入地方产业发展，除落实县府推动青年政策的目的，也让县府与青年共同引领的苗栗青年“菁”品牌走向全国。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779952-564643-600x402.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779952-564643-600x402.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779952" class="wp-caption-text">可爱创意的徐圈圈插画。 （许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>劳青处从108年起推出的青年品牌市集，期望透过职人间相互共学分享，以创新思维在各自领域酝酿茁壮，开拓更多可能性的同时，也盼能与青年朋友一同努力，为苗栗产业未来带来崭新的面貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779953-564644-600x382.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779953-564644-600x382.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779953" class="wp-caption-text">图8 皇手作客制化精美永保固维修的唯一手工木笔。 （许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13779954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779954-564645-600x409.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13779954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779954-564645-600x409.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779954" class="wp-caption-text">LAIKA 以天然香味为活力泉源，雅致的产品。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市集活动特订于周末假日举办，现场有艺文团队展演、DIY手作体验，以及指定任务打卡送现金抵用券、满百送百等活动，内容丰富多元，期盼每场次都能带给所有大朋友、小朋友精彩的体验。7月起将紧锣密鼓的启动三场次市集活动，接下来每场次详细活动内容，将陆续发布于“苗栗好青年-劳工及青年服务赞”粉丝专页喔！（https://www.facebook.com/Youthinmiaoli/）</p>
