<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>牛仔节手记：4大人气地标</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779626-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-05-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>牛仔公园里的空中缆车。（林采枫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月13日讯】（记者林采枫报导）人，积成山，人，汇成海。尽管事前有所预料，但在进入<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">牛仔公园</a>的那一刻，望着眼前不见终点的万头攒动，我，还是震惊了。时隔三年，全面回归的卡尔加里<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>，真真切切诠释了什么叫人山人海，什么是万丈红尘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779616" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779616-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779616-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-02-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779616" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">牛仔公园</a>大门。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卡尔加里总人口160万，但在10天的<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>期间，数量翻番，且几乎全部拥挤在市中心占地208英亩的“城中城”──牛仔公园内，其密度可想而知。疫情后的首个大型户外狂欢，果然与去年完全不可同日而语。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779617-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779617-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-03-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779617" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔公园大门。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园的两个主要入口处均设置了拍照大相框，上面是红色的“<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">卡尔加里牛仔节</a>” 字样，两侧雕刻着装马刺的牛仔靴、缠绕的套马绳索、红底白花的牛仔巾和配有锃亮带扣的牛仔腰带。如此意义非凡的景点，不拍照留念实在是说不下去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779614" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779614-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779614-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-01-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779614" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔公园大门。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你观看了牛仔节大游行，那么一定会对两个口号印象深刻，不管是游行队伍，还是路边观众，时不时就要喊两嗓子，释放着西部牛仔特有的风情，一，二，三，“Ya-hoo！”“Yee-haw！”公园内建了三座雅虎字样的雕塑，意尤未尽的，可以尽情重温。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779618-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779618-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-07-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779618" class="wp-caption-text">雅虎字样的雕塑。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13779619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779619-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779619-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-06-600x427.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779619" class="wp-caption-text">雅虎字样的雕塑。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经常登上媒体头图的，也最能代表牛仔公园气氛的即是牛仔公园拱门和<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BA%E4%B8%AD%E7%BC%86%E8%BD%A6.md#1">空中缆车</a>，两者如果联袂出场，更是锦上添花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13779625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779625-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779625-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-04-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779625" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔公园里的<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BA%E4%B8%AD%E7%BC%86%E8%BD%A6.md#1">空中缆车</a>。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13779626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13779626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779626-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13779626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13779626-20220711-LinCaiFeng-Calgary-Stampede-Park-05-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13779626" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔公园里的空中缆车。（林采枫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
