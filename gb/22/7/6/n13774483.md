<h6>麻州“古老荣誉炮兵连”（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company）参加7月4日的国庆日游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年07月06日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）震耳欲聋的礼枪声回响在“<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E7%94%B1.md#1">自由</a>之路”两侧的大厦之间，穿着殖民时期服装的游行队伍及成千上万的民众在欢快的音乐中穿过波士顿的街道。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-18e0cbcb-6b1a-4f84-8642-ad212cd7a492" data-id="player-18e0cbcb-6b1a-4f84-8642-ad212cd7a492"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/18e0cbcb-6b1a-4f84-8642-ad212cd7a492?r=16x9&amp;d=152&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F22%2F7%2F6%2Fn13774483.md#1"></a>
<p>7月4日，<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>市中心举办了盛大的游行庆典，重温《<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》，庆祝美国独立246周年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774460-DSC09366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774460-DSC09366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774460" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，波士顿市中心有游行庆典，庆祝美国独立246周年。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“作为市长，我会参加很多很多的活动和节日，但很少有像独立日这样代表波士顿历史遗产核心的庆典。”波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）在游行开始之前说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774452-DSC09284.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774452-DSC09284-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774452" class="wp-caption-text">在7月4日的国庆日庆典中，麻州“古老荣誉炮兵连”（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company）向国旗致敬。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天上午9点，包括麻州古老荣誉炮兵连（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company）和乐队志愿者在内的游行队伍首先在波士顿市政府广场集合，与众多围观民众一起唱美国国歌，向国旗致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774453-DSC09291.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774453-DSC09291-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774453" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）在7月4日的国庆日庆典中感慨<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E7%94%B1.md#1">自由</a>的可贵。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们波士顿如果没有这样大的自由，就什么也不是。今天是对这种自由的庆祝，也是在提醒我们，捍卫自由需要努力和牺牲。”吴弭说。</p>
<p>游行队伍从市政府广场出发，经过“自由之路”，来到Granary墓园，向埋葬在这里的美国先贤们致敬。古老荣誉炮兵连的卫士们面向墓园敬礼，穿着殖民时期服装的礼枪队也向天鸣枪致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774454-DSC09325.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774454-DSC09325-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774454" class="wp-caption-text">乐队在7月4日的波士顿国庆日庆典中演奏乐曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13774455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774455-DSC09349.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774455-DSC09349-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774455" class="wp-caption-text">穿着殖民时期服装的礼枪队参加7月4日的波士顿国庆日游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13774457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774457-DSC09355.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774457-DSC09355-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774457" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，波士顿人在Granary墓园追忆美国的先贤们。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，游行队伍及民众来到旧州府大楼（Old State House）。古老荣誉炮兵连指挥官Robert Imlach站在阳台上，宣读《<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》，重温1776年波士顿人首次听到这份文件的时刻。“为了强化这篇宣言，我们怀着深信神明保佑的信念，谨以我们的生命、财富和神圣的荣誉，相互保证，共同宣誓。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774459-DSC09360.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774459-DSC09360-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774459" class="wp-caption-text">成千上万民众参加7月4日的波士顿国庆日游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13774461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774461-DSC09377.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774461-DSC09377-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774461" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市长吴弭（Michelle Wu）、市议会议长费连（Ed Flynn）参加7月4日的波士顿国庆日游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着《独立宣言》朗读结束，乐队奏响了礼乐，旧州府大楼两侧喷出礼花，迎来民众的热烈掌声和欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774464-DSC09418.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774464-DSC09418-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774464" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，古老荣誉炮兵连指挥官Robert Imlach在旧州府大楼的阳台上朗读《独立宣言》，重温1776年波士顿人首次听到这一文件的时刻。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13774463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774463-DSC09422.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774463-DSC09422-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774463" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，波士顿人在旧州府大楼前重温《独立宣言》，庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E6%97%A5.md#1">美国独立日</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自佛罗里达的安吉洛·沃特森（Angelo Watson）和夫人德西蕾（Desiree Watson）穿着国旗装参与了游行。他们说，他们很喜欢这次庆典，这让他们第一次来波士顿旅游就感到非常激动。</p>
<p>“这里的7月4日是非常非常大的事，我们非常高兴，”德西蕾说，“有很多国旗，一大群来自不同地方的人们，在这里庆祝一个大日子。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13774449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13774449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774449-DSC09257.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13774449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13774449-DSC09257-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13774449" class="wp-caption-text">麻州“古老荣誉炮兵连”（Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company）的4名华裔成员参加7月4日的国庆日游行。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古老荣誉炮兵连作为美国第3古老的军事组织，每年都参与波士顿的独立日游行。在游行之后，中尉（Lieutenant）麦克尔·哈伦（Michael Haran）在芬纽厅（Faneuil Hall）外说，他加入并喜欢这个团体的原因之一，就是它是一个延续传统的组织。</p>
<p>“我非常热爱这一点。在当今的时代，像我们这样保有传统是非常重要的。”哈伦说。◇</p>
