<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《玩很大》出“史上最长任务” 众星见状傻眼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776601-20220708-sanlih-004-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《综艺玩很大》史上最长最硬的任务，艺人们要挑战彩绘长达200公尺、631片木栈板的满洲港口吊桥。（三立提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年07月08日讯】（大纪元记者林森山报导）台湾外景实境节目《综艺<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%BE%88%E5%A4%A7.md#1">玩很大</a>》经过前两周播出基隆海科馆的打卡任务后，日前节目前往台湾最南端的恒春<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9E%A6%E4%B8%81.md#1">垦丁</a>拍摄，此次面临史上最长的任务，挑战彩绘长达200公尺、631片木栈板的满洲<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%AF%E5%8F%A3%E5%90%8A%E6%A1%A5.md#1">港口吊桥</a>，众人得知后全都吓傻直呼：“根本就是不可能的任务！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13776602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13776602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776602-20220708-sanlih-1325643.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13776602" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776602-20220708-sanlih-1325643-600x800.jpg" alt="综艺玩很大" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13776602" class="wp-caption-text">《综艺<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%BE%88%E5%A4%A7.md#1">玩很大</a>》节目画面，艺人们挑战彩绘吊桥。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>节目固定班底雷艾美因病请假，这次特别找了知名职篮啦啦队成员的梓梓董梓甯来代班，她平常因为跳啦啦队，早就练就了一身好体力，追赶跑跳的关卡完全难不倒她。</p>
<p>而平常热爱手作的梓梓，对于彩绘吊桥、制作红龟粿、采茶泡茶等任务，都相当有兴趣，整个战力百分百，第三季开播至今，一直落后的黄队，能否因为梓梓的加入而领先黑队呢？令人相当期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13776591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13776591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776591-20220708-sanlih-001.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13776591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776591-20220708-sanlih-001-600x338.jpg" alt="梓梓 董梓甯" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13776591" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BC%E8%89%BA%E7%8E%A9%E5%BE%88%E5%A4%A7.md#1">综艺玩很大</a>》节目画面，知名职篮啦啦队成员梓梓董梓甯前来代班。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次节目前往恒春<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9E%A6%E4%B8%81.md#1">垦丁</a>拍摄，原本路途中众人都怀抱着期待的心，想要在拥有无限阳光、海水的国境之南度假，却没想到却面临彩绘吊桥的任务。</p>
<p>主持人林柏昇更直接询问制作单位：“我们节目要不要改叫‘做工的人’？”这次不但工程浩大，还要在体感温度高达40度的阳光下作画，对于艺人的体力更是一大考验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13776594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13776594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776594-20220708-sanlih-002.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13776594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13776594-20220708-sanlih-002-600x338.jpg" alt="综艺玩很大" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13776594" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BC%E8%89%BA%E7%8E%A9%E5%BE%88%E5%A4%A7.md#1">综艺玩很大</a>》推出彩绘吊桥的任务，图为艺人为吊桥漆上护木漆。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
