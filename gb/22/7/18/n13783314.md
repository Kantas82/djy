<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台风汛期将届 桃园请渔民将渔船做妥避风措施</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783316-565329-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>竹围靠港渔船船只缆绳紧系码头。（桃园市农业局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年07月18日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）每年的7月至10月是<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>旺季，为避免强烈风雨造成<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E8%88%B9.md#1">渔船</a>财损或威胁到渔民的<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E8%BA%AB%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8.md#1">人身安全</a>，对于在港内避风的渔船，请渔民系紧缆绳，并做好相关避碰措施，以防强风造成缆绳断裂，导致船只漂流或船体因彼此碰撞而受损。而渔船如遇紧急状况，应立即利用船上通信系统与附近电台或邻近渔船联系，取得适时的救助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13783317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13783317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783317-565330.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13783317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783317-565330-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13783317" class="wp-caption-text">竹围海巡人员确认船只进出。<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桃园市农业局指出，为防范未然，<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E9%A3%8E.md#1">台风</a>来袭前夕，请<ahref="https://github.com/srkrpg322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E8%88%B9.md#1">渔船</a>主将渔船移泊至邻近具规模，并且有防护能力的渔港停泊，台风期间也请渔船主留有包含干部船员在内人员，进行调整缆绳及加缆、排除甲板及机舱积水与紧急操船之应变。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13783318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13783318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783318-565331.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13783318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783318-565331-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13783318" class="wp-caption-text">永安靠港渔船船只缆绳紧系码头。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若有需要留船员在船上防台，请主动向在地渔会（桃园区渔会：03-3835601、中坜区渔会：03-4861017）或安检所（竹围安检所：03-3838837、永安安检所：03-4861794）索取“台风期间进港渔船及船员通报单”，填报紧急联络人电话及预定留船之台湾及非台湾籍船员人数后，送交当地安检所，以利紧急联络。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13783319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13783319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783319-565332.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13783319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783319-565332-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13783319" class="wp-caption-text">永安渔港船只靠港停泊。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基于人命安全考量，市府灾害应变中心会依渔港静稳度、防护能力及台风路径、强弱等实际状况，于灾害应变范围内，依《灾害防救法》相关规定下达船员上岸避风命令，渔船船员（含台湾籍与非台湾籍船员）均须上岸避风，且船主须对非台湾籍船员一视同仁自行安置及管理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13783320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13783320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783320-565333.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13783320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/07/id13783320-565333-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13783320" class="wp-caption-text">永安渔民将渔船紧系缆绳。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台风汛期将届，桃园农业局提醒从事海洋渔业辛勤的渔民们，事前做好防台准备，并注意自身安全，方能助于降低灾害带来的损失。</p>
<p>台风动态资讯可至交通部中央气象局网站查询<ahref="https://www.cwb.gov.tw/V8/C/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.cwb.gov.tw/V8/C/</a>）。◇</p>
