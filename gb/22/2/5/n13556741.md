<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>基隆湖海路巨石落路面 双向交通管制</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月05日讯】（大纪元记者陈秀媛台湾基隆报导）基隆湖海路<ahref="https://github.com/ctqkam3603/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E7%9F%B3.md#1">巨石</a><ahref="https://github.com/ctqkam3603/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E8%B7%AF%E9%9D%A2.md#1">落路面</a>，双向<ahref="https://github.com/ctqkam3603/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E7%AE%A1%E5%88%B6.md#1">交通管制</a></p>
<figure id="13556745" aria-describedby="caption-13556745" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556745-539657-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556745-539657-450x600.jpg" alt="基隆市湖海路巨石崩落" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13556745" class="wp-caption-text">基隆市湖海路<ahref="https://github.com/ctqkam3603/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E7%9F%B3.md#1">巨石</a>崩落（基隆市议员张秉钧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13556744" aria-describedby="caption-13556744" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556744-539656-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556744-539656-450x600.jpg" alt="基隆市湖海路巨石崩落" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13556744" class="wp-caption-text">基隆市湖海路巨石崩落（基隆市议员张秉钧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13556743" aria-describedby="caption-13556743" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556743-539655-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13556743-539655-450x600.jpg" alt="基隆湖海陆巨石崩落阻道" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13556743" class="wp-caption-text">基隆湖海陆巨石崩落阻道（基隆市议员张秉钧提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基隆市往万里方向湖海路2KM处于5日13时许，2巨石崩落，占据车道影响交通，为了安全目前双向<ahref="https://github.com/ctqkam3603/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E7%AE%A1%E5%88%B6.md#1">交通管制</a>中，基隆市政府工务处调度机具已于下午3时许抵达县常，预计晚上8时可以清除完成。</p>
<p>基隆市议员张秉钧到场关切，中华派出所 员警据报到场发现，边坡一颗数吨重巨石，因不堪连日下雨冲刷，松动滚落道路中央，占据一个半车道，所幸未造成人员伤亡，为免土石持续崩落危及过往人车，立即实施道路管制，即使通报市府相关单位到场清除路面落石。</p>
<p>基隆市警察局第四分局提醒市民朋友，中央气象局近期发布基隆地区豪大雨特报，行经山区、海边或低洼地区，请特别留意车前状况，如有发现危害公众往来安全之情事，请暂时不要通过，并避免于附近逗留，以免发生危险，同时拨打110电话，警方将即刻到场疏导交通，并联系业管单位协助处置。</p>
