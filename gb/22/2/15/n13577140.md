<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这些款式过时了﹗穿出时尚品味先避开雷区</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577283-FotoJet-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月16日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）很多<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>达人都会在新的一年开始的时候，观察总结自己的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E8%A1%A3%E4%B9%A0%E6%83%AF.md#1">穿衣习惯</a>，思考确定自己的风格，并了解新一年，特别是春夏季的时尚趋势，再依据这些信息，整理自己的衣橱，保留那些适合自己风格、百搭又不易<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%87%E6%97%B6.md#1">过时</a>的单品，剔除一些冲动购买、不适合自己或过季的单品，可捐可送。</p>
<p>当然，现在调整自己的衣橱也不算晚。如果你想让自己拥有<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>的清新和律动，不妨看看哪些单品在2022年已<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%87%E6%97%B6.md#1">过时</a>，不适合再穿了。</p>
<h2>1.运动员<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A1%E7%BA%B9%E8%A3%A4.md#1">条纹裤</a>装已过时</h2>
<p>2022年条纹上衣、裙装、大衣都继续大行其道，但是像运动装的、侧面带条纹的裤子不要再穿了。尽管几年前这样的裤装非常流行，但在今年，此款式已不再受时尚设计师的青睐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577433-shutterstock_2076908686.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577433-shutterstock_2076908686-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577433" class="wp-caption-text">侧面带条纹的裤子在2022年已经不再流行。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13577346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577346-GettyImages-1367172660-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577346-GettyImages-1367172660-1-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577346" class="wp-caption-text">条纹上衣、裙装、裤装都是流行趋势。2022年1月27日，法国巴黎时装周高订秀场。（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2.紧身牛仔夹克不再流行</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13577359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577359" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577359-GettyImages-1348321045.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577359-GettyImages-1348321045-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577359" class="wp-caption-text">合身或小的牛仔夹克在2022年不再流行。(Photo by Frazer Harrison/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>合身或小的牛仔夹克在今年不再流行。很多人喜爱休闲的夹克上装，宽松款依旧在本季受到青睐。但整体更倾向于较大廓形的夹克或外套。</p>
<p><strong>改装方法：</strong>可外套或直接改穿一件Shacket。</p>
<p>Shacket在今年依旧流行。所谓的Shacket就是把shirt衬衣和夹克Jacket两者合一而成，代表衬衣和外套合二为一的设计。以往Shacket常见的材质都是薄呢、法兰绒等，当然也有仿皮、毛绒、条绒今年流行大格图案、超大剪裁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577300-GettyImages-1370224147.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577300-GettyImages-1370224147-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577300" class="wp-caption-text">黑色高领衫配黑色纱裙，外罩橙红色夹克外套。2022年1月纽约时装周秀场。（Dia Dipasupil/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13577313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577313-GettyImages-853571272.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577313-GettyImages-853571272-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577313" class="wp-caption-text">超贴身、开叉的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%A3%A4.md#1">牛仔裤</a>不再流行。经典款紧身牛仔裤可采用与大上衣搭配削弱贴身的因素。（Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for White）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3.超贴身的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%A3%A4.md#1">牛仔裤</a>不流行</h2>
<p>紧身裤多年受到很多时尚人士的青睐， 特别对于身材好的人而言，这种款型的裤子可以强调腿部线条的优美，凸显姣好的身材。但设计师建议，2022年不要再穿着，因为它过于强调腿型的重要。今年深色、直腿裤型是牛仔裤的流行趋势。</p>
<p><strong>挽救方法</strong>：如果是经典款的紧身牛仔裤，可与大毛衣、大西装、长衬衫搭配，遮盖、削弱贴身的因素。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577305-GettyImages-1370223586.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577305-GettyImages-1370223586-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577305" class="wp-caption-text">白色上装配直筒牛仔裤。2022年2月纽约时装周秀场。（Dia Dipasupil/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4.短小西装</h2>
<p>2022年着衣重点在于廓形，肩宽设计、宽大的造型在今年依旧是流行重点之一。大西装、宽松西装在2022依旧统领西装时尚的现代风。短小的西装外套不再流行。</p>
<p><strong>改装方法</strong>：可选用大西装，或与西装宽裤、洋装搭配；短夹克，特别是格子夹克。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577297-GettyImages-1369670589.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577297-GettyImages-1369670589-450x659.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="659" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577297" class="wp-caption-text">身着超大西服套装的Amber Stevens West于2022年2月9日在加利福尼亚州圣莫尼卡出席了孔雀公司的新系列 “BEL-AIR “的首映派对。（Photo by Matt Winkelmeyer/Getty Images)(Matt Winkelmeyer/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13577328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577328" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577328-GettyImages-1366640298.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577328-GettyImages-1366640298-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577328" class="wp-caption-text">Lorena Vergani2022年1月24日参加法国巴黎时装周2022春夏迪奥高订秀场。（Anthony Ghnassia/Getty Images For Dior）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13577287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577287-GettyImages-151311160.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577287-GettyImages-151311160-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577287" class="wp-caption-text">超级贴身、低腰、裤缝带线条都是2022不再流行的元素。（Mark Von Holden/Mark Von Holden/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5.锥型裤、低腰裤、开叉裤</h2>
<p>锥型裤、香烟裤、瘦腿裤在本季都不再流行，可以收起来或捐出去了。低腰裤系宽大皮带也不再流行。低腰裤在弯腰、下蹲时不够方便，也容易漏光，同时也不如中腰或高腰裤舒适，如果紧身，也很容易在腰胯部挤出赘肉，非常挑剔体型。今年裤腿开叉的版型也不再流行。</p>
<p><strong>改装方法：</strong>2022年，阔腿裤、宽松版型都是流行趋势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577373-GettyImages-1370224150.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577373-GettyImages-1370224150-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577373" class="wp-caption-text">仿皮阔腿裤是今年的流行大势。2022年2月纽约时装周秀场。（Dia Dipasupil/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6.单纯实用的单品</h2>
<p>时尚并不是只追求外表，而是与实用并重。2022年的趋势，淘汰那种只实用而缺乏时尚元素的单品，比如只为单纯保暖、而没有设计感的羽绒背心。当然，只是外表华丽，而无任何实用价值单品的时尚寿命也是不会长久的。</p>
<p><strong>改装方法</strong>：选用既具良好功能性、又时尚的单品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577376-GettyImages-905596546.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577376-GettyImages-905596546-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577376" class="wp-caption-text">此类V领毛衫不再流行，可暂时收起来，或者叠穿。（John Phillips/Getty Images for MBFW）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7.领毛衣</h2>
<p>传统的短小贴身V领毛衣，也不再流行。可选用宽大些的毛衣，宽松的圆领、高领，带有绞花、或者单线条纹路的毛衣也是流行。</p>
<p>改装方法：可与裙装、衬衣叠穿；可选有粗大绞花的大毛衣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13577383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13577383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577383-GettyImages-1370223597.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13577383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13577383-GettyImages-1370223597-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13577383" class="wp-caption-text">白色休闲圆领绞花长毛衣，可作为连身裙穿。是今年的流行趋势。2022年2月纽约时装周秀场。（Dia Dipasupil/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
