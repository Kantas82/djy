<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>围炉拼厨艺 5组台湾音乐人端出创意年菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548547-2202020156211487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>告五人、宇宙人、麋先生、家家、Tizzy Bac共5组台湾音乐人日前齐聚，进厨房来一场“新春厨神大赛”。（相信音乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月02日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）“<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%8A%E4%BA%94%E4%BA%BA.md#1">告五人</a>”、“<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%87%E5%AE%99%E4%BA%BA.md#1">宇宙人</a>”、“<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%8B%E5%85%88%E7%94%9F.md#1">麋先生</a>”、Tizzy Bac和<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%AE%B6.md#1">家家</a>5组台湾音乐人日前难得放下工作，拨空相聚围炉，展开一场“新春厨神大赛”。在节目中，各组艺人铆足全力秀厨艺，带来各式创意年菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548549-2202020156331487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13548549" title="告五人、宇宙人、麋先生、家家、Tizzy_Bac" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548549-2202020156331487-600x330.jpg" alt="告五人、宇宙人、麋先生、家家、Tizzy_Bac" width="600" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548549" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%8A%E4%BA%94%E4%BA%BA.md#1">告五人</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%87%E5%AE%99%E4%BA%BA.md#1">宇宙人</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%8B%E5%85%88%E7%94%9F.md#1">麋先生</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/bdeoiy3452/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%AE%B6.md#1">家家</a>、Tizzy_Bac献上创意年菜。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年迈向成军10周年的麋先生搬出3道料理，分别是日式肉豆腐、剥皮辣椒鸡汤和酒蒸蛤蜊，现场香气逼人，让人看了口水直流。</p>
<p>原来，吉他手喆安对料理充满兴趣，去年疫情期间把握时间，到朋友的日本料理店见习，这回端出肉豆腐更让主唱圣皓大赞“是可以拿出去卖的程度了”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548554" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548554-2202020156191487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13548554" title="麋先生" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548554-2202020156191487.jpg" alt="麋先生" width="455" b="809" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548554" class="wp-caption-text">麋先生的料理让现场香气逼人。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家家去年与圣皓合作的单曲《闭上眼睛会想起的人》近来成为KTV点唱大热门，2人这回在厨神大赛中再现好默契，一同带来丰盛的4道菜：酸辣汤饺、炒辣虎菜、好小菜和虎年必备的萝卜糕，相当应景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548552-2202020156261487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13548552" title="家家 麋先生" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548552-2202020156261487-600x386.jpg" alt="家家 麋先生" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548552" class="wp-caption-text">家家（左）与麋先生圣皓在厨神大赛中再现好默契。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宇宙人则端出了“三人成虎，虎虎生风”套餐，分别是主唱小玉用腐皮和豆芽制作的清爽小菜“虎（腐）皮卷”，以及贝斯手方Q称为是豆腐之中王者的“麻婆豆虎（腐）”，吉他手阿奎则准备老虎酱拌面，一共3道菜，都凑上了虎字谐音，让大伙赞赏是企划概念最完整的年菜组合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548550-2202020156311487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13548550" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548550-2202020156311487-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548550" class="wp-caption-text">宇宙人的年菜套餐大玩“虎年”谐音哏。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>告五人在节目中化身期间限定的“告虎人”登场，菜色则以健康取胜，分别端出毛豆虾仁、青葱鸡胸肉，惜食的他们特别收集其他组用不完的食材，以苍蝇头的形式端出加码的第3道菜“虎姑肉”，使出浑身解术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548553-2202020156241487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13548553" title="告五人" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548553-2202020156241487-600x396.jpg" alt="告五人" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548553" class="wp-caption-text">告五人在节目中化身期间限定的“告虎人”搞笑登场。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主唱云安说：“看前辈们平常演出很辛苦，所以选了这3道菜来照顾他们的健康。”而已经完成料理的圣皓，趁云安掌厨时，与主唱犬青和鼓手哲谦唱起告五人夯歌《好不容易》，现场一片热闹。</p>
<p>Tizzy Bac鼓手前源则独挑大梁掌厨，以剥皮辣椒为核心，端出鸡汤和煎蛋共2道系列菜色。他很享受一个人做菜，笑道：“有句俗话说‘一个厨房容不下2个人’，我觉得这样不会人多手杂。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13548551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13548551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548551-2202020156281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13548551" title="Tizzy_Bac 前源" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13548551-2202020156281487-600x464.jpg" alt="Tizzy_Bac 前源" width="600" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13548551" class="wp-caption-text">Tizzy_Bac鼓手前源独挑大梁掌厨。（相信音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>平时就有做菜习惯的他，在煎蛋要翻面时，大家都放下手边工作围观上去，等着看他施展好身手，结果一次就成功，博得满堂彩。</p>
<p>5组艺人团聚，充满浓浓年节气氛，也展望新的一年尽快推出新作品与粉丝见面。</p>
<p>特别节目“新春厨神大赛”在今晚（大年初二）晚间8点于相信音乐日常B’in Now YouTube频道首播。</p>
