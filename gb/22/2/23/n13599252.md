<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：世界上最大的5种温和巨马</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599269-et-horse-web-89-565-6789-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>郡马是世界上最大的马种。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月23日讯】（英文大纪元Epoch Inspired Staff报导／文韵编译）世界上，<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>的品种大约有300个，有些品种非常令人印象深刻而且也是最庞大的。每种马都有它的独特品质，使其能够承担特定的任务。</p>
<p>大<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC.md#1">马</a>通常被称为“温柔的巨人”，因为它们除了强大的力量外，还具有耐心而且温顺。大型品种通常是草马，主要是为了工作而培育的。</p>
<p>在这里，我们重点介绍世界上最大的五个品种。</p>
<h2>1. 克莱兹代尔（Clydesdales）</h2>
<p>当今世界上最&#33879;名的大型马品种之一是克莱兹代尔马。这些马起源于苏格兰（Scotland）的克莱兹代尔，最初被用作农场马、拉车和耕田。</p>
<p>现代，它们被用于游行、小农场和拉力比赛。一匹成熟的克莱兹代尔马的标准重量约为1,800至2,200磅之间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13599273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599273-shutterstock_97822700.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599273-shutterstock_97822700-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599273" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599278-shutterstock_1295712325.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599278-shutterstock_1295712325-600x460.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599278" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599281-shutterstock_515405785.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599281-shutterstock_515405785-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599281" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管与其它<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%9E%8B%E9%A9%AC%E7%A7%8D.md#1">大型马种</a>相比，该品种的马体型较轻，但它因其高步态而脱颖而出。</p>
<p>大多数克莱兹代尔马呈海湾色，脚上和脸上有白色斑点，不过也有些马的肚子下面有白色斑点。</p>
<p>对于那些热衷饲养这些马的人来说，定期梳理其小腿上的羽毛以消除刺激的风险是很重要的。</p>
<h2>2. 郡马（Shires）</h2>
<p>郡马是世界上最大的马种。像克莱兹代尔一样，它们最初是为工作而饲养的，传统上用在运河系统中拖动驳船，拉车、犁和其它重型农用设备。</p>
<p>该品种马的标准身高在68～76英寸之间，体重在1,800～2,400磅之间。由于体型庞大，外观令人印象深刻，表露它们不可思议的力量和能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13599290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599290-shutterstock_283893638.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599290-shutterstock_283893638-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599290" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599298-shutterstock_652970878.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599298-shutterstock_652970878-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599298" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599301-shutterstock_3593347-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599301-shutterstock_3593347-1-600x553.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="553" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599301" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>郡马现被用于啤酒厂运送麦芽酒，休闲骑马，以及承担林业工作。这些马也是新骑手初学骑术阶段最完美的品种之一。</p>
<p>然而，随着工业革命导致农场机械化，这些马在世界上的数量明显减少，濒临灭绝。</p>
<h2>3. 佩彻伦马（Percherons）</h2>
<p>佩彻伦马的品种得名于它的原产地——法国惠斯纳（Huisne）河谷周围的地区，最初被称为佩彻（Perche）。</p>
<p>成熟的佩彻伦马的标准身高在1.55米（约5英尺）至1.93米（约6.3英尺）之间，具体取决于它的繁殖地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13599304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599304-shutterstock_1214364427.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599304-shutterstock_1214364427-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599304" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599306-shutterstock_1036487725.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599306-shutterstock_1036487725-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599306" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599307-shutterstock_1954794634.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599307-shutterstock_1954794634-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599307" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种马粗壮的颈部显示受东方品种的影响，然而佩彻伦马具有大型马匹相同的能力和力量。</p>
<p>传统上，这些马被用于战场。在现代，它们被用于游行和休闲骑行。它们冷静的气质，也是重型骑手的理想选择。</p>
<h2>4. 比利时马（The Belgian horse）</h2>
<p>比利时马主要以其温顺的性情和强大的力量而闻名，是佛兰芒（Flemish）“大马”的后代。</p>
<p>大马是为战斗而培育的，起源于佛兰德斯（Flanders）地区。一匹成熟的比利时马体重大约在1,800至2,200磅之间，平均身高在64至68英寸之间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13599313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599313-shutterstock_758125339.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599313-shutterstock_758125339-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599313" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599315-shutterstock_82998265.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599315-shutterstock_82998265-600x439.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599315" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599317-shutterstock_632322224.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599317-shutterstock_632322224-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599317" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>比利时马的典型颜色是海湾色、玫瑰色或栗子色。在现代，比利时马用于拉力比赛。它们长期以来一直在此类比赛中占据优势。</p>
<h2>5. 荷兰草马（The Dutch draft）</h2>
<p>荷兰草马的出现是第一次世界大战后比利时草马和阿登马（Ardennes）杂交的结果。</p>
<p>荷兰草马体型魁梧，就像它们的父母品种一样，是世界上最强壮的马匹之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13599319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599319-shutterstock_1787073947-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599319-shutterstock_1787073947-1-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599319" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599323-shutterstock_1093581092.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599323-shutterstock_1093581092-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599323" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13599325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13599325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599325-shutterstock_1398181946.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13599325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13599325-shutterstock_1398181946-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13599325" class="wp-caption-text">(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在早期，这些马在格罗宁根（Groningen）和泽兰（Zeeland）相当受欢迎，它们被用于繁重的农活和拖运。</p>
<p>荷兰草马现被用来在农场表演拉木头和参加耕作比赛。</p>
<p>一匹成熟的荷兰草马身高在60至68英寸之间，比其它大型品种的马要矮。◇</p>
