<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆元宵 动手做棉纸撕贴虎灯笼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581229-Snap1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>元宵提灯笼，虎虎生风庆虎年。（图／茗昕提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>在中国，威猛的老虎被称为“兽中之王”。民间流传着很多有关虎的神话传说，人们相信虎能驱凶避邪、镇鬼消灾，因此也将其作为吉祥的象征。很多人喜欢在家中挂一张虎画，有些人则在幼儿的鞋子上绣上老虎的形象，以祈愿小孩平安长大。</p>
<p>我们今天来做的“虎灯笼”也同样具有祈福的意涵，祈愿大家在新的一年里健康与平安。</p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>玻璃纸（黄、蓝、桃红和绿色）、剪刀、一般美工刀或纸雕用美工刀、口红胶或双面胶带(细)、提灯竹棒或细木棒(足以支撑灯笼重量者即可)、彩线、云彩纸或丹迪纸、特殊彩色棉纸或云龙棉纸、白胶。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>确定灯笼的大小尺寸，本例是将A4云彩纸裁去10公分左右。</li>
<li>用直尺和铅笔将3张云彩纸分别划好黏合边线。</li>
<li>用直尺及无墨水原子笔分别将3张云彩纸黏合边线划过一次，使纸张折线变得明显及漂亮。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581272-wenhua1-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581272 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581272-wenhua1-1-600x553.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="553" /></a></li>
<li>折出黏合边。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581273-wenhua1-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581273 alignnone" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581273-wenhua1-2-600x354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a></li>
<li>用湿水彩笔在棉纸上画出3个大小不一的虎头(圆形或略呈椭圆形皆可)及3对外耳朵与3对小内耳(如下图，虎头配件示例)。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581274-wenhua1-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581274 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581274-wenhua1-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></li>
<li>组合外耳与内耳。用现成的白色及黑色圆形贴纸，组合贴成虎眼。并用黑色签字笔画出虎脸上的花纹及鼻、嘴。并将虎脸与虎耳黏合起来。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581275-wenhua1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581275 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581275-wenhua1-4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581276-wenhua1-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581276 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581276-wenhua1-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581277-wenhua1-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581277 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581277-wenhua1-6-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a></li>
<li>将完成的虎头分别放在3张云彩纸上，并用铅笔轻轻地点一下记号定位。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581278-wenhua1-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581278 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581278-wenhua1-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></li>
<li>用铅笔分别在云彩纸上画出树枝、树叶的形象，因为需要用美工刀雕刻出树枝、叶，所以植物形象要简单以免太困难招来挫败感！这是要让老虎有从森林中来的感觉。第三张云彩纸要写上吉祥话──花开富贵迎新春、福虎生丰接平安，所以画上简易的图案即可(如下图)。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581279-wenhua1-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581279 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581279-wenhua1-8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581280-wenhua1-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581280 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581280-wenhua1-9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></li>
<li>?用美工刀依铅笔画的样式刻出图案。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581281-wenhua1-10.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13581281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581281-wenhua1-10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></li>
</ol>
<p>10. 本例需要用到黄色-月亮、照到月光的树叶；蓝色-云朵；绿色-植物；桃红色-花朵。依整个的图案大小裁剪玻璃纸，所需玻璃纸尺寸比所要贴的图案略大一些。裁好玻璃纸后，在图案周边涂上口红胶或是用细双面胶带也可(这2种材质的胶可以让玻璃纸很平整的贴上)。(如下图)</p>
<figure id="attachment_13581283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13581283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581283-wenhua1-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581283-wenhua1-12-600x643.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="643" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13581283" class="wp-caption-text">背面1</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13581282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13581282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581282-wenhua1-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581282-wenhua1-11-600x644.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="644" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13581282" class="wp-caption-text">背面2</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13581284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13581284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581284-wenhua1-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581284-wenhua1-13-600x633.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="633" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13581284" class="wp-caption-text">背面3</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.在3片纸张的黏合边上一一黏上双面胶带或涂上一些白胶，基本上是涂(黏)胶在一个边即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13581285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13581285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581285-wenhua1-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581285-wenhua1-14-600x644.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="644" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13581285" class="wp-caption-text">正面</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581286-wenhua1-15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581286 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581286-wenhua1-15-600x641.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="641" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581288-wenhua1-16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581288 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581288-wenhua1-16-600x644.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="644" /></a></p>
<p>12. 一一将2张纸的黏合边黏合，完成后是一个三角柱状的灯笼。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581269-wenhua1-17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13581269 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13581269-wenhua1-17-600x510.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="510" /></a></p>
<p>13. 在黏合边(灯笼内)黏上挂线：在彩线两末端分别打个结(可避免彩线因重量而滑出)，用胶带黏于黏合边上，拿出小木棍就可以提灯笼啰！</p>
