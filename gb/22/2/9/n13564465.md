<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>政大南加校友会新春线上团聚 分享工作经验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564476-bb19c844603e587e91d7240f3b05631c-600x400.png" />
<h6>活动首先播放侨委会首长新春祝贺影片。（洛杉矶华侨文教服务中心）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月09日讯】由政大南加州校友会主办的“<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%8A%A0%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E7%BA%BF%E4%B8%8A%E8%B4%BA%E6%96%B0%E6%98%A5.md#1">美加校友线上贺新春</a>”活动，于2月5日晚上热闹展开，包括南北加州、宾州、芝加哥以及亚特兰大等地区的政大校友<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BF%E4%B8%8A%E7%9B%B8%E8%81%9A.md#1">线上相聚</a>，分享在美求学<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A5%E4%BD%9C%E7%BB%8F%E9%AA%8C.md#1">工作经验</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13564479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13564479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564479-2b303ae7d5d7d5b81a4427f565c8357f.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13564479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564479-2b303ae7d5d7d5b81a4427f565c8357f-600x347.png" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13564479" class="wp-caption-text">校友合影。（洛杉矶华侨文教服务中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13564480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13564480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564480-c046f57557fe70ed2914dc3fc13f5e91.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13564480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564480-c046f57557fe70ed2914dc3fc13f5e91-600x419.png" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13564480" class="wp-caption-text">美加地区政大校友踊跃参与并进行经验分享。（洛杉矶华侨文教服务中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6%E6%96%87%E6%95%99%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">洛杉矶文教中心</a>主任陈敏永偕廖俪云副主任出席活动，陈敏永向校友介绍侨委会委员长童振源上任以来的侨务施政理念，以及侨务工作。他并代为转致童振源贺状，恭贺侨务促进委员洪巍续任会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13564477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13564477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564477-b0ef6969bb07085d0fe1746d40592f12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13564477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564477-b0ef6969bb07085d0fe1746d40592f12-600x181.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="181" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13564477" class="wp-caption-text">洛侨中心主任陈敏永致词。（洛杉矶华侨文教服务中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5日，政大校长郭明政、前校长郑丁旺，以及前考选部部长董保城教授等贵宾也在台北上线，与校友欢庆黄历新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13564478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13564478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564478-3201549550cb335d4163dda7b936f8eb.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13564478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13564478-3201549550cb335d4163dda7b936f8eb-600x238.png" alt="" width="600" b="238" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13564478" class="wp-caption-text">政大校长郭明政致词。（洛杉矶华侨文教服务中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郭明政向校友分享近期的成果与计划，期许在过去的各项基础上，持续巩固政大在人文社会科学领域的品牌，并以世界的政大为愿景，建立国际名声。郑丁旺与董保城等特别感谢政大回馈母校的精神。</p>
<p>当天线上活动，包括颁发情定终身奖、最佳造型奖，分享参与社团经验，以及合唱校歌等。◇</p>
