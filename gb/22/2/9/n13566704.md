<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡尔加里路口农贸市场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13566754-20220128-LEEYUN-CrossRoadFarmersMarket-03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>路口农贸市场。（李蕴／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月10日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/yjnkcd3462/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/yjnkcd3462/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%AF%E5%8F%A3%E5%86%9C%E8%B4%B8%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">路口农贸市场</a>（Cross Road Farmers Market）成立于1987年，现今坐落在1235 26 Ave SE，是卡城仅有的两个全年营业的农贸市场之一，数十年来为广大<ahref="https://github.com/yjnkcd3462/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a>人提供了一个购物、就餐、休闲的好去处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13566756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13566756" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13566756-20220128-LEEYUN-CrossRoadFarmersMarket-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13566756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13566756-20220128-LEEYUN-CrossRoadFarmersMarket-01-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13566756" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yjnkcd3462/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%AF%E5%8F%A3%E5%86%9C%E8%B4%B8%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">路口农贸市场</a>。（李蕴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每周的周五至周日，早上9点至下午5点，路口农贸市场的新老客户纷至沓来，挑选新鲜果蔬、当地产蛋奶蜂蜜，品尝美味香肠、肉制品，购买特色家用物品、等等。这里有超过100家商铺，大都出售自制物品及食品，很多在大商场买不到的商品可以在这里找到哦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13566757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13566757" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13566757-20220128-LEEYUN-CrossRoadFarmersMarket-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13566757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13566757-20220128-LEEYUN-CrossRoadFarmersMarket-02-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13566757" class="wp-caption-text">路口农贸市场。（李蕴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
