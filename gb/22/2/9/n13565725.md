<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>糙齿海豚搁浅八里海岸 热心加入救援行列</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565727-540160-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>糙齿海豚。（八里区公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月09日讯】（大纪元记者宋顺澈台湾新北报导）3头<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%99%E9%BD%BF%E6%B5%B7%E8%B1%9A.md#1">糙齿海豚</a>今天<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%90%81%E6%B5%85.md#1">搁浅</a>在<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">新北市</a>八里挖仔尾沙滩附近，八里区公所获报后立刻联系新北市救难协会八里分会成员前来支援，现场会同新北市政府动保处、海巡第3巡防区第8岸巡队、海洋委员会<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%B4%8B%E4%BF%9D%E8%82%B2%E7%BD%B2.md#1">海洋保育署</a>及及中华鲸豚协会进行救援，经紧急救援，送往八斗子岸置中心安置，后续视海相好转后再行出海野放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13565728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565728-540161.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565728-540161-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565728" class="wp-caption-text">救护人员加以保湿。（八里区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13565729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13565729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565729-540162.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13565729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13565729-540162-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13565729" class="wp-caption-text">救护人员勤加保护。（八里区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%90%81%E6%B5%85.md#1">搁浅</a>的是3只公的<ahref="https://github.com/fpchdd3367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B3%99%E9%BD%BF%E6%B5%B7%E8%B1%9A.md#1">糙齿海豚</a>，现场经专业人员判断，海豚生命迹象良好，惟因现场海相不佳，判断无法安全的将其推送回海中，于是现场人员协助专业人员安抚海豚，进行保湿，联系各单位紧急调救援物品，在施打镇静剂后，进行安置作业。</p>
<p>区长陈炳仲表示，民众如发现搁浅或迷航鲸豚，可利用鲸豚24小时救援专线0928-539977或海巡服务专线“118”通报救援。</p>
