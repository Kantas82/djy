<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>省府扩大3至4岁儿童学前教育计划</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月18日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者杨清清加拿大温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">17</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，<ahref="https://github.com/pvggkj3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81.md#1">卑诗省</a>教育厅宣布，随着《<ahref="https://github.com/pvggkj3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%86%E5%A4%87%E8%B5%B7%E6%AD%A5.md#1">准备起步</a>》（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Just B4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">幼儿教育计划在本学年由一个校区扩大至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个校区，将会有更多家庭和学前儿童获得早期学习机会。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">《<ahref="https://github.com/pvggkj3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%86%E5%A4%87%E8%B5%B7%E6%AD%A5.md#1">准备起步</a>》属于一项以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">岁儿童为对象的半日制注册学前托儿计划。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">省政府正拨款</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">125,000</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元扩大该幼儿教育计划，旨在为幼童进入幼稚园之前一年提供支援。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">《准备起步》计划透过使用学校现有的《卑诗茁壮开展》（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">StrongStart BC </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，简称</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">SSBC) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">计划的空间，与该计划一起运作。这个合作形式将可尽量善用校内的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> SSBC </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">员工、空间和资源。在大多数情况下，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">SSBC </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的幼儿教育工作者也会负责《准备起步计划的教学。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> SSBC</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">课程在上午授课，而同一批员工则会在下午负责《准备起步》计划。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">《准备起步》计划于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020-21 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">学年开始在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 67</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">校区（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Okanagan Skaha</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）推行，并将于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 2021-22 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">学年扩展到另五个校区。推行该计划的新校区包括：<ahref="https://github.com/pvggkj3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E4%BC%AF%E8%8C%A8%E7%A6%8F%E5%BE%B7.md#1">阿伯茨福德</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Abbotsford</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">34</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">校区（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dormick Park </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小学）；</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/pvggkj3510/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B3%E5%85%89%E6%B5%B7%E5%B2%B8.md#1">阳光海岸</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> ( Sunshine Coast ) 46</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">校区（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Halfmoon</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小学）；</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">列治文</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">38</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">校区；环太平洋</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> ( Pacific Rim )70</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号校区；以及甘碌</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&#8211;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">汤普森（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kamloops-Thompson</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">73</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号校区（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Arthur Hatton </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小学）。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这次扩大划预计可以把相关学校名额由</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个增至超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">70</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个。在学年期，该计划将会每星期最多提供五天的课程。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?◇</span></p>
