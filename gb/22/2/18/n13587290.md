<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：俄罗斯幼童的冬季写真 可爱极了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587330-Elena-Algazina-photographer-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>俄罗斯摄影师为儿童拍摄的“乡村”系列照。（Elena Algazina提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年02月18日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（英文大纪元记者Anna Mason报导／张雨霏编译）这一系列照片中的孩子们穿着厚厚的小<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E5%A4%A7%E8%A1%A3.md#1">皮大衣</a>，拿着一篮子水果在雪地里拍照，与周边古朴美丽的背景相得益彰，使他们看起来既像可爱的<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>套娃，又像童话故事中的人物。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“给儿童拍照是件非常开心的事情”，来自<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>圣彼得堡的44岁儿童和家庭<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>埃琳娜</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?阿尔加齐娜（Elena Algazina）告诉《大纪元时报》，“最重要的是，他们不会摆姿势，完全是最真实的展现。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">当埃琳娜在她的衣橱里发现一件她小时候的棕色外套时，这个展示穿着<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E5%A4%A7%E8%A1%A3.md#1">皮大衣</a>的孩子们的“乡村”系列照就此诞生了。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13587331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587331-Elena13-1200x802.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587331-Elena13-1200x802.png" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587331" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13587332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587332" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587332-Elena15-1200x803.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587332-Elena15-1200x803.png" alt="" width="601" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587332" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“我妈妈把它当作一件纪念品”，埃琳娜说，“它在衣柜里放了30多年了，很多苏联孩子小时候都有这样的皮大衣、毡靴、<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">帽子</a>和围巾。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">因此，当她的儿子长到2岁时，埃琳娜想让他穿上这件皮大衣，给他拍些照片。为了配合整体造型，她特意到旧货市场淘来毡靴，还租了一顶传统<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">帽子</a>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这张照片最终选在古色古香的木屋背景下拍摄。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13587334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587334" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587334-Elena14-1200x749.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587334-Elena14-1200x749.png" alt="" width="601" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587334" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13587335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587335-Elena23-1200x760.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587335-Elena23-1200x760.png" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587335" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">随着第一张照片的成功，受到鼓舞的埃琳娜购买了另一件差不多尺寸的米色皮草大衣。但这次，她觉得画面中如果呈现的是2岁左右的一对兄妹就再理想不过了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">她说，位于圣彼得堡的舒瓦洛夫卡（Shuvalovka）村非常适合作为画面的背景，离举世闻名的彼得夏宫和&#33879;名的喷泉不远。与此同时，她又很幸运地在她朋友的孩子中找到了非常完美的小<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E7%89%B9.md#1">模特</a>。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13587338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587338" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587338-Elena11-1200x764.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587338-Elena11-1200x764.png" alt="" width="599" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587338" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13587339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587339" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587339-Elena12-1200x802.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587339-Elena12-1200x802.png" alt="" width="601" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587339" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在拍摄现场，以利沙（Elisha）和娜塔莉亚（Natalya）坐在木制台阶上，两人各拿着一个百吉饼，同时穿着精美的厚皮大衣和可爱的靴子，戴着厚皮帽子，围着羊毛披肩。当一切准备就绪时，一只猫意外地进入画面，营造出更加生活化的氛围。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">埃琳娜说，拍摄这对小<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E7%89%B9.md#1">模特</a>的经历“非常令人难忘”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">当埃琳娜在她的<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/elenaalgazina/">社交平台</a>分享出一张乡村系列照片后，迅速在网络上获得热传。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">埃琳娜说，随着她的其它几张照片在网络上大受欢迎，来自不同国家的艺术家经常给她留言，希望得到她的授权，允许他们临摹她的照片画油画或水彩画等。</span></p>

<figure id="attachment_13587341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13587341" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587341-Elena27-1200x707.png"><img class=" wp-image-13587341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13587341-Elena27-1200x707.png" alt="" width="599" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13587341" class="wp-caption-text">（Elena Algazina提供）</figcaption></figure>
