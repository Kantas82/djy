<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>特制欧式台味面包 烤地瓜黑芝麻面包松软可口</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13604967-7a7d3ce1a8565c101412db893ebe0bd9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香酥松软地瓜黑芝麻面包。（出色文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong>：现代人讲究饮食健康，对于“台式面包”的甜香浓往往要十分克制，深怕吃进去太多糖分、油脂和热量。“<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%BC%8F%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">欧式面包</a>”虽然低糖低油，但是口感扎实、略带酸味，亚洲人可能吃不惯。《超人气杂粮果干欧式面包》一书介绍与众不同的烘培手法，取用台湾在地当季蔬果为食材，佐以欧式面包制作方法，详细剖析烘培技法与流程，打造“台欧”新款面包，让你吃起来一点都不违和。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a>黑芝麻面包</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a>与黑芝麻的组合，混合了松软可口的烤地瓜，与烘焙得香喷喷的黑芝麻，制作而成润泽香酥的美味欧包，适合当作点心，也可以做料理，更是餐桌上令人着迷的芬芳。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;">面团材料</span></h4>
<p>高筋面粉 400 公克<br />
法国面包专用粉 500 公克<br />
干酵母 4 公克<br />
盐 18公克<br />
蜂蜜 30 公克<br />
黑芝麻 80 公克<br />
地瓜丁 100 公克<br />
水 800 公克</p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;">馅料材料</span></h4>
<p>A. 地瓜丁 300 克</p>
<figure id="attachment_13604968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13604968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13604968-P32.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13604968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13604968-P32-450x275.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13604968" class="wp-caption-text">材料。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">制作工法与流程</span></h3>
<p>搅拌 L5M6/24℃<br />
基本发酵 60mins</p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;">翻面</span></h4>
<p>基本发酵 50mins<br />
分割 300g<br />
中间发酵 40mins</p>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;">整型 圆柱</span></h4>
<p>最后发酵 60mins<br />
烤箱 220℃/210℃<br />
烤焙 划刀/蒸气/22mins</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h4><span style="color: #000080;">面团搅拌</span></h4>
<p>1. 将高筋面粉与法国面包专用粉放入钢盆中，加入干酵母、盐、蜂蜜、水，以低速搅拌5 分钟后高速搅拌6分钟，搅拌至面团表面光滑莹亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605027-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605027-1-1-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605027" class="wp-caption-text">将高筋面粉与法国面包专用粉放入钢盆中，加入干酵母。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605028-1-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605028-1-2-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605028" class="wp-caption-text">加入盐。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605029-1-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605029-1-3-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605029" class="wp-caption-text">加入蜂蜜。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605030-1-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605030-1-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605030" class="wp-caption-text">加入水。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605031-1-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605031-1-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605031" class="wp-caption-text">以低速搅拌 5 分钟后高速搅拌 6 分钟，搅拌至面团表面光滑莹亮。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 加入烘焙过的黑芝麻、烤熟的地瓜丁进行搅拌，完成时面团温度宜维持在24℃上下。将面团静置，进行基本发酵约60 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605032-2-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605032-2-1-450x279.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605032" class="wp-caption-text">加入烤熟的地瓜丁进行搅拌。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605033-2-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605033-2-2-450x278.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605033" class="wp-caption-text">加入烘焙过的黑芝麻。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #000080;">基本发酵、翻面</span></strong></p>
<p>3. 基本发酵约60 分钟后，在面团上撒上少许手粉，倒扣在工作台上，再撒上少许面粉，稍拉长， 由左向右折1 / 3，再由右向左折1 / 3。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605073" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605073-3-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605073-3-1-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605073" class="wp-caption-text">基本发酵约60分钟后，在面团上撒上少许手粉，倒扣在工作台上，再撒上少许面粉。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605074" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605074-3-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605074-3-3-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605074" class="wp-caption-text">稍拉长面团。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605075" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605075" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605075-3-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605075" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605075-3-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605075" class="wp-caption-text">面团由左向右折1 / 3。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605076" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605076-3-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605076-3-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605076" class="wp-caption-text">面团再由右向左折1 / 3。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放入发酵箱， 稍微压平整后，再静置发酵50 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605077" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605077-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605077" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605077-4-450x236.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605077" class="wp-caption-text">放入发酵箱，稍微压平整。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 基本发酵完成后，将面团倒扣在工作台上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605078" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605078" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605078-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605078-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605078" class="wp-caption-text">基本发酵完成后，将面团倒扣在工作台上。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 面团表面撒上少许手粉，用手掌轻压面团，将空气排出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605089" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605089-6-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605089-6-1-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605089" class="wp-caption-text">面团表面撒上少许手粉。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605090" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605090-6-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605090-6-2-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605090" class="wp-caption-text">用手掌轻压面团，将空气排出。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 以每团约300 公克分割成数等份。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13605177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605177" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605177-e6b9dc476b06b1e0bafa117cf2e29242.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605177-e6b9dc476b06b1e0bafa117cf2e29242-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605177" class="wp-caption-text">面团每团约300公克。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13605091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13605091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605091-7-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13605091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13605091-7-2-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13605091" class="wp-caption-text">以每团约300公克分割成数等份。（出色文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 用手掌轻压面团，将空气排出后，双手掌捧住面团边缘，往后推、往内缩，手掌底部略施力， 让面团边缘顺势滚入底部，整型成圆球形。</p>
