<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗栗旅游节“用栗买起来”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608637-542689-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>苗县长徐耀昌感谢裕隆公司与企业界的协助配合五倍卷行销苗栗 （许享富 ／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>报导）苗栗县府为活络县内经济产业，规划“苗栗<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%8A%82.md#1">旅游节</a>”配合五倍券，鼓励游客到<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E6%A0%97.md#1">苗栗</a>旅游、消费，就有机会抽中总值上千万的大奖。活动进入倒数计时，县府在头份尚顺广场加码推出大型扭蛋快闪，搭配精彩节目演出。</p>
<p>县长徐耀昌27日率同多位县议员，鼓励游客把握<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/228%E8%BF%9E%E5%81%87.md#1">228连假</a>“最后冲一波、用栗买起来”，成功吸引人潮带动商机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13608643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608643-542694.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608643-542694-600x402.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608643" class="wp-caption-text">头份市代表与议员共襄盛举。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长徐耀昌表示，县府推出苗栗<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%8A%82.md#1">旅游节</a>鼓励大家到苗栗消费，只要110年10月8日至111年2月28日期间，在苗栗合法税籍商家消费及合法旅宿取得发票或收据，即可到苗栗旅游节的官网上登录发票抽千万大奖，奖项内容有百万休旅车、GOGORO、10万现金等，消费越多，中奖机会越大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13608638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608638-542690.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608638-542690-600x376.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608638" class="wp-caption-text">在地饶舌歌手Yappy的精釆歌曲揭开活动热潮（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13608642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608642-542693.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608642-542693-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608642" class="wp-caption-text">表演团体与县长徐耀昌合影。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徐耀昌说，苗栗不仅有南庄和三义双国际慢城，不论山线或海线，18乡镇市也都各有特色景点和人文风情，现在又是赏花、采果、泡温泉、品尝美食的最佳时节，在在都值得游客细细品味；迎接<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/228%E8%BF%9E%E5%81%87.md#1">228连假</a>到来，交通部观光局统计苗栗县平均订房率超过7成2，居全台之冠，显示游客对苗栗有很高的青睐度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13608641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608641-542692.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608641-542692-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608641" class="wp-caption-text">小地方工作室创意精美产品。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13608639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608639-542691.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608639-542691-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608639" class="wp-caption-text">用栗买起来天使农场的产品。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苗栗旅游节登录发票总内在品质已逾6亿5000万元，28日就是登录的最后截止日，县府文化观光局利用连假的26、27两日，开出大型扭蛋“振兴号”快闪活动，安排获得两次Top Rapper头衔的饶舌歌手Yappy开唱，迷思魔幻剧团、怀旧乐团、亲子台姐姐带动跳等表演，以及在地文创、手作、农产品特色市集，在活动最后倒数第2天，鼓励民众把握良机最后再冲一波。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13608644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13608644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608644-542695.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13608644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13608644-542695-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13608644" class="wp-caption-text">热闹的尚顺广场。（许享富 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
