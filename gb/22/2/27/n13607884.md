<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州十大最佳与最差幼儿园排名揭晓</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13607897-kindergarten-g9667c842a_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>托儿中心示意图。（grey_beard/Pixabay）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者赵琼玉澳洲墨尔本编译报导）KindiCare网站最近对<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>4500个<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">幼儿园</a>进行了评级，将其从“一般”到“优秀”做了一个<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E5%90%8D.md#1">排名</a>，以便家长、监护人、<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%98%E5%84%BF%E6%9C%8D%E5%8A%A1.md#1">托儿服务</a>供应商、幼儿园教师、主管和政府能够对澳洲幼儿园的服务质量进行比较。</p>
<p>据《太阳先驱报》报导，KindiCare的评估指标包含<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E5%84%BF%E5%9B%AD.md#1">幼儿园</a>的质量、表现和客户满意度，满分为10分。同时还参考了政府对幼儿园服务质量的审计、评级、审查、幼儿园未来走向和KindiCare汇总的社区反馈。</p>
<p><strong>根据KindiCare网站对<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>幼儿园的最新评级，维州<ahref="https://github.com/havifh3963/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E5%90%8D.md#1">排名</a>前10位的幼儿园依次为：</strong></p>
<p>东墨尔本（East Melbourne）的Powlett Reserve Children&#8217;s Centre（9.79分）和Oakleigh South的Goodstart Early Learning Hughesdale（9.79分）并列第一；</p>
<p>Patterson Lakes的Patterson Lakes Kindergarten（9.78分）；</p>
<p>South Yarra的Renown Kindergarten（9.77分）；</p>
<p>Bayswater的Birch Street Children and Family Centre（9.76分）；</p>
<p>Nunawading的Starfish Early Learning Centre（9.75分）；</p>
<p>Tarneit的Penrose Promenade Kindergarten、Wyndham Vale的Manor Lakes Kindergarten和Brighton的Firbank Grammar School均获得9.74分，并列第六；</p>
<p>Hoppers Crossing的The Grange Kindergarten、Clyde North的Rivercrest Early Learning Centre和Truganina的Arndell Park Kindergarten均获得9.73分，并列第七；</p>
<p>Glenroy的York Street Kindergarten（9.72分）和Burwood的Presbyterian Ladies College Early Learning（9.72分）并列第八；</p>
<p>Ringwood的Yarra Valley Grammar School Early Learning Centre、Moonee Ponds的Penleigh &amp; Essendon Grammar School和Ashburton的Craig Family Centre均获得9.71分，并列第九；</p>
<p>Parkville的Royal Children&#8217;s Hospital Early Learning和Belmont的Play Child Care &amp; Kindergarten（9.69分）并列第十。</p>
<p><strong>维州排名最后10位的幼儿园依次为：</strong></p>
<p>Daylesford的Daylesford Family Daycare（6.28分）；</p>
<p>Glenroy的Sharaf Family Daycare（6.38分）和Ballarat的St Alipius Kindergarten（6.38分）；</p>
<p>Dandenong的Century Star Early Education和Officer的Montessori Beginnings（6.49分）；</p>
<p>Ferntree Gully的BYK KIDS（6.60分）（还有6个幼儿园的评分也是6.60分）；</p>
<p>Bundoora的Dallas Family Daycare（6.70分）（还有6个幼儿园的评分也是6.70分）；</p>
<p>Dandenong的Al Nur Family Daycare（6.91分）（还有10个幼儿园的评分也是6.91分）；</p>
<p>California Gully的California Gully Primary School OSCHC（6.97分）；</p>
<p>Mentone的Acacia Ave Preschool（7.02分）（还有32个幼儿园的评分也是7.02分）；</p>
<p>Elwood的Andy&#8217;s Before and After School Care（7.13分）（还有34个幼儿园的评分也是7.13分）。</p>
