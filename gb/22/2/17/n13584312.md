<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒸布丁变成甜蒸蛋？ 15分钟蒸出绵滑布丁全攻略</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584427-Caramel-pudding.jpg" />
<h6>避免蒸布丁变成甜蒸蛋，须掌握数个重要诀窍，15分钟便可蒸好绵密滑嫩的布丁，香甜味不输焦糖版。（18食堂提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>家中没有烤箱，用电锅<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E5%B8%83%E4%B8%81.md#1">蒸布丁</a>，但不知为何却作成了“甜蒸蛋”？本食谱提供避开地雷区的诀窍，此外，还列出数个重要关键步骤，让你在15分钟内便能蒸出绵密、滑嫩、香浓的布丁，而且香甜味不输<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96%E5%B8%83%E4%B8%81.md#1">焦糖布丁</a>。</p>
<p><strong>小窍门：</strong></p>
<p>1. <span style="color: #800000;">蛋黄多，布丁较绵密有蛋香。</span>也可改成全蛋，味道略有差异。</p>
<p>2. <span style="color: #800000;">去蛋腥用香草荚最优</span>，也可改成兰姆酒2小匙，或香草精1小匙，或香草粉1/2小匙。</p>
<p>3. <span style="color: #800000;"><ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E5%B8%83%E4%B8%81.md#1">蒸布丁</a>一定要加香草荚和动物性鲜奶油，才不会蒸出类似甜蒸蛋的口味。</span></p>
<p>4. 动物性鲜奶油如改成鲜奶，奶香会减少些。</p>
<p>5. <span style="color: #800000;">容器以耐热玻璃或陶瓷较佳。</span></p>
<p>6. 使用的容器不同，蒸煮时间也会不同。布丁液如会晃动，蒸煮时间就须延长。</p>
<p><strong>食材</strong><strong>（7人份）：</strong></p>
<p>鸡蛋2颗（110g）</p>
<p>蛋黄4个（120g）</p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;">[牛奶液]</span>材料：</p>
<p>细砂糖60g</p>
<p>鲜奶（全脂）400cc</p>
<p>动物性鲜奶油80cc</p>
<p>香草荚1/2支</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;">[焦糖液]</span>材料：</p>
<p>细砂糖60g</p>
<p>热开水30cc</p>
<p>冷开水30cc</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p><strong>制作布丁液</strong><strong>步骤1～5：</strong></p>
<p>1. 备好所有食材。先取一碗，用叉子稍微打散蛋液（鸡蛋2颗+蛋黄4颗），备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584486" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584486-Caramel-pudding-step-1-e1645104250419.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584486-Caramel-pudding-step-1-e1645104250419.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="227" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584486" class="wp-caption-text">添加适量的蛋黄，布丁较绵密且有蛋香。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 剖开“香草荚”，用刀子刮出“黑色香草籽”，备用。（香草荚主要功用为去蛋腥&amp;增加布丁香气）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584489" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584489-Caramel-pudding-step2-e1645104317574.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584489-Caramel-pudding-step2-e1645104317574.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584489" class="wp-caption-text">加入香草荚（半根），避免味道像甜蒸蛋，还可去蛋腥，增加布丁香气（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 取一锅，放进<span style="color: #ff6600;">[牛奶液]</span><strong>（动物性鲜奶油除外）</strong>，小火烹煮并搅拌3～4分钟。煮好即先取出香草荚，并放至微温。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584493" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584493-Caramel-pudding-step3-e1645104394613.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584493-Caramel-pudding-step3-e1645104394613.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="221" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584493" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋和牛奶比例拿捏得当，才不会作出“甜蒸蛋”。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将<span style="color: #000000;">[牛奶液]和动物性鲜奶油</span>，慢慢倒进蛋液，并搅拌均匀，布丁液完成。（<span style="color: #800000;">不可一次全倒，很容易变成蛋花汤</span>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584498" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584498-Caramel-pudding-step4-e1645104426473.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584498-Caramel-pudding-step4-e1645104426473.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="227" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584498" class="wp-caption-text">轻轻搅拌，以免搅进空气，使布丁液内产生气泡。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. <span style="color: #800000;">将已完成的布丁液，用筛网过滤2次，去除鸡蛋系带和过大物质。（布丁滑嫩细致的关键步骤）</span></p>
<p>（<strong>这个食谱配方</strong>，<strong>单吃就很像几X甜了</strong>，<strong>不一定要煮焦糖液</strong>！）</p>
<p>（瓶底如要加焦糖液，请先跳至步骤10～12）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584500" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584500-Caramel-pudding-step5-e1645104472960.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584500-Caramel-pudding-step5-e1645104472960.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584500" class="wp-caption-text">作出滑嫩细致的布丁，关键步骤是将布丁液用极细孔洞的工具过滤2次。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>蒸布丁步骤6～7：</strong></p>
<p>6. 取一电锅，外锅倒进量米杯（刻度8）的水量，摆进蒸架，放上已装布丁液的保罗瓶，瓶口均需用锡箔纸包好，防止水蒸气滑落，造成布丁坑洞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584503" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584503-Caramel-pudding-step6-e1645104551389.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584503-Caramel-pudding-step6-e1645104551389.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584503" class="wp-caption-text">保罗瓶瓶口用锡箔纸包好，方能蒸出滑嫩布丁。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. <span style="color: #800000;">电锅锅盖（如图）撑上一支筷子，留缝稍降电锅的高温</span>，按下开关蒸煮，约12～15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584506" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584506-Caramel-pudding-step7-e1645104586615.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584506-Caramel-pudding-step7-e1645104586615.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584506" class="wp-caption-text">蒸布丁时，锅盖撑上一支筷子，以免锅内温度过快提升，布丁液因过熟而产生孔洞。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 电锅开关跳起，取出布丁放凉后，即摆进冰箱冷藏4～6个小时。（<span style="color: #800000;">冷藏时，要盖上保罗瓶上盖，布丁表面才不会过干</span>，也可避免吸入过多冰箱味）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584511" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584511-Caramel-pudding-step8-e1645104631765.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584511-Caramel-pudding-step8-e1645104631765.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584511" class="wp-caption-text">冷藏前，盖上保罗瓶上盖，布丁表面才不会过干。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. <span style="color: #800000;">冷藏过后，香草籽和鲜奶油的味道变得更加香浓。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13584513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584513" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584513-Caramel-pudding-step9-e1645104682845.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584513-Caramel-pudding-step9-e1645104682845.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584513" class="wp-caption-text">冷藏过的布丁，味道更香浓。（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>制作</strong><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>[</strong><strong>焦糖液]</strong></span><strong>步骤10～12</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>10. 取一小锅，倒进细砂糖和冷开水，先轻轻摇晃锅身，让糖和水稍微混和。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584514" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584514-Caramel-pudding-step10-e1645104738107.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584514-Caramel-pudding-step10-e1645104738107.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584514" class="wp-caption-text">（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 全程小火，刚开始切勿晃动锅子或搅拌糖水。（约3分钟）就会出现锅缘焦糖化，这时才用汤匙将焦糖化部分，由外往内轻轻拌入均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584516" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584516-Caramel-pudding-step11-e1645104816566.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584516-Caramel-pudding-step11-e1645104816566.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584516" class="wp-caption-text">（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.（再过2～3分钟），颜色由浅咖变深咖。先关火，再倒进热开水（小心焦糖突然喷溅）。觉得浓稠，可再多加进一些热开水来调整。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13584518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13584518" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584518-Caramel-pudding-step12-e1645104865927.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13584518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13584518-Caramel-pudding-step12-e1645104865927.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13584518" class="wp-caption-text">（18食堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Q&amp;A</strong><strong>：</strong>（节录自《<span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://icook.tw/recipes/86371">焦糖布丁 15分钟_电锅料理 逼近几X甜</a></span>》留言）：</p>
<p>Q：全蛋黄可以吗？使用植物性奶油可以吗？</p>
<p>A：全蛋黄的话，布丁体会非常绵密！鲜奶油只能用动物性的，才能更添奶香</p>
<p>味。</p>
<p>Q：不加香草荚可以吗？</p>
<p>A：一定要添加香草荚，或香草粉，或兰姆酒。不加的话，会很像甜蒸蛋。</p>
<p>Q：香草荚长什么样的？要去哪里买?</p>
<p>A：香草荚就是图二那只黑黑的（我只用半根）。在贩售烘焙材料的“食品材料行”可买得到，放在冷冻柜中。</p>
<p>Q：为什么把香草籽刮出？之后还要加进去，增加香气吗？</p>
<p>A：布丁体最后只会保留香草籽，所以香草籽才会事先刮出来。一起煮比较香，把香草荚的香气利用完全。（网站专文，禁止转载）</p>
