<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探索日本寿司历史 为什么只用海鲜做寿司？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13590449-shutterstock_404160610-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本寿司多搭配海鲜类一起食用是有历史渊源的。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>提到日本食物，很多人都会想到<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>。然而，寿司的出现却不是源自于日本。此外，<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">日本寿司</a>多是搭配<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>类为主，这是为什么呢？就让我们一起来探索“<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">日本寿司</a>”的前世来源吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11189109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11189109" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/shutterstock_295189109.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11189109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/shutterstock_295189109-450x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11189109" class="wp-caption-text">日本<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>来源的时间很早。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>寿司的历史</h3>
<p>东南亚种植稻米的地区在公元前就出现了“<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%9F%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">熟寿司</a>”的前身，并和<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱼</a>露一起流传到邻近区域。</p>
<p>起因是在水田里的小渠道抓到了<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱼</a>，将之跟煮好的饭拌在一起会产生乳酸发酵，经此手续制作而成的食物可以长期保存。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%9F%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">熟寿司</a>很可能是在公元前4~3世纪，和稻作一起从中国传到日本，到了7~8世纪已遍布日本全国。</p>
<p>当时的<span style="color: #000080;">熟寿司，因为用来发酵的饭粒会变得黏黏的，吃的时候会把饭粒弄掉。也就是说，不是当作主食而是像腌渍物、酱菜那样用来配饭的</span>。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>类会使用鲍鱼、香鱼、鲫鱼、贻贝、竹荚鱼、鲷鱼、沙丁鱼、青花鱼、鲑鱼等，种类繁多。此外，现在也会用鹿、山猪等肉类来制作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13589931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13589931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589931-767ebc5dd58eaee6f4eb0f82e8a8f2e6.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13589931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589931-767ebc5dd58eaee6f4eb0f82e8a8f2e6-450x688.png" alt="" width="450" b="688" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13589931" class="wp-caption-text">寿司的历史。（漫游者文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公元538年忌杀生食肉的佛教传入日本，公元675（天武4）年，天武天皇下达了禁肉令，禁止食用牛、马、狗、猴、鸡等肉类，就此奠定日本人以鱼为主的饮食基础。</p>
<p>日本在平安时代开始将“鲊”和“鮨”称为“スシ”（读音为“sushi”），当时汉字写作“酒志”或“须之”）。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13589932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13589932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589932-86a94b6779019ca09bba309e655874b7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13589932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589932-86a94b6779019ca09bba309e655874b7-450x646.png" alt="" width="450" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13589932" class="wp-caption-text">寿司的历史。（漫游者文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13589901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13589901" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589901-9789864894161_bc.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13589901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13589901-9789864894161_bc-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13589901" class="wp-caption-text">《图解寿司辞典》书封。（漫游者文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
