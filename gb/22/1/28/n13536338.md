<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台虎山环状步道 登高眺望101</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536348-535648-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从圆满广场俯瞰台北101大楼。（刘宸豪／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月28日讯】（大纪元记者施芝吟报导）※疫情期间，敬请民众出门务必戴好口罩、勤消毒、多洗手、出入，落实实名制QR码与纸本登记，遵守相关防疫规定。并请随时留意中央流行疫情指挥中心发布之最新讯息。平安、健康，度过金<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>。</p>
<figure id="13536352" aria-describedby="caption-13536352" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536352-535653-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536352-535653-450x253.jpg" alt="四兽广场的石柱造景特色地标。" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536352" class="wp-caption-text">四兽广场的石柱造景特色地标。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536351" aria-describedby="caption-13536351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536351-535652-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536351-535652-450x253.jpg" alt="圆满广场可俯瞰台北101大楼。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536351" class="wp-caption-text">圆满广场可俯瞰台北101大楼。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536349" aria-describedby="caption-13536349" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536349-535650-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536349-535650-450x253.jpg" alt="终于到达圆满广场，看到台北101大楼了！" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536349" class="wp-caption-text">终于到达圆满广场，看到台北101大楼了！（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536343" aria-describedby="caption-13536343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536343-535641-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536343-535641-450x253.jpg" alt="虎啸亭。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536343" class="wp-caption-text">虎啸亭。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于台北市信义区的<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B1%B1.md#1">虎山</a>，与附近的象山、豹山、狮山并称“四兽山”。除了为人所知的象山，虎山的名气也不遑多让。</p>
<figure id="13536355" aria-describedby="caption-13536355" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536355-535790-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536355-535790-450x253.jpg" alt="步道入口处有个“四兽山-虎山”艺术品。中间是蕨类，虎山中的蕨类多达70多种。" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536355" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a>入口处有个“四兽山-<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B1%B1.md#1">虎山</a>”艺术品。中间是蕨类，虎山中的蕨类多达70多种。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536356" aria-describedby="caption-13536356" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536356-535791-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536356-535791-450x253.jpg" alt="虎山环状步道登山口处。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536356" class="wp-caption-text">虎山环状<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E9%81%93.md#1">步道</a>登山口处。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从松山慈惠堂可以进入“虎山溪步道”登山口，一开始的路线是平缓的铺石路，虎山溪水涓涓从旁流过，行走间充满凉爽之意。</p>
<figure id="13536341" aria-describedby="caption-13536341" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536341-535638-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536341-535638-450x253.jpg" alt="松山慈惠堂登山口。" width="602" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536341" class="wp-caption-text">松山慈惠堂登山口。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>步行约3分钟，即可看到长满青苔的拱桥，一片绿荫中，这座石拱桥充满古意，拍起照来十分有特色！顺着虎山溪前行，会看到虎啸亭跟一个小广场。在虎啸亭稍微休息后，继续前进探索虎山步道之美吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13536340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13536340" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536340-535637.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13536340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536340-535637-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13536340" class="wp-caption-text">虎山溪步道有丰富的生态环境，更营造出中式园林般的复古美景，石拱桥也成为许多人慕名而来的打卡热门地标。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536342" aria-describedby="caption-13536342" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536342-535640-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536342-535640-450x253.jpg" alt="走上石拱桥上可以看到幽美的景色。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536342" class="wp-caption-text">走上石拱桥上可以看到幽美的景色。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536344" aria-describedby="caption-13536344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536344-535642-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536344-535642-450x253.jpg" alt="从另一个角度所拍摄的虎啸亭。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536344" class="wp-caption-text">从另一个角度所拍摄的虎啸亭。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再约步行15分钟，即可抵达“四兽亭”、“四兽广场”。此处有以著名的虎、豹、狮、象四兽作为标记的广场造景，石刻立柱代表四兽山耸立于此标志。</p>
<figure id="13536347" aria-describedby="caption-13536347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536347-535645-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536347-535645-450x253.jpg" alt="四兽广场。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536347" class="wp-caption-text">四兽广场。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536346" aria-describedby="caption-13536346" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536346-535644-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536346-535644-450x253.jpg" alt="四兽广场是虎山溪步道的重要景点，透过虎、豹、狮、象四兽的概念，打造了一个意象广场。" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536346" class="wp-caption-text">四兽广场是虎山溪步道的重要景点，透过虎、豹、狮、象四兽的概念，打造了一个意象广场。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虎山步道沿途会经过许多宫庙，仿佛是在保佑过路的旅人。通过四兽广场后，走一段木栈道，抵达南天宫后，再经过真光禅寺，若走真光禅寺路线，可通往虎山生态步道。而选择往忠义宫方向走，则是较为陡峭的阶梯路程！续走到制高点，即可看见“圆满广场”，这里不仅是歇息的好地方，辽阔的视野更是赏景的最佳地点，如果天气好，可以看到台北101大楼。</p>
<figure id="13536350" aria-describedby="caption-13536350" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536350-535651-450x342.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536350-535651-450x342.jpg" alt="圆满广场。" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536350" class="wp-caption-text">圆满广场。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536353" aria-describedby="caption-13536353" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536353-535654-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536353-535654-450x253.jpg" alt="下山沿路都是阶梯，顺畅好走。" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536353" class="wp-caption-text">下山沿路都是阶梯，顺畅好走。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>爬虎山，建议大家一定要走到虎山观景台，欣赏整个大台北壮阔的美景。此外，在步道中，也有隐藏着一只石老虎喔！快点上山找找它在哪儿吧！◇</p>
