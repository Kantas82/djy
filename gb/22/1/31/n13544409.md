<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>洛城圣峪华协“台湾华语文中心”福虎生风</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544438-BB3A724D-B983-46B1-A7B3-B7023D25A97E-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文化课程主题为“福虎祥风爱心虎”。（洛侨中心）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年01月31日讯】开学啰！洛杉矶<ahref="https://github.com/ccmfrm3873/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E5%B3%AA%E5%8D%8E%E5%8D%8F%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">圣峪华协中文学校</a>“<ahref="https://github.com/ccmfrm3873/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%8D%8E%E8%AF%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">台湾华语文学习中心</a>”第二期课程于1月下旬展开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13544442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13544442" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544442-143CAA7F-4FEB-49B6-8688-616D57D8C50B.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13544442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544442-143CAA7F-4FEB-49B6-8688-616D57D8C50B-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13544442" class="wp-caption-text">学员认真学习剪纸。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13544446" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13544446" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544446-79124AA8-D04C-41CA-8850-AFF9F98A724E.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13544446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544446-79124AA8-D04C-41CA-8850-AFF9F98A724E-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13544446" class="wp-caption-text">学员开心展示剪纸作品。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>适逢<ahref="https://github.com/ccmfrm3873/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中国新年</a>到来，圣峪华协会长杨贤怡首先以“一元复始，万象更新”向全体师生贺年。他并致赠每位同学精致小礼物，包括由侨务委员会赠送的2022年“侨见台湾之美”精美月历、虎年小提灯，以及观光局记事本等。礼品充分展现台湾自然与人文的美丽风景，助学员进一步认识台湾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13544449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13544449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544449-C76698A5-AFAD-40B9-9286-8A97CE915921.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13544449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544449-C76698A5-AFAD-40B9-9286-8A97CE915921-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13544449" class="wp-caption-text">侨委会致赠的虎年提灯活泼可爱。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化课程由刘玉美老师向学员介绍新年习俗及十二生肖，学员们剪纸制作可爱小老虎，并以书法练习“福、虎、祥”等正体中文字。接着由林晓靑老师带领学员复习拼音、数字、练习四声及抑扬顿挫，认识“虎”字相关成语；另学习电话应答实用对话，师生互动活泼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13544452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13544452" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544452-S__233537580.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13544452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13544452-S__233537580-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13544452" class="wp-caption-text">学员开心展示剪纸作品。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2022年在欧美地区将有45所“<ahref="https://github.com/ccmfrm3873/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%8D%8E%E8%AF%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">台湾华语文学习中心</a>”运营，各地18岁以上人士均可报名学习具台湾自由特色之华语文，并认识台湾经验。侨委会欢迎侨界先进踊跃向主流推广宣传“台湾华语文学习中心设置计划”，在侨校或侨团推广成立更多“台湾华语文学习中心”，课程细节请洽：<ahref="https://github.com/ccmfrm3873/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E5%B3%AA%E5%8D%8E%E5%8D%8F%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">圣峪华协中文学校</a>（SFVCCA Chinese School）。</p>
