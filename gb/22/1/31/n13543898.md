<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一锅一餐 省时美味的地瓜泡菜锅饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/id13558631-p50-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>地瓜泡菜锅饭。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div><strong>编者按：</strong>来吃“<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E9%A5%AD.md#1">锅饭</a>”吧！在被时间追着跑、身心俱疲很想好好吃一顿饭时，只要将蔬菜、海鲜、肉类放入锅中炊煮，倒入快速高汤、加入白米，不需要花费太多心思与时间，一锅集结营养和美味的丰盛料理，轻松上桌。</div>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%85%E9%A5%AD.md#1">锅饭</a></strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>主材料</strong></span></h3>
<p>米 ························2杯<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a> ······················1条（300g）<br />
韩式<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a> ··················130g<br />
黑芝麻、葱花 ··············各少许<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B8%83.md#1">昆布</a>高汤 ··················300ml</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>调味料</strong></span></h3>
<p>野生芝麻油 ·················1大匙<br />
浓酱油 ·····················1大匙<br />
砂糖 ·······················1小匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B8%83.md#1">昆布</a>高汤</strong>作法</span></h2>
<p>煮锅饭时最常使用的高汤，和米饭很搭，能够增添鲜甜的滋味。</p>
<p>水500ml＋昆布1片(5cm×5cm)<br />
水煮滚后放入昆布，煮约5分钟，待冷却后即可使用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1 .白米洗干净，用清水浸泡30分钟后沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543916-p51-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543916-p51-1-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543916" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 .地瓜去皮、洗净后，切成厚度2公分的圆片；将泡菜上的酱料刮干净，再切成小片状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543917-p51-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543917-p51-2-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543917" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 . 饭锅预热后淋1大匙野生芝麻油，先放切好的泡菜拌炒，再放入1小匙砂糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543918-p51-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543918-p51-3-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543918" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 .稍微翻炒后，放入浸泡过的米、1大匙浓酱油拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543919-p51-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543919-p51-4-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543919" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 .接着放上地瓜、倒入300ml昆布高汤，用中火煮5分钟后，再转小火续煮10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13543915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13543915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543915-p50.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13543915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13543915-p50-450x615.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="615" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13543915" class="wp-caption-text">地瓜泡菜锅饭。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 .煮好后，不掀盖关火焖5分钟，最后撒上黑芝麻及葱花即可。⊙</p>
