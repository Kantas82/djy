<a href="https://d3uam2jhpjrvu5.cloudfront.net/A8cY3gtyj"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nesqhf3299/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>1月22日在侨教中心，嘉宾们挥毫迎春。左起：侨教中心主任潘昭荣、林贤琪老师、伍振中老师、牛顿高地社区发展协会主任John Rice、经文处处长孙俭元、侨务咨询委员蒋宗壬、纽英崙中华书法会会长黄周丽桃和林卓培。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）为庆祝中华民国111年<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>的到来，波士顿经文处及侨教中心联合侨团，于1月22日和23日举办“<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%8E%E6%98%A5%E6%8C%A5%E6%AF%AB.md#1">迎春挥毫</a>”活动。在侨教中心和中华公所，多位本地区书法家现场书写<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A5%E6%98%A5.md#1">挥春</a>，赠与来客。</p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-a549d9d4-9e85-444e-bb19-3d6e36054bb0" data-id="player-a549d9d4-9e85-444e-bb19-3d6e36054bb0"></div></div><a src="//youmaker.com/assets/player/a549d9d4-9e85-444e-bb19-3d6e36054bb0?r=16x9&amp;d=191&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F22%2F1%2F25%2Fn13527637.md#1"></a>
<p>波士顿地区今年的“<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%8E%E6%98%A5%E6%8C%A5%E6%AF%AB.md#1">迎春挥毫</a>”活动由纽英崙中华公所、纽英崙艺术学会、中华书法会、驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处及波士顿华侨文教服务中心联合举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13527577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527577-DSC05316.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527577-DSC05316-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527577" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日，嘉宾们在侨教中心挥毫。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13527585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527585-DSC05339.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527585-DSC05339-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527585" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，纽英崙中华书法会会长林卓培书写<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A5%E6%98%A5.md#1">挥春</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13527590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527590-DSC05348.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527590-DSC05348-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527590" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，纽英崙中华书法会会长黄周丽桃书写挥春。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月22日，侨教中心的活动以嘉宾们现场挥毫揭幕。纽英崙中华书法会会长林卓培、黄周丽桃，本地著名书画艺术家伍振中在台前笔走龙蛇；经文处处长孙俭元、侨教中心主任潘昭荣、侨务咨询委员蒋宗壬也提笔书写挥春。牛顿高地社区发展协会主任莱斯（John Rice）亦参与其中，在伍振中老师的指导下学写中文字。</p>
<p>“今年我写了‘虎’。我会看明年的（生肖）动物是什么。我会为下一个1月，下一年准备好。”莱斯说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13527578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527578-DSC05325.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527578-DSC05325-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527578" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，义工们裁剪用于书写<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>的红纸。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13527580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527580-DSC05336.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527580-DSC05336-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527580" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，林贤琪老师的拓印艺术受到小朋友的喜爱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天中午前后，许多侨胞及喜爱书法的人士来到侨教中心，观看书法家们创作，并带走墨迹刚干的书法作品。此外，林贤琪老师还带来了拓印艺术展示，印出来的生肖图案受到访客们，尤其孩子们的喜爱。</p>
<p>“随着时代的变迁，手写<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>越来越少。所以，这边有这么多书法专家帮我们手写春联，我觉得非常有意义。”孙俭元说。他欢迎大家来侨教中心，观赏和索取书法家们写下的春联。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13527583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527583-DSC05350.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527583-DSC05350-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527583" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，伍振中老师（中）向来宾们赠送他的艺术作品。左为经文处处长孙俭元，右为中华公所主席雷国辉。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13527582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527582-DSC05337.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527582-DSC05337-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527582" class="wp-caption-text">1月22日在侨教中心，书法家们写就挥春，供大家索取。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周日在纽英崙中华公所，长年参与迎春挥毫的林卓培、黄周丽桃、伍振中、甄云龙、池元山和梅宇国都再度出席。来自中国山东，目前随夫人在哈佛大学访学的书法艺术家常惟璞也到场共襄盛举。中华广教学校的老师们还把一些学习中文的孩子带了过来，与书法家们一起教他们运笔书写汉字。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13527588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527588-DSC05356.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527588-DSC05356-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527588" class="wp-caption-text">1月23日在中华公所，艺术家常惟璞（左）和甄云龙（右）挥毫迎春。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13527591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527591-DSC05359.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527591-DSC05359-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527591" class="wp-caption-text">1月23日在中华公所，伍振中老师（右）指导广教学校的孩子们书写汉字。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>书法会会长林卓培对此感到非常高兴。他说，已经很多年没看到孩子们来参加挥毫了，很高兴看到他们再来体验中华文化。林卓培亦感谢各位书法家和主办方的支持，让这个活动每年都能成功举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13527593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13527593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527593-DSC05371.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13527593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13527593-DSC05371-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13527593" class="wp-caption-text">1月23日在中华公所，主办方嘉宾和挥毫的艺术家们合影。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所主席雷国辉说，很荣幸能与大家一起举办这个活动，鼓励年轻一辈来这里实习中华文化。他还特别称赞了前来体验书法的广教学校学生们，鼓励他们“努力学习，多多欣赏”这项博大精深的艺术。◇</p>
