<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>落实视听歌唱业及零售大卖场防疫宣导</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528610-538906-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>	钱柜服务人员清洁包厢。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）因应新冠肺炎新型变种病毒(Omicron)肆虐，国内本土传播感染案例增加，为强化视听歌唱业、零售大卖业等相关业者<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB%E6%8E%AA%E6%96%BD.md#1">防疫措施</a>，嘉义市政府建设处全力戒备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13528611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528611" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528611-538907.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528611-538907-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528611" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%B1%E6%9F%9C.md#1">钱柜</a>提供杀菌过的麦克风套。<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>建设处长田长沛24日下午4时偕同消保官黄崇杰、法务部调查局嘉义市调查站调查官郑明辉、市府建设处、卫生局及警察局联合宣导小组前往本市零售大卖场、视听歌唱业场所，现场勘查防疫及物价波动情形，督促业者严格遵守量体温、加强环境清消、员工健康管理，并提醒现场消费者遵守唱歌不脱口罩等规定，齐心合作才能保护自己及家人，平安健康过新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13528612" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528612-538908.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528612-538908-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528612" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E4%B9%90%E7%A6%8F.md#1">家乐福</a>今年新增容留人数标示牌。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>田长沛表示，疫情升温，我们来到大卖场、KTV宣导并检查有无做好防疫工作，发现均有做好实联制、量体温及酒精擦拭手部，相较去年，<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E4%B9%90%E7%A6%8F.md#1">家乐福</a>更于今年新增容留人数标示牌，人数到达客满，即禁止消费者入内，但是还是请大家做好防疫工作，保护自己，也保护他人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13528613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528613" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528613-538909.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528613-538909-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528613" class="wp-caption-text">麦克风上面装了麦克风套。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄崇杰表示，家乐福对于年节的食品设有特价专区、买一送一专区，以家乐福的物价来说，算是平稳的情况。</p>
<p>建设处表示，当日查核<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%B1%E6%9F%9C.md#1">钱柜</a>KTV、家乐福嘉义店二家，业者与民众均自主配合防疫指引，确实执行入口处实联制、量体温等顾客管理，并加强通风换气，定时执行相关环境及物品清洁消毒，民众除饮食外，均全程佩戴口罩。因传播管道包含飞沫传染及接触传染，业者对于从业人员及顾客经常接触的环境及物品，包含洗手间、包厢、麦克风等，务必要提高清洁及消毒的频率，才能全力的防堵疫情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13528614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528614" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528614-538910.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528614-538910-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528614" class="wp-caption-text">防疫实联制。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>建设处强调，中央流行疫情指挥中心日前宣布除外出时有饮食需求、符合指挥中心或主管机关的相关<ahref="https://github.com/nesqhf3299/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB%E6%8E%AA%E6%96%BD.md#1">防疫措施</a>等少数例外情形，能暂时脱下口罩外，外出一律全程佩戴口罩，其中唱歌、拍照及直播、录影等活动进行时，均须佩戴口罩，倘经查获场所人员劝导不听，将依传染病防治法处三千元以上一万五千元以下罚锾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13528615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528615" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528615-538911.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528615-538911-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528615" class="wp-caption-text">定时清洁手推车。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13528616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528616" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528616-538912.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528616-538912-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528616" class="wp-caption-text">建设处长田长沛视察卖场。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13528617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13528617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528617-538913.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13528617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13528617-538913-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13528617" class="wp-caption-text">卫生局进行稽查工作。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
