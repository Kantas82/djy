<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>人气甜点不藏私 DIY酸甜滋味草莓蛋糕卷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517768-51c545fe72787d8236eee7ef4de5348c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>草莓酸甜滋味搭配绵密鲜奶油，有一种幸福的感觉！（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：等待了一年，只有在岁末才能吃到的“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a>”，如果再搭配绵密滑顺的鲜奶油及柔软的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>，一口咬下真是难以形容的满足感。现在，在家就可以自己动手做，而且可以作为新年赠送亲友的伴手礼，诚意十足。日本人气甜点师森崎茧香不藏私，将自己在意大利和法国的厨房累积丰厚经验，确实的传授出大人味、微甜疗愈蛋糕的做法，简单好学，不需要烤模也能做出美观、美味的甜点。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a></span></h2>
<p>依照不同的装饰手法，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>（Rollcake）也能摇身一变成为华丽的装饰<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>！不仅好切分，也方便携带。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517757" style="width: 308px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517757-30.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13517757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517757-30-308x400.jpg" alt="" width="308" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517757" class="wp-caption-text">只要会做海绵蛋糕片，要做任何蛋糕都会省力很多。图为<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">草莓蛋糕卷</a>（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料 长度约33cm的蛋糕卷1条</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><a style="color: #000000;" href=><a href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/22/1/16/n13508641.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">海绵蛋糕片</a></strong></span> — 1 片<br />
鲜奶油<br />
液态鲜奶油（乳脂肪比例45%） — 300ml<br />
细砂糖 — 1 又1/2 大匙<br />
糖浆<br />
基本糖浆 — 2 大匙 <strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">（制作方法如下）</span></strong><br />
樱桃白兰地Kirsch — 1/2 大匙<br />
草莓 — 12 个</p>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">基本糖浆制作方法～</span></strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">让海绵蛋糕片湿润，与奶油融为一体的“糖浆”作法。</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span>?好做的份量<br />
细砂糖 — 2 大匙<br />
水 — 4 大匙</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【作法】</strong></span><br />
1. 在耐热大碗中倒入细砂糖和水。<br />
2. 用600W 的微波炉加热30 秒，放凉。</p>
<p><strong>※ 如果搅拌后细砂糖没有全部溶解，就再多加热10 秒。</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">准备</span></h3>
<p>将糖浆的材料拌匀。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">草莓蛋糕卷</a>作法</span></h3>
<p>１.草莓去蒂后，取出6个当装饰用，剩下的切成1.5cm 块状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517758" style="width: 267px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517758-31-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13517758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517758-31-1-267x400.jpg" alt="" width="267" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517758" class="wp-caption-text">草莓去蒂后，取出6 个当装饰用（左），剩下的切成1.5cm 块状（右）。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>２.在大碗中放入鲜奶油的材料，将大碗底部放在冰水中，以手持搅拌器打至8 分发（取出1/5 放入冰箱冷藏）。剩下的继续打至9 分发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517759" style="width: 267px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517759-31-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13517759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517759-31-2-267x400.jpg" alt="" width="267" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517759" class="wp-caption-text">大碗4/5份量的鲜奶油，小碗1/5份量的鲜奶油。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>３.将海绵蛋糕片上的烘焙纸撕下，表面朝上横放。将离尾端1cm 的地方斜斜地切掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517760" style="width: 267px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517760-31-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13517760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517760-31-3-267x400.jpg" alt="" width="267" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517760" class="wp-caption-text">海绵蛋糕片上的烘焙纸撕下，表面朝上横放。将离尾端1cm 的地方斜斜地切掉。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>４.准备新的烘焙纸，放上海绵蛋糕片。整片刷上糖浆，涂上鲜奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517761" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517761-31-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517761-31-4-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517761" class="wp-caption-text">准备新的烘焙纸，放上海绵蛋糕片。整片刷上糖浆，涂上鲜奶油。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>５.距离尾端1cm 的地方不要涂鲜奶油，离身体越远的地方，鲜奶油要涂得越薄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517762" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517762-31-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517762-31-5-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517762" class="wp-caption-text">鲜奶油涂得薄薄的即可。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>６.将内馅用的草莓间隔2cm 排成横向２列。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517889" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517889-32-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517889-32-1-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517889" class="wp-caption-text">将内馅用的草莓间隔2cm 排成横向２列。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>７.将海绵蛋糕片往前折卷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517890" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517890-32-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517890-32-2-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517890" class="wp-caption-text">将海绵蛋糕片往前折卷。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>８.翻起底下的烘焙纸，一口气卷好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517891" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517891-32-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517891-32-3-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517891" class="wp-caption-text">翻起底下的烘焙纸，一口气卷好。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>９.将尺压在烘焙纸上确实包好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517892" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517892-32-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517892-32-4-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517892" class="wp-caption-text">将尺压在烘焙纸上确实包好。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>１０.卷好后拿下烘焙纸包上保鲜膜，放进冰箱冷藏1 个小时以上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517893" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517893-32-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517893-32-5-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517893" class="wp-caption-text">卷好后拿下烘焙纸包上保鲜膜，放进冰箱冷藏 1 个小时以上。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>１１.取下保鲜膜，以温热的刀子将蛋糕卷两端切掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517894" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517894-32-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517894-32-6-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517894" class="wp-caption-text">取下保鲜膜，以温热的刀子将蛋糕卷两端切掉。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>１２.以汤匙倒上事先取出的鲜奶油，用汤匙背面左右移动画出纹路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517895" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517895-32-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517895-32-7-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517895" class="wp-caption-text">用汤匙背面左右移动画出纹路。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>１３.尽量平均地倒在整条蛋糕卷上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13517896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13517896" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517896-32-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-13517896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13517896-32-8-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13517896" class="wp-caption-text">尽量平均地倒在整条蛋糕卷上。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>１４.放上装饰用的草莓。⊙</p>
