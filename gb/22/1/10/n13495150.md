<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：男子发现被遗弃小狗 帮它找到新家</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495152-Coco-dog-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一男子发现了一只被遗弃的小狗，帮它找到永远的家。（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年01月10日讯】（ 英文大纪元记者Louise Bevan／王净一编译）有一天，一个经常在家乡喂养流浪狗的塞尔维亚男子发现了一只被遗弃的小狗，这牵动了他的心弦。这只小狗饥肠辘辘，孤身一人，正用一只鞋作掩护，这名男子根本不忍心丢下它不管。</p>
<p>两年前，48岁的戈兰·马林科维奇（Goran Marinkovic）在克拉列沃（Kraljevo）街头发现了这只小狗，起名叫可可（Coco），当时外面很冷。</p>
<p>走在一条堆满垃圾的狭窄小路上，戈兰听到了哭声，并跟随声音来到了一个令人伤感的地方。在一只黑色运动鞋旁边，孤零零地坐着一只长着黄黑相间毛发的小狗。戈兰猜测，这只小狗是在母亲不在的情况下用这<ahref="https://github.com/ainhrm380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E9%9E%8B.md#1">双鞋</a>作为保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13495154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495154-coco46789-890-450x512.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13495154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495154-coco46789-890-450x512.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="512" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495154" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13495157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495157-coco6-87-1200x741-600x371.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495157-coco6-87-1200x741-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495157" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他告诉《大纪元时报》，“我就是这样发现它的。”</p>
<p>他说，它“又饿、又冷、身上又湿”。戈兰给了它一些腊肠，在它狼吞虎咽之后，它在鞋子旁边翻了个身，顺势露出了肚子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13495159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495159-coco13-1200x696-600x348.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495159-coco13-1200x696-600x348.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495159" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13495162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495162-coco8-600x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495162-coco8-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495162" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13495165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495165-coco7-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495165-coco7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495165" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>戈兰拍摄下了这一令人痛心的场景，随后在脸书（Facebook）上分享照片，并写道：“这只鞋对它来说很有用，可以躲避雨水和阳光。这只小狗被扔进了垃圾桶，我无意中发现了它。如果我们为它找到一个能够并且愿意照顾它的人，那就太好了”。</p>
<p>由于不忍心丢下它，戈兰抱起可可，把它送到一个兽医那里进行评估。他不确定它是否有足够的能力生存。</p>
<p>但这只小狗的<ahref="https://github.com/ainhrm380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%82%E7%94%9F%E6%84%8F%E5%BF%97.md#1">求生意志</a>仍然很强。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13495169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495169-coco5-5-6-1200x741-600x371.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495169-coco5-5-6-1200x741-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495169" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>戈兰在脸书上分享了可可获救和随后康复的照片，希望为它找到一个完美的永久家园。他想从申请者中筛选出一个潜在的主人，但由于与可可已经建立了特别的感情，他向国际救援领域工作的朋友发出了求救，来帮助它找到理想的家庭。</p>
<p>他终于等来了好消息。打完针后，可可被送到了德国。今天，它是一只快乐、活泼的小狗，在一个充满爱的家庭里过着最好的生活，这要感谢戈兰的宽广胸怀和善良的举动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13495174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495174-Coco2-600x450.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13495174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495174-Coco2-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495174" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13495180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13495180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495180-Coco-450x600.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13495180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13495180-Coco-450x600.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13495180" class="wp-caption-text">（由戈兰·马林科维奇提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“它有一个美丽的家可以尽情享受”，戈兰说，“每一只从街上被救的狗找到一个家，对我来说都是一个胜利。”</p>
<p>可可的救助者每天持续喂养90至110只流浪狗或被遗弃的狗，他希望分享可可的故事能够鼓励其他人力所能及地提供帮助。</p>
<p>“不要把动物扔在街上，因为它们是活生生的生命”，他补充说，“我只是想唤醒人们的良知，这样就不会再有这种情况发生了。”</p>
