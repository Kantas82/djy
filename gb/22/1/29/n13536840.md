<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老中青共同守护 南机场夜市</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536864-535026-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南机场夜市。（刘宸豪／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者施芝吟台湾台北报导）生活在台北又热爱美食小吃的饕客，一定不会错过“南机场<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>”。<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%9C%BA%E5%9C%BA%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">南机场夜市</a>座落于国民住宅建筑物之间的道路，从早到晚都有不同的美食摊贩，形成了极有活力的特殊景象。</p>
<figure id="13536853" aria-describedby="caption-13536853" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536853-535017-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536853-535017-450x253.jpg" alt="南机场夜市人潮。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536853" class="wp-caption-text">南机场<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>人潮。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>受疫情影响，各大夜市爆发关店潮，<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%9C%BA%E5%9C%BA%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">南机场夜市</a>秉持诚信、美味平价的初衷逆势成长。南机场夜市自治会会长林督钦表示，这边主要消费群是周边居民，他形容：“南机场夜市是中正万华的厨房。”林督钦指出，南机场夜市都有二、三代传承，美味依在，重视环境整洁与餐点安全，大家对南机场夜市印象还不错！</p>
<figure id="13536851" aria-describedby="caption-13536851" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536851-535016-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536851-535016-450x253.jpg" alt="华家药炖排骨、鸡肉饭。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536851" class="wp-caption-text">华家<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%AF%E7%82%96%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">药炖排骨</a>、鸡肉饭。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536849" aria-describedby="caption-13536849" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536849-535015-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536849-535015-450x253.jpg" alt="民众吃药炖排骨。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536849" class="wp-caption-text">民众吃<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%AF%E7%82%96%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">药炖排骨</a>。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536848" aria-describedby="caption-13536848" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536848-535014-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536848-535014-450x253.jpg" alt="华家药炖排骨很受民众欢迎。图为老板娘。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536848" class="wp-caption-text">华家药炖排骨很受民众欢迎。图为老板娘。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.好吃炸鸡：该店已有30年历史，选用当天电宰的新鲜鸡肉油炸，鲜嫩多汁的炸鸡，刚好满足大口吃肉的欲望。这家特色就是便宜又大碗的超大鸡排，尺寸比手掌还要大，CP值很高。</p>
<figure id="13536844" aria-describedby="caption-13536844" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536844-535010-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536844-535010-450x253.jpg" alt="好吃炸鸡。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536844" class="wp-caption-text">好吃炸鸡。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.无名手推车烧饼： 2019~2021年，连续3年荣获米其林必比登推荐的小吃。卖烧饼30多年的婆婆，跟亲戚学做江苏烧饼。只要烧饼出炉就马上被秒杀。主要贩卖简单4种饼类：长烧饼、咸酥饼、红豆饼，以及甜酥饼。</p>
<figure id="13536847" aria-describedby="caption-13536847" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536847-535013-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536847-535013-450x253.jpg" alt="烧饼放入炉火中烤。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536847" class="wp-caption-text">烧饼放入炉火中烤。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536846" aria-describedby="caption-13536846" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536846-535012-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536846-535012-450x253.jpg" alt="烧饼放入炉中火烤。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536846" class="wp-caption-text">烧饼放入炉中火烤。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536845" aria-describedby="caption-13536845" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536845-535011-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536845-535011-450x253.jpg" alt="每天有不少民众排队购买无名手推车烧饼。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536845" class="wp-caption-text">每天有不少民众排队购买无名手推车烧饼。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.猪高饭：“猪高饭”许多饕客应该都不陌生，好吃的猪蹄膀饭，必须皮Q肉嫩，搭配好的卤汁及配菜。?“铭乡猪高饭”的猪肉使用中药材熬煮，肉质鲜甜，Q弹带劲油而不腻，淋上独家秘方古早味酱汁提味，更加口齿留香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13536843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13536843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536843-535009.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13536843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536843-535009-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13536843" class="wp-caption-text">铭乡猪高饭的蹄膀。（刘宸豪／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.八栋圆仔汤：林督钦所经营的甜品店位在国宅第八栋，1977年开业，至今已超过40年。除了卖圆仔，也有各式甜汤与冷饮，复古传统的手艺，掳获众多老饕的胃和心。找个时间去感受在地人情味吧！◇</p>
