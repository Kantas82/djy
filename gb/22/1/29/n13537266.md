<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>基隆庙口夜市 台湾指标 国际驰名</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537268-535036-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>基隆庙口夜市全台驰名。（陈秀媛／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者陈秀媛台湾基隆报导）<ahref="https://github.com/vkkpsn345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E9%9A%86%E5%BA%99%E5%8F%A3%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">基隆庙口夜市</a>名列“世界20大名市集”，短短几百公尺内就聚集近200个摊位，<ahref="https://github.com/vkkpsn345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>种类众多，咸的、甜的、热的、冷的应有尽有，许多摊位的历史都是70年以上，每摊都是独一无二的基隆庙口文化，可以满足老饕不同的口腹之欲，让人来了还想再来。</p>
<p>基隆市爱四路商圈林立，每到下午4点就变成夜市，与仁三路奠济宫庙口两侧的摊位连成一片，夜市中每位经营的老板都巧心创作料理，用料实在而且价钱公道、色香味俱全，难怪每天都相当热闹。</p>
<figure id="13537269" aria-describedby="caption-13537269" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537269-535037-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537269-535037-450x338.jpg" alt="奠济宫庙口两侧摊位林立。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13537269" class="wp-caption-text">奠济宫庙口两侧摊位林立。（陈秀媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>著名小吃有天妇罗、鼎边趖、肉羹、蚵仔煎与卤肉饭等，不论是海鲜还是香烤鸡肉、水果冰品或各种小吃都口味道地，还有阿华炒面，经常能看见里面坐满人，门口还排着长长队伍的画面。</p>
<figure id="13537272" aria-describedby="caption-13537272" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537272-535040-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537272-535040-450x338.jpg" alt="螃蟹羹满满人潮。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13537272" class="wp-caption-text">螃蟹羹满满人潮。（陈秀媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13537271" aria-describedby="caption-13537271" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537271-535039-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537271-535039-450x338.jpg" alt="各种海鲜口味道地。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13537271" class="wp-caption-text">各种海鲜口味道地。（陈秀媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13537270" aria-describedby="caption-13537270" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537270-535038-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13537270-535038-450x338.jpg" alt="阿华炒面老饕最爱。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13537270" class="wp-caption-text">阿华炒面老饕最爱。（陈秀媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/vkkpsn345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E9%9A%86%E5%BA%99%E5%8F%A3%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">基隆庙口夜市</a>是台湾的指标夜市，也是个充满活力且聚集台湾传统<ahref="https://github.com/vkkpsn345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>及小吃的地方，有许多令人怀念的老味道，让老饕想起来就口水直流，除了本地人来这里打牙祭之外，观光客也相偕而来，如果想尝一尝台湾的特有美食小吃，基隆庙口可不能错过！◇</p>
