<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寿喜烧沾蛋黄 微咸滑顺让你一口接一口</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538224-P.070-071-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>寿喜烧。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%AE%E6%B6%AE%E9%94%85.md#1">涮涮锅</a>（しゃぶしゃぶ）是把肉片放入滚烫的汤中涮一下就立刻夹起来吃，而<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%96%9C%E7%83%A7.md#1">寿喜烧</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%AE%E6%B6%AE%E9%94%85.md#1">涮涮锅</a>不同，是把肉片和各种食材放到锅中一起滚煮的锅物。把寿喜烧酱煮过的食材，沾点<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E9%BB%84.md#1">蛋黄</a>品尝，微咸又滑顺的口感，让你一口接一口！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%96%9C%E7%83%A7.md#1">寿喜烧</a></span></h2>
<p><strong><u>45</u></strong><strong><u>～50</u></strong><strong><u>分钟／2</u></strong><strong><u>～3</u></strong><strong><u>人份</u></strong><u>?</u></p>
<p>洋葱 1/2颗（100g）<br />
综合菇类 200g<br />
大白菜 3片（90g）<br />
葱 10cm<br />
火锅牛肉片 300g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E9%BB%84.md#1">蛋黄</a> 2颗<br />
<strong>高汤</strong><br />
熬汤用小鱼干 15只<br />
海带 10×10cm1片<br />
清酒（或米酒）1大匙<br />
水 5杯（1L）<br />
柴鱼片 4把（20g）</p>
<p><strong>自制<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE%E7%90%83.md#1">虾球</a></strong><br />
冷冻虾仁 13只（约200g，或用鲜虾去壳）<br />
蛋白 2颗<br />
糯米粉 3大匙<br />
盐 少许<br />
太白粉 3大匙</p>
<p><strong>酱汁</strong><br />
糖 1/2大匙<br />
酱油 1又1/2大匙<br />
味醂 1大匙</p>
<h2>?<span style="color: #339966;"><strong>制作高汤</strong></span></h2>
<p>1.在预热过的汤锅中放入熬汤用的小鱼干，以中火炒1分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538225" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538225-P.73-01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538225-P.73-01-450x360.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538225" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.放入海带、清酒、水，以中火煮15分钟。接着撒下柴鱼片后立刻关火。静置1分钟后用筛网过滤，保留清澈高汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538226" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538226-P.73-02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538226-P.73-02-450x295.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538226" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>自制<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE%E7%90%83.md#1">虾球</a></strong></span></h2>
<p>3.冷冻虾仁泡冷水解冻，之后用筛网将水沥干。用食物调理机将虾仁打成泥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538227-P.73-03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538227-P.73-03-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538227" class="wp-caption-text">。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.除了太白粉外，将其他自制虾球食材放入碗中，充分搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538228" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538228-P.73-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538228-P.73-04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538228" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.在保鲜膜上放上一口大小的步骤④，捏出丸子形状后，冷藏5分钟使虾球成形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538248-P.73-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538248-P.73-05-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538248" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.拿掉保鲜膜，在虾球表面均匀裹上太白粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538230" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538230-P.73-06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538230-P.73-06-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538230" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.在汤锅里倒入3杯水煮滚，放入步骤⑥的虾球煮2～3分钟，等熟了之后捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538231-P.73-07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538231-P.73-07-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538231" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><strong>开始料理</strong></span></h2>
<p>8.洋葱切丝，综合菇类、大白菜叶洗净均切成一口大小，葱斜切。用厨房纸巾将牛肉片的血水吸干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538232-P.73-08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538232-P.73-08-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538232" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.将汤锅预热好后，放入洋葱以大火炒1分钟，之后把洋葱集中到一边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538233-P.73-09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538233-P.73-09-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538233" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.放入牛肉和酱汁食材，继续翻炒2～3分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538234-P.73-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538234-P.73-10-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538234" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.所有食材放入锅中，转小火，慢慢倒入步骤②的高汤滚煮即完成。吃的时候可以沾蛋黄享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13538235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13538235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538235-P.73-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13538235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13538235-P.73-11-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13538235" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #000000;">?★享受美味的秘诀★</span></strong></p>
<p>1.推荐吃的时候可以用卡式炉持续加热，就能一直吃到热腾腾的美味，等汤汁减少再慢慢添加。如果咸度不够，可以加入酱油补足。</p>
<p>2.煮好的食材沾蛋黄吃，口感更滑顺也更道地。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">超轻松食材替代法</span></h2>
<p><strong>自制虾球</strong><strong>→</strong><strong>市售虾球、鱼丸</strong></p>
<p>觉得自制虾球费时又麻烦的话，可以直接购买50g的市售虾球，也可用等量的鱼丸替代。</p>
