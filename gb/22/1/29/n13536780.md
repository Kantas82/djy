<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文化路其实不只是夜市</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536782-535768-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文化路商圈。（许嘉麟／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者许嘉麟台湾<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）说到嘉义的<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>，大部分人都会想起位于<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%B7%AF.md#1">文化路</a>的“文化路夜市”，但严格说来它并不是夜市，而是一个有很多“在夜市可以看到的”摊贩的商圈！</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%B7%AF.md#1">文化路</a>上的店家各有各的休息时间，如果想吃遍所有知名店家，游客得多来几趟才能达成。</p>
<h2>在地人隐藏版推荐</h2>
<p>知名的郭家鸡肉饭、林聪明沙锅鱼头、黄毛ㄚ头东山鸭头、方柜仔卤味、炒螺肉等，一到假日总是率先挤满人潮，如果不想等待，建议往两旁或小路里走走。</p>
<figure id="13536784" aria-describedby="caption-13536784" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536784-535770-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536784-535770-450x300.jpg" alt="阿娥豆花。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536784" class="wp-caption-text">阿娥豆花。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536783" aria-describedby="caption-13536783" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536783-535769-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536783-535769-450x300.jpg" alt="郭家鸡肉饭与焦家好味道面食馆。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536783" class="wp-caption-text">郭家鸡肉饭与焦家好味道面食馆。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郭家隔壁“焦家好味道”面食馆的小笼汤包与红油抄手、林聪明附近的“品安豆浆豆花”、邮局前面的“香东山鸭头”、文化公园旁边的“黄记凉面凉圆”，都是会令人惊喜的选择。也有天气转凉后才出现在圆环旁骑楼下的“一盅炖补汤”，一碗加上各式中药材熬煮、还有一只鸡腿的鸡汤仅百元，料好又实在。</p>
<figure id="13536789" aria-describedby="caption-13536789" style="width: 343px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536789-535774-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536789-535774-450x675.jpg" alt="黄毛ㄚ头东山鸭头。" width="343" b="515" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536789" class="wp-caption-text">黄毛ㄚ头东山鸭头。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536788" aria-describedby="caption-13536788" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536788-535773-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536788-535773-450x300.jpg" alt="黄毛ㄚ头东山鸭头。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536788" class="wp-caption-text">黄毛ㄚ头东山鸭头。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536787" aria-describedby="caption-13536787" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536787-535772-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536787-535772-450x300.jpg" alt="一盅炖补汤。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536787" class="wp-caption-text">一盅炖补汤。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化路还有特别的“午夜场”。晚上10时过后，多数店家准备打烊时，会出现另一波摊贩，“珍珍蚵仔煎海产粥”的虱目鱼跟蚵仔煎，“贤记刈包”的刈包与四神汤、香菇肉羹，以及圆环旁的“叶记牛肉面”，可说是隐藏版的文化路必吃美食。</p>
<figure id="13536790" aria-describedby="caption-13536790" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536790-535775-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536790-535775-450x300.jpg" alt="贤记刈包。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536790" class="wp-caption-text">贤记刈包。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536785" aria-describedby="caption-13536785" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536785-535771-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536785-535771-450x300.jpg" alt="珍珍蚵仔煎海产粥。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536785" class="wp-caption-text">珍珍蚵仔煎海产粥。（许嘉麟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不能错过的饮料，还有<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>人必推的“源兴御香屋”，采用新鲜水果、当天现榨果汁，搭配台湾茶叶，如招牌的葡萄柚绿茶、冬季限定的柳丁绿茶、酸酸甜甜的冻顶柠檬、洛神柠檬等，都是屹立不摇的招牌。</p>
<p>文化路<ahref="https://github.com/pxlgjm352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>主要摊贩集中于中山路至垂杨路之间，除了文化路，两旁小路里也有许多美味店家。商圈周边规划有多个停车场，不用担心停车问题。◇</p>
