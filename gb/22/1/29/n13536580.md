<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吃喝玩乐在瑞丰 越夜越精彩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536584-536097-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>三五好友相招，大家作伙来去踅瑞丰夜市。（李晴玳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者李晴玳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/zauykv312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84.md#1">高雄</a>报导）高雄夜市走透透，就属“南六合、北瑞丰 ”最知名。若论规模最大、人气最旺的个中翘楚，首推左营区的瑞丰夜市。</p>
<figure id="13536586" aria-describedby="caption-13536586" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536586-536099-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536586-536099-450x300.jpg" alt="高雄市规模最大、人气最旺的观光夜市——瑞丰夜市。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536586" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/zauykv312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84.md#1">高雄</a>市规模最大、人气最旺的观光夜市——瑞丰夜市。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有别于六合夜市的街市型态，瑞丰夜市以占地广阔、规划完善、品类多样的优势，吸引着在地居民、他方旅人前来朝圣打卡。</p>
<p>坐拥左营黄金地段，号称占地五千坪、云集上千摊商的瑞丰夜市，设有美食区、服饰百货区、游艺区及停车场等。每当华灯初上，明亮多彩的市招，川流不息的人潮，交织成一幅庶民风情画。高雄最精彩的夜生活，就从这里开始……</p>
<p>瑞丰夜市美食区，从南屏、裕诚路口向两端延伸，采Ｌ型集中管理。这里的小吃不但亲民而且美味。除了牛排、烤肉等传统大餐，更有咖喱、炖饭等异国料理；而“卤凤爪 ”、“油吃鬼 ”、“糖葫芦 ”等经典小吃，更成为高雄人的集体记忆。</p>
<figure id="13536590" aria-describedby="caption-13536590" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536590-536762-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536590-536762-450x300.jpg" alt="瑞丰夜市烧烤美食。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536590" class="wp-caption-text">瑞丰夜市烧烤美食。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536585" aria-describedby="caption-13536585" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536585-536098-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536585-536098-450x300.jpg" alt="三五好友相招，大家作伙来去踅瑞丰夜市。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536585" class="wp-caption-text">三五好友相招，大家作伙来去踅瑞丰夜市。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536587" aria-describedby="caption-13536587" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536587-536100-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536587-536100-450x300.jpg" alt="民众购买糖葫芦。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536587" class="wp-caption-text">民众购买糖葫芦。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536583" aria-describedby="caption-13536583" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536583-536096-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536583-536096-450x300.jpg" alt="一家大小开心踅夜市、啖美食。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536583" class="wp-caption-text">一家大小开心踅夜市、啖美食。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瑞丰夜市的最大特色就是商品多样化，流行服饰、皮包鞋帽、日用杂物、3C配件，甚至还有按摩指压，在这里都可以开心挖到宝。</p>
<p>这里更是小朋友的游乐世界！这里有各式各样的<ahref="https://github.com/zauykv312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E6%88%8F.md#1">游戏</a>摊，包括投篮、射气球、套圈圈、弹珠台、棒球九宫格、夹娃娃机等，亲子共享欢乐时光。</p>
<figure id="13536588" aria-describedby="caption-13536588" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536588-536101-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536588-536101-450x300.jpg" alt="在瑞丰夜市流行服饰区，摊商业者叫卖“每顶帽子150元”！" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536588" class="wp-caption-text">在瑞丰夜市流行服饰区，摊商业者叫卖“每顶帽子150元”！（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13536589" aria-describedby="caption-13536589" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536589-536102-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13536589-536102-450x300.jpg" alt="小朋友再接再厉套圈圈。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13536589" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友再接再厉套圈圈。（李晴玳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大家相招来去踅夜市！你将发现除了满足舌尖上的味蕾，还可以鉴赏精心的创意，一盏灯、一个招牌、一张桌子，瑞丰摊商利用有限的小小空间，开展出自己的风格特色。只要你多留心，便可挖掘隐藏在夜市角落的台湾美学与文化。◇</p>
<p>交通资讯：</p>
<p>开市时间：每周二、四、五、六、日／下午6：30至凌晨1：00</p>
<p>捷运：搭乘高雄捷运至巨蛋站，出站后约400公尺抵达夜市。</p>
<p>公车：于高雄火车站搭乘高雄市公车91、301、24号，于高雄市立三民家商下车，再步行即可到达瑞丰夜市。</p>
