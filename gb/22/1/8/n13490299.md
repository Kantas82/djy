<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新北市树林区名师挥毫 春联报喜贺新岁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490303-536121-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>树林火车站的站长与书法名师们一起合影留念。（区公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月08日讯】（大纪元记者宋顺澈台湾新北报导）新北市书法学会、新北市树林美术协会及新北市树林客家乡亲会书法名家们于1月8日早上在火车站2楼大厅举办“名师<ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A5%E6%AF%AB.md#1">挥毫</a> <ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>报喜贺新岁”现场<ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A5%E6%AF%AB.md#1">挥毫</a>写春联赠送活动，现场贴心准备春联对联句范例让民众挑选喜爱的金句请老师书写，透过张贴崭新的春联、招喜的“桃符”来除旧布新，迎接新<ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13490301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13490301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490301-536119.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13490301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490301-536119-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13490301" class="wp-caption-text">民众热烈索取<ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%A5%E8%81%94.md#1">春联</a>。（区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13490302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13490302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490302-536120.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13490302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490302-536120-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13490302" class="wp-caption-text">有艺术图画的春联深获民众喜爱。（区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13490304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13490304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490304-536122.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13490304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490304-536122-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13490304" class="wp-caption-text">获得春连的民众喜悦之情溢于言表。（区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13490305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13490305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490305-536123.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13490305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13490305-536123-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13490305" class="wp-caption-text">艺术书法的图文。（区公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>欢喜迎接黄历<ahref="https://github.com/ugusjm389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>，祝贺大家平安顺利好过年，树林区公所与中嘉家和有线电视共同合作举办2022年“春联报喜贺新岁-名师挥毫活动”活动，活动共计2场次，分别于2022年1月8日（六）及1月9日（日）上午9时至12时于山佳老人会馆，想要免费获得虎年春联者，9日趁早到山佳老人会馆排队领取。</p>
<p>活动开场由新北市树林区乡土文化艺文协会带来充满年节气氛的“春到福到好运到”的精彩舞蹈表演迎嘉宾，加上家和有线电视摊位抽好礼活动，台湾铁路管理局台北运务段的台铁交邮廉享趣大小互动闯关GO活动，让参加民众新春拿好礼，开心过好年。</p>
<p>树林区公所区长王坤南表示，树林区每年举办名师挥毫活动，让家家户户以春联点缀喜迎新年，平安过好年，活动现场也落实配戴口罩等防疫措施，让民众安心拿春联。本次书法名家也欢欣为民众服务，现场表演挥毫的力道、美学、功力，迎来民众的喝采，无论是隶书、楷书、草书，再加上虎年意象的文字及图画，让春联看起来更加活泼有趣，新北市树林区公所祝福大家虎年平安、好运旺旺来。</p>
