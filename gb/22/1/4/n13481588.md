<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2022年整理三款卷发造型不费时</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481604-534111-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2022年发型趋势“蝴蝶烫、曲奇卷、泰迪卷”。（业者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月05日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）新的一年到来，除了换上新装，发型也是转换整体造型与心情的方法。无论是层次卷度、内卷外弯、零碎剪裁，都能以不同的风格来迎接新年。好剪才发廊分享三款不费时、起床就能够出门的长发烫发造型，以及三款热门趋势短发造型。</p>
<p><strong>“零费时”长<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B7%E5%8F%91.md#1">卷发</a> 散发甜美与自信</strong></p>
<p>相较于去年的不规则卷度、绕卷吹弯的繁复整理技巧，今年融入了低层次甚至接近零层次的剪裁架构，不需费时整理，就能在不经意中散发甜美与自信。</p>
<p><strong>蝴蝶烫：柔美优雅</strong></p>
<figure id="13481603" aria-describedby="caption-13481603" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481603-534110-450x673.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481603-534110-450x673.jpg" alt="蝴蝶烫。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481603" class="wp-caption-text">蝴蝶烫。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481605" aria-describedby="caption-13481605" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481605-534112-450x673.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481605-534112-450x673.jpg" alt="蝴蝶烫。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481605" class="wp-caption-text">蝴蝶烫。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卷度简易的蝴蝶烫，能自然的让女孩的整体氛围变得更加柔美。及肩的微弯卷度，视觉上能够延伸脖颈的线条；再透过浏海的轮廓设计，集中呈现五官的立体度。发型师Eason说明，此款烫发风格只要简单吹整完就能出门，还可以呈现出发质的柔亮光泽，容易整理又能展现俐落优雅的气质。</p>
<p><strong>曲奇卷：清新甜美</strong></p>
<figure id="13481601" aria-describedby="caption-13481601" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481601-534108-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481601-534108-450x675.jpg" alt="曲奇卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481601" class="wp-caption-text">曲奇卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481602" aria-describedby="caption-13481602" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481602-534109-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481602-534109-450x675.jpg" alt="曲奇卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481602" class="wp-caption-text">曲奇卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整体呈现出无害甜美的卷度氛围，适合可爱甜美气质的女孩，或者是想要有减龄效果的人。发型师Patrick说明，这款低层次自然波纹的设计，好整理且能营造甜美温暖的气氛；再加上柔和卷度的空气感浏海，修饰脸型外更有无辜可爱的感觉。如果再搭配上雾感棕色发色，会让整体气质更有清新感。</p>
<p><strong>泰迪卷：热情活泼</strong></p>
<figure id="13481599" aria-describedby="caption-13481599" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481599-534106-450x677.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481599-534106-450x677.jpg" alt="泰迪卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481599" class="wp-caption-text">泰迪卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481600" aria-describedby="caption-13481600" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481600-534107-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481600-534107-450x675.jpg" alt="泰迪卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481600" class="wp-caption-text">泰迪卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此款发型卷纹较小，且纹理非常一致。层层堆叠的小卷纹能够呈现出充满活力的氛围，同时也不会显得发量过于厚重。发型师Leo说明，这款泰迪卷非常适合活泼可爱的人，能够展露你的活力自信，再搭配上短浏海的设计，突显五官的立体轮廓，修饰脸型，成为活力女孩。</p>
<p><strong>短发趋势 剪出“一头大人的从容”</strong></p>
<figure id="13481598" aria-describedby="caption-13481598" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481598-534105-450x242.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481598-534105-450x242.jpg" alt="2022年短发趋势剪出“一头大人的从容”。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481598" class="wp-caption-text">2022年短发趋势剪出“一头大人的从容”。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>延续一股短发热潮，想以新发型展开新年的女孩们请注意，2022年的大势短发呈现了“知性、从容、展自信”三大要点，能瞬间增长气势。</p>
<p><strong>云雾卷：敢爱敢恨</strong></p>
<figure id="13481595" aria-describedby="caption-13481595" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481595-534103-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481595-534103-450x675.jpg" alt="云雾卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481595" class="wp-caption-text">云雾卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481597" aria-describedby="caption-13481597" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481597-534104-450x649.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481597-534104-450x649.jpg" alt="云雾卷。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481597" class="wp-caption-text">云雾卷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>云雾卷属于中分短发，中性又带点大气，微<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B7%E5%8F%91.md#1">卷发</a>的设计让人平常出门也简单好整理。发型师Rumi说明，云雾卷的发型结构是一体成型的，层层相连的卷度，能堆叠出厚实的微卷发尾，在脸部形成阴影，塑造出稳重神秘形象，如果搭配上湿润的造型产品，除了能营造出线条感，也能增添大气俐落的氛围。</p>
<p><strong>木丹剪：独立个性</strong></p>
<figure id="13481593" aria-describedby="caption-13481593" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481593-534101-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481593-534101-450x675.jpg" alt="木丹剪。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481593" class="wp-caption-text">木丹剪。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481594" aria-describedby="caption-13481594" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481594-534102-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481594-534102-450x675.jpg" alt="木丹剪。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481594" class="wp-caption-text">木丹剪。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>木丹剪一体成型的纹理设计，灵感源自于“栀子花”瓣型，层层堆叠、轮廓线条分明、同时又相互包覆。发型师Owen说明，木丹剪属于日系风格的低层次短发剪裁，藉由发尾的外翘来修饰下巴偏尖或偏长的部分，具有小脸视觉效果，更有个性，也让整体氛围充满俐落感。</p>
<p><strong>棉花烫：温暖气质</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13481590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13481590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481590-534099.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13481590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481590-534099-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13481590" class="wp-caption-text">棉花烫。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13481591" aria-describedby="caption-13481591" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481591-534100-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13481591-534100-450x675.jpg" alt="棉花烫。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13481591" class="wp-caption-text">棉花烫。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>低层次的“棉花烫”内弯比起“韩系波波烫”C弯，多了一点温柔知性的气质，适合卡在留长过渡期的女孩们。发型师Wen说明，把发长设定在肩上的弯度，不只能修饰脖颈处的线条，还能延伸肩颈的比例，整理起来也非常简单，不用花时间手绕，顺顺的吹干，卷度就能自然成型。◇</p>
