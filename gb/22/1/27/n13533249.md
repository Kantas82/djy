<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市加强督导电影院防疫 市民安心看电影</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533251-539228-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>秀泰影城一楼入口处。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市政府观光新闻处27日会同卫生局在<ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">黄历新年</a>前夕到辖内三家电影院，加强电影院<ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>宣导，一起守护市民健康，让大家安心看电影迎虎年。</p>
<figure id="13533252" aria-describedby="caption-13533252" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533252-539229-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533252-539229-450x337.jpeg" alt="秀泰影城入场前量测体温手部消毒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533252" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%80%E6%B3%B0%E5%BD%B1%E5%9F%8E.md#1">秀泰影城</a>入场前量测体温手部消毒。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为期9天的<ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">黄历新年</a>年假即将到来，许多强档大片抢攻<ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%80%E6%B3%B0%E5%BD%B1%E5%9F%8E.md#1">秀泰影城</a>、嘉年华影城及in89豪华影城等三家电影院大萤幕，到电影院看电影更是许多人过年必去行程。惟新冠肺炎新型变种病毒（Omicron）肆虐，国内本土传播感染案例增加。</p>
<figure id="13533253" aria-describedby="caption-13533253" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533253-539230-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533253-539230-450x337.jpeg" alt="秀泰影城散场清消。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533253" class="wp-caption-text">秀泰影城散场清消。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/hknvps3706/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>优先，市府上紧发条防疫不松懈，要求业者依其防疫规定，务必落实“出入人员全程佩戴口罩”、“实联制”、“量测体温”等防疫措施，同时观影前后加强影厅环境清消、以及民众经常触碰的把手、扶梯、电梯等定时消毒、清洁，让市民有个安全舒适的观影空间。</p>
<figure id="13533254" aria-describedby="caption-13533254" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533254-539231-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533254-539231-450x337.jpeg" alt="嘉年华影城入场检视实联制。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533254" class="wp-caption-text">嘉年华影城入场检视实联制。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，疫情变化多不可掉以轻心，市府提醒市民朋友做好“拱手不握手、勤洗手”，务必遵守防疫规范，出入全程佩戴口罩、并应于饮／食用完毕后，尽速佩戴口罩等相关规定，大家携手合作做好防疫，共享美好假期与精彩刺激的电影。规劝不听，将依“传染病防治法”开罚3,000元以上至15,000元以下罚锾。</p>
<figure id="13533255" aria-describedby="caption-13533255" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533255-539232-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533255-539232-450x337.jpeg" alt="观光新闻处会同卫生局一起看电影院防疫。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533255" class="wp-caption-text">观光新闻处会同卫生局一起看电影院防疫。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13533256" aria-describedby="caption-13533256" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533256-539233-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533256-539233-450x337.jpeg" alt="in89影城出入口实联制与体温监测。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533256" class="wp-caption-text">in89影城出入口实联制与体温监测。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13533257" aria-describedby="caption-13533257" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533257-539234-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13533257-539234-450x337.jpeg" alt=" in89影城场外清消。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13533257" class="wp-caption-text">in89影城场外清消。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
