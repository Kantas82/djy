<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>掌握3窍门 5分钟煮一锅香醇奶茶</h2>
<a href="https://d2cenl0crtmzsc.cloudfront.net/Z6iucH1ye"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yrfqrx350/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/yrfqrx350/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>掌握3窍门，5分钟便可煮好一锅香醇浓郁的奶茶。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按</strong><strong>：</strong>喜爱喝奶茶，却担心市售<ahref="https://github.com/yrfqrx350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E9%A5%AE.md#1">茶饮</a>内含的糖量、人工添加剂和茶的品质。此时，不妨来杯自己煮的奶茶。Tasty Note教你掌握3个窍门，5分钟煮好一锅香醇浓郁的奶茶，轻松享受无地雷、无食安疑虑的饮料。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #800000;">小窍门</span>：</strong></p>
<p>1. 火力从最初到最后都必须是大火！煮红茶一定要用煮开的热水。</p>
<p>2. 牛奶于沸腾后香味会散去，绝对不能让牛奶沸腾。换言之，看到一点起泡那刻就要立刻关火。</p>
<p>3. 不同品种茶叶煮出来浓度不尽相同。茶叶挑选，以大吉岭或锡兰红茶搭配牛奶最合适。</p>
<p><strong>食材（1</strong><strong>～2</strong><strong>人份）</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>红茶茶叶2汤匙（10公克）</p>
<p>全脂牛奶1杯</p>
<p>水1杯</p>
<p><strong>时间：</strong>5分钟</p>
<p><strong>步骤</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>1. 首先用大火煮一锅水至滚烫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13520153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520153" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520153-brew-milk-tea-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520153-brew-milk-tea-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520153" class="wp-caption-text">煮开200cc的水。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 水滚后，持续开着大火，放入红茶茶叶煮3分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13520156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520156" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520156-brew-milk-tea-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520156-brew-milk-tea-2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520156" class="wp-caption-text">选择茶味比绿茶浓的红茶。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 在大火煮茶叶的3分钟当中，锅子会如照片一样，请别担心会烧干。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">＊此步骤很重要，绝对不要转为弱火。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13520159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520159-brew-milk-tea-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520159-brew-milk-tea-3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520159" class="wp-caption-text">茶叶被煮开的时候，会慢慢飘出茶香。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 3分钟一到，加入全脂牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13520161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520161-brew-milk-tea-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520161-brew-milk-tea-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520161" class="wp-caption-text">水和牛奶最佳比例是1:1。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 继续煮至沸点。此时请寸步不离地盯着水面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13520163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520163-brew-milk-tea-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520163-brew-milk-tea-5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520163" class="wp-caption-text">煮奶茶，自始至终都得是大火。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. <span style="color: #800000;">沸点一到，即冒泡的那一刻，就立刻关火。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13520166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520166" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520166-brew-milk-tea-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520166-brew-milk-tea-6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520166" class="wp-caption-text">煮牛奶时，沸点一到，立刻关火，以免破坏香醇口感。（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着滤掉茶叶渣，就得到一壶浓浓香香的奶茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13520172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13520172" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520172-brew-milk-tea-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13520172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13520172-brew-milk-tea-7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13520172" class="wp-caption-text">（Tasty Note提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Q&amp;A</strong>（节录自“<span style="color: #000000;"><a style="color: #000000;" href="https://icook.tw/recipes/266591">锅煮奶茶作法</a></span>”留言）：</p>
<p>Q: 水一杯是几cc？</p>
<p>A: 大概200cc。</p>
<p>Q: 把一般红茶包剪开吗？</p>
<p>A: 是的。如果你是用茶叶的话，直接倒进锅里煮，就好了。</p>
<p>Q: 水和牛奶比例是1:1吗？</p>
<p>A: 对！水和牛奶比例是1:1。</p>
<p>Q: 如果要加糖，在什么时候加？</p>
<p>A: 煮好后加糖。</p>
<p>Q: 如果奶茶凉掉了，再重新加热，是否也一样不能让奶茶煮滚至沸腾状态呢？</p>
<p>A: 是的，因为牛奶煮至沸点就会失去奶香味，注意不要煮沸。（网站专文）</p>
