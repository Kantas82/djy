<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>如何改造，可使房屋升值超50%？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499222-01.jpg" />
<h6>巧妙改造老房子，可增大居住空间、提升房产价值。（Liberty Building Contractors）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月12日讯】房子老旧、空间不足，但又不想搬家？那就在您家现有土地上进行房屋扩建、加建或推倒重建，让您在熟悉的社区中拥有梦想家居，不仅可省去印花税和重新买房的麻烦，还能充分利用您的房产净值（equity），减轻资金压力。巧妙改造可大幅增加居住空间，甚至使房子升值50%或更多！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class=" wp-image-13499223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499223-02.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499223" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E6%89%A9%E5%BB%BA.md#1">悉尼房屋扩建</a>项目实拍：正在施工中的一个房屋扩建项目。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E8%80%81%E6%88%BF%E6%94%B9%E9%80%A0.md#1">悉尼老房改造</a>：加建阁楼多出3间房，焕发“阳光活力”</h2>
<p>位于Mosman的这个老房改造扩建案例，是一家拥有古典欧式风格的单层老平房。随着家庭的成长，业主需要更多居住空间。<ahref="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Liberty Building Contractors</a>（下文简称Liberty）巧妙增建了一层60平方米的二楼，包括一间睡房，一间起居室，和一间带天窗的主浴室。同时进行全屋升级装修，让老房子焕然一新。室内配色糅合了浅色系木地板、浅灰及白色的墙面，带来明亮、自然的温馨感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499224-03.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499224" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E7%BF%BB%E6%96%B0.md#1">悉尼房屋翻新</a>扩建：Mosman扩项目，老房子焕然一新。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499225-04.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499225" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼房屋改造加建案例：在平房基础上加建一层，采用尖顶式阁楼造型。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>您知道吗？往上扩建（加层）的复杂程度和技术要求，比起在平地上扩建要高出许多，并非所有builder都能胜任。而Liberty的加建成品非常出色，与原有建筑完美融合。</p>
<p>加建部分采用尖顶阁楼造型，无论是屋顶的尖顶外观还是阁楼内部的挑高空间，都让人印象深刻。为了改善采光，阁楼的走廊及浴室都安装了天窗，为房子注入“阳光活力”。阳光透过天窗，照亮走廊以及木质楼梯，让有限的空间更感温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499226-05.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499226" class="wp-caption-text">加建的起居室：令人印象深刻的尖顶式挑高空间。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499227" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499227-06.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499227" class="wp-caption-text">天窗照亮阁楼走廊，在白色墙面间形成柔和反射，让有限的空间更感温馨。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499228" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499228-07.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499228" class="wp-caption-text">天窗照亮浴室，与淡灰色扇贝型纹理瓷砖墙相映成趣。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贴墙的木质楼梯独具匠心：单侧木质扶手设在墙上，靠近客厅的另一侧没有常见的栏杆，而是采用了一整块透明玻璃隔断，简约时尚，既强化了采光效果、让空间感最大化，同时又保障了住户上下楼梯的安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499229-08.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499229" class="wp-caption-text">木质楼梯采用透明玻璃隔断，让采光和空间感最大化。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499230" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499230-09.gif" alt="" width="400" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499230" class="wp-caption-text">设计巧妙的洗衣房，采用隐藏式全开折叠门。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E6%89%A9%E5%BB%BA.md#1">悉尼房屋扩建</a>：超宽大开放式家居，空间延伸至室外</h2>
<p>另一个悉尼房屋扩建案例，是位于Silverdale的一个成长中的大家族。业主感到原有的厨房、饭厅太小。Liberty为客户进行了大幅扩建，贯通厨房、饭厅、客厅，打造了一个超宽大的开放式生活空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499231-10.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499231" class="wp-caption-text">客厅、厨房、饭厅贯通，超宽大生活空间。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饭厅边上，就是超宽全开式折叠玻璃门，不仅视野开阔、引入自然采光，更让饭厅空间无缝延伸至室外露台，即使是举办30人的大型家庭Party都游刃有余！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499232-11.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499232" class="wp-caption-text">超宽全开式折叠玻璃门，让饭厅空间延伸到露台。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499233-12.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499233" class="wp-caption-text">宽敞的带顶棚露台，是个理想的休闲娱乐空间。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13499234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499234-13.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499234" class="wp-caption-text">饭厅俯瞰：餐桌外就是敞亮的露台及后院泳池景致，令人心情舒畅。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>厨房部分，采用量身定制的L型橱柜布局，搭配大型中岛台。中岛台兼具料理准备区及餐桌双重功能，更加实用。中岛台上方的金属吊灯，让饭厅区带来优雅的设计感。配色方面，采用白色橱柜搭配中灰色墙面及羊毛色哑光地砖，在保持整体亮度的同时又不觉刺眼，营造出温暖舒适的家居气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499235-14.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499235" class="wp-caption-text">厨房特写：L型橱柜布局，搭配大型中岛台，方便实用。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饭厅旁的起居区域，是一面手工打造的石头焦点墙，搭配一个单体火炉，粗犷温暖的质感十分引人注目，同时起到了起居区域和会客厅之间的半隔断作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13499236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13499236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://bit.ly/Consult_Liberty"><img class="size-full wp-image-13499236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13499236-15.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13499236" class="wp-caption-text">画龙点睛之笔：手工精心打造的石头焦点墙，搭配单体火炉。（Liberty Building Contractors）</figcaption></figure>
