<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北港工艺坊生肖联展 45为工艺家 为虎年祈福</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502745-537098-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>工艺家带着自己的工艺品大合照（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月14日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）为了迎接即将到来的<ahref="https://github.com/acrpyg398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>，云林北港工艺坊，即日起至2月28日止，特别举办生肖特展，邀请四十五位工艺家一起展出以老虎为主题的作品，希望藉由作品展增添年味，同时为虎年祈福！</p>
<figure id="13502757" aria-describedby="caption-13502757" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502757-537109-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502757-537109-450x300.jpg" alt="展出的石雕作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502757" class="wp-caption-text">展出的石雕作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13502756" aria-describedby="caption-13502756" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502756-537108-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502756-537108-450x300.jpg" alt="剪黏作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502756" class="wp-caption-text">剪黏作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13502755" aria-describedby="caption-13502755" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502755-537107-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502755-537107-450x300.jpg" alt="笔雕作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502755" class="wp-caption-text">笔雕作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13502754" aria-describedby="caption-13502754" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502754-537106-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502754-537106-450x300.jpg" alt="苏荣凌的木雕作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502754" class="wp-caption-text">苏荣凌的木雕作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次展出作品有木雕、石雕、 笔雕、 陶艺、剪黏彩绘、拼布包、 油画、水墨、凿花、 电动花灯、十字绣等各式各样的作品！以“祈福旗福”为主题，展现各自对新一年的期许与祝福！</p>
<figure id="13502746" aria-describedby="caption-13502746" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502746-537099-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502746-537099-450x300.jpg" alt="萧源松的木雕作品 虎耀 " width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502746" class="wp-caption-text">萧源松的木雕作品 虎耀 （廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由萧源松工艺师创作的木雕作品“虎耀”，虎虎生风的姿态，希望大家都能<ahref="https://github.com/acrpyg398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%8E%E5%B9%B4.md#1">虎年</a>行大运。作品不但气势十足，还散发着浓浓的香气！</p>
<figure id="13502747" aria-describedby="caption-13502747" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502747-537100-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502747-537100-450x300.jpg" alt="张旭辉和他的作品，作品中的老虎嘴里咬着如意、脚下踩着元宝" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502747" class="wp-caption-text">张旭辉和他的作品，作品中的老虎嘴里咬着如意、脚下踩着元宝（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“招财如意”，作品中的老虎嘴里咬着如意、脚下踩着元宝，古迹维修木雕师张旭辉表示，一般过年，民间比较忌讳在家摆饰老虎这种凶猛的动物，为了化戾气为祥和，他极具巧思的让老虎的嘴里咬着如意，脚上踩着元宝，这样既招财又就充满吉祥之气。</p>
<figure id="13502753" aria-describedby="caption-13502753" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502753-537105-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502753-537105-450x300.jpg" alt="花灯工艺家颜三泰骑在他的电动花灯上 为虎年祈福" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502753" class="wp-caption-text">花灯工艺家颜三泰骑在他的电动花灯上 为虎年祈福（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花灯工艺家 颜三泰：“今年遇到虎年，所以做这只老虎迎春纳福，让大家可以接到福气。”</p>
<figure id="13502748" aria-describedby="caption-13502748" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502748-537101-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502748-537101-450x300.jpg" alt="木雕工艺家林朝金贤伉俪和她的作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502748" class="wp-caption-text">木雕工艺家林朝金贤伉俪和她的作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作品“悠福自在”描述一只安详自在的老虎俯卧在草原上，木雕工艺家林朝金希望疫情期间，大家要保持一颗轻松自在的心，好好迎接新的一年！</p>
<figure id="13502750" aria-describedby="caption-13502750" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502750-537102-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502750-537102-450x300.jpg" alt="妈祖画家曾良美和她的作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502750" class="wp-caption-text">妈祖画家曾良美和她的作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妈祖画家曾良美以油画描述在春暖花开时节，一只虎妈妈带着小老虎，倘佯在充满阳光的草原上，她期望她的一年充满阳光和希望。</p>
<figure id="13502752" aria-describedby="caption-13502752" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502752-537104-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502752-537104-450x300.jpg" alt="拼布家周秀惠和她的作品。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502752" class="wp-caption-text">拼布家周秀惠和她的作品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拼布家周秀惠说，今年是生肖展的第七年，她每年都参加，今年她把老虎的图腾应用在包包上，希望能把福气带着走，她希望生肖<ahref="https://github.com/acrpyg398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%94%E5%B1%95.md#1">联展</a>能继续办下续，把<ahref="https://github.com/acrpyg398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/12%E7%94%9F%E8%82%96.md#1">12生肖</a>全部完成。</p>
<figure id="13502751" aria-describedby="caption-13502751" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502751-537103-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13502751-537103-450x300.jpg" alt="庄秀月的十字绣作品" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13502751" class="wp-caption-text">庄秀月的十字绣作品（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>庄秀月以十字绣描述四只老虎全家福，开心的草地上嬉戏打滚，她说，疫情期间大家只能待在家里，她希望在疫情严峻的新一年，大家要珍惜和家人相处的时间，快快乐的过每一天。◇</p>
