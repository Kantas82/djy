<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宣信附幼 免废市集零元交易</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504613-537259-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	嘉义市宣信附幼，免废市集零元交易。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月14日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）为了传达“惜物”与“资源重生”的概念给孩子们，嘉义市宣信附幼于2022年1月14日上午举办一场全园家长与师生共同参与有意义的“免废<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%82%E9%9B%86.md#1">市集</a>”活动。</p>
<p>活动开始，由黄金木校长、吴凤科技大学幼保系黄珮书教授及大班李苡萌小朋友三位敲锣开启序幕，每位小老板都很愿意分享物品，现场充满欢乐年节走春<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%82%E9%9B%86.md#1">市集</a>的氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504614" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504614-537260.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504614-537260-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504614" class="wp-caption-text">活动开始，由黄金木校长（右1）、吴凤科技大学幼保系黄珮书教授（左1）及大班李苡萌小朋友三位敲锣开启序幕。（宣信国小提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市宣信国小附设幼儿园在岁末年终，孩子们发现家中有一些被“珍藏已久的宝贝”，总是没有使用到它的机会，就这样闲置一旁。今天在幼儿园户外游戏场的榕树与榄仁树道下共约有200位家长与师生一起及吴凤科技大学9位师生来共襄盛举，不必以物易物，也不是跳蚤市场，所以没有提供金钱买卖！全部都是“免费”的！想分享什么物品都可以，让家里没有被用到的东西，成为下一位有缘人的礼物。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504615" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504615-537261.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504615-537261-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504615" class="wp-caption-text">小班家长王先生说：“小芸虽只有三岁，藉由这次学习对物品的取舍，幼儿园办这样的活动让孩子有机会学习<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B1%E4%BA%AB.md#1">共享</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%9C%E7%A6%8F.md#1">惜福</a>的观念”。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小班家长王先生说：“小芸虽只有三岁，藉由这次学习对物品的取舍，幼儿园办这样的活动让孩子有机会学习<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B1%E4%BA%AB.md#1">共享</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/xzadft364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%9C%E7%A6%8F.md#1">惜福</a>的观念”。中班紫嫣小朋友分享:我今天拿到好多好喜欢的礼物，我也把自己喜欢的玩具送给别人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504616" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504616-537262.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504616-537262-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504616" class="wp-caption-text">有招牌的店家，豆豆蓉柑仔店。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>附幼园主任简淑芳说: “将家中用不到但觉得丢掉很浪费的东西整理干净后，带到学校，家长和宝贝一起当摊主，将物品送给有缘人!也可以到其他摊位到处走走，家长很期待所以参与踊跃”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504617-537265.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504617-537265-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504617" class="wp-caption-text">想分享什么物品都可以，让家里没有被用到的东西，成为下一位有缘人的礼物。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场共有60个家庭小摊位，有些家庭还特别标示摊位名称，整个树下走道及幼儿园游戏场边，摆放各式各样琳琅满目的免费物品，非常有教育意义，宣信国小校长黄金木说: “免‘废’市集用意在分享及再利用，孩子与家长共同检视家中的物品，透过市集与别人交换或给予，是很好的环保教育。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504618-537267.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504618-537267-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504618" class="wp-caption-text">公主香香店摊位小主人很用心。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的活动让这些物品再生，带着宝贝一起学习节约资源、减少浪费、爱物惜物的精神。今天大家体验了“独乐乐不如众乐乐”大家共享共玩，还有共创欢乐与分享的幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13504619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504619-537268.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504619-537268-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504619" class="wp-caption-text">摊位小主人招呼客人来店分享。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13504620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504620" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504620-537269.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504620-537269-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504620" class="wp-caption-text">小主人的店内分享物全是名牌玩具。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13504621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13504621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504621-537270.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13504621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13504621-537270-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13504621" class="wp-caption-text">吴凤科大幼保系同学带小朋友去南田市场踏查，制作出有意义的桌游。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
