<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：用纸做成的鸟类雕塑 刻划生动逼真</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501833-artist-Niharika-Rajput-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>印度一位年轻的艺术家擅长制作精美的纸鸟雕塑作品。（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年01月13日讯】（英文大纪元Epoch Inspired Staff报导／张雨霏编译）<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a>一位年轻的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>擅长制作精美的纸鸟<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%95%E5%A1%91.md#1">雕塑</a>作品，她对鸟儿的刻划细致生动，从鸟喙到<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>再到爪子，每一个细节都是精雕细琢，做出来的成品非常逼真，以至于经常被人误认为是鸟儿的标本。</p>
<p>来自新德里的30岁<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>尼哈里卡?拉杰普特<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/paperchirrups/">Niharika Rajput</a>）一直以来都非常热爱大自然。“我喜欢用罐子搜集萤火虫，在夜间观赏蜘蛛织网，和瓢虫玩耍，鲜艳的红色总是能吸引我的注意力。”她告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13501834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501834" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501834-Niharika-work-in-progress-2-1200x1005.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501834-Niharika-work-in-progress-2-1200x1005.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501834" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>起初，尼哈里卡主要是设计和制作三维小雕像，后来，为了体现她对于自然的热情，她开始将创作的对象转移到野生动植物身上。但是，当她遇到一只白胸翠鸟（white-throated kingfisher）时，其鲜艳的色彩和独特的特征深深地吸引了她，从此她便将<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>作为她的主要题材。</p>
<p>直到一次偶然的采风，她在喜马拉雅山北部看到了成群的红嘴蓝鹊展翅起舞，这种生物美丽的外表和姿态更加震撼她的心灵，自此，她开始专注创作<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E7%B1%BB.md#1">鸟类</a>作品。</p>
<p>在创作之初，她深入研究了不同材料的表现形式。一开始，她使用环氧树脂和纤维材料来制作，但最终确定以纸张作为她的首选媒介，她说“这种材料完美地复制了<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>的质感”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13501837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501837-Indian-Roller._high-76-copy-1200x801.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501837-Indian-Roller._high-76-copy-1200x801.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501837" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13501838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501838-Nikkis-Art-Piece5379-1200x801.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501838-Nikkis-Art-Piece5379-1200x801.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501838" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让她的鸟儿作品尽可能逼真，尼哈里卡花时间精心研究并利用多张照片，保证自己能360度全方位看到鸟的各位部位，然后她画出草图，标识出不同部位的羽毛和面部特征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13501835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501835" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501835-barn-owl-bts-1-1200x833.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501835-barn-owl-bts-1-1200x833.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501835" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于具体制作流程，她先用编织线和环氧树脂搭建一个骨架，里面用纸填充；然后用纸条覆盖该基础结构以产生一个平整的表面，接下来在该表面上黏贴每根单独剪裁好的羽毛。</p>
<p>“我首先要把所有的羽毛粘起来，从尾羽开始向上延伸”，尼哈里卡解释道，“一旦所有的羽毛都粘在身体上，我就用丙烯颜料给它们上色。至于面部特征和细节，例如爪子和鸟喙，则用环氧树脂制成并固定。</p>
<p>她说，每只鸟可能需要两周到三个月的时间才能完成，但这些努力都得到了回报。“有时人们把它们误认为是真正的鸟类标本。我认为这是一种恭维话，因为这是我终极的追求目标。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13501843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501843" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501843-Splendid-Fairy-4web-7-1200x720.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501843-Splendid-Fairy-4web-7-1200x720.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501843" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13501840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501840" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501840-Indian-Roller._high-res-17jpg-copy-1200x900.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501840-Indian-Roller._high-res-17jpg-copy-1200x900.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501840" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尼哈里卡对自然的热爱激发了她的艺术创作，但她表示，她希望通过自己的作品，一方面让更多的人感受到大自然的美丽，另一方面提高人们对于动植物的保护意识。</p>
<p>她积极参与<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a>及其它地区的野生动物保护工作，与儿童和当地社区一起举办艺术研讨会。她还举办鸟类节，让尽可能多的人参与进来，提高人们对濒危野生动物的认识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13501839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13501839" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501839-Splendid-Fairy-Wren-final-7-1200x1172.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13501839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13501839-Splendid-Fairy-Wren-final-7-1200x1172.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13501839" class="wp-caption-text">（尼哈里卡?拉杰普特提供）</figcaption></figure>
