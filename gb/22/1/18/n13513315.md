<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：少女艺术家绘八千平方英尺军警壁画</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513319-et-mural-web-17687-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一位来自伊利诺伊州的少年艺术家画了一幅8,000平方英尺的壁画，突显美军战绩。（Madeline Deiters and ThreeLine Art提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2022年01月18日讯】（英文大纪元Epoch Inspired Staff报导／林达编译）美国伊利诺伊州马里恩的<ahref="https://github.com/vqlpkt3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">少女艺术家</a>画了一幅8,000平方英尺的<ahref="https://github.com/vqlpkt3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A3%81%E7%94%BB.md#1">壁画</a>，突显美军战绩，从硫磺岛战役到越南战争；从伊拉克自由行动到九一一事件。</p>
<p>14岁的玛德琳?戴特斯（Madeline Deiters）是名在家自学的九年级学生，她在9月11日稍后开始绘制这幅<ahref="https://github.com/vqlpkt3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A3%81%E7%94%BB.md#1">壁画</a>，八周后完成，壁画环绕着一座大型工业建筑的三面墙壁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13513333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513333" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513333-Madeline-Deiters-8-600x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13513333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513333-Madeline-Deiters-8-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513333" class="wp-caption-text">（Madeline Deiters和<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/threelineart/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ThreeLine Art</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13513336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513336-Madeline-Deiters-2-600x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13513336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513336-Madeline-Deiters-2-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513336" class="wp-caption-text">（Madeline Deiters和<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/threelineart/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ThreeLine Art</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13513338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513338-Madeline-Deiters-3-600x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13513338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513338-Madeline-Deiters-3-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513338" class="wp-caption-text">（Madeline Deiters和<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/threelineart/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ThreeLine Art</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>壁画描绘的是士兵、警察和海军陆战队，一架洛克希德C-130大力神军用运输机、以及硫磺岛（Iwo Jima）战役和9?11事件的升旗仪式。</p>
<p>“我的父亲、叔叔、祖父和曾祖父曾参加过二战、越战、沙漠风暴（Desert Storm）行动、伊拉克自由行动和持久自由行动”，麦迪告诉《大纪元时报》，“老实说，我对越南战争以及急救人员的工作不太了解。</p>
<p>“因此，我必须做一些深入的研究，以便准确地设计和绘制徽章、士兵及其装备，和那些标志性照片”，她补充道，“画出如此与人们心灵相通的画面，也是一副重担。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13513341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513341" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513341-Madeline-Deiters-7-600x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13513341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513341-Madeline-Deiters-7-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513341" class="wp-caption-text">（Madeline Deiters和<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/threelineart/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ThreeLine Art</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13513345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13513345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513345-Maddie-15-600x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13513345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13513345-Maddie-15-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13513345" class="wp-caption-text">（Madeline Deiters和<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/threelineart/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ThreeLine Art</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>她描绘了一名在越战纪念碑前饱受煎熬的军人，表现出几代人的牺牲和英雄主义场景。最突出的是一只巨大的美国老鹰，画在拐角的两面墙上。</p>
<p>大壁画的提议通过后，她面临几个难题：坏天气、设备和油漆短缺。</p>
<p>这位爱国艺术家希望壁画能够发出积极的信息，以抵消媒体谤警、呼吁“撤警”的一些负面影响。</p>
<p>她说，收到的积极回应“排山倒海”，“许多人因此而佩戴海军陆战队或陆军徽章，对壁画上的<ahref="https://github.com/vqlpkt3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%9B%E8%AD%A6.md#1">军警</a>表现出尊敬和感谢之情，尤其是对那些越战士兵的奉献和牺牲”，她说，“我对此深感震惊。”</p>
<p>退伍军人常常停下来向她讲述他们因公殉职失去亲人的故事。</p>
<p>至于前景，玛德琳说她自己有可能在全城的建筑物上都画上壁画。</p>
