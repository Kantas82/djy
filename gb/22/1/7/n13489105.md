<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>增强记忆力 中医帮助你</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2022年01月08日讯】世界上有这样一种人，他们的<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B0%E5%BF%86%E5%8A%9B.md#1">记忆力</a>超强。比如美国的《雨人》（Kim Peek），他可以把自己一生中看过的12,000多本书，一字不漏地背诵下来。说是看，实际上是翻，他的速度非常快，一目十行就看完了，过目不忘。后来，根据他的经历改编的电影《雨人》，获得奥斯卡最佳影片奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489146-Fotolia_70829927_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489146-Fotolia_70829927_Subaion_L-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489146" class="wp-caption-text">（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的功能是与生俱来的，所以记忆跟先天的秉赋很有关系，当然也离不开后天的营养和训练。比如在很多古老的部落，他们的历史不是以文字记载下来的，而是通过口口相传。在部落中找一个年轻人，让他背诵下来部落的整个历史，有几十万字，也许是诗歌的形式，也许是说唱的形式，然后再传给下一代。这样代代相传。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>看记性</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>认为<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B0%E5%BF%86%E5%8A%9B.md#1">记忆力</a>跟脑髓有关，“脑为髓海”，髓是肾脏的肾精生化而成。肾为先天之本，跟遗传有很大关系。出生以后，肾精把营养物质化生成精微来供给肾脏，所以也需要后天的营养和补充。</p>
<p>总结来说，记忆力包括两个方面，一是先天，二是后天。当年纪渐大后，身体机能开始下滑，肾精不足，无以充实髓海，即出现记忆力减退或不灵敏的状态。有的人亏损得更多，髓海空虚，就导致老年痴呆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489171-Fotolia_41168140_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489171-Fotolia_41168140_Subaion_L-450x338.jpg" alt="脑" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489171" class="wp-caption-text">记忆力减退。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所以，保养肾脏，补充肾精以充髓，是中医提高记忆力的方法。<br />
古人非常重视养生。在尝百草时，神农找到了可以提高记忆力的草药，记录下来，流传于世。</p>
<h2>第一味：<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9C%E5%BF%97.md#1">远志</a></h2>
<p>《神农本草经》记载<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9C%E5%BF%97.md#1">远志</a>：“益智慧，耳目聪明，不忘，强志倍力，久服，轻身不老。”</p>
<p>李时珍《本草纲目》说：“其功专于强志益精，治善忘。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489174-shutterstock_2016309245.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489174-shutterstock_2016309245-450x297.jpg" alt="远志" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489174" class="wp-caption-text">远志。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现代医学根据古代的记载，对远志进行了研究，通过老年痴呆的动物模型在用药前后的对比，发现远志确实有增强记忆力、提高学习能力的功效。<br />
远志服食起来很方便，可以直接服用，也可以做成汤或粥。</p>
<p><strong>【远志饮】</strong><br />
材料：远志10克<br />
做法：水煎30分钟，取汁，早晚两次温服。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489145" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489145-Fotolia_39442583_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489145-Fotolia_39442583_Subaion_L-450x406.jpg" alt="远志饮" width="450" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489145" class="wp-caption-text">远志饮。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【远志莲子粥】</strong><br />
材料：远志30克，莲子15克，粳米50克<br />
做法：将远志泡去心皮，焙干，与莲子同研为粉。煮粳米粥，候熟，入远志莲子粉，再煮一二沸。<br />
用法：随意食用。<br />
功效：补中，益心志，聪耳明目。适用于健忘、怔忡、失眠等症。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489176" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489176-shutterstock_454646761.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489176-shutterstock_454646761-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489176" class="wp-caption-text">远志莲子粥。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>第二味：<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B5%E8%8A%9D.md#1">灵芝</a></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B5%E8%8A%9D.md#1">灵芝</a>非常名贵，所以不如远志用得广泛，赤灵芝在所有灵芝中效果最好。《神农本草经》记载灵芝：“益心气，补中，增智慧，不忘。久食轻身不老，延年益寿。”它因此被称为“仙草”。</p>
<p>现代医学研究显示，赤灵芝有抗衰老的作用，对激活脑细胞很有帮助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489130-Fotolia_118795556_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489130-Fotolia_118795556_Subaion_L-450x307.jpg" alt="赤灵芝" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489130" class="wp-caption-text">赤灵芝。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【灵芝饮】</strong><br />
材料：灵芝10克<br />
做法：切片，代茶饮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489180-shutterstock_156242876.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489180-shutterstock_156242876-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489180" class="wp-caption-text">灵芝饮。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【灵芝薄荷饮】</strong><br />
材料：灵芝2克，薄荷5克，谷芽5克，白糖25克<br />
做法：灵芝切片，薄荷切小段，谷芽炒香备用。把灵芝、炒谷芽放入锅内，加250毫升水，放白糖，煮至汤汁变稠，下薄荷，再煎煮10分钟。<br />
功效：补脑益智。适用于烦热、气虚烦劳等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13489136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13489136" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489136-Fotolia_32360778_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13489136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13489136-Fotolia_32360778_Subaion_L-450x420.jpg" alt="灵芝薄荷饮" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13489136" class="wp-caption-text">灵芝薄荷饮。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E9%83%A8%E6%93%8D.md#1">手部操</a>：枪打鸟</h2>
<p>最后介绍一个<ahref="https://github.com/awgodf3330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E9%83%A8%E6%93%8D.md#1">手部操</a>“枪打鸟”，防止老年痴呆，增加记忆力。</p>
<p>右手比成“枪”，左手比成“V”字，代表鸟，用“枪”对着“鸟”；然后左手换成“枪”，右手换成“鸟”；如此往复，逐渐加快速度。“鸟”的数量也可以增加，伸出三个指头代表三只鸟，四个指头代表四只鸟。</p>
<p>这样不停地运动双手，对于增强记忆力很有帮助。</p>
