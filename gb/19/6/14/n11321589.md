<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6 Head餐馆背靠悉尼大桥，面对港湾和悉尼歌剧院。（6 Head提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月14日讯】<ahref=" https://6head.com.au/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">6HEAD牛排餐馆</a>坐落在悉尼岩石区Campell’s Cove的的一座1839年建成的古老砂岩建筑内。悉尼港海景近在咫尺，餐馆背靠悉尼大桥，面对港湾和悉尼歌剧院。</p>
<p>餐厅的室内设计灵感源于，殖民时期<ahref="https://6head.com.au/story/about-us/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">6HEAD的传说</a> ，并且融入该地区的古典风格。每一块手工瓷砖、碎片、石块和复古地板，都让这座古典建筑更加特殊而典雅。餐馆内设计时尚，且有现代感</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321729" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-02.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-02.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="503" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321729" class="wp-caption-text">6 Head餐厅的室内设计灵感源于殖民时期6HEAD的传说。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11321732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321732" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-04.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-04.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="501" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321732" class="wp-caption-text">6 Head餐厅的室内设计灵感源于殖民时期6HEAD的传说。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>取名6 Head是来源于澳大利亚早期的有趣故事。1788年，由HMS Sirius号船运来了六头黑牛，交给当时的犯人看管，因犯人一去吃午餐就忘记了看管， 结果这六头牛不翼而飞。8年后，有人却在悉尼八十公里外的内陆，发现了100头牛。而这一百头牛，正是当时不翼而飞的六头牛的后代。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321731" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-03.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-03.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="501" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321731" class="wp-caption-text">6 Head餐馆内设计时尚而有现代感。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://6head.com.au/story/the-chef/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">餐厅主厨Sean Hall</a>拥有丰富的烹调经验。他来自南非，成长于一个知名养牛场——Johannesburg。他从小就喜爱烹调。烹调牛排是Sean的长处。后来，他曾在英国名厨Jamie Oliver集团公司工作。丰富的烹调经验为主厨Sean设计6HEAD的菜单，扩展了更大空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321761" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-Shef.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-Shef.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="351" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321761" class="wp-caption-text">6HEAD牛排餐馆主厨Sean Hall。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 Head的牛排的供应商是三家澳大利亚最知名的Collinson &amp; Co、Mayura Station 和 Tender Valley，每一块牛排都具有最佳品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321733" style="width: 502px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-05.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-05.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="502" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321733" class="wp-caption-text">6HEAD牛排餐馆每一块牛排都具有最佳品质。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新鲜和优质的臀腰牛排、肋眼牛排、T骨牛排、牛眼牛排、苏格兰圆角牛排以及西冷牛排是<ahref="https://6head.com.au/eat-drink/all-day-menu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">6 Head的招牌菜</a>。Sean创意灵感，厨艺可从6 Head的菜谱中充分体现出来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-06.png"><img class=" wp-image-11321734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-06.png" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="450" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321734" class="wp-caption-text">（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11321735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321735" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-07.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-07.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="351" b="514" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321735" class="wp-caption-text">6 Head的标志牛排酱是Veal Jus。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃牛排， 粘酱料是不可少的， 主厨Sean有秘方。 6 Head的标志牛排酱是Veal Jus， 还有发酵辣椒酱、黑胡椒酱和蘑菇酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11321736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321736" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-08.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-08.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="450" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321736" class="wp-caption-text">（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 Head 的菜单丰富多样， Sean创意灵感，厨艺可从每道菜中充分体现出来。</p>
<h2>Burrata马苏里拉奶酪</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11321737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321737" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-09.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-09.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="451" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321737" class="wp-caption-text">Burrata 马苏里拉奶酪。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马苏里拉牛奶酪，配上蜂蜜釉无花果，百里香橄榄油和陈年香醋， 味道清爽，奶香微带醋酸，</p>
<h2>主厨Sean的另一类前菜Lamb Tartare鞑靼羊肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11321738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11321738" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11321738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/20190614-Chi-Jin-6HEAD-10.jpg" alt="悉尼海港著名精品牛排馆——6 Head" width="449" b="271" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11321738" class="wp-caption-text">前菜Lamb Tartare鞑靼羊肉。（6 Head提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>羊肉配青葱、刺山柑、莳萝泡菜、姜黄蛋黄， 用芥末、黑蒜泥蛋黄酱、大蒜脆片调味，滋润鲜美。</p>

<h2>Char-grilled Quail明火烤鹌鹑</h2>
