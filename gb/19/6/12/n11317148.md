<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林表扬林之光、孝行楷模、社教有功人员</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/fb4577b9877a4f8718741cc395245d88-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>县长颁奖给获奖人员 。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾<ahref="https://github.com/wlvhkt3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林年度盛事“<ahref="https://github.com/wlvhkt3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97%E4%B9%8B%E5%85%89.md#1">云林之光</a>”、“孝行模范”暨“社教有功-惠风奖”联合表扬大会12日下午在文化处表演厅盛大举行，县长张丽善颁奖表扬郑竹玲等得奖人，与他们分享得奖喜悦及荣耀，教育处邱孝文处长及各界代表也到场观礼祝贺，场面温馨感人。</p>
<p>张丽善致词时表示:能获得<ahref="https://github.com/wlvhkt3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97%E4%B9%8B%E5%85%89.md#1">云林之光</a>表扬实属不易，每位受奖者都是在艺术、文学、科学、体育等各领域表现优异。获奖者追求卓越，永不懈怠的精神，更是值得我们感佩与学习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11317152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9df8ed9786a5eab115cf5931977aa6af.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317152 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9df8ed9786a5eab115cf5931977aa6af-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317152" class="wp-caption-text">县长颁奖给获奖人员 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11317153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/b99786fb0df21e714d31fcd42972176c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317153 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/b99786fb0df21e714d31fcd42972176c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317153" class="wp-caption-text">县长颁奖给获奖人员 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11317158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1508690c88efe96a3f77ba87b8ff1e98.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317158 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1508690c88efe96a3f77ba87b8ff1e98-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317158" class="wp-caption-text">县长颁奖给获奖人员 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>荣获2019<ahref="https://github.com/wlvhkt3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>之光团队奖共有安庆国小等28个团队，个人奖则有137人获得，得奖者之中郑竹玲选手荣获2018印尼雅加达巨港亚洲运动会软式网球个人混双金牌、个人单打银牌，堪称“云林之光”、“台湾之光”。</p>
<p>荣获“孝行模范”共有林月、萧应聪、黄铭德、钟淑虹、许祐瑜、范胜腾、黄惠珍、庄淑贞、李虹霏、黄峻翊及曾毓妊等11位得奖者；其中，林月女士并获选为全国孝行奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11317156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/cd5cd3e22be203b60c752fb0c053aa5d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317156 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/cd5cd3e22be203b60c752fb0c053aa5d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317156" class="wp-caption-text">县长颁奖给孝行楷模得奖者 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林月女士原生家庭生活困苦，从小就被林吴不碟收为家中唯一养女，并协助家中农作。十九岁时嫁为人妇，一边工作、一边照顾父母及4个小孩。在独自辛苦多年的努力下，孩子都已长大成人，各自在外成家立业。现家中只留下她与超过百岁的母亲。林月女士的一眼失明、一眼白内障视力不佳，但仍无畏的扛起照顾百岁年迈母亲的重大责任，在一般人的这个年纪，可能都在含饴弄孙，林月仍每天帮母亲喂食、洗澡、上下厕所。她无怨无悔的付出，日复一日的尽孝，给全天下子女作最好的表率。</p>
<p>“社教有功-惠风奖”项目，绩优团体奖由奉天宫及云林县幸福艺文协会二单位获得；个人组─杨松田、陈重夫、张玫婷、林秀容、林铭信、蓝雅燕、江明轩、叶又嘉、郭国辉、蔡月桃等十员列入本年度本县社会教育有功-惠风奖表扬。获奖人员用他们的热诚，长期对社会教育工作付出，无私奉献，共同营造真善美和谐社会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11317151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/ab41f88769ee677bd14fdf18347dcf2d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317151 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/ab41f88769ee677bd14fdf18347dcf2d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317151" class="wp-caption-text">县长颁奖给获奖人员 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11317155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/cdae14235ba58e543a9489989f758583.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317155 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/cdae14235ba58e543a9489989f758583-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317155" class="wp-caption-text">县长张丽善与安定国小礼生合影。 （廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11317154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11317154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/e251118e1c7fa671d0778d861b00dbe7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11317154 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/e251118e1c7fa671d0778d861b00dbe7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11317154" class="wp-caption-text">建国国中独轮车表演 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年表扬大会现场也特别邀请了荣获绩优学校团队担任演出，包括建国国中独轮车表演、大东国小打击乐表演、安定国小低年级舞蹈表演及公诚国小直笛表演，为整场表扬大会添色不少，表扬大会活泼丰富又不失隆重。</p>
