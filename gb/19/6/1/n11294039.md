<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《信用诈欺师JP》收视破十 6月4日KKTV开播</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045832100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（左起）东出昌大、长泽雅美与小日向文世演绎《信用诈欺师JP》。（KKTV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年06月01日讯】由<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%B3%BD%E9%9B%85%E7%BE%8E.md#1">长泽雅美</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%87%BA%E6%98%8C%E5%A4%A7.md#1">东出昌大</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E6%97%A5%E5%90%91%E6%96%87%E4%B8%96.md#1">小日向文世</a>主演的《信用诈欺师JP 2019特别篇-运势篇》，邀来<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%9D%91%E4%B8%80%E8%BE%89.md#1">北村一辉</a>、中山美穗、<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BF%E6%9C%AB%E5%87%89%E5%AD%90.md#1">广末凉子</a>参演，5月18日在日本首播时，夹杂着电影版上映的气势，收视拿下10.3%的好成绩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11294061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045836100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294061" title="Hirosue Ryoko, Kitamura Kazuki, Miho Nakayama" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045836100707-600x424.jpg" alt="Hirosue Ryoko, Kitamura Kazuki, Miho Nakayama" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294061" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BF%E6%9C%AB%E5%87%89%E5%AD%90.md#1">广末凉子</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%9D%91%E4%B8%80%E8%BE%89.md#1">北村一辉</a>、中山美穗参演《信用诈欺师JP》。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《信用诈欺师JP》在2018年推出就受到观众欢迎，连配角“五十岚”在日本也拥有1亿2,000万的“五十岚”亲卫队支持，让饰演该角色的小手伸也人气直升，甚至于今年推出个人外传《信用诈欺师IG》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11294062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045756100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294062" title="?Nagasawa Masami" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045756100707-600x399.jpg" alt="?Nagasawa Masami" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294062" class="wp-caption-text">小手伸也与<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%B3%BD%E9%9B%85%E7%BE%8E.md#1">长泽雅美</a>演绎《信用诈欺师》剧照。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11294071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294071" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045823100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294071" title="Higashide Masahiro, Kohinata Fumiyo" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045823100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Higashide Masahiro, Kohinata Fumiyo" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294071" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%87%BA%E6%98%8C%E5%A4%A7.md#1">东出昌大</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E6%97%A5%E5%90%91%E6%96%87%E4%B8%96.md#1">小日向文世</a>演绎《信用诈欺师JP》剧照。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然是作为电影版的宣传计划之一，但小手伸也表示希望能获得好成绩，可以继续把外传拍下去。而《信用诈欺师JP 2019特别篇-运势篇》则是为了庆祝富士电视台开台60周年的特别企划，描述由长泽雅美饰演的“达子”与拥有黑道背景的投资家“阿久津晃”（北村一辉饰）过招。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11294066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045813100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294066" title="Kitamura Kazuki" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045813100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Kitamura Kazuki" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294066" class="wp-caption-text">北村一辉参演《信用诈欺师JP》剧照。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次由东出昌大饰饰演的“阿弟”与由小日向文世饰演的“理查”，碰上的遗物整理公司的董事长“渡边若叶”（中山美穗饰）与在中华餐厅工作的“韭山波子”（广末凉子饰），三人在剧中运势低迷，盼能扳回一城。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11294070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294070" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045806100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294070" title="Miho Nakayama" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045806100707-600x399.jpg" alt="Miho Nakayama" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294070" class="wp-caption-text">中山美穗参演《信用诈欺师JP》剧照。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11294069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11294069" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045818100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11294069" title="Hirosue Ryoko" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/190601045818100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Hirosue Ryoko" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11294069" class="wp-caption-text">广末凉子参演《信用诈欺师JP》剧照。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《信用诈欺师JP 2019特别篇-运势篇》KKTV预计6月4日上架。目前正在日本上映中的电影版，台湾预计8月上映。</p>
