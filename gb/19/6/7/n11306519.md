<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/50.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/50-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11306569" /></a><br />
