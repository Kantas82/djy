<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Jessica郑秀晶姐妹同行 秀今夏机场时尚穿搭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040438031487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国艺人姊妹Jessica（右）及Krystal（郑秀晶）日前分别以时尚知名品牌早秋2019 KAN U包款及外套造型现身机场。（FENDI TAIWAN MKT &#038; COMMUNICATION提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年06月04日讯】韩国艺人姊妹Jessica（郑秀妍）及Krystal（<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E7%A7%80%E6%99%B6.md#1">郑秀晶</a>）日前分别以知名<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>品牌早秋2019 KAN U<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E6%AC%BE.md#1">包款</a>及外套造型现身机场，出发前往美国拍摄真人秀，其<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>休闲兼备的打扮，成为今夏出游女性的最佳示范。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11299499" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040423081487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11299499" title="Jessica,Krystal" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040423081487.jpg" alt="Jessica,Krystal" width="455" b="683" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩国艺人姊妹Jessica（右）及Krystal（<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E7%A7%80%E6%99%B6.md#1">郑秀晶</a>）分别以时尚知名品牌早秋2019 KAN U<ahref="https://github.com/eqdwou3731/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E6%AC%BE.md#1">包款</a>及外套造型现身机场。（FENDI TAIWAN MKT &amp; COMMUNICATION提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>姐姐Jessica背上品牌本季全新主打的包款，为黑色迷你尺寸，实用袋盖饰以经典FF标志金属袋扣，烫印亮丽的品牌字样，为包款注入独有的精髓。而细腻的仿古金色润饰肩带及袋扣亦彰显包款的时尚格调。</p>
<p>妹妹Krystal则搭配了早秋2019的亮面白色尼龙外套，衬衫风格外套搭配经典衣领，两侧开衩的设计更能特显品牌经典FF Logo内衬。而Krystal特地把袖口上折，令FF Logo内衬露出，也为整体造型增添一丝时尚感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11299500" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040422561487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11299500" title="Jessica" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040422561487.jpg" alt="Jessica" width="452" b="678" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Jessica背上品牌本季全新主打的包款，仿古金色润饰肩带及袋扣，更彰显包款的时尚格调。（FENDI TAIWAN MKT &amp; COMMUNICATION提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11299503" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040422471487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11299503" title="Krystal" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/1906040422471487.jpg" alt="Krystal" width="453" b="710" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Krystal（郑秀晶）日前与姊姊一起现身机场，准备出发前往美国拍摄真人秀。（FENDI TAIWAN MKT &amp; COMMUNICATION提供）</figcaption></figure>
