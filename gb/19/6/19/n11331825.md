<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>10种不能放入冰箱存放的水果和蔬菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/banana_1340116028-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>某些水果和蔬菜品种，在冰箱的寒冷或潮湿环境中会受到不良影响。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E7%AE%B1.md#1">冰箱</a>在现代人的日常生活中必不可少，但要注意不是所有东西都应该放进冰箱。某些食物，尤其是某些<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>，在冰箱寒冷或潮湿环境中会受到不良影响。</p>
<p>24/7 Wall St.网站表明，将食物放在温暖的空气中会迅速变质并滋生有毒细菌，这是很危险的，许多食物的腐烂速度比人们想像的要快；另一方面，一些蔬果如果放入<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E7%AE%B1.md#1">冰箱</a>，它们的质地和外观会被破坏，味道也会受损。以下10种蔬果最好在室温下<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">不要放进冰箱的10种<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">1、香蕉</span></h3>
<p>香蕉生长在温暖的气候环境，不喜欢寒冷，香蕉的细胞壁没有天然防御寒冷的物质，所以它们会分解，使香蕉变黑、变软烂。其次，如果香蕉没有完全成熟，冰箱的寒冷将阻止香蕉变熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10079782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10079782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/banana-1025109_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10079782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/banana-1025109_1920-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10079782" class="wp-caption-text">香蕉是一种热带水果。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">2、冬南瓜（Butternut squash，又称奶油南瓜）</span></h3>
<p>这种美味的冬南瓜一旦被切成小块就应冷藏，但整个南瓜在凉爽但不寒冷的地方，能保持良好状态。冰箱里的湿度可能会加速南瓜的腐烂。</p>
<p>不把冬南瓜放入冰箱的另一个原因：它很大，占用空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8582504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8582504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/1610302138392483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8582504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/1610302138392483-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8582504" class="wp-caption-text">奶油南瓜。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">3、干豆（Dried beans）</span></h3>
<p>如果将干豆<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a>在密封容器中，置于阴凉、黑暗的地方，干豆将能保存多年（根据一些研究，长达三十年）。</p>
<p>在潮湿的冰箱内，它们会吸收水分并变质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9457324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9457324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Fotolia_71206529_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9457324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Fotolia_71206529_Subaion_L-600x385.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9457324" class="wp-caption-text">豆类（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">4、大蒜</span></h3>
<p>冰箱是潮湿的地方，水分会使大蒜发霉。此外，大蒜气味很容易在冰箱封闭空间内干扰其它食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10918556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10918556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/heart4-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10918556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/heart4-1-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10918556" class="wp-caption-text">大蒜中含有一种名为硫化氢的物质，而这种物质可以保护心脏。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">5、绿叶药草（Leafy herbs）</span></h3>
<p>九层塔和其它叶类芳香药草，如香菜、欧芹或薄荷等，在冰箱内比室温下更快枯萎或变黑，并且可能吸收其它食物的气味。</p>
<p>将这些绿叶植物当作花朵处理，修剪两端并将其根部放在冷水罐中，然后在室温下存放即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10695551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10695551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9bfb10d2a0cf894ab735e78df50bd981.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9bfb10d2a0cf894ab735e78df50bd981-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10695551" class="wp-caption-text">薄荷是唇形科植物，全株入药，也是著名香料植物。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">6、甜瓜</span></h3>
<p>哈密瓜、蜜瓜和西瓜应在室温下储存，以获得最佳风味。美国农业部研究表明，如果不冷藏，这些瓜可更有效地保留抗氧化剂。只有切片甜瓜才应存入冰箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10453439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10453439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f5e7d9f2a74f756dd6e4ca57bce55b61.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10453439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f5e7d9f2a74f756dd6e4ca57bce55b61-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10453439" class="wp-caption-text">西瓜和蜜瓜不要放入冰箱。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">7、洋葱</span></h3>
<p>冰箱缺乏通风会降低洋葱保质期。它们也不应存放在塑料袋中。洋葱喜欢凉爽、干燥、黑暗的环境，最好是空气流通的地方。</p>
<p>只有整个去皮或切碎的洋葱应冷藏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9574414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9574414" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/onion-e1503928935705.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9574414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/onion-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9574414" class="wp-caption-text">洋葱。(Rotozld/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">8、土豆</span></h3>
<p>土豆像洋葱一样应该存放在凉爽、干燥和黑暗的地方（为了避免它们发芽或发霉），冰箱低温会破坏它们的淀粉，将其转化为糖，并可能使土豆变硬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7556287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7556287" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1501301343492483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-7556287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1501301343492483-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7556287" class="wp-caption-text">土豆含碳水化合物，可通过提高血清素水平，来缓解头痛症状。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">9、红薯</span></h3>
<p>当红薯冷藏时，它们的细胞结构破裂并且变硬，使其更难烹饪。红薯更喜欢凉爽的室温环境，且远离光线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11009258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11009258" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/shutterstock_83182750.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11009258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/shutterstock_83182750-600x400.jpg" alt="地瓜又名甘薯、红薯，有补中和血、益气生津、宽肠胃、通便秘的功效。" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11009258" class="wp-caption-text">地瓜又名甘薯、红薯，有补中和血、益气生津、宽肠胃、通便秘的功效。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">10、西红柿</span></h3>
<p>采摘之后，<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>里的细胞依然活着，它们还会继续生产新的香气物质。但在冷藏的低温下，细胞内的“香气”产量就会急剧下降。</p>
<p>在温度低于39oF（35oF被认为是理想的冰箱温度）存放，<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>中的挥发成分含量显着降低，而番茄中的糖类（主要是葡萄糖和果糖）以及酸类（主要是苹果酸、柠檬酸）的含量并没有明显改变。也就是说，进过冰箱之后，番茄的酸甜依旧，但却少了几分“番茄味儿”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11284067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11284067" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/tomato_267812054.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11284067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/tomato_267812054-600x399.jpg" alt="每天吃2颗番茄，可预防肺阻塞和肺癌，延缓肺功能退化。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11284067" class="wp-caption-text">每天吃2个番茄，可预防肺阻塞和肺癌，延缓肺功能退化。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>将西红柿储存在50oF以下则可能导致过早软化和表面疤痕。</p>
