<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宝山乡“甜笋王”出炉 甜度高达7.4</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387510-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新竹县宝山乡绿竹笋评鉴，“甜笋王”出炉，由农夫林凤琴夺得。（赖月贵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年06月13日讯】（大纪元记者赖月贵台湾新竹报导）新竹县<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%B1%B1%E4%B9%A1%E5%86%9C%E4%BC%9A.md#1">宝山乡农会</a>与宝山乡公所13日举办绿<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E7%AC%8B.md#1">竹笋</a>评鉴，聘请桃园区农改场专家针对外观、肉质、色泽、柔嫩度、甜度等项目进行评比，“<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%AC%8B%E7%8E%8B.md#1">甜笋王</a>”出炉，是由农夫<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%87%A4%E7%90%B4.md#1">林凤琴</a>夺得，甜度高达7.4！</p>
<figure id="11320046" aria-describedby="caption-11320046" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9fb2d63c9ead55e5e2d76aea2eb9adfe-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/9fb2d63c9ead55e5e2d76aea2eb9adfe-450x300.jpg" alt="获评“甜笋王”的绿竹笋" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11320046" class="wp-caption-text">获评“<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%AC%8B%E7%8E%8B.md#1">甜笋王</a>”的绿<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E7%AC%8B.md#1">竹笋</a>。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每年5至9月是绿竹笋盛产季节，新竹县宝山乡拥有清澈水质与肥沃土壤，产出的绿竹笋无论是甜度、柔嫩度皆属优良之品，评鉴共分甜笋王、特等奖、头等奖、贰等奖、三等奖、优等奖等各种奖项；?今年有高龄九十多岁农夫<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E7%9F%B3%E6%BA%90.md#1">徐石源</a>参加评鉴，获得“特等奖”，由新竹县副县长陈见贤亲自颁发奖状和奖金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11320114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11320114" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387514.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11320114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387514-602x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11320114" class="wp-caption-text">高龄九十多岁农夫<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E7%9F%B3%E6%BA%90.md#1">徐石源</a>参加评鉴，获得“特等奖”非常开心。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11320106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11320106" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387512.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11320106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387512-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11320106" class="wp-caption-text">新竹县宝山乡绿竹笋评鉴，“头等奖”颁奖。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为推广在地食材、促进在地消费，现场也摆出由在地餐厅主厨用甜笋及在地食材制作的特色料理，有“细粉翠玉笋”、“米香鲜笋卷”、“鲜笋拼盘”、“竹吮珍堡、熏鸡起司堡”及“南瓜笋包”等，色香味俱全。</p>
<p>陈见贤说，绿竹笋富含维生素、矿物质，高纤低热量、不含胆固醇，而新竹全县绿竹笋栽培面积约351公顷，宝山是种植面积最大的乡镇，栽培面积约90公顷，今年虽受气候影响，年产量从700多公吨降至500多公吨，不过品质一如往常优等。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E5%B1%B1%E4%B9%A1%E5%86%9C%E4%BC%9A.md#1">宝山乡农会</a>表示，绿竹笋美味爽口，夏天时来道冷笋沙拉，清凉美味又健康，挑选时记得“白、弯、矮、肥”四大原则，一定能品尝到最当季的天然美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11320109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11320109" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387513.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11320109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/387513-602x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11320109" class="wp-caption-text">美味的绿竹笋料理。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宝山乡长邱坤桶表示，6月16日(日)将在风景优美的宝山水库旁举办“绿笋同乐会-2019年绿竹笋产业文化展售促销活动”，当天除了有新鲜、优质绿竹笋展售之外，还安排艺文表演活动，冰山挖笋大赛、趣味蒙眼剥竹笋大赛、竹笋大餐大请客、竹笋造型棉花糖、闯关游戏等，活动内容丰富多元，欢迎乡亲踊跃参加。</p>
