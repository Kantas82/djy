<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>笑眼拉不拉多犬 “呼吸都会胖”笑翻网友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/edccd2a0ebd910f29fbc2d8766209e44-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>笑眼拉不拉多犬“顾呷”。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月01日讯】（大纪元记者赖依琴台湾台北报导）笑起来眼睛像月弯弯的“顾呷”（Go+），是一只呆萌的<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E4%B8%8D%E6%8B%89%E5%A4%9A%E7%8A%AC.md#1">拉不拉多犬</a>。“顾呷”总是乖乖的待在主人身旁，吐着舌头呆呆的笑，这样沉稳、乖巧的狗狗得人疼，难怪才3年，就从2公斤的小狗狗，成了45公斤的棉花糖女孩！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11626288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/93a14bb3ae7c121c514b8481e82c80af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/93a14bb3ae7c121c514b8481e82c80af-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626288" class="wp-caption-text">用心的主人当然也帮顾呷拍过开箱文。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>顾呷主人日前在脸书社团“爆瘦公社”说：“我家的毛孩从2公斤到我怀里，3年的时间现在已经45公斤了”、“一天两餐，一餐一个米杯（500毫升）的饲料，已经吃一年多，但体重从没有回头过”，而且主人每天也带狗狗散步3次、每次30分钟，每周游泳1或2次，“医生说它呼吸就胖了，想请问各路高手有解吗？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11626281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/e360ed903f77d92223e2a0a6bd976e9d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/e360ed903f77d92223e2a0a6bd976e9d-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626281" class="wp-caption-text">医生说顾呷“呼吸也会胖”，但它不只呼吸，还吃了许多东西呢。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>照片让其他网友看了纷纷狂笑，还有人反问“阿嬷是不是有偷喂啦”、“一定是阿嬷偷偷给它吃鸡腿”，也有网友表示“健康就好，但怕老了身体会差”、“传说中的中年发福吗”、“原来毛小孩也有易胖体质”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11626282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/909c97cbe958733b5adff988b54457f4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/909c97cbe958733b5adff988b54457f4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626282" class="wp-caption-text">小时候的顾呷才2kg，在机车踏板显得娇小。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11626283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/414c90c90f97c8675fef3f352fc2e99b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/414c90c90f97c8675fef3f352fc2e99b-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626283" class="wp-caption-text">长大的顾呷塞满了踏板! （粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11626284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/3901947be3d4e226d8bd1c0bdcec9f64.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/3901947be3d4e226d8bd1c0bdcec9f64-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626284" class="wp-caption-text">疼爱顾呷的主人，抱着它也是很吃力的。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11626285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/0547c40e476ab161efb89b9eb1ae1d15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/0547c40e476ab161efb89b9eb1ae1d15-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626285" class="wp-caption-text">顾呷：“我都有认真游泳啊。”（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小时候的顾呷才2公斤，在机车踏板显得娇小。长大的顾呷塞满了踏板！疼爱顾呷的主人，抱着它也是很吃力的。顾呷说：“我都有认真游泳啊！”</p>
<p>呆萌女孩顾呷也有它的交友圈喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11626286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/202a61478249bc67c5a47d6a6d886554.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/202a61478249bc67c5a47d6a6d886554-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626286" class="wp-caption-text">顾呷有个好朋友“猪猪”。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11626287" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11626287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/7c6cdcfd0aee04d3392ef60835776f41.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11626287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/7c6cdcfd0aee04d3392ef60835776f41-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11626287" class="wp-caption-text">两只狗狗有着最萌“体型差”。（粉专“那只拉拉”授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>顾呷有个好朋友“猪猪”。两只狗狗有着最萌“体型差”。</p>
<p>也有网友解答，“饲料有差，有的饲料真的比较油，我家狗狗其中一餐不吃饲料改吃鲜食，没有运动，有变瘦”、“我觉得跟体质有关系，我家的狗狗早餐吃鲜食，不加任何调味，晚上吃饲料，还是肥”。主人表示，当然很关心顾呷的健康，也希望顾呷能保持活力、快乐，继续开心的陪伴他们一家人。</p>
