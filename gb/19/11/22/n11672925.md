<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第40届青龙电影奖 《寄生上流》成大赢家</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/191121122710100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第40届青龙电影奖主要获奖者合照。（主办方提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年11月22日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）第40届<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E9%BE%99%E7%94%B5%E5%BD%B1%E5%A5%96.md#1">青龙电影奖</a>颁奖典礼21日晚间于仁川Paradise City举行，由金憓秀与柳演锡担任主持人，<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E9%9B%A8%E7%9B%9B.md#1">郑雨盛</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B5%E5%AE%87%E9%95%87.md#1">赵宇镇</a>、李光洙、曹政奭、<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%A6%E5%A8%A5.md#1">润娥</a>与秀英、朴炯植、丁海寅、金惠允、朴素淡等演员参与，抗癌成功的金宇彬也以颁奖嘉宾身份惊喜登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11672957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11672957" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/191121103958100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11672957" title="Kim HyeSoo and Yoo YeonSeok" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/191121103958100649-600x400.jpg" alt="Kim HyeSoo and Yoo YeonSeok" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11672957" class="wp-caption-text">金憓秀（金惠秀）、柳演锡担任第40届<ahref="https://github.com/hzxdic3950/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E9%BE%99%E7%94%B5%E5%BD%B1%E5%A5%96.md#1">青龙电影奖</a>主持人。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
