<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】顺德生滚鸡汤</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>感恩节，是美国最盛大的节日之一，香气逼人的火鸡、温馨的家人团聚、各地盛行的庆祝游行，都让初冬的天气增添了一份暖意。</p>
<p>感恩节虽不是中国人的传统节日，却是饕餮的绝佳机会。吃不惯火鸡，可以吃点传统的美味，今天就为大家介绍罗厨做的这道<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%BA%E5%BE%B7%E7%94%9F%E6%BB%9A%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">顺德生滚鸡汤</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669881" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fe7979efbe589fc76c8e292de9fe52d5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669881 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fe7979efbe589fc76c8e292de9fe52d5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669881" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A1%BA%E5%BE%B7%E7%94%9F%E6%BB%9A%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">顺德生滚鸡汤</a>清香鲜甜，肉质嫩滑。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>顺德生滚鸡汤的主要食材是“三黄鸡”，也就是羽毛、鸡脚和鸡喙都呈黄色的鸡种。这种鸡肉质嫩滑，皮脆骨软，味道鲜美。“三黄鸡”一直是国内各大超市和家禽市场逢年过节主推的品种，而且都非常畅销。</p>
<p>别看“三黄鸡”体型较小，但分量很足，成年鸡的体重一般在3～4斤。“三黄鸡”一般是放养的，不是拿饲料喂养，所以它的肉质和口感更好，鸡汤更鲜甜。</p>
<p>这道菜的主要配料是生姜，罗厨介绍说，这个汤重点突出生姜的鲜辣味和上等靓鸡的清香鲜甜。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调了制作这道菜的美味秘诀：首先，将鸡洗净的时候，要沥干水分，猛火阴油煎鸡的时候就可以快速地把姜片和鸡煎香，缩短其香气挥发的时间，这是关键。其次，滚汤时姜片可以多一些，并用中火滚之。另外，要一次性加足水，不然，影响鸡汤的味道和浓度。最后，过程中还要耐心地把滚出汤面的杂质、油质去掉，这样可以呈现清澈的鲜汤。</p>
<p>★顺德生滚鸡汤</p>
<p>主料：半只三黄鸡（切块）、姜（切片）。<br />
配料：葱、香菜。<br />
调料：白酒、胡椒粉、盐、糖。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 半只三黄鸡洗干净，吸干水分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669865" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/51d585daa793ac1337bb3a38177302a7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669865 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/51d585daa793ac1337bb3a38177302a7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669865" class="wp-caption-text">三黄鸡半只洗干净，吸干水分切件。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 去皮生姜切片，约25片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/933f3619bea8a69e0b1c5fc7d26cfb1a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669866 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/933f3619bea8a69e0b1c5fc7d26cfb1a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669866" class="wp-caption-text">生姜去皮，切约25片。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 三黄鸡切小块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669869" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/ed94022230ab2f1319493619dc8b9950.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669869 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/ed94022230ab2f1319493619dc8b9950-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669869" class="wp-caption-text">三黄鸡切小块。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热油锅，将生姜片慢慢炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669870" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/4-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669870 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/4-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669870" class="wp-caption-text">热油锅将生姜片炒香。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放入鸡块慢慢炒，炒香后添加少许白酒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669871" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5c951a289c226275f01f38a5c6ff8ded.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669871 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5c951a289c226275f01f38a5c6ff8ded-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669871" class="wp-caption-text">放入鸡块慢慢炒。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 加入煮滚的开水，需要一次性加足水量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/e629c1f86d1a98252861ef97bef66e36.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669872 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/e629c1f86d1a98252861ef97bef66e36-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669872" class="wp-caption-text">加入煮滚的开水。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 过程中需要细心将表面浮油全部去掉，添加胡椒粉1/4茶匙、盐1茶匙、糖少许，然后最后一次去油。保持中火滚约5～6分钟。鸡汤熬至浅浅金黄清透干净为好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669874" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/adb3f4a9240ac8fe489aaf26a6a67d49.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669874 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/adb3f4a9240ac8fe489aaf26a6a67d49-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669874" class="wp-caption-text">过程中细心将汤面的油撇去。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11669877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/8c49f35ce8e93f28e150c0b91d453d89.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669877 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/8c49f35ce8e93f28e150c0b91d453d89-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669877" class="wp-caption-text">保持中火滚约5～6分钟即可。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11669875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669875" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/d704412a0c16da6482235d00a2db4dc5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669875 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/d704412a0c16da6482235d00a2db4dc5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669875" class="wp-caption-text">鸡汤呈浅浅的金黄、清透干净为好。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将滚好的鸡汤放入烧热的砂煲中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/f8745e39aeba4bd3e4099fcee9b0a796.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669878 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/f8745e39aeba4bd3e4099fcee9b0a796-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669878" class="wp-caption-text">将滚好的鸡汤放入烧热的砂煲中。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 加入香菜和葱，小火慢炖即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11669867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/af0888ab854faf5d9ebd8493917290b0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669867 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/af0888ab854faf5d9ebd8493917290b0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669867" class="wp-caption-text">最后加入香菜和葱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11669868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669868" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/effcbc0dbb95f0ede393cc5a60a0cccf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669868 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/effcbc0dbb95f0ede393cc5a60a0cccf-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669868" class="wp-caption-text">美味的顺德生滚鸡汤做好了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11669880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11669880" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/17dd1d71e9b4740420fd113d8e461c07.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11669880 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/17dd1d71e9b4740420fd113d8e461c07-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11669880" class="wp-caption-text">做好的顺德生滚鸡汤，呈浅浅的金黄，清透干净。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
