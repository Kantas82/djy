<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】面团焗鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c111400c0e42f6e31261d2c678c1782a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>有别于传统的炖鸡、炒鸡和炸鸡，也完全不同于西餐中的烤鸡，面团焗鸡吸收了焗烘面粉的麦香和鲜荷叶显着的自然香气，美味达到极致。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>新年的菜肴少不了大鱼大肉，肉质细嫩、滋味鲜美的鸡肉一定是节日餐桌上不可缺少的菜肴。鸡肉蛋白质含量较高，脂肪含量较低，而且蛋白质中的氨基酸含量与蛋乳中的氨基酸谱式极为相似，因此可成为优质蛋白质的来源。鸡肉也是磷、铁、铜和锌的良好来源，并且富含丰富的维生素B12、维生素B6、维生素A、维生素D和维生素K等。</p>
<p>如此美味营养的鸡肉，该如何烹饪才能让人品出节日美食的新意呢？今天我们为大家介绍罗厨为大家制作的这道<a href="https://github.com/sloxqr240/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%9B%A2%E7%84%97%E9%B8%A1.md">面团焗鸡</a>。</p>
<p>有别于传统的炖鸡、炒鸡和炸鸡，也完全不同于西餐中的烤鸡，这道<a href="https://github.com/sloxqr240/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%9B%A2%E7%84%97%E9%B8%A1.md">面团焗鸡</a>极具特色，用面团和荷叶将腌制好的整鸡包裹，然后放入烤箱中，除了焗鸡的原始香气、鲜美肉汁外，焗烘面粉的麦香和鲜荷叶显着的自然香气被鸡肉完全吸收，各种风味相得益彰，美味达到极致。</p>
<h2>面团焗鸡</h2>
<p>主料：三黄鸡1只、面粉3磅。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ac1e1c38fee00aef3f72b1e445067299.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743362" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ac1e1c38fee00aef3f72b1e445067299-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
辅料：葱2根、香菜3根。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3338bac3fa7a967eb01358cc20c17256.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743363" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3338bac3fa7a967eb01358cc20c17256-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料（腌鸡用）：海盐2汤匙、生抽2汤匙、老抽1汤匙、葱2根、香菜3根抓碎取其汁。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鸡洗净沥干水分。用海盐、生抽、老抽涂匀鸡身和内腔。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-1-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743347" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-1-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-2-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743348" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-2-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将葱和香菜抓碎取汁，涂匀鸡身和内腔，用保鲜纸封好腌制40分钟。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743350" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 面粉中放入砂糖，用水搅拌，搓成柔软的面团。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743351" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 将面团用保鲜膜包好，静置15分钟。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743352" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 把面粉团擀成面皮，大小至可以包住整只鸡为好。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743354" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 先把荷叶平铺在面皮上，用几滴香油涂在荷叶面上，包住鸡身，上面覆盖一张涂有香油的荷叶，再用面皮整个包住。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743356" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/6-1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/6-2-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743355" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/6-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 面皮包好的鸡放入烤箱烘烤约40～50分钟。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743357" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 剥去包在鸡外面的面皮、荷叶。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-2-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743358" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-2-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a> <a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-1-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11743359" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8-1-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
