<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>改造金龟车和机车合体萌翻网友：哪里买得到？</h2>
<a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/Q4o7oE7Bx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/focbmg344/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/focbmg344/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年12月04日讯】（大纪元记者嫣华综合报导）网路上近期出现一台从没看过的摩托车，让人眼睛为之一亮，流线型、简单大方的造型看起来可爱极了！网友看到后纷纷留言询问：“哪里买得到？”不过识货的人可能看出来了，它可是大有来头的喔！</p>
<p>原来这台<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E8%BD%A6.md#1">机车</a>是<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%BE%9F%E8%BD%A6.md#1">金龟车</a>的组合体，虽然福斯经典“金龟车”目前已经告别历史舞台，不过，它仍是很多古董车迷心目中的<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E5%B9%BB%E8%BD%A6%E6%AC%BE.md#1">梦幻车款</a>；日前有名国外设计师 布伦特?沃克（Brent Walker）巧手改造出具备金龟经典身型的轻型<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E8%BD%A6.md#1">机车</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11700226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11700226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/93cdf70eb6426779b6d0cdd52d44a86d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11700226 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/93cdf70eb6426779b6d0cdd52d44a86d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11700226" class="wp-caption-text">一部蓝色的福斯<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%BE%9F%E8%BD%A6.md#1">金龟车</a>Beetle。(示意图与本文无关）<ahref="https://flickr.com/photos/georgie56/48909388427/">George, Flickr</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在朋友热情赞助下，布伦特获得了一台外观老旧的初代福斯金龟车（Volkswagen Beetle）；或许设计师也是对金龟车情有独钟的古董车迷吧，打算让金龟车的热情继续燃烧下去，于是动手将它进行拆解，重新设计改造组合。</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/Q4o7oE7Bx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/focbmg344/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/focbmg344/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/p7OEB-eKiYA" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>首先设计师切割、拆卸老车的前、后叶板，再重新焊接组合成他所要的弯曲弧度，最后完成了2台流线型、精致的机车车壳；上面少不了舒适的座椅、脚踏板，还有前头一个方便操作的大把手。</p>
