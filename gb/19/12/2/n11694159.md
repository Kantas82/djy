<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>头上多了小尾巴遭遗弃 美独角兽萌狗受欢迎！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/76923998_2596675430413410_7193394540839960576_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国动物救援中心“Mac's Mission”11月收留了一只只有10周大的“特殊”小狗狗。它的额头中间多长了一条小尾巴，看起就像只毛茸茸的“独角兽”！（macsmission.org授权提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月02日讯】（大纪元记者赖依琴台湾台北报导）美国一间动物救援中心11月收留了一只<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%AB%E9%81%97%E5%BC%83.md#1">被遗弃</a>在郊外、只有10周大的“特殊”小<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>。它的额头中间多长了一条<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%BE%E5%B7%B4.md#1">小尾巴</a>，看起就像只毛茸茸的“<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E8%A7%92%E5%85%BD.md#1">独角兽</a>”！独一无二的可爱外型，也让它大受网友欢迎，成为网红<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>！</p>
<p>英国广播公司（BBC）11月13日报导，美国密苏里州的动物救援中心“Mac&#8217;s Mission”专门收容有特殊需求的动物，在收留这只特殊狗狗后，将它命名为“纳沃”（Narwhal，意为独角鲸）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75472763_2578086815605605_4243327242047848448_n.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11702139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75472763_2578086815605605_4243327242047848448_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702139" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E8%A7%92%E5%85%BD.md#1">独角兽</a>”小狗狗！（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11694165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11694165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/226c559dcda2d1f6b6245509ab94e30e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11694165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/226c559dcda2d1f6b6245509ab94e30e-600x465.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="465" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11694165" class="wp-caption-text">救援中心的人与纳沃。（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纳沃的照片在脸书引起关注、快速窜红，也让各界更加了解Mac&#8217;s Mission成立的宗旨。</p>
<p>救援中心负责人史蒂芬（Rochelle Stephen）告诉BBC，许多有肢体残缺、唇颚裂、受创等“特殊需求”的动物，接受安乐死机会很高，有必要给这些动物一个机会，而他们的机构提供的正是这个机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702140" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75649390_2602120103202276_4937545148520202240_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11702140 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75649390_2602120103202276_4937545148520202240_n-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702140" class="wp-caption-text">救援中心的人帮它取了一个名字“纳沃”（Narwhal，意为独角鲸）。（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11694163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11694163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/508726573beac2da3ea98d26193837fa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11694163 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/508726573beac2da3ea98d26193837fa-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11694163" class="wp-caption-text">纳沃额头上这条尾巴并不会造成任何疼痛。（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>史蒂芬表示，纳沃“身体没有疼痛，一玩可以玩好几个小时”。而且它已经看过兽医，经过X光检查显示，它额头中间尾巴并未连结到任何部位，没有特殊作用，不能左右摇摆，只让它成为“最酷的小狗”。</p>
<p>它额头上这条尾巴也不会造成任何疼痛，因此没有“基于医疗目的移除”的必要，不需进行移除手术。</p>
<p>目前纳沃还未开放认养，救援中心希望再等它“长大一点”，确认它脸上的尾巴不会长太大，造成纳沃生存上的困扰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75650521_2572118096202477_2373213814679142400_n.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11702141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/75650521_2572118096202477_2373213814679142400_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702141" class="wp-caption-text">纳沃正在接受健康检查。（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mac&#8217;s Mission脸书上有关纳沃的照片和影音吸引了许多关注。有网友说，多出的<ahref="https://github.com/jvegqd373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%BE%E5%B7%B4.md#1">小尾巴</a>“如果无害，你们千万别移除这个让它独一无二的特征”，还说纳沃“实在太漂亮，谢谢你们出手救了它”。还有网友说：“纳沃，看看你，网路大红人。你的独一无二将帮助许多其它的狗狗。干得好，小兄弟。”</p>
<p>史蒂芬表示，能因此获得“额外的关注”，让人们更关心这些动物，是很了不起的事。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11694161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11694161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/80ec647ec6d377e6c0e0659918143e26.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11694161 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/80ec647ec6d377e6c0e0659918143e26-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11694161" class="wp-caption-text">独一无二“独角兽”纳沃讨人喜欢！（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11702143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/78215383_2614750681939218_5968899287440949248_n.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11702143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/78215383_2614750681939218_5968899287440949248_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702143" class="wp-caption-text">纳沃的照片和影音吸引了许多关注。（macsmission.org授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
