<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾北管艺术大展 12/7传艺中心开幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/750ac2b4db4487461eaed266fc21b437-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“憨子弟?疯乱弹－台湾北管艺术大展” 12/7传艺中心开幕全体与会来宾与轩社。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月07日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）国立传统<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">艺术</a>中心7日举办“憨子弟?疯乱弹－台湾<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%AE%A1.md#1">北管</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">艺术</a>大展”开幕式，文化部次长萧宗煌、传艺中心主任陈济民与各界来宾及参展轩社为活动揭开序幕。</p>
<p>活动共展出<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%AE%A1.md#1">北管</a>文物350余件，展期自即日起至110年5月23日止，并借此盛会举办为期两天（12/7、8）的“台湾北管艺术交流活动”，开幕日共计19个借展轩社、公部门典藏单位及民间收藏家共同与会。8个社团在行政大楼后方集结，彩街〈文昌街）至庙埕广场，现场热闹滚滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11707153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5630a99b042a1a2ff1c9753272333c04.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707153 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5630a99b042a1a2ff1c9753272333c04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707153" class="wp-caption-text">12/7日北管社团在行政大楼后方集结踩街〈文昌街）至庙埕广场。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11707154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707154" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/26718c18a1f6356c4cdeb7ae132af001.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707154 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/26718c18a1f6356c4cdeb7ae132af001-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707154" class="wp-caption-text">12/7日北管社团在行政大楼后方集结踩街〈文昌街）至庙埕广场。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11707155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/226e6b0267d97307d600216f321920f5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707155 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/226e6b0267d97307d600216f321920f5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707155" class="wp-caption-text">12/7日北管社团在行政大楼后方集结踩街〈文昌街）至庙埕广场。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化部次长萧宗煌表示，北管子弟团，可说是台湾汉人社会最早的音乐教室之一，“北管”曾经是台湾最风靡的流行音乐，热闹喧腾、气势十足，不仅被广泛应用在庙会祭典和婚丧喜庆等各种场合，也与台湾人的生命礼俗息息相关。从俗谚“吃肉吃三层，看戏看乱弹。”就能显示它特殊的地位。萧宗煌次长特别感谢各借展单位的支持与信任，提供全国民众一览传统北管风华的机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11707156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b717c723d76ed090bd7f5cfa3ad9275a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707156 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b717c723d76ed090bd7f5cfa3ad9275a-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707156" class="wp-caption-text">文化部次长萧宗煌（左）颁赠感谢状。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11707157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2bce2eaebc95936d4ed356c777f6c6be.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707157 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2bce2eaebc95936d4ed356c777f6c6be-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707157" class="wp-caption-text">文化部次长萧宗煌、传艺中心主任陈济民与全体借展轩社代表合影。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>传艺中心主任陈济民强调，本次展有5大特色：品项最丰富、地域最宽广、历史最绵长、工艺最精美及文资最宝贵。完整呈现台湾北管音乐、戏剧、阵头、信仰、组织与社会互动等展示内容，带领大家认识这位熟悉却又陌生的老朋友，是台湾近期规模最盛大的北管艺术主题展览。展件精彩，诚挚邀请大家前来传艺中心参观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11707158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3296f873168e906f71ae281fc39b3e97.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707158 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3296f873168e906f71ae281fc39b3e97-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707158" class="wp-caption-text">参加轩社，台上、台下北管大会奏、经典曲目〈风入松〉。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11707159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11707159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2c66074dd4ef133b79eeb79c9f5701d2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11707159 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2c66074dd4ef133b79eeb79c9f5701d2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11707159" class="wp-caption-text">参加轩社，台上、台下北管大会奏、经典曲目〈风入松〉。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>传艺中心同时也借此盛会办理两日（12/7-12/8）“台湾北管艺术交流活动”，邀集全台各地共九组重要轩社一同带来北管音乐与戏曲的真实呈现，让民众有机会实际近距离体验台湾传统重金属音乐的震撼，更将以观摩竞演的方式，让北管子弟齐聚一堂，以艺术进行文化情感的交流与对话，再次诉说台湾北管艺术的铿锵风华。</p>
