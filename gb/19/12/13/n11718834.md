<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】清热防癌绿芦笋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/780e9d4c74a900b77cfe0342dc2f9f251.jpg" />
<h6>芦笋Asparagus officinalis天门冬科。（郑清海／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月13日讯】（大纪元记者郑清海报导）</p>
<p><strong>羽叶轻飘小白花</strong><br />
<strong>种在田中清晨挖</strong><br />
<strong>消暑防崖癌人人爱</strong><br />
<strong>清香利尿风味佳</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11718837" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9e983fd6dc3d47a3fb5fca9e37f9ee4c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/9e983fd6dc3d47a3fb5fca9e37f9ee4c-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11718836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f0df6fdbd678d1598a7b78fe6a49acbe.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f0df6fdbd678d1598a7b78fe6a49acbe-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花钟形绿白色至淡黄色，通常是雌雄异株，偶有雌雄同株之情形。 （郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>植物阅读趣：</strong></p>
<p><strong>芦笋Asparagus officinalis天门冬科</strong></p>
<p>又名石刁柏、龙须菜。多年生开花草本植物。高约100～150公分，具有粗壮的茎与羽毛状的叶子。而叶子其实是叶状枝，呈针状。花钟形绿白色至淡黄色，通常是雌雄异株，偶有雌雄同株之情形。果为小的红色浆果，对人类是有毒的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718838" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/282eb5ff6db160500c7cabce9acf37b2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/282eb5ff6db160500c7cabce9acf37b2-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">又名石刁柏、龙须菜。多年生开花草本植物。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>明李时珍《本草纲目》言芦笋能“瘿结热气、利小便”；根称为小百部，能润肺镇咳、祛痰、杀虫等。早在两千年前《神农本草经》上已将芦笋列为“上品之上”，称久服轻身益气延年。故中国栽种及了解芦笋亦超过两千年历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4e43b5b6c3b0319ea95396c92a75bb5f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4e43b5b6c3b0319ea95396c92a75bb5f-450x680.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="680" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">叶子其实是叶状枝，呈针状。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于芦笋精致的香味和利尿特性，早已被用来作为一种蔬菜和药物。罗马人甚至利用阿尔卑斯山高处将芦笋冷冻，作为伊比鸠鲁盛宴之食材。为了快速运送该蔬菜，奥古斯都皇帝还创造了“芦笋舰队”。</p>
<p>台湾曾经大力推广改良，成功打入国际市场，栽培面积最高峰曾达1万8000公顷。1978年外销产值接近1亿2000万美金，是世界芦笋罐头主要出口国家，曾誉为“芦笋王国”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e26d735b6ffd3e5b1b3a75ebb8ed2a69.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e26d735b6ffd3e5b1b3a75ebb8ed2a69-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《神农本草经》上已将芦笋列为“上品之上”，称久服轻身益气延年。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦笋具有低糖、低脂肪、高纤维素的特点。还有丰富的微量元素，其蛋白质含量虽不高，但氨基酸组成比例适当。所以经常食用芦笋也可以预防高脂血症和心脑血管疾病。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11718842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/25caaa7e0aebff9fbfd911bf03ee04ad.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11718842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/25caaa7e0aebff9fbfd911bf03ee04ad-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高约100～50公分，具有粗壮的茎与羽毛状的叶子。（郑清海／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
