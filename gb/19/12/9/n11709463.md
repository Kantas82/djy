<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台东慢食节 民众赏美食不忘撑香港</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ca18723d3b8c84d16231d2a5ab8bbc45-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东慢食节，民众采购香港美食支持香港。（龙芳／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东县年度最后一场<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%A2%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">慢食节</a>，以“团圆餐桌”为主题，7、8两日在台东市区铁花新聚落登场，共邀请37间来自纵谷、海线、南回、市区的店家共襄盛举，各店家除了端出自家经典好滋味，也以团圆为主题特制家族拿手菜，活动吸引了来自全台各县市的人参与，气氛热闹温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11709467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11709467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ae7db40db709731d04e64977d29ecf87.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11709467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ae7db40db709731d04e64977d29ecf87-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11709467" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a><ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%85%A2%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">慢食节</a>，气氛热闹又温馨。 （龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11709468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11709468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b61ae7cc94cf9f2bf22b60afd9fad06b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11709468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b61ae7cc94cf9f2bf22b60afd9fad06b-450x362.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11709468" class="wp-caption-text">台东慢食节，崁顶部落布农族的母子带来传统部落<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11709469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11709469" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e07b18f99b7ba8ad480cf5985cc8530c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11709469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e07b18f99b7ba8ad480cf5985cc8530c-450x346.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11709469" class="wp-caption-text">台东慢食节，店家现场料理花生。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场摊位有来自台东山海间的多元族群，包括闽、客、阿美、布农、鲁凯、排湾、卑南、达悟族等，以及从国内外移居台东的移居者，有崁顶部落布农族的小米饭、闽南家族的过年咸甜年糕、德国南方的家族团聚料理面疙瘩、难得一见的烤全猪也出炉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11709470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11709470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1be9750586157fa3e7115542f11e3bc0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11709470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1be9750586157fa3e7115542f11e3bc0-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11709470" class="wp-caption-text">台东慢食节，难得一见的烤全猪。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两位香港新移民以道地的港式家乡味展现香港传统<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>，美食上面不忘书写着“撑香港”和“香港加油”的心声，摊位前大排长龙，有民众说要以实际行动支持香港。</p>
<p>土坂部落的Vuvu们带领大家制作芋头团子rinumul，不少民众报名试身手，挽起袖子将煮熟的小芋头以杵臼捣出黏性，再搓揉成团后再沾上花生粉，第一次试做的民众直呼有趣。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11709471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11709471" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/588d15c534f2400d8bdf23d198a60eed.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11709471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/588d15c534f2400d8bdf23d198a60eed-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11709471" class="wp-caption-text">台东慢食节，民众体验以杵臼制作芋头团子。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
