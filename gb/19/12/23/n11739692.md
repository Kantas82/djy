<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【C2食光】圣诞义式脆饼 手感温度首选</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/Jc2M5"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bdgbqq390/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>圣诞义式脆饼是意大利传统点心，非常简单易做，口感偏硬、脆、香又低脂低卡，午后时光享用不负担喔！（C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>瑞雪纷飞的季节，是感恩的季节。浓厚的节庆气氛，让生活紧凑的人们有了舒缓身心的机会。不妨，趁着圣诞佳节的到来，邀请亲戚好友来一场暖胃又暖心的聚餐派对，除了交换礼物之外，餐桌上的手作<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E5%B9%B2.md#1">饼干</a>更显温度和温馨。为你介绍这一款<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E5%BC%8F%E8%84%86%E9%A5%BC.md#1">义式脆饼</a>制作过程简单不复杂，成功率高不出错！</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【圣诞<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E5%BC%8F%E8%84%86%E9%A5%BC.md#1">义式脆饼</a> Biscotti】</strong></span></p>
<p>Biscotti这个字的来自中世纪拉丁文biscotus，有二次烘焙的意思，这个义式脆饼需经由二次烘烤而成，是意大利传统点心，非常简单易做，吃起来的口感偏硬、脆、香。这种<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E5%B9%B2.md#1">饼干</a>容易保存，很适合外出旅行携带。而且相较其他的饼干，Biscotti较低脂低卡，因此很受到大众欢迎。</p>
<p>圣诞节的下午搭配咖啡或茶，享受美好的午茶<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">食光</a>～</p>
<p><strong>【食材】</strong><br />
无盐奶油20g<br />
糖50g<br />
蛋2颗<br />
盐少许<br />
泡打粉1/4小匙<br />
中筋面粉200g<br />
杏仁角60g</p>
<p><strong>【其他食材】</strong><br />
黑巧克力酱<br />
白巧克力酱<br />
杏仁角<br />
干燥莓果碎</p>
<p><strong>【做法】</strong><br />
1.将糖及奶油打散，之后打入一颗蛋，打匀后再打另一颗蛋打匀。<br />
2.混入过筛面粉及泡打粉拌匀，加入少许盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11739712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11739712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/biscotti02.png"><img class="wp-image-11739712 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/biscotti02-450x315.png" alt="食光,脆饼" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11739712" class="wp-caption-text">面粉及泡打粉必须过筛才混入拌匀，最后加入少许盐。（C2<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.再混入杏仁角拌成团，压平后放入预热好的烤箱以200度烤30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11739718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11739718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/biscotti03.png"><img class="wp-image-11739718 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/biscotti03-450x315.png" alt="食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11739718" class="wp-caption-text">（<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.取出，待冷却切0.8公分厚片铺平，再以160度烤15分钟即可。</p>
