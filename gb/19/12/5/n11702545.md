<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【C2食光-节气料理】小雪迎冬 膳食宜肥甘味厚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/30ade563c52d901607b88bef13fd0aec-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南瓜派更多料理方式，请看小影片： (大纪元制图)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>报导）进入冬天的第二个节气“<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E9%9B%AA.md#1">小雪</a>”后，天气明显渐冷，宜早睡晚起，多晒太阳，温补身体。</p>
<p>在冬季时节，建议的饮食有：牛奶、起司、花生、大豆、胡萝卜、芹菜等。</p>
<p><strong>示范料理?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11702557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/bda6cd70c8a6105d6b52deaa26919c18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/bda6cd70c8a6105d6b52deaa26919c18-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702557" class="wp-caption-text">“蔬菜牛奶炖鸡腿”搭配鸡肉跟蔬菜一起炖煮，不但暖心暖胃，也能为自己跟家人补充满满的营养。（<ahref="https://github.com/bdgbqq390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着东北季风增强，凉意更加明显。冬天生产的牛奶，不仅营养丰富，也有助于提高身体的御寒能力。香醇的牛奶，搭配鸡肉跟蔬菜一起炖煮，不但暖心暖胃，也能为自己跟家人补充满满的营养。</p>
<p><strong>更多料理方式，请看小影片：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11702548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702548" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8095e17b34992053225deaad7e9ffbf5.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-11702548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/8095e17b34992053225deaad7e9ffbf5.png" alt="" width="400" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702548" class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜牛奶炖鸡腿更多料理方式，请看小影片： (大纪元制图)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>食材：</strong>鸡腿肉1只洋葱1/2颗蒜头1颗马铃薯1颗红萝卜1条四季豆牛奶500ml月桂叶1片黑胡椒适量</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>1.汤锅烧热后放油，再放入蒜，洋葱炒香。</p>
<p>2.放入鸡肉（鸡皮向下）煎至上色后，加入牛奶。</p>
<p>3.煮滚后，加入月桂叶、红萝卜、马铃薯、玉米笋，转小火，加盖再煮约20－30分钟。</p>
<p>4.最后放上煮过的四季豆，撒些盐及胡椒调味。</p>
<p><strong>“营养师的贴心小叮咛”</strong></p>
<p><strong>?by 陈小薇营养师</strong></p>
<p>每百公克马铃薯热量约77大卡，维生素C含量高达苹果的6～8倍，另外，淀粉组成里含有特殊的抗性淀粉（resistant starch, RS），这是指存在碳水化合物中的一种淀粉型态，也被称为难消化淀粉。</p>
<p>抗性淀粉与膳食纤维相同，不能被人体的肠胃道所消化吸收，进而分解成为葡萄糖进入人体，而是被肠道中的细菌利用，产生短链脂肪酸与部分气体，加上抗性淀粉本身所含有较低热量，能产生更持久的饱足感。</p>
<p>抗性淀粉可以再细分四类：RS1、RS2、RS3、RS4，马铃薯属RS2，是食材本身的淀粉颗粒有“完全无法糊化”的物质（resistant starch granules）。</p>
<p>营养师小叮咛：马铃薯发芽后，会大量产生茄碱（Glycoalkaloid），茄碱是种天然的杀虫剂，具有毒性，并且加热也不易破坏毒性，会导致急性中毒症状，如呕吐、腹痛、腹泻。</p>
<p><strong>示范料理??南瓜派</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11702556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e6c39c74078b60fd830920d1a1a0c35d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e6c39c74078b60fd830920d1a1a0c35d-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702556" class="wp-caption-text">“ 南瓜派”是美国感恩节大餐的传统料理。 （C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南瓜派是美国感恩节大餐的传统料理。南瓜派历史由来很久，据说几世纪前就有文献记载了。</p>
<p>传统南瓜派做法，使用南瓜、苹果、姜粉、肉桂、豆寇、丁香及糖制作而成，不过，因为烤具不如现代先进，所以没有派皮，而是用苹果与南瓜层层堆叠，代替派皮。</p>
<p>加了香料的南瓜派，可以让身体变得暖和，吃起来香甜不腻。不妨感受一下，异国节日的风情，手工的心意，不只感谢上天赐予食物，同时上桌慰劳家人，和身边的人们说声“谢谢”吧！</p>
<p><strong>更多料理方式，请看小影片：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11702547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702547" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/bbcd810952fd21b3b8959d50b9832884.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-11702547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/bbcd810952fd21b3b8959d50b9832884.png" alt="" width="400" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702547" class="wp-caption-text">南瓜派更多料理方式，请看小影片： (大纪元制图)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>派皮食材：</strong>中筋面粉130g冰无盐奶油60g盐2g蛋黄1颗冰牛奶30c.c.内饀：蒸熟的南瓜泥200g炼乳100g鸡蛋1颗肉桂粉1/2小匙姜泥1/2小匙豆蔻粉1/2小匙盐少许</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>1. 奶油切骰子大小后，放入冰箱。</p>
<p>2. 拿出模具，奶油涂在底面、侧面，放入冰箱。</p>
<p>3. 取蛋黄、面粉过筛，将准备好的奶油放在面粉中间，用派刀一直切到奶油变很细（这个作业用手弄容易就溶化，不要让奶油溶化）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/29391ed5ab9c4389a5e7798f509e01d2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/29391ed5ab9c4389a5e7798f509e01d2-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702549" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 做一个山后，中间做个洞。</p>
<p>5. 放入蛋黄、盐巴、牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5a420d9bf435dd29d4e56eee3f9c06b2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5a420d9bf435dd29d4e56eee3f9c06b2-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702550" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 用手指在洞里将蛋牛奶搅拌好、从外往内拌入。</p>
<p>7. 确认蛋液跟面粉混好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b2eeb48dd89ce3fb8ab516f1f0d2deac.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/b2eeb48dd89ce3fb8ab516f1f0d2deac-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702551" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 用保鲜膜包起来压扁后冰起来。（注意：不要揉太久！到做完此面团都要是冰的，烤时才会有脆的口感。）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/44311c901c1529616bca60fc08df7f3e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/44311c901c1529616bca60fc08df7f3e-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702552" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 冰完后，将面团擀至比派模大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702553" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f4a21cec36aadea32fe00fab189e1a3d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/f4a21cec36aadea32fe00fab189e1a3d-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702553" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.铺入派模内，用木棒滚一下，切掉旁边多余的皮，用叉子做几个洞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11702554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11702554" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/841da930ace1e6d3dfbd9d3c971ead95.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11702554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/841da930ace1e6d3dfbd9d3c971ead95-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11702554" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.待派皮冷却加入南瓜馅。</p>
<p>12.烤箱维持180℃烤30－35分钟。◇</p>
