<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】年菜系列之二：砂锅滋补石斑鱼</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>石斑鱼通常都呈褐色或红色，因其身上有明显的条纹和斑点而得名。石斑鱼肉质细嫩少刺，没有腥味与土味，是低脂肪、高蛋白的鱼类，所以被称为“海鸡肉”。</p>
<p>石斑鱼还含有铁、钙、磷、镁、硒、钾、胶质、OMEGA-3脂肪酸等营养素，OMEGA-3脂肪酸中，EPA对血管壁的壁内层细胞有调节作用，是抗血管硬化的重要元素之一，而DHA则是大脑皮质中细胞膜的成分，是负责传递讯息的重要物质。</p>
<p>石斑鱼是海鲜中的珍品之一，滋补养颜，肉质细嫩、鲜甜，而砂锅相对平衡的环境温度，使得鱼的鲜香成分溢出得更多，加之罗厨独特的烹制方法及配料，更令深海石斑鱼炖出来的鱼汤香气四溢、香醇可口，是老少皆宜的滋补佳品。</p>
<p>另外提醒您，若是选购新鲜非冷冻的鱼，如果闻起来有股氨水味，说明鱼已腐坏；鱼眼应该是炯炯有神的，不能混浊或呈灰白色；鱼鳃则应该是明亮且带点透明感的红色；鱼身摸起来应该富有弹性。</p>
<h2>砂锅滋补石斑鱼</h2>
<p>主料：深海石斑鱼1尾，约1.2磅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4128278ef25bcc3fde49095604791fd8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4128278ef25bcc3fde49095604791fd8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：小红萝卜3根切厚片、葱1根切段、姜块切3片、红枣5粒开边、枸杞子20粒、当归3小片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6a717fa0a1f445d933cb648412d41c31.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6a717fa0a1f445d933cb648412d41c31-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料：自制葱油0.5汤匙、盐1茶匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 把锅烧热后，放0.5汤匙葱油。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 加入姜片、葱段，将石斑鱼煎香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 添加50盎司矿泉水煮沸。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-2-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 将鱼和汤一起移至砂锅中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 添加小红萝卜、葱、姜、红枣、枸杞、当归等配料。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加盖温火慢炖15分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 开锅盖，将鱼汤表面的杂质去干净。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 添加黄酒2汤匙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 再加盖慢火煮一会儿。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 最后加1茶匙盐。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品鱼汤香气四溢。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/11-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/11-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/833080113a1323135005e757e8485ec6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11766557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/833080113a1323135005e757e8485ec6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
