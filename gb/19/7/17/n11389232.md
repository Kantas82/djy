<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本新建学校 船形外观向塔斯马尼亚精神致敬</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月17日讯】（大纪元记者沈诗昂<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）维州政府即将在墨尔本港（Port Melbourne）的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E4%BA%BA%E5%BC%AF.md#1">渔人弯</a>（Fishermans Bend）修建一所新<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">学校</a>，以满足当地不断增长的人口需要。</p>
<p>据《太阳先驱报》报导，这所新中学共有四层楼，从外观上看像是一艘船，以示对塔斯马尼亚精神的致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11389251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_02-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389251" class="wp-caption-text">即将新建的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E4%BA%BA%E5%BC%AF.md#1">渔人弯</a>（Fishermans Bend）中学效果图。（schoolbuildings.vic.gov.au）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11389252" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/GettyImages-50809509.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/GettyImages-50809509-600x739.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="739" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389252" class="wp-caption-text">这所新中学从外观上看像是一艘船，以示对塔斯马尼亚精神的致敬。（TORSTEN BLACKWOOD/AFP/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">学校</a>里还将分别修建机器人以及数字技术和制造实验室。</p>
<p>Albert Park区议员弗利（Martin Foley）表示，该学校的修建，将满足渔人弯地区越来越多的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E7%94%9F.md#1">学生</a>入学需求。</p>
<p>据悉，该中学是维州即将修建的七所教学楼式的学校之一，早期施工将于本月底开始，新学校将于2022年投入使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11389254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_01-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389254" class="wp-caption-text">即将新建的渔人弯（Fishermans Bend）中学效果图。（schoolbuildings.vic.gov.au）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于维州人口快速增加，维州的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E7%94%9F.md#1">学生</a>人数也在急剧增加。到2023年，维州公立学校将额外增加80,000名学生 ，届时，维州公立学校学生将达到1,074,000名以上。</p>
<p>维州政府在去年11月选举之前，曾承诺将在八年内修建100所新学校。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11389255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_04-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389255" class="wp-caption-text">即将新建的渔人弯（Fishermans Bend）中学效果图。（schoolbuildings.vic.gov.au）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11389256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_05-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389256" class="wp-caption-text">即将新建的渔人弯（Fishermans Bend）中学效果图。（schoolbuildings.vic.gov.au）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11389257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11389257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11389257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/190715_FishermansBendSS_06-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11389257" class="wp-caption-text">即将新建的渔人弯（Fishermans Bend）中学效果图。（schoolbuildings.vic.gov.au）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前，政府已在Prahran、South Melbourne 和Richmond修建了教学楼式的新学校，并准备在Docklands、McKinnon和Fitzroy也建造教学楼式的学校。</p>
<p>维州教育厅长默里诺（James Merlino）表示，维州正在经历着重大的改变，大量学校的修建是为了跟上维州的发展步伐。</p>
