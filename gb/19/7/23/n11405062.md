<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超级皮卡COLORADO ROX车展获奖</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月24日讯】（大纪元工商记者赵凯<ahref="https://github.com/sxaqjg3248/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>编译报导 ）霍顿（Holden）的新超级皮卡 COLORADO ROX在奥克兰的2019年CRC Speedshow 车展展出，除了赢得更多粉丝外，还被展会授予“Best Outdoor Site”奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Holden-Speedshow-Award_LR-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11415475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Holden-Speedshow-Award_LR-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415475" class="wp-caption-text">Holden Speedshow 奖杯。（提供图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们很高兴看到，展会上人们对COLORADO ROX非常感兴趣，COLORADO ROX赢得展会奖项更是锦上添花的事情。”全国营销经理Marc Warr说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/holden-Marc-Warr_Speedshow-Award_LR-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11415477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/holden-Marc-Warr_Speedshow-Award_LR-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415477" class="wp-caption-text">Holden全国营销经理Marc Warr</figcaption></figure>
<p>“许多人在COLORADO ROX前自拍，攀进高大车体内参观的人也是络绎不绝。” Warr说。</p>
<p>ROX的鲜明特点受到了观众的特别赞誉，其中包括35英寸的大型轮胎，车内全部紧凑型运动座椅，雄伟车身和升高的悬架，及后甲板载货区和0.5米的扩展托板。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/SatelliteqblobcolurldataablobkeyidablobtableMungoBlobsablobwhere1238061009562assbinarytrueasiteHolden.co_.nz_.pagespeed.ce_.QV_0MmXqii-1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-11415495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/SatelliteqblobcolurldataablobkeyidablobtableMungoBlobsablobwhere1238061009562assbinarytrueasiteHolden.co_.nz_.pagespeed.ce_.QV_0MmXqii-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415495" class="wp-caption-text">Colorado Rox （图片来源：Holden官网）。</figcaption></figure>
<p>在COLORADO ROX出现在Speedshow车展的几个小时前，官方宣布另一新车2020款Corvette将在<ahref="https://github.com/sxaqjg3248/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>上市。</p>
<p>“新款Corvette的出现引起了全世界高性能汽车爱好者的注意，尤其在新西兰” Warr说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415502" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Colorado-Rox-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11415502 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Colorado-Rox-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415502" class="wp-caption-text">Colorado Rox （图片来源：Holden官网）。</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们将在适当的时候发布更多细节，但目前我们可以确认的是，它将是右舵车，拥有6.2升V8发动机，功率369KW。 ”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415507" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Satellite-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11415507 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Satellite-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415507" class="wp-caption-text">Colorado Rox （图片来源：Holden官网）。</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们的经销商对2020款Corvette立即表现出了强烈的兴趣，在周末的展会上也收到了很多正面的反馈。我们非常期待新的Corvette到来。”</p>
