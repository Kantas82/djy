<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这些食物是“天然降压药” 你吃对了吗？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/natural-food-lower-blood-pressure2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>含钾丰富的食物无异于天然降血压药。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>你知道吗？<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%84%B6.md#1">天然</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%8D%E5%8E%8B%E8%8D%AF.md#1">降压药</a>就藏在超市里。</p>
<p>高<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%BE.md#1">钾</a>、低钠的饮食有助于预防和改善高血压。钾离子进入人体后，能放松血管，帮助排除体内导致血压上升的钠，维持血液中电解质平衡，降低高血压、心脏病和中风风险。</p>
<p>然而，多数人没有吃到足够的<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%BE.md#1">钾</a>，世界卫生组织（WHO）建议从食物中摄取，并指出，当钾的每日摄取量增加到3510mg～4680mg时，<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%8D%E8%A1%80%E5%8E%8B.md#1">降血压</a>效果最明显。</p>
<p>含钾丰富的食物无异于<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%84%B6.md#1">天然</a><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%8D%E5%8E%8B%E8%8D%AF.md#1">降压药</a>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">“天然降压药”<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AB%E9%92%BE%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">含钾食物</a>大公开</span></h2>
<p>下表汇整了常见的高钾食物：</p>
<figure id="attachment_11419663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11419663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/food-rich-in-potassium0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11419663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/food-rich-in-potassium0-600x942.jpg" alt="含钾食物是天然降压药，上表汇整了常见的高钾食物。（大纪元制表）" width="600" b="942" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11419663" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AB%E9%92%BE%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">含钾食物</a>是天然降压药，表格汇整了常见的高钾食物。（大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于钾离子易溶于水，在处理食物时，可先清洗后再切块，并避免长时间将食物浸泡于水中；烹煮后最好保留汤汁，如此可以减少钾离子的流失。</p>
<p>需要注意的是，患有肾脏病或服用钾离子补充剂的患者，可能需要限制钾的摄取量。</p>
<p>下次去超市时，多留意一下含钾食物吧。</p>
