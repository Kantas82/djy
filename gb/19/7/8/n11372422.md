<p>【大纪元2019年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者米蓝达综合报导）可爱到令人想咬一口，这些卡通萌咖，你最喜欢哪一个？</p>

<p>本来Piewpun只是想要让自己的中餐变得不一样、多一些特色；当她将每天亲手做出的午餐<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md#1">便当</a>开始分享在IG上，没想到成了当红爆品，受到众人瞩目。</p>
<p>被问从哪里得到“萌<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md#1">便当</a>”的灵感，她说，“我只是在网路上看到用咖喱饭团做成的熊猫家族照片，觉得它们真的好可爱，也想试着做做看。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11374434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11374434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-16-450x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Piewpun决定主题和角色的过程很简单，“我喜欢可爱卡通的每一个角色，只需决定想做哪一个角色，想想当下有什么食材，然后去google相关角色的照片作参考 。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11374389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11374389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-6-450x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">日本宫崎骏卡通的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E7%8C%AB.md#1">龙猫</a>家族是她的最爱。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Piewpun最爱又最喜欢创造的卡通角色是宫崎骏的“<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E7%8C%AB.md#1">龙猫</a>”，她特别喜欢它灰灰的、毛茸茸又肥嘟嘟的笨重样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11374394" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11374394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-10-450x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宫崎骏卡通龙猫家族是她的最爱。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax</a>)</figcaption></figure>

<p>她还坚持只用健康、天然又安全的食材，“我不只是做好看的，饭团缤纷的颜色都是使用天然原料，好让饭团充满耀眼明亮的色彩，每粒饭团都是100%安全健康可以吃的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11374391" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11374391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/Cute-Foodies-by-Peaceloving-Pax-8-450x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">缤纷多彩的小鸡。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/cutefoodies/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cute Foodies by Peaceloving Pax</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>您一定也很好奇， 这些超萌便当需要花多长时间才能做出来，制作流程如何？</p>
<p>Piewpun透露，从决定做哪个角色、心中勾勒出图像，然后寻找网路参考图片、准备食材，到做出超萌饭团放在午餐盒或摆盘上，整个过程大概需要1~2小时。</p>
