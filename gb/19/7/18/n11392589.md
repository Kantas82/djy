<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“高冷”猫咪撞脸跩猫布偶 一见钟情日日厮守</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“哈利”站在跩猫玩偶的旁边，一脸“主人，你在跟我开玩笑”的震惊模样。(Courtesy of <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Harry and Friends
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年07月20日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君综合报导）你是否喜欢搜集与家宠或者喜欢的动物，例如柯基、柴犬、橘子猫等外型相似的布偶或周边商品？是的话，你肯定能理解这位主人第一眼看到跟家中喵喵长相如出一辙的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%81%B6.md#1">玩偶</a>时，内心有多么喜悦！</p>
<p>一位纽西兰女孩爱猫成痴，家中除了养有两只<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>，还帮忙照顾五只猫咪！她因此为爱猫们特别开了粉专“Harry and Friends”，其中领衔主演的黑白色<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%94%E9%A9%B0%E7%8C%AB.md#1">奔驰猫</a>“哈利”（Harry），就是不小心与跩猫<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%81%B6.md#1">玩偶</a>“撞脸”的苦主。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11396411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11396411 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-1-600x399.jpg" alt="小哈利。(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Harry and Friends&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小哈利。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>据动物网媒“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo）报导，家住纽西兰基督城（Christchurch）的史密斯（Beka Smyth）有天和朋友购物时，发现店里陈列架顶部有只大玩偶，让她当场看傻眼的是——这只脸部表情有点跩跩的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>玩偶，与哈利简直如出一辙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11396412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11396412 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-2-600x399.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Harry and Friends&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">表情跩跩的猫咪玩偶。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>想当然尔，史密斯登时手刀将玩偶买下来，准备让它回家与爱猫哈利相见欢。</p>
<p>哈利当然也是看傻了眼，但它并不是被吓到，不解的眼神仿佛在说着：“史密斯，你在开我玩笑吗？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11392603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11392603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11392603 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-05-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11392603" class="wp-caption-text">“哈利”站在跩猫玩偶的旁边，一脸“史密斯，你在跟我开玩笑”的模样。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>史密斯随后将这只跩猫玩偶放到哈利的玩具专区，这只<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%94%E9%A9%B0%E7%8C%AB.md#1">奔驰猫</a>好奇心“爆棚”，上下打量新玩具。转眼间，哈利和跩猫玩偶蹭在一起了。再几个天，这一猫一偶，从此过着“焦不离孟，孟不离焦”的甜蜜生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11396415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11396415 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-5-450x600.jpg" alt="哈利紧依它跩猫玩偶。(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/&quot; rel=&quot;noopener&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;&gt;Harry and Friends&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">哈利紧依跩猫玩偶。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11396413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11396413" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11396413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/harry-3-600x450.jpg" alt="哈利紧依它跩猫玩偶。(Courtesy of Harry and Friends)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11396413" class="wp-caption-text">哈利紧依跩猫玩偶。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然哈利跟跩猫玩偶的神情像似，但这只外表高冷的奔驰猫其实性格很暖，特别喜欢向史密斯讨“爱的抱抱”。</p>
<p>史密斯说，“哈利看起来像是极度讨厌任何人事物，事实上它却是最黏人、最爱抱抱的小伙伴。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11392597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11392597" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-02.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11392597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-02.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="640" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11392597" class="wp-caption-text">外表高冷的奔驰猫“哈利”。(Courtesy of Harry and Friends)</figcaption></figure>
<p>不仅如此，“哈利”也很在意史密斯的安危。她在粉专上透露“哈利”喜欢紧紧黏着她，就连沐浴时也不放过。因为“哈利”可能担心史密斯的安危，直到她离开浴室才作罢！</p>
<p>同猫咪朝夕相处，史密斯很清楚猫的个性并非所谓“冷淡只爱罐罐的漂亮生物”。</p>
<p>她告诉“大纪元”，和猫咪生活最棒的地方是它们的特质与陪伴，“它们给我的生活带来许多欢乐，也是比大家观念里更忠心的动物！它们会跟着我在屋里忙进忙出，当我不在时想念我，我难过或生病时紧紧紧依偎在身边”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11392657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11392657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-08.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11392657 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190718-Yichun-Harry-Harry-and-Friends-08-600x536.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11392657" class="wp-caption-text">对史密斯而言，猫咪并非是高冷、只爱罐罐的漂亮生物，它们很温暖也很懂得陪伴。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Harry-and-Friends-925864111091820/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Harry and Friends</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然被猫咪“冷处理”过的你，看了这段话可能还有些怀疑，但对史密斯来说，外冷内热的哈利猫就是“乖猫”的最佳定义。</p>
