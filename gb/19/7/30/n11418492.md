<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>警友办事处主任简添福赠湿纸巾 员警酷暑拭汗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/3c65d1ee4b971595d243f0becf8638da-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>警友办事处主任简添福赠湿纸巾让员警酷暑拭汗。（大园分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月30日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园分局新任分局长黄炳训上周拜访桃园警友办事处主任简添福，茶叙时黄炳训谈到近日气温酷热，同仁于高温之下认真执勤，回到所内都是满身大汗，非常的辛劳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11418496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11418496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/0447d17ca70fcb6b069f663dd72bf04b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11418496 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/0447d17ca70fcb6b069f663dd72bf04b-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11418496" class="wp-caption-text">酷暑中执勤应注意补充水分并珍惜善用湿纸巾，预防发生中暑情况。（大园分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>简添福当时表示将捐赠一批<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BF%E7%BA%B8%E5%B7%BE.md#1">湿纸巾</a>提供同仁擦汗之用，冰凉后的湿纸巾可以迅速降低体温，免除<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%98%E8%AD%A6.md#1">员警</a>汗透重衫的湿热不适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11418495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11418495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/f4b8ed1578489ec0ca3e925c4b2e2f5f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11418495 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/f4b8ed1578489ec0ca3e925c4b2e2f5f-600x375.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11418495" class="wp-caption-text">高温之下认真执勤，回到所内都是满身大汗。（大园分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>30日10时，简添福与办事处顾问来到分局致赠百箱<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BF%E7%BA%B8%E5%B7%BE.md#1">湿纸巾</a>，黄炳训代表接受，黄炳训除感谢警友的关怀鼓励，并叮咛<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%98%E8%AD%A6.md#1">员警</a>酷暑中执勤应注意补充水分并珍惜善用湿纸巾，预防发生中暑情况。◇</p>
