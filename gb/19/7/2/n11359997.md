<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Jolin被称“上个世代巨星”施文彬自嘲上上世代</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907021037151470-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>公视台语台新闻《青春！咱的梦》节目记者会7月2日在台北举行。图左起为施文彬、流氓阿德。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年07月02日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）金曲奖甫于上周落幕，25岁的新科国语歌王<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/leo%E7%8E%8B.md#1">Leo王</a>受访时脱口而出说：“<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E4%BE%9D%E6%9E%97.md#1">蔡依林</a>（Jolin）是上个世代的超级大明星。”<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E6%96%87%E5%BD%AC.md#1">施文彬</a>今天（2日）出席活动时，搞笑说：“那我不就是上上个世代的巨星了？”<br />
<figure id="attachment_11360032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11360032" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502422122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502422122-ss1.jpg" alt="施文彬" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11360032" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11360032" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E6%96%87%E5%BD%AC.md#1">施文彬</a>出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>新科台语歌王<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B0%93%E9%98%BF%E5%BE%B7.md#1">流氓阿德</a>则表示，世代交替，音乐不断推陈出新，嘻哈风格的<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/leo%E7%8E%8B.md#1">Leo王</a>打破大家对主流音乐歌王的印象，“身为台湾人，我们活在这片土地，我们可以写我们想写的歌，讲我们想讲的话，唱我们想唱的歌，自由才是我们最可贵的。”</p>
<p>双金曲台语歌王施文彬与<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B0%93%E9%98%BF%E5%BE%B7.md#1">流氓阿德</a>今日一同出席记者会，为节目《青春！咱的梦》站台。谈及音乐梦，流氓阿德表示拿了金曲奖后，隔天起床看到金门日报头版报导“金门歌手获金曲奖”，仿佛“衣锦还乡”的感觉，期许自己继续做出喜欢的音乐，直呼：“我人生的下半场现在才开始。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11360034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11360034" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502392122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502392122-ss1.jpg" alt="流氓阿德" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-11360034" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11360034" class="wp-caption-text">流氓阿德出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当被问到“获得金曲奖后是否会调涨价码？”时，50岁的流氓阿德回应：“这个交给公司来处理！还是要持续努力工作，平常心面对！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11360038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11360038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502252122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/1907020502252122-ss1-600x575.jpg" alt="施文彬、陈宏宜、流氓阿德" width="600" b="575" class="size-large wp-image-11360038" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11360038" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）施文彬、陈宏宜、流氓阿德出席节目记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
