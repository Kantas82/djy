<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾蔬果节8月开锣  献上水果王国之佳品</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月28日讯】台湾素有“水果王国”之称，盛产热带与亚热带水果。今年的8月9至8月19日的11天期间，<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>人与亚省华人有福了，不用特别寻找，参加“2019年第二场<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C%E8%8A%82.md#1">台湾蔬果节</a>”（The 2nd Taiwan Fruits &amp; Vegetables Festival 2019），就可在15家<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%BB%9F%E5%8D%8E%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">大统华超市</a>(温哥华与亚省两地)，<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%8D%E8%B4%B9%E5%93%81%E5%B0%9D.md#1">免费品尝</a>及购买优质的台湾蔬果!</p>
<figure id="attachment_11420537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11420537" style="width: 471px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/TaiwanFruitVegeFest-Poster-22x28_FINAL-e1564554775601.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11420537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/TaiwanFruitVegeFest-Poster-22x28_FINAL-e1564554775601.jpg" alt="台湾蔬果节海报（大统华超市提供）" width="471" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11420537" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C%E8%8A%82.md#1">台湾蔬果节</a>海报<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%BB%9F%E5%8D%8E%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">大统华超市</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾地处热带及亚热带地区，四面环海，冬季温暖，夏季炎热，光照充足，盛产各种鲜甜可口的蔬果，成就了台湾这个得天独厚的“水果王国”！</p>
<p>有些<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">台湾水果</a>是人们知道或品尝过的，有些连名字都从来没有听闻过。今年“台湾蔬果节”带给<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>与亚省人的热带与亚热带水果有：<span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">红肉火龙果、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">芭乐、</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">红心芭乐、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">杨桃、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">台湾小玉西瓜</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">(</span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">黄肉</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">)、</span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">台湾玉文芒果、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">水莲、</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 标楷体, cursive;"><span style="font-size: medium;">台湾绿竹笋等</span></span></span></span>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11415271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11415271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/dargonfruit-02-e1564346604357.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11415271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/dargonfruit-02-e1564346604357.jpg" alt="图：台湾台湾素有“水果王国”之称，盛产热带与亚热带水果。图为台湾的火龙果。（台湾农业委员会网站）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11415271" class="wp-caption-text">图：台湾台湾素有“水果王国”之称，盛产热带与亚热带水果。图为台湾的火龙果。（台湾农业委员会网站）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾农业委员会特委托外贸协会(TAITRA)为专案管理单位，积极协助台湾生鲜蔬果拓展国际市场，建立优质品质形象。今年8月9至19日期间，由大统华超市(T &amp; T Supermarket)执行，在加拿大西部开展“2019年第二场台湾蔬果节行销计划”，希望能让台湾优质农产品在加拿大扎根，让更多海外人士认识台湾蔬果的美好!</p>
<figure id="attachment_11420536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11420536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/TaiwanFruitVegeFest-MTStoreBanner_134x36-25Percent_FINAL-e1564554721979.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11420536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/TaiwanFruitVegeFest-MTStoreBanner_134x36-25Percent_FINAL-e1564554721979.jpg" alt="台湾蔬果节海报（大统华超市提供）" width="600" b="161" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11420536" class="wp-caption-text">台湾蔬果节海报（大统华超市提供）</figcaption></figure>
