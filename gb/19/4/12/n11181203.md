<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽英崙侨界热情欢迎韩国瑜</h2>
<a href="https://dqgl8k1bfvgsc.cloudfront.net/D1cmYiK9q"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>中华民国高雄市市长韩国瑜（右三）与夫人李佳芬（右二）接受美国波士顿侨界热诚欢迎，与侨领同桌。左起：中华公所主席陈家骅、波士顿国民党常委张韵兰、侨务咨询委员陈毓璇。（刘景烨／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年04月12日讯】（大纪元<span class="s1">记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">10</span><span class="s2">日，中华民国高雄市市长<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%91%9C.md#1">韩国瑜</a>夫妇抵达访美首站<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>。次日，他应邀前往剑桥市<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E4%BD%9B%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">哈佛大学</a>，与费正清中心学者及嘉宾闭门座谈。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">众人拱“选总统”</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">当晚，他在波士顿华埠帝苑酒楼与<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E8%83%9E.md#1">侨胞</a>见面。面对侨胞们热烈的“选总统”呼声，他没有正面回应，但表示，中华民国总统务必做到“台湾安全，人民有钱”。他说，不仅台湾人关心台湾，像<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E4%BD%9B%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">哈佛大学</a>学者这样的外国人也在关心台湾，因而呼吁<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E8%83%9E.md#1">侨胞</a></span><span class="s2">2020</span><span class="s1">年积极回台湾投票。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-3Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-3Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181305" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%91%9C.md#1">韩国瑜</a>市长表示，中华民国总统务必做到“台湾安全，人民有钱”。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">“我是个很重感情的人。”韩国瑜感慨。他说来到波士顿，便感受到侨胞们对家国故乡的感情。他希望侨胞们保重身体，获侨胞回应：“你也一样”。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-9Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-9Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181322" class="wp-caption-text">700多人挤爆了欢迎晚宴的帝苑餐厅。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">韩国瑜携夫人李佳芬在下午</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">点半左右到达帝苑酒楼，在</span><span class="s2">72</span><span class="s1">桌、</span><span class="s2">700</span><span class="s1">多位侨胞的欢呼声中进场。与韩市长夫妇同桌的嘉宾包括<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8B%B1%E5%B4%99.md#1">纽英崙</a>资深侨胞及领袖陈毓璇、中华公所主席陈家骅、波士顿国民党常委张韵兰、经文处处长徐佑典等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-7Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-7Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181317" class="wp-caption-text">主持人江汉邀请韩夫人李佳芬（中）也上台跟大家说几句话。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">晚会期间，群情激动，侨胞们纷纷围拢拍照，欢呼着挥舞中华民国国旗，在主持人江汉呼吁下才逐渐就坐。唱功闻名侨界的</span><span class="s1">“You &amp; Me”乐团歌手</span><span class="s2">Novia</span><span class="s1">领唱美国国歌，韩国瑜领唱中华民国国歌。而后，来宾们高呼“中华民国万岁”。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-5Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-5Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181311" class="wp-caption-text">晚宴主持人江汉。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11181313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-6Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-6Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181313" class="wp-caption-text">“You &amp; Me”乐团歌手Novia领唱美国国歌。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">韩国瑜向晚宴主办人、全美台湾同乡联谊会前会长马涤凡，以及徐佑典赠送礼品，感谢他们辛苦筹备侨界活动。好不容易获得</span><span class="s2">20</span><span class="s1">分钟的进餐时间后，韩国瑜又马不停蹄与每一桌来宾合影留念。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-4Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-4Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181307" class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长徐佑典（左）获得市长韩国瑜赠送的高雄市纪念茶杯。中间红衣女士为晚宴主办方的马涤凡。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在等待韩国瑜到来之前，老牌名歌星杨燕唱了许多首歌曲以娱嘉宾。韩国瑜在跟各桌轮流照相时因劳累提前离席后，杨燕又再度应邀上台演唱许多脍炙人口的好听歌曲，获得来宾们热烈的掌声。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-8Han.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11181319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-8Han-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181319" class="wp-caption-text">韩国瑜堪称台湾人气最旺的市长，所到之处都是被人群包围。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">哈佛座谈“接地气的力量”</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">为准备好哈佛座谈，韩国瑜提前一天到达波士顿休息整备。这场闭门座谈会之前，韩国瑜对媒体透露，他最重要的工作，是要介绍他对</span><span class="s2">2018</span><span class="s1">年选举的观察和亲身参与的经历。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">有港媒提问他对“一国两制”的看法。韩国瑜回应说，他已多次强调，台湾的主流民意完全无法接受一国两制。“我不是说了好多次吗？麻烦你看着我的眼睛，看着我的灵魂深处。”他说。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">韩国瑜在</span><span class="s2">12</span><span class="s1">点半左右到达哈佛大学，中午先与费正清中心学者们餐叙，与会者包括费正清中心主任宋怡明、台湾研究小组召集人戈迪温、波士顿大学国际关系暨政治系教授傅士卓、波士顿学院政治系教授陆伯彬、史密斯学院政府学系助理教授史诺兰等等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11181296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11181296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-2Han.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11181296 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-2-2Han-e1555048993870-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11181296" class="wp-caption-text">哈佛大学费正清中心主任宋怡明（右一）亲自迎接韩国瑜市长（左一）。（黄剑宇／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">随后，韩国瑜参观哈佛大学校园，并在教职员俱乐部与数十位学者、嘉宾座谈。他的演讲题目为“接地气的力量”（The Power of Down to Earth）。演讲后，他还接受师生提问。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">高雄市副市长叶匡时、行政暨国际处长王智立、新闻局长王浅秋、中正大学政治所特聘教授廖达琪、立委陈宜民、高雄市议员黄绍庭等人也随行，一同参加哈佛午宴及闭门座谈。</span></p>
<p class="p1">在韩国瑜离开波士顿前，还发生了一段小插曲。原订与12日早上与侨胞进行的早餐会，因巴士司机“大迷航”，15分钟的路程开了一个半小时仍未到。为赶赴飞往洛杉矶的班机，韩国瑜只好放弃与侨胞的餐会，直奔机场，并通过手机视讯同与会侨胞致歉。现场侨胞祝韩国瑜一路顺风，并齐喊口号：“台湾安全、人民有钱”。</p>
