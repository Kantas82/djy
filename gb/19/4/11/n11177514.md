<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台星王家梁外形获青睐 为东京知名百货代言</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204211487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾男星王家梁荣任东京百货观光大使。（日昇正亮娱乐 x Fun Japan Taiwan提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年04月11日讯】台湾戏剧男星<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%AE%B6%E6%A2%81.md#1">王家梁</a>（Edison）最近新加盟“<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%98%87%E6%AD%A3%E4%BA%AE.md#1">日昇正亮</a>”娱乐，随即接下新代言工作及戏剧邀约，王家梁对此开心表示：“希望接下来的角色，能挑战自己，拍武打动作戏！”而他高大英挺与帅气外形也被东京知名百货相中，挑大梁担任观光大使。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11177536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11177536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204241487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11177536" title="Edison" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204241487-600x401.jpg" alt="Edison" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11177536" class="wp-caption-text">台湾男星<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%AE%B6%E6%A2%81.md#1">王家梁</a>荣任东京百货观光大使。<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%98%87%E6%AD%A3%E4%BA%AE.md#1">日昇正亮</a>娱乐 x Fun Japan Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上周赴东京工作，王家梁受到主办方高规格接待，备受礼遇，适逢樱花满开，也体验到人生首次赏樱趣。王家梁在结束拍摄工作后，在主办方的安排下前往新宿御苑，当天天气极好，他生平首见“樱吹雪”，看到淡粉樱花随风飘扬，不禁大呼：“真是美极了！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11177539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11177539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204371487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11177539" title="Edison" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204371487-600x401.jpg" alt="Edison" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11177539" class="wp-caption-text">王家梁上周赴东京工作，受到主办方高规格接待。（日昇正亮娱乐 x Fun Japan Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首次体验穿和服的王家梁，意外发现感觉衣服满紧的，自己步伐大又快，提醒自己要小心慢慢走，担心稍一不慎衣服会迸开。说到此，体格壮硕的他，怕此行工作紧凑无法健身，特别在飞行前一天把握时间去健身房做了1.5小时的运动，尽力维持好身形，相当敬业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11177537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11177537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11177537" title="Edison" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204281487-600x401.jpg" alt="Edison" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11177537" class="wp-caption-text">台湾男星王家梁外形健康阳光，获日本东京等百货邀请担任观光大使。（日昇正亮娱乐 x Fun Japan Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王家梁首度担任百货观光大使，这也是他们首次邀请海外艺人，双方都是第一次，合作起来特别新鲜，对方独爱王家梁挺拔的外形与允文允武的气质，在百货公司取景拍摄时，更频被说帅。现场吸引女社员、柜哥柜姐们围观。而此行工作的安排相当顺利、愉快，让他直呼学习到很多，也留下美好的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11177540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11177540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204341487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11177540" title="Edison" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904102204341487-600x401.jpg" alt="Edison" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11177540" class="wp-caption-text">王家梁（Edison）最近新加盟“日昇正亮”娱乐，随即接下新代言工作及戏剧邀约。（日昇正亮娱乐 x Fun Japan Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
