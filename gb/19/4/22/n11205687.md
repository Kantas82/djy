<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宋康昊演《寄生上流》 与导演奉俊昊四度合作</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032241100649-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会4月22日上午在THE WESTLN CHOSUN举行，主演群及导演出席活动。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年04月23日讯】韩国电影《<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%84%E7%94%9F%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%81.md#1">寄生上流</a>》（Parasite）昨日（22日）于首尔市中区THE WESTLN CHOSUN举行制作发表会，主演<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E5%BA%B7%E6%98%8A.md#1">宋康昊</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%96%84%E5%9D%87.md#1">李善均</a>、赵如晶、<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E5%AE%87%E6%A4%8D.md#1">崔宇植</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E6%B7%A1.md#1">朴素淡</a>、张惠珍及导演奉俊昊出席活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11207060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032230100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207060" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032230100649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207060" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%84%E7%94%9F%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%81.md#1">寄生上流</a>》发表会，主演群及导演出席活动。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207089" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032257100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207089" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032257100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207089" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E6%B7%A1.md#1">朴素淡</a>4月22日出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207080" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032331100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207080" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032331100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207080" class="wp-caption-text">赵如晶4月22日出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>即将于第72届坎城影展世界首映、并代表南韩角逐金棕榈奖的讽刺悬疑电影《寄生上流》（Parasite），是导演奉俊昊时隔10年回归南韩影坛的新作品。本次他再次与影帝<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E5%BA%B7%E6%98%8A.md#1">宋康昊</a>合作，这也已是两人第四度合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11207053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207053" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422040754100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207053" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422040754100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207053" class="wp-caption-text">宋康昊出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207096" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032245100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207096" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032245100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207096" class="wp-caption-text">朴素淡出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演笑说：“这将会是我所有作品当中最非比寻常的一部电影，但我保证演员的身上没有寄生虫，一切都很卫生。”宋康昊则说：“导演的作品总是富有想像力和洞察力。”宋康昊还表示，他认为这不仅是导演个人的进化，也是韩国电影的再进化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11207065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207065" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032236100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207065" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032236100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207065" class="wp-caption-text">宋康昊出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207098" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032335100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207098" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032335100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207098" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%94%E5%AE%87%E6%A4%8D.md#1">崔宇植</a>出席韩国电影《寄生上流》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于新作即将在坎城影展首次亮相，导演奉俊昊说：“能入选坎城影展，我觉得很荣幸也很紧张。电影中的贫富差距是所有观众都能有所共鸣的，不过这也是一部非常韩国的电影，外国人可能没办法百分之百完全理解。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11207055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032301100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207055" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032301100649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207055" class="wp-caption-text">导演奉俊昊4月22日出席韩国电影《寄生虫》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207092" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032315100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207092" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032315100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207092" class="wp-caption-text">张惠珍4月22日出席韩国电影《寄生虫》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11207095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207095" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032310100649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207095" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190422032310100649.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207095" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%96%84%E5%9D%87.md#1">李善均</a>4月22日出席韩国电影《寄生虫》发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《寄生上流》描述一家四口全是无业游民，爸爸基泽（宋康昊 饰）成天游手好闲，直到长子基宇（崔宇植 饰）动起歪脑筋，靠着伪造的文凭来到富豪朴社长（李善均 饰）的家应征家教，两个天差地远的家庭因而被卷入一连串意外事件中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11207167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11207167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190423034205100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11207167" title="Parasite" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/190423034205100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Parasite" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11207167" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《寄生上流（Parasite）》剧照。（CATCHPLAY提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩国电影《寄生上流》将于5月底于韩国上映。</p>
