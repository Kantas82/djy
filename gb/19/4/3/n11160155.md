<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鱼池加码春游奖励 游村落送红茶抽住宿券</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/b057351e991e4ade5422997217bf5c6e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东池村长介绍该村的意象及特色景点。（黄淑贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月03日讯】（大纪元记者林萌骞台湾南投报导）继中央、县府之后，<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E6%B1%A0.md#1">鱼池</a>乡3日加码推春游奖励，只要到全乡13<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%91%E8%90%BD.md#1">村落</a>旅游，任选意象景点，打卡拍照上传社群网站、按分享，集4村落送<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>茶包礼盒，集6村落赠红茶茶叶礼盒，集13村落则可参加<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%BF%E5%88%B8.md#1">住宿券</a>、餐券、门票抽奖。</p>
<p>目前正值春假旅游旺季，<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E6%B1%A0.md#1">鱼池</a>乡内观光资源丰富，有知名的日月潭及九族文化村两大观光区，还有丰富的地景风貌及人文背景，为得天独厚的观光资源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11160158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11160158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/686d031f1c25950bf5dcc46deb0d3ba7-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11160158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/686d031f1c25950bf5dcc46deb0d3ba7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11160158" class="wp-caption-text">鱼池乡观光旅游业者说明推出的加码优惠方案，以及所提供的抽奖奖品。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乡公所与代表会为提升观光发展，响应观光局春季旅游补助方案，除中央及县府提供的“基本补助”外，更结合全乡13个村的意象和景点，推出游鱼池、打卡拍照、上传社群网站，再按分享，就送<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>、抽<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%BF%E5%88%B8.md#1">住宿券</a>等奖励方案。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11160159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11160159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/c110ed8892f5edc9c41ad8a4f7c21780-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11160159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/c110ed8892f5edc9c41ad8a4f7c21780-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11160159" class="wp-caption-text">鱼池乡观光旅游业者说明推出的加码优惠方案，以及所提供的抽奖奖品。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上午的活动现场，乡公所邀请13个村的村长，一一为民众介绍该村的意象及特色景点；乡内的观光旅游业者及鱼池乡农会，也都依序说明各家业者推出的加码优惠方案，以及所提供的抽奖奖品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11160160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11160160" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/7c9cab7a0c6e2aa4eafca01bffcd7c25-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11160160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/7c9cab7a0c6e2aa4eafca01bffcd7c25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11160160" class="wp-caption-text">参加鱼池春游奖励方案记者会的来宾合影。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鱼池乡长刘启帆表示，游客打卡拍照的地点不限，可任选13个<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%91%E8%90%BD.md#1">村落</a>的意象、景点、特色饭店、民宿、餐厅、商店、农场、学校等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11160161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11160161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d2095060e052101c1f5ce0d9d0552193-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11160161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d2095060e052101c1f5ce0d9d0552193-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11160161" class="wp-caption-text">鱼池乡唯一的客家村村长介绍该村的意象及特色景点。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他说，活动期间为4月3日至6月30日，集4个村落地点，送日月潭红茶茶包礼盒；集6个村落地点，赠日月潭红茶茶叶礼盒；集13个村落地点，则符合满点礼抽奖，奖品包括住宿卷、餐券、门票等。</p>
<p>至于赠品领取地点，乡公所表示，周一至周五的上班时间，上午8时至下午5时，游客可持手机显示上传后的讯息到公所服务台领取，例假日则请于公所图书馆柜台领取。</p>
