<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒙娜丽莎在铁片里向我微笑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/a9eae336e1aa95092b3c844eb83da301-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这张大幅的蒙娜丽莎，是铁片经过冲床技术，冲出无数个孔洞，设计出来的图像。（王金丁提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>走进厂房，第一眼看到的是墙壁上的蒙娜丽莎在铁片里向我微笑。</p>
<p>偶一抬头，又看到一幅“蒙娜丽莎的微笑”工艺品，用的是一个个不同颜色的金属垫片组成的图像，这位蒙娜丽莎的笑容就更清晰了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/806ad9061ab9e7b65af1984a14a439f2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184101 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/806ad9061ab9e7b65af1984a14a439f2-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184101" class="wp-caption-text">导览员解说蒙娜丽莎的金属创意。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导览小姐说，那是铁片经过板金的冲床技术，冲出无数个孔洞，设计出来的图像，创意的巧思让人赞叹。</p>
<p>那边，许多学生围成一圈，唧唧喳喳地组装着各种金属艺术品，走近一看，有笔筒、手机架还有音乐小屋，几双手指雀跃地忙着寻找不同造型的金属。一位女学生听到音乐小屋发出的音乐，惊讶地说：“铁片真的可以转弯啊！”第一次了解金属也有这么柔软的性格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/f9a4e19102b915576c74d0777aa2b42d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184104 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/f9a4e19102b915576c74d0777aa2b42d-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184104" class="wp-caption-text">学生们自制工艺品体验金属的柔软。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184110" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/b35bdd5dbade7b79588af16275cc0833.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184110 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/b35bdd5dbade7b79588af16275cc0833-300x201.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184110" class="wp-caption-text">猫头鹰全身都是金属打造，是小朋友最爱。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184114" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/495e19a177738b68a19643b959dc5972.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184114 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/495e19a177738b68a19643b959dc5972-300x201.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184114" class="wp-caption-text">金属创意的坦克车。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184109" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/49c11c876f6348eae23335dc4ed9df09.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-11184109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/49c11c876f6348eae23335dc4ed9df09-300x201.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184109" class="wp-caption-text">金属笔筒轻松就能折叠铁片，感受金属的柔软。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184112" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/70eb4ad5f8778db0bbdc42ee7e9c16c7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-11184112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/70eb4ad5f8778db0bbdc42ee7e9c16c7-300x201.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184112" class="wp-caption-text">琳琅满目的金属工艺品。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>南台湾，一家金属公司厂房的展示馆里，举目所见，墙角、天花板，琳琅满目的金属制作的艺术品。门口，还开着两朵灿烂的玫瑰花，婀娜的枝叶托着铜色花朵，姿态唯妙唯肖，让人感觉满室生香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/b5fd987792b7a737cb546a01b807835c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184106 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/b5fd987792b7a737cb546a01b807835c-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184106" class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰花婀娜的枝叶托着铜色花朵，唯妙唯肖，都是金属制作的。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/03580fecf0c46b78ee51ee702bedb0e2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184107 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/03580fecf0c46b78ee51ee702bedb0e2-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184107" class="wp-caption-text">“黑面琵鹭”用了九种不同的刀具，冲出约三万个圆孔，才能让黑面琵鹭展翅飘落沙洲。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>仔细看，你会发现这里处处惊喜。那幅“黑面琵鹭”图，用了九种不同的刀具，由冲出约三万个圆孔组成，才能让黑面琵鹭潇洒地展翅飘落沙洲上。我站在黑面琵鹭前睁大着眼睛，心里赞叹着板金技术的细腻与创意。</p>
<p>这一个盒子里最不起眼的大铁钉，你的手随意贴上去，将盒子翻来覆去，一堆铁钉就随着手型变化。另外还有一个“人体电池”的游戏，当你潮湿略带盐分的手分别与铝、铜板接触时，这两种金属会溶解手上的水分，借着金属间的电位差，铜板带的电荷通过人体往铝板移动而形成了电流，从电压计可以看到不同金属的电位差，设计的用意，让人进一步了解金属的功能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/92c884d1686567a90dca8deb31d9cf45.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184105 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/92c884d1686567a90dca8deb31d9cf45-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184105" class="wp-caption-text">“人体电池”的游戏，借着金属的电位差，使铜板的电荷通过人体往铝板移动而形成了电流。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>总经理郭治华说：“金属比较不容易了解，我们利用各种金属不同的特性，以板金的技术，艺术化的设计成生活中的东西，让民众轻松地了解金属也有不同的性质。”置身这个金属世界里，才感到原来金属可以变得这么柔软，这么可亲。</p>
<p>这些创意品到底是怎么做出来的？郭治华说，板金的制程有雷射、切割、凹床、焊接，都是关键技术，技术员各司其职，一件工艺品设计出来了，须要串联各个制程，共同去完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d0e48d01451179875649bc2423721a87.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184103 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d0e48d01451179875649bc2423721a87-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184103" class="wp-caption-text">趋吉辟邪的剑狮亮丽抢眼，是创意板金的发挥。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11184102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184102" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/73b8623082c58f403941c9a4e7c8aeb7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11184102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/73b8623082c58f403941c9a4e7c8aeb7-450x672.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="672" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184102" class="wp-caption-text">剑狮金钢创意巧思外，运用了板金所有的制程。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>像那座跟人一般大小的“剑狮金钢”，胸前就是含着双剑的剑狮，融入了台南安平在地的“剑狮”文化。改造了八卦图、龟、凤的形状，展现在剑狮金钢身上各个角落，从设计、雷射、凹床、焊接、图装一直到阻力，每个制程都用上了，用另一种方式，让人认识板金细致的技术。</p>
<p>郭治华说：“这些巧妙的创意如何产生的？整体团队都在动脑，不同的想法经过整合后又会产生新的创意，工厂的战斗力提升起来了，这是我们没有预期的效果。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11184116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11184116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/78e2791cb46a2ee78690f29cf71a836e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11184116 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/78e2791cb46a2ee78690f29cf71a836e-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11184116" class="wp-caption-text">工场里展示的板金制程。（王金丁提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>板金界前辈流传，歹铁仔也能变黄金。郭治华说：“我们把废铁变成了艺术品。”</p>
<p>几位技术员脸孔躲在防护罩后面专注地工作着，一位年长的技术员从四射的火花里走来，嘴上的胡须还沾着红色火点，拍拍我的肩膀：“老弟，只要用柔软的心对待它，坚硬的金属也会变柔软。”</p>
<p>厂房里，几位同学还在高兴地制造钱币，走出展示馆时，壁上的蒙娜丽莎仍然在金属洞洞里向我微笑，我一路思索着那洞里的奥妙。◇</p>
