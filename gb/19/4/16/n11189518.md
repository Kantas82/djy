<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第123届波士顿马拉松 非洲选手双夺冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-1Marathon-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第123届波士顿马拉松大赛男女精英组冠军合影。左为来自肯亚的男子组冠军Lawrence Cherono，右为埃塞俄比亚的女子组冠军Worknesh Degefa。（徐明／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月16日讯】 （大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）<span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">15</span><span class="s2">日上午，第</span><span class="s1">123</span><span class="s2">届<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>在华氏</span><span class="s1">58</span><span class="s2">度的阴天下开跑。今年，来自非洲埃塞俄比亚和肯亚的选手分别摘得女子和男子精英组<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B.md#1">冠军</a>。两国选手更包揽了男子精英组的前</span><span class="s1">6</span><span class="s2">名，以及女子精英组前</span><span class="s1">10</span><span class="s2">名中的</span><span class="s1">7</span><span class="s2">位。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11189536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-6Marathon.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11189536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-6Marathon-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在终点线附近，选手“大部队”在群众的欢呼声中抵达。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">上午</span><span class="s1">9</span><span class="s2">点，手摇轮椅组选手起跑的时候，天上还下着小雨。到了</span><span class="s1">9</span><span class="s2">点半，女子精英组起跑时，天色开始放晴。在起跑线</span><span class="s1">26.2</span><span class="s2">英哩外的波士顿科普利广场（</span><span class="s1">Copley</span><span class="s2">）终点线附近，观众开始聚集。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">埃塞俄比亚女选手</span><span class="s1">Worknesh Degefa</span><span class="s2">从大约半程的时候就开始领跑，路上一度领先第二名</span><span class="s1">0.5</span><span class="s2">英哩。到达距起点</span><span class="s1">23</span><span class="s2">英里处时，波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">马拉松</a>官方推特播报：“（</span><span class="s1">Degefa</span><span class="s2">）远远领先其他跑者，没有人在（她身后）视线之内。”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">上午</span><span class="s1">11:55</span><span class="s2">，</span><span class="s1">Degefa</span><span class="s2">挥舞着双臂冲线，以</span><span class="s1">2</span><span class="s2">小时</span><span class="s1">23</span><span class="s2">分</span><span class="s1">31</span><span class="s2">秒的成绩摘得女子精英组<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B.md#1">冠军</a>，比第二名肯亚的</span><span class="s1">Edna Kiplagat</span><span class="s2">领先</span><span class="s1">42</span><span class="s2">秒。上届的女子冠军</span><span class="s1">Desiree Linden</span><span class="s2">则退居到第五名。麻州州长贝克（</span><span class="s1">Charlie Baker</span><span class="s2">）为</span><span class="s1">Degefa</span><span class="s2">颁发奖杯。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">男子精英组的竞争非常激烈。当前三名选手来到终点线前最后的直道时，他们相互间距不足</span><span class="s1">5</span><span class="s2">米。原本稍微落后的肯亚选手</span><span class="s1">Lawrence Cherono</span><span class="s2">在终点线前</span><span class="s1">100</span><span class="s2">米左右突然加速，在群众激动的欢呼声中反超对手，以</span><span class="s1">2</span><span class="s2">小时</span><span class="s1">7</span><span class="s2">分</span><span class="s1">57</span><span class="s2">秒的成绩夺冠。</span><span class="s1">2</span><span class="s2">秒之后，曾一度领跑的埃塞俄比亚选手</span><span class="s1">Lelisa Desis</span><span class="s2">也冲过了终点线。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">波士顿市长华殊（</span><span class="s1">Martin Walsh</span><span class="s2">）为</span><span class="s1">Cherono</span><span class="s2">颁奖。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11189533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-4Marathon.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11189533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-4Marathon-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">男子精英组前三名竞争很激烈，在终点线前不远，肯亚选手Lawrence Cherono（中）神情专注，准备超越对手；领跑的埃塞俄比亚选手Lelisa Desis意识到这一点，神情焦急。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">在终点线附近，</span><span class="s1">Cherono</span><span class="s2">一边跑来跑去，一边对媒体说：“我当时非常专注，因为我从未赢过一场重大的马拉松比赛。我下定决心要赢。”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">这也是</span><span class="s1">Cherono</span><span class="s2">第三次在美国参加马拉松比赛。“我非常感激，我非常感激波士顿的人们。”他说。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">而与冠军差之毫厘的</span><span class="s1">Desis</span><span class="s2">则显得有点低落地坐了下来。此外，美国男选手</span><span class="s1">Daniel Romanchuk</span><span class="s2">夺得手摇轮椅组比赛冠军，成为</span><span class="s1">1993</span><span class="s2">年来首位在波马男子手摇轮椅组夺冠的美国男选手。在女子手摇轮椅组比赛中，来自瑞士的</span><span class="s1">Manuela Schar</span><span class="s2">摘得她自</span><span class="s1">2017</span><span class="s2">年来的第二个冠军。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11189529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-2Marathon.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11189529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-2Marathon-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美国Daniel Romanchuk（中）夺得轮椅组男子冠军，瑞士的Manuela Schar（右）赢得轮椅组女子冠军。（徐明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">波马赛事自</span><span class="s1">1897</span><span class="s2">年开跑，通常在“<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD%E8%80%85%E6%97%A5.md#1">爱国者日</a>”举办，至今已连续举办</span><span class="s1">123</span><span class="s2">届。波马是世界上最古老的年度马拉松赛事，与伦敦、柏林、芝加哥、纽约、东京马拉松并列为“六大马”。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">波士顿体育协会（</span><span class="s1">BAA</span><span class="s2">）的选手查询系统显示，今年台湾共有</span><span class="s1">153</span><span class="s2">名选手注册参赛，中国大陆有</span><span class="s1">507</span><span class="s2">人，香港有</span><span class="s1">177</span><span class="s2">人，澳门也有</span><span class="s1">6</span><span class="s2">名选手注册。今年波马共有</span><span class="s1">30,349</span><span class="s2">名选手注册参赛。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11189531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-3Marathon.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11189531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-3Marathon-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">获得50-54岁女子组第2名的中国籍刘春华。（徐明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11189539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-5Marathon.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11189539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1-1-5Marathon-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">来自台湾的选手披着青天白日满地红的国旗到达终点。（台北经文处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">今年的波马也是</span><span class="s1">2013</span><span class="s2">年爆炸案以来，首次与爆炸纪念日“同一波士顿日（</span><span class="s1">One Boston Day</span><span class="s2">）”同天举办的波马比赛。在终点线旁边，黑色的木制纪念碑上写着：“我们完成了赛跑，我们夺回了终点线，我们铭记</span><span class="s1">2013</span><span class="s2">年</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">15</span><span class="s2">日。”◇#</span></p>
