<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猫狗头戴教授制作的花冠 华贵模样令人惊艳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/THUMB1-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>网红猫咪们戴上花冠，格外惹爱。(<a target="_blank" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）雅尔莉（Yarely Alejandre）在大学教授英语文学，她动手制作第一个<ahref="https://github.com/axjmzj3414/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%86%A0.md#1">花冠</a>时，只想着给自家澳洲牧羊犬弗蕾雅（Freya）拍些有趣相片，没想到IG网友反响太热烈，小道具最终发展成了一个品牌。雅尔莉由此创办了花冠公司“<span style="color: #00ccff;"><a style="color: #00ccff;" href="https://freyasfloralcompany.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya’s Floral Company</a></span>”，业余为人和宠物制作各色<ahref="https://github.com/axjmzj3414/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%86%A0.md#1">花冠</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161502" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161502 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-0-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161502" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161494" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161494 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-1-450x401.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161494" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/2husketeers/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2husketeers/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“其实那时我对制作花冠一点都不懂，我只是把花绞在一起。”家在美国加州圣地亚哥的雅尔莉告诉网媒“Bored Panda”，“我得到了如此积极的反馈，为了满足更多人的喜爱，我决定开始为其它狗狗制作花冠。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161504" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161504 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161504" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/maple.cat/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">maple.cat/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161505" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11161505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161505" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>雅尔莉使用人造花来制作精美的作品，有环形、绑带式的，也有宠物项圈饰花；顾客还可以自己提设计方案。她希望出自她手的每只花冠都独一无二，即使用同样的花朵，其排列组合也不一样。当然，如果顾客喜欢，她至多可以为他们制作三个一模一样的花冠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161503" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161503 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-2-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161503" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161506" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161506 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-6-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161506" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>自从雅尔莉开始帮人制作花冠，很多饲主都找上门来，想让自家宠物也拥有这样的奇妙头饰。雅尔莉最棒的宠物模特之一，是芝加哥的IG网红猫莱昂尼达斯（Leonidas，昵称莱昂）。莱昂是一只缅因猫（Maine Coon，又称因库恩猫），其<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">IG粉专</a></span>拥有151万粉丝。</p>
<p>莱昂出镜的一系列戴冠照片，是如此的梦幻和富有时尚感，每每吸引成千上万的网友点赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161507 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-7-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161507" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161508" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11161508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-8-450x507.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="507" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161508" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>莱昂由此也成为雅尔莉的灵感之源。她说，“莱昂的‘妈咪经理人’（momager）给了我巨大的支持和许多建议。我很自豪地宣布，莱昂是最多花冠的拥有者。”在制作花冠时，她也经常会设想莱昂戴上后是什么样子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161509" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161509 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-10-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161509" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161510" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161510 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-11-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161510" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/aussie_freya/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">aussie_freya/Instagam</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>莱昂的“妈咪经理人”珍妮（Jane）则表示，“我经常看到狗戴花冠的照片，我觉得猫戴上也会很棒！幸运的是，莱昂是一只非常放松随和的小猫，容许我把冠戴在它头上。”</p>
<p>“网友非常喜欢戴花冠的莱昂”，珍妮肯定地说，“这些照片在所有照片中总是反响最热烈。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161511" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-12.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11161511 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-12-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161511" class="wp-caption-text">(<span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/leo.mainecoon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">leo.mainecoon/Instagram</a></span>, Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11161512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161512" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11161512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-13-450x562.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161512" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>雅尔莉的正职是大学副教授，说到业余制作花冠的动力何在，她透露，“我是墨西哥裔，讲西班牙语。我去墨西哥旅行的时候，从当地的缤纷色彩和强大活力中汲取了灵感。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11161513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11161513" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11161513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Freyas-Floral-14-450x516.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="516" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11161513" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Freya&#8217;s Floral Company</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然女顾客通常都很享受戴花冠，但动物的配合度就不那么高了。“弗莱雅花冠”的<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.instagram.com/freyasfloralco/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">IG粉专</a></span>上有弗莱雅的不少乖巧留影，不过雅尔莉坦承，这只雌性牧羊犬时常也会“耍小性”。</p>
