<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>舒缓妈妈紧张情绪～ 盘点8款入门精油</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/woman-3251826_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>研究指出，香氛气味能够直接影响人的情绪。藉由好闻的气味，能够提升心灵的平静。（weddingclub／pixabay）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者赖玟茹台湾台北报导）研究指出，香氛气味能够直接影响人的情绪。像是因<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">压力</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A7%E5%BC%A0.md#1">紧张</a>焦虑所引起的<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">压力</a>问题，藉由好闻的气味，能够提升心灵的平静。</p>
<p>现在不管是<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E8%81%8C%E5%A6%88%E5%A6%88.md#1">全职妈妈</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%8C%E4%B8%9A%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3.md#1">职业妇女</a>的生活日常，共通点就是一天24小时永远不够用，不管是兼顾工作与家庭，或是在家专职顾小孩，都处在一个分秒必争的压力环境中。居家生活美学作家与设计师──Judy老师，今天将与大家分享8款<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E6%B2%B9.md#1">精油</a>，献给想纾压的妈妈们。</p>

<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">◎ 精油入门? 推荐给香氛新手妈咪</span></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">茶树精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>澳洲</p>
<p>茶树精油拥有杀菌、抗菌力强等特性。能够帮助妈妈在日常生活中，预防幼儿感冒，增强自体保护免疫力。另外在居家清洁上，茶树精油是个好帮手，将2滴茶树精油加入清水中，充分混合后，再喷洒在有异味的地方，兼具净化空气、杀菌的作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/aefef303bb83343accf22a35d9f9f9de.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215857 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/aefef303bb83343accf22a35d9f9f9de-450x497.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="497" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215857" class="wp-caption-text">茶树精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">薄荷精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>美国</p>
<p>薄荷精油特有的清凉感，在日常生活中使用相当广泛，像牙膏、漱口水及口香糖等。当妈妈感到精神不济、头晕现象的时候，运用薄荷精油将可让您振奋精神，并能缓解身体的不舒服感受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/8d26c0d478db3b1eb9f3b1aa01ccd841.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215861 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/8d26c0d478db3b1eb9f3b1aa01ccd841-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215861" class="wp-caption-text">欧薄荷精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">柠檬精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>南欧地区</p>
<p>清新的柠檬香味，对妈妈来说想必并不陌生，时常在洗衣清洁用品上出现，香味很为人所知，但却常忽略了它的功效。柠檬具有优质的促进血液循环的效用，能够缓解职场女性下肢的静脉曲张问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/fd46a0cac3ee72d1096e3756ee010297.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215863 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/fd46a0cac3ee72d1096e3756ee010297-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215863" class="wp-caption-text">柠檬精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">尤加利精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>澳洲</p>
<p>尤加利具净化持质，除了可帮助身心顺畅，调整肌肤机能之外。当幼儿玩耍碰撞、跌倒及肌肉酸痛等问题时，也是日常很好的跌打损伤精油，藉由精油搭配手部的按摩，都能够缓解瘀青与肌肉酸痛等不适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1c75c73f4eebcad5d4c332cd5b3894a1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215862 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1c75c73f4eebcad5d4c332cd5b3894a1-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215862" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲尤加利精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">◎ 彻底舒压??推荐给压力满点妈咪</span></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">甜橙精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>主要产地：</strong>意大利、法国、美国</p>
<p>甜橙拥有丰富的阳光能量，当妈妈感到疲惫压力的时候，或已经迈入忧郁倾向的时候，闻一闻甜橙精油，能够赋予我们满满正面能量，扫除忧郁、释放压力。Judy老师表示，透过甜橙精油搭配按摩肚子的方式，对能温和改善幼儿便秘、胀气的一款精油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215858" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/3a90906fa8f623cb111f600b32039dc1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215858 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/3a90906fa8f623cb111f600b32039dc1-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215858" class="wp-caption-text">甜橙精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">薰衣草精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>主要产地：</strong>地中海、法国、英国</p>
<p>薰衣草本身就是安眠的香草，气味中有镇静纾压的成分，对处在<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%A7%E5%BC%A0.md#1">紧张</a>压力下的妈咪，能够减轻愤怒和心理疲惫感，轻闻薰衣草精油心情会感到平静与放松，并有助于睡眠。另外薰衣草精油亦能在工作中使用，能提升高效率的工作情绪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/a4662b73ba60b66272bdbbc2b3ea0632.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215864 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/a4662b73ba60b66272bdbbc2b3ea0632-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215864" class="wp-caption-text">薰衣草精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>

<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">玫瑰天竺葵精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>南非、埃及、地中海地区</p>
<p>玫瑰天竺葵精油是天竺葵中的特殊品种，用途配方上都跟天竺葵一样，不过它的气味为略带薄荷的玫瑰香气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d817a7d693e82826b8069da994337220.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215852 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/d817a7d693e82826b8069da994337220-450x552.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="552" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215852" class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰天竺葵精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #33cccc;">花梨木精油</span></h2>
<p><strong>产地：</strong>亚马逊河、巴西和秘鲁、南法地区</p>
<p>花梨木本身拥有漂亮的木材纹路，常被做为高级家具的材料。花梨木精油取自花梨木的木心，厚实的木质香气中，又伴随着细腻的花朵香气与柑橘类精油的清甜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11215855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11215855" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/6e2c30b07d38465b152f292f09c29803.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11215855 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/6e2c30b07d38465b152f292f09c29803-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11215855" class="wp-caption-text">花梨木精油。（香气盒子提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玫瑰天竺葵与花梨木精油拥有一个共通点，就是皆能刺激肾上腺皮质作用，感到疲劳是因肾功能虚弱，而出现的慢性疲劳。这两支精油可以帮助我们重现活力。运用这两款精油按摩腰、背部，通常都能很好的缓解腰、背部疼痛，及慢性疲劳的症状，同时这两款的香味皆属于能疗愈身心的花香气息。◇</p>
