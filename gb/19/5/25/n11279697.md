<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗弟远走哥哥心碎 “弟弟笑脸抱枕”成良伴</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>希望史宾塞可以从洛基的抱枕和家人的爱护中得到失去弟弟的安慰！(Courtesy of <a target="_blank" href="https://www.facebook.com/target="_blank"bethany.fisher.777"  rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年05月25日讯】（大纪元记者林秀璟编译报导）史宾塞（Spencer）和洛基（Rocky）是一对感情深厚的<ahref="https://github.com/lvjvgz3598/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E5%85%84%E5%BC%9F.md#1">狗兄弟</a>，十年来，它们形影不离，从不曾分开。能够在一起生活和游戏，对史宾塞和洛基而言是最快乐的事，直到有一天，史宾塞再也找不到洛基。</p>
<p>“它们俩没有一个晚上不在一起。”家在苏格兰的饲主斐雪（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>）告诉动物网媒“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo），史宾塞和洛基从出生后就一直生活在一起，睡在同一个狗窝、用同一个饭碗，即便出门也是相约蹓跶，如影随形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279968" style="width: 553px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11279968 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-1.jpg" alt="" width="553" b="418" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>但不久前弟弟洛基被检查出患有肿瘤，因为已是末期，兽医无法医治。于是，洛基只能默默地等待死神的降临。斐雪提到，“我们无法想像洛基走了之后，史宾塞将如何渡过只有它自己的日子？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11279969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>就在洛基离世那天，史宾塞整晚就在屋子里走动不断寻找洛基的踪影。当遍寻不着洛基，史宾塞流泪了，变得很憔悴、很难过。</p>
<p>斐雪希望这两只感情很好的小狗能够继续在一起，所以就将洛基的骨灰放在狗窝上面的架子上，但尽管如此，哥哥史宾塞还是很落寞，变得一蹶不振。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279970" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11279970 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>于是，狗主人做了一个印有洛基笑脸照片的<ahref="https://github.com/lvjvgz3598/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%B1%E6%9E%95.md#1">抱枕</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11279971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-4-450x614.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of Beth Fisher)" width="450" b="614" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人说，“自从<ahref="https://github.com/lvjvgz3598/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%B1%E6%9E%95.md#1">抱枕</a>到家，史宾塞就一直紧贴着，还将它从沙发上咬到自己的窝里。现在它有东西可依偎，看起来心安多了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11279972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-5-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>史宾塞再也离不开抱枕了；睡觉的时候，也把脸颊依偎在洛基的照片上。尽管好弟弟不能再回来，但自己也上了年纪的史宾塞在入睡时，能有弟弟还在身边的感觉，还是一种心灵的安慰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11279973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我不认为斯宾塞能够走出洛基离去的伤痛，但我希望它能学会在没有洛基的情况下生活下去。”斐雪也表示，她和家人不会让洛基感到孤独。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-7-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11279983 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-7-1-600x443.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Beth Fisher&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>希望史宾塞可以从洛基的抱枕和家人的爱护中得到失去弟弟的安慰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11279979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11279979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/spencer-7-450x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洛基资料照。(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/bethany.fisher.777" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beth Fisher</a>)</figcaption></figure>
