<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2019三奇稻间美径 热气球嘉年华登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/57df2c1eec86eee82438759908f776d0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019冬山三奇稻间美径的热气球嘉年华升空了。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月30日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）冬山三奇稻间美径第二届的<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">热气球嘉年华</a>即将在6/8、9及6/15、16登场。除了两个系留升空球外，还多了一个造型球及小型移动式热气球，以及热气球博物馆区。除了要带给民众更加不同的视觉体验外，更利用多元的互动方式让民众们不仅只是看稻浪、搭乘热气球，还可以一边了解热气球的历史典故。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289023" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3afcc72da671f5915ea26157f86c2f92.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11289023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3afcc72da671f5915ea26157f86c2f92-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289023" class="wp-caption-text">2019冬山三奇稻间美径的<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">热气球嘉年华</a>升空了。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11289024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ba2e3ef4fdf4baa79533dd13b6161540.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11289024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ba2e3ef4fdf4baa79533dd13b6161540-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289024" class="wp-caption-text">2019冬山三奇稻间美径热气球嘉年华除了两个系留升空球外，还有造型球。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬山乡长林峻辅表示，冬山三奇稻间美径热气球嘉年华，去年首度举办的热气球创造了超过十万人次！今年活动共计四天，一天两场，早场6点至8点，午场16点30分至18点30分，系留升空每人500元，未满110公分禁止搭乘，每场限量120位，现场排队售票。买不到票的民众们也不要灰心，除了热气球系留升空外，公所在下午场次也开放了免费的地面体验活动（walk in），只要在活动当天下午场时间，于FB“幸福冬山”按赞并在“稻间美径”打卡出示在场工作人员，即可兑换体验券两张，民众可以走进热气球中，听专业的讲解员解说热气球的构造与原理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/221c7aac540ccd0a3c555c0312a8f7c1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11289027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/221c7aac540ccd0a3c555c0312a8f7c1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289027" class="wp-caption-text">2019冬山三奇稻间美径的热气球嘉年华除了两个系留升空球外及小型移动式热气球。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11289026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/61b00067905f4fa31c1a4c23ebe57e9d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11289026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/61b00067905f4fa31c1a4c23ebe57e9d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289026" class="wp-caption-text">2019冬山三奇稻间美径的热气球嘉年华除了两个系留升空球外及小型移动式热气球。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林乡长表示，另外在傍晚亦有两天的热气球光雕秀，热气球、加压火焰，伴随着音乐节奏再加上冬山乡的稻浪，稻浪中的热气球－只有在冬山才看得到！</p>
<p>另外，搭乘热气球展望三奇稻间美径的游客，还可以获得一张宜兰限定的“宜兰好玩卡”，整合了宜兰大众运输、旅游景点、观光工厂等资讯，让民众在宜兰的旅游更便利好玩。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/30c61aa17f4dda65c288ccd049c60139.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11289029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/30c61aa17f4dda65c288ccd049c60139-450x636.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="636" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289029" class="wp-caption-text">2019冬山三奇稻间美径的热气球嘉年华活动场次。（冬山乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
