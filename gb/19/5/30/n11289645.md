<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传承客家米食文化 “德高旺粽”创意十足</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/de172faee0c8f76c8b02e28cb7ce933d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月30日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E8%8A%82.md#1">端午节</a>将临，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县政府农业处30日结合关山镇德高社区在县府大礼堂办理“德高旺粽”活动，参加者不但可以体验亲手包粽的乐趣，还可以把自己包的粽子带回家，吸引近百位县府员工和民众参与，现场气氛热烈、香味四溢。</p>
<p>绑粽体验下午2点开始分组进行，德高社区准备了在地圆糯米，以及萝卜干制成的馅料，有些妈妈手脚俐落，一下子就绑出许多“有腰身”的漂亮粽子，也有些人则是边包边学。副县长张志明也下场包粽与大家同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/facdf21294a9aa3a1b294e4774b7de55.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11289651 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/facdf21294a9aa3a1b294e4774b7de55-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289651" class="wp-caption-text">手脚俐落的妇女很快就绑好许多粽子。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11289650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/be33b7c49c2d42edf26ea12c7f58ca52.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11289650 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/be33b7c49c2d42edf26ea12c7f58ca52-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289650" class="wp-caption-text">台东县副县长张志明(右2)也学包粽与大家同乐。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张志明表示，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%AF%E5%8D%88%E8%8A%82.md#1">端午节</a>包粽子是中华传统<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">文化</a>，德高社区近年参与农村再生计划，透过肉粽与德高地名的巧妙结合，以“德高旺粽”口号推广<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6.md#1">客家</a>米食产业，非常有意义。德高社区发展协会理事长刘源利则指出，当地以稻米为生，社区妈妈研发出10多种创意养生客家粽，既推广客家米食，也增加经济收入。</p>
<p>客语老师吴芳美也在现场介绍<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6.md#1">客家</a>粽的做法，她强调馅料均采用在地食材，其中最重要的香味来源是萝卜干，这也是当地稻作秋收后农民利用空档自己栽种的萝卜制成。同时也结合原住民产业，研发出红藜、红麹、小米、野姜花等特色风味养生粽，健康又好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/db388be3c5d0bc39c876a7d07d4e2bf2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11289652 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/db388be3c5d0bc39c876a7d07d4e2bf2-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289652" class="wp-caption-text">客语老师吴芳美介绍客家粽的做法和<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">文化</a>意涵。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴芳美说，端午家家户户插菖浦，客家人则是挂葛藤邪驱，背后有一传说。唐朝末年，黄巢作乱，途中见一客家妇女分别肩背、手牵男孩奔逃，经询问妇人告知肩背的是兄长之子，手牵的是自己幼子。黄巢被其德义所感动，遂告知将葛藤挂于门上做标记，便不会受到侵扰。</p>
<p>妇女回村告诉村民，于是家家户户采集葛藤挂在门上，因而保护了全村人的平安。从此，端午节挂葛藤和菖浦成为客家人世代相传的习俗。吴芳美表示，每年在推广客家粽子的同时，也传承客家勤俭习俗和传统文化，更具意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11289648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/35234b5543740f078d8c19ba5cfb5f80.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11289648 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/35234b5543740f078d8c19ba5cfb5f80-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289648" class="wp-caption-text">关山镇德高社区研发出10多种创意养生客家粽。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11289649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11289649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/8dbaf78b537b0e875dd16a5d248be958.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11289649 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/8dbaf78b537b0e875dd16a5d248be958-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11289649" class="wp-caption-text">台东县政府农业处结合关山镇德高社区办理“德高旺粽”活动，婆婆妈妈热情参与。 （龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
