<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：布袋戏演给谁看</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5d2523ec73224ffe8e9ead7a8a9d15f5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>弘宛然古典布袋戏团。（传艺中心提供）
</h6>
<hr>

<p>锣鼓喧天。庙前<br />
布袋戏台上正热烈地搬演着<br />
好戏呀！好戏吗？<br />
观众则稀少到几乎没有<br />
　<br />
所有早年的情况顿时狂奔向我：<br />
那时几乎也都在庙前<br />
观众很多，大人小孩男女老幼都有<br />
热闹滚滚，有时还挤得水泄不通<br />
叫卖零食的喊叫声此起彼落<br />
戏台上则热闹地上演着<br />
戏偶有许多样貌<br />
文弱书生有，吟诵着诗词<br />
孔武有力的武士有<br />
甚至有生毛带角的<br />
携着武器的，不携武器的<br />
正派与反派厮杀成一团<br />
使利器，吐剑光，甚至不必近身就致对方于死地<br />
胜的扬眉吐气猛追<br />
败的或反抗或奔逃<br />
好像有些是虚拟的，天地外才发生的事<br />
把什么天空地道都闹得沸沸扬扬<br />
观众的情绪随戏码而起伏波动<br />
后来戏转到电视台<br />
剧情更离奇<br />
人物更怪异<br />
轰动到小孩子在学校考试时回答历史题目<br />
曰：明末民族英雄误答为戏中人物史艳文<br />
我每天的午饭配布袋戏<br />
　<br />
可是现在呢？现在的情况就在眼前：<br />
虽然戏台上正热烈地搬演着<br />
观众却少之又少，一点热闹不起来<br />
这样的布袋戏演给谁看？<br />
有人说现在的布袋戏是搬演给神明看的<br />
是吗？不是？<br />
这是什么道理？是时光把戏码搬走了？<br />
是搬演者技术差？<br />
是剧情不离奇？<br />
或是人物不怪异？<br />
还是观众已冷血了？<br />
还是现在的戏码来自宇宙第几度空间？<br />
谁也给不出答案。</p>
<p><strong>For Whom is Glove puppetry Performed</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>It’s deafening sound of gongs and drums. In front of the temple<br />
It’s warm performing glove puppetry on the stage<br />
A good theatre! Is it a good theatre?<br />
The spectators is scarce to almost zero</p>
<p>It runs wildly toward me all the situation of yore:<br />
It’s almost in front of the temple too<br />
Many spectators are there, there are all adult, child, male, female<br />
Bustling and exciting, even to be jam-packed with people sometimes<br />
The sounds which the retailers shout their wares rise here and subside there<br />
There are performing with bustling and exciting on the stage<br />
There are many appearances of the puppets<br />
There are gentle and frail scholars, they who recite poems and ci poem<br />
There are strong and powerful knights<br />
Even there are the animal fur in their bodies and horns on their heads<br />
There are either takes arms or not<br />
Honest and upright fight with the oppose<br />
Hold weapon, throw up sword light, even cause death the oppose with not near him<br />
The winners pursue and attack closely who filled with pride elation<br />
The fails may counterattack or escape hurriedly<br />
It likes the fabricate thing which just come about outside the earth<br />
Makes trouble into bubbling with noise everywhere<br />
Rise here and subside there the spectator’s mood follow theatrical programme<br />
It turns to television hereafter<br />
It’s more fantastic than before in the plot<br />
It’s more monstrous than before in the figure<br />
Makes a great sensation that the children to answer the question of history test:<br />
Responded by mistake that the nation hero is Se En Wen<br />
And I match my lunch with glove puppetry performance</p>
<p>However, what about now? The situation of now is right before my eyes:<br />
Though it’s warm performing on the stage<br />
The spectators are as few as it can be and without bustling<br />
For whom is thus glove puppetry performed?<br />
Someone said that the puppetry performance now was performed for God<br />
Yes? No?<br />
What’s the reason? Is it the time carry away the theatrical programme?<br />
Is it the skill difference the performer?<br />
Is it the plot isn’t fantastic?<br />
Is it the figure not monstrous?<br />
Or is it the spectator is cold-blooded?<br />
Or is it the theatrical programme comes from what dimensional space of universe?<br />
No one can give the answer.@</p>
