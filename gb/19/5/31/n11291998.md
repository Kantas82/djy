<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：一甲子．一瞬</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2006/07/607041214491703-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>落日的晚霞（大纪元记者李志合摄影）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>一甲子，一瞬<br />
一瞬，一甲子<br />
　<br />
那些青春飞扬的日子<br />
多少甜美和欢笑在里头灿亮<br />
历经一甲子的风霜雨雪<br />
任我们怎么叽叽喳喳地召回<br />
现在它们也只能像繁星，闪烁在回忆里<br />
　<br />
一甲子只是一瞬<br />
一甲子虽由一个一瞬又一个一瞬堆叠而成<br />
其实却也只是一瞬<br />
　<br />
现在时已黄昏<br />
光华已渐褪去<br />
我才来插班，是否<br />
能看到还有多少个一瞬<br />
堆叠出另一个一甲子<br />
　<br />
珍惜呀珍惜每一个一瞬<br />
珍惜现在这个一瞬<br />
去堆叠另一个一甲子<br />
至少让此时的晚霞能够灿黄昏！</p>
<p>注：内人蜜子初中毕业后六十年，该届同学于今年五月廿五日在东港举办同学会，到有廿五人。作者忝为临时插班生（眷属？），亦插一脚，谨致赠每人拙作散文集《晚霞灿黄昏》一册，祈望众人真能如书名，晚霞灿黄昏！※</p>
<p><strong>One Jia Zi, in the twinkling of an eye </strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>One Jia Zi, in the twinkling of an eye<br />
In the twinkling of an eye, one Jia Zi</p>
<p>Those days of flying in the youth<br />
How many sweet and laugh brilliant in it<br />
Experienced wind and frost and rain and snow<br />
They are now like dense stars, shines in the recall<br />
In spite of how summon with chirping</p>
<p>One Jia Zi, just a moment<br />
Though a Jia Zi is piled one by one of a moment<br />
In fact, it’s just a moment</p>
<p>And now it’s the dusk<br />
The glory is took off slowly<br />
I just to join a class according to my grade<br />
If it can be there how many moment<br />
Pile into another Jia Zi</p>
<p>Oh cherish , cherish every moment<br />
Cherish this moment of now<br />
To pile another Jia Zi<br />
At least make the afterglow of this time can brilliance in the dusk</p>
<p>*There was a gathering of my wife class mate held on 25 May this year in Donggang after she has finished school 60 years, 25 numbers were appeared. The author be a show time blue(family?), was took party in it also, and presented every one a book of his prose collection “Brilliant in the Dusk with Afterglow”, wishing all of them will brilliant in the dusk with afterglow truly as the name of the book.@</p>
