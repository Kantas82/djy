<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚洲智慧机器人竞赛    联大电子工程学系开办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/63bf74e8013419008e699d53b672324b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>校长蔡东湖致词。 （联合大学提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%AB%8B%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">国立联合大学</a>电子工程学系，5月25日中午12时在电子系馆举办亜洲智慧型<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E5%99%A8%E4%BA%BA.md#1">机器人</a>大赛，共计有十二个项目丶三个组及五个级别（国小丶国中丶高中丶大专及社会人士）306个队伍报名参加，约有上千位选手、指导老师及家长莅临本系参加比赛，是一个相当受到国内重视的竞赛项目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11280422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11280422" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/df2ff2f26af0acbb4f3a6f2f2aaa67eb-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11280422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/df2ff2f26af0acbb4f3a6f2f2aaa67eb-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11280422" class="wp-caption-text">B02项目比赛场地。 （联合大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该活动目的在于促进各级学校重视<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%80%E8%83%BD%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">技能教育</a>，提升机电整合知识与技能之教学水准、提升各级学校师生手脑并用之实作水准，培养创造思考之能力与习惯、促进各级学校师生电机、电子、电脑、控制、资讯等相关技术应用于<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E5%99%A8%E4%BA%BA.md#1">机器人</a>之整合能力，并促进各级学校师生于教学及学习上之相互交流与观摩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11280423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11280423" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/958a62cdc86eaf48737dd971c947e643-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11280423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/958a62cdc86eaf48737dd971c947e643-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11280423" class="wp-caption-text">B09项目比赛场地。 （联合大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11280424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11280424" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9a7f99fa9258e4918cc9e5467c72f845-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11280424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9a7f99fa9258e4918cc9e5467c72f845-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11280424" class="wp-caption-text">B10项目比赛场地。 （联合大学提供）</figcaption></figure>
