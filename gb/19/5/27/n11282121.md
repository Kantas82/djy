<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>热情加勒比海Cha Cha Cha 一口接一口</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年05月27日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
这家餐厅文如其名，进去就想跳两下，墙上挂得琳琅满目、色彩鲜艳。看它的外观以为是一家西班牙餐厅，因为它有海鲜饭、西班牙的果汁酒。细问之下原来是加勒比海餐厅，以及古巴风味料理美食。老板以及侍者都十分的热情好客，菜单也都非常的丰富。<figure id="attachment_11282138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/IMG_1048-5.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/IMG_1048-5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11282138" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Cha Cha Cha的室内装潢。（旧金山<ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%B7%B4%E9%A3%8E%E5%91%B3%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">古巴风味餐厅</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>我们叫了一份沙拉、一份前菜以及一份主食：<br />
1. 家庭沙拉House Salad &#8211; Mix greens, red cabbage, roasted coconut flakes cucumbers &#038; tomatoes in coconut vinaigrette<br />
2. 辣酱炒虾Cajun Shrimp &#8211; House Signature! Sauteed in a spicy cream sauce<br />
3. <ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E9%A5%AD.md#1">西班牙海鲜饭</a>Paella Mixta &#8211; Spanish rice with saffron, mussels, seasonal fish, shrimp, calamari, scallops, chicken &#038; andouille sausage</p>
<p>沙拉看起来很普通，跟一般的餐厅沙拉没什么不同，但是一入口便知道有分别。沙拉上面洒了烤过的椰子片，并且用椰子醋料理，这可是从来没吃过的口味，爽口又美味，两下子就被我们吃光了！<figure id="attachment_11282139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/House-Salad-7.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/House-Salad-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11282139" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家庭沙拉House Salad。（<ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%8A%A0%E5%8B%92%E6%AF%94%E6%B5%B7%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">旧金山加勒比海餐厅</a>Cha Cha Cha，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>前菜选的是虾子做成的辣料理，感觉上有点像是在吃马来西亚菜，又有点像新奥尔良的辣味，蘸着面包吃十分够味。左左右右，看看旁边桌上好像都有点这道菜。看着大家辣得唏哩呼噜，还是一口接一口地吃！<figure id="attachment_11282140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Cajun-Shrimp-servered-9.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Cajun-Shrimp-servered-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11282140" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辣酱炒虾Cajun Shrimp。（<ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%8A%A0%E5%8B%92%E6%AF%94%E6%B5%B7%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">旧金山加勒比海餐厅</a>Cha Cha Cha，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>下一道叫的就是非常出名的海鲜饭，黄色的饭上面有各式海鲜料理，有鱼、有虾、有干贝、有墨鱼、有淡菜，另外还有鸡和本店特制的香肠。我常常到很多餐厅，<ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E9%A5%AD.md#1">西班牙海鲜饭</a>都煮得不对劲，可是这一家倒是做得恰到好处！<figure id="attachment_11282141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Paella-Mixta-2-10.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/Paella-Mixta-2-10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-11282141" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Cha Cha Cha西班牙海鲜饭Paella Mixta。（旧金山<ahref="https://github.com/mhyfql3155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E5%B7%B4%E9%A3%8E%E5%91%B3%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">古巴风味餐厅</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>在旧金山地区有3家Cha Cha Cha都是越夜越美丽，这次去的餐厅就坐落于有名的海特街（Haight Street）。Haight and Ashbury是有名的嘻皮发源地，吃完饭后可以在路上走走，有许多嘻皮店可以逛，也可以看到各式各样色彩缤纷的建筑。周末到了，不妨来吃上一顿，顺便到街上走走，看看不同的风味。#<figure id="attachment_11282143" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/IMG_1049-6.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/IMG_1049-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" class="size-medium wp-image-11282143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Cha Cha Cha的室内装潢有迪士尼电影《Coco》的墨西哥风味。（旧金山古巴风味餐厅，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
