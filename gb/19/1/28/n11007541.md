<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这些美味鸡翅的秘诀大公开</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月28日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B1%E6%B2%B9.md#1">酱油</a>是很多人的料理法宝，尤其对于厨艺不太精湛的人，不知如何调味时，加入几滴酱油，往往会提鲜不少。酱油拌饭更是很多小孩的最爱。</p>
<p>然而，进入速食时代以后，很多古法食品工艺都被机械化生产甚至是香料勾兑替代，包括<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%B1%E6%B2%B9.md#1">酱油</a>。年轻的一代人普遍对于酱油的来历所知甚少。其实，古法酿制酱油是一门不简单的手艺——天时、地利、人和缺一不可，而且还要有足够的耐心。</p>
<p>新加坡网红<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">炸鸡</a>4fingers就特别强调，只选用来自<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%9D%A5%E8%A5%BF%E4%BA%9A.md#1">马来西亚</a>槟城的天然生晒发酵酱油，让炸鸡的香味更浓厚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11007563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11007563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-02.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11007563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-02.jpg" alt="4fingers只选用马来西亚槟城的发酵酱油" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11007563" class="wp-caption-text">和面粉混合好的熟黄豆被放置入发酵室中（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4fingers选用的酱油来自传承两代，有逾50年酿造历史的酱油厂。老板介绍道，全年炎热的<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%9D%A5%E8%A5%BF%E4%BA%9A.md#1">马来西亚</a>有着得天独厚的气候，阳光充足，让酿造出的酱油香味与众不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11007564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11007564" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-03.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11007564" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-03.jpg" alt="4fingers只选用马来西亚槟城的发酵酱油" width="599" b="238" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11007564" class="wp-caption-text">发酵过的黄豆被挪到室外，放入大缸中和盐水混合，进一步发酵（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酿制一支好酱油，要从原料的采购下手。老板称，他们只选用含高蛋白质的优质黄豆，煮熟后和面粉搅拌均匀，再转入发酵室。发酵是很重要的一步，如发酵条件掌握得好，做出的酱油就会很香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11007565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11007565" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-04.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11007565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-04.jpg" alt="4fingers只选用马来西亚槟城的发酵酱油" width="601" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11007565" class="wp-caption-text">百日后的经过马来西亚烈日暴晒发酵后的黄豆变得黑黑的，很像我们炒菜用的豆豉（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11007562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11007562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11007562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-01-600x335.jpg" alt="4fingers只选用马来西亚槟城的发酵酱油" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11007562" class="wp-caption-text">酱油厂的老板在巡视酱油缸的情况（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在发酵室完成发酵的黄豆会被移至室外的大缸中，加入盐水后，在阳光下暴晒100天。</p>
<p>百日后，酱油成熟，便可抽取酱油了。这第一抽就是所谓的“头抽”，质量是最好，最香醇且蛋白质含量最高的。4fingers<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E9%A6%99%E9%85%B1%E6%B2%B9.md#1">蒜香酱油</a>口味的鸡翅就是用这些天然头抽包裹后炸出来的，难怪一推出就红到不行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11007569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11007569" style="width: 728px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-06.gif"><img class="size-full wp-image-11007569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/20190128-Chi-Jin-4fingers-06.gif" alt="4fingers只选用马来西亚槟城的发酵酱油" width="728" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11007569" class="wp-caption-text">美味的鸡翅刷上古法酱油后会再回锅炸至酥脆，yummy!! （视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果想尝尝古法酱油和<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">炸鸡</a>的组合，就动身去找4fingers吧。</p>
