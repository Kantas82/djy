<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《SKY Castle天空之城》KKTV过年期间开播</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190130053135100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩剧《SKY Castle天空之城》收视率打破《孤单又灿烂的守护神—鬼怪》所创下的纪录。（KKTV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心综合报导）去年11月开播的韩剧《<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/sky-castle%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA%E4%B9%8B%E5%9F%8E.md#1">SKY Castle天空之城</a>》首播收视仅有1.72%，但因内容触及韩国升学教育弊病且角色性格鲜明，再加上有<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%89%E6%99%B6%E9%9B%85.md#1">廉晶雅</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%B3%B0%E5%85%B0.md#1">李泰兰</a>、尹世雅、<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%91%9E%E4%BA%A8.md#1">金瑞亨</a>、郑俊镐、<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%82%B3%E5%93%B2.md#1">金炳哲</a>等实力派演员参演，每播出一集，收视率都上升1至3%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11012850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11012850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190130053147100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11012850" title="Yum Jung Ah in SKY Castle" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190130053147100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Yum Jung Ah in SKY Castle" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11012850" class="wp-caption-text">由<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%89%E6%99%B6%E9%9B%85.md#1">廉晶雅</a>主演的韩剧《<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/sky-castle%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA%E4%B9%8B%E5%9F%8E.md#1">SKY Castle天空之城</a>》在韩国拥有高人气。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上周该剧于韩国播出时创下22.3%超高收视率，打破由孔刘、李栋旭及金高银演出的《孤单又灿烂的守护神—鬼怪》所创下的最高收视纪录20.509%，同时也创下韩国有线（付费）电视台收视新高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11012858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11012858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1707110202281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11012858" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1707110202281487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11012858" class="wp-caption-text">由孔刘与李栋旭主演的《灿烂的守护神-鬼怪》收视率曾达到20.509%。（卫视中文台提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该剧不仅成功吸引了一般观众，连防弹少年团（BTS）队长RM（金南俊）及“少女时代”队长太妍都是该剧的忠实观众，SEVENTEEN成员DINO（李灿）也于本日直播的音乐节目《SHOW CHAMPION》中，模仿了<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%82%B3%E5%93%B2.md#1">金炳哲</a>所饰演的“车教授”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11012865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11012865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190130053143100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11012865" title="SKY Castle" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/190130053143100707-600x400.jpg" alt="SKY Castle" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11012865" class="wp-caption-text">韩剧《SKY Castle天空之城》有<ahref="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%B3%B0%E5%85%B0.md#1">李泰兰</a>、崔元英等人参演。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日前，《SKY Castle天空之城》因播出时间与球赛直播撞期，电视台决定停播一次，引发观众向电视台抗议，一度登上新闻版面，由此可看出该剧的话题性及高人气。韩国将在2月1日播出完结篇，台湾的KKTV也拿下台湾播出权，并将于过年期间上架。</p>
