<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒙特利湾 超人气“菲尔鱼市场”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月08日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
说实在这是第一次百合舍不得介绍的餐馆，为什么呢？4.5 Star的餐馆，便宜又好吃，人潮汹涌，去晚了点，长龙见不到底！</p>
<p>百合和吃货朋友前一天特别计划早早出发到<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E7%89%B9%E5%88%A9%E6%B9%BE.md#1">蒙特利湾</a>（Monterey Bay），预计早上11点钟可以到餐馆，这样就可以避开人龙。车开到餐馆的时候却是找不到停车位，来了一位和善的警长，他说：“我们这个小镇都是渔民，你随便停，从来不开罚单。”<figure id="attachment_10962015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8929.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8929-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962015" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962015" class="wp-caption-text">百合在菲尔鱼市场门前。（百合提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>既然有警长作保证，我们就大剌剌地随便停车。我们以为是最早到的，没有想到餐馆已经半满，队也排了快10分钟才轮到我们。派一个人排队，于是我就到后面去溜搭溜搭。<figure id="attachment_10962018" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8935.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8935-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962018" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962018" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E7%89%B9%E5%88%A9%E6%B9%BE.md#1">蒙特利湾</a>（Monterey Bay）。<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10962019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8933.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8933-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962019" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962019" class="wp-caption-text">菲尔鱼市场的新鲜鱼货。<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这家餐馆前前后后都是海，前面停泊船只，后面就是一个超级大海滩。无敌海景、海浪澎湃，就在眼前。户外的座位也不少，因为天气寒冷，我们便坐在搭棚内，雨哗啦哗啦地下，别有一番情调。<figure id="attachment_10962023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962023" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Fried-Fish.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Fried-Fish-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962023" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962023" class="wp-caption-text">炸鱼餐（Fried Fish）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>菜送上来，海鲜又大、又多、又新鲜，旁边有他们自己的鱼市场，吃不完还可以兜着走。他的<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%BC%8F%E7%82%96%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">意式炖海鲜汤</a>（Cioppino）是我吃过最大碗的，碗内的海鲜捞不完。<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%A4%E8%9C%8A%E6%B5%93%E6%B1%A4.md#1">蛤蜊浓汤</a>（Clam Chowder）也不小，千万不要自己一个人吃一大碗，免得被塞住，后面好戏都要错过。<figure id="attachment_10962025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8938.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IMG_8938-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962025" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962025" class="wp-caption-text">蒸贻贝（Steamed Mussel）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10962026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Steamed-Clam.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Steamed-Clam-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962026" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962026" class="wp-caption-text">蒸蛤蜊（Steamed Clam）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>他家的炸鱼十分有名，不怕油炸的话可以试试。另外这里盛产<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%9D%E9%B2%9C%E8%93%9F.md#1">朝鲜蓟</a>（Artichokes），所以千万不要错过烤朝鲜蓟（Grilled Artichoke）。各样贝壳类的海鲜，以及大虾也都非常新鲜、菜色多，可以大家分着吃。桌子有食堂的样子，很随意自在，抓起来大口大口地吃也不怕。<figure id="attachment_10962027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/grilled-artichoke.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/grilled-artichoke-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962027" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962027" class="wp-caption-text">烤<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%9D%E9%B2%9C%E8%93%9F.md#1">朝鲜蓟</a>（Grilled Artichoke）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10962028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10962028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Snapper-Tacos.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Snapper-Tacos-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10962028" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10962028" class="wp-caption-text">鲷鱼玉米饼餐（Snapper Tacos）。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>以前来到这个小镇都是来划船，不知道这里有一家这么好吃的餐馆，如果各位吃客想要来，记得一定要早点到，不然等一个小时是正常的。吃完饭可以到海边走走，或是去划船，玩玩再回家。如果怕太多人的话，可以在周间去，错开周末的拥挤。#</p>
