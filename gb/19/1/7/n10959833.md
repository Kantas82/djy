<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旧金山道地的西班牙餐厅</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月07日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
这日在旧金山玩了一整天，旧金山是个迷人的城市，白天有白天的美，夜晚有他吸引人之处。今天想试试不同的料理，吃什么好呢？最后决定去试试<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">西班牙餐厅</a>——Picaro Tapas Restaurant。</p>
<p>西班牙最有名的食物就是<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">海鲜炒饭</a>，但是这一大盘炒饭叫来，其它什么都吃不下了，于是今天决定跳过海鲜炒饭，尝尝它的小碟。<figure id="attachment_10959874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Chorizo-Salteado-Cantimpalitos.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Chorizo-Salteado-Cantimpalitos-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10959874" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炒<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E9%A6%99%E8%82%A0.md#1">西班牙香肠</a>Chorizo Salteado “Cantimpalitos” &#8211; Sautéed Spanish Sausage。（<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10959876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Patatas-Bravas.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Patatas-Bravas-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10959876" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辣马铃薯（土豆）Patatas Bravas &#8211; Spicy Potatoes。（<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>小碟菜中分为两种：荤食与素食，平日肉吃太多，于是今天少挑点肉，多吃点菜。叫了一盘<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E9%A6%99%E8%82%A0.md#1">西班牙香肠</a>，另外配上了辣马铃薯、热炒香菇和煎洋蓟、马铃薯和洋葱煎蛋（这一餐道菜可是本餐厅经典），而且是众望所归，看起来不怎么样，却是今天最好吃的一道菜。这几样小菜两人吃吃就饱到不行。<figure id="attachment_10959877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Alcachofas-a-la-Plancha.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Alcachofas-a-la-Plancha-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10959877" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤朝鲜蓟Alcachofas A La Plancha &#8211; Grilled Artichokes。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10959878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Champinones-al-Ajillo.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/Champinones-al-Ajillo-600x312.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="312" class="size-large wp-image-10959878" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大蒜蘑菇Champi?ones al Ajillo &#8211; Sautéed Mushrooms With Garlic。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>餐厅内布置，古色古香，十分有西班牙味道。这个似乎是情人约会的好地方，看到一对对的情侣你侬我侬，万分的罗曼蒂克。当然来到了这里自然是免不了要叫一道水果酒Sangria，微醺的酒香醇好喝，没两下子喝酒吃菜全都进了五脏庙。半醉半醒之中离开了餐厅，带着满肚子的西班牙。#</p>
