<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加州清酒品赏会托伦斯热闹登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1-IMG_0866-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加州清酒品赏会于1月13日在托伦斯热闹登场。(大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年01月16日讯】（大纪元工商记者Linda Jin 洛杉矶报导）1月13日，日本<ahref="https://github.com/kgrmye347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E6%B8%85%E9%85%92%E5%93%81%E8%B5%8F%E2%80%9D%E4%BC%9A.md#1">“清酒品赏”会</a>于托伦斯的Miyako Hybird Hotel举办，主办方是All Japan News Inc。现场涌入了许多喜爱日本文化和对清酒有兴趣的民众。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10978712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978712" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/2-IMG_0919-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/2-IMG_0919-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978712" class="wp-caption-text">Wismettac公司推广的几款清酒获多人好评，询问度很高。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办方表示，此次活动有超过三十种品牌的清酒参加，现场还提供了日式烧烤、章鱼烧、关东煮、味噌汤等和风味美食让入场的民众品尝，小酌清酒的同时还可以吃到地道的<ahref="https://github.com/kgrmye347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">日本料理</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10978716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/4-IMG_0813-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/4-IMG_0813-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978716" class="wp-caption-text">参与清酒品赏会的民众还可以日式烧烤佐酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10978722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/5-IMG_0823-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/5-IMG_0823-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978722" class="wp-caption-text">参与清酒品赏会的民众还可以章鱼烧佐酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10978723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/6-IMG_0837-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/6-IMG_0837-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978723" class="wp-caption-text">参与清酒品赏会的民众还可以关东煮佐酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10978741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/9-IMG_0818-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/9-IMG_0818-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978741" class="wp-caption-text">参与清酒品赏会的民众还可以味噌汤佐酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“清酒”主要是以米为原料酿造发酵，在日本有渊远流长的制作历史。酒精浓度平均在15%左右，是日本最具代表性的酒类，因此也称<ahref="https://github.com/kgrmye347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E9%85%92.md#1">日本酒</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10978713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/3-IMG_0781-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/3-IMG_0781-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978713" class="wp-caption-text">西方人士也喜欢品赏清酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最适合饮用清酒的温度介于摄氏五度到摄氏六十度间，这也是所有饮用温度范围最广的酒类。另外，清酒亦可以应用在料理上，最常见的是利用清酒去鱼腥味，增添鲜美。</p>
<p>清酒近来在世界各地广受欢迎，欧美地区也逐渐出现了饮用清酒的风潮。因为清酒是搭配寿司与生鱼片等日式料理的最佳选择，所以当这些日式料理流行至西方国家后，清酒被接受的程度也就越高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10978724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/7-IMG_0929-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/7-IMG_0929-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978724" class="wp-caption-text">越来越多欧美民众接受清酒。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛杉矶移民者众多，日本的美食还有清酒文化一直很受其他族裔喜爱。活动当天，还有演奏日本传统乐器“三味线”，有些女士还穿着传统和服参与品酒活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10978737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/8-IMG_0931-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/8-IMG_0931-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978737" class="wp-caption-text">女士们穿着传统和服参与品酒活动。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10978743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10978743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/10.IMG_0891-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10978743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/10.IMG_0891-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10978743" class="wp-caption-text">加州清酒品赏现场演奏“三味线”。(大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
