<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走春必选2019屏东热带农业博览会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/fd9fac4ebbdb7a20382407e04b55bd76-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019屏东热带农业博览会等你来走春。（屏东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者郑池南台湾屏东报导）</p>
<p><a src=""></a><a href="https://d2wf95os3tobj4.cloudfront.net/C6pYSYrux"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yezrrt3460/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/RsUAW19YnmE" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>大家盼望已久的<ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2019屏东热带农业博览会</a>将于1月29日盛大开幕啰！今年的屏东新年很热闹，除了有热带农业<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">博览会</a>活动打头阵，紧接着台湾灯会登场，旅游人气指数强强滚，是过年<ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">走春</a>最佳选项。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11014909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11014909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/58615a2d78626c31fb3bb020a24657d2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11014909 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/58615a2d78626c31fb3bb020a24657d2-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11014909" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E5%8D%9A.md#1">热博</a>艳阳高照、热情洋溢，连蔬果都长的特别大。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">热带农业博览会</a>活动继2016复办以来，已迈入第4年，年年创入园人数新高，逐渐建立起好口碑，由地方活动升格为年度旅游大盛事，已成为游客过年必定指定到访热门景点，让屏东农业软实力被大家看见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11014911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11014911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/c167677779f15e5c2d79d45d61c47b66.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11014911 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/c167677779f15e5c2d79d45d61c47b66-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11014911" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E5%8D%9A.md#1">热博</a>园区五彩缤纷装置艺术，让你<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E6%98%A5.md#1">走春</a>目不暇给。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">热博</a>今年共设置27个特色主题区，包含热带果园、迎春花海、稻田彩绘、农委会农业科技馆、树艺雅石景观区、向阳优能绿能应用区、智慧型科技农业、光电绿能猪馆、马场体验、兰海馆、玫瑰馆、农村再生、梦想市集、梦境盆栽区、悠活农业体验馆&amp;休闲农业元气馆、瓜果长廊、香草区、青草作物、客家产业馆、可可馆、靓彩鱼love区、杂粮迷宫、食农体验、兰艺馆、原民特作、汪汪绿乐园、地景艺术、梦想一亩园，集结屏东农、渔、畜、休闲产业，展现屏东农业多样化作物育成品种、栽培技术、农特产创意市集。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11014912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11014912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/9a63b2f3e19bb349457ff545700c2767.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11014912 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/9a63b2f3e19bb349457ff545700c2767-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11014912" class="wp-caption-text">2019台湾灯会30周年在屏东，登观景台鸟瞰3D彩绘稻田，可见双猪提灯迎新春美图，是全家拍照留念好景点。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2019热博</a>较往年创新的部分，为与农委会合作科技展，推广当今最火红的智慧农业。</p>
<p><ahref="https://www.2019ptae.tw/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2019热博</a>吉祥物“阳宝”惊喜亮相，个性热情活泼、圆滚滚笑容的杨宝，象征太阳孕育大地万物般，敞开双手欢迎大家来热博玩。今年暖冬热博农作生长特别良好，地景较往年更争奇斗艳，瓜棚果实累累，百花盛开，最适合阖家旅游拍照打卡留念好去处，活动至2月17日止（除夕休园），免门票，详情可上<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2019%E7%83%AD%E5%8D%9A.md#1">2019热博</a>官网<ahref="http://www.2019ptae.tw" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.2019ptae.tw</a>查询。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11014916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11014916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/093624554cb2f4ee274bf4f3aee6fcc7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11014916 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/093624554cb2f4ee274bf4f3aee6fcc7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11014916" class="wp-caption-text">热博吉祥物“阳宝”散发热情与温暖阳光，欢迎全国民众利用9天年假来屏东，白天游热博，夜晚赏花灯，包你满载而归。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
