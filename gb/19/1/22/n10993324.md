<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BOSS x Jeremyville假日系列 引领猪年时尚潮流</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IP002-IMG_0052-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>HUGO BOSS与 Jeremyville携手打造了本季度的一系列成衣和配饰的限量款式。（HUGO BOSS提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月22日讯】BOSS 携手艺术家 Jeremyville 打造全新假日季。 BOSS的时尚感与Jeremyville的趣味创作碰撞出的艺术火花，打造了本季度的一系列成衣和配饰的限量款式。在己亥年来临之际，让我们一同沉浸在Jeremyville打造的满载梦想的卡通次元，探索全新假日系列带来的不一样的穿搭风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10993339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993339" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-women-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993339 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/IP001-IMG_0032-600x479.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="479" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993339" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>艺术家Jeremyville为这个合作系列特别创作了四款卡通形象，其中不仅包括他笔下经典的兔子形象，更有为了迎接中国新年特别创作的小猪。每个独立的卡通角色会化身为钥匙扣和小型摆件。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10993344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993344" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-men-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/015_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993344" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10993345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993345" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-men-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/020_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993345" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10993346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993346" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-men-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/021_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993346" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些单个摆件还可以任意组合，为即将到来的猪年带来新的气象与时尚。</p>
<p>HUGO BOSS 的标识与 Jeremyville 的插画相结合，这些大胆的设计以明亮的色彩印在鞋履、包袋、证件夹及手机壳上。如，他创作的城市乐园会印在饰以“HB”字母组合图案的意大利涂层帆布背包上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10993349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993349" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-women-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/002_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993349" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10993351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993351" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-men-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/003_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993351" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BOSS 与 Jeremyville 邂逅相遇，这是一次全新的尝试。在本次合作系列的设计中，你可以发现内心的珍贵品质：趣味、同理心、爱、对自我完善的追求、以及对自然和动物的尊重。你可一边寻觅着新年心中美好的愿景，一边在探寻的旅途中收获不同往日的趣味与优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10993352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-women-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/006_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993352" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10993354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10993354" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.hugoboss.com/boss-men-chinese-new-year/"><img class="wp-image-10993354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/004_BOSS_Jeremyville_Holiday-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10993354" class="wp-caption-text">（HUGO BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>
