<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA哈登轰61分生涯新高 追平科比</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月24日讯】（大纪元记者乔奕综合报导）休士顿<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E7%AE%AD.md#1">火箭</a>24日对上纽约尼克斯，当家球星<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E7%99%BB.md#1">哈登</a>（James Harden）燃烧小宇宙，全场轰下<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/61%E5%88%86.md#1">61分</a>，为个人<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E6%B6%AF%E6%96%B0%E9%AB%98.md#1">生涯新高</a>，也追平2009年科比（Kobe Bryant）的得分纪录。</p>
<figure id="10998834" aria-describedby="caption-10998834" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/db104ad4d4a492d2adce360e22742944-450x331.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/db104ad4d4a492d2adce360e22742944-450x331.jpg" alt="哈登。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10998834" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E7%99%BB.md#1">哈登</a>。（Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="10998833" aria-describedby="caption-10998833" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/deacbf512b5c3e9d32272fe3b3f13045-450x639.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/deacbf512b5c3e9d32272fe3b3f13045-450x639.jpg" alt="哈登。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10998833" class="wp-caption-text">哈登。（Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="10998832" aria-describedby="caption-10998832" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/e140a016c8c49b0ee02dac02e3346004-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/e140a016c8c49b0ee02dac02e3346004-450x300.jpg" alt="哈登。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10998832" class="wp-caption-text">哈登。（Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然主控保罗（Chris Paul）缺阵，<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E7%AE%AD.md#1">火箭</a>仍在哈登带领下火力大放送。锲而不舍的尼克斯在终场前追到仅有1分差，不过戈登（Eric Gordon）投进大号三分球也粉碎尼克斯逆转希望，终场就由火箭以114：110击败尼克斯。</p>
<p>哈登此役上场40分钟缴出<ahref="https://github.com/fhwnic3872/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/61%E5%88%86.md#1">61分</a>、15篮板、4助攻，罚球线上则是25投22中，创下生涯单场得分新高，也连续21场比赛至少取得30分，成为NBA史上第4长纪录。同时也是继2000年的“侠客”欧尼尔（Shaquille O’Neal）后，首次有球员完成单场60分、15篮板。</p>
<p>戈登此役贡献20分、4篮板、2抄截，法瑞德（Kenneth Faried）则有11分、8篮板、3抄截、3阻攻。◇</p>
