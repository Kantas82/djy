<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多台湾商会法律讲座：为民众排忧解惑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/15797ddeeb2fa6cd_ttl7day595_DSC_0110-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>0110图示：左起：注册金融理财师姚姝姗 （Susan Yao-Arkilander）、家庭法律律师侯岷（Pamela Bautista-Hou）、地产过户律师林姵君（Jennie Lin）、多伦多台湾商会会长郉增辉。（伊铃/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月15日讯】（大纪元记者伊铃报导）1月12日，多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/tjhvfp364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾商会</a>在驻多伦多台北经济文化中心举行<ahref="https://github.com/tjhvfp364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%8B%E8%AE%B2%E5%BA%A7.md#1">法律讲座</a>，特邀房地产法律专家、家庭法律专家和投资顾问，为侨社民众讲解法律及税务常识，为民众排忧解惑。活动吸引众多民众参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10975563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10975563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b163a3948_ttl7daynFr_DSC_0138-e1547492904477.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10975563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b163a3948_ttl7daynFr_DSC_0138-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10975563" class="wp-caption-text">多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/tjhvfp364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾商会</a><ahref="https://github.com/tjhvfp364/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%8B%E8%AE%B2%E5%BA%A7.md#1">法律讲座</a>：为民众排忧解惑。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，地产过户律师林姵君（Jennie Lin）讲解房地产法律；家庭法律律师侯岷（Pamela Bautista-Hou）主讲家庭法及家庭信托；注册金融理财师姚姝姗 （Susan Yao-Arkilander）主讲如何合理避税。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10975566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10975566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b162b264c_ttl7day8pP_DSC_0146-e1547492973286.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10975566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b162b264c_ttl7day8pP_DSC_0146-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10975566" class="wp-caption-text">注册金融理财师姚姝姗 （Susan Yao-Arkilander）。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10975567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10975567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b162b264c_ttl7dayf4I_DSC_0111-e1547493011727.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10975567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579c81b162b264c_ttl7dayf4I_DSC_0111-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10975567" class="wp-caption-text">家庭法律律师侯岷（Pamela Bautista-Hou）。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10975599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10975599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579cdc93726f235_ttl7dayrMb_DSC_0098-e1547494289961.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10975599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1579cdc93726f235_ttl7dayrMb_DSC_0098-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10975599" class="wp-caption-text">地产过户律师林姵君（Jennie Lin）。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三位专业人士用浅白易懂的语言和角度，把本行业基本常识讲述给观众，并现场为民众答疑。精彩的演讲获得得现场民众的欢迎及热烈掌声。</p>
<p>台湾商会会长郉增辉表示，在社区发现，许多人因为不懂法律常识，造成很多困扰。因此特别邀请三位资深专业人士为大家传授与民众生活相关的法律常识。这些信息非常重要，与民众生活息息相关。籍此希望能给社区人士在了解基本常识的同时，也能在遇到具体问题时，知道如何寻求法律帮助。</p>
