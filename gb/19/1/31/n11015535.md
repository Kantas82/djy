<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新年假期 台东“红藜光房”铁花村迎宾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1e98d9ae159e993b174622d55a3cbb62-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东红藜入菜，健康美味。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）新年9天连假即将到来，今年到台东旅游，在台东市区的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E8%8A%B1%E6%9D%91.md#1">铁花村</a>即可观赏到一座长约10公尺的“<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%97%9C.md#1">红藜</a>光房”。由全透明玻璃组装的光房内部，布置著有“谷物中的红宝石”之称的艳丽红藜，还有红藜系列商品，夜晚时，光房还会投射灯光，充满新年喜庆的氛围。</p>
<p>行政院农委会水土保持局<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>分局与台东县政府、台东县农会以及太麻里地区农会携手，31日上午共同为<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%97%9C.md#1">红藜</a>光房揭幕，同时宣布为期3个月的“台东红藜季”起跑。今年有21个店家加入活动，跨领域结合台东红藜的吃、喝、玩、乐、赏景点5感体验，成为台东新春限定游程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11015537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11015537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/c40bb136117ea355e9198e6370e5f858-450x308.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11015537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/c40bb136117ea355e9198e6370e5f858-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11015537" class="wp-caption-text">设在台东铁花新聚落的“红藜光房”。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水保局台东分局局长王志辉表示，台东红藜已建立品牌形象，国内知名企业与在地餐饮业一同投入相关研发，从加工食品的谷物棒、烘培食品、酵素、乖乖、风味酒等，甚至跨足到高级宠物食品，以及面膜、美妆等生技领域的应用，未来将进军国际市场，带动与创生地方产业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11015539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11015539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/ce6471219382f9ee3a4c9ffa11f0f70d-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11015539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/ce6471219382f9ee3a4c9ffa11f0f70d-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11015539" class="wp-caption-text">台东红藜寿司，健康美味。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东县长饶庆铃也强调，9天连假，预期台东会涌入20万观光人次。台东红藜展示场设在<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E8%8A%B1%E6%9D%91.md#1">铁花村</a>，让游客可以近距离“赏红藜”、“尝红藜”、“买红藜”，实际体验台东优质农产品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11015540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11015540" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/5f3ae0cbec887042a87e90e65cb1dab4-450x327.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11015540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/5f3ae0cbec887042a87e90e65cb1dab4-450x327.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11015540" class="wp-caption-text">台东红藜入菜，健康又浪漫。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东红藜是原民部落重要的粮食，营养成分丰富，近年成为健康养生的新宠，可以入菜也可观赏。本次也在铁花新聚落展出4件红藜相关装置艺术作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11015541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11015541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/8e9f65ffd0173858984b192370dbdd08-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11015541 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/8e9f65ffd0173858984b192370dbdd08-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11015541" class="wp-caption-text">台东红藜充分运用到加工食品与生技产品。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11015543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11015543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/3365ba4873661bb326d6704b0ebdedbb-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11015543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/3365ba4873661bb326d6704b0ebdedbb-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11015543" class="wp-caption-text">台东红藜野餐，别有风味。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
