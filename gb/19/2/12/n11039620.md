<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Erika刘艾立任特派记者 首访韩星张基龙</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120532481487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩星张基龙（左）为了首次海外台北见面会，特别接受《MTV我爱偶像》特派记者Erika刘艾立（右）的专访。（MTV TW提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年02月12日讯】（大纪元记者杨泠泠报导）26岁韩国男星<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%9F%BA%E9%BE%99.md#1">张基龙</a>近日为了首次海外台北见面会，特别接受《<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/mtv%E6%88%91%E7%88%B1%E5%81%B6%E5%83%8F.md#1">MTV我爱偶像</a>》特派记者Erika（<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E8%89%BE%E7%AB%8B.md#1">刘艾立</a>）的专访，畅谈他在《Go Back夫妇》、《我的大叔》和《过来抱抱我》等戏剧作品的拍摄过程和挑战心情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11039639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11039639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120532451487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11039639" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120532451487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11039639" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%9F%BA%E9%BE%99.md#1">张基龙</a>也在现场挑战比手画脚大考验。（MTV TW提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>演技备受肯定的张基龙也在现场挑战比手画脚大考验，需在2分钟内用肢体语言让Erika猜出正确答案，才可以获得节目所准备的冬天进补圣品—麻油鸡。为了吃到麻油鸡，张基龙使出浑身解数，装可爱卖萌也要让Erika答对。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11039640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11039640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120532411487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11039640" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120532411487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11039640" class="wp-caption-text">张基龙也在现场挑战比手画脚大考验。（MTV TW提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于爱情，张基龙也透露自己属于发直球告白的类型，因此Erika特别教了张基龙几句中文台词，透过镜头让粉丝也可以感受到张基龙的个人吸引力。</p>
<p>站在身高187公分的张基龙旁边，让Erika显得娇小许多。结束访问后Erika还送上刚发行的新专辑《天鹅说》给张基龙，张基龙露出很惊喜的表情，突然手伸入口袋中翻找，并拿出手指爱心给Erika，立刻让Erika大喊“他真的很会！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11039642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11039642" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120534051487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11039642" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902120534051487.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="681" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11039642" class="wp-caption-text">韩星张基龙（左）为了首次海外台北见面会，特别接受《<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/mtv%E6%88%91%E7%88%B1%E5%81%B6%E5%83%8F.md#1">MTV我爱偶像</a>》特派记者Erika<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E8%89%BE%E7%AB%8B.md#1">刘艾立</a>（右）的专访。（MTV TW提供）</figcaption></figure>
