<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>百年新竹公园樱花开了 赏花野餐品茗正当时</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/c9112d91eff1aea168c0a660ccc68176-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>24日（日）将举办赏花季活动。（林宝云／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者林宝云台湾新竹报导）<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>开了！拥有百年历史的<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">新竹公园</a>樱花目前盛开约七到八成，到了最好的赏花时节，新竹市府于将在24日（日）举办新竹公园<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">赏花季</a>活动，邀市民共同赏花、野餐、逛市集、听音乐，一同感受春天气息。</p>
<p>市长林智坚表示，投入超过六亿经费进行跨域亮点再生工程的<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">新竹公园</a>，丽池园林去年完工，焕然一新，特别着重在无障碍设计，让全部的市民都可以体验公园的美；“透过公共建设的改造，加上优质的艺文活动，让大家感受到城市的改变与美好”。</p>
<figure id="11057726" aria-describedby="caption-11057726" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/acefb01034cba52b53d3fb9df84ec446-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/acefb01034cba52b53d3fb9df84ec446-450x338.jpg" alt="丽池园林水岸景致" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057726" class="wp-caption-text">丽池园林水岸景致。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11057724" aria-describedby="caption-11057724" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3b42d290759e227ba95121e8e73428fa-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3b42d290759e227ba95121e8e73428fa-450x338.jpg" alt="市长林智坚邀市民赏花感受春天气息" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057724" class="wp-caption-text">市长林智坚邀市民赏花感受春天气息。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新竹公园内大约有850棵<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>树，历经一年多施工，丽池园林日前重新启用，年初并完成樱花移植。城销处长颜章圣表示，丽池园林恢复水岸景致，并强调自然、绿色、亲水及保留古迹与老树，再造日式园林建筑景观风貌。每年2、3月正是新竹公园樱花绽放的季节，邻近的十八尖山沿途山路也有杜鹃花、茶梅、南美朱槿等百花盛开。新竹市政府为此特别规划“2019新竹市<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">赏花季</a>-拜访春天”系列活动。</p>
<p>24日新竹公园“野餐日”于上午11时开始邀请民众自备野餐垫，带着全家或与情人、朋友一起至圆形舞台区的草地野餐，舞台区的音乐会，邀请了蓝色警报、达康.COME、广原会三味线、凹与山、安妮塔克及街头杂耍等表演者带给民众欢乐的野餐时光。</p>
<p>活动当天有春日市集，特别挑选了春日精选良品及甜点、咖啡、轻食等还有新竹知名饭店为无暇自备野餐餐点的朋友推出限量餐点，供民众挑选，除此之外，今年特别安排茶席体验，置身此处赏花品茗，别有风味。</p>
<figure id="11057728" aria-describedby="caption-11057728" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9e0cfcaf76b0ef1ed1f4b9c10b7fdc09-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9e0cfcaf76b0ef1ed1f4b9c10b7fdc09-450x600.jpg" alt="樱花开了赏花正当时" width="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057728" class="wp-caption-text">樱花开了，赏花正当时。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11057731" aria-describedby="caption-11057731" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/27cc2d56a5c627cf686a5579c6d2b173-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/27cc2d56a5c627cf686a5579c6d2b173-450x338.jpg" alt="樱花开了" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057731" class="wp-caption-text">樱花开了。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11057730" aria-describedby="caption-11057730" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/d0988fc30d9ef05d2b74d5f0130856c5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/d0988fc30d9ef05d2b74d5f0130856c5-450x338.jpg" alt="赏花野餐正当时" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057730" class="wp-caption-text">赏花野餐正当时。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="11057729" aria-describedby="caption-11057729" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/e32167e84472d68109ac9baf5e99c8c0-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/e32167e84472d68109ac9baf5e99c8c0-450x338.jpg" alt="赏花品茗正当时" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-11057729" class="wp-caption-text">赏花品茗正当时。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>城市行销处表示，除了白天赏樱，晚上也特别安排樱花灯光的设置，让民众下班、下课晚间空闲之余能到公园欣赏樱花，体验夜间不同的美景。新竹市暖冬加码补助到3月3日止，到新竹赏花，参与新竹公园野野餐赏樱与十八尖山健行闯关活动，一同感受春意生机盎然的新竹美景。</p>
