<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>支持唐宝宝就业培力 扶轮社元宵传爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/b5023613b71e60a93a4edaf4a40d85df-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中正扶轮社联合5友社捐款大合照。 (唐氏症关爱者协会提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者李晴玳台湾高雄报导）支持<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">唐宝宝</a><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%9A.md#1">就业</a>培力、自力更生，国际扶轮3510地区包括中正、西北、爱河、繁星、兴达港、大心等社友元宵传爱，联合<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%B5%A0.md#1">捐赠</a><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E6%B0%8F%E7%97%87.md#1">唐氏症</a>关爱者协会24万元，期抛砖引玉号召社会善心，一起帮助<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">唐宝宝</a>筑梦飞翔。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E6%B0%8F%E7%97%87.md#1">唐氏症</a>关爱者协会/唐氏症基金会积极推动“天生我才<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%9A.md#1">就业</a>力培训计划”，旨在扶持协助唐宝宝及心智障碍者，透过专业服务培训，谋求自力更生的机会。国际扶轮3510地区中正等社友获闻该会全台小作所据点，自筹经费尚多短缺，携手慷慨捐输，力挺唐宝宝就业圆梦。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%B5%A0.md#1">捐赠</a>仪式19日于唐氏症关爱者协会南区欢喜工作坊会举行，由中正社社长王裕彦、西北社社长张简牧宗及各社代表共同捐赠支票，社友们随后并与学员一同搓汤圆，欢庆元宵，祈祝圆梦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11056270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11056270" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/6bf2fdcacd2cf0fd0f54ad615cd2d854.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/6bf2fdcacd2cf0fd0f54ad615cd2d854-450x225.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11056270" class="wp-caption-text">中正<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E7%A4%BE.md#1">扶轮社</a>联合5友社捐款。 (唐氏症关爱者协会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>王裕彦表示，南区欢喜工作坊前年的修缮工程，系由前社长李文章及秘书刘清吉协助，因此了解学员们的需要及经费需求，此次结合元宵庆祝活动，举办“快乐工作?努力筑梦”捐赠仪式，期抛砖引玉号召更多伙伴响应，一起帮助唐宝宝筑梦飞翔。</p>
<p>唐氏症基金会董事长林正侠指出，据统计全台特教学校毕业生，投入职场比例往往不到两成，唐氏症基金会推动“天生我才就业力培训计划”，于全台据点规划有烘焙、手工皂、代工等个别特色课程，以及产品，更成立“爱不啰嗦”、“爱皂事”等自有品牌，期帮助毕业生们学以致用，精进工作及生活自理技能与社会接轨。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11056272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11056272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5386c45fd748e41254b8be251fcd424b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5386c45fd748e41254b8be251fcd424b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11056272" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E7%A4%BE.md#1">扶轮社</a>社友与学员搓汤圆同乐。 (唐氏症关爱者协会提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11056273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11056273" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9af8d354bdeeb12eabd37b5fd80ffa05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/9af8d354bdeeb12eabd37b5fd80ffa05-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11056273" class="wp-caption-text">唐宝宝太鼓表演开场热闹非凡。 (唐氏症关爱者协会提供)</figcaption></figure>
