<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小狗狗曾遭胶带缠嘴弃桥下 与救命恩人重逢深情示谢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/7-32-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>曾遭胶带缠嘴的小狗狗路易，见到了爬到桥下救他的好心人霍特，激动感恩，让霍特泪盈。（<a href="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Facebook: Griffith Animal Hospital
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年02月14日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云纽约采访报导）“我很震惊，因为我觉得它不会记得我了。”美国印第安纳州居民鲍勃·霍特（Bob Hoelter）感慨说。</p>
<p>上月一个寒夜，霍特准备从家里去3公里外的商店买东西。通常开车去的他，忽然觉得自己需要些锻炼，于是改为散步去。这个不经意的决定，让他救下了一个命运多舛的小生命。</p>
<p>霍特经过一座桥时，听到了一些动静。他驻足细听，声音又从桥下传来了。他说，那是世上最悲伤的呜咽声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11045045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11045045" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/BRIDGE.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11045045 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/BRIDGE-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11045045" class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Bob Hoelter)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我看着河水和岸边，看不到东西，但我听到了。我在岸边四处走，环顾着四周。”他回忆道，哭声是从桥的下方传来的。</p>
<p>尽管不知道呼救的是什么，他当即决定要救这个小东西。他跪在地上，开始沿着涵洞爬。“我掏出手电筒——我总是随身带手电，我爬到了下面，终于看见它了！”</p>
<p>抬眼望着他的，是一只恐惧颤抖的小狗。接下来看到的景象让他难以置信：小狗的嘴部被用绝缘胶带封了一圈。</p>
<p>“我心想，‘哦，天哪不要！’”霍尔特说。他当即把小狗裹在夹克里，攀回马路，赶往不远处的格里菲斯动物医院（Griffith Animal Hospital），心下希望医院还有人在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-31-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043265" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他冲进医院大门的那一刻，医院办公室经理洛芮·卡瓦西奇（Lori Kovacich）永远不会忘记。“我赶紧起身到厅里接过了它。”她回忆说，自己当即把小狗交给医护人员，都没有来得及问这位好心人的名字和电话号码。</p>
<p>霍特看到小狗已得救，就欣慰地往商店去了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/2-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/2-16-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043268" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>小狗已经很虚弱，胶带下的皮肤伤得很厉害——兽医估计它已被缠了好几天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3-9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11043269 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3-9-600x292.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043269" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>取下胶带后，他们给4个月大的小狗注射了抗生素、涂了药膏，之后用毯子和毛绒动物给它铺了一张床。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/4-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/4-6-450x542.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="542" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043271" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11043272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5-5-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043272" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后他们发现，小狗的一只后腿骨折了，需要动手术。他们猜测是有人从桥上把狗扔了下来，没有扔到水里，狗摔落在地上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11045049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11045049" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/xray.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11045049 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/xray-450x929.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="929" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11045049" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛芮在医院脸书发帖写道：“我在这里工作30年，没想过会看到这一幕……真有这种事。”</p>
<p>最先注意到她的帖子的网友中，有一对夫妇——医院老顾客道格和玛丽·维亭（Doug &#038; Mary Witting）。</p>
<p>维亭夫妇最近刚失去一只爱犬，他们有个共识就是家里近期不会再添狗了，而这只小狗的悲惨遭遇让他们改变了主意。</p>
<p>“我心想，‘那可爱的小脸，它需要我。我可以7天24小时给它爱，我也需要它。’”玛丽联系媒体“渡渡鸟”（<em>The Dodo</em>）后，告诉其记者。</p>
<p>随后，这对爱心夫妇去了医院，一见之下立即决定收养，小狗狗得名路易（Louie）。</p>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src=></a><a href="https://d2gp1icp8dv5pn.cloudfront.net/V8rJnrVjg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sxvshi319/www/master/t/ntdtv/facebook.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(facebook视频或图片.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/sxvshi319/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/post.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fphoto.php%3Ffbid%3D2746812562003242%26set%3Da.467455226605665%26type%3D3&amp;width=500&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=570&amp;appId" width="500" b="570"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>路易终于可以去一个有爱的大家庭，大家都很高兴，只是，救下它的那个人在哪里？</p>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src=></a><a href="https://d2gp1icp8dv5pn.cloudfront.net/V8rJnrVjg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sxvshi319/www/master/t/ntdtv/facebook.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(facebook视频或图片.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/sxvshi319/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/post.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fphoto.php%3Ffbid%3D2779525608731937%26set%3Da.695796610438191%26type%3D3&amp;width=500" width="500" b="502"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>无巧不成书，霍特的侄女看到了医院发的帖子，最终让大家见到了她的叔叔。</p>
<p>团聚的那一天，最为激动的不是“别人”，正是路易——它认出了<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E5%91%BD%E6%81%A9%E4%BA%BA.md#1">救命恩人</a>。</p>
<p>“我很震惊，因为我觉得他不会记得我”，霍特说，“他们让它坐下，它直直地向我跑来。”路易舔着霍特，激动地和他亲昵了好一阵子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043278" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/7-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/7-3-450x926.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="926" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043278" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11043276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/6-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/6-2-450x926.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="926" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043276" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>看到路易有了一个理想的家，霍特很开心；不过他也有点不敢想，如果那天晚上他不是步行，而是开车去商店，就不会听到路易的呜咽声。他也庆幸那晚医院还有人值班，觉得这真是“天时地利人和”。</p>
<p>“老实说，这让我想到，每个人的人生都有上苍安排的意义。我们都有自己的使命。”霍特说，“我以后得多走走路了，不过我可不想再在桥下发现狗了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11043279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11043279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/8-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11043279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/8-3-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11043279" class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/Griffith-Animal-Hospital-228490937200372" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Griffith Animal Hospital/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛芮告诉大纪元，看到路易的故事如此广传，她“既惶恐又暖心，直到现在还忍不住流泪”。</p>
<p>她也透露，情人节这天，小狗狗正受邀参加当地小学的派对，和关心它的孩子们见面。目前，他们每半月给路易拍X光片，观察伤腿愈合情况，不出意外的话，将在一个半到两个月后给它拆线。</p>
