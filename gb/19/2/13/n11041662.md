<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>朱智勋谈《ITEM》收视 认为做好自己更重要</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043844100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>朱智勋、陈世娫、金刚于、金釉俐、朴元相、吴承勋及导演金成旭出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会资料照。（KKTV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年02月13日讯】最新韩剧《ITEM》，11日于韩国举行首映记者会，<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B1%E6%99%BA%E5%8B%8B.md#1">朱智勋</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E4%B8%96%E5%A8%AB.md#1">陈世娫</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%88%9A%E4%BA%8E.md#1">金刚于</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%87%89%E4%BF%90.md#1">金釉俐</a>、朴元相、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%89%BF%E5%8B%8B.md#1">吴承勋</a>及导演金成旭等人出席活动。在被问及是否有收视率压力时，朱智勋表示，若能将自身的优点充分发挥，就会获得观众的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043840100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042030" title="ITEM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043840100707-600x400.jpg" alt="ITEM" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042030" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B1%E6%99%BA%E5%8B%8B.md#1">朱智勋</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E4%B8%96%E5%A8%AB.md#1">陈世娫</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%88%9A%E4%BA%8E.md#1">金刚于</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%87%89%E4%BF%90.md#1">金釉俐</a>、朴元相、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%89%BF%E5%8B%8B.md#1">吴承勋</a>及导演金成旭出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11041988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11041988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043827100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11041988" title="ITEM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043827100707-600x400.jpg" alt="ITEM" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11041988" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫、金刚于、金釉俐、朴元相、吴承勋及导演金成旭出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日前演员丁一宇在自己的新剧记者会上谈及自己是朱智勋的粉丝，对此，朱智勋笑说：“我有看到新闻，现在大家都为了给观众更好的戏剧内容而努力，同时段播出的戏剧，能够这样互相打气我觉得很好，这样世界一定会更美好的！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11041992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11041992" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043856100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11041992" title="Ju Ji Hoon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043856100707.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11041992" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主持人说：“尽管如此，《ITEM》的成绩应该更好吧！”朱智勋妙回：“我妈一定是这么想的！但丁一宇的妈妈一定会支持他儿子的戏！”幽默发言引来笑声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043934100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042012" title="Ju Ji Hoon and Jin Se-yeon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043934100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon and Jin Se-yeon" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042012" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11042015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043937100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042015" title="Ju Ji Hoon and Jin Se-yeon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043937100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon and Jin Se-yeon" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042015" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朱智勋以一身灰蓝色绣花西装帅气出席，拍照时也比出了他招牌的“6”的手势，代表对现场记者以及看到照片的观众们满满的感谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11041993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11041993" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043756100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11041993" title="Ju Ji Hoon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043756100707.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11041993" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋比出了他招牌的“6”手势，代表对大家的感谢。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11042019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043750100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042019" title="Ju Ji Hoon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043750100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042019" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋以灰蓝色绣花西装出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他表示，接下《ITEM》的原因是故事本身很有趣，也相当好奇拍成电视剧后会呈现怎样的内容，在跟导演讨论后，发现该剧本可以拍成很厉害的电视剧，但因为剧情需要许多特效，制作方花了很多功夫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11041987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11041987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043824100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11041987" title="ITEM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043824100707-600x400.jpg" alt="ITEM" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11041987" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫、金刚于、金釉俐、朴元相、吴承勋出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>参与过《与神同行》拍摄的他，也分享了很多关于该电影的拍摄经验。朱智勋坦言：“这部剧跟与神同行一样有需要很多想像的地方，所以我以为会比较轻松。但我错了。不过也因为《与神同行》的经验，也帮助我跟导演沟通，排除拍戏过程的困难。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042010" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043905100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042010" title="Ju Ji Hoon" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043905100707.jpg" alt="Ju Ji Hoon" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042010" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>被问及会不会有收视压力时，朱智勋表示：“我年纪虽不大，但每长一岁，就觉得长辈说的话都是有道理的，他们说‘外国的月亮比较圆’，会觉得别人的戏看起来比较厉害，我想很多人也都有这种想法。但我们的月亮也可以是不一样的月亮，将我们的优点好好发挥，拍出有趣的作品，观众也会支持我们的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042009" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043820100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042009" title="ITEM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043820100707-600x400.jpg" alt="ITEM" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042009" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫、金刚于、金釉俐、朴元相、吴承勋出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饰演“犯罪侧写师”的陈世娫则表示，这个职业其他戏剧也有出现过，但多半都比较冷静冷酷，但自己所饰演的申素英心中有不为人知的伤痛，个性更人性化许多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11041998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11041998" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043741100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11041998" title="Jin Se-yeon, JIN SE YUN" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043741100707.jpg" alt="Jin Se-yeon, JIN SE YUN" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11041998" class="wp-caption-text">陈世娫出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>睽违四年穿上时装街拍现代剧，她坦承“穿现代服感觉怪怪的”，一开始拍摄时还觉得旁边穿时装的临演只是一般的路人。而陈世娫在台词也下了功夫，“为了要讲的很自然，做了很多练习”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042003" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043734100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042003" title="Jin Se-yeon, JIN SE YUN" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043734100707.jpg" alt="Jin Se-yeon, JIN SE YUN" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042003" class="wp-caption-text">陈世娫出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于大家关心的“收视公约”，朱智勋表示，如果收视成绩不错，会跟制作组讨论，之后再给大家一个满意的答案，“再请大家多多帮忙（提升收视率）。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11042008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11042008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043810100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11042008" title="ITEM" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190213043810100707-600x400.jpg" alt="ITEM" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11042008" class="wp-caption-text">朱智勋、陈世娫、金刚于、金釉俐、朴元相、吴承勋出席韩剧《ITEM》首映记者会。（KKTV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>KKTV已于2月12日起上架跟播由朱智勋主演的最新韩剧《ITEM》，每周二、三12:00更新。</p>
