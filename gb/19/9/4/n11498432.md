<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>善心人士捐赠宜县四辆救护车 两辆消防警备车</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/a96499de7986e4e8f412619511c44a67-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>善心人士捐赠宜县四辆救护车  两辆消防警备车大合影。（谢月琴／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年09月04日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）宜兰县4辆<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8A%A4%E8%BD%A6.md#1">救护车</a>、2辆<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E8%AD%A6%E5%A4%87%E8%BD%A6.md#1">消防警备车</a>联合<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%B5%A0.md#1">捐赠</a>典礼于9月4日上午11时许，总金额高达1294万元，假县政府县民大厅及红砖广场办理，由县长林姿妙主持捐赠仪式，并代表受赠事宜。</p>
<p>此次<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%90%E8%B5%A0.md#1">捐赠</a>者有林张秀女士、常如山先生及刘贞华女士、谢明琬女士、台湾省城隍庙、张显松先生及嵩雷精密工业公司、萧淑惠女士及萧宗义先生等6名捐赠者。 每位捐赠者都有一段感人故事:</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/79021db71cdcb245880129c1db2fcd92.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498434 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/79021db71cdcb245880129c1db2fcd92-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498434" class="wp-caption-text">90岁的林张秀女士捐苏澳消防分队<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8A%A4%E8%BD%A6.md#1">救护车</a>一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林张秀女士为苏澳镇人，以经营杂货店维生，平素热心公益，助人为乐，曾经捐赠消防局救助器材车及救护车各一辆，今为庆祝其90岁大寿，缩衣节食，又再捐苏澳消防分队救护车一辆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/01f5cb1e0947fed92a0dfc7a6c6782ea.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498435 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/01f5cb1e0947fed92a0dfc7a6c6782ea-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498435" class="wp-caption-text">常如山先生、刘贞华女士捐赠宜兰县府救护车一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>常如山先生、刘贞华女士为发迹台南的爱尔丽医美集团的董事长及总经理，投入公益活动不遗余力，至今已于全台各地捐赠17辆救护车，因宜县去年普悠玛列车翻车事件，有感于宜兰县迫切需要医疗资源，立即决定捐赠宜兰县府救护车一辆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/04fe7d36c280fbc761deccedb5c96bb7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498437 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/04fe7d36c280fbc761deccedb5c96bb7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498437" class="wp-caption-text">谢明琬女士遵照女儿江宇涵遗愿，捐赠罗东消防分队救护车一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢明琬女士为冬山乡人，为慈济师姐，其小女儿江宇涵因病辞世，为缅怀及遵照女儿遗愿，捐赠罗东消防分队救护车一辆，帮助更多人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/55fffae0076169740b782a196527ddef.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498439 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/55fffae0076169740b782a196527ddef-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498439" class="wp-caption-text">台湾省城隍庙捐赠宜兰县政府消防局救护车一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾省城隍庙为北台湾妈祖文化节的主办单位之一，为善不落人后，每年皆捐赠台北市消防局救护车，今年亦捐赠桃园市消防车，让神明无论有形无形之间都在保佑社会大众。108年因北台湾妈祖文化节恰逢宜兰县南天宫值东主办，故捐赠宜兰县政府消防局救护车一辆以共襄盛举。此救护车将全程参与北台湾妈祖文化节9/6-9/8的海陆绕境宜兰县活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/00f476f82584eb768aca912eea1af28d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498440 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/00f476f82584eb768aca912eea1af28d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498440" class="wp-caption-text">张显松先生捐赠员山消防分队<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E8%AD%A6%E5%A4%87%E8%BD%A6.md#1">消防警备车</a>一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张显松先生为员山乡人，亦为嵩雷精密工业公司董事长，曾经捐赠大同乡、员山乡公所、农会等单位数辆公务车，得知员山消防分队消防警备车老旧，急需汰换，立刻捐赠员山消防分队消防警备车一辆供救灾使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/32d73152d55be276a56d5ecbfadfa36a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498441 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/32d73152d55be276a56d5ecbfadfa36a-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498441" class="wp-caption-text">萧淑惠女士及萧宗义先生兄妹捐赠礁溪消防分队警备车一辆。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萧淑惠女士及萧宗义先生兄妹，为礁溪乡人，萧淑惠为钢琴老师；萧宗义曾任礁溪乡玉石村村长，亦是礼仪师，其父母十多年前曾经捐赠一部消防警备车(春苑号)供礁溪消防分队救灾使用，近来听闻消防局警备车普遍老旧，特捐赠礁溪消防分队警备车一辆，传承父母的善行义举。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11498443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11498443" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/9e3eda5161beb4e0ff595c9dc58109e2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11498443 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/9e3eda5161beb4e0ff595c9dc58109e2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11498443" class="wp-caption-text">善心人士捐赠宜县四辆救护车 两辆消防警备车大合影。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰县政府消防局108年度截至6月30日止救护次数已达11,616次，各个捐赠者皆希望能对真正需要的地方提供更有效的帮助，发挥“人溺己溺，人饥己饥”精神，热心公益，回馈乡梓，共捐赠救护车4辆及消防警备车2辆，供宜兰县政府消防局执行紧急救护暨救灾勤务使用。</p>
