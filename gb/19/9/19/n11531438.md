<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：家庭越野车 2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail AWD</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年09月19日讯】22年前，Toyota推出了第一款小型SUV，此乃第一代的Toyota RAV4，当时外型有活力。之后的更新却变成方方正正，独欠活力，到这一次改款，终于在外型上变成更粗犷，前脸带有同厂皮卡（Pick-Up）的设计，而且更推出了Trail版本。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531450" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531450" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_05-e1568865022533.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531450" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531450" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>这周测试车是Toyota RAV4 Trail AWD，跟其他RAV4最大不同的，就是前脸的六角进风口设计，整体感觉更“越野”，车身离地距也升高了半吋，但是总车高却减了0.2吋。测试车颜色是双色（Dual Tone），浅蓝色是车下半身颜色，上半则是白色，看起来更有活力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_18-e1568865055196.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531454" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>第五代RAV4的引擎排汽量仍是2.5公升，直四自然吸气引擎，但这是新一代Dynamic Force引擎，动力更强，马力提升至203匹，而扭矩则有184磅呎，比旧有的176匹及172磅呎扭矩提升不少。全新RAV4同时也将变速箱升级，采用8速自动变速箱。XLE以下版本采用Active Torque AWD四驱系统，当系统侦测到轮胎打滑时，便会由前轮驱动转为四轮驱动。而Trail及Limited则进一步采用Dynamic Torque Vectoring AWD，可最多将50%动力转至后轮，而且左右可独立分配。多模式（Multi-Terrain Select）的越野系统，可用作应付不同地形，例如泥泞（Mud）、沙地（Sand）、碎石地（Rock）及泥地（Dirt），另外有雪地（Snow）及Eco（省油）的按键。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531458" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_24-e1568865089939.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531458" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531458" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>进入车内，由于测试车是Trail版本，故此车内部分地方用上了鲜橙色，以凸出Trail的越野个性，缝线也是以橙色为主。新RAV4的中控台萤幕颇大，XLE或以下型号是7吋，而Trail及Limited版则是8吋，运行Entune 3.0系统，可支援Apple CarPlay，扬声器则由入门版的6个，到Limited版的11个。中控台最下方是无线充电板，可为手机充电。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_21-e1568865073319.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531456" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>RAV4的前座均装有加热设备，Trail版及Limited版则有冷冻设备，而Limited更有后座椅加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_28-e1568865097124.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531460" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>变速杆个头大，而且越野模式旋扭设在变速杆与驾驶席中间，距离较远使用起来有点不便，但是转档时又很顺畅，还是可以的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_23-e1568865081204.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531457" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>驾驶新一代RAV4，动力明显比上一代优胜，而且方向盘没有以往“重”，转动更轻松，前座椅的冷冻模式不强，纵使开启第三段，仍然不够凉快。RAV4配备的天窗并不是全景式，与其他同级对手比较，差了一点，最喜欢是Trail及Limited版增加了越野模式及Dynamic Torque Vectoring AWD，想到野外走走也可应付了。当然，RAV4最受人欢近的仍是因为Toyota的可靠性吧，无怪RAV4已超越Toyota的Corolla及Camry的销量呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11531451" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531451" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_06-e1568865030309.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531451" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531451" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11531452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11531452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_09-e1568865039415.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11531452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/2019_Toyota_RAV4_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11531452" class="wp-caption-text">2019 Toyota RAV4 Trail。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
