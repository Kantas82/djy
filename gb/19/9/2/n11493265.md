<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美食爱好者最宠的餐馆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image009-600x400.png" />
<h6>Big John’s 傍晚灯火通明（The Big John's Pizza提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年09月02日讯】Big John&#8217;s Pizza于1985年初在邦迪的邦迪路（Bondi Road Bondi）以“约翰比萨”（John&#8217;s Pizza）而命名的一家餐馆。这家Pizza 店由业主Big John亲自上手烤制比萨，被美食爱好者交口称赞为邦迪最好吃的披萨。声名远扬之后Big John 餐馆为了满足客户的订餐需求，又特别增加了3名烤披萨的厨师、3名送餐司机和一个专门的电话接单员来满足不但增加的订餐需求。餐馆比萨饼送货上门服务使其获得了巨大的成功，于是BIG JOHN&#8217;S PIZZA PTY LTD公司正式成立。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11493292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image001.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image001-600x66.png" alt="" width="600" b="66" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11493294" style="width: 389px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image002.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-11493294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image002.png" alt="" width="389" b="393" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Big John本人（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11493295" style="width: 539px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image003.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11493295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image003.jpg" alt="" width="539" b="405" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Big John亲手建餐厅（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1989年，在<ahref="http://www.bigjohnspizza.com.au/">Sans-Souci</a> ?的BIG JOHN&#8217;S PIZZA RESTAURANT顺利开张，之后又在1996年初开张了邦迪第三家Pizza店。Big John&#8217;s Pizza因其美味可口的Pizza、友好热情的服务和轻松的就餐氛围 而成为了人们谈论美食时的话题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11493296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image004.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image004-600x68.png" alt="" width="600" b="68" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在Sans-Souci 的Big John’s 餐馆，除了有美味的Pizza，餐馆的烤排骨和海鲜也是颇受客人喜爱的，特别是超大的海鲜拼盘，份量足够4-6人食用，供应的新鲜海鲜是当天从鱼市采购来的新鲜海鲜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11493297" style="width: 546px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image005.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11493297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image005.jpg" alt="" width="546" b="409" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">超大拼盘海鲜，超级美味新鲜，货真价实（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11493298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image006.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image006-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">整块烤排骨，红亮，柔软，香味浓郁（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老板 Big John是一位心直爽快之人，他透露自己一个经营成功，能做到菜品份量足足的秘密：因为餐馆的是他自己的财产，不用付房租，这一部分费用就可以直接用在提供的食品上，对客户来说就是花相同于其它餐馆的钱，却可以在Big John得到超大份的食品，而且品质俱佳，真正是名副其实的“Big John， Big Meal，Big Value”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11493299" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image007.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image007-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一公斤超大烤 T—Bone 牛排（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11493301" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image008.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Pizza品种繁多，奶酪醇香，口味丰富（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11493290" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image010.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11493290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/image010-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">众明星照高挂餐厅内（The Big John&#8217;s Pizza提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Sans Souci餐馆除楼下用餐座位，还特别增加了楼上座位容量和“The Side Bar”， 让人们可以一边品尝美食一边观赏表演。那里也是举办生日的好去处。</p>
<p>John’s 已经是连续五年获得本地餐厅卓越奖，难怪声名远播，深受欢迎。</p>
