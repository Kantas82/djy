<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：珍惜相聚时光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/11/1205090720522153-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Syl&#8217;s Flowers （Syl Lebar / 大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>多相聚吧！<br />
多珍惜相聚的好时光！</p>
<p>年轻时候是相聚的好时光<br />
那时候，活力十足，神采奕奕<br />
可以蹦蹦跳跳，无忧无虑<br />
遨游在天空地阔间<br />
让无限青春尽量发挥<br />
该多加利用，不能随意放过</p>
<p>年老时更需珍惜相聚的时光<br />
那是人生最珍贵的时候<br />
也是最美好的时候<br />
有很多可以相聚的活动呀<br />
到公园，到活动中心<br />
阅读书报，下棋，听音乐……<br />
再不然，聊聊天回忆年轻的日子也很好<br />
多享受这段黄昏时光！<br />
那是与安乐相随的好时光！<br />
那是多彩缤纷的好时光！</p>
<p>不论什么时候<br />
多相聚，多培养感情吧！<br />
多相聚，多增广见识吧！<br />
多相聚，多发光发热吧！<br />
多珍惜相聚的好时光吧！</p>
<p><strong> Treasure the Time to Gather together</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Be more and more to gather together!<br />
Be more and more to treasure the good time gather together!</p>
<p>It’s the good time to gather together while youth<br />
We are vitality, with radiate look<br />
Can gambol, free from any worry<br />
Can roam in the wide heavens and earths<br />
Let the youth limitless bring into play to the full<br />
Ought to use it at the best, can’t put it aside</p>
<p>It must treasure the time to gather together the more while in age<br />
It’s the most to treasure the time in our life<br />
And is also the most beautiful time<br />
There are also many activities we can gather together<br />
May go to the park, may go to the activity center<br />
To read books and news paper, play chess, enjoy music…<br />
If not, it’s well also to chat about the recollection of the day of youth<br />
Be more to enjoy this section of dusk!<br />
It’s the good time which accompanies by ease and comfort!<br />
It’s the good time which is colors in riotous profusion!</p>
<p>No matter whenever it is<br />
Be more to gather together, be more to plant the good sensibility!<br />
Be more to gather together, be more to enrich the knowledge and experience!<br />
Be more to gather together, be more enough to make the life emit light and heat!<br />
Be more and more to treasure the good time gather together!@</p>
