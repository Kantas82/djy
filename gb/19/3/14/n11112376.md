<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>价钱实惠 优质天妇罗丼饭</h2>
<a href="https://divmj5v44rbg.cloudfront.net/C6qWtioMm"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ebexlp3377/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月14日讯】日本知名连锁<ahref="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%A6%87%E7%BD%97%E4%B8%BC%E9%A5%AD.md#1">天妇罗丼饭</a>店TENYA（天丼てんや）自去年底登陆青衣城后，一直人气高企，人龙不断。TENYA天丼在日本有超过200多间门市，以价钱实惠闻名。来到香港开店，价钱一样亲民，低至49元就能吃到一个<ahref="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%A6%87%E7%BD%97.md#1">天妇罗</a>丼饭，每个饭还送面豉汤。</p>
<p>港店的餐牌和日本的有八成相似，食谱、份量全部跟足日本店做法，部分食材由日本直送，包括九州的华味鸟、青森县产帆立贝和米饭、日本的<ahref="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%A6%87%E7%BD%97.md#1">天妇罗</a>炸粉、鸡蛋、味噌汤和乌冬等。酱油和日本店一样，来自日本一间超过200年历史的酱油厂，用特选鲣鱼片熬煮出来的秘制酱油，入口香甜。刚进入店内，便闻到很香浓的酱油店</p>
<p>全场只得40多个座位，由于太多人排队的缘故，基本上所有食客吃完就走，没有坐很久的。如果人数是单数的话，侍应会请你留空一个位，留给只有一个人的客人搭台。虽然餐厅设计有点像日式连锁快餐的格局，不过这里不是快餐厅形式。全程只需坐定定点餐，有专人送餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11112441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230121366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112441" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230121366-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112441" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅全场只得40多个座位，由于太多人排队的缘故，基本上所有食客吃完就走。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坐下看看餐牌，点了两个天妇罗冷面定食，一个配荞麦面，另一个则配乌冬。另外点了人气海老穴子天丼、<ahref="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%98%9F%E5%A4%A9%E4%B8%BC.md#1">巨星天丼</a>，再加一个浅草海老天丼。我们还加配了一件华味鸟，华味鸟采用来自九州养殖场的鸡只，饲养方法较天然。还要了一个豆腐雪糕做甜品。</p>
<p>由于天妇罗是即叫即炸，保证新鲜，因此需要等约15分钟。先上来是两个天妇罗冷面定食，天妇罗冷面定食款式配菜是固定的，每款均有两只黑虎虾、蟹柳、南瓜、莲藕和四季豆。配冷面的酱油混合wasabi和葱粒，入口不会辛辣还很醒胃。荞麦面和乌冬烟韧好吃。天妇罗的炸粉不会太厚，南瓜香甜。另加的华味鸟（天妇罗鸡）入口嫩滑，可惜没什么鸡味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11112487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230251366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112487" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230251366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112487" class="wp-caption-text">天妇罗乌冬定食。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11112492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231501366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112492" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231501366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112492" class="wp-caption-text">天妇罗荞麦面定食。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_11112505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112505" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232271366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112505" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232271366-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112505" class="wp-caption-text">华味鸟入口嫩滑，可惜没什么鸡味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ebexlp3377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%98%9F%E5%A4%A9%E4%B8%BC.md#1">巨星天丼</a>、海老穴子天丼和浅草海老天丼的天妇罗款式种类都不同。我特别喜欢巨星天丼里的炸紫菜，紫菜沾上一些饭和天妇罗粉后再炸，炸得香脆可口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11112518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232211366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112518" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232211366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112518" class="wp-caption-text">巨星天丼有青森县帆立贝、黑虎虾、鱿鱼等多款海鲜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海老穴子天丼里的穴子，是鳗鱼的一个品种，味道却不像平时食的鳗鱼，颇特别。浅草海老天丼里有三只炸虾，适合喜欢虾的朋友。特别欣赏他们的饭，珍珠米粒粒饱满，入口烟韧。每张台上都有一樽餐厅的自家酱油，让客人按自己的喜好添加，再加上七味粉好吃得没话说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11112507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112507" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230391366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112507" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140230391366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112507" class="wp-caption-text">海老穴子天丼加配华味鸟。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11112509" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231201366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112509" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231201366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112509" class="wp-caption-text">穴子味道不像平时食的鳗鱼，颇特别。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11112521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112521" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140232081366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112521" class="wp-caption-text">浅草海老天丼里有三只炸虾，适合喜欢虾的朋友。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后是豆腐雪糕，配上日本小蕃薯和红豆，豆腐雪糕有香浓的豆腐味但不甜，伴着香甜的红豆和蕃薯吃刚刚好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11112524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11112524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231371366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11112524" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903140231371366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11112524" class="wp-caption-text">豆腐雪糕有香浓的豆腐味但不甜，伴着香甜的红豆和蕃薯吃刚刚好。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里堂需要食加一。最后不得不提一提这里的外卖盒，他们的外卖盒是两层的，让饭面与酱油都可以分隔开，很贴心，抵赞！#</p>
