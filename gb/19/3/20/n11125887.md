<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2019年温哥华国际汽车展 全新体验</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/080_Alfa_Romeo_Stelivo_Q-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Alfa Romeo Stelvio Quadrifoglio。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者李奥报导）2019年的<ahref="https://github.com/aknrvm2933/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md">温哥华</a>国际<ahref="https://github.com/aknrvm2933/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md">车展</a>，已踏入第99届，今年车展展出的车辆可观性更胜旧年，而车展期间正是本地春假，正好作为家庭活动之一。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/aknrvm2933/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md">温哥华</a>国际汽<ahref="https://github.com/aknrvm2933/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md">车展</a>一如以往数年，位于温哥华市中心的温哥华会议展览中心（Vancouver Convention Center）西翼举行，一连六天，从3月19日至3月24日开放予公众参观。今年车展有超过400辆新车展出，包括各国品牌汽车、电动车、卡车、概念车及超级跑车。此外，参观者更可到场外试驾部分汽车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/005_GCOTY-e1553045465979.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125901 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/005_GCOTY-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Nissan Leaf夺得年度环保汽车奖。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/006_GTOTY-e1553045541371.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125899 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/006_GTOTY-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Jaguar I-PACE夺得年度环保多用途汽车奖。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上率先安排予传媒参观，并由各家厂商代表介绍新车及概念车。一如既往，首先是由AJAC颁发2019年度环保汽车及2019年度环保多用途汽车两项大奖，入选汽车包括Honda Clarity PHEV,Honda Insight及Nissan Leaf，最后由Nissan Leaf获得。而入选多用途汽车则有Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid,Jaguar I-PACE及Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV，最终由Jaguar I-PACE夺得此殊荣，而I-PACE在之前已获得了2019年多用途车奖（2019 Canadian Utility Vehicle of the Year）及最佳高级电动车奖（Best Premium EV in Canada）。</p>
<p>Subaru Canada介绍了全新的2020年款Subaru Legacy，新Legacy将会有两种引擎选择，包括2.5升四缸Boxer引擎及3.6升六缸Boxer引擎。两款均有标配Symmetric AWD四驱系统及Lineartronic CVT变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/011_Subaru_Legacy-e1553045774351.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125923 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/011_Subaru_Legacy-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Subaru Legacy。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>Genesis Canada表示他们的创新网上销售方式，从试车到买车，再到定期维护服务，均会有专人到客人家，得到广泛肯定，在新一年Genesis将会推出新车，包括SUV/CUV车款。而在车展中，Genesis则发表了G90概念车，概念车采用全新钻石形进风口设计，车内设施更是豪华高级。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/014_Genesis_G90_Concept-e1553045840508.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125927 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/014_Genesis_G90_Concept-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Genesis G90概念车。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lexus Canada的总裁Robert Tsang表示，在Lexus推出了UX车系之后，Lexus的销售量连连创新高，同时在车展上发布了Lexus IS300 F-Sport Black Line特别版及LC500 Inspiration特别版，IS300 Black Line特别版全车均是采用黑色，而且是经日本专人配对不同黑色，达到最佳效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/021_Lexus_IS300_Black_Line-e1553045903717.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125931 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/021_Lexus_IS300_Black_Line-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Lexus IS300 Black Line。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/023_Lexus_RC500-e1553045952101.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125932 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/023_Lexus_RC500-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Lexus RC500 Inspiration Series。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>加拿大的汽车品牌Felino，由加拿大赛车手Antoine Bessette创办，以Montreal为基地，制造充满跑感的超级跑车。在车展中发布了cB7R超级跑车，外形富有蝙蝠车（Batmobile）的味道，cB7R采用7升V8引擎，马力输出700匹，扭矩580磅呎，只会限量制造10辆在全球销售，定价为275,000美元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/042_Felino_cB7R-1-e1553046066290.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125938 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/042_Felino_cB7R-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Felino cB7R。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/047_Felino_cB7R-e1553046124519.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125941 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/047_Felino_cB7R-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Felino cB7R驾驶舱。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>PFAFF Mclaren在温哥华车展上展出了Mclaren Senna，是第一次在加拿大西部展出该车，Mclaren Senna在全球只有500辆，采用4升V8双涡轮增压引擎，马力输出达800匹，极速335公里/时，剩车重约为1,200公斤，0-200公里/时加速只需6.8秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/034_Mclaren_Senna-e1553046185988.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125942 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/034_Mclaren_Senna-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Mclaren Senna〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/035_Mclaren_Senna-e1553046225855.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125943 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/035_Mclaren_Senna-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Mclaren Senna驾驶舱。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，温哥华国际车展中也有很多其它值得一看的新车，包括Lamborgini Gallard LP570-4,Lamborgini Urus,Ferrari 488及其它超级跑车。最新的SUV／CUV则有Alfa Romeo Stelvio Quadrifoglio,Audi Q8,Lincoln Aviator,Ford Explorer ST,Jeep Gladiator,Kia Telluride及Hyundai Palisade，Nissan展出的由电动车Leaf改装成跑车的Leaf Nismo RC与Toyota最新的Supra都是绝对要看一看的。#</p>
