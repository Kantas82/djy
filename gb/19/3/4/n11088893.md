<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第24届“多伦多之夜”新春联欢圆满落幕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1588cb6a6e17c1c5_ttl7dayjhe_DSC_0510-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第24届“多伦多之夜”新春联欢晚会圆满举行，吸引 700位嘉宾出席盛会。（伊铃/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月05日讯】（大纪元伊铃报导）<ahref="https://github.com/bvyxbc327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">多伦多台湾商会</a>(TMAT) 年度盛事——第24届“<ahref="https://github.com/bvyxbc327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E4%B9%8B%E5%A4%9C.md#1">多伦多之夜</a>”新春联欢晚会，于3月2日晚在Markham Event Center登场，加拿大政界、商界、侨界共 700名嘉宾出席了盛会。</p>
<p>出席当天晚会的嘉宾包括：驻加拿大经济文化代表处代表陈文仪、驻多伦多台北经文处处长徐咏梅、聨邦国会议员陈圣源、叶嘉丽、Robert Oliphant、马万里(Majid Jowhari)；安省省议员韦邱佩芳、彭锦威（Billy Pan）、白必勤(Aris Babikian)；多伦多市副市长黄旻南、万锦市长薛家平、区议员李国贤等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11088907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11088907" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1588cb5a70abb915_ttl7dayaiJ_DSC_0436.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11088907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1588cb5a70abb915_ttl7dayaiJ_DSC_0436-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11088907" class="wp-caption-text">驻加拿大台北经济文化代表陈文仪致辞。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11089825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11089825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0415.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11089825" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0415-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11089825" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bvyxbc327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">多伦多台湾商会</a>会长郉增辉致辞。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会还为三家受捐的慈善机构捐款，其中士嘉堡综合医院获5,000元、华人肾病基金会获3,000元、Home on the Hill 获3,000元。另外，主办方给海外三位学生颁发奖学金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11088912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11088912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1588cb6d32301dfd_ttl7dayMwt_DSC_0531.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11088912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1588cb6d32301dfd_ttl7dayMwt_DSC_0531-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11088912" class="wp-caption-text">大会为慈善机构捐款。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11089826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11089826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0527.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11089826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0527-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11089826" class="wp-caption-text">大会为慈善机构捐款。（伊铃/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长郉增辉致辞，感谢所有的来宾及赞助商的支持，以及在2年的任期中，各台商精英的信任，有机会服务商会。他希望未来台湾商会能继续秉承他的理念，加强商会与主流社会商界、政界及各族裔社区的交流，将商会推上更高一层楼。</p>
<p>当天的晚会为来宾提供丰富的美食及精彩的娱乐节目，并设幸运抽奖为来宾助兴。据郉会长介绍，现场来宾热情高涨，晚会所有义卖品、奖卷销售一空，现场共筹现金8,000多元。整个晚会在温馨、热闹的气氛中圆满落幕。</p>
