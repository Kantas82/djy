<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：故乡病了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/1512160910291770-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>环保署28日分析过去3年的细悬浮微粒数据（PM2.5），全台湾只有台东仍符合空品标准，其他PM2.5年度平均值都超过标准值。（大纪元档案照片）（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>在我的记忆深处<br />
我的故乡<br />
山明水秀<br />
天青气朗<br />
阳光普照<br />
从小，我就在这里<br />
尽情玩耍、奔跑、蹦跳、上学、工作<br />
逐日长大<br />
那是我非常喜爱的地方</p>
<p>世事沧桑，我迁居他乡<br />
几十年后回来<br />
所看到的故乡却也沧海了：<br />
山明水秀哪里去了？<br />
天青气朗哪里去了？<br />
阳光普照哪里去了？<br />
张眼看去<br />
空间几乎是灰濛濛的<br />
许多人戴着口罩<br />
许多人不敢大口呼吸<br />
以前的绿野不再那么鲜绿了</p>
<p>探究再探究<br />
原来这几十年来<br />
从云嘉南高雄等地<br />
还有境外<br />
飘来许多尘霾<br />
这些有毒的气体<br />
为大武山所阻，便落在这里<br />
故乡因此病了<br />
而且病得不轻<br />
所有的空间、景物都变了样了<br />
常常多了一层灰<br />
不止灰濛濛，看不清晰<br />
有人甚至因此罹癌、肺部受害<br />
这怎么是我的故乡？<br />
难怪在外地常听到<br />
有人说台语时把我的故乡的潮字读成潮流的音<br />
唉，连故乡名字的语音都变了<br />
不，都病了</p>
<p>这是我的故乡吗？<br />
至于我是否也和以前一样？<br />
今早我探首镜子<br />
猛然发现，原来<br />
我也老了<br />
嗯，我也老了<br />
世事沧桑！</p>
<p><strong> My home town is ill</strong> ?Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>In the depth of my memory<br />
My home town is<br />
Green hills and clear waters<br />
Blue in sky and fresh in air<br />
Glittering generality<br />
From my childhood, I<br />
Play, run, leap, go to school and work here<br />
Grow day after day<br />
It’s the place I love very much</p>
<p>However, the vicissitude of life, I move to strange land<br />
Come back after a few years<br />
My home town I saw is also vicissitude:<br />
Where is green hills and clear water?<br />
Where is blue sky and fresh air?<br />
Where is glittering generality?<br />
I open my eyes to see<br />
The space is almost dim cover with grey<br />
Many people wear respirator<br />
Many people dare not free to breathe<br />
The green field isn’t such green and fresh as before</p>
<p>I search and search<br />
It turns out these few years<br />
The smog that waft from<br />
Yunlin and Chiayi and Tainan and Kaohsiung etc.<br />
And addition to waft from abroad<br />
These poison gas<br />
Be blocked by Dawu Shan, all falls on there<br />
So that my home town is ill<br />
And its illness is not at small in degree<br />
All space and sights are changed<br />
It often more has a layer of dust<br />
No only gloomy, can’t see anything clearly<br />
Someone even has ill of cancer, and harm of lung<br />
How can it be my home town?<br />
Not to blame that I often hear on abroad that<br />
Some one will sound the current Dao into De of my hometown ChaoChou in language of Taiwan<br />
Ah, even the tone of my home town is changed<br />
Yes, all is ill</p>
<p>Is this my home town?<br />
As to me, am I the same of before?<br />
When I look in front of the mirror<br />
I find suddenly,<br />
I’m old also originally<br />
En, I’m old also<br />
It’s the vicissitude of life!@</p>
