<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>EXO访台庆功会 700名额3分钟即抢空</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155231487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《EXO的爬梯子世界旅行第二季》拍摄画面。（LINE TV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月06日讯】由韩国男团EXO主持的<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9E%E5%A2%83%E8%8A%82%E7%9B%AE.md#1">实境节目</a>《<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/exo%E7%9A%84%E7%88%AC%E6%A2%AF%E5%AD%90%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C%E7%AC%AC%E4%BA%8C%E5%AD%A3.md#1">EXO的爬梯子世界旅行第二季</a>》近来在LINE TV台湾中字独播后，获得热烈回响。Suho与Kai两位成员将在3月30日出席“TAIWAN LINE TV”<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%8A%9F.md#1">庆功</a>记者会并与开放700位粉丝同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11092604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11092604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155181487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11092604" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155181487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11092604" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/exo%E7%9A%84%E7%88%AC%E6%A2%AF%E5%AD%90%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C%E7%AC%AC%E4%BA%8C%E5%AD%A3.md#1">EXO的爬梯子世界旅行第二季</a>》拍摄画面。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昨天（5日）开放报名后，短短3分钟内涌入超过700位粉丝报名。据主办方LINE TV透露，记者会当天预计会有精彩片花重温、访问EXO成员，并由他们亲自分享拍摄花絮，让粉丝近距离亲见两位的风采。</p>
<p>同时<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%8A%9F.md#1">庆功</a>会上，还有机会邀请幸运粉丝上台获得Suho、Kai亲签海报及大合照，让粉丝们都相当期待。有关庆功记者会的资讯，可上官网、LINE TV粉丝团动态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11092606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11092606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155061487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11092606" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155061487-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11092606" class="wp-caption-text">《EXO的<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%AC%E6%A2%AF%E5%AD%90.md#1">爬梯子</a>世界旅行第二季》拍摄画面。图为成员Kai。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《EXO的<ahref="https://github.com/vvzrfn3290/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%AC%E6%A2%AF%E5%AD%90.md#1">爬梯子</a>世界旅行第二季》来到南台湾高雄、垦丁，在拍摄时就引发热烈讨论，他们品尝南台湾美食与乐游南台湾，在LINE TV台湾中字独播后，不仅打破该平台综艺馆观看流量纪录，并连续5周综艺排行第一名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11092608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11092608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155001487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11092608" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1903060155001487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11092608" class="wp-caption-text">《EXO的爬梯子世界旅行第二季》拍摄画面。图为成员Suho。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
