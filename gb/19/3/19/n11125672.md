<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中美贸易战 或为台湾水产业转机</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-1Seafood-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾冷冻水产公会携20家厂商参加波士顿北美海产展（Seafood Expo North America）。图为台湾展区，“台湾青”主题展版与众不同。（刘景烨／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）<span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">17</span><span class="s2">日，北美最大的<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E4%BA%A7%E5%B1%95.md#1">海产展</a>（</span><span class="s1">Seafood Expo North America）</span><span class="s2">在波士顿会展中心开幕。在为期</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">天的展览中，来自</span><span class="s1">50</span><span class="s2">多个国家的</span><span class="s1">1,000</span><span class="s2">多家厂商到场展出它们经营的水产食品及水产品加工设备。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">今年，台湾冷冻水产公会携</span><span class="s1">20</span><span class="s2">家台湾厂商参展。公会总干事吴姿蓉及理事长陆根田表示，<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%BE%8E%E8%B4%B8%E6%98%93.md#1">中美贸易</a>战或为<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%B0%B4%E4%BA%A7.md#1">台湾水产</a>业的契机，一些<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86.md#1">台商</a>已经看到欧美老客户有回归趋势，希望继续以优质产品，赢回被中国大陆以低价方式拉走的订单。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11125705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-2Seafood.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125705 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-2Seafood-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125705" class="wp-caption-text">陆根田是冷冻水产公会理事长，也是兰扬食品公司的董事长。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">16</span><span class="s2">日中午，驻波士顿台北经济文化办事处处长徐佑典在华埠龙凤酒家设宴欢迎参加和观摩<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E4%BA%A7%E5%B1%95.md#1">海产展</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86.md#1">台商</a>团队。吴姿蓉谈到，公会今年特请专业设计师设计台湾展区招牌，以“台湾青”为主题色调，希望让来宾一眼就看到台湾馆的位置。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11125707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-3Seafood.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11125707" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-3Seafood-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125707" class="wp-caption-text">经文处设宴招待参展厂商。左二：波克莱台商会会长杨罗东；左四：大波士顿台商会会长李以蕙；她右边是冷冻水产公会总干事吴姿蓉，经文处处长徐佑典，公会理事长陆根田；其余为今年新加入展商代表。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">“（</span><span class="s2">中美）</span><span class="s2">贸易战对我们来说是个机会，有可能订单会转移到我们这边。”吴姿蓉说。她说，过去因为客户流入中国大陆，有些台商便不再参加海产展；但今年，有曾退出展览的企业重新参展，原因是他们接到流失老客户的咨询。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">吴姿蓉表示，与中国大陆产品相比，台湾的水产品虽然价格较高，但是质量优越，卫生有保障。例如，台湾出口到日本的鳗鱼，口感、品质不输日本当地的鳗鱼。她还谈到，除了“樱花虾”等台湾特色水产外，台商的一大优势在于加工食品。她认为，加工食品容易烹调，适合家庭日常消费。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11125711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-7Seafood.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125711 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-7Seafood-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125711" class="wp-caption-text">台商带来鱼丸、花枝丸、海味煎饼等加工食品参展。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">陆根田是冷冻水产公会理事长，也是兰扬食品公司的董事长。他表示，兰扬公司的订单最近增加不少，预计不久后，北美地区的订单数量能增加</span><span class="s1">20%</span><span class="s2">到</span><span class="s1">30%</span><span class="s2">；同时，公会许多会员的订单量都有所增加。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">陆根田还谈到，如果产品的品质、卫生和包装优秀，即便价格比中国大陆、越南、泰国等地略高，也能吸引客户。他举例说，去年参展后，有客户帮助台商把产品卖到了</span><span class="s1">Costco</span><span class="s2">超市。</span></p>
<p class="p1">他还谈到，历年参展过程中，他见证了各国企业在产品创新、品质、卫生、包装等方面的不断进步，这也激励着台湾企业不断改进产品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-5Seafood.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125709 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-5Seafood-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125709" class="wp-caption-text">厨师在展馆内烹调水产，供来宾们免费品尝。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-6Seafood.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11125710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-6Seafood-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125710" class="wp-caption-text">北美海产展“鲜”味十足；不知是烟薰鱼，还是鱼薰烟？（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">今年，兰阳食品公司的主要展品是日式水产小吃。多个台湾展商也带来冷冻水产，或鱼丸、鱼豆腐等加工水产参展。“阿中丸子”的鱼丸内藏蛋黄夹心，油炸过后，入口弹性十足，内馅香味四溢。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">陆根田说，美国是<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%B0%B4%E4%BA%A7.md#1">台湾水产</a>品的第</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">大出口地，他希望通过这个展览，“把好的品质介绍到北美”。◇</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11125708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11125708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-4Seafood.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11125708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1-1-4Seafood-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11125708" class="wp-caption-text">多个台湾展商带来加工水产参展；“阿中丸子”的鱼丸内藏蛋黄夹心，油炸过后，弹性可口。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
