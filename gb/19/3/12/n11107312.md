<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宜兰扶轮社获全球奖助金计划捐赠宜兰县</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/e9f2cbb18012f1340144bef57442d2b9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>国际扶轮3490地区宜兰扶轮社捐赠“宜兰县社区式认知症极早期筛检诊断服务计划经费”捐赠典礼活动礼成大合照。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年03月12日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9C%E5%85%B0%E5%8E%BF%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C.md#1">宜兰县政府</a>卫生局于3月12日于县民大厅，举办“宜兰县社区式认知症极早期筛检诊断服务计划经费”捐赠仪式。为感谢国际扶轮热心公益，借此县府与县内各大医疗院所共同协力提升宜兰县<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B1%E6%99%BA%E7%97%87.md#1">失智症</a>照护能力，并肯定投入在地社会服务及专业医疗照护之形象，持续投入多元失智症照护服务。</p>
<p>依据2018年国际<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B1%E6%99%BA%E7%97%87.md#1">失智症</a>协会（ADI）资料，推估2018年全球新增1千万名失智症患者，平均每3秒就有一人罹患失智症，但却有将近7成以上之失智老人未被发现，显示社区长者于长期照护及失智关怀上之高度需求。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11107326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11107326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/9995912b7d3291d360165d75ee886159-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11107326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/9995912b7d3291d360165d75ee886159-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11107326" class="wp-caption-text">宜兰县县长林姿妙与捐赠单位启动健康幸福种子。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E7%A4%BE.md#1">扶轮社</a>社长陈致舟表示，有感于政府预算有限，更看到偏乡资源不足，透过本项全球奖助金计划办理“宜兰县社区式认知症极早期筛检诊断服务”，于社区中筛选8,000位65岁以上长者，达成早期发现、早期治疗，不但减轻照顾者的负担，同时减少医疗照护费用的支出。陈社长表示会持续的做下去。这项奖助计划案是由宜兰扶轮社提出，获得国际扶轮总社、国际扶轮3490地区宜兰扶轮社及3310地区支持之全球奖助金计划案＃GG1876494经费计新台币148万元赠与<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9C%E5%85%B0%E5%8E%BF%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C.md#1">宜兰县政府</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11107324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11107324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/44657f39e346145fd3ca627e27e934b5-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11107324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/44657f39e346145fd3ca627e27e934b5-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11107324" class="wp-caption-text">宜兰县县长林姿妙与捐赠单位。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰县卫生局透过本计划帮助县内偏乡资源不足区、有特殊需求之民众获得极早期认知筛检诊断服务，让宜兰县失智症民众及家属极早接受相关专业服务及家庭支持，减轻家庭负担及社会成本。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11107322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11107322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/b3b2555b8e9aab51cf78f9535e4de2ba-450x302.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11107322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/b3b2555b8e9aab51cf78f9535e4de2ba-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11107322" class="wp-caption-text">宜兰县县长林姿妙致赠感谢状给捐赠单位宜兰<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E7%A4%BE.md#1">扶轮社</a>。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林姿妙县长表示，感谢国际扶轮总社、国际扶轮3490地区及3310地区积极投入社会服务、慷慨解囊，结合关怀弱势理念，共同提升社会大众对社会公益的重视，另感谢县内各大医疗院所及卫生所一直以来坚守岗位、用专业与爱守护我们的宜兰乡亲，相信以公部门结合民间公益力量及本县专业医疗群，能将资源更有效运用，提升本县失智症照护服务的普及性、有效性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11107318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11107318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0592baf230617453aa6fbf1691a270fa-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11107318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0592baf230617453aa6fbf1691a270fa-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11107318" class="wp-caption-text">捐赠仪式_国际扶轮3490地区宜兰扶轮社全球奖助金计划案，宜兰县长代表受赠。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11107316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11107316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/3104710ed9213725eb260302c8053a4e-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11107316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/3104710ed9213725eb260302c8053a4e-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11107316" class="wp-caption-text">平均年龄85岁以上德仁日照中心不老顽童海带舞迎宾表演。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
