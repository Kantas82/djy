<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>感恩节长周末 嘉甸拿高速公路关闭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/shutterstock_151039505-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>本周感恩节长周末，嘉甸拿高速公路因维护工作关闭。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p class="tgt" data-section="0">【大纪元2019年10月07日讯】（大纪元记者楚方明<ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>编译报导）<span class="tgt" data-section="0" data-sentence="0" data-group="0-0"><ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%81%A9%E8%8A%82.md#1">感恩节</a>长周末期间</span>，<span class="tgt" data-section="0" data-sentence="0" data-group="0-0"><ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E7%94%B8%E6%8B%BF.md#1">嘉甸拿</a><ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF.md#1">高速公路</a>因维护<ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E9%97%AD.md#1">关闭</a>。</span></p>
<p class="tgt" data-section="2"><span class="tgt" data-section="2" data-sentence="0" data-group="2-0"><ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>市府表示，从周六（10月12日）凌晨12点01分到周一凌晨5点，427号/QEW<ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF.md#1">高速公路</a>和DVP高速公路之间的<ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E7%94%B8%E6%8B%BF.md#1">嘉甸拿</a>双向车道都将<ahref="https://github.com/dhfknp3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B3%E9%97%AD.md#1">关闭</a>。</span></p>
<p class="tgt" data-section="4"><span class="tgt highlight" data-section="4" data-sentence="0" data-group="4-0">市府在新闻公布中表示:“在即将到来的冬季来临之前，工作人员将对嘉甸拿做重要的维护和改善，以让该高速公路保持最佳状态。”</span></p>
<p class="tgt" data-section="6"><span class="tgt" data-section="6" data-sentence="0" data-group="6-0">维护工作包括道路划线、空中及地面标志检查、坑洞及裂缝修补、涂鸦清除及路灯维修等。</span></p>
<p class="tgt" data-section="6">责任编辑：文芳</p>
