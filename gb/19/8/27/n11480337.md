<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>礁溪温泉马拉松12/7开跑 林昀儒力挺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/c6198b04abaa9edc7c9b29ecb02eec26-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>出国比赛林昀儒会特别注意饮食。他说：平时训练的时候也会到龙潭湖跑步。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月27日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）全台唯一以温泉为主题的“2019礁溪温泉杯樱花陵园马拉松嘉年华”今年12月7日（六）在礁溪国小开跑，礁溪乡公所27日举行活动记者会，其中有“桌球神童”之称的宜兰子弟林昀儒特别到场力挺，为“礁溪温泉马拉松”宣传，号召民众参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11480339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/4f8acd6a1b4b64a82eebd2f7e8f77d96.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/4f8acd6a1b4b64a82eebd2f7e8f77d96-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480339" class="wp-caption-text">左起礁溪乡长张永德、桌球神童 林昀儒、宜兰县教育处长王泓翔。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/98a61fa5eb62a720f1b074b92757cfb9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/98a61fa5eb62a720f1b074b92757cfb9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480345" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC%E5%85%AB%E5%B1%8A%E7%A4%81%E6%BA%AA%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">第八届礁溪温泉马拉松</a>12月7日开跑。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本月刚满 18岁的桌球好手林昀儒是台湾最年轻的世界桌球锦标赛国手之一，目前排名世界第 13名，被誉为台湾20年来最强选手，有机会挑战明年的东京奥运。林昀儒本周才刚结束捷克桌球公开赛，就立刻返回宜兰出席礁溪马拉松活动宣传，希望带动在地运动风气。乡长张永德表示，林昀儒喜欢打桌球也为自己打球，比赛不是为了得奖，最主要是他会沉浸在这个运动的范围里面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11480341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/bc5d596c06e3c6d9ee7ae62ea8e7dadc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/bc5d596c06e3c6d9ee7ae62ea8e7dadc-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480341" class="wp-caption-text">林昀儒与宜兰县教育处长王泓翔。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/d637242b9024ba2ac580efc92966156d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/d637242b9024ba2ac580efc92966156d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480342" class="wp-caption-text">林昀儒为粉丝签名。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>礁溪乡长张永德表示，考量跑者来自全台各地，今年在台中、新竹、桃园与台北等人口密集区都设置接驳专车，跑者可选购接驳专车到礁溪会场，礁溪乡拥有优美的自然风光与全国唯一的平地温泉，跑者完赛后可搭免费接驳车到礁溪火车站、礁溪地景广场、礁溪转运站以及汤围沟温泉公园等站体验在地观光热点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11480343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/ec133e365eacfd825ad91144b0f4f69d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/ec133e365eacfd825ad91144b0f4f69d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480343" class="wp-caption-text">礁溪乡长张永德特别为林昀儒选的号码（生日）。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11480346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11480346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/6ae50d7ca923e34daee8dce67bf8d901.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11480346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/6ae50d7ca923e34daee8dce67bf8d901-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11480346" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AC%AC%E5%85%AB%E5%B1%8A%E7%A4%81%E6%BA%AA%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89%E9%A9%AC%E6%8B%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">第八届礁溪温泉马拉松</a>12月7日开跑。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张永德表示，今年赛事预计吸引海外跑者加入，报名页提供英文与日文，服务海外的跑友，也有三天两夜与四天三夜行的交通住宿加购行程，要让跑者透过马拉松更认识台湾。</p>
<p>本次活动纪念品有以宝特瓶与咖啡渣制成的环保机能除臭咖啡纱纪念衫，也提供全马与半马组跑者运动风衣，风衣为冰丝材质，含湿率符合人体皮肤的生理需要，具有光滑凉爽、透气、抗静电、防紫外线等特点，适合运动时使用。活动资讯请至Facebook【礁溪温泉马拉松】【真爱趣礁溪】粉丝专页、礁溪乡观光旅游网http://www.jiaoxi-tourism.tw/Portal/Default.aspx查询。◇</p>
