<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“2019多伦多侨民青少年夏令营”成果丰硕</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年08月08日讯】由<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">加东中文学校联合会</a>主办的“2019年<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E4%BE%A8%E6%B0%91%E9%9D%92%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E8%90%A5.md#1">多伦多侨民青少年夏令营</a>”，于7月29日至8月2日在驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处文化中心举行。</p>
<p>侨务委员会遴派民俗体育老师萧鸿伟前来巡回教学，计有30位6岁至15岁的儿童及青少年学员参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438178" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/fcefaaf066b26e45c78939f9c45179a8" rel="attachment wp-att-11438178"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11438178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/fcefaaf066b26e45c78939f9c45179a8-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438178" class="wp-caption-text">高年级学员扯铃表演。</figcaption></figure>
<p>一周有趣又具台湾特色的扯铃课程，经萧鸿伟编排动作，将台湾意象及技巧融入教学，包括扯铃单铃花式、双铃花式、抛扯铃技巧、跳绳等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/0cb25d41aaae0066d92f3ec0a155c1dc" rel="attachment wp-att-11438149"><img class="wp-image-11438149 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/0cb25d41aaae0066d92f3ec0a155c1dc-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438149" class="wp-caption-text">女学员扯铃表演。</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自国立金门大学三位志工学生协助教学，寓教于乐，学员们热切投入，认真学习。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438188" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/607e0ddc69cb2bfeb63f2221a040407c" rel="attachment wp-att-11438188"><img class="wp-image-11438188 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/607e0ddc69cb2bfeb63f2221a040407c-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438188" class="wp-caption-text">美工剪纸装饰及布置艺术课程，学员课堂上认真学习一隅。</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动期间，多伦多天主教教育局第8区教育委员Gary Tanuan前来中心拜会，就夏令营运作及活动情形，与主办单位理事长王元培及孙国祥、董至中交换意见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/862508bab53189b0b25937af020bf236" rel="attachment wp-att-11438174"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11438174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/862508bab53189b0b25937af020bf236-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438174" class="wp-caption-text">Gary Tanuan(左二)拜会中心之际，与中心主任孙国祥(右二)、副主任董至中(右一)、<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">加东中文学校联合会</a>理事长王元培(左一)、文化老师萧鸿伟(右三)合照。</figcaption></figure>
<p>结业式展演学习成果，纯熟的技艺，赢得家长热烈掌声与肯定。萧鸿伟担任压轴表演，精湛的技艺，更令观众赞叹不已。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438186" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/3ec1c699b09c79e92fa8d9d9249d8e08" rel="attachment wp-att-11438186"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11438186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/3ec1c699b09c79e92fa8d9d9249d8e08-450x181.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="181" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438186" class="wp-caption-text">学员以“四季花园”为主题的美工剪纸装饰及布置艺术课程成果展现。</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化中心大厅展示学员手工艺、书法及国画作品，今年以“四季花园”为主题，运用美工剪纸等手工艺作品装饰，展现花园的繁富绚丽，获得来宾及家长一致的赞赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11438201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11438201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/19/8/7/n11438143.md#1/0c9edf4cd8772e9727952ae93dd2d08f" rel="attachment wp-att-11438201"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11438201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/0c9edf4cd8772e9727952ae93dd2d08f-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11438201" class="wp-caption-text">全体学员合照。（主办方提供文图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化中心主任孙国祥、副主任董至中应邀出席了8月2日下午四时举行的结业成果展。孙国祥致词感谢老师及志工一周来的热忱教学与服务，期勉老师及家长继续传承传统中华文化，教导学习正体字，更希望小朋友明年持续报名参加。</p>
