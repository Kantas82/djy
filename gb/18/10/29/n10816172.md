<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鸡胸肉怎么煮最嫩？5种瘦身食材处理妙招</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/chicken-breast2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鸡胸肉怎么煮最嫩？教你五种常用瘦身食材的处理妙招。（Shutterstock/大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>编者按：<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>怎么煮最嫩？水果用水泡多久再洗更干净？你会清理<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BF%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱿鱼</a>吗？来看五种常用瘦身食材的处理妙招！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">嫩煮<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a></span></h3>
<p>鸡胸肉是减重食谱常用到的食材，一次不用煮太多，大约1～2片即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%AE%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">煮鸡胸肉</a>过程，淋上1汤匙清酒，然后煮约6分钟即可关火，让鸡胸肉在锅内闷个10分钟，以增加嫩度。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">清洗水果</span></h3>
<p>能吃到新鲜、洗干净的苹果、香瓜、梨子等水果，是减重时最愉快的时刻。建议洗水果时，除了挑选专用的果类洗洁精，也可把水果浸泡在生水里，约5～6分钟，接着再好好清洗水果的外皮。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">煮出好吃的水煮蛋</span></h3>
<p>看似简单的料理，却大有学问。如果想吃半熟的水煮蛋，只要把蛋放入锅里，煮5～7分钟；如果想吃全熟的鸡蛋，得花12分钟。</p>
<p>此外，这里透露一个小技巧给各位，就是把水煮蛋捞出后，放在窗户旁让冷风吹一阵子后再拨蛋壳，会快速方便很多。当然，有人吃水煮蛋时喜欢沾点盐，别忘了选择低钠盐喔！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">轻松切<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10816210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10816210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/luoli11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10816210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/luoli11-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10816210" class="wp-caption-text">切<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>步骤1～2。（图/三采文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 用刀子抓好切入角度与深度，然后前后切开酪梨。</p>
<p>2. 接着用双手来转开酪梨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10816211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10816211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/luoli22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10816211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/luoli22-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10816211" class="wp-caption-text">切酪梨步骤3～4。（图/三采文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再用刀子摘除里面的籽。</p>
<p>4. 之后再慢慢削去酪梨外皮或用汤匙挖果肉。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;">干净地清理<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BF%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱿鱼</a></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10816212" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10816212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/squid11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10816212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/squid11-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10816212" class="wp-caption-text">处理鱿鱼步骤1～2。（图/三采文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 用剪刀剪开鱿鱼。</p>
<p>2. 接着把鱿鱼摊开，清除内脏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10816213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10816213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/squid44.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10816213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/squid44-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10816213" class="wp-caption-text">处理鱿鱼步骤3～4。（图/三采文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用剪刀剪下鱿鱼脚。</p>
<p>4. 最后清除鱿鱼嘴及四周的杂质。</p>
