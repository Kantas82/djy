<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>就是不想动 小狗狗秒变液体滑下沙发</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10615386 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月17日讯】养<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>的你，是否从宠物还是宝宝的时候就开始照顾它呢？每天看着宠物宝宝“一眠大一寸”，原本瘦弱柔软的小生命，日益健康、茁壮，内心的喜悦就跟当爸妈的成就感差不多！你最喜欢宠物宝宝的哪个时刻？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10838032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10838032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/0ffae5bd23bdad6a76d9ddc85031484f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10838032 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/0ffae5bd23bdad6a76d9ddc85031484f-450x806.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="806" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10838032" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>狗在沙发上躺得够久了，终于想到要下地走走。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很多人都喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>或其他小动物，来看看这只小狗做了什么可爱的举动吧！这只小<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>叫做Noor，目前10个月大，住在荷兰，家中有主人的2个孩子，还有一只前辈<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>。Noor从小就跟大家一起生活，感情要好也过得自在。</p>
<p>都说孩子的教育，身教重于言教，环境的影响也很重要，因此有“孟母三迁”的故事流传。而从Noor身上似乎也可以看见一些“耳濡目染”的端倪……</p>
<p>一般来说，养过猫咪的人知道，这种软绵绵又习惯性慵懒的生物，无论年纪是轻是长，都喜欢蜷缩在角落，或把自己塞进稍微拥挤一点的小容器，可能这样能有心灵慰藉，觉得很有安全感吧！</p>
<p>猫咪的这种特殊习性，也被人戏称说“猫咪其实是液体”，能适应各种各样的空间，也可以把自己塑造成各种各样的型态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10838035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10838035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a87090ddcaacb499a9c3050ceacda6e5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10838035 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a87090ddcaacb499a9c3050ceacda6e5-450x807.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="807" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10838035" class="wp-caption-text">但小家伙似乎还没完全醒，<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%87%92%E6%B4%8B%E6%B4%8B.md#1">懒洋洋</a>的不想动。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>回到影片的主角──小<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>Noor，午后时分，它<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%87%92%E6%B4%8B%E6%B4%8B.md#1">懒洋洋</a>地瘫在沙发上，享受微风和悠闲时光，然后，Noor想起了什么，觉得该下地走走，但它居然不像小狗一样欢蹦乱跳，反而学起“液体”猫咪！</p>
<p>Noor放松自己的小狗躯干，顺着沙发就这样“滑”到地上，感觉其实必须出点力气支撑才对，但它小家伙就是不愿意“出脚”，总之想要不费力气地从沙发上下来。原来不只猫咪可以是“液体”，跟猫咪朝夕相处的小狗也有成为“液体”的潜力！真是太逗了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10838041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10838041" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/acbed2ceee0a621339388bd1d7b40dea.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10838041 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/acbed2ceee0a621339388bd1d7b40dea-450x802.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="802" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10838041" class="wp-caption-text">小狗顺势“滑”到地上，拉长的样子好像麻糬呀！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10838042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10838042" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/dbd77c1f0b4137d354b561a6779de301.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10838042 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/dbd77c1f0b4137d354b561a6779de301-450x806.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="806" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10838042" class="wp-caption-text">终于下地了，结果还是躺着！狗狗到底还想睡多久呢？（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天真的小动物真是太可爱了！你家的<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>小宝贝是否也曾闹出笑话？或是做了一些让人摸不着头绪的有趣动作呢？欢迎与我们分享喔！</p>
