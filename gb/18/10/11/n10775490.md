<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>布里斯本办事处盛大举办107年双十酒会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/f1abedbbefbdc1b289cb5771f77877fe-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台北驻布里斯本经济文化办事处洪振荣处长伉俪贵宾们合影。（尼尔森／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月11日讯】驻布里斯本办事处于10月8日晚间举办驻地107年<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E5%8D%81.md#1">双十</a>酒会，昆士兰各界出席踊跃，宾客逾400人。酒会以道地台湾美食小吃飨宴嘉宾，另邀台商陈列台湾精品，推动台昆经贸交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10775517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/34f7abc75c186a7f2bbdbf8483a0aff4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775517 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/34f7abc75c186a7f2bbdbf8483a0aff4-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775517" class="wp-caption-text">10月8日晚间，400多位来宾在SUNPAC共同庆祝107年中华民国（台湾）<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E5%8D%81.md#1">双十</a>国庆酒会。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次酒会到场祝贺的贵宾包括<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%87%8C%E6%96%AF%E6%9C%AC%E5%B8%82%E9%95%BF.md#1">布里斯本市长</a>Graham Quirk、昆州发展厅长Cameron Dick、代表多元文化厅长的Peter Russo州议员、“自由国家党”影子多元文化厅长John-Paul Langbroek、州议员Brent Mickelberg、Jon Krause、联邦众议员Jim Chalmers、Graham Perrett等昆州三级政府政要，另有昆州政府官员、各地方市议员、商业界、学术界、诺鲁等领事团成员、以及侨界人士等，人数更胜于往年。</p>
<p>酒会一开始播放由中华民国外交部制作的“以人为本，幸福共好”影片，介绍台湾一年多来在科技创新、经济多元、文化融合、民主自由、活力社会、踏实外交及国际合作等政府重要施政与成就。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10775515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3997fd634c4e326b808354a7e78a0b76.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775515 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3997fd634c4e326b808354a7e78a0b76-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775515" class="wp-caption-text">洪振荣处长伉俪与来宾举杯共祝中华民国（台湾）国运昌隆。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10775520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775520" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/332bd2cc60d5b721fdc6ab2153c1b2bc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775520 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/332bd2cc60d5b721fdc6ab2153c1b2bc-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775520" class="wp-caption-text">布里斯本Graham Quirk市长（右5）、洪振荣处长（右4）和夫人（右6）、宋永福副处长（左2）及侨领们与外宾合影。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10775518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/55adbb96559791717ec49d476b108019.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775518 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/55adbb96559791717ec49d476b108019-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775518" class="wp-caption-text">客家民俗舞蹈团在国庆酒会上演出。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酒会主人驻布里斯本办事处洪振荣处长强调，蔡英文总统就任后对内提出经济创新，国家改革及强化国防，对外主张踏实外交，国际参与及新南向政策，目前业已逐渐展现成果，台湾经济荣景及国际支持都超越过往。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%87%8C%E6%96%AF%E6%9C%AC%E5%B8%82%E9%95%BF.md#1">布里斯本市长</a>Graham Quirk、昆州发展厅长Cameron Dick、州议员Peter Russo、联邦国会议员Jim Chalmers、Graham Perrett等政要在致词时除祝贺我国庆，并一致肯定台湾近年各项发展成就，是昆士兰及澳洲重要经贸伙伴，他们以台湾友人身份，祁愿台澳双方在既有基础上提升各项实质关系。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10775522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e2c9e31c1353171776bf5d8b08d5afe7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775522 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e2c9e31c1353171776bf5d8b08d5afe7-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775522" class="wp-caption-text">布市知名台菜餐馆“百家千味”提供酒会外烩及果雕展示。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻处今年再度邀请布市知名台菜餐馆提供酒会外烩，准备<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%81%E8%82%89%E9%A5%AD.md#1">鲁肉饭</a>、炒米粉、咸酥鸡、台式卤味及珍珠奶茶等道地台湾美食小吃，另外再次购买噶玛兰金奖威士忌供宾客品尝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10775519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/242947f7904f9604bc1c11ee48ac5235.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775519 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/242947f7904f9604bc1c11ee48ac5235-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775519" class="wp-caption-text">3家台商专程到国庆酒会共襄盛举。包括曾家石斑鱼、BeLEAF(相信叶子）&amp; Armigo（亚美果）公司。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10775521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10775521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2f9eb79abbd54ef0fc3100c9652931aa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10775521 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2f9eb79abbd54ef0fc3100c9652931aa-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10775521" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾们兴奋地前来了解台湾的优质产品。（尼尔森／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，也远从台湾邀请3家台商专程到国庆酒会现场陈列展品，向各界介绍台湾精品。养殖石斑鱼闻名的“曾家石斑鱼”公司在现场提供香煎石斑鱼，获得嘉宾一致的赞赏。另一家是BeLEAF（相信叶子）公司，在台湾培育了大面积的澳洲茶树，提炼100%天然的茶树精油，做成不易过敏的各种保养清洁用品。</p>
<p>还有一家Armigo（亚美果）公司, 是在国际创新发明竞赛中得到金奖的纯天然染髪剂和防皱纹的面模、眼膜。很多贵宾们兴奋地前来了解台湾的优质产品。</p>
