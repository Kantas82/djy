<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多充满创意的异国风味餐厅－五味厨房</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月24日讯】当一家大小出去吃饭时，可能老人喜欢中餐，大人喜欢西餐，小孩喜欢披萨，就算三，五好友想聚一聚，各人也是口味不同。什么餐馆能将这一切人的口味都满足呢？位于万锦市7500 Woodbine Ave的5味厨房餐馆就能让每个人都能吃到自己喜欢的菜肴。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/kgrmye347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E5%91%B3%E5%8E%A8%E6%88%BF.md#1">五味厨房</a>是一家很特殊的，能满足一家大小不同国家口味的餐馆。餐馆刚开业不久，有一对西人夫妇带着两个孩子到这里晚餐，本以为就像以前那样，孩子要什么就全家都吃什么，谁知道结果是，孩子选了意大利拉面，太太选了日本寿司，先生选了龙虾拉面。全家大小吃得其乐融融。</p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;"><a class="button alert radius" href="http://bit.ly/2OzjbCO">品尝多国风味美食：五味厨房</a></h2>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">订座热线：<a class="button success round" href="tel:+1(647)492-4088">(647) 492-4088</a></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/kgrmye347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E5%91%B3%E5%8E%A8%E6%88%BF.md#1">五味厨房</a>的两位主厨是Raymond 和 Ken。Raymond在香港和多伦多做过30多年的法国餐，意大利餐，泰餐，中餐，Ken 做过10几年的日本餐。他们了解到很多家庭就餐时，由于餐馆不能做多种国家的风味，所以大人往往不能点自己喜欢的餐饮的烦恼，就萌生创意，开了这家很独特的餐馆。</p>
<p>Raymond和Ken是两位非常富有创意的人，为不同口味的人创造出美味的食物是他们的最大兴趣。比如，一道看起来平常的意大利拉面Pasta, 他们做出来，你会觉得很新鲜和清爽。为什么呢？他们很看重食材的选择。他们选的是最好厂家生产的最好的新鲜面条，而番茄酱他们是用蕃茄做的，不是像一些餐馆用茄膏加水和面做的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10793497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10793497" style="width: 482px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3-32.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10793497 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3-32-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="482" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10793497" class="wp-caption-text">五味意大利面（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>看看几道创意菜:</h2>
<p>香草牛油焗龙虾 ：自创的龙虾做法。新鲜香草浇汁，还用青口打底，起到肉香汤浓的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803599" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803599" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/f89055b2849f9cdf8a2b6968ba5980aa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803599" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/f89055b2849f9cdf8a2b6968ba5980aa.jpg" alt="" width="456" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803599" class="wp-caption-text">香草牛油焗龙虾 (5wei-lobster) (商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10803592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803592" style="width: 446px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b60489a0896b2ca4f0cb222815cbd121.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b60489a0896b2ca4f0cb222815cbd121.jpg" alt="" width="446" b="436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803592" class="wp-caption-text">龙虾拉面 （5wei-lobster ramen）(商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泰餐有用秘制配方做成的绿咖喱和红咖喱汁。有一个西人，吃了他们点咖喱餐之后非常喜欢，一周来三次都吃同样的咖喱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803588" style="width: 431px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9774e27f738bd634938af80fe9f3c210.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803588 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9774e27f738bd634938af80fe9f3c210.jpg" alt="" width="431" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803588" class="wp-caption-text">泰餐咖喱 （5wei-curry）(商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烤羊扒（Lamb)：秋冬之际，暖身味美的食补</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803584" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/443d81e4394d69ae934eb0e2fb489175.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/443d81e4394d69ae934eb0e2fb489175.jpg" alt="" width="405" b="494" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803584" class="wp-caption-text">烤羊扒(5wei-lamb)(商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菲力牛排和老虎虾? (Filet mignon and tiger shrimp): 中西方人都很喜欢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803544" style="width: 426px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/619f746aa89323ebd794a44178ce68d3.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10803544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/619f746aa89323ebd794a44178ce68d3.jpg" alt="" width="426" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803544" class="wp-caption-text">正宗扒房的牛扒汁(5wei-fillet and shrimp)(商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>意大利海鲜斑马意粉: 创新的斑马意粉，配各式海鲜和烤蒜蓉面包：</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803497" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1-83.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803497 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1-83-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803497" class="wp-caption-text">意大利海鲜斑马拉面（5wei-zebra pasta）（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>创新的夏威夷寿司碗（poke bowl）。Poke Bowl 是夏威夷传出的，风靡世界的健康轻食。以寿司米做底，上面配许多蔬菜和生鱼片，在多伦多内很流行，但在万锦市就没有。一般来说，Poke Bowl通常只配Tuna生鱼片, 但他们可以配几款不同的鱼，也可以配虾扫罗或蔬菜扫罗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10803501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10803501" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4-21.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10803501 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4-21-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10803501" class="wp-caption-text">夏威夷寿司碗(5wei-poke bowl)（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
