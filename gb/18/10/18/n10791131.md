<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>45,000m2 家传43年田园房产</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/glenbrook-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大纪元合成图（图片来源Barfoot &#038; Thompson）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月18日讯】（大纪元工商记者李维报导）喜欢大自然和<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E5%9C%BA.md#1">牧场</a>的你，千万不要错过这套位于 5/334 Glenbrook Road, Glenbrook 的<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">房产</a>，她原来是一个大<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%A7%E5%9C%BA.md#1">牧场</a>，家族承传了43年，牧场和房产都精心维护了43年。主人现在决定忍痛割爱，选择小型房产过退休生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10791144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/glenbrook2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/glenbrook2-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791144" class="wp-caption-text">大纪元合成图（图片来源Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818C.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818C-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791157" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818F.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818F-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791160" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<p>非常方便地点。2分钟上22号公路，7分钟上1号高速，距离未来的Drury和Pukekohe大都会中心仅10公里远，到各种商业和休闲设施都很方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10791161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818V.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818V-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791161" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818L-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791162" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.5公顷平缓大地，周围环绕着成熟的树木和树篱，其中有90年历史的橡树、<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>圣诞树Pohutukawa、还有木兰和枫香树等，在小屋的前面还有一个大果园，里面有各种果树，让您随时可以采摘各种新鲜水果。精心规划且成熟的花园里，还有一个铺了新人造草坪的正规网球场，铺了方砖的休息区连通花园小径，方便全家娱乐健身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10791163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818Y.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818Y-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791163" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<p>牧场分成3块，围栏完善，上面还有一个11米x 18米的高举架工棚，可以用来饲养马匹、牛羊，放养走地鸡，也可以进一步发展果园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10791165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818A.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818A-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791165" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818B.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818B-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791167" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818O.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818O-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791168" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10791169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10791169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818S.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10791169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/764818S-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10791169" class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Barfoot &amp; Thompson</figcaption></figure>
<p>这套260平方米的4居室<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">房产</a>，坐拥美丽的田园景观，家庭起居室宽敞明亮，厨房布置精美，配有Rimu橱柜和经典的不銹钢台面，以及高端烹饪设施。4间宽敞双人房，独立餐厅和客厅，另外还有一间书房，为家人提供更多自由空间。</p>
<p>在主房之外还有一套2居室小屋，可以用作客人房或者作为Airbnb单独出租。</p>
