<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园市长选举抽签  江3底定、胜利、有够赞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/79cfec12d643b781305353a1afb77886-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园市长选举抽签，抽号次后合影。（徐乃义／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月19日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市长选举19日上午在桃园市选委会抽签，5位市长候选人中，除民进党<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>、国民党<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>未到场，由竞选办公室人员代抽外，其余都是本人到场，其中1号为朱梅雪、2号陈学圣、3号<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E4%B8%BD%E7%8E%AF.md#1">杨丽环</a>、4号吴富彤、5号郑文灿。候选人喊出“郑文灿、有够赞”、“杨丽环、江3底定”以及竖起两只大拇指喊赞！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10794371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10794371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/013b25a4343904ed6ade1c658d471d27.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10794371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/013b25a4343904ed6ade1c658d471d27-450x572.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10794371" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E4%B8%BD%E7%8E%AF.md#1">杨丽环</a>抽中3号，立刻拿出“杨丽环、江3底定”的卷轴。（杨丽环团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抽签顺序则是按照当初市长参选登记顺序，依序是<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>、朱梅雪、吴富彤、杨丽环，陈学圣、郑文灿是由竞选办公室主任武九龄、孙婉晴代抽，武九龄抽中2号立刻用两只大拇指比出“胜利”手势，孙婉晴除比出5个指头外，也用台语喊出“郑文灿、有够赞”，郑文灿青年军在场外为郑加油、造势；朱梅雪抽到1号相当高兴，立刻竖起左手大拇指喊赞；吴富彤则抽到4号后，不忘推销“四通八达亚洲城”政见，杨丽环则是拿出书写“杨丽环、江3底定”的卷轴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10794373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10794373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/307eadc9464715a0ad62a0522da13cc1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10794373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/307eadc9464715a0ad62a0522da13cc1-450x390.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10794373" class="wp-caption-text">陈学圣竞选办公室主任武九龄抽到2号，立刻竖起两只大拇指喊赞。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朱梅雪、杨丽环纷纷下战帖，要求郑文灿阵营就中油观塘第三天然气接收站、藻礁、大潭电厂等重大议题进行公开辩论。抽到1号，朱梅雪强调，这是首次工人参选，要找回工人的尊严。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10794372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10794372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fac2ccb08c5272a1a731d40a2cd78f66.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10794372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fac2ccb08c5272a1a731d40a2cd78f66-450x240.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="240" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10794372" class="wp-caption-text">陈学圣推出剪刀剪布的LOGO，让人莞尔。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抽到2号的陈学圣，面对抽中五号的郑文灿，立即设计剪刀剪布的LOGO，让人莞尔。◇</p>
