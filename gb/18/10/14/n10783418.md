<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>10000m2大地，自住、土地银行</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月15日讯】（大纪元工商记者李维<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a><ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0.md#1">奥克兰</a>报导）可能很难再碰到这样的机会了。这套房产就在即将开发的Drury 大都会中心里，走路到现在的Drury 商业中心，两分钟就上1 号高速，距离即将开建的第二条高速（Great South路）入口更近，未来的市郊火车站也会建在附近。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10783428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1-51.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1-51-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783428" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>交通枢纽 更加方便</h2>
<p>Drury是<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0.md#1">奥克兰</a>的南大门，北靠马努考市（Manukau），西接未来的Pukekohe大都会中心，南接汉密尔顿、陶朗加、惠灵顿及北岛大部分地区。计划中的第二条高速公路，就是从马努考市中心到Drury，再转向西边，延伸到Pukekohe大都会中心。这里也将成为火车和公交车的中转站，到哪里都会方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10783433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563S.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563S-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783433" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>工业中心 发展迅速</h2>
<p>根据奥克兰统一规划，这里属于未来城区居住区，考虑到Drury在南奥克兰的交通枢纽地位，而且将来Drury大都会中心和一个大型工业园区都将开发和建立，这一带的发展将会突飞猛进。可以预见，未来城区转变成城区住宅区，应该为时不会太远。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10783435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563Q.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10783435 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563Q-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783435" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%9C%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">土地银行</a>或开发 潜力巨大</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10783434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563O.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563O-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783434" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1公顷（10330平方米）大地，将拥有不可限量的开发潜力——如果您觉得没有那么多精力，可以简单地作为<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E5%9C%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">土地银行</a>，到时进行土地再分割。这块形状规整、平坦的大地，沿着道路100米，容易分割；如果您有精力和财力，可以自己开发新房。业界人士预计，这个地点房产的开发，在未来相当长的一段时间里，热度都不会降低。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10783436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563K.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563K-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783436" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>理想田园生活地点</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10783430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/12-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/12-2-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783430" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就是作为田园房产，这里也是绝佳地点，在享受静谧乡村风光的同时，又拥有城市生活的方便。5分钟到Papakura镇中心，15分钟到Manukau市中心，购物、休闲都很方便，每天通勤上班也不远。几分钟就到Drury小学，还在Drury基督学校和Rosehill高中的学区里面。</p>
<p>这套3居室乡村别墅经过了几次大的整修，仍保留古典风格，彩色玻璃前门、镂空的外部门廊和高高的天花板。原木门窗，燃木壁炉，开放生活空间连通两面带棚大阳台，阳光充足，一年四季都可享受户外生活。另外一面也有带棚阳台与卧室相连。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10783438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563L-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783438" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10783439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563G.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563G-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783439" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10783441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10783441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563I.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10783441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/761563I-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10783441" class="wp-caption-text">84 Appleby Road, Drury（Barfoot &amp; Thompson）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一个10 x 8米的三舱大仓房，2个带棚马厩还有冲洗舱。4个全围栏牧场可以放马或饲养牛羊，也可以放养走地鸡，享受自家的走地鸡蛋。牧场周围有成熟的树木、灌木，还有果树，还有蔬菜园可以享受自家种植的蔬菜。</p>
