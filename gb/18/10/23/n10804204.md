<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲最受欢迎奶瓶——Tommee Tippee</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-01.jpg" />
<h6>Closer To Nature金奖奶瓶。（Tommee Tippee提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月24日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E5%A5%B6%E7%93%B6.md#1">澳洲奶瓶</a>哪个好？全球数百万父母信赖的<ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tommee Tippee</a>，是英国婴童喂哺第一品牌，拥有超过50年历史。Tommee Tippee出品的Closer To Nature奶瓶设计巧妙，造型贴近母乳，荣获澳洲2018年My Child Excellence Awards“<ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">最受欢迎的婴儿奶瓶</a>”金奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804218" style="width: 376px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10804218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-02-376x400.jpg" alt="" width="376" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tommee Tippee荣获2018年My Child“最受欢迎的婴儿奶瓶”金奖。（Tommee Tippee提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>仿若母乳 宝宝更爱</h2>
<p><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Closer To Nature</a>奶瓶名副其实——其造型及质感均贴近母乳。根据Tommee Tippee对超过1,200名受访用户的调查，92%的父母表示，对宝宝进行瓶喂时，在头3次尝试内就让宝宝接受了Closer To Nature奶嘴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804219" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-03.gif" alt="" width="500" b="307" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">母乳造型的获奖设计。（Tommee Tippee视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>Closer To Nature奶瓶特色：</h3>
<p>? 奶嘴造型、质感均贴近母乳；<br />
? 奶嘴柔软，螺旋纹可灵活伸缩，模仿母乳哺喂的动作；<br />
? Easi-Vent敏感排气阀，防胀气（anti-colic），避免宝宝吸入过多空气；<br />
? 宽瓶口，易清洗，可高温消毒；<br />
? 不含 BPA。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804229" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-04.gif" alt="" width="500" b="306" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">人体工学设计瓶身。（Tommee Tippee视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804222" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-05.gif" alt="" width="500" b="281" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">抗胀气Easi-Vent排气阀。（Tommee Tippee视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804223" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-06.gif" alt="" width="500" b="281" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">方便清洗。（Tommee Tippee视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>源自英国 50年不断创新</h2>
<p>作为英国婴童喂哺第一品牌的<ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tommee Tippee</a>，在过去50多年中，与数百万家庭共同成长，不断开发出创新产品。</p>
<p>Tommee Tippee近年推出的<ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Perfect Prep?智能奶粉冲调机</a>，只需2分钟即可冲调出贴近体温的牛奶，方便，精准，省时省力，让半夜喂奶的妈妈少一份操劳。在英国，每5分钟就售出一台！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10804940" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank"><img class="size-full wp-image-10804940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-07-1.gif" alt="" width="500" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Perfect Prep?智能奶粉冲调机。（Tommee Tippee提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10804225" style="width: 561px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-10804225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/20181024-Chi-Jin-Tommee-Tippee-08-561x400.jpg" alt="" width="561" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">资料图：Tommee Tippee部分产品。（Tommee Tippee提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Tommee Tippee还有消毒锅、吸奶器、温奶壶等一系列产品，全方位满足妈妈们的育儿需求。更多信息，请点击访问<ahref="http://bit.ly/2Ap5jT4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tommee Tippee网站</a>。</p>
