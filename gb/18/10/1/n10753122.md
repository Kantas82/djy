<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Los Altos、Los Altos Hills上市房</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月01日讯】（大纪元记者李欧整理报导）小家碧玉、大户人家这样的对比，大概能形容Los Altos和Los Altos Hills房产的差异。但其实这两个城市的房价都属湾区中的顶层，面积也比其他城市大上不少，尤其是“富豪”路线的Los Altos Hills。最新的房市数据指出Los Altos仍处在火热阶段，售价一直高于开价；Los Altos Hills近3个月来售价皆低于开价，中间价和平均价近乎持平发展，而Los Altos Hills居民的商办购物要依靠Los Altos，或邻近的Mountain View。</p>
<p>一、251 Biarritz Circle, Los Altos, CA 94022<br />
<figure id="attachment_10753143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/01_265225063-0.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/01_265225063-0-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753143" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753143" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，前院草坪与双车位车库。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10753145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/02_265225063-32.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/02_265225063-32-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753145" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753145" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，此屋后院简洁干净，适合亲友小聚。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这是本次赏房面积最小、最便宜的房子，但却是生活最便利的，隔壁就是优秀的学校Los Altos Hogh，3分钟车程就到市中心，有众多餐厅和咖啡馆、药店、银行、邮局、加油站等等，满足生活所需。<br />
屋内设计简约迷人，厨房不大但有中岛结合早餐吧，由外而内有客厅、餐厅、家庭室，采半开放式设计；主卧有步入式衣橱，全都在一层楼空间。后院有绿地、大树和餐桌与火坑。双车位车库，车道可额外停车。<figure id="attachment_10753147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.33.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.33-600x347.png" alt="" width="600" b="347" class="size-large wp-image-10753147" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753147" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷地产</a>：251 Biarritz Circle, Los Altos, CA 94022。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、2085 Crist Drive, Los Altos, CA 94024<br />
<figure id="attachment_10753150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/03_265453043-28.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/03_265453043-28-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753150" class="wp-caption-text">2014年兴建的工匠式房产。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10753158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/04_265453043-29.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/04_265453043-29-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753158" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753158" class="wp-caption-text">迷人的中庭可通往多个室内空间。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这个2014年兴建，近乎全新的单层住宅，空间规划出众，有5卧4卫、大型中岛厨房、家庭室，而且多个房间都能通往户外中庭和后院，直接打开拉门就能互通，隐私安全。<br />
全户核桃色木地板、高天花板，建材高级。交通上，两三分钟即可开上280高速或85公路，附近有购物区，不远处也有高尔夫球俱乐部。这里也享有良好的学区，公立小学、中学都在10分钟以内的车程。<figure id="attachment_10753161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.46.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.46-600x347.png" alt="" width="600" b="347" class="size-large wp-image-10753161" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753161" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷地产</a>：2085 Crist Drive, Los Altos, CA 94024。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、27800 Edgerton Road, Los Altos Hills, CA 94022<br />
<figure id="attachment_10753165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/05_264621023-33.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/05_264621023-33-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753165" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753165" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>，隐秘山林间的豪宅。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10753168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/06_264621023-6.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/06_264621023-6-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753168" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753168" class="wp-caption-text">以天然石材墙面作为屏风墙。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
住宅坐落在高耸的橡树和阳光明媚的Los Altos Hills，完全体验宁静的乡村生活氛围，并坐拥3.42英亩土地。40年的房产整个经过精心改造，近期厨房更升级顶级家电。户外有2200多平方英尺的巨大甲板，足以招待众多宾客。<br />
Los Altos Hills住宅以豪华、隐私著称，这里也不例外，经过蜿蜒车道才能抵达；室内以乡村风格设计，入门就是一堵天然石材墙面，从橱柜、天窗、吧台皆是豪华订制建材。而且，这里属于优秀的Palo Alto学区。<figure id="attachment_10753171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.56.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.42.56-600x317.png" alt="" width="600" b="317" class="size-large wp-image-10753171" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753171" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>：27800 Edgerton Road, Los Altos Hills, CA 94022。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、26520 Conejo Court, Los Altos Hills, CA 94022<br />
<figure id="attachment_10753174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07_252095213-24.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/07_252095213-24-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753174" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753174" class="wp-caption-text">湾区赏房，带泳池且隐私的单层住宅。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10753176" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/08_252095213-11.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/08_252095213-11-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10753176" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753176" class="wp-caption-text">开放式空间的家居设计。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这处带泳池的豪宅，房价近期降了9.1%。虽没有山林作为屏障，但同样保有隐私，周围种满鲜花灌木。屋内有华丽的胡桃木和瓷砖地板；厨房、餐厅和客厅采开放式空间，以落地窗通往泳池或户外。<br />
户外有多处花园区，可以展现屋主的园艺爱好，房间内皆能享受阳光与植物的清新。交通上，距离Los Altos Village仅有1.5英里。同样拥有杰出的Palo Alto学校。#<figure id="attachment_10753177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10753177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.43.07.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2.43.07-600x300.png" alt="" width="600" b="300" class="size-large wp-image-10753177" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10753177" class="wp-caption-text">湾区赏房：26520 Conejo Court, Los Altos Hills, CA 94022。</figcaption></figure></p>
