<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>200人直接传递爱心 罹癌妻惊喜 夫泪崩</h2>
<a href="https://d3cacbcgqcawbc.cloudfront.net/C1yabhPnl"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wtnrng3147/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10615386 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月01日讯】亚曼达（Amanda Kofoed）是一位平凡的30岁女性，她与丈夫克林特（Clint Kofoed）和4个孩子生活在美国爱达荷州（Idaho），目前正在修习教育学位，毕业后她希望能成为一名小学教师。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4fb4b4d4bcee31e9735edf39ae369dc9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805580 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4fb4b4d4bcee31e9735edf39ae369dc9-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805580" class="wp-caption-text">这对夫妻有4个孩子。然而，妻子最近被检查出罹患<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%8C%E7%97%87.md#1">癌症</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d9ac826ebceccd91f2805028a80e1b62.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805581 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d9ac826ebceccd91f2805028a80e1b62-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805581" class="wp-caption-text">她很担心自己有一天会突然无法继续照顾孩子们。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但是，天有不测风云，2016年10月，她被检查出罹患<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%8C%E7%97%87.md#1">癌症</a>──典型霍奇金氏淋巴瘤（classical Hodgkin lymphoma），并且已到了第三阶段。</p>
<p>这种淋巴细胞癌变的疾病的5年存活率为86%，在美国有千分之二的民众曾在一生中罹患此病，而早期治疗复育率较高，年轻病人往往能在治疗后生存40年甚至更久。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6cd642b76c3b7bfc91f3512f33a1531b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805583 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6cd642b76c3b7bfc91f3512f33a1531b-600x341.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805583" class="wp-caption-text">妻子没有保险，没办法的情况下，他们求助<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8B%9F%E8%B5%84.md#1">募资</a>平台，希望筹措庞大的医疗费。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>治疗的方式可藉由<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%96%E7%96%97.md#1">化疗</a>、放射线治疗或<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E9%AB%93%E7%A7%BB%E6%A4%8D.md#1">骨髓移植</a>，她选择终止学程并辞去工作，接受为期6个月的高强度化疗。然而，治疗的费用极高，加上他们有4个孩子，沉重的负担已远远超出这<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E4%BA%BA.md#1">家人</a>的能力。</p>
<p>亚曼达与克林特不得已向群众<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8B%9F%E8%B5%84.md#1">募资</a>平台（GoFundMe）请求协助，平台人员马上答应他们，并准备在一间咖啡馆录制募款影片。</p>
<p>不过，这对夫妻不知道的是，平台人员早已发起募款行动，拍摄当天，他们邀请希望捐款的民众来到咖啡馆，超过200位善心的民众直接把爱心传递给亚曼达和克林特。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/895d7507296b6fa96c31aa5c8d63636b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805584 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/895d7507296b6fa96c31aa5c8d63636b-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805584" class="wp-caption-text">录影到一半，忽然有一位女子在他们的桌上放了100美元。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片（Video credit: Newsflare）?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-d318e54c-86e0-41af-5023-a10c3bf132fd" data-id="player-d318e54c-86e0-41af-5023-a10c3bf132fd"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/d318e54c-86e0-41af-5023-a10c3bf132fd?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F24%2Fn10805520.md#1"></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_10805588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0577386a212aef39d28cb97124e87b24.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805588 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0577386a212aef39d28cb97124e87b24-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805588" class="wp-caption-text">募资团队事前已将这对夫妻的情况公布网路，募款行动其实早已开始。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805589" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0c3e6169a5e2699b189bf067276f81f2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805589 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0c3e6169a5e2699b189bf067276f81f2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805589" class="wp-caption-text">人们从网路获得资讯，并主动希望帮助。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们是平凡的人们，有年轻人，也有年长者，还有妈妈带着孩子，民众鱼贯而入，将手中的100元美金捐款放在亚曼达与克林特的桌上，并给予这对夫妻鼓励的拥抱。这一幕让许多人感动落泪。</p>
<p>当天他们就募集了1万3千美元（约新台币40万元）；自2016年11月的慈善影片发布，半年后，他们总共募集了6万美元（约新台币185万元）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805590" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/04d25882420f30dd5b2116c185493cc4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805590 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/04d25882420f30dd5b2116c185493cc4-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805590" class="wp-caption-text">现场来了大约200位善良的民众，他们直接将爱心捐款赠与这对夫妻。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805591" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d632f120be2666cb523a640867d647bb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10805591 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/d632f120be2666cb523a640867d647bb-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805591" class="wp-caption-text">没有料到这样的情况，丈夫难掩激动之情，数度泪崩。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚曼达接受<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%96%E7%96%97.md#1">化疗</a>后，逐渐恢复健康，生活也渐渐回归正常。她对于接受大家的支持仍感到内疚，但她也会教育孩子拥有这种慈善家精神，特别是对待对抗癌症的人。</p>
<p>克林特说，他的妻子从来不想要看起来“需要被帮助”，“但她确实倾注了所有的努力”。亚曼达说，“我告诉孩子，接受别人的爱是多么美好的祝福，我们也可以为别人做这件事。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10805593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805593" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/edfbb10d424f183049c185dc346cef6f.png"><img class="wp-image-10805593 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/edfbb10d424f183049c185dc346cef6f-600x334.png" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805593" class="wp-caption-text">单单一天他们已募集了超过1万3千美元。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10805595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10805595" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6fda8befc2b447bf6d424ce0b245e3c6.png"><img class="wp-image-10805595 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6fda8befc2b447bf6d424ce0b245e3c6-600x334.png" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10805595" class="wp-caption-text">真是太令人感动了！感谢这些善良的人们，也希望妈妈能顺利治疗康复！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚曼达希望在秋季回到学校并完成学位。尽管患病处仍需要一些放射性治疗，但她对自己的期许是“不要活在恐惧之中”，亚曼达说：“深呼吸接受吧！我其实很幸运，希望能这样和<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E4%BA%BA.md#1">家人</a>们正常的生活，而且能活得很好！”</p>
