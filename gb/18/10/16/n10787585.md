<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>千万别给你的鸟儿喝酒 它会一头扎进酒杯</h2>
<a href="https://d1b0mjr6ppvor4.cloudfront.net/Q4pNF4ugd"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者安琪综合报导）人喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%96%9D%E9%85%92.md#1">喝酒</a>不是什么稀罕事，但动物喜欢喝酒就比较新鲜了。喝酒<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8A%E7%98%BE.md#1">上瘾</a>的人往往见了酒有点“拔不动腿”，即使不想一醉方休，至少也要“尝两口”。那么嗜酒的动物会是什么表现呢？一点都不比人“逊色”。</p>
<p>很多西方人喜欢在吃鸡肉时来一杯红酒，但在英国西萨塞克斯郡斯托灵顿的迪恩威（Dean Way），有一只叫施妮泽尔的<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E9%B8%A1.md#1">母鸡</a>也喜欢品红酒。一旦发现家中有“杯中酒”，它想方设法地要“小醉”一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/025e00bcf2b568374f22ee1dbca69273-e1539815651550.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10790927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/025e00bcf2b568374f22ee1dbca69273-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790927" class="wp-caption-text">这只英国<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E9%B8%A1.md#1">母鸡</a>喜欢喝红酒。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>你看它，虽然主人一次次试图阻止它：“施妮泽尔，你要干什么？你要干什么？！”当然它还是尽量保持“淑女”风度，稍作矜持，它果断地将樱桃小口深深扎进它面前的酒杯里，足足地啄了一口红酒，马上就变成红脸母鸡了。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #993300;">看完整影片?</span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-b2d3613c-1050-42dd-598f-cde80fe59a4b" data-id="player-b2d3613c-1050-42dd-598f-cde80fe59a4b"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/b2d3613c-1050-42dd-598f-cde80fe59a4b?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F16%2Fn10787585.md#1"></a></p>
<p>还有一只小<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>酷爱喝啤酒，它眼巴巴地等着主人往比它还要高的酒杯里倒酒。主人刚把瓶口挪开，它就在主人的惊呼中急不可耐地一头扎进了酒杯&#8230;&#8230;真是“醉生梦死”的写照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/78fadbacef04612402b07877e4add0bf-e1539816421869.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10790930 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/78fadbacef04612402b07877e4add0bf-e1539816566715-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790930" class="wp-caption-text">这只没有酒杯高的小<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>似乎在说：“再倒，这点酒不在话下。”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>可见酒不但能使人理智不清，爱上酒的动物也会发狂。饲养鸟类的饲主要留心了：可千万不能当着它们的面<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%96%9D%E9%85%92.md#1">喝酒</a>，一旦它们认识了这东西，可“酒”麻烦了。</p>
