<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>八德荣家荣民节庆祝大会  无私的奉献精神</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/66551a894f3c13592639a5ef2d9ae809-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>八德荣家主任古道中（左3）表扬6位荣民楷模。（徐乃义／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月31日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）庆祝国军退除役官兵辅导委员会成立第64周年暨第40届<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%A3%E6%B0%91%E8%8A%82.md#1">荣民节</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%BE%B7%E8%8D%A3%E5%AE%B6.md#1">八德荣家</a>31日办理庆祝大会，表扬6位荣民楷模、17位荣民自治干部、杰出伙食委员苏绳武，以及长期公益演出或合作的民间单位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10820171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/da5a97aac79e869322db2e16bab08cc0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10820171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/da5a97aac79e869322db2e16bab08cc0-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10820171" class="wp-caption-text">荣民伯伯妈妈很热真的唱国歌。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%BE%B7%E8%8D%A3%E5%AE%B6.md#1">八德荣家</a>主任古道中表示，民国68年10月31日，将先总统蒋公诞辰纪念日订为“<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%A3%E6%B0%91%E8%8A%82.md#1">荣民节</a>”，肯定荣民对国家的贡献，感谢荣民前辈们用自己的青春，在大时代守护我们家园、守护台湾宝岛。</p>
<p>古道中说，我们不能忘记，荣民半生戎马，在战场上冲锋陷阵，在灾难中赴汤蹈，将生命最辉煌灿烂的时光，交付给国家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10820168" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4bf75b9292f81b1b07c0dd2351c7862a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10820168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4bf75b9292f81b1b07c0dd2351c7862a-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10820168" class="wp-caption-text">八德荣家主任古道中（左3）表扬公益表演团体。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>古道中表示，八德荣家透过简单而隆重的仪式，向在场所有荣民楷模的卓越贡献，表达崇高的敬意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10820169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e39c528b3ddb7b926c64f8c66a75d99f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10820169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e39c528b3ddb7b926c64f8c66a75d99f-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10820169" class="wp-caption-text">八德荣家荣民节庆祝大会葫芦丝表演。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他感谢荣民自治干部这一年来对荣家的协助，感谢各民间单位的付出，每一个奋斗的身影，每一片奉献的心意，八德荣家永远记得这无私的奉献精神！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10820170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/02a16808eedd2fe41db514f18d05cb25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10820170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/02a16808eedd2fe41db514f18d05cb25-450x292.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10820170" class="wp-caption-text">八德荣家荣民节庆祝大会穹音葫芦丝乐。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
