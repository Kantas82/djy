<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>费德勒胜利助威 瑞士夺霍普曼金杯</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年01月07日讯】（大纪元记者尚上礼综合报导）在澳洲伯斯举行的<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9C%8D%E6%99%AE%E6%9B%BC%E6%9D%AF.md#1">霍普曼杯</a>冠军战，由<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E5%A3%AB.md#1">瑞士</a>对上德国，<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Roger Federer</span>）先在男单赛事以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>（<span lang="EN-US">4</span>）：<span lang="EN-US">7</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>，逆转世界第四的德国新星兹维列夫（<span lang="EN-US">Alexander Zverev</span>），混双再联手本西琪（<span lang="EN-US">Belinda Bencic</span>）以<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>拿下致胜点，率<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9E%E5%A3%AB.md#1">瑞士</a>以<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>力压德国，暌违<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年后，<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a>再度于<ahref="https://github.com/ezkcme3586/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9C%8D%E6%99%AE%E6%9B%BC%E6%9D%AF.md#1">霍普曼杯</a>称王。</p>
<p><span lang="EN-US">2018</span>新赛季，费德勒持续维持稳定表现，在第一点男单，面对曾二度打败过他的兹维列夫，首盘力拼到抢七局，以<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">7</span>先丢一盘。次盘兹维列夫发球突陷困境，费德勒一口气连破<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个发球局，以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>扳平。第三盘费德勒在第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5</span>两局又完成破发，早早取得<span lang="EN-US">5</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>领先，轻松以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>拿下第一点。总计后两盘<span lang="EN-US">14</span>局只让对手保住两个发球局。</p>
<p>本西琪在女单以<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>不敌前球后克柏（<span lang="EN-US">Angelique Kerber</span>），德国再次与瑞士打平。但在关键混双，费德勒搭档本西琪，对决兹维列夫和克柏，双方打得相当激烈，首盘互保僵持到最后，由费德勒先驰得点。次盘率先破掉德国的发球局，以再见<span lang="EN-US">ACE</span>拿下胜利。</p>
<p>瑞士队夺得<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年来的首冠，而费德勒回忆<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年搭档当年球后辛吉丝（<span lang="EN-US">Martina Hingis</span>）夺冠时才<span lang="EN-US">19</span>岁。如今的他已经<span lang="EN-US">36</span>岁“高龄”，<span lang="EN-US">19</span>冠加冕，排名还位于世界第二，费德勒说：“这真的很不真实，但去年重新回来后，今年可以再拿到冠军，这真的是无与伦比的感觉！”<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
