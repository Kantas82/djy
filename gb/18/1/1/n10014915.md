<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一家八代种葡萄 法国庄主家藏三百年酒窖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040524-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酒庄主Marc Vullien在自家已有3百年历史的酒窖里。（龚简／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年01月02日讯】<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">从门外走下几步台阶，打开黑木门，眼前摆着三排整齐的木酒桶，灰白的拱形天花板可看到砖缝，地面上铺着厚厚的小石子。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">这就是我家的酒窖，有</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">百年了。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">”</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">庄主</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Marc Vullien</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">说。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">大家知道吗？法国阿尔卑斯勃朗峰地区也盛产葡萄酒，而且历史悠久。这个产区就是<ahref="https://www.jinlisting.com/listing/%E8%90%A8%E7%93%A6%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E9%85%92%E4%BA%A7%E5%8C%BA-vin-de-savoie/">萨瓦葡萄酒产区</a>（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Vin de Savoie</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">）。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">在过去的</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">250</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">年来，</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Marc Vullien</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">家族世世代代都与葡萄酒艺相连结，到他这一辈已是第八代了，而他和太太</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Fran?oise</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">一起种植的</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">8</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">公顷葡萄园和自酿葡萄酒，也足足有</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">40</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">个年头了。</span></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10015076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10015076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040529.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10015076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040529-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10015076" class="wp-caption-text">酒窖位于一家普通人家的地下室，门外仍保留古老的农作具。（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="western">“<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">对我来说，这是世界上最棒的职业。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">”</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Vullien</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">说：</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">因为从葡萄藤发芽，到结果，到酿酒，然后得出自己的劳动成果</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">——</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">葡萄酒，这个过程是最棒的。你知道吗？法国人最重视的是餐桌上的佳肴，加上一瓶自己亲手酿制的好酒，这是生活重要的一部分。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">”</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Vullien</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">住在人口不到</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">600</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">的小镇——</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Fréterive</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">，这是萨瓦葡萄酒十几个优质村庄生产区之一，这里也是法国葡萄苗产地之一，占国家产量的</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">15%－20%</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">得益于阿尔卑斯山脉有众多的滑雪场，那里成了萨瓦葡萄酒的主要市场。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">我今天早上才出门送货到滑雪场回来，高山上一路雪景简直美极了，我最喜欢了。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">”</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">他说。</span></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10015068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10015068" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040521.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10015068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040521-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10015068" class="wp-caption-text">Marc Vullien在他的品酒室。（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">在</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">的品酒室里，装饰也是古色古香的。作为酒农，他满腹是葡萄酒的知识，随口能说出一段<ahref="https://github.com/hkeiuv3495/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E9%85%92.md#1">法国葡萄酒</a>历史的故事：你知道为什么法国葡萄酒常常用木桶来装吗？在高卢罗马时代，罗马人用陶瓷容器装酒，</span><span style="font-size: medium;">但在运输的过程中，很容易打碎，于是高卢人想到了用木桶，上面盖上盖子，不易遭损坏。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">对于葡萄种，</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">了如指掌，他透露，萨瓦种植葡萄的历史悠久，许多葡萄品种是从这里传出去的。大家认识的西拉葡萄（</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Syrah</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">）和维欧尼（</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Viognier</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">）葡萄，它们的母亲是白</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">梦杜斯（</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Mondeuse blanche</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">），这是萨瓦地区特有的一个品种。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">今天，<span style="color: #000000;">西拉和</span>维欧尼（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Viognier</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">）是法国罗讷河谷的主要葡萄品种。关于维欧尼品种，第一次记载出现在</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">1781</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">年，但据说，早在</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">2000</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">多年前，已由古罗马人种植。</span></span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10015073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10015073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040526.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10015073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/P1040526-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10015073" class="wp-caption-text">酒窖的角落里，珍藏着几十瓶50－60年的陈酒。“是我父亲留下来的。”Marc Vuillen说。（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="western">“<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">红酒里含有许多对人体有益的物质，特别是许多矿物质，葡萄的皮呢，也有药用。”一说到葡萄，</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">就滔滔不绝。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">他对葡萄酒的配菜也深有研究，“我的几款酒就是我和太太专门配制出来的，其它地方找不到的。”</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">强调，在葡萄配菜的技巧上，</span><span style="color: #000000;">菜肴一定要做得好</span><span style="color: #000000;">。他举例子说：“比如霞多丽白葡萄酒，适宜配湖水鱼，梦杜斯适宜配味道比较浓的菜。”为了方便客户，</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">专门为不同的葡萄酒做了个配菜介绍。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">对于葡萄酒庄主来说，一年四季都是重要的季节，然而，这个冬天，是</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">最后一次给滑雪场送货了，“我要退休了”，他说。因为</span>他膝下两个女儿都对继承父业不感兴趣，他唯有把葡萄园出卖或者出租。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="western"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">家族世代遗传的祖业即将结束，</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Vullien</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">未免感到惋惜，“不过，我已经决定，在我退休前，在家里酒窖的门上，放上祖宗</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="fr-FR">Louis Vullien</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">的牌子。</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">”</span></span></span></p>
