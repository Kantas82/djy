<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：坚持</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/11/1209200420302382-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>要坚持下去！要努力不懈！不要中途停下来！不要放弃工作！ </p>
<p>天下没有白吃的午餐。胜利不会从天上掉下来。成功不可能一蹴而及。机会成本虽然是经济学上的原理，用在其他方面也是同样行得通的。要有所获得，必需付出代价。坚持是所付代价中最重要的。</p>
<p>坚持需要有极大的耐力，坚强的意志，不馁的信心，永远不退缩。</p>
<p>一曝十寒显然没有成功的可能；遇到困难，就畏缩不前，或放弃工作，更会和成功绝缘。人生不如意事，十常八、九；如果一遇不如意事，就畏缩不前或放弃工作，不坚持到底，是不可能有所成就的，必也忍苦耐烦，乃能期其有成。</p>
<p>爬山者是最好的例子。一步一步爬上去，到很累了仍未达山巅，他如果放弃，便没法爬到了；他一定要咬紧牙关，任汗去流，任气去喘，甚至任血去流，一步一步爬上去，坚持到底，不中辍，乃能达成目标，爬上最高峰，成就那一趟爬山壮举。</p>
<p>做其他任何事也像爬山，一定要坚持努力，继续不辍，才能把事做成功。</p>
<p>拿破仑说：“成功属于最坚忍者。”要坚持下去！要努力不懈！不要中途停下来！不要放弃工作！</p>
<p> <strong>Insist on</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>You ought to insist on! You ought to strive! Never stop half way! Don’t give up the work!<br />
There’s no such thing as a free lunch on earth. The victory will not fall from the sky. Succession can’t reach in one step. Although opportunity cost is the principle of economics, it’s validness also in other thing. Whoever must pay for his success. Insist on is the most price he will pay.<br />
Insist on need extreme patience, firm determination, no down heart of faith, never shrink back forever. It’s apparently invalid that one day’s sun and ten day’s frost; it will absolutely rid from success if shrink or give up when face difficulty. Life will meet you halfway if shrink or give up and not insist on to terminate when face difficulty, it’s impossible to be success, the person who exercises patience and endures pain, he will be success.<br />
The climber of the mountain is the best example. A person who climbs one step after one step, and still not reach the top of the mountain till very tired, if he give it up, he can not do the climbing; he must grit his teeth, let him streaming in sweat, has hardly recovered his breath, even bleed, climb one step after one step, insist on, not stop in half way, and will climb to terminate, climb to the highest, achieve that great undertaking of mountain-climbing.<br />
It’s the same thing like mountain-climbing, be sure to insist on strive, not stop, successively, can achieve a work.<br />
Napoleon Bonaparte said once: success belongs to the man who insists the most. You ought to insist on! You ought to strive! Never stop half way! Don’t give up the work! @</p>
