<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>扳倒前世界第二 谢淑薇澳网再闯16强</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/GettyImages-907477284-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>谢淑薇。 (Photo by Pat Scala/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">【大纪元2018年01月21日讯】（大纪元记者尚上礼综合报导）澳洲网球公开赛，<ahref="https://github.com/vtolib370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%B7%91%E8%96%87.md#1">谢淑薇</a>继上一轮打败前球后穆古露莎（<span lang="EN-US">Garbine Muguruza</span>）后，<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日再以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">7</span>：<span lang="EN-US">5</span>打败前世界第二德旺丝卡（<span lang="EN-US">Agnieszka Radwanska</span>），再度闯进<span lang="EN-US">16</span>强，追平台湾大满贯赛女单最佳成绩。</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">德旺丝卡因为去年结婚再加上受伤，目前世界排名<span lang="EN-US">35</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年曾排到第二，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年拿到<span lang="EN-US">WTA</span>年终赛冠军，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年还二度重返世界第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>。两人曾经两度交手，各拿<span lang="EN-US">1</span>胜。</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm"><ahref="https://github.com/vtolib370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%B7%91%E8%96%87.md#1">谢淑薇</a>面对人高马大的德旺丝卡，再度展现多变打法，开赛第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>局接连破发，虽在第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>局被回破，第<span lang="EN-US">8</span>局再破发，仅用<span lang="EN-US">33</span>分钟就以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>拿下首盘。次盘开赛前，德旺丝卡喊出伤停，治疗并包扎左膝再上阵。双方展开破发大战，谢淑薇第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>局率先破发，德旺丝卡也完成两次破发，双方打成<span lang="EN-US">4</span>平。谢淑薇稳住局面，第<span lang="EN-US">12</span>局把握<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个赛末点，以<span lang="EN-US">7</span>：<span lang="EN-US">5</span>获胜，生涯第二度闯进<ahref="https://github.com/vtolib370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E7%BD%91.md#1">澳网</a>女单<span lang="EN-US">16</span>强。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10075204" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/GettyImages-907476784.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10075204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/GettyImages-907476784-600x370.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">谢淑薇。 (Photo by Pat Scala/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>谢淑薇于<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年曾从会外赛一路打进女单会内<span lang="EN-US">16</span>强，创下台湾新纪录，如今相隔十年，<ahref="https://github.com/vtolib370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E7%BD%91.md#1">澳网</a>再缔造佳绩。<span lang="EN-US">16</span>强赛将碰上大会第<span lang="EN-US">21</span>种子德国柯贝（<span lang="EN-US">Angelique Kerber</span>），柯贝曾拿下澳网女单冠军，也是目前本届澳网女单夺冠最热门人选。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
