<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一学就会的“手运动” 手部疾病不上身</h2>
<a href="https://dmt6yqw0je6jw.cloudfront.net/T6iohXr6w"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ydbzqt347/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>现代人就算全身不活动，手也难闲着：滑手机，点鼠标，敲键盘??时间久了会觉得指关节卡卡，甚至导致“手机手”、“鼠标手”等各种手部状况。而常做洗衣服、擦地板等家务事，或是长期从事过度用手的工作，积累久了也会感到手部酸痛不适。</p>
<p>这些腕部或手部长期、重复、持续的动作，容易引起过度疲劳和损伤。日常生活中，怎样保持手指灵活、预防手部病症呢？下面三个“手运动”可以帮助<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E9%83%A8%E4%BF%9D%E5%81%A5.md#1">手部保健</a>，避免疾病上身！</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>动作一：</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10072569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10072569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10072569 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise-600x550.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="550" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10072569" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E9%83%A8%E4%BF%9D%E5%81%A5.md#1">手部保健</a>运动之一。（吴辉／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 手掌直立，指尖向上；</p>
<p>2. 四指向掌内九十度弯曲，再轻轻捏握，指尖触及掌底；</p>
<p>3. 指尖再向内缩，呈握拳状；</p>
<p>4. 指节一节节向上松开，再回到手掌直立状态。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>动作二：</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10072571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10072571" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10072571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise2-600x806.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="630" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10072571" class="wp-caption-text">手部保健运动之二。（吴辉／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 从食指、中指、无名指到小指，依顺序将拇指指尖与其余四指指尖触碰；</p>
<p>2. 接着用拇指触碰小指的第一、第二、第三个指节，以及指节下方的掌横纹。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>动作三：</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10072572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10072572" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10072572 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/hand-exercise3-600x269.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10072572" class="wp-caption-text">手部保健运动之三。（吴辉／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 手掌朝上打开；</p>
<p>2. 手指向掌心内轻轻抓握，从小指开始呈花状依次收拢，过程中向内转动手腕。</p>
<p>焦点手部上肢诊所院长黄思暖表示，如果是因为腕部手术治疗而导致手部僵硬，这些动作也可以帮助复原。但如果手部有某处疼痛、麻木、受伤等状况，须请手科医师或手部治疗师根据具体的病症进行治疗。</p>
