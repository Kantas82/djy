<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刘世芳退选 陈菊送暖拥抱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/f3bacbaff23d603ef318c72fb3503c60-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>刘世芳退选，陈菊送上拥抱。（李怡欣／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年01月12日讯】（大纪元记者李怡欣台湾高雄报导）立委<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E4%B8%96%E8%8A%B3.md#1">刘世芳</a>宣布退出<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84%E5%B8%82%E9%95%BF.md#1">高雄市长</a><ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%9A%E5%86%85%E5%88%9D%E9%80%89.md#1">党内初选</a>，高市长<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E8%8F%8A.md#1">陈菊</a>12日带着鲜花探访表达不舍，陈菊拥抱<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E4%B8%96%E8%8A%B3.md#1">刘世芳</a>，并向媒体表示，自己的回忆录遭到扭曲，结果也伤害到刘，对此，她感到对刘很抱歉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E8%8F%8A.md#1">陈菊</a>表示，几天前刘世芳跟她报告，她相当不舍，但支持刘的决定并肯定其勇气，而她相当不舍，刘世芳因她而备受攻击，承受不应该的伤害，下一步，将转向支持谁，刘世芳三缄其口，不过对于<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E6%BD%AE%E6%B5%81.md#1">新潮流</a>与陈菊子弟兵的2018年高雄议员将积极辅选，也看出取舍端倪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10052601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10052601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/e138db32e306daec0309a277f07d013e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10052601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/e138db32e306daec0309a277f07d013e-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10052601" class="wp-caption-text">刘世芳(右)宣布退出<ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84%E5%B8%82%E9%95%BF.md#1">高雄市长</a><ahref="https://github.com/phwbor381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%9A%E5%86%85%E5%88%9D%E9%80%89.md#1">党内初选</a>，高雄市长陈菊(左)12日到服务处，送上鲜花与拥抱，表达对子弟兵退选的不舍。（李怡欣／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>何以一本书掀起震荡？千算万算，陈菊始料未及，为了长远大计与党内和谐，陈菊也只能不舍送上拥抱，媒体提问，此次退选是否也与猎雷舰引发的余波有关？陈菊回答，没这回事，绝对清清白白。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10052605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10052605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/6e377e767b91c969b760b88b62bb4ef6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10052605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/6e377e767b91c969b760b88b62bb4ef6-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10052605" class="wp-caption-text">陈菊(右)与市府三位副市长，12日到刘世芳(左)服务处慰问。（李怡欣／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
