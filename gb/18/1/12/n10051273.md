<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：富有多种色彩的雪花 你一定没见过</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258f47b3d77ac2c3b447ced06a5eef5-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年01月12日讯】（大纪元记者陈俊村报导）<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1.md#1">雪花</a>是大自然创造的艺术品，它们不但具有水晶般的质地，而且每片的图案都是独一无二的。而加拿大摄影师科马雷卡（Don Komarechka）还拍到富有多种<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>的雪花，让人更惊叹于大自然的神奇造化。</p>
<p>科马雷卡表示，他从事<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1.md#1">雪花</a>的摄影将近10年的时间，见过数以千计的各种雪花，迄今仍乐此不疲。而最让他感到着迷的，莫过于第一次发现雪花的中央出现<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="Snowflake-a-Day No. 13" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/donkom/39007148292/in/photolist-22qVQcw-21FYNXw-22CABAq-HeHMWv-ZKZkzP-DoW7wD-CuKGJR-Ef3VR3-S3UrqZ-EEWsuw-E8qbKS-22LVfKm-22mHwPJ-RNGd4n-RGvHqR-PG6VrX-Q7hJBx-Qy3bUF-RwMpUb-T6YQwp-Sb1TEJ-S2VS6F-RkBAKt-QAV5K7-Q56Y9b-F33GpT-PPfTMB-EgVpbi-EmgHbq-E6xj88-E8SzQj-F45V4H-Dyua2o-DMXkus-DF9zum-CE9xhB-D7iZfw-qSSaiC-qcMDBi-qNewQa-r4XsJL-NLhWGn-RhAF5t-E89Cyw-E2YCAQ-DuWRW1-CKTWsD-qXRZum-qjLhNf-rfH18G" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4558/39007148292_268c9294a2_z.jpg" alt="Snowflake-a-Day No. 13" width="600" b="600" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center></p>
<p>科马雷卡解释说，雪花的样式和颜色，可能是它在形成时的畸形所造成的，包括厚度、湿度甚至风等外力的改变，都可能产生不一样的结果。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="Snowflake-a-Day No. 3" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/donkom/23954232397/in/photolist-CuKGJR-Ef3VR3-S3UrqZ-EEWsuw-E8qbKS-22LVfKm-22mHwPJ-RNGd4n-RGvHqR-PG6VrX-Q7hJBx-Qy3bUF-RwMpUb-T6YQwp-Sb1TEJ-S2VS6F-RkBAKt-QAV5K7-Q56Y9b-F33GpT-PPfTMB-EgVpbi-EmgHbq-E6xj88-E8SzQj-F45V4H-Dyua2o-DMXkus-DF9zum-CE9xhB-D7iZfw-qSSaiC-qcMDBi-qNewQa-r4XsJL-NLhWGn-RhAF5t-E89Cyw-E2YCAQ-DuWRW1-CKTWsD-qXRZum-qjLhNf-rfH18G-qPPoSz-pH68wg-pvNS1p-qpksZt-qp9wrb-qgHTkk" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4524/23954232397_d941bfbc6f_z.jpg" alt="Snowflake-a-Day No. 3" width="600" b="600" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center></p>
<p>尽管乍看之下，雪花中央出现多种色彩似乎令人难以置信，但科马雷卡说，它的原理很简单，就是<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%96%84%E8%86%9C%E5%B9%B2%E6%B6%89.md#1">薄膜干涉</a>（thin film interference），但这需要罕见的条件才能形成。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="Snowflake-a-Day No. 2" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/donkom/37910132365/in/photolist-ZKZkzP-DoW7wD-CuKGJR-Ef3VR3-S3UrqZ-EEWsuw-E8qbKS-22LVfKm-22mHwPJ-RNGd4n-RGvHqR-PG6VrX-Q7hJBx-Qy3bUF-RwMpUb-T6YQwp-Sb1TEJ-S2VS6F-RkBAKt-QAV5K7-Q56Y9b-F33GpT-PPfTMB-EgVpbi-EmgHbq-E6xj88-E8SzQj-F45V4H-Dyua2o-DMXkus-DF9zum-CE9xhB-D7iZfw-qSSaiC-qcMDBi-qNewQa-r4XsJL-NLhWGn-RhAF5t-E89Cyw-E2YCAQ-DuWRW1-CKTWsD-qXRZum-qjLhNf-rfH18G-qPPoSz-pH68wg-pvNS1p-qpksZt" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4521/37910132365_c3ee2049a8_z.jpg" alt="Snowflake-a-Day No. 2" width="600" b="600" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center></p>
<p>所谓<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%96%84%E8%86%9C%E5%B9%B2%E6%B6%89.md#1">薄膜干涉</a>，就是当一束光波照射在薄膜上时，由于折射率不同，光波会被薄膜的上界面与下界面分别反射，因相互<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E6%B6%89.md#1">干涉</a>而形成新的光波，进而呈现色彩缤纷的现象。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="Snowflake-a-Day No. 7" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/donkom/25025491528/in/photolist-E8qbKS-22LVfKm-22mHwPJ-RNGd4n-RGvHqR-PG6VrX-Q7hJBx-Qy3bUF-RwMpUb-T6YQwp-Sb1TEJ-S2VS6F-RkBAKt-QAV5K7-Q56Y9b-F33GpT-PPfTMB-EgVpbi-EmgHbq-E6xj88-E8SzQj-F45V4H-Dyua2o-DMXkus-DF9zum-CE9xhB-D7iZfw-qSSaiC-qcMDBi-qNewQa-r4XsJL-NLhWGn-RhAF5t-E89Cyw-E2YCAQ-DuWRW1-CKTWsD-qXRZum-qjLhNf-rfH18G-qPPoSz-pH68wg-pvNS1p-qpksZt-qp9wrb-qgHTkk-CMbbZx-rzriYQ-omynDg-qybQGE" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4569/25025491528_dd0aabd293_z.jpg" alt="Snowflake-a-Day No. 7" width="600" b="600" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center></p>
<p>科马雷卡说，雪花在形成时，其<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%99%B6.md#1">冰晶</a>内部通常会出现空洞或泡泡，而其内部也会形成得比顶部或底部慢。这迫使泡泡上下两端的冰晶层变得非常薄，薄到光会产生<ahref="https://github.com/kenflc3962/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E6%B6%89.md#1">干涉</a>作用。<br />
</p>
<p><center><a title="Snowflake-a-Day #25" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/donkom/31867679655/in/photolist-Qy3bUF-RwMpUb-T6YQwp-Sb1TEJ-S2VS6F-RkBAKt-QAV5K7-Q56Y9b-F33GpT-PPfTMB-EgVpbi-EmgHbq-E6xj88-E8SzQj-F45V4H-Dyua2o-DMXkus-DF9zum-CE9xhB-D7iZfw-qSSaiC-qcMDBi-qNewQa-r4XsJL-NLhWGn-RhAF5t-E89Cyw-E2YCAQ-DuWRW1-CKTWsD-qXRZum-qjLhNf-rfH18G-qPPoSz-pH68wg-pvNS1p-qpksZt-qp9wrb-qgHTkk-CMbbZx-rzriYQ-omynDg-qybQGE-kS23RS-ozMKdu-CYULgJ-BojbjQ-kVq4u1-k5NXpz-jNcDR2" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/380/31867679655_3abc390472_z.jpg" alt="Snowflake-a-Day #25" width="600" b="600" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center></p>
