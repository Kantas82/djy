<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花莲强震 台东第一时间驰援</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/14a0af6a4759a800a23c65bda224124f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东特种搜救人员在花莲灾区瓦砾中探测寻找生还者。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月07日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）花莲6日深夜发生强震，邻居的台东县消防局台东大队所属特搜分队连夜紧急动员，赶往灾难现场投入<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E7%81%BE.md#1">救灾</a>工作，清晨4点35分从统帅饭店救出一名陈姓生还者，目前仍持续参与救援中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10122197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10122197" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/aa8f953374f75b7d0da3e542c9716386.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10122197 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/aa8f953374f75b7d0da3e542c9716386-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10122197" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>特种搜救人员在花莲灾区瓦砾中探测寻找生还者。 （台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%8E%B2%E5%BC%BA%E9%9C%87.md#1">花莲强震</a>撼动全台，确认台东无灾情后，台东县长黄健庭立即指派特种搜救队前往花莲救援，消防局紧急通知编制内人道救援警义消尽速返队，凌晨1点30分出发，4时许赶抵花莲市向现场指挥官报到，为全国各县市最早抵达的救援部队。</p>
<p>县府表示，第一梯次紧急出动特种搜救队成员19名，由吴宝龙带队，消防救助器材车2辆、指挥车1辆、后勤车5辆、搜救器材货柜等。后续集结搜救犬与及救护队共11车38人、3只搜救犬、高级救护队及各种精密生命探测工具与破坏工具等，目前指挥站位于花莲县警察局花莲分局前广场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10122198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10122198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1aef7a59d9f2a6ccafa8f35cd3a6fde5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10122198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1aef7a59d9f2a6ccafa8f35cd3a6fde5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10122198" class="wp-caption-text">花莲6日强震，台东县消防局特搜分队紧急动员，连夜赶往灾难现场投入<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E7%81%BE.md#1">救灾</a>工作。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
