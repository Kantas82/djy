<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吉祥年夜饭送来狗年旺运</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月07日讯】过年啦！华人社区最隆重的节日就要到了——过大年的心情永远不会消褪！即使身在海外，仍然盼望在团团圆圆的年夜饭宴席上能够满溢着着传统的吉祥祈福……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10128192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10128192" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_19.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10128192 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_19-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10128192" class="wp-caption-text">Spice Temple精巧别致的包间。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Spice Temple高贵优雅的大厅（客户提供）墨尔本<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/spice-temple%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">Spice Temple餐馆</a>今年为您带来惊喜贺年大餐！为庆祝2018中国新年，Spice Temple餐馆将于2月16日（周五）至3月4日（周日）特别推出<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%B9%B4%E5%AE%B4.md#1">贺年宴</a>。</p>
<p>我们的独家<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%B9%B4%E5%AE%B4.md#1">贺年宴</a>菜单由Neil Perry及头厨Andy Evans (Spice Temple) 、Peter Robertson (Jade Temple)携手共同设计，专为庆祝狗年而定制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10135800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10135800" style="width: 360px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_02.gif"><img class="wp-image-10135800 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_02.gif" alt="" width="360" b="240" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10135800" class="wp-caption-text">Spice Temple精美料理（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们的贺年宴中融入了庆贺传统新年的元素，包括富贵，幸福，健康，长寿，兴旺发达等祝福，” Neil说，“我们邀请食客来此体验具有传统中国宴席特征的美食，每道菜都代表一种祈福。”</p>
<p>这款寓意吉祥的大餐定价为每人$99，每人再加$55即可搭配酒水。</p>
