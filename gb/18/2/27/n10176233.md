<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>五结乡2018元宵走尪暨庙宇嘉年华</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/12de05d683fe7978b85f5cb4fa4ea320-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>五结乡立幼儿园小朋友表演走尪舞。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）宜兰县五结乡2018<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md#1">元宵</a><ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%B0%AA.md#1">走尪</a>暨<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%99%E5%AE%87%E5%98%89%E5%B9%B4%E5%8D%8E.md#1">庙宇嘉年华</a>民俗文化活动。无形文化传承盛事“<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%B0%AA.md#1">走尪</a>”活动将在107年3月2、3日于五结乡利泽老街举行，并以大型Q版妈祖和陈银生与永安宫妈祖庙建筑展与乡亲共同欢渡<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md#1">元宵</a>佳节，欢迎民众阖家莅临。</p>
<p>开幕典礼将以节庆米食文化为主题及俏皮新娘闹元宵，双喜迎春呈现佳宾来开场。</p>
<p>今年走尪除了元宵节当天的八大庄传统走尪、阵头拜庙及三进出过火无形文化科仪，并在次日扩大邀请宜兰县内各大庙宇及企业参与五福聚合来走尪传承“传统走尪”。过火和传统走尪三进三出是活动传承无形文化的菁华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10176236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10176236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/27/n10176233.md#1/%e6%b0%b8%e5%ae%89%e5%ae%ae%e5%aa%bd%e7%a5%96%e5%bb%9f15%e7%b1%b3%e9%ab%98q%e7%89%88%e5%aa%bd%e7%a5%96%e5%95%9f%e5%8b%95%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10176236"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10176236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/e8fad7f9dfc18254765ee3cd5bd6b246-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10176236" class="wp-caption-text">永安宫妈祖庙15米高Q版妈祖启动。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>民俗文化活动的学童体验，能深入了解本地无形文化资产并获得在提灯笼夜巡后猜灯谜的题材。在元宵节的夜晚来延续提灯笼赏灯的习俗，家家户户的大小朋友提着灯笼，相约闹元宵。“提灯笼夜巡”亲子踩街、猜灯谜送礼物重拾童年的趣味，并在元宵晚会的校园学子尬舞中将过年气氛带到最高点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10176237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10176237" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/27/n10176233.md#1/%e4%ba%94%e7%b5%90%e9%84%89%e9%95%b7%e7%b0%a1%e6%9d%be%e6%a8%b9%e3%80%81%e5%bc%b5%e6%98%8e%e8%8f%af%e7%b8%a3%e8%ad%b0%e5%93%a1%e7%8f%be%e5%a0%b4%e7%99%bc%e6%94%be%e7%99%bc%e8%b2%a1%e9%87%91%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10176237"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10176237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5e933977eed31c817c61d9b834c310d9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10176237" class="wp-caption-text">五结乡长简松树、张明华县议员现场发放发财金。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“爆尪靖新娘”往昔在利泽地区是元宵节一项重要的活动，今年的活动中更开放新人佳偶保平安的祭典，保存这项无形文化，让参与的民众都能在民俗文化的勋陶下幸福满分，欢迎乡亲一起来让王公为您加持。另有创意踩街、钻轿下求平安、歌仔戏等等节目。兰阳戏剧团错配姻缘的演出，让乡亲重享昔日庙埕看戏的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10176238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10176238" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/18/2/27/n10176233.md#1/%e6%b0%b8%e5%ae%89%e5%ae%ae%e5%aa%bd%e7%a5%96%e5%bb%9f%e4%ba%94%e5%8d%81%e4%ba%8c%e7%94%b2%e7%b9%9e%e5%a2%83%e5%87%ba%e7%99%bc%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10176238"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10176238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/e78bb1db94bba27387b6c79803e5a133-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10176238" class="wp-caption-text">永安宫妈祖庙五十二甲绕境出发。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
