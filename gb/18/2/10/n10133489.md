<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看尽仙人掌──美国索诺兰沙漠植物园</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月11日讯】美国西部的<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E5%88%A9%E6%A1%91%E9%82%A3%E5%B7%9E.md#1">亚利桑那州</a>因大峡谷而著名，其实，除此之外，它各种各样、千奇百怪的<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E4%BA%BA%E6%8E%8C.md#1">仙人掌</a>也同样令人惊叹不已。</p>
<p>2018年1月底，我飞来一个叫做Scottsdale的小小老城，它坐落在<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E5%88%A9%E6%A1%91%E9%82%A3%E5%B7%9E.md#1">亚利桑那州</a>首府凤凰城边上，极平静极安全。在酒店前台见到这个叫做索诺兰<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%BC%A0.md#1">沙漠</a>植物园（Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum）的画册，于是，有趣的沙漠<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E4%BA%BA%E6%8E%8C.md#1">仙人掌</a>探险开始了！</p>
<p><strong>仙人掌博物馆?</strong></p>
<p>这个植物园占地面积不小，慢走慢看要两个小时。可以说，在这里把一辈子的仙人掌都看了。</p>
<p>Cactus，就是仙人掌，大概有50多个家族，2000多种类，教人无法一一细数。多数都是肉质植物，有很大的本事储蓄水分。</p>
<p>如果在<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%BC%A0.md#1">沙漠</a>里出不来了，嘬食仙人掌可以延长生命，但有些会有毒。亚利桑那州法律严禁采摘仙人掌。</p>
<p>落日时分，到处都是两人多高粗大的柱形仙人掌，叫北美洲巨人柱（Saguaro），是索诺兰沙漠特有的品种，构成植物园的标志性画面。相比之下，趴在地面上的叫桶状仙人掌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/2-24-e1518299504961.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/2-24-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133529" class="wp-caption-text">落日时分的北美洲巨人柱是索诺兰沙漠植物园的标志性画面。</figcaption></figure>

<p>有的很漂亮，像女人头上的发饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/4-16-e1518299809690.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/4-16-600x760.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="760" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133553" class="wp-caption-text">像女人头上的发饰</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有各种地面仙人掌群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5-11-e1518299882715.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5-11-600x758.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="758" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133555" class="wp-caption-text">接地气的仙人掌群。</figcaption></figure>
<p>长成球状的就是仙人球了，很早就被人们收入室内做观赏植物，也许是因为它的花绚丽多彩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/9-7-e1518300236600.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/9-7-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133566" class="wp-caption-text">圆滚滚的仙人球。</figcaption></figure>
<p>仙人掌旁边经常有针状尖叶子的树叫扁轴木树（Palo Verde），是亚利桑那州的州树。它的根会寻找水源，鸟儿们喜欢站在它的枝头啼叫顺便拉屎。这样仙人掌就在肥沃的鸟粪中滋生，在扁轴木树的树荫下成长……大自然很奇妙，很完美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/11-8-e1518300310123.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133567" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/11-8-600x759.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="759" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133567" class="wp-caption-text">扁轴木树与仙人掌是好邻居。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>各种肉质植物</strong></p>
<p>植物园内生长很多种芦荟（Aloe），亚洲女人喜欢用它们做面膜，对皮肤有滋养的功用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/12-2-e1518300431819.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/12-2-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133568" class="wp-caption-text">滋养皮肤的芦荟。</figcaption></figure>
<p>丝兰（Yucca）有着剑一样的尖锐叶子，开花时，白花高大而招眼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/13-2-e1518300485126.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/13-2-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133569" class="wp-caption-text">叶子如针的丝兰。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10133570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/14-1-e1518300514654.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/14-1-600x758.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="758" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133570" class="wp-caption-text">丝兰的花高耸如剑。</figcaption></figure>
<p>我的前室友走时从我家里拿走了不少东西，唯一留给我的是一盆她养的小小的丝兰，放在我洗衣房众多的肉肉植物中。她说：“我养的不是花，是药。”丝兰可以敷在烫伤的伤口上，帮助减轻伤痛。</p>
<p>上周由于时差和新工作的辛苦，晚上迷糊中灌暖水袋时，把全部滚烫的开水浇在身上。疼痛难忍之时，忽然记起已经被我养大养旺盛的那株丝兰。撕下几片叶子，把浓浓的黄绿汁液抹在烫伤的皮肤上，确实疼痛减轻了不少。</p>
<p>还有著名的龙舌兰（Agave）。 墨西哥最好喝的酒就是龙舌兰酒（Tequila），也是我曾经的最爱，入口温软，有水果的沁香，前几年回国时带给家人共享，备受喜爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10133571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/15-e1518300571219.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/15-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10133571" class="wp-caption-text">著名的龙舌兰。</figcaption></figure>
<p>植物园内盛开各种沙漠野花，颜色都很鲜艳。</p>
