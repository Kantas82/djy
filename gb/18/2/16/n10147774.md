<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #0000ff;">研究：<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>有五种<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%A7%E6%A0%BC.md#1">性格</a> 你的猫是哪种？</span></h2>
<figure style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/cat-323262_1920-e1518749255404.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/cat-323262_1920-e1518749255404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>有五种普遍存在的<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%A7%E6%A0%BC.md#1">性格</a>。你的猫属于哪一种？（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月16日讯】（大纪元记者高秀媛<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>墨尔本报导）人们常说，猫有九条命。澳洲的研究人员发现，猫还有五种显着的性格类别。</p>
<p>南澳大学（University of South Australia）的研究人员对2800多只猫的主人进行了问卷调查，对其结果进行分析后发现，猫有五种普遍存在的性格。你的猫属于哪一种？你又该如何“因材施教”呢？</p>
<h2>易受惊的（Skittish）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10147804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10147804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/42eb6ddf9cc70eb6e281e9546825575f-e1518750216964.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10147804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/42eb6ddf9cc70eb6e281e9546825575f-e1518750216964.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10147804" class="wp-caption-text">易受惊型的。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高分者——对人和其它猫感到不安和恐惧</p>
<p>在这一项上得分高的猫也许会受益于家中可以藏匿的地点。</p>
<p>低分者——冷静并信任人和其它猫</p>
<p>在这一项上得分低，可能显示你的猫很好地适应了周边的环境。</p>
<h2>外向的（Outgoing）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10147802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10147802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/c7c1211e0a20e1dd94163f70aa7193c9-e1518750278841.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10147802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/c7c1211e0a20e1dd94163f70aa7193c9-e1518750278841.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10147802" class="wp-caption-text">外向型的。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高分者——好奇并好动</p>
<p>外向型的猫咪可能会受益于更多的玩具和玩耍时间。</p>
<p>低分者——漫无目的，对事情提不起精神</p>
<p>在这一项上得分低的猫咪并不常见，也许是上了年纪或出现健康问题的征兆。</p>
<h2>强势的（Dominant）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10147803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10147803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/3c68d6793ab0c2a16598dde331e6cd0a-e1518750322541.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10147803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/3c68d6793ab0c2a16598dde331e6cd0a-e1518750322541.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10147803" class="wp-caption-text">强势型的。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高分者——欺负其它猫，且十分好斗</p>
<p>强势的猫无论在家里或是你的社区中，可能都很难和其它猫相处。</p>
<p>低分者——顺从，且对其它猫很友好</p>
<p>得分低的猫可能会很适应有很多只猫咪的家庭。</p>
<h2>冲动的（Spontaneous）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10147805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10147805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/cdedf64ac1464d602d415845a5bf7559-e1518750359472.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10147805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/cdedf64ac1464d602d415845a5bf7559-e1518750359472.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10147805" class="wp-caption-text">冲动型的。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高分者——易冲动，飘忽不定</p>
<p>如果你的猫在这一项上得分很高，考虑一下它的环境中是否有令其感到压力的事物。</p>
<p>低分者——行为可预测，有所节制</p>
<p>这项得分低可能意味着你的猫很适应其环境，并喜欢有规律的生活。</p>
<h2>友好的（Friendly）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10147807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10147807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/00e9077ac22a84bf05769fd8ab379393-e1518750396282.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10147807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/00e9077ac22a84bf05769fd8ab379393-e1518750396282.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10147807" class="wp-caption-text">友好型的。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高分者——对人友好，充满情感</p>
<p>得分高的猫可能很适应家中的人和其它动物。</p>
<p>低分者——孤僻且易怒</p>
<p>不友好的猫可能天生喜欢独处，或可能在交友上遇到麻烦。如果你原本可爱的猫突然变得不友好，可能意味着它在经历沮丧、痛苦或疾病。</p>
