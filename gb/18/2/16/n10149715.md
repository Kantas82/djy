<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月17日讯】埃瑞克（Erick）和妻子育有三个女儿，妻子又怀上了第四胎，他们一直以为是个女儿，甚至连名字都已经选好了。</p>
<p>到了<ahref="https://github.com/hoxgwd311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%86%E5%A8%A9.md#1">分娩</a>的那一天，埃瑞克陪着妻子在产房内等待女儿的出生。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10445614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
孩子终于出生了！当医生们告诉埃瑞克说，这是一个重9磅15盎司的男婴时，平时沉着冷静的他此时既震惊又兴奋。</p>
<p>女婴变成了男婴！</p>
<p>他惊讶得张大了嘴，手捂着脑袋，简直不敢相信眼前的一切。突然，他似乎缓过味儿来，于是双举着拳头，并用拳头击打着椅子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10521738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10521738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180628-jeff-06.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10521738 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180628-jeff-06-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10521738" class="wp-caption-text">当医生们告诉埃瑞克说，这是一个重9磅15盎司的男婴时，他既震惊又兴奋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“是个<ahref="https://github.com/hoxgwd311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B7%E5%AD%A9.md#1">男孩</a>！” 埃瑞克欢呼着说，“哦，天哪！”</p>
<p>“你确信吗？” 他问医生。医生让他看了<ahref="https://github.com/hoxgwd311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF.md#1">婴儿</a>后，他这才彻底相信了。他坐在了椅子上，仍旧沉浸在惊喜之中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10521741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10521741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180628-jeff-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10521741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180628-jeff-07-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10521741" class="wp-caption-text">医生让他看了<ahref="https://github.com/hoxgwd311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF.md#1">婴儿</a>后，他这才彻底相信了，旧沉浸在惊喜之中。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>产房里充满了笑声。</p>
<p>看来，埃瑞克和妻子不得不给这个意外的男婴重新起个名字了。</p>
