【大纪元2018年02月19日讯】在传统的英文教学中，英语教师在第一堂课通常是教26个英文字母，大部分的教师会在黑板上从ABC开始，依序地写上一连串的字母，底下的学生面对这么枯燥的“符号”，除了硬背及不断地练习，似乎别无他法了。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-220b5006-57f5-4337-6596-9e6a4210f454" data-id="player-220b5006-57f5-4337-6596-9e6a4210f454"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/220b5006-57f5-4337-6596-9e6a4210f454?r=16x9&amp;s=720x840&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F2%2F18%2Fn10154444.md#1"></a>
<p>印度有一位英语教师，以非常独特且<ahref="https://github.com/zbhtyx3190/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%89%E8%B6%A3.md#1">有趣</a>的方法，教导学生轻松愉快地将26个字母记起来。他的教学方式被其他人用<ahref="https://github.com/zbhtyx3190/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%86%E9%A2%91.md#1">视频</a>记录下来，在网络上热传，看过的人都赞叹不已，称他是天才老师。</p>
<p>首先，他在黑板上划了一排整齐的短直线，一边划着，一边还配合手上的动作，哼着一首引人入胜的歌曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10525392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10525392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/7d94a931c000488f29c7bc69a79e795d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10525392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/7d94a931c000488f29c7bc69a79e795d-600x575.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="575" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10525392" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/zbhtyx3190/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%86%E9%A2%91.md#1">视频</a>截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你是他的学生，此时应该会全神贯注地看着老师及黑板，心里纳闷着：老师接下来要教什么呢？</p>
<p>接着，这位老师开始连接线条，每个人都惊呆了，原来这是字母表！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10525393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10525393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/235379b0f24d0a2e4a6a2b421fb3488f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10525393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/235379b0f24d0a2e4a6a2b421fb3488f-600x575.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="575" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10525393" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
