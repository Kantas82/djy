<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：绿森林中的红地毯 春赏红藜正当时</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5ad910831631875497de81ee316ffbe3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2018台东红藜季10日在森林公园中热闹登场。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月11日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）每当<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%97%9C.md#1">红藜</a>盛开，台东原住民就知道，新的一年即将展开。行政院农委会水土保持局台东分局办理春赏红藜季，在色彩缤纷的盛开红藜中热闹登场。今年除了南回田区可赏红藜，台东<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%AE%E6%9E%97%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">森林公园</a>中也有约4分地种植了上千株的红藜田区，春游台东，3月2日前，在台东市就可欣赏到难得一见的红藜地景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133964" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1d31361af2c17fcdc060d8a20ebb3fed.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10133964 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/1d31361af2c17fcdc060d8a20ebb3fed-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%97%9C.md#1">红藜</a>叶铺成的红地毯。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10133963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/e3d79f166afa7ad878e983e98f82bc5b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/e3d79f166afa7ad878e983e98f82bc5b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>红藜目前进入盛产期。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%BF%9D%E5%B1%80.md#1">水保局</a>台东分局10日上午在<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%AE%E6%9E%97%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">森林公园</a>举行台东红藜季开幕仪式，天公作美，一扫连日阴霾，艳阳高照，局长王志辉表示，象征台东红藜正火红。水保局藉由跨区域整合台东县政府、台东农改场、农会等相关单位，推动行销台东红藜产业，并透过特色旅游、伴手礼设计等，让台东红藜提升至三级化观光产业，以及六级化的多元化产业活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133966" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/ef56b55a3cb34fa86654987c7c0ba7c4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/ef56b55a3cb34fa86654987c7c0ba7c4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红藜色泽艳丽，引人喜爱。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>谷物界红宝石 创造近30亿年产值</h3>
<p>红藜是原住民传统农作物，是部落重要的粮食，也是酿酒的酵母，营养丰富，有谷物界红宝石之称。台东红藜是台湾最大的红藜产区，由于生长于无污染的环境，品质好，近年来成为健康市场的新宠，也吸引许多知名企业采购，除上架贩售原生谷物外，还有红藜蛋卷、红藜米汉堡，红藜酵素等当红产品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/8698103eb9da9739d7f92f894bccf067.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/8698103eb9da9739d7f92f894bccf067-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红藜营养丰富，有谷物界红宝石之称。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10133967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/45eb6c250bf1e569c79b923e0add20d1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10133967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/45eb6c250bf1e569c79b923e0add20d1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东原住民美食，红藜小米阿拜。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东县长黄健庭表示，台东红藜栽植面积从最初的20公顷增加到目前300公顷，产量大增，不但市场价格好，每年也为部落创造了近30亿元的产值，带动农村收益。立委刘櫂豪指出，已争取到中央1270万建置台东红藜加工厂，未来将大幅提升产量与稳定产品品质，以符合大型企业品牌的需求。</p>
