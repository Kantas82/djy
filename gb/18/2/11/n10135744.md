<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>男子钓上一只被鱼网窒息的海龟  迅速展开救援行动</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月12日讯】有时候，出海打鱼可能钓上来一些意想不到的东西，比如海带，海藻，垃圾。</p>
<p>但是一只<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%9F.md#1">海龟</a>被<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E6%B0%91.md#1">渔民</a>钓上来之后却得到了及时的救助。真是福兮祸所倚。</p>
<p>据估计，多年来，数百万<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%BE%9F.md#1">海龟</a>落入渔网。它们被困住之后无法游泳、无法进食、或严重受伤，很多海龟就这样死去了。这使得世界上七种海龟中的其中六种处于濒临灭绝状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10132040" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/42a7b1a2ea931cd368633517c10cc176.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10132040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/42a7b1a2ea931cd368633517c10cc176.jpg" alt="海龟很容易被渔网困住，并会被杀掉。但是有渔民为了解救它们，剪断渔网后放生了。还有一个海龟受恩报恩的小故事。图为海龟被渔网所困。（视频截图）" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海龟很容易被渔网困住，并会被杀掉。但是有<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%94%E6%B0%91.md#1">渔民</a>为了解救它们，剪断渔网后<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BE%E7%94%9F.md#1">放生</a>了。图为海龟被渔网所困。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>了解到这些情况后，这位渔民对被鱼网窒息的海龟迅速展开了营救行动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133832" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5555.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10133832 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/5555-450x356.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海龟很容易被渔网困住，并会被杀掉。但是有渔民为了解救它们，剪断渔网后<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BE%E7%94%9F.md#1">放生</a>了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他拿出刀子切断渔网，然后将海龟轻轻放回水中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10133831" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/8888.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10133831 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/8888-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">他拿出刀子切断渔网，然后将海龟轻轻放回水中。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海龟晃晃悠悠地游向远方。</p>
