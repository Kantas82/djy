<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“忠”犬迎新 维州多族裔民众同庆中国新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1747-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在维吉尼亚州中国新年欢庆活动上，各族裔小朋友合唱《新年好》。（李莎/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月13日讯】（大纪元记者林乐予美国<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%90%89%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维吉尼亚州</a>报导）喜气洋洋的舞龙舞狮，奔放豪迈的蒙古族舞，古风古韵的汉服展示??2月10日，维吉尼亚州的<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中国新年</a>欢庆活动上，浓郁的“年味”不但吸引了众多华裔移民，也让当地的西方民众大开眼界。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10138119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A2418.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A2418-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">精彩的舞龙表演。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1164.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1164-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>小朋友表演的蒙古族筷子舞。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138252" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1851.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138252" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1851-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汉服表演。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1237.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1237-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马里兰州高中生蔡博容表演古筝演奏。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然下着瓢泼大雨，但大华府亚裔社区服务中心（ACSC）在<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%90%89%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维吉尼亚州</a>卢瑟捷克森中学（ Luther Jackson Middle School）举办的<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">中国新年</a>欢庆活动，仍然吸引了许多人参加，甚至还有从宾州专程前来的西方民众。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10138228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1691.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1691-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">活动吸引了众多中西方民众。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1904.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1904-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">活动吸引了众多中西方民众。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国会议员康纳利（Gerry Connolly）和康斯托克（Barbara Comstock）以及众多州、市级的民选官员连续多年参加这一活动，一同体验独具特色的中国新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10138212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1284.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1284-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国会议员康纳利（Gerry Connolly）连续多年参加中国新年欢庆活动。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138241" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/NewYear1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138241" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/NewYear1-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国会议员康纳利（Gerry Connolly）和康斯托克（Barbara Comstock，前排左四）以及众多州、市级的民选官员连续多年参加这一活动，体验独具特色的中国新年。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>配合<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E5%B9%B4.md#1">狗年</a>的主题，今年活动推广的传统理念是“忠”，现场还有猜灯谜、<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%B2%81%E9%92%B1.md#1">压岁钱</a>、发福字、象棋对弈等丰富的中国文化体验项目，大小朋友们在这里玩得不亦乐乎。就读于麦克林高中（Mclean High School）的Izzie Germain已经学了三年中文，很喜欢汉语和中国文化，虽然认得的汉字还很有限，但她也饶有兴趣地猜起了灯谜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10138234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A2023.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A2023-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">就读于麦克林高中（Mclean High School）的Izzie Germain学习中文三年了，她和母亲一起来参加新年欢庆活动。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1621.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1621-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">现场有猜灯谜、<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8B%E5%B2%81%E9%92%B1.md#1">压岁钱</a>、发福字、象棋对弈等丰富的中国文化体验项目。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10138246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1640.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10138246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/AM3A1640-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">现场还有猜灯谜、压岁钱、发福字、象棋对弈等丰富的中国文化体验项目。（李莎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全天的节目演出缤纷多彩，有<ahref="https://github.com/gzptzn309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>的中国舞、多族裔小朋友合唱《新年好》、越南舞蹈、韩国跆拳道、印度舞蹈和器乐演奏等。来自河南的范宇恒一年前移民到美国，现在在马里兰州的普茨维尔高中（Poolesville High School）学习。第一次登台表演的范宇恒带来了吉他弹唱《童年》，他说，看到台下有各种肤色、各种文化背景的观众，他感到“挺自豪的”。</p>
