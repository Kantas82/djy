<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>唱响圣诞颂歌 Strathfield市府带你体验更多欢乐</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;">【大纪元2018年11月26日讯】今年的<ahref="https://github.com/disqoz3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E9%A2%82%E6%AD%8C.md#1">圣诞颂歌</a>歌唱会（Strathfield Christmas Carols）将于12月8日在<ahref="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Bressington Park</span></a>唱响。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10875323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10875323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/"><img class="wp-image-10875323 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/A01V9685-copy-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10875323" class="wp-caption-text">汇聚至欢乐海洋的方法就是来到Strathfield<ahref="https://github.com/disqoz3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E9%A2%82%E6%AD%8C.md#1">圣诞颂歌</a>歌唱会。（活动主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>节日活动将带给你和家人一个神奇的<ahref="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span style="color: #0000ff;">圣诞表演</span></a>，与圣诞老人会面，家庭野餐，美食摊位以及适合儿童的各类活动。</p>
<p>有趣的活动不止这些！还有圣诞精灵影院将放映特别影片，以及晚间壮观的烟花表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10875325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10875325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/"><img class="wp-image-10875325 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ZCOX1179-copy-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10875325" class="wp-caption-text">汇聚至欢乐海洋的方法就是来到Strathfield圣诞颂歌歌唱会。（活动主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的节日活动会更加特别，位于Homebush的Underwood 路上的Bressington Park经过了新设计的升级翻修，一个新的运动场、步道、游乐场、板球网和新的设施。市政府很高兴为当地居民带来这一梦幻般的公园，将在圣诞颂歌歌唱会前举行开幕仪式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10875328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10875328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/"><img class="wp-image-10875328 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ZCOX1974-copy-600x422.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10875328" class="wp-caption-text">汇聚至欢乐海洋的方法就是来到Strathfield圣诞颂歌歌唱会。（活动主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在你的日历中记录下欢乐活动的地址电话吧！了解更多详情，<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://strathfieldevents.nsw.gov.au/major-events/christmas-carols/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>点击这里</strong></a></span></p>
<p>时间：12月8日，下午2点公园开幕；傍晚6点圣歌歌唱会；晚上8点精灵影院；晚上约9:45烟花表演。</p>
<p>地点：Bressington Park, Underwood Rd, Homebush<a style="border: 0;" src="https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d3313.384303625276!2d151.0765845152098!3d-33.85398548066031!2m3!1f0!2f0!3f0!3m2!1i1024!2i768!4f13.1!3m3!1m2!1s0x6b12a4ccfb5c21fb%3A0xcfe49b1ed2023e7c!2sBressington+Park!5e0!3m2!1szh-CN!2sth!4v1543253891373" width="600" b="450" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
