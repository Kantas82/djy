<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金马55星光红毯 胡歌、华仔绅士风范帅气</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811170804001487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第55届金马奖于17日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，左起：刘德华与胡歌。（台北金马执委会／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年11月17日讯】（大纪元郑宜芬台北报导）第55届<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>于17日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%BE%B7%E5%8D%8E.md#1">刘德华</a>（华仔）身穿经典剑领西装、后梳油头，大方稳重又亲切；<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E6%AD%8C.md#1">胡歌</a>以干净油头搭配绅士风西装，展现温文儒雅的轻熟男气质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10858285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10858285" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811170700371487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10858285 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811170700371487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10858285" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%BE%B7%E5%8D%8E.md#1">刘德华</a>压轴走红毯，一出场就让粉丝尖叫连连。（台北金马执委会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10858284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10858284" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811170800061487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10858284 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1811170800061487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10858284" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E6%AD%8C.md#1">胡歌</a>（左）。（台北金马执委会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张震穿着条纹西装与蝴蝶领结，散发奇幻古典氛围；旅美投手王建民体格健壮高大；星光大道主持人刘杰中以深蓝丝绒剑领西装展现正式感。</p>
