<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>追忆“漫威之父”逝世 周边成长2成</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ee6b6a4fd06f9fc870f9b8a4f3394f50-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《钢铁人》与《蜘蛛人》的指环扣，受到台湾消费者青睐。（乐天市场提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月23日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）“<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%AB%E5%A8%81.md#1">漫威</a>之父”史丹．李（Stan Lee）于本月12日辞世，为漫威迷投下一枚震撼弹，他一生创造出无数英雄角色，包括<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%A2%E9%93%81%E4%BA%BA.md#1">钢铁人</a>﹑<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E7%A5%9E%E7%B4%A2%E5%B0%94.md#1">雷神索尔</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%98%E8%9B%9B%E4%BA%BA.md#1">蜘蛛人</a>等经典英雄，无论在影评及收益上都获得极大的肯定，也带动周边商品热卖，一夕翻红。</p>
<figure id="10870631" aria-describedby="caption-10870631" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1bb4a62676c66a6960706bdbd0d0fb21-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1bb4a62676c66a6960706bdbd0d0fb21-450x450.jpg" alt="日本影迷喜爱搞笑的《死侍》杯缘装饰。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10870631" class="wp-caption-text">日本影迷喜爱搞笑的《死侍》杯缘装饰。（乐天市场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="10870629" aria-describedby="caption-10870629" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/956578348deaf8c7f831e6b0c8d6aa78-450x433.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/956578348deaf8c7f831e6b0c8d6aa78-450x433.jpg" alt="镀黑外壳的《黑豹》行动电源据有王者霸气感。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10870629" class="wp-caption-text">镀黑外壳的《黑豹》行动电源据有王者霸气感。（乐天市场提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乐天市场指出，站上<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%AB%E5%A8%81.md#1">漫威</a>商品达上千品，“漫威”、“复仇者”等关键字搜寻量暴增3成，周边商品销量也较上月成长2成，可见台湾人对于漫威宇宙拥有高度热衷。</p>
<p>许多漫威迷争相购入漫威相关商品，台湾消费者大多喜欢3C周边商品、联名T恤，行动电源、手机保护壳等3C周边商品销售量较去年同期增加3成；而日本人则偏好精致的公仔或实用小物等，各有喜好。◇</p>
