<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>洪青体育会70周年庆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-1HongQing-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿洪青会长Victor Lee(站立持麦克风者)介绍唯一健在的创始人黄才芳(左，坐轮椅者)。(黄剑宇/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者黄剑宇波士顿报导）10月27日晚，波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/wfncjj363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E9%9D%92%E4%BD%93%E8%82%B2%E4%BC%9A.md#1">洪青体育会</a>在帝苑大酒楼举办70周年志庆，筵开57席。与会者除了众多华人侨社代表，还有麻州众议员黄子安、侨教中心主任欧宏伟、纽约洪青兄弟团等。82岁高龄的洪青创始人黄才芳(Harry Ming)等元老也出席庆典。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10827164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/DSC09135-2-e1541192600368.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10827164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/DSC09135-2-600x497.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="497" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">麻州众议员黄子安（右）给波士顿洪青会长Victor Lee颁发褒奖状。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10826566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-4HongQing-2-e1541169268635.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-4HongQing-2-600x370.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">侨教中心主任欧宏伟（中）代表侨委会给波士顿洪青会颁发褒奖状。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10826557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-3HongQing-2-e1541169180224.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-3HongQing-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纽约洪青会特意带来了“智勇双全”的奖牌。左起：波士顿洪青会长Victor Lee、纽约洪青会长Victor Chan、纽约洪青副会长Kyle Yuen、波士顿洪青副会长Bobby Tam。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>晚会中，黄子安与欧宏伟分别给洪青会长Victor Lee和副会长Bobby Tam颁发了褒奖状；纽约<ahref="https://github.com/wfncjj363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E9%9D%92%E4%BD%93%E8%82%B2%E4%BC%9A.md#1">洪青体育会</a>会长Victor Chan和副会长Kyle Yuen还特意带来了“智勇双全”的奖牌。</p>
<p>麻州州长贝克(Charlie Baker)和副州长白利朵(Karyn Polito)，波士顿市长华殊(Marty Walsh)、麻州众议员麦家威(Aaron Michlewitz)、麻州参议员宾加利(Joseph Boncore)和(Sal Didomenico)等政要也在庆典前发来贺信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10826553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-2HongQing-2-e1541169083351.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-2HongQing-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洪青特有的“群狮拜贺”，红、黄、银、绿、紫、白、黑、灰八色彩狮齐舞，庆祝洪青70周岁。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10826570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-5HongQing-2-e1541169347242.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-5HongQing-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">庆祝洪青70周岁大合影。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10826579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-6HongQing-2-e1541169405880.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-6HongQing-2-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">庆祝洪青70周岁的特制蛋糕。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚司仪是黄韵红(Rebecca Huang)和洪青副会长谭伟明(Bobby Tam)。洪青的一头白狮为大家表演了“醉狮”，另一头黑狮高难度的“高凳舞狮”拉开了晚会序幕。之后还上演了洪青特有的“群狮拜贺”，红、黄、银、绿、紫、白、黑、灰八色彩狮齐舞，庆祝洪青70周岁。</p>
<p>会长Victor Lee感谢众位嘉宾到场，还隆重介绍了洪青创始人黄才芳老先生。黄才芳表示，回顾70年前一同创会的其他7位创始人，感慨70年来经历的难忘之事，赢得热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10826583" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-7HongQing-e1541169474988.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10826583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1-3-7HongQing-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪门元老同庆。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽约和波士顿洪青体育会的会长与代表兄弟们在台上举杯祝酒，与在场嘉宾们共贺70周年志庆。</p>
<p>波士顿洪青体育会(Gung Ho Club)是全美洪青体育会中历史最悠久，组织最庞大的一团，其成员主要是在美国长大的华人后代ABC。</p>
<p>当晚，参与庆典的华裔青年不少，还带来了家人和西人朋友同庆。尽管当天白天下雨，洪青成员们还是在中国城牌楼举办了舞狮庆祝活动。◇</p>
