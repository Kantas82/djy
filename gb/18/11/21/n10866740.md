<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玉之珍爱 大都会毕绍普玉器收藏展精品回顾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-round-crop-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>清 翡翠山水图屏 （大都会艺术博物馆提供） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>玉质纯净温润，东汉许慎《说文解字》以“仁义礼智信”比喻“玉”有五德：“石之美，有五德：润泽以温，仁之方也；?理自外，可以知中，义之方也；其声舒扬，尃以远闻，智之方也；不桡而折，勇之方也；锐廉而不技，絜之方也。”是以玉石自古备受神州子民的喜爱与推崇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10866756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-TempleAssistant.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10866756 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-TempleAssistant-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清中期 碧玉童子 （<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%83%BD%E4%BC%9A%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%8D%9A%E7%89%A9%E9%A6%86.md">大都会艺术博物馆</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国古人视玉为珍品，并非仅因其数量或材质珍稀，更非借此“炫富”；而是因为玉质内蕴道德与精神理想，故成为古代士人自我惕励、须臾不敢离身的君子之征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10866753" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-leather-polishing-wheel.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10866753 size-thumbnail" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-leather-polishing-wheel-150x120.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="120" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">打磨<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%89%E5%99%A8.md">玉器</a>用的皮制砣轮（<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%83%BD%E4%BC%9A%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%8D%9A%E7%89%A9%E9%A6%86.md">大都会艺术博物馆</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10866752" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-gourd-polishing-wheel.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10866752 size-thumbnail" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-gourd-polishing-wheel-150x120.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="120" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">打磨<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%89%E5%99%A8.md">玉器</a>用的砣轮（大都会艺术博物馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10866751" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-drill.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10866751 size-thumbnail" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-drill-150x120.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="120" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雕玉用钢针（大都会艺术博物馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玉质坚硬，不同于一般金属以切割成型，需以硬度更高的宝石琢磨方成，过程费力耗时。清朝乾隆皇帝重视且资助精致工艺的发展，中国制玉技术于此时达到顶峰。</p>
<p>“玉”在古代所指广泛，举凡水晶、玛瑙等美石都可称为“玉”。但真正的“玉”只有两种：<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%AF%E7%8E%89.md">软玉</a>（和田玉）和<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0.md">翡翠</a>。前者玉色不透明，质地较软，通常用于玉雕；后者质硬，色泽半透明，多用于珠宝饰品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10866748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-bottle.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10866748 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6_29_METJade-bottle-600x486.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="486" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清中期 <a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%A1%E7%BF%A0.md">翡翠</a>玉雕葫芦 （大都会艺术博物馆提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽约大都会艺术博物馆的“玉之珍爱”（A Passion for Jade: Heber Bishop and His Collection）特展（业已落幕），展出名扬四海的美国玉器收藏家毕绍普（Heber?R.?Bishop<em>，</em>1840—1902）一生珍藏的中国玉器文物。毕绍普既是糖果大亨，也是位活跃的艺术支持者，凭借其中国和其他文化玉器收藏之质与量，堪称西方玉器收藏巨擘。</p>
<p>一百年前的收藏家多好瓷器，但是毕绍普独爱玉的温润可人、玉德润身。1902年，毕绍普身后将其毕生收藏委托大都会博物馆管理。“玉之珍爱”在展现清代精湛繁复的玉器工艺之际，也使观者一窥毕绍普独步当时的收藏品味与眼光。</p>
<p>今刊出展览之部分精品，以飨读者。</p>
