<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世贤国小师生创作交趾陶装置艺术成果发表</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/63b5ee917c76ab25e0ba10038f900d38-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>揭幕后大合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E8%B4%A4%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">世贤国小</a>“嘉义市公立学校师生创作<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E8%B6%BE%E9%99%B6.md#1">交趾陶</a>装置艺术计划”成果发表记者会于11月21日(三)上午9时，在学校东侧校门口进行揭幕典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5bd717ca7947c9a7d9b5f4b4f4657ab6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865601 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5bd717ca7947c9a7d9b5f4b4f4657ab6-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865601" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市政府文化局副局长叶国梁表示，本计划<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E8%B4%A4%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">世贤国小</a>经费补助只有15万元，如果金额较大的要向教育处申请。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次与<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E8%B6%BE%E9%99%B6.md#1">交趾陶</a>艺师侯吉村合作，技术指导世贤国小师生制作，首创全国以交趾陶艺术模式制作的交趾陶椅，为交趾陶艺术生活化融入校园及社区景观，再添吸睛亮点！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6d1ec28c7291c57b0ac0987a38c3411a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865604 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6d1ec28c7291c57b0ac0987a38c3411a-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865604" class="wp-caption-text">世贤国小校长何宪昌表示，没有艺术也可以活下去，但没有艺术就没有文化。 文化是民族的血脉，若没有文化传承这个民族也就消失了。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>校长何宪昌表示，这是学校第二次加入“嘉义市公立学校师生创作交趾陶装置艺术计划”融入校园的创新活动，聘请侯吉村老师将传统色彩丰富的交趾陶融入生活实用机能。以创意造型的拼贴艺术椅为作品主题，也受到交趾陶大师谢东哲老师的启发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a23f6a8e40ebb8659035f2336cc786cb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865607 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a23f6a8e40ebb8659035f2336cc786cb-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865607" class="wp-caption-text">功绩卓著的世贤国小4位主任，（左起）教务处张主任、学务处陈主任、总务处陈主任及辅导室吕主任。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>进东侧校门可看到眼前呈现的三张交趾陶座椅，很谐和的融入校园景观，除了是公共艺术，也是供亲师生及社区里民休憩的座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6c6aa1ee73908cc00fa77ce75f9b3ecf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865609 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6c6aa1ee73908cc00fa77ce75f9b3ecf-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865609" class="wp-caption-text">鲸游大海椅，创作的小朋友手指着自己的作品。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>世贤国小致力于优质交趾陶特色课程方案的设计与实作，今年6月初先举办全校“创意休闲椅设计比赛”，征求学生的创意发想，共有33人获奖，期间再透过学校艺文领域教师及侯吉村共同讨论，决定参考其中主题明显的作品，作为三张实体“交趾陶艺术造型椅”的创作原型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/88bdacf04ec8c2b4eb3f123e3ee5c6b0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865610 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/88bdacf04ec8c2b4eb3f123e3ee5c6b0-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865610" class="wp-caption-text">书本文具椅，有的小朋友一次创作二件作品。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>六年3班骆思璇同学创作的“叶子椅”与二年4班詹彦儒同学发想的“书本文具椅”，以及由二年5班郑淯慈同学的“快乐小鲸鱼”、六年1班赵芷恩同学的“虎鲸的书世界”及六年1班谢致翔同学创作的“嬉游大海”等三幅作品延伸出的“鲸游大海椅”，孩子们精彩的创意成为三张椅子的造型来源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865615" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/512cc190f5b7f8f4e274e31f2212d375.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865615 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/512cc190f5b7f8f4e274e31f2212d375-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865615" class="wp-caption-text">叶子椅，创作的小朋友迎来许多掌声。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>侯吉村说，大人的想法反而比较呆板，小朋友纯净思想加上生活的经验发想出的造型，非常好，加上在地元素，充满校园生活的元素，孩子的想像力真是令人赞叹。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10865618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/d7eb05af216ff0285ae3af9cd58d6cc1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865618 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/d7eb05af216ff0285ae3af9cd58d6cc1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865618" class="wp-caption-text">议会何文琮秘书代表议长来校关心小朋友的活动。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10865619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1e393f0af01fba6f0dd094d0d05baba7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865619 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/1e393f0af01fba6f0dd094d0d05baba7-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865619" class="wp-caption-text">六年级小朋友分享创作的点滴。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10865621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10865621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/4d58c0945522684b0b1ba84103869c4e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10865621 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/4d58c0945522684b0b1ba84103869c4e-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10865621" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友才小二就有很好的思考创造力，相信将来会有一番作为。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
