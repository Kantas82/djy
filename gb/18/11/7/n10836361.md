<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超有爱！黄金猎犬深情款款看着主人讨抱抱</h2>
<a href="https://dycmgeziwp90b.cloudfront.net/Q7ibXQ7xa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wqsjdc301/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10615386 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月08日讯】你见过眼神如此柔和的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>吗？时常挂着笑脸又平易近人的大狗狗Louie，最喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%B1%E6%8A%B1.md#1">抱抱</a>，尤其见到主人刚进家门，或是坐在椅子上，Louie总是忍不住要过来撒娇，让人超级喜欢！如果哪天也被Louie这么深情款款的看着自己，感觉都要触电了，心都融化啦！</p>
<p>社群网站Istargram的“网红狗狗”Louie是一只4岁的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>，跟它的“人类父母”住在美国加州，平日最喜欢到处跑跳，逢人就微笑，邻居们都很喜欢它。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10846146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10846146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3353d76a87d405a8cd1a433b317093a6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10846146 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3353d76a87d405a8cd1a433b317093a6-450x482.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="482" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10846146" class="wp-caption-text">Louie是一只很爱<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%B1%E6%8A%B1.md#1">抱抱</a>的黄金猎犬。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10846149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10846149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3431a1d78abb7f7134e0f8330fc901b6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10846149 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3431a1d78abb7f7134e0f8330fc901b6-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10846149" class="wp-caption-text">它最喜欢用热情的拥抱迎接主人回家。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10846151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10846151" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/decd4170b0e62ad2af164dad4f673173.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10846151 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/decd4170b0e62ad2af164dad4f673173-450x504.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="504" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10846151" class="wp-caption-text">Louie大方表达自己的情感。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Louie的主人是一对恩爱的伴侣，他们不仅关心彼此，也对家中<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A0%E7%89%A9.md#1">宠物</a>──狗狗Louie还有猫咪Oliver照顾有加，或许就是在这么信任且<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%89%E7%88%B1.md#1">有爱</a>的环境下长大，Louie和Oliver虽然时不时会上演嬉闹成分居多的“猫狗大对抗”，但同时也很相亲相爱。</p>
<p>生活中有可爱猫狗陪伴，确实增添不少乐趣，尤其Louie的个性随和又好玩乐，光是看着Louie在沙堆里玩耍，就觉得生活中什么都是新鲜事，到处都能找乐子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10846154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10846154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6fd007cb604194934e8438187140a4aa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10846154 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/6fd007cb604194934e8438187140a4aa-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10846154" class="wp-caption-text">主人温柔地抱着Louie，像哄孩子睡觉。Louie真是<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B8%E7%A6%8F.md#1">幸福</a>无比！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人特别喜欢Louie，甚至帮它申请了专属账号，把Louie从小到大乐在生活的照片通通放上网。后来，主人也新增了影像段落。像这支影片，就在记录Louie等待主人回家的热情迎接画面，还有许多Louie向主人讨抱抱的可爱镜头，真的很暖心！看了都想养一只黄金猎犬了！</p>
<p>毛茸茸的Louie像一只会动的大玩偶，不管是待在家中的沙发、地毯上、餐厅的椅子或干脆和主人一起躺在地上，Louie的脸上都带着满足的笑容，真是一只非常<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B8%E7%A6%8F.md#1">幸福</a>的狗狗。</p>
