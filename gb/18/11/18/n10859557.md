<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猛玛山下的美食The EATery</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月18日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
踏着秋日的气息，我们一行12人拍摄秋景的摄影队，往着Mammoth一路疾行。日出、日落、高山、流水，看遍红色黄色，满山满谷秋色无边。一整天下来已经数不清楚上了多少高山，下了多少湖泊？带着疲惫的身体、愉悦的心情，饥肠辘辘地到处找寻美味。</p>
<p>有位同行曾经来过此处，极力推荐The EATery，我们摸不着方向，于是便跟着走。车子停在停车场，就已见到几个个巨大的<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92.md#1">啤酒</a>桶，想必这一家餐厅是自己酿造啤酒。事实上就是“猛玛酿酒公司Mammoth Brewing Company”。餐馆外面也有许多桌椅，如今是秋日，寒风袭来，所以没有顾客坐在花园，但在夏天此处一定热闹非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10859587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/07_BAR.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10859587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/07_BAR-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">餐厅<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E5%90%A7.md#1">酒吧</a>。（<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐厅的楼下是<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E5%90%A7.md#1">酒吧</a>以及小商店，吧台内供应各式<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92.md#1">啤酒</a>，我们便叫了多种类的品尝式小杯啤酒。<br />
Golden Trout Kolsch<br />
Yosemite Pale Ale<br />
Double Nut Brown<br />
Wild Sierra Session Saision<br />
Epic IPA<br />
IPA 395</p>
<figure id="attachment_10859588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/01_Beer.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10859588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/01_Beer-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">品尝式小杯啤酒。（<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旁边的商店有卖各式的丅恤及小纪念品，想必这一家餐厅名气一定很大，不然为何还贩卖纪念品？叫菜就在楼下排队，我看板子上面没有几道菜，随意点了几样，想想大概不怎么样。拿了啤酒走上二楼，菜很快就送上来了。看看菜的份量，我们都吓儍了，这些八成是给巨人吃的，就是两人份的也吃不完。我们叫了有：</p>
<figure id="attachment_10859589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/02_BBQ-Pulled-Pork-Sandwich-Stack.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10859589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/02_BBQ-Pulled-Pork-Sandwich-Stack-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">拉猪肉</a>三明治。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烤<ahref="https://github.com/bbwpee3877/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89.md#1">拉猪肉</a>三明治BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwich Stack<br />

<p>三明治高到和脸一样大，不知如何入口？我叫的沙拉，光是上面散的肉就把我撑饱了。我觉得这一家的拉猪肉很可能是他们的镇店之宝，因为所有的酱汁都是他们自己调制的，送上来的那几份，看起来都是架势不凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10859593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/05_BBQ-Pulled-Pork.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10859593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/05_BBQ-Pulled-Pork-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤拉猪肉。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除此之外他们还外送（Order a Keg）以及接各式派对，如果有空能够到这一个滑雪胜地，又喜欢喝酒吃肉的食客不要忘了来这一家。#</p>
