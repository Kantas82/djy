<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>她穿姥姥1957年礼服赴毕业舞会 亮相惊喜好大</h2>
<a href="https://d377tzsbzf1wvt.cloudfront.net/R6eYZKo7d"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/urzlpx322/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/urzlpx322/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）和自己的妈妈及姥姥多蒂（Dottie）一样，美国加州少女凯西（Cassie）喜欢复古款的裙装，这还要从她小时候说起。</p>
<p>当时，多蒂给女儿黛比（Debbie）一件老式无肩礼服，让小外孙女“扮公主”玩。裙子实在太漂亮了，凯西的妈妈不声不响地收好，小姑娘则盼望着长大后穿上它的那一天。这件连衣裙是一件淡粉色的<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E4%BC%9A%E7%A4%BC%E6%9C%8D.md#1">舞会礼服</a>，是姥姥1957年参加初中<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B8%9A%E8%88%9E%E4%BC%9A.md#1">毕业舞会</a>时穿的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10559669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10559669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7f9889bd5bd7de6fcea16dad769947d9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10559669 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7f9889bd5bd7de6fcea16dad769947d9-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10559669" class="wp-caption-text">多蒂（右）1950年代年参加初中<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B8%9A%E8%88%9E%E4%BC%9A.md#1">毕业舞会</a>时的留影。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-19cb77eb-3b7c-4513-7dbf-908d329c6a20" data-id="player-19cb77eb-3b7c-4513-7dbf-908d329c6a20"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/19cb77eb-3b7c-4513-7dbf-908d329c6a20?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F13%2Fn10559558.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>特别的大日子终于到来了——凯西高中毕业了。在加拿大温哥华岛举行的毕业舞会，标志着她已长大成人，即将开启未来的人生之路。选穿哪件礼服，少女已打定主意，只是，她想对姥姥保密。</p>
<p>直到凯西毕业这一天，多蒂还蒙在鼓里；孙女没让自己出谋划策，老人多少有点黯然呢——凯西和父母找些借口搪塞过去了。</p>
<p>为此，少女的心理压力肯定也满大的。不知不觉，毕业典礼来临，全家人都飞来加拿大，秘密就要揭开了！</p>
<p>当姥姥和姥爷在草坪上等候时，凯西盛装走下了露台的楼梯。多蒂实在太期待这一刻了，她激动地摀住双眼，身体像在微微发抖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10559585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10559585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/NTD-PRO294853496316UU6U5I4I54-1024x576.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10559585 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/NTD-PRO294853496316UU6U5I4I54-1024x576-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10559585" class="wp-caption-text">奶奶激动地摀住双眼，身体微微颤抖。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>放下双手，睁开眼睛，她惊见外孙女穿着半世纪前她的那件<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E4%BC%9A%E7%A4%BC%E6%9C%8D.md#1">舞会礼服</a>！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10559584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10559584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dressjpg.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10559584 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dressjpg-600x326.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10559584" class="wp-caption-text">奶奶惊见孙女穿着六十多年前她自己的舞会礼服！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>老人显然完全缺乏准备，她低头掏出手帕拭着泪水，更紧紧将外孙女拥在怀里。毫无疑问，那一刻为人祖母的幸福无以言表。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10559586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10559586" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screenshot-2017-09-08-11.33.33.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10559586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Screenshot-2017-09-08-11.33.33.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10559586" class="wp-caption-text">奶奶紧紧将孙女拥在怀里。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这条裙子一定带回了老人许多美好的回忆，还有满满的骄傲、慈爱和“受宠若惊”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10559598" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10559598" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/34879395_102119.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10559598 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/34879395_102119-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10559598" class="wp-caption-text">这条裙子一定带回了老人许多美好的回忆，还有满满的骄傲、慈爱和“受宠”的幸福。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凯西的舅妈吉莲（Angela?Gillam）提前知道秘密，架好了录像设备。她在YouTube分享这段<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%8A%E5%96%9C.md#1">惊喜</a>影片，并在介绍中写道：“不用说，每个人都流下了好多泪水！”影片迄今获得800多万次观看。</p>
