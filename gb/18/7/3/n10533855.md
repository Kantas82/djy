<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兽医给美洲豹准妈妈做剖腹产 结果有惊有喜</h2>
<a href="https://dylb6p9h9pzya.cloudfront.net/P9pBHW6Wb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ukkmvh324/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10533974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月04日讯】这只影片展示医务人员帮助一支<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E8%B1%B9.md#1">美洲豹</a>准妈妈做剖腹产的过程，不用多说，他们真的干得很漂亮！</p>
<p>这只<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E8%B1%B9.md#1">美洲豹</a>名字叫西娜（Xena），以前曾经怀孕并自然生产，但不知什么原因，它把上一胎刚出生的<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E8%B1%B9.md#1">幼豹</a>杀死了——当然，听起来是有些残忍，但这在<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">动物</a>界还是不少见的现象。杀死幼仔的事多数是雄豹干的，但是雌豹杀死自己幼崽的事情也时常发生。有的专家认为这和动物的压力反应有关，特别容易发生在圈养的动物身上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a-6-600x322.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10533921 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/a-6-600x322.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533921" class="wp-caption-text">西娜很长寿。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>正是因为担心西娜再次干出杀幼崽的事情，<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">动物</a>园的兽医们决定为它做剖腹产手术，这场手术是在西班牙的一家兽医医院El Bosque动物医院做的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Vid-01-NTD-Jaguar-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10533916 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Vid-01-NTD-Jaguar-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533916" class="wp-caption-text">医务人员帮助一支美洲豹准妈妈做剖腹产。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>医生们将西娜做全身麻醉、剃毛、消毒，在无菌的手术室进行剖腹产手术，手术十分成功，但是却有一个严重问题——新出生的<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BC%E8%B1%B9.md#1">幼豹</a>完全不会呼吸，一动也不动！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b-2-600x318.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10533920 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b-2-600x318.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533920" class="wp-caption-text">新出生的幼豹完全不会呼吸。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>医务人员立刻行动——胸内直接注射药物、心脏按压，甚至口对口人工呼吸都用上了，8分钟之后，小幼豹才恢复心跳，大家给它取名阿里（Ali）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c-5-600x318.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10533933 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c-5-600x318.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533933" class="wp-caption-text">小幼豹名叫阿里（Ali）。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很幸运，阿里被放回妈妈身边后，没有再发生母豹杀幼崽的事情，希望它能像它的妈妈西娜那样长寿吧！</p>
