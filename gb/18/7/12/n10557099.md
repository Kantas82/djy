<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>港文凭试放榜 首次有9个5**状元</h2>
<a href="https://dtfavhq5d1ym2.cloudfront.net/V0zY84Y6p"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imabdi3119/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/imabdi3119/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月12日讯】<span class="s1">香港第六届中学<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%87%AD%E8%AF%95.md#1">文凭试</a>放榜，</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">男</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">女考个</span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">科5**以上，全部来自传统名校。其中一名考生取得九个5**，是<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%87%AD%E8%AF%95.md#1">文凭试</a>开办六年来首次。除了选学医外，多位状元选读政治或法律，盼为社会公义发声。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10557120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10557120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807100517001366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10557120" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807100517001366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10557120" class="wp-caption-text">2018年<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%AD%A6%E6%96%87%E5%87%AD%E8%AF%95.md#1">中学文凭试</a>（DSE）中国语文科卷一试场相片。（考评局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">首位考取9个5**的“超级状元”，是喇沙书院黄子衡。他修读8科连同数学延伸部分，全部考取5**，志愿是读医。钢琴和大提琴都考取演奏级的他，今次考多一门音乐科，没想到因此摘冠。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1704070038281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10557126 aligncenter" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1704070038281366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">黄子衡表示：“当时选择九科，希望多一些保障，有多一科可以让大学去考虑，选择音乐是因为我很醉心音乐，我的人生就是音乐，没有音乐不行。”</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">完整影片：</span></p>
<p class="p1"><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-1dce9384-20c0-4dab-78b2-4745253eee21" data-id="player-1dce9384-20c0-4dab-78b2-4745253eee21"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/1dce9384-20c0-4dab-78b2-4745253eee21?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F12%2Fn10557099.md#1"></a></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另外，8名状元分别来自圣保罗男女中学、皇仁书院、男拔、女拔和德望书院。皇仁书院有一名8个5**状元，志愿是读医。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">皇仁书院状元袁玮谦指：“因为曾我打球打到脱臼，见医生真的可以帮到人脱离所谓苦难，所以我认为医生是一份很有意义的工作。”</span></p>
