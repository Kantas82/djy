<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老人落冰河 消防队上演高效“17分钟救援”</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYAKg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zdzfji373/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/zdzfji373/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月25日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）爱沙尼亚塔林（Tallinn）市有位老人，钓鱼时不幸落入结冰的哈库（Harku）河中。消防队接到目击者报告，紧急驱车到现场将其救上岸，前后只用了17分钟。高效<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>过程后被剪辑成小影片，获得网友称叹。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>委员会通讯处（Communication Office of The Rescue Board）顾问梅里斯．皮勒（Meelis Piller）说：“有人从窗户看到他，并打电话给112（爱沙尼亚的消防队及救护车电话）。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10589815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10589815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/260fa3281aa276dc62fdc76a9db2d3e1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10589815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/260fa3281aa276dc62fdc76a9db2d3e1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10589815" class="wp-caption-text">消防队员为节省时间，在车上着装。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紧急电话进来后，消防队员西姆．马尔瓦（Siim Malva）和马可仕．塔梅（Margus Tamme）立即冲了出去。影片中显示，消防车紧急赶往救援现场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10589816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10589816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/43eefc2bda60fec59b62eb83025f05d3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10589816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/43eefc2bda60fec59b62eb83025f05d3-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10589816" class="wp-caption-text">消防队员小跑步前往河边。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-19993e0f-86cf-497e-53b0-ed6ff6c5489c" data-id="player-19993e0f-86cf-497e-53b0-ed6ff6c5489c"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/19993e0f-86cf-497e-53b0-ed6ff6c5489c?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F25%2Fn10589373.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>消防队员花3分钟准备着装待命，并争分夺秒在车里进行着装；否则多3分钟，遇险者或许就救不回来了——在冷水中15分钟就可能致命，何况是一位老人。</p>
<p>下车后，消防队员们战战兢兢，小跑步前往现场。他们到达河边，已经有其他同仁先到。在河面放上冰上救援滑板后，消防队员们立即向老人遇险的位置趋近。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10589823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10589823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e9032882c2b70d3e2b4f8f641cb6cef0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10589823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e9032882c2b70d3e2b4f8f641cb6cef0-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10589823" class="wp-caption-text">落在<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%B2%B3.md#1">冰河</a>中的老人。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所幸受困老人还未冻僵，等到消防队员来到身边后，立即抓住了滑板。当时他的双手还拿着一个碎冰锥，来防止救援未到时自己陷得更深。</p>
<p>老人攀上滑板后就趴在上面，上岸后立即被送往医院进行体检。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10589829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10589829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5ac5b7c36f8a229321b71188fd254e1c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10589829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5ac5b7c36f8a229321b71188fd254e1c-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10589829" class="wp-caption-text">老人正在攀上滑板。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从爱沙尼亚的紧急救援服务，可以看出他们动作相当快速，下午2:14收到求救讯息，下午2:31掉进<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%B2%B3.md#1">冰河</a>里的老者就被拉出河面，间隔仅仅17分钟。</p>
<p>谢天谢地，并非每位掉进河里的人都能那么<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B8%E8%BF%90.md#1">幸运</a>，更何况是冰河。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10589818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10589818" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/342bab32112340cea3bf01f8b401ed1a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10589818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/342bab32112340cea3bf01f8b401ed1a-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10589818" class="wp-caption-text">老人趴于滑板上。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而此事件也告诉我们，在<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%96%84%E5%86%B0.md#1">薄冰</a>上活动相当<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B1%E9%99%A9.md#1">危险</a>。</p>
<p>看了影片后，网友纷纷留言：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“喔，天呐，可怜的男子，竟然掉进冰河，好险消防队员设法及时抢救了他。”“（指消防队）很专业，谢谢他们！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“几年前我在爱沙尼亚度过了一个学期，从那时起就欣赏爱沙尼亚人的效率。”“他很<ahref="https://github.com/zdzfji373/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B8%E8%BF%90.md#1">幸运</a>，有人及时伸出援手，再多几分钟就会呈现失温状态了。”</p>
