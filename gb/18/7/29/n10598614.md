<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>酪梨放入鸡胸煎煮 光用看的就口水直流！</h2>
<a href="https://d3caox32lipp7h.cloudfront.net/S5yoS75cc"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rrjbqx321/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）原产于美洲的<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>（又称<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%B2%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">牛油果</a>、鳄梨），被金氏世界纪录誉为“最营养的水果”，除了直接食用，也是上好的食材。在此就介绍一道结合<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>的美味酪梨料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10599782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10599782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cf3270c24c145ed18fb38b22901d4286.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10599782 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cf3270c24c145ed18fb38b22901d4286-450x396.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10599782" class="wp-caption-text">超级营养的<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>首先，要准备好以下食材：</strong></span></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a><br />
葡萄醋1/3杯<br />
牛至（又称奥勒冈草）1又?汤匙<br />
大蒜粉1汤匙<br />
红辣椒粉1又?汤匙<br />
橄榄油<br />
番茄（切碎）<br />
酪梨（去籽去皮切碎）<br />
洋葱（切碎）<br />
罗勒（切碎）<br />
盐<br />
胡椒粉</p>
<p>注：1汤匙约15毫升，1杯约240毫升。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #000000;">步骤：</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10599754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10599754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/53000806d3033f028423a63ffa586642.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10599754 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/53000806d3033f028423a63ffa586642-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10599754" class="wp-caption-text">将鸡胸肉割成如<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E8%A2%8B.md#1">口袋</a>样。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 将鸡胸肉用刀子往侧边插入切割，记得不要插穿另一侧，要将鸡胸肉割成像“<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E8%A2%8B.md#1">口袋</a>”。</p>
<p>2. 在一个大碗中加入1/3杯葡萄醋、牛至、大蒜粉、红辣椒粉、盐、胡椒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10599767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10599767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c39d6dfbdbf8fc42ca451c3255a1627e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10599767 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c39d6dfbdbf8fc42ca451c3255a1627e-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10599767" class="wp-caption-text">将鸡胸肉放进此碗中搅拌。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把鸡胸肉放进第二步骤使用的碗中，搅拌一下，<ahref="https://github.com/rrjbqx321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8C%E5%88%B6.md#1">腌制</a>4至5小时。</p>
<p>4. 准备另一个碗，将番茄、酪梨、洋葱、罗勒以上四样食材切碎放入，再加进盐、胡椒、橄榄油，将碗内食材搅拌一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10599746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10599746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/63fe96397400a308f7a1f2e02d26af98.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10599746 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/63fe96397400a308f7a1f2e02d26af98-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10599746" class="wp-caption-text">第四步骤做的混合物放鸡胸肉里。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将第四步骤做好的混合物放入鸡胸里，并用三根牙签将鸡胸开口固定。</p>
<p>6. 将煎锅用中高火加热，倒入一些橄榄油。把第五步骤的鸡胸肉放入煎锅，煮至金黄咖啡色，然后降至中火。继续煎，中途记得将鸡胸肉翻转一下，煎至完美。</p>
<p>7. 用一些罗勒点缀。</p>
<p>这时就可以享用鸡胸肉和酪梨做成的美食啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10599741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10599741" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1f217dca4270975193f496dd641deec0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10599741 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1f217dca4270975193f496dd641deec0-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10599741" class="wp-caption-text">美味的鸡胸肉美食完成啰！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
