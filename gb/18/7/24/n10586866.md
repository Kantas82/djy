<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>4款欧姆蛋料理 使用夹链袋制作超快捷</h2>
<a href="https://d22qnxkzs9qeoh.cloudfront.net/J3qTYMD6r"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yacvxo3623/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B9%E9%93%BE%E8%A2%8B.md#1">夹链袋</a>是许多人爱用的日常用品，可以用来存放物品，也可用来替食物保鲜，但是，各位知道夹链袋也能用来料理吗？在此就为各位介绍使用夹链袋制作美味的<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%A7%86%E8%9B%8B.md#1">欧姆蛋</a>（Omelette，又称<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%BC%8F%E8%9B%8B%E9%A5%BC.md#1">西式蛋饼</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%84%E5%88%97.md#1">奄列</a>），烹饪方法非常简单，而且只需5分钟！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10587743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10587743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3732a6c369ac62d09534c4f7d48bcfa3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587743 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3732a6c369ac62d09534c4f7d48bcfa3-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10587743" class="wp-caption-text">耐高温<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B9%E9%93%BE%E8%A2%8B.md#1">夹链袋</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>需要准备什么？</strong></span></h2>
<p>? 杯子<br />
? 耐高温夹链袋<br />
（推荐用Costco就能买到的ZIPLOC密保诺拉链式冷冻保鲜夹链袋，适用于微波炉，可耐热到105°C ，且不含双酚A。）<br />
? 中等大小的平底深锅，装半满的沸水<br />
? 鸡蛋及配料（下方列出四种）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10587744" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10587744" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/590610d92087036938bc36b2ab02b359.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587744 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/590610d92087036938bc36b2ab02b359-450x433.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10587744" class="wp-caption-text">杯子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-79c95546-3a5f-4733-7787-02ec987a9bf5" data-id="player-79c95546-3a5f-4733-7787-02ec987a9bf5"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/79c95546-3a5f-4733-7787-02ec987a9bf5?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F24%2Fn10586866.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>做法：</strong></span></h2>
<p>? 步骤1：取一个夹链袋，将其打开，将打开的袋子放入杯中。</p>
<p>? 步骤2：将<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%A7%86%E8%9B%8B.md#1">欧姆蛋</a>的所有成分放入袋中，从鸡蛋开始，然后加入所有配料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10587759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10587759" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7d1244cdb609140eae7c72da11f2cca5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587759 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7d1244cdb609140eae7c72da11f2cca5-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10587759" class="wp-caption-text">将所有食材放进夹链袋里。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>? 步骤3：锁好袋子，确保密封良好。大力摇晃袋子，使各种食材混合在一起。</p>
<p>? 步骤4：将所有配料均匀混合成的“蛋混合物”向下推到夹链袋底部。</p>
<p>? 步骤5：此时的锅子应当已放在炉子上，水轻轻沸腾。将装有蛋浆混合物的夹链袋放进水中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10587760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10587760" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/137b8b690a4b61768dde5fcfb471d2f9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587760 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/137b8b690a4b61768dde5fcfb471d2f9-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10587760" class="wp-caption-text">将装有食材的夹链袋放进水中煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>确保水位高于袋子有蛋混合物的部分，并确保袋子牢固密封，至少浸泡5分钟，直到蛋浆混合物完全煮熟。</p>
<p>? 步骤6：将夹链袋从水中取出，打开拉链，将成品放在盘子上，再加点自己喜欢的装饰和调味料就可以吃啰！</p>
