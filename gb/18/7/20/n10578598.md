<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>消防员深井中苦救金毛 狗狗热情示谢融化人心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC8197400000578-0-image-a-41_1490887926348-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（视频截图／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月21日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）一只<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>从深井中获救后，围着抱它上来的消防员翻来滚去，表达感谢，还给恩人一个大大拥抱，暖心一幕被分享出来，感动众多网友。</p>
<p>据英国《每日邮报》报导，去年3月，一只<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%AF%9B.md#1">金毛</a>被困在安徽省合肥市一口3米深的枯井里。一位叫苗康雷（Miao Kanglei，音）的消防员下井将它救了上来。这只宠物狗太开心了，围着年轻人开始撒欢示谢。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-6732e743-38e3-4abd-44f7-ffd5c6ac4665" data-id="player-6732e743-38e3-4abd-44f7-ffd5c6ac4665"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/6732e743-38e3-4abd-44f7-ffd5c6ac4665?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F20%2Fn10578598.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>安徽消防部门人员告诉媒体，狗狗落入的这口井位于双峰路和北淮南路的交界处，当时饲主正带着它在那里散步。</p>
<p>爱犬失足后，饲主立即联系消防部门求助。合肥消防队赶到后，在井口搭了一个救援用三角架，将苗康雷放到了井下。</p>
<p>影片记录了这位25岁的消防员帮狗狗解困的过程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578616" style="width: 306px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC77C4E00000578-0-image-a-29_1490886766842.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10578616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC77C4E00000578-0-image-a-29_1490886766842.jpg" alt="" width="306" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578616" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>救援中，<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%AF%9B.md#1">金毛</a>看上去很害怕，在消防员怀里四脚乱踢、挣扎不休。苗的同事则加紧向上收紧绳子，连人带狗将他们拉了上来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578615" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578615" style="width: 306px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC77BE700000578-0-image-a-31_1490886779212.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10578615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC77BE700000578-0-image-a-31_1490886779212.jpg" alt="" width="306" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578615" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>年轻人的橘色衣服上沾满泥水，完成救援的他显得很疲惫，坐在地上休息。但没过多久，狗狗的感恩举动就让他完全沉浸在喜悦之中 。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578614" style="width: 306px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC76E8200000578-0-image-a-10_1490886634985.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10578614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC76E8200000578-0-image-a-10_1490886634985.jpg" alt="" width="306" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578614" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就在他筋疲力竭时，获救金毛超开心地黏上来蹭他的腿，还站起来拥抱他。消防员欣慰地拍拍它的头；在狗狗围着他摇尾巴打滚时，也和狗狗玩了一会儿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10578613" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10578613" style="width: 306px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC76E8F00000578-0-image-a-30_1490886771620.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10578613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3EC76E8F00000578-0-image-a-30_1490886771620.jpg" alt="" width="306" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10578613" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他表示，狗狗的表现让他很感动。</p>
