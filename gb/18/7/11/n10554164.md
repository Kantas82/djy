<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>婴儿车装了这玩意儿后 竟也可用来“遛狗”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者陈安报导）<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF%E8%BD%A6.md#1">婴儿车</a>是如今父母们离不开的工具，安有座椅或小床的小推车，可以充当宝宝们外出时的舒适“小窝”，不过，大家应该没看过婴儿车可以顺便“遛狗”的吧？</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看影片完整版&gt;&gt; </span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-875063c5-2b0a-40d7-79c7-424e235b1c4a" data-id="player-875063c5-2b0a-40d7-79c7-424e235b1c4a"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/875063c5-2b0a-40d7-79c7-424e235b1c4a?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F11%2Fn10554164.md#1"></a></p>
<p>影片中的男子，推着一台<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF%E8%BD%A6.md#1">婴儿车</a>，令人眼睛为之一亮的是，车旁竟有一只<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>也跟着“滑动”，这到底是怎么回事？！仔细一看，原来是婴儿车的轮子上安装了个小<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%91%E6%9D%BF.md#1">滑板</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10556792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10556792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2c8da62f4f8a0c3d25bd1f2d86f00fdc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10556792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2c8da62f4f8a0c3d25bd1f2d86f00fdc-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10556792" class="wp-caption-text">婴儿车装了“<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%91%E6%9D%BF.md#1">滑板</a>”，可以顺便遛狗。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>婴儿车结合滑板，并不是新鲜事，这样的设计，让爸爸妈妈们在推车时只要脚下一蹬，就享受到凉风吹拂的快感。不过影片又展示了一种新的应用方式：“遛狗”，不过前提是&#8230;&#8230;<ahref="https://github.com/liyrqe362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>要“够乖”。</p>
<p>网友看后纷纷回应：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“可以让狗狗和宝宝一起散步，好甜蜜、聪明。”“狗狗、宝宝同时过马路时，这是一个安全的好方法！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“好可爱，老狗狗长途跋涉，如果很累的时候还可以‘搭便车’，这毛孩主人好！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10556794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10556794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/92de22c46c6cbb0f83f31e0990672dee.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10556794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/92de22c46c6cbb0f83f31e0990672dee-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10556794" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗踩“滑板”看镜头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
