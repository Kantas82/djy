<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全垒打大赛星光黯淡 投手充数参与？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-812187156-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2017年全垒打大赛冠军法官（Aaron Judge）(Photo by Mike Ehrmann/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月11日讯】（大纪元记者宋昇桦报导）距离<ahref="https://github.com/bnfhly397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E5%9E%92%E6%89%93%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">全垒打大赛</a>还不到一个礼拜，参加选手很欠缺，明星球员参赛目前只有国民少年强打<ahref="https://github.com/bnfhly397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E6%B3%A2%EF%BC%88bryce-harper.md#1">哈波（Bryce Harper</a>)撑场，去年<ahref="https://github.com/bnfhly397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E5%9E%92%E6%89%93%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%9B.md#1">全垒打大赛</a>冠军洋基法官（Aaron Judge），和前年冠军怪力男（Giancarlo Stanton）都确认不参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10555128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10555128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-546313776-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10555128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-546313776-1-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10555128" class="wp-caption-text">2016年全垒打大赛冠军怪力男（Giancarlo Stanton）(Photo by Harry How/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛杉矶道奇队新秀 Max Muncy是第一位确认参加这次全垒打大赛的球员，入围国联明星赛小熊队 Javier Baez和队友 Kyle Schwarber在昨天也点头参加。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bnfhly397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E6%B3%A2%EF%BC%88bryce-harper.md#1">哈波（Bryce Harper</a>)是因为今年明星赛主场关系才报名参加，不然他自从2013年全垒打大赛，赢得了亚军以后就没再参与了，波士顿红袜队的 Mookie Betts和洛杉矶天使队的 Mike Trout表示他们没有兴趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10555138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10555138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-456606358.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10555138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-456606358-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10555138" class="wp-caption-text">国民队少年强打哈波(Bryce Harper)(Photo by Elsa/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年全垒打大赛星光黯淡，就有投手网站专栏作家 Alex Fast，建议让红人队 Michael Lorenzen参加全垒打大赛，因为他是今年连三个打数打出全垒打的投手，破了大联盟的纪录，而且在七月一号代打打出满贯炮。</p>
<p>自从大谷翔平加入大联盟之后，现在掀起一股二刀流风，投手兼打击，专栏作家表示透过他的参与能创造大联盟的新历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10555145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10555145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-988401060-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10555145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-988401060-2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10555145" class="wp-caption-text">红人队投手 Michael Lorenzen(Photo by Joe Robbins/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前全垒打大赛纪录是由前年(2016年)怪力男（Giancarlo Stanton）61发全垒打所保持，去年全垒打大赛冠军洋基法官（Aaron Judge）有五发全垒打超过500英尺，也让人惊艳。</p>
