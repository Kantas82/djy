<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>两只座头鲸跳水中芭蕾 舞动胸鳍像天使翅膀可爱极了</h2>
<a href="https://dmepc9x6zy689.cloudfront.net/N3gohiZYa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gmzozr3243/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月10日讯】空拍机在夏威夷海洋上空，拍到了两只<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A7%E5%A4%B4%E9%B2%B8.md#1">座头鲸</a>“共舞”并淘气喷水的难得画面！体型巨大又美丽的座头<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%B8.md#1">鲸</a>，挥舞一双特长的胸鳍，当翻转时，浅色胸鳍在海中就像一对天使的翅膀，可爱极了！</p>
<p>多么惊奇又令人赞叹的画面呀！两只<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A7%E5%A4%B4%E9%B2%B8.md#1">座头鲸</a>在水中“打闹”，一会儿喷水，一会儿钻到伙伴肚子底下，接着又跳起了优雅双人“<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%AD%E8%8A%AD%E8%95%BE.md#1">水中芭蕾</a>”，让人看得目不暇给。</p>
<p>座头<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%B8.md#1">鲸</a>生性温和、好奇，澳洲有座头鲸在潜水员身边<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%B3%E8%88%9E.md#1">跳舞</a>、转圈，南极也有座头鲸看到人类的<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%B9%E8%89%87.md#1">船艇</a>没有躲开，而在船边游来游去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10603486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10603486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-31_155845.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10603486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-31_155845-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10603486" class="wp-caption-text">两只座头鲸在水中“打闹”，又跳起了优雅双人“<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%AD%E8%8A%AD%E8%95%BE.md#1">水中芭蕾</a>”。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>它们很有“家庭观念”，通常会成双成对出没，有时也会出现座头鲸父母带着孩子的“家庭出游”场景。活泼的座头鲸喜欢跃出水面，喷发水柱并且发出丰富的鸣叫，是许多赏鲸者的最爱。</p>
<p>但全世界仅剩3.5万只座头鲸，不起眼的海洋垃圾──钓鱼线和渔网，座头鲸一旦被勾住就无法自己脱困。目前救援团队只好以“发现一只救一只”的方式帮助它们。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10603504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10603504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-31_155905.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10603504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2018-07-31_155905-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10603504" class="wp-caption-text">活泼的座头鲸喜欢跃出水面，喷发水柱并且发出丰富的鸣叫。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
