<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法：</span></h2>
<p><strong>熊的棉被和枕头：</strong></p>
<p>1. 将蛋和鲜奶油一起搅拌均匀，增加棉被的蓬松口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10604379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10604379" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/12044e3a6641f0cf6b7a4aad7a62e280.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10604379 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/12044e3a6641f0cf6b7a4aad7a62e280-450x375.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10604379" class="wp-caption-text">将蛋和鲜奶油打散混合。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 加入切碎的巧达起司到蛋汁中，再加入盐和楜椒粉搅拌均匀。</p>
<p>3. 热锅后放入奶油，倒入蛋汁，以小火煎熟。</p>
<p>4. 将煎好的蛋皮切下1/3当枕头，剩下的2/3蛋皮当棉被。</p>
<p>5. 在枕头中间放入莫札瑞拉起司，并卷成枕头的造型。</p>
<p>6. 将做好的枕头和棉被放一旁静置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10604386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10604386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/157e40b7cd40315793f0b35877d9ed5f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10604386 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/157e40b7cd40315793f0b35877d9ed5f-450x367.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10604386" class="wp-caption-text">先制作蛋皮当棉被和枕头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>熊宝宝饭团：</strong></p>
<p>1. 热锅后放入奶油，将饭炒热。</p>
<p>2. 在锅里加入蛋汁、酱油和蕃茄酱继续拌炒均匀，直到整体颜色一致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10604394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10604394" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c5d03ca6ae228d3a873daffb113033ca.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10604394 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c5d03ca6ae228d3a873daffb113033ca-450x367.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10604394" class="wp-caption-text">将饭拌炒到整体颜色一至。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将炒饭用塑胶袋捏出两个椭圆造型，一大一小，大的当身体，小的当头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10604404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10604404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dd8a2a35cd47f08bb740eab010f186e8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10604404 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dd8a2a35cd47f08bb740eab010f186e8-450x368.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10604404" class="wp-caption-text">用塑胶袋捏造形。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 捏出4个小椭圆造型当手和脚。</p>
<p>5. 剪下2个半圆形的起司片当成耳朵和鼻子。</p>
<p>6.?拼装熊宝宝的四肢。</p>
<p>7.?将烤好的海苔片和起司片拼出眼睛和鼻子造型。</p>
<p>8.?帮熊宝宝盖上棉被就完成了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10603092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10603092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/768e289d1b5fd3f68f7d657c4eb6785f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10603092 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/768e289d1b5fd3f68f7d657c4eb6785f-450x372.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10603092" class="wp-caption-text">在盘子里拼装熊宝宝的造型。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10603094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10603094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1434558acff4996aeae815aee3f7f05c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10603094 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1434558acff4996aeae815aee3f7f05c-450x371.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10603094" class="wp-caption-text">完成品。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
