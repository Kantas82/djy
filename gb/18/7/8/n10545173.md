<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>三仙台日出光雕 五颗热气球迎曙光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dfae1a429554f04da2bf2d79ce4ed1f4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东三仙台日出光雕音乐会。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月08日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）台东<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83.md#1">热气球</a>嘉年华7日上午4点在成功镇<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E4%BB%99%E5%8F%B0.md#1">三仙台</a>进行<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E5%87%BA.md#1">日出</a>光雕音乐会，当清晨曙光出现，大自然的美景与五颗热气球相互辉映，让现场洋溢一片欢呼。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E4%BB%99%E5%8F%B0.md#1">三仙台</a>原本就以曙光<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E5%87%BA.md#1">日出</a>闻名，此次光雕音乐会以跨海八拱桥为背景，搭配斯洛伐克的布鲁诺小丑造型球、英国与法国的猫头鹰及猫头鹰宝宝造型球，与台湾的乳牛球、蜡笔球共五颗<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83.md#1">热气球</a>的光雕展演，当曙光在天际露面，现场球迷纷纷拿起手机或相机捕捉最美的瞬间。</p>
<figure id="10545177" aria-describedby="caption-10545177" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/46ae66b95c22c9383830091b9025ea55-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/46ae66b95c22c9383830091b9025ea55-450x300.jpg" alt="台东三仙台日出光雕音乐会。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10545177" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>三仙台日出光雕音乐会。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="10545176" aria-describedby="caption-10545176" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/8777e9850e2275c699a8e245aafbe66a-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/8777e9850e2275c699a8e245aafbe66a-450x300.jpg" alt="台东三仙台日出光雕音乐会。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-10545176" class="wp-caption-text">台东三仙台日出光雕音乐会。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许多游客清晨3点即已陆续涌入三仙台风景区，联外道路两旁停满车，有些民众前一晚就入住成功镇，生怕错过美景。台东县政府也在清晨2点40分从台东火车站及台东转运站以接驳公车接民众抵达三仙台活动会场。</p>
<p>主办单位今年邀请了刚获得原住民舞蹈比赛冠军的马兰复音青歌队以及来自新北市的非洲螃蟹手鼓团一起表演迎接曙光，让现场充满原住民活力元素。</p>
