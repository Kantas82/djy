<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10537908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-2-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月07日讯】下午茶时光，怎么可以少了甜点的陪伴呢？！不过，甜点选择太多，吃什么好呢？来个缤纷<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E4%B8%B9%E9%BA%A6.md#1">水果丹麦</a>吧！一个人独享刚刚好，跟姐妹、家人分享也超棒！</p>
<p>新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>遇上刚烤好的酥脆丹麦饼皮，甜而不腻的美好滋味，让你吮指回味！</p>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a><br />
（苹果、杏桃、草莓、覆盆莓、蓝莓）<br />
杏桃果酱<br />
奶油乳酪 1/2杯<br />
奶油 1汤匙<br />
糖 5汤匙<br />
蛋 1汤匙<br />
香草精 1/2茶匙<br />
蛋液 少许<br />
起酥皮 适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c35b7eb8577a0f63d40c4a73fe205416.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c35b7eb8577a0f63d40c4a73fe205416-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538903" class="wp-caption-text">想吃甜点吗？那你绝不能错过甜而不腻的缤纷<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E4%B8%B9%E9%BA%A6.md#1">水果丹麦</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.食材处理：将新鲜水果（苹果、杏桃、草莓），切片备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dafdab0cdc4f42ef14fd9b23c301dd21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/dafdab0cdc4f42ef14fd9b23c301dd21-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538901" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜水果切片备用。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.自制酱料：将奶油乳酪、奶油放入容器中，使用叉子均匀拌在一块；再加入糖、蛋、香草精均匀搅拌。放置一旁备用。</p>
<p>3.起酥皮切成4等份。取其中一块，折对角，呈现三角形；将三角形尖朝上，两边稍微用刀片划开后、并摊开，接着，边框交错摆放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/43a79e72caebda8977d6e3b7c7870e5a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/43a79e72caebda8977d6e3b7c7870e5a-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538927" class="wp-caption-text">取其中一块，折对角，将三角形尖朝上，两边稍微用刀片划开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10538928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/446b73c7f26391aea1d5364228343caf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/446b73c7f26391aea1d5364228343caf-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538928" class="wp-caption-text">再将另一边，稍微用刀片划开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10538929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538929" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/bf024398c6fd6088338a4db3ef56e7cc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/bf024398c6fd6088338a4db3ef56e7cc-450x262.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="262" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538929" class="wp-caption-text">摊开铺平后，将边框右向左交错摆放。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10538930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538930" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/af0a5c95b35bd96a56686aa92da40ee4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/af0a5c95b35bd96a56686aa92da40ee4-450x275.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538930" class="wp-caption-text">再将边框左向右交错摆放。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.于饼皮中间，添加一点奶油酱，再摆上新鲜水果，如：杏桃、覆盆莓、蓝莓、苹果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/06723a8a09ef73ec4dd818ace8a01994.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/06723a8a09ef73ec4dd818ace8a01994-450x364.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="364" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538931" class="wp-caption-text">饼皮中间，添加一点奶油酱。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.边框刷上蛋汁，摆放于烤盘。接着，放进<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%AE%B1.md#1">烤箱</a>烘烤约25分钟，温度约控制在摄氏195oC／华氏385oF。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/780936aa1f5e5c0a661c8d5cb0a03fb6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/780936aa1f5e5c0a661c8d5cb0a03fb6-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538932" class="wp-caption-text">边框刷上蛋汁。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.香味诱人的水果丹麦烤好，再刷上一点杏桃果酱，就可以享用啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10538905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10538905" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/6f7c04ab1f48423dbdf06a6be04e8094.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10538905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/6f7c04ab1f48423dbdf06a6be04e8094-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10538905" class="wp-caption-text">诱人香气配上新鲜水果，好吃的滋味，让你一吃就爱上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【备注】食材皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</strong></p>
