<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JORug"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rspjap308/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rspjap308/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月16日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）台湾高雄市<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%9B%AD.md#1">林园</a>区<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%B4%8B%E6%B9%BF%E5%9C%B0%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">海洋湿地公园</a>，是全台唯一的“水母湖”，继两个月前出现罕见的“蓝色仙后”水母后，近日再发现极罕见的“金色仙后”水母。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%B4%8B%E6%B9%BF%E5%9C%B0%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">海洋湿地公园</a>是袖珍型的潟湖生态区，让湿地公园声名大噪的“倒立水母”，巅峰时高达上万只，可爱的模样更是吸引国内外旅客争相到访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10419863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10419863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805230708332378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10419863 size-medium" title="组图：台湾首见“蓝色仙后”水母 游客惊艳" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805230708332378-450x450.jpg" alt="组图：台湾首见“蓝色仙后”水母 游客惊艳" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10419863" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%9B%AD.md#1">林园</a>区海洋湿地公园，出现极罕见的“蓝色仙后”水母，令游客大为惊艳。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10566411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160620352378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10566411 size-medium" title="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160620352378-450x600.jpg" alt="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566411" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市林园区海洋湿地公园的水母湖，7月16日发现罕见的“金色仙后”水母。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“仙后水母”中文别名为“倒立水母”，通常呈现白色、咖啡色透明状。此前出现罕见的“蓝色仙后”水母，令游客大为惊艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10566444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566444" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160718072378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10566444" title="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160718072378-600x337.jpg" alt="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566444" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市林园区海洋湿地公园的夕阳美景。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10567798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10567798" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807162105502378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10567798 size-full" title="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807162105502378.jpg" alt="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10567798" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市林园区海洋湿地公园的夕阳美景。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近日有人发现金色的倒立水母，记者采访专注生态的专业摄影师、“手机猎人”<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0.md#1">宋永泰</a>说，“蓝色仙后”、“金色仙后”水母很特别，而且当地的“黄金夕阳”真的很美，他并提供精彩照片与影片和全球大纪元读者分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10566413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10566413" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160718262378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10566413 size-full" title="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807160718262378.jpg" alt="罕见“金色仙后”水母 现身台湾水母湖" width="337" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10566413" class="wp-caption-text">台湾高雄市林园区海洋湿地公园的夕阳美景。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/people/%E5%AE%8B%E6%B0%B8%E6%B3%B0/100001829995306">摄影师、手机猎人宋永泰提供</a>）</figcaption></figure>
