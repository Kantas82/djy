<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩式泡菜锅饱足感十足 香辣好过瘾</h2>
<a href="https://d1nvr7ffvd2d6n.cloudfront.net/W4kL8HoXs"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/eahkqg304/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/eahkqg304/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10615386 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月21日讯】近年韩国文化的盛行，其中，韩国料理多样选择，更是让喜爱<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F.md#1">韩式</a>料理的人，增添许多美食的口袋名单。常见的韩式锅物──<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a>锅，以<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F.md#1">韩式</a>泡菜为原料，加入嫩豆腐、蔬菜、菇类、海鲜、猪肉和牛肉，以及特制辣酱混合烹调而成。</p>
<p>诱人<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a>香气，再配上一碗香Q白饭，真是绝配！这时的你，是不是也想马上尝尝这道饱足感十足的料理啊？快跟小编一起备料，动手做吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10565763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10565763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c063e1e2acc9b8933b8864929e26bbde.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10565763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c063e1e2acc9b8933b8864929e26bbde-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10565763" class="wp-caption-text">居家自己料理，不但食材丰盛，又能吃得超过瘾。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>嫩豆腐<br />
海鲜<br />
牛肉<br />
猪肉<br />
野菇<br />
泡菜<br />
葱花<br />
韩式辣酱</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.将嫩豆腐、海鲜（或牛肉、猪肉、野菇）等食材，放入锅中炖煮。</p>
<p>2.再依个人喜好，添加泡菜和韩式辣酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10565798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10565798" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d41ef8d229b01941ef41d17c72632cf2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10565798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d41ef8d229b01941ef41d17c72632cf2-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10565798" class="wp-caption-text">喜欢吃辣的你，可依照告人喜好多添加些韩式辣酱。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.等锅中食材炖煮至熟，最后，撒上点葱花，就可以开饭啰！</p>
<p><strong>【备注】</strong>口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
