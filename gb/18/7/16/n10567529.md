<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美国大联盟全垒打大赛 哈波47秒敲9轰夺冠</h2>

<h6>国民强打哈波（Bryce Harper）在全垒打大赛的决赛加时赛胜出，以19发全垒打拿下冠军。(Rob Carr/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月17日讯】2018年美国大联盟明星周年度盛事揭开序幕，7月16日晚全垒打大赛在华盛顿DC<ahref="https://github.com/mclaau366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B0%91.md#1">国民</a>球场点燃战火，17日则是明星赛压轴好戏。在国民主场涌进4万3千多名球迷的欢呼声中，国民强打<ahref="https://github.com/mclaau366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E6%B3%A2.md#1">哈波</a>（Bryce Harper）在全垒打大赛的决赛加时赛胜出，以19发全垒打拿下冠军。</p>
<h2>全垒打大赛</h2>
<p>7月16日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/mclaau366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%8C%E6%A3%92%E5%A4%A7%E8%81%94%E7%9B%9F.md#1">职棒大联盟</a>进行万众瞩目的全垒打大赛，华盛顿<ahref="https://github.com/mclaau366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B0%91.md#1">国民</a>地主球星<ahref="https://github.com/mclaau366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E6%B3%A2.md#1">哈波</a>领衔，面对其他7位首次参战选手的挑战。</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JWxvo"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mclaau366/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/mclaau366/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ixfr9tS9CiU" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>比赛一开始先由头号种子阿吉拉尔（Jesus Aguilar）率先碰上霍斯金斯（Rhys Hoskins），结果没想到最终是由霍斯金斯以17:12爆冷击败对手，取得晋级下一轮的资格。</p>
<p>国民一哥哈波是美国国旗穿上身，从球棒、袖套到球鞋都有星条旗的图案，展现国家风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10568316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10568316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000402472.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10568316 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000402472-600x400.jpg" alt="WASHINGTON, DC - JULY 16: Bryce Harper of the Washington Nationals and National League competes during the T-Mobile Home Run Derby at Nationals Park on July 16, 2018 in Washington, DC." width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10568316" class="wp-caption-text">国民一哥哈波美国国旗穿上身，从球棒、袖套到球鞋都有星条旗的图案，展现国家风格。 (Patrick Smith/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在前两轮，哈波各以13轰闯过勇士强打弗里曼（Freddie Freeman）和道奇黑马曼西（Max Muncy）的阻拦，来到决赛遭遇小熊强棒史瓦柏（Kyle Schwarber），在比赛时间剩下47秒时铆足全力，硬是狂敲9支全垒打，追平史瓦柏的18轰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10568317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10568317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000397292.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10568317 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000397292-600x402.jpg" alt="WASHINGTON, DC - JULY 16: Bryce Harper of the Washington Nationals and National League hits his final home run to win the T-Mobile Home Run Derby at Nationals Park on July 16, 2018 in Washington, DC. Harper defeated Kyle Schwarber of the Chicago Cubs and National League 19-18." width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10568317" class="wp-caption-text">哈波在3轮竞技共挥出45轰夺冠。 (Patrick Smith/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈波在3轮竞技共挥出45轰，史瓦柏虽然打出更多的55轰却惜败；扛出最大轰的是小熊球星贝兹（Javier Baez），在第一轮就敲出飞行479呎的球；8位参赛者合敲221轰也是史上最多的一次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10568321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10568321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000391610.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10568321 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000391610-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10568321" class="wp-caption-text">哈波在3轮竞技共挥出45轰夺冠。(Rob Carr/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10568306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10568306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000403134.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10568306 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000403134-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10568306" class="wp-caption-text">国民主场4万3千多名球迷观看比赛。(Patrick McDermott/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次比赛是分4组进行，按8位参赛者目前全垒打支数，第1对第8，第4对第5，第2对第7，第3对第6，前两组出线者对决，后两组出线者硬碰硬，再出线者争夺冠军。</p>
<p>首轮的对战组合分别是：酿酒人的阿吉拉尔（24轰）对上费城人的霍斯金斯（14轰）、太空人强打布雷格曼（Alex Bregman，20轰）对上小熊重炮史瓦柏（Kyle Schwarber，18轰）；国民一哥哈波（23轰）对勇士当家重炮弗里曼（Freddie Freeman，16轰），以及道奇黑马曼西（Max Muncy，22轰）对上小熊内野美记制造机贝兹（Javier Baez，19轰）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10568312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10568312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000402598.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10568312 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-1000402598-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10568312" class="wp-caption-text">比赛是分4组进行，按8位参赛者目前全垒打支数，第1对第8，第4对第5，第2对第7，第3对第6，前两组出线者对决，后两组出线者硬碰硬，再出线者争夺冠军。(Patrick Smith/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>遗憾的是，今年的全垒打大赛多名好手都选择不参加，以29轰并列第一的红袜马丁尼兹（J.D.Martinez）和印地安人拉米瑞兹（Jose Ramirez）都未参战，3位25轰的贾吉（Aaron Judge）、林多（Francisco Lindor）与特劳特（Mike Trout）也休息。</p>
