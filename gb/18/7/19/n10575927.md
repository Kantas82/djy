<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台加艺文节落幕 古谣歌声仍绕梁</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月20日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（记者余天白温哥华报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在刚刚过去的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">末，如果你走在温哥华市中心繁忙的街道上，可能会时不时听到一阵似乎从东方高山中乘风飘来、又经溪水洗涤后的悠扬民歌声，让人不禁放慢脚步，去一探这都市中淳朴之音的源头。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">这一被形容为“能从歌中看山听风”的天籁之音发自台湾“<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E6%AD%A6%E5%8F%A4%E8%B0%A3%E4%BC%A0%E5%94%B1.md#1">泰武古谣传唱</a>”团体的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名少男少女口中。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2018</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8A%A0%E8%89%BA%E6%96%87%E8%8A%82.md#1">台加艺文节</a>在温哥华市中心女皇剧院广场（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Queen Elizabeth Theatre Plaza</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）上演，邀请台湾和本地的艺文团体举行集演唱、舞蹈、画展、盆栽展、民俗手工艺展于一堂的文艺盛事。闻声国际的<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E6%AD%A6%E5%8F%A4%E8%B0%A3%E4%BC%A0%E5%94%B1.md#1">泰武古谣传唱</a>演唱团此次也获邀从台湾专程飞至温哥华，在<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8A%A0%E8%89%BA%E6%96%87%E8%8A%82.md#1">台加艺文节</a>现场面向公众进行多次献唱。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10575950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fullsizeoutput_905-e1532041926819.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fullsizeoutput_905-e1532041926819.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575950" class="wp-caption-text">图：排湾族少年们身着为祭典准备的隆重族服，赤脚站在舞台上放声吟唱。（摄影：余天白）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0304-e1532042178928.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0304-e1532042178928.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575946" class="wp-caption-text">图：台加艺文节在温哥华女皇剧院广场举行，图为艺文节现场与艺术表演。 （余天白/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0345-e1532042145253.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0345-e1532042145253.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575948" class="wp-caption-text">图：台加艺文节在温哥华女皇剧院广场举行，图为艺文节现场与工艺品展示。 （余天白/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">泰武古谣传唱的演唱内容为台湾原住民族群</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&#8211;</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">排湾族的传统古调。排湾族少年们身着为祭典</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">备的隆重族服，赤脚站在舞台上放声吟唱，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">干</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">净的声音将原住民质朴的内心毫无保留地展现在这片远隔重洋的异域土地上。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“排湾族没有文字，我们就把很多的故事、历史、文化放在歌谣里面。很多歌谣是对唱式的，歌词是即兴的。虽然旋律是固定的，但是演唱的人会根据不同的地方、不同的时间唱不同的歌。”泰武古谣传唱音乐指导查马克向《大纪元》介绍道。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10575942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575942" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0241-1-e1532041912446.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0241-1-e1532041912446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575942" class="wp-caption-text">图说：台湾民俗手工艺展上用红包纸、厕所纸芯、杂志纸等制作的工艺品体现出巧思妙手与环保理念。图为红包纸制成的公鸡饰品。（摄影：余天白）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0258-e1532042206983.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0258-e1532042206983.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575944" class="wp-caption-text">图：台湾“真善美社会福利基金会”指导智力残障人士做手工用于义卖，鼓励其生活自立。图为基金会从台湾寄出、在台加艺文节上售卖的玫瑰花与玩具熊。（摄影：余天白）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10575943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10575943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0243-1-e1532042223301.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_0243-1-e1532042223301.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10575943" class="wp-caption-text">图：台加艺文节在温哥华女皇剧院广场举行，图为艺文节现场与工艺品展示。 （余天白/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">除泰武古谣传唱外，前太阳马戏团（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cirque Du Soleil</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）首席舞者张逸军也受邀成为本届艺文节艺术总监并亲自出演。艺文节现场还有本地台湾舞蹈团体、苏格兰传统乡间舞蹈、由多族裔成员组成的“世界之声”乐团、巴西舞蹈、国际标</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">舞等艺文演出轮番上台，台湾民俗手工艺展、由加西台湾艺术家协会举办的“花花世界”画展也吸引市民观看。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“因为温哥华是一个多元文化的社会，所以我们希望在呈现台湾元素的同时带进苏格兰、巴西这些其它文化，做一个文化的交流。”主办方台加文化协会理事长林挺睦对《大纪元》表示。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
