<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大温独立屋正进入买方市场</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月20日讯】（<span style="font-weight: 400;">记者岳川温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，大温地产局（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）发布今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份统计报告。由于销售放缓，挂牌房屋数量增加，独立屋售挂比降至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，表明大温正悄然进入<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B0%E6%96%B9%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">买方市场</a>。均衡市场则应在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">之间。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大温地产局局长菲尔</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">莫尔称：“随着需求下降，独立屋正在进入<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B0%E6%96%B9%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">买方市场</a>。待售房屋供应增加，这是过去几年未曾见过的现象。利率的上升、高房价和抵押贷款限制性更大的规定等等因素，均抑制了买家。”</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">城市屋和公寓的供应也有所改善，但尚不足以进入均衡市场。二者售挂比分别为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">24.9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">33.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">尽</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">管销售势头不及近期，但仍为卖方市场。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">总体而言，今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，该地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">MLS</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">房屋销量总计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,425</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">37.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，较今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月小幅峰值后仅一个月就下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。上月交易总量较最近</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份平均销量低</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">28.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2012</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年以来</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份最低水平。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">销售放缓正抑制整体价格上涨，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份综合基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价（涵盖所有住宅类型）较去年同期上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但与今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,093,600</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元相比则几乎持平。同时，综合基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价还掩盖了市与市之间、社区和社区之间的巨大差异。例如，惠斯勒（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Whistler)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、斯阔米</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">甚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Squamish</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）、新威斯敏斯特（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">New Westminster</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）和匹特草原（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Pitt Meadows</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的综合基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价同比上涨均超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，而西温房价一般比上年低</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">各类房型销量及售价 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></h2>
<p><b>独立屋：</b> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">销量：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">766</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套；较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">42%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；较今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">926</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套）：下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">17.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。大温地产局将一套典型独立屋定价为：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,598,200</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月仅高出</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，较今年年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。独立屋价格跌幅最大的地区：温西，基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价较去年低</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；西温则低</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。宝云岛（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bowen Island</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）和阳光海岸（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sunshine Coast</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）涨幅最大，独立屋售价同比分别上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15.8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><b>附属物业：</b> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">销量：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">419</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套（含城市屋和双拼屋）；较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">37.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，较今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：下降近</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。由于城市屋供应依然紧张，这类市场的房价虽然增长似乎停滞，但并未下跌。附属物业基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价格目前为：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">859,800</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元；较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：较今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：几乎无变化。惠斯勒和斯阔米</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">甚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">城市屋房价同比：上涨逾</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；措瓦森（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Tsawwassen</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）涨幅最低：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><b>公寓：</b> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">销量：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1240</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套；同比：下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">34.9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：环比：下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。相比于城市屋，公寓销量的降幅更大，但由于卖方市场强劲，库存紧张，房价仍在缓涨中。大温地区公寓基</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">准</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">价为：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">70.4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元；较去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">17.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">；较今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月：上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。莫尔证实，城市屋和公寓市场的价格涨幅显示放缓迹象。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">枫树岭（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Maple Ridge</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）公寓价格年涨幅达惊人的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">40.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，居大温之冠。匹特草原以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">37.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的涨幅紧随其后，惠斯勒公寓涨幅为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。另外</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">——</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">北本拿比、高贵林、新威斯敏斯特、高贵林港、满地宝和斯阔米</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">甚</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">——</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">公寓价格均上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">以上。公寓价格涨幅最低的是温西，为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在整个大温，地区不同，房价差别也很大。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">欲</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">了解按地区、按物业类型统计的房价，请查看大温地产局地产报告中的详细数据。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
