<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《人狼》红毯活动 郑雨盛姜栋元忙与粉丝自拍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190217581487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，男星郑雨盛与粉丝们自拍。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月19日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）韩国电影《人狼》昨（18）日于首尔永登浦区时代广场Time Square举行红毯活动，参演该电影的人气明星<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E6%A0%8B%E5%85%83.md#1">姜栋元</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E9%9B%A8%E7%9B%9B.md#1">郑雨盛</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%AD%9D%E5%91%A8.md#1">韩孝周</a>、金武烈及SHINee成员崔<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%89%E8%B1%AA.md#1">珉豪</a>和导演金知云皆出席，吸引数千名粉丝到场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10574396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190215561487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574396" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190215561487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574396" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，SHINee成员崔<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%89%E8%B1%AA.md#1">珉豪</a>也出席。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10574422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190215521487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574422" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190215521487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574422" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，SHINee崔珉豪于红毯替粉丝签名。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这天的红毯活动，演员们及导演都尽力回应粉丝的热情，不仅拿粉丝们的手机自拍，也于现场直接替众多粉丝签名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10574404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190220261487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574404" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190220261487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574404" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%AD%9D%E5%91%A8.md#1">韩孝周</a>于红毯与粉丝们自拍。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10574386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574386" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190224321487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574386" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190224321487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574386" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，金知云导演替粉丝签名。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10574411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190224291487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574411" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190224291487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574411" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，金武烈与粉丝们自拍。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，主演《人狼》的<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E6%A0%8B%E5%85%83.md#1">姜栋元</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/boadjj333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E9%9B%A8%E7%9B%9B.md#1">郑雨盛</a>，更是因为长时间回应粉丝们的各种拍照及签名请求，迟迟没有走完红毯，让不少在红毯尾端等待的粉丝着急呼喊：“哥哥快来！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10574376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574376" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190020141487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574376" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190020141487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574376" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，主演的姜栋元与粉丝自拍。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10574383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190217551487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574383" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190217551487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574383" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，郑雨盛回应粉丝的签名请求。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于姜栋元耐心回复粉丝们的要求而迟迟未能上台，当天活动的主持人还笑说：“姜栋元今天似乎打算和所有的粉丝都拍到照啊！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10574377" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190020111487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574377" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190020111487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574377" class="wp-caption-text">电影《人狼》举办红毯活动，姜栋元为回应粉丝请求，迟迟走不完红毯。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10574389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190218021487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574389" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190218021487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574389" class="wp-caption-text">电影《人狼》举办红毯活动，郑雨盛为回应粉丝请求而迟迟未能走完红毯。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这天的红毯活动，因为众星们在红毯上“回应粉丝请求”的时间非常长，因此演员们登台谈话的时间也被压缩得很短。姜栋元表示，很感谢大家前来，希望大家能喜爱这次的电影。</p>
<p>韩孝周也同样表达：“这么热的天气，还有这么多人前来支持我们，真的很感谢。希望大家关注《人狼》。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10574416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10574416" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190220321487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10574416" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1807190220321487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10574416" class="wp-caption-text">韩国电影《人狼》于18日举办红毯活动，韩孝珠现身红毯替粉丝们签名。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>电影《人狼JIN-ROH》将于7月25日于韩国上映。</p>
