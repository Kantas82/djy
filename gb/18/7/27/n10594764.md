<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗狗有叼“巨木”怪癖？好个大力金钢牙！</h2>
<a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/W6tLP4FSu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gxhqsr332/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/gxhqsr332/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月27日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/gxhqsr332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>喜欢乱咬东西的特性令人困扰，破了财又要花时间善后。但是有只狗狗，它叼东西的样子不仅令人笑哈哈，也令人吃惊，真可谓“大力金钢牙”。</p>
<p>美国加州特拉基（Truckee）的克利夫．兰姆森（Cl??iff Lambson），发现自家<ahref="https://github.com/gxhqsr332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>威力（Wally）做出了个惊人举动，令他忍不住拍了下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b3ffa32527f2e1d54e2b1e59775b8d72.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10597181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/b3ffa32527f2e1d54e2b1e59775b8d72-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597181" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗叼着一个东西，从远处走来。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>影片拍摄于太浩湖（Lake Tahoe）附近，我们可以看到威力正在咬一个东西，仔细一看，它竟咬了一块“<ahref="https://github.com/gxhqsr332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%9C%A8.md#1">巨木</a>”！</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-25779722-2f06-4d53-6473-ec06aa298185" data-id="player-25779722-2f06-4d53-6473-ec06aa298185"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/25779722-2f06-4d53-6473-ec06aa298185?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F27%2Fn10594764.md#1"></a></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>天呐，这大块头，即使人类搬，可能也会搞得气喘吁吁。结果它竟咬着，发出轻巧走路声：“哒，哒，哒……”走呀走，走呀走，尾巴晃呀晃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1cd9c01efd5bb4e5f599ca43396b0667.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10597183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1cd9c01efd5bb4e5f599ca43396b0667-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597183" class="wp-caption-text">原来狗狗叼着一块<ahref="https://github.com/gxhqsr332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%9C%A8.md#1">巨木</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后威力走向拍摄影片的主人这，一点都不喘，好像这一切根本“没什么”，只是放下巨木时，狗狗嘴巴咬合一下“活动筋骨”。主人对它这叼重物的“勤劳”可爱行为忍不住笑了出来。</p>
<p>真不知道这只狗狗到底在干嘛，闲闲没事咬这么大的东西，是在训练“咬合力”吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4400eed958ca4585531dc7afcf0d3e19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10597185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4400eed958ca4585531dc7afcf0d3e19-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597185" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗叼巨木转弯。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人克利夫说：</p>
<p>“我们在散步，威力有一个长棍子，当它找到这块巨木后，它就把小棍棍换成这‘大棍棍’。</p>
<p>“当它第一次叼起那块巨木时，感觉它的重量大约为40磅（约18公斤），这大块头真是挺重的，使狗狗在走路的时候，后脚都会从地面上抬起。</p>
<p>“对于威力来说，这是很正常的行为，它很喜欢玩，而且总是让我们开怀大笑。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cf8fc5ede5d304909ebe565639ad9ff5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10597188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/cf8fc5ede5d304909ebe565639ad9ff5-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597188" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗放下巨木。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>真是“狗如其名”，名字叫做“威力”，实际上，也确实非常有“威力”，堪称“狗界大力士”。</p>
<p>看了这大力士的行为后，网友纷纷留言：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“真是强壮的狗狗，那真是很大的木头。”“真是意志坚强的小家伙，很可爱！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“强壮的狗狗，大部分的狗狗只能叼小木头，它能叼巨木块！”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“虽然那是腐枯的木头，但还是令人印象深刻。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10597191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10597191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/25f355d42570e07bdbdace9f22b470f1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10597191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/25f355d42570e07bdbdace9f22b470f1-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10597191" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗完成“壮举”后看着镜头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
