<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第49届美东台湾人夏令会在宾州举行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3275-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第四十九届美东台湾人夏令会中的台美关系座谈会。（童云／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<figure id="attachment_10542658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/WUC.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10542658 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/WUC-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542658" class="wp-caption-text">第四十九届<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E4%B8%9C%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BA%BA%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美东台湾人夏令会</a>于7月1日至4日在<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BE%E5%B7%9E.md#1">宾州</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%88%87%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">西切斯特大学</a>（West Chester University)举行。(TACEC官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">【大纪元2018年07月06日讯】（大纪元记者童云西切斯特报导）第四十九届<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E4%B8%9C%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BA%BA%E5%A4%8F%E4%BB%A4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美东台湾人夏令会</a>于7月1日至4日在<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BE%E5%B7%9E.md#1">宾州</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%88%87%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">西切斯特大学</a>（West Chester University)举行。这一活动由TAC/EC和下一代<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾美国人协会</a>（TANG)联合举办。今年的主题是：“爱台湾乡土，护台湾主权”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10542662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/TACEC.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10542662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/TACEC-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542662" class="wp-caption-text">第四十九届美东台湾人夏令会会场。（TACEC官网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10542621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542621" style="width: 444px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0089.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-10542621 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0089.jpeg" alt="" width="444" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542621" class="wp-caption-text">第49届美东台湾人夏令会的演唱团体。（图由李彦辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10542623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542623" style="width: 446px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0245.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-10542623 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0245.jpeg" alt="" width="446" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542623" class="wp-caption-text">第49届美东台湾人夏令会的演唱团体。（图由李彦辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">该活动理事长翁进治女士介绍说，该活动始于1970年。“作为一个少数族裔，能够坚持不懈地做近半个世纪，不是一件容易的事情。除了我们之外，像这样的台湾美国人在夏天的会议在中西部、南部、西部和东南部也有召开。”她说该年会的宗旨是促进公众对台湾美国人在文化、宗教、教育、社会，和经济活动的关心，从而改善社区的友谊、互助和合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10542625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542625" style="width: 431px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0100.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-10542625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0100.jpeg" alt="" width="431" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542625" class="wp-caption-text">新当选的宾州众议员Helen Tai在第49届美东台湾人夏令会致辞。（图由李彦辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10542624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542624" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0171.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-10542624 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_0171.jpeg" alt="" width="440" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542624" class="wp-caption-text">第49届美东台湾人夏令会参会来宾。（图由李彦辉提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动召集人谢己博士表示，共有500多人参加这次活动。“今年的夏令会有丰富多彩的活动。每天上午有专题演讲，邀请世界名流前来讲座。晚上有各种文化娱乐活动、恳亲会和各种台湾小吃等。”许多团体包括北美医师协会、北美妇女会、全美同乡会、北美台商会，和北美教授协会等也参加了这些活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10542609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3256.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10542609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3256-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542609" class="wp-caption-text">文化展中的民俗画：春耕。（童云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">这次夏令会得到蔡英文总统和侨务委员会的支持。蔡总统在贺电中说：“欣悉订于本（107）年7月1日至4日举办‘第49届美东台湾人夏令会’，特电致贺。至盼籍由此项盛事，凝聚侨胞团结向心，蓄积民间交流能量，弘宣台湾民主历程，践履人文核心价值，携手为促进台美友好关系贡献心力。敬祝活动圆满成功，诸位健康如意。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10542610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3258.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10542610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3258-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542610" class="wp-caption-text">文化展中的民俗画：采茶。（童云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">协调人之一的黄小芳女士介绍了这次夏令会中的文化部分，比如插花、文化展、摄影展和客家文化等。一般所说的客家人，是由于各种原因由中原地区向南走，移到南方穷乡僻壤与世隔绝地区，但仍保持中原之风俗习惯及语言。据信薛仁贵、郭子仪等都是客家人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10542611" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10542611" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3264-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10542611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_3264-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10542611" class="wp-caption-text">插花展（Ikebana Exhibition）中的展品之一。（童云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>联合举办这次夏令会的下一代<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾美国人协会</a>（TANG)于2003年成立，全部由义工组成，根据年龄分成六个部分：3到6岁的学龄前儿童、小学组、初中组、高中组、年轻人和家长。其宗旨是“希望下一代能得到台湾社区的爱护与培养，从而能够达到自我、充满热情地追寻自己的梦。”</p>
