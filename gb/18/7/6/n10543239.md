<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿贺国庆 节目精彩缤纷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-1July4th-2-e1530906927701.jpg" />
<h6>波士顿流行乐团烟花汇演在贝壳剧场举办，吸引数千人前往观赏。(黄剑宇/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月07日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/jcxdqq3957/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>报导）7月4日上午，迎着华氏90度的烈日，波士顿人在市政府附近举办了盛大的<ahref="https://github.com/jcxdqq3957/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>游行活动，庆贺“独立宣言”签署242周年。</p>
<p>在市长华殊(Martin Walsh)的带领下，游行队伍和民众从市政府出发，经Tremont街来到Granary墓园向先贤致敬，而后到华盛顿街的旧州府大楼重温“独立宣言”，最后在市政府大楼后面的Faneuil大厅外演奏音乐，结束庆典。</p>
<h2>向长眠Granary墓园先贤致敬</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10543478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-3July4th-2-e1530906304770.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-3July4th-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543478" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍在Tremont街的Granary墓园外向先贤致敬。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10543484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-4July4th-2-e1530906406210.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-4July4th-2-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543484" class="wp-caption-text">Gerald Wellman在Granary墓园介绍先贤事迹。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10543487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-5July4th-2-e1530906470328.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-5July4th-2-600x350.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543487" class="wp-caption-text">市长华殊(右二)、警察局长埃文斯(左一)在Granary墓园向先贤致敬。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长华殊、市议员费连(Ed Flynn)、警察局长埃文斯(William Evans)等政要带头，包括警察、荣誉士兵、军乐队和志愿者在内的游行队伍从市政府出发，来到<ahref="https://github.com/jcxdqq3957/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>公园旁边的Granary墓园向先贤致敬。</p>
<p>据庆典司仪Gerald Wellman(退休东波士顿高中青年预备军官训练营指挥官)介绍，包括麻州首任州长约翰?考克(John Hancock)、“独立宣言”签署人之一Robert Treat Paine在内的多位美国先贤都沉眠于此。旁边教堂的钟声适时地响起，悼念开国前辈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10543496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-7July4th-2-e1530906703103.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-7July4th-2-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543496" class="wp-caption-text">穿着独立时期服装的游行者鸣枪庆贺。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>游行队伍亦在墓园外向先贤们敬礼，鸣枪致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10543254" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-2July4th-2-e1530900175195.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-2July4th-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543254" class="wp-caption-text">旧州府大楼前，人们欢呼庆贺美国独立。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10543490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-6July4th-2-e1530906547432.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-6July4th-2-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543490" class="wp-caption-text">麻州荣誉炮兵连指挥官在旧州府大楼宣读“独立宣言”。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而后在旧州府大楼前，摩肩接踵的波士顿居民和游人与麻州荣誉炮兵连(Ancient and Honorable Artillery Company)的指挥官Charles McCarthy一同回顾“独立宣言”。麻州荣誉炮兵连是北美洲最古老、全世界第三古老的特许军事组织，也是麻州州长的荣誉警卫。当McCarthy读到殖民地宣布独立时，楼下的民众们齐声欢呼，挥舞美国国旗，仿佛回到当年美国独立的时刻。</p>
<p>游行的终点是古老的Faneuil大厅。这个大厅由殖民地时期的商人Peter Faneuil捐赠。独立战争之前，革命者们曾在此举办许多会议，这所大厅也因而被庆典司仪Wellman称为“美国自由的摇篮”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10543495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10543495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-8July4th-2-e1530906641173.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10543495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-1-8July4th-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10543495" class="wp-caption-text">Middlesex县志愿者组成的乐队在Faneuil Hall大厅前演奏。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，参与游行的Middlesex县志愿者乐队在这里演奏乐曲，结束这场一个小时的游行庆典。</p>
<p>“今天我们的愿望是铭记为我们的国家、为一个新国家而战斗过的爱国者！我们今天也要反思作为美国人的意义所在。”市长华殊说。</p>
<h2>流行乐团及烟花汇演</h2>
<p>除了游行，波士顿庆祝美国<ahref="https://github.com/jcxdqq3957/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86.md#1">国庆</a>的另一传统亮点是波士顿流行乐团烟花汇演(Boston Pops Fireworks Spectacular)。3日晚该团在晚上8点进行彩排预演，吸引了数千观众前往贝壳剧场(Esplanade)欣赏演出。未料刚开始演出半小时就遇到大雨，演出立即中止，观众或撑伞或离开找地方躲雨，当然也有被迫淋湿以消暑气。约半小时后雨停了，乐团继续演出。</p>
<p>4日一早贝壳剧场就陆续聚集民众，迟到者就围着查尔斯河畔占好位置，以便观赏晚上的烟花。今年的波士顿流行乐团指挥仍由Keith Lockhart担任，他说很荣幸已连续24年了。当晚演出嘉宾有牛顿市长大的歌星Rachel Platten，两届艾美奖、一届奥斯卡奖得主的86岁歌舞演员Rita Moreno、以及Fourth Estate和Indigo Girls等。</p>
<p>整个庆典活动在高度维安戒备下，平安顺利圆满落幕，应归功于辛苦的国民警卫队、州警、波士顿及剑桥市警局和消防队、MBTA交警、紧急部门等参与安保的成员。◇</p>
