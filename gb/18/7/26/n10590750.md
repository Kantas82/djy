<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>孔雀也穿婚纱？纯白无瑕的羽毛 白孔雀开屏脱俗不凡</h2>
<a href="https://dylb6p9h9pzya.cloudfront.net/D1sXpEJqv"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jjslrw3280/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月05日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%94%E9%9B%80.md#1">孔雀</a>常见的种类为蓝孔雀与绿孔雀，蓝孔雀又名印度孔雀，羽毛颜色为宝蓝色，尾屏绿色，富有金属光泽；绿孔雀又名爪哇孔雀，羽毛颜色以翠绿色为主，下背闪耀紫铜色光泽。但是你有看过全身上下包含羽毛，只有单一颜色的孔雀吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10590815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10590815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-250.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10590815 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/1-250-600x484.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="484" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10590815" class="wp-caption-text">一般<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%94%E9%9B%80.md#1">孔雀</a>的羽毛颜色多彩缤纷，在<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%B1%8F.md#1">开屏</a>时相当华丽，非常吸引人的目光。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般孔雀的羽毛颜色多彩缤纷，羽毛还有着金属般的光泽，尾部拖着长达1米以上的羽毛，加上羽毛上如眼睛般特殊的花纹，孔雀在<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E5%B1%8F.md#1">开屏</a>时相当华丽，非常吸引人的目光。</p>
<p>而这个蓝孔雀的<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%BA%E5%9B%A0%E7%AA%81%E5%8F%98.md#1">基因突变</a>种──<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E5%AD%94%E9%9B%80.md#1">白孔雀</a>，不是因为得了白化症，而是产生色素的基因发生突变，导致全身的羽毛都成了白色的情况。白孔雀全身羽毛无杂色，纯白无瑕，眼睛呈淡红色。就像一般看到的孔雀，突然间穿着一袭雪白的婚纱，华丽耀眼却又脱俗不凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10590813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10590813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4-64.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10590813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4-64-600x484.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="484" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10590813" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E5%AD%94%E9%9B%80.md#1">白孔雀</a>全身羽毛无杂色，纯白无瑕，华丽耀眼却又脱俗不凡。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白孔雀是相当珍稀少见的，因为平均一千只蓝孔雀中才会出现一只白孔雀，所以看到白孔雀开屏时，反而被人们视为一件非常<ahref="https://github.com/jjslrw3280/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B8%E8%BF%90.md#1">幸运</a>的事情，也因为如此，白孔雀还象征着纯洁与幸运。</p>
