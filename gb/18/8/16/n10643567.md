<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>口湖食鳗节  尝鳗鱼特餐  游口湖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/eec8190d8e88dd4519d418abd1d85f11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>口湖食鳗节邀请名厨设计鳗鱼创意风味餐及日式鳗鱼料理。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月16日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）为了行销口湖的<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89.md#1">观光</a>和产业，云林县政府与<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E6%B9%96%E4%B9%A1.md#1">口湖乡</a>公所今年再度举办第二届“口湖食鳗节”，期间自9/22起自9/24日连续三天，民众除了可以订桌享用由名厨设计的<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B3%97%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鳗鱼</a>创意风味餐、日式鳗鱼饭，还可体验口湖鳗鱼特色产业与口湖的生态美景！</p>
<p>16日在<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E6%B9%96%E4%B9%A1.md#1">口湖乡</a>万善爷庙举行宣传记者会，县长李进勇、乡长林哲凌邀请全国乡亲不必远道日本，只要来趟口湖，就能品尝<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B3%97%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鳗鱼</a>的美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10643570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10643570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/16/n10643567.md#1/%e8%a8%98%e8%80%85%e6%9c%83%e7%8f%be%e5%a0%b4%e9%b0%bb%e9%ad%9a%e9%a4%90%e5%85%8d%e8%b2%bb%e5%93%81%e5%98%97%ef%bc%81" rel="attachment wp-att-10643570"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10643570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/23b9e083fdce757a30917bbe97c68074-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10643570" class="wp-caption-text">记者会现场鳗鱼餐免费品尝！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李进勇表示，台湾鳗鱼养殖历史已有50年，口湖乡鳗鱼养殖面积及产量均为全国第一，是台湾的鳗鱼之乡，但95％外销日本，为了让国人也能享受国产的鳗鱼，特别举办口湖鳗鱼节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10643572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10643572" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/16/n10643567.md#1/%e7%b8%a3%e9%95%b7%e6%9d%8e%e9%80%b2%e5%8b%87%ef%bc%88%e5%8f%b3%e5%9b%9b%ef%bc%89%e5%91%bc%e7%b1%b2%e5%85%a8%e5%9c%8b%e9%84%89%e8%a6%aa%e4%b8%8d%e7%94%a8%e5%88%b0%e6%97%a5%e6%9c%ac%ef%bc%8c%e4%be%86" rel="attachment wp-att-10643572"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10643572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/71535a98f71fa3094023df7b24c305e0-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10643572" class="wp-caption-text">县长李进勇（右四）呼吁全国乡亲不用到日本，来口湖就可以品尝美味的鳗鱼大餐。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林哲凌表示，去年举办了第一届的口湖食鳗节，引起很大回响，今年加码规划一连三天的系列活动，包括鳗鱼特色餐会还有轻旅行，有得吃有得玩，欢迎民众来漫游口湖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10643573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10643573" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/16/n10643567.md#1/%e5%8f%a3%e6%b9%96%e9%84%89%e9%95%b7%e6%9e%97%e5%93%b2%e5%87%8c%ef%bc%88%e5%8f%b3%ef%bc%89%e6%ad%a1%e8%bf%8e%e5%85%a8%e5%9c%8b%e9%84%89%e8%a6%aa%e4%be%86%e5%8f%a3%e6%b9%96%e5%93%81%e5%98%97%e9%b0%bb" rel="attachment wp-att-10643573"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10643573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02dfea34f9ba060c161db8ea6351663c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10643573" class="wp-caption-text">口湖乡长林哲凌（右）欢迎全国乡亲来口湖品尝鳗鱼风味餐。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三天系列活动：9月22日上午10点到下午3点，在金湖万善爷庙广场举办百桌“鳗鱼风味餐”，每桌10人分6800元，共10道菜色，包括黑蒜炖凤凰、樱花虾鳗鱼饭、何首鳗鱼养生锅，肉骨子弹鳗等。每道菜都是用云林在地的食材，当天还有趣味竞赛、农特产展售、免费试吃鳗鱼特色料理及在地特产。订桌电话：05-7892165。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10643574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10643574" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/18/8/16/n10643567.md#1/%e5%8f%a3%e6%b9%96%e9%84%89%e9%95%b7%e6%9e%97%e5%93%b2%e5%87%8c%ef%bc%88%e4%b8%ad%ef%bc%89%e5%92%8c%e4%ba%8c%e4%bd%8d%e5%90%8d%e5%bb%9a%e6%ad%a1%e8%bf%8e%e5%85%a8%e5%9c%8b%e9%84%89%e8%a6%aa%e4%be%86" rel="attachment wp-att-10643574"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10643574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f08c48b3330342ca711cf2d2f1f02338-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10643574" class="wp-caption-text">口湖乡长林哲凌（中）和二位名厨欢迎全国乡亲来口湖品尝鳗鱼风味餐。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9/23日日式特餐会，于口湖乡的埔南村的第一鳗坡举行，由荣获全国模范渔民的严竹英精心规划精致日式鳗鱼饭！每份450元，订餐请洽05-7895673。</p>

<p>9/24日（中秋节），由金湖休闲农场发展协会带领游客到马蹄蛤主题馆，体验下水摸蛤乐趣，中午到口湖游客中心享用农村风味餐、下午漫游宜梧滞洪池及第一鳗波工坊，体验蒲烧鳗鱼三角饭团DIY。每人费用899元，报名请洽：05-7906601。</p>
