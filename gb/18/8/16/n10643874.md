<p>【大纪元2018年08月16日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a><ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E5%90%8C%E4%B9%A1%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾客家同乡会</a>举办2018年“客家<ahref="https://github.com/bsfxrt319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md#1">中秋</a>敬老庆双十”活动，会长邱文滨暨全体理事团队热忱邀约您前来。</p>
