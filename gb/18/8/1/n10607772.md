<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巨无霸冰山缩时摄影 居民：没看过这么大的</h2>
<a href="https://dol9bue5bysdj.cloudfront.net/X3wPF1mus"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/oneuom382/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/oneuom382/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月02日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）一座<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%97%A0%E9%9C%B8.md#1">巨无霸</a><ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B1%B1.md#1">冰山</a>漂到<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E9%99%B5%E5%85%B0.md#1">格陵兰</a>岛西部的伊纳苏特（Innaarsuit）海岸，上个月被网友尼尔森（Oline Nielsen）用缩时摄影方式拍下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10609294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10609294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c55f47e67ed761a6ed973adcfa917b1c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10609294 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c55f47e67ed761a6ed973adcfa917b1c-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10609294" class="wp-caption-text">漂在<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E9%99%B5%E5%85%B0.md#1">格陵兰</a>岛西部的<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%97%A0%E9%9C%B8.md#1">巨无霸</a><ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B1%B1.md#1">冰山</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>影片中可以看到一座雪白的大山，在水面上漂浮着。这座冰山真是够巨大，旁边的房子对它来说像似小朋友在玩的“小屋<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E5%9E%8B.md#1">模型</a>”。</p>
<p>冰山移动过程中，还一度剧烈上下晃动了一阵，感觉真是很恐怖。幸好冰山不是在平地上移动，不然这些屋子被冰山扫一下那可不得了，马上就如汽车压<ahref="https://github.com/oneuom382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E5%B9%B2.md#1">饼干</a>，轻松粉碎响出酥脆声。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看影片完整版…<br />
</span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-950b7cea-673e-4540-401b-1ab77ab5bda1" data-id="player-950b7cea-673e-4540-401b-1ab77ab5bda1"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/950b7cea-673e-4540-401b-1ab77ab5bda1?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F1%2Fn10607772.md#1"></a></p>
<p>该村庄有169名居民，一位当地村委会成员苏珊娜．伊莱亚森（Susanna Eliassen）告诉格陵兰广播公司（KNR）：“我可以感受到居民的关心，我们过去有遇过大冰山，可是我们没看过这么大的冰山。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10609301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10609301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/8dfaa19a75d1420ffa978f825e643e7a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10609301 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/8dfaa19a75d1420ffa978f825e643e7a-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10609301" class="wp-caption-text">冰山突然晃了一下，水面产生波动。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>伊纳苏特议会主席彼得森（Karl Petersen）告诉加拿大广播公司：“我们非常担心且害怕。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10609304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10609304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e0257ba87462a93f84641cd56ac49efb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10609304 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e0257ba87462a93f84641cd56ac49efb-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10609304" class="wp-caption-text">冰山渐渐漂离镜头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在遇到这巨无霸冰山时，当地政府和警方处于高度警戒状态，还将搜救直升机移到这人烟稀少的偏远村庄附近。</p>
<p>当时这个冰山上的冰还曾崩落，导致大浪冲向村庄，后来村民紧急撤离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10609328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10609328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/000_17K213.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10609328 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/000_17K213-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10609328" class="wp-caption-text">其他人拍的巨无霸冰山照片。(MAGNUS KRISTENSEN/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>那这个大块头，它到底多重呢？</p>
<p>据《纽约时报》报导，“丹麦气象研究所的一位专家告诉丹麦广播公司，卫星数据显示它大约有650英尺（约198公尺）宽，几乎向空中延伸了300英尺（约91公尺），重达1100万吨。”</p>
