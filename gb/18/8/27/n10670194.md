<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东湾豪宅价格调整期 享受大房好时机</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月27日讯】（大纪元记者李欧旧金山湾区综合报导）本周来看到几处Pleasanton、Fremont、Milpitas的豪宅。随着湾区房价飙高，东湾也逐渐增加数百万级房产，例如本周介绍的4处房产都在两三百万之间，房大地大有很好的生活空间，而且其中有3个房产近期都有价格调整，给买家带来更好的机会。</p>
<p>一、6008 Tillman Ct., Pleasanton, CA 94588<br />
<figure id="attachment_10670222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01_IS2fghshps530y1000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01_IS2fghshps530y1000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10670222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670222" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">东湾地产</a>，4千呎以上的大宅，空间舒适。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10670223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02_IS6ua6x0cyc0zx1000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02_IS6ua6x0cyc0zx1000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10670223" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670223" class="wp-caption-text">天花板挑高的客厅、餐厅区。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这户大宅自今年4月上市以来，价格至今已经降低310,000美元，现在是买家喊价的好时机！2014年曾升级超过20万美元。美丽的家坐落在普莱森顿最负盛名的街区之一“The Preserve保护区”，拥有专业园景庭院，大型主人休息室和3个壁炉、4个车库、2个中央供暖和空调。<br />
大门入口是挑高的门厅，客厅、餐厅享有无压迫的空间。宽敞的卧室位于楼下，楼上设有开放式阁楼区。大型景观阳台、厨房和家庭房都能看见美丽的后院。房产拥有优质学校，高速公路、购物中心都在附近不远处。<figure id="attachment_10670224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.26.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.26-600x342.png" alt="" width="600" b="342" class="size-large wp-image-10670224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670224" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">东湾地产</a>：6008 Tillman Ct., Pleasanton, CA 94588。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、39723 Benavente Ave., Fremont, CA 94539<br />
<figure id="attachment_10670228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03_ISal6k7qt5lkvv0000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03_ISal6k7qt5lkvv0000000000-600x280.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="280" class="size-large wp-image-10670228" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670228" class="wp-caption-text">代理商仅提供一张前门图片。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
新的Fremont上市房，位在受追捧的Kimber Park Community社区。但代理商仅提供一张图片，可能因为对房产的信心极佳。据描述，是两层楼房产，一楼有生活和正式用餐区挑高天花板，也有一间全套卧室；全新的厨房有花岗岩柜台、S.S电器、隐藏式照明。<br />
螺旋形楼梯有大片玻璃窗户，主人套房配有拱形天花板，宽敞的步入式衣柜，独立浴缸和淋浴，以及大型阳台区。全屋新的内墙涂料、新颖的耐磨木地板、升级的地毯和地板。附带3车车库、中央供暖和空调、新设计的前院和后院还有水疗中心。<br />
享有杰出学区，Gomes小学（10），Hopkins初中（10），Mission San Jose High（10）都在步行距离之内。几分钟到公园、网球场、商店、餐馆、伊丽莎白湖、图书馆和Fremont BART。<figure id="attachment_10670226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.38.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.38-600x301.png" alt="" width="600" b="301" class="size-large wp-image-10670226" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670226" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>：39723 Benavente Ave., Fremont, CA 9453。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、664 Quince Lane, Milpitas, CA 95035<br />
<figure id="attachment_10670231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04_240484063-5.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04_240484063-5-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10670231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670231" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，此房产门前巨大的圆形车道。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10670232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/05_240484063-29.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/05_240484063-29-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10670232" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670232" class="wp-caption-text">旋转楼梯和超大拱形窗扇。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
想俯瞰湾区美丽景致的机会来了，Milpitas这户占地1.1英亩，住宅6千多平方英尺的大豪宅属于封闭式社区的其中一户。这处订制宅邸位在山坡上，离Great Mall仅10分钟车程。2013年历经多次降价后，2014年以近240万卖出；今年升价超过100万再度上市。<br />
门前有豪气的圆形大车道，前门望去便是亮丽景色。巨大的双主卧、酒店风格宽敞的客厅带有吧台、图书馆、带壁炉的办公室、重新装修的厨房、媒体/游戏室，所有重新装修的浴室、木地板、大理石地板、储物室！户外有泳池、多个露台、游乐区域，还有可停超过5辆的车库。<figure id="attachment_10670229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.49.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.34.49-600x339.png" alt="" width="600" b="339" class="size-large wp-image-10670229" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670229" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>：664 Quince Lane, Milpitas, CA 95035。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、47532 Avalon Heights Ter., Fremont, CA 94539<br />
<figure id="attachment_10670235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/06_ISa9x8jcw44zl60000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/06_ISa9x8jcw44zl60000000000-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" class="size-large wp-image-10670235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670235" class="wp-caption-text">东湾地产，山坡边的大宅可赏湾区海景。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10670237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/07_ISir0q7ghj1iql0000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/07_ISir0q7ghj1iql0000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10670237" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670237" class="wp-caption-text">亮堂雅致的客厅。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这处Fremont大房卖家曾花费超过百万美元装修，上市后经过多次调整价格，今年8月最新降价1.8%。超过半英亩的土地，可从后院泳池俯瞰湾区海景，更有阶梯走到下坡处散步，还能在露台户外厨房烧烤边欣赏风景。<br />
室内有有2间主人套房、高品质的桦木硬木地板和大理石、办公室、3万美元升级的订制橱柜、3个壁炉。房子位于Fremont一个著名的Avalon门控社区，靠近珪谷的中心地带。距离680和880高速公路也很近。#<figure id="attachment_10670233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10670233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.35.01.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9.35.01-600x335.png" alt="" width="600" b="335" class="size-large wp-image-10670233" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10670233" class="wp-caption-text">湾区买房：47532 Avalon Heights Ter., Fremont, CA 94539。</figcaption></figure></p>
