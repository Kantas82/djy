<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>神奇豪宅 隐身百年再现法国贵族生活传奇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070093-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>从曼丁豪宅阳台远眺穆兰市全景。（龚简／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月12日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/xgoxlu390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%AF%E6%98%93%E6%96%AF%C2%B7%E6%9B%BC%E4%B8%81.md#1">路易斯·曼丁</a>（Louis Mantin）膝下无儿无女，他有个愿望，把自己的豪华别墅保持完整，以便在一百年后展示予游客。那是1905年，曼丁临终前写下遗嘱，把别墅遗赠给<ahref="https://github.com/xgoxlu390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%86%E5%85%B0.md#1">穆兰</a>市政府，并嘱咐“这栋房子要见证19世纪末（法国）资产者的生活方式”。</p>
<p>曼丁的愿望神奇般被实现了。他去世5年后，即1910年，<ahref="https://github.com/xgoxlu390/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%86%E5%85%B0.md#1">穆兰</a>市把其收藏无数珍贵古董和文物的别墅作为博物馆向公众开放，然而，因为二次世界大战的出现，为保护别墅，博物馆被关闭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10633194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070097.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070097-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">曼丁个人画像（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070053.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070053-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">客厅（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070051.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070051-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">客厅（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633164" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070050-e1534085377347.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10633164 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070050-e1534085377347-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豪华吊灯（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一关就是近百年，别墅的宝藏终得以保存下来。经过三年的翻新，2010年，正好相隔了一个世纪，“曼丁别墅”又重新开放，真正地向世人展示19世纪末，法国富人贵族充满现代化和品味的生活方式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10633168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070058.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070058-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">名贵壁画（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070060-e1534085480989.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10633170 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070060-e1534085480989-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">走廊（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曼丁（生于1851年）出身自一个富商家庭，他继承了父亲和祖父的财产。曼丁曾是一名律师，但他最大的爱好则是收藏古董。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10633172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070064.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070064-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">曼丁幼年时阖家画像（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070081.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070081-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">曼丁的卧室（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10633182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070080.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10633182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/P1070080-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">曼丁的卧室（龚简／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1893年，曼丁花了三年时间，兴建了这栋三层高哥特式别墅，而建筑设计又包含了众多艺术元素，如文化复兴风格的装饰、路易十八时代的画作、配上彩色玻璃窗现代浴室……#</p>
