<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乃木坂46成员访台 超商吉祥物变粉丝讨签名</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055747100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日本人气女团乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（右）及松村沙友理（左）访台。（7-ELEVEN提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月15日讯】日本人气女团<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%83%E6%9C%A8%E5%9D%8246.md#1">乃木坂46</a>成员<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%85%83%E7%9C%9F%E5%A4%8F.md#1">秋元真夏</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E6%9D%91%E6%B2%99%E5%8F%8B%E7%90%86.md#1">松村沙友理</a>于今（15）日下午造访台湾7-ELEVEN“未来超商X-STORE”。吉祥物“PLEASE美眉”及“小桃”除了为两人准备了小惊喜，还瞬间变成粉丝，拿着乃木坂46的最新单曲《以自我为中心！》请两人签名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055801100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640695" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055801100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640695" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%83%E6%9C%A8%E5%9D%8246.md#1">乃木坂46</a>成员<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%85%83%E7%9C%9F%E5%A4%8F.md#1">秋元真夏</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E6%9D%91%E6%B2%99%E5%8F%8B%E7%90%86.md#1">松村沙友理</a>为超商吉祥物签名并合照。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6月就曾访台拍摄广告的乃木坂46，工作时喝到工作人员准备的黑糖珍珠撞奶时感到惊喜，一问之下才知道是超商的饮品。随后超商也邀请乃木坂46为饮料代言，成员们也对于能为台湾国民饮品代言感到不可思议。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055726100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640708" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055726100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640708" class="wp-caption-text">日本人气女团乃木坂46成员秋元真夏造访“未来超商X-STORE”。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“乃木坂46”在6月来台时就曾表示希望能到X-STORE体验，但因行程紧凑无法达成愿望，本次终于如愿前往该店。秋元真夏与松村沙友理入店时，脸部辨识系统便即刻启动，萤幕上出现自己名字，还有亲切招呼声。从选购商品到体验自助结账等高科技，让两位成员惊讶大呼：“すごい！”（太厉害了！）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055810100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640704" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055810100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640704" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（右）及松村沙友理（左）造访X-STORE。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10640706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055734100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640706" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055734100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640706" class="wp-caption-text">日本人气女团乃木坂46成员松村沙友理造访“未来超商X-STORE”。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两人摊开“访台必买清单”，从饮料、面膜、零食等都有，在挑选饮料时，冰箱门自动打开也让两人吓了一跳。此外，店内的6台OPEN!CHANNEL及e-pop不定时播放各种广告，也让两人觉得新奇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640718" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055718100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640718" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055718100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640718" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（左）及松村沙友理（右）于超商购物。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10640719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055710100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640719" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055710100707-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640719" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（右）及松村沙友理（左）于超商购物。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10640723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055701100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640723" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055701100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640723" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（左）及松村沙友理（右）于超商购物。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在两人参观完该店后，OPEN!家族的“PLEASE美眉”及“小桃”冲到台前，表演了苦练多日的舞蹈，并于舞蹈结束后献上她们最喜爱的“黑糖珍珠撞奶”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055826100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640724" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055826100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640724" class="wp-caption-text">超商吉祥物PLEASE美眉（左）及小桃（右）为乃木坂46成员献上舞蹈。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>PLEASE美眉及小桃，还带着X-STORE限定版的icash2.0，以及乃木坂46的最新单曲《以自我为中心！》CD，让秋元真夏与松村沙友理签名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10640725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10640725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055753100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10640725" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180815055753100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10640725" class="wp-caption-text">乃木坂46成员秋元真夏（右）及松村沙友理（左）为吉祥物签名与合照。（7-ELEVEN提供）</figcaption></figure>
