	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10598385 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-14-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年09月05日讯】大家常说，狗是人类最忠实的朋友，所言不虚！快看看这只<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E7%8A%AC.md#1">忠犬</a>，对主人的健康也非常照顾，它知道<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%AE%E9%85%92%E8%BF%87%E9%87%8F.md#1">饮酒过量</a>有害健康，当主人准备拿起<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E9%85%92.md#1">红酒</a>杯时，<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>就“出手”阻止了，令人会心一笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10639411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5522e5f5d0f2485dc285cd5026a802b7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10639411 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5522e5f5d0f2485dc285cd5026a802b7-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">主人养了一只懂得照顾自己健康的狗，一旦想要喝<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E9%85%92.md#1">红酒</a>，就会被阻止！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>惬意的午后，有些人喜欢坐在舒服的沙发上阅读，有些人喜欢听音乐，另外一些人则喜欢品味红酒。不过如果已经喝过一杯，而且又养了一只懂得照顾主人健康的<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E7%8A%AC.md#1">忠犬</a>，接下来会发生什么事呢？</p>
<p>忠心耿耿的爱犬会阻止你喝酒！就像影片中的那样，一但想要伸手拿红酒杯，<ahref="https://github.com/hjtdvm325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>也跟着“出手”轻轻把主人的手拨开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10639507" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ffcba1189482d68aeaa009e65c3c7b76.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10639507 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ffcba1189482d68aeaa009e65c3c7b76-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">忠犬缓缓地拨开主人蠢蠢欲动想拿酒杯的手。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“不行喔……已经第三杯了。”狗狗一脸淡定，但它也很坚持主人饮酒节制，适可而止。“不要偷偷来……我都有看见。”就算缓缓地靠近，也会被缓缓地制止，狗狗真的很有原则！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f57de4cb-b4bc-4fe3-55cb-14b9429aef16" data-id="player-f57de4cb-b4bc-4fe3-55cb-14b9429aef16"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f57de4cb-b4bc-4fe3-55cb-14b9429aef16?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F15%2Fn10639349.md#1"></a></p>
<p>红酒的研究显示，红酒的成分含有抗氧化成分白藜芦醇（resveratrol），可以减少癌症发生的概率，并维持脑力，而红酒中的类黄酮（flavonoids）则可以增加好的胆固醇，避免血栓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10639508" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/607b36c82a62bb10ba54ed9785e6627a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10639508 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/607b36c82a62bb10ba54ed9785e6627a-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">还“语重心长”地望着主人：“主人要明白我的苦心啊！”（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而开瓶后的红酒与空气接触，更会持续“成长”变化，每个阶段都有不同的香醇，是可以让人细细品味个中乐趣的微妙饮品。但研究也显示，红酒饮用过量比喝烈酒更伤脑，建议一周一瓶较适当喔！</p>
