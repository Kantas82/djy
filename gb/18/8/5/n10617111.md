<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东湾西北拉面  手打面Q好劲道</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月06日讯】（大纪元特约记者食客湾区纽华克报导）<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E5%BA%97.md#1">湾区面店</a>越开越多，<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%89%93%E9%9D%A2.md#1">手打面</a>的选择也相应增加，许多人将“手打”一词与美味放在一起，岂知“手打”并不一定是美味，面粉的搭配比例、“出色的手打”再加上水煮的争分夺秒，才能呈现出美味的手打面。位于东湾纽华克（Newark）的西北拉面馆，虽然地处偏僻，但以独家的高、中筋面粉比例、现点现打的出色手打面，以及浓郁香醇的汤头，让许多人一吃就成为回头客，拥有不少粉丝支持。</p>
<h2>1.<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E8%A5%BF%E5%8C%97%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">东湾西北拉面</a>：<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E5%A4%A7%E7%9B%98.md#1">羊大盘</a>article content begin -->3.99</h2>
<p>湾区少有提供的皮带面搭配澳洲羊排的软嫩鲜香、土豆的软糯、青椒的脆以及含有羊排鲜香的香醇鲜辣的酱汁，许多人都愿意来上一盘，放眼望去，<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E5%A4%A7%E7%9B%98.md#1">羊大盘</a>点单率极高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617214" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F.md#1">湾区面食</a>，羊大盘。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2.<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E8%A5%BF%E5%8C%97%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">东湾西北拉面</a>：西北特色凉皮$6.50</h2>
<p>凉皮，绝对是店家另一道点单率极高的餐点。与一般凉皮使用的的麻酱不同，这里的凉皮是搭配店家调制的辣油，十分的油润辣香且不油腻，面筋因吸满了酱汁充满辣香，些许的黄瓜丝更是带来一丝清爽，只不过因为有蒜末的缘故，吃完后口气有点大，这点需有心理准备。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617215" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F.md#1">湾区面食</a>，西北特色凉皮。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3.<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E5%BA%97.md#1">湾区面店</a>：蕃茄牛腩拌面article content begin -->0.99</h2>
<p>店家的面条是由独家的高、中筋面粉比例、现点现打制成，不使用任何添加剂，面条十分Q弹有劲。烹制酥软的牛腩为微酸带甜的酱汁增添不少牛肉的鲜香及油润；些许蛋丝除带来蛋香外，略为粗口的质地为这碗面带来不同的口感；大口吃着<ahref="https://github.com/mefzku3937/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%89%93%E9%9D%A2.md#1">手打面</a>，除能感受面条在口中释放的弹牙劲道，吸附在面条上的浓郁酱汁带来的是更多的满足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617216" class="wp-caption-text">湾区面食，蕃茄牛腩拌面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4.湾区面店：酸菜鱼羊汤面article content begin -->0.99</h2>
<p>鱼与羊搭配在一起，就只有一个鲜字可以形容。乳白色的汤底搭载着3大块鱼排、数块小羊排及酸菜，手打面的Q劲口感不在话下，鱼排鲜嫩中带着厚实、鲜嫩的羊排不失劲度及酸香不失脆口的酸菜，带来了3种不同的口感；乳白色的汤底带来预期般的浓郁鲜美滋味，些许的香油除带来油润更增添余香；幸亏有酸菜带来的酸脆，让人在油润中不感油腻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617219" class="wp-caption-text">西北拉面，酸菜鱼羊汤面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃上一口这样的面，让人全身暖洋洋，实实在在的感受到了滋补。</p>
<h2>5.湾区面店：西北特色丁丁炒面$8.99起跳</h2>
<p>丁丁炒面可以说是新疆最具代表性的美食之一。有些人会对丁丁这个词感到疑惑，丁丁是指人名？还是地名呢？其实丁丁就是将面切成小条状，且面里的其他配菜也都切成相同大小的“丁”状，这才是丁丁炒面名称的由来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/05-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617221" class="wp-caption-text">西北拉面，西北特色丁丁炒面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丁丁炒面得用汤匙来享用，吃上一口炒面，等于一次将面、菜和酱汁全部送入口中，慢慢咀嚼让所有食材在嘴里融合起来，这样才能品尝出丁丁炒面真正的美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10617223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/07-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617223" class="wp-caption-text">湾区手打面，红烧牛肉面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10617225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/08-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617225" class="wp-caption-text">湾区手打面，酸辣臊子汤面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10617227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10617227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10617227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/09-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10617227" class="wp-caption-text">湾区手打面，油泼面。（石岚/摄影）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店家还有许多特色汤面像是：香辣牛肉面、酸辣臊子汤面、油泼面等等，都值得大家尝尝。西北拉面馆位于Newark，Central 街上，拥有自己停车场，停车便利，喜欢面食的你们可别错过。#</p>
