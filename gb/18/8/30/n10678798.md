<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>树上猫崽哭2天 他爬上去发现精美“猫巢”</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYJ5B"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rlilro332/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rlilro332/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）两只没断奶的小猫在树上哀叫，它们的妈咪似乎失踪了，整整两天没出现，于是，<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>小喵行动就此展开。树上的<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>小窝很像“<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E5%B7%A2.md#1">鸟巢</a>”，同样令人惊讶的是救难人员感激<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>很轻松背后的心酸。</p>
<p>2016年9月13日动物救援组织SMART于YouTube上传一部影片：美国加州救难人员纳夫（Nav）走到一座房前与一女子碰面，到底发生什么事呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/23baa5d38511e36f15590581b095204a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10680798 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/23baa5d38511e36f15590581b095204a-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女子指向树，表示小喵咪在上面。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来是SMART接到一通求救电话，得知有未断奶的小喵咪被困在了树上。</p>
<p>纳夫到达现场后表示：“我想我听到一些‘哭声’，不过那棵树是在这里吗？”女子手指着旁边的树，纳夫就明白了，等待救援的<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>就在上面！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bd5448d0ab3c7dda67fa0a5886af8c23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10680802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bd5448d0ab3c7dda67fa0a5886af8c23-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小喵咪在“猫巢”里。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纳夫观察一阵后，决定去拿一下伸缩梯。他很快又回到树边架梯往上爬，拨开树枝，他发现了两只可爱小喵咪。这“猫巢”相当特别，猫妈妈简直就是学鸟在树上筑巢。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-1ba0fe24-0816-457e-6112-efe55180d131" data-id="player-1ba0fe24-0816-457e-6112-efe55180d131"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/1ba0fe24-0816-457e-6112-efe55180d131?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F30%2Fn10678798.md#1"></a></p>
<p>不久，纳夫用手将它们抓出来，小猫咪哇哇叫的声音很可爱，不过它们应该已经肚子很饿了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d7c5b0f0c296a9d72529f475537f8bea.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10680812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d7c5b0f0c296a9d72529f475537f8bea-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纳夫抓出小喵咪。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们把两只超可爱的小喵咪装进宠物提箱，女子在影片后段表示希望能有人愿意领养，也期望小喵咪能有一个美好的家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/079f8760ed7e92d9e9143287beca3a90.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10680815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/079f8760ed7e92d9e9143287beca3a90-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小喵咪被放入手提宠物箱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实，此救援过程对于纳夫来说相当轻松，树只有10英尺（约3米）高。在SMART出勤遇到的“树救援”都会从20英尺（约6米）起跳，为让救难人员安全攀高，他们需要掌握一些关于绳子、索具的技术。</p>
<p>这次小猫救援则不用，一架6英尺（约1.83米）高的伸缩梯就完成任务了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10680820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f6651de2cd480516c9a278fd07a941ba.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10680820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f6651de2cd480516c9a278fd07a941ba-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小猫模样相当可爱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因此SMART在YouTube影片描述中，透露了救难人员不为人知的辛酸：“纳夫感激这次救援很轻松，因他刚刚在一个有大量跳蚤出没的公寓大楼周围爬行，为的就是要寻找一只<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%97%E5%9B%B0.md#1">受困</a>几天的病猫。虽然辛苦，但这就是他们平时在做的事……”</p>
<p>这些救动物的英雄，和消防队员一样，要勇于深入险境和令人不堪的地方，帮助宝贵生命脱离苦难，善行值得赞许。</p>
