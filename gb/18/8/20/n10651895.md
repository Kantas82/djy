<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玻璃栈道突然“碎”了 男子当场吓破胆</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月12日讯】近年来相当流行漫步天空步道，各地相继出现不同特色的天空步道，有的是用钢条建成的，有的则是木栈道的设计，还有的是像“U”字型步道，让游客可以站在步道上眺望整个山谷景观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10651922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10651922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4955165150fb731a830d1afecdce9f9f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10651922 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4955165150fb731a830d1afecdce9f9f-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10651922" class="wp-caption-text">男子若无其事的走在<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83.md#1">玻璃</a>步道上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其中，最刺激的是使用透明<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83.md#1">玻璃</a>所建造的步道，透明的地板设计让360度全景一览无遗，最为人所知的就是<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%A4%A7%E5%B3%A1%E8%B0%B7.md#1">美国大峡谷</a>的天空步道，建造在峡谷的悬崖边，游客可以在高度1,158公尺上漫步，心脏够大颗的人可以去挑战看看。</p>
<p>最近中国也出现许多天空步道，有些还是全玻璃打造的<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83%E6%A0%88%E9%81%93.md#1">玻璃栈道</a>，不少胆小的游客一踏上玻璃栈道，吓到腿软、大哭。在河北邯郸市东太行景区的玻璃栈道，更首创玻璃碎裂的特殊效果，因为效果太逼真，网路上也流传许多游客被特效吓破胆的影片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10651924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10651924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/34965e988a5d62e0baa8b0155b7e1b79.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10651924 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/34965e988a5d62e0baa8b0155b7e1b79-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10651924" class="wp-caption-text">突然裂痕蔓延在玻璃步道上，发出令人惊吓的声响。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10651979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10651979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/31bdbff4087815d2196c6a31d93a9d9f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10651979 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/31bdbff4087815d2196c6a31d93a9d9f-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10651979" class="wp-caption-text">男子惊慌的跌在地上，又赶紧爬起来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这名男子一开始在<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83%E6%A0%88%E9%81%93.md#1">玻璃栈道</a>上一派轻松地走着，走着走着，突然脚下的玻璃<ahref="https://github.com/zojlqq3079/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A3%82%E5%BC%80.md#1">裂开</a>了，玻璃上出现蜘蛛网纹还伴随着碎裂声，男子吓得大喊，连滚带爬地抓住旁边的栏杆，还将脚缩了起来不敢踩在玻璃上。</p>
<p>而另一位女士在玻璃栈道上边走边拍，看起来一点都不害怕，还在镜头后面解说“你们一走喀嚓喀嚓这玻璃”，相当的淡定，跟这位吓坏的男子的反应可说是天壤之别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10651992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10651992" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a276118066838461fcfd8c7bc5684880.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10651992 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a276118066838461fcfd8c7bc5684880-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10651992" class="wp-caption-text">在河北邯郸市东太行景区的玻璃栈道，更首创玻璃碎裂的特殊效果。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但这种特效也引起民众对景区安全感到担忧，万一特效把游客吓得做出过激的反应怎么办？如果玻璃栈道真的碎了，游客还以为是特效，发生安全问题怎么办？这类话题热度依旧在。</p>
