<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚运跆拳道女子53公斤 苏柏亚摘台湾第三金</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月20日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台北报导）台湾中华队<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">跆拳道</a>女子<span lang="EN-US">53</span>公斤级<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%9F%8F%E4%BA%9A.md#1">苏柏亚</a>，8月20日在雅加达—巨港<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%BF%90.md#1">亚运</a><ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%86%E6%8B%B3%E9%81%93.md#1">跆拳道</a>赛事一路过关斩将，在决赛中以29比10击败南韩选手河敏娜，为中华队拿下第三面<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%89%8C.md#1">金牌</a>。</p>
<p>中华队跆拳道好手<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%9F%8F%E4%BA%9A.md#1">苏柏亚</a>20日在<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%BF%90.md#1">亚运</a>跆拳道女子53公斤级对打赛，先是以26比6轻取日本选手，接着击败香港选手罗倩儿，晋级4强。</p>
<p>苏柏亚虽然在准决赛与哈萨克选手的对战中陷入苦战，但挺住压力面对第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>回合落后的局面，在第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>回合强攻<span lang="EN-US">11</span>分，终场以<span lang="EN-US">11</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>闯进决赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10652668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10652668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1808200742072378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10652668" title="亚运跆拳道女子53公斤 苏柏亚摘台湾第三金" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1808200742072378-600x421.jpg" alt="亚运跆拳道女子53公斤 苏柏亚摘台湾第三金" width="600" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10652668" class="wp-caption-text">中华队跆拳道女将苏柏亚（红色护具）20日成功挺进2018雅加达亚运跆拳道女子53公斤级决赛，对上韩国好手，双方互不相让，力拼<ahref="https://github.com/bdysqw3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%89%8C.md#1">金牌</a>。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苏柏亚在决赛中以强悍的攻势让韩国强敌河敏雅无力招架，终场<span lang="EN-US">29</span>：<span lang="EN-US">10</span>大胜韩国，摘下金牌，也是台湾本届亚运的第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>金。</p>
