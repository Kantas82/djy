<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宋智孝演《Lovely Horribly》为单眼看人致歉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101915100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会，左起朴施厚、宋智孝、咸&#150034;静、李起光、崔汝珍。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月09日讯】（大纪元记者乐慧萱编译报导）最新韩剧《Lovely Horribly》9日下午于首尔永登浦区KBS2TV举行制作发表会，导演及主演<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%96%BD%E5%8E%9A.md#1">朴施厚</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%99%BA%E5%AD%9D.md#1">宋智孝</a>、“Highlight”成员<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E8%B5%B7%E5%85%89.md#1">李起光</a>、“T-ARA”成员咸&#150034;静（慇晶）、崔汝珍等人出席活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10627261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101923100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627261" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101923100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627261" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E6%96%BD%E5%8E%9A.md#1">朴施厚</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%99%BA%E5%AD%9D.md#1">宋智孝</a>出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在剧中饰演电视剧编剧“池乙顺”的宋智孝，笑说：“剧中乙顺的头发挡住了一只眼睛，演到现在还没有用两只眼睛注视过其他演员，真的对不起大家了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10627265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627265" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101945100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627265" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101945100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627265" class="wp-caption-text">宋智孝出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋智孝表示，自己觉得与众不同的造型就是该剧独特的一部分，而女主角“乙顺”虽然经历了众多不幸的事情，却依然坚强开朗、积极乐观的生活着，是很值得学习的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10627267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627267" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101930100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627267" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101930100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627267" class="wp-caption-text">朴施厚及宋智孝出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>朴施厚于剧中饰演天生拥有好运气的顶级明星“刘弼立”，在34岁事业黄金时期突然遇到了意想不到的难关。他表示：“很开心与演员、现场工作人员们合作，大家相互配合，拍摄现场非常愉快，接到剧本时被独特有趣的题材所吸引。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10627263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627263" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101938100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627263" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101938100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627263" class="wp-caption-text">朴施厚出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>之前由他主演的韩剧《我的黄金光辉人生》深受广大观众的喜爱并创下了非常好的收视率。被问及对于前作的好成绩是否感到压力时，他表示：“跟压力比起来，我对作品的期待更大，用积极的态度对待拍摄就会有好的结果。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10627271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627271" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101951100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627271" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101951100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627271" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E8%B5%B7%E5%85%89.md#1">李起光</a>出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10627272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627272" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101956100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627272" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809101956100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627272" class="wp-caption-text">咸&#150034;静出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10627279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10627279" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809102002100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10627279" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/180809102002100707.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10627279" class="wp-caption-text">崔汝珍出席《Lovely Horribly》制作发表会。（全景林／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《Lovely Horribly》讲述顶级明星“刘弼立”与电视剧作家“池乙顺”相遇后发生一连串怪奇事件。同日同时出生的这两人，如同生命共同体般，当一方幸福时另一方就会遭遇不幸，是一对在偶然和命运、幸运与不幸间相互牵制的男女的浪漫爱情故事。该剧将于韩国时间8月13日晚间10点播出。</p>
