<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>密西根湖掀巨浪 有人被卷走！游客牵人链救援</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月17日讯】英国媒体报导，8月5日在美国<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%86%E8%A5%BF%E6%A0%B9%E6%B9%96.md#1">密西根湖</a>的沙滩上，大群游客顶着巨大风浪，手拉手形成巨大<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E9%93%BE.md#1">人链</a>，协助<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>人员，搜救先前失踪的几名游泳客，场面十分壮观。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">观看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-fcad85ca-fbf2-45c4-54d3-2fe219440300" data-id="player-fcad85ca-fbf2-45c4-54d3-2fe219440300"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/fcad85ca-fbf2-45c4-54d3-2fe219440300?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F17%2Fn10645535.md#1"></a></p>
<p>当时至少约有6名泳客在密西根州格兰德港的国家公园和城市沙滩游泳时，被强风刮起的<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E6%B5%AA.md#1">巨浪</a>卷走失踪，巨大的风浪也给<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>工作造成了极大困难。</p>
<p>鉴于当时海滩上游客众多，救援人员便想出了让游客们拉手组成几条巨大<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E9%93%BE.md#1">人链</a>，共同协助寻找失踪人员的办法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10646147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10646147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-113.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10646147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-113-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10646147" class="wp-caption-text">鉴于当时海滩上游客众多，救援人员请游客们共同协助寻找失踪人员。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一开始，大约有30～40人左右，手拉手站成一排，齐心协力进行搜寻，但由于水越来越深，不得不分成两排。</p>
<p>一名来自怀俄明州的65岁男性被救回，紧接着又有几名失踪人员被成功找到，由于他们受到了不同程度伤害，因而还需接受住院治疗。</p>
