<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>93岁大桥结构稳固 爆破后文风不动</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYpdi"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sjycbn3117/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10603171 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10-450x58.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="58" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月14日讯】当<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%85%B1%E5%BB%BA%E7%AD%91.md#1">公共建筑</a>年久失修，通常会面临拆迁再重建的命运，美国阿肯色州一座“高龄”93岁的大桥，当局决定以爆破方式将它拆除。不过，当炸药启动，一阵轰天巨响之后，大桥居然文风不动，令人惊讶它完善的建筑结构！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671642" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3b981577ba1ba79285d4f43f2e65bb5f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10671642 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3b981577ba1ba79285d4f43f2e65bb5f-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671642" class="wp-caption-text">当局预计以炸药爆破拆除这座“年久失修”的大桥。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2016年10月11日，北小石城（North Little Rock）阿肯色河上的百老汇大桥（Broadway Street Bridge）预计爆破拆除。当炸药准备就绪，按下爆破按钮之后，砂石粉尘夹杂着巨大声响传到岸上，围观的群众聚精会神注目着。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671644" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/fbb37dba7ce77d76bb23b4cec631600d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10671644 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/fbb37dba7ce77d76bb23b4cec631600d-450x455.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671644" class="wp-caption-text">一阵巨响，烟硝漫天。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没想到，烟尘渐渐散去后，大桥依然屹立不摇！有人不禁笑了出来，佩服大桥抵御爆破冲击的“实力坚强”，也有人怀疑：“刚刚真的有炸到桥吗？”民众对于这不寻常的景象感到好奇，比起爆破前，有更多人纷纷拿起手机拍摄和录影。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片（Video credit: Newsflare）?</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-217da490-7b0a-4cb7-6346-2fca8ffd9d28" data-id="player-217da490-7b0a-4cb7-6346-2fca8ffd9d28"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/217da490-7b0a-4cb7-6346-2fca8ffd9d28?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F28%2Fn10671235.md#1"></a></p>
<p>阿肯色州公路部门发言人施特雷斯勒（Danny Straessle）表示，拱桥和桥面板理论上要在30秒内坠入水中，但显然炸药失效了。“虽然<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%A5%E6%A2%81.md#1">桥梁</a>没有坍塌，但推论其结构已严重受损，随时可能倒下。”他呼吁民众不要靠近，因为大桥现在的情况非常危险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671646" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5b915c0cf154230da1806d82338f6b51.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10671646 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5b915c0cf154230da1806d82338f6b51-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671646" class="wp-caption-text">但却令人一头雾水，因为<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%A5%E6%A2%81.md#1">桥梁</a>闻风不动！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果炸药无法使桥梁坍崩，施特雷斯勒说，那代表桥梁被打造得非常稳固，接下来将会采取一些“机械”（mechanical）手段来完成拆迁任务。当局最后于下午决议求助驳船，利用2艘拖船把大桥拉下来。报导指出，新的百老汇大桥预计在169天内开放通行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10671648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/0d17c018706998ce537bd0f4bda11559.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10671648 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/0d17c018706998ce537bd0f4bda11559-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10671648" class="wp-caption-text">似乎是炸药量估算错误，网友则戏称此桥真的太坚固了。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有人询问，如果这么大桥这么坚固，还要拆了它吗？网友表示，爆破点位于桥梁的“关节”，照理说能轻易使桥梁坍塌，附带损害更小，也让碎片易于管理；不过，爆破失败，推测是爆破小组或许在强度计算上出了差池，许多建物也都能抵御某种程度的爆破，不代表结构依旧安全。</p>
