<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冰滑小坡藏危险 好心青年援手失足老人</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYJ50"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rlilro332/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rlilro332/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月28日讯】（大纪元记者陈安报导）冰天<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E5%9C%B0.md#1">雪地</a>里，除可能冻伤外，滑倒也是潜在的危险。加拿大一位青年帮助雪地<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%8C%E5%80%92.md#1">跌倒</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA.md#1">老人</a>，虽动作平凡简单，却温暖人心。</p>
<p>今年1月1日元旦，安大略省皮尔区伯纳姆瑟普路（Peel Regional）东边，有位正在吃汉堡王的民众看到一幕暖心景象，这景象当时被民众行车记录器录了下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1e8ccf1d76b2b740dba9a6b9992e40ef.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10671854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1e8ccf1d76b2b740dba9a6b9992e40ef-600x384.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="384" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA.md#1">老人</a>走冰滑的斜坡。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E5%9C%B0.md#1">雪地</a>里，一名老人正要走过积雪的小斜坡，模样看来有点吃力。由于路面太滑，一不小心，老人家还摔躺在地，缓缓转身试图面朝地，拾起物品准备往上爬。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-42dfda86-f81c-409e-7dfa-fb1a70d7f457" data-id="player-42dfda86-f81c-409e-7dfa-fb1a70d7f457"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/42dfda86-f81c-409e-7dfa-fb1a70d7f457?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F28%2Fn10671285.md#1"></a></p>
<p>面对这冰滑滑的小坡，老人真是无奈。所幸这时遇到一位着连帽衫的好心年轻人路过，他赶紧伸出援手，拉老人家一把。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/528d86ecf08327b3ce03bd3a8c048d18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10671858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/528d86ecf08327b3ce03bd3a8c048d18-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">老人在冰滑斜坡上<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%8C%E5%80%92.md#1">跌倒</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过程中，年轻人手上的食物还不小心落至地上，但他依然专注帮助老人，很快就将对方拉上平地。</p>
<p>幸好老人家没事，也谢谢这位好心人即时帮忙，不然老人家自己一人爬真是挺辛苦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10671861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/7851b7a03ffa80078912d552bb586791.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10671861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/7851b7a03ffa80078912d552bb586791-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">好心青年前去<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%90%80%E6%89%B6.md#1">搀扶</a>老人。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在不会下雪地区的朋友们可能觉得，有些老人过马时需要<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%90%80%E6%89%B6.md#1">搀扶</a>，看了这段影片，到了会下雪的地区，不要忘记关照走冰滑斜坡的行动不便人士喔。不是每个人都能像这位老人家一样幸运，倒了没事。简单一举动，社会更温暖。</p>
