<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：印尼龙目岛6.9级地震 死伤至少351人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011706354-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>印尼龙目岛地震后，人员伤亡严重。图为需要救济的民众。(ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月06日讯】8月5日，印度尼西亚<ahref="https://github.com/tfvizm3815/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E7%9B%AE%E5%B2%9B.md#1">龙目岛</a>发生6.9级<ahref="https://github.com/tfvizm3815/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E9%9C%87.md#1">地震</a>，截至目前出现120多次余震，死亡人数逐渐攀升，目前已知至少造成142人死亡、209人受重伤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10619024" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011706394.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10619024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011706394-600x400.jpg" alt="龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tfvizm3815/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E7%9B%AE%E5%B2%9B.md#1">龙目岛</a><ahref="https://github.com/tfvizm3815/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E9%9C%87.md#1">地震</a>后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10619049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011729822.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10619049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011729822-600x400.jpg" alt="龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5日晚间7点46分，震中位于龙目岛北部Loloan地区东南东外2公里处，震源深度为10.5公里。距离龙目岛很近的巴厘岛也有强烈震感。其中Tanahsong Daya附近发生了4.9级余震，Tanjung发生了5.4级余震。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10619032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011701410.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10619032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011701410-600x400.jpg" alt="龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10619034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011706352.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10619034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011706352-600x400.jpg" alt="龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>地震突发之后不久，印尼当地政府发布海啸预警。直到6日凌晨解除预警。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10619036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011704956.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10619036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/GettyImages-1011704956-600x400.jpg" alt="龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龙目岛地震后，出现人员伤亡。 (ADEK BERRY/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
