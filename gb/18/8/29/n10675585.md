<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>酸酸甜甜“辣味桔酱鸡” 开胃又下饭</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10603615 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月06日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">客家菜</a>，又称东江菜、闽西菜。客家庄传统料理大多以酱油、油膏等烹调方式，且客家人劳动出汗多亦需补充盐分以维持体力，因此饮食倾向多油多盐的重口味，不过目前已有偏向少油少盐的改良式客家菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675620" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d084d4b4b4c8cd54aa919ed9aa55840f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675620 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d084d4b4b4c8cd54aa919ed9aa55840f-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酸酸甜甜“辣味<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%94%E9%85%B1.md#1">桔酱</a>鸡” 开胃又下饭。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">客家菜</a>中，有一种相当重要的调味料──客家<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%94%E9%85%B1.md#1">桔酱</a>，传统的吃法就是桔酱搭配油膏来沾白斩鸡或五花肉，酸酸甜甜的口味，配上桔子的香气，不仅开胃也可以减少油腻感。</p>
<p>这次的料理像是<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%9D%AF%E9%B8%A1.md#1">三杯鸡</a>的客家版，是一道开胃又下饭的美味料理，特别使用客家桔酱来增添风味，让这道口味酸甜的“辣味桔酱鸡”来征服你的味蕾吧！</p>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><strong>●食材：</strong><br />
<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡腿肉</a> 500g<br />
姜片 30g<br />
辣椒片 15g<br />
香菜 30g</p>
<p><strong>●调味料：</strong><br />
客家桔酱 50g<br />
甜辣酱 20g<br />
糖 1小匙<br />
米酒 50cc<br />
水 100cc</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 将<ahref="https://github.com/rzcqqb352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡腿肉</a>切块，以滚水汆烫半熟后，备用。<br />
2. 起油锅爆香姜片，将姜片煸至金黄后，加入辣椒片拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675623" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/6912663b83ab1de43f400c65f07b047d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675623 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/6912663b83ab1de43f400c65f07b047d-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">起油锅爆香姜片，将姜片煸至金黄后，加入辣椒片拌炒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10675622" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33a82a9387eebfb37364ee8f3893c898.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675622 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33a82a9387eebfb37364ee8f3893c898-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入桔酱、甜辣酱、米酒、糖、胡椒、胡椒粒，煮开。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再加入桔酱、甜辣酱、米酒、糖、胡椒、胡椒粒，煮开。<br />
4. 接着加入鸡腿肉拌煮，再加入水、香菜后，以中火炒至酱汁浓稠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675621" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5319f13c9ee44dbbf7d429ba48878972.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675621 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5319f13c9ee44dbbf7d429ba48878972-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入鸡腿肉拌煮，再加入水、香菜，以中火炒至酱汁浓稠。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 最后挑出香菜即可呈盘，香菜、辣椒片装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675619" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/015e4d7a5893036693f1cb88ab905ae8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675619 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/015e4d7a5893036693f1cb88ab905ae8-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">香菜也可以换成其它香料，增添鸡肉的风味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>※贴心提醒：</strong>香菜也可以换成其它香料，例如：香草、迷迭香、百果香、薄荷等，可以增添鸡肉的风味。</p>
