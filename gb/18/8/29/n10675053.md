<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>粉色系“莓果棒棒糖” 带你找回幸福的滋味</h2>
<a href="https://dh48pl2d5tlkv.cloudfront.net/G3moqw39w"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imabdi3119/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/imabdi3119/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10603168 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-9-450x58.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="58" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月22日讯】这道“<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%93%E6%9E%9C.md#1">莓果</a><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E6%A3%92%E7%B3%96.md#1">棒棒糖</a>”甜点，黏满草莓<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">巧克力</a>球的外观，甜蜜蜜的味道在嘴中扩散出来，唤醒你充满粉红泡泡的少女心！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9784b6f0128fac81d4046bf06cbb4e52.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675103 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9784b6f0128fac81d4046bf06cbb4e52-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675103" class="wp-caption-text">粉色系“<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%93%E6%9E%9C.md#1">莓果</a><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E6%A3%92%E7%B3%96.md#1">棒棒糖</a>” 带你找回幸福的滋味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在国外，还有一家专门制造手工棒棒糖的公司，推出了一款星球棒棒糖，以十大行星为主题设计而成，且为了营造出宇宙的感觉，还特别将一侧的棒棒糖球体做成黑色，相当特别。</p>
<p>“棒棒糖”是由西班牙糖果商加倍佳创始人恩里克?伯纳特（Enric Bernat）所发明的，在糖果中插入小棍，使糖果可以让人们拿在手上吃，却不沾手，相当受孩子们的喜爱。</p>
<p>而棒棒糖除了由普通的硬糖所制成，发展到现在还有各种各样的形式，像是棉花糖棒棒糖，可以将棒棒糖做成更复杂的卡通造型；或是蛋糕棒棒糖，将蛋糕捏成球状后裹上<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">巧克力</a>，并在巧克力上做装饰，当成礼物送出去也很适合喔！</p>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A6%86%E7%9B%86%E5%AD%90.md#1">覆盆子</a>果泥 20g<br />
西点转化糖浆 5g<br />
奶油 5g<br />
白巧克力 45g<br />
可可粉 10g<br />
草莓巧克力脆球 20g</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A6%86%E7%9B%86%E5%AD%90.md#1">覆盆子</a>果泥、西点转化糖浆、奶油放入钢盆中，以隔水加热小火煮至奶油融化。<br />
2.加入白巧克力拌匀，加入过筛可可粉拌匀，制成果泥巧克力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c343762aff638336560a3e4ae64942ee.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675107 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c343762aff638336560a3e4ae64942ee-450x445.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675107" class="wp-caption-text">将果泥巧克力捏成球状，就可以开始装饰啰！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将果泥巧克力捏成球状，裹上装饰用的巧克力脆球即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675105" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/0f1ec22213ec8b9993ed42dd515eb879.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10675105 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/0f1ec22213ec8b9993ed42dd515eb879-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675105" class="wp-caption-text">裹上装饰用的巧克力脆球。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
