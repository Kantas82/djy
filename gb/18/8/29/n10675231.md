<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赖清德三度视察嘉县灾情 宣布以工代赈方案</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e50cfbc7e7676a7eca414281f5a825aa-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>行政院长赖清德第三度视察嘉义县灾情。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月29日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）0823大雨带给嘉义县严重<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%B9%E6%B0%B4.md#1">淹水</a>灾情，行政院长<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%96%E6%B8%85%E5%BE%B7.md#1">赖清德</a>29日第三度到<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>县视察灾况，嘉义县长张花冠、立委陈明文、蔡易余也都陪同赖清德视察包括布袋镇贵舍里、考试里，东石乡东崙村、西崙村、掌潭村了解处置情形并慰问灾民及国军弟兄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/799a1aa04265472b76c0ae55ecb3ff27.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10675234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/799a1aa04265472b76c0ae55ecb3ff27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675234" class="wp-caption-text">行政院长<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%96%E6%B8%85%E5%BE%B7.md#1">赖清德</a>视察嘉义县灾情。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>善用抽水机及救灾人员调度得宜，加大抽排能量，包括东石和布袋多数地区<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%B9%E6%B0%B4.md#1">淹水</a>清形已改善许多，不少居民皆开始清理家园，赖清德等人也依序慰问灾民，询问家园清理状况，提醒民众清理完后要记得消毒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bb8e2780a3e93b48bc1f956852d9efef.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10675235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bb8e2780a3e93b48bc1f956852d9efef-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675235" class="wp-caption-text">行政院长赖清德视察嘉义县灾情。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>让大家松了一口气，截至29日早上12点，东石乡塭仔村、栗仔崙(东崙村、西崙村)洲仔村现已无淹水，仅剩局部低漥道路积水；掌潭村道路现已抽干。布袋镇贵舍里(新十全驾训班)现已抽干，且无积水。考试里现无淹水，仅剩道路积水10公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e727874fe7af7e88ceb678202396770c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10675236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e727874fe7af7e88ceb678202396770c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675236" class="wp-caption-text">行政院长赖清德视察嘉义县灾情。 （嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>赖清德表示，目前劳动部已提出“<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%A5%E5%B7%A5%E4%BB%A3%E8%B5%88.md#1">以工代赈</a>”方案，每月有170小时可以申请，每小时140元，鼓励民众参与在地家乡重建，帮助灾民清理家园，希望透过此方案加上补助及善心灾款，让灾民的生活可以尽早步入正轨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675237" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/518a23e3978671608413d836fe539c4d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10675237" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/518a23e3978671608413d836fe539c4d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675237" class="wp-caption-text">至地方信仰中心膜拜。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>赖清德强调，后续的水利治理方案中央会与地方研议，加强督导执行把治水工作做好，而环境消毒也会请国军弟兄帮忙，并请相关单位将嘉义县列为重点地区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10675238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10675238" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/dcd7b882217f70b4d394a98bf301fc70.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10675238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/dcd7b882217f70b4d394a98bf301fc70-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10675238" class="wp-caption-text">国军弟兄协助消毒。 （嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张花冠表示，赖清德三度到嘉义县勘灾，显现赖清德对于嘉义县灾情的重视，也感谢总统蔡英文及赖清德的中央全力支援，让灾情可以顺利控制。</p>
<p>张花冠指出，针对嘉义县的受灾户，目前每户有县府提供5000元及行政院2万协助救灾，并请社会局成立劝募天然灾害现金救济专户及县府员工捐出1日所得赈灾，协助灾民早日回归正常生活。◇</p>
