<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾古早味点心 “紫白玫瑰”芋头酥</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月20日讯】外形为圆球状的<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%8B%E5%A4%B4.md#1">芋头</a>酥、以芋泥作为甜馅制成的台湾酥饼点心，在台湾<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E7%82%B9%E5%BF%83.md#1">传统点心</a>里占有一席之地，而这种传统的小点心，也深受长辈们的欢迎。</p>
<p>使用台中大甲盛产的<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%8B%E5%A4%B4.md#1">芋头</a>，自制而成的<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%8B%E5%A4%B4%E9%85%A5.md#1">芋头酥</a>，散发浓郁的芋头香，渐层的紫色螺旋饼皮，像极了紫色玫瑰；外皮层次分明，香酥而不油腻，配上里头绵密的芋头内馅，一口咬下超满足！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10674972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/11-22.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674972 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/11-22-450x401.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674972" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%8B%E5%A4%B4%E9%85%A5.md#1">芋头酥</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备食材】</h2>
<p><strong>●油皮：</strong><br />
冷水 114g<br />
细砂糖 26g<br />
食用紫色色膏 2g<br />
中筋面粉 260g<br />
奶油 104g（可用猪油代替）</p>
<p><strong>●油酥：</strong><br />
低筋面粉 170g<br />
奶油 85g</p>
<p><strong>●馅料：</strong><br />
芋头馅 240g</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 冷水、细砂糖倒入钢盆中拌匀，再加入食用紫色色膏拌匀后，加入中筋面粉与奶油揉成团，并松弛10分钟成油皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10674973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/22-17.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674973 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/22-17-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674973" class="wp-caption-text">冷水、细砂糖、紫色色膏拌匀后，加入中筋面粉与奶油揉成团。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 低筋面粉加入奶油，以二比一成分揉成无颗粒雪白色的团状油酥。<br />
3. 油皮分割每个50公克，油酥分成每个25g、芋头馅分成每个25g。<br />
4. 油皮包入油酥并擀卷二次成三折，再擀至擀面棍长度，再卷并一切为二。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10674974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33-12.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674974 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33-12-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674974" class="wp-caption-text">油皮包入油酥。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10674975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/44-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674975 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/44-13-450x380.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674975" class="wp-caption-text">擀卷二次成三折，再擀至擀面棍长度。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10674976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674976" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/55-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674976 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/55-6-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674976" class="wp-caption-text">擀卷后再一切为二。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 一半油皮酥包入内馅再收口并微压一下，并以上火210℃／下火190℃烤约15分钟至金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10674978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10674978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/77-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10674978 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/77-3-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10674978" class="wp-caption-text">以上火210℃／下火190℃烤约15分钟至金黄。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>※贴心提醒：</strong>油皮包入油酥，擀卷收口处要皮薄一些。油皮酥包入内馅再收口，并稍微压一下，让可以让层层折痕清楚呈现。</p>
