<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黑冰好吓人！女子踩冰上站不稳连狗狗都打滑</h2>
<a href="https://d282b678eon3c.cloudfront.net/J9tMNemQd"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/idzwdk333/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603171 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-10-450x58.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="58" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月18日讯】住在英国西南部的一位女子，雪季外出仍不忘爱美，穿上帅气的粗跟高跟长靴，但不易察觉的“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%86%B0.md#1">黑冰</a>”（black ice）居然出现在她家门口！让她与爱犬瞬间像在溜花式<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0.md#1">冰</a>刀，滑个不停。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%86%B0.md#1">黑冰</a>，是指路面上一层薄的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0.md#1">冰</a>壳，虽然冰本身并不是黑色的，但视觉上透明，往往可以透过它看到下面黑色的路面。人或车走在上面，很难看清有冰的区域，容易导致侧滑失控。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e84ba32ed09b8751a89837c4e5e16b57.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10663665 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/e84ba32ed09b8751a89837c4e5e16b57-600x390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663665" class="wp-caption-text">女子穿上帅气的粗跟高跟长靴，但不易察觉的“黑冰”居然出现在她家门口！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10663656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663656" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-62.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10663656 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-62-600x470.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="470" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663656" class="wp-caption-text">人或车走在黑冰上面，很难看清有冰的区域，容易导致侧滑失控。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女子居住的肯特郡为沿海区域，位于伦敦的西南部，2018年2月已连续下了好几天的大雪，气象播报，大雪受到“极地<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%A9%E6%B6%A1.md#1">漩涡</a>”（polar vortex）的影响。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">观看完整影片（Credit: Newsflare）?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-ff57b74a-b12a-49b2-6016-db510c0a3794" data-id="player-ff57b74a-b12a-49b2-6016-db510c0a3794"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/ff57b74a-b12a-49b2-6016-db510c0a3794?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F24%2Fn10663470.md#1"></a></p>
<p>被英国人称为“来自东方的猛兽”（Beast from the East）的极地<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BC%A9%E6%B6%A1.md#1">漩涡</a>，有点像飓风，是一团会绕着南极或北极逆时针快速旋转并移动的气旋。</p>
<p>就像一些台湾人在台风天愿意玩命泛舟，或许对于高纬度国家来说，大雪和极地漩涡虽然可怕，面对时因为习惯了也没那么紧张，女子还是可以打扮得漂漂亮亮，穿上高跟靴子出门。</p>
<p>当然，以上都是不好的示范，珍惜生命才是对自己和他人负责任的表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-38.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10663662 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-38-600x473.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663662" class="wp-caption-text">长靴女自家门口踩冰<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E6%BB%91.md#1">打滑</a>停不了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10663664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-24.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10663664 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-24-600x567.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663664" class="wp-caption-text">她瞬间像在溜花式冰刀，滑个不停。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于影片中的女子，脚底像抹油一般频频<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E6%BB%91.md#1">打滑</a>个不停，甚至最后放声大喊：“救我！”看起来实在很夸张。网友也讽刺，真的太爱演了。但冬天的结冰地面可不是开玩笑，大家还是要小心。</p>
<p>网友分享，住在一到冬季就会地面结冰的地区，不仅小孩容易滑倒，自己也有多次“爬行”的纪录，因为连站都站不稳；有人回应表示赞同，她因为无法安全开车，最后只好搬回温暖的城市。</p>
