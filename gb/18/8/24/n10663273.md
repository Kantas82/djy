<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>总统蔡英文视讯关心嘉义县灾情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3929a4cee45e1fe04740afea666f47ab-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义县长张花冠与副县长吴芳铭与国军准备进入灾区勘灾、救灾。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）致灾性<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%AA%E5%A4%A7%E9%9B%A8.md#1">豪大雨</a>造成<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>县严重<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%B9%E6%B0%B4%E7%81%BE%E6%83%85.md#1">淹水灾情</a>，县长<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%8A%B1%E5%86%A0.md#1">张花冠</a>24日上午再度召开灾害应变中心工作会报，做出高潜势地区进行预防性撤离等多项指示；总统蔡英文关心嘉义县灾情，中午与张花冠进行视讯，了解灾况及处置情形，强调中央会全力支援地方救灾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a85ae4423f1c9be3e563d803c33bbb69.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/a85ae4423f1c9be3e563d803c33bbb69-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663276" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长<ahref="https://github.com/athhov341/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%8A%B1%E5%86%A0.md#1">张花冠</a>协助撤离民众。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>24日凌晨至上午12时全国最高雨量就落在嘉义县，12小时累积雨量484毫米，布袋、义竹等15个乡镇市24小时累积雨量都已超过500毫米，鹿草乡更达到783毫米，县长张花冠24日凌晨前往沿海地区了解灾况、探视收容所安置的民众后，上午在消防局灾变中心听取相关单位工作简报，以掌握最新灾情与处置情形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ff01ae1d6c04369da98959b972cdd0ba.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ff01ae1d6c04369da98959b972cdd0ba-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663277" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长张花冠与副县长吴芳铭涉水进入灾区。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>截至24日中午，嘉义县有11乡镇、47处淹水，撤离156人，收容134人，学校淹水22所，警力投入627人。8/24 13:00截至目前收容安置情形：布袋镇：16人安置于布袋镇公所。东石乡：18人安置于福灵宫；53人安置于洲仔代天府。朴子市：15人安置于朴子市公所。太保市：29人安置于后潭镇福宫香客大楼；25人安置于新埤集会所。鹿草乡：21人安置于后堀活动中心。六脚乡：3人安置于湾北活动中心。新港乡：4人安置于新港卫生所。民雄乡：13人安置于民雄乡公所。义竹乡：18人安置于修缘禅寺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663278" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f5facfd9b0d362a4231ab177482b7acc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/f5facfd9b0d362a4231ab177482b7acc-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663278" class="wp-caption-text">张县长搭橡皮艇进入布袋淹水区域。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国军单位也有南区联合测考中心、陆军机步234旅、五二工兵群及嘉义后备指挥部投入救灾，支援云豹甲车及轻、中型战术轮车、悍马车等5类21辆次，兵力241员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/718cd73e1f2517411b226107618ef6c8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/718cd73e1f2517411b226107618ef6c8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663279" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县长张花冠与副县长吴芳铭探视安置中心长辈。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张花冠指示水利处持续申请中央支援调度抽水机、掌握各抽水站和乡镇公所抽水机运作情形；针对水保局已对嘉义县发布87条土石流红色警戒和9条土石流黄色警戒，张花冠要求包括阿里山乡、竹崎乡、番路乡、大埔乡等山区乡镇高潜势危险区域进行预防性撤离，并完全收容安置所准备工作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c6d662581e5b74ccaeee419abc1708eb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c6d662581e5b74ccaeee419abc1708eb-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663280" class="wp-caption-text">嘉义县消防局以橡皮艇撤离灾区民众。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在交通部分，张花冠指示建设处密切掌握道路和桥梁，视情况进行封路封桥措施，并请警察局配合警戒工作，以免发生意外事故，农业处也应密切掌握蔬菜供应与价格，落实调节工作，避免灾后发生价格大幅波动情形。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10663281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663281" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/97f78846cc945a1f9a3bdc580643aa56.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/97f78846cc945a1f9a3bdc580643aa56-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663281" class="wp-caption-text">国军勘灾及救灾。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10663282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10663282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/579e8d644873b0efd16eb3a5868e558c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10663282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/579e8d644873b0efd16eb3a5868e558c-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10663282" class="wp-caption-text">国军勘灾及救灾。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
