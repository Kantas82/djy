<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>啄木鸟驻足行进车窗 听到贴心话反应令人惊奇</h2>
<a href="https://d1eqe8yfgc9j6n.cloudfront.net/E8raCFhpy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hcmkav320/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/hcmkav320/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月04日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）美国芝加哥有位男子开车过程中，遇到一只<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%84%E6%9C%A8%E9%B8%9F.md#1">啄木鸟</a>停落在车窗上。男子不断和它讲话，鸟儿始终痴痴望向他，最后还飞进车里，跳到他的手臂、肩膀上，有如见到故人，不忍离去。</p>
<p>在2016年4月拍摄的影片中，我们可以听到这位司机对着站在车窗的<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%84%E6%9C%A8%E9%B8%9F.md#1">啄木鸟</a>说：“你想要看看芝加哥？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10615688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10615688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ae00113670c8d71d5562600b6d9f5c87.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615688 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ae00113670c8d71d5562600b6d9f5c87-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10615688" class="wp-caption-text">男子问鸟儿：““你想要看看芝加哥？”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸟儿默然，男子再问：“耶？怎么了？你还好吗？”此时似乎遇到红绿灯，车停了下来。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f7d0aaa3-58b3-4e27-4ebc-4c131c3a1e9c" data-id="player-f7d0aaa3-58b3-4e27-4ebc-4c131c3a1e9c"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f7d0aaa3-58b3-4e27-4ebc-4c131c3a1e9c?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F4%2Fn10615009.md#1"></a></p>
<p>车停口未停，“好奇男”继续对啄木鸟说道：“你现在想要离开？你还好吗？你想要走了？看看你，你很漂亮！”鸟儿闭上眼睛，是不好意思了？</p>
<p>虽然鸟儿没回应，但过了一会儿，它继续痴痴望着男子，这时红绿灯转绿，<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">车子</a>可以继续开动了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10615690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10615690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1fb420eeae09d9149bf381eaa66b3a9f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615690 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1fb420eeae09d9149bf381eaa66b3a9f-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10615690" class="wp-caption-text">男子称赞啄木鸟很漂亮，鸟儿闭眼表示不好意思？（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男子开车向前，凉风不断吹着鸟儿，男子也一直不厌其烦地对它“自言自语”。</p>
<p>小鸟在风中的忍耐力毕竟有限，这时啄木鸟终于……受不了这好奇男的“百般问候”，赶紧飞呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10615701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10615701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c119e90732235213bcdb8209aae265ea.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615701 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c119e90732235213bcdb8209aae265ea-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10615701" class="wp-caption-text">鸟儿被风吹着。男子关切的说：“风会很大了。”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哦！神奇的事出现了，原来它不是飞走，而是从车窗飞了进来，落在了男子的手臂！</p>
<p>男子立刻惊讶又冷静地说道：“哇，你现在跑到里面来了，离我实在非常近，你还好吗？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10615691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10615691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/84c3201954a926788b1fa1ea34be73e4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615691 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/84c3201954a926788b1fa1ea34be73e4-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10615691" class="wp-caption-text">鸟儿飞到男子的手臂。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然鸟儿还是保持静默，但看来它挺喜欢这一直讲话的“好奇男”！</p>
<p>看完影片，不少网友觉得这真是奇妙的邂逅：啄木鸟不仅不怕生，还飞到陌生人身上，可能是有很深的缘分吧？！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10615694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10615694" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1b1cd9e3ffd4c29bf932cde15b39b6e1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615694 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1b1cd9e3ffd4c29bf932cde15b39b6e1-450x438.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10615694" class="wp-caption-text">啄木鸟跑到男子肩膀上。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>致力帮助拯救候鸟的“芝加哥鸟类撞击监测组织”（Chicago Bird Collision Monitors）负责人安妮特．普林斯（Annette Prince）表示，虽然影片中的鸟儿看起来很可爱，不过，“这是一只飞到市中心车窗上茫然不适的黄腹吸汁啄木鸟”。</p>
<p>她说，数千只这样的鸟将在5月间飞经芝加哥。“它们不是城市鸟类，不应该在城里，更不用说待在<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">车子</a>上。它们非常困惑，但不一定会被人吓到。”</p>
<p>普林斯还说，黄腹吸汁啄木鸟常在受惊后迷失方向，城市对它们来说就像是外国。“它们就像来到芝加哥的小游客。”</p>
