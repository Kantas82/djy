<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>外星人家庭聚会后开UFO回家？警车紧追其后</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JLbt1"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rdnfbz347/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月12日讯】（大纪元记者陈安报导）警察驶车抓嫌犯是很正常的事，但警察驶车抓UFO……这到底发生什么事？！</p>
<p>2016年7月29日，爱尔兰韦克斯福德郡（Wexford）的戈里（Gorey）街上突然冒出一架“<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E7%A2%9F.md#1">飞碟</a>”。虽然好像很帅，不过飞碟后方有台发出响亮鸣笛声的<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E8%BD%A6.md#1">警车</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10631159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4d1746ce42ccb01e54a15bddc0254039.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10631159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4d1746ce42ccb01e54a15bddc0254039-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E8%BD%A6.md#1">警车</a>追着<ahref="https://github.com/rdnfbz347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E7%A2%9F.md#1">飞碟</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>员警似乎觉得前方那家伙很可疑，一直紧追不放。但不知为何，飞碟好像隔音效果特别好，听不到鸣笛声，继续向前行……</p>
<p>哈哈，这亲眼看到的人大概都傻了。当场还有路人忍不住把这搞笑画面拍下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10631157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/341f6f398e2d5ae7c10ae84033297d78.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10631157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/341f6f398e2d5ae7c10ae84033297d78-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">飞碟难得一见，怎能不拍一下呢？（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-c53494ac-d0b3-46bd-5dde-09803b081c1c" data-id="player-c53494ac-d0b3-46bd-5dde-09803b081c1c"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/c53494ac-d0b3-46bd-5dde-09803b081c1c?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F8%2Fn10624148.md#1"></a></p>
<p>这飞碟到底是在干嘛？原来，这是当地艺术家阿里．凯末尔．阿里（Ali Kemal Ali）的作品，用来当作第三空间乍现艺术展（Third Space Pop Up Art Show）的一部分，此艺术展在前年7月29日于戈里公园（Gorey Park）举办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10631178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/23ba9de7dfce8243ac2e4fe0b4ddfd1d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10631178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/23ba9de7dfce8243ac2e4fe0b4ddfd1d-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这飞碟原来是用来展览的？！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网友看后逗趣留言：“家庭聚会后外星人开车回家。”</p>
<p>还有人说：“它不会飞，所以要改称叫‘UO’。”（注：UFO为“unidentified flying object”的简写，flying意为“飞行的”。）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10631173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1cfb35aeb0c6ec354e25b469facf4bc8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10631173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1cfb35aeb0c6ec354e25b469facf4bc8-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">追了一阵子，警车还是继续追。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
