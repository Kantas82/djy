<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鹦鹉为音乐陶醉 头如电风扇 狂转到晕眩</h2>
<a href="https://d37g5boc7rm625.cloudfront.net/X1dojBqYg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imggnv371/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/imggnv371/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月08日讯】（大纪元记者陈安报导）动物闻歌起舞时有所闻，舞风千姿百态，有的摆动身体，有的晃动手脚扭扭脖子，有的还会摆动耳朵……现在，就来看看<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>特别的“<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E9%A3%8E%E6%89%87.md#1">电风扇</a>”舞！</p>
<p>美国康乃狄克州有只站在白色鸟笼上的<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>，听到现场美妙<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>，情不自禁一直<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%AC%E5%A4%B4.md#1">转头</a>，这转头可不是左看右看、身体左摆右摆而已，而是像<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E9%A3%8E%E6%89%87.md#1">电风扇</a>那样，360度大旋转！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c85a8b2223d4d819391b07be32116e7d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10620857 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c85a8b2223d4d819391b07be32116e7d-450x662.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="662" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620857" class="wp-caption-text">正在跳“电风扇舞”的鹦鹉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-86e71c28-bb45-44c2-6b72-187853143d08" data-id="player-86e71c28-bb45-44c2-6b72-187853143d08"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/86e71c28-bb45-44c2-6b72-187853143d08?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F7%2Fn10620428.md#1"></a></p>
<p>鹦鹉的“舞姿”虽单调，看起来却非常逗趣，在场的女主人跟着唱和，而旁边的女孩也忍不住灿笑帮它打节拍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1312e7d59290d5c31b10bc1f2b1c195f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10620859 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1312e7d59290d5c31b10bc1f2b1c195f-450x670.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="670" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620859" class="wp-caption-text">女孩在鹦鹉旁灿笑、打拍子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只见这只鹦鹉的头一直转，一直转，转个不停。光看它持续转动的时间就觉得，好累呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ba306f1aacab25ce751c9a6609f541c1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10620864 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/ba306f1aacab25ce751c9a6609f541c1-450x669.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620864" class="wp-caption-text">“电风扇”终于停止运转了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从影片播放秒数可得知，这只鹦鹉竟至少转了31秒……就在第32秒时，奇妙的一刻出现了，鹦鹉停止<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%AC%E5%A4%B4.md#1">转头</a>了！不过，可能是刚刚转得太多次，鹦鹉竟然呈现“头晕”状态，整个头不受控制，不断左右左右轻轻晃动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10620867" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10620867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1cb787227ebf453a36551d878395e72d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10620867 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1cb787227ebf453a36551d878395e72d-450x672.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="672" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10620867" class="wp-caption-text">鹦鹉的头虽停止转动，却头晕不止。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>等鹦鹉的头慢慢恢复正常时，眼睛却还没恢复正常，只见那黑眼珠无法自主地摆动，一副头晕未解貌，在旁的女主人、女孩见这搞笑的奇景都忍不住惊叹笑出声。</p>
<p>大约头晕8秒钟，鹦鹉“外表”终于平静下来……</p>
