<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>抢购东湾超值房</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6_Metro-Crossing-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东湾新房：Metro Crossing小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月09日讯】暑假刚开始时，为大家介绍了很多<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E6%96%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">南湾新房</a>，南湾身处硅谷中心，一直是火热房市的最中心地段，房价也相对比较高。而与南湾相邻的东湾房价则更加亲民。如果您在有限的预算下，希望买到更宽敞舒适的住房，那麽东湾无疑是很好的选择。下面就为您介绍几处正在热销，且值得投资的东湾新建小区：</p>
<p>1. 位于Fremont，Robson Homes开发的The Abbey小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701164" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1_the-abbey.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1_the-abbey-600x287.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701164" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%96%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">东湾新房</a>：The Abbey小区。<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7%E7%BB%8F%E7%BA%AA.md#1">湾区房地产经纪</a>Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个小区位于湾区顶尖校区之一的Mission San Jose校区内，从小学到初中高中都十分优秀，距离著名的Mission San Jose高中仅仅1.5英里。总共有79套3层式的独立别墅，HOA月管理费为article content begin -->79。房屋均为3到4居室，居住面积在1,795到2,381平方英尺之间，售价为140万到170万之间。</p>
<p>2. 位于Fremont，Robson Homes开发的Orchard Heights小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2_orchard-heights.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2_orchard-heights-600x349.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701167" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%B9%BE%E6%96%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">东湾新房</a>：Orchard Heights小区。<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7%E7%BB%8F%E7%BA%AA.md#1">湾区房地产经纪</a>Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样位于顶尖的Mission San Jose校区，均为豪华的2层独立别墅，且都有独立的花园，共有4种户型可供选择，4到5房，居住面积在2,792到3,601平方英尺之间，价格在230万起。</p>
<p>3. 位于Fremont，Robson Homes开发的Birdsong小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3_birdsong.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3_birdsong-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701169" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%96%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区新房</a>：Birdsong小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样位于顶尖的Mission San Jose校区，靠近Fremont风景秀丽的中央公园，均为3层的双拼别墅，3到4个卧房，居住面积为1,660到2,114平方英尺，价格在130万起。</p>
<p>4. 位于Fremont，TriPointe开发的Apex at Mission Stevenson小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4_Apex-at-Mission-Stevenson.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4_Apex-at-Mission-Stevenson-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701172" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%96%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区新房</a>：Apex at Mission Stevenson小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>靠近中央公园，学区中等，有联体别墅和公寓可供选择，2到4房，居住面积为1,023到1,848平方英尺之间，价格在80多万到115万左右。</p>

<p>6. 位于Fremont，Toll Brothers开发的Metro Crossing小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6_Metro-Crossing.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6_Metro-Crossing-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701174" class="wp-caption-text">东湾新房：Metro Crossing小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个大型豪华公寓式小区位于Fremont市的Warm Springs地区，距离南湾较近，可步行到BART新开设的 Warm Springs站，北通奥克兰、旧金山，南面可达圣何西。小区内有4种不同类型的公寓建筑，每栋楼都设有公共电梯。共有560套住房，小区内设有游泳池、健身房等公共设施。房屋有1居室到4居室多种不同户型，居住面积在789到3,300平方英尺之间，价格则在85万到160万不等。开车5分钟可达大华99华人超市。该小区不出售给投资出租用途的买家。</p>

<figure id="attachment_10701178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9_Glass-Bay.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9_Glass-Bay-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701178" class="wp-caption-text">东湾新房：Glass Bay小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>10. 位于Newark，William Lyon Homes开发的Bayshores小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/10_Bayshores.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/10_Bayshores-600x351.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701180" class="wp-caption-text">东湾新房：Bayshores小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>11. 位于Newark，Lennar开发的Lighthouse小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11_lighthouse.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11_lighthouse-600x325.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701183" class="wp-caption-text">东湾新房：Lighthouse小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lighthouse小区同样紧挨着上面的Glass Bay小区。这个全部为3层联排别墅的Lighthouse小区共有4种户型，88套住房。2到4房，居住面积在1,424到2,109平方英尺之间，售价在91.5万到111万之间。</p>
<p>12. 位于Union City，D.R.Horton 开发的Monarch at Soares Ranch小区</p>
<figure id="attachment_10701209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10701209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/012_Monarch.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10701209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/012_Monarch-600x342.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10701209" class="wp-caption-text">东湾新房：Monarch at Soares Ranch小区。（湾区房地产经纪Li Jin提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里近880高速公路，位于92号大桥和84号大桥之间，共63套3层的联体别墅，2到4房，居住面积在1,441到2,341平方英尺之间。8月份上市，售价在85万左右起。</p>
<p>在选购新房时，切记要找到一位值得信赖的地产经纪人一起去选购，经纪人的佣金都是由卖方承担，对于买方而言无论您是否有经纪人房价和购房手续费用都是一样的。</p>
