<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Mountain view、Sunnyvale上市房</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月16日讯】（大纪元记者李欧整理报导）本周来到Mountain view、Sunnyvale看房，与上周的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/18/9/9/n10700898.md#1" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Campbell、Santa Clara</a>相比，这里更加精华，房价明显上升几十万不等，但土地面积更小，庭院逐渐成为一种奢侈，室内空间也较为拥挤，这就是住在核心区所要取舍之处。不过在这里的学区相对更好，房产体质强健，对房产泡沫的抵抗力强大。</p>
<p>一、1854 Appletree Lane, Mountain View, CA 94040<br />
<figure id="attachment_10718662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_257905973-0.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_257905973-0-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718662" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718662" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">南湾上市房</a>，经过更新的前院与墙外粉刷。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10718663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_257905973-7.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_257905973-7-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718663" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718663" class="wp-caption-text">小巧精致的客厅。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这是一个干净且宁静的社区，房子是经典的加州牧场风格，并经过大范围更新。室内有一个带木梁天花板的餐厅、中央壁炉的开放式起居室，以及两扇可欣赏后院景致的落地窗、明亮的厨房虽不大但设备齐全，包括一个轻松的早餐角落。<br />
屋内的卧室、卫生间也得到升级，有新的地毯、油漆、梳妆台、淋浴设备等等。除了主卧单独卫浴，其它3间卧室共用一间的大浴室。这里享有优质校区，从小学到高中都有很棒的公立学校。身处硅谷核心，一切都很便利。<figure id="attachment_10718664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.10.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.10-600x376.png" alt="" width="600" b="376" class="size-large wp-image-10718664" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718664" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">南湾地产</a>：1854 Appletree Lane, Mountain View, CA 94040。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、2451 Benjamin Drive, Mountain View, CA 94043<br />
<figure id="attachment_10718665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718665" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_257035363-2.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_257035363-2-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718665" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718665" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">南湾地产</a>：新建的现代式建筑。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10718666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_257035363-4.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_257035363-4-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718666" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718666" class="wp-caption-text">开放空间的客厅与厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这处位在Monta Loma社区的新建现代式房产，提供全新的生活空间，还包括一套姻亲套房。室内阳光充足，瓷砖地板，采用辐射取暖。客厅与厨房、餐厅采开放空间；天花板是红木木梁带天窗。<br />
附近有众多商店、健身房、餐馆，Costco也在不远处，或可到Palo Alto儿童博物馆，更可步行前往火车站。这里享有Los Altos高中（买家自行验证）。<figure id="attachment_10718667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718667" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.19.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.19-600x367.png" alt="" width="600" b="367" class="size-large wp-image-10718667" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718667" class="wp-caption-text">南湾地产：2451 Benjamin Drive, Mountain View, CA 94043。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、781 Peekskill Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087<br />
<figure id="attachment_10718668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718668" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_252043803-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_252043803-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718668" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718668" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">南湾上市房</a>，两层楼4房房产。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10718670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_252043803-5.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_252043803-5-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718670" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718670" class="wp-caption-text">开放式厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
位在Cumberland社区，房产两层楼经多处更新，客厅和厨房同样采开放空间，厨房虽有高级订制橱柜，不过华人买家需要注意，此厨房无抽油烟机。此房除了主屋外，后院还有独立小屋，配有订制橱柜和推拉门，可直通游泳池、烧烤区。<br />
住在Sunnyvale不必担心生活购物问题，餐馆、商店、公园、图书馆、学校都很邻近。这里也享有优质学区，从小学到高中都有不错的评级。<figure id="attachment_10718671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.30.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.30-600x364.png" alt="" width="600" b="364" class="size-large wp-image-10718671" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718671" class="wp-caption-text">南湾上市房：781 Peekskill Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、417 Molino Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94086<br />
<figure id="attachment_10718672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_258299603-0.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_258299603-0-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718672" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718672" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>：417 Molino Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94086。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10718673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718673" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_258299603-5.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_258299603-5-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10718673" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718673" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，半开放式的环状厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
在这街道干净、邻居花园修整得宜的社区，该房产也得到升级，进门有带壁炉的客厅和用餐区，入内是半开放式的厨房，空间宽大并附有吧台，最后面的起居室也带有壁炉，且有落地窗直通迷人的后院。<br />
孩子可以轻松到达Cumberland Elementary、Sunnyvale Middle和Homestead High等优秀学校（买家验证）。购物、餐饮无不便利。#<figure id="attachment_10718674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10718674" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.39.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/12.54.39-600x363.png" alt="" width="600" b="363" class="size-large wp-image-10718674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10718674" class="wp-caption-text">湾区赏房：417 Molino Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94086。</figcaption></figure></p>
