<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>第70届艾美奖揭晓 《权力的游戏》摘最佳剧集</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035225964-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>当地时间17日晚，第70届电视艾美奖颁奖典礼在洛杉矶揭晓，《冰与火之歌：权力的游戏》（Game of Thrones）获最佳剧集奖。 (ROBYN BECK/AFP/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月18日讯】（大纪元记者佟亦加报导）当地时间17日晚，第70届电视<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BE%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">艾美奖</a>颁奖礼在洛杉矶微软剧院（Microsoft Theater）举行，好莱坞明星大腕齐聚一堂，各奖项一一揭晓。《冰与火之歌：权力的游戏》（<em>Game of Thrones</em>）拿下剧情类最佳剧集，视帝后分别由马修·瑞斯（Matthew Rhys）和克莱尔·福伊（Claire Foy）夺得。</p>
<p>去年缺席参评的<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E6%9D%83%E5%8A%9B%E7%9A%84%E6%B8%B8%E6%88%8F%E3%80%8B.md#1">《权力的游戏》</a>以22项的提名回归领跑，最终将当晚最大奖剧情类最佳剧集收入囊中，这也是自2015年以来，该剧第三次摘得这项大奖。</p>
<p>而《了不起的麦瑟尔夫人》（<em>Rachel Brosnahan</em>）一连拿最佳喜剧类剧集、最佳导演、最佳编剧、最佳女主角、最佳女配角共五奖，成当晚最大赢家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10722095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10722095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177318-450x587.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10722095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177318-450x587.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10722095" class="wp-caption-text">马修·瑞斯（Matthew Rhys）凭借《美国谍梦》（The Americans）夺得最佳男主角。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>男女主角方面，主演《美国谍梦》（<em>The Americans</em>）的马修·瑞斯终于结束陪跑，收获期盼已久的最佳剧情类男主角奖杯。《王冠》（<em>The Crown</em>）的女主角克莱尔·芙伊获得剧情类最佳女主，面对伊丽莎白二世这一挑战极高的角色，她拿捏得恰到好处，演技颇受认可和好评。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10721977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10721977" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177624-450x610.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10721977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177624-450x610.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="610" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10721977" class="wp-caption-text">《王冠》（The Crown）的女主角克莱尔·福伊（Claire Foy）荣膺视后。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>凭借《嗜血娇娃》（<em>Killing Eve</em>）首次入围剧情类女主角的亚裔演员吴珊卓（Sandra Oh），与安迪·萨姆伯格（Andy Samberg）颁发最佳喜剧编剧奖。只见她先来了一个假动作，直接撕掉信封说：“得奖的是——《爱乐之城》（<em>La La Land</em>）！”调侃去年的奥斯卡颁奖嘉宾，错把最佳影片颁发给《爱乐之城》的乌龙事件。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723373" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035225612-450x603.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10723373 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035225612-450x603.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="603" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723373" class="wp-caption-text">吴珊卓（Sandra Oh）与安迪·萨姆伯格（Andy Samberg）颁发最佳喜剧编剧奖，调侃2017年奥斯卡颁奖乌龙事件。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在颁奖现场，出现浪漫感人的一幕：凭借执导奥斯卡金像奖获得综艺节目最佳导演的格伦·维斯（Glenn Weiss），上台发表完获奖感言后，突然单膝下跪，向台下的女友求婚，令全场爆发欢呼和掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10722251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10722251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035178396-450x619.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10722251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035178396-450x619.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="619" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10722251" class="wp-caption-text">凭借执导奥斯卡金像奖获得综艺节目最佳导演的格伦·维斯（Glenn Weiss），在颁奖礼现场向女友求婚。(Frazer Harrison/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>热门剧集《西部世界》（<em>Westworld</em>）去年在<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BE%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">艾美奖</a>获21项提名，紧随<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E6%9D%83%E5%8A%9B%E7%9A%84%E6%B8%B8%E6%88%8F%E3%80%8B.md#1">《权力的游戏》</a>。但在当晚的在颁奖礼上，只有桑迪·牛顿（Thandie Newton）捧得最佳女配角奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10721996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10721996" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177180-450x676.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10721996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035177180-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10721996" class="wp-caption-text">桑迪·牛顿（Thandie Newton）凭借《西部世界》（Westworld）获得最佳女配角奖。(Frazer Harrison/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10722003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10722003" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035142382-450x676.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10722003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035142382-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10722003" class="wp-caption-text">比尔·哈德尔（Bill Hader）以《巴瑞》（Barry）获得喜剧类剧集最佳男主角奖。(Frazer Harrison/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>《鲁保罗变装皇后秀》（<span class="st"><em>RuPaul&#8217;s Drag Race</em>）</span>拿下该节目首个最佳竞技类真人秀奖，这也是艾美奖史上首次有同一届的“最佳竞技类真人秀”与“最佳真人秀主持人”由同一个节目获得。</p>
<p>第70届艾美奖颁奖礼，由《周六夜现场》（Saturday Night Live）的科林·乔斯特（Colin Jost）和迈克尔·彻（Michael Che）担任主持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10722015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10722015" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035132584-450x645.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10722015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1035132584-450x645.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="645" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10722015" class="wp-caption-text">第70届艾美奖颁奖典礼由科林·乔斯特（Colin Jost）和迈克尔·彻（Michael Che）双人主持。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
