<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>素甜点“紫薯蒙布朗”入口即化层次丰富</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615386 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月22日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E5%B8%83%E6%9C%97.md#1">蒙布朗</a>，又称法式<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a><ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>，是一种法式甜点，因样似欧洲覆雪的阿尔卑斯山山峰──白朗峰而得名，最初以甜栗子泥为主原料，佐生奶油制成，后来也发展出巧克力、香草等多种口味，层次丰富入口即化，令人回味无穷。</p>
<p>这道<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E5%B8%83%E6%9C%97.md#1">蒙布朗</a>料理不加蛋、奶，因此吃全素的朋友朋友也可以吃喔！口感滑顺的<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%96%AF.md#1">紫薯</a>泥和<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a>泥，是将蒸好的紫薯和栗子，压成泥后还要再过筛，完全去除纤维质，因此每一口都相当细致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/239ebea72eaebe72e9a8b87dd9f2f3ee.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10689236 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/239ebea72eaebe72e9a8b87dd9f2f3ee-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689236" class="wp-caption-text">素甜点“<ahref="https://github.com/hviotz3804/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%96%AF.md#1">紫薯</a>蒙布朗”入口即化层次丰富。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><strong>●塔皮</strong><br />
椰子油 57克<br />
砂糖 45克<br />
海盐 1克<br />
豆奶 20克<br />
低筋面粉 73克<br />
发粉 3克</p>
<p><strong>●内馅</strong><br />
栗子泥 214克<br />
栗子 50克<br />
植物性鲜奶油 107克</p>
<p><strong>●外层</strong><br />
紫薯 80克<br />
植物性鲜奶油 50克</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 先做塔皮，将椰子油软化加砂糖，加入豆奶、海盐拌匀，加入过筛粉类拌均匀，放入冷冻30分钟。<br />
2. 紫薯先蒸熟后，压成泥，再过筛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9eabfd34f6e21510fa73ace32684494f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10689234 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9eabfd34f6e21510fa73ace32684494f-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689234" class="wp-caption-text">紫薯先蒸熟后，压成泥，再过筛。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 栗子泥过筛，再把蒸熟的栗子切碎加入栗子泥内拌均匀。<br />
4. 打发植物性鲜奶油。分别加入栗子泥、紫薯泥拌匀，再放入挤花袋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/c4466ce2747fdbc62d823c60556a6c5a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10689233 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/c4466ce2747fdbc62d823c60556a6c5a-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689233" class="wp-caption-text">过筛栗子泥。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将塔皮从冷冻拿出来，分割成每个20g放入法式圈，接着放入烤箱，上火为摄氏200度、下火为摄氏160度，烤12分钟后，放凉。<br />
6. 将栗子馅挤在塔皮上面，再挤紫薯泥把栗子馅包起来即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9de07dcb3e6fdcfc332bbff8aa0c3a42.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10689231 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9de07dcb3e6fdcfc332bbff8aa0c3a42-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689231" class="wp-caption-text">将塔皮分割成每个20g放入法式圈，接着放入烤箱。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10689235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d955759ac59227cb89fa6c9922a6b3a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10689235 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0d955759ac59227cb89fa6c9922a6b3a-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689235" class="wp-caption-text">四周挤紫薯泥作装饰。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
