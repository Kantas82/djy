<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>钓鱼不成钩到一只白头鹰 人鹰奇缘小记</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYSaa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rlangk376/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rlangk376/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月05日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）<ahref="https://github.com/rlangk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%92%93.md#1">海钓</a>爱好者兰迪．萨德勒（Randy Sadler）与两个儿子在加拿大海湾垂钓，大鱼没上钩竟然钓来一只白头鹰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10693203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10693203" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/eagle.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10693203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/eagle.jpg" alt="" width="462" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10693203" class="wp-caption-text">“老爸，看啊！我们钓到什么啦？”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只鹰在懊恼中不失庄严。原本把鱼饵闪光片解开后就互道“莎哟娜啦．再见！”啦，却见它猛拍翅膀在水面上起起落落，毫无进展，看起来仿佛要洗澡或洗脚似的？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10693202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10693202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/dinner.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10693202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/dinner.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10693202" class="wp-caption-text">“真是的！我以为那闪亮亮的粉色东西是我的晚餐……”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>唉啊，原来大礼服吃了水，沉重到起空不了了！威武一世，此刻啥也施展不开……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10693204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10693204" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/soaked.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10693204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/soaked.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10693204" class="wp-caption-text">落汤“鹰”实在有够呛！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>悉心呵护 羽翼渐干</strong></span></h2>
<p>再把白头鹰请回船上来吧。希望鹰儿能再展“鹰”姿重点就在于——晾干。首先，用大毛巾盖住它的头，遮住它视线敏锐、远眺千里的大眼睛，让它稍安勿躁。两个爪子也得抓牢。接着，用大毛巾擦干羽毛……</p>
<p>鸟儿的天性就是要飞的。虽然眼睛蒙住了，脚也被拘着，但鹰的翅膀不停地拍啊翻啊，就是有一股动力要飞！要飞……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10693200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10693200" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/almost-there.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10693200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/almost-there.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10693200" class="wp-caption-text">“对不起，我知道这很闹心。再忍一下吧！”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>耐心与时间可以治愈一切，鹰的羽毛渐渐干了。还得喂一些流质补充体力，以免它脱水。终于，放飞的时刻到了，它仍然“披头盖脸”，但双脚不再紧握，自由地钩着树棍。</p>
<p>到了甲板上空旷的最佳位置，“掀起了你的盖头来”，白头鹰双脚往下一蹬，挥舞双翅腾空而去！它转了个弯，旋向船头前方的空间翱翔而去，缓缓消失在远方的天际之间……</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>缘分的相遇</strong></span></h2>
<p>这可是个令人心动不已的机遇，毕竟钓鹰奇遇不是每天都会上演的啊。生命与生命的互动多么奇妙！举手之劳，帮助另一个生命由困顿走向抒放，放生的人高兴，观赏的人也得到升华，仿佛也放飞了什么，特别开心！</p>
