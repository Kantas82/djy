<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台西老客运站活化 青银对话彩绘竹山</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6a94d5a2a85f88d75dddf320359ebf38-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>竹山的银发长辈们在台西客运站展示充满在地美好生活回忆的画作。（黄淑贞提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月04日讯】（大纪元记者林萌骞台湾南投报导）在停驶的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E8%A5%BF.md#1">台西</a>客运站，竹山<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E5%88%9B.md#1">青创</a>业者4日展开第二场活化市集活动，由年轻艺术工作者小孟带领，邀银发长辈将回忆中的小镇生活点滴化成炫丽色彩画作，向摆摊的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E5%86%9C.md#1">青农</a>和年轻一辈述说竹山的人文历史。客运站没有老去，它正一代代地承载着小镇的故事和青年梦想。</p>
<p>教育界退休的黄姓银发长辈表示，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E8%A5%BF.md#1">台西</a>客运站是代表温馨回忆的象征。小时候，外婆每年都会从隔壁乡镇的云林县林内乡坐公车来竹山，在台西客运站下车，为疼爱的外孙女送来亲手缝制的新衣服，温馨的画面一直停格在脑海中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689525" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/18/9/4/n10689523.md#1/%e5%9c%a8%e5%81%9c%e9%a7%9b%e7%9a%84%e5%8f%b0%e8%a5%bf%e5%ae%a2%e9%81%8b%e7%ab%99%ef%bc%8c%e9%8a%80%e9%ab%ae%e9%95%b7%e8%bc%a9%e5%80%91%e5%b0%87%e5%9b%9e%e6%86%b6%e4%b8%ad%e7%9a%84%e7%ab%b9%e5%b1%b1" rel="attachment wp-att-10689525"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d721d972d3b5b1018edfa87b63c40a67-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689525" class="wp-caption-text">在停驶的台西客运站，银发长辈们将回忆中的竹山小镇生活点滴化成炫丽色彩画作，竹山<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E5%86%9C.md#1">青农</a>则在旁边摆摊。（黄淑贞提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家乡彩绘活动现场，几位竹山最具代表性的在地小农，包括郁菁优质农特产行及金圆有机农场等，为参与的民众展示竹山最出名的各品种地瓜，以及用有机农法栽培的手工茶，还有海盐、红茶、中药卤包制作的茶叶蛋，和健康美味的红茶冻等天然安心食材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689527" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/18/9/4/n10689523.md#1/%e6%95%99%e8%82%b2%e7%95%8c%e9%80%80%e4%bc%91%e7%9a%84%e9%8a%80%e9%ab%ae%e9%95%b7%e8%bc%a9%ef%bc%8c%e5%88%86%e4%ba%ab%e5%85%85%e6%bb%bf%e5%b0%8f%e6%99%82%e5%80%99%e6%ba%ab%e9%a6%a8%e5%9b%9e%e6%86%b6" rel="attachment wp-att-10689527"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a262683f0d2e72c048f94d6033b19abe-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689527" class="wp-caption-text">教育界退休的银发长辈，分享充满小时候温馨回忆的台西客运公车和外婆送来的新衣服。（黄淑贞提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青农和和消费者面对面，传达他们对农业职人的坚持，也经由这些在地小农的辛勤工作，时时刻刻为大家在食材上做良心的把关。透过五感，青农深入分享地瓜品种的知识，及包罗万象的料理秘方；而关于台湾茶的繁衍发扬，更有着说不完的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689528" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/18/9/4/n10689523.md#1/%e9%8a%80%e9%ab%ae%e9%95%b7%e8%bc%a9%e5%88%86%e4%ba%ab%e5%b0%8f%e6%99%82%e5%80%99%e7%9a%84%e7%be%8e%e5%a5%bd%e5%9b%9e%e6%86%b6%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10689528"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/57dc805090e090b58befe06018295877-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689528" class="wp-caption-text">银发长辈分享小时候的美好回忆。（黄淑贞提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小镇的美好未来，不只年轻人在关心，其实许许多多的银发长者也默默地在守护着。这次的小镇生活市集，从关心在地长者做起，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E5%88%9B.md#1">青创</a>业者运用所长，带领大家看见在地生活的美好样貌，更让老客运站，在充满青银跨世代的欢笑交流中，燃起代代不熄的生命力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10689529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10689529" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/18/9/4/n10689523.md#1/%e7%ab%b9%e5%b1%b1%e5%9c%a8%e5%9c%b0%e9%95%b7%e8%bc%a9%e5%88%86%e4%ba%ab%e5%b0%8f%e6%99%82%e5%80%99%e7%9a%84%e7%be%8e%e5%a5%bd%e5%9b%9e%e6%86%b6%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10689529"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10689529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/185c97a8fc79b3f06608dad7d4db7e74-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10689529" class="wp-caption-text">竹山在地长辈分享小时候的美好回忆。（黄淑贞提供）</figcaption></figure>
