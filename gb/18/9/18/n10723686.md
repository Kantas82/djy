<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“黑寡妇”出席艾美奖 甜蜜挽男友走红毯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809181019391487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在第70届艾美奖颁奖典礼活动上，史嘉蕾?乔韩森（左）挽着男友走红毯。 ( Matt Winkelmeyer/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月18日讯】（大纪元记者黄钟台北报导）第70届艾美奖颁奖典礼于台湾时间18日登场，由《<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%9C%AB%E5%A4%9C%E7%8E%B0%E5%9C%BA.md#1">周末夜现场</a>》主播<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%91%E6%9E%97%E2%80%A7%E7%BA%A6%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9.md#1">科林?约斯特</a>和黑人男星<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%A6%E5%8F%AF%E2%80%A7%E9%BD%90.md#1">麦可?齐</a>合作担纲主持人。“<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%AF%A1%E5%A6%87.md#1">黑寡妇</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E5%98%89%E8%95%BE%E2%80%A7%E4%B9%94%E9%9F%A9%E6%A3%AE.md#1">史嘉蕾?乔韩森</a>（ Scarlett Johansson）特别向《复仇者联盟4》剧组告假，出席颁奖盛会力挺男友科林约斯，她穿着一袭白色高衩礼服，两人甜蜜相视而笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723966" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809181019281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10723966" title="775090872DC00406_70th_Emmy_" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1809181019281487.jpg" alt="775090872DC00406_70th_Emmy_" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723966" class="wp-caption-text">在第70届艾美奖颁奖典礼活动上，<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E5%98%89%E8%95%BE%E2%80%A7%E4%B9%94%E9%9F%A9%E6%A3%AE.md#1">史嘉蕾?乔韩森</a>（左）挽着男友?约斯特走红毯。 ( Matt Winkelmeyer/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
