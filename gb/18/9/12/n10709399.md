<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>复育野生动物 倒闭动物园成美洲豹诞生地</h2>
<a href="https://d377tzsbzf1wvt.cloudfront.net/J2wMcfyhw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ukkmvh324/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月28日讯】有一家人把这座濒临倒闭的<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E5%9B%AD.md#1">动物园</a>买下来，重新整顿；现在动物园恢复生机，也即将迎接新生命──一只小<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E8%B1%B9.md#1">美洲豹</a>宝宝。来看看<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E5%9B%AD.md#1">动物园</a>为了协助美洲豹成为新手爸妈，背后做了哪些努力吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/91f4a83bd5515d741407268827c32ec2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745886 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/91f4a83bd5515d741407268827c32ec2-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745886" class="wp-caption-text">这里是英国大人小孩都喜欢的<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2%E8%B1%B9.md#1">美洲豹</a>园区。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/538af98583ce39f073b196cfc95e6e2c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745887 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/538af98583ce39f073b196cfc95e6e2c-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745887" class="wp-caption-text">曾经有一段几乎要倒闭的落魄过往。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>天堂<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>园（Paradise Wildlife Park）曾经是英国最落魄的动物园，幸好一位对动物有爱心的男子及他的家人挽救了这一切。</p>
<p>桑普森（Peter Sampson）和家人出资买下动物园后，以此作为<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E8%82%B2.md#1">复育</a><ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>的基地，他们与许多育种计划合作，为了后代而保护野生动物的数量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ad9ed9046c7648a0b919c99c8022f1eb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745888 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ad9ed9046c7648a0b919c99c8022f1eb-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745888" class="wp-caption-text">幸好桑普森和家人出资买下动物园。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/8b55871e637a9cc1ca9ed9f0c03b4e50.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745889 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/8b55871e637a9cc1ca9ed9f0c03b4e50-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745889" class="wp-caption-text">希望能多多<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E8%82%B2.md#1">复育</a>野生动物。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美洲豹没有明显的繁殖季节，但是观察美洲豹的行为可以发现，园中的美洲豹正处于动情周期，饲育员希望协助两只美洲豹顺利交配以产下美洲豹宝宝。肉食动物负责人琼斯（Ian Jones）表示，母豹Kedera时常仰躺在地上撒娇翻滚，代表它还处于动情周期。</p>
<p>猫科动物在这段期间，每隔15～20分钟会交配一次，大约维持一周。依照目前的观察，公豹Kumal和它的伴侣Kedera进展得很顺利。琼斯说，这两只美洲豹分别从23个月和20个月大时就开始一起饲养，希望以后能成为一对美洲豹父母，“它们一起度过了18个月，有很长时间培养感情。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3b284db4028926ce2ec7c1ea5227c1c7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745884 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3b284db4028926ce2ec7c1ea5227c1c7-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745884" class="wp-caption-text">这两只美洲豹从小一起长大。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片（Video credit: Barcroft TV）?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-348cda85-34b2-4a67-4912-94f4957ae914" data-id="player-348cda85-34b2-4a67-4912-94f4957ae914"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/348cda85-34b2-4a67-4912-94f4957ae914?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F12%2Fn10709399.md#1"></a></p>
<p>不过，这对“青梅竹马”在成熟后的父母之路并没有那么顺利，它们也曾经历好几个月的努力未果。琼斯说，猫在动情周期通常不会外出觅食，可是美洲豹比较食物导向，它们还是会被食物吸引；他希望这次的繁殖期间，Kumal和Kedera会有好消息。</p>
<p>翌年，琼斯的许愿成真，也可能是时候到了，这对年轻的美洲豹爸妈有了第一个宝宝──小美洲豹Keira，一只雌性幼崽。可爱的美洲豹宝宝就像<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB.md#1">小猫</a>一样，琼斯把它抱在手里，但这并不是为了好玩或娱乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab8a6bbc4f9fa30c363d2a734c38c958.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745890 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab8a6bbc4f9fa30c363d2a734c38c958-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745890" class="wp-caption-text">期待成真！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10745891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/fa1f3b3bbdb3276affe2dae7f0b20938.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745891 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/fa1f3b3bbdb3276affe2dae7f0b20938-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745891" class="wp-caption-text">小美洲豹很健康。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>琼斯解释，Keira约12～14周大时需要接种疫苗，我们希望它习惯接触人类，等到要打针的时候，压力才不会太大。现在每隔2～3天，琼斯和其他饲育员都会固定进来看看Keira，把它抱起来，让它习惯跟人类的互动，但每次的时间不会太长，只有几分钟而已。</p>
<p>小美洲豹的诞生，是天堂野生动物园对物种保育的重要一步。琼斯表示，美洲豹就和大多数野生的大型猫科动物一样，数量持续下降，因此在园区复育这些生命真的很重要。我们还在考虑是否让它们回归野外，至少现在先保留它们的基因构成，能让成功概率大幅提升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10745894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10745894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ef5205181536b51f3c363c740f11ba28.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10745894 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ef5205181536b51f3c363c740f11ba28-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10745894" class="wp-caption-text">然而全世界的美洲豹数量仍持续下降。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
