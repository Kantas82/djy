<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>您去过这样的海边吗？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月18日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E6%B8%85%E5%8D%97%E9%81%93.md#1">忠清南道</a>海岸给人的第一印象是小渔村和附近的大大小小的岛屿。踏上海滩，因为坡度和缓，非常适合在沙滩上散步。旁边一阵波涛卷上来又冲回去。退潮时，海滩上到处都是螃蟹、牡蛎、贝类等。从那美丽的景象抬头望去，蓝蓝的天空和红霞交汇在一起，夜空温馨而美丽。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BF%A0%E6%B8%85%E5%8D%97%E9%81%93.md#1">忠清南道</a>因为民心厚道且有这种舒适的自然环境，很多生活忙碌的人们为了疗愈身心而经常去忠南度假。</p>
<p>忠清南道有不少具有这种特征的的沿海城市，从北到南有唐津、泰安、保宁、舒川等。</p>
<p>首先是唐津。唐津的独木村是唯一一个可以在所有地方看到日出、日落、月出的旅游景点。光着脚沿着海岸线散步的时候，抬起头来遥望天空，壮观的景象就在眼前展开。挂在露积峰的落日景象怎能不快快拍下来呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723949" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/aa7c002bcb386a9f6112b1cd04408caf-600x380.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/aa7c002bcb386a9f6112b1cd04408caf-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723949" class="wp-caption-text">唐津</figcaption></figure>
<p>由兰草、芝草、荷花和海棠花点缀的兰芝岛和海水浴场、马戏馆、游乐场、海洋公园、体育公园、露营公园等鳞次栉比的插桥湖是唐津的另一个景象。唐津市目前在建设长期滞留型住宿园区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3cf5c6a19b55babceef855fb31212a58-600x471.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3cf5c6a19b55babceef855fb31212a58-600x471.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723950" class="wp-caption-text">兰芝岛海水浴场</figcaption></figure>
<p>唐津下面是泰安。</p>
<p>泰安有<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>唯一的海岸国立公园。因为三面环海，有28个海水浴场、114个岛、42个港口和浦口散布着，在世界上最美丽的千里浦树木园内还可以欣赏到晚霞、大海、花木的盛宴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5ab644d0d63fd438d27bdd505a44509c-600x356.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5ab644d0d63fd438d27bdd505a44509c-600x356.png" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723952" class="wp-caption-text">泰安</figcaption></figure>
<p>沿着海滨路有沙滩体验场、捕鱼体验场、盐田体验场等，如果时间能赶得好，还可以同时享受到“王虾庆典”、“国际沙雕展”、“郁金香庆典”、“光之盛典”、“荷花庆典”等。蟹酱、葫芦章鱼汤、烤王虾、石斑鱼酱汤、花蟹汤、烤海鳗等都是特产。</p>
<p>泰安下面的保宁是代表性的年轻旅游景点。每年举行的保宁泥浆节上，外国年轻人远多于当地人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f54eb41b43316074b37ad9ac5d327802-600x381.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f54eb41b43316074b37ad9ac5d327802-600x381.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723954" class="wp-caption-text">大川海水浴场</figcaption></figure>
<p>大川海水浴场提供高空滑索（ZIPTREK）和空中自行车等刺激的运动项目。吊在滑索上飞过大海的高空滑索和沿着海岸线在海面上掠过的“空中自行车”将给人们留下难忘的回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723955" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0936240b80b5474a80e16c066bd53794-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0936240b80b5474a80e16c066bd53794-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723955" class="wp-caption-text">烤蛤蜊</figcaption></figure>
<p>保宁最好的美食是烤蛤蜊。因为提供无限续吃，可以尽情享受又筋道又柔软的烤蛤蜊的美味。</p>
<p>此外还有武昌浦的神奇海路、圣住山自然休养林、保宁湖、乌栖山、外因列岛、乌川港、竹岛、忠清水营城、煤炭博物馆、开花艺术公园等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10723956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10723956" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/dd7b05d26bf60074bca58e473409f235-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-10723956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/dd7b05d26bf60074bca58e473409f235-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10723956" class="wp-caption-text">梭子蟹（花蟹）和钱鱼</figcaption></figure>
<p>保宁市的南边舒川郡。如果说保宁是“动态观光”，那么舒川可以说是“缓慢治愈”和“学习自然”。漫步在旅游胜地，从山茶树林和蛎鹬开始，可以看到世界5大气候带的生态系统、5000种海洋资源、神秘的新城里芦苇地、候鸟群舞、海洋松树林等。每当这时，人们就会更加关注大自然。这里的美食，梭子蟹（花蟹）和钱鱼当属最佳名品。</p>
