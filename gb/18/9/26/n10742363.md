<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>太会演了！大白鲨浅水区翻滚挣扎 别出手援助</h2>
<a href="https://dnuodzs8nln8v.cloudfront.net/F8zbmJSGv"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wtnrng3147/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月14日讯】浅海区出现不寻常的动静，有人在墨西哥<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E5%8A%A0%E5%88%A9%E7%A6%8F%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">下加利福尼亚</a>（Baja California）远海海域发现一只大白鲨！而且它周围的海水颜色呈现较深的色泽，难道这只<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%A8%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲨鱼</a><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%97%E4%BC%A4.md#1">受伤</a>了吗？虽然是海中的上层掠食者，接近它会有危险，人们到底应不应该出手援助呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742436" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/24ce335fd484916ea35e35000ab8a0f7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742436 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/24ce335fd484916ea35e35000ab8a0f7-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742436" class="wp-caption-text">浅海区域出现不寻常的风波。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/cf013f13adf19da1b337d0095aa03553.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742437 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/cf013f13adf19da1b337d0095aa03553-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742437" class="wp-caption-text">原来是一只大白鲨。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只大白鲨看起来有点奇怪，它好像在<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A3%E6%89%8E.md#1">挣扎</a>，而且水面的颜色变深了，让人不禁怀疑是否它身受重伤，正在流血。不过，摄影的人表示，当时的区域距离基地台非常遥远，没有讯号，无法打电话求助，加上一行人并非专业，也不敢贸然行动，最后只是将影片记录下来。</p>
<p>摄影人强调，他们在与大白鲨有一段距离的地方拍摄，全员完全没有以任何方式接触、骚扰、移动或限制的方式对待大白鲨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/76e0b079ac6f18416824603183b59ee7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742438 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/76e0b079ac6f18416824603183b59ee7-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742438" class="wp-caption-text">它看起来很痛苦的样子，水面的颜色也越来越深。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742439" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/56a90697850a8d93c1ca88e3e995f065.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742439 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/56a90697850a8d93c1ca88e3e995f065-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742439" class="wp-caption-text">但其实，<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%A8%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲨鱼</a>并没有<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%97%E4%BC%A4.md#1">受伤</a>，这是它的“欺敌”招数！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>仔细观察影片可以发现，这只大白鲨其实正在“演戏”，它完全掌握自己在海里的状况，包括所在位置和水深，大白鲨也从来没有静止超过3分钟。</p>
<p>此外，深色的海水并不是大白鲨的血，而是大白鲨尾巴搅动海床泥沙所致。最终大白鲨安然离开。</p>
<p>这支影片后来被白鲨研究人员回顾分析，他们得出结论：大白鲨“潜伏”在浅水区，其实正在寻找<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%F0%AB%9A%89%E9%B1%BC.md#1">??鱼</a>作为食物来源。</p>
<p>研究人员表示，这只看似无助的鲨鱼，真实身份根本是狩猎场主人。这也意味着，任何试图“帮助”这只庞然大物非常危险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7c299e881c85d277a523ba93e171d60d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742440 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7c299e881c85d277a523ba93e171d60d-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742440" class="wp-caption-text">拨弄泥沙让水面的颜色变深，看似<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%A3%E6%89%8E.md#1">挣扎</a>，其实完全掌握情况。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742441" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b5f3f8b1e1e779eb5433eb6d03a1211d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742441 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b5f3f8b1e1e779eb5433eb6d03a1211d-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742441" class="wp-caption-text">真是闻所未闻呀！如果真的碰到掠食者“遇难”还是要小心啊！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，摄影者后来也在网路发文表示，当天他们一行3人后来都因为<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%F0%AB%9A%89%E9%B1%BC.md#1">??鱼</a>而受伤；经过数周的抗生素和药物治疗，他们都已康复。</p>
<p>其实??鱼一般很温驯，不会主动攻击人类，在某些海域甚至有固定的观光喂食行程，??鱼乐于享用大餐，也很愿意与人类亲近。或许可以推断，大白鲨的捕食动作已惊动到??鱼，因而展现出防御性攻击。</p>
