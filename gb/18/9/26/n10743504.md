	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10440919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2-450x51.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="51" /></a></p>
<p>老妇人家中藏着秘密，她不让任何人进门。她离群索居，完全与世隔绝。她不信任任何人，从不让人进入她的屋内。</p>
<p>露西是同楼层的邻居，她知道老人有精神问题，而且没有家人，老妇人深居简出让她担忧，她努力得到老人的信任。</p>
<p>有一天，老人打开房门，露西趁机进了她的屋内……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10750374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10750374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/83a946431.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10750374 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/83a946431-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10750374" class="wp-caption-text">老人打开房门，露西趁机进了她的屋内……（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“哦，不！”她发现了可怕的事实。“她让我进门时，我看到的景象我永远也不会忘。”“太恐怖了，没人应该过这样的日子！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10750390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10750390" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/83a946431-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10750390 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/83a946431-1-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10750390" class="wp-caption-text">她先帮老妇人洗个澡，这是13年来她第一次洗澡。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>露西决定要做些什么，她先帮老妇人洗个澡，这是13年来她第一次洗澡，然后让女儿露比陪着老人，她开始帮老人打扫，花了60小时，把家里都清空。</p>
<p>之她罗列出老人需要的东西，在脸书上分享了出去，号召大家捐出家具、衣服和食物。</p>
<p>为了保护老人的隐私，她称呼76岁的老妇人为“我的女士（My Lady）”，帖子很快被疯传……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10744936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10744936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/50a279d1d9b23cb49518152939fb802b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10744936 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/50a279d1d9b23cb49518152939fb802b-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10744936" class="wp-caption-text">她罗列出老人需要的东西，在脸书上分享了出去，她称呼76岁的老妇人为“我的女士”，帖子很快被疯传&#8230;&#8230;示意图。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
