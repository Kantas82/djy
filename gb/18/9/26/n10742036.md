<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>洋葱片！收到孙女送的毯子 奶奶打开秒泪崩</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月28日讯】老<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E5%A5%B6.md#1">奶奶</a>最近被检查出罹患了肺癌第四期，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E5%A5%B3.md#1">孙女</a>希望给予<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E5%A5%B6.md#1">奶奶</a>大力支持，她悄悄把爷爷常穿的墨绿色上衣缝在<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%AF%E5%AD%90.md#1">毯子</a>上，包成礼物送给奶奶。</p>
<p>当奶奶拆开礼物，看到这一幕又惊又喜，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E5%A5%B3.md#1">孙女</a>说：“奶奶带<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%AF%E5%AD%90.md#1">毯子</a>去医院吧！就像（天上的）爷爷抱着你一样！”感人的画面让大家都哭了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/56ba20783c35d7dbc9ca82e559cfe540.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742143 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/56ba20783c35d7dbc9ca82e559cfe540-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742143" class="wp-caption-text">准备要去医院就诊的奶奶，收到孙女送她的惊喜礼物，是一条美丽的毯子。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ad307ef761d415e0e0122a8c3ae98185.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742145 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ad307ef761d415e0e0122a8c3ae98185-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742145" class="wp-caption-text">不过真正的“惊喜”还在后头，孙女请奶奶摊开毯子看看里面……（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2017年圣诞节前两周，这位奶奶不幸被检测出罹患肺癌，而且已经到了第四期，她准备要开始接受化疗。</p>
<p>奶奶的孙女很孝顺，希望给奶奶更多支持鼓励，她向奶奶要了一件爷爷过去常穿的上衣，打算准备一件惊喜的礼物。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片（Video credit: Newsflare）?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-4b5fe856-1de8-4478-5be0-dc26abe36aa1" data-id="player-4b5fe856-1de8-4478-5be0-dc26abe36aa1"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/4b5fe856-1de8-4478-5be0-dc26abe36aa1?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F26%2Fn10742036.md#1"></a></p>
<p>当全家人在客厅聊天时，孙女把一床藕紫色的毯子送给奶奶，请奶奶“拆开”。奶奶看到礼物感到很开心，但她不知道为什么要把这张毯子铺开，毯子不就是毯子吗？</p>
<p>当奶奶缓缓张开，发现里面似乎还有一件别的织物，毯子中央映入眼帘的那件熟悉上衣，正是自己丈夫生前常穿的墨绿色长衫！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab7a41bcfdba7ba01a0f476e8645ce52.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742146 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ab7a41bcfdba7ba01a0f476e8645ce52-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742146" class="wp-caption-text">之前孙女向奶奶要了一件爷爷常穿的上衣，把它缝在毯子的内里。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7eb73d998527f4b6fd50c49470756000.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742147 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7eb73d998527f4b6fd50c49470756000-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742147" class="wp-caption-text">爷爷4年前过世了，如今奶奶要住院，孙女想，如果这么做，仿佛爷爷抱着奶奶。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“原来是为了这个！”奶奶惊呼，前几天孙女特地找奶奶拿走一件爷爷的衣服，神秘兮兮地不告诉她原因，今天答案终于揭晓。</p>
<p>奶奶感动地看着这张有爷爷上衣“特殊镶嵌”的毯子。孙女说：“奶奶可以带着这条毯子去医院，就像爷爷在旁边陪伴你、搂着你一样！”</p>
<p>听到孙女的话，奶奶忍不住掩面大哭！奶奶很想念爷爷，他们很相爱，结褵56年，在那个民风淳朴的年代，爷爷在与奶奶第二次约会时就向她求婚，他们相知相惜，有了孩子，也有了孙子，这段幸福的时光维持超过半个世纪。然而4年前爷爷过世了……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10742148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/da818fd3a17f1ad3d5c2df00d864acad.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742148 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/da818fd3a17f1ad3d5c2df00d864acad-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742148" class="wp-caption-text">奶奶忍不住落下眼泪。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10742152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10742152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4b60ffde898e1e1a9b9141811a709a8a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10742152 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4b60ffde898e1e1a9b9141811a709a8a-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10742152" class="wp-caption-text">这真是太感人了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罹患<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%8C%E7%97%87.md#1">癌症</a>固然是很大的不幸，但是对奶奶来说，没有爷爷在身旁的支持与陪伴是更大的打击。孙女说，当得知奶奶的病况，她就很希望找到某种方法，让奶奶感受到爷爷的陪伴。“当我起心动念，突然就有灵感了，就是那条毯子！”</p>
<p>孙女说，“一开始奶奶还反应不过来，可是当奶奶知道（盖着毯子就像爷爷在抱她）背后的含义时，她马上大哭，这一幕让我们也都很感动。”家人们也都被温馨的气氛影响，湿了眼眶，仿佛爷爷又回到了大家的身边，孙女赞叹，“这真是神奇的一刻！”</p>
