<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“就是要剪草” 你见过这样修草坪的吗？</h2>
<a href="https://d3siwajeg0khti.cloudfront.net/R8tYvsQAl"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yzovrq360/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/yzovrq360/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月10日讯】虽然有些人视自家门前的<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E5%9D%AA.md#1">草坪</a>为负担，总是犯愁为草坪<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E8%8D%89.md#1">剪草</a>，但也有人把剪草看成一种运动，当成一个乐趣，甚至有时，如果不剪草，心里就长草。<br />
<strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong><br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f96228ef-d825-43f4-7631-7a3ce53d8c6b" data-id="player-f96228ef-d825-43f4-7631-7a3ce53d8c6b"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f96228ef-d825-43f4-7631-7a3ce53d8c6b?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F8%2Fn10700281.md#1"></a></p>
<p>美国宾州纽卡斯尔（New Castle）的一位老兄，实在受不了漫长的灰濛濛的冬天，可是草长莺飞的二月天却迟迟不来，于是，一场雪后，他向漫长的、迟迟赖着不肯走的冬天委婉地下了驱逐令。</p>
<p>这位老兄拿出搁置了一个冬天的<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E8%8D%89.md#1">剪草</a>机，来到自家门前那块<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E5%9D%AA.md#1">草坪</a>上。只见他身着红色短袖T恤，下身穿黑色短裤，头戴鸭舌帽，脚上也毫不含糊，踩着一双露脚跟的凉鞋，虽然满天乌云，却没忘了在鼻子上架副墨镜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10700299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10700299" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/c-600x356.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10700299 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/c-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10700299" class="wp-caption-text">他推着剪草机，在草坪上来来回回，认认真真地“剪雪”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“突突突”，他推着剪草机，在草坪上来来回回，认认真真地“剪雪”。看起来他对自己的工作很满意，得意地为自己竖起了大拇指。</p>
<p>虽然草坪被雪覆盖，根本无草可剪，但是没关系，“我的草坪我做主。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10700302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10700302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b-3-600x363.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10700302 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b-3-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10700302" class="wp-caption-text">这位宾州匹兹堡的老兄，推着四处冒烟的剪草机，淡定地剪着草。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宾州匹兹堡还有一位老兄，他也跟自家的草坪较上了劲。虽然剪草机已经四处冒烟，可他却不肯停下来检查一下发动机，仍然淡定地推着它继续剪草，愣是把剪草机变成了烟雾发射器。<br />
