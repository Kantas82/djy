<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Cupertino、Saratoga上市房</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月22日讯】（大纪元记者李欧整理报导）Cupertino、Saratoga一直都是华人所爱，优良学区、生活便利造就这里的高房价。Cupertino生活相对方便，购物快速，有众多华人商店，高科技聚集，近年独立房减少，建商推出众多联排屋和康斗；Saratoga偏向豪华地产，虽较为郊区，却是很多富豪的偏爱，一直都是全美收入前十名的城市。来瞧瞧本周有哪些上市房。</p>
<p>一、21761 Olive Avenue, Cupertino, CA 95014<br />
<figure id="attachment_10734003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_264188053-0.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_264188053-0-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734003" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734003" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷地产</a>，此房屋外观、庭院做过翻修。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10734004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_264188053-8.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_264188053-8-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734004" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734004" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷地产</a>，此房餐厅与客厅相连。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
欢迎来到华丽订制的Monta Vista住宅，靠近屡获殊荣的Cupertino学校：Lincoln Elementary、Kennedy Middle和Monta Vista High（买家自行验证）。这里交通便利，接近Stevens Creek大道、85公路，附近商家众多。<br />
室内客厅与餐厅相连，入内是厨房和起居室，要留意厨房炉火设置在中岛，但没有抽油烟机，买家可视需求升级家电。每个卧室都有宽敞的壁橱空间，一楼卧室可当客房或办公室。大型主卧室设有步入式衣柜、双水槽、天窗。<figure id="attachment_10734006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.42.40.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.42.40-600x349.png" alt="" width="600" b="349" class="size-large wp-image-10734006" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734006" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷地产：21761 Olive Avenue, Cupertino, CA 95014。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>二、10161 Lebanon Drive, Cupertino, CA 95014<br />
<figure id="attachment_10734007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_256662043-2.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_256662043-2-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734007" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734007" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷地产，2018年新房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10734008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_256662043-4.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_256662043-4-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734008" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734008" class="wp-caption-text">优雅的曲线阶梯和时尚木地板。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
2018年全新订制——托斯卡纳风格住宅，提供主屋5间卧室、4间半浴室、书房、媒体室，附带2车位车库，和1个含厨房的姻亲套房。屋内设计经过精心制作，全屋时尚灰色木地板，厨房宽敞配有高级不銹钢家电。<br />
有最受欢迎的Cupertino学校：Monta Vista High、Kennedy Middle和Stevens Creek Elementary（买家自行验证）。房子位于库市西边一个安静社区，但离280高速路和Stevens Creek大道不远。<figure id="attachment_10734011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.42.54.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.42.54-600x364.png" alt="" width="600" b="364" class="size-large wp-image-10734011" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734011" class="wp-caption-text">硅谷地产：10161 Lebanon Drive, Cupertino, CA 95014。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>三、18000 Rodeo Creek Hollow, Saratoga, CA 95070<br />
<figure id="attachment_10734014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_264690043-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_264690043-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734014" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734014" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">南湾地产</a>，地中海风格的优秀房产。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10734015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_264690043-7.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_264690043-7-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734015" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734015" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">南湾地产</a>，高级核桃木色订制厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
Saratoga一向以好学区、大面积房产著称，当然这里也不例外，优良学区：Saratoga High、Redwood Middle、Argonaut Elementary（买家自行验证）。房产有五千多平方英尺，户外还有一万多呎的空间。<br />
此处生活优异，后院犹如秘密花园，但位在Saratoga Sunnyvale道路旁，步行距离就有商店区、酒庄。室内低调奢华，厨师级厨房、古典壁炉、酒窖等，皆能显示出买家的卓越品味。<figure id="attachment_10734017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.43.05.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.43.05-600x395.png" alt="" width="600" b="395" class="size-large wp-image-10734017" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734017" class="wp-caption-text">南湾地产：18000 Rodeo Creek Hollow, Saratoga, CA 95070。</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>四、14911 Farwell Avenue, Saratoga, CA 95070<br />
<figure id="attachment_10734020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_243547813-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_243547813-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734020" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734020" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，主屋和3车位车库。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10734021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_243547813-37.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_243547813-37-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10734021" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734021" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>，后院有坡地、草地和独立小屋。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
自今年7/24上市以来，8月降价6.8%，9月又降9.3%，整整降了91万美元！这里2014年卖出187.88万，2017年重建，配备有Nest、Ring、Gates等智能家居，和带wifi摄像头的防盗系统。同样拥有优秀学区（买家自行验证）。<br />
房产主楼有4间卧室和高级厨房、户外甲板赏景区；另有一配有卫浴、餐厨的独立小房产位在后院；后院草坪大到可以踢球；停车更有两个独立车库，加上户外空间，能容纳众多车辆。交通依靠Los Gatos-Saratoga道路联外。<figure id="attachment_10734022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10734022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.43.18.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1.43.18-600x357.png" alt="" width="600" b="357" class="size-large wp-image-10734022" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10734022" class="wp-caption-text">湾区赏房：14911 Farwell Avenue, Saratoga, CA 95070。</figcaption></figure></p>
