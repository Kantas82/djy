<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马英九桃园扫街 对陈学圣寄予厚望</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/bd401a96d55141d0f18bd9db3548a232-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>前总统马英九（中）高喊“包中”。（陈学圣服务团队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月02日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）前总统<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E8%8B%B1%E4%B9%9D.md#1">马英九</a>2日陪同国民党市长候选人<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>，第三度造访<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市八德区大湳市场<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%AB%E8%A1%97.md#1">扫街</a>拜票，受到乡亲的热情欢呼，马英九表示，民进党执政下的台湾，民众怨声载道，我们国民党不能不反应出这种现况。学圣在这段期间声势也在上涨，我们（国民党）对他<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>）寄予厚望。”，逛街民众各个兴奋不已，拿出手机要求与前总统马英九及市长候选人陈学圣合影留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10684501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10684501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/8bf1bf25676cb3c5386c1aea5436a6e8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10684501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/8bf1bf25676cb3c5386c1aea5436a6e8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10684501" class="wp-caption-text">前总统<ahref="https://github.com/pubjsx3410/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E8%8B%B1%E4%B9%9D.md#1">马英九</a>超人气，等待拍照握手的民众绵延1000公尺以上。（陈学圣服务团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前总统马英九的超人气，等待拍照握手的民众绵延1000公尺以上，中华民国妇女联合会八德区支会会长黄淑惠与会员们致赠凤梨，预祝陈学圣支持度越来越旺，八德区公有市场自治会冯宝惠及会员们也送上肉粽、白萝卜及蒜，预祝陈学圣高票当选。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10684504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10684504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1c608305b74f2b25628ed8a3b5fa22a7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10684504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1c608305b74f2b25628ed8a3b5fa22a7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10684504" class="wp-caption-text">前总统马英九超人气，受到夹道欢迎。（陈学圣服务团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>马英九说，民进党执政下的台湾，民众怨声载道，我们国民党不能不反应出这种现况，陈学圣在这段期间声势也在上涨，我们对他寄予厚望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10684503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10684503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5a46714050f82af93568835d680ecfab.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10684503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5a46714050f82af93568835d680ecfab-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10684503" class="wp-caption-text">前总统马英九（右2）、陈学圣（左2）等品尝美食。（陈学圣服务团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马英九下午前往平镇区建安宫参拜开漳圣王祈福、出席桃园市平镇区女青年会专题演讲及前往平镇区市议员候选人陈万得的竞选总部替候选人站台。◇</p>
