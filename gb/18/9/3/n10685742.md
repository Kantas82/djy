<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>你从没吃过的火锅！创意“哈密瓜芒果火锅”</h2>
<a href="https://dtfavhq5d1ym2.cloudfront.net/R5tAY5ayq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/eizauy3553/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/eizauy3553/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10615386 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月11日讯】喜欢有天然甜味汤底的朋友们有福啰！你能想像用<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%92%E6%9E%9C.md#1">芒果</a>跟<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E5%AF%86%E7%93%9C.md#1">哈密瓜</a>做成的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">火锅</a>吗？教你用这两种香甜的水果，做成一道丰盛的火锅料理！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10685765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10685765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b890cee4f07102eeb744d78088c37579.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685765 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/b890cee4f07102eeb744d78088c37579-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10685765" class="wp-caption-text">你从没吃过的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%94%85.md#1">火锅</a>！创意“<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E5%AF%86%E7%93%9C.md#1">哈密瓜</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%92%E6%9E%9C.md#1">芒果</a>火锅”。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈密瓜的皮可拿来熬高汤，前提是它的皮一定要洗干净，并连籽一同熬煮当作汤底，就可以不用放味素了。</p>
<p>煮汤并非将所有材料都放到锅里就完成，秘诀是事先将食材的香味提出来，再以不一样的顺序加入汤锅里。</p>
<p>哈密瓜跟芒果可以先炒过，将果香带出来，芒果最好是选择青芒果，青芒果的味道比较酸，能够把食材的鲜甜味拉出来。</p>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p><strong>●食材：</strong><br />
鲜鱼<br />
芒果（也可加哈密瓜）<br />
姜片<br />
葱段（或葱花）<br />
菇类<br />
中华豆腐块<br />
鱼板<br />
包心白菜（或高丽菜）<br />
红萝卜片（或块状）<br />
绿色蔬菜</p>
<p><strong>●调味料：</strong><br />
沙拉油<br />
盐<br />
柴鱼精<br />
米酒</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 将鱼骨、哈密瓜皮拿来熬高汤<br />
2. 热锅冷油，将鲜鱼两面煎至表面金黄，锁住甜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10685763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10685763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a99036a058e27602f1a17ab61c40b521.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685763 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a99036a058e27602f1a17ab61c40b521-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10685763" class="wp-caption-text">热锅冷油，将鲜鱼两面煎至表面金黄，锁住甜味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 锅里下姜片、包心白菜、红萝卜、哈密瓜、菇类一同拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10685764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10685764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/412dce99422affa4189ae3d9a19487a0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685764 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/412dce99422affa4189ae3d9a19487a0-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10685764" class="wp-caption-text">将鲜香菇与葱段下锅干煸至微焦。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10685761" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10685761" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0bb617695a9f1ec3d190eef579b8e0ac.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685761 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/0bb617695a9f1ec3d190eef579b8e0ac-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10685761" class="wp-caption-text">锅里下姜片、包心白菜、红萝卜、哈密瓜、菇类一同拌炒。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 稍微拌炒后，再加入高汤炖煮，之后再加入芒果一起煮。<br />
5. 最后下调味料，加入绿色蔬菜、豆腐块、鱼板、葱段或葱花即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10685762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10685762" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d7a76b7a7e415eee68044418e948988f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685762 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d7a76b7a7e415eee68044418e948988f-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10685762" class="wp-caption-text">稍微拌炒后，加入高汤炖煮，再加入芒果一起煮。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
