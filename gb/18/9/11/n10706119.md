<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>天生捕鱼高手 爆红黄金猎犬捕获罕见鱼中鱼</h2>
<a href="https://d377tzsbzf1wvt.cloudfront.net/N9urYNnKb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wqsjdc301/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月05日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">我们都知道狗是天生的打猎专家，它们擅长追赶猎物、鸟类等会动的生物，不过，有些狗不只会上山打猎，还会下海<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%95%E9%B1%BC.md#1">捕鱼</a>呢！这只叫作拉尼（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rani</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>与主人和另外两只黄金猎犬住在美国的密西西比州。它很喜欢在湖里<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E6%B3%B3.md#1">游泳</a>，更是天生的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%95%E9%B1%BC.md#1">捕鱼</a>高手。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">其实，拉尼的捕鱼功夫在网路上已经小有名气了，它的主人为它拍摄了许多捕捉鱼和乌龟的影片，引起了许多网友的热烈讨论。这次，它捕到了一条鱼中鱼！只见她轻轻地潜入水中，不用两秒钟的功夫，便捉到了一条大口黑鲈。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10706187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10706187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d85ee1c66677d0e172700d2442f9859c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10706187 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/d85ee1c66677d0e172700d2442f9859c-600x301.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10706187" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">黄金猎犬</a>拉尼发现了左边有东西跳出水面。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>观看完整影片（Credit: Jukin）?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-acd4217e-edd7-4f83-526b-bfcea845da36" data-id="player-acd4217e-edd7-4f83-526b-bfcea845da36"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/acd4217e-edd7-4f83-526b-bfcea845da36?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F9%2F11%2Fn10706119.md#1"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大鱼试图挣脱，但是拉尼一点都不担心，淡定的把落入水中的鱼再次衔起，缓缓游回岸上。这时主人才发现，原来拉尼捕得不是一条大鱼，而是一条大口黑鲈咬着另一条蓝鳃太阳鱼的鱼中鱼，太不可思议了！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10706190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10706190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9e6048d22b822512a8d451cacb91bd6b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10706190 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9e6048d22b822512a8d451cacb91bd6b-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10706190" class="wp-caption-text">大鱼掉到水里了，拉尼轻巧地潜入水中。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10706192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10706192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/93b660ffbf3da019bd5c57ce28ed68e5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10706192 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/93b660ffbf3da019bd5c57ce28ed68e5-600x386.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10706192" class="wp-caption-text">大鱼试图挣脱，但是拉尼一点都不担心，淡定的把落入水中的鱼再次衔起，缓缓游回岸上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">那么，拉尼是用什么方法捕鱼呢？主人解释道，拉尼在捕鱼时通常会先丢一些小面包块在水面上当作诱饵，吸引鱼过来。然后将头靠在水面上，静静地等待着恰当的时机，再以闪电般的速度把头深入水中抓鱼。它每天都会为自己捕上一只新鲜的鱼，作为三餐的一部分。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10706194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10706194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-26.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10706194 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-26-600x383.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10706194" class="wp-caption-text">拉尼捕得不是一条大鱼，而是一条大口黑鲈咬着另一条蓝鳃太阳鱼的鱼中鱼，太不可思议了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">网友纷纷表示，拉尼的捕鱼技术实在太强了，甚至比自己还好。有人开玩笑说，拉尼其实是伪装成狗的熊。也有人问到，拉尼怎么处理捕到的鱼跟乌龟？主人回答，它会把鱼和朋友一起享用，把乌龟放生。它很喜欢乌龟，所以不会伤害它们。也有很多人表示，希望自己家里的黄金猎犬也能像拉尼一样厉害。</span></p>
