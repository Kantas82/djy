<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>满满起司牵丝的早餐 马铃薯火腿千层起司派</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10615386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月26日讯】你也喜欢吃咸食吗？浓浓的起司奶香味、火腿培根烟熏味、以及<ahref="https://github.com/snthxw327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>的绵密口感，这道<ahref="https://github.com/snthxw327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF%E5%8D%83%E5%B1%82%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">马铃薯火腿千层起司派</a>绝对会让你爱不释手、一口也不想停下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/7-15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483701 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/7-15-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/snthxw327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF%E5%8D%83%E5%B1%82%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">马铃薯火腿千层起司派</a>。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/snthxw327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 5颗<br />
火腿 8～10片<br />
切达起司 2杯（丝）<br />
莫札瑞拉起司 2杯（丝）</p>
<p>蘑菇 500ml<br />
洋葱 1颗</p>
<p>蛋 2颗<br />
鲜奶油 1杯<br />
黑胡椒粉 2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉 1茶匙<br />
盐 2茶匙<br />
奥勒冈香草粉 2茶匙</p>
<p>培根 5片<br />
香芹末 1/4杯</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml；“1汤匙”=1 Tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙；“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 烤箱预热至摄氏180度／华氏350度。<br />
2. 将培根切碎，炒熟。<br />
3. 将洋葱和蘑菇切块，炒到变软。<br />
4. 马铃薯去皮、切片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483694" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-94.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483694 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-94-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">火腿片铺在第二层，薄铺一层切达起司，再铺马铃薯片。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 方形烤盘的底部均匀地涂上奶油，将切片马铃薯铺在底层。<br />
6. 火腿片铺在第二层，薄铺一层切达起司。<br />
7. 铺一层马铃薯、一层火腿，再将炒熟的蘑菇和洋葱薄薄地铺上，然后薄铺一层切达起司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483695" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-88.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483695 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-88-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将炒熟的蘑菇和洋葱薄薄地铺上。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 再放上剩下的马铃薯，最后铺一层厚厚的莫札瑞拉起司丝。<br />
9. 在一个碗里打蛋、倒进鲜奶油、盐、胡椒、黑胡椒、奥勒冈香草粉，充分搅拌后倒进烤盘里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483696" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-82.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483696 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-82-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烘烤约40分钟，或烤到起司呈现深金褐色。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 烘烤约40分钟，或烤到起司呈现深金褐色。<br />
11. 取出，稍微冷却后切片，放上些许炒过的培根、撒上香芹末。完成。</p>
