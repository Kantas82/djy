<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月19日讯】<span class="s1">没有不值得救助的生命，即使它对你来说微不足道，却可能是谁的宝贝。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF.md#1">俄罗斯</a>一个寒冷的日子里，一名男子竟然脱光衣服“<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%B3%E6%B2%B3.md#1">跳河</a>”。他不是要锻炼身体，也不是神智不清，而是为了“它”——掉进水里的狗狗。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">男子抱着黑狗，抓紧岸边垂下的救命绳。仔细观察，男子在被拉上岸的过程中，脸部表情纠结成一团，就能明白这个任务有多么不容易。最后众人一同使力，成功将他们拉上岸。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10482817" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10482817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-11-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">男子跳进河里<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E7%8B%97.md#1">救狗</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">惊魂甫定的黑狗，看起来是流浪在外的毛孩，它上岸后一度腿软，身体也相当僵硬。没关系，甩一甩身上的水，活动一下筋骨就好。在众人关怀的视线下，狗狗逐渐恢复平日的活力。太好啦！</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">而<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">热心</a><ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E7%8B%97.md#1">救狗</a>的男子冷得发抖，幸好一旁有人立刻送上毛巾让他擦拭；看到镜头，他还露出笑容并比出胜利的V字手势，真是个<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%96%E7%94%B7.md#1">暖男</a>！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10482819" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10482819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-7-450x437.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="437" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">真正的<ahref="https://github.com/qblrme335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%96%E7%94%B7.md#1">暖男</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">希望他回家后有好好暖和一下身子，免得做了好事却换来感冒。</span></p>
