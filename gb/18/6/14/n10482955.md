<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一张蓝紫梦幻绣球花海照片 让世界看见台湾</h2>
<a href="https://d1x2hvndm8f89d.cloudfront.net/K1lUeMnrx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/acnndw3533/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/acnndw3533/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月21日讯】万朵<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%A3%E7%90%83%E8%8A%B1.md#1">绣球花</a>绽放，田野间变成梦幻的蓝紫色花海，你能想像这有多么浪漫吗？夏季限定！<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>绣球花（又称<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E9%98%B3%E8%8A%B1.md#1">紫阳花</a>）盛开，游客赏游、拍摄许多绣球花海美景，纷纷上传社群网站分享，由于紫蓝花海太过梦幻，网友们几乎不敢相信这绝世美景竟然就在台湾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10483998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/007-airi___0312_4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/007-airi___0312_4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10483998" class="wp-caption-text">万朵<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%A3%E7%90%83%E8%8A%B1.md#1">绣球花</a>绽放，田野间变成梦幻的蓝紫色花海，好不浪漫！（airi___0312/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">花季</a>从5～7月的<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E9%98%B3%E8%8A%B1.md#1">紫阳花</a>开始探出头来，绽放又大又抢眼的美丽笑脸等着人们一探花容。绣球花又名紫阳花、八仙花，原产于日本再经由欧洲改良的品种又称“西洋紫阳花”。</p>
<p>姹紫嫣红、红蓝交织层次丰富的绣球花，高度约1～2公尺的灌木在中海拔的<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>大片盛开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10483999" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/009-vivianhaung.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/009-vivianhaung-600x750.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10483999" class="wp-caption-text">台湾网友Vivian Huang的PO文照片，是<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B3%E6%98%8E%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阳明山</a>的绣球花美景。（vivianhaung/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在社群网站Instagram（简称IG）的官方账号中，有一则台湾网友Vivian Huang的PO文照片，该图是<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B3%E6%98%8E%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阳明山</a>的绣球花美景。IG官方介绍该图，“这是在台湾的阳明山国家公园，Vivian Huang停在绣球花前闻香气。”仅短短3天就超过全球145万个网友按赞（截至2018年6月15日已近151万个赞），这一张照片让全世界网友看见台湾绣球花之美！</p>
<p>“光线从云朵间的缝隙中射下来，让这些花看起来更加耀眼。”该照片原PO网友Vivian Huang 2017年6月20日接受台媒“ETNEWS新闻云”访问时表示，“真的是又惊又喜”！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #ff6600;">梦幻花海何处赏？</span></h2>
<p>每年5～7月是台湾绣球花盛开的季节，全台湾有许多景点像阳明山竹子湖、杉林溪等，都有绣球花海吸引游客赏花消暑。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>台北 阳明山绣球花海</strong></span></p>
<p>阳明山是如假包换的台北后花园，搭乘公车就可以抵达观赏夏日盛开的绣球花海。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10483993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/001-wen_long0624.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/001-wen_long0624-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10483993" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">花季</a>从5～7月的紫阳花开始探出头来，等着人们一探花容。（wen_long0624/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>阳明山 竹子湖大梯田</strong></span></p>
<p>一层一层阶梯式的梯田上，绣球花也跟弯弯曲曲、很有层次的展现，白色、蓝色、粉色领先开放，垫底的紫红色要等到6月才会上场。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>阳明山 水车寮高家绣球花田</strong></span></p>
<p>在5月初举得高高的花团纷纷现身了，蓝色为主调的小山壁背景筑起蓝白绣球花墙，被团团花海围住的画面既浪漫又唯美。首登上官方IG的照片美景，就是在这里拍摄的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10483994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/002-muskahung.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/002-muskahung-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10483994" class="wp-caption-text">台湾绣球花盛开，游客拍摄许多绣球花海美景上传网路分享。（muskahung/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>宜兰 明池国家森林游乐区</strong></span></p>
<p>随着四季变换不同缤纷色彩的明池，夏天则轮到赏绣球花上场；在云雾环绕下上万朵的绣球花多了一分冷艳清净和脱俗不凡的美。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>苗栗 高山青鳟鱼养殖场</strong></span></p>
<p>这里的绣球花丛藏在深山里，坐在石头上看着溪流两侧花开锦簇的蓝色花团，就像掉到童话故事里一样美得有点不真实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10483997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/006-gengorfu.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/006-gengorfu-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10483997" class="wp-caption-text">姹紫嫣红、红蓝交织层次丰富的绣球花，在中海拔盛开。（gengorfu/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>新竹 关西花鸟园</strong></span></p>
<p>位于新竹关西的台湾花鸟园是全台首座平地温室花园，把适存于低温、中海拔的300多种万朵绣球花在平地的园区中开放。5月底绣球花季开跑。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>南投 杉林溪生态度假园区</strong></span></p>
<p>杉林溪生态园区的绣球花季自5月展开约至8月止，园区内处处可见深蓝色与淡紫、深紫互相混合争艳，也有温暖的粉红色绣球花交错盛开。而园区内有几棵红、橘、绿渐层的槭树，将景色妆点成梦幻、唯美的令人陶醉！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10484000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10484000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/013-airi___0312_1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10484000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/013-airi___0312_1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10484000" class="wp-caption-text">深蓝色与淡紫、深紫互相争艳，也有温暖的粉红色交错盛开。（airi___0312/Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
