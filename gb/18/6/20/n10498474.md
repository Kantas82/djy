<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大埔墟熟食中心啖啖香港情</h2>
<a href="https://d2p23c289x62lr.cloudfront.net/W7l2CZMEh"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/emljlk376/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/emljlk376/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月20日讯】每隔一段时间我便会到<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%9F%94%E5%A2%9F%E7%86%9F%E9%A3%9F%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">大埔墟熟食中心</a>吃早餐，大埔墟熟食中心里面有三十多间食店，大部分都是从前旧墟大排档搬过去，几十年的老街坊亦由旧墟跟着去现在大埔墟街市2楼的熟食中心。</p>
<p>虽然对我来说，进去可以说是“山长水还”之外，还得早点去才能吃到好东西，但我仍然很喜欢到那里吃东西。除了为美食，更是为了寻回昔日那份与香港老街坊畅聚的情怀，享受久违了的人情味！</p>
<p>“早起的鸟儿有虫吃”，这句话真的很适合形容这里的食店，因为这里多间食店的食品都是每日新鲜制造，只有一定数量，卖完就会关门，所以想食一定要早到。我每次都是早上七、八点就到达，里面早就坐满了老街坊，好不热闹，大家见面除了互打招呼，还会嘻嘻哈哈地聊天，食店的老板和员工满场走，和街坊食客打成一片，谈笑风生好融洽。即使像我这些外来人，他们都会热情招待，任何人到这里，都会给他们的热情和互动的环境气氛感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10499180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223244100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499180" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223244100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499180" class="wp-caption-text">早上七、八点到达时，熟食中心里面早就坐满了老街坊，好不热闹。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10499191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223309100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499191" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223309100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499191" class="wp-caption-text">早上七、八点到达时，熟食中心里面早就坐满了老街坊，好不热闹。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次我先到<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E8%AE%B0%E7%82%B9%E5%BF%83.md#1">林记点心</a>坐下喝杯茶，食几碟点心，伙记阿姨热情帮我泡茶，又帮我倒茶呢！还一直问我想吃什么，帮我去拿，看见我拍照，以为我是游客说随便拍，又介绍哪几款特色点心好吃，热情到不得了。当我想要拿起点心时，她怕点心太热我会被烫到，主动帮我拿，还一直等我选完，帮我拿回坐位，真的很贴心呢…！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10499116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223251100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499116" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223251100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499116" class="wp-caption-text">很多新鲜出笼的手造点心。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10499175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223247100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499175" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223247100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499175" class="wp-caption-text">虾饺、烧卖、牛肉球、鱼翅饺，应有尽有。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我点了即拉叉烧肠、排骨和传统的鹌鹑蛋烧卖，鹌鹑蛋烧卖蛋下面的猪肉烧卖入口时，有很香浓的猪肉鲜味，排骨亦是很传统怀旧的味道，即拉叉烧肠也一样很新鲜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10499098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223254100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499098" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223254100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499098" class="wp-caption-text">即拉叉烧肠、排骨和传统的鹌鹑蛋烧卖。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10498868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10498868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223301100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10498868" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223301100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10498868" class="wp-caption-text">鹌鹑蛋烧卖蛋下面的猪肉烧卖入口时，有很香浓的猪肉鲜味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10499099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10499099" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223305100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10499099" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223305100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10499099" class="wp-caption-text">即拉叉烧肠很新鲜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>于我还要试其它店的食物，所以只吃了这三款便结账，三款点心加两个茶位，都只是44元，非常平民地道的价钱，重点真是新鲜好食。结账的时候老板娘梁太都出来招呼，友善到不得了，倘若不是要带朋友试食其它东西，真的会坐着不舍得这么快走呀！</p>
<p>到东记了～来到东记当然是吃他们最有名的炸猪排上海面，东记的炸猪扒以大大块见称，咬下去鲜嫩松软，因为每块猪排都是由用人手敲到松软，再蘸上炸浆，炸至金黄松脆。而且他们会主动剪好再上，好贴心，真的每一块都是心血。自制炸酱够辣够香，配上上海粗面味道一流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10498864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223320100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10498864" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223320100484-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10498864" class="wp-caption-text">自制炸酱够辣够香，配上上海粗面味道一流。猪排炸至金黄松脆，还剪好再上。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猪扒面很大碗，即使男士一碗都一定饱。他们的炸鸡翼同样出色，同样是下单后即炸，我看到很多人都会外带回家慢慢吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10498865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223313100484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10498865" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/180619223313100484-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10498865" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E8%AE%B0%E4%B8%8A%E6%B5%B7%E9%9D%A2.md#1">东记上海面</a>。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后移步到<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E8%AE%B0.md#1">苏记</a>吃他们的特制<span style="font-weight: 400;">法国吐司</span>，口感很像家庭感觉得蛋煎法国吐司，不像平日吃到的炸到整件都是油，少了油腻感。法国吐司不用糖浆改用炼奶，中间亦有夹心令口感更丰富。他们自家制的法国吐司已经有五十多年历史了！再配一杯奶茶真的很满足。老板也是连街坊怎么称呼都记得一清二楚。这里真的周围都是老街坊，啖啖都是人情味。#</p>
