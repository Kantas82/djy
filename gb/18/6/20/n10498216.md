<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梦幻缤纷鲜果冰棒 养颜美容消暑不发胖</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10554314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月15日讯】夏季高温不断向上攀升，想吃冰<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%9A%91.md#1">消暑</a>对抗严酷暑气，可是又不想出门人挤人排队？又想保持窈窕体态怕发胖吗？</p>
<p>自然又健康的<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>，除了切块冰冰箱，还能自制<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%A3%92.md#1">冰棒</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>可以依个人喜好做变化，混搭出独一无二的梦幻缤纷鲜果冰棒，这次要教大家的口味是“芒果覆盆子冰棒”，既能<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%9A%91.md#1">消暑</a>对抗这个夏天，还能养颜美容不用怕发胖！</p>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>芒果 适量<br />
覆盆莓 适量<br />
牛奶 1/3杯<br />
优格 2份（半杯、1/3杯）<br />
糖 2份（1汤匙）<br />
小豆蔻粉 少许</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>先将切块的芒果放入容器中，依序加入牛奶、优格、糖、小豆蔻粉搅拌均匀。接着，放入果汁机中，让食材均匀混合后，约倒入4/5至<ahref="https://github.com/iiholu351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%A3%92.md#1">冰棒</a>容器内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3e619eefdbdef9a61ddacfd806ac5d69.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10498287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3e619eefdbdef9a61ddacfd806ac5d69-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜甜切块芒果，加上牛奶。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再将准备好的覆盆莓放入容器中，加入糖、优格拌匀。再放入放入果汁机中，让食材均匀混合后，倒入刚才的冰棒容器中，并填满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/09b4dc9e35a0a02373a10fa901d5efcd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10498285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/09b4dc9e35a0a02373a10fa901d5efcd-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">养颜美容的覆盆莓。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，放入冰箱冷冻一个晚上。隔天，就可以享用冰凉健康又消暑的芒果覆盆子冰棒啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/edeafc6d477db5aaf3c44be4b83d1f88.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10498283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/edeafc6d477db5aaf3c44be4b83d1f88-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高温炎热夏季，不妨自己动手做疗愈鲜果冰棒，在家消暑吃得又健康！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>【备注】食材可依个人喜好调整变换。</strong></p>
