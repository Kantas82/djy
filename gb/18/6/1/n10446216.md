<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台林口世大运社会住宅 租金不到2万元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4471b9f02fcb1272c74e31a3fb5860c7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>内政部长叶俊荣（左）与世大运滑轮溜冰金牌选手陈映竹（右）在林口世大运选手村社会住宅举办公告出租记者会。（陈懿胜／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月01日讯】（大纪元记者陈懿胜台北报导）<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E5%A4%A7%E8%BF%90.md#1">世大运</a><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%89%E6%89%8B%E6%9D%91.md#1">选手村</a>也可以成为“你”的家！内政部1日在林口世大运选手村会住宅中办公告出租记者会，内政部长叶俊荣表示，选手村7月2日起受理申请，它是一个融合生活、教育、休闲的高品质<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%85.md#1">社会住宅</a>社区，<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E9%87%91.md#1">租金</a>依市价打8折后，前3年再打8折，就是要让北部地区民众“住得到”、“住得好”、“住得起”，享受“高品质”、“低租金”，让入住的民众都能享有便利舒适的居住环境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446233" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f93367d915c27d1c0a692efe1d55255f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f93367d915c27d1c0a692efe1d55255f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446233" class="wp-caption-text">林口<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E5%A4%A7%E8%BF%90.md#1">世大运</a><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%89%E6%89%8B%E6%9D%91.md#1">选手村</a><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%85.md#1">社会住宅</a>。（内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10446228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446228" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1d0108cc0c481c92fe71dece1ffd8e59.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1d0108cc0c481c92fe71dece1ffd8e59-450x304.png" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446228" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅房型。（内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10446226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446226" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/112f8c6aa3c910b2db5d4e49d3682271.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/112f8c6aa3c910b2db5d4e49d3682271-450x269.png" alt="" width="450" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446226" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅房型。 （内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10446219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446219" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8de686c3ea990ff1fe47943c1ca07bf1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8de686c3ea990ff1fe47943c1ca07bf1-450x151.png" alt="" width="450" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446219" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅房型。（内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶俊荣表示，林口世大运选手村社会住宅提供3,408户住宅单位及82户商店设施，住宅单位除442户提供经济部作为国际青年创业聚落，59户提供新北市作为公益、教育等使用外，另预留407户规划引进非营利团体及办理多元入住实验方案使用，其余2,500户全部对外出租，为有史以来出租户数最多，可协助小家庭、单身青年、经济与社会弱势者等多元居住及生活的需求。</p>
<p>叶俊荣表示，另外为留下大家对世大运共同的美好记忆，除在社区的公共空间设有世大运纪念区，也规划保留部分户数给各国参赛好手回台观光时居住，社区并设有经济部国际青年创业聚落，让社会住宅的内涵更丰富与具有温度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1fee059441f8ce1b0c8234818ca6f930.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1fee059441f8ce1b0c8234818ca6f930-450x209.png" alt="" width="450" b="209" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446218" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%9F%E9%87%91.md#1">租金</a>。（内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶俊荣说，世大运选手村社会住宅有“一高一低”跟“四多”，拥有绿建筑及智慧建筑规划，社福设施与邻里共享，再加上住都中心专业管理，让住户享有“高品质”的生活；租金依实价登录及市场租金估价，弱势户64折，一般户8折，开办前3年再打8折，就是要让入住的民众享有“低租金”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446221" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/df8dcddd1f84ee977cbe016cbe48ae31.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/df8dcddd1f84ee977cbe016cbe48ae31-450x279.png" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446221" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅申请对象。 （内政部提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>林口世大运选手村社会住宅2,500户全部对外出租，“多户数”全面招租；1至4房型的“多房型”，让各类型的住户都能够入住；“多申请类别”分为6大类，以扩大照顾多元民众需求；“多内涵”则是结合社福与青创，打造“国际青年创业聚落”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a9303a3a84383fc63350388a23947049.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a9303a3a84383fc63350388a23947049-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446232" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村社会住宅三人房客厅。（陈懿胜／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10446229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10446229" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20b24e47e6b55db9bf662143ec081566.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20b24e47e6b55db9bf662143ec081566-450x245.png" alt="" width="450" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10446229" class="wp-caption-text">林口世大运选手村会住宅三人房主卧房。（陈懿胜／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶俊荣表示，各房型都提供基本的设施设备，像是冷气、流理台及瓦斯炉，租户也可选择选配家具，像是餐桌椅、沙发及茶几、床架、衣柜等家具，只要每月额外负担400元到1,000出头的费用，就可“租”到耐用的家具。</p>
<p>叶俊荣强调，内政部来当包租公，不仅要提供租金便宜，有高品质的服务，更要有优质的管理，期盼选手村社会住宅的推动，一来可以改变现在的租屋环境，更让青年拥有更好的居住空间，“让年轻人在成家立业的第一哩路走得顺畅，让弱势得到更多的支撑”，住得自在、快乐。◇</p>
