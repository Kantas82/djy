<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>游艇司机突掉头 海中捞出一物让乘客超傻眼</h2>
<a href="https://d26c5rnrr5nvsd.cloudfront.net/D3eYPNXEg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zkoiap363/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/zkoiap363/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10435201 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-2-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月28日讯】（大纪元记者莫琳报导）一天下午，一艘<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%89%87.md#1">游艇</a>正在澳洲南斯德布鲁克岛（South Stradbroke island）外海航行，游艇司机杰米?尔理（Jamie Early）突然瞥见水中好像有什么东西，立刻掉头回去。船上满载的乘客一头雾水：发生了什么事？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520267" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10520267 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-01-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520267" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%89%87.md#1">游艇</a>司机突然掉头在找东西，游客们不知发生了什么事。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尔理暂停游艇，侧身往海里捞啊捞的，乘客有的盯着尔理，有的也跟着侧身看尔理在干什么。整船游客的脸上露出一堆的问号：不过是出来游船，为何司机停下来捞东西呢？他到底在捞什么呀？</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-d438433b-77db-4e83-54f4-dfccdf172a84" data-id="player-d438433b-77db-4e83-54f4-dfccdf172a84"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/d438433b-77db-4e83-54f4-dfccdf172a84?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F28%2Fn10520262.md#1"></a></span></strong></p>
<p>果然不久，尔理从海里抓到了一只不慎落水的小<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E9%BC%A0.md#1">袋鼠</a>，暂时把它安置在游艇驾驶座上方，一手扶着小袋鼠，一手继续快速航行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10520276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-02-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520276" class="wp-caption-text">小艇司机从海里捞出一只落水的小<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E9%BC%A0.md#1">袋鼠</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全身湿透的小袋鼠看起来好可怜，坐在前排的游客也伸出手，帮忙扶住惊魂甫定的小袋鼠。</p>
<p>“它非常放松，我把它放在船头，它冻坏了。”尔理说，“我们只是把它送上了岸。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10520413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10520413" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10520413 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180628-shouhuihsu-wallaby-03-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10520413" class="wp-caption-text">游艇司机从海里捞出的宝贝温暖了一船的游客。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于小袋鼠为何会落水呢？原来它是为了躲避狗狗的攻击，不慎掉落水中。幸好遇到好心的驾驶，及时将它捞上岸。不然，它可能就要溺毙了。</p>
<p>小袋鼠上岸后恢复得很好，感谢爱心司机尔理。</p>
