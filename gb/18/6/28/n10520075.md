<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>八哥学人叫 主人反过来学它叫！</h2>
<a href="https://d12gl57lrewmhb.cloudfront.net/B3pjsQQeb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月02日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%93%A5.md#1">八哥</a>鸟生活在草原、山区的树林中，善于鸣叫，也会<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E4%BB%BF.md#1">模仿</a>其它鸟叫声，经过训练，还能模仿人类说话。</p>
<p>这种喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E4%BB%BF.md#1">模仿</a>人家声音的鸟儿，如果有人模仿它叫会怎么样？</p>
<p>影片中，一只<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%93%A5.md#1">八哥</a>鸟站在一位戴帽子、长白胡子的男子手上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10528552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10528552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/704ec57bb4ae36a61baf045f321e5238.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10528552 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/704ec57bb4ae36a61baf045f321e5238-450x311.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10528552" class="wp-caption-text">爱<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E8%AF%AD.md#1">学语</a><ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%B1%E6%AD%8C.md#1">唱歌</a>的八哥鸟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男子对着它发出一些叫声，它也对应着发出一些叫声，突然它不唱了。</p>
<p>男子命令它：“唱！”“快点！”唔，它好像听懂，立刻又开始<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%B1%E6%AD%8C.md#1">唱歌</a>。</p>
<p>男子这次学鸡叫，八哥也学鸡叫，又吹口哨，八哥同样也吹口哨。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版&#8230;</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-b550c3e9-ea44-499e-6f10-cfac50ab0899" data-id="player-b550c3e9-ea44-499e-6f10-cfac50ab0899"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/b550c3e9-ea44-499e-6f10-cfac50ab0899?r=16x9&amp;s=720x620&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F28%2Fn10520075.md#1"></a>
<p>后来呢，角色转换了！换成男子学它叫，八哥发出一种怪声，好像是鸭叫？男子就跟着学。它又学鸡叫，男子也学……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10528551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10528551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5a1ecf31fea6b38373fcff965939a93f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10528551 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5a1ecf31fea6b38373fcff965939a93f-450x283.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10528551" class="wp-caption-text">两位互相学习歌唱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就这样不断地玩，是不是很有趣呢？</p>
<p>网友看后纷纷回应：“大叔好笑！”“好棒！”“太棒了！好一只八哥，鸟与人的奇缘，一辈子怀念八哥鸟，很好玩，以前养过。”</p>
