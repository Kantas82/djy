<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>香辣酥脆白饭再添一碗 经典好食左宗棠鸡</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10537908" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-2-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月08日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A6%E5%AE%97%E6%A3%A0%E9%B8%A1.md#1">左宗棠鸡</a>，又名左将军鸡，相传它的由来，与清末著名的左宗棠将军有关。是一道经典的<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%98%E8%8F%9C.md#1">湘菜</a>，这道经典<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">料理</a>，使用鸡腿肉去骨切丁裹粉，用中火过油锁住鸡肉的鲜嫩后沥油，加入辣酸番茄酱油浓汁烹炒而成，是一道讲究火候的料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10496472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10496472" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b43fd5618b47bb9372e174408903da31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10496472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b43fd5618b47bb9372e174408903da31-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10496472" class="wp-caption-text">经典<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">料理</a>你也可以自己来，<ahref="https://github.com/cvfbgl3438/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A6%E5%AE%97%E6%A3%A0%E9%B8%A1.md#1">左宗棠鸡</a>够味上桌！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【鸡肉腌制】</h2>
<p>去骨鸡腿肉<br />
鸡蛋 1颗<br />
盐 1茶匙<br />
糖 1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉 1/3茶匙<br />
芝麻油 1/3茶匙<br />
生粉 6汤匙</p>
<h2>【调味酱料】</h2>
<p>油 1汤匙<br />
干辣椒 7个<br />
蒜末 1茶匙<br />
番茄酱 3汤匙<br />
酱油 2汤匙<br />
糖 2汤匙<br />
醋 2汤匙<br />
水 1杯<br />
柠檬 1/2颗<br />
生粉水 1汤匙<br />
葱末 1汤匙</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>先将去骨鸡腿肉切成块状，放入容器中，打入一颗新鲜鸡蛋，依序放入盐、糖、胡椒粉、芝麻油和生粉（太白粉）均匀搅拌，让每块鸡肉充分吸，腌制15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10496413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10496413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c42e9ca9d7b6096ca7846c77dcdfdb3f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10496413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c42e9ca9d7b6096ca7846c77dcdfdb3f-450x428.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10496413" class="wp-caption-text">这道经典料理，主要使用的是去骨鸡腿肉。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着，将腌好的鲜嫩鸡肉块裹上太白粉，下锅油炸。将鸡肉块煎炸至表面呈金黄色时，即可起锅，放置一旁备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10496415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10496415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3f4a085b2c29d3189b8d4c8008a21adb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10496415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3f4a085b2c29d3189b8d4c8008a21adb-450x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10496415" class="wp-caption-text">将去骨鸡腿肉裹粉，准备下锅油炸。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>热锅倒入1汤匙的食用油，放入干辣椒和蒜末爆香，倒入番茄酱、酱油、糖、醋和水，再挤上点柠檬汁、撒上葱末点缀一下，最后，别忘了来点太白粉水勾芡，并将锅内酱汁均匀搅拌。</p>
<p>接着，倒入黄金鸡丁大火拌炒，让鸡丁加速吸收酱汁，收汁后即可起锅，盛盘上桌准备大快朵颐一番！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10496416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10496416" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/ea78db2ccce383a69e81fabb4d462478.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10496416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/ea78db2ccce383a69e81fabb4d462478-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10496416" class="wp-caption-text">左宗棠鸡香辣滋味配上酥脆口感，让人忍不住想多添几碗白饭。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【备注】</h2>
<p>生粉：可使用太白粉和玉米粉<br />
口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
<h2>【菜名探源】</h2>
<p>美国人一提起中国菜，都晓得这道“左将军鸡”“General Tao’s Chicken”，比起“左宗棠将军”“General Tao Tsung Tang”，或拼音为“General Zuo Zong Tang”更加出名。</p>
<p>左宗棠，湖南湘阳人，是道光年间的举人，“湘军”的将领。有一回，左宗棠将军战胜凯旋返家时，其夫人为了恭贺他，亲自下厨烹煮了一道他最喜爱的香辣鸡丁。</p>
<p>此后，每当左将军胜利归来，在犒赏士兵们的佳肴中一定有这道菜，于是心存感激的士兵们，就将这道菜尊称为“左将军鸡”或“左公鸡”。后来这道佳肴透过士兵们而流传到民间，人们就直呼它为“左宗棠鸡”了。</p>
