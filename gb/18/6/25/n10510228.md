<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10554314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月27日讯】快看，前方有3只<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1.md#1">火鸡</a>围着一棵树在<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%95%E5%9C%88.md#1">绕圈</a>圈！火鸡们迷路了吗？它们似乎并没有发现自己陷入了一个“<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E9%99%90%E5%9B%9E%E5%9C%88.md#1">无限回圈</a>”，傻傻地跟着前一只走，就这样走个没完……小火鸡感觉有点傻愣，你们头不晕吗？</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #339966;">看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-a63d3ed9-dc08-44f8-5259-deae63115799" data-id="player-a63d3ed9-dc08-44f8-5259-deae63115799"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/a63d3ed9-dc08-44f8-5259-deae63115799?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F25%2Fn10510228.md#1"></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1.md#1">火鸡</a>绕着某个物体转圈的样子看起来很<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%90%9E%E7%AC%91.md#1">搞笑</a>，有人说，因为火鸡本来就是群体性的动物，会聚在一起很正常，当有一只火鸡不小心开始绕着物体兜圈，其他火鸡也不疑有他，会跟着一直走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8b0e3c738c247cd9103b81ff4b41c57b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510343 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8b0e3c738c247cd9103b81ff4b41c57b-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网友发现了奇怪的现象，三只小火鸡绕着一棵树转圈，无法停止！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10510434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/11baf5a6c0600609a6430c0e2f562dfc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510434 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/11baf5a6c0600609a6430c0e2f562dfc-450x454.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="454" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小火鸡绕着绕着有点焦虑，感觉走很久了呀！怎么还没到头？（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，因为火鸡不够聪明，它们完全没有发现其实不管前面或后面有多少只火鸡，自己只要在圈圈中，就是那第一只领头的火鸡，也是最后一只尾随的火鸡。只要有一只火鸡跳出回圈，可能就会拯救了整群火鸡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510446" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eaf6fbdc7b3fa9f728ea52dbb417d0a0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510446 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eaf6fbdc7b3fa9f728ea52dbb417d0a0-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">不要放弃，感觉就要到了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>之前曾发生过类似的“诡异事件”──15只火鸡绕成一个巨大的圆圈，一边走一边向中心看，圈的里面是一只猫的尸体！这样的画面被网友认为是在举行什么奇怪的仪式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510448" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1840a1d4aa74c9949a5d66ef4aca0131.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510448 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1840a1d4aa74c9949a5d66ef4aca0131-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">但专家表示，火鸡会这么做，其实只是出自“形成小团体抵抗外侮”的天性。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过专家表示，火鸡们向中心看只是在确认这只猫到底是否还具备威胁。因为，会这样<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%95%E5%9C%88.md#1">绕圈</a>的火鸡都是母的，不像公火鸡具有尖爪和壮硕的体型能保卫自己，母火鸡喜欢有小团体共同抵御外侮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e287358cc75a77a7d26bec647375c44c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510449 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e287358cc75a77a7d26bec647375c44c-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">问题出在，火鸡不够聪明，它们完全没有发现其实不管前面或后面有多少只火鸡，自己只要在圈圈中，就是那第一只领头的火鸡，也是最后一只尾随的火鸡。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而，就像影片中绕圈圈的3只火鸡一样，专家认为，绕着猫尸体的15只火鸡当中，没有一只发现这只猫其实已经死了。所以它们只是不断地确认、确认、确认。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10510784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0629a395f1ff06edcd31dee428072087.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10510784 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0629a395f1ff06edcd31dee428072087-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">还来如此！所以火鸡才会一直绕着圈圈绕不出来呀～（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你认为火鸡是不小心被同伴“带坏”才“误入歧途”，事实可能跟你想的不太一样！有民众曾拍到一只火鸡绕着石碑，左边绕过来张望一会儿，又从右边绕回去。它一直觉得自己在探路！</p>
<p>火鸡有时候真的挺呆萌的，让人好气又好笑～真是败给火鸡了！</p>
