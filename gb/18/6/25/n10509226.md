<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乌鸦又来弄小动物 狗狗好可怜 被猛啄一口吓得瞬间弹跳</h2>
<a href="https://d11dfuybe1ur8g.cloudfront.net/O3c4mTDey"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/imabdi3119/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/imabdi3119/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月19日讯】坏心眼<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E9%B8%A6.md#1">乌鸦</a>又来<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%BA%E8%B4%9F.md#1">欺负</a>小动物了！会不会太夸张了点？小<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗的主人就在旁边，这只黑色大鸟依旧不改恶意捉弄的念头，看准角度就往小狗的屁屁用力一<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%84.md#1">啄</a>，痛得小<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗直蹦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10509236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/87231567c8d633fecbe83a888b0ad126.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509236 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/87231567c8d633fecbe83a888b0ad126-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10509236" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E9%B8%A6.md#1">乌鸦</a>偷偷摸摸跑到小狗背后，趁它不注意，用力一<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%84.md#1">啄</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小小狗儿真可怜！史上最无良的鸟类就是乌鸦，明明没什么事，就要去捉弄别的小动物。</p>
<p>有人说，乌鸦的“<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BB%E5%87%BB.md#1">攻击</a>”是为了争取地域和食物，但这只小狗只是跟主人散步，就飞来横祸……下次遇到乌鸦还是小心点比较好！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10509245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/103d2d6ba4bc42b07faf3ba7b4ee857f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509245 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/103d2d6ba4bc42b07faf3ba7b4ee857f-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10509245" class="wp-caption-text">小狗被吓到整个弹跳起来，好可怜喔！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>眼看这只大乌鸦偷偷跟在小狗后面，冷不防就用鸟喙刺向小狗狗的屁屁，痛得小狗马上弹跳呜呜大叫！无辜的狗狗赶紧跑到主人身边。幸好主人居中，把两只动物挡住，不然小狗又要被<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%BA%E8%B4%9F.md#1">欺负</a>第二次了。</p>
<p>其实，乌鸦也不是很怕人类，甚至有啄人头顶、拉人眼皮的前科，所以主人也要小心点啊！别自己也被乌鸦欺负了！</p>
