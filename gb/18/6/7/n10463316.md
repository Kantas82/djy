<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>林青霞视如己出 继女邢嘉倩也爱护两妹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/04-DSC_5115-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>邢嘉倩。（宋祥龙／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10615386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月15日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E9%9D%92%E9%9C%9E.md#1">林青霞</a>有三个可爱的女儿<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%A2%E5%98%89%E5%80%A9.md#1">邢嘉倩</a>、邢爱林与邢言爱，虽然大女儿邢嘉倩并非亲生，但林青霞对她同样疼爱有加，视如己出。</p>
<p>而<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%A2%E5%98%89%E5%80%A9.md#1">邢嘉倩</a>也称赞<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E9%9D%92%E9%9C%9E.md#1">林青霞</a>是“世界上最好的<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%A7%E6%AF%8D.md#1">继母</a>”，她对两个妹妹也是十分爱护。她2015年4月在Instagram分享一张姐妹们的旧照，三位青春少女拥着几只小狗，十分温馨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10481076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10481076" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/012-b4c6aae052b6afac6dd4e398a34e16b9-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10481076" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/012-b4c6aae052b6afac6dd4e398a34e16b9-1-600x555.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="555" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10481076" class="wp-caption-text">嘉倩对两个妹妹十分爱护，她曾分享一张姐妹们的旧照，十分温馨。 （邢嘉倩Instagram）</figcaption></figure>
<p>媒体报导，曾被誉为“东南亚第一<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%B3.md#1">美女</a>”的林青霞，嫁给香港<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%8C%E5%95%86.md#1">富商</a>邢李?后，先后生下女儿邢爱林与邢言爱。</p>
<p>不过，对丈夫与前妻张天爱所生的女儿邢嘉倩，林青霞也一样视如己出，母女间毫无隔阂。</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>观看完整影片?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-35841077-f0ee-4b06-54df-45cd0a9b7f50" data-id="player-35841077-f0ee-4b06-54df-45cd0a9b7f50"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/35841077-f0ee-4b06-54df-45cd0a9b7f50?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F7%2Fn10463316.md#1"></a></p>
<p>去年底曾有陆媒报导，邢嘉倩透过公关转述林青霞对她的疼爱。她回忆，一天她突然在家中尖叫，林青霞立即飞奔上前，因她猜测继女应该是月事来潮而受到惊吓，让邢嘉倩感受到后妈竟是如此关心自己。</p>
<p>邢嘉倩大赞：“林青霞是世界上最好的<ahref="https://github.com/vfpurz3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%A7%E6%AF%8D.md#1">继母</a>。”而自己的生活轶事，都会同林青霞分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10481062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10481062" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/01-B15_0435.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10481062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/01-B15_0435-600x867.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="867" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10481062" class="wp-caption-text">林青霞嫁给邢李?后，对丈夫与前妻所生的女儿邢嘉倩，一样视如己出。（王仁骏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10481063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10481063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/04-DSC_5115.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10481063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/04-DSC_5115-600x850.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="850" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10481063" class="wp-caption-text">邢嘉倩大赞：“林青霞是世界上最好的继母”。（宋祥龙／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邢嘉倩的生母张天爱也曾公开表示对林青霞的感谢，“我好感谢林青霞，她对我的女儿好好，见到她好像见朋友一样，我真的好开心。”</p>
