<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>主人“绝招”太催眠 不肯睡的5小狗乖乖入梦</h2>
<a href="https://d3v04vdnqz6gui.cloudfront.net/U0y5G2a5o"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thmxpb3749/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月28日讯】到了该睡觉的时间了，但5只<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>宝宝却怎么也不肯睡觉。看到男主人来了它们都非常开心，都高高地翘起头，有的前脚搭在狗窝的栏杆上，向着主人摇尾吠叫撒娇，想要同他一起再玩一会儿，场面十分吵闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10518677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10518677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180627GS89a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518677 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180627GS89a-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10518677" class="wp-caption-text">5只<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8B%97.md#1">小狗</a>宝宝怎么也不肯睡觉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而家在美国加州的这位男主人也有自己哄小狗睡觉的<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%9D%E6%8B%9B.md#1">绝招</a>——唱歌。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?<br />
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-9a2145ac-046e-48f1-48f1-81db2d7b8026" data-id="player-9a2145ac-046e-48f1-48f1-81db2d7b8026"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/9a2145ac-046e-48f1-48f1-81db2d7b8026?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x960&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F27%2Fn10518226.md#1"></a></span></strong></p>
<p>随着男主人开始用低沉、温柔的嗓音开始唱一首摇篮曲的时候，很明显地能够看到，小狗们都渐渐地安静下来，停止了乱叫。</p>
<p>接下来，在摇篮曲的歌声中，5只小狗又都乖乖地坐好。但歌声太<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%82%AC%E7%9C%A0.md#1">催眠</a>，小狗们开始敌不过睡意，一个接一个地打哈欠，然后在睡意的驱使下，开始在狗窝内找自己感到舒适的地方趴下来，乖乖地开始睡觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10518678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10518678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180627GS89b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518678 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180627GS89b-600x455.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10518678" class="wp-caption-text">小狗宝宝们乖乖地开始睡觉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居然这么神奇，效果来得这么快！这一幕让正在录影的女子直呼：“要不是我亲眼看到，真的难以相信。”</p>
<p>似乎是为了证实主人<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%82%AC%E7%9C%A0.md#1">催眠</a>曲的魔力，当他的歌声一停下来，立刻就有2只小狗醒过来了。它们抬头看向主人，男子赶紧继续唱歌，最后，5只小狗终于全部乖乖入睡。</p>
<p>该视频收到很多网友点赞。有网友留言说：“太感动了，想起我爸爸做的事情”；“连我也想打瞌睡了”……</p>
<p>但也有网友质疑说，“为什么我自己一唱歌，狗狗就跑来咬我，或是跑到另一个房间去呢？”</p>
