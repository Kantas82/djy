<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台大校友会年会 近百校友同乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00075-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在黄处长（中）监交下，新会长谢邦基（左）从陈子坤（右）手中接过印信正式就任。（温文清/大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月09日讯】（大纪元记者温文清芝加哥报导）大芝加哥区台湾大学<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B.md#1">校友</a>会2018年6月2日在侨教中心举行<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">年会</a>暨同乐晚会，上百位<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B.md#1">校友</a>欢聚一堂，谢邦基接任新会长。</p>
<p>驻芝台北经文处黄钧耀处长及夫人杨淑娟、侨教中心主任王伟赞、副主任赖贞利、中华会馆主席黄于纹等嘉宾出席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10468739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00092.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468739 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00092-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468739" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%A4%A7%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台大校友会</a>理事会合影。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">年会</a>由居维莱主持，伴随着《杜鹃花开》的音乐，台大校园剪影一页页翻开。校友们持校旗及各院院旗入场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10468734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00028.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468734 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00028-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468734" class="wp-caption-text">黄钧耀处长致辞。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄钧耀处长致辞表示自己和夫人淑娟都是台大毕业，回忆当年在台大度过的美好时光令人难忘，他同时也表彰<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%A4%A7%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台大校友会</a>推动台湾参加WHA的努力。</p>
<p>黄于纹则表示自己虽不是台大校友，但却是校友之友，很多好朋友都是台大校友，她提到去年双十国庆园游会上，与台大校友会合作愉快，希望今后继续合作，并祝会务蒸蒸日上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10468731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00014.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468731 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00014-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468731" class="wp-caption-text">陈子坤会长致辞。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈子坤会长感谢各位理事及校友们的支持，过去一年举办健康、税务讲座、大专院校野餐会、欢迎台大校长及教授团来访、情人节晚会、及欢送何震寰处长等，并在校友们的支持下回馈母校2万美元。</p>
<p>在黄处长监交下，新会长谢邦基从陈子坤手中接过印信正式就任。谢邦基表示，今年是台大建校90周年，台大校友于43年前第一次召开校友会，希望能够承前启后，将传统保持下去。谢邦基并向陈子坤赠送“热心公益 造福校友”的奖牌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10468727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00097.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468727 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00097-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468727" class="wp-caption-text">台大校友合唱团演唱歌曲。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10468742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468742" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00158.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468742 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00158-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468742" class="wp-caption-text">江玲珠表演彩带舞。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10468746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00199.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468746 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00199-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468746" class="wp-caption-text">王庆敏表演歌曲演唱。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00237.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10468751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00237.jpg" alt="" width="1" b="1" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_10468753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10468753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00237-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10468753 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/DSC00237-1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10468753" class="wp-caption-text">谭祖德表演螳螂拳。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天多才多艺的台大校友们带来丰富多彩的余兴节目，台大校友合唱团送上台大校歌和《望春风》，赵丽芬美声演唱《偶然》及《红豆词》，蓝一飞带来《托斯尼小夜曲》，江玲珠伴着《春江花月夜》表演彩带舞，王庆敏则演唱《原乡人》和《祝福》，四花瓣舞蹈团带来热辣的拉丁舞蹈《Ring My Bell》，江传香则深情演唱《永恒的回忆》及猫王Elvis Presley的经典情歌《Can’t help falling in love》，谭祖德为大家表演螳螂拳，并加以详细解说，最后在陈莉明演唱《潇洒走一回》，傅家瑛等伴舞，活动在温馨的气氛中成功结束。◇#</p>
