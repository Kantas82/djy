<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这是谋杀吧？鹦鹉享受猫大哥暖心拍头 下一秒跪地求饶</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月19日讯】慵懒的午后，花<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>还有<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%BB%E4%BA%BA.md#1">主人</a>在庭院享受徐徐微风，<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>老大瘫软在木桌上，而黄绿色小鹦鹉则享受着<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%BB%E4%BA%BA.md#1">主人</a>温柔的<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%8C%87%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">手指按摩</a>。</p>
<p>看见主人食指不停抚摸<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>下巴，激起一旁花猫老大的好奇心，“很痒是吧，我来帮你饶饶”，花猫伸出小肉手，轻拍鹦鹉头毛。“这鸟头，还不错嘛”，殊不知，花猫只轻轻拍3、4下，就露出调皮本性，越拍越大力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10459597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-06_115117.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10459597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2018-06-06_115117-600x512.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="512" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10459597" class="wp-caption-text">黄绿色小鹦鹉则享受着主人温柔的<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E6%8C%87%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">手指按摩</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然鹦鹉装镇定看着远方，小小心灵早已吓出一身冷汗，“大哥啊，你有必要这样吗？”，惶恐失措的鹦鹉看着主人求救。</p>
<p>“这样还痒吗？还痒吗？”花猫朝着鹦鹉的头用力“巴”下去，天啊，这根本要谋杀吧！！鹦鹉露出惊恐眼神，整个头都要被打到桌面，鸟尾巴翘到都要飞天了，“别玩我呀大哥，放过我吧！”看来这只鹦鹉是被当作<ahref="https://github.com/crzlhp3279/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E5%8C%85.md#1">沙包</a>玩弄了！（汗）</p>
