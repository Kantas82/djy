<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“武则天”翻糖蛋糕庆生 陈美凤又惊又喜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290540572122-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《大时代》首映会6月29日在台北举行。图为陈美凤。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）“台湾最美的欧巴桑”<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E7%BE%8E%E5%87%A4.md#1">陈美凤</a>29日出席最新八点大戏《<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">大时代</a>》试片会，她恰逢7月1日生日，电视台集结所有演员为她庆生，端出“武则天”翻糖蛋糕，让陈美凤又惊又喜，许愿希望新戏收视长红。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524452" style="width: 334px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290541062122.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290541062122.jpg" alt="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" title="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524452" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E7%BE%8E%E5%87%A4.md#1">陈美凤</a>恰逢7月1日生日。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10524656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524656" style="width: 560px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-005.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/20180629-005.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="824" class="size-full wp-image-10524656" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524656" class="wp-caption-text">收到“武则天”翻糖蛋糕，让陈美凤又惊又喜。（脸书影片截图／大纪元合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男女主角<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%9F%E7%A5%96%E5%B9%B3.md#1">江祖平</a>、江俊翰、Gino、邱琦雯双生双旦偕老中青三代演员盛装出席。许久不见的江祖平将养女的角色发挥淋漓尽致，一场要跟养父磕头求情的戏，竟磕破额头，让工作人员感到心疼，但她直说：“为了戏好，我愿意！”</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%9F%E7%A5%96%E5%B9%B3.md#1">江祖平</a>跟游安顺的父女感情戏十分催泪，她说这个剧本“好哭”，过去一个月都很悲伤，每天心情都不开心，游安顺听了笑说：“我有这么苦吗？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524457" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290542272122.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290542272122.jpg" alt="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" title="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524457" class="wp-caption-text">江祖平在新戏中将养女的角色发挥淋漓尽致。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从演员定装到现在已经3个多月，制作人王珮华笑说演员群都很配合，“减重令”一下每个人都消了一大圈，连工作人员也开始跟着吃过水便当、不喝含糖饮料，江俊翰更自爆自己到现在已瘦了10公斤，连爱犬艾蜜莉也瘦了3公斤。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C.md#1">小甜甜</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%8F%AF%E6%98%80.md#1">张可昀</a>说这次的角色非常难，很苦命却要兼顾幽默感，希望鼓励胖胖的女生或不完美的女生勇敢一点。她在剧中常有下跪戏，希望能呈现出戏剧张力让观众看到，她自己则常常忘记伤口疼痛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290542162122.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290542162122-600x381.jpg" alt="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" title="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524472" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">大时代</a>》首映会6月29日在台北举行。图为<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C.md#1">小甜甜</a>、邱琦雯、江祖平。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《大时代》第一部曲由陈美凤演绎的“武则天”揭开序幕，江祖平、邱琦雯、张柏舟、游安顺、郭子乾、王彩桦、马力欧、江俊翰、Gino、吴皓昇、杨绣惠、<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E5%8F%AF%E6%98%80.md#1">张可昀</a>、马念先、陈妍安、张?心、蔡佳麟、乔华国、王希华和洪瑞霞等演员登场，该剧7月3日接档《幸福来了》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10524462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10524462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290540512122.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10524462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806290540512122-600x378.jpg" alt="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" title="民视最新八点大戏“大时代”首映会" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10524462" class="wp-caption-text">《大时代》首映会6月29日在台北举行，众演员现场亮相。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
