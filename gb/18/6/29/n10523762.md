<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10615386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月09日讯】近年<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>文化的盛行，在韩剧中时常能看见拥有缤纷色彩的韩国料理，其中韩国宫廷剧中，繁复的韩式<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%AB%E5%BB%B7%E5%BE%A1%E8%86%B3.md#1">宫廷御膳</a>料理，有别于常见的韩式料理，不仅丰富而且还很精致，更暗藏各种养生健康的小秘方。</p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-db8414c8-cd89-4e44-410c-aa86dc868b8e" data-id="player-db8414c8-cd89-4e44-410c-aa86dc868b8e"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/db8414c8-cd89-4e44-410c-aa86dc868b8e?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F29%2Fn10523762.md#1"></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>宫廷料理自“李式王朝”时代奠定根基，料理依据五行配置，讲究“红、绿、白、黑、黄”五色食材，以及“咸、甜、酸、苦、辣”五味俱全，以东方“医食同源”的概念是烹饪菜肴。人们相信五色是对应于宇宙，因为这些颜色，纯粹而不相混，对人体有益。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10523835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10523835" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1a2c73619830c949e3bcc073c5e62257.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10523835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1a2c73619830c949e3bcc073c5e62257-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10523835" class="wp-caption-text">繁复的韩式<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%AB%E5%BB%B7%E5%BE%A1%E8%86%B3.md#1">宫廷御膳</a>料理，有别于常见的韩式料理，不仅丰富而且还很精致，更暗藏各种养生健康的小秘方。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10523834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10523834" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/300f90f384d306d20fc8d5d72ab5a853.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10523834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/300f90f384d306d20fc8d5d72ab5a853-450x390.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10523834" class="wp-caption-text">韩国宫廷料理自王朝时代奠定根基，料理依据五行配置，以东方“医食同源”的概念烹调菜肴。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩国宫廷料理经典菜肴——<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%9D%E8%8A%82%E5%9D%82.md#1">九节坂</a>（韩语：???），料理的名称是从三个汉字组成；韩国宫廷宴会或重要节日庆典时才会品尝到，在韩国习俗中其有“和谐”的涵义。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%9D%E8%8A%82%E5%9D%82.md#1">九节坂</a>在韩国现代演变，成为韩国过年或婚礼宴席菜肴，居多已省略为薄饼搭配6种食材，称为七节坂。</p>
<p>九节坂使用一款特别的木制拼盘，中间一格放置薄饼，周围8格环绕多色配料，按照五味五色放置，如蛋丝、红萝卜丝、炒牛肉丝、木耳丝、黄瓜丝、豆芽、香菇丝等，口味以清淡为主。取一片薄饼，将8种食材包裹起来，沾点酱油、芥末或辣椒酱食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10523837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10523837" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8dd2f5df1e2615e0da359d6c2915977d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10523837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8dd2f5df1e2615e0da359d6c2915977d-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10523837" class="wp-caption-text">韩国宫廷料理经典菜肴——九节坂（韩语：???），在韩国习俗中其有“和谐”的涵义。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一道美味的韩式宫廷菜肴——<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E4%BB%99%E7%82%89.md#1">神仙炉</a>（韩语：???），使用木炭火锅烹制，将萝卜煮熟后取出切成细丝，用佐料拌好放在火锅的最下面，以蔬菜为基础，再依序添加牛肉、虾、银杏、牛蒡、鲍鱼等，多达25种食材炖煮而成，一道精致火锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10523850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10523850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/82da927cd02e59efd8b253e8b23978de.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10523850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/82da927cd02e59efd8b253e8b23978de-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10523850" class="wp-caption-text">韩式宫廷菜肴——<ahref="https://github.com/mtbdtp3421/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E4%BB%99%E7%82%89.md#1">神仙炉</a>，使用木炭火锅烹制，以蔬菜为基础，并由近25种食材炖煮而成，一道精致火锅。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
