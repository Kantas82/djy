<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>八哥犬听见主人说“我爱你” 反应爆笑</h2>
<a href="https://d1ntfewljph5k2.cloudfront.net/S2ePRRgKg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月14日讯】一张皱皱扁平的脸，两颗大眼珠，就算不哭不笑不闹安静的坐着，<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%93%A5%E7%8A%AC.md#1">八哥犬</a>也能给人一种呆萌的感觉。</p>
<p>影片中的<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E5%93%A5%E7%8A%AC.md#1">八哥犬</a>圆睁着两只水汪汪大眼睛，萌萌地看着镜头，当主人说“<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%91%E7%88%B1%E4%BD%A0.md#1">我爱你</a>（I love you）”，它竟表情超认真，用汪模汪样的腔调跟着说：“Ｉ love you.（拗凹嗷唷呜）”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10479905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10479905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1bc8e290d373ab6b4d61b2dce2bb475e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10479905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1bc8e290d373ab6b4d61b2dce2bb475e-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10479905" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗一副萌样。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人又说了一次，小八哥依然用“狗语”重复一遍，等到第三遍，更精彩的来了！主人加进更多情感地说：“I love you！”</p>

<p>狗狗“心思敏锐”，感受到主人的这份“浓情蜜意”，马上“<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%B0%E5%A4%A9%E9%95%BF%E5%95%B8.md#1">仰天长啸</a>”，噢呜呜～～！！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10479933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10479933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/fullsizeoutput_80.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10479933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/fullsizeoutput_80-600x425.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10479933" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗深受感动，<ahref="https://github.com/cmkuvs3974/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%B0%E5%A4%A9%E9%95%BF%E5%95%B8.md#1">仰天长啸</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这狗狗对主人的纯真情感，不仅笑翻网友们，也让网友表示：“我也想拥有这只八哥仔！”</p>
<p>或许有人会觉得八哥犬看起来很“面目狰狞”或很“忧郁”，但其实它很友善，拥有一颗“宝宝心”。</p>
