<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>指尖陀螺魅力太强大 狗狗超兴奋无法抵挡</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月18日讯】喔喔喔喔！这是什么东东？风靡大小朋友的“<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%87%E5%B0%96%E9%99%80%E8%9E%BA.md#1">指尖陀螺</a>”在地毯上原地<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%8B%E8%BD%AC.md#1">旋转</a>，看得柴犬晕头转向，阿柴甚至为了近距离了解这个不明物，把头压得低低的，超级专注，看得都快斗鸡眼了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474535" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-75.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-75-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柴犬看着地毯上<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%8B%E8%BD%AC.md#1">旋转</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%87%E5%B0%96%E9%99%80%E8%9E%BA.md#1">指尖陀螺</a><ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B4%E5%A5%8B.md#1">兴奋</a>得不得了。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10474536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-70.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-70-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿柴只要盯着<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%80%E8%9E%BA.md#1">陀螺</a>一段固定时间后，就会间歇性突然狂吠几声！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%80%E8%9E%BA.md#1">陀螺</a>不停飞速旋转，阿柴大概从来没看过这类神秘的小东西，尾巴也不禁跟着陀螺起舞，<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B4%E5%A5%8B.md#1">兴奋</a>地摇摇摇！而且，不知怎的，阿柴只要盯着陀螺一段固定时间后，就会间歇性突然狂吠几声！然后又恢复斗鸡眼……呃，专注的眼神，继续观察。</p>
<p>只是，一直原地转圈的陀螺其实满无聊的，阿柴看久了也有点爱困，但当主人让陀螺静止，重启加速后，阿柴居然也被重新点燃兴致，甚至祭出新招应对！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-64.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-64-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿柴为了近距离观察，把头压得低低的，超级专注，看得都快斗鸡眼了！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>它歪着头，两只前脚不停抓……觉得<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗的逻辑很难理解吗？错！请仔细看一下慢动作……阿柴是在“隔空”帮陀螺加速啦！超有才的！</p>
<p>一开始的狂吠就没什么道理了，应该纯粹是太兴奋了吧？而且阿柴真的很纠结，面对“不明物”既想靠近又怕受伤害，明明想加入“快转”行列，又不敢真的去碰到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-61.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-61-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">它歪着头，两只前脚不停抓在干嘛？是在“隔空”帮陀螺加速啦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当陀螺旋转越来越慢、快要停下来时，阿柴也会用鼻子“隔空”顶一顶，还有用脚“隔空”推一推，它真心不希望陀螺停下来啊！看<ahref="https://github.com/lyawwr377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗这么替陀螺紧张的模样，真不知该说它很天真还是很可爱？</p>
