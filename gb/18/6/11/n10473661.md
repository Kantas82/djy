<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月11日讯】大家对于<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%A4%B4%E8%89%BA%E4%BA%BA.md#1">街头艺人</a>印象是什么呢？扮演铜像、表演魔术、表演乐器、唱歌、跳舞、溜溜球、扯铃&#8230;&#8230;在众多的街头表演中，各位应该没看过真的会“变形”的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%98%E5%BD%A2%E9%87%91%E5%88%9A.md#1">变形金刚</a>吧！</p>
<p>《<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%98%E5%BD%A2%E9%87%91%E5%88%9A.md#1">变形金刚</a>》中<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BB%84%E8%9C%82.md#1">大黄蜂</a>这个角色大家应当不陌生，这个令许多小朋友甚至大朋友都觉得很帅的机器人，如果出现在现实中，人们会有什么反应呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10476597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10476597" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/67983c8ee0f36c816a2692e285d0e817.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10476597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/67983c8ee0f36c816a2692e285d0e817-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10476597" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%BB%84%E8%9C%82.md#1">大黄蜂</a>帅气与小朋友击掌。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美国密西根州安娜堡市有个<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%A4%B4%E8%89%BA%E4%BA%BA.md#1">街头艺人</a>名叫洁斯琳?佩琪（Joslyn Alana Paige），自制可以给人穿的大黄蜂装，不仅外观上与大黄蜂相似，还能“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%98%E8%BA%AB.md#1">变身</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">跑车</a>。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版&#8230;</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-61fae065-7de9-4f5c-515b-43310dd34c5a" data-id="player-61fae065-7de9-4f5c-515b-43310dd34c5a"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/61fae065-7de9-4f5c-515b-43310dd34c5a?r=16x9&amp;s=720x620&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F11%2Fn10473661.md#1"></a>
<p>影片中的街头艺人原本呈现一个站立的帅气机器人样，跟小朋友酷酷击掌后，立即变成一台<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">跑车</a>，四轮着地，重点是还会发出引擎声向前行进！吸引了路人目光，尤其小朋友看了更是开心到不行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10476584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10476584" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/afd8f7ee9fd171583e57ca89713507ee.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10476584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/afd8f7ee9fd171583e57ca89713507ee-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10476584" class="wp-caption-text">大黄蜂要<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%98%E8%BA%AB.md#1">变身</a>跑车啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实这位街头艺人从2011年就开始常在脸书上分享“变身”表演心得，一直到有路人在2014年5月拍下这段有趣的影片，并上传到YouTube，才让他声名大噪。</p>
