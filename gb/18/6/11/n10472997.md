<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>她用锤子和刀撬开活贝蚌 做出一串珍珠手链</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月25日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%A4%E8%9C%8A.md#1">蛤蜊</a>（贝蚌）壳内藏有<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md#1">珍珠</a>的事时有发生，但“<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%9A%8C%E5%8F%96%E7%8F%A0.md#1">开蚌取珠</a>”后，立即制作出一串<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md#1">珍珠</a>项链或手链的事却不多见。这段视频就让你亲眼见上一回。</p>
<p>一个售卖<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%A4%E8%9C%8A.md#1">蛤蜊</a>的货摊，招牌上用中文写着“现取珍珠”，摊主不仅承诺“<ahref="https://github.com/ryiabx3480/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%9A%8C%E5%8F%96%E7%8F%A0.md#1">开蚌取珠</a>”，还会把取出的珍珠串成一串项链或手链，引发消费者关注。</p>
<p>一名说外语的客人选中了一个大大的蛤蜊后，女摊主先是用锤子对着蛤蜊敲打，将紧闭的蚌壳打得松动。然后拿起一把刀嵌入蚌壳的缝内，再接着用锤子把刀敲入蚌壳内，借用锤子的力量，将蚌壳撬开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10473030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10473030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture-10-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女摊主先是用锤子对着蛤蜊敲打，将紧闭的蚌壳打得松动。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-672e1186-60cc-4519-6a23-3a64a9d630c2" data-id="player-672e1186-60cc-4519-6a23-3a64a9d630c2"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/672e1186-60cc-4519-6a23-3a64a9d630c2?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F11%2Fn10472997.md#1"></a>
<p>这时，蚌壳内的颗颗珍珠呈现在观众眼前。女摊主将珍珠取出放入水中，清洗干净后，再将珍珠放在一块布上。乳白色的珍珠看起来很漂亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10473031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10473031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture1-4-600x378.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蚌壳内的颗颗珍珠呈现在观众眼前。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>摊主开始下一步工序：摊位上有一个打磨机械，女摊主将每颗珍珠进行打磨后，交给男摊主制成一条珍珠手链。</p>
<p>最后，消费者举着现场“开蚌取珠”做成的手链和剩下的两颗珍珠，在镜头前进行讲解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10473032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture2-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10473032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Capture2-1-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">消费者举着现场“开蚌取珠”做成的手链。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，“开蚌取珠”也并非次次打开有奖，有时开出一串好珍珠，有时空欢喜一场，不过，很多人还是喜欢这种消费方式。</p>
<p>另一方面，珍珠虽美，但其形成的背后却有辛酸。珍珠的形成是由于沙粒或异物进到蚌壳内，为了将它排出，贝蚌会不断分泌一种特殊的液体将异物层层包裹，经年累月才慢慢生成一颗珍珠，因此珍珠也被看作是贝蚌的眼泪，是贝蚌痛苦与努力才结成的珍贵产物。</p>
