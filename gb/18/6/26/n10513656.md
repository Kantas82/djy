<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>畅游台18 悠游云端乐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/87f10f5c91a1223af1e024c4d8522c9e-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台18线公共空间营造-公兴-松山制茶。（阿管处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）来感受台18沿线三村幸福满满的旅游魅力，交通部观光局<ahref="https://github.com/ytrikp332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>国家风景区管理处为推动台18线周边（触口、公兴、公田）地区<ahref="https://github.com/ytrikp332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%82%E5%85%89%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8.md#1">观光旅游</a>发展，壮大<ahref="https://github.com/ytrikp332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>地区整体观光旅游，连续两年协助台18阿里山公路沿线的特色店家，建构区域观光旅游动线，并整合与营造观光氛围，打造出具在地风格与特色的旅游内涵，让游客得以深度感受、体验大阿里山台18沿线的茶区氛围与独特风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f23aed91f5086c68279dc490bfe8b385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f23aed91f5086c68279dc490bfe8b385-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">公兴松山制茶举办美食party。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了展现本计划执行成果与强化行销台18周边旅游魅力，于107年6月28日（四）下午4时整，在阿里山管理处环境教育中心举办“畅游台18、悠游云端乐”台18云端乐园成果发表推介会，成果发表会现场将邀请本计划参与的特色店家，透过将本年度空间氛围改造、特色商品开发、形象影片……等成果，以图文方式呈现，并搭配各店家特色旅游相关商品，展现本年度计划推动成果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513661" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/70664ae0a739deab3dd0fd34c5d9bb78.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/70664ae0a739deab3dd0fd34c5d9bb78-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台18线公共空间营造-隙顶-古早味餐厅。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10513662" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3900d1c33d1118f7c75b163afc621410.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3900d1c33d1118f7c75b163afc621410-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台18线公共空间营造-隙顶-茗达制茶。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了静态成果展现之外，更邀请“木人艸－农食美学”团队，透过派对的方式，搭配餐饮美学布置，呈现台18与阿里山地区多元文化风貌，打造具视觉与味觉的美食飨宴，藉以推广台18特色旅游与观光氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513664" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/21aad9b4fb6991f465f4a340f61c3700.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/21aad9b4fb6991f465f4a340f61c3700-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台18线公共空间营造-隙顶-旭隆制茶。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10513663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/487249f525a5876ee820d292517613f1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/487249f525a5876ee820d292517613f1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台18线公共空间营造-隙顶-春馨制茶。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>成果发表会以“畅游台18、悠游云端乐”为口号，呈现台18沿线触口、公兴、公田三村“可食-茶餐、茶食、在地蔬果；可看-观云海、观日出、观夕阳、观日落；可体验-茶染、柿染、采茶、制茶、泡茶、咖啡冲泡”的丰富观光资源，让每位游客皆能感受专属台18沿线的独特魅力、体验触口、公田、公兴等地区的观光产业与文化感动。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513666" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/64eaf585812303d9ee302a5563ebc9ea.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10513666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/64eaf585812303d9ee302a5563ebc9ea-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台18线公共空间营造-触口村-好柿抵嘉。（阿管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
