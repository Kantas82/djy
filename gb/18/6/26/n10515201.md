<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猎豹全速追猎黑斑羚 羚羊纵身一跃 真聪明！</h2>
<a href="https://dkx26vh7i5y84.cloudfront.net/F3uDrgyFa"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sxvshi319/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/sxvshi319/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月27日讯】在非洲一个野生动物园，两只饥饿的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E8%B1%B9.md#1">猎豹</a>追逐<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%96%91%E7%BE%9A.md#1">黑斑羚</a>（Impala，又称高角羚），希望为自己挣到一份美餐。<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%9A%E7%BE%8A.md#1">羚羊</a>为了活命，做出一个意想不到的举动，震惊了动物园的游客。</p>
<p>这些令人难以置信的镜头是在南非克鲁格国家公园（Kruger National Park）意外拍摄到的，该公园是狮子、豹子、大象、水牛和犀牛五大类动物聚集地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10515214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10515214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-02-15-at-7.21.23-AM.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-02-15-at-7.21.23-AM-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10515214" class="wp-caption-text">在非洲一家野生动物园，被饥饿的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E8%B1%B9.md#1">猎豹</a>追逐的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%96%91%E7%BE%9A.md#1">黑斑羚</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一天，一群游客正在公园巡游，他们从来没有想到会亲眼看到如此令人兴奋的罕见场景。</p>
<p>游客们坐在汽车上游览，突然，一群<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%9A%E7%BE%8A.md#1">羚羊</a>飞奔而来，穿过柏油马路冲进旁边的树林，猎豹在后面紧紧追逐。游客们纷纷停车拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10515215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10515215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-02-15-at-7.21.46-AM.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-02-15-at-7.21.46-AM-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10515215" class="wp-caption-text">在非洲一家野生动物园，两只饥饿的猎豹追逐黑斑羚。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，一只羚羊再次蹿出树林，跑到马路上，一只猎豹在后面紧追，另一只猎豹则在前面围堵。这只惊恐的黑斑羚在被追杀的紧急时刻，为了活命，大胆地做出一个决定。</p>
<p>但见黑斑羚纵身一跃，跳入一辆车内，令剧情逆转。看到这个画面，会让你的心脏怦怦跳，游客们纷纷抽气惊呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10515219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10515219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/vid-n136a-imapala-cheetahs.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/vid-n136a-imapala-cheetahs-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10515219" class="wp-caption-text">黑斑羚纵身一跃，跳入一辆车内。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑斑羚行动敏捷，奔跑迅速，栖息在非洲南部和中部的森林和草原之中，以其优雅的姿势和杰出的跳跃能力而出名。受惊的时候，它们可以跳起3米高、9米远。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10515211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10515211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/cheetah-chases-impala.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10515211 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/cheetah-chases-impala-600x323.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10515211" class="wp-caption-text">没抓到猎物的猎豹。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而猎豹是奔跑速度最快的陆地动物，有能力在3秒内从静止不动加速到时速100公里（62英里/小时）。</p>
<p>所以面对猎豹紧追不放，黑斑羚除了求救游客的车，看不出有什么别的出路。此举很聪明，也是情理之中的举动。</p>
