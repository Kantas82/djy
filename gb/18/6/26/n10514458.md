<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>见过“生日蛋糕形”龙卷风吗 中间还会打雷</h2>
<a href="https://d265ujbtchne9r.cloudfront.net/X0rFzYanm"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fngikk303/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/fngikk303/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月29日讯】各位应当多少都有看过<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>的模样，即使没亲眼见过，也会在电视或网路上看到。</p>
<p>这玩意儿会不断旋转不断旋转，像是个空中漩涡，会把各种各样的东西卷向天，是人见人怕的天象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10519576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10519576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/27247195301_883ae32e57_o.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10519576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/27247195301_883ae32e57_o-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10519576" class="wp-caption-text">许多人看到的都是“笋状”<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>。<ahref="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fireboatks/27247195301" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Lane Pearman/Flickr</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过各位常看到的龙卷风，大多是像上图这“笋形”，或“比萨斜塔形”，应该没有看过“圆柱蛋糕形”的龙卷风吧？！</p>
<p>龙卷风直径平均200~300公尺，直径最小不过几十公尺，只有极少数直径达到1000公尺以上，不同直径的龙卷风整体形状也就差很多。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版&#8230;</strong></span></p>

<p>影片中记录的是发生在美国德州达拉斯附近的一场罕见龙卷风。奇特的是，龙卷风就长得像“圆柱蛋糕状”，伴着黑黑的云气，慢慢地转呀转，偶而还会“<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E9%9B%B7.md#1">打雷</a>”。哇呜，太恐怖了吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10519610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10519610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d1e9f8c39b5c2bb61b9fe8e8ded3a32e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10519610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d1e9f8c39b5c2bb61b9fe8e8ded3a32e-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10519610" class="wp-caption-text">超大龙卷风，还会<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E9%9B%B7.md#1">打雷</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友看后纷纷回应：“诸恶莫作，众善奉行。”“超级恐怖！”“<ahref="https://github.com/fngikk303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%8E%E8%B5%B7%E4%BA%91%E6%B6%8C.md#1">风起云涌</a>，天地变色。”“好似内有天空之城！”“风云变色让人看了不寒而栗！”“喔！不得了，快逃。”</p>
