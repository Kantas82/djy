<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李优喝多多绿挨瞪 戒糖保身材</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月26日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）有“电眼潮模”封号的歌手<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BC%98.md#1">李优</a>，现身<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B4%E6%92%AD.md#1">直播</a>节目《<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E8%A1%8C%E7%BF%8E%E8%B7%9D%E7%A6%BB.md#1">流行翎距离</a>》，为了维持162公分，43公斤的好身材，经纪人只要看到她喝有糖的饮料，都会狠瞪她的手，连她最爱的饮料“多多绿”，都规定一天只能喝一杯，而且必须是微糖的。</p>
<p>发片前没有忌口令时，<ahref="https://github.com/iqfoxa377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BC%98.md#1">李优</a>笑说自己喝手摇饮糖度都选“全糖”，而且只要碰到她的爱店，都直接先买两杯回家储存。</p>
<p>经纪人从她赴日拍照前就开始把关，警告她日本订制的宣传服剪裁很合身，只要胖一点就可能穿不下，她才乖乖戒含糖饮料，最后底线就是一天一杯多多绿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10513035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e68b8ffd8fb5302cc4c462eda16a86c3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10513035 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e68b8ffd8fb5302cc4c462eda16a86c3-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10513035" class="wp-caption-text">李优维持162公分，43公斤的好身材。 (17Media)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10513034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10513034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/267173657180534480ecec3d23448704.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10513034 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/267173657180534480ecec3d23448704-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10513034" class="wp-caption-text">有“电眼潮模”封号的歌手李优。 (17Media)</figcaption></figure>
<p>看李优食欲极佳，现场早午餐的炒洋菇，水果优格接连大口吃，主持人解婕翎忍不住问：“李优你老实说，你们公司是不是苦毒你，都不让你吃东西？”李优笑答：“因为宣传期都要一直维持体重”。</p>
<p>李优身兼画家，演员，网红，潮模，歌手等多种身份，网路拥有大批粉丝。一吃大家直呼：“迷惘美连吃东西也好空灵喔，原来网美也会吃东西，她吃每样东西姿势都优雅得像一只猫耶！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10513036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10513036" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/fb35cc9fccf63f2a0a08ec5c58b81b93.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10513036 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/fb35cc9fccf63f2a0a08ec5c58b81b93-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10513036" class="wp-caption-text">李优一天只能喝一杯饮料，而且必须是微糖的。 (17Media)</figcaption></figure>
