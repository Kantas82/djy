<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卡车居然会喷火？美国赛车场上出现惊人景象</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10445614 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月28日讯】尼尔达内尔（Neal Darnell）是一名美国职业<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%9B%E8%BD%A6.md#1">赛车</a>手，他在职业生涯中参与了各种类型的赛车运动，期间一直对<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E6%9C%BA.md#1">飞机</a>的喷射引擎感到有兴趣，有天他突然想到，为何不能将一个军用<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%98%E6%96%97%E6%9C%BA.md#1">战斗机</a>的引擎安装在<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E8%BD%A6.md#1">卡车</a>上？于是这台造价不菲的卡车诞生了。</p>
<p>这台超级改装<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E8%BD%A6.md#1">卡车</a>“SHOCKWAVE”不管是外观、还是性能，都很吸睛，卡车上被装置了三台喷射<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E5%8A%A8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">发动机</a>，每台发动机都能提供1200马力，最快的速度居然可超过600Km/h！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-170.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-170-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这台超级改装卡车“SHOCKWAVE”不管是外观还是性能，都很吸睛！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10501276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-127.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-127-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卡车上被装置了三台喷射<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E5%8A%A8%E6%9C%BA.md#1">发动机</a>，每台发动机都能提供1200马力。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在一场展示会上，观众们都希望能见识到这台超级卡车的威力，为了应对这台超级喷射卡车的强大火力，工作人员事先在路面上洒了许多水，对路面做保护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501275" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-140.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-140-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">车子一发动，这台超级改装卡车立即喷射出巨大的火焰。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当车子一发动，这台超级改装卡车立即喷射出巨大的火焰，引擎甚至产生有如<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E6%9C%BA.md#1">飞机</a>起飞的声响，看到这震撼的画面，民众们热血沸腾、各个叫好。</p>
<p>说时迟那时快，在三台喷射引擎火焰的极度高温烘烤下，强大的火力融化了柏油路上的沥青，更震惊的是，马路还宛如纸片一样，被吹得四处散落！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-107.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-107-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">看到这震撼的画面，民众们热血沸腾、各个叫好。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居然连本身就是由高温制成的柏油路都会被吹翻啊！很难想像真正的温度到底有多高，连驾驶者事后采访都表示，在驾驶座上都感觉自己被炙热所烘烤！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-114.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-114-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">强大的火力融化了柏油路上的沥青。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友们纷纷回应：“这太惊人了！”、“这是我见过的最酷的事情之一！”、“不敢相信这样的车真的存在。”</p>
