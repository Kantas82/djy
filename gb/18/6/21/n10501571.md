<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>真正的“龙吸水”！惊人巨型水龙卷蔚为奇观</h2>
<a href="https://dycmgeziwp90b.cloudfront.net/T1gM8436o"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ukkmvh324/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月21日讯】这里是美国路易斯安那州的格兰德艾尔海岸，当地居民发现一根<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E5%A4%A7.md#1">巨大</a>的白色绳子从天而降—— 是“<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7.md#1">水龙卷</a>”！虽然水龙卷在这个地方并不算<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%95%E8%A7%81.md#1">罕见</a>，然而很少看到如此大规模的，还是令人啧啧称奇。</p>
<p>与地面上的<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>类似，<ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7.md#1">水龙卷</a>的形成是由于大气不稳定所造成的。水龙卷通常出现在温暖水面的上空，或上下对流旺盛的地方，例如积雨云区或锋面的边缘。其上端与雷雨云相接，下端直接延伸到水面，一边旋转，一边移动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10501576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10501576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-21-600x403.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10501576" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ukkmvh324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A8%E5%A4%A7.md#1">巨大</a>的水龙卷。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水龙卷通常是垂直向下的，其移动路径一般为直线，但有时因上空的风比地面的风大，它的上部会顺气流方向倾斜，就可以根据其倾斜方向判断出移动路径，采取避难措施。水龙卷经常在春夏及夏秋季节交替时出现，但都持续不久，通常几分钟内就会消失。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10501581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10501581" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10501581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-12-450x501.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10501581" class="wp-caption-text">当地居民也啧啧称奇。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若是较大型的水龙卷，有时还会将海里的鱼吸到空中，在其他地方下起“鱼雨”呢！</p>
