<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猫咪向胖鸽伸出爪 鸽子的回应让人大跌眼镜</h2>
<a href="https://d2sn4gemyo21al.cloudfront.net/P2f6RtHCw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thjshy393/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/thjshy393/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10466034 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-5-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月11日讯】猫和鸟应该属于彼此都不太喜欢对方的动物，有些<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>还会捕猎鸟类。但视频中的猫咪和<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">鸽子</a>却是一个例外。两个风马牛不相及的动物黏在一起，又啃又啄，亲密无间。</p>
<p>捕猎是猫的天性，一般的<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>有机会的话都会抓小动物。但视频中的这只猫咪任由<ahref="https://github.com/thjshy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">鸽子</a>在身上又啃又啄，一副对鸽子宠溺的样子。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/thumbnail_e-1-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10473425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/thumbnail_e-1-1.jpg" alt="" width="426" b="478" /></a></p>
<p>视频显示，猫咪挥爪和鸽子开玩笑，被鸽子霸气地推倒在地，双方打闹在一起。鸽子在猫咪头颈部位啄来啄去，猫咪被折腾得有些不耐烦，推开鸽子站起身，但很快又被鸽子再次扑倒在地。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-b9ee02ad-7b42-4a70-681c-9904c5431533" data-id="player-b9ee02ad-7b42-4a70-681c-9904c5431533"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/b9ee02ad-7b42-4a70-681c-9904c5431533?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F8%2Fn10466009.md#1"></a>
<p>鸽子太喜欢猫咪软软的身子，黏在猫咪身上不肯离开。这只猫咪脾气超好，看似很喜欢这只鸽子，最多用爪子推它一把，或挠它一下，大多数时间是躺在地上，任由鸽子对自己进行“虐待”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10466026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10466026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-05-25-at-12.28.43-PM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10466026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-05-25-at-12.28.43-PM-600x300.png" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10466026" class="wp-caption-text">猫咪大多数时间是躺在地上，任由鸽子对自己进行“虐待”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猫咪对鸽子最厉害的一次，也就是一把推开鸽子，希望摆脱鸽子纠缠离开现场，没想到鸽子紧追不舍，很快又被鸽子扑在地上“啃咬”。</p>
<p>看到猫咪和鸽子亲密相处的景象，你有何感想呢？是被它们的友谊感动，还是觉得猫咪太老实，被鸽子欺负？</p>
<p>有网友表示，这真是一只心胸大度的猫咪！它和鸽子一定是前世缘分不浅。</p>
