<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月15日讯】如果你辛辛苦苦种了果树，有动物来<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E5%90%83.md#1">偷吃</a>，你会做什么事？！</p>
<p>挪威有只麋<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%BF.md#1">鹿</a>，这天忽然闯入住户索尔海姆（Unni Solheim）的家，它到底来做什么的呢？观光？不是&#8230;&#8230;是光天化日<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E5%90%83.md#1">偷吃</a><ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">苹果</a>！还吃得非常悠哉。</p>
<p>这户人家有个小型<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%BA.md#1">除草机</a>，这时主人应该是很<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E7%88%BD.md#1">不爽</a>，辛辛苦苦种的<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">苹果</a>竟然被偷吃，就拿这台除草机要吓跑麋<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%BF.md#1">鹿</a>。一开始，他先“路过”它。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483070" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dd637a8973c025a01957ab935bd53306.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dd637a8973c025a01957ab935bd53306-600x386.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%BA.md#1">除草机</a>都来了，麋鹿还大胆继续偷吃果子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没想到麋鹿一点都没害怕，完全不把这“小车”放在眼里，主人这时更<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E7%88%BD.md#1">不爽</a>了，碎念几句话后，让除草机直接往麋鹿的方向冲。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版&#8230;</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-ee2b5c74-0189-4cc0-4d56-8c0bc230f132" data-id="player-ee2b5c74-0189-4cc0-4d56-8c0bc230f132"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/ee2b5c74-0189-4cc0-4d56-8c0bc230f132?r=16x9&amp;s=720x640&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F13%2Fn10480451.md#1"></a>
<p>吃美食被打断，这时麋鹿也不爽了！就直接把自己的脚伸到无比高的地方，硬邦邦的蹄就这样直接往除草机上瞬间踏下去！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483073" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4e3bba50c3c043a896106288a9e93b61.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10483073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4e3bba50c3c043a896106288a9e93b61-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">没想到鹿脚可以伸这么高，这一脚下去除草机哭哭了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>碰！机器发出响亮的声音，还伴随着类似当机的声音：Bi～</p>
<p>有网友说，这台割草车很像瑞典公司Husqvarna制造的Automower 265 ACX型号割草机，价格约5000美元。</p>
<p>假如真的是这台，嗯&#8230;&#8230;希望这主人能记取教训，不要没事拿这么昂贵的割草机撞鹿了，草食性动物也是不好惹的！</p>
