<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>开胃超下饭 腰果缤纷炒鸡丁</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10615386 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月01日讯】百变<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E6%9E%9C.md#1">腰果</a>好处多！除了当零嘴单吃，烘焙后洒上盐或糖后，更是止不住嘴馋，让人忍不住想一口接一口。</p>
<p>说到入菜，<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E6%9E%9C.md#1">腰果</a>不仅能增添料理风味和口感，配上鲜嫩多汁的<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%81.md#1">鸡丁</a>，更是开胃超下饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10480219" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dc4fba82f377b0264053b93955569221.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10480219 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dc4fba82f377b0264053b93955569221-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">缤纷又美味的<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E6%9E%9C%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%81.md#1">腰果鸡丁</a>，让你健康放心大口吃！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>鸡胸肉 1块<br />
蒜末 少许<br />
葱末 少许<br />
姜末 少许<br />
绿花椰菜 约90克<br />
毛豆 适量<br />
甜椒 适量<br />
腰果 适量</p>
<h2>【鸡肉腌制配方】</h2>
<p>水 1/4杯<br />
盐 1/2茶匙<br />
黑胡椒 1/4茶匙<br />
木薯粉（树薯粉）2汤匙<br />
橄榄油 1/8杯</p>
<h2>【调味酱汁】</h2>
<p>酱油 1/4杯<br />
蜂蜜 1汤匙<br />
白胡椒 1/8汤匙<br />
木薯粉（树薯粉）1/2汤匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_10480301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/66e83cce9bd7312cb4f5313af24b82b5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10480301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/66e83cce9bd7312cb4f5313af24b82b5-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腰果入菜料理，不仅能增添风味和口感，配上鲜嫩低卡<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%81.md#1">鸡丁</a>，更是开胃超下饭。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先将买来的新鲜鸡胸肉稍微拍打，切成鸡丁放入容器中，加入腌料：水、盐、黑胡椒，木薯粉（树薯粉）和橄榄油，稍微拌匀，腌制10分钟。</p>
<p>利用滑嫩鸡丁吸收腌料的时间，将蒜头拍碎切成末，葱、姜切末，以及甜椒切成块状备用。</p>
<p>热锅加入橄榄油，放入蒜末和姜末爆香，接着放入切成丁状的鸡胸肉，拌炒至鸡胸肉表面略呈金黄色，加入绿花椰菜、毛豆、甜椒下锅均匀拌炒，盖上锅盖闷一下。</p>
<p>接着淋上调味酱汁：酱油、蜂蜜、白胡椒、木薯粉（树薯粉）提味，再倒入腰果、葱末，均匀翻炒到食材都煮熟，即可起锅，享用缤纷又美味的<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B0%E6%9E%9C%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%81.md#1">腰果鸡丁</a>！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10480221" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8e91eb4ef1b867fcfdf8d8b3a03eb586.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10480221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/8e91eb4ef1b867fcfdf8d8b3a03eb586-450x445.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="445" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最后，倒入腰果、葱末，均匀翻炒到食材都煮熟，即可起锅，享用缤纷又美味的腰果鸡丁！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
