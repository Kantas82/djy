<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浓浓起司香 马铃薯起司肉饼让人停不了口</h2>
<a href="https://d3siwajeg0khti.cloudfront.net/M6vf7vXdy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月12日讯】你也是<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>爱好者吗？当你咬一口<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E8%82%89%E9%A5%BC.md#1">马铃薯起司肉饼</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>的咸香再加上<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>绵密的口感，一定让你爱不释手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10456929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10456929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/6-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10456929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/6-13-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10456929" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E8%82%89%E9%A5%BC.md#1">马铃薯起司肉饼</a>上菜啰！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 3颗（中等大小）<br />
培根 5片<br />
虾夷葱末 1/3杯<br />
碎起司 1杯<br />
莫札瑞拉起司 9片</p>
<p>盐 1茶匙<br />
香蒜椒盐 1茶匙<br />
黑胡椒粉 2茶匙</p>
<p>面粉 2杯<br />
蛋 4颗<br />
起司饼干 3杯</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml；“1汤匙”=1 Tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙；“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 起司饼干捣碎、研磨。<br />
2. 培根切碎，并炒至焦香。<br />
3. 马铃薯削皮、切块，同时煮一锅水，水滚后放入马铃薯块（可加些许盐巴），盖上锅盖，煮约10～15分钟，直到软烂后捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10456923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10456923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-42.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10456923 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-42-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10456923" class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯削皮、切块，放入滚水煮到软烂后捞起。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 用纸巾擦乾马铃薯、放凉。<br />
5. 取一个大碗，将马铃薯捣成泥，加入炒过的培根、虾夷葱末、起司、盐、香蒜椒盐、黑胡椒，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10456924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10456924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-29.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10456924 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-29-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10456924" class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯捣成泥，加入培根、葱末、起司等。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. <ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%AE%B1.md#1">烤箱</a>预热至摄氏175度或华氏350度。<br />
7. 挖一大匙薯泥放在手心，捏成圆饼状，并放上一片莫札瑞拉起司，再挖一大匙薯泥，包住起司，并稍微整型。<br />
8. 取一烤盘，放上烘焙纸。<br />
9. 将包好的薯泥饼分别均匀地裹上面粉、蛋汁、起司饼干末，并放在烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10456926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10456926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-27.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10456926 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-27-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10456926" class="wp-caption-text">薯泥饼分别均匀地裹上面粉、蛋汁、起司饼干末。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10456928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10456928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-25.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10456928 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-25-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10456928" class="wp-caption-text">沾好的起司饼放到烤盘上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 放进<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E7%AE%B1.md#1">烤箱</a>，烘烤约30分钟。完成。</p>
