<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>患有焦虑症的狗狗 离开主人后竟做出这种事来 令网友吃惊</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月08日讯】在面对<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E8%99%91.md#1">焦虑</a>时，你是否也会想逃脱呢？这只<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗名叫Bandit，它因为患有严重的分离焦虑，会破坏地毯、咬电线、甚至有时候会为了寻找主人而逃脱，主人为了避免它伤害自己，把它关在厨房里，但有一天它还是逃脱了，记录器记下了整个过程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10455925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10455925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w3-600x502.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="502" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗跳上流理台后，四处张望，发现<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E6%9D%BF.md#1">木板</a>上面有个<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%9D%E9%9A%99.md#1">缝隙</a>，它闻到了外面自由的味道～（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狗狗首先是踮起脚跟，手扶着流理台，接着巧妙地跳上流理台上，站稳后，开始四处闻闻，闻到外面的味道，接着再次踮起脚跟，攀上<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E6%9D%BF.md#1">木板</a>，一看，果然有个出口耶！开始一脚踏上白色的接缝处，头开始往外伸，像是有练过<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%AF%E9%AA%A8%E5%8A%9F.md#1">软骨功</a>似的，一个劲儿的往木板上面的<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%9D%E9%9A%99.md#1">缝隙</a>钻出，脚还滑了几下，最后，咻！就逃跑到外头去了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10455938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10455938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w5-600x503.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="503" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">狗狗探头到缝隙外，全身使力，手脚并用、不停划动着，拼尽全力要从缝隙钻出！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10455947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10455947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/w6-600x501.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="501" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用不了多久，狗狗就成功从厨房逃脱出去啦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个画面令网友吃惊，在youtube上吸引了840万次的浏览，及许多留言。许多饲主都分享他们的经验，盼望狗狗的<ahref="https://github.com/xazhws3249/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E8%99%91.md#1">焦虑</a>症能获得改善！</p>
