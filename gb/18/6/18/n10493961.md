<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>非洲草原小树 竟能“长出”七只大狮子？！</h2>
<a href="https://dh48pl2d5tlkv.cloudfront.net/P0fAA2STe"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/xsumwp3307/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月22日讯】猴子、猫、狗爬树各位多少应有看过，而<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>爬树应当也看过，可是&#8230;&#8230;众狮子爬<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E6%A0%91.md#1">小树</a>各位就没看过了吧？</p>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>塔桑尼亚的格鲁美地保护区（Grumeti Reserve），一名45岁导游埃达．申波克（Adas Shemboko）与游客在旅途中，突见一幕有趣景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494677" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/477EF3AC00000578-5202021-image-a-72_1513862273800eeeee.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10494677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/477EF3AC00000578-5202021-image-a-72_1513862273800eeeee-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494677" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E6%A0%91.md#1">小树</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>还很少。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有两只狮子在小树上，可能是在上面能居高临下，更有“大王”的感觉吧！周围慢慢冒出越来越多狮子，它们都在“觊觎”这棵能享“居高临下”的小树。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看影片完整版&#8230;</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-0bd29764-ec10-4973-51ca-bb5f81d208d9" data-id="player-0bd29764-ec10-4973-51ca-bb5f81d208d9"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/0bd29764-ec10-4973-51ca-bb5f81d208d9?r=16x9&amp;s=720x620&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F18%2Fn10493961.md#1"></a>
<p>一只只慢慢地爬上树，不到90秒时间，这“观景平台”上竟从两只狮子暴增到七只！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/566q000461p982pn1721ddddd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10494679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/566q000461p982pn1721ddddd-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494679" class="wp-caption-text">小树快超载啦！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导游埃达斯说：“这是一件美妙的事，白天很热时，这种情况并不常发生，狮子很懒，白天往往会睡觉。”</p>
<p>那&#8230;&#8230;它们为什么要这么“前仆后继”的爬小树呢？</p>
<p>导游给了个解释：“在一年中的此时，狮子们为躲避苍蝇（这种苍蝇会和牛羚一起出现）爬上树是很常见的，不过在白天这很热时段做此事是有点不寻常的。”</p>
