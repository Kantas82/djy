<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马铃薯还有这种做法！起司薯片塔好吃又好玩</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10615386 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月02日讯】除了炸烤薯块以外，烤薯片也很美味，<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>薄片均匀裹上帕玛森<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>和调味料，烘烤过后，边缘的焦脆、中间的软嫩，双重享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-106.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-106-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494403" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E8%96%AF%E7%89%87%E5%A1%94.md#1">起司薯片塔</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 3颗<br />
融化的奶油 3汤匙<br />
帕玛森<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>（丝） 2汤匙<br />
盐 少许<br />
大蒜粉 1茶匙<br />
百里香叶 1茶匙<br />
黑胡椒 适量<br />
有凹槽的烤盘（或马芬烤盘）<br />
大碗公</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml<br />
“1汤匙”=1 tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙<br />
“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.烤箱预热至摄氏190度／华氏375度。<br />
2.在烤盘上刷上奶油。<br />
3.将马铃薯削皮、切成薄片。（建议使用切片器。）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-129.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-129-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494400" class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯片和奶油、帕玛森起司、百里香等调味料拌匀。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.切好的马铃薯片放入大碗公中，倒入融化的奶油、帕玛森起司、百里香、大蒜粉、盐、黑胡椒，拌匀，让马铃薯片均匀裹上。<br />
5.将马铃薯片叠起来，放入烤盘凹槽中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-115.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-115-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494401" class="wp-caption-text">将马铃薯片叠起来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.撒上少许黑胡椒、百里香。<br />
7.放入烤箱，烘烤1小时（摄氏190度／华氏375度），烤到边缘和最上面呈现金黄色，中间熟透。<br />
8.取出烤箱，静置5分钟，从烤盘中取下。<br />
9.撒上帕玛森起司和百里香点缀。<br />
10.完成。</p>
