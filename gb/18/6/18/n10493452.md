<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>优格代替鲜奶油 培根马铃薯杯杯口感更清爽</h2>
<a href="https://d2nbk5p6zab41i.cloudfront.net/H6v2LnYYp"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10586563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-12-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月24日讯】这道料理略为费工，<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>泥以优格代替鲜奶油，口感更加清爽，绝对是马铃薯爱好者的必列菜单之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493505" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-92.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-92-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493505" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E6%9D%AF%E6%9D%AF.md#1">培根马铃薯杯杯</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 1颗（尽量选又大又长）<br />
切达起司 1/2杯<br />
优格 60 ml<br />
虾夷葱末 60 ml<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a> 1～2片</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml<br />
“1汤匙”=1 tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙<br />
“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 烤箱预热至摄氏200度／华氏400度。<br />
2. 整个马铃薯用锡箔纸包起来，放入烤箱，烤30分钟。</p>
<p>3. 从烤箱取出后，再次预热至摄氏175度／华氏350度。<br />
4. 切掉马铃薯的头和尾，留中段。<br />
5. 将中段挖空（留底部），挖出来的马铃薯泥放入大碗中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493501" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-148.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-148-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493501" class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯中段挖空（留底部）。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 薯泥加入切达起司、优格、葱末（优格和葱末不用全加，留一些点缀成品），均匀搅拌。<br />
7. 将搅拌好的薯泥，填入马铃薯，封好。<br />
8. 外层裹上一条<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%B9%E6%A0%B9.md#1">培根</a>，并用牙签固定培根。<br />
9. 放入烤箱，烤30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493503" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-104.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-104-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493503" class="wp-caption-text">外层裹上一条培根。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 取出后，在马铃薯上方铺上切达起司。<br />
11. 放入烤箱，以摄氏175度／华氏350度烤15分钟。<br />
12. 取出后，在马铃薯上方淋上优格，洒上葱末。完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493504" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-95.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-95-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493504" class="wp-caption-text">在马铃薯上方铺上切达起司。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
