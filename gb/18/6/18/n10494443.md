<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蔬果甜味碰上浓郁起司 迷你焗烤小甜椒</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10615386 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-18-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月25日讯】无论是青椒、黄椒、橘椒、还是红椒，通通都可以拿来做成迷你<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%97%E7%83%A4.md#1">焗烤</a>椒。</p>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p>黄<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E6%A4%92.md#1">甜椒</a> 1颗<br />
红<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E6%A4%92.md#1">甜椒</a> 1颗<br />
橘甜椒 1颗<br />
青椒 1颗<br />
融化的奶油 3汤匙<br />
盐 1/2茶匙<br />
黑胡椒 1/2茶匙<br />
意大利式蕃茄酱 160克<br />
莫札瑞拉起司 3杯<br />
甜罗勒 5克<br />
帕玛森起司（丝） 少许<br />
迷你意大利辣香肠 1/3杯<br />
香芹末 少许</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml<br />
“1汤匙”=1 tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙<br />
“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.烤箱预热至摄氏177度／华氏350度。<br />
2.黄甜椒、红甜椒、橘甜椒、青椒剖半、挖籽、去蒂。<br />
3.罗勒切丝。<br />
4.将彩椒放在铺了调理纸的烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-160.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-160-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494496" class="wp-caption-text">彩椒放在铺了调理纸的烤盘上。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.在彩椒内刷上奶油，撒上少许盐、黑胡椒，抹上义式蕃茄酱（每个约20克）。<br />
6.放入莫札瑞拉起司片、罗勒。<br />
7.撒上帕玛森起司丝。<br />
8.铺上迷你意大利辣香肠片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10494500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10494500" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-108.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10494500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-108-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10494500" class="wp-caption-text">放入烤箱，烘烤15分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.放入烤箱，烘烤15分钟（摄氏177度／华氏350度）。<br />
10.取出烤箱，撒上香芹末。<br />
11.完成。</p>
