<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月11日讯】不管是天气热没食欲，还是天气冷想暖身，都很适合来点辣的食物，番茄酱的微酸、韩国辣椒酱与辣椒粉的辣味、蜂蜜的缓和，做一道<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E8%BE%A3%E5%91%B3%E9%B8%A1%E6%9F%B3%E7%84%97%E7%83%A4%E9%80%9A%E5%BF%83%E7%B2%89.md#1">韩式辣味鸡柳焗烤通心粉</a>，刺激一下味蕾吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-96.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-96-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493924" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E8%BE%A3%E5%91%B3%E9%B8%A1%E6%9F%B3%E7%84%97%E7%83%A4%E9%80%9A%E5%BF%83%E7%B2%89.md#1">韩式辣味鸡柳焗烤通心粉</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p>鸡里肌（鸡柳） 450公克<br />
辣椒粉 5汤匙<br />
韩国辣酱 1.5杯<br />
番茄酱 3/4杯<br />
蒜末 1.5汤匙<br />
姜末 1.5汤匙<br />
油 1汤匙<br />
黑胡椒粉 2茶匙<br />
海盐 2茶匙<br />
蜂蜜 1/2杯<br />
通心粉 200公克<br />
莫札瑞拉起司 1杯<br />
青葱末 少许</p>
<p>大碗公<br />
铸铁平底锅</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml；“1汤匙”=1 tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙；“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">观看完整影片?</span></strong><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-8ca81c4a-b06d-4c81-72ee-e0d413ace40e" data-id="player-8ca81c4a-b06d-4c81-72ee-e0d413ace40e"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/8ca81c4a-b06d-4c81-72ee-e0d413ace40e?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F18%2Fn10493904.md#1"></a></p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.先将通心粉煮熟。<br />
2.烤箱预热至摄氏200度／华氏400度。<br />
3.鸡柳切块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-152.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-152-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493918" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉、辣椒粉、韩国辣酱等调味料拌匀，腌渍20分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.取一大碗公，放入鸡肉、辣椒粉、韩国辣酱、番茄酱、蒜末、姜末、油、黑胡椒粉、海盐、蜂蜜，搅拌均匀，腌渍20分钟。<br />
5.倒入平底锅，拌炒，盖上锅盖煮20分钟。<br />
6.倒入熟的通心粉，拌炒，让通心粉稍微入味后，铺上莫札瑞拉起司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-123.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-123-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493919" class="wp-caption-text">倒入熟的通心粉，拌炒，让通心粉稍微入味。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.整锅放入烤箱，烤15分钟。<br />
8.洒上葱末，完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10493923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10493923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-100.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10493923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-100-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10493923" class="wp-caption-text">整锅放入烤箱，烤15分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
