<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纯真的好朋友 小女孩教小白犀牛学走路？</h2>
<a href="https://dfxrfb9070id6.cloudfront.net/N5sNyGrag"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pdheqb3476/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月07日讯】小朋友与小动物的互动，往往让人们感到很温馨可爱，肯亚野生动物保护区上传一段影片。</p>
<p>影片中有一名3岁小女孩，身旁跟着一只小小<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">犀牛</a>，名叫林戈（Ringo），这只犀牛可不是一般的犀牛，它是一只濒危的白犀牛。</p>
<p>在肯亚野生动物保护区约364平方公里的土地上，除了林戈以外，还有另外3头濒危白<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">犀牛</a>。</p>
<p>小女孩一开始接触林戈时，还有点害怕会闪躲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10454809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10454809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1cae2934641bb1410f82034ffe131b65.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10454809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1cae2934641bb1410f82034ffe131b65-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10454809" class="wp-caption-text">女孩与小犀牛一起<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B9%92%E8%B7%9A%E5%AD%A6%E6%AD%A5.md#1">蹒跚学步</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但没多久小女孩便感到放心，开心与这可爱的壮硕小家伙一起走在路上“<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B9%92%E8%B7%9A%E5%AD%A6%E6%AD%A5.md#1">蹒跚学步</a>”。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看影片完整版&#8230;</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f27b034f-0c7c-4ad2-5586-08654c4ce2ca" data-id="player-f27b034f-0c7c-4ad2-5586-08654c4ce2ca"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f27b034f-0c7c-4ad2-5586-08654c4ce2ca?r=16x9&amp;s=720x620&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F4%2Fn10453217.md#1"></a>
<p>她开心地跟妈妈说：“妈妈，我刚才教会它怎么走在我身边！”“妈，我只是教它怎么跟着我走。”</p>
<p>看着两小无猜一起<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%A3%E6%AD%A5.md#1">散步</a>的画面，让网友直呼：“好可爱。”“小孩和小动物都是<ahref="https://github.com/pdheqb3476/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%AF%E7%9C%9F.md#1">纯真</a>的。”“好温馨的画面，赞啦！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10454808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10454808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/be01d0c8dbe13fb3c8869dd701824dfe.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10454808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/be01d0c8dbe13fb3c8869dd701824dfe-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10454808" class="wp-caption-text">两小无猜的背影。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>肯亚野生动物保护区发言人表示，孩子拥有这奇妙机会与林戈亲密共处，他们也能从中学会野生动物相关知识，“只有教孩子们去尊重并喜欢动物，才能改变生物濒临绝种局面”。</p>
<p>最后他也希望家长把孩子带来肯亚野生动物保护区，学习怎么与动物友好相处。</p>
