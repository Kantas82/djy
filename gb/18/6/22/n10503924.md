<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>树上小家伙竟落水 暖男义不容辞救助无尾熊</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JY67r"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jjdlwc348/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月23日讯】水里有什么东西正载浮载沉……是狗狗？小熊？</p>
<p>哎呀，没想到是<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E5%B0%BE%E7%86%8A.md#1">无尾熊</a>（树袋熊）！总是在树上的小家伙，不知道为什么会掉进水里。男子发现后，想出了这样的方法来救它——<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%91%E5%B9%B2.md#1">树干</a>。</p>
<p>由于<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E5%B0%BE%E7%86%8A.md#1">无尾熊</a>离岸边有点距离，于是男子利用<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%91%E5%B9%B2.md#1">树干</a>伸向它，让它抓住树枝后，再慢慢拉回岸边。不过，可别忘了无尾熊是有爪子的！</p>
<p>谨慎的男子不敢贸然伸手，因此他从较无威胁性的后方进行—— 提起无尾熊的后颈，小心翼翼地把它拉近。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10506063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10506063" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-222.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10506063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-1-222-450x483.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="483" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10506063" class="wp-caption-text">男子帮助<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E6%B0%B4.md#1">落水</a>的无尾熊。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而或许是惊魂未定，小家伙趴在岸边发挥了好一会儿呆愣的天性，于是男子再助一臂之力，帮它平安上岸。太好啦！谢谢这位善良的<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%96%E7%94%B7.md#1">暖男</a>。</p>
<p>无尾熊是<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>代表性的动物之一，其英文“Koala”源自澳洲原住民的方言，意思是“不喝水”。无尾熊有一身浓密的灰褐色短毛，特征是一对毛茸茸的大耳朵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10503932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10503932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10503932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-13-450x464.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="464" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10503932" class="wp-caption-text">小家伙惊魂未定。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>个性温驯的它们以尤加利树叶和嫩枝为食，多数时间待在树上，连睡觉也一样。</p>
