<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一片烤焦吐司看出夫妻恩爱程度 准确度惊人</h2>
<a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/L0iDtYGNb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/afvaic3202/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/afvaic3202/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10440919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Video-link-2-450x51.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="51" /></a><br />
自我有记忆以来，家里就是母亲掌厨。有件事令我印象很深刻。</p>
<p>当时我才8岁，父亲忙了一天回到家。母亲正在准备晚餐，父亲和我坐在餐桌边。她端来果酱和烤焦的吐司给父亲，不是部分而是整片都烤焦了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533604" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/01ab4994ebef3fcece2392f360532bbe.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/01ab4994ebef3fcece2392f360532bbe-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533604" class="wp-caption-text">示意图。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我等着，看谁会先出声。父亲却默默地吃着吐司，并问我功课写了没有，今天过得好不好。</p>
<p>我不记得我说了什么，但我记得听到母亲跟父亲说，抱歉把土司烤焦了。</p>
<p>我永远忘不了父亲的回答。他说：“亲爱的，我喜欢烤焦的吐司，谢谢你！”</p>
<p>后来，我问父亲，是否真的喜欢烤焦的吐司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10533605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10533605" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f5a525077d5072628dcea027771cd022.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10533605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f5a525077d5072628dcea027771cd022-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10533605" class="wp-caption-text">示意图。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他搭上我的肩膀说：“你母亲忙了一天，她今天一定很累了。而且，烤焦的吐司……”</p>
<p>他继续说：“你知道吗，人生总会有不完美的人和事物。我离完美还很远，而且常忘记生日和结婚纪念日。但这些年我学到一点……”</p>
