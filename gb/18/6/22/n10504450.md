<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大自然生气了？伐木工砍树 最后竟落荒而逃</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月22日讯】万物皆有灵，你相信植物也有感情吗？这棵<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%A0%91.md#1">大树</a>看起来应该是森林里的长老了，<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%90%E6%9C%A8%E5%B7%A5.md#1">伐木工</a>手持锯子，正在进行砍伐的作业。</p>
<p>咦，他<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%AF%E6%9C%A8.md#1">锯木</a>的方式好像有点特别？实在是令人摸不着头绪。就在他锯到一半时，突然察觉异状——<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%A0%91.md#1">大树</a>竟然从正中央整个迸裂！天哪！发生了什么事？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-1-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10504460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-1-23-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%90%E6%9C%A8%E5%B7%A5.md#1">伐木工</a>正在<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%AF%E6%9C%A8.md#1">锯木</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他吓得连锯子都来不及收，先逃命再说。高高的树干瞬间一分为二，先从左边开始倒下，接着是右边，最后只剩下中间一截屹立不摇，简直就像大树的怒气爆炸一样。</p>
<p>薪水低、工时长，加上具有高度危险性，伐木工因此成为美国“最差职业”排行榜前十名的常客。锯木的机械稍微操作不慎，可能就会出人命，而巨木倒下、木材滚落也可能造成人员死伤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-2-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10504461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-2-13-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大树突然从中间<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A3%82%E5%BC%80.md#1">裂开</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而台湾有三分之二的面积是山地，目前所面临的重大危机是“山老鼠”的横行。丰富的山林资源引来盗伐者觊觎，加上巡山员严重不足，珍贵的<ahref="https://github.com/cqksji3539/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E6%9C%A8.md#1">林木</a>屡遭窃取，如红桧、牛樟木都是山老鼠的最爱。</p>
