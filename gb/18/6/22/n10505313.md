<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中西合璧夏日舞展感动观众</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-1Dance-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文诚中文学校的学生们舞动折扇，刚柔并济。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月22日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨卫斯里镇报导）6月16日晚，指导麻州多个<ahref="https://github.com/evfmhh399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E5%9B%A2.md#1">舞团</a>、多所中文学校舞蹈班的<ahref="https://github.com/evfmhh399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E5%90%9B%E6%9F%94.md#1">蔡君柔</a>老师，邀请各舞团与她的舞蹈学生们一同在卫斯理高中礼堂呈现了一场<ahref="https://github.com/evfmhh399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E8%A5%BF%E5%90%88%E7%92%A7.md#1">中西合璧</a>的舞展。</p>
<p>在这场名为“绽-夏日盛宴(A Dance in Bloom)”的演出中，<ahref="https://github.com/evfmhh399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E5%90%9B%E6%9F%94.md#1">蔡君柔</a>执教的儿童班、成人班和妈妈班分别登场，展现她编导的中国舞、民族舞和流行舞。孩子们可爱的表演引来观众一波接一波的欢呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10505325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-4Dance-e1529674497823.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-4Dance-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505325" class="wp-caption-text">纽英崙中华艺术协会<ahref="https://github.com/evfmhh399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E5%9B%A2.md#1">舞团</a>展现水袖霓裳。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10505328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-5Dance-e1529674541591.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-5Dance-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505328" class="wp-caption-text">蔡君柔CJT Dream Dance坊的独舞孔雀舞。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_10505334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-8Dance-e1529674673766.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-8Dance-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505334" class="wp-caption-text">佳韵舞蹈团演绎“天山姑娘”。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英崙中华艺术协会、佳韵舞蹈团等多个华人舞团、勒星顿、文诚、世纪等中文学校，以及蔡君柔参与的波士顿现代舞团(The Dream Project II)也来共襄盛举，诠释她原创的、风格各异的舞蹈。</p>
<p>纽英崙艺术协会会长、资深摄影师梅芳芳带着她的长焦相机到场观看、记录这个优雅的舞展，并为演出最后的“回(Rebirth)”现代舞节目深受感动。“他们跳的很有张力，感觉里面很有情绪。彩排的时候我看了一次，现再看了一次，看了两次我还想看第三次。”梅芬芳说</p>
<p>剑桥合唱团团长屠泽宽对流行舞表演所展现的动感印象深刻。“非常有活力。”他说。</p>
<p>波士顿侨教中心主任欧宏伟也到场观看了演出，并盛赞蔡君柔是波士顿舞蹈界的“台湾之光”。他表示，要在一场演出中合理搭配儿童和成人的舞蹈，中国舞、现代舞等不同的风格，可谓相当有难度。“(舞展)展现了蔡君柔老师的功力，相当了不起，美不胜收。”欧宏伟说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10505338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-9Dance-e1529674723419.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-9Dance-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505338" class="wp-caption-text">来自新疆的陈逍旸（右）演出后和蔡君柔老师合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10505321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-2Dance-e1529674405624.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-2Dance-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505321" class="wp-caption-text">蔡君柔(右)谢幕时接受献花，并答谢观众。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10505323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10505323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-3Dance-e1529674453390.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10505323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-2-3Dance-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10505323" class="wp-caption-text">所有参加演出的成员大合照。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>从谢幕舞台上下来的蔡君柔感慨说，筹备了将近半年之后，这次舞展的成功给了她很大的动力。她表示，接下来希望在夏令营的舞蹈课中为孩子们带来优质的教学；此外，她与她所在的现代舞团也在筹画下一场演出。“希望明年给大家带来更专业的作品。”蔡君柔说。至于为何这次她本人未亲自担纲演出，她表示希望把第一次做好，所以先不急着上场表现，等明年就可以参加演出了。◇</p>
