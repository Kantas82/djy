<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全新Peugeot 5008 法式七座SUV</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/Peugeot-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大纪元合成图。（图片来源：Peugeot 官网）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月10日讯】（大纪元工商记者李维<ahref="https://github.com/zbhtyx3190/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>奥克兰报导）都说浪漫的法国人懂设计，而标致有型的全新Peugeot 5008 SUV带给您的却不仅仅是浪漫！这款荣获欧洲NCAP最高五星级安全评级的七座豪华运动休旅，能够让您悉心呵护一家老小的好爸爸梦想得以成真！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900528" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900528" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/7-seat-peugeot-5008-suv.18-450x227.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/7-seat-peugeot-5008-suv.18-450x227.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="227" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900528" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为拥有200余年历史的经典汽车品牌复兴的一部分，Peugeot汽车以其最先进的新一代 i-Cockpit 唯我座舱打造的全新<ahref="https://github.com/zbhtyx3190/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E5%BA%A7suv.md#1">七座SUV</a>，为中型SUV细分市场树立了一座新的丰碑。</p>
<p>采用流线型设计的底盘、车身和设备旨在最大限度地提升您的驾驶乐趣和燃油效率，而宽大轴距则最大限度地为乘员增加了内部空间。以THP涡轮增压汽油和BlueHDi涡轮增压柴油发动机及新型EAT6自动变速箱提供动力的全新Peugeot 5008 ，标志着高性能SUV新时代的到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900529" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-162-fr.340053.17-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-162-fr.340053.17-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900529" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新5008 SUV凭借其时尚前卫的设计、最先进的一流技术装备及宽敞舒适的内部空间，为您和您的家人带来非同寻常的多重感官享受及舒心惬意的旅行体验。</p>
<h2>威风凛凛的SUV外观</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10900530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900530" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-suv-range.43-450x180.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-suv-range.43-450x180.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="180" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900530" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经全新设计的Peugeot 5008 SUV以其犀利的外观赢得瞬间吸引力。从醒目的前脸设计到黑钻石车顶（GT车型标配），再到大型合金轮毂……，随处可见的冲击力，一看便知是一款真正的SUV。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-gt-suv-7-seat.43-450x141.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-gt-suv-7-seat.43-450x141.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="141" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900531" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新5008承袭了Peugeot家族的点阵式进气格栅、LED狮眼大灯及狮爪式LED尾灯，但长而水平的发动机罩、对比鲜明的车轮拱饰、不銹钢车顶架及宽大的车身比例则营造出精致而富有动感的强大SUV外观。</p>
<p>Allure车型标配18英寸金刚石抛光合金轮毂、不銹钢门槛护板。顶配GT车型则采用19英寸合金轮毂、带转弯功能的全LED前大灯、镀铬镜盖和更宽的轮拱。</p>
<h2>独一无二的唯我座舱</h2>
<p>全新5008 SUV通过新一代i-CockpitR唯我座舱及其高科技环境唤醒您的感官。12.3英寸抬头数字仪表板、8.0英寸电容式触摸屏、紧凑型多功能方向盘及电动脉冲自动变速箱为您提供独特的沉浸式驾驶体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900532" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-suv-7-seats.6.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-10900532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-suv-7-seats.6.jpg" alt="" width="428" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900532" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.3英寸仪表板位于方向盘上方，贴近您的前方道路，因而可以大大提高安全性。8英寸电容屏为您提供多媒体需求，包括3D卫星导航、带Apple CarPlay和Android Auto界面的屏幕镜像（Mirror Screen）技术及蓝牙免提。</p>
<p>Allure车型标配无钥匙进入和启动、隐私玻璃、可调的Blue Mood车内氛围灯、无线充电底座及多功能可折叠座椅，并为第二排乘员配备了飞机式托盘（Aero Styled Trays）、遮阳帘和地板储物箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-349-fr.340071.17-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-349-fr.340071.17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900533" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>GT车型采用Alcantara面料装饰（包括仪表板），并配备开放式全景玻璃天窗、带加热、记忆和5种模式按摩功能的八向电动驾驶员座椅及免提电动尾门。可打开40厘米的全景玻璃天窗，同时配备夜间气氛照明灯及光照太强时使用的电动遮阳帘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-195-fr.340075.17-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-195-fr.340075.17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900536" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于GT版专属的i-CockpitRAmplify套件，只要按下仪表板中央按钮，可立即激发四种感官体验。</p>
<p>&#8211; 视觉： 通过颜色和照明强度调节；</p>
<p>&#8211; 听觉：通过音乐氛围设置和声学配置；</p>
<p>&#8211; 触觉：通过多点式（mult i- point）座椅按摩程序；</p>
<p>&#8211; 嗅觉：通过香味扩散器和三种香味选项。</p>
<p>除此之外，GT版还为您提供两种感官氛围：一种是“提振”（Boost）氛围，用于精力充沛的驾驶；另一种是“放松”（Relax）氛围，让您享受更加平静的体验。两者均可定制，目的是为乘员提供令人难忘的驾乘体验。</p>
<h2>多功能宽敞空间</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10900535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-340-fr.340057.17-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-340-fr.340057.17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900535" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.84米轴距使全新5008成为中型SUV中“最宜居”的车款。第二排设三个分体式折叠座椅。该座椅既可调节靠背倾斜度，又可前后移动，且每个座椅均配备ISO-FIX接口。第三排设两个可拆卸的分体式折叠座椅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-suv-boot.340064.17-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-suv-boot.340064.17-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900534" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>折叠第三排座椅，行李箱容量为952升；拿掉第三批座椅，容量增至1060升；拿掉第三排座椅并折叠第二排座椅，容量增至2150升；折叠第二和第三排座椅，再折叠前排乘员座椅，最大装载长度增至3.2米。</p>
<h2>动力与经济的完美结合</h2>
<p>Allure采用1.6升THP汽油发动机，可输出121千瓦最大功率和240牛米峰值扭矩，百公里综合油耗只有7.3升。GT版搭载2.0升BlueH DI柴油发动机，可输出133千瓦最大功率和400牛米峰值扭矩，百公里综合油耗只有4.8升。全系配备EAT6六速自动变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugoet-5008-suv-accessories.17-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugoet-5008-suv-accessories.17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900537" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灵活机动的全新5008 SUV具有高档汽车的所有动态特征和声学舒适性。高性能的BlueHDi和THP发动机，结合最新一代EAT6自动变速箱、电动助力转向和广泛的流线型设计，为全新5008缔造了非同寻常的抓地力和驾驶乐趣。</p>
<p>得益于EMP2平台及其卓越的动态特征，全新5008在舒适性和道路操控性之间实现了完美平衡。EMP2架构还具有更紧凑的回转圆，使您的所有操控都会更加轻松。</p>
<p>Allure车型标配Peugeot高级抓地力控制系统（AGC）。这是一种增强型牵引力控制系统，该系统优化了对雪地、沙地和泥地的抓地力，并配备陡坡缓降辅助功能，使车辆能够以稳定安全的速度下坡。</p>
<h2>强大的安全保障</h2>
<p>荣获欧洲NCAP最高五星级安全评级的全新5008 SUV标配一整套安全装备，包括6个安全气囊、拖车稳定控制、LED日间行车灯、限速标志识别、主动车道偏离警告、主动盲点检测、行人检测紧急制动、倒车摄像头以及前后停车传感器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10900539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10900539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-142-fr.340079.17-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-10900539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/peugeot-5008-2016-142-fr.340079.17-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10900539" class="wp-caption-text">全新Peugeot 5008 SUV。（图片来源：Peugoet官网）</figcaption></figure>
