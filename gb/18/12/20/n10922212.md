<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>涂醒哲展百大政绩十大贡献 1500多天如7-11全年无休</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/0f8c494f8aac50edcd7135978b421468-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	嘉义市长涂醒哲将四年施政成果集结成册，做成给市民朋友的情书。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年12月20日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）服务满4周年，嘉义市长<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%82%E9%86%92%E5%93%B2.md#1">涂醒哲</a>20日在市府一楼中庭举办“信任幸福新嘉义 感恩记者会”，涂醒哲将四年施政成果集结成册，做成给市民朋友的情书，内容详述百大<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E7%BB%A9.md#1">政绩</a>十大贡献，短暂的4年，写下轰轰烈烈的政绩。</p>
<p>理应花8年来做的事，4年内就完成，任内1500多个日子如7-11全年无休，市民感念之余也不舍；所以应市民要求，<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%82%E9%86%92%E5%93%B2.md#1">涂醒哲</a>向市民报告百项<ahref="https://github.com/gwrncs3448/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%BF%E7%BB%A9.md#1">政绩</a>、十项贡献等亮眼施政成果；整筛志工陈玉洲女士代表市民献花、送上手工卡片，感谢涂醒哲推动整合型健康筛检，让先生从鬼门关前捡回一命，现场气氛温馨感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/4d74a4b04ffc01e26d6c6f68a9e00e96.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922216 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/4d74a4b04ffc01e26d6c6f68a9e00e96-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922216" class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲、市长夫人郑玉娟及副市长张惠博(左)。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲简报指出，4年来以“沟通、水清、空气好，路平、灯亮、顾健康”为施政目标，积极打造嘉义市为幸福宜居城市。不仅期待已久的铁路高架化美梦成真，经济稳健发展，也让改变落实在每个角落，如重现鹭桥观浪的弥陀映月桥落成启用，兰潭整修美化，风华再现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/81ec96ebbb21946b9052a557d64808e6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922217 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/81ec96ebbb21946b9052a557d64808e6-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922217" class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲率各单位主管与市民说，谢谢市民四年来的鞭策。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“一流团队，才能做出最好的政绩。”涂醒哲感谢一级主管及同仁辛劳，四年来打下多项重要基础建设，为整个都市带来翻转。未来大方向也已规划完成，其中不少更已获中央支持，正在进行中。期待嘉义市能在稳固基础上永续发展，相信会是市民的幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/73882b16ba7c610ce314197d895d6dcc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922220 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/73882b16ba7c610ce314197d895d6dcc-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922220" class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲与媒体人合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>政绩不仅看得见，市民感受也相当深刻。卫生局整筛志工陈玉洲分享，先生郭丁福因误食药品，肝指数飙到1万多，还好参加整筛，在鬼门关前救回一命，“这是给市民最好的礼物”。陈玉洲感谢市长夫人郑玉娟，4年来把市长借给嘉义市民，让市民享有幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/c7aa1f414464628ce3d2eb195b47ed5a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922221 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/c7aa1f414464628ce3d2eb195b47ed5a-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922221" class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲与嘉中校友会长团合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲表示，过去4年来，每天8点忙到深夜，一千五百多个日子如7-11全年无休，天天都在思考如何让嘉义市更进步。从政这条路不好走，过程中造谣抹黑不断，但还好感谢有市民和市政团队，让他一生清廉勤政爱乡土。虽然无缘再为市民服务，但缘尽情未了，“嘉义市是我的故乡，情永远在”，一样都珍惜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/4dd3409d3c8aa718c42b4f6d0e351618.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922223 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/4dd3409d3c8aa718c42b4f6d0e351618-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922223" class="wp-caption-text">市政顾问说涂市长不懂选举时的眉角，才会失去连任机会，不过这也是更上一层楼的契机。 （李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲今日也特别感谢夫人郑玉娟一路相挺。他说，在台大做教授、当医师，应该是最好的工作之一。自己从台北市卫生局长、疾病管制局长做到卫生署长。过程中太太最反对，但为了理念、理想，愿意割舍、承担，作背后最大支持。涂醒哲将花束转赠给夫人，“谢谢太太”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10922224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/cdd10d9825623b235021571646f4b52a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922224 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/cdd10d9825623b235021571646f4b52a-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922224" class="wp-caption-text">蔡文旭议员与市长涂醒哲是最要好的朋友。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10922225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/a46afe56baf40ca2700ed13fe833bfaf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922225 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/a46afe56baf40ca2700ed13fe833bfaf-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922225" class="wp-caption-text">北荣里里长黄福林也依依不舍的与市长涂醒哲合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10922228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/3bf8bb45df909a1c2ed6dea9e60b76d8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922228 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/3bf8bb45df909a1c2ed6dea9e60b76d8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922228" class="wp-caption-text">几位好友也来合拍留念。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10922215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10922215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/e5677cb0b81677d908146acc17df102d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10922215 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/e5677cb0b81677d908146acc17df102d-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10922215" class="wp-caption-text">整筛志工陈玉洲女士(中)代表市民献花、送上手工卡片，感谢涂醒哲推动整合型健康筛检，让先生(右)从鬼门关前捡回一命，现场气氛温馨感人。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲将四年施政成果集结成册，做成给市民朋友的情书，内容详述百大政绩十大贡献，也勾勒未来愿景。记者会由复国幼儿园可爱唱跳开场，最后在涂启文医师及嘉义女声合唱团“感恩的心”歌声中落幕，会后不少民众与市长合影签名留念，为记者会画下美好句点。◇</p>
