<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周汤豪领军众星高雄跨年 现场被民众挤爆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310701561487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“2019爱?Sharing 高雄梦时代跨年派对”主持群，左起（依序）为“浩角翔起”、Janet与艾力克斯。（民视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年12月31日讯】（大纪元台湾台北记者站报导）由<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B1%A4%E8%B1%AA.md#1">周汤豪</a>领军并压轴表演的“2019爱?Sharing <ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84%E6%A2%A6%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">高雄梦时代</a>跨年派对”于今晚（31日）在高雄展开。主持人“<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E8%A7%92%E7%BF%94%E8%B5%B7.md#1">浩角翔起</a>”彩排时，更直呼被现场满满的人潮感动到不行；与艾力克斯搭档主持的Janet，因为还要表演，也难得紧张，大呼过瘾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944636" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812310720541487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944636" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812310720541487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944636" class="wp-caption-text">“2019爱?Sharing <ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%9B%84%E6%A2%A6%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">高雄梦时代</a>跨年派对”主持群，右起（依序）为“<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E8%A7%92%E7%BF%94%E8%B5%B7.md#1">浩角翔起</a>”、Janet与艾力克斯。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年晚会演出卡司包括安心亚、<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>、恺乐、曹雅雯、Janet+雪铃兔波波、87乐团、嘻哈新声?ZI、阿达+JayRoll、蔡佳麟、黄小琥，压轴则由<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B1%A4%E8%B1%AA.md#1">周汤豪</a>以及压轴后由乐团<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%B8%89%E5%A4%AD.md#1">八三夭</a>带来精彩演出，还有神秘嘉宾白冰冰带来演唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720511487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944199" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720511487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944199" class="wp-caption-text">安心亚带来大阵仗舞群，带来《呼呼》、《女孩 站出来》等歌。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>歌手<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>一身黑色劲装现身，带来《如果雨之后》，感受到现场观众热情的他不断跟现场观众问好，接着演唱《永不失联的爱》”时，周兴哲说：“很开心跟大家一起度过2019希望跟大家都是永不失联的爱。”可爱的祝贺词萌翻全场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720481487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944201" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720481487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944201" class="wp-caption-text">周兴哲一身黑色劲装现身，在“梦时代”跨年晚会现场带来《如果雨之后》等歌。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10944202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944202" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720451487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944202" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310720451487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944202" class="wp-caption-text">周兴哲一身黑色劲装现身，在“梦时代”跨年晚会现场带来《如果雨之后》等歌。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周兴哲说2018也是自己大丰收的一年，访问时提到Eric周兴哲熟悉的都是情歌，但最近曲风想要“有点不一样”，周兴哲说这次邀请国际知名制作人 Freddy Haggstam一同合作，打造出与过去截然不同的音乐面貌。</p>
<p>从“蝴蝶姊姊”成功转型，变身为唱跳歌手的恺乐，今晚穿着紧身银色短洋装搭配银色长靴出场，演唱完新歌《恋爱泡泡糖》之后，随即问候大家，感受现场观众热情的她，还一连演唱了《三人行》、《致未来的恋人》、《久等了》，带热现场气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944407" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310928441487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10944407" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/1812310928441487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944407" class="wp-caption-text">恺乐一身紧身银色短洋装搭配银色长靴，整个人散发电力带热现场。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“87乐团”主唱艾成也带来新歌《霸气》震撼开场，keyboard手王瞳扭伤还没好，还是绑着束带蹬上高跟鞋敬业登台，接着唱毕《管不着》后，艾成说“87乐团”用了六年时间准备，真的很荣幸能站在这个舞台，也感性诉说他们曾不被外界看好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944627" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311230481487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10944627" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311230481487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944627" class="wp-caption-text">“87乐团”主唱艾成以新歌《霸气》震撼开场。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>艾成介绍起乐团成员吉他手大雄、贝斯手乐咖、鼓手何豪杰以及王瞳，向所有观众致贺新年，他说起刚发行专辑录音筹备过程中，遇到很多困难，一度想放弃，但努力这么多年，不想让歌迷失望，也鼓励所有人不管遇到多少困难，看到“87乐团”就是坚持梦想的范例，要大家一起坚持下去、实现梦想。之后他们又演唱了《安放》《杰作》《作伙作梦》与《三天三夜》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944628" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311230501487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944628" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311230501487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944628" class="wp-caption-text">“87乐团”keyboard手王瞳。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动尾声，周汤豪压轴登场，先与高雄在地表演团体“振宗艺术团”的舞龙舞狮开场，接连演唱了《角头》、《马戏团猴子》、《射手》、《I am the man》，他开心表示跟大家度过2018最后一天。周汤豪也在手机灯海时演唱夯歌《帅到分手》，还转身让镜头带到身后一片手机灯海，画面震撼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224041487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944630" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224041487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944630" class="wp-caption-text">“2019爱?Sharing 高雄梦时代跨年派对”于31日热闹展开，周汤豪压轴登场。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10944632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944632" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224071487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944632" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224071487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944632" class="wp-caption-text">“2019爱?Sharing 高雄梦时代跨年派对”于31日热闹展开，周汤豪压轴登场。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而当晚高雄梦时代现场，市长与夫人也在现场高歌，并祝福市民2019平安快乐，接着与周汤豪、白冰冰等人一起倒数迎接新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10944644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224011487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944644" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311224011487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944644" class="wp-caption-text">乐团<ahref="https://github.com/vmzknt305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%B8%89%E5%A4%AD.md#1">八三夭</a>于“2019爱?Sharing 高雄梦时代跨年派对”压轴演出之后，带来演出。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10944633" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10944633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311238541487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10944633" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1812311238541487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10944633" class="wp-caption-text">高雄市长韩国瑜（右二）与市长夫人（右三）也在现场高歌，并与周汤豪（左二）等人一起倒数迎接新年。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
