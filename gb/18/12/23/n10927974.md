<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>印尼海啸 流行乐队演唱会舞台顷刻坍塌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/4cbc62209735213c8cfc5a9ba5db9bcb-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>印尼Sunda Strait附近海难遭遇海啸，印尼流行乐队Seventeen演出舞台顷刻坍塌。（大纪元合成图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年12月23日讯】<span class="s3">12</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s3">22</span><span class="s1">日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/ajbsgc392/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%B0%BC.md#1">印尼</a>四人流行乐队</span><span class="s3">Seventeen</span><span class="s1">在Sunda Strait附近海滩的度假村举行演唱会遭遇灭顶之灾。<ahref="https://github.com/ajbsgc392/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%95%B8.md#1">海啸</a>突然降至，顷刻舞台坍塌。生还的歌手流泪表示海中很多浮尸。此次海啸至少</span><span class="s3">62</span><span class="s1">人死亡，近</span><span class="s3">600</span><span class="s1">人受伤。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s4">12</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s4">23</span><span class="s1">日网上流传一段视频显示，</span><span class="s4">22</span><span class="s1">日晚<ahref="https://github.com/ajbsgc392/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%B0%BC.md#1">印尼</a>流行乐队正在丹戎勒松（</span><span class="s4">Tanjung Lesong</span><span class="s1">）的度假村进行演出，乐队的主要歌手</span><span class="s1">背对观众，手持话筒、伴随音乐进行热身，当他正准备转身正面亮相观众时，<ahref="https://github.com/ajbsgc392/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%95%B8.md#1">海啸</a>突然降至，冲破了舞台的背景幕布，同时舞台也顷刻坍塌。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">眼见海水迎面撞向观众席，同时视频中爆发出人们惊魂的尖叫声，随后视频的画面嘎然而止。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">幸运存活的乐团歌手</span><span class="s4">Riefian Fajarsyah</span><span class="s1">在社交媒体（</span><span class="s4">Instagram</span><span class="s1">（简称</span><span class="s4">IG</span><span class="s1">）</span><span class="s4">)</span><span class="s1">上哭着介绍此次灾难及乐团成员的生还情况。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">据《南华早报》报导，乐团的三名成员</span><span class="s4">Andi</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s4">Herman</span><span class="s1">及</span><span class="s4">Ujang</span><span class="s1">，包括他的妻子</span><span class="s4">Dylan Sahara</span><span class="s1">都失踪。乐团吉他手</span><span class="s4">Bani</span><span class="s1">及经理人</span><span class="s4">Oki</span><span class="s1">在此次海啸事件中丧生。这次海啸导致至少</span><span class="s4">62</span><span class="s1">人死亡、近</span><span class="s4">600</span><span class="s1">人受伤。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">另据东网消息，该名歌手周日于电视直播表示，当他被海浪冲到海上时，看到身边有很多的浮尸，他还介绍，由于恶劣气候导致道路堵塞，救援进展缓慢。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">责任编辑：骆亚</span></p>
