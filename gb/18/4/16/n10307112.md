<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA奥拉迪波32分助威 步行者扳倒骑士</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月16日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬台北报导）去年在季后赛第一轮就被克里夫兰<ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%91%E5%A3%AB.md#1">骑士</a>四战横扫的印地安纳<ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E8%A1%8C%E8%80%85.md#1">步行者</a>，命运捉弄两队今年季后赛首轮再度狭路相逢，新成员<ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E6%8B%89%E8%BF%AA%E6%B3%A2.md#1">奥拉迪波</a>（Victor Oladipo）不让憾事再度发生，16日轰进全场最高32分，帮助<ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A5%E8%A1%8C%E8%80%85.md#1">步行者</a>以98:80拿下好彩头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10307116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10307116" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d2a6d40b936bba514a804bf89c29dcea.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10307116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d2a6d40b936bba514a804bf89c29dcea-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10307116" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E6%8B%89%E8%BF%AA%E6%B3%A2.md#1">奥拉迪波</a>。 (Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>意外的是，<ahref="https://github.com/gvujva311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%91%E5%A3%AB.md#1">骑士</a>开赛就呈现荒腔走板的步调，防守不到位，攻击也失手，奥拉迪波、史蒂芬森（Lance Stephenson）、博格达诺维奇（Bojan Bogdanovic）及透纳（Myles Turner）连番进分，步行者首节就以33:14取得近20分的领先。首节仅有2分进账的詹姆斯（LeBron James）在第二节恢复手感拿到12分，可惜仍不敌溜马多点开花攻势，半场结束以38:55落后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10307118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10307118" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/43c4b9f312fb2d1fcd1bfbbd0f0a2baf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10307118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/43c4b9f312fb2d1fcd1bfbbd0f0a2baf-450x330.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10307118" class="wp-caption-text">詹姆斯。 (Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>詹姆斯、洛夫（Kevin Love）及史密斯（J.R. Smith）力图振作，第三节追到8分落后，不过奥拉迪波在第四节的三分球，拉出一波14:9的攻势，再次打乱骑士的节奏，最后让詹姆斯无奈地离开球场。</p>
<p>过去8年以来，詹姆斯和骑士在季后赛首轮拿下12胜、0败，此役首尝败绩，詹姆斯表示，现在只是落后一场而已，相较于此前在总冠军战以1:3落后，说什么都还太早。</p>
<h2>绿衫军延长赛击退雄鹿</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10307122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10307122" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ba347e6170e54a8303db97720b3a0c29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10307122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ba347e6170e54a8303db97720b3a0c29-450x543.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="543" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10307122" class="wp-caption-text">霍佛德。 (Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10307121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10307121" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d619f323aeea924bd96a84b2c280bf46.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10307121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d619f323aeea924bd96a84b2c280bf46-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10307121" class="wp-caption-text">洛齐尔（右）。 (Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10307119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10307119" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d96f33171accfee7d6eb6eac0751e8dd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10307119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d96f33171accfee7d6eb6eac0751e8dd-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10307119" class="wp-caption-text">塔图姆（白衣）。 (Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另两支东区劲旅波士顿凯尔特人与密尔瓦基雄鹿对决，可就战到延长赛，绿衫军靠霍佛德（Al Horford）、洛齐尔（Terry Rozier）及塔图姆（Jayson Tatum）在延长战的优异表现，帮助凯尔特人最后以113:107开出季后赛红盘。</p>
<p>带着4分优势的绿衫军，碰到雄鹿在第四节的顽强抵抗，虽开节靠洛齐尔、布朗（Jaylen Brown）和拉金（Shane Larkin）的拿分将差距拉大到9分，波格登（Malcolm Brogdon）及米德顿（Khris Middleton）挺身而出带领雄鹿收复失土，剩下不到1分钟，布莱德索（Eric Bledsoe）三分球追到1分差。</p>
<p>霍佛德罚进2球后，亚德托昆波（Giannis Antetokounmpo）随后也灌进2分，霍佛德再罚进2分，最后11.1秒双方合演三分大战，波格登先轰进追平三分，洛齐尔三分冷箭超前，米德顿再投进追平三分，将比赛带进延长。</p>
<p>雄鹿延长赛失准，霍佛德和塔图姆先帮助凯尔特人取得三分的领先，洛齐尔再靠罚球连下5分，绿衫军最后取得胜利；另外两场赛事中，奥克拉荷马雷霆以116:108击退犹他爵士，休士顿火箭则以104:101险胜明尼苏达森林狼。</p>
