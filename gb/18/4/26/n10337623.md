<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看见台湾阿里山森铁 Google搭铁路台车拍摄</h2>
<a href="https://dh48pl2d5tlkv.cloudfront.net/F9enaS9Qs"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wtnrng3147/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者陈懿胜台北报导）Google 25日宣布新增<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/google%E8%A1%97%E6%99%AF.md#1">Google街景</a>台湾特蒐景点，透过“Google街景背包外借计划”所拍摄的私房景点，并首度加入<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>森林铁路沿途景致，这也是Google将街景背包安装在铁路台车上进行拍摄的台湾首例。</p>
<p>此次更新超过150个室内外景点，其中有超过30个景点系考量人文、自然因素变迁而重新拍摄的热门景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10334322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10334322" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/21093ac2d1b44f7a7ad4162f1d9c4c9f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10334322 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/21093ac2d1b44f7a7ad4162f1d9c4c9f-600x285.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10334322" class="wp-caption-text">风吹沙。<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/google%E8%A1%97%E6%99%AF.md#1">Google街景</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Google亚太区街景计划负责人魏芗君表示，在Google街景服务在台推出即将迈入10年之际，开心能透过重新拍摄台湾热门景点、添增更多新景点，为全球使用者带来最新的台湾景点资讯。未来将会持续透过多元的拍摄手法，克服各地不同的地理环境限制，为使用者们带来更多街景内容。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">观看完整影片?</span></strong></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-cb777e46-8c72-42dd-6159-d6684a20798d" data-id="player-cb777e46-8c72-42dd-6159-d6684a20798d"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/cb777e46-8c72-42dd-6159-d6684a20798d?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&navurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F4%2F26%2Fn10337501.md#1&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F4%2F26%2Fn10337623.md#1"></a>
<p>Google表示，本次重新拍摄的景点大多为民众特别喜爱造访的风景名胜、人文要地等，如高美湿地、七星潭风景区、赤崁楼等。透过街景影像的更新，使用者现可在Google街景平台上看到这些景点的最新风貌，更可善加利用景点的最新资讯来计划出游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10334321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10334321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d871b813576ace70181bc395a4ca57f6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10334321 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/d871b813576ace70181bc395a4ca57f6-600x284.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10334321" class="wp-caption-text">南澳神秘海滩。（Google街景）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Google说，本次的更新中，有多达50个景点拍摄是Google街景团队透过“Google街景背包外借计划”，与数个来自学术界、非营利组织等第三方单位的合作拍摄成果。<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>森林铁路特蒐景点，由Google街景团队、嘉义林管处及台北艺术大学建筑与文化资产研究所共同纪录完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10334319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10334319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/bc269e61f26e8d905b626519075f38d7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10334319 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/bc269e61f26e8d905b626519075f38d7-600x285.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10334319" class="wp-caption-text">豆腐岬风景区。（Google街景）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Google表示，为了完整捕捉台湾第一条自建登山铁路的风景，Google街景团队首次在台湾尝试将街景背包架设于铁路台车上拍摄，以呈现出阿里山森林铁路独特的登山铁路建造工法，并能让使用者饱览沿线横跨热带、亚热带、温带的多样化植被风貌。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10334320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10334320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a178888cf7459dcc15c5ec4d1cbef941.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10334320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a178888cf7459dcc15c5ec4d1cbef941-600x283.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10334320" class="wp-caption-text">阿里山森林铁路。（Google街景）</figcaption></figure>
