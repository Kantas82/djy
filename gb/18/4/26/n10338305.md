<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>探访坵隐农场 了解蚯蚓生态</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/2327e55660568241bca5e05e619168e3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坵隐农场主人王廷宏。 (施芝吟)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者施芝吟台北报导）周休二日有哪些地方是亲子同游的好去处呢？不用人挤人，又能远眺美人山，待在绿意盎然的世外桃源，令人心旷神怡，甚至可以进一步认识<ahref="https://github.com/fbacqn3783/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9A%AF%E8%9A%93.md#1">蚯蚓</a>、摸蚯蚓大便等，趁着假期，带着孩子到天籁度假酒店，来趟“坵隐”之旅吧！</p>
<p>在台北公园路搭乘1717皇家客运，一个半小时后抵达天籁，天籁以欧风的建筑外观与绿意盎然的阳明山相互辉映。卸下行李，换上泳衣，迫不及待跑去<ahref="https://github.com/fbacqn3783/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89.md#1">温泉</a>区，先经过樱花道，一片盛开樱花在眼前展现，拥山入汤，身心灵彻底放松，享受青峦叠嶂之美，若饿了，旁边还有补给站可以吃茶叶蛋、喝茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10338307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/6e9e0cacf011d050818adced6c66b5aa.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/6e9e0cacf011d050818adced6c66b5aa-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338307" class="wp-caption-text">阳明山天籁度假酒店。 (阳明山天籁度假酒店)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338314" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/6e1ab2feddb1c24f2175da9e3cec1242.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/6e1ab2feddb1c24f2175da9e3cec1242-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338314" class="wp-caption-text">户外<ahref="https://github.com/fbacqn3783/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89.md#1">温泉</a>。 (阳明山天籁渡假酒店)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，这里还有水世界，位于B1的亲子互动区，设有水中攀爬区、亲子趣味游戏区、肩背移动按摩区、亲子趣味设施等多样选择，让全家大小一齐共享这愉悦的水世界；嬉水滑水道，约一层楼高；专为亲子规划的嬉水滑水道，360度的回转弯设计，可感受在滑行时冲刺乐趣。结束户外玩乐，吃点东西，搭上天籁专车前往坵隐农场。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/fbacqn3783/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9A%AF%E8%9A%93.md#1">蚯蚓</a>让他 拾回亲子关系</h2>
<p>坵隐农场主人王廷宏在啤酒厂工作时，发现废弃物的麦芽渣能作为蚯蚓的饲料，蚯蚓的排泄物松软有如土壤，且富含有机质及营养成分，无臭无味，因此取名为“蚯蚓土”，不仅可作为有机肥及培养土施用，还可以铺在鸡舍作为垫料，减缓异味。</p>
<p>在啤酒厂担任副厂长的王廷宏，每天早出晚归的忙碌生活，让他与家人关系日渐疏远，年幼的2个女儿甚至把他当作陌生人，索性辞职开始从事蚯蚓粪的工作。“现在天天跟小孩见面，关系变更好了呢！”王廷宏笑着说。</p>
<p>他原本在山上养40、50万只蚯蚓，但是一场水灾，带走他的心血；之后在金山找了一田地，投入数十万搭建网室，防止禽鸟、猫、狗入侵，走到网室内，一眼看去是橘色塑胶箱子装着满满的土，里面约有5、6万只蚯蚓，一边挖泥土找蚯蚓，一边听着王廷宏介绍蚯蚓的特性，进而了解蚯蚓的一生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10338310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ac5db201e8b7e81d45e2aed092f99ca2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ac5db201e8b7e81d45e2aed092f99ca2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338310" class="wp-caption-text">橘色箱子内有上万只蚯蚓。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/e23a092be42ac69158c969bacdd662c9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/e23a092be42ac69158c969bacdd662c9-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338313" class="wp-caption-text">她们在找蚯蚓的卵。 (阳明山天籁渡假酒店)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/9e91d8ee9e6861c17873839b19f397f2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/9e91d8ee9e6861c17873839b19f397f2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338311" class="wp-caption-text">蚯蚓卵跟刚孵出来的小蚯蚓。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蚯蚓的便便含有丰富的有机物、腐植酸及微生物菌群。可以改善土壤物理机能，使粘土疏松、沙土凝结，进而让土壤空气流通，加速微生物繁殖，有利于植物吸收养分；增强保水性、保肥性；防止使用化肥过量所带来的危害；能分解土壤中的矿物质，供植物利用；对植物、人、畜无害，还可以增强植物对病虫害的抵抗力，抑制植物土传病的危害，改善作物品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10338318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/5dbfc9263ffbed658209d118a3332d54.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/5dbfc9263ffbed658209d118a3332d54-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338318" class="wp-caption-text">王廷宏贩售的蚯蚓茶、蚯蚓土。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看完蚯蚓，来到“鸡动境界”，每一只鸡都很肥大、毛色很漂亮，鸡只除吃饲料外，也吃有机蔬菜、益菌，这些鸡偶尔也当起清道夫吃吃杂草。仔细一看，鸡舍里头很干净，也没什么臭味。王廷宏表示，这要归功于蚯蚓便便，铺在鸡舍作为垫料，减缓异味，鸡蛋也不会有腥味，吃起来也很扎实，鸡只也不容易生病，之前在山上养鸡，遇到霸王寒流，鸡只仍活碰乱跳的；他也参考国外文献，自行研发出蚯蚓茶，蚯蚓茶则有微生物的拮抗作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10338315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b081352cdc32fdefe59cfb92a6ad72b5.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b081352cdc32fdefe59cfb92a6ad72b5-450x253.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338315" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友喂鸡。 (阳明山天籁渡假酒店)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1a2b5532861324c437e46a4c11edc7a4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1a2b5532861324c437e46a4c11edc7a4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338317" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋卷完成，可以现吃。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三区是“蔓蔓世界”，这里种植爬藤类植物，有小黄瓜、美浓瓜、灯笼果、豆类植物等，经由植物的攀爬而让蔓蔓世界有绿色隧道的感觉，一抬头就能顺利采果；“植物小学堂”则有一百多种植物，如香菜、柠檬香茅、圆叶当归、越南香菜、芳香万寿菊等，可以摸、闻、看。最后来到体验区，用蚯蚓土来做植物DIY，鸡蛋卷，打一颗蛋到机器内，熟了就会自己浮出来，很好玩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10338319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/e0778bd4c6ed939a3f5465b909b5ab22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/e0778bd4c6ed939a3f5465b909b5ab22-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338319" class="wp-caption-text">灯笼果，吃起来酸酸的。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b856ec011a12ec069b4ea78365230325.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b856ec011a12ec069b4ea78365230325-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338316" class="wp-caption-text">植物小学堂内，有一百多种植物。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10338321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10338321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a05be455d8b0d7591351ee4308480b60.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10338321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a05be455d8b0d7591351ee4308480b60-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10338321" class="wp-caption-text">观赏用的南瓜。 （施芝吟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王廷宏坚持以“友善大地”的理念来经营坵隐农场，蚯蚓、蚯蚓便便种植天然有机蔬果，进而复育更多健康土地，不仅替消费者把关，也找回了女儿的拥抱。◇</p>
