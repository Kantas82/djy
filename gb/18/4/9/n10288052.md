<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>尖沙咀楼上café 摩天轮游乐场tea set</h2>
<a href="https://dmepc9x6zy689.cloudfront.net/A6uTWqQBh"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/emljlk376/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/emljlk376/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年04月09日讯】朋友推介我到一间位于<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E6%B2%99%E5%92%80.md#1">尖沙咀</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A5%BC%E4%B8%8Acafe.md#1">楼上café</a>试试，他说里边很多特色卖相又可爱的食物，我看见一定会喜欢的。听到他这样说我便立刻去试试看！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10288143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10288143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1804090427121366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10288143" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/1804090427121366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10288143" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E6%B2%99%E5%92%80.md#1">尖沙咀</a>的White button café有很多特色卖相又可爱的食物。<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想不到这间White button café这么大、座位宽敞楼底又高，两边都是大大的玻璃窗很光亮。厢位大得可以让六人坐，桌面上有巴黎铁塔装饰，坐位还有垫子，四周还有可爱的装饰，以及乾花摆设，很多位置都适合拍照！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010041" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427281366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010041" class="wp-caption-text">café座位宽敞楼底又高，两边都是大大的玻璃窗很光亮。<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11010042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427321366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010042" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427321366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010042" class="wp-caption-text">四周还有可爱的装饰，以及乾花摆设。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>打开餐牌看到很多特色食物很特别，而且还是未见过的造型，我几乎所有都想试试，当然是没可能的事，哈哈！打开餐牌便给像游乐场一样的下午茶餐吸引着，立刻下单试试，这个下午茶餐包括两杯特饮， 特饮可以选择餐牌上任何一款，我要了水晶球特饮和很特别蓝色叫首尔塔的特饮，还有加了他们很有名的梳乎厘班戟配士多啤梨忌廉和叫龙卷风的甜品，另加一杯柚子蓝蝶花乳酸特饮和一壶玫瑰水蜜桃茶。</p>
<p>Pookie tea set 一出现我和朋友都被吸引，这个下午茶餐套餐好像游乐场一样，6款甜点放在小丑图案盘上面，4款咸点则放在摩天轮上面，摩天轮还可以转动，真的很有趣可爱啊！看到都开心！4款咸点分别是爆谷鸡球、迷你芝士汉堡、脆香薯角、蜜糖法兰克福肠。6款甜点分别是野莓马卡龙、焦糖爆谷慕丝、草莓法式泡芙、榛子拿破仑、香蕉芝士饼、法式朱古力挞。这个下午茶餐套餐很好吃，我觉得甜品尤其出色，真是每一款都好吃呀～想不到café都能够有这个水准，即使一般偏甜不是人人能都接受的马卡龙，它都做得甜度刚刚好，基本试任何人都可以接受，值得称赞！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427361366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11010030 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427361366-e1548747985444-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010030" class="wp-caption-text">Pookie tea set的6款甜点放在小丑图案盘上面。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11010032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427221366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010032" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427221366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010032" class="wp-caption-text">4款咸点则放在摩天轮上面。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水晶球特饮真是让我爱不释手，真是很像水晶球造型一样可爱好玩，打开里面还有彩色的闪灯，连细节都一丝不苟，喝下去是青苹果黑珍珠，好喝！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427251366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010022" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427251366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010022" class="wp-caption-text">水晶球特饮和柚子蓝蝶花乳酸特饮。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11010024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427151366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010024" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427151366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010024" class="wp-caption-text">水晶球特饮真是很像水晶球造型一样可爱好玩，打开里面还有彩色的闪灯，喝下去是青苹果黑珍珠。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很漂亮蓝色的首尔塔是柚子蓝柑，虽然是蓝色但喝下去不难喝，还满香柚子味呢！不过，玫瑰水蜜桃茶则淡而无味了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090430041366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010035" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090430041366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010035" class="wp-caption-text">首尔塔虽然是蓝色但喝下去不难喝，还满香柚子味呢！（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11010028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427431366-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11010028 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427431366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010028" class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰水蜜桃茶淡而无味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吃到一半香喷喷的梳乎厘奄列送到了，香喷喷热呼呼入口松软有鸡蛋的香气，配上士多啤梨忌廉和雪糕口感一流，难怪这么有名，这一个真是必食推介！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427521366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010026" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427521366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010026" class="wp-caption-text">梳乎厘奄列香喷喷热呼呼入口松软有鸡蛋的香气，配上士多啤梨忌廉和雪糕口感一流。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后送到的是龙卷风，像大粉红色云一样的棉花糖， 棉花糖下面是士多啤梨慕丝，里面有柚子酱还能吃到柚子皮，配搭在一起酸酸甜甜很开胃，记得要吃快点，因为棉花糖会溶，吃到一半还看到邻桌来了一个棉花糖雪人，棉花糖雪人还会冒烟呢，真是很可爱有趣呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11010016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090430091366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010016" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090430091366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010016" class="wp-caption-text">“龙卷风”像大粉红色云一样的棉花糖， 棉花糖下面是士多啤梨慕丝。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11010019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11010019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427401366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11010019" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1804090427401366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11010019" class="wp-caption-text">士多啤梨慕丝里面有柚子酱还能吃到柚子皮。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这间café不论环境和食物卖相女生一定喜欢，最适合三五知己共聚，很多位置适合拍照，现场光线充足喜欢自拍亦可，怎么拍都可以，值得去试！◇</p>
