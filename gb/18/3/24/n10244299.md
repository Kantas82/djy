<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>娱乐笔记：《温蒂的幸福剧本》温暖诠释姊妹温情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/a9bde4c7dd4a279eee784083b6424d0b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年03月24日讯】相信多数人都听过《星际争霸战》（Star Trek），这系列早已因电影、影集为人所熟知，但《星际争霸战》的剧本创作，则是极为罕见的影视素材。电影《<ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E8%92%82%E7%9A%84%E5%B9%B8%E7%A6%8F%E5%89%A7%E6%9C%AC.md#1">温蒂的幸福剧本</a>》（Please Stand By）即以此为故事框架，再辅以主角的成长与<ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%8A%E5%A6%B9.md#1">姊妹</a>温情为内核，演译一段温馨故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/ce700e67123a8a498928af8b9d8680fe.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/ce700e67123a8a498928af8b9d8680fe-600x283.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244308" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E8%92%82%E7%9A%84%E5%B9%B8%E7%A6%8F%E5%89%A7%E6%9C%AC.md#1">温蒂的幸福剧本</a>》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女主角温蒂患有<ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E9%97%AD%E7%97%87.md#1">自闭症</a>，父母双亡后被送到特殊的教养机构，以期被训练到能拥有立足社会的基本能力，21岁的她也是《星际争霸战》的死忠粉丝，闲暇之余甚至还亲自创作此系列的剧本。某天，温蒂发现片商正在限期征集《星际争霸战》剧本，已错过邮寄时机的她，决定瞒着众人前往洛杉矶，亲自将剧本交给片商。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244303" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/3254d215668e6ee9afb40778c4743b13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/3254d215668e6ee9afb40778c4743b13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244303" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10244302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5b09f64b857b3863cced6bd3e334d94e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/5b09f64b857b3863cced6bd3e334d94e-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244302" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">女主角形象鲜明</span></h2>
<p>《温蒂的幸福剧本》片长仅约93分钟，电影本身小而精，只用约半小时的篇幅，就能将温蒂当下的生活概况，鲜明地烙印进观众的脑海。患有<ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E9%97%AD%E7%97%87.md#1">自闭症</a>的她，虽已被训练到生活足以自理，但仍能轻易从诸多细节看出她与一般人的不同，如生活规律到宛如电脑程式，星期几要穿哪种衣服，均严格贯彻到底；人际交往上有些许障碍，有赖他人加以包容；受打击时会无法控制情绪等，角色形象相当立体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/c608f358833aa57f993c6d2a35d024c8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/c608f358833aa57f993c6d2a35d024c8-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244310" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温蒂虽非身心健全的正常人，但角色的光芒与诸多电影中才华洋溢的要角相比，亦毫不逊色。表面上她虽无优秀文凭或体面工作等傲人成就，但仍展现出可观的才气，许多人都称赞其创作的剧本。在机遇来临时，勇于克服不安与恐惧，毅然踏出舒适圈追梦，也非一般人都有决心做到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/16c53afe9e5a34c7c130417421707b55.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/16c53afe9e5a34c7c130417421707b55-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244306" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;">达珂塔?芬妮奉献出色演出</span></h2>
<p>担纲女主角的达珂塔?芬妮（Dakota Fanning），首次挑战身心障碍人士，演出表现也令人赞许，将温蒂与众不同的形象，诠释得有说服力，不至于让人感到过于刻意、生硬。虽然与真正的一流演员相比，尚有许多进步空间，但足以看出其潜力，相信假以时日必能交出更亮眼的成绩单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/90c0676024c0cc87b8fceb0ea3ba7858.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10244305 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/90c0676024c0cc87b8fceb0ea3ba7858-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244305" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10244307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/331a1e2d68cb8089451d6f612ddda1bc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/331a1e2d68cb8089451d6f612ddda1bc-600x284.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244307" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温蒂的递送剧本之旅，当然是本片的重头戏，这部分剧情颇有公路旅行的味道，从小到大第一次独自出远门的她，自然面临诸多挑战。这段旅程看似极为生活化，均是日常生活中有可能遇到的曲折，但在编剧的编织下，俨然成了一出跌宕起伏、蕴含剧情张力的冒险。虽然没有真正紧张刺激的情节，但在导演的诠释下仍有足够的可看性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1c32a94c81a181ecea2048bbb0d17e30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/1c32a94c81a181ecea2048bbb0d17e30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244304" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #339966;"><ahref="https://github.com/atmdgd314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%8A%E5%A6%B9.md#1">姊妹</a>亲情进一步升华</span></h2>
<p>就结局的呈现而言，《温蒂的幸福剧本》并非将重点着重在温蒂能否就此成功踏进电影事业，而是让她与姊姊奥黛莉的手足之情，得到进一步升华。奥黛莉从小就对温蒂呵护有加，是长姊如母的典范。不过她对已成年的温蒂仍信心不足，因此温蒂此趟冒险之旅，可说是让姊姊对她建立信心的过程。结尾奥黛莉放心地让温蒂抱着她年幼的女儿，看似微不足道之举，在此前提下，便显得意义非凡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10244311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244311" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/47bdb2eb5e2064c019103623fb05f193.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/47bdb2eb5e2064c019103623fb05f193-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244311" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10244309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10244309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/9bf9dedfe35bbbeb6cbef24f9ab5e542.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10244309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/9bf9dedfe35bbbeb6cbef24f9ab5e542-600x284.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10244309" class="wp-caption-text">《温蒂的幸福剧本》电影剧照。（威视电影提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>姊妹俩的家人温情，可说是《温蒂的幸福剧本》的最大亮点，温馨、动人程度，甚至可超越同样也以姊妹亲情为卖点的《冰雪奇缘》（Frozen），而且还凭借不算多的剧情比重，就达成此成就，更显得难能可贵。◇</p>
