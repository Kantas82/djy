<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马铃薯皮都被你扔了？这样一拌营养又好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>下厨时最常舍弃掉看似没有用的“蔬菜皮”。实际上它们非常营养。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">带皮的蔬菜，似乎都是皮里面的部分比较鲜嫩适口；相比之下，皮就显得粗糙乏味了。像是马铃薯、萝卜、茄子、黄瓜，我们常常把皮削掉，丢进垃圾桶。其实，这些皮含着满满的养分，有些皮甚至比里面的肉更有<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>。只要稍加处理，就能变成健康又可口的小菜。料理讲师教你一招，让不起眼的<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C%E7%9A%AE.md#1">蔬菜皮</a>“起死回生”！</span></p>
<p>◆剩的食材：<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C%E7%9A%AE.md#1">蔬菜皮</a> Vegetable</p>
<p>一般下厨时，最常舍弃掉看似没有用的“蔬菜皮”，把它们都丢掉实在太可惜，这些吸收阳光与土壤满满养分的皮，同样富含着<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>，只要能有效地调理运用，美味依旧不减，而且能做的料理还不少喔。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>零碎食材处理与吃法！</strong></span></h2>
<p><strong>食材：</strong></p>
<p>红白萝卜皮 + 小黄瓜皮 + 马铃薯皮 150g、盐适量</p>
<p>[蜂蜜<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A5%E6%9C%AB%E7%B1%BD.md#1">芥末籽</a>酱]：法国芥末籽酱 1.5大匙、橄榄油 60g、蜂蜜 1大匙、柠檬汁适量、盐适量</p>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>1. 蔬菜皮洗净切丝备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10359306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10359306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10359306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10359306" class="wp-caption-text">洗净所有蔬菜皮，切成细丝，倒入滚水锅中烫熟后捞起。</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 煮一锅加盐的热水，煮沸后放入切好的蔬菜皮。氽烫后，放入冰水中冰镇，捞起沥干水分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10359310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10359310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10359310 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic2-e1525360469558-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10359310" class="wp-caption-text">放入冰水中冰镇，捞起沥干水分。</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将蜂蜜<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A5%E6%9C%AB%E7%B1%BD.md#1">芥末籽</a>酱的材料混合均匀后，与蔬菜皮拌匀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10359374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10359374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic3-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10359374 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/pic3-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10359374" class="wp-caption-text">料理完毕的“芥末籽拌蔬菜皮”。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Tips</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>1. 除了蔬菜皮，也可直接用一般零碎的蔬菜盐抓腌，待软化后制作。</p>
<p>2. 可用泰式甜鸡酱、胡麻酱、韩式辣酱甚至果醋来做，能延伸的口味很多。@</p>
