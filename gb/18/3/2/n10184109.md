<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>厉害！墨尔本男子巧编织 爆红网络</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年03月02日讯】（大纪元记者宋清宁澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）最近，墨尔本一位名为“辣椒菲利”（Chili Philly）的<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%96%E7%BB%87.md#1">编织</a><ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>走红<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BA%A4%E5%AA%92%E4%BD%93.md#1">社交媒体</a>，他用钩针编织出各种貌似食物的大<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">帽子</a>，戴到头上拍出照片，再上传到Instagram上，引来众多粉丝关注。</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BBLoCBJhcCA/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1063.4999999965657%7D" width="658" b="722" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BA3p0q-hcB_/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1050.5000000011933%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BEzhzyMBcNC/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A488.999999997759%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>据《悉尼晨锋报》报导，今年23岁的弗格森（Phil Ferguson）以前并不会钩织，但他一直对制作东西感兴趣。他从Youtube视频上自学了钩织技术，并且很享受<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%96%E7%BB%87.md#1">编织</a>的过程。</p>
<p>他说： “真的，任何人都可以学会，它只不过是一个钩针而已。但是会有人去钩织一个夹着生菜和番茄的汉堡包<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%BD%E5%AD%90.md#1">帽子</a>吗？或者是一个半剥开的香蕉？ 会有人想到吗？”</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/4vm6beBcHp/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1165.8000000024913%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/0ltF-sBcP_/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A483.0000000001746%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/zDxIiPhcHB/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A491.199999996752%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><br />
通常在他拍摄的照片中，弗格森的脸上都流露出一种忧郁的神情，好像自己被打扮成一个大蘑菇一点也不好笑。</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/6CDRNCBcHZ/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1079.0000000015425%7D" width="658" b="864" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/3ssXr0BcLJ/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1084.6999999994296%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/wnggKLBcFY/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1107.800000001589%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>弗格森曾在珀斯科廷大学（Curtin University）学习美术，然后在2014年搬到了<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>。他说他刚到墨尔本时，并不认识这里搞艺术的人，做这些创意手工是为了结交朋友，而这也的确让他交到了很多朋友。</p>
<p>弗格森目前在Instagram上拥有近14万名粉丝。他强调说，一个人不会一下就能成为<ahref="https://github.com/ugwazd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BA%A4%E5%AA%92%E4%BD%93.md#1">社交媒体</a>的名人。他说： “如果你想引起人们的注意，就需要做一些出人意料的事情，并且还要做出名堂来。我很清楚我要做什么，为什么这么做，以及我怎么样能让观众有回应。如果你做的事情和别人一样，你就没有给观众一个追随你的机会，他们可能已经追随了500名插画师，或者一大群热门人物。”</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/wC8bvWhcA7/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A476.5000000006694%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/3-vKiyBcFp/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A482.89999999906286%7D" width="658" b="864" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/71--qRBcIN/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1109.3000000000757%7D" width="658" b="864" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>弗格森认为，独特性和连贯性是至关重要的。他说： “如果你分析一下，我不是一个大家预期会编织的人，而且我在照片中看起来很伤心，这很有趣，我知道如果我一直保持这样的形象，就会奏效，我并不是胡乱地发一些无聊的照片。”</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/_lT6e0BcAY/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A480.99999999976717%7D" width="658" b="886" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/4QwYo2hcPY/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=8&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A487.1000000021013%7D" width="658" b="820" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>今年2月，他在悉尼的澳洲设计中心 （Australian Design Centre） 举办了个人作品展。 现在，他在思考如何让自己的作品更具有表现力，而不仅仅只是戴个帽子照张相。弗格森已经开始钩织服装了。</p>
