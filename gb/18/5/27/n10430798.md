<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>窈窕淑女坐在“大锅盖”前 抬手敲击余音绕梁</h2>
<a href="https://d2oemtubh889hy.cloudfront.net/A3abFrtjb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dwbyin3339/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月29日讯】（大纪元记者许彦报导）窈窕淑女盘腿坐在一个“大锅盖”前，背景是繁茂的树林，她在做什么呢？耐心等待！当女子抬手，在“大锅盖”上开始敲击时，一阵<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%B1%81.md#1">天籁</a>般的音乐传来，让大家忍不住赞叹。</p>
<p>这种乐器叫做hang drum，中文名为<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E7%A2%9F%E9%BC%93.md#1">手碟鼓</a>。视频中演奏手碟鼓的是日本音乐家Yuki Koshimoto。她从2009年在伦敦第一次接触到这种乐器之后，便深深爱上了它。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_d-42.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_d-42-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430810" class="wp-caption-text">窈窕淑女盘腿坐在一个大锅盖前，背景是繁茂的树林。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>视频显示，随着Yuki Koshimoto双手敲击<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%8B%E7%A2%9F%E9%BC%93.md#1">手碟鼓</a>，清脆的乐声飘起，给人一种空灵深邃的感觉，令人的心恢复宁静。</p>
<p>听完手碟鼓的演奏，真有一种<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E9%9F%B3%E7%BB%95%E6%A2%81.md#1">余音绕梁</a>、三日不绝的感受。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span></p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-908123ff-eb34-4032-53dc-c340508ee737" data-id="player-908123ff-eb34-4032-53dc-c340508ee737"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/908123ff-eb34-4032-53dc-c340508ee737?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F5%2F27%2Fn10430798.md#1"></a>
<p>手碟鼓是一种形如飞碟的乐器，由两片氮化过的钢结合在一起，里面中空，经由敲击不同位置而发出不同的声音。</p>
<p>手碟鼓首次于2001年法兰克福音乐博览会亮相，最早是由瑞士音乐家Felix Rohner与Sabina Sch?rer创制的，这两人花了三十年时间研究钢板以及世界各地不同的乐器，最后推出手碟鼓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-05-14-at-11.16.17-AM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-05-14-at-11.16.17-AM-600x268.png" alt="" width="600" b="268" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430809" class="wp-caption-text">随着Yuki Koshimoto双手敲击手碟鼓，清脆的乐声飘起。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种鼓上面有很多凹点，正上方也有凸点，每个点都有一个被调过音的音高。下方则是有个洞，让乐器共鸣的空气可以流动，原理与小鼓的洞类似。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430807" class="wp-caption-text">手碟鼓是一种形如飞碟的乐器。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于手碟鼓制造商不多，以及制造此乐器的高需求和复杂性，这种乐器在市场上相当稀有，顶级生产商手上有很多等待名单，部分订单需后推到数年以后才能拿到，因此与其它乐器相比，价格昂贵很多。</p>
