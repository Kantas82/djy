<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>抢救新竹濒危食虫植物21年  关键一役在今日</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/84e10541b1f5595af1b49089f95a9fb2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>荒野保护协会自八十六年起至今，每个月都进入莲花寺湿地进行调查与复育工作。（荒野保护协会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者林宝云台湾新竹报导）位于西滨公路凤鼻隧道旁的<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%8A%B1%E5%AF%BA%E6%B9%BF%E5%9C%B0.md#1">莲花寺湿地</a>，是<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E5%8F%B6%E8%8C%85%E8%86%8F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">长叶茅膏菜</a>等濒临灭绝的<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E8%99%AB%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">食虫植物</a>及湿地植物的野外“唯一栖地”，自八十六年起，<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%92%E9%87%8E%E4%BF%9D%E6%8A%A4%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">荒野保护协会</a>即在此地做<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E8%82%B2.md#1">复育</a>至今。</p>
<p>目前内政部营建署暂定为地方级重要湿地，但国防部以严重影响营区射击任务使用的理由，建请剔除。内政部重要湿地审议小组将于12/25针对该湿地再评定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10423135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10423135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/d981a8020433a16571ad1153271bd188.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10423135 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/d981a8020433a16571ad1153271bd188-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10423135" class="wp-caption-text">新竹濒危<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E8%99%AB%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">食虫植物</a>。<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%92%E9%87%8E%E4%BF%9D%E6%8A%A4%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">荒野保护协会</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>荒野保护协会自八十六年起，每个月都进入<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%8A%B1%E5%AF%BA%E6%B9%BF%E5%9C%B0.md#1">莲花寺湿地</a>进行调查与<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8D%E8%82%B2.md#1">复育</a>工作，至今已二十一年。理事长刘月梅表示，该湿地共有七种珍稀植物，除了<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E5%8F%B6%E8%8C%85%E8%86%8F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">长叶茅膏菜</a>、小毛毡苔、宽叶毛毡苔、长距挖耳草等四种食虫植物，还有桃园草、点头飘拂草、田葱等三种湿地植物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10423132" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10423132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4198b990ac5a0460a942538988d4a345.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10423132 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4198b990ac5a0460a942538988d4a345-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10423132" class="wp-caption-text">荒野保护协会自八十六年起至今，每个月都进入莲花寺湿地进行调查与复育工作。（荒野保护协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>莲花寺湿地是长叶茅膏菜唯一野外生育地，也是“台湾特有种植物”桃园草唯一的野外栖地，数量不到100棵，严重低于稳定族群所需的400棵植株，一旦莲花寺湿地不保，代表桃园草即将灭绝。刘月梅与荒野保护协会新竹分会分会长张正敏共同呼吁，希望大家能够重视并一起抢救新竹濒危食虫植物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10423133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10423133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20482403f68f63016eb6edc75ad368c7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10423133 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20482403f68f63016eb6edc75ad368c7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10423133" class="wp-caption-text">新竹濒危食虫植物。（荒野保护协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
