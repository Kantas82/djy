<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>福建厦门暴雨来袭 一女子险被洪水冲走</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月08日讯】周一（7日），<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%A6%E9%97%A8.md#1">厦门</a>遭遇今年以来首场特大<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%B4%E9%9B%A8.md#1">暴雨</a>，一名女生在行走时被暴雨形成的洪水冲走，所幸被救起；还有人不得不划着皮艇出行。</p>
<p>据中国天气网5月7日消息，<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A6%8F%E5%BB%BA.md#1">福建</a>多地遭遇<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%B4%E9%9B%A8.md#1">暴雨</a>袭击，<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%A6%E9%97%A8.md#1">厦门</a>已发布全省首个暴雨红色预警。预计，8日、9日厦门、漳州等仍有明显降雨，局地暴雨。由于雨势凶猛，导致厦门多地道路积水严重，厦门环岛路部分沙滩塌方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10371094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/aaaFotoJet.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10371094 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/aaaFotoJet-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371094" class="wp-caption-text">厦门暴雨来袭，环岛路沙滩塌方。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据报，受暴雨影响，厦门岛内多处道路封闭。截至7日14时，厦门机场因天气原因，共取消航班10架次，15个进港航班备降福州，118个进出港航班延误。</p>
<p>由于短时间内强降水，厦门多地出现内涝，市民出行和日常生活受到影响，甚至出现了划着皮艇出行的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10371024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371024" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/440beca5gy1fr38mm2qdzj20zk0qon20.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10371024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/440beca5gy1fr38mm2qdzj20zk0qon20.jpg" alt="" width="440" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371024" class="wp-caption-text">厦门内涝，市民划着皮艇出行。（微博图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10371037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371037" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/321Untitled_meitu_2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10371037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/321Untitled_meitu_2.jpg" alt="" width="428" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371037" class="wp-caption-text">厦门内涝，市民划着皮艇出行。（微博图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有的地方洪水淹没到胸口。有网民调侃：“厦门现在分为两个区：一个湖里，一个海里。”</p>
<p>一段网传视频显示，一名打着雨伞的女生在街头的汪洋中行走时，直接被暴雨形成的洪水冲走，在浊水中沉浮，所幸几名男生赶上将其拉住。有猜测说事发地为厦门大学。事后，厦门大学回应称，该视频内容并非发生在厦大，但目前官方尚没有提供事发具体地点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10371058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371058" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/eeeFotoJet_meitu_3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10371058 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/eeeFotoJet_meitu_3-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371058" class="wp-caption-text">一名打着雨伞的女生在行走时被暴雨形成的洪水冲走，所幸被救起。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>暴雨过后，不少路段出现了大量活鱼，也有人在暴雨的间歇期忙着摸鱼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10371067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/wwwFotoJet_meitu_4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10371067 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/wwwFotoJet_meitu_4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371067" class="wp-caption-text">暴雨过后，有人在街头忙着抓鱼。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《厦门日报》官方微信7日下午发布的图片显示，厦门大学校园内多处积水严重，石阶出现“瀑布”，底层学生宿舍、教室被淹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10371070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10371070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/FFUntitled_meitu_1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10371070 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/FFUntitled_meitu_1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10371070" class="wp-caption-text">厦门大学宿舍石阶形成“瀑布”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>强降雨已导致包括厦门大学、厦门城市职业学院在内的部分学校停课。#</p>
