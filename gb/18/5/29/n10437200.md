<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月31日讯】想必各位一定有坐过<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E9%9C%84%E9%A3%9E%E8%BD%A6.md#1">云霄飞车</a>、海盗船，各位起初的心情都是怎么样呢？好奇、兴奋、害怕、紧张，可能主要就这些感受吧？</p>
<p>影片中的一个男子进行<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E9%9C%84%E9%A3%9E%E8%BD%A6.md#1">云霄飞车</a>“<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E4%BD%93%E9%AA%8C.md#1">初体验</a>”，哈哈，不知各位有没有像他这样害怕，整个男人雄壮威武的特质尽失。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10437647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10437647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/531bb9e29a0ab2bd014710fdda987bc4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10437647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/531bb9e29a0ab2bd014710fdda987bc4-600x473.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10437647" class="wp-caption-text">两男子表情成强烈对比。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还没开始快速移动时，他用惊慌<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E5%8F%AB.md#1">尖叫</a>的方式说话，一直快速吐气放松那完全放松不了的心情，并且单手抓住旁边比较稳重的男子之手，头也紧依偎着。</p>
<p>云霄飞车开始开始移动了！男子马上双手紧抱身旁男子惨叫，啊──────！很快的，已经怕到叫不出声音，只剩惊恐的表情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10437629" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10437629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/e563f0e1cab0798db8249f9d4c1b137b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10437629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/e563f0e1cab0798db8249f9d4c1b137b-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10437629" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%96%E5%8F%AB.md#1">尖叫</a>中的男子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喔，不对，原来是降到平缓的地方才这样呀，等到又遇到<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%BA%E6%BF%80.md#1">刺激</a>了的路线，马上又尖叫了，啊───好惨烈的声音啊，简直形象崩毁了啊！</p>
