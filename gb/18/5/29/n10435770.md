<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>傻眼！有“贼”若此 遭祸店家啼笑皆非</h2>
<a href="https://d3qwx9e7uaergm.cloudfront.net/S2dcdTPno"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vknsbz352/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/vknsbz352/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月31日讯】<span class="s1">遇到<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%81%B7.md#1">小偷</a>或<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A2%E5%8C%AA.md#1">抢匪</a>可不是开玩笑的事，但是这段影片里的“<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BC.md#1">贼</a>”，却有着令人忍不住嘴角上扬的趣味性。怎么回事？让我们来看看。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">首先，超市里一名女性顾客要结账，当她将纸钞与硬币放在收银台上时，一只小<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>突然飞了过来，停在一旁；当老板忙着帮前一位客人打包商品时，冷不防，这只鸟就叼走了纸钞！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10435785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435785" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-1-20.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10435785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-1-20.jpg" alt="" width="504" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435785" class="wp-caption-text">小<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>飞到收银台上。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">大家全都看傻了眼，难道鸟儿也识货，知道钱可以买东西吗？也可能它只是想把花花绿绿的纸钞拿去筑巢而已，哈哈。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">接着，是一种让人好气又好笑的野生动物──<ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%B4%E5%AD%90.md#1">猴子</a>。因为它们的聪明总是用在令人困扰的地方，例如影片里那只自行开门闯进店家，“洗劫”店里财物的猴崽子。说到这点，高雄的柴山也经常出现台湾猕猴抢夺学生的早餐，或是溜进住家偷走食物等。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10435787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10435787" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-2-8.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10435787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-2-8.jpg" alt="" width="503" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10435787" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/vknsbz352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%B4%E5%AD%90.md#1">猴子</a>闯进店家洗劫。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">由于台湾猕猴为保育类动物，因此居民无法出手伤及它们，然而人猴冲突愈演愈烈，不知道是否有和平相处的最佳解决之道呢？</span></p>
