<h2 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>一、鳄梨<ahref="https://github.com/mjerqg3317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE%E4%BB%81.md#1">虾仁</a>酱</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_10439273" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/911d24097b1b5d9e14519c954021792c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10439273 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/911d24097b1b5d9e14519c954021792c-450x420.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美味的鳄梨虾仁酱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>食材：</strong></p>
<p>1鳄梨（酪梨）<br />
1柠檬<br />
1汤匙黄油<br />
1杯虾<br />
?杯洋葱<br />
?杯绿色红辣椒<br />
1/2杯奶油奶酪<br />
盐、胡椒<br />
面包</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1.? 我们先准备鳄梨，纵向切穿，两手往相反方向扭，分成两半。用刀轻敲籽核，很容易清除。用刀直接将鳄梨肉上划格子，尽量不要削破鳄梨皮。用勺子挖出，就成了鳄梨小丁！放入碗中，捣碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10439185" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/88606c905658dba59f32367f90edcc8b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10439185 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/88606c905658dba59f32367f90edcc8b-450x422.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="422" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用刀直接将鳄梨肉上划格子，用勺子挖出，就成了鳄梨小丁！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.? 在热煎锅中加入四分之一条黄油。入虾仁稍煎至金黄色。取出虾，切成小块。<br />
3.? 将虾粒加入鳄梨泥中，然后加入蒜末、红绿甜椒、奶油芝士、盐与胡椒粉，混合均匀。<br />
4.? 以摄氏230度烘烤面包5分钟。<br />
5.? 面包切片，将鳄梨鲜虾酱抹上，鲜美醇郁！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>二、烤<ahref="https://github.com/mjerqg3317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a>加鳄梨、<ahref="https://github.com/mjerqg3317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E6%A6%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">石榴果</a></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_10439331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/f0a17a677bf18f39e53662aecb50ce4b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10439331 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/f0a17a677bf18f39e53662aecb50ce4b-450x426.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="426" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤地瓜加鳄梨、石榴果，鲜艳欲滴。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>食材：</strong></p>
<p>1地瓜<br />
橄榄油<br />
1鳄梨<br />
1柠檬<br />
?杯洋葱<br />
1/2杯番茄<br />
盐、胡椒<br />
1杯石榴籽</p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 准备鳄梨，方式和鳄梨鲜虾酱一样。<br />
2. 地瓜切成两半，两面撒上橄榄油，以摄氏205度烘烤45分钟。<br />
3. 加入柠檬挤汁、切碎的洋葱和番茄。最后以盐和胡椒调味。搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10439211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/085ccdd08191925a7b7a2cfeb711959d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10439211 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/085ccdd08191925a7b7a2cfeb711959d-450x419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="419" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">轻刮烤软的地瓜，以增加口感的层次，并挖出一个凹槽等一下放鳄梨酱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 轻刮烤软的地瓜，以丰富质感，并挖出一个凹槽，上面撒上盐和胡椒粉，以摄氏205度烘烤10分钟。<br />
5. 将鳄梨酱填入凹槽，上面撒上艳红的石榴籽，既可以单独食用，也可以用来涂抹恢复柔软的面包哦。</p>
