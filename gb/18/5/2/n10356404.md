<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>倾倒有害物品？市府为你指定地点和时间</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月03日讯】（大纪元<ahref="https://github.com/imggnv371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A5%E5%A4%AA%E5%8D%8E.md#1">渥太华</a>记者林妲编译报导）从2018年4月开始到10月底期间，渥太华市民可在以下周日的8点至4点，到市府指定的废物处理仓库倾到化学品垃圾和其他有害废料：<br />
</p>
<li>April 29, 2018，OC Transpo Park and Ride，1201 Trim Road</li>
<li>May 6, 2018，Tunney&#8217;s Pasture Sir Frederick Banting Drive</li>
<li>June 3, 2018，Waste Connections of Canada，3354 Navan Road</li>
<li>June 24, 2018，OC Transpo Park and Ride，3355 Fallowfield Road</li>
<li>August 19, 2018，Kanata Research Park，411 Legget Drive</li>
<li>September 16, 2018，Tunney&#8217;s Pasture，Sir Frederick Banting Drive</li>
<li>September 30, 2018，OC Transpo Park and Ride，1201 Trim Road,</li>
<li>October 27, 2018，Barrhaven Snow Disposal Facility</li>
<p>有害废料特指：气溶胶容器、丙烷气瓶、消毒剂、荧光灯泡/管、灭火器罐、化肥和杀虫剂、水银温度计、针头和注射器、药品、油漆和涂料、烤箱和窗户清洁剂以及游泳池清洁剂。</p>
