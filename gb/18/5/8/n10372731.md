<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>玻璃屋café</h2>
<a href="https://d1ed4zj8eeit74.cloudfront.net/Q6zUSYohy"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/emljlk376/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/emljlk376/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月08日讯】我很喜欢光线充足、舒适的餐厅，最好有窗户可以让自然光可以照射到室内，这样坐着就会舒适又写意。可惜香港不仅楼价惊人，连租金也一样高得吓人，所以很多餐厅，特别是新式的餐厅面积都是小小的，虽然很精致，但是没有窗户，而且为了打造气氛通常灯光也调得比较暗。</p>
<p>最近我发现到这间又大又多窗户，还有超高楼底的餐厅，餐厅面积不但很大，还有很多不同的坐位，四周都很有特色，并有一整排落地玻璃，而且还有日式榻榻米座位呢！从外面看好像一间玻璃屋子一样，真的很配这间餐厅的名字“<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8B%E5%AD%90%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB.md#1">屋子生活</a>”。我当然第一时间约朋友去试试！进入餐厅立刻选了一张靠近窗户的榻榻米坐下，超大的落地玻璃不会被遮挡，往外看视野非常广阔，自然光透入感觉微暖很舒服。餐厅很贴心每张台的地下都会有插座，我还看到有一桌，两个学生坐着一起用手机电脑，一起做功课，有插座，便不怕电子产品没有电啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10375394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375394" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082350431366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375394" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082350431366.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375394" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅有一整排落地玻璃，而且还有日式榻榻米座位呢！从外面看好像一间玻璃屋子一样。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>放在桌面上的餐牌也很有特色，外观是练习簿，在背面还有九因歌呢！打开餐牌里面除了食物名称之外，还有手绘图画，真的很可爱有趣呢！里面还标记了哪些是人气食物，我们点了两个人气套餐加一个沙拉和两款甜品。</p>
<p>先上来的是公园沙拉，酱料是日式沙律醋颇醒胃，沙律菜的新鲜度不及番茄，番茄入口新鲜爽甜，猪肉有点粗糙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10375398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349551366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375398" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349551366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375398" class="wp-caption-text">公园沙拉。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着就是宫城明太子饭团+值日饭团餐，和七味鸡唐扬定食。饭团味道没什么特别，七味鸡炸得皮脆肉嫩，配上七味粉味道更显突出，好吃！套餐配的味增汤很浓郁，喜欢喝味增汤的朋友一定喜欢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10375404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349401366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10375404 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349401366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375404" class="wp-caption-text">宫城明太子饭团+值日饭。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10375406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349311366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375406" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349311366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375406" class="wp-caption-text">宫城明太子饭团+值日饭团餐，套餐有配一个沙拉。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10375417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349341366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375417" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349341366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375417" class="wp-caption-text">七味鸡唐扬定食套餐配的味增汤很浓郁，还有一碗添加了日式饭素的白饭。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10375429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349371366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375429" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349371366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375429" class="wp-caption-text">七味鸡炸得皮脆肉嫩。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10375452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349511366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10375452" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349511366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375452" class="wp-caption-text">苹果茶（左）及抹绿奶茶。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后就要试这间餐厅有名的甜品了！甜品对于我这个个甜品迷一定不会错过，先试吃综合鲜果窝夫配柚子雪葩，柚子雪葩上用鲜果拼成了一个卡通的脸很可爱，窝夫外脆内软还有蛋香，柚子雪葩很香柚子味，真的超好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10375464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349471366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10375464 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349471366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375464" class="wp-caption-text">柚子雪葩。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后是刚焗好便立端上还胀鼓鼓的伯爵红茶梳乎厘，配上香草雪糕，梳乎厘有很香的伯爵茶味道，而且甜度适中，香草雪糕内有新鲜香草籽，好味道！餐厅甜品真是很出色，来这间餐厅一定要点来吃呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10375466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10375466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349441366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10375466 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805082349441366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10375466" class="wp-caption-text">伯爵红茶梳乎厘配香草雪糕。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里坐得舒服有特色又多适合拍照打卡的位置，当天我轻轻轻松松就坐了两个多小时，渡过了一个悠闲下午。提醒大家如果在繁忙时间的话，这间餐厅入坐起，每桌限时90分钟，大家可以跟我一样选一个悠闲的下午，与三五知己共聚啊！#</p>
