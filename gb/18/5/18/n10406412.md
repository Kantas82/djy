<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超暖！猫咪无助哀号 勇男跳入冰冻海水救援</h2>
<a href="https://do0q2uqs2ncwe.cloudfront.net/N4eMU7k2v"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/irqzmd3895/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/irqzmd3895/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9595887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月19日讯】（大纪元记者林海柔报导）在冰冻的海面上传来<ahref="https://github.com/irqzmd3895/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>咪害怕的叫声，有人看到货船外面靠近船尾附近有一只猫，一位男士勇敢下水游泳前去救援。在上岸前，猫咪发抖的尖叫声让人听了很心疼，可见海水温度异常冰冷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406542" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_d-26.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10406542 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_d-26-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406542" class="wp-caption-text">男子朝货船游去，准备救援<ahref="https://github.com/irqzmd3895/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB.md#1">猫</a>咪。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>货船停靠在<ahref="https://github.com/irqzmd3895/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0.md#1">乌克兰</a>港口伊利乔夫斯克（Illichivsk），有一只猫咪不知怎么跑到船尾冰冷的海水中，不知该如何回到岸上，它害怕地大声喵叫。</p>
<p>还好有人听到猫咪求救声。一位男子不畏冰冷勇敢下海救猫，他身穿御寒救生衣，在冰冻海水中游泳，当时海水温度极冷，不论对人或是猫咪来说，在冰冻海水中游泳容易发生危险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/9-1-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10406548 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/9-1-2-600x324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406548" class="wp-caption-text">男子身穿御寒救身衣，在冰冻海水中游泳也不怕。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在冰冻的海水中游泳会面临什么情况呢？如果没有御寒救生衣贸然在冰水中游泳，很有可能呼吸困难或是冻伤，更严重的情况是肢体结冻，失去知觉……</p>
<p>男子身穿救身衣在海中缓慢前进，猫咪看着男子离自己越来越近，惊慌失措，更加大声地喵喵叫。最后男子一把抱起猫咪往回游，他一边游泳一边贴心地想将猫咪举高，无奈猫咪还是免不了浸泡冰冷海水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-1-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10406557 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-1-5-600x351.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406557" class="wp-caption-text">男子将猫咪放在胸前，用仰式游回岸边。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>男子将猫咪放在胸前，用仰式往回游，猫咪身上没有防护衣，身体碰到冰冷海水想必冷得发抖，那趟回程中，猫咪断断续续尖叫不停。</p>
<p>到达岸边时，猫咪一跃而上，大概因为全身湿冷吹风有些受不了吧，它颤抖的尖叫声，让人感觉到它的承受力快到极限了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406588" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10406588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-1-4-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406588" class="wp-caption-text">猫咪到达岸边时，它颤抖的尖叫声，表示冷得快受不了了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>众人纷纷靠近支援，另一名男子下来帮忙，他抱起猫咪首先想到的是，藏在外套里，让它感受到自己体温好过些，然而，双手抱紧猫咪之后面临一个问题，该怎么爬上去呢？有些难度！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-1-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10406604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7-1-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406604" class="wp-caption-text">一位男子将猫咪抱在怀里保暖，众人一起将他拉上来。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友纷纷留言赞美他“真是一位大暖男”，幸好有其他人帮忙拉上来，最终<ahref="https://github.com/irqzmd3895/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E7%8C%AB.md#1">救援猫</a>咪大获成功！究竟猫咪是如何待在海上，跑到船身外呢？至今依然是个谜！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10406623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10406623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/8-1-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10406623 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/8-1-1-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10406623" class="wp-caption-text">可爱的黑白猫咪，幸好遇到一群好心人救了它。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
