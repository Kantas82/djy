<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一对狮子扑倒非洲水牛大快朵颐 看剧情如何反转</h2>
<a href="https://d2bd0i85jg8bcc.cloudfront.net/O8yRAoxkb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jtvmbo3495/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/jtvmbo3495/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9595887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月17日讯】有一天，一对<ahref="https://github.com/jtvmbo3495/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>发现一头离群的水牛，心中大乐。它们理所应当地认为当日的晚餐解决了，却没想到剧情发生反转。那么，当这对狮子正在大快朵颐的时候，遭遇了什么变故呢？</p>
<p>这段视频中的拍摄地是南非胡德斯普雷特（Hoedspruit）的蒂姆巴瓦蒂私人自然保护区（Timbavati Private Nature Reserve），由实习公园管理员兰恩（Oliver Lane）拍摄。当时拍摄者在距离25米远的地方，目睹了一群<ahref="https://github.com/jtvmbo3495/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2%E6%B0%B4%E7%89%9B.md#1">非洲水牛</a>从<ahref="https://github.com/jtvmbo3495/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>口中救下一头负伤水牛的惊险场面。</p>
<p>视频开始，一对狮子在跟踪一头水牛。水牛并不惧怕狮子，反而转身面对狮子，狮子被吓得倒退几步。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10403449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10403449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_e-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10403449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_e-5-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10403449" class="wp-caption-text">狮子发现一头离群的水牛，心中大乐。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着剧情发展，狮子在水牛的威慑下并未放弃，它们围着水牛伺机发动攻击。水牛也没有退缩，双方对峙了数秒钟。</p>
<p>突然，狮子一跃而起，扑到水牛背上啃咬，水牛努力想挣脱背上的狮子未果，反而被狮子扑倒在地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10403456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10403456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-04-30-at-11.15.25-PM-copy.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10403456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-04-30-at-11.15.25-PM-copy-600x370.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10403456" class="wp-caption-text">狮子和水牛对峙。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水牛也很勇猛，翻身起来再次试图挣脱狮子。这时，另一只狮子也跃上水牛尾部啃咬，终于水牛不敌两只狮子的进攻，四脚朝天倒在地上，任由狮子在身上啃咬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10403461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10403461" style="width: 551px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Capture-25.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10403461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Capture-25.jpg" alt="" width="551" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10403461" class="wp-caption-text">两只狮子即将扑倒水牛。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两只狮子扑倒水牛后，高兴地享受美餐。这头水牛眼看就要命丧狮口，发出痛苦的呻吟。</p>
<p>在这千钧一发之际，一群水牛赶了过来，驱赶行凶的狮子。其中一只狮子起初不想放弃到嘴美食，继续啃咬受伤的水牛。但最终，在这群水牛围攻下，狮子落荒而逃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10403463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10403463" style="width: 553px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Capture1-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10403463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Capture1-4.jpg" alt="" width="553" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10403463" class="wp-caption-text">在这千钧一发之际，一群水牛赶了过来，驱赶行凶的狮子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水牛拥有非洲草原上最厉害的犄角，这也是狮子最怕的。面对体型庞大的<ahref="https://github.com/jtvmbo3495/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2%E6%B0%B4%E7%89%9B.md#1">非洲水牛</a>，狮子也有“阴沟翻船”的可能。非洲水牛的求生本能也极强，大有“舍得一身剐，敢把皇帝拉下马”的勇气。</p>
<p>在非洲的动物世界里，狮子是杀死非洲水牛最多的动物，而非洲水牛又是杀死狮子最多的动物，它们是天生一对强劲对手。</p>
