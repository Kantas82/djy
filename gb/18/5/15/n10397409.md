<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>豪宅欣赏系列 ─ 来自西海岸之风的设计灵感</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月16日讯】宅邸位于最让人艳羡的社区，如同一块建筑宝石般拥有精致的景色和壮阔的峡谷景观，拥有约8,000英尺的居住空间。建筑的设计灵感来自西海岸之风。包豪斯风格的大门，带入足够的光源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10397486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10397486" style="width: 568px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-85.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10397486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-85-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="568" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10397486" class="wp-caption-text">（Barry Cohen 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10397490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10397490" style="width: 567px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-42.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10397490 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-42.jpg" alt="" width="567" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10397490" class="wp-caption-text">（Barry Cohen 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10397491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10397491" style="width: 575px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-40.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10397491 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-40.jpg" alt="" width="575" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10397491" class="wp-caption-text">（Barry Cohen 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>门厅的特色是天花板的教堂穹顶式设计，以及坚固的IPE 大门，玻璃壁灯、报警器、家庭自动化系统、砖石地板、射灯、IPE镶板墙、定制的内嵌胡桃木衣橱，还有可以俯瞰远景的广阔落地窗。豪华的客厅可以通往厨房和餐厅，有宽板硬木地板、射灯，覆盖整个墙壁的现代风格的内嵌式玻璃燃气壁炉，尽显禅风。直达天花板的落地窗，还有宽敞的包豪斯风格大门，屋内处处皆可俯瞰花园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10397488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10397488" style="width: 415px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-71.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10397488 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-71.jpg" alt="" width="415" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10397488" class="wp-caption-text">（Barry Cohen 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10397492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10397492" style="width: 567px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6-26.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10397492 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/6-26.jpg" alt="" width="567" b="350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10397492" class="wp-caption-text">（Barry Cohen 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美食厨房配置特色宽板硬木地板、定制的实木橱柜、垃圾处理箱以及充足的存储空间，灯光及定制吊顶、人造石台面、内置储藏室、不锈钢Blanco品牌的水槽和现代的可伸缩的水龙头，无不衬托奢华之风。可俯瞰花园的窗户和宽敞的瀑布岛早餐吧台，让每日的就餐时光成为了品味生活之一隅。</p>
