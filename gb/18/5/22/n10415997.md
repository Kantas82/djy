<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一只猎鹰追逐数万只鸟群 场面如电影般魔幻</h2>
<a href="https://d12ogqhrsnt8ma.cloudfront.net/L2c5ZDUmu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/idzwdk333/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10327568 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年05月30日讯】大<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6.md#1">自然</a>总有各种神奇景观。这天，成千上万<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8B%E9%B8%9F.md#1">椋鸟</a>在晚霞的天空中群聚飞舞，队形变幻无穷，壮丽又震撼。</p>
<p>若配上电影《哈利波特》的背景音乐，仿佛飞散的沙尘聚之成形，魔幻、神秘；而配上电音舞曲，仿佛一阵阵光影，随着重低音效忽聚忽散，波动不停，又如光影水舞秀般，交织成美丽的视觉艺术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416045" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416045" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155535.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416045" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155535-600x511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416045" class="wp-caption-text">成千上万<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8B%E9%B8%9F.md#1">椋鸟</a>在晚霞的天空中群聚飞舞。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而这如梦似幻的椋鸟齐飞画面，其中竟藏着一个秘密。</p>
<p>仔细一看，椋鸟群中一只“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E9%B9%B0.md#1">猎鹰</a>”来回穿梭，看来，这庞大阵形吸引了猎鹰目光。它试图利用这机会抓住一群猎物，猎鹰扑向鸟儿，鸟儿立即飞散开来，随即变化队形，一来一往间，“单枪匹马”的猎鹰依旧不敌“万马奔腾”的椋鸟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_160148.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_160148-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416058" class="wp-caption-text">仔细一看，椋鸟群中一只“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E9%B9%B0.md#1">猎鹰</a>”来回穿梭，看来，这庞大阵形吸引了猎鹰目光。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>椋鸟，台湾称为“紫翅椋鸟”，而日本称作“星椋鸟”，椋鸟的习性经常群聚成群，数十到数千，多则甚至数十万只以上，专家认为，数量庞大椋鸟群聚一同飞行是为了御敌。</p>
<p>在台湾成长的人们可能难以想像这种壮观场面，在欧洲国家则经常出现，它们的夜栖地常离居住区不远，嘈杂又臭气冲天，让不少当地人难忍皱眉。而在丹麦，黑压压一大片椋鸟，甚至被称为“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%A4%AA%E9%98%B3.md#1">黑太阳</a>”的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6.md#1">自然</a>奇景，每年吸引数以万计的观光客观赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416097" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155634.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155634-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416097" class="wp-caption-text">在丹麦，黑压压一大片椋鸟被称为“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E5%A4%AA%E9%98%B3.md#1">黑太阳</a>”的自然奇景。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>历史上也有不少因椋鸟而发生的麻烦，譬如1949年伦敦成群椋鸟在“大笨钟”的时针上落脚时，竟让老钟一度停摆；还有曾发生椋鸟被飞机引擎吸入，造成坠机的惨剧。尽管如此，椋鸟群飞如电影般壮丽、魔幻的画面，还是令人啧啧称奇。</p>
