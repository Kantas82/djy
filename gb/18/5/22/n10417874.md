<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>核桃修平划痕？小兵立大功！9个清洁小窍门</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7d3347b91b692b278ba6fc6a4513bb06-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小兵也可以立大功。赶快来看一看这些随手可得、花不了几文钱的实用小窍门。（视频截图/大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10327568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）什么？用核桃来修平橱柜门面？用口香糖可以清理耳塞？赶快来学一学……</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1. 橱柜的门被刮得一道一道？来几颗核桃。</strong></span></p>
<p>先吃点心再做事吗？别扯了！哇，用核桃在柜子门上擦，再以圆圈方式运动晕开——光泽可人！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420808" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled22.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10420808 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled22-450x431.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420808" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">2. 水龙头堵塞，滴不出水？<ahref="https://github.com/tjoxzl3456/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%86%8B.md#1">白醋</a>仙子伴随<ahref="https://github.com/tjoxzl3456/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93.md#1">小苏打</a>来也！</span></strong></p>
<p>加点水用袋子整个包好扎紧。静置一会，剪开袋子，绽亮如新！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420807" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled21-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10420807 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled21-2-450x422.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420807" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">3. 耳机用久了，耳塞部分难免看来有点恶心。</span></strong></p>
<p>别急，有口香糖吗？借用一下。拨一小块这样按捏一下，清洁溜溜！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420809" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled23-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10420809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled23-2-450x426.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="426" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420809" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">4. 油拌入<ahref="https://github.com/tjoxzl3456/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93.md#1">小苏打</a>，不是要烘焙，而是……</span></strong></p>
<p>看，小朋友的贴纸到处都是，该清理了。就这样涂上去，然后不费吹灰之力就可以抹掉，您又有个干净的新桌子啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420810" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10420810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled24-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420810" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">5. 水池有异味了？</span></strong></p>
<p>没问题。看肉桂粉来帮忙。热水冲掉后，嗯，芳香可亲！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">6. 唉呀，粉白的墙壁被刮脏了！</span></strong></p>
<p>小菜一碟，再有请<ahref="https://github.com/tjoxzl3456/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%86%8B.md#1">白醋</a>仙子，如此这般喷上去，一擦就没事儿啦！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">7. 细瓶口造成清洗瓶子的挑战，这是常遇到的问题……</span></strong></p>
<p>用细砂子可以解决。像这样摇一摇，涮一涮。沙子的微粒可以帮助我们清理瓶子内部的每个角落！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10420812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled26-450x429.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420812" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>打扫家居，总是有触及不到的死角让人望尘莫及；到户外拍摄一些美景，唉，镜头搞脏了——锦囊中还有哪些妙计？……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10420813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10420813" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled27.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10420813 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Untitled27-450x427.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10420813" class="wp-caption-text">卷筒卫生纸轴也可以做这个用！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
