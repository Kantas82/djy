<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>头社丝瓜专区野放蜜蜂   黄金花海忙授粉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/7af11f08ac177b9defc4c6e97841310f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>头社活盆地丝瓜产销班第九班班长黄辰政（左4）及第十班班长李阿松（右1） 欣喜迎接25箱蜜蜂。（黄淑贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月22日讯】（大纪元记者林萌骞台湾南投报导）蜜蜂是田间最具授粉效率的昆虫，但因农药及气候变迁等不利因素，造成野外蜜蜂数量大幅减少。鱼池乡公所与代表会、农会合作，22日在日月潭<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E7%A4%BE.md#1">头社</a>活盆地野放25箱，约12万5千只蜜蜂，为40公顷夏季丝瓜生产专区担负授粉重任，帮助瓜农能有好收成。</p>
<p>日月潭<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%B4%E7%A4%BE.md#1">头社</a>活盆地为全国最高海拔丝瓜生产专区，夏季产量占全国之半。因瓜类花粉粒黏重，不易靠风传播，需借助蜜蜂授粉才有高结果率及良好果型。</p>
<p>今年乡公所、代表会及农会三方合作，共补助5个丝瓜产销班购买25箱蜜蜂，平均放置于丝瓜田间野放，利用蜂只吸吮花蜜自然受粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416477" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/18/5/22/n10416472.md#1/%e9%a0%ad%e7%a4%be%e6%b4%bb%e7%9b%86%e5%9c%b0%e9%87%8e%e6%94%be25%e7%ae%b1%e8%9c%9c%e8%9c%82%ef%bc%8c%e6%af%8f%e7%ae%b1%e7%b4%84%e6%9c%895%e5%8d%83%e9%9a%bb%e8%9c%9c%e8%9c%82%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10416477"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10416477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/737ceda37543339b6d8c7f0f85f3ad60-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416477" class="wp-caption-text">头社活盆地野放25箱蜜蜂，每箱约有5千只蜜蜂。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鱼池乡农会理事长刘启行表示，以近几年的野放经验，蜜蜂对丝瓜农的帮助相当大，提高了丝瓜结果率3至5成。另一项值得欣喜的是，去年野放的蜜蜂有部分存活下来了，相对于前几年的<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E6%94%BE%E8%9C%9C%E8%9C%82.md#1">野放蜜蜂</a>几乎全部阵亡，头社农友对友善环境的认识已有提升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416476" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/18/5/22/n10416472.md#1/%e9%ad%9a%e6%b1%a0%e9%84%89%e8%be%b2%e6%9c%83%e7%b8%bd%e5%b9%b9%e4%ba%8b%e7%8e%8b%e5%a8%81%e6%96%87%ef%bc%88%e5%b7%a61%ef%bc%89%e3%80%81%e7%90%86%e4%ba%8b%e9%95%b7%e5%8a%89%e5%95%9f%e8%a1%8c%ef%bc%88" rel="attachment wp-att-10416476"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10416476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2c70a6c05196b7f5d6b21787b40a66e8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416476" class="wp-caption-text">鱼池乡农会总干事王威文（左1）、理事长刘启行（右1）及鱼池乡长陈锦伦（右2）共同参与头社活盆地蜜蜂野放。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鱼池乡长陈锦伦指出，由于气候变迁及农友用药问题，让野生蜜蜂锐减，丝瓜产量也降低许多，经过近年来的野放措施，弥补大自然中的蜜蜂数量，提高授粉绿，使得产量回升，同时也让农友们意识到安全用药及生态环保的重要。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/18/5/22/n10416472.md#1/%e8%9c%9c%e8%9c%82%e5%bf%99%e8%91%97%e5%88%b0%e4%b8%8b%e4%b8%80%e7%9b%ae%e6%a8%99%e7%82%ba%e7%b5%b2%e7%93%9c%e6%8e%88%e7%b2%89%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10416475"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10416475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/d35580cbe53c8eb30866404382d54acb-450x365.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416475" class="wp-caption-text">蜜蜂忙着到下一目标为丝瓜授粉。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10416474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/18/5/22/n10416472.md#1/%e8%9c%9c%e8%9c%82%e5%bf%99%e8%91%97%e7%82%ba%e7%b5%b2%e7%93%9c%e6%8e%88%e7%b2%89%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-10416474"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10416474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/ee1285446c4d5cd220bd5ca4c512070e-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416474" class="wp-caption-text">蜜蜂忙着为丝瓜授粉。（黄淑贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>头社活盆地丝瓜产销班第九班班长黄辰政及第十班班长李阿松，都是种植丝瓜28年的资深瓜农，他们特别能感受蜜蜂为丝瓜授粉的重要性，有了蜜蜂的帮忙，丝瓜结果才会多，产量才能提高。</p>
