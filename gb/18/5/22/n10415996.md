<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一只猎鹰飞入黑压压鸟群中 竟交织成电影奇幻场面</h2>
<a href="https://d3twnfu6dd6xpm.cloudfront.net/O0xHyhsSo"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/idzwdk333/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月28日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6.md#1">大自然</a>总有各种<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E5%A5%87%E6%99%AF%E8%A7%82.md#1">神奇景观</a>。成千上万椋鸟在晚霞的天空中群聚飞舞，队形变幻无穷，壮丽又震撼。</p>
<p>若配上<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E5%88%A9%E6%B3%A2%E7%89%B9.md#1">哈利波特</a>背景<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90.md#1">音乐</a>，仿佛飞散的沙尘聚之成形，魔幻神秘；而配上电音舞曲，仿佛一阵阵光影，随着重低音效忽聚忽散波动不停，又如光影水舞秀般，交织成美丽的视觉艺术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416047" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155555.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416047" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155555-600x620.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="620" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416047" class="wp-caption-text">成千上万椋鸟在晚霞的天空中群聚飞舞，队形变幻无穷，壮丽又震撼。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而这如梦似幻的椋鸟齐飞画面，其中竟藏着一个秘密。</p>
<p>仔细一看，椋鸟群中一只“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E9%B9%B0.md#1">猎鹰</a>”来回穿梭，看来，这庞大阵形吸引了猎鹰目光。它试图利用这机会抓住一群猎物，猎鹰扑向鸟儿，鸟儿立即飞散开来，随即变化队形，一来一往间，“单枪匹马”的猎鹰依旧不敌“万马奔腾”的椋鸟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_160148.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_160148-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416058" class="wp-caption-text">仔细一看，椋鸟群中一只“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%8E%E9%B9%B0.md#1">猎鹰</a>”来回穿梭，看来，这庞大阵形吸引了猎鹰目光。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，椋鸟经常群聚成群，数十到数千，多则甚至数十万只以上，专家认为，数量庞大椋鸟群聚飞行是为了御敌，而这画面却如电影般壮丽魔幻，令人啧啧称奇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10416064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10416064" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155713.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10416064" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2018-05-22_155713-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10416064" class="wp-caption-text">椋鸟经常群聚成群，数十到数千，多则甚至数十万只以上，专家认为，数量庞大椋鸟群聚飞行是为了御敌。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
