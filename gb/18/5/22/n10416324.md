<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>特色花园Café</h2>
<a href="https://d7b58bkqqk9ju.cloudfront.net/R6ewDz8wv"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/emljlk376/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/emljlk376/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月22日讯】假日的湾仔少了繁忙的上班族，显得特别优闲，这次跟朋友试试这间传闻中很有文青气息的花园café，装一下文青！</p>
<p>这间café本是一间花艺店，一进门口便看见好多鲜花和盆栽，经过花店进入便是café，四周包括楼梯都放满花草植物和盆栽，坐在其中真的好像置身于花园，赏心悦目之余，整个人也很放松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10418670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352331366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418670" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352331366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418670" class="wp-caption-text">café的四周，包括楼梯都放满花草植物和盆栽，坐在其中真的好像置身于花园。<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>café的面积不大，坐位不多，一眼看过去只有一张大桌子、几张四人桌，以及两张二人桌。桌子和椅子是木制，很有田园气息。高高的楼底吊着两盏大灯，灯罩是剪纸做成的很像雀笼漂亮又特别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10418676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352291366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418676" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352291366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418676" class="wp-caption-text">café高高的楼底吊着两盏大灯，灯罩是剪纸做成的，很像鸟笼，漂亮又特别。<ahref="https://github.com/emljlk376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐厅的食物采用了很多新鲜健康食材例如甘蓝、藜麦、红菜头、无花果、干果，也有素食选择非常健康。</p>
<p>餐厅是半自助形式，自己去拿餐牌选择好食物先去收银处点餐付款，然后收银员会给你一个号码牌后回到座位坐，食物做好后由侍应送上，水、餐具还有调味料都是自助形式的。</p>
<p>我和朋友也不太饿，所以点了一个羽衣甘蓝杂菜沙律、玛嘉烈特芝士薄饼加一杯薄荷青柠梳打和一杯美式咖啡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10418681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352511366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418681" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352511366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418681" class="wp-caption-text">这次点了一个羽衣甘蓝杂菜沙律、玛嘉烈特芝士比萨、加一杯薄荷青柠梳打，和一杯美式咖啡。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沙律分量颇多蔬菜也新鲜上面放了一些干果，配搭颇清新，不错！薄饼饼底做得很薄如果能够再脆一些就更好了，最喜欢上面原粒番茄很新鲜正好中和薄饼的油腻感，赞！薄荷青柠梳打是闷热天气下的首选，清新又解渴，美式咖啡就中规中矩没有特别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10418688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352401366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418688" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352401366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418688" class="wp-caption-text">羽衣甘蓝杂菜沙律，沙律上面放了一些干果，配搭颇清新。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10418683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352441366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418683" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352441366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418683" class="wp-caption-text">玛嘉烈特芝士比萨。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10418687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352471366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418687" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352471366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418687" class="wp-caption-text">最喜欢比萨上面整颗番茄，很新鲜正好可以中和薄饼的油腻感。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10418689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10418689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352371366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10418689" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805222352371366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10418689" class="wp-caption-text">薄荷青柠梳打是闷热天气下的首选，清新又解渴。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这间café真是很多新鲜蔬菜沙律的配搭，很适合像我这类喜欢少肉多菜的人，由其夏天闷热得没胃口不想吃肉来个新鲜的蔬菜沙律真的很清爽呢！#</p>
