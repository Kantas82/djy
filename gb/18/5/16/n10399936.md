<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>猩宝一定看过妈妈离世 获救后不停紧抱自己</h2>
<a href="https://d2fq83y0z2h6n4.cloudfront.net/X6bDP3e8m"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cqgugh3693/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月17日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">国际动物救援组织</a>从<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%86%E7%BD%97%E6%B4%B2.md#1">婆罗洲</a>的一所住宅救出一只小猩猩乔斯。猩宝宝的眼神充满恐惧，一点也不让人碰；除此之外，还有一种非常奇特的举动……</p>
<p>2016年，“<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87.md#1">国际动物救援组织</a>”（International Animal Rescue ，以下简称IAR）从<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%86%E7%BD%97%E6%B4%B2.md#1">婆罗洲</a>的一所住宅救出一只2岁的金红毛小猩猩——乔斯（Joss）。救援人员试图接近它，但小乔斯不愿被人碰，好像永远处在极度的恐慌与惊惧中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10399996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10399996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/16f0888b402f13874de15e0fbd488829.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10399996" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/16f0888b402f13874de15e0fbd488829-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10399996" class="wp-caption-text">受创过度的小乔斯眼神里充满恐惧，紧抱自己，一点也不让人碰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们还观察到一种从来没见过见的行为—— 乔斯会用双臂紧紧地环抱自己，并且一次又一次地将自己摔在地上，然后把头撞在墙上。救援人员相信，在被抢走并作为宠物出售之前，小乔斯一定是目睹了妈妈的死。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10400826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/306D47B400000578-3410124-image-a-20_1453381469890.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10400826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/306D47B400000578-3410124-image-a-20_1453381469890-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400826" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10400833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/orangutan.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10400833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/orangutan-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400833" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>IAR的<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%BD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">兽医</a>贾克琳．吴（Jaclyn Eng）说：“乔斯不断地拥抱自己，因为它失去了应该从母亲那里得到的身体接触和爱抚。从出生到现在它的生命必定是充满创伤和压力，所以它得用这种反常的方式来宣泄……”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10400825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC045400000578-0-image-m-53_1459854066134.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10400825 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC045400000578-0-image-m-53_1459854066134-600x456.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400825" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.internationalanimalrescue.org/diary/update-rescued-orangutan-joss?currency=USD" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">International Animal Rescue</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10400835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Orangutan-2_3555527b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10400835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Orangutan-2_3555527b-600x375.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400835" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在IAR疗养了几周后，乔斯的情况有所改善。全面康复可能需要好几年的时间，但至少在印尼<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E6%89%93%E9%82%A6.md#1">吉打邦</a>（Ketapang）的这个中心，乔斯感受到了周围的人关怀。</p>
<p>视频中，小乔斯已经顺利由为期一周的学前班结业，它表现良好，不再惧怕别的猩猩孤儿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10400842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC03D200000578-0-image-a-22_1459853677144.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10400842 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC03D200000578-0-image-a-22_1459853677144-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400842" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.internationalanimalrescue.org/diary/update-rescued-orangutan-joss?currency=USD" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">International Animal Rescue</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10400843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10400843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC04B200000578-0-image-a-23_1459853697364.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10400843 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/32DC04B200000578-0-image-a-23_1459853697364-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10400843" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://www.internationalanimalrescue.org/diary/update-rescued-orangutan-joss?currency=USD" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">International Animal Rescue</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来，要上小学喽！大伙挤进红色“校车”去上学，认真地演练包括猴子杠、绳索等各种训练。所有生存技能要全部娴熟过关才能晋级到高级的森林班、荒岛班，最后才能毕业 ——回归自然。</p>
<p>小猩猩们，加油啊！</p>
<p>国际动物救援组织总部设于英国。在印尼，私养<ahref="https://github.com/cqgugh3693/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E6%AF%9B%E7%8C%A9%E7%8C%A9.md#1">红毛猩猩</a>（Orangutan）当宠物是违法的。猩猩婴儿经常会被捕捉及卖作为奖品或宠物。在捕捉的过程中一般会涉及杀死其母亲。世界自然保护联盟将其列为濒危动物的极危等级。</p>
