<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>豹崽受不到亲妈善待 “奶妈”开心接纳好温馨</h2>
<a href="https://d1eqe8yfgc9j6n.cloudfront.net/I3m8pxRXr"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/idzwdk333/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/idzwdk333/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10432491 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-1-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月30日讯】小豹崽和豹妈妈在一起会有危险，所以必须离开妈妈，但它的新奶妈是谁，你绝对想不到。</p>
<p>在俄罗斯符拉迪沃斯托克（Vladivostok）的萨德罗戈德动物园（Sadgorod Zoo），一只小豹崽刚出生不久，因它的妈妈有虐待小<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%B9%E5%AD%90.md#1">豹子</a>的劣迹，所以工作人员为了小豹崽能活命，不得不令其和妈妈分开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10432480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10432480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/index-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10432480 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/index-1-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10432480" class="wp-caption-text">豹妈妈有虐待小<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%B9%E5%AD%90.md#1">豹子</a>的前科，所以小豹崽不得不和妈妈分开。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>动物园业主之一阿斯诺维娜（Alyona Asnovina）说：“我们试图自己喂它，但小豹崽的情况非常糟糕，我们不得不紧急为它寻找一个奶妈。”</p>
<p>原本工作人员想把小豹子交给一头刚生下小羊的母羊抚养，但豹崽不接受这个新妈妈。</p>
<p>工作人员只好让豹崽和一只名叫塔西（Tessy）的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%AF%9B%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">金毛猎犬</a>一起生活。这只可亲的狗妈妈已经有了自己的四只小狗，它非常高兴地开始喂养小小豹，而小小豹也一下子就接受了狗狗奶妈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10432481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10432481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-04-14-at-9.23.04-AM-1024x551.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10432481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Screen-Shot-2018-04-14-at-9.23.04-AM-1024x551-600x323.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10432481" class="wp-caption-text">小豹崽一下子就接受了狗狗奶妈。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>塔西奶水十分充足。动物园的兽医阿加福诺夫（Viktor Agafonov）说：“小豹崽体重在增加，它非常活泼，玩得很开心，<span class="st">吸吮</span>狗养母的奶，感觉很好。”</p>
<p>这只小豹崽不仅喝奶妈塔西的奶水，还在塔西身上爬来爬去，有时候甚至爬到塔西的头上。塔西一点也不生气，任由小豹崽在自己身上爬上爬下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10432482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10432482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_h-9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10432482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/thumbnail_h-9.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10432482" class="wp-caption-text">小<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%B9%E4%BB%94.md#1">豹仔</a>体重在增加，它非常活泼。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狗妈妈抚养小豹崽的视频上传网络后，网友们纷纷发表评论。有人说，这只豹妈妈已经吃掉几只自己生下的小豹崽，这只小豹能获救真好。还有人说，应该把豹子妈妈送进监狱。另一网友说，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%AF%9B%E7%8C%8E%E7%8A%AC.md#1">金毛猎犬</a>是最好的狗。</p>
