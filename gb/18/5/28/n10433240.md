<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬季来临，再来一碗那热腾腾的猪骨面</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>悉尼最有名的猪骨面，热腾腾，让您心满意足。（客户提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月28日讯】去年， 大约的这个时候，悉尼有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki 在Parramatta开业， Mikazuki日本餐馆出色的美食和优质的服务吸引着四方的来客，餐厅每天爆满。</p>
<p>现在， 喜爱Mikazuki美食的人们又有多一个去处，在新开张的Strathfield分店，食品同样是那么好吃，手捧那碗热腾腾的猪骨面有一种心满意足的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433360" style="width: 382px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_01.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-10433360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_01.png" alt="" width="382" b="113" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433360" class="wp-caption-text">Mikazuki日本餐馆“三日月”温暖您的冬季。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mikazuki的中文意思是“三日月”，是最漂亮的弓形弯月的月亮。在日本传统中指农历8月3日的秋季弦月。 寓意Mikazuki的美食是最好的。</p>
<p>在Strathfield的Mikazuki 分店菜单<span style="color: #000000;">里</span>不仅集中了Mikazuki的精华菜品，还新增加了一些适合冬季的美食，如海鲜乌冬面火锅，和牛肉火锅，韩氏泡菜猪肉火锅和传统的乌冬面Nabe 火锅。 这些火锅的用料新鲜，火锅调味纯正。</p>
<p>日本餐馆Mikazuki 的总厨Billy对每道菜的准备、口味调和等细节上下了很大功夫，从而使每一道菜品美味且美观。并将菜谱的食品量化，严格培训员工，保证Mikazuki每道菜品保持同样的口味和品质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433364" style="width: 307px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_03.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-10433364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_03.png" alt="" width="307" b="251" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433364" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki有美味绝伦的各式火锅！（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本餐馆Mikazuki呈现给顾客很多别<span style="color: #000000;">致</span>美观又美味的菜品，如照烧和牛盖饭（Teriyaki Wagyu Beef Don）牛肉细嫩，恰到火候，利口清爽，回味咸甜，配料精致，色泽爽快。是非常受欢迎的一道午餐便饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433365" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_04.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10433365 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_04.jpg" alt="" width="299" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433365" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki美味的照烧和牛盖饭。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日本餐馆Mikazuki的总厨Billy有很多拿手菜，到了这里一定要品尝寿司和刺身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433366" style="width: 189px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_05.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10433366 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_05.png" alt="" width="189" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433366" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki主厨的拿手料理寿司和刺身。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据Billy介绍，制作寿司并不是一件容易的事，需要花费很大功夫。首先要了解做寿司米的品质和煮出寿司饭的不同口感。煮寿司饭还有根据季节、温度来增减水量和调味。用于制作寿司的海鲜和配料更不能马虎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433367" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_06.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10433367 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_06.png" alt="" width="235" b="233" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433367" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki主厨的拿手料理寿司和刺身。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Billy利用原料的不同色彩，做出的寿司形态各异，五颜六色，犹如一件件艺术品，而且会感到这里的寿司和刺身的与众不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433369" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_07.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-10433369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_07.png" alt="" width="440" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433369" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki主厨的拿手料理寿司和刺身。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在日本餐馆Mikazuki用餐，一定不可错过“火山卷”（Volcano roll） ，以其形似喷发的火山命名。 火山卷细嫩柔软滋润，混合的鲜贝味，蟹肉味，鲜鱼蛋，鳄梨果味，芝麻香，香葱，酱香，入口即刻冲击着所有味蕾，香味的配搭完美令人赞叹。</p>
<p>Miikazuki有很多美食可供选择， 满足所有人的口味，而且价位合理。在Miikazuki您会吃得满意，吃得舒服， 乌冬面火锅是冬季的最佳选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10433371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10433371" style="width: 525px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_08.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-10433371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180528_zhuangyuan_RST_08.jpg" alt="" width="525" b="234" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10433371" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼最有名的日本餐馆Mikazuki在Strathfield开分店啦。（客户提供）</figcaption></figure>
