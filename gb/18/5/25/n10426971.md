<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾美食讲座 波城两地举办</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-1TaiwanFood-2-e1527261399323.jpg" />
<h6>学员们聚精会神地聆听黄景龙大厨授课。(黄剑宇/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月25日讯】（大纪元记者黄剑宇波士顿报导）“2018台湾<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>国际巡回<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B2%E5%BA%A7.md#1">讲座</a>”于5月19、20日两天下午在波士顿安良工商会会所举办，侨务委员会遴选派出的台北市侬来餐厅总监黄景龙，台中市飨食天堂行政主厨李启肇两位厨艺大师全程讲授，约50多名社区民众报名并参与讲座，反响热烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10427156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-2TaiwanFood-2-e1527260900503.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-2TaiwanFood-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427156" class="wp-caption-text">台塑无骨牛小排。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10427165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-3TaiwanFood-2-e1527261007323.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-3TaiwanFood-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427165" class="wp-caption-text">麻油鸡饭。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次活动由波克莱台湾商会主办，经文处处长徐佑典和侨教中心主任欧宏伟也前来观摩。</p>
<p>19日，两名大厨示范了台塑无骨牛小排、醉香麻油鸡饭、水煮鱼这三道台式名菜的做法，还有甜点铜锣烧。20日，示范了桔酱白斩鸡、生炒花枝羮、金沙排骨和中华干烧大明虾4道名菜。</p>
<p>参加<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B2%E5%BA%A7.md#1">讲座</a>的既有餐馆老板、专业厨师，也有喜好台湾美食的侨胞，还有很多慕名而来的饕客。</p>
<p>两位大厨倾囊相授，很仔细的讲解做菜的工序，还现场解答疑问，甚至传授了制作酱料小秘诀。</p>
<p>煸炒水煮鱼的多种配料时，观众席的学员们纷纷被食材炒出的辛辣味道呛的摀住鼻子连打喷嚏，两位大厨还强忍着，坚守着继续完成炒锅示范，所以黄景龙戏说大厨们是冒着生命危险在为大家做菜，要珍惜。</p>
<p>杨罗东会长向大家提醒，由于没买到合适的鲈鱼，所以换成了更鲜美的石斑，并感谢提供石斑的鳕鱼角(Cape Cod)的台湾商家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10427170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-4TaiwanFood-2-e1527261046295.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-4TaiwanFood-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427170" class="wp-caption-text">水煮鱼。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10427174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-6TaiwanFood-2-e1527261124311.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-6TaiwanFood-2-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427174" class="wp-caption-text">生炒花枝羹。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10427180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427180" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-7TaiwanFood-2-e1527261179616.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-7TaiwanFood-2-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427180" class="wp-caption-text">金沙排骨。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>

<h2>鳕鱼角“美食之家”</h2>
<p>除了在唐人街举办的这两场示范外，两位大厨还于5月18日前往鳕鱼角Falmouth“美食之家”餐厅进行厨艺示范，向当地主流社会人士宣介绍台湾美食，计50余人参加。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10427192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-9TaiwanFood-2-e1527261376862.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-3-9TaiwanFood-2-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427192" class="wp-caption-text">左起：欧宏伟主任、安良会长陈仕维、大厨李启肇、大厨黄景龙、徐佑典处长、郭竞儒、波克莱台商会长杨罗东、陈玉瑛。(黄剑宇/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处处长徐佑典感谢两位大厨的辛劳与付出，特别请麻州众议员Sarah Peake颁发感谢状，肯定他们促成美食厨艺交流，并积极行销台湾特色美食。</p>
<p>台湾美食国际巡回讲座如今已是侨委会的年度活动之一，不但有美加东团，美加西团，美中团等巡回展演路线。◇</p>
