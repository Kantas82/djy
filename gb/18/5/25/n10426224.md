<a href="https://d1qenr1x5ix28c.cloudfront.net/B9utxHvHz"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lhnyld3630/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10427400 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年05月25日讯】动物们总会有一些令人匪夷所思的行为。影片中，有两只<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>面对面。一只雄赳赳气昂昂，另一只很“嗨”，不知道在兴奋什么，一直跳、一直跳，跳个不停。</p>
<p>它们两位看起来好像是要打架的样子，相视一阵，马上就互相冲向对方。大家猜谁会赢呢？没想到，竟然是看起来很幼稚一直跳的“小朋友”胜出，那个看起来比较“稳重”的简直<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E5%A0%AA%E4%B8%80%E5%87%BB.md#1">不堪一击</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10427380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_78.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10427380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_78-600x354.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427380" class="wp-caption-text">鸟鸟跳一下，对方就倒地了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>或许鸟也有魔法？那只“小朋友”在敌人面前，竟然随便跳了一下，对手马上就摔倒了！根本就没碰到对方呀，太强噜，简直是“隔空打鸟”。</p>
<p>人类这有一种学说叫“面相学”，可透过面相来看出对方的能力、健康、财富、吉凶祸福&#8230;&#8230;不过有时总会冒出一些“<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E5%8F%AF%E8%B2%8C%E7%9B%B8.md#1">不可貌相</a>”的高人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10427392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10427392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_79.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-10427392 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fullsizeoutput_79-600x404.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10427392" class="wp-caption-text">鸟鸟：“我是<ahref="https://github.com/lhnyld3630/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E5%8F%AF%E8%B2%8C%E7%9B%B8.md#1">不可貌相</a>的唷。”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看完影片，我们或许也可得出一个结论：“原来鸟鸟也是不可貌相的！”</p>
