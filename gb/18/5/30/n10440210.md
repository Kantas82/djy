<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>精彩一瞬！天空骤亮 万丈白光球体划过天际</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2020-8-24-toronto-car-parade_06-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月06日讯】哇～这真是难得一见的天文奇景！在<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD.md#1">泰国</a>有一位网友分享了他的<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%8C%E8%BD%A6%E8%AE%B0%E5%BD%95%E5%99%A8.md#1">行车记录器</a>，画面中，原本是晚间的夜空，突然变得像白天一样明亮！一颗超级明亮的<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%98%9F.md#1">流星</a>画过天际！当他把这段奇景放到网路上之后，马上引起广传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10440599" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-185.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10440599 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-185-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD.md#1">泰国</a>网友在一个寻常的晚间开车上高速公路。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是发生在2015年底泰国曼谷的天文奇景。泰国网友表示，那天晚上，他如往常一样开着车，突然间，变得像白天一样明亮！</p>
<p>高架的高速公路上路灯明亮，连远方的建筑物细节都能看得清清楚楚，这时，<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA.md#1">天空</a>中出现了一颗闪耀着万丈光芒的白色光球！从天际的东边飞向西边，时间大约有3秒钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10440600" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-146.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10440600 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-146-600x511.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="511" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">突然间，<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E7%A9%BA.md#1">天空</a>变得像白天一样！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10440601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-107.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10440601 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-107-600x417.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一颗白色光球就从天际飞腾出来，大家纷纷惊叹它的巨大和它的光亮程度。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那倏忽即逝的移动光球，拖着长长的尾巴，像串着宝石的金丝线，非常美丽；看到巨大且明亮的<ahref="https://github.com/qfsaof3083/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%98%9F.md#1">流星</a>，那份惊艳，也让人一时间忘却言语。</p>
<p>泰国媒体报导，这一段视频引发网友热烈讨论，大家纷纷惊叹这颗流星的巨大和它的光亮程度，不少天文迷也表示，要去寻找可能出现的陨石或陨石坑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10440603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-79.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10440603 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-79-600x416.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">万丈光芒的白色光球从天际的东边飞向西边。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10440610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-80.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10440610 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-80-600x532.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="532" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">流星拖着长长的尾巴，然后从天空的另一边隐没，时间大约有3秒钟。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，天文学家似乎没有找到这颗流星或陨石掉落地面的确切位置。</p>
<p>这精彩一瞬的天文奇景，有幸被这位泰国网友记录下来，流星除了带给大家惊奇，也希望带来平安幸福～</p>
