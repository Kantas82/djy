<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA76人两分饮恨 凯尔特人闯东冠决赛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/GettyImages-956666096-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>塔图姆。 (Photo by Maddie Meyer/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月10日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）费城<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人最后一分钟<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次的关键失误，加上塔图姆（<span lang="EN-US">Jayson Tatum</span>）的超前分，让波士顿<ahref="https://github.com/julcls378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E5%B0%94%E7%89%B9%E4%BA%BA.md#1">凯尔特人</a>最后以<span lang="EN-US">114</span>：<span lang="EN-US">112</span>让<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人提前打包，绿衫军连续<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年在东区冠军赛与克里夫兰骑士对决。</p>
<p>没有退路的<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人与不想将战线延长的绿衫军，开赛就互不相让，拜恩斯（<span lang="EN-US">Aron Baynes</span>）补灌史马特（<span lang="EN-US">Marcus Smart</span>）没进的球，<ahref="https://github.com/julcls378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E5%B0%94%E7%89%B9%E4%BA%BA.md#1">凯尔特人</a>首节以<span lang="EN-US">25</span>：<span lang="EN-US">24</span>领先一分。易篮再战，两队分数依旧纠缠，不过在上半场结束一分多钟，拜恩斯和塔图姆<span lang="EN-US">2</span>记三分弹，帮助绿衫军以<span lang="EN-US">61</span>：<span lang="EN-US">52</span>继续保持优势。</p>
<p>下半场安比德（<span lang="EN-US">Joel Embiid</span>）和萨里奇（<span lang="EN-US">Dario Saric</span>）携手抗敌，加上雷迪克（<span lang="EN-US">J.J. Redick</span>）适时外线火力，三节打完，追到<span lang="EN-US">82</span>：<span lang="EN-US">83</span>仅<span lang="EN-US">1</span>分落后。决胜节开节<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人拉出一波<span lang="EN-US">12</span>：<span lang="EN-US">7</span>的攻势，莫里斯（<span lang="EN-US">Marcus Morris</span>）和霍福德（<span lang="EN-US">Al Horford</span>）再将分数讨回，史马特强袭灌篮，<span lang="EN-US">109</span>：<span lang="EN-US">109</span>让比赛重回原点。</p>
<p>此时掌握球权的<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人，萨里奇被霍福德成功抄球，塔图姆把握机会投进<span lang="EN-US">2</span>分，安比德失误掉球，萨里奇犯了洛齐尔（<span lang="EN-US">Terry Rozier</span>）一规，让洛齐尔<span lang="EN-US">2</span>罚全进拉开<span lang="EN-US">4</span>分领先，雷迪克三分冷箭又让<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人燃起逆转希望，就在此刻，又让绿衫军站上罚球线。史马特两罚中一，<span lang="EN-US">76</span>人掌握最后机会，但西蒙斯（<span lang="EN-US">Ben Simmons</span>）竟发生传球失误，错失最后一击的机会。</p>
