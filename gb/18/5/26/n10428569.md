<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>摄影师在雷暴中拍摄闪电 却巧遇神来之笔</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月27日讯】（大纪元记者伊遐报导）这部影片是去年保罗．史密斯（Paul Smith）在美国俄克拉荷马州埃德蒙德（Edmond）摄录的。当时天空闪着<ahref="https://github.com/luvafs343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E6%9A%B4.md#1">雷暴</a>，他试图捕捉闪电，却幸运地遇到了“红色小精灵”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10428482" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10428482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/c9e28fadcfb628f2c2e116c969c5b752.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/c9e28fadcfb628f2c2e116c969c5b752-600x330.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10428482" class="wp-caption-text">天空是一块干净的画布，等待神的创作。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/luvafs343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%89%B2%E7%B2%BE%E7%81%B5.md#1">红色精灵</a>”（Red sprites）是辉煌壮丽的条纹闪光，非常难得一见。发生的原因可能是在<ahref="https://github.com/luvafs343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%B7%E6%9A%B4.md#1">雷暴</a>之上产生的大规模正电荷放电。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10429798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10429798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/b546bd4c7f33dae182b3e085e8466f59.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10429798 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/b546bd4c7f33dae182b3e085e8466f59-600x331.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10429798" class="wp-caption-text">是小精灵在放烟火吗？（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大约都在海拔80公里以上的空中形成，通常成群出现，然后以光速10%的速度下落 ，可能千分之一秒后又有另一组出现，持续时间可能不到1秒钟！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10429806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10429806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/e29b5e17039350064500988ead2a744a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10429806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/e29b5e17039350064500988ead2a744a-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10429806" class="wp-caption-text">优雅的五个音符。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爱德蒙德这个城镇地势辽阔，有许多草原、丘陵和矮树丛。属亚热带，气候湿润。天气变化频繁，夏季持续炎热。长时间严重的干旱往往导致野火和暴雨，也是龙卷风出没之地。</p>
<p>原来要看到这个奇妙的景象，要有足够强烈的雷暴，而且天空要够黑，但视野要够清晰……得要这样天时、地利、人和都具备才行哩！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10429811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10429811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4177e2c297fb69d1663ed074964143be.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10429811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4177e2c297fb69d1663ed074964143be-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10429811" class="wp-caption-text">雄壮的大合唱。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大自然之妙，的确使人心生敬畏。这是不是也很像<ahref="https://github.com/luvafs343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E4%BC%A0%E7%9C%9F%E9%9F%B3%E6%95%88.md#1">高传真音效</a>的频率解析图呢？</p>
<p>中文里天籁是指自然的声音，西方人也说星星会唱歌。这也许就是红色电光小精灵举办的小小即兴音乐会哟！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10428468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10428468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/aded9977b57f36e0a475fad8bfca779a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/aded9977b57f36e0a475fad8bfca779a-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10428468" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/luvafs343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%89%B2%E7%B2%BE%E7%81%B5.md#1">红色精灵</a>”交响曲。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一起来睁大眼睛看，能不能抓住小精灵闪现的瞬间？</p>
