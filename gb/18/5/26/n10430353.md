<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>长见识了 原来海星迈着这么多小脚优雅走路</h2>
<a href="https://db8nk66phkh66.cloudfront.net/H1k7ryM2h"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/prvlql3569/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/prvlql3569/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430402 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月27日讯】（大纪元记者许彦报导）对很多人来说，<ahref="https://github.com/prvlql3569/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%98%9F.md#1">海星</a>并不是陌生的<ahref="https://github.com/prvlql3569/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%B4%8B%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">海洋生物</a>，然而亲眼见过海星行动的人恐怕不多。在这段视频中，看到海星迈着优雅的小碎步走路，你会觉得真是长见识了。</p>
<p>这段罕见的视频是一位叫哈洛克（Zeb Hallock）的网友在美国北卡罗莱纳州的外滩群岛拍摄到的，作为主角的<ahref="https://github.com/prvlql3569/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%98%9F.md#1">海星</a>看起来就像是外星生物，大小和餐盘差不多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430390" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/people_excited_to_see_starfish_walking_on_the_beach_20171124015536.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/people_excited_to_see_starfish_walking_on_the_beach_20171124015536-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430390" class="wp-caption-text">海星看起来像外星生物，大小和餐盘差不多。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈洛克透露：“当时是傍晚，大概是下午6点左右，在靠近水面的沙滩上，我们注意到有海星四处走动。”</p>
<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-790823b1-a2b9-418b-5bc7-562be0b37b8a" data-id="player-790823b1-a2b9-418b-5bc7-562be0b37b8a"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/790823b1-a2b9-418b-5bc7-562be0b37b8a?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F5%2F26%2Fn10430353.md#1"></a>
<p>“有些海星在潮水中，但也有一些海星在沙滩上漫步，步伐非常非常缓慢，我们小心地将它们放回了海中。”他表示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430388" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/gallery-1507542874-walking-starfish-viral-video.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/gallery-1507542874-walking-starfish-viral-video-600x305.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430388" class="wp-caption-text">海星体外有很多密集的管足，每条管足末端都有一个吸盘。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>海星体外有很多密集的管足，每条管足末端都有一个吸盘。海星靠管足的移动和吸盘吸力在海底运动，速度非常慢，一分钟只能移动几厘米。但海星迈着小碎步，走路的姿势看起来非常优雅，有点像跳舞的感觉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/maxresdefault-12-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430389" class="wp-caption-text">海星靠管足的移动和吸盘吸力在海底运动。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哈洛克说，当时在海滩的其他人也对海星产生了兴趣，并帮助将沙滩上的海星弄回水中。</p>
<p>网友Tisha Leclerc 评论道：“不知道它们有脚！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10430387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10430387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/capture19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10430387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/capture19-600x488.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="488" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10430387" class="wp-caption-text">海星迈着小碎步，走路的姿势看起来非常优雅。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Michele Peck补充说:“谢谢！那些可怜的东西（海星）如果没有（人）帮助，就活不下去了。”</p>
<p>尽管许多人对这种不同寻常的生物着迷，但也有人会感到害怕。</p>
<p>Vina Coladad在推特上写道：“每当我看到这些时，我都会更加惊慌，现在我不太喜欢海星了。”</p>
<p>Nadia Pin补充说：“如果我看到走向我，我会迈开腿跑掉。”</p>
