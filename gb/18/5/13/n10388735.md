<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>胡瓜帅儿戏剧出道 网友赞：像小赵又廷</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E7%93%9C.md#1">胡瓜</a>的儿子<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E9%87%8A%E5%AE%89.md#1">胡释安</a>（安安）即将踏入演艺圈。12日，有媒体报导，胡释安在美国加州大学河滨分校大众传播科系毕业后，已在去年服完兵，现正拍摄王小棣导演的新戏《<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/20%E4%B9%8B%E5%90%8E.md#1">20之后</a>》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388739" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1510120750332384.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10388739 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1510120750332384.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="689" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388739" class="wp-caption-text">赵又廷资料照。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E9%87%8A%E5%AE%89.md#1">胡释安</a>虽然鲜少受到外界关注，但曾被<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%A5%AF.md#1">小祯</a>在脸书曝光近照，被不少网友夸赞“好帅”，甚至有人认为他像“小赵又廷”，但也有人说他还是有几分像<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E7%93%9C.md#1">胡瓜</a>跟<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%A5%AF.md#1">小祯</a>。</p>
<p>据《苹果日报》报导，胡瓜也透露过儿子有意出道，对戏剧表演很感兴趣，也接受表演培训与试镜。</p>
<p>昨天中午，便有媒体在富锦街公园看到导演王小棣正拍摄新戏《<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/20%E4%B9%8B%E5%90%8E.md#1">20之后</a>》，并带着安安和两名女演员走位。而身高184公分的安安，拍戏中看似轻松，在做完惊吓表情后，一度忍不住笑场。之后正式开拍，只拍两遍就收工，拍摄相当顺利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805122310361487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388752" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1805122310361487-600x782.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388752" class="wp-caption-text">胡盈祯（小祯）与弟弟胡释安（安安）合影资料照。<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/imkarenhu/photos/a.583919681725807.1073741828.582117211906054/1272434579540977/?type=3&amp;theater" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">小祯脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在相关报导出来时，小祯随后也在脸书转发，还幽默写道：“我好开心啊啊～因为记者从今以后就会去追你啦！”似乎乐见弟弟出道后也帮她转移了媒体的关注。</p>
