<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：多伦多庆祝法轮大法洪传26周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/09-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月12日早上，法轮功学员在多伦多市府广场庆祝世界法轮大法日暨师尊华诞。（艾文/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）5月12日（周六），来自多伦多及附近城市的部分法轮功学员约1,000人，在多伦多市政广场参加了法轮大法洪传26周年盛大庆祝活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388725" style="width: 607px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/05-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10388725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/05-2.jpg" alt="" width="607" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388725" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员集体炼功。（周行/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10388709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388709" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11699.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11699.jpg" alt="" width="604" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388709" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员集体炼功。（艾文/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天，多伦多阳光灿烂，法轮功学员先是集体炼功，然后以歌、舞及及诗歌等文艺形式，庆祝世界法轮大法日暨法轮大法创始人李洪志大师华诞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388710" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_1-1686.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_1-1686.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388710" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员集体炼功。（艾文/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>法轮大法（也称法轮功）自1992年传世以来，已经吸引了上亿人步入修炼。这些修炼人遵从真、善、忍的理念，不断的在修炼中净化自己，成为了越来越好的人。法轮大法已经洪传到世界100多个国家，获得各地政府及民间的无数褒奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388712" style="width: 605px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11715.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11715.jpg" alt="" width="605" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388712" class="wp-caption-text">法轮功学员集体炼功。（艾文/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>法轮功学员在一首诗歌《永恒的历程》中，道出了他们得法修炼，身心升华，明白做人意义及宇宙法理后的喜悦心情。并表达了他们对李洪志大师衷心的感激，以及坚持修炼，坚持讲真相反迫害，完成自己历史使命的信心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10388713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388713" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11833.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/20180512_11833.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388713" class="wp-caption-text">庆祝集会现场。（艾文/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10388714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388714" style="width: 608px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/24.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/24.jpg" alt="" width="608" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388714" class="wp-caption-text">文艺表演。（周行/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10388718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10388718" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/23.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10388718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/23.jpg" alt="" width="604" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10388718" class="wp-caption-text">鼓声阵阵。（周行/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
