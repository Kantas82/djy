<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>骨盆不正让你腰痛？3招自我检测骨盆歪斜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-0-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>3个方法，自我检测骨盆歪斜。（苹果屋提供／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E7%9B%86%E6%AD%AA%E6%96%9C.md#1">骨盆歪斜</a>，容易造成腰痛、偏头痛甚至椎间盘突出。3个方法，自我<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%80%E6%B5%8B.md#1">检测</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E7%9B%86.md#1">骨盆</a>歪斜。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%BF%E5%8A%BF.md#1">姿势</a>不良，就容易出现<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E7%9B%86%E6%AD%AA%E6%96%9C.md#1">骨盆歪斜</a></span></h2>
<p>所谓“<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AA%A8%E7%9B%86.md#1">骨盆</a>歪斜”是因为<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%BF%E5%8A%BF.md#1">姿势</a>不良造成支撑骨盆的韧带、骨头和关节移位、倾斜的状态。分两类型，往左右其中一边倾斜型，或是往上下其中一边倾斜型。再者，姿势不佳不仅对骨盆有影响，臀部肌肉（臀大肌等）也会不平衡并引起腰痛。</p>
<p>荐骨、髋骨和尾骨等构成的骨盆，是连接上、下半身很重要的骨头，其周围只靠肌肉、韧带、关节来支撑，所以一旦有姿势不良的坏习惯，就容易造成移位或歪斜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12833541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12833541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-1-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-12833541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-1-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" alt="骨盆是连接上、下半身很重要的骨头，姿势不良容易造成骨盆移位或骨盆歪斜。（苹果屋提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12833541" class="wp-caption-text">骨盆是连接上、下半身很重要的骨头，姿势不良容易造成骨盆移位或骨盆歪斜。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>好发年龄：各年龄层</p>
<p>门诊科别：骨科、复健科</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">你有骨盆歪斜吗？简单3方法，自我<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%80%E6%B5%8B.md#1">检测</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12833606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12833606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-2-3.24.21-health11-600x690.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-12833606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-2-3.24.21-health11-600x690.jpg" alt="骨盆歪斜的自我检测方法。（苹果屋提供）" width="600" b="690" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12833606" class="wp-caption-text">骨盆歪斜的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E6%88%91%E6%A3%80%E6%B5%8B.md#1">自我检测</a>方法。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>CHECK.01?髋关节位移</strong></p>
<p>仰躺，全身放松。左右两脚脚跟之间保留点距离，脚尖自然张开。检查脚张开的程度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12833608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12833608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-3-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-12833608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-3-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" alt="髋关节位移可能是臀部肌肉（臀肌）的平衡太差。（苹果屋提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12833608" class="wp-caption-text">髋关节位移可能是臀部肌肉（臀肌）的平衡太差。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>左右两脚张开的角度不同、张的太开，有可能是髋关节位移。照Ｘ光无异常的话，应是臀部肌肉（臀肌）的平衡太差。锻炼臀肌即可。</p>
<p><strong>CHECK.02 骨盆左右位移</strong></p>
<p>闭上眼睛，在直线上直直地往前走10步。</p>
<p>张开眼睛，若是在直线上就没有问题。若往左右其中一遍偏移，即表示骨盆往那个方向位移。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12833609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12833609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-4-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-12833609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-4-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" alt="骨盆歪斜的另一种情况是骨盆左右位移，容易造成偏头痛、颈部肌肉疲劳。（苹果屋提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12833609" class="wp-caption-text">骨盆歪斜的另一种情况是骨盆左右位移，容易造成偏头痛、颈部肌肉疲劳。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>骨盆左右不对称，姿势变差、肌力低下。由于一边较高，左右两脚的长度以及肩膀的高度也会不同，症状也是左右其中一边较明显。</p>
<p>容易造成偏头痛、颈部肌肉疲劳。</p>
<p><strong>CHECK.03 骨盆前后位移</strong></p>
<p>身体贴在墙上直直地站立，头、臀部和脚跟要确实贴在墙上。</p>
<p>检查背和墙壁之间的距离。如果可以塞进一个拳头，则属于骨盆往前倾斜；如果间距比手掌窄，属于骨盆向后倾斜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12833610" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12833610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-5-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-12833610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/Abnormal-pelvic-tilt-5-3.24.21-health11-600x400.jpg" alt="骨盆前倾、后倾，容易造成腰痛、腰椎椎间盘突出。（苹果屋提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12833610" class="wp-caption-text">骨盆前倾、后倾，容易造成腰痛、腰椎椎间盘突出。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>直直地站着，骨盆却向前倾，形成腰向后倾（酥腰）的状态。久坐办公桌的人通常后倾情况较多，施加腰部负担，脊椎就容易歪斜。</p>
<p>容易造成腰痛、腰椎椎间盘突出。</p>
