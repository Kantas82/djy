<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>享受大自然疗愈能量 台东森林公园逍遥游</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/c1e04b8782fc8f42ee07e5a664eeba39-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东森林公园入口。（王知涵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年03月03日讯】（大纪元记者王知涵台湾报导）寸土寸金的市区，<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C%E6%A3%AE%E6%9E%97%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">台东森林公园</a>是少见位处市区的森林，而且美景足可媲美欧洲。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C%E6%A3%AE%E6%9E%97%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">台东森林公园</a>占地辽阔，骑单车徜徉其中，微风轻拂，好舒服、好放松！靠近海滨，一大片木麻黄防风林，远观望去宛如黑森林。走进公园，林木丛密却不杂乱，小桥流水清幽谧静，仿佛进入童话世界森林，带着一些神秘感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788721" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/c8421b2872ca980ad6143b888b9d4916.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/c8421b2872ca980ad6143b888b9d4916.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">木麻黄大道。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>台东森林公园里的三大名湖</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12788685" style="width: 4096px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_170812.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788685" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_170812.jpg" alt="台东森林公园, 琵琶湖" width="4096" b="2304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">位于台东森林公园里的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%B5%E7%90%B6%E6%B9%96.md#1">琵琶湖</a>。（莫琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%B5%E7%90%B6%E6%B9%96.md#1">琵琶湖</a>位在卑南溪出海口南边，是地下涌泉形成的天然湖。湖边设置环湖步道、原木栈道和观景水榭，湖景如诗如画。湖水清澈，水中鱼类植物一览无遗，不论是研究水中生态，或是赏景赏鸟，即使简单围绕琵琶湖散步，都是生活一大享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788686" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4e0953497836d473ab3d970dd79c6972.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4e0953497836d473ab3d970dd79c6972.jpg" alt="台东森林公园, 鹭鸶湖" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">位于台东森林公园里的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%AD%E9%B8%B6%E6%B9%96.md#1">鹭鸶湖</a>。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%AD%E9%B8%B6%E6%B9%96.md#1">鹭鸶湖</a>原为沼泽湿地经过人工整治而成，分为左右两湖，所以原取名鸳鸯湖。沼泽地本就蕴育沼泽生物，吸引白鹭鸶驻足觅食，另名鹭鸶湖。湖面平静无纹，欣赏鹭鸶湖感受静谧之美，有被大自然疗愈，安抚情绪的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788689" style="width: 4096px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_165400.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788689" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_165400.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="4096" b="2304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">活水湖是个道道地地的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">人工湖</a>。（莫琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活水湖是个道地的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">人工湖</a>，主要是防洪调节水量之用。湖泊面积广阔，这里也被用来举办龙舟比赛和铁人三项等水上活动。</p>
<h2>寛广步道，骑单车一派悠闲</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12788726" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3d158f82aa10a6965e721bcc062035d9.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3d158f82aa10a6965e721bcc062035d9.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">林园大道。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12788710" style="width: 4096px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_164350.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_164350.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="4096" b="2304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在公园里骑脚踏车好不惬意。（莫琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>森林公园广阔，地势平坦且风景优美，很适合散步谈心，但如果想一览全貌，建议骑乘自行车。</p>
<p>园内多条步道蜿蜒交错，可以见到两侧遍植木麻黄的木麻黄大道。还有经过绿树成荫的林园大道，两旁树荫下还设置了多个长木椅供游人休憩。往琵琶湖的森林浴大道则是不同品种植物，秋季时树叶呈现黄色、绿色、红色多层次感，非常美丽，犹如置身欧洲庄园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788718" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/845d258965d4ca2de2574c95a1ae65d3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/845d258965d4ca2de2574c95a1ae65d3.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2048" b="1152" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">森林浴大道。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>景观造景引人入胜</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12788727" style="width: 2048px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/effd43de25db892c6eaa289134b81644.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/effd43de25db892c6eaa289134b81644.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2048" b="1152" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花絮隧道。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12788731" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7c0475754e180b7c39abaa5811402f5c.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7c0475754e180b7c39abaa5811402f5c.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树屋。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沿途总会遇上一些美而脱俗的巧思设计，就像是送给游人的小惊喜，例如花架隧道，在单调的隧道铺设大量植被，绿意盎然，还有那树屋，单纯的栈道平淡无奇，但和大树结合一体，而成为特别造型的观景平台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788729" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/04fe739fffb4770fa016ac77e56aa17d.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/04fe739fffb4770fa016ac77e56aa17d.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">绿水桥。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12788730" style="width: 2592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/57da86c20740773ff1df25c0573182fd.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/57da86c20740773ff1df25c0573182fd.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="2592" b="1944" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">赏湖曲桥。（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>特意架设鹭鸶桥、赏湖曲桥、绿水桥方便赏景，赏湖凉亭以原木搭建，仿古设计，才能匹配美丽的湖泊，与湖泊相映衬。</p>
<p>集结了老少咸宜的各项优点，还设有溜冰场和儿童戏水区，能带给亲子同游最快乐的时光。想远离城市纷扰，放松心情，台东森林公园是绝对值得一游的好地方！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12788737" style="width: 4096px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_173156.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12788737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P_20150723_173156.jpg" alt="台东森林公园" width="4096" b="2304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东森林公园是台湾第一座大型滨海公园，此为公园外围风景。（莫琳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
