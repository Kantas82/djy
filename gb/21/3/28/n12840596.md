<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>松软奶香雪花酥DIY 自用送礼两相宜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/id12844804-23-1-512x400.jpg" />
<h6>浓郁奶香雪花苏。（图／橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">雪花酥</a>来自于牛轧糖的变化甜点，食材上比牛轧糖多添加了饼乾和果干，吃起来不单是浓郁奶香，口味上有多样变化，重点是口感“酥酥香香”的，加上果粒的香甜，吃下去非常美妙。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8A%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">雪花酥</a>的食材做起来色彩缤纷，甜点颜值也相当高，是一款送礼自用两相宜的精致点心。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>雪花酥</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>奶油??????100克<br />
棉花糖?????400克<br />
奶粉??????100克<br />
奇福饼干????500克<br />
蔓越莓干????50克<br />
烤熟杏仁片???50克<br />
烤熟南瓜子???50克<br />
枸杞??????30克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">装饰</span></h3>
<p>防潮糖粉????30克<br />
奶粉??????30克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 烤盘放上ㄧ张烘焙布备用。</p>
<p>2 将奇福饼干折半及坚果和蔓越莓干及枸杞放烤箱，以100度保温备用。</p>
<h3>TIPS：可放入自己喜欢的坚果，调整为自己喜好的风味哦！</h3>
<p>3 将奶油和棉花糖，用不沾平底锅加热至融化，再加入奶粉搅拌均匀。</p>
<p>4 将保温的食材加入平底锅中拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12840625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12840625" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/id12840625-9f6aa3dc30755d71c851dd823ed57176.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12840625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/id12840625-9f6aa3dc30755d71c851dd823ed57176-450x293.png" alt="雪花饼" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12840625" class="wp-caption-text">平底锅内的棉花糖和奶油融化完全后，放入烤过的奇福饼干、坚果、蔓越莓干及枸杞搅拌。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 倒入铺上烘焙布的烤盘中，盖上另一张烤焙布，带上手套将食材压平，让厚度平均约2公分厚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12840626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12840626" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/id12840626-06a30f7221a57b02f690b5564746f0a7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12840626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/id12840626-06a30f7221a57b02f690b5564746f0a7-450x290.png" alt="雪花饼" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12840626" class="wp-caption-text">倒入铺上烘焙布的烤盘中，盖上另一张烤焙布，带上手套将食材压平。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 定型后取下烘焙布，撒上装饰用奶粉及防潮糖粉，翻面再撒上装饰奶粉。</p>
<p>7 移至觇板上切成2.5ｘ2.5公分正方形即可。</p>
<h3>TIPS：可以依照要装入的饼干袋大小切块。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12801369" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12801369" style="width: 119px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e588e81adbe0b427268720cdbbfe9b89.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12801369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e588e81adbe0b427268720cdbbfe9b89-450x570.jpg" alt="港点" width="119" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12801369" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">港点</a>小王子郑元勋的<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%B4%E6%89%8B%E7%A4%BC.md#1">伴手礼</a>点心》书封。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
