<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北温惊爆持刀攻击案酿1死6伤</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年03月28日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">27</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周六</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，位于北温<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E6%81%A9%E8%B0%B7%E5%9B%BE%E4%B9%A6%E9%A6%86.md#1">林恩谷图书馆</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lynn Valley Library</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）发生随机<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%81%E5%88%80%E6%94%BB%E5%87%BB.md#1">持刀攻击</a>案，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名女性身亡，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人送医。警方表示，凶嫌已经落网。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">急救人员约在下午</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">时</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">45</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分接获报案后，大批警力及</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">辆<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8A%A4%E8%BD%A6.md#1">救护车</a>赶赴现场救援。有多名受害者在图书馆内、外被刺伤，事件中有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名女性身亡，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人受伤送医。<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E6%81%A9%E8%B0%B7%E5%9B%BE%E4%B9%A6%E9%A6%86.md#1">林恩谷图书馆</a>正好位于当地的购物中心，案发当时附近有许多上街购物人士。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据现场画面，一名男子被逮捕前持刀刺伤自己。一名目击者告诉加拿大广播公司，攻击者被制伏后，搭上<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8A%A4%E8%BD%A6.md#1">救护车</a>离开现场。警方指嫌犯疑似是<ahref="https://github.com/uzzisv325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A4%E7%8B%BC%E7%8A%AF%E6%A1%88.md#1">孤狼犯案</a>，但未说明凶嫌犯案动机等相关细节。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
