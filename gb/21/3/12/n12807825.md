<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】黑椒煎南瓜比目鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/b0fa4c596308b51fe14bd37fdaa8c289-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的黑椒煎南瓜比目鱼，风味独特，不容错过。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>自古以来，黑胡椒就因其在调味与医学上的双重价值而备受珍视。黑胡椒的香馥来自其含有的胡椒碱。常简称作“胡椒”的黑胡椒粉，是全世界使用最广泛的香料之一，在世界各地的餐桌上都可见到。</p>
<p>比目鱼富含蛋白质、维生素A、维生素D、钙、磷、钾等营养成分，尤其维生素B6的含量颇丰。科学家对超过1000种食物做过特别的调查，以精确的分析数据解析热量和营养成分，并罗列所谓的“营养评分”，比目鱼被评为十个最有营养的食物之一（第四名）。</p>
<p>比目鱼具有祛风湿、活血通络等功效。其所含的不饱和脂肪酸易被人体吸收，有助于降低血中胆固醇，而且比目鱼还富含大脑的主要组成成分DHA，经常食用可增强智力。</p>
<p>南瓜除了独特的甘甜风味之外，也含有丰富的营养成分。它有极丰富的氦基酸、胡萝卜素、维生素，尤其它胡萝卜素的含量，在所有瓜类中可说是翘楚。</p>
<p>对爱美的女性而言，南瓜是非常适合的食物。因为其不仅有美容功效，且南瓜中含有果胶，能调节胃内食物的吸收速度，使得糖类吸收减缓。食用南瓜也比较容易获得饱足感，想控制身材者可藉食用南瓜降低淀粉的摄取量。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%A4%92%E7%85%8E%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E6%AF%94%E7%9B%AE%E9%B1%BC.md#1">黑椒煎南瓜比目鱼</a>，以黑胡椒为主要调料。比目鱼和南瓜搭配会给你带来意想不到的美味惊喜，蜂蜜与黑胡椒汁的风味融合在一起，更是会给你带来难忘的美食体验。这绝对是一道不容错过的绝佳美味。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E6%A4%92%E7%85%8E%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E6%AF%94%E7%9B%AE%E9%B1%BC.md#1">黑椒煎南瓜比目鱼</a></h2>
<p>主料：比目鱼、南瓜</p>
<p>配料：红葱头、蒜末、红椒粒、清汤（或水）</p>
<p>调料：盐、糖、生粉、蜂蜜、黑胡椒、生抽、蚝油、生粉水、老抽</p>
<p>1. 将比目鱼洗干净去骨去皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-39.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-39-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将比目鱼洗干净去骨去皮。（<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将鱼肉切成大小适中的鱼块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807846" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将鱼肉切成鱼块。（<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用海盐、糖、生粉、生抽和油腌制鱼肉待用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腌制鱼肉待用。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将南瓜去皮去核后切成大方块状，放入蒸箱蒸约12分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将南瓜去皮去瓤后切成大方块状。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将鱼两面煎至金黄色，捞起待用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将鱼两面煎至金黄色。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将葱、蒜等配料用锅底余油炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将配料用锅底余油炒香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 加入蜂蜜、黑胡椒、生抽、清汤和蚝油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807855" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入蜂蜜、黑胡椒、生抽和蚝油等调料。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 加入生粉水勾芡，再放老抽调色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807858" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/8-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/8-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放老抽调色。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 取出蒸好的南瓜摆盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/9-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/9-5-450x305.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取出蒸好的南瓜。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 把鱼块放于蒸好的南瓜面上，让南瓜吸收鱼汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-4-450x295.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把鱼块放于南瓜块上。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 把调好的蜂蜜黑椒汁淋在鱼块和南瓜上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12807863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12807863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把蜂蜜黑椒汁淋在鱼块和南瓜上。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
