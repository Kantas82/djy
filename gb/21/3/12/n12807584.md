<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>孩子的最爱软绵奶油餐包卷 赶快学起来！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_1264-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在家用烤箱就可以做奶油卷了，一次做一大盘超有成就感，如果吃不完就先放冷冻，要吃时退冰回烤，不失美味。（图／如何出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：介绍这一款<ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a><ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A4%90%E5%8C%85.md#1">餐包</a>卷的作法比较繁杂一点，看着步骤图可以更仔细一步步学起来，做完一大盘超有成就感！</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A4%90%E5%8C%85.md#1">餐包</a>卷不只有着油亮亮的肤质，还有着漂亮的中凸多层好身材，内涵就是软绵绵、<ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E9%A6%99.md#1">奶香</a>重又有拉丝感，加上底部带着微微焦脆的<ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a>味，难怪大家爱不释手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_1266.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_1266-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奶油卷底部带着微微焦脆的奶油味，非常酥香好吃。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/yktsex3923/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9%E9%A4%90%E5%8C%85%E5%8D%B7.md#1">奶油餐包卷</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12808413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_2403.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_2403-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">做完一大盘奶油餐包可以吃好几天。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">模具</span></h3>
<p>40×30 公分烤盘材料(可做24份)</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>高筋面粉 750g<br />
全蛋 2颗<br />
奶粉 25g(可省略)<br />
无盐奶油 120g (切块，室温软化)<br />
砂糖 90g<br />
盐 10g<br />
速发酵母 10g<br />
牛奶 320ml<br />
鲜奶油 100ml</p>
<p><strong>[ 另外准备 ]</strong><br />
蛋 1 颗(拌匀)<br />
无盐奶油 55g (软化备用，放入挤花袋中)<br />
无盐奶油 40g(融化)</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e17aeab2222716945dfad6f3287ab715.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e17aeab2222716945dfad6f3287ab715-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">无盐奶油软化后，放入挤花袋中。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 烘烤前 10 分钟，烤箱预热 200 度C （上下火）。<br />
2. 鲜奶油与二颗全蛋先拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-01-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜奶油与二颗全蛋先拌匀。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将干性材料(鲜奶油、奶油、牛奶除外)放入搅拌盆，先用面团钩稍微拌匀粉类。加入鲜奶油、蛋液和牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-02-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将干性材料放入搅拌盆，先用面团钩稍微拌匀粉。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-03-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入鲜奶油、蛋液和牛奶。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 低速开始搅拌成团，可适时停下机器刮缸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-04-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">低速开始搅拌成团。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-05-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可适时停下机器刮缸。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 搅拌至爬钩后，面团有弹性可以延展拉起状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808347" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-06-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">搅拌至爬钩后，面团有弹性可以延展拉起状态。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入软化奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808348" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-07-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入软化奶油。（图／如何出版社提供</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 先以低速搅拌确认奶油吃进面团中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808349" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-08-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">以低速搅拌确认奶油吃进面团中。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 接着以中速搅拌成光滑状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808355" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-09-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中速搅拌成光滑状态。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 切下一块面团，确认薄膜产生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808356" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-10-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">切下一块面团，确认薄膜产生。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 揉圆后盖上湿布，基础发酵 20 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808357" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-11-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">揉圆放入玻璃碗内。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808358" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-12-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">盖上湿布，基础发酵 20 分钟。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808359" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-13-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团发酵后膨胀。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 秤重切割成 2 等份，排气滚圆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808360" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-14-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">秤重切割成 2 等份。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808361" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-15-450x253.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团排气滚圆。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12808362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-16-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">排气滚圆后的面团。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 盖上湿布，中间发酵松弛 10-15 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808369" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808369" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-17-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">盖上湿布，中间发酵松弛 10-15 分钟。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13. 将面团擀压成长 36 公分的圆形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808370" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-18-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将面团擀压成长 36 公分的圆形。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14. 可适当拍掉侧边气体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-19-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可适当拍掉侧边气体。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>15. 用刮刀切割成 12 等份(另一面团以同样方式 操作。)。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12808372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12808372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-6-20-450x300.jpg" alt="奶油卷制作方法" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用刮刀切割成 12 等份。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16. 将每一等份取出，平整面朝下，准备整形成卷 状。<br />
17. 擀平面团，并轻拉长尾端，细端处靠近自己。</p>
