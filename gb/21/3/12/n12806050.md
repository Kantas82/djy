<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作英国茶点 欧式风情酥松司康满足味蕾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_6770-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>英式下午茶经典的点心“香酥司康”一定不能缺席！（图／如何出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>随意抹上果酱、奶油或打发的鲜奶油，还有传统吃法搭配 clotted cream <ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%9D%E8%84%82%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">凝脂奶油</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B8%E5%BA%B7.md#1">司康</a>饼，是英国家庭必备的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E5%BF%83.md#1">点心</a>，也是英国人<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E5%8D%88%E8%8C%B6.md#1">下午茶</a>不能缺少的灵魂餐点。好吃的司康香醇顺口，松软带着奶香啊～</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P1710221.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P1710221-450x338.jpg" alt="下午茶" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">沏一壶茶搭配<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B8%E5%BA%B7.md#1">司康</a>就是“英式<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E5%8D%88%E8%8C%B6.md#1">下午茶</a>”了！想要有点甜咸的口感，可以准备好果酱和奶油，浓郁香甜丰富味蕾。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">英式风情<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E6%9D%BE%E5%8F%B8%E5%BA%B7.md#1">酥松司康</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12806929" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_6789.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/IMG_6789-450x300.jpg" alt="司康" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">司康搭配果酱、<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%9D%E8%84%82%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">凝脂奶油</a>都非常美味。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">模具</span></h3>
<p>5~6cm 司康模</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（可做6个）</span></h3>
<p>中筋面粉 250g<br />
泡打粉 8g<br />
无盐奶油 60g<br />
细砂糖 50g<br />
全蛋 1 颗<br />
动物性鲜奶油 120ml<br />
盐 1/2 小匙<br />
蔓越莓干 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 烘烤前10分钟，将烤箱预热190度C（上下火）。<br />
2. 面粉、泡打粉过筛，与盐、砂糖拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-01-450x338.jpg" alt="泡打粉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面粉、泡打粉过筛，与盐、砂糖拌匀。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 放入切成小块的冰奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-02-450x338.jpg" alt="冰奶油" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入切成小块的冰奶油。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 以拌切方式切成细砂砾状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-03-450x338.jpg" alt="面粉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">以拌切方式切成细砂砾状。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 全蛋先与鲜奶油混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-04-450x338.jpg" alt="鲜奶油" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">全蛋先与鲜奶油混合。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将蛋奶混合液体分次加入步骤4，用叉子帮助成团，此时面团黏黏湿湿的是正常的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-05-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋奶混合物分次加入干面粉（步骤4）。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806879" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-06-450x338.jpg" alt="面粉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用叉子帮助面粉混合物成团。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806881" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-07-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团黏黏湿湿是正常的。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806882" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-08-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋奶混合液与干面粉的完成品！（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 成团后，用保鲜膜包覆，放进冰箱冷藏 30 分钟-1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806892" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-09-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">成团后，用保鲜膜包覆，放进冰箱冷藏 30 分钟 -1 小时。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 取出面团后，在面团两面都铺上保鲜膜来擀平，将面团压整、对折，做6-8次，帮助成品有松软层次的口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806893" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-10-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取出面团后，在面团两面都铺上保鲜膜来擀平， 将面团压整。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806894" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-11-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将面团对折压整。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806895" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-12-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团压整、对折这个动作要6-8次。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806896" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-13-450x338.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团压整、对折，可以帮助成品有松软层次的口感。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 放上适量蔓越莓干（蔓越莓干可以视情况切小块），如果不放就是原味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806913" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-14-450x338.jpg" alt="蔓越莓干" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放上适量蔓越莓干(蔓越莓干可以视情况切小块)。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 擀到厚度约2.5公分，用压模压出，表面涂上蛋液。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-15-450x338.jpg" alt="压模" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">擀到厚度约 2.5 公分，用压模压出。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 放进烤箱烘烤20分钟，直到表面金黄即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12806916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-16-450x300.jpg" alt="烘烤" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放进烤箱烘烤20分钟。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12806918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12806918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-6-17-450x338.jpg" alt="烘烤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烘烤成品表面会呈现金黄色。（图／如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">贴心小提醒</span></h2>
<p>· 材料的液体类，可以加入水、牛奶、优格，或是鲜奶油替换。<br />
· 鲜奶油口味最绵密也最好吃，牛奶口味较清爽，优格口味适中。<strong>＠</strong></p>
