<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】松茸海参炖鸡汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>松茸海参炖鸡汤。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>节庆时分，<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B4%E5%AE%A2%E8%8F%9C.md#1">宴客菜</a>可以选择一些高贵、特殊的食材，例如<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E8%8C%B8.md#1">松茸</a>就有讨吉利的象征。日本人称在非时令的梅雨季节长出的松茸为“早松”，其价值更高。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E8%8C%B8.md#1">松茸</a>好生于养份不多而且比较干燥的林地，一般在秋季生成，通常寄生于赤松、偃松、铁杉、日本铁杉的根部，也有寄生在黑松根部的例子，但较为少见。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">松茸<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">炖鸡汤</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【材料】</strong></span></h3>
<p>松茸4安士<br />
发好的<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a>1磅<br />
黄毛鸡半只<br />
元肉（桂圆）1安士<br />
姜3片<br />
红萝卜花6片<br />
水6杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/1-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781436" class="wp-caption-text">姜、红萝卜花、黄毛鸡、松茸、海参、元肉。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【作法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 松茸清理干净后切片，海参切大块，鸡斩成大件。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-e1614600756590.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/2-e1614600756590-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781437" class="wp-caption-text">海参切大块。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 松茸、海参和鸡肉都用姜汁走烫过，捞出沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781438" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉用姜汁走烫过。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把松茸外的所有材料放进慢炖锅中，加入6杯滚水，盖上锅盖炖1小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781439" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781439" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781439" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781439" class="wp-caption-text">把松茸外的所有材料放进慢炖锅中，加入6杯滚水。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再加入松茸片多炖30分钟后，便可加盐调味，汤渣可捞出沾点酱油吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781440" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/cb8c83318c6348d2d2afe2ea5302a3a7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/cb8c83318c6348d2d2afe2ea5302a3a7-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781440" class="wp-caption-text">加入松茸片多炖30分钟后，便可加盐调味，汤渣可捞出沾点酱油吃。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 如有剩下的鸡汤，隔天可放入一个炖盅内再炖热食用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12781442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12781442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12781442" class="wp-caption-text">如有剩下的鸡汤，隔天可放入一个炖盅内再炖热食用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
