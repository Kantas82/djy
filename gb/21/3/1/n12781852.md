<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>徐明义画集8—一行白鹭上群山（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/fc579313d3285c271635acef07acdf31-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—一行白鹭上群山（彩墨）。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%80%E8%A1%8C%E7%99%BD%E9%B9%AD%E4%B8%8A%E7%BE%A4%E5%B1%B1.md#1">一行白鹭上群山</a></h2>
<p>2013年，我在台中大墩文化中心个展。一位老先生拉着我，找到挂在墙角的一幅画作，说：“你就画你这样的画，其它的就让别人去画。”他当时很鼓励我用大色块、大墨块的构图，认为这样的画很有特色，也很有张力。</p>
<p>如今，随着年岁的增长，年逾古稀的我，却不自觉的又慢慢的走回头路，开始玩起墨来了，渐渐的在墨韵中去发现，并找到水墨交融的真趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12781879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/fc579313d3285c271635acef07acdf31-e1614582659993.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12781879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/fc579313d3285c271635acef07acdf31-600x577.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="577" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%86.md#1">徐明义画集</a>8—<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%80%E8%A1%8C%E7%99%BD%E9%B9%AD%E4%B8%8A%E7%BE%A4%E5%B1%B1.md#1">一行白鹭上群山</a>（<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。63×64cm。（图片来源：<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>A flock of egrets flew to mountains</h2>
<p>(ink and color painting)</p>
<p>In 2013, my individual exhibition was held at the Da dun Cultural Center in Taichung. An old gentleman grabbed me to find a painting hanging on the corner and said, &#8220;You just draw painting like you drew this one, leave others with other people&#8221; He encouraged me to use large color block, large ink block for composition, he thought that this kind of painting is very unique, character and very tension.</p>
<p>Nowadays, as I am getting older, more than seventy years old, unconsciously and slowly return to back route, starts playing Chinese ink, and gradually explore from the ink rhyme and find the true interest of blend ink and wash.@</p>
