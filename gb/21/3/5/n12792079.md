<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>惊螫鹅颈桥“打小人” 发现多张高官照片</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/45672654c9894fa5beb3c9e170b2f6e6-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年03月05日讯】（大纪元记者梁珍香港报导）今天（5日）是中国24节气“<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%8A%E8%9E%AB.md#1">惊螫</a>”，有不少市民按传统习俗到铜锣湾<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%85%E9%A2%88%E6%A1%A5.md#1">鹅颈桥</a>底“<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E5%B0%8F%E4%BA%BA.md#1">打小人</a>”，希望今年事事顺利。记者晚上8时许到达<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%85%E9%A2%88%E6%A1%A5.md#1">鹅颈桥</a>底已见人头涌涌，在疫情下打小人路边档前仍约有200名市民排队。</p>
<p>六十多岁档主接受探访时表示今年客人多数祈求“身体健康”、“赶走疫情”及“工作顺利”等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/93f9ca6da8465cd476e42e467bade47d.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12792121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/93f9ca6da8465cd476e42e467bade47d-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">铜锣湾鹅颈桥底排队<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%93%E5%B0%8F%E4%BA%BA.md#1">打小人</a>市民众多。（梁珍／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场市民<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%85%AC%E5%AD%90.md#1">刘公子</a>称他专程来打小人，因昨天47人的聆讯结果令他“一肚气”又“喊餐饱”，必须来消消气。他心目中有六名“小人”但不便开名，但港人应该不难猜到。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792117" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3e65caa7dff81678adf90c83ba3be306.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12792117" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/3e65caa7dff81678adf90c83ba3be306-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">现场市民<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%85%AC%E5%AD%90.md#1">刘公子</a>称他专程来打小人，因昨天47人的聆讯结果令他“一肚气”。（梁珍／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>记者现场所见地上留下不少高官的照片，包括：张建宗、李惠琼、邓炳强等。有街坊表示今年有较年轻的市民来打小人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/be72835bfe55584f8c51c147a325cdf8.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12792119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/be72835bfe55584f8c51c147a325cdf8-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">地上有人留下数张高官的照片。（梁珍／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12792118" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e5deb9904b2897af39ef8f3c959a4520.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12792118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e5deb9904b2897af39ef8f3c959a4520-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">地上有人留下数张领导人及高官的照片。（梁珍／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2位年轻市民今年第一次来“打小人”，原因是今年社会上整体环境及对未来生活的期望都较差，虽然打小人这个仪式不知道是否有助实现愿望，但仍想给自己一个小希望，给自己多点正能量。@</p>
<figure id="attachment_12792120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/805cce6001fc4951051e28e6a1b18df0.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12792120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/805cce6001fc4951051e28e6a1b18df0-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在疫情下打小人路边档前仍约有200名市民排队。（梁珍／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
