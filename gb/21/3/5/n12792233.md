<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不败经典单品 丹宁牛仔外套穿出时尚风</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1269296024-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>丹宁牛仔街头风万年不败，帅气无人能敌。（Gareth Cattermole/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年03月11日讯】（大纪元记者林溪编译报导）有没有一种单品是不分性别、年龄，人人都适合穿着的呢？想必牛仔<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E5%A5%97.md#1">外套</a>是大家心目中的共同选择。</p>
<p>牛仔布，又称<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B9%E5%AE%81.md#1">丹宁</a>布，由denim音译得名。在纺织业中，定义为数支粗纱以斜纹交织而成的布料，经纱用靛青色染过、纬纱则是灰、白原色，因此牛仔布料近看时能在蓝中看到点点的灰白，那便是背面的纬纱颜色。</p>
<p>当假日出游还在犹豫该穿什么时，牛仔<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E5%A5%97.md#1">外套</a>能解决你的烦恼，依照着各式不同剪裁，能展现清新优雅氛围，以及街头率性风格。<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%A4%96%E5%A5%97.md#1">牛仔外套</a>多变的样式，已成为经典不败的活跃时尚单品，更拥有一年四季都百搭的实穿性。</p>
<h2>经典款</h2>
<p>靛青色是牛仔布料最经典的颜色，胸前两个口袋以及明显的车线、金属扣子也是其标志性装饰，是男女皆可穿着的款式。若觉得款式太普通无趣，立领的细节变化内搭洋装、帽Ｔ，各自带来优雅知性及街头率性，休闲又好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12794891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-824293236.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12794891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-824293236-450x674.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">电视名人 Luann D&#8217;Agostino 穿着经典<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E5%A4%96%E5%A5%97.md#1">牛仔外套</a>，搭配白底红花洋装，展现浪漫随意的轻松风格。（Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12796686" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1228588717.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12796686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1228588717-450x622.jpg" alt="丹宁" width="450" b="622" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法国演员班杰明?瓦赞（Benjamin Voisin）出席第68届圣赛巴斯提安国际影展，简单的牛仔外套和帽Ｔ，展现街头率性风格，下身选择西装裤，又能让造型不至于随便。 （ANDER GILLENEA/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>风衣牛仔外套</h2>
<p>传统风衣穿腻了吗？牛仔风衣的御寒性虽然比不上传统材质，但象征自由独立的<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B9%E5%AE%81.md#1">丹宁</a>和简约大气的风衣组合，却带出另一种强烈韧性。</p>
<p>有腰身的款式适合女性，单穿搭配长靴或紧身裤就能立刻出门；宽松款则男女皆宜，既轻松又耐看，虽然材质硬挺，依然走路若有风。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12795002" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1302014503.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1302014503-450x676.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔, 时装周" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴黎2021春夏<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E8%A3%85%E5%91%A8.md#1">时装周</a>，风衣款式的牛仔外套单穿搭配长靴是个好选择。（Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo) (Photo by Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12795001" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1277623396.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1277623396-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔, 时装周" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴黎2021春夏<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E8%A3%85%E5%91%A8.md#1">时装周</a>，法国时装品牌巴尔曼（Balmain）秀场，宽大的风衣牛仔外套帅气有型。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>立体装饰牛仔外套</h2>
<p>牛仔所象征的“自由”精神，透过牛仔布料承传至今，当然也容许你在上面挥洒自己的个性，立体装饰丰富了配色，涂鸦式的文字、图案相当活泼，让牛仔外套不再单调。</p>
<p>这类款式搭配特别合适和深色系服装搭配，例如皮裤、黑帽Ｔ都是不错的选择，散发出狂野独特的魅力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12795056" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1302033745.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-1302033745-450x676.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴黎2021春夏时装周，有如街头涂鸦般的立体拼贴装饰，令人眼花撩乱却有个性。（Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo) (Photo by Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12795058" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-904925270.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795058" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-904925270-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tina Neumann出席电影《Hilfe, ich hab meine Eltern geschrumpftn》首映会，牛仔外套上的拼贴装饰十分童趣，搭配短板造型，看起来青春洋溢。（Andreas Rentz/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>oversize牛仔外套</h2>
<p>oversize版型分为强调肩部宽度的剪裁、以及消减肩膀轮廓的落肩款。前者多加有垫肩、泡泡袖剪裁等，而牛仔布硬挺的特性更是锦上添花，让过大的剪裁线条自然俐落，穿着时身材纤细的人能加强份量，丰腴的人也能隐藏身材；落肩款就柔和许多，通常伴随宽大袖子，视觉上给人舒服的感受。</p>

Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo) (Photo by Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>牛仔西装外套</h2>
<p>牛仔单品与西装外套的组合相当有趣，融合正式与休闲，搭配简单的黑白灰三色，就是套完美的造型。当然不同的内搭和裤子会带来完全相异的感受，而颜色上也有区分，深色比浅色更为正式一些。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12795067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-51268899.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-51268899-450x695.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔" width="450" b="695" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">妮可?基嫚（Nicole Kidman）出席第61届威尼斯影展，带有腰身的牛仔西装外套衬出她优雅的气质。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>短板牛仔外套</h2>
<p>摩登的短板外套本身就能起到增加视觉身高、修长双腿、显瘦的效果，而短板牛仔外套更增添精神，为整体服装增加层次感，轻盈有型，男女都适合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12795165" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-139854678.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12795165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/GettyImages-139854678-450x676.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 牛仔" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美国女演员萝拉?邓恩（Laura Dern）出席2012年电影独立精神奖颁奖典礼。落地花洋装外穿短板牛仔外套，相当适合春天。（Alberto E. <h2>彩色牛仔外套</h2>
<p>牛仔的经典颜色是靛青，但满街的靛青看久了总觉得腻，不妨尝试彩色牛仔外套吧！最简单的就是黑、灰、白三色，带有酷酷的中性感；缤纷的色彩和牛仔的粗犷相对比，撞击出耳目一新的感受，无论男女穿搭都很亮眼。</p>
