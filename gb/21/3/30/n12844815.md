<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手工苏打牛轧饼 香酥脆咸不腻味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857080-ee405895d8573a46453c76b81e48976c-600x400.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>香酥脆咸的<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93%E9%A5%BC.md#1">苏打饼</a>，搭配柔软奶香的牛轧馅，吃起来酥软兼具，是大人小孩都喜欢的点心。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93.md#1">苏打</a>牛轧饼</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">约35片</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料A</span></h3>
<p>砂糖?????110克<br />
水麦芽????100克<br />
水???????35克<br />
盐巴???????2克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料B</span></h3>
<p>蛋白??????15克<br />
砂糖??????15克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料C</span></h3>
<p>奶油??????30克<br />
奶粉??????40克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">其他</span><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>青葱<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93%E9%A5%BC.md#1">苏打饼</a>干???适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 材料A全部混和并用中火煮至130～135度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12857082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857082" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857082-259319fefa682ba766b4232ef8d79964.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12857082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857082-259319fefa682ba766b4232ef8d79964-450x281.png" alt="砂糖" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857082" class="wp-caption-text">砂糖、水麦芽、水、盐巴全部混合并用中火煮至130～135度。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 材料B使用打蛋器，快速打发至硬性发泡状（前端成弯钩状不下垂）。</p>
<p>3 将材料Ｂ边搅拌边加入材料Ａ，并搅拌均匀成蛋白糊。</p>
<p>4 将材料C加入蛋白糊，并搅拌均匀成浓稠状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12857083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857083" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857083-7bb09d64640818d85f2d7abf9657de43.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12857083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857083-7bb09d64640818d85f2d7abf9657de43-450x285.png" alt="" width="450" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857083" class="wp-caption-text">奶油、奶粉加入蛋白糊里。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 将馅料夹入两片青葱<ahref="https://github.com/sjycbn3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E6%89%93.md#1">苏打</a>饼干中间即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12857088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857088-4d6e1e5273f7d66e3691ff1ecd6e3bc9.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12857088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857088-4d6e1e5273f7d66e3691ff1ecd6e3bc9-450x297.png" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857088" class="wp-caption-text">将馅料夹入两片青葱苏打饼干中间即可。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
