<hr>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%81%E9%A6%99.md#1">沁香</a></strong></h2>
<p>偶尔在画画的当儿会突发奇想：技巧再好也比不上境界的深奇，境界应该是比技巧更重要吧。</p>
<p>就如同吾人画工笔画，好像只要时间足够，就能够有一张色彩斑斓的、瑰丽的、有装饰性的画出来，而全然可以不顾它是不是有内涵、有思想；也不管会不会把它给画“板”了。（板就是死板、呆板）</p>
<p>这张画，就是在这种偶然跑来小小的心灵触动之下动笔的——题名作“<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%81%E9%A6%99.md#1">沁香</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12799792" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e6d1d6e6baec14298f0a6232d578547f-e1615293515743.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12799792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/e6d1d6e6baec14298f0a6232d578547f-600x577.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="578" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%86.md#1">徐明义画集</a>8—沁香（<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）68×69cm。（图片来源：<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Aroma moisturizing??ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Occasionally, a sudden whim came up when I am painting: no better drawing skills can compete with the deeper realm, which is certainly more important than drawing skill.</p>
<p>Take drawing the traditional Chinese realistic painting for instance, it seems that a colorful, magnificent and decorative painting can be drew as long as time is enough and ignore connotation and thought behind; does not care whether the painting is drew as a “board” (Board means rigid and dull without connotation and thought inside the painting) this is not correct.</p>
<p>This painting was mad by occasional heart touched &#8211; the title is given &#8221; Aroma moisturizing&#8221;.＠</p>
