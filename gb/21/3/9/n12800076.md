<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花卉香草焚香棒 充满创意巧思的香氛法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P147-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“花卉鼠尾草焚香棒”必须先风干三周后才可以点燃使用。（图／采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>焚香棒是让屋里弥漫花香的好方法。介绍您这一款创意巧思的<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B0%9B.md#1">香氛</a>法，请确认使用的是白色<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%A0%E5%B0%BE%E8%8D%89.md#1">鼠尾草</a>，因为料理用的鼠尾草燃烧起来味道可不好闻。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">花卉<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%A0%E5%B0%BE%E8%8D%89.md#1">鼠尾草</a>焚香棒</span></h2>
<p>香调：花香调<br />
准备时间：10 分钟<br />
制作分量：2 支<br />
干燥时间：3 周</p>
<p>使用器具：料理用棉线<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E6%B0%9B.md#1">香氛</a>材料：新鲜白鼠尾草1小束（或干燥的白鼠尾草1把）、新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%96%B0%E8%A1%A3%E8%8D%89.md#1">薰衣草</a>的茎1把、新鲜<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AB%E7%91%B0%E8%8A%B1.md#1">玫瑰花</a>瓣1把、<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%B7%E8%BF%AD%E9%A6%99.md#1">迷迭香</a>嫩枝1根、秘鲁圣木2根</p>
<figure id="attachment_12800100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12800100" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P146.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12800100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/P146-450x608.jpg" alt="鼠尾草" width="450" b="608" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12800100" class="wp-caption-text">香氛准备材料：白鼠尾草、<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%96%B0%E8%A1%A3%E8%8D%89.md#1">薰衣草</a>的茎、<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AB%E7%91%B0%E8%8A%B1.md#1">玫瑰花</a>瓣1、<ahref="https://github.com/focbmg344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%B7%E8%BF%AD%E9%A6%99.md#1">迷迭香</a>嫩枝、秘鲁圣木。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">制作步骤</span></h3>
<p>1. 以秘鲁圣木为轴心，利用较长的香草类植物缠绕花瓣，将所有材料扎成一束，以料理用棉线绑紧。</p>
<p>2. 吊挂风干三周，直到完全干燥。使用时，放在耐热的盘子、广口瓶或锡盒上，一端点火燃烧。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Tips 也可直接使用干燥材料，但制作上可能会稍微困难。＠</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12800037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12800037" style="width: 113px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6e553810a068af8e0852f8217c20b640.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12800037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/6e553810a068af8e0852f8217c20b640-450x600.jpg" alt="香氛" width="113" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12800037" class="wp-caption-text">《天然香氛60款》书封。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
