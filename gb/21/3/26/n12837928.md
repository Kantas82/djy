<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东三大日音乐节   梦幻卡司接力开唱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490253-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>灭火器。（屏东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年03月26日讯】（大纪元记者简惠敏台湾屏东报导）“<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E4%B8%89%E5%A4%A7%E6%97%A5%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E8%8A%82.md#1">屏东三大日音乐节</a>”4月9、10、11日将在屏东县体育馆前草地举办，继前两周发表的Hebe<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E9%A6%A5%E7%94%84.md#1">田馥甄</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AD%E7%81%AB%E5%99%A8.md#1">灭火器</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%8A%BF%E5%AE%89.md#1">陈势安</a>等卡司于网路造成轰动后，屏东县政府26日将3天的卡司一次公布，让粉丝们期待度破表。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E4%B8%89%E5%A4%A7%E6%97%A5%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E8%8A%82.md#1">屏东三大日音乐节</a>今年迈入第四届，是屏东县政府打造的大型音乐活动，在每年春天挑选3天举办。今年第一天“经典回忆那年代”邀请情歌王子<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%8A%BF%E5%AE%89.md#1">陈势安</a>、戴爱玲、<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E8%8A%B3.md#1">万芳</a>、金智娟；第2天“文青悠游练缱绻”有华语乐坛天后Hebe<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E9%A6%A5%E7%94%84.md#1">田馥甄</a>、阎奕格、鼓鼓 吕思纬、李友廷与白安；第3天“摇滚热血来朝拜”则有人气乐团<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AD%E7%81%AB%E5%99%A8.md#1">灭火器</a>、告五人、娄峻硕及好乐团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12837931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12837931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490254.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12837931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490254-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12837931" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%87%E8%8A%B3.md#1">万芳</a>。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县府传播暨国际事务处表示，今年疫情已相对稳定，但还请大家观赏表演时做好防疫措施，请于活动出入口处依指示配合测量体温并配戴口罩入场，以保护自身及他人的健康。无法到现场的民众，也可透过电视频道MTV台、MOD?、MTV综合台、MOD三立综合台、三立新闻网、三立新闻FB、Vidol视频网、Line Today等，一同欣赏精彩的表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12837932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12837932" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490255.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12837932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490255-600x471.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12837932" class="wp-caption-text">陈势安。（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12837933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12837933" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490256.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12837933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/490256-600x385.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12837933" class="wp-caption-text">娄峻硕（屏东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除“屏东三大日音乐节”外，屏县府也将于4月1日到4月3日，在垦丁举办“台湾祭”活动。县府表示，洋溢热带岛屿风情的垦丁，一直是独立乐团、野台开唱的圣地，“台湾祭”就是为让独立乐团和乐迷重拾当年“春呐”的音乐感动。连续两周的大型音乐，势必造成一房难求，呼吁全台粉丝手刀快抢车票、订旅宿，准备提起行囊到屏东听音乐，享受屏东温暖多一度的美好假期。</p>
