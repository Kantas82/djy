<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>一年一度的雪雁大迁徙 壮观场面难得一见</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/5b21907d3163ac95-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>迎着朝阳起舞的雪雁。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>每年的二月底至三月初，大批的<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E9%9B%81.md#1">雪雁</a>在北归的途中会在美国东部宾州的中溪野生动物管理区(Middle Creek Wildlife Management Area)聚集停留栖息。一般都有十几万只，最多的一次是2017年。那年有二十多万只雪雁在中溪停留。</p>
<p>今年的高峰出现在三月九日，有十二万只<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E9%9B%81.md#1">雪雁</a>在中溪中转“加油”。我很幸运的赶上了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812015" style="width: 1189px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_02.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_02.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙, 候鸟" width="1189" b="790" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812015" class="wp-caption-text">在水中栖息的雪雁。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中溪野生动物管理区占地6254英亩，最大的人工湖有360英亩，也是雪雁的主要栖息地。还有大大小小30多个水塘与湿地。管理区还有756英亩游客止步的野生动物繁殖区。</p>
<p>沿着人工湖有七个观景点，其中二号观景点柳树塘(Willow Pond)无疑是最理想的。而观赏雪雁活动的最佳时段是日落日出时分。</p>
<p>我在日落前一个多小时赶到了柳树塘，迎面就被湖面上像雪花一般密密麻麻白白一片的雪雁惊呆了。</p>
<p>大多数雪雁在水中栖息，也有在水上嬉戏，在空中展翅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812014" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812014" style="width: 1204px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_03.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_03.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙, 候鸟" width="1204" b="791" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812014" class="wp-caption-text">水中嬉戏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812013" style="width: 1207px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_04.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_04.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙, 候鸟" width="1207" b="778" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812013" class="wp-caption-text">水中嬉戏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812012" style="width: 1261px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_05.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_05.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1261" b="916" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812012" class="wp-caption-text">水中嬉戏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812011" style="width: 1268px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_06.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_06.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1268" b="845" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812011" class="wp-caption-text">空中展翅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812010" style="width: 1285px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_07.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_07.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1285" b="898" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812010" class="wp-caption-text">空中芭蕾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雪雁择偶态度保守，一夫一妻，从一而终。家庭生活模式是男主外，女主内。雪雁妈妈负责抱窝，雪雁爸爸负责守护。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812009" style="width: 1270px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_08.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_08.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1270" b="831" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812009" class="wp-caption-text">比翼双飞。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812008" style="width: 1271px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_09.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_09.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1271" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812008" class="wp-caption-text">夫唱妇随。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812007" style="width: 1236px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_10.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_10.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1236" b="821" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812007" class="wp-caption-text">倦鸟晚归。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>突然间，所有的雪雁呼啦啦一下子从水面上、地面上起飞，扑天盖地从头上呼啸而过。这场景太过惊心动魄！也不知道它们中谁是总司令，又是如何发号施令的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812006" style="width: 1257px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_11.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_11.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1257" b="820" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812006" class="wp-caption-text">黎明时的柳树塘。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812005" style="width: 1272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_12.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_12.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1272" b="804" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812005" class="wp-caption-text">残月飞鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二天起了个大早，5:30就架好三脚架在水边等待日出的精彩时分。雪雁们尽管都还凫在水面上休息，但嘴里鼓噪不停。有道是：“柳塘边，晓风残月，听取雁声一片。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812004" style="width: 1268px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_13.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_13.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1268" b="843" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812004" class="wp-caption-text">晨曦中的雪雁。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>霎那间，水面上惊起一片雪雁。人群中有人高喊了一声：“太阳升起来了！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812003" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812003" style="width: 1329px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_14.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_14.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1329" b="845" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812003" class="wp-caption-text">日出柳树塘。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812002" style="width: 1329px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_15.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_15.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1329" b="883" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812002" class="wp-caption-text">日照柳树塘。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12812001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812001" style="width: 1333px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_16.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_16.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1333" b="886" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812001" class="wp-caption-text">金色的狂欢。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几万只的雪雁在朝阳下高歌起舞，场面惊人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12812000" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12812000" style="width: 1332px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_17.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12812000" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_17.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1332" b="868" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12812000" class="wp-caption-text">晨舞。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水中的雪雁迎着朝阳张开翅膀，尽情抖动。新的一天开始了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12811999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12811999" style="width: 1337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_18.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12811999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/20210315_helen_chou_bird_18.jpg" alt="雪雁, 迁徙" width="1337" b="893" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12811999" class="wp-caption-text">一路往北。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而心急的一批已经在蓝天上列队，在领头雁的带领下飞向北方。它们中很多将一路向北，向北，直到北极。</p>
