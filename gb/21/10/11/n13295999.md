<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>省钱又好吃的欧式茶点：葡萄干燕麦饼干</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13317908-d0b9f75d1ead3ac03ab252cdbc5ba8b8-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>葡萄干燕麦饼干（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这款饼干用到的材料少、作法简单，需要现学现卖的时候，保证可以一举成功，风味也不会让人失望，一口咬下会先品尝到酥脆口感，接着会传来<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84.md#1">葡萄</a>干的甜味，以及燕麦的独特风味，绝对会让你一口接一口。我想不出有谁可以抗拒它，老少咸宜、男女通吃、人见人爱！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">示范影片</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13296133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296133" style="width: 164px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296133-771260ec9c0fa94b6414ac03fa2e8c60.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13296133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296133-771260ec9c0fa94b6414ac03fa2e8c60.jpg" alt="" width="164" b="164" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296133" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h2>
<p>中筋面粉100g<br />
无盐奶油110g<br />
二号砂糖80g<br />
泡打粉1茶匙<br />
燕麦120g<br />
全蛋1颗<br />
盐巴1小撮<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84.md#1">葡萄</a>干50g<br />
兰姆酒 10g<strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E5%B9%B2.md#1">葡萄干</a>泡酒</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.将<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E5%B9%B2.md#1">葡萄干</a>浸泡在兰姆酒中。如果是要做给小朋友吃的，请省略此步骤。</p>
<p>Tips:浸泡时间最佳为30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296211-b5704b359709f1da506308c95a083d8b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296211-b5704b359709f1da506308c95a083d8b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296211" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-1（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>制作面团</strong></span></h3>
<p>2.将常温奶油和砂糖、盐搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296215" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296215-d4d1dfbf6857ffdc6b57b1d04402f5d7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296215-d4d1dfbf6857ffdc6b57b1d04402f5d7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296215" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-2（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.加入蛋液，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296216" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296216-008c4442ff54fc7544ae089f5247f642.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296216-008c4442ff54fc7544ae089f5247f642-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296216" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-3（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入过筛的中筋面粉，搅拌成面团状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296218-12494d3285591bb3209f958ef0512848.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296218-12494d3285591bb3209f958ef0512848-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296218" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-4（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>加入配料</strong></span></h3>
<p>5.加入燕麦，混合均匀。</p>
<p>Tips: 燕麦与葡萄干分别加入拌匀，会较好操作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296221" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296221-0011f03972ec2372a2cfad113582fe20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296221-0011f03972ec2372a2cfad113582fe20-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296221" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-5（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.加入葡萄乾和未被吸收的兰姆酒，混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296227-334a527e2a4a06766786fc3e9d1d2f69.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296227-334a527e2a4a06766786fc3e9d1d2f69-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296227" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-6（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>成型＆烘烤</strong></span></h3>
<p>7.将面团依个人喜好揉成适当的圆球大小，再压扁。</p>
<p>Tips:若发现面团非常沾手，可放置于冷藏20分钟后再进行压制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296232-adbb140d4727aba82f9e8a660427cf6f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296232-adbb140d4727aba82f9e8a660427cf6f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296232" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-7（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13296234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296234" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296234-7d3b66dc61a86d8e64b63320cf094930.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296234-7d3b66dc61a86d8e64b63320cf094930-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296234" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄燕麦饼干-8（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将烤箱预热至170度，烘烤约15分钟。</p>
<p>Tips: 将其烤至金黄色泽即可，可视情况加烤2~3分钟。</p>
<p>要做就多做一点，反正最后都会被抢光！<span style="color: #000000;">?<strong>⊙</strong></span></p>
