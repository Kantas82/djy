<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作贵妇的午茶甜点：达克瓦兹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313172-15e1edb75ba57cb125933a921b20d62f-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>达克瓦兹。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>外层酥脆，内馅柔软轻盈的<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%BE%E5%85%8B%E7%93%A6%E5%85%B9.md#1">达克瓦兹</a>，一口咬下就会强烈感受到杏仁<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E5%B9%B2.md#1">饼干</a>和内馅的香气，优雅又迷人，让人一秒到法国。因其制作过程相似，被喻为是<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%8D%A1%E9%BE%99.md#1">马卡龙</a>的姊妹<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>，但成功率高且甜度相较较低，也掳获了不少人的心。想要举办一场沙龙Salon吗?达克瓦兹当成下午<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6.md#1">茶</a>绝对不漏气!</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">示范影片</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13296082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296082" style="width: 196px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296082-96a843476067b74b122be39db8e1b229.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13296082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296082-96a843476067b74b122be39db8e1b229.jpg" alt="" width="196" b="196" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296082" class="wp-caption-text">（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E5%B9%B2.md#1">饼干</a>体】</strong></span></h3>
<p>杏仁粉80g<br />
糖粉55g<br />
低筋面粉10g<br />
盐巴1g<br />
二号砂糖35g<br />
蛋白110g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【内馅】</strong></span></h3>
<p>牛奶80g<br />
香草籽酱 少许<br />
二号砂糖40g<br />
玉米粉5g<br />
奶油100g<br />
蛋黄2颗<br />
盐巴一搓<br />
柠檬皮 适量<strong>?</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>模型</strong></span></h3>
<p>椭圆形<ahref="https://github.com/zmkilp3104/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%BE%E5%85%8B%E7%93%A6%E5%85%B9.md#1">达克瓦兹</a>模<strong>?</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>混合粉材</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 将杏仁粉、低筋面粉、糖粉过筛混合拌匀，加入盐巴。</p>
<p>Tips：做达克瓦兹时，所有粉材都要过筛，成品的口感和外观才会细致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296086" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296086-86cbdbcee53ae3889a9a456f1464da50.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296086-86cbdbcee53ae3889a9a456f1464da50-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296086" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-8（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>打发蛋白</strong></span></h3>
<p>2. 用持手搅拌器将蛋白以高速打发，起泡后分两次加入砂糖，打发至硬性发泡，提起蛋白霜尖角不会下垂的状态。</p>
<p>Tips：打至硬性发泡后，可以再用慢速打20秒，可降低消泡的情形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296088-facebd6d9234b0646b9826ec0a7b6b33.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296088-facebd6d9234b0646b9826ec0a7b6b33-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296088" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-9（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13296089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296089" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296089-52801e23b9359e0d561423f61d5c8335.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296089-52801e23b9359e0d561423f61d5c8335-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296089" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-12（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>面糊完成</strong></span></h3>
<p>3. 将步骤1过筛好的粉材分两次加入打发蛋白中，以切拌的方式混合至没有粉末。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296093-52f1111be89e4c69ca94a8af0bcf8f18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296093-52f1111be89e4c69ca94a8af0bcf8f18-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296093" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-13（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13296095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296095-3607c48f04999130db456a936372648b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296095" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296095-3607c48f04999130db456a936372648b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296095" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-14（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>入模</strong></span></h3>
<p>4. 利用挤花袋将面糊入模，挤的时候要从模型边缘往内填充，填满至与模型同高。</p>
<p>Tips：模具下面记得要垫上烘焙纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296097" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296097" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296097-57a12d5aa7d0f8a7db3c73c89653967d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296097" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296097-57a12d5aa7d0f8a7db3c73c89653967d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296097" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-15（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用刮板抹平表面。刮的次数越少越好，避免消泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296098-5af8e5a57ebd0d41efb22ac9b7e79e83.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296098" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296098-5af8e5a57ebd0d41efb22ac9b7e79e83-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296098" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-17（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 慢慢把模具拿起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296100" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296100-7ccdf8c790912a96cf5dfa0779bfa98f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296100-7ccdf8c790912a96cf5dfa0779bfa98f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296100" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-18（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>烘烤</strong></span></h3>
<p>7 .撒上糖粉，且需要撒两次，放入预热后的烤箱，以上火180度、下火160度，烘烤16分钟。</p>
<p>Tips：饼干糊是湿的，撒上糖粉会被吸附，所以要撒两次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296101-85b5c40cfea387b33fd7d75c3f01e8ca.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296101-85b5c40cfea387b33fd7d75c3f01e8ca-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296101" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-20（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?</strong><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>制作内馅</strong></span></p>
<p>8. 将牛奶、香草籽酱加热搅拌，加热至锅边冒小泡即可关火备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296103-4878de964e84778fac08f8db72097e27.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296103-4878de964e84778fac08f8db72097e27-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296103" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-1（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9 .将蛋黄、砂糖搅拌均匀后，加入过筛的玉米粉和低筋面粉，搅拌至没有颗粒。<br />
Tips：蛋黄跟砂糖搅拌到反白即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296107-20a8c49845528d5c8f8a84b5eea31efb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296107-20a8c49845528d5c8f8a84b5eea31efb-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296107" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-2（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 放入盐巴，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296108" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296108-b59d450745d2dbcdb41b05cb45390713.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296108-b59d450745d2dbcdb41b05cb45390713-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296108" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-3（采实文化提供）<strong>?</strong></figcaption></figure>
<p>11 .倒入步骤10的加热牛奶，一边快速搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296110" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296110-0cb961947ef5494c1cdd276c73844c44.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296110-0cb961947ef5494c1cdd276c73844c44-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296110" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-4（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 将牛奶面糊倒入不沾锅中，以中小火加热搅拌至半固态状态即可。</p>
<p>Tips：搅拌的时候不要太大力，让卡士达酱均匀凝固即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13296116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13296116" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296116-352360a24969ce798235e15c6be71138.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13296116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13296116-352360a24969ce798235e15c6be71138-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13296116" class="wp-caption-text">达克瓦兹-5（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13.将内馅过筛，使口感更滑顺。</p>

<p>14. 加入柠檬皮，增加香气。</p>
