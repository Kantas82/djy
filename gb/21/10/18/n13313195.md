<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>动手做：山景公布栏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313206-0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>漂亮的山景让人在室内都能感受到大自然的气息。（图／张铭芬提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>人在狭小的办公室里待久了，情绪或做事效率较会受到不良的影响。若有开阔的空间感，心情会较舒畅。适当的环境布置是一个改变环境局限的好方法，既可有效降低闷在办公室里的负面感受也可提高心情指数，在布置过程中，也可享受手做的疗愈感和成就感喔！</p>
<p>*? *? *</p>
<p><strong>材料:</strong> 云彩纸、粉彩纸、水彩纸、云龙棉纸、水彩颜料和蜡笔、剪刀、双面胶带、白胶、裁剪数条约0.5公分宽的细纸条及2公分宽的粗纸条、原子笔芯、蜡光色纸。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p><strong>1、构思山景概略图：</strong>有近景：大树、夕阳、草、花、河流；远景：山、云朵。山峦、河流是视觉主要引导者，带出框框里深远的视觉感受。可以画草图协助构思(实际做时需要放大)，笔者喜欢直接在“画面上”思考，所以直接在<ahref="https://github.com/yomtaa338/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%91%8A%E6%A0%8F.md#1">布告栏</a>上一一做出各配件，再视情况调整各配件最后的大小。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313228-wenhua1-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313228 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313228-wenhua1-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>2、剪出大小不一的山:</strong>用绿色系的彩色纸张(云彩纸、粉彩纸或丹迪纸等皆可)、依序剪出从近(画面上左侧边、墨绿色、大山)至远(浅蓝绿、浅黄色、小山)的山头。</p>
<p><strong>3、安排一间小屋：</strong>屋子的大小确定后，如下图所示剪出梯形的屋顶、长方形的屋身、大小略有差异的门和小小的门把，再用深浅颜色的相叠做出有明暗感的门及把手，黏组成一间小屋。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313229-wenhua1-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313229 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313229-wenhua1-2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313286-wenhua1-3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13313286 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313286-wenhua1-3.jpg" alt="" width="490" b="626" /></a></p>
<p><strong>4、剪一条蜿蜒的河流，</strong>先用蜡笔大致画上一些石头、青苔或水草，再用淡淡的水彩刷上夕阳下的黄河流。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313231-wenhua1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313231 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313231-wenhua1-4-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a></p>
<p><strong>5、做云朵:</strong></p>
<p>(1)为保持桌面清洁，先在桌面上放上旧报纸、铺平云龙棉纸(纤维较粗做斯贴画的质感比较好），再用淡淡的黄色、橙色水彩在棉纸上刷上夕阳下的云，阴干。</p>
<p>(2)上好色的云龙棉纸晾干后，用干净的水彩笔沾清水，在棉纸上画出想要的云朵形状，再用手将画了形状的云撕开。????? <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313232-wenhua1-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313232 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313232-wenhua1-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>6、做树干：</strong></p>
<p>(1)剪一段纸藤，将其反卷摊开，依树干粗细及大致位置剪出略由粗至稍细的形状。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313288-wenhua1-6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13313288 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313288-wenhua1-6.jpg" alt="" width="545" b="414" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313289-wenhua1-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313289 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313289-wenhua1-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313287-wenhua1-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313287 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313287-wenhua1-8-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a></p>
<p>(2)在背面贴上双面胶带，在<ahref="https://github.com/yomtaa338/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%91%8A%E6%A0%8F.md#1">布告栏</a>上“种”树，“长”出枝条。</p>
<p><strong> 7、做较远的树(配景的树)：</strong></p>
<p>(1)依6的方法做树干，每棵树做一根直树干即可。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313236-wenhua1-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313236 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313236-wenhua1-9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>(2)拿绿色系的纸剪出样式画的树叶丛，背面贴至少上、下2片双面胶带。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313237-wenhua1-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313237 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313237-wenhua1-10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>? (3)把远树“种”好。</strong>种好后，可以用黄色及灰色蜡笔粗略画上明暗，让远树略有立体感。</p>
<p><strong>? 8、做小花朵：</strong></p>
<p>(1)剪0.5公分宽的细纸条(当花芯)。运用原子笔芯将细纸条卷成小圆柱状。在笔芯上绕几圈后就可以取下，徒手卷好它；也可以卷至完成后才取下笔芯，最后需要涂上一点白胶固定纸卷花芯，多卷几个花芯备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313238-wenhua1-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313238 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313238-wenhua1-11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313239-wenhua1-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313239 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313239-wenhua1-12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313240-wenhua1-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313240 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313240-wenhua1-13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313242-wenhua1-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313242 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313242-wenhua1-14-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>(2) 剪2公分宽的纸条(剪花瓣、当花朵)，用剪刀一一剪出细细的花瓣，每一刀都剪约1.5公分，也就是粗纸条上约留0.5公分的宽度不剪。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313243-wenhua1-15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313243 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313243-wenhua1-15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313211-wenhua1-16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313211 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313211-wenhua1-16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>(3)组合花朵:</strong>将花瓣条卷黏于花芯外，在花芯外围涂上一层薄薄的白胶，将剪好的粗纸条包围花芯；一手握住花芯，另一手紧抓粗纸条缠绕花芯，可以绕几圈就涂一点白胶固定之，粗纸条最末端也需要涂上一点白胶固定。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313212-wenhua1-17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313212 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313212-wenhua1-17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313214-wenhua1-18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313214 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313214-wenhua1-18-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313215-wenhua1-19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313215 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313215-wenhua1-19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13313216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13313216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313216-wenhua1-20.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313216-wenhua1-20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13313216" class="wp-caption-text">背视图</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>? (4)整理花朵形状:</strong>轻轻将花瓣往外拨。也可以抓几瓣往内弯曲，让花朵看起来更自然些。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313217-wenhua1-21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313217 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313217-wenhua1-21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313218-wenhua1-22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313218 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313218-wenhua1-22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>9、剪小草:</strong></p>
<p>(1)剪一张长方形(宽度视草的长度而定)，这么大的布置图，有近处与远处的草，所以从5公分到1.5公分的草都需要。</p>
<p>(2)在长方形纸张上随意剪出三角形(有宽一点的，有窄一点的都可以)也就是一前一后的剪出三角形。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313219-wenhua1-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313219 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313219-wenhua1-23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>(3)剪出绿色系，也配上一些黄色系的(因为是黄昏的山景所以需要一些黄色的)草。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313220-wenhua1-24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313220 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313220-wenhua1-24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>(4) 在每片(棵)草的背面下方一一贴上不留胶的双面胶带。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313221-wenhua1-25.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313221 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313221-wenhua1-25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><strong>10、剪近景的树叶:</strong></p>
<p>(1) 将蜡光色纸剪成小一点的长方形，要有大一点的也要有小一些的，因为树叶有大、小片；颜色也不会都一样，所以绿色系及黄色系的都需要。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313222-wenhua1-26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13313222 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313222-wenhua1-26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>(2)将长方形纸片一次折几层也可单张对折，将对折好的纸张，剪出叶片的半形。(如下图)</p>
