<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全职妈妈DIY翻新数百件旧家具 走红网络</h2>
<a href="https://d1z1i6xmekcnf1.cloudfront.net/C2yKAfo5q"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ydbzqt347/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>美国一位有创意的新手妈妈玛吉·麦高正热衷于对破损和丢弃的家具进行翻新改造，并借此获得一份可观的额外收入。（玛吉·麦高提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月15日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Bevan报导／张雨霏编译）美国一位有创意的新手妈妈正热衷于对破损和丢弃的<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">家具</a>进行<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%BB%E6%96%B0.md#1">翻新</a><ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B9%E9%80%A0.md#1">改造</a>，并借此获得一份可观的额外收入。她的事业非常成功，以至于她现在正在教别人如何去尝试DIY。</p>
<p>26岁的玛吉?麦高（Maggie McGaugh）与丈夫和儿子哈里森（Harrison）住在德州的沃思堡。自从辞去工作在家全职抚养孩子后，她发现了自己在升级<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B9%E9%80%A0.md#1">改造</a>方面的天赋。</p>
<p>“当我在2020年1月生下儿子后，我感到很无聊”，玛吉告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13306394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306394" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306394-Maggie-DIY-15.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306394-Maggie-DIY-15.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306394" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我去了一家旧货店，花几美元淘了一个面包盒子，之后我花了整整两天的时间对其进行改造。我便爱上了这个过程、这种艺术形式，并且还能获得一份额外的收入。”</p>
<p>自此，她开始在网上、庭院大卖场或旧货店，甚至是在垃圾箱里四处寻找二手<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">家具</a>，并将自己的改造作品发布到网络上。不久，她的多个社交媒体开始累积大量粉丝，其中一个<ahref="https://www.tiktok.com/@maggiemcgaugh?is_copy_url=1&amp;is_from_webapp=v1">平台</a>已经拥有超过100万的粉丝群。</p>
<p>去年春天，她在没有任何经验的情况下开始自己的DIY创作，但截至目前，她已经<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BF%BB%E6%96%B0.md#1">翻新</a>了数百件家具，并从垃圾填埋场中拯救下来1,000多磅重的家具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13306395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306395" style="width: 604px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306395-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-8-1-1200x781.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306395-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-8-1-1200x781.jpg" alt="" width="604" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306395" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13306396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306396-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-7-1-1200x604.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306396-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-7-1-1200x604.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306396" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13306397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306397" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306397-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-6-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306397-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-6-1.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306397" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>玛吉偏爱二十世纪中叶现代风格、装饰派艺术和波西米亚风格，几乎所有的翻新作品在上架和销售之前，都涉及清洁、打磨、上漆和更换五金零件等工序。有时，一件物品可能被完全改头换面。</p>
<p>她曾将一个破损的唱片机变成一个狗床，将一台老式电视机改造成一个独特的植物展示柜。</p>
<p>玛吉翻新后的成品要么捐赠给有需要的人，要么出售以获利，要么留在自己家里，要么送给家人和朋友。其中出售的物品价格最高可达到材料成本的四倍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13306398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306398" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306398-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-5-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306398-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-5-1.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="474" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306398" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13306399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306399" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306399-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-4-1-1200x690.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306399-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-4-1-1200x690.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306399" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>完全自学成才的玛吉会接受朋友的建议，并通过反复试验得出有效的翻新方法。她说，过程中偶尔会遇到挑战，但她会修正每次翻新时发现的任何问题。</p>
<p>“如果颜色看起来不太好，我会重新粉刷”，玛吉说，“侧面坏了，我必须换上新的胶合板。抽屉少了一块木料，我会用木头填充物、锯末和木胶来修补它。”</p>
<p>迄今为止，玛吉最喜欢的成品是给自己家翻新的一套餐室设备，这也是促使她走红网络的第一个标志性DIY作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13306400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306400" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306400-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-3-1-1200x571.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306400-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-3-1-1200x571.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306400" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13306401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13306401" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306401-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-1-1-1200x773.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13306401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13306401-Stay-at-Home-Mom-Upcycles-Old-Furniture-Into-Creations-Worth-Hundreds-of-Dollars-1-1-1200x773.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13306401" class="wp-caption-text">（玛吉?麦高提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着为DIY初学者提供大量的信息和意见，玛吉创建了自己的电子课程，教其他人如何按照自己的方式翻新家具。她的第一条建议是“从小处着手”，比如从一个小物件和单一颜色开始，随着经验的积累，再慢慢扩大规模和志向。</p>
<p>“老实说，我喜欢与全世界分享我的视频”，她说，“分享这些升级改造后的作品并激励其他人去尝试，真是太令人兴奋了。”</p>
<p>“尝试这个爱好还可以帮助你付清贷款、账单，甚至是抵押贷款。此外，它还可以让你以极低的价格来装饰布置你的整个家。这都是我的经验之谈！”</p>
