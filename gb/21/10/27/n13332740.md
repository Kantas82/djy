<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>富含维生素和矿物质 5种绿叶蔬菜料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/shutterstock_394590805-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>多吃深绿色蔬菜，补充钙、钾、镁等电解质可预防抽筋。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编按】漂亮又美味的各类好菜，让人一看就忍不住赞叹！从今天起，作菜就不用伤脑筋了，从本书介绍的304种菜色中，挑选自己当下最想吃的食谱来做吧，不管是从红、黄、绿的颜色来选，还是从肉、鱼、<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>的菜色来考量，各式各样的搭配组合，让作料理的人也乐趣十足。</p>
<h1><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>绿色<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">叶菜</a></strong></span></h1>
<p>富含<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E7%94%9F%E7%B4%A0.md#1">维生素</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%BF%E7%89%A9%E8%B4%A8.md#1">矿物质</a>的各种<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">叶菜</a>类<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>。秋冬两季的叶菜更加鲜甜，价格也很划算，请务必试试看。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>满满的芝麻＋麻油，鲜味和香气满点</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>韩式茼蒿<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">沙拉</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_13332777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13332777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13332777-f10fdcc2e59e680532b239debd952457.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13332777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13332777-f10fdcc2e59e680532b239debd952457-450x259.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13332777" class="wp-caption-text">（PCuSER电脑人文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?冷藏3～4天<strong>　</strong>?冷冻OK</p>
<p><strong>材料（4餐份）与前置处理作业</strong></p>
<p>茼蒿 1袋（170g）<br />
<strong>　</strong>→将长度切成4㎝。<br />
长葱1根<br />
→斜切成薄片。在耐热调理盆中放入茼蒿和长葱，盖上保鲜膜后放入微波炉中加热2分钟。取出挤干水分。<br />
<strong>A</strong>焙煎白芝麻 1大匙<br />
<strong>A</strong>麻油2大匙<br />
<strong>A</strong>盐 1/2小匙<br />
韩式海苔适量<br />
↓<br />
在较大的调理盆中放入茼蒿、长葱、<strong>A</strong>混合。用手将韩式海苔撕碎后撒上。</p>
<p><strong>制作时间 </strong>TOTAL <strong>10</strong>分钟<u><br />
</u></p>

<strong>　</strong>→将长度切成3㎝。<br />
