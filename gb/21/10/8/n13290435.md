<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：体态优美的印度寿带鸟 令人叹为观止</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290663-Gyana-Mohanty-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>印度摄影师莫汉蒂捕捉到一系列令人叹为观止的印度寿带鸟的特写镜头。（莫汉蒂提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月08日讯】（英文大纪元记者Louise Bevan报导／张雨霏编译）<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a>一位<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>捕捉到一系列令人叹为观止的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6%E5%AF%BF%E5%B8%A6%E9%B8%9F.md#1">印度寿带鸟</a>（Indian paradise flycatcher）的特写镜头，充分展示了这种体态优美的鸟儿的独特魅力。</p>
<p>屡获殊荣的<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1%E5%B8%88.md#1">摄影师</a>莫汉蒂（Gyana Mohanty）来自奥里萨邦的克塔克（Cuttack），现在在孟买工作。他虽然是一位野生动物摄影师，但尤其热衷拍摄鸟类。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6.md#1">印度</a><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%B8%A6%E9%B8%9F.md#1">寿带鸟</a>是我最喜欢的鸟类之一，我甚至每天都想去捕捉它们的身影”，39岁的莫汉蒂告诉《大纪元时报》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13290664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290664" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290664-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290664-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-5.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290664" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13290665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290665" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290665-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290665-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-9.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290665" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“在马哈拉施特拉邦的赖加德县（Raigad），有一个叫Kalhe的村庄，那里的水域很小，所以你可以很容易地发现这些美丽的鸟儿”，他解释说，“冬天的一个清晨，我带着我的尼康装备在日出时分赶至那里，我很幸运在同一天注意到了两只雄性鸟。”</p>
<p>莫汉蒂惊叹道，这两只雄性<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%B8%A6%E9%B8%9F.md#1">寿带鸟</a>正俯冲入水“当着所有人的面”洗澡。他手持相机，拍下了一组令人惊叹的小鸟栖息、潜水和飞行中的“靓影”，它们标志性的蓝黑色羽冠和长长的丝带状<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%BE%E7%BE%BD.md#1">尾羽</a>得到最充分的展示。</p>
<p>他在Instagram上为各地的鸟类爱好者分享出<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/p/CRv_SCYqz3Z/">这组照片</a>，获得了上万网友的点赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13290666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290666" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290666-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290666-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-10.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290666" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13290667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290667" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290667-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290667-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-8.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="565" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290667" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“由于这个村庄毗邻森林，因此捕捉到<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6%E5%AF%BF%E5%B8%A6%E9%B8%9F.md#1">印度寿带鸟</a>的概率非常高”，莫汉蒂分享道，“除了长长的尾巴，我还喜欢他们头上漂亮的黑色羽冠。”</p>
<p>除了雄性寿带鸟，莫汉蒂当天还观察到了雌性寿带鸟、黑枕王鹟（black-naped monarchs）、橙头地鸫 (Orange-headed thrush)、朱雀和斑文鸟（scaly-breasted munias）。</p>
<p>印度寿带鸟原产于亚洲，在印度几乎随处可见。莫汉蒂说它们在冬季更活跃。这种中型鸟类因其对飞行昆虫的饮食偏好而得名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13290668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290668" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290668-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290668-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-4.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="536" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290668" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13290671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13290671" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290671-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13290671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13290671-ET-Gyana-Mohanty-Indian-Paradise-Fly-Catchers-bird-7.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="564" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13290671" class="wp-caption-text">（莫汉蒂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据<ahref="https://ebird.org/species/aspfly1">eBird</a>网站的说法，这种鸟类的羽毛要么是黑白色，要么是肉桂色，它们喜欢栖息在阔叶林中，而且叫声很刺耳。</p>
<p>莫汉蒂也喜欢拍摄两栖动物和爬行动物，在他的<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/gyana.mohanty_photography/">Instagram </a>页面上分享了他令人惊叹的作品集。</p>
<p>从做自己喜欢做的??事情的角度来看，他给别人的建议非常简单：“永远追逐梦想。”</p>
