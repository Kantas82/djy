<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>云林推出“云飨猪”品牌 严格要求４大保证</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299104-521646-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>为让民众吃得安全又安心，云林县政府特别推出“云飨猪”在地品牌，并成立云飨猪展售教育馆，12日正式揭牌启用。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾<ahref="https://github.com/limayp382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>报导）云林县是<ahref="https://github.com/limayp382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%85%BB%E7%8C%AA.md#1">台湾养猪</a>第一大县，一年养猪数量高达160万头，约200亿产值，为让民众吃得安全又安心，特别推出“<ahref="https://github.com/limayp382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E9%A3%A8%E7%8C%AA.md#1">云飨猪</a>”在地品牌，并在<ahref="https://github.com/limayp382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">云林</a>肉品市场成立云飨猪展售教育馆，12日正式揭牌启用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13299103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13299103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299103-521643.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13299103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299103-521643-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13299103" class="wp-caption-text">县长与养猪业者签属合作意向书。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长张丽善指出，云林县是养猪大县，肩负台湾2,300万消费者把关的责任，不仅严禁厨余养猪，成立零瘦肉精联盟，更创立“<ahref="https://github.com/limayp382/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E9%A3%A8%E7%8C%AA.md#1">云飨猪</a>”品牌，并严格要求4大保证：无药物残留、取得在地零瘦肉精牧场、提供相关动物福利措施；加工业者需取得产销履历、CAS、HACCP、ISO22000，两项认证以上；产端、屠宰端、分切端均须经过严格产销履历认证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13299109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13299109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299109-521652.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13299109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299109-521652-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13299109" class="wp-caption-text">云飨猪展售教育馆，除展售外也做教育，让民众了解猪只饲养、屠宰分切、拍卖等历程。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13299105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13299105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299105-521649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13299105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299105-521649-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13299105" class="wp-caption-text">云飨猪展售教育馆二楼展售零瘦肉精联盟产品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13299108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13299108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299108-521651.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13299108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13299108-521651-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13299108" class="wp-caption-text">消费者可以透过 猪形图案 选择自己要买的猪肉部位。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张丽善表示，云飨猪展售教育馆，除展售外也做教育，让民众了解猪只饲养、屠宰分切、拍卖等历程，欢迎全国乡亲来云林品尝在地生产健康美味、零瘦肉精猪肉，另外搭配5倍券消费及云林县今年度“振兴经济现金回馈方案”，只要累计消费满500元以上者，还可以凭发票兑换10%现金回馈。</p>
<p>农业处长吴芳铭表示，云飨猪以“飨”字，以盛宴款待宾客，品牌策画近半年，利用疫情期间进行馆舍相关规划，一楼展售区，民众可买到包含云飨猪、零瘦肉精联盟、云林良品在内等相关猪肉加工商品。</p>
