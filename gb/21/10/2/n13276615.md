<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>环球购物中心桃园A19登场 台湾首家新概念绿色智能商场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276619-519946-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>桃园青埔商圈愈来愈活络，A19锁定家庭、饲养宠物与退休的客层。（Global Mall提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月02日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾桃园报导）<ahref="https://github.com/mvzvlt3463/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%AF%E7%90%83%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">环球购物中心</a>（Global Mall）桃园A19于30日起试营运，不仅携手乐天集团打造运动生活圈，也是台湾首家永续<ahref="https://github.com/mvzvlt3463/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD.md#1">智能</a>商场，有超过70个品牌、全台最大百货攀岩场与电影院进驻，滑雪俱乐部也将于年底前登场。</p>
<p>Global Mall桃园A19营业面积近8000坪，为地上7层、地下2层的商场，有超过70个品牌、全台最大百货攀岩场与电影院进驻，接下来5至7楼还有滑雪俱乐部、主题餐厅及健身房将于11~12月陆续登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276621" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276621-519947.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276621-519947-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276621" class="wp-caption-text">全台百货最大攀岩馆-原岩攀岩馆，设有10米高的上攀墙。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>台湾首家永续<ahref="https://github.com/mvzvlt3463/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD.md#1">智能</a>商场</h2>
<p>桃园青埔商圈愈来愈活络，A19锁定家庭、饲养宠物与退休的客层，进驻品牌以餐饮、娱乐、住居生活服务占比各达4成，其中有5成以上为百货、桃园独家或新形态店型。例如Ur Kitchen共享厨房，提供料理订席、聘请厨师代客料理、姊妹淘聚会；喜乐时代影城打造法拉利等级皮革沙发影厅，还与米其林必比登推荐美食合作电影院美食；为因应青埔住户宠物需求，也打造宠物公园与绿能电动机车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276623-519949.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276623-519949-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276623" class="wp-caption-text">Ur Kitchen共享厨房，提供料理订席、聘请厨师代客料理、姊妹淘聚会。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276617" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276617-519944.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276617-519944-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276617" class="wp-caption-text">韩虎啸旗下新品牌“初虎韩式小食堂”主打韩式家常菜。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276625" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276625-519951.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276625-519951-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276625" class="wp-caption-text">“喜乐时代影城”与必比登美食餐厅合作，待餐饮解封开放后，将推出正宗印尼料理“磐石坊”及“御品元”冰火汤圆。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276626" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276626" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276626-519952.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276626-519952-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276626" class="wp-caption-text">“喜乐时代影城”共有16厅、法拉利等级皮革座椅。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Global Mall近年积极建构ESG绿色永续概念商场，现更将桃园A19商场打造为“永续发展教育基地”，商场空间大量运用环保再生建材，导入循环减碳、创新智能概念，还有百货首家智能回收机、鱼菜共生系统、用环保回收制成的沙发与桌椅，家乐福也是全台首家影响力概念店。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276618-519945.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276618-519945-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276618" class="wp-caption-text">休憩区特别与“小智研发”合作，使用回收如宝特瓶、玻璃回收做成沙发、桌子。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因应疫后趋势，A19也导入创新智能概念，是全台可使用最多13家行动支付的百货，提供APP智能预约、自助结账、数位菜单、租借儿童安全手环、互动式智能看板，同时还设有特斯拉、保时捷充电座共13格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276627-519953.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276627-519953-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276627" class="wp-caption-text">百货独家推出“儿童安全手环”距离警示，不怕小孩走丢。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13276624" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276624-519950.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276624-519950-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276624" class="wp-caption-text">“湛卢ROBAR”百货首店推智能点餐，引进机械手臂冲煮精品咖啡，更跨界联展“爱丽丝梦游仙境”主题展。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>青埔地区已是桃园市重点发展区域，A19站周围有桃园国际棒球场、亚洲硅谷中心、桃园会展中心、观光旅馆等，串联起桃园机场捷运，并特别携手乐天集团打造运动生活圈，结合球场观赛扩大服务。A19同时还有全台最大百货攀岩场，10米高的上攀墙、多角度抱石墙面，让亲子或是新手都能体验攀岩乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13276622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13276622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276622-519948.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13276622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13276622-519948-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13276622" class="wp-caption-text">“GM LIVE直播区”，每周至少五场直播，开放式环境与民众互动。（Global Mall提供）</figcaption></figure>
