<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>教你自制清爽不辣的韩国小菜：白菜水泡菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330087-P.74-75-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白菜水泡菜。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>白菜水<ahref="https://github.com/lwpugm333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a>是我小时候家里的人用来做汤冷面的食材，一直到现在我都还记得这个清爽的口感。尤其没食欲的时候来上一口真的是很不错。它不会辣，腌渍的汤汁也可以食用，因此适合拿来做冷面的汤底。</p>
<p>如果不爱吃辣或是想换个<ahref="https://github.com/lwpugm333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泡菜</a>口味的人，这道<ahref="https://github.com/lwpugm333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">小菜</a>一样是烤肉的好朋友，搭配着吃很美味。它看起来简单，但其实搓洗与盐渍的过程比较久，不过，等待会是值得的。</p>
<p><strong>制作时间：</strong>1天，发酵型泡菜<br />
<strong>保存期限：</strong>1个月<br />
<strong>最佳尝鲜期：</strong>3-7天最好吃</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>主要食材</strong></span></h2>
<p>大白菜 <u>????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?2kg</p>
<p>TIPS：山东大白菜或是韩国大白菜都可以yo。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>腌渍材料</strong></span></h2>
<p>温水 <u>????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?3000g<br />
海盐 <u>??????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?250g<br />
糖 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?50g</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>配菜</strong></span></h2>
<p>白萝卜 <u>????????????????????????????? </u>?200g_切丝<br />
洋葱 <u>????????????????????????????????? </u>?200g_切丝<br />
红萝卜 <u>????????????????????????????? </u>?200g_切丝<br />
红辣椒 <u>??????????????????????????????? </u>?60g_切丝<br />
青葱 <u>??????????????????????????????????? </u>?50g_切段<br />
海盐 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?20g</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>汤底</strong></span></h2>
<p>洗米水 <u>????????????????????????????????????? </u>?2000g（米500g、水2000g）</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">TIPS：</span>不需要米，只要取洗第三次米用的水。前两次洗米水比较脏不建议用，洗好的米可以拿去煮饭。</p>
<p>蒜末 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?60g<br />
姜末 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?60g<br />
水梨 <u>????????????????????????????????? </u>?200g_切块<br />
海盐 <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?20g<br />
生虾酱 <u>????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?50g</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>工具</strong></span></h2>
<p>蒸笼布、纱布或是茶包袋</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h2>
<p>1.将大白菜纵切四分之一后，放入盐渍材料中，盐渍约10-12小时。每2-3小时就将正在盐渍的大白菜翻动一下，让它更均匀。</p>
<p>2. 将白萝卜、洋葱、红萝卜都切成差不多长度的细丝；红辣椒先切段后，剖半去籽再切细丝；青葱切段。将这些配料加入20g海盐抓腌拌匀。</p>
<p>3. 把姜末、蒜末放到蒸笼布中，浸泡在洗米水中搓一搓。（如图A）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13330088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330088-P.77-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330088-P.77-1-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330088" class="wp-caption-text">A将装有蒜末、姜末的布包放在洗米水中，用手反复挤压、搓出汁液。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>POINTS：</strong>用蒸笼布或纱布包起来搓，味道的精华会出来，但不会有食材渣影响口感。</span></p>
<p>4. 将盐渍好的大白菜水洗2-3次后，用手由上往下把水挤干。（如图B、C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13330089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330089" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330089-P.77-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330089-P.77-2-450x380.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330089" class="wp-caption-text"><strong>B 大白菜盐渍到菜梗用手折不会断掉的程度，就可以了。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</strong></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13330090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330090" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330090-P.77-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330090-P.77-3-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330090" class="wp-caption-text">C 将大白菜清洗2-3 遍后，尽量把水挤干。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>POINTS</strong>?：先从白菜厚的地方挤，盐水更容易挤干净，不好的味道也会被挤掉。</span></p>
<p>5. 把抓腌好的配菜塞入大白菜一层一层的叶片中。（如图D、E）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13330091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330091-P.77-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330091-P.77-4-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330091" class="wp-caption-text">D 将切丝的配菜塞入大白菜的叶片里。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13330092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330092-P.77-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330092-P.77-5-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330092" class="wp-caption-text">E 每一层叶片都要掀开来塞入配菜，再叠好（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>POINTS</strong>?：一层一层铺在白菜梗上味道才会均匀，要花点时间但很好吃yo。</span></p>
<p>6. 将塞好配菜的大白菜放入保存容器中，加入步骤3的洗米水以及装有姜末蒜末的布包，再加入水梨、海盐、生虾酱，然后一起腌制。（如图F）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13330093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13330093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330093-P.77-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13330093" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13330093-P.77-6-450x380.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13330093" class="wp-caption-text">F 将所有食材放入容器中，倒入洗米水到淹过食材即可。（台湾广厦有声图书有限公司提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 盖上盖子或封保鲜膜放室温约12小时后，进冰箱冷藏2-3天就可以吃了。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>TIPS：</strong></span>可以将肉或是蔬菜切丝，用做好的水泡菜白菜叶卷起来做成白菜肉卷或是蔬菜卷。</p>
