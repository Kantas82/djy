<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>谁道秋色唯有美东靓 美西赏秋不遑多让</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331167-cb8bb432774c451e3ec65d30c15ac198-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋色里的大提顿国家公园(Grand Teton National Park)。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>一提到秋天赏叶，多数人会首选美东。美东的<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E8%89%B2.md#1">秋色</a>的确美，我在上一集“<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/21/9/26/n13261167.md#1">借问秋色何处寻 玩家首推指湖区</a>”还特别介绍了纽约州指湖区独特的秋水加红叶的风光。其实美西的<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E8%89%B2.md#1">秋色</a>，尤其在西部特有的荒山巨石的映衬下，显得更大气、更纯粹，也更苍凉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331168" style="width: 593px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331168-b1c4ff8f6225cef8b68dea6b5dbaeceb.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331168-b1c4ff8f6225cef8b68dea6b5dbaeceb-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="593" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331168" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%A1%E5%AE%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">锡安国家公园</a>(Zion National Park)的日落时分。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从赌城Las Vegas沿15号州际高速公路一路往北，2个半小时后就到达了被誉为“赌城后花园”的<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%A1%E5%AE%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">锡安国家公园</a>。锡安公园不大，但风光险胜。公园内维京河(Virgin River)蜿蜒而过，四面陡峭的红色悬崖耸立，连公园内的马路都是用当地特产的红石子铺成。</p>
<p>一入秋，树叶开始变黄。日落时分站在Canyon Junction Bridge（峡谷枢纽桥）上观日落是件十分赏心悦目的事。前面是维京河，后面是锡安公园最著名的守望者山峰(Watchman Peak)。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331171-dcf3e70f403d825be55f7f977ccee7da.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331171-dcf3e70f403d825be55f7f977ccee7da-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331171" class="wp-caption-text">锡安国家公园的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红岩黄花，是锡安国家公园秋天的特色。</p>
<p>从锡安国家公园沿着9号公路出来开到路尽头，往右（往南）上89号公路就可以去<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%B3%A1%E8%B0%B7.md#1">大峡谷</a>国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)，趁那里还没关门(每年10月中至第二年的5月中季节性关门)赶紧去看看雄壮的大峡谷及耀眼的金秋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331185" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331185-a8a523780112c681f5133bbcf3f7ac94.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331185-a8a523780112c681f5133bbcf3f7ac94-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331185" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%B3%A1%E8%B0%B7.md#1">大峡谷</a>国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一进公园大门就目不暇接。马路两边是枯黄的草地，远处的森林里绿色的松柏间夹着金黄的白桦与白杨，偶尔有几棵红色的橡树。气势夺人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331186" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331186-e2cabbcfbccc91e788ff4ee70a7f92fc.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331186-e2cabbcfbccc91e788ff4ee70a7f92fc-450x277.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331186" class="wp-caption-text">大峡谷国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园里的小路也是金黄与翠绿相间，美的让司机不愿开车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331187" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331187-3a78c2308f3f23f79a1e4419da45af1a.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331187-3a78c2308f3f23f79a1e4419da45af1a-450x252.jpg" alt="" width="602" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331187" class="wp-caption-text">大峡谷国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车子开过，山坡上不时有大片的金黄色列队欢迎游客。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331188" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331188-ef054ddb4dd085bec95633094cd66bda.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331188-ef054ddb4dd085bec95633094cd66bda-450x272.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331188" class="wp-caption-text">大峡谷国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沿着步道走到峡谷边，从峭壁缝里顽强长出来的灌木也要为大峡谷添一分秋色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331189" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331189" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331189-9f7018608b89e8261b4fc3d343cbc034.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13331189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331189-9f7018608b89e8261b4fc3d343cbc034-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331189" class="wp-caption-text">大峡谷国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋阳下的陡峭石壁亦少了几分狰狞，面目柔和了不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331190" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331190-7333cc6009e35e4b7004858f905a4fef.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331190-7333cc6009e35e4b7004858f905a4fef-450x278.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331190" class="wp-caption-text">大峡谷国家公园北缘(Grand Canyon National Park North Rim)的秋色。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那天我运气够好，在离开公园时遇上绚丽的晚霞。枯木与金叶在落日与晚霞的照耀下，美得令人惊心动魄。</p>
<p>若在9号公路尽头往左（往北）上89号公路的话，一个多小时就到了另一个袖珍的国家公园，布莱斯峡谷国家公园(Bryce Canyon National Park)。</p>
<p>布莱斯峡谷以各种各样千奇百态的红色岩柱(hoodoos)闻名。金色的白杨与红色的怪石头是布莱斯峡谷的特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331198-9de2c55dae94aaa2e1cb7a225f55005e.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331198-9de2c55dae94aaa2e1cb7a225f55005e-450x280.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331198" class="wp-caption-text">布莱斯峡谷国家公园(Bryce Canyon National Park)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13331199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331199-76f4cd65faa3b1d9d3a43c4485764098.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13331199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331199-76f4cd65faa3b1d9d3a43c4485764098-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331199" class="wp-caption-text">布莱斯峡谷国家公园(Bryce Canyon National Park)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你有时间有精力，那就必须再往北跑的远一点，必须去看看秋天的黄石与<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%8F%90%E9%A1%BF%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">大提顿国家公园</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331201-b5e2dad126cbbcfc409fef7bbffb20e3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13331201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331201-b5e2dad126cbbcfc409fef7bbffb20e3-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331201" class="wp-caption-text">秋天的<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E7%9F%B3%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">黄石公园</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E7%9F%B3%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">黄石公园</a>名气响啊！一说大家都会提：老忠诚喷泉！猛玛温泉！但秋雨枯草里的黄石大家见过吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331202-d9aebedbd45994b235607eb3033b03e7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13331202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331202-d9aebedbd45994b235607eb3033b03e7-450x608.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="608" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331202" class="wp-caption-text">秋天的黄石公园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>运气好还能遇见几头贴秋膘的牦牛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13331203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13331203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331203-d95f8a89f35e0b0801023e39d607e5d9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13331203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13331203-d95f8a89f35e0b0801023e39d607e5d9-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13331203" class="wp-caption-text">秋天的黄石公园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋雨料峭中躲在黄石大酒店面朝黄石湖发呆也十分惬意。</p>
<p>出了黄石公园南大门就进入了<ahref="https://github.com/yawhga366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%8F%90%E9%A1%BF%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">大提顿国家公园</a>(Grand Teton National Park)。有位朋友说：黄石野，大提顿秀。很经典的总结。相比黄石，大提顿小多了。可它的山山水水却是我最心水的。无他，一个“秀”字。如果光线配合的话极容易出大片。</p>

<p>大提顿里住宿，最难订的数简尼湖渡假村(Jenny Lake Lodge)。但订到订不到，都要去它的大堂看一看。</p>
