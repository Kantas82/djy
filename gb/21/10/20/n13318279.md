<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>认识加州新建筑法规：SB9</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13318306-1611271949222124-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加州新建筑法案：只要你家的土地总面积不小于2400平方英尺，新法规允许屋主将一个单户地块分割成两个地块，在地块上增加第二个家，或者在每个地块上建复式房屋。（简沐/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月21日讯】</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;">作者：文哥</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2021</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月，媒体公</span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">布</span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">了<ahref="https://github.com/yadmko3863/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a>州长加文?纽森（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Gavin Newsom</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）签署了一项新建筑法案——</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Senate Bill 9?</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">SB9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">原文：</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Senate Bill 9, which Gov. Gavin Newsom signed last month?</span></span></span><span style="font-family: Microsoft YaHei;"><span style="font-size: medium;">—?</span></span><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">allows some single-family homeowners to split their lots and build a duplex on each side. &#8230; The law requires a lot size to be at least 2,400 square feet, It allows property owners to split a single-family lot into two lots, add a second home to their lot or split their lot into two and place duplexes on each. meaning the new split lots would be 1,200 square feet each.</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">这里面的意思是，只要你家的土地总面积不小于</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">2400</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">平方英尺，新法规允许屋主将一个单户地块分割成两个地块，在地块上增加第二个家，或者在每个地块上建复式房屋。这就意味着新的分割地块每块</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">1200</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">平方英尺。而</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">SB10</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">法案只要是在交通发达的地段，可以建造多个单元。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">这项法案的通过对于我们屋主和即将置业的屋主来说无疑是一个“利好”，尤其对于家庭人口多的屋主，更是解决了一大难题。以往我们申报加建都有土地的<ahref="https://github.com/yadmko3863/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B9%E7%A7%AF%E7%8E%87.md#1">容积率</a>局限，附属住宅单元</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">(Accessory Dwelling Unit</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">ADU)</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">虽说解决了一部分住房尺寸，但对于<ahref="https://github.com/yadmko3863/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a>迅速膨胀的住房需求来讲可能也只是杯水车薪。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;">有分析人士认为，它可能会带来加州房市的繁荣，可能会使房价上涨。新法规出台后，很多屋主都满怀期望的开始酝酿自己家的新屋前景：有些人想在自家充分的利用上有限的土地面积；还有的屋主是土地足够大完全可以多盖些房子；另有部分准备投资的屋主，认为赚钱的大好机会来了。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">文哥近段时间接到了不少屋主咨询，关于</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">SB9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">法规是否开始在自家所在的城市实施了的问题？自家土地状况符合不符合条件？文哥团队的同事们逐个咨询所在城市的规划师（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">Planner</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">），有些城市的规划师答复是：“新法规暂时没有开始正式实施”，而有些答复是：“大约明年二月开始实施”。再加上申报许可证（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">permit</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）需要几个月，看看你自己是否能够耐心地等待。所以文哥想说：“咱们能等就等，不能等就继续现在所要做的”您说呢？</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">了</span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">解完</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: STSong;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="en-US">SB9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">法案，如果你对关于相关申请，规划复式住宅的价格、工期、申报等还有其</span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">它</span></span></span><span style="font-family: serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">疑问，不妨向文哥问询。◇</span></span></span></p>
