<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>甩开防疫胖 展现健康自信美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283556-518488-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>减肥瘦身。（Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月06日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）防疫期间除了压力与烦恼，还有生活型态的变化，加上多吃、少动，造成让人困扰的宅家防疫胖。如何瘦得健康又安心，屈臣氏药师建议三大方法，避免身材走样，让大家在家也能拥抱健康美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13283555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13283555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283555-515822.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13283555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283555-515822-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13283555" class="wp-caption-text">饮食清淡、搭配摄取酵素、多运动，是维持窈窕身形的重点。（屈臣氏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>一、肠胃净化超顺畅</strong></p>
<figure id="13283557" aria-describedby="caption-13283557" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283557-518489-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283557-518489-450x450.jpg" alt="减肥瘦身。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13283557" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%BA%AB.md#1">瘦身</a>。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>防疫在家期间，热量摄取大增，不只因为追剧而大吃零食，也会因为心理压力而不自觉进食，不小心就容易摄取不必要的热量。日常饮食除了可考虑亲自下厨、控制清淡低油，水分的摄取量也要足够，也可搭配营养补充品，像是酵素、益生菌等帮助整肠健胃，减轻身体负担。</p>
<figure id="13283558" aria-describedby="caption-13283558" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283558-518490-450x510.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283558-518490-450x510.jpg" alt="减肥瘦身。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13283558" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%BA%AB.md#1">瘦身</a>。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>二、运动按摩超紧致</strong></p>
<figure id="13283559" aria-describedby="caption-13283559" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283559-518491-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283559-518491-450x338.jpg" alt="减肥瘦身。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13283559" class="wp-caption-text">减肥瘦身。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饮食之外，运动也是很重要的一环。防疫期间若不方便去健身房，可以选择较不受空间限制的运动，如弹力带训练等，透过小道具就能轻松动起来，并于运动后搭配按摩放松肌肉，让线条更好看。</p>
<figure id="13283560" aria-describedby="caption-13283560" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283560-518492-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13283560-518492-450x450.jpg" alt="减肥瘦身。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13283560" class="wp-caption-text">减肥瘦身。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>三、彩妆修容超显瘦</strong></p>
<p>除了饮食与运动，女孩也可以透过修容与遮瑕的美妆技巧，突显五官及脸部线条。此外，上妆前先进行简单的脸部按摩，不但可帮助后续上妆更服贴，肌肤也会更显紧实。◇</p>
