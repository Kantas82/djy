<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>行走的秋意 加拿大露易斯湖登高</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302633-20211011-Sunny-Devis-Thumb-01A-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在魔指峰顶俯瞰露易斯湖。（Sunny/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年10月13日讯】加拿大亚省<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">落基山</a>异常炎热的天气带来了意想不到的结果，今年的秋天格外美丽。从树叶刚开始变色，露易斯湖（Lake Louise）的步道就挤满了游人，周末尤甚。我们选在一个金松颜色最盛的时节，来到露易斯湖。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">落基山</a>有两个景点的停车场在周末必须早到才能找到停车位，一个是梦莲湖（Moraine Lake），另一个就是露易斯湖。去梦莲湖须在早上6点半之前到，露易斯湖呢，不要晚于7点半。</p>
<p>露易斯湖有一条著名的环道，从酒店开始，上阿格尼斯湖（Lake Agnes）步道，经镜湖（Mirror Lake）、小蜂巢、阿格尼斯茶屋，至<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%9C%82%E5%B7%A2.md#1">大蜂巢</a>（Big Beehive），然后下到六冰川茶屋，再从平川步道（Plain of Six Glaciers）走回酒店，全程需要8~10个小时。</p>
<p>我们原本的计划是去阿格尼斯湖，到<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%9C%82%E5%B7%A2.md#1">大蜂巢</a>，然后原路返回。然而世间事大抵如此，计划不如变化快。</p>
<h2>步道湖光</h2>
<p>清晨的露易斯湖薄雾散去，虽然气温只有10度，但并不感到寒冷。如果时间充裕，你可以在露易斯湖酒店餐厅吃早餐，看着窗外的湖山，享受一杯香浓的咖啡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13302635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302635-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Louise-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302635-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Louise-01-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302635" class="wp-caption-text">秋季的露易斯湖。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13302638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302638-20210925-Sunny-Lake-Louise-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302638-20210925-Sunny-Lake-Louise-04-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302638" class="wp-caption-text">秋季的露易斯湖。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿格尼斯湖步道非常好走，只有3.5公里，在林中穿梭，偶尔能看到湖水粼粼的波光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13302646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302646-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302646-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-02-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302646" class="wp-caption-text">阿格尼斯湖秋景。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13302649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302649-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-04.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13302649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302649-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-04-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302649" class="wp-caption-text">阿格尼斯湖秋景。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13302652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302652-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13302652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302652-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-07-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302652" class="wp-caption-text">阿格尼斯湖秋景。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>镜湖相当小，很容易就错过了。建议你到水边看看，湖内倒映的是大蜂巢，这时你才能体会为什么叫镜湖。</p>
<p>走到茶屋，歇歇脚，吃点东西，坐在石头上看阿格尼斯湖醉人的绿色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13302658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302658-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13302658" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302658-20211011-Sunny-Lake-Agnes-05-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302658" class="wp-caption-text">阿格尼斯湖绿色醉人。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚省有一种独特的石头，叫斑彩石（Ammolite），是菊石的化石残骸。其迷人的色彩当属落基山独有，就如同这美丽的湖，将湖光山色涵入一汪碧水。古人喜欢把秋水比成美人的明眸，秋波善睐，让人心底多了些许温柔。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AD%94%E6%8C%87%E5%B3%B0.md#1">魔指峰</a></h2>
<p>从茶屋沿着岸边走到湖的另一端是登大蜂巢的路口，途中偶遇一对来自卑诗省的夫妇问路，他们在纠结是登大蜂巢还是去<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AD%94%E6%8C%87%E5%B3%B0.md#1">魔指峰</a>（Devils Thumb）。经过一番讨论，他们建议我们一起去魔指峰。</p>
<p>从阿格尼斯湖到大蜂巢顶，沿路都是金松（Larch），松针在掉落之前会转成明艳的金黄色，落基山登高赏秋大多赏的就是金松。明黄的松针、黑色的树干，在蓝天下热烈而张扬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13302669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302669-20211011-Sunny-Larch-08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302669-20211011-Sunny-Larch-08-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302669" class="wp-caption-text">明艳的金松。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到山顶岔路口，左走上大蜂巢峰顶，中间去六冰川茶屋，右边是魔指峰的步道入口。没有做过任何研究的我们，走上去魔指峰的路。</p>
<p>爬过石阶，绕到山的阳面，平川山谷豁然开朗。对面的冰川似乎触手可及，时而传来雪崩隆隆的声响，沿着悬崖的步道下方是冰川融化形成的溪流，蜿蜒汇入露易斯湖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13302684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302684-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302684-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-01-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302684" class="wp-caption-text">平川山谷，景色开阔。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13302686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302686-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302686" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302686-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-02-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302686" class="wp-caption-text">平川山谷，景色开阔。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13302687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13302687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302687-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13302687" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13302687-20211011-Sunny-Plain-of-Six-Glaciers-03-600x224.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="224" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13302687" class="wp-caption-text">平川山谷，景色开阔。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>途中有一段非常难走的砾石路，必须借助松枝树根攀爬，抬头忽然看到更高处岩石峭壁上怡然自得的高山羚羊，感慨非常。借着其他游客的鼓励，我们终于登上魔指峰。</p>
<p>先生讲过一个故事，新东方的学生曾经问一位副校长，他所到之处哪里最美？这位副校长回答：加拿大的露易斯湖。先生说他一直不敢苟同，直到今天立于魔指峰顶。</p>
<p>站在高山之巅，平视冰川，俯瞰山谷，山色烟光渲染了十分的秋意，远山含黛，翠湖凝碧，此时此景，有什么比胁下生出双翼更让人向往呢？</p>
