<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—隐居（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13328101-4fa37c6263eff6548901b9e0987433da-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—隐居（彩墨）。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9A%90%E5%B1%85.md#1">隐居</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</h2>
<p>李白诗：“问余何意栖碧山，笑而不答心自闲，桃花流水杳然去，别有天地非人间。”——你问我为什么要住在这儿？我告诉你，这儿不是人间世俗的扰攘可比，这儿可是桃花流水、风光旖旎的世外桃源呢。</p>
<p>在画里，远远一道流泉曲折蜿蜒而下，流经一座巨大突起的岩块，再冲激贮入一泓深潭。在山腰凹处住着几户人家，你问他们为什么要住在这儿？他们就会吟李白的这首诗来回复你。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13328101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13328101" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13328101-4fa37c6263eff6548901b9e0987433da.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13328101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13328101-4fa37c6263eff6548901b9e0987433da.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="578" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13328101" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%868.md#1">徐明义画集8</a>—<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9A%90%E5%B1%85.md#1">隐居</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。70×69cm。（图片来源：徐明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Live in privacy/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Poem of Li Bai: &#8220;Ask me why live in Bishan, laughing instead of answering with easy mind, peach flowers and rivers flow away suddenly, there is Xanadu not belong to the human world&#8221; &#8211; You asked me why I want to live here? I tell you that the disturbing human world is scarcely comparable to here, here is Xanadu with peach flowers and rivers.</p>
<p>In this painting, far away a meandering winding spring flush down, flowing through a huge protuberance rock into a deep pool. A few houses around the mid-slope of mountain, when you ask why they want to live here, they will reply to you with this Li Bai poem.@</p>
