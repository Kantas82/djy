<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2021/22秋冬时尚趋势 舒适与极简并存</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13339378-20211029_fashion_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2021 - 2022时尚人士着装的主要考量因素就是简单、宽松，穿着自在舒适。(Getty Images／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月29日讯】（大纪元记者李平编译报导）有人说<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>最不靠谱，不到一季就不再流行了，有人形容，时尚追随者有如盲目的羊群，还没搞清楚往哪边走，领头羊已经转换了方向。</p>
<p>潮流是一种独特的审美表达，持续时间确实很短，然而<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>法则却极<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%80%E5%8D%95.md#1">简单</a>，只要是让人穿上就感到自信、舒服的都可称为“时尚”。现代的时尚精神更是强调做自己、突出自我个性，不必为外观所困扰。</p>
<p>当我们掌握了时尚的基本原则，了解一些时尚趋势和被流行淘汰的“时尚逆流”，在现有衣橱中配搭、规避不潮的穿搭，及时增添有流行元素的单品和配饰，游走于“不败”的时尚尖端。</p>
<p>2021/22时尚界着装的主要考量因素，朝着<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%80%E5%8D%95.md#1">简单</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BD%E6%9D%BE.md#1">宽松</a>，穿着<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%9C%A8%E8%88%92%E9%80%82.md#1">自在舒适</a>考量。下面在介绍本季流行趋势的同时，会穿插2021/22被时尚淘汰的“逆潮流”，讲解哪些是要注意和避开的穿搭地雷，并提供造型建议。</p>
<h2>流行趋势一：<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%BD%E6%9D%BE.md#1">宽松</a>牛仔裤</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13337403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337403" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337403-GettyImages-1340117070.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337403-GettyImages-1340117070-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337403" class="wp-caption-text">宽松的牛仔裤，舒适放松。德国2021秋冬时装周Levi时装秀。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13339341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13339341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13339341-GettyImages-1327881464.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13339341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13339341-GettyImages-1327881464-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13339341" class="wp-caption-text">2021 年 7 月 9 日在意大利罗马举行的2021 Altaroma时装秀。(Elisabetta Villa/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>宽松的牛仔裤，给人轻松休闲的感觉，深色水洗牛仔裤是本季主要趋势。超长或宽松的牛仔裤，可混搭针织上衣、抹胸外套及马丁靴。</p>
<h3>过时潮流一：牛仔裤过多修饰+破洞</h3>
<p>牛仔裤上大量点缀，如珠子、水钻、卡通、漏洞，以及抢眼的刺绣，特别膝盖上大个破洞，今年已不再流行。</p>
<p><strong>替代造型</strong>：直筒蓝色或深蓝色牛仔裤。营造陈旧感，可选磨边牛仔裤。小微损、小装饰在2021尚可接纳。小白T恤，外披短款牛仔服也是不错的选择，也符合当下潮流。</p>
<h2>流行趋势二：剪裁合身、同色分层穿搭</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13337409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337409" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337409-GettyImages-1340342462.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337409-GettyImages-1340342462-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337409" class="wp-caption-text">合体的剪裁、同色、同色系分层穿搭在本季大受青睐。德国2021秋冬时装周秀场。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<p>当下时尚主旨是关乎舒适、简洁和简约的装饰。合体的剪裁、同色系分层穿搭，在视觉及搭配上都体现舒适与极简主义的时尚原则，适合各种颜色。</p>
<h3>过时潮流二：贴身款外套</h3>
<p>本季不再流行紧身的小外套、西装、夹克等。</p>
<p><strong>替代造型：</strong>短夹克换为大号西装外套或剪裁宽大的夹克。</p>
<h2>流行趋势三：超大号衬衣 、合身马甲</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13337411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337411-GettyImages-1340583739.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337411-GettyImages-1340583739-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337411" class="wp-caption-text">中性的条纹衬衣，黑色小马甲，马丁靴，时尚帅气。德国2021秋冬时装周秀场。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<p>流行宽大的扣领衬衫（Button-down shirt）／男友衬衣，白色或柔和色都可；可单穿，也可外搭合身马甲。</p>
<h3>过时潮流三：过多缀饰的衬衫和紧身上衣</h3>
<p><strong>繁琐华丽的女衫：</strong>蕾丝、蝴蝶结等不再流行。时尚的原则永远都是“少胜多”。要有基本款的服装，在其基础上可添加时尚单品和配件。</p>
<p><strong>替代搭配：</strong>单色、天然材质的T恤或吊带／跨栏背心，露脐装与高腰牛仔裤和长裤搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13339229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13339229" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13339229-GettyImages-1327111851.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13339229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13339229-GettyImages-1327111851-450x741.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚" width="450" b="741" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13339229" class="wp-caption-text">面料、颜色从头到脚、从里到外的谐和，舒适温暖。巴黎时装周2021-2022秋冬时装秀场。( Thierry Chesnot/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>

<h2>流行趋势四：从头“毛”到脚</h2>
<p>针织面料从头穿到脚，视觉上显得温暖舒适。然而时尚是怀旧的，本季复古印花的服装也正卷土重来，需选色调、图案协调的衣饰配搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13337407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337407" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337407-GettyImages-1340342426.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337407-GettyImages-1340342426-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337407" class="wp-caption-text">秋季无须紧实的包裹，露骨更不符合时尚法则，镂空恰逢时机在本季流行。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<h3>过时潮流四：紧身针织连衣短裙、钟型裙／喇叭裙</h3>
<p>毛衣短裙、喇叭短裙等都不再流行。A型裙依然很受欢迎，但最好选更实用、多变的中裙；轻质的天然面料及长到小腿脚踝的长丝裙也很适合。</p>
<p><strong>替代搭配：</strong>毛衣裙可换直筒或略微外翻的长款超大针织衫。喇叭裙可选带蓬松袖的宽松款；中长裙，侧面镂空或前排带纽扣，都会为裙装增色不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13337401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337401" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337401-GettyImages-1327334088.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337401-GettyImages-1327334088-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337401" class="wp-caption-text">轻薄长裙，丝裙在秋冬温暖地区是好选择。巴黎时装周2021-2022秋冬时装秀场。(Photo by Kristy Sparow/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>流行趋势五：彩色皮衣、高饱和度衣装</h2>
<p>饱和的颜色可以带来愉悦的心情，红色的亮面皮革长西装一出场就超级吸睛，彰显出服装个性化，也提升质感。皮革与西装套装的结合，让职场穿搭不再沉闷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13337415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337415" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337415-shutterstock_2040391790.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337415-shutterstock_2040391790-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337415" class="wp-caption-text">饱和度高的皮大衣是本季流行的趋势。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>过时潮流五：超大的开襟毛衫，天然毛皮大衣</h3>
<p>2021-2022年秋冬选择有小纽扣或大纽扣的剪裁开衫。V领、蓬松的袖子和宽松的设计是本季的主要趋势。动物皮毛大衣不再流行。</p>
<p><strong>替代搭配：</strong>饱和度高的颜色本季很流行，不论单品还是叠层穿搭，都会成为点睛之笔。人造裘皮外套、仿毛马甲，彩色仿皮衣让都是很好的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13337408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337408" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337408-GettyImages-1340342428.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337408-GettyImages-1340342428-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337408" class="wp-caption-text">橘褐色半长小开衫，配以同色针织衣裤，白色小帽，打造休闲活泼的秋装。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13337404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13337404" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337404-GettyImages-1340342394.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13337404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13337404-GettyImages-1340342394-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13337404" class="wp-caption-text">仿皮毛大衣是趋势，为了取暖和靓丽而杀戮动物已不再时尚。德国2021秋冬时装周秀场。(Photo by Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>流行趋势六：木底鞋、木屐、靴子</h2>
<p>说起木底鞋，Dior 2018秋冬秀场上的那双掀起了木底鞋的潮流。虽然有人认为，木屐缺乏柔韧度，看起来笨重，无法更好与脚面弧度契合。</p>
<p>近年木底鞋时的热情感染紧跟潮流的时髦人士，掀起一波流行潮鞋，迈入新世纪，木底鞋依旧风光。如今流行的木底鞋并不是丑样的，如果你想选购一双不曾穿过的鞋款，那么建议可以尝试木底鞋。</p>
<p>经典简约的马丁靴，是一年四季都会重新流行的款式，坚持中性风格，在日常着装中展现这种大胆的轮廓让人别树一帜，永不过时。</p>
