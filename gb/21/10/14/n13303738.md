<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>木匠爸爸为儿子打造豪华木制跑车 真的能开</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304007-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-15-1200x675-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>越南一位经验丰富的木匠Truong Van Dao近期用橡木为儿子打造了一辆世界上最昂贵的布加迪（Bugatti）超级跑车模型。（Truong Van Dao提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月14日讯】（英文大纪元Epoch Inspired Staff报导／张雨霏编译）<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97.md#1">越南</a>一位<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E5%8C%A0.md#1">木匠</a>近期用橡木为儿子制造了一辆世界上最昂贵的<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%8A%A0%E8%BF%AA.md#1">布加迪</a>（Bugatti）<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">超级跑车</a>模型，他精湛的工艺和非凡的创意使他一举成名。</p>
<p>这款<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%8A%A0%E8%BF%AA.md#1">布加迪</a>限量版豪华高性能<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E8%B7%91%E8%BD%A6.md#1">超级跑车</a>Centodieci正品价值近900万美元。从小就在<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E5%B7%A5.md#1">木工</a>艺环境的熏陶下长大的Truong Van Dao耗时40天成功仿造出这款令人惊叹的可驾驶的新颖跑车。</p>
<p>“我出生在一个手工艺村，从小就接触木雕”，30岁的Dao告诉《大纪元时报》，“我从事<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E5%B7%A5.md#1">木工</a>已经十多年了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13304005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304005-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-14-1200x675.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304005-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-14-1200x675.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304005" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13304015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304015" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304015-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-20-1200x675.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304015-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-20-1200x675.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304015" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“当我带孩子去公园玩时，我就有了要造出一辆可驾驶的木制汽车的想法，我的孩子们喜欢这些迷你电动车。”</p>
<p>在2020年12月制作这辆布加迪之前，Dao雕刻了一辆法拉利Aperta，但它没有动力只能直行。“我后来对其进行升级改进，使它能够平稳转向并配备了一个电动机”，他说。</p>
<p>当然，Dao的跑车没有原车型那么大；它们是缩小版，以便一个小男孩可以驾驶。但这并没有削弱他作品中令人印象深刻的精致细节和卓越的工艺。</p>
<p>Dao首先建造了一个木制底盘，安装上电动机和传动系统。以此为基础，再安装车身，主要部件是厚厚的木板，然后他需要在上面精雕细琢，完美展现出每辆时尚汽车必备的流畅线条。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13304008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304008" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304008-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-1-1200x671.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304008-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-1-1200x671.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304008" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13304009" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304009" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304009-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-5-1200x674.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304009" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304009-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-5-1200x674.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304009" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13304010" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304010-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-7-1200x676.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304010-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-7-1200x676.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304010" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>引擎盖、挡泥板、保险杠和汽车尾部几乎都是用链锯雕刻的，他说这确实加快了工作进度。电锯发挥不了作用时，木刨、凿子和砂纸等工具都会派上用场。</p>
<p>像轮辋、格栅、引擎盖装饰和方向盘等更精细的细节部分，都是用木头制作的。挡风玻璃使用的是有机玻璃，并安装了真正的电灯。</p>
<p>汽车内饰包括木制桶形座椅，甚至还有雕刻精美的变速箱和仪表板。</p>
<p>最后一道工序，Dao给汽车喷上漆，使其呈现出木材的丰富色调，反射出华丽的抛光油漆效果。这样，成品就打造完成了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13304006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304006-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-11-1200x669.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304006" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304006-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-11-1200x669.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304006" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13304011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304011" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304011-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-8-1200x675.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304011" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304011-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-8-1200x675.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304011" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13304012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13304012" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304012-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-10-1200x674.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13304012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13304012-ET-Truong-Van-Dao-wooden-Bugatti-10-1200x674.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13304012" class="wp-caption-text">（Truong Van Dao提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就性能而言，Dao表示，布加迪的时速大概可以达到20公里，但为了安全起见，他将速度减慢了一半左右。</p>
<p>“成功造出这辆布加迪之后，我又制造了一辆宝马Hommage、一辆兰博基尼Sian，以及近期完成的一辆法拉利GTO。”Dao说。</p>
<p>这位木工经常在社交媒体上展示他的作品。他在雕刻汽车期间还拍摄了几段延时视频，后半部分还录制了试驾过程，画面显示他与儿子在路上炫耀他们的最终产品。</p>
<p>?<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/NDWoodworkingArt/videos/2783594258531233/">点击这里观看影片</a></p>
<p>这些视频发布到社交平台后很快就火了，Dao已经成为当地的名人。</p>
<p>“我从没想过我会在仿造这些汽车后出名”，他告诉《大纪元时报》，“许多国外新闻网站都分享了我的故事。在<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97.md#1">越南</a>，我的照片随处可见。”</p>
<p>“当人们不断夸我时，我非常高兴，有人甚至开玩笑说，我是世界上最疯狂的<ahref="https://github.com/ksodae325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E5%8C%A0.md#1">木匠</a>。”</p>
