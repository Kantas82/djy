<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>栾树花海配溪畔甜根子草 秋季限定田野风光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310629-1-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋意渐浓，乡间河床上甜根子草陆续开花，白茫茫花海绽放满荒野，加上道路旁成排的台湾栾树红、黄花盛开色彩缤纷。（曾晏均／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年10月18日讯】（大纪元记者曾晏均台湾报导）凉风乍起，一叶知秋。乡道旁，台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">栾树</a>（又称：黄金雨）一片片黄花随风摇曳飘落，树下落英缤纷。抬头仰望高空，天际间成群鸽子翱翔而过，白羽衬蓝天，乡村的田野云淡风轻，闲境入幽情，呈现一幅天凉好个秋的美丽画面！</p>
<p>时序进入深秋，秋意渐浓，乡间河床砂砾地上<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E6%A0%B9%E5%AD%90%E8%8D%89.md#1">甜根子草</a>陆续开花，白茫茫花海绽放满荒野，加上道路旁成排的台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">栾树</a>红、黄花盛开色彩缤纷，绿美化道路两旁景观，映衬妆点大地秋之色彩，也许你一个不小心，转角就能遇见秋季的美树浪漫。</p>
<p>秋天是<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">台湾栾树</a>最美的季节，枝头上恣意的变装秀，随着一年四季各有风采，春天长出新芽时呈浅绿色，盛夏转为深绿色，一入秋天，满树金黄璀璨花开的 十分茂密，一串串结成果实后呈艳红色，果皮剥落又转成咖啡色，因此又称为“<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E8%89%B2%E6%A0%BE%E5%8D%8E.md#1">五色栾华</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310628" style="width: 1024px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310628-1-2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13310628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310628-1-2.jpg" alt="" width="1024" b="868" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310628" class="wp-caption-text">秋天是<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%A0%BE%E6%A0%91.md#1">台湾栾树</a>最美的季节，枝头上恣意的变装秀，随着一年四季展现风采。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13310627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310627" style="width: 1920px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310627-1-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13310627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310627-1-1.jpg" alt="" width="1920" b="1030" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310627" class="wp-caption-text">绿道栾树黄、红、绿三色花盛开相互辉映。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾栾树的花语：热情、开朗和朝气，文人称其为秋神的使者，常引用了清朝古籍《<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E5%90%8D%E5%AE%9E%E5%9B%BE%E8%80%83.md#1">植物名实图考</a>》中贴切诗词来形容仲秋的栾树，“绛霞烛天，丹撷照岫，先于霜叶，可增秋谱。”风恋栾花，天酿醉霞，妆扮亮丽的秋日风韵，将这个时节演绎得令人陶醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310630" style="width: 1536px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310630-1-4.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13310630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310630-1-4.jpg" alt="" width="1536" b="864" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310630" class="wp-caption-text">乡间河床上<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E6%A0%B9%E5%AD%90%E8%8D%89.md#1">甜根子草</a>陆续开花，白茫茫花海绽放荒野。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>甜根子草银白花浪横扫溪床，搭配绿道栾树黄、红、绿三色花相互辉映，台湾原野美极了﹗趁着秋高气爽，到郊外走走吧，当你漫步在<ahref="https://github.com/ixbbwc3288/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E8%89%B2%E6%A0%BE%E5%8D%8E.md#1">五色栾华</a>树下，静静享受这专属于秋天限定版的美景，郁闷的心情也跟着转换，随之跳跃、缤纷浪漫了起来。@*</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310631" style="width: 1280px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310631-1-5.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13310631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310631-1-5.jpg" alt="" width="1280" b="1199" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310631" class="wp-caption-text">乡间河床上甜根子草陆续开花，白茫茫花海绽放荒野。（曾晏均／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
