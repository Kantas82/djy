<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【缩时料理】铁铸锅做出蓬松软嫩“厚蛋烧”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310444-6a1504e2f56cb4d9531ad88927875575-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>不用平底锅，使用铁铸锅也可以做成蓬松软嫩的“厚蛋烧”。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【编者按】“<ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9A%E8%9B%8B%E7%83%A7.md#1">厚蛋烧</a>”又称为玉子烧，是使用蛋液煎成的日本蛋卷，一口咬下有多层次的堆叠口感，因为有厚度品尝起来很令人满足。料理工具有专属的玉子烧平底锅，如果没有也可以使用尺寸偏小的平底锅，或是铁铸锅都可以。下面推荐的食谱即是使用铁铸锅来制作，除了份量十足、蓬松软嫩，是一款平易近人的食谱。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AD%A9%E4%BB%94%E9%B1%BC.md#1">魩仔鱼</a><ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9A%E8%9B%8B%E7%83%A7.md#1">厚蛋烧</a></span></h2>
<p>厚蛋烧使用平底锅制作困难度比较高，可以善用较小尺寸的staub<ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%B8%E9%93%81%E9%94%85.md#1">铸铁锅</a>，简单的步骤转眼间就完成。蓬松的厚度，十分有份量。这道菜不需要额外添加高汤也可以制作。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">建议锅具尺寸</span></h3>
<p>14cm staub</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（４人份）</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AD%A9%E4%BB%94%E9%B1%BC.md#1">魩仔鱼</a>&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;３０g<br />
鸡蛋&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..４颗</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">Ａ</span></h3>
<p>酱油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;１／２大匙<br />
味醂&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;２大匙<br />
水&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;２大匙<br />
柴鱼片&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;２.５g<br />
橄榄油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将鸡蛋打入调理碗内，加入魩仔鱼、Ａ搅拌均匀。</p>
<p>2. 锅内倒入橄榄油，转中火加热，开始冒出薄烟后，转动锅子，让锅内侧面也都布满油。</p>
<p>3. 放入步骤１，底部凝固后，利用硅胶锅铲反复搅拌。蛋液变成半熟状后，盖上锅盖，调成微火，继续加热约５分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310441" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310441-01b90ad35f94cfd882aa688e921636b1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13310441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310441-01b90ad35f94cfd882aa688e921636b1-450x352.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310441" class="wp-caption-text">铁铸锅的底部食材凝固后，利用硅胶锅铲反复搅拌。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13310442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310442-978b0bb2d002f274a70faff3a130424f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13310442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310442-978b0bb2d002f274a70faff3a130424f-450x347.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310442" class="wp-caption-text">蛋液变成半熟状后，盖上锅盖，调成微火，继续加热约５分钟。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 开始膨胀后即可熄火，如果没有膨胀的话，再继续加热２～３分钟。不要取下盖子，继续放置１５分钟（使用余热焖煮）。使用硅胶锅铲在食材与锅缘间轻划，倒扣于盘子上取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310443" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310443-6900a9db07e42fbd2da9aec1d433233e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13310443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310443-6900a9db07e42fbd2da9aec1d433233e-450x352.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310443" class="wp-caption-text">蛋液开始膨胀后即可熄火。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%A3%AB.md#1">起士</a><ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%A7%86%E8%9B%8B.md#1">欧姆蛋</a></span></h2>
<p>将魩仔鱼厚蛋烧换成西式风味的应用料理。加入牛奶，吃起来口感更柔软蓬松、滑顺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13310445" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13310445" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310445-138717e3d847a6403cf471d60e20f838.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13310445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13310445-138717e3d847a6403cf471d60e20f838-450x442.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13310445" class="wp-caption-text">魩仔鱼厚蛋烧加入牛奶可以做成<ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E5%A7%86%E8%9B%8B.md#1">欧姆蛋</a>，吃起来更滑顺好入口。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">建议锅具尺寸</span></h3>
<p>14cm staub</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（４人份）</span></h3>
<p>帕玛森<ahref="https://github.com/bopsop395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%A3%AB.md#1">起士</a>&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;１５g<br />
鸡蛋&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..４颗<br />
牛奶&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１大匙<br />
橄榄油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>将鸡蛋打入调理碗内，再放入帕玛森起士、牛奶搅拌均匀。烹调方式与魩仔鱼厚蛋烧<strong>（请参照上方食谱）</strong>相同。<strong>⊙</strong></p>
