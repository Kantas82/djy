<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【组图】加拿大加斯佩半岛美景——鸟岛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311121-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland1-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加斯佩（Gaspé）博纳旺图鸟岛的最高处，约有20万只不同鸟类筑巢。（青云提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>文、图 | 青云</strong></span></p>
<p>加拿大东部有着绵延千里的海岸线，顺着圣劳伦河（St-Laurent）沿岸直达加拿大东端<ahref="https://github.com/phbyxm3262/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AD%81%E5%8C%97%E5%85%8B.md#1">魁北克</a>省境内的<ahref="https://github.com/phbyxm3262/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%96%AF%E4%BD%A9.md#1">加斯佩</a>半岛（Gaspé Peninsula）是备受推崇的自驾车之旅。</p>
<p>游客抵达<ahref="https://github.com/phbyxm3262/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%96%AF%E4%BD%A9.md#1">加斯佩</a>半岛，必游佳景之一就是派赫塞巨石（法语：Rocher Percé，英语：Percé Rock），而乘船参观完巨石，游客的下一站必定是博纳旺图岛（法语：?le-Bonaventure，英语：Bonaventure Island，意为“好运”）。</p>
<p>博纳旺图岛是北美最大的候鸟栖息地，是北美最大的<ahref="https://github.com/phbyxm3262/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E5%B2%9B.md#1">鸟岛</a>，也是世界第二大鸟岛，夏季有超过28万只各种鸟类栖息，包括11万6千只北方塘鹅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311123" style="width: 4032px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311123-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland2-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311123-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland2-2.jpg" alt="" width="4032" b="1960" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311123" class="wp-caption-text">夏季有超过28万只各种鸟类在岛上栖息。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311124" style="width: 4272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311124-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland3-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311124-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland3-2.jpg" alt="" width="4272" b="2848" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311124" class="wp-caption-text">夏季有超过28万只各种鸟类在岛上栖息。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该岛面积4.16平方公里，形状大致呈圆形。下船从码头步行，绕岛半圈回来，至少需要大约3个小时。</p>
<p>因为交通便利，博纳旺图岛是世界上最容易接近的<ahref="https://github.com/phbyxm3262/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E5%B2%9B.md#1">鸟岛</a>，所以夏季游客络绎不绝。小岛距离附近的派赫赛镇（法语Percé）5公里，与派赫赛巨石（法语：Rocher Percé，英语：Percé Rock）遥相辉映，成为该地区绝佳美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311125" style="width: 4032px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311125-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland4-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311125" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311125-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland4-2.jpg" alt="" width="4032" b="1960" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311125" class="wp-caption-text">博纳旺图岛上最常见的鸟类是北方塘鹅，这里是世界上最大的塘鹅群之一。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311126" style="width: 4272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311126-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland5-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311126-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland5-2.jpg" alt="" width="4272" b="2848" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311126" class="wp-caption-text">塘鹅宝宝头上的绒毛还是白色，被父母紧紧护佑着。幼鸟长大之后，头顶毛色会变黄。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>按照官方记录，有218种鸟类迁移或生活在该岛上。岛上最常见的鸟类是北方塘鹅，这里有世界上最大的塘鹅群之一。同时，还可以观察到燕鸥、黑色海鸠、鲱鸥、巨大的黑背鸥、风暴海燕、大鸬鹚、双冠鸬鹚、大西洋海鹦，以及北方山雀和黑莺等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311127" style="width: 4272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311127-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland6-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311127-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland6-2.jpg" alt="" width="4272" b="2848" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311127" class="wp-caption-text">还有更多的鸟类，只有乘船才能观赏，它们更喜欢栖息在岛上的峭壁处。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13311119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311119" style="width: 4272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311119-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland8-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311119-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland8-2.jpg" alt="" width="4272" b="2848" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311119" class="wp-caption-text">也有的鸟喜欢在水边凹陷的峭壁里乘凉。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据悉，加拿大最后一艘航空母舰HMCS Bonaventure号，即以该鸟岛命名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13311122" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13311122" style="width: 4032px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311122-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland10-2.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13311122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13311122-20180803_Montreal_GaspeBirdIsland10-2.jpg" alt="" width="4032" b="1960" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13311122" class="wp-caption-text">博纳旺图鸟岛与附近的派赫赛巨石和派赫赛镇遥遥相望。（青云提供）</figcaption></figure>
