<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>客家小炒争霸赛 县长恭贺“阿戊嫂的店”夺冠</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483001_medium-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>徐耀昌县长特颁 神厨客庄。（陈文敏／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年02月04日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗报导）苗栗三湾“阿戊嫂的店”荣获客委会“2020<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E5%B0%8F%E7%82%92.md#1">客家小炒</a><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%89%E9%9C%B8%E8%B5%9B.md#1">全国争霸赛</a>”全国总决赛冠军，为县争光，2月4日<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%BF%E9%95%BF.md#1">县长</a>徐耀昌特地颁赠“客庄厨神”木匾，表达恭贺之意，并欢迎大家新春假期来苗栗旅游，品尝好食的客家好味道。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E5%B0%8F%E7%82%92.md#1">客家小炒</a>是客家传统料理中四炆四炒的一道美食，也是每个客家乡亲从小到大舌尖上的共同记忆。因为去年新冠病毒疫情严重冲击国内民生产业，客庄餐厅更是首当其冲，客委会希望透过举办大型料理赛事，行销客家美食，刺激客庄观光餐饮业，以吸引大家到访客庄观光，活络当地经济，特地举办首届“2020客家小炒<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A8%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%89%E9%9C%B8%E8%B5%9B.md#1">全国争霸赛</a>”，赛事竞争相当激烈，先在全台北、中、南、东4区举办初赛，再由各区选出优胜店家进入总决赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12733376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12733376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483003_medium-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12733376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483003_medium-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12733376" class="wp-caption-text">得冠军的客家小炒 。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%BF%E9%95%BF.md#1">县长</a>徐耀昌4日上午到阿戊嫂的店赠匾表扬恭贺，他指出，三湾“阿戊嫂的店”，主厨刘雪玲老师一路过关斩将，展现精湛厨艺，在中区初赛从30队参赛队伍中脱颖而出荣获亚军，之后持续研究精进菜色，最终在全国总决赛32间优胜店家中拿下冠军，表现亮眼、实至名归。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12733375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12733375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483002_medium-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12733375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483002_medium-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12733375" class="wp-caption-text">传统食材制作惊艳的客家小炒。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徐耀昌表示，苗栗是观光大县，景色优美，农历新年将届，欢迎大家新春期间来苗栗采果泡汤赏花，别忘了也要品尝客家好味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12733379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12733379" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483006_medium-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12733379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483006_medium-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12733379" class="wp-caption-text">以客家小炒的创意粽子。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刘雪玲表示初赛后，为在总决赛获得佳绩，他在五天内总共练菜7次，不断研究改善，决定在客家小炒内加入传统米饭呈现客家精神，最终不负众望获得总决赛冠军，他很开心能以客家小炒等美食提升客庄知名度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12733380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12733380" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483007_medium-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12733380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483007_medium-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12733380" class="wp-caption-text">客家小炒 。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12733378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12733378" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483005_medium-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12733378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/483005_medium-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12733378" class="wp-caption-text">徐耀昌县长目睹客家小炒制作。（许享富／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
