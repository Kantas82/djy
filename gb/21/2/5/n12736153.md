<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】避风塘炒鱼排</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3bf9629df78168778aa4ee4e7e1b20e1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这道避风塘炒鱼排选用了多种配料， 配料的独特香气和鱼排主料完美融合，整道菜的质感和口感层次分明，外形粗犷却不失细腻。（普照荟苑提供） 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>在香港粤菜馆的食谱中有一款经典的菜式叫“<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%BF%E9%A3%8E%E5%A1%98%E8%8F%9C%E5%BC%8F.md#1">避风塘菜式</a>”，名声十分响亮。避风塘，其实是当地渔民在飓风时节躲避台风的一个个渔港。</p>
<p>每到台风季节，香港的渔民们就会把船停到避风塘里躲避暴风雨。几天不能出海捕鱼，日子长了，水上渔家开始以特殊的烹调方式来制作新鲜的海鲜食材，把海鲜油炸后入菜，竟有意想不到的美味收获，特别是加上大量炸香蒜蓉的方法，浓浓的蒜香、酥脆的口感，味道十分吸引人，也由此形成了“避风塘”菜肴的风格。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%BF%E9%A3%8E%E5%A1%98%E7%82%92%E9%B1%BC%E6%8E%92.md#1">避风塘炒鱼排</a>选用的是比目鱼，比目鱼富含蛋白质、维生素A、维生素D、钙、磷、钾等营养成分，尤其维生素B6的含量颇丰，而脂肪含量较少，另外，比目鱼还富含大脑的主要组成成分DHA，经常食用可增强智力。</p>
<p>罗厨将两条比目鱼的鱼柳肉取出，然后将鱼骨架切方块成鱼排。鱼排用海盐腌制后裹生粉，入油锅要炸两次，这样会使鱼排更酥脆。在第二次炸鱼排时，添加一点糖。油炸过程中，砂糖融化成糖浆沾于鱼排面上，不仅可以去腥味，也会使鱼排更脆。</p>
<p>出锅上碟的<ahref="https://github.com/tyhool308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%BF%E9%A3%8E%E5%A1%98%E7%82%92%E9%B1%BC%E6%8E%92.md#1">避风塘炒鱼排</a>，肥而不腻，肉汁鲜美，鱼皮酥化甘香，并且略带鲜辣椒的香气，吐司的面粉香味及各种配料独特的香脆，不但没有盖住比目鱼独有的鲜美，而且提升了整道菜的完美口感。</p>
<h2>避风塘炒鱼排</h2>
<p>主料：将比目鱼架切成鱼排。</p>
<p>配料：葱头 、青红辣椒 、香菜段 、芹菜 、吐司 、豆豉 、蒜蓉</p>
<p>调料：海盐 、白糖、生粉、生抽、胡椒粉</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将比目鱼排洗净吸干水分后剁成鱼排。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>2. 用海盐将鱼排腌制15分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>3. 将腌好的鱼排沾满生粉。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>4. 用八成油温将鱼排炸至酥脆。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>5. 炸好后捞起。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>6. 放入锅中复炸。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>7. 第二次起镬时，放入白砂糖2汤匙。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>8. 然后将面包吐司、蒜蓉一同炸香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>9. 将鱼排、吐司捞起沥干油分。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>10. 将葱、辣椒等配料用生抽、胡椒粉炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a>11. 最后把鱼排放入镬中，添加海盐炒匀即可上碟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12736182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
