<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA湖人双星耀眼 射下老鹰</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/482572-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>詹姆斯。（Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年02月02日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）亚特兰大<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E9%B9%B0.md#1">老鹰</a>和洛杉矶<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%96%E4%BA%BA.md#1">湖人</a>2日碰头，湖人由双星<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B4%E7%BB%B4%E6%96%AF.md#1">戴维斯</a>（Anthony Davis）和<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A9%B9%E5%A7%86%E6%96%AF.md#1">詹姆斯</a>（LeBron James）领军，一共5人得分上双，率<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%96%E4%BA%BA.md#1">湖人</a>以107：99抓下<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E9%B9%B0.md#1">老鹰</a>，老鹰小将杨恩（Trae Young）25分、16助攻，创下本季助攻新高，成为缺憾。</p>
<figure id="12728136" aria-describedby="caption-12728136" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/482575-450x597.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/482575-450x597.jpg" alt="戴维斯。" width="338" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12728136" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B4%E7%BB%B4%E6%96%AF.md#1">戴维斯</a>。（Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12728135" aria-describedby="caption-12728135" style="width: 417px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/482574-450x483.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/482574-450x483.jpg" alt="詹姆斯（左）、戴维斯（右）。" width="417" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12728135" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A9%B9%E5%A7%86%E6%96%AF.md#1">詹姆斯</a>（左）、戴维斯（右）。（Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卡佩拉（Clint Capela）用拿手灌篮，施洛德（Dennis Schroder）切入上篮，双方拉开比赛序幕，加索尔（Marc Gasol）和戴维斯禁区强攻，湖人拉出差距，不过柯林斯（John Collins）及赫尔特（Kevin Huerter）外线点火，老鹰随后逼平，詹姆斯最后三罚全中，首节湖人在客场以28：25开响第一枪。</p>
<p>第二节双方你来我往，互不相让，最后一分钟，湖人铁桶防守，滴水不漏，造成杨恩投球失准，加里纳利（Danilo Gallinari）突破上篮被加索尔封盖，这时波普（Kentavious Caldwell-Pope）伺机三分出手，湖人带着4分优势进入下半场。</p>
<p>戴维斯篮下威力无法挡，不过杨恩穿针引线，单节独砍12分，老鹰反携1分领先进入末节。第四节读秒前2分04秒，卡佩拉轰炸篮框，老鹰落后2分，戴维斯跳投，詹姆斯三分外线，湖人双星再把分数拉开，詹姆斯再连拿6分，老鹰只好俯首称臣。◇</p>
