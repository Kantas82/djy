<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周末三日新增确诊1158宗   21死</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年02月02日讯】（<span style="font-weight: 400;">大纪元加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/uoghuz361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>讯）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日（周一），<ahref="https://github.com/uoghuz361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81%E9%A6%96%E5%B8%AD%E5%8D%AB%E7%94%9F%E5%AE%98.md#1">卑诗省首席卫生官</a>邦妮?亨利</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Bonnie Henry)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">医生公布<ahref="https://github.com/uoghuz361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%AC%E7%9C%81%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">本省疫情</a>的最新情况。在过去的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">72</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时内，省内录得</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,158</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宗新增确诊个案，累计个案总数增至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">67,937</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宗。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">数据显示，过去</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日卑诗省新增</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">21</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宗死亡个案，累计死亡个案总数增至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,210</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宗</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">目前有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">289</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名住院患者，当中</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">79</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人在深切治疗部，本省目前有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4,134</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宗现存新型冠状病毒个案，有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7,242</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人因确认接触过已知个案正接受定期的<ahref="https://github.com/uoghuz361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%85%B1%E5%8D%AB%E7%94%9F%E8%A7%82%E5%AF%9F.md#1">公共卫生观察</a>。</span>◇</p>
