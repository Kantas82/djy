<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>利泽简永安宫妈祖除疫祈福走尪绕境</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485428-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>五结乡利泽简永安宫元宵节除疫祈福绕境准备。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年02月26日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）五结乡利泽简永安宫2021年<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>无形<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E8%B5%84%E4%BA%A7.md#1">文化资产</a><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%B0%AA.md#1">走尪</a>活动，因疫情做了大调整。</p>
<p>原来庆典及八大庄头车队汇集参与<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%95%E5%A2%83.md#1">绕境</a>，26日改由永安宫部分信众，以徒步方式绕境，使用人力抬妈祖銮轿，陪着妈祖除疫绕境大利泽地区八大庄各庄头角落，傍晚四点多回到利泽简老街，进行传统的<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%B0%AA.md#1">走尪</a>和三进殿过火仪式。另外，走尪公益路跑延至6月19日举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12776457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12776457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485430.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12776457" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485430-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12776457" class="wp-caption-text">五结乡乡长沈德茂发放钱母卡、米荖。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12776454" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12776454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485427.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12776454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485427-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12776454" class="wp-caption-text">五结乡乡长沈德茂及黄适超主委，带领信众在妈祖庙前祈福团拜。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12776456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12776456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485429.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12776456" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485429-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12776456" class="wp-caption-text">牛转乾坤喜迎春，发放钱母卡、米荖。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>早期先民渡海来台，天灾瘟疫四起，先民信仰妈祖文化，每年<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5%E8%8A%82.md#1">元宵节</a>抬着神轿神尊<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%95%E5%A2%83.md#1">绕境</a>各庄头，深信妈祖神力能将瘟疫消除，妈祖走尪习俗除疫驱瘟，保佑先民身心健康。</p>
<p>永安宫黄适超主委有感于疫情，决定恢复徒步抬神轿传统走尪绕境，深入八大庄各角落，全程27公里，由利泽简出发，绕境成兴村、龙德里、顶寮里、季新村、利泽村，以虔诚的信仰力量，为众生除疫祈福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12776464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12776464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485432.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12776464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485432-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12776464" class="wp-caption-text">五结乡利泽简永安宫元宵节除疫祈福绕境出发。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12776461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12776461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485431.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12776461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/485431-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12776461" class="wp-caption-text">五结乡利泽简永安宫元宵节除疫祈福绕境队伍。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月26日元宵节上午8点，由五结乡乡长沈德茂及黄适超主委，带领信众在妈祖庙前团拜后，将发放钱母发财金和吃平安的米荖，接着永安宫以徒步及人力抬轿，跟随妈祖銮轿绕境，沿路民众拿香参拜妈祖，并和随香者换香，永安宫也会沿路发送妈祖銮轿压轿金。</p>
<p>下午三点，绕境队伍会进入传统艺术中心文昌祠，约在四点多回到利泽简老街，进行抬轿跑步的传统走尪，在最后一趟过火之后，进行三进殿过火仪式。</p>
