<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味之年菜系列】花雕烧红鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/50714dc4e464b93687a18be277549316-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这道花雕烧红鱼充满独特的江南风味，有蒜子肉特点的红鱼能最大程度吸收花雕酒和五花肉的味度，鱼肉嫩滑可口。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>花雕酒属于发酵酒中的黄酒，堪称中国黄酒中的奇葩。据记载，花雕酒起源于6000年前的山东大汶口文化时期，代表了源远流长的中国酒文化。在各地的花雕酒当中，字号最老的当属浙江绍兴的花雕酒。</p>
<p>选用上好糯米、优质麦曲，辅以江浙明净澄澈的湖水，用古法酿制，再贮以时日，产生出独特的风味和丰富的营养。据科学鉴定，花雕酒含有对人体有益的多种氨基酸、糖类和维生素等营养成分，被称为“高级液体蛋糕”。花雕酒酒性柔和，酒色橙黄清亮，酒香馥郁芬芳，酒味甘香醇厚。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道花雕烧红鱼，选用2磅左右的红鱼一尾，以五花肉为配料，辅以姜片和葱段，整道菜充满了独特的江南风味，有蒜子肉特点的红鱼能最大程度吸收花雕酒和五花肉的味度，做出来的鱼肉嫩滑可口。</p>
<p>罗厨强调，为保证鱼肉容易熟且入味，首先要将鱼中骨两侧各拉一刀，在鱼身最厚处也要切一刀，而且鱼下锅之前要用盐腌制10分钟。慢火烧鱼的时候，要放2汤匙的白酒，这样可以很有效地去土腥味。</p>
<p>今天是中国传统的元宵佳节。在中国人眼里，过完元宵节才算真正过完新年。罗厨今天特别奉献这道花雕烧红鱼，鱼的谐音“余”不仅仅代表着盈余，也象征着降妖除鬼。在此，特别祝福大家在新的一年里，全家健康、团团圆圆、幸福美满，美味常伴身边。</p>
<h2>花雕烧红鱼</h2>
<p>主料：红鱼一尾</p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱段、五花肉片</p>
<p>调料：白酒、花雕酒、美极酱油、生抽、老抽、生粉水</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1.?红鱼洗净沥干水分，在鱼中骨两侧各拉一刀，在鱼身最厚处切一刀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777495" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-69.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-69-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在鱼中骨两侧各拉一刀。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.?放2茶匙盐腌制红鱼10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777498" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-47.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-47-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放2茶匙盐腌制红鱼10分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.?将葱、姜、五花肉爆香至五花肉出油，然后捞起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777500" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-44.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-44-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将葱、姜、五花肉爆香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.?再把鱼两面煎至黄焦后捞起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777502" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-41.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777502" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-41-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把鱼两面煎至黄焦。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.?利用镬底余油把姜片、葱段、五花肉爆炒后，放白酒和开水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777506" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-25-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放白酒和开水。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.?取出葱段，放入花雕酒、美极酱油等调料，放入红鱼慢火烧10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777508" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入红鱼慢火烧10分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.?再次放入葱段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777509" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">再次放入葱段。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.?加盖再烧15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777510" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加盖再烧15分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.?将烧好的鱼上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777512" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将烧好的鱼上碟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.?将鱼汤收汁至更加香浓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777514" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鱼汤收汁。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.?放入老抽和生粉水搅成浓汁，淋于鱼身上即可享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12777521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12777521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浓汁淋于鱼身上即可。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
