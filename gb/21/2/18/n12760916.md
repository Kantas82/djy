<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味之年菜系列】香芋荷叶蒸鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5fe7fd9be2b80563351f505e9e34c89b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>香芋清香细滑，吸收了腊味和鸡肉的汁水，鸡肉吸收了腊味风干之后独具的香气， 通过蒸笼蒸后，形成了一道无可替代的功夫菜，堪称年节喜庆的上等佳肴。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鸡肉是<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>餐桌上不可缺少的一道料理，鸡肉代表着吉祥，将吉祥端上桌，明年事事都顺心又顺利。“鸡”不仅与“吉”同音，而且“鸡”的台语与“家”同音，象征着一家大吉大利的意思。</p>
<p>香芋有一种独特的香草味，不仅营养丰富，而且色、香、味俱佳，曾被人称为“蔬菜之王”。香芋含有较多的粗蛋白、淀粉、聚糖（粘液质）、粗纤维和糖，蛋白质的含量比大豆等高蛋白植物都要高。</p>
<p>芋头还含有一种黏液蛋白，被人体吸收后能产生免疫球蛋白，或称抗体球蛋白，可提高机体的抵抗力和免疫力。故中医认为芋艿能解毒，对人体的痈肿毒痛包括癌毒有抑制消解作用，可用来防治肿瘤及淋巴结核等病症。对于癌症手术后放射化疗以及康复，也有辅助治疗作用。</p>
<p>香芋烹煮方法有很多种，烧、炒、炖皆可，用其与鸡肉、猪肉一起炖、烧，其味香而不腻、酥而不烂，历来是逢年过节、喜庆筵席上必备的上乘菜肴。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8A%8B%E8%8D%B7%E5%8F%B6%E8%92%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">香芋荷叶蒸鸡</a>，采用清蒸的方法，完全保留了食材的营养成分，香芋清香、松软、细滑，充分吸收了腊味和鸡肉的汁水，鸡肉的肉质香滑爽口，同时也吸收了腊味风干之后独具的香气，三种食材各具特色，通过蒸笼蒸后，形成了一道无可替代的功夫菜，堪称年节喜庆的上等佳肴。</p>
<p>制作这道菜时，首先要用盐、糖、胡椒粉等调料腌制鸡肉，鸡肉腌好后放入香菇、腊肉、姜丝、葱、红枣等配料捞匀，这样会使鸡肉充分入味。罗厨强调，这道菜营养丰富、香气四溢，特别适合冬日享用。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8A%8B%E8%8D%B7%E5%8F%B6%E8%92%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">香芋荷叶蒸鸡</a></h2>
<p>主料：三黄鸡半只、香芋1.5磅</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a0f8c273ae4161a78244030642b1c41e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a0f8c273ae4161a78244030642b1c41e-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">三黄鸡一只（<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>配料：香菇、腊肉、姜丝、葱、红枣</p>
<p>调料：海盐、糖、淀粉、胡椒粉、生抽、老抽、蚝油、葱油</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 先将香芋去皮，切成小块状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-45.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-45-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">香芋去皮切小块。（<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 香菇一个切件，腊肉一条切丁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-28-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腊肉切丁。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将荷叶放入蒸笼里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-29-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">荷叶放入蒸笼。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将香芋块放于蒸笼荷叶之上，加盖蒸15分钟至熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-28-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">香芋放荷叶之上。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将三黄鸡洗净沥干水分后切件。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760953" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">三黄鸡洗净沥干水分后切块。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入调料腌制后放入配料捞匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760955" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉用盐、糖、胡椒粉腌制后放入葱、姜、香菇拌匀。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 把鸡肉放在香芋之上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760958" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将鸡肉放在香芋上。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 把腊肉放在鸡肉和香芋上面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760959" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最上面摆放腊肉。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 放入蒸箱再蒸8分钟至熟取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760961" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入蒸箱蒸8分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 放入姜丝、香菜、葱白、辣椒粒、红椒丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760962" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/10-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放姜丝、香菜、葱白等配料。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 淋上热油闻到葱香味即可，最后放2汤匙生抽即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12760964" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12760964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/11-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">淋上热油后放2汤匙生抽。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
