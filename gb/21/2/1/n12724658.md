<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【地中海炖汤】疗愈汤品 希腊番茄炖牛肉丸</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/f0445044c9fce7817692ffb9528f9d4a-e1612149143337-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>希腊牛肉丸加上自制的番茄酱，百吃不厌，不吃还会想念。(图／日日幸福提供)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：多汁Q弹嫩香的希腊肉丸子是餐馆热门菜，搭配地中海的<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E9%85%B1.md#1">番茄酱</a>食谱，一入口清爽酸甜，立即能感受到地中海的艳阳景致，身子不自觉都热了起来。</p>
<p>“希腊番茄炖<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89%E4%B8%B8.md#1">牛肉丸</a>”一道菜就将地中海的佳肴特色都充分表现，健康咸香美味又可口，百吃不厌之外，有时还会想念。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">希腊番茄炖<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89%E4%B8%B8.md#1">牛肉丸</a> Greek mearballs in tomato sauce</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">主厨秘诀</span></h3>
<p>这道<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炖牛肉</a>丸也可以改成猪绞肉取代，记得要选要脂肪含量少的猪绞肉喔！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>6人份</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E9%85%B1.md#1">番茄酱</a>材料</span></h3>
<p>洋葱300g<br />
大蒜50g<br />
九层塔叶30g<br />
整粒番茄罐头2罐<br />
特级初榨橄榄油100cc<br />
白酒200cc<br />
干燥奥勒冈叶1/2小匙<br />
清水300cc<br />
海盐7g<br />
现磨黑胡椒　适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>丸材料</span></h3>
<p>洋葱250g<br />
新鲜迷迭香1支<br />
牛绞肉500g<br />
鸡蛋1个<br />
海盐8g<br />
现磨黑胡椒0.5g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">准备工作</span></h3>
<p>番茄酱：洋葱、大蒜、九层塔叶切碎；罐头整粒番茄用食物调理机搅打成泥备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>丸：洋葱切碎；新鲜迷迭香取叶切碎备用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1. 深锅中先注入橄榄油，以中小火炒软洋葱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724693" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8bad29cd286f65ade0f71cd5af0c9eef.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724693" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8bad29cd286f65ade0f71cd5af0c9eef-450x300.jpg" alt="橄榄油" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">深锅中先注入橄榄油，以中小火炒软洋葱。(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 接着放入大蒜炒香，再注入白酒煮滚。<br />
3. 加入番茄酱所有材料，以小火煮约1小时。<br />
4. 等待时，将牛肉丸所有材料搅拌至有黏性。<br />
5. 搓揉成大小均匀的牛肉丸，至于烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724694" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/88c7a7c1b8873e093123ab6915fde413.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/88c7a7c1b8873e093123ab6915fde413-450x300.jpg" alt="牛肉丸" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">搓揉成大小均匀的牛肉丸，至于烤盘上。(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 烤箱预热至250℃，放入烤盘烘烤4分钟。<br />
7. 将牛肉丸放入番茄酱汁中炖煮10分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724695" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/65404dcaf42f86d6198288a3a92b4f55.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/65404dcaf42f86d6198288a3a92b4f55-450x300.jpg" alt="番茄酱" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将牛肉丸放入番茄酱汁中炖煮10分钟即可。(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12724634" style="width: 118px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/0d4d03e716f1d061c8f5b8a2a83ffa7f.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12724634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/0d4d03e716f1d061c8f5b8a2a83ffa7f-450x573.jpg" alt="地中海" width="118" b="150" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《好吃必学的102道<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%AD%E6%B5%B7%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">地中海料理</a>》书封。(图／日日幸福提供)</figcaption></figure>
