<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大自然的神工鬼斧 – 冬天的安大略湖（下）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_17-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>日落时分。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/21/2/3/n12729731.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">大自然的神工鬼斧 – 冬天的安大略湖（上）</a></p>
<p>此时，太阳已经接近水平面。冬日的余晖慢慢转为粉色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_18-450x286.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="286" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC%E5%86%B0.md#1">煎饼冰</a>（ice pancake）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>湖水中那一个个圆圈圈是在寒冷的地区的河流或海洋中才可见，叫<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC%E5%86%B0.md#1">煎饼冰</a>（ice pancake）。是快速的水流在水面角落堆积再遇上严寒急冻出现的，厚度一般有10公分。而煎饼冰互相碰撞时，堆积的碎冰和推挤的压力就形成了煎饼冰外围一圈环。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729789" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_19-450x299.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">夕阳下的灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天很幸运，等到了粉色的晚霞，不枉我在寒风中等待的一个多小时。</p>
<p>如果机缘巧合，还能看到更壮观的<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E9%9B%95.md#1">冰雕</a>。</p>
<p>2017年3月8日，罗城遭遇了一场时速130公里的飓风，一下全城近二十万家断电，大树被连根拔起，火车也被吹出轨，还差点把在停车场行走的我给卷走。可这场灾难性的飓风也吹出了<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E7%95%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">安大略湖</a>沿岸美丽冻人的千里<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B0%81.md#1">冰封</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_20-450x655.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="655" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E5%B0%81.md#1">冰封</a>堤坝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走过无数次的灯塔堤坝成了溜冰跑道。那天没敢上去走一走。我当时脑洞大开：穿上冰鞋从背后的山坡上加速冲下来，会不会就直接在对岸的多伦多着陆啊？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729787" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_21.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_21-450x624.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="624" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">堤坝的冬夏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对比一下冬夏两季的堤坝景色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729786" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_22-450x300.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">银装素裹的灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那天的灯塔像什么？像不像一个在走T台的模特？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729785" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_23-450x309.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冰糕椅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是我平时常常歇脚的长椅，今天成了冰糕椅，美的令人不舍得碰一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_24-450x675.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浪击冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>岸边的冰挂应该就是这样形成的：底下的湖水不断的拍击，风不断的吹，温度不断的降……</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729783" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_25-450x309.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">张牙舞爪的冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_26-450x300.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冰花。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随着太阳慢慢西下，光线变得越来越精彩。而此时我的苹果手机因温度过低而已经罢工了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_27.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_27-450x304.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6:59pm 夕阳下的<ahref="https://github.com/myujtv3571/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E7%95%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">安大略湖</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_28-450x304.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7:03pm 夕阳下的安大略湖。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_29.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_29-450x253.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7:05pm 夕阳下的安大略湖。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_30.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_30-450x252.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7:21pm 夕阳下的安大略湖。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
