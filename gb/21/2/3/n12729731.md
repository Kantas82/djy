<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大自然的神工鬼斧 – 冬天的安大略湖（上）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>安大略湖畔的冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>北美五大淡水湖之一的<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E7%95%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">安大略湖</a>北靠加拿大的安大略省，南接美国纽约州，美加两国的国境线从湖中央穿过。两岸的风景一年四季都吸引了无数摄影爱好者。而一到冬天，冰雪寒风的神工鬼斧又在岸边雕塑出各种各样的自然<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E9%9B%95.md#1">冰雕</a>，美不胜收，更是摄影师们流连忘返的打卡地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729809" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_02-450x297.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729809" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E7%95%A5%E6%B9%96.md#1">安大略湖</a>上的灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这灯塔座落在纽约上州罗彻斯特市（Rochester）的“依朗得奎特湾水上公园”（Irondequoit Bay State Marine Park）内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729808" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729808" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_03-450x279.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729808" class="wp-caption-text">通往灯塔的堤坝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般到一月底二月初，湖边的自然<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E9%9B%95.md#1">冰雕</a>规模已经蛮壮观了。我基本上会从那时起时不时的背着相机去湖边遛达遛达。当然衣服要防风保暖，鞋子要防水防滑。</p>
<p>前几天气温骤降，又连续下了几天雪。今天放晴，是出去转转的好机会。来到湖边，堤坝上已积满了冰雪，离水越近冰越厚，几乎是一步一滑小心翼翼往前挪。堤坝上已经有同好抗着相机脚架走在前面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729807" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729807" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_04-450x299.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729807" class="wp-caption-text">堤坝护栏上的<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%8C%82.md#1">冰挂</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729806" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_05-450x295.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729806" class="wp-caption-text">护栏上的<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%8C%82.md#1">冰挂</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729805" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_06-450x640.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="640" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729805" class="wp-caption-text">护栏上的冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>护栏上的冰挂与岩洞里的钟乳石极其相似。手劲大的一掰就能掰下来。不过多数人都很自觉的保护这些冰挂，使更多人能欣赏到它们的美丽与独特。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729804" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_07-450x294.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729804" class="wp-caption-text">护栏上的冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729803" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_08-450x305.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729803" class="wp-caption-text">堤边灌木冰雕。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石堆中的灌木戴上了冰雪手套，胖乎乎的非常可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729802" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_09-450x300.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729802" class="wp-caption-text">座落在Webster Park的灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从第一个灯塔出来沿着湖边往东不远就是韦伯斯特公园（Webster Park）。一条长长的笔直伸进湖水的堤坝通往另一个灯塔。今年的灯塔像不像穿着婚纱的新娘？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729801" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_10-450x298.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729801" class="wp-caption-text">Webster Park的堤坝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>堤坝上也是积了厚厚一层冰，慢慢的一步一挪走到灯塔前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_11-450x307.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729799" class="wp-caption-text">护栏上的冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>慢慢西斜的太阳开始给冰挂镀上一层金色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729797" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_12-450x307.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729797" class="wp-caption-text">被冰挂遮盖的大石。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这块被冰挂严严实实包起来的大石头像不像一块诱人的奶油蛋糕？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_13-450x289.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729796" class="wp-caption-text">冰雪覆盖的堤坝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12729795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729795" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_14-450x301.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729795" class="wp-caption-text">夕阳下的安大略湖。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>因为这堤坝是笔直朝北伸进大湖，所以堤坝上的冰挂比第一个更壮观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729793" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_15-450x327.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="327" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729793" class="wp-caption-text">阳光透过冰挂。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是我拍摄冰挂最喜欢的时刻与角度。当然难度也不小。看看我给朋友Joe拍下的工作照就知道一张好照片来之不易啊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12729792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12729792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12729792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210203_Lake_Ontario_Helen_Chou_16-450x317.jpg" alt="安大略湖" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12729792" class="wp-caption-text">好照片来之不易。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
