<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温哥华市府取消在灰点社区建临时庇护所计划</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年02月25日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者李木子加拿大温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">温哥华市政府发言人</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">23</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日发布声明，经过实地考察，并与卑诗省住房和<ahref="https://github.com/rembom3599/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BD%9C%E5%9C%A8%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5%E8%80%85%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">潜在经营者协会</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">B.C. Housing and potential operators</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）协商后，认为当地由于缺乏（必要的）的服务，并且建筑年久失修和交通不便等原因，政府决定放弃将位于灰点社区（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">West Point Grey</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）西端的杰瑞寇旅社（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jericho Hostel</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）作为无家可归者的临时住所的计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">画</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，市政府宣布了一项</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">“自愿过渡</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">”（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">voluntary transition</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）计划，准备将斯特拉斯科纳公园（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strathconapark</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）帐篷城的露营者转移到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jericho Hostel</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Motel 2400</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">改造的临时住所，这两处房产均为市政府所有。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据周二的声明，市政府和卑诗省房屋局（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">B.C. Housing,</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）仍将继续</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Motel 2400</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">计</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">画</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但尚未作出决定。市府发言人表示，工作人员也正在审查</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">“一些潜在的（其</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">它</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）地点”，用于<ahref="https://github.com/rembom3599/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B4%E6%97%B6%E4%BD%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">临时住房</a>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">周一（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">22</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）<ahref="https://github.com/rembom3599/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81%E6%80%BB%E6%A3%80%E5%AF%9F%E9%95%BF.md#1">卑诗省总检察长</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Attorney General</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）尹大卫（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">David Eby</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）在新闻发布会上宣布，在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">West Hastings </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">街</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15-27</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号的前陆军与海军百货公司（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Army and Navy department store</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）所在地将新建</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">60</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个收容所，另已有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">180</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元的资金到位，准备在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Terminal </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">大街</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">875 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">号在筹备</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">60</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个床位的<ahref="https://github.com/rembom3599/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B4%E6%97%B6%E4%BD%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">临时住房</a>项目。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">温哥华市议员皮特．弗莱（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Pete Fry</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">CBC</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的早间节目中曾表示，虽然该项目遭到了附近居民的反对，但最终是因为实用性的原因，导致杰瑞寇旅社（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jericho Hostel</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）被放弃。市府也表示，来自西区富人区居民的抵制不是决定因素。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">家住在斯特拉斯科纳（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strathcona</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的市民弗莱（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fry</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）则认为，西区应分担这个城市的社会责任，并认为东区目前不公平地承担了过重的份额。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
