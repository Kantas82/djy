<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>春夏减龄穿搭 经典小黑裙尽显女力时尚</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-71519257-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Romilly Collins 身着电影《第凡内早餐》中奥黛丽?赫本的经典黑裙装扮。这件原版由纪凡希（Hubert de Givenchy ）设计的黑色洋装是为历史上一件经典的小黑裙。（Peter Macdiarmid/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span class="s1">【大纪元2021年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）说到黑色衣服，如今可说是人手一件，但谈到<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8.md#1">经典</a>的黑色服装，你会想到什么呢？</span><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>人士脑中第一个浮现的答案绝对是<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E9%BB%91%E8%A3%99.md#1">小黑裙</a>。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E9%BB%91%E8%A3%99.md#1">小黑裙</a>的历史并非一帆风顺，在维多利亚时期，小黑裙是丧服的象征；二战爆发后，因物资缺乏，使得简单大方的小黑裙成为女性的日常工作服。但</span><span class="s1">真正将小黑裙带进<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>界的是法国女性时装品牌“香奈儿”创始人</span><span class="s1">可可香奈儿（</span><span class="s5">Gabrielle Bonheur &#8220;Coco&#8221; Chanel</span><span class="s1">）。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">一战结束后的</span><span class="s6">20</span><span class="s1">年代，又称“咆哮年代（</span><span class="s6">Roaring Twenties</span><span class="s1">）”，工业化与就业机会增加，带动社会传统与现代意识冲撞，服装自是不遑多让：装饰艺术盛行、女性主义抬头&#8230;&#8230;在这样的氛围下，可可香奈儿发表了</span><span class="s5">H line</span><span class="s1">直筒小黑裙，重新定义女性<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">时至今日，小黑裙已经广为流行，甚至拥有专属缩写“</span><span class="s1">LBD”（Little Black Dress）。</span><span class="s1">因应场合选择样式，能正式能休闲，实穿度相当高！搭配不同配件，也可以营造迥异风格，堪称女孩子衣柜中的必备单品。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">就让我们来瞧一瞧女星及</span><span class="s1">各大</span><span class="s1">时装周，是如何展示这件<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8.md#1">经典</a>时尚的小黑裙的。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12761925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-87946781.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12761925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-87946781-450x723.jpg" alt="演员, 时尚, 穿搭, 小黑裙" width="450" b="723" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法国名模兼演员安娜?蒙格拉丝（Anna Mouglalis）出席第26届坎城影展。（Francois Durand/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">微微提高的腰线和及膝裙摆长度，使身形显得更修长，春夏最简单又经典的背心小黑裙，最适合娇小的女孩。细看上图这件两层衣料背心裙，外层优雅的镂空雕花蕾丝网纱让服装更加精致；领口、腰线、裙摆一致以亮片点缀，增添华丽感。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">裸色鱼纹鳞片高跟鞋和霸气的太阳眼镜，为整体造型带来强烈的气场。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12761923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-53057650.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12761923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-53057650-450x867.jpg" alt="演员, 时尚, 穿搭, 小黑裙" width="450" b="867" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美国女演员凯蒂?荷姆斯（Katie Holmes）出席《蝙蝠侠：开战时刻》欧洲首映。（MJ Kim/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">凯蒂?荷姆斯身穿这件Ｖ领口服装，乍看之下没有上一件小圆领来的庄重正式，但细节依旧不马乎。</span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">胸前以压皱布料交叉缠绕，营造丰富层次感同时凸显腰身，整体线条自然流畅；裙摆依稀可见是以两层网纱相叠，微微的波浪裙摆有着淡淡的浪漫感，搭配朴实简单的珠宝项链、手环，显尽典雅气质；珠串高跟凉鞋造型特殊，相当吸晴。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12762298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-154573344.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12762298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-154573344-450x653.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 歌手" width="450" b="653" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美国创作歌手泰勒丝（Taylor Swift）出席自己的专辑发布会。（Larry Busacca/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泰勒丝的这件Ａ字伞裙低调却实穿，根本不挑身形，人人都可适合，向外散开的裙型能遮掩腰际与大腿赘肉，受到许多女性欢迎。</p>
<p>黑色Ａ字小黑裙也是经典款式，平口设计凸显颈部与锁骨线条，微微澎起的三角形袖口修饰肩部轮廓，胸前双层布设计，给人可爱俏皮的感觉，整体简约没有多余装饰，将泰勒丝的招牌红唇与鲜红指甲油彩，衬托的恰到好处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12761983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-605691514.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12761983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-605691514-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭, 纽约, 时装周" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纽约2016-2017秋冬时装周。（Slaven Vlasic/Getty Images for New York Fashion Week）</figcaption></figure>
<p>细肩带也是夏日首选。虽然是小黑裙，但采用了白色肩带，无形中打亮上半身带来好气色，一字领设计有着清凉感，鱼尾裙设计则巧妙的修饰了身体曲线，戴上墨镜、踩着平底拖鞋，显得悠闲自在，但手中的糖果色水饺包相当吸睛，马上提升了时尚度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12761989" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-947475828.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12761989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-947475828-450x676.jpg" alt="时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">意大利混血女星伊莉莎?瑟娜薇（Elisa Sednaoui）出席卡地亚（Cartier）鸡尾酒会。（Stefania M. D&#8217;Alessandro/Getty Images for Cartier）</figcaption></figure>
<p>细肩带小黑裙内打底衬衫，可爱俏皮，卡通漫画印花很引人瞩目，与外层的小黑裙相互衬托，融合正式与休闲感，双层Ｖ领的选择凸显了颈部线条，再坠上一颗小而精致的珠宝，将视觉重点引导至脸部，是为一套精心设计的造型。</p>
<h2>与外套相搭</h2>
<p>春季微寒、夏季怕晒，进冷气房又觉得冷，该怎么办？这时百搭的小黑裙绝不是空口说白话，搭上各式外套都能为其带来新风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12762107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1276424394.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12762107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1276424394-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米兰2020-2021秋冬时装周。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>棒球外套带来年轻的朝气，和小黑裙相搭有着青春活泼的可爱气息，搭配球鞋、帆布鞋都是不错的选择，厚底凉鞋则带来更强的气场，气势逼人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12762133" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-535080196.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12762133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-535080196-450x676.jpg" alt="时装, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法国时尚品牌路易?威登（Louis Vuitton）早春系列走秀。（Fernanda Calfat/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>硬挺的皮革和柔软的小黑裙形成对比，毛绒棕褐色围巾衔接两种不同风格，融合甜美与干练。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12762132" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1273209410.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12762132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1273209410-450x677.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 穿搭" width="450" b="677" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加拿大超模可可?罗洽（Coco Rocha）出席克里斯蒂安?西里亚诺（Christian Siriano）时装品牌走秀。（Jamie McCarthy/Getty Images for Christian Siriano）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样万用又百搭的牛仔外套+小黑裙，这是常见的春夏组合，短版的牛仔外套更休闲，同时将腰线提升，搭配高跟踝靴，看起来率性俐落、可爱又减龄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12762134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-123198123.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12762134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-123198123-450x675.jpg" alt="影展, 时装, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">英国演员凯特?温斯蕾（Kate Winslet）出席第68届威尼斯影展。（Frederic Nebinger/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西装外套就显得正式许多，经典黑与藕紫色相搭，有着高贵的气息，从正式场合到出门吃饭都可以，也适合各年龄层<ahref="https://github.com/pzzmpo381/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>。<strong>@*</strong></p>
