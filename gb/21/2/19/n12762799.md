<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>初鹿牧场啜饮浓醇鲜奶 体验喂食动物的乐趣</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/cf994cd48540a211770306a6f55e81c4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>初鹿牧场。（图/王知涵提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者王知涵台湾报导）旅游是连系家庭情感的好时机，趁着假日带着全家前往台东<ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E9%B9%BF%E7%89%A7%E5%9C%BA.md#1">初鹿牧场</a>走走。园区有许多好玩元素，包括大片草地让孩子尽情跑跳，喂食逗趣的动物，还有啜饮最浓醇的<ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E9%B9%BF%E9%B2%9C%E4%B9%B3.md#1">初鹿鲜乳</a>；在阳光普照的日子，感受初鹿牧场满满活力。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E9%B9%BF%E7%89%A7%E5%9C%BA.md#1">初鹿牧场</a>位处于山坡上，可眺望花东纵谷与都兰山，有脱离尘嚣之感。来到这里，试着将大脑放空，到诗情画意的落羽松步道漫步，或到树木繁密的森林浴步道大口大口呼吸<ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%AC%E5%A4%9A%E7%B2%BE.md#1">芬多精</a>，在林荫下享受大自然满满地疗愈能量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12766844" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/947d02ef971291af6cdce90c1f13b994.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/947d02ef971291af6cdce90c1f13b994-450x253.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">眺望都兰山和花东纵谷。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12766841" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/fd4a50ea82438a7a8897e7c596de76cf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/fd4a50ea82438a7a8897e7c596de76cf-450x338.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">初鹿牧场的森林浴步道。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12766843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8efdbc0166fd7cd3cbfc7a2502cd9461.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8efdbc0166fd7cd3cbfc7a2502cd9461-450x253.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">初鹿牧场的落羽松步道。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>走进占地六十七公顷的初鹿牧场，首先映入眼帘就是宠物区，跟哈士奇狗狗差不多大小的迷你马、幼龄小猪猪，还有蹦蹦跳跳的兔兔们，只要一些饲料喂食，就可以近距离看到这些萌翻了的小动物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12766847" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/d5f16da7964b39ca4d91d459dbec4a79.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766847" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/d5f16da7964b39ca4d91d459dbec4a79-450x338.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宠物马。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12766848" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1c02ed66325fead7c8e18515f6a96177.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1c02ed66325fead7c8e18515f6a96177-450x253.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宠物马。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而努比亚山羊似乎是这里最热情的动物了，总是靠近通道旁的栅栏，眼巴巴盯着游客手中的牧草，长条型瞳孔目不转睛行注目礼。</p>
<p>吽！吽！吽！乳牛体积庞大，好抢镜。大乳牛佣佣懒懒晒着太阳享受日光浴，它们动作迟缓，像迟暮老人，看起来一点也不带劲，但可别小覻喔，它们可是初鹿<ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6.md#1">鲜奶</a>优质制造商呢，不仅有口碑，还挂品质保证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12766839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3fcb09027dc07ad175ff342003aee7f7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3fcb09027dc07ad175ff342003aee7f7-450x253.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">正在享受日光浴的乳牛。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到绿油油的青青草原，可以让孩子体验滑草，大人也可以尝试骑马。马场六匹强健壮硕的骏马，虽然没有迷你马娇巧可爱平易近人，但它们外表高大有个性，远远望去凛然生威。</p>
<p>“初鹿<ahref="https://github.com/afkdqf3350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6.md#1">鲜奶</a>”浓、纯、香是当地的特产。初鹿牧场园区设置商品专卖店，有各式乳制品，鲜奶、奶酪、炼乳、优格、奶昔、冰棒、冰淇淋、牛奶棒、牛奶饼干、牛奶面霜、牛奶沐浴乳……种类繁多。遇到用餐时刻，游客在森林咖啡店可以点些松饼轻食裹腹，或者到义式厨房品尝美味披萨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12766846" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e9c21a21918d61c110d1769866fb16f6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12766846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/e9c21a21918d61c110d1769866fb16f6-450x338.jpg" alt="初鹿" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“鲜乳吧”前面的戏水区。（图/王知涵提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>餐厅外有喷水池也有戏水区，水柱喷发消解烈阳炙热感，午后在溜滑梯、荡秋千消耗孩子用不完的精力。</p>
<p>生活不用太复杂，简简单单就是幸福！亲近自然和可爱动物互动，喂兔、喂马、喂羊、喂牛，在大草坪快乐奔跑追逐，渴了来杯冰冰凉凉初鹿鲜奶，吃个鲜奶酪解馋，再漫步于落羽松森林步道﹔累了，在观景台静静欣赏都兰山美景，让心灵沉淀，悠闲度过无忧无虑的休闲假期。<strong>@*</strong></p>
