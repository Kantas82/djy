<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>洪家栋镜头下：人烟稀少的踏青好去处</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4c87ddfadcaa87ce26350858bc07361c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年02月19日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）摄影专家<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%B6%E6%A0%8B.md#1">洪家栋</a>说，远离喧嚣，远离<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>的重压，到目前人烟稀少的<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B3%A2%E6%8B%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E4%BF%9D%E6%8A%A4%E5%8C%BA.md#1">西波拉国家野生动物保护区</a>（Cibola National Wildlife Refuge），<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B8%8F%E9%9D%92.md#1">踏青</a>露营。当你仰望宽阔的天际，心胸为之舒展；晚上仰望星空夜晚，观赏星罗棋布的大自然美景，寄语人生，在那空灵的天籁一隅，仍然充满光明与希望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12761769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761769" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2d0c126883d083b22188dc9079849cf7.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2d0c126883d083b22188dc9079849cf7-600x389.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761769" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%B6%E6%A0%8B.md#1">洪家栋</a>镜头下的<ahref="https://github.com/kywtza365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B3%A2%E6%8B%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E4%BF%9D%E6%8A%A4%E5%8C%BA.md#1">西波拉国家野生动物保护区</a>的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12761779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761779" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/52357f15826965ac815f2898fda55919.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/52357f15826965ac815f2898fda55919-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761779" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日前，前往西波拉国家野生动物保护区的洪家栋说，该保护区占地面积有18,444 英亩，科罗拉多河流经过此地，目的是保护和开创新沼泽和曲折蜿蜒区，以便因为气候变化迁居的野生动物和水禽有个过冬天的暂区休息地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12761770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761770" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/283a7eaefb8c22a204fb77503af51fd4.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/283a7eaefb8c22a204fb77503af51fd4-600x388.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761770" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12761773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/12d92f9fd0fdda8133bc762f11331b3e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/12d92f9fd0fdda8133bc762f11331b3e-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761773" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西波拉这个地名取自传说中的七个金城的神话。1800年，蒸汽船航经科罗拉多河，载运民生货品来到西波拉地域，顺便买当地的木材来做蒸气船的燃料。目前，西波拉是一个小农业耕作区域，也开放给游客来享受观赏野生动物、摄影、打猎、钓鱼和露营。</p>
<p>洪家栋表示，这里是州立动物保护区，冬天鸟类、水禽来此过冬。目前，如加拿大的雁等很多鸟类移居于此栖居，暂度冬天。此处宛如天然动物园，有5-6百只丹顶鹤、白鹅、雁等，游客可以在栅栏外观赏，带上望远镜观测鸟类生态，天然美景；该处树木在冬天一片淡黄色，别具风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12761776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761776" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/18ba697dd64b83b9c0486be2b8a3d098.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/18ba697dd64b83b9c0486be2b8a3d098-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761776" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12761778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761778" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8da9b982811f4af055a6cbc5329a18bf.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/8da9b982811f4af055a6cbc5329a18bf-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761778" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12761781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761781" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/fe56388e7f3fb6cb1d6d68109381a708.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12761781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/fe56388e7f3fb6cb1d6d68109381a708-600x614.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="512" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761781" class="wp-caption-text">洪家栋镜头下的西波拉国家野生动物保护区的自然景观。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12761815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12761815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a169a345e5444f41dea7122e33164016.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12761815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/a169a345e5444f41dea7122e33164016-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12761815" class="wp-caption-text">在西波拉野生动物保护区看到的新墨西哥的州鸟（Roadrunner ），洪家栋昵称跑路家。（洪家栋）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洪家栋说明，西波拉人烟稀少，远离城市，充分呈现大自然的奥妙；加上科罗拉多河流经此地，河上月光景色旖旎。科罗拉多河全长大约2,333公里，流经美国七个州及墨西哥两个州，供给南加水源。自行开车单程约4小时，沿途的风景有棕榈泉（Palm Spring）的风力发电风车、巴顿将军的博物馆，馆外有坦克车，亦为参观之处。</p>
<p>当地居民喜欢数天露营于此，他建议民众可开休旅车、RV或扎营过夜，但入夜气温下降，一定要准备保暖衣物。该园区开放时间上午8点到下午4:30。车辆环绕线（Auto loop）有3英里长，路名是Canada Goose Drive；客访中心电话928-857-3252；GPS：33.3635.-114.6664。◇</p>
