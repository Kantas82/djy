<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温岛公共服务雇员共享工作空间即将开放</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/6-13-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2月18日，卑诗省长贺谨参观位于温哥华岛的公共服务雇员共享办公室。（省府图片）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年02月20日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者杨清清加拿大温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">18</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97%E7%9C%81%E9%95%BF.md#1">卑诗省长</a>贺谨（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">John Horgan</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和省儿童及家庭发展厅长狄恩（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mitzi Dean</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）共同宣布，在<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E%E5%B2%9B.md#1">温哥华岛</a>兰福市（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Langford）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">设立首个全新的共享工作空间</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(co-working space)</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，以加强公共服务雇员的工作弹性。</span></p>
<p>这个位于兰福市Lakepoint One大楼的共享工作空间总面积930平方米，将于2月22日（下周一）正式开放。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12763291" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-52-e1613766329805.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12763291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/1-52-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（省府图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12763289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-33-e1613766615906.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12763289" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/3-33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（省府图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新办公室设有共享工作空间和私人房间，可以观赏到兰福湖（Langford Lake）的景色。它还配备了电动车充电站、自行车存放处、日用储物柜和淋浴间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12763292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-32-e1613766378425.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12763292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/2-32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（省府图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12763293" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-31-e1613766497531.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12763293" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/4-31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（省府图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12763294" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-19-e1613766574375.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12763294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/5-19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（省府图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据省府的数据，有超过2000名省府雇员居住在兰福（Langford）、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">科尔伍德(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Colwood)、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">梅特乔辛(</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Metchosin)、苏克（Sooke）、</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">维尤罗亚尔（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">View Royal）和高地（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Highlands）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">等西海岸社区。新办公室的设立可为这些雇员省去前往维多利亚市中心的通勤时间，同时减低<ahref="https://github.com/ahgqzs359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%AE%A4%E6%B0%94%E4%BD%93.md#1">温室气体</a>排放。◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span></p>
