<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>选对裤子 轻松穿出大长腿比例</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲报导）夏天到了，随着气温升高，各式流行单品也纷纷推出，美式休闲服装品牌Gap藉由风格、长度、剪裁各异的裤款，让不同需求的消费者都能选择适合自己的穿搭，适度搭配上衣、饰品等，就可以展现个人品味。</p>
<figure id="12972460" aria-describedby="caption-12972460" style="width: 374px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972460-497514-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972460-497514-450x600.jpg" alt="萝卜裤是轻松展现随兴、休闲的百搭单品。" width="374" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12972460" class="wp-caption-text">萝卜裤是轻松展现随兴、休闲的百搭单品。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 萝卜裤：显瘦、透风，一件搞定</p>
<figure id="12972459" aria-describedby="caption-12972459" style="width: 373px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972459-497513-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972459-497513-450x675.jpg" alt="上宽下窄的萝卜裤，可修饰臀部及大腿肉。" width="373" b="560" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12972459" class="wp-caption-text">上宽下窄的萝卜裤，可修饰臀部及大腿肉。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12972458" aria-describedby="caption-12972458" style="width: 377px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972458-497512-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972458-497512-450x600.jpg" alt="上宽下窄的萝卜裤，可修饰臀部及大腿肉。" width="377" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12972458" class="wp-caption-text">上宽下窄的萝卜裤，可修饰臀部及大腿肉。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>萝卜裤是一种介于修身裤与阔腿裤之间的裤型。裤身宽松、裤脚较收，整体形状类似于萝卜，相比正式的修身裤与慵懒地阔腿裤，萝卜裤是轻松休闲的百搭单品。萝卜裤上宽下窄的版型，能够修饰臀部及大腿位置的赘肉。宽松版型搭配格纹织面布料，能适应各种天气变化。不论是显瘦或是舒适度，都能一件搞定。</p>
<p>2. 高腰阔腿裤：舒服又俐落</p>
<figure id="attachment_12972455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12972455" style="width: 374px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972455-497509.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12972455" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972455-497509-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="374" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12972455" class="wp-caption-text">超高腰<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%A3%A4.md#1">牛仔裤</a>，能够将腰部视觉向上拉伸。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>腰裤款能够将腰部视觉向上拉伸，进而让腿部视觉效果更加修长，选择百搭的黑、白色及大地色系，让女性消费者轻松拥有大<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E8%85%BF.md#1">长腿</a>比例。高腰及超高腰等款式，搭配微微阔腿裤及落地感的版型设计，便能轻松打造出修身长腿LOOK。</p>
<p>3. 工装风长裤：个性化百搭选择</p>
<figure id="12972457" aria-describedby="caption-12972457" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972457-497511-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972457-497511-450x300.jpg" alt="复古风正流行，工装风格有修身效果。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12972457" class="wp-caption-text">复古风正流行，工装风格有修身效果。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12972456" aria-describedby="caption-12972456" style="width: 373px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972456-497510-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12972456-497510-450x600.jpg" alt="复古风正流行，工装风格有修身效果。" width="373" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12972456" class="wp-caption-text">复古风正流行，工装风格有修身效果。（Gap提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>复古风正流行，工装风格长裤拥有修身及缩口等裤型设计，达到显瘦修身效果，同时又百搭，上装搭配简单的T恤或是帽T就能轻易展现出一种独立的生活态度，而多口袋的设计更是提升机能性，众多优点是时下个性化穿搭首选。◇</p>
