<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>列治文20家餐厅获临时露台经营许可</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月12日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者李木子加拿大温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">为遏制</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">COVID-19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">病毒的传播，卑诗省首席卫生官员邦妮．亨利（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bonnie Henry</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）医生目前仍禁止餐厅提供<ahref="https://github.com/dqzwxg352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A0%82%E9%A3%9F.md#1">堂食</a>服务，该禁令至少要持续到5月长周末之后。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">为支持受困的餐饮业，各地市府为餐厅修建临时露台大开绿灯，简化审批程序，加快发放临时露台许可证，方便顾客可以<ahref="https://github.com/dqzwxg352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B7%E5%A4%96%E7%94%A8%E9%A4%90.md#1">户外用餐</a>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">目前在列治文，约有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">家餐馆和餐饮场所已经获得了临时露台许可证，它们是：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sockeye City, The Fish Man, Tandoori King Cafe, Gyu-kaku, 75 West Coast and Grill, Davood&#8217;s Bistro, Cora Breakfast and Lunch, Milltown Pub &amp; Grill, Tugboat Annie&#8217;s, Arc Iberico, CF Richmond Centre, Five Roads Brewing, Lulu Island Winery, Country Vines, Pioneer Pub, Fuggles &amp; Warlock, Afuri Ramen, O&#8217;Hares, Xialongkan and Tasty BBQ Restaurant.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大家可以前往这些餐厅<ahref="https://github.com/dqzwxg352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%B7%E5%A4%96%E7%94%A8%E9%A4%90.md#1">户外用餐</a>，享受三文鱼、印度菜、<ahref="https://github.com/dqzwxg352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E5%B9%BF%E5%9C%BA.md#1">美食广场</a>的饭菜和啤酒。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> ◇◇</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
