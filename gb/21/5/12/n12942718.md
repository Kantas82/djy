<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>柳松菇入菜 爽口鲜甜寿司卷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943176-P.135-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏季食欲不振吗？把蔬菜做一点小变化看起来就很可口了。图为“酪梨松菇加州卷”。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：富含大量氨基酸的<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B3%E6%9D%BE%E8%8F%87.md#1">柳松菇</a>具有独特气味，口感清香，入菜特别美味，烹煮方式也相当多元。如果夏季食欲不振，将蔬菜做一点变化，做一份清爽鲜甜的手卷也是不错的选择。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>松菇加州卷</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B3%E6%9D%BE%E8%8F%87.md#1">柳松菇</a>味道清香，菇柄脆嫩爽口，沾附面糊炸至酥脆后卷入酸甜醋饭中，再搭配富有天然油脂的<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>，营养又健康。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【食材】</span></h3>
<p>柳松菇 1朵<br />
酪梨（切薄片） 1颗<br />
小黄瓜（切条） 1条<br />
红萝卜（切条） 1条<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%9D%E8%94%93%E7%94%9F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">萝蔓生菜</a> 2片<br />
腌渍嫩姜片 适量<br />
海苔 1张<br />
芝麻 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>饭】</span></h3>
<p>米 1杯<br />
水 1杯<br />
昆布 1片<br />
白醋 150c.c.<br />
糖 100g<br />
盐 50g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味料】</span></h3>
<p>酥炸粉（或面粉） 4大匙<br />
美乃滋 适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12943174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943174-P.134.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943174-P.134-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943174" class="wp-caption-text"><span style="color: #000000;">制作酪梨松菇加州卷的材料与调味料</span>。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 昆布先刷洗过后，取锅放入米、水（两者比例1:1）以及昆布，放到电锅中，外锅倒2杯水，蒸30分钟。</p>
<p>2. 在锅中放入醋、糖、盐、以及作法1中蒸过的昆布，用小火一边煮一边搅拌到颗粒完全溶解，完成<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">寿司</a>醋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12943179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943179" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943179-P.136-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943179-P.136-1-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943179" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中放入醋、糖、盐。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943180-P.136-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943180-P.136-2-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943180" class="wp-caption-text">放入蒸过的昆布，用小火一边煮一边搅拌到颗粒完全溶解，完成寿司醋。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将煮好的寿司醋分次加到蒸好的米饭中，一边以切拌方式拌匀一边试味道，调成喜欢的风味，即制成寿司饭。</p>
<h3>T.I.P 建议趁热时将寿司醋拌入饭中，可加速吸收。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12943181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943181" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943181-P.136-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943181-P.136-3-450x377.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943181" class="wp-caption-text">将煮好的寿司醋分次加到蒸好的米饭中，一边以切拌方式拌匀一边试味道。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将酥炸粉拌入适量的水，调制成面糊备用。准备一锅浅油，将柳松菇均匀沾裹面糊后下锅油炸，尽量炸干一点后捞起沥油。</p>
<h3>T.I.P 面糊沾裹厚一点，可以形成酥脆的口感。或者用手沾一点面糊丢到油锅中炸，即可制成面衣。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12943257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943257-P.137-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943257-P.137-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943257" class="wp-caption-text">将酥炸粉拌入适量的水，调制成面糊备用。准备一锅浅油，将柳松菇均匀沾裹面糊。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943258-P.137-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943258-P.137-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943258" class="wp-caption-text">柳松菇均匀沾裹面糊后下锅油炸，尽量炸干一点后捞起沥油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943259-P.137-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943259-P.137-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943259" class="wp-caption-text">柳松菇尽量炸干一点后捞起沥油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 寿司竹帘上先铺保鲜膜，再摆上海苔片（粗糙面向上），把寿司饭均匀铺到海苔的3/4左右后，翻面。</p>
<h3>T.I.P 海苔上的饭不要铺满，以免卷起后米饭溢出。铺的时候饭要铺齐海苔两端，卷好时厚度才会均匀。可先在手上沾一点醋水避免黏手。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12943260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943260-P.137-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943260-P.137-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943260" class="wp-caption-text">寿司竹帘上先铺保鲜膜，再摆上海苔片（粗糙面向上），把寿司饭均匀铺到海苔的3/4左右。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 在海苔中间位置挤一条美乃滋后，放上柳松菇、红萝卜条、小黄瓜条、姜片、<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%9D%E8%94%93%E7%94%9F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">萝蔓生菜</a>，再从下往上卷起来塑形。</p>
<h3>T.I.P 一边卷一边压实，做好的寿司才不易松开。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12943261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943261-P.137-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943261-P.137-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943261" class="wp-caption-text">寿司饭均匀铺到海苔的3/4左右后翻面，在海苔中间位置挤一条美乃滋。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943264" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943264-P.137-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943264-P.137-6-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943264" class="wp-caption-text">在海苔上面放上柳松菇、红萝卜条、小黄瓜条、姜片、萝蔓生菜。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943265-P.137-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943265-P.137-7-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943265" class="wp-caption-text">将寿司饭从下往上卷起来塑形。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943266" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943266-P.137-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943266-P.137-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943266" class="wp-caption-text">寿司饭卷好的形状。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着在桌上铺一层保鲜膜，先摆一排酪梨片，再接着放上<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8%E5%8D%B7.md#1">寿司卷</a>后，用保鲜膜卷起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12943267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943267-P.137-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943267-P.137-9-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943267" class="wp-caption-text">在桌上铺一层保鲜膜，先摆一排酪梨片，再放上<ahref="https://github.com/reqcja3941/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8%E5%8D%B7.md#1">寿司卷</a>。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12943268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12943268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943268-P.137-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12943268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12943268-P.137-10-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12943268" class="wp-caption-text">用保鲜膜卷起来。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将寿司卷切片后，再拆开保鲜膜，并撒上芝麻即完成。</p>
