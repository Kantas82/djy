<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>王妍霞一生忠党爱国  追思告别式党旗覆棺表彰</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975641-IMG_6690-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>徐炳勋（右），陈奕芳（左）及侨团代表出席追悼会吊祭。（孙玉玟／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年05月26日讯】（大纪元记者卢淑君休斯顿报导）侨务促进委员<ahref="https://github.com/ctafrp314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%A6%8D%E9%9C%9E.md#1">王妍霞</a>女士，在与新冠病魔斗争五个月后，于5月7日因多器官衰竭于医院离世，享寿70岁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12975644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12975644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975644-IMG_6693-e1621990181973.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12975644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975644-IMG_6693-600x569.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="569" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12975644" class="wp-caption-text">徐炳勋（右），陈奕芳（左）及侨团代表出席追悼会吊祭。（孙玉玟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>追悼会于5月23日（星期日）上午9时至11时，在永福殡仪馆举行。因疫情持续，亲友、侨团派代表前往吊祭之外，以线上直播海外亲友送最后一程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12975639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12975639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975639-IMG_6675-e1621990306839.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12975639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975639-IMG_6675-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12975639" class="wp-caption-text">国民党<ahref="https://github.com/ctafrp314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%9A%E6%97%97%E8%A6%86%E6%A3%BA.md#1">党旗覆棺</a>。（孙玉玟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天治丧委员会主委唐心琴和何怡中等协助现场布置以温馨、庄严又有温度的纯白色。除了美国主流政要，亲友及各工商界的挽联、花圈、花篮等，来自台湾国内的政要及国民党政要员等，包括前总统马英九、前副总统吴敦义、市长韩国瑜、国民党党主席江启臣、立委蒋万安、温玉霞、赖士葆等人送上挽联致意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12975642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12975642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975642-IMG_6691-e1621990222967.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12975642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975642-IMG_6691-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12975642" class="wp-caption-text">孙玉玟（右）代表美南分会致赠水晶奖座。（孙玉玟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12975643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12975643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975643-IMG_6692-e1621990248793.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12975643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975643-IMG_6692-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12975643" class="wp-caption-text">黎淑瑛（右）代表世华的总会长陈阡蕙颁赠奖杯。（孙玉玟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处经济组组长徐炳勋、侨教中心主任陈奕芳宣读侨委会的吊唁文；各社团代表致词并颁赠奖杯及奖状由<ahref="https://github.com/ctafrp314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%A6%8D%E9%9C%9E.md#1">王妍霞</a>的夫婿朱江表接受。分别有世华工商妇女会美南分会会长孙玉玟、名誉总会长黎淑瑛，代表世华的总会长陈阡蕙颁赠奖杯，华侨救国总会美南办事处代表李迎霞，台湾同乡联谊会会长冯启丰，糖城台湾同乡联谊会会长陈焕泽，美南艺文社社长唐心琴等，王妍霞生前的好友陈逸玲、文丽、戴医师、成彦邦、陈紫薇夫妇等皆出席送好友最后一程、致哀悼之情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12975638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12975638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975638-IMG_6653-e1621990337643.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12975638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12975638-IMG_6653-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12975638" class="wp-caption-text">夫婿朱江和其女儿向王妍霞做最后道别。（孙玉玟／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后，国民党美南支部常委甘幼蘋宣读中国国民党的“覆棺证书”，在国歌歌曲演奏之下，由中国国民党休士顿分部常委唐心琴，率领代表们进行国民党<ahref="https://github.com/ctafrp314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%9A%E6%97%97%E8%A6%86%E6%A3%BA.md#1">党旗覆棺</a>仪式，场面庄严隆重。王妍霞的棺木在家属夫婿朱江和其女儿，侨领代表和侨界好友们护送之下，随即火化。待疫情过后将骨灰移回台湾安葬。</p>
