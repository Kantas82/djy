<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>恒丰银行23周年行庆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959559-image0-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>恒丰银行董事长吴文龙（前排右三）和董事团队在23周年庆晚宴上合影。
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年05月19日讯】（大纪元记者卢淑君休斯顿报导）<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%92%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">恒丰银行</a>为庆祝成立<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/23%E5%91%A8%E5%B9%B4.md#1">23周年</a>之<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%8C%E5%BA%86.md#1">行庆</a>于5月14日（星期五）晚间于Pops Bros, Steakhouse举行周年庆晚餐，接着5月16日（星期日）举办行23周年庆高尔夫球赛，该银行在积极抵抗疫情，逆势而上获利稳定成长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12959563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959563-aa99715723f516e0320719164253af79-e1621396410313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959563-aa99715723f516e0320719164253af79-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959563" class="wp-caption-text">董事长吴文龙（右五）和罗复文处长（右四）及<ahref="https://github.com/hhpibc3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%81%92%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">恒丰银行</a>董事们及团队合影。 (卢淑君／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12959562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959562-7e3221e128dfafa59cf514e7c6efc6c9-e1621396461722.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959562-7e3221e128dfafa59cf514e7c6efc6c9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959562" class="wp-caption-text">侨领骆健明，叶宏志（左一、二），恒丰银行公关经理周明洁，总经理张永男（右一、二）和各界嘉宾合影。 (卢淑君／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>16日当天持续下雨，气象局于前日报导会有40-80%可能下雨。但是，参加的球友风雨无阻，当日比赛有142报名几乎全数出席，董事长吴文龙表示，恒丰银行自1998年开业以来，每年都举办高尔夫球赛，以球会友，健康休闲活动促进友谊，几十年来凝聚一群高球战友，虽然当天下雨仍准时赴赛，情谊坚定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12959565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959565-80ebf044df3788ed6378b801df6fa560-e1621396522890.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959565" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959565-80ebf044df3788ed6378b801df6fa560-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959565" class="wp-caption-text">Nguyen Trac BA（左），吴文龙（中）和罗复文合影。 (卢淑君／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12959569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959569-5d16c41af691cd2fb7dab1f7f0b61046-e1621396569917.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959569-5d16c41af691cd2fb7dab1f7f0b61046-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959569" class="wp-caption-text">吴文龙（右二）和球友撑伞在球场准备开打前合影。</figcaption></figure>
<p>台北经文处罗复文处长，副董事长高成渊，总经理张永男，台塑副总吴尧明，味全经理李乐罡及越南驻休士顿总领事Nguyen Trac BA，另外还有远从达拉斯赶来祝贺的，前台商会会长陈世修及各界嘉宾陆续报到并寒暄之后，大家撑伞前往球场，不畏风雨热烈喜庆开打。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12959561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959561-image1-e1621396637223.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959561-image1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959561" class="wp-caption-text">董事长吴文龙与夫人及孙子女合影。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12959571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12959571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959571-e7e62c4e8a132c59a8f55f882d973aca-e1621396972665.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12959571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12959571-e7e62c4e8a132c59a8f55f882d973aca-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12959571" class="wp-caption-text">董事长吴文龙加码现金$999.99由Thomas Lee 抽中并合影。</figcaption></figure>
<p>球赛在进行一小时左右，天空不做美开始出现雷声，吴文龙邀请所有人回到二楼宴会廰，品尝美食和调酒，并进行最高潮的现金抽奖，恒丰银行准备了超过万元的现金进行抽奖，所有参赛人员人人有奖，最大奖现金$888.88由Jackie O Cheong抽到，董事长吴文龙再加码现金$999.99由Thomas Lee抽中。</p>
