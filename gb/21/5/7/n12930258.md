<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>九十岁阿嬷初体验 四结福德庙庆祝母亲节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930260-496782-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>五结乡四结福德庙埕举行一次别开生面（敷面膜），庆祝母亲节活动。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月07日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）宜兰县五结乡四结福德庙管理委员会为感谢诵经团、义女、志工为庙宇付出，于5月7日<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E4%BA%B2%E8%8A%82.md#1">母亲节</a>前夕，联合上四村长办公处，邀请在地婆婆、妈妈在四结福德庙埕举行一次别开生面（敷面膜），赠送化妆品庆祝母亲节活动。</p>
<p>“福德庙数位九十岁志工阿嬷，听闻化妆品公司，将要提供面膜给来参加庆祝活动的婆婆、妈妈体验，都娇羞的表示，这是我人生第一次敷面膜初体验。”四结福德庙主任委员高志肇表示，为庆祝<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E4%BA%B2%E8%8A%82.md#1">母亲节</a>，邀请五结乡上四村婆婆、妈妈及为庙宇付出的诵经团、义女、志工在庙埕切蛋糕、敷面膜、发康乃馨、赠送化妆品等欢渡母亲节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12930261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12930261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930261-496783.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12930261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930261-496783-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12930261" class="wp-caption-text">高龄九十八岁林阿款阿嬷代表切蛋糕。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12930264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12930264" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930264-496786.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12930264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930264-496786-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12930264" class="wp-caption-text">林茂辉董事长为阿嬷敷面膜。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12930263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12930263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930263-496785.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12930263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930263-496785-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12930263" class="wp-caption-text">年轻的妈妈为阿嬷敷面膜。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>提供面膜的东春化妆品公司林茂辉董事长，也到场祝贺社区婆婆妈妈母亲节快乐。林茂辉表示，今天对很多八、九十岁以上的妈妈，敷面膜可能是人生初体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12930265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12930265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930265-496787.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12930265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930265-496787-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12930265" class="wp-caption-text">四结福德庙主任委员高志肇逐一发送康乃馨给婆婆、妈妈们。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12930262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12930262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930262-496784.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12930262" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12930262-496784-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12930262" class="wp-caption-text">高龄九十八岁林阿款（前排中）阿嬷使用珍珠膏近60年了。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈荣楷总干事表示，林茂辉董事长为四结福德庙虔诚信徒，听闻本庙要办理母亲节活动，向来热心公益的林董事长，慨然答应提供一百份化妆品（包含:保湿面膜、珍珠膏、香水洗发精、蜗牛水凝胶.抗菌液态皂..等），给社区婆婆妈妈当母亲节礼物。陈荣楷表示，还有东春化妆品公司也提供行销国内外五十余年久富盛名的“丽人白蓉霜”（珍珠霜）给大家体验。</p>
