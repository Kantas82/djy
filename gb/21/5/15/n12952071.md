<p>三头娶不上媳妇的单身狮大概有自己的情报网，得知狮老爷与狮太太度蜜月去了，想趁着这机会登堂入室夺人妻女。它们目标明确，目不斜视，大摇大摆的从我们身边走过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952100-20210515_africa_helen_chou_606.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952100-20210515_africa_helen_chou_606-600x455.jpg" alt="非洲, 狮子" width="600" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952100" class="wp-caption-text">身强力壮的雄狮。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>这三只雄狮大概也知道，就这么想让留守的狮姨太太们带着娃跟它们私奔几乎是不可能的。但趁老爷不在家过来撩拨撩拨挖挖墙角总可以吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952116" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952116-20210515_africa_helen_chou_608.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952116-20210515_africa_helen_chou_608-600x401.jpg" alt="非洲, 狮子" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952116" class="wp-caption-text">撒尿划地盘的雄狮。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12952123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952123-20210515_africa_helen_chou_609.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952123-20210515_africa_helen_chou_609-600x403.jpg" alt="非洲, 狮子" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952123" class="wp-caption-text">对游客视而不见的雄狮。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>它们先是直接撒泡尿划地盘，然后如入无狮之境般的在这群狮子的领地上嬉戏，热情四射秀秀肌肉。等火候差不多了就扬长而去，在附近暗戳戳的等待蜜月归来精疲力尽的老爷。这可是现实版的“趁你弱，要你命”啊！一场腥风血雨的江湖格杀即在眼前。</p>
<p>我们虽然没有目睹狮老爷与年富力强的单身狮之间的遭遇战，但其惨烈程度可以想像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952145-20210515_africa_helen_chou_611.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952145-20210515_africa_helen_chou_611-600x387.jpg" alt="非洲" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952145" class="wp-caption-text">进餐的斑鬣狗。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这群狮子不远处我们目睹了一群斑鬣狗饱餐狮子们吃剩下的猎物。</p>
<p>火山口盆地里还有不少难得一见的鸟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952148" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952148-20210515_africa_helen_chou_612.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12952148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952148-20210515_africa_helen_chou_612-450x674.jpg" alt="灰冠鹤" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952148" class="wp-caption-text">灰冠鹤(Crown Crane)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>优雅漂亮的灰冠鹤也是乌干达的国鸟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952155" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952155-20210515_africa_helen_chou_613.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12952155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952155-20210515_africa_helen_chou_613-450x674.jpg" alt="蛇鹫" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952155" class="wp-caption-text">蛇鹫(Secretarybird)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_12952159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952159" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952159-20210515_africa_helen_chou_614.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12952159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952159-20210515_africa_helen_chou_614-450x300.jpg" alt="灰颈鹭鸨" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952159" class="wp-caption-text">灰颈鹭鸨(Kori Bustard)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12952166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952166-20210515_africa_helen_chou_615.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952166-20210515_africa_helen_chou_615-600x372.jpg" alt="红脸地犀鸟" width="600" b="372" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952166" class="wp-caption-text">红脸地犀鸟(Southern Ground Hornbill)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_12952170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952170-20210515_africa_helen_chou_616.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952170-20210515_africa_helen_chou_616-600x399.jpg" alt="珠鸡" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952170" class="wp-caption-text">珠鸡(Guineafowl)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这珠鸡颜值明显就高了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12952174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12952174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952174-20210515_africa_helen_chou_617.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12952174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12952174-20210515_africa_helen_chou_617-600x401.jpg" alt="非洲长尾鵙" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12952174" class="wp-caption-text">非洲长尾鵙(Magpie Shrike)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有点像喜鹊的白肩鹊鵙，也叫“非洲长尾鵙”。</p>
<p>据说有2万5千多头动物生活栖息在恩戈罗恩戈罗自然保护区。其中黑犀牛就有24头左右。我们远远的看到了4头。这下非洲大陆的五大猛兽：狮子，大象，非洲水牛，金钱豹和犀牛，我们都看到了！</p>
<p>至此，我们的首次Safari圆满吿一段落。</p>
