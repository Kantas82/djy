<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>下厨疗愈身心 云朵莓果披萨DIY缓解焦虑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945754-P.51-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>颜值高又美味的披萨，使用蓝莓、橄榄油等食材，营养健康又美味。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：近期疫情严峻，很多人都出现慌张焦虑的情绪，但是影响了心情对身体的免疫力未必有帮助，转移一下注意力，让我们来<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E5%8E%A8.md#1">下厨</a>疗愈身心。</strong></p>
<p><strong>不用上咖啡馆自己也可以泡咖啡、搭配香喷喷又精致的<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a>餐，只要跟着文青私厨一步一步的制作保证不出错，披萨配料众多，有起司丝、<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8E%93.md#1">蓝莓</a>又搭配<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E6%B2%B9.md#1">橄榄油</a>，健康美味，很值得尝试！</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">云朵焦糖莓果<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【饼皮材料】</span></h3>
<p>高筋面粉 120g<br />
盐 2g<br />
糖 5g<br />
干酵母 3g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E6%B2%B9.md#1">橄榄油</a> 1大匙<br />
温水 75c.c.</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【配料】</span></h3>
<p>起司丝 30g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8E%93.md#1">蓝莓</a> 适量<br />
棉花糖 适量<br />
薄荷叶 2片</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【酱汁】</span></h3>
<p>奶油 30g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a> 30g<br />
糖 50g<br />
盐 1大匙<br />
鲜奶油 90c.c.</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945753-P.50.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945753-P.50-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945753" class="wp-caption-text">制作材料、酱汁和配料。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 准备一个大调理盆放入面粉，将干酵母先加少许的水调开后倒入面粉中，并加入盐与糖。慢慢分次倒入30-40?C左右的温水，将面粉搓揉成团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945755" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945755-P.52-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945755-P.52-1-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945755" class="wp-caption-text">准备一个大调理盆放入面粉，将干酵母先加少许的水调开后倒入面粉中。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945756-P.52-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945756-P.52-2-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945756" class="wp-caption-text">分次倒入30-40?C左右的温水，将面粉搓揉成团。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 接着加入橄榄油，揉至面团表面成光滑状态后，把面团往底部收拢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945757-P.52-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945757-P.52-3-450x379.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945757" class="wp-caption-text">加入橄榄油。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945758" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945758-P.52-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945758-P.52-4-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945758" class="wp-caption-text">揉至面团表面成光滑状态后，把面团往底部收拢。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945759" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945759-P.52-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945759-P.52-5-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945759" class="wp-caption-text">把面团往底部收拢。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将揉好的面团放置到玻璃碗内，盖上保鲜膜静置40分钟使其发酵。待面团发酵膨胀到原本的一倍大后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945760" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945760-P.52-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945760-P.52-6-450x378.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945760" class="wp-caption-text">揉好的面团放置到玻璃碗内，盖上保鲜膜静置40分钟使其发酵。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 在台面撒上手粉后放上面团，再均匀撒上手粉，拿起面团用手拉开后，再用手指撑开面皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945774-P.53-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945774-P.53-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945774" class="wp-caption-text">在台面撒上手粉后放上面团，再均匀撒上手粉。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945776" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945776-P.53-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945776-P.53-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945776" class="wp-caption-text">面团用手拉开后。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945777-P.53-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945777-P.53-3-450x341.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945777" class="wp-caption-text">用手指撑开面皮。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945778" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945778-P.53-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945778-P.53-4-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945778" class="wp-caption-text">用手指撑开面皮。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用叉子在面皮上搓洞后，放入预热好的烤箱，以220?C烤8-10分钟至金黄上色，即制作成披萨皮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945779" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945779-P.53-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945779-P.53-5-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945779" class="wp-caption-text">用叉子在面皮上搓洞后。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将奶油、<ahref="https://github.com/cenvmt335/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>、糖、盐入锅煮成焦糖色，再放入鲜奶油拌匀，即完成酱汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945780-P.53-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945780-P.53-6-450x340.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="340" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945780" class="wp-caption-text">将奶油、蜂蜜、糖、盐入锅煮成焦糖色。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945781-P.53-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945781-P.53-7-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945781" class="wp-caption-text">放入鲜奶油拌匀，即完成酱汁。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 在披萨皮上先涂抹酱汁，再撒上起司丝、棉花糖、蓝莓，放入烤箱回烤5分钟，最后以薄荷叶装饰即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12945782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945782-P.53-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945782-P.53-8-450x336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945782" class="wp-caption-text">在披萨皮上先涂抹酱汁。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945783" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945783-P.53-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945783-P.53-9-450x339.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945783" class="wp-caption-text">撒上起司丝。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945784-P.53-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945784-P.53-10-450x394.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945784" class="wp-caption-text">撒上棉花糖。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12945785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12945785" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945785-P.53-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12945785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945785-P.53-11-450x395.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12945785" class="wp-caption-text">撒上蓝莓，放入烤箱回烤5分钟，最后以薄荷叶装饰即完成。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
