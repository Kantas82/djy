<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>古树被伐 也许不是出于安全原因</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919704-IMG_6505-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>工人在尔湾市Bryan Ave的路旁伐树。（李梅／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年05月03日讯】（大纪元记者李梅橙县报导）4月底的最后一周，<ahref="https://github.com/abqqad3453/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%96%AF%E7%A7%91%E7%89%B9%E6%A0%91%E6%9C%A8%E6%9C%8D%E5%8A%A1%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8.md#1">大斯科特树木服务公司</a>（Great Scott Tree Service Inc.）的工人在<ahref="https://github.com/abqqad3453/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%94%E6%B9%BE%E5%B8%82.md#1">尔湾市</a>Bryan Ave路旁锯树，那些是尔湾市保留下来的不多的一行高大挺拔的古树，树皮平滑，白色带点棕色，大部分树高15米左右，原产地澳洲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12919706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919706-IMG_6510-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919706-IMG_6510-1-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919706" class="wp-caption-text">工人在<ahref="https://github.com/abqqad3453/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%94%E6%B9%BE%E5%B8%82.md#1">尔湾市</a>Bryan Ave的路旁伐树。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>工人坐在大吊车的车斗里，一段一段地锯着其中一棵大树。铲车再把锯下的大段树干送入拖车里。这种来自澳洲的树很少见到了，管理人员说，树龄大概有几十年了，因为不安全，所以要锯掉；然后补种上其它种类的树，因为这种树已经很难找到了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12919708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919708-IMG_6514.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919708-IMG_6514-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919708" class="wp-caption-text">工人在尔湾市Bryan Ave的路旁伐树。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12919710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919710-IMG_6524.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919710-IMG_6524-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919710" class="wp-caption-text">工人在尔湾市Bryan Ave的路旁伐树。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12919717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919717" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919717-IMG_6536.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919717-IMG_6536-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919717" class="wp-caption-text">工人在踞树。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据谷歌搜索，这种树为<ahref="https://github.com/abqqad3453/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E6%A1%89%E6%A0%91.md#1">澳洲桉树</a>（Corymbia torelliana，俗名Cadaghi或Cadaga），树高可达25-30米，树龄约在50-150年之间，如果没经人工修剪，会长成高大的球状，并且不容易倒伏。按照澳洲《昆士兰州政府1992年自然保护法》，桉树被列为“最不需要担心的”树。</p>
<p>市管理人员说，他们请了第三方公司对树进行了检测，发现树的中间或根部已经受损，如附图出现彩色部分就表示树中心已经不行了。不过，那些从上往下锯下来的每截树段，目视看上去都很好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12919711" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919711" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919711-IMG_6519.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919711" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919711-IMG_6519-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919711" class="wp-caption-text">树木检测截面示意图。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在锯树最后阶段，一名工人拿着长电锯水平地绕着树干底部锯了一圈，并且间隔地插上细枝，之后经铲车轻轻一带，这段树就掉下来了。这个树的根部直径最大处约有1米半，在离地只有吋左右的根部截面上没看到损伤（见附图）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12919713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919713-IMG_6556.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919713-IMG_6556-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919713" class="wp-caption-text">从截面看树根处没有损伤。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12919721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12919721" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919721-IMG_6560.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12919721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12919721-IMG_6560-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12919721" class="wp-caption-text">工人站在树根处。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>即使树真的有损害，难道除了伐树就没有别的方法治理或加固吗？管理人员说：“如果树倒了，砸到人或造成财务损失，那政府的责任就大了。”</p>
<p>周末沿着Bryan Ave路旁走，看到六、七棵树的树根，都没有太大的损伤；前两天看到的大树根截面上被戳下了两小块，并且出现从中心向外扩散的裂纹。◇</p>
