<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>厚红茶吐司～独家配方比外面卖的还好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984241-P.86-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>比外面卖的还好吃的红茶吐司。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>我很喜欢红茶的香气和味道，但市面上的<ahref="https://github.com/mmmcpr3473/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">红茶吐司</a>总是没有我想要的浓郁感。为了成功做出理想中的红茶<ahref="https://github.com/mmmcpr3473/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">吐司</a>，我花费很长的时间研究、测试、调整比例，<br />
才完成这款茶香醇浓的厚<ahref="https://github.com/mmmcpr3473/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">红茶吐司</a>，想要茶味淡一点的人，自己减少茶叶量即可哦！</p>
<p>【份量】</p>
<p>3个多功能烤模</p>
<p>【材料】</p>
<p>高筋面粉 400g<br />
盐 7g<br />
速发干酵母（金*） 6g<br />
砂糖 30g<br />
脱脂奶粉 8g<br />
水 164g<br />
无盐奶油 25g<br />
牛奶 180g<br />
伯爵茶叶（使用茶包或茶叶末） 18g<br />
刷液：全蛋液（全蛋10：水1） 适量<br />
*速发干酵母（金）为法国燕子牌强力即发酵母（高糖）。</p>
<p>【搅拌时间】</p>
<p>依实际情况调整，约为（面团勾）低速3-5分钟／中速6-8分钟</p>
<p>【面团温度】 28°C</p>
<p>【水温】（以使用搅拌机为例）<br />
夏天0-5°C<br />
春、秋天5-10°C<br />
冬天10-15°C</p>
<p>【烤箱】</p>
<p>第二次发酵时预热至170°C</p>
<p>【How To Make】</p>
<h3>制作红茶液</h3>
<p>1. 在锅中放入牛奶和茶叶，煮到微滚。<br />
tip 伯爵茶建议使用茶包内已经打碎的茶叶，如果是比较大片的伯爵茶叶，必须先以搅拌机打成细碎状，之后添加到<ahref="https://github.com/mmmcpr3473/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">吐司</a>里才不会影响口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984199" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984199-P.88-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984199-P.88-1-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984199" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 沸腾后转小火，让茶叶浸泡2分钟。<br />
tip 牛奶在高温久煮下容易变质，煮到微滚后就要转小火，也不要煮太久。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984201" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984201-P.88-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984201-P.88-2-300x325.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984201" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 过滤出120g的红茶液，并保留一半煮过的碎茶叶备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984207" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984207-P.88-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984207-P.88-3-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984207" class="wp-caption-text">3. 过滤出120g的红茶液，并保留一半煮过的碎茶叶备用。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>制作面团</h3>
<p>4. 将无盐奶油和红茶叶之外的所有材料（包含红茶液）放入盆中，用面团勾以低速搅拌3-5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984223" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984223-P.88-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984223-P.88-4-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984223" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 加入无盐奶油后，先以低速稍微搅拌均匀，再转中速搅拌6-8分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984226" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984226-P.88-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984226-P.88-5-300x325.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984226" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入红茶叶，以低速搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984228" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984228-P.88-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984228-P.88-6-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984228" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 取一小块面团出来，若可拉出透光、光滑薄膜，表示面筋已顺利成形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984230" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984230-P.89-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984230-P.89-1-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984230" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 面团滚圆至表面光滑后，铺上保鲜膜或拧干的湿布预防面团干掉，静置70-80分钟，进行第一次发酵。</p>
<p>tip 请参考“确认发酵状态”的章节。发酵时间需要根据室温环境或面团温度调整。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984231" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984231-P.89-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984231-P.89-2-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984231" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 将面团分割成每团125g后分别滚圆（共6颗），用保鲜膜或拧干的湿布盖起来预防面团干燥，并静置20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984235" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984235-P.89-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984235-P.89-3-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984235" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 将面团拍一拍排气后再次滚圆整形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984238" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984238-P.89-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984238-P.89-4-300x325.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984238" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 将面团光滑面朝上摆入吐司烤模，盖上保鲜膜或拧干的湿布预防面团干燥，进行第二次发酵。等面团膨胀到高出吐司烤模1公分左右时，即发酵完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984239" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984239-P.89-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984239-P.89-5-300x325.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984239" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 在表面薄薄刷上一层全蛋液后，放入预热至170°C的烤箱，以170°C烘烤18-20分钟，取出后脱模。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12984240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12984240" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984240-P.89-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12984240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12984240-P.89-6-300x324.jpg" alt="吐司" width="300" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12984240" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
