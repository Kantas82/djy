<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>防疫优先  税务纾困持续相挺</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955891-498437-600x400.png" />
<h6>	缴税困难可延期或分期。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月17日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）因应疫情严峻，5/15至5/28全国休闲娱乐场所应关闭，全民<ahref="https://github.com/ntrwbs3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>，各行各业受到冲击，嘉义市政府财政税务局将主动协助店家朋友，能直接减免就主动进行，需要申请也会主动辅导，帮忙减轻税务上的负担，并提供下列<ahref="https://github.com/ntrwbs3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8E%E5%8A%A1%E7%BA%BE%E5%9B%B0.md#1">税务纾困</a>措施，陪民众<ahref="https://github.com/ntrwbs3406/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>一起度难关。</p>
<figure id="12955887" aria-describedby="caption-12955887" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955887-498434-450x450.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955887-498434-450x450.png" alt=" 娱乐业者可减娱乐税。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12955887" class="wp-caption-text">娱乐业者可减娱乐税。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一、娱乐业者可减娱乐税：（一）受疫情影响之查定课征娱乐业者，如娱乐设施未使用，可申请依实际营业概况，核实减征娱乐税。（二）针对5/15-5/28应关闭的全国休闲娱乐场所等，该局依主管机关提供清册，主动停征娱乐税。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12955888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12955888" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955888-498435.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12955888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955888-498435-450x450.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12955888" class="wp-caption-text">营业场所可减房屋税。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二、营业场所可减房屋税：（一）饭店未使用楼层、营利事业停业或营业面积缩减，可申请适用较低的非住家非营业用房屋税率2%。（二）针对5/15-5/28应关闭的全国休闲娱乐场所等，该局依主管机关提供清册，主动调降房屋税率。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12955890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12955890" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955890-498436.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12955890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955890-498436-450x450.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12955890" class="wp-caption-text">车辆停用免牌照税。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三、车辆停用免牌照税：受疫情影响，车辆暂时不使用，可向监理机关办理停驶，车辆停用期间，免征使用牌照税。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12955891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12955891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955891-498437.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12955891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12955891-498437-450x450.png" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12955891" class="wp-caption-text">缴税困难可延期或分期。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>四、缴税困难可延/分期:（一）民众受疫情影响被居家隔离或确诊、检疫，致不能如期缴纳房屋税，可于缴纳期间内向该局申请延期或分期缴纳，不必事前申请。（二）展期届满时仍接受隔离治疗，自隔离治疗结束之次日起再展延20日。</p>
<p>如有相关疑问，请拨打该局免付费电话0800-536969或纳税服务科05-2224371转分机160~162），将有专人为您说明。</p>
