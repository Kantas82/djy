<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罗复文布达休士顿地区侨务委员任命令</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989144-731787d5ae66a032ff8a00393a86673c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗复文（左五）偕办事处同仁与侨务委员合影。（图／TECO提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月01日讯】（休斯顿讯）驻<ahref="https://github.com/ofwhjp3411/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%91%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">休士顿</a>台北经文处在5月27日（星期四）上午举办休士顿地区<ahref="https://github.com/ofwhjp3411/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%8A%A1%E5%A7%94%E5%91%98.md#1">侨务委员</a><ahref="https://github.com/ofwhjp3411/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%BB%E5%91%BD%E4%BB%A4.md#1">任命令</a>布达典礼，处长罗复文代表颁发侨务委员任命令予休士顿地区续任及新任侨务委员张世勋、刘秀美及陈建宾。组长何仁杰、张家华、副领事陈伟玲、侨教中心主任陈奕芳、副主任杨容清及台湾书院副主任陈譓如特到场观礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12989143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12989143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989143-7e03799e1ac746da6dd19af7ce7cb887-e1622482290622.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12989143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989143-7e03799e1ac746da6dd19af7ce7cb887-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12989143" class="wp-caption-text">罗复文颁发张世勋（左）务委员<ahref="https://github.com/ofwhjp3411/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%BB%E5%91%BD%E4%BB%A4.md#1">任命令</a>。（图／TECO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗复文祝贺三位<ahref="https://github.com/ofwhjp3411/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%8A%A1%E5%A7%94%E5%91%98.md#1">侨务委员</a>获蔡英文总统任命，肯定三位委员对侨社的贡献，并期许任内与经文处携手合作，凝聚侨胞向心，协助政府推动国民外交，促进台美双边交流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12989145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12989145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989145-4e03c8fce62201cdecbf8b205fbcf323-e1622482320557.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12989145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989145-4e03c8fce62201cdecbf8b205fbcf323-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12989145" class="wp-caption-text">罗复文颁发刘秀美（左）侨务委员任命令。（图／TECO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>张世勋分享担任文教中心台湾文化导览志工心得，并表示任内将持续与导览团队合作，行销优质台湾文化。刘秀美表示任内将团结传统侨社支持政府，并将积极参与公共事务，让主流社会看到台湾的美。陈建宾盼与各社团合作，落实台湾节，提升台湾能见度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12989146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12989146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989146-2feb8aa3c736c8bbcf7a4c091fee6141-e1622482348460.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12989146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12989146-2feb8aa3c736c8bbcf7a4c091fee6141-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12989146" class="wp-caption-text">罗复文颁发陈建宾（左）侨务委员任命令。（图／TECO提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼结束后，罗复文与三位侨务委员就未来业务推动交换意见，并致赠具台湾特色伴手礼，三位委员均承诺任内将竭心尽力，为台湾打拼。</p>
