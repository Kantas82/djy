<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>疫情期间订购外卖送餐 不要选这12种食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1902061641102483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>疫情期间，更多人在家中待的时间变长，购物习惯也发生很大变化。特别在封锁期间，网购成为一种主要方式。在饮食方面，除了自己在家做饭增加外，网购或电话订购外卖送餐服务也大幅增加。(fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年05月05日讯】（大纪元记者夏雨综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/hsvbvd398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>期间，更多人在家中待的时间变长，购物习惯也发生很大变化。特别在封锁期间，网购成为一种主要方式。在饮食方面，除了自己在家做饭增加外，网购或电话订购<ahref="https://github.com/hsvbvd398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E5%8D%96.md#1">外卖</a><ahref="https://github.com/hsvbvd398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%81%E9%A4%90.md#1">送餐</a>服务也大幅增加，但并非每种食物都适合<ahref="https://github.com/hsvbvd398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E5%8D%96.md#1">外卖</a>送餐。</p>
<p>在忙碌了一天回家后，发现冰箱空了，又不想再外出用餐，外卖<ahref="https://github.com/hsvbvd398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%81%E9%A4%90.md#1">送餐</a>是现代社会的便捷选择；尤其是目前瘟疫大流行持续期间，很多餐馆禁止堂食，只提供外卖服务，且餐点选择范围已超越了标准的比萨饼或中餐传统外卖。</p>
<p>但是，在订购食物时可能需要三思而后行。24/7 Tempo网站审查了健康和营养方面的资料，以及餐馆员工的建议，整理了一份订购餐点外卖尽量不要选择的食品清单。</p>
<p>在编制这份清单时，24/7 Tempo考虑到送餐途中食品变质风险，或者食品若无法长时间放在冷藏容器中会造成的健康风险，以及食品本身的健康和营养资料等因素。</p>
<p>以下是基于24/7 Tempo这份订购外卖送餐，建议不要选择的食品清单中较为常见的12种食品清单。</p>
<h2>1. 寿司料理</h2>
<p>送餐的寿司中的鱼经常被错误地贴上标签，而且寿司若在室温下放置太长时间也会危害健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12821398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12821398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/sushi-354628_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/sushi-354628_1920-600x400.jpg" alt="寿司" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12821398" class="wp-caption-text">图为寿司与生鱼片，与本文无关。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>2. 蛋</h2>
<p>快餐店和外送店的鸡蛋大多可能是用微波炉加热，而不是用锅子煎炸或煮熟的鸡蛋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12128577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12128577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/fried-18967_1280.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12128577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/fried-18967_1280-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12128577" class="wp-caption-text">图为普通煎蛋，与本文无关。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>3. 牛扒（Steak)</h2>
<p>吃牛扒的最享受的是先咬一口，感觉牛扒边缘酥脆，脂肪在嘴里融化。但如果你订购牛扒外卖，将无法体验这种感觉。牛扒的肉可能会不冷不热，耐嚼，脂肪也会凝结。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11580619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11580619" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/steak-3805771_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11580619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/steak-3805771_1920-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11580619" class="wp-caption-text">牛扒示意图。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>4. 冰沙（Smoothies）</h2>
<p>冰淇淋店不送货是有原因的。冰沙不仅融化迅速，而且也不是健康饮品选择。</p>
<p>冰沙通常富含糖和奶制品（dairy）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12369657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12369657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/smoothies-2253423_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12369657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/smoothies-2253423_1920-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12369657" class="wp-caption-text">示意图。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>5. 炸薯条</h2>
<p>冷的炸薯条的味道实在不怎么样，且无法在家中加热恢复到原本口味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12147115" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12147115" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/fries.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12147115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/fries-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12147115" class="wp-caption-text">薯条。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6. 米粉</h2>
<p>米粉一旦变凉，往往会变硬；如果是汤米，米粉浸在汤或调料中也吸收液体。</p>
<p>无论哪种方式，米粉最好选择新鲜食用，而不是外卖送餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6180862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6180862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/07/807200644391983.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-6180862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/07/807200644391983-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6180862" class="wp-caption-text">炒米粉。（林仕杰／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>7. 汤</h2>
<p>众所周知，热的液体会导致塑料容器浸出化学物质，且大多数外卖选择的容器都不使用不含BPA的塑料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8924391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8924391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Fotolia_90641381_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8924391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Fotolia_90641381_Subaion_L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8924391" class="wp-caption-text">鸡汤示意图，和本文无关。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>8. 沙拉</h2>
<p>沙拉通常每周预制一次或两次，然后放入塑料容器中，直到食用时添加调料。</p>
<p>由于制作沙拉的蔬菜水分含量高，且可以生吃，因此，沙拉蔬菜是大肠杆菌和沙门氏菌等细菌的温床。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871991-shutterstock_1783991306.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12871991" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871991-shutterstock_1783991306-600x479.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="479" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871991" class="wp-caption-text">沙拉示意图，和本文无关。(ShutterStock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 任何含有牛油果的食物</h2>
<p>牛油果会迅速地被氧化，所以当你收到食物时，它可能会变成棕色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9943418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9943418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2017-11-27-12598-20171202-hannah-Avocado-Fotolia_36078196_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9943418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2017-11-27-12598-20171202-hannah-Avocado-Fotolia_36078196_Subaion_L-600x535.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9943418" class="wp-caption-text">牛油果。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>10. 鲜榨果汁</h2>
<p>鲜榨果汁听起来很不错，但由于这种饮料未经巴氏杀菌，也可以作为细菌载体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11700831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11700831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/papaya-milk-drink2-1103.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11700831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/papaya-milk-drink2-1103-600x308.jpg" alt="木瓜蜜奶不仅有着木瓜清香和绵密口感，还有美白除斑、护眼、降脂的益处。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11700831" class="wp-caption-text">果汁，和本文无关。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>11. 鲜果沙拉</h2>
<p>订购水果餐时，新鲜水果沙拉似乎是一种安全、健康的选择。但是，某些水果往往带有细菌，包括利斯特氏菌，除非水果被彻底清洗，否则很难清除。</p>
<p>然后水果被用手切开，以生水果块或丁保存几天，从而增加了污染的风险。</p>
<h2>12. 米饭</h2>
<p>生米中带有蜡状芽孢杆菌，这种细菌可以经受烹饪的热度。如果米饭做好后立即吃，这不是问题；但是如果煮熟的米饭整天放在暖锅里，细菌就会扩散并可能导致疾病。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12655080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12655080" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1452011075.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12655080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1452011075-600x400.jpg" alt="白米饭是很好的养胃食物，可作为主食适当的吃。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12655080" class="wp-caption-text">白米饭，和本文无关。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>网络资料显示，蜡样芽胞杆菌是一种条件致病菌，通常可在室温下长时间放置的炒饭或米饭中发现，由其引起的病症通常成为“炒饭综合征”。该菌生长的最适温度为28℃～37℃，其繁殖体较耐热，需100℃×20分钟才被杀死，其芽胞可耐受100℃×30分钟或干热120℃×60分钟才能被杀死，这也是它不同于其它食源性致病菌的重要特征。</p>
