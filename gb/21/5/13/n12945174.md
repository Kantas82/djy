<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市持续夜市宣导  戴口罩禁止试吃鼓励外带</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945177-497712-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>文化路夜市宣导。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>报导）政府的关心，摊商及民众会更重视，为提醒民众再次强化防疫作为，嘉义市政府建设处、卫生局12日晚间前往弥陀<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>、文化路夜市、嘉乐福夜市<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC.md#1">宣导</a>，要求摊商<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E7%BD%A9.md#1">口罩</a>戴好戴满，逛夜市的市民朋友戴口罩、干洗手、避免边走边吃、鼓励外带，大家要再次提高警觉，把防疫工作做到最好。</p>
<figure id="12945178" aria-describedby="caption-12945178" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945178-497713-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945178-497713-450x338.jpg" alt=" 嘉乐福夜市宣导。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12945178" class="wp-caption-text">嘉乐福<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a><ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC.md#1">宣导</a>。（<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>建设处长田长沛表示，嘉义市属于服务型城市，服务范围包括云嘉嘉南等地区，更是云嘉南地区的消费休闲中心，需要商家、民众大家一起来把防疫工作做足做好，因此建设处与卫生局再次前往夜市商圈宣导，提升民众警觉心。12日的夜市摊商<ahref="https://github.com/kvspqe3378/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E7%BD%A9.md#1">口罩</a>都有戴好戴满，防疫意识很高，更有摊商主动提供酒精等让民众清洁消毒。</p>
<figure id="12945180" aria-describedby="caption-12945180" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945180-497714-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945180-497714-450x300.jpg" alt="弥陀夜市宣导。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12945180" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀夜市宣导。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>建设处表示，从去年疫情开始持续至今，市府推动防疫工作持续不间断，现在疫情更加严峻，夜市与传统市场除了戴口罩、勤洗手，更全面禁试吃。</p>
<figure id="12945181" aria-describedby="caption-12945181" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945181-497715-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945181-497715-450x300.jpg" alt="弥陀夜市宣导。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12945181" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀夜市宣导。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫生局也呼吁，疫情紧张的时刻，个人健康管理要做的更好，例如用餐不语、避免边走边吃、尽量采外带方式。</p>
<figure id="12945182" aria-describedby="caption-12945182" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945182-497716-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945182-497716-450x675.jpg" alt=" 弥陀夜市宣导。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12945182" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀夜市宣导。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义市传统市场及夜市禁止试吃及戴口罩规定执行至6月8日，市府除持续强化宣导之外，更要求市场各自治会及管理单位，务必要求所辖摊商遵守戴口罩等防疫规定并积极向民众宣导做好防疫。◇</p>
<figure id="12945183" aria-describedby="caption-12945183" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945183-497717-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12945183-497717-450x300.jpg" alt=" 弥陀夜市宣导。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12945183" class="wp-caption-text">弥陀夜市宣导。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
