<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>造价上亿 WooSox新棒球场正式启用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946492-DSC01549-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>耗资上亿美元的极地公园是全美最贵的小联盟球场之一，最多可容纳上万名观众。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-acecbaf5-f166-4b0c-9ba9-f46858848cec" data-id="player-acecbaf5-f166-4b0c-9ba9-f46858848cec"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/acecbaf5-f166-4b0c-9ba9-f46858848cec?r=16x9&amp;d=&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F21%2F5%2F13%2Fn12946627.md#1"></a>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月13日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A5%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9.md#1">渥斯特</a>市报导）“我们现在宣布，极地公园开放了！”在主持人宏亮的声音中，2000多名观众纷纷为这个造价超过1亿美元的新<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83.md#1">棒球</a>场鼓掌欢呼。</p>
<p>这是5月11日下午的<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A5%E6%96%AF%E7%89%B9.md#1">渥斯特</a>市（Worcester）“极地公园（Polar Park）”<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83.md#1">棒球</a>场的剪彩仪式。这意味着波士顿红袜棒球队（Red Sox）的附属3A球队——渥斯特<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%A2%9C%E9%98%9F.md#1">红袜队</a>（Woo Sox）正式搬进了新家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946512-DSC01395.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946512-DSC01395-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946512" class="wp-caption-text">极地公园举办开幕第一场比赛。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们今天来这里，很激动，这是个好天气，我们很高兴看今天这场比赛。”渥斯特<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E8%BF%B7.md#1">球迷</a>布莱恩·比索（Brian Beeso）在<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E5%9C%BA.md#1">球场</a>3楼的高台上说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946501-DSC01469.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946501-DSC01469-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946501" class="wp-caption-text">吉祥物们向观众打招呼。黄色的是WooSox吉祥物Smiley Ball。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12946502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946502" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946502-DSC01440.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946502" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946502-DSC01440-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946502" class="wp-caption-text">北极熊吉祥物向害羞的小女孩打招呼。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这个阳光明媚的日子，麻州州长查理·贝克（Charlie Baker）、副州长卡琳·白丽朵（Karyn Polito）、大联盟名人堂球星佩德罗·马丁内斯（Pedro Martinez）等多位来宾到场，为新<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E5%9C%BA.md#1">球场</a>剪彩及投出第一球。为观众献唱美国国歌的则是6度艾美奖得主詹姆斯·泰勒（James Taylor）和儿子亨利（Henry）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946495" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946495" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946495-DSC01503.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946495" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946495-DSC01503-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946495" class="wp-caption-text">为观众献唱美国国歌的是6度艾美奖得主詹姆斯·泰勒（右）和儿子亨利。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>球场外的停车楼和其它设施至今仍未竣工，蓝色调的球场外墙对面到处都是工地。这座新球场耗资超过1亿美元，是全美造价最贵的小联盟球场之一，最多可容纳近1万名观众。<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%90%83%E8%BF%B7.md#1">球迷</a>最低只需要8到9美元的门票就可以进场观看球赛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946510-DSC01385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946510-DSC01385-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946510" class="wp-caption-text">极地公园外观。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这个球场是世外美景，这是个美丽的球场，我们会在这里玩得很好。”渥斯特球迷黛博拉·邦巴德（Deborah Bombard）在球场的食品区说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946500" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946500" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946500-DSC01493.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946500-DSC01493-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946500" class="wp-caption-text">众多观众进场看球。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“极地公园（Polar Park）”的名字来自于极地饮料公司（Polar Seltzer）。这个球场原计划在今年4月对观众开放，但是，职棒大联盟因为疫情而推迟了3A球队的比赛。</p>
<p>“很高兴能待在户外，我们被困在室内太久了，室外是我们必须去的。”Polar饮料公司副总裁克里斯·克罗利（Chris Crowley）与夫人贝琪（Betsy）在球场的3楼高台说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946505" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946505-DSC01426.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946505-DSC01426-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946505" class="wp-caption-text">工作人员在比赛开始前维护球场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于麻州防疫政策的限制，球场目前只能开放1/4的座位。记者在渥斯特<ahref="https://github.com/ibqjkn3026/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%A2%9C%E9%98%9F.md#1">红袜队</a>售票网站上看到，本周的门票已几乎全部售罄。</p>
<p>渥斯特红袜队过去的名字是“波塔基红袜队（Paw Sox）”。当时该队的主场是罗德岛波塔基市（Pawtucket）的麦考伊球场（McCoy Stadium）。2018年，波塔基红袜队宣布了迁往麻州渥斯特市的计划，结束了在罗德岛的50年生涯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12946497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12946497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946497-DSC01478.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12946497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12946497-DSC01478-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12946497" class="wp-caption-text">渥斯特红袜队球员们亮相。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>按照赛程，本赛季渥斯特红袜队会在极地公园打60场比赛。在5月11日的比赛中，他们以8-5的比分战胜了雪城都会队（Syracuse Mets），为第一场主场比赛画上圆满句号。◇</p>
