<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>抗旱对策 省水小物热销</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12964666-497866-1.jpg" />
<h6>省水小物方便日常使用。（屈臣氏提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月21日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）台湾水情告急，各<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BA%93.md#1">水库</a>蓄水量纷纷创新低，加上近日天气越来越热，旱象难解！因应水情严峻，除了节约用水外，面对迫在眉睫的限水措施，<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%82%A8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">储水</a>更是当务之急，民众可利用折叠收纳储水桶、<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E6%B0%B4.md#1">省水</a>洗衣精等，让未来每一滴水资源都能被更谨慎的使用。</p>
<figure id="12963747" aria-describedby="caption-12963747" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963747-497869-450x426.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963747-497869-450x426.jpg" alt="分段式马桶省水器。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963747" class="wp-caption-text">分段式马桶<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E6%B0%B4.md#1">省水</a>器。（POYA HOME宝家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>POYA HOME宝家推出“省水专区”，用省水概念来整修厨房及卫浴水材，推出柔水省水器、二段式<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%B4.md#1">节水</a>器、加长莲蓬头沐浴组、分段式马桶省水器等。整体系列商品引发消费者询问度，带动相关沐浴省水器商品业绩翻倍成长。</p>
<figure id="12963746" aria-describedby="caption-12963746" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963746-497868-450x605.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963746-497868-450x605.jpg" alt="沐浴省水器。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963746" class="wp-caption-text">沐浴省水器。（POYA HOME宝家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12963748" aria-describedby="caption-12963748" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963748-497870-450x462.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963748-497870-450x462.jpg" alt="家常软管莲蓬头沐浴组。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963748" class="wp-caption-text">家常软管莲蓬头沐浴组。（POYA HOME宝家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>P&amp;G宝侨家品举例，一般家户每日耗水量22%都来自于衣物洗涤，尤其为了确保清洗干净，许多消费者洗衣服时习惯先浸泡1次，或甚至重复洗2次；用水量仅次于马桶冲厕28%、高居第2。想要省水的话，近年受到消费者青睐的洗衣胶囊，仅须1次洗程即可轻松达到衣物清洁抗菌的效果。</p>
<figure id="12963744" aria-describedby="caption-12963744" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963744-497867-450x319.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963744-497867-450x319.jpg" alt="洗衣胶囊可节省用水量。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963744" class="wp-caption-text">洗衣胶囊可节省用水量。（P&amp;G宝侨家品提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>针对首当其冲、面临限水措施的民众，屈臣氏也精选多款“省水系”好物，包括湿纸巾、卸妆棉、干洗发、制汗爽身、香氛喷雾、免洗衣物等，让无水可用的状况下可以应急；另外还有清洁必备的干洗手、酒精液等，让防疫不会因为限水出现漏洞。</p>
<figure id="12963742" aria-describedby="caption-12963742" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963742-497865-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963742-497865-450x450.jpg" alt="茶树抗菌干洗手。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963742" class="wp-caption-text">茶树抗菌干洗手。（屈臣氏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12963741" aria-describedby="caption-12963741" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963741-497864.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963741-497864.jpg" alt="干洗发喷雾。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963741" class="wp-caption-text">干洗发喷雾。（屈臣氏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12963740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12963740" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963740-497863.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12963740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963740-497863-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12963740" class="wp-caption-text">卸妆棉。（屈臣氏提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12963749" aria-describedby="caption-12963749" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963749-497871-450x202.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12963749-497871-450x202.jpg" alt="Airbnb吁携手共度干旱难关。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12963749" class="wp-caption-text">Airbnb吁携手共度干旱难关。（Airbnb提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>旅宿平台Airbnb也与环保团体台湾干净水行动联盟携手，分享适合外出的省水小妙招，齐心对抗疫情及旱象。</p>
<p>１.民众出外可以自带环保杯，多用饮水机。</p>
<p>２.<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%B4.md#1">节水</a>沐浴法：以淋浴取代泡澡，并在使用沐浴清洁用品时，先将水关掉；待抹好后，再冲洗身体。</p>
<p>台湾干净水行动联盟理事长彭桂枝表示：“面对干旱，水源调度及开发仅能治标；唯有正视气候变迁方能治本。建议民众改变现有的用水习惯，落实上水（饮用及清洁）、中水（上水使用后之回收水）及下水（产业废污水）分流处理，让每滴水可以被妥善循环运用。”◇</p>
