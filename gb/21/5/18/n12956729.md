<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—樱花季（彩墨）。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/wgvzlr3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">樱花季</a></h2>
<p>四十年前曾经去武陵农场旅游，但见众多老荣民在农场上种植高冷蔬菜，空气中充满鸡屎、猪粪的味道，苍蝇满天飞。后二十年再去，已然人去山空，老荣民的房舍也被拆光了，杳无去处。原种高丽菜的斜坡改种樱花，因为植株尚小，稀稀疏疏的，殊乏看头。</p>
<p>近年再度前往，樱花树已蔚然成林。开花时节，满山遍谷，怒放时有如“烟花三月”，游人如织，已然是另一处著名的旅游景点了。</p>

<h2>Cherry blossoms season (ink and color painting)</h2>
<p>Forty years ago, I made a tour to Wu Ling Farm, saw many veterans planted high-cold vegetables on the farm. The air filled with the smell of chickens’ and pigs’ feces, flies are everywhere. After twenty years, I made a tour to Wu Ling Farm again, veterans already gone and left with empty mountain, the veterans’ old houses also tore down, no one knows where they are now. The originally cabbage planted slope has?been replaced by cherry blossoms, which are still small, sparse, not worth seeing.</p>
<p>In recent years I visited again, cherry trees have grown up.? In blossom season, they are fully filled with mountains and valleys, in full blooming moment it is just like &#8220;fireworks in March,&#8221;, full of visitors, it already became a famous tourist point.@</p>
