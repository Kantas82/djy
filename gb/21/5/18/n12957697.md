<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>防范新冠疫情 苗栗县全县同步大消毒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957699-498475-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。（苗县府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月18日讯】（大纪元记者陈文敏台湾苗栗综合报导）鉴于近日全台严重特殊传染性肺炎<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>确诊人数大幅增加，苗县疫情已提升为“准三级警戒”，并因应中央流行疫情指挥中心宣布国内已出现感染源不明之本土案例，县府5月18日特别召集18个乡镇市公所，同步在各乡镇市的人潮出入频繁热点，办理环境<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%AF%92.md#1">消毒</a>，宣示用最大的努力与决心，给县民安全、健康的<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%85%E4%BD%8F%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83.md#1">居住环境</a>，让乡亲可以安心生活，减少<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%96%AB%E6%83%85.md#1">疫情</a>的干扰。</p>
<figure id="12957702" aria-describedby="caption-12957702" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957702-498477-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957702-498477-450x338.jpg" alt="防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12957702" class="wp-caption-text">防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E6%AF%92.md#1">消毒</a>。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12957700" aria-describedby="caption-12957700" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957700-498476-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957700-498476-450x600.jpg" alt="防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12957700" class="wp-caption-text">防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为防止疫情扩大，县府已发函通令要求18乡镇市公所消毒<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>大队，加强进行户外人潮聚集公共空间、交通场站、大型营业场所、公园、学校及民众洽公机关等场所周边环境大规模消毒作业，并每日统计执行成果及进度，以落实消毒作业，提供给民众安全健康的生活环境。</p>
<figure id="12957703" aria-describedby="caption-12957703" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957703-498478-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957703-498478-450x338.jpg" alt="防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12957703" class="wp-caption-text">防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县长徐耀昌特别呼吁，为防堵疫情延烧，全体县民应积极落实<ahref="https://github.com/vxdaxr3116/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>工作并配合相关防疫措施，并再次提醒民众，务必做好个人防护，减少外出，戴口罩、勤洗手、少聚会，保护自己，也保护他人，落实居家及社区防疫，同时感谢大家共体时艰，也谢谢各乡镇市公所第一线环境消毒人员，这段时间以来的辛劳及坚守冈位，并期勉大家共同努力，互相加油打气，平安度过疫情！</p>
<figure id="12957704" aria-describedby="caption-12957704" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957704-498479-450x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12957704-498479-450x800.jpg" alt="防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12957704" class="wp-caption-text">防范新冠疫情，苗栗县全县同步大消毒。（苗县府提供）</figcaption></figure>
