<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【＃用故事突破同温层】医护人员脸上的口罩痕 是不畏病毒的印记</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086563-505841-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>病人视角下的医护1。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年07月14日讯】（文／Emine报导）之前因为低烧两个礼拜的关系，只好去医院做新冠筛检。进到隔离室后，进来筛检的护理师和医生都是不同人，他们的<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A3%E7%BD%A9.md#1">口罩</a>痕迹都很重。</p>
<figure id="13086564" aria-describedby="caption-13086564" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086564-505842-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086564-505842-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护2。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086564" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%BB%E6%8A%A4.md#1">医护</a>2。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13086565" aria-describedby="caption-13086565" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086565-505843-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086565-505843-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护3。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086565" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%BB%E6%8A%A4.md#1">医护</a>3。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了隔离病房，照顾我的护理师，即使在高风险的工作场所，还是很<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%9F%94.md#1">温柔</a>的跟我聊天，仿佛在告诉我：“不要担心，你不会得肺炎的。即使得了，你还有我在。”</p>
<p>每几个小时，就会有护理师进来看我，而每次进来的都是不同人。那晚，我没有睡！在房内，我听到的是门外从没停过的脚步声。</p>
<figure id="13086566" aria-describedby="caption-13086566" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086566-505844-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086566-505844-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护4。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086566" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的医护4。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13086567" aria-describedby="caption-13086567" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086567-505845-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086567-505845-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护5。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086567" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的医护5。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13086571" aria-describedby="caption-13086571" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086571-505848-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086571-505848-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护6。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086571" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的医护6。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>隔天，筛检结果一出来，他们就用最快的速度帮我安排到普通病房，离开隔离病房的时候，我看到他们欣慰的眼神，很多护理师跟医生看起来都很年轻，看起来才刚毕业，他们却愿意到隔离病房那么高风险的地方工作，说话也都很<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%9F%94.md#1">温柔</a>，感觉在<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E6%8A%9A.md#1">安抚</a>病人的<ahref="https://github.com/zhfjaw375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%83%85%E7%BB%AA.md#1">情绪</a>。</p>
<figure id="13086570" aria-describedby="caption-13086570" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086570-505847-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086570-505847-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护7。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086570" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的医护7。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13086568" aria-describedby="caption-13086568" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086568-505846-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13086568-505846-450x450.jpg" alt="病人视角下的医护8。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13086568" class="wp-caption-text">病人视角下的医护8。（手术室小绿人 (@apple.palette)提供）</figcaption></figure>
