<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>每周为《综艺》献唱 浩子等待金曲奖开奖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13075628-2107080201131487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>浩子宅在家等待金曲听牌中。（民视提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月08日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）台湾外景节目《<ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BC%E8%89%BA%E6%96%B0%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">综艺新时代</a>》本周将播出到彰化芳苑采蚵，节目主持人是今年金曲歌王候选人的<ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E5%AD%90.md#1">浩子</a>，除了在家防疫外，他把握这段时间精进歌艺，每周一录自弹自唱歌曲为节目献声。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E5%AD%90.md#1">浩子</a>除了技巧不生疏外，精进自己才能远眺“金曲奖”，制作团队也替他紧张期待“开始听牌”。</p>
<p>而在这一集被评为剥蚵最丑的<ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E7%BF%94.md#1">阿翔</a>，防疫期间在家搭帐棚、做游乐园、煮早餐之外，居然已经被小孩当成牛来拖了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13075630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13075630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13075630-2107080201181487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13075630" title="阿翔" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13075630-2107080201181487-600x337.jpg" alt="阿翔" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13075630" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E7%BF%94.md#1">阿翔</a>（前）宅在家扮演牛与孩子同乐。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来是为了配合本集牛车采蚵的主题，阿翔则想出了奇招可以逗小孩开心也呼应节目，扮演“牛”戴着女儿快乐出航，阿翔也和工作人员透露非常怀念外景生活，希望疫情好转能出去录影。</p>
<p>另外节目固定班底<ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%AF%AE.md#1">篮篮</a>，则是宅在家里猛开直播，每天和不同的艺能界的前辈庹宗康、阿KEN等人开聊。当网红很有心得的她也教观众居家自拍姿势教学，古怪又可爱的表情迷倒不少粉丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13075631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13075631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13075631-2107080201161487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13075631" title="篮篮" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13075631-2107080201161487-600x337.jpg" alt="篮篮" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13075631" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/abyzuj301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%AF%AE.md#1">篮篮</a>宅在家里猛开直播，与艺人与粉丝互动。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>
