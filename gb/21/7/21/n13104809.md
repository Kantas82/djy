<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东奥7·21：中国女足首战0比5大败给巴西</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104846-content_GettyImages-1329845253-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月21日讯】（大纪元记者黄家熙香港报导）<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E4%BA%AC%E5%A5%A5%E8%BF%90.md#1">东京奥运</a>会赛事于今天（21日）下午开踢<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%90%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83.md#1">女子足球</a>分组赛，F组的<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD.md#1">中国</a>以0:5大比数败给南美代表队<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%A5%BF.md#1">巴西</a>。全场中国射门次数8次，但射正数仅3次，其中两次均为中场王双包办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13104849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104849-bc5f310a47d68582073057eff3713e96.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104849-bc5f310a47d68582073057eff3713e96-600x195.png" alt="" width="600" b="195" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104849" class="wp-caption-text">东奥赛事今（7月21日）下午开踢<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%90%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83.md#1">女子足球</a>分组赛，<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD.md#1">中国</a>0比5大败给<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%A5%BF.md#1">巴西</a>。(KOHEI CHIBAGARA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13104848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104848-content_GettyImages-1329846687.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104848-content_GettyImages-1329846687-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104848" class="wp-caption-text">东奥赛事今（7月21日）下午开踢女子足球分组赛，中国0比5大败给巴西。(Koki Nagahama/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13104843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104843-content_GettyImages-1234086400.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104843-content_GettyImages-1234086400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104843" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E4%BA%AC%E5%A5%A5%E8%BF%90.md#1">东京奥运</a>会赛事于今天（21日）下午开踢女子足球分组赛，F组的中国以0:5大比数败给南美代表队巴西。全场中国射门次数8次，但射正数仅3次，其中两次均为中场王双包办。(KOHEI CHIBAGARA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴西一波波的凌厉攻势，使对手毫无还击余地，控球率58%，射门18次，射正9次。中国队犯规次数比巴西多3次，更另有7次越位，巴西越位次数为零。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13104845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104845-content_GettyImages-1234086772.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104845-content_GettyImages-1234086772-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104845" class="wp-caption-text">东奥赛事今（7月21日）下午开踢女子足球分组赛，中国0比5大败给巴西。(KOHEI CHIBAGARA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13104844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104844-content_GettyImages-1234086483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104844-content_GettyImages-1234086483-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104844" class="wp-caption-text">东奥赛事今（7月21日）下午开踢女子足球分组赛，巴西5比0大胜中国。(KOHEI CHIBAGARA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场已领先2比0的巴西，下半场再由35岁老将兼本场赛事功臣Marta于74分钟多添一球，而最后于完场前1分钟由身穿16号球衣的Beatriz破网而锦上添花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13104841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13104841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104841-content_GettyImages-1234086057.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13104841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13104841-content_GettyImages-1234086057-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13104841" class="wp-caption-text">35岁巴西老将兼本场赛事功臣Marta。(KOHEI CHIBAGARA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另两场于下午、傍晚举行的女足赛事为英国2比0胜智利、瑞典3比0胜美国。周六（24日）中国与赞比亚的对决将同样于<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%AB%E5%9F%8E%E4%BD%93%E8%82%B2%E5%9C%BA.md#1">宫城体育场</a>进行。@</p>
