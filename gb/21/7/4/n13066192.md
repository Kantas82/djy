<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旧金山寿司Yama Sushi 新鲜滑嫩 回味悠然</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066308-1c2faae840ff1ae064aa764847ae3621-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Nigiri Sushi握寿司。（旧金山日式料理Yama Sushi提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年07月04日讯】文：余亨之?大纪元<br />
夏日食欲不振，轻食的寿司（Sushi），清爽不油腻、养眼好吃，是上佳的选择。Yama Sushi店的<ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8%E5%8D%B7.md#1">寿司卷</a>“晶莹软润”，缤纷多彩；刺身光滑有弹性、纹理匀称饱满；搭配鳗鱼酱、飞鱼卵，口感丰富，深受当地西人、亚裔人喜爱。有的食客中午在Yama Sushi订了餐，食后意犹未尽，晚上再订一次，口福吃到饱。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">旧金山日式料理</a>：精彩纷呈的<ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8%E5%8D%B7.md#1">寿司卷</a></h2>
<p>Yama Sushi 店有20款寿司卷，其中的加州卷（California Roll）是标配。把冷却的醋饭铺开，撒上芝麻，放入烤熟的蟹柳、牛油果、黄瓜丁，将醋饭裹成卷，切成一段段的，再浇上蛋黄酱和自制的蔬菜酱。寿司里边加上牛油果、蛋黄酱，深受老美和亚裔人喜爱，是日本寿司在美国本土化的点睛之笔！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">火山卷（Volcano Roll）：</span></strong>在加州卷上面铺上三文鱼，淋上Spicy Mayo（辛辣酱和蛋黄酱等多种配料调整而成），然后放入烤箱中烘焙。取出后，再淋上鳗鱼酱，就是著名的火山卷（Volcano Roll）。喜欢芝麻的，可以要求撒上芝麻。金色的寿司卷上，芝麻粒粒可见，褐色的鳗鱼酱鲜咸提味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066314-2cc391d1e1a1e7271114c13c60d7348c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066314-2cc391d1e1a1e7271114c13c60d7348c-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066314" class="wp-caption-text">火山卷和飞鱼卵。（<ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">旧金山日式料理</a>Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">樱花卷（ Sakura Rolls ）：</span></strong>在加州卷上面，铺上金枪鱼和飞鱼卵。值得一提的是，鱼卵里包含了一条小鱼成长所需的所有营养素，比鱼肉的营养更浓缩，含有大量的美味氨基酸与核酸。天然飞鱼卵的颜色是淡黄色，但经常染制成红色或金黄色，有着温和的咸味，带着一丝甜味，口感爽脆、粒粒分明。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">红龙（Red Dragon）：</span></strong>香酥嫩滑的天妇罗虾，被裹在卷的里面；上面铺上金枪鱼、牛油果、飞鱼卵和烤香的蟹柳。里酥外滑，口感丰富，营养丰富。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066316-be61bf141178e10d02ee838678cc0311.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066316-be61bf141178e10d02ee838678cc0311-600x335.png" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066316" class="wp-caption-text">红龙卷。（旧金山日式料理Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">龙卷（Dragon Roll）：</span></strong>里边裹上刚炸好的天妇罗虾，上面铺上绵软润滑的牛油果，和烤鳗鱼。与红龙相比，里面去掉了刺身，非常适合不吃生鱼的食客。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066317-Dragon-Roll.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066317-Dragon-Roll-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066317" class="wp-caption-text">龙卷。（旧金山日式料理Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1sushi.md#1">旧金山Sushi</a>：握寿司晶莹软润</h2>
<p>“寿司的美味，关键在醋饭”。好的寿司饭团，饭粒有光泽，粒粒分明，吃起来软润香甜。把米饭用手握成饭团，加上生鱼片等食材；有时用一条海苔，把饭团和食材绑在一起。捏法不一样，形状不一样，口味也不一样。Yama的握寿司有：金枪鱼握寿司、甜虾握寿司、鳗鱼握寿司、三文鱼握寿司等。值得一提的是，Yama还有特供的大对虾握寿司（Amaebi Nigiri Sushi），是他家高颜值的金牌寿司。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066308-1c2faae840ff1ae064aa764847ae3621.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066308-1c2faae840ff1ae064aa764847ae3621-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066308" class="wp-caption-text">Nigiri Sushi握寿司。（旧金山日式料理Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1sushi.md#1">旧金山Sushi</a>：刺身新鲜滑嫩</h2>
<p>Yama Sushi切好的生鱼片，不论是三文鱼，还是金枪鱼，鱼肉结实、光滑、有亮泽、富有弹性。每一片生鱼片的切口完美，切割后的生鱼片，纹理分布均匀，上桌一段时间后仍能够保持弹性。这样的刺身，吃起来如同滑嫩多脂的果冻般的口感及淡雅鲜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066318-9f0c63530cfcc83586e7d4b2c7a66b39.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066318-9f0c63530cfcc83586e7d4b2c7a66b39-600x375.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066318" class="wp-caption-text">Sashimi刺身。（旧金山日式料理Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yama Sushi不仅有物美质优的各类寿司、刺身，还有一百多道开胃菜、小吃、点心等。其中比较受欢迎的，有“肥牛乌东面”，浓郁的牛肉汤乌东面，铺上几大片特级肥牛，淋上酱汁，撒上芝麻葱花，香气扑鼻。还有“烤鸭汤面”，猪骨汤面铺上片好的美味烤鸭，香醇可口。#</p>
<figure id="attachment_13066320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13066320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066320-Roast-duck-ramen.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13066320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13066320-Roast-duck-ramen-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13066320" class="wp-caption-text">烧鸭汤面。（<ahref="https://github.com/jkufre301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">旧金山寿司</a>店Yama Sushi提供）</figcaption></figure>
