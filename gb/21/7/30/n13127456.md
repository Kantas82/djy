<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>你能找到被困在爱达荷州悬崖边丢失的狗吗？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127464-et-dog-honey-WEB890-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>当时一只名叫蜜熊 (Honey Bear)的流浪狗不知何故困在德拉山的一侧。 (由罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和救援队提供) 
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月30日讯】（英文大纪元EMG INSPIRED STAFF报导／文韵编译）你能发现<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%A4%B1.md#1">走失</a>的小狗在爱达荷州(Idaho)这个看似荒芜的悬崖边上吗？</p>
<p>这是7月初伍德河消防和<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E9%98%9F.md#1">救援队</a>(Wood River Fire and Rescue)接到的任务，当时一只名叫蜜熊 (Honey Bear)的<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B5%AA%E7%8B%97.md#1">流浪狗</a>不知何故困在德拉山(Della Mountain)的一侧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13127472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127472-et-dog-honey-546-890-.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13127472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127472-et-dog-honey-546-890--600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127472" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B5%AA%E7%8B%97.md#1">流浪狗</a>蜜熊不知何故困在德拉山的一侧。 (由罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4%E9%98%9F.md#1">救援队</a>提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13127476" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127476-et-dog-honey-546-890-545.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13127476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127476-et-dog-honey-546-890-545-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127476" class="wp-caption-text">寻找流浪狗蜜熊的传单。 (罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和救援队提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>7月3日星期六，接到狗被困的电话后，下午6点后，<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E9%98%B2%E9%98%9F%E5%91%98.md#1">消防队员</a>出动到现场，并幸运地发现可怜的蜜熊——只是山坡上的一个小斑点。</p>
<p>伍德河消防救援队说，这只可怜的小狗从海利(Hailey)的雪松街(Cedar Street) 附近失踪后，已经<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E5%A4%B1.md#1">走失</a>了五天。</p>
<p>凯彻姆(Ketchum)消防队的救援人员通过科罗拉多峡谷(Colorado Gulch)到达山顶，然后下降800英尺，放置锚点进行救援。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13127486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127486-et-dog-honey-546-890-890.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13127486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127486-et-dog-honey-546-890-890-600x1301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="976" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127486" class="wp-caption-text">你找到流浪狗蜜熊了吗？(罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和救援队提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13127498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127498" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127498-et-dog-honey-546-890-55.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13127498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127498-et-dog-honey-546-890-55-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="411" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127498" class="wp-caption-text">消防队的救援人员。(罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和救援队提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13127506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127506-et-dog-honey-546-55.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13127506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127506-et-dog-honey-546-55-600x404.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127506" class="wp-caption-text">消防队的救援人员找到了流浪狗。左图：（由伊莱·麦克尼斯／木河消防和救援队和劳拉·麦克莱恩/凯彻姆消防队提供）；右图。 (由伊莱·麦克尼斯／木河消防和救援队提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在WRFR广播电台第二站瞭望人员的引导下，消防员伊莱·麦克尼斯(Eli McNees )从悬崖边上下来，看到了焦虑但快乐的蜜熊。</p>
<p>麦克尼斯将狗固定住，他们一起被吊起来。</p>
<p>成功后，团队收集他们的装备并下到克罗伊峡谷(Croix Canyon)；据消防救援报导，这只小狗在晚上 11 点左右与主人团聚。</p>
<p>一只迷失在荒山边某处的狗，现在找到了，并快乐地回家了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13127518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13127518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127518-212478094_291201166135292_5503748063015715934_n.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13127518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13127518-212478094_291201166135292_5503748063015715934_n-600x453.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13127518" class="wp-caption-text">(罗恩·贝特曼／木河消防和救援队提供)</figcaption></figure>
