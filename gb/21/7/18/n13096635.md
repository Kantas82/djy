<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>职场穿搭推荐 商务休闲装穿出属于你的自信</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098945-GettyImages-455173698-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，意大利品牌 Michael Kors。（Peter Michael Dills/Getty Images for Michael Kors）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p class="p1">【大纪元2021年07月26日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）疫情之下，相信有不少人渐渐习惯整天穿着睡衣、短裤和拖鞋坐在床上办公，但如今各地都在逐步解封，是时候面对必须回归公司上班的现实了！至于该穿什么<span class="s1">？</span><span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%86%E5%8A%A1%E4%BC%91%E9%97%B2%E8%A3%85.md#1">商务休闲装</a></span>从熟悉的衬衫、外套、九分裤、洋装等，职场必胜<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>让你从细节展现自我风格，营造美好的第一印象。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">那么，什么是<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%86%E5%8A%A1%E4%BC%91%E9%97%B2%E8%A3%85.md#1">商务休闲装</a>呢？这</span>并没有明确的定义<span class="s1">，基本上不像传统的商务服装那样正式，却仍然属于“</span><span class="s1">足够专业”</span><span class="s1">的范畴，力求简单、整齐、得体。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">来看看我们给的以下建议吧，让你兼顾舒适度与<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>感</span><span class="s1">：</span></p>
<blockquote><p><span class="s1">＃上衣：衬衫、优雅的无袖上衣、高领上衣、中性或纯色连衣裙</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p>＃下装：铅笔裙、及膝裙、正装裤（卡其色、灯芯绒、斜纹布或棉质）。</p>
<p>＃外套：西装外套、小外套、针织外套。</p>
<p>＃鞋子：包鞋（乐福鞋、芭蕾舞鞋）、跟鞋（细高跟鞋、猫跟鞋）<span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p><span class="s1">＊极力避免：细肩带、短版露肚上衣、运动服、露肩、低胸、牛仔布、破洞、浮夸的款式与色彩；鞋子则不宜穿着</span><span class="s1">凉鞋、拖鞋、草编鞋等。</span></p></blockquote>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">看看以下几种搭配，你适合哪一种：</span></p>

<figure id="attachment_13098945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13098945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098945-GettyImages-455173698.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13098945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098945-GettyImages-455173698-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13098945" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，意大利品牌 Michael Kors。（Peter Michael Dills/Getty Images for Michael Kors）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p1">优雅上衣</h2>
<p>上班除了衬衫还能穿什么？其实不少休闲上衣搭配俐落的下半身，都能成为商务休闲服的选择，立领上衣、泡泡袖、胸前打折、经典条纹等设计也能为你添加个人特色。</p>
<p>质料可以选择丝质、麻料、棉麻混纺等，轻柔舒适又不失格调，但注意不宜过度宽松，且易皱材质应烫平，以维持专业感。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_13096670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13096670" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096670-GettyImages-1282076968.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13096670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096670-GettyImages-1282076968-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13096670" class="wp-caption-text">2021 春夏首尔时装周，服装品牌 LIE。（Justin Shin/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13096672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13096672" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096672-GettyImages-1229092564.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13096672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096672-GettyImages-1229092564-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13096672" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，土耳其设计师品牌 ?zlem Kaya。（Ekrem Serif Egeli/Getty Images for IHKIB）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p1">连身裤／裙</h2>
<p class="p1">连身裤／裙最大的好处是不用思考搭配，一件套上去就能出门，也可搭配简单的项链点缀，让整体造型加分。建议这种服装要挑选合身、有腰身的款式，或是系上皮带，才能展现出精神饱满、准备做事的氛围。</p>
<p class="p1">另外，颜色则不宜过浅，否则容易给人飘飘然的度假感，黑、灰、咖、深蓝色等，都是比较安全保守的色彩，也较为得体。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13096678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13096678" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096678-GettyImages-462605845.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13096678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096678-GettyImages-462605845-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13096678" class="wp-caption-text">柏林时装周，奥地利设计师品牌 Lena Hoschek。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for Mercedes Benz）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13096677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13096677" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096677-GettyImages-127691343.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13096677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13096677-GettyImages-127691343-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13096677" class="wp-caption-text">春夏巴黎时装周，法国高级时装品牌 Lanvin。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p1">中长半身窄裙</h2>
<p>窄裙是套装中常见的搭配，但我们不用那么拘束，改搭配优雅上衣、衬衫，打造微正式感。<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>要点注意上下身都要合身，不宜过紧，避免让职场同事、上司觉得性感或暴露；也不应过于宽松，会失去窄裙的造型特色，避免看起来像是搭错衣服。</p>
<p>穿着适合自己的衣服，会让你更有<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E4%BF%A1.md#1">自信</a>，更加散发光彩！另外，裙长最好及膝或过膝，看起来较为庄重。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13098352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13098352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098352-GettyImages-807586062.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13098352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098352-GettyImages-807586062-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13098352" class="wp-caption-text">法国模特卡罗琳?德?迈格雷（Caroline de Maigret）出席香奈儿高订时装秀。（FRANCOIS GUILLOT/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13098724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13098724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098724-GettyImages-455173094.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13098724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098724-GettyImages-455173094-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13098724" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，意大利品牌 Michael Kors。（Peter Michael Dills/Getty Images for Michael Kors）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>俐落的长裤</h2>
<p>直筒裤与锥型裤绝对是商务休闲装的好伙伴。俐落简洁的直筒裤，能完美修饰腿型，挑选时注意腰、臀两处需合身，如此搭配直筒裤管，有助于拉长下身线条，<ahref="https://github.com/xijmdi3035/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>有型，宽松的裤管也有透气通风的作用。</p>
<p>上宽下窄的锥形裤在臀部、大腿处预留了更多空间，对需要长时间坐在办公桌前的上班族特别友善，相当舒适。</p>
<p>花色上可以考虑经典格纹、条纹，能丰富整体造型，搭配跟鞋、包鞋或短靴都十分好看；也能考虑反折裤管或直接选购九分、七分裤，露出一点脚踝营造双腿纤细的效果。另外，由于两者皆属较宽松的版型，因此建议上衣需合身，或是扎进裤头，才不会显得邋遢随便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13098737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13098737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098737-GettyImages-605947668.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13098737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13098737-GettyImages-605947668-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13098737" class="wp-caption-text">法国品牌 SANDRO 走秀 。（Anthony Kwan/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>西装外套／小外套</h2>
<p>办公室坐久了冷气总是越吹越冷，相信大家都有这种经验，这时外套最适合上班穿搭了。</p>
<p>西装外套绝对是最佳选择，不仅本身具有份量与质感，还能与各种单品混搭，不必整套穿着，简单的上衣外搭西装外套就能拥有微正式感，带出简约却不失体面的帅气。</p>
<p>小外套也是不错的单品，它有着精致的细节，能适当展露个人风格，丝质、棉质或针织质料都很适合上班穿着；短版的造型能修饰身形比例，并给人轻盈、灵活的感受。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在工作职场保持舒适和专业非常重要，透过将日常着装与正式服装结合，创造属于自己的商务休闲风格吧！</span></p>
