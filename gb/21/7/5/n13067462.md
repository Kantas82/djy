<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年度天文摄影大赛入围作品精选：璀璨星空</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067472-dolpin-copy-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一星云形状酷似海豚的头部。（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Yovin Yahathugoda [Sri Lanka]）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者笛睿编译报导）今年的<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E6%9E%97%E5%A8%81%E6%B2%BB%E5%A4%A9%E6%96%87%E5%8F%B0.md#1">格林威治天文台</a>（Royal Observatory Greenwich）第13届年度天文摄影师（Astronomy Photographer of the Year）竞赛收到了4500多份作品，有的来自专业摄影师，有的来自业余摄影师。</p>
<p>这次竞赛由<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E6%9E%97%E5%A8%81%E6%B2%BB%E5%A4%A9%E6%96%87%E5%8F%B0.md#1">格林威治天文台</a>和英国广播公司（BBC）联合举办。已选出的入围作品，从火星的日落到土星壮观的景色，到太空里的新星苗圃，这些照片带领观众穿梭于浩瀚的<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%87%E5%AE%99.md#1">宇宙</a>。9月16日将有一个网络仪式宣布获奖的照片。以下是BBC选出的部分入围作品。</p>
<p><strong>巨石阵上空的新智彗星（Comet NEOWISE over Stonehenge）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067452-Stonehenge-copy-e1625445076449.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067452" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067452-Stonehenge-copy-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067452" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/James Rushforth [UK]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新智彗星去年造访了我们太阳系一次。这张照片拍下了它扫过英国巨石阵上空的景象。新智彗星上一次造访太阳系已经是6800多年前，那时候巨石阵还不存在。科学家估计巨石阵是在距今5000年前建成。</p>
<p><strong>海豚头星云（Dolphin Head Nebula</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067472" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067472-dolpin-copy-e1625445407127.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067472-dolpin-copy-600x591.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="591" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067472" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Yovin Yahathugoda [Sri Lanka]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这片星云看起来很像一只海豚的头部，是一个由星际风和其它因素共同勾勒而成的太空气泡。中间的亮星沃夫–瑞叶星（Wolf-Rayet）促成了它的形态。</p>
<p><strong>望向太空的太阳黑子（Sunspot Looking Out Into Space</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067474-spot-copy-e1625445521492.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067474-spot-copy-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067474" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Siu Fone Tang [USA]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这张照片提供了太阳黑子和周围等离子体的视觉效果。这些照片经过氢-α过滤器拍摄，再把好几帧照片叠加在一起，最后经过图像软件处理而成。反色的效果使对比感增强。</p>
<p><strong>彗星的离子尾（The Exceptionally Active Ion Tail of Comet 2020F8 SWAN</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067477-comet-copy-e1625445607197.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067477-comet-copy-600x753.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="753" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067477" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Gerald Rhemann [Austria]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2020年科学家新发现一颗彗星C/2020F8（SWAN），当时在南北半球都看得见，拍摄了这张非常细致的照片。后来这颗彗星碎裂了。</p>
<p><strong>空间站白日一瞥（A Daytime Transit</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067483-station-copy-e1625445759484.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067483-station-copy-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067483" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Andrew McCarthy [USA]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2020年10月14日，国际空间站（ISS）经过月球的一瞬间。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%87%E5%AE%99.md#1">宇宙</a>之魂（The Soul of Space</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067485-soul-copy-e1625445840845.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067485-soul-copy-600x834.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="834" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067485" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Kush Chandaria [UK]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>灵魂星云（Soul Nebula）的近照。这张照片的摄影师钱达里亚（Kush Chandaria）年仅13岁。</p>
<p><strong>玫瑰（The Rose</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067503" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067503-rose-copy.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067503-rose-copy-600x476.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067503" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Josep Drudis [USA]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是Messier 57星云在红外线波段的图像。红色是氢，绿色和蓝色是氧，深红色是氮。</p>
<p><strong>火星日落（Martian Sunset</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067506-martian-copy-e1625446467713.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067506-martian-copy-600x155.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="155" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067506" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/John White [UK]/NASA-JPL/Caltech）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些照片是美国宇航局（NASA）的好奇号（Curiosity）探测车拍摄。不过是英国的摄影师怀特（John White）从好奇号原始数据档案中39万张照片里面挑出了这组连续性照片。</p>
<p><strong>昴宿姐妹（Pleiades Sisters</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067513" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067513-sister-copy-e1625446560930.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067513-sister-copy-600x634.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="634" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067513" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Jashanpreet Singh Dingra [India]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14岁的摄影师丁格拉（Singh Dingra）拍摄了昴宿星团（Pleiades）的照片。昴宿星团中经常见到最明亮的七颗星，因此又被称为七姐妹星团。总计曝光时间为3小时零2分。</p>
<p><strong>双色面纱星云（Bicolor Veil Nebula</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13067518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067518-bicolor-copy-e1625446633375.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13067518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067518-bicolor-copy-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067518" class="wp-caption-text">（Royal Observatory Greenwich/Péter Feltóti [Hungary]）</figcaption></figure>
<p>面纱星云是一片超新星爆炸后的残骸，由一部装在SkyWatcher Newton望远镜上的相机拍下。◇#</p>
