<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳失业率攀升至5.1%</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113779-06d1be82c1a34ecdcbd9aac3195119bf-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月西澳的失业率急剧上升—从4.6%上升到5.1%，超过了十年来全澳最低失业率。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月25日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者彭妮澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月西澳的失业率急剧上升</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">从</span></span>4.6%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">上升到</span></span>5.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，超过了十年来全澳最低失业率。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据西澳人报网报导，虽然西澳从</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份全澳最低的失业率上升到</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份高于全澳平均水平，但全澳的失业率降至</span></span>2011<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年以来的最低水平，为</span></span>4.9%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳大利亚统计局周四（</span></span>7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>15<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日）表示，全澳失业率连续第八个月下降。</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份的数据是在<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%B7%A5%E8%A1%A5%E8%B4%B4.md#1">保工补贴</a>（</span></span>Job Keeper<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）结束之后，在新州开始封锁之前发布的。西澳的失业率为</span></span>5.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，高于全澳平均水平；维多利亚州失业率最低，为</span></span>4.4%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳大利亚失业总人数减少</span></span>2.2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人，至</span></span>67.91<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人，比去年</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月减少了</span></span>30.37<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人。对于</span></span>15<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁</span></span>-24<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的年轻人来说，这是个好消息，</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份青年失业率下降了</span></span>0.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>10.2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。这比去年同期下降</span></span>6.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。就业人数增加了</span></span>2.91<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">更多澳大利亚人从事全职工作，全职就业人数增加了</span></span>5.16<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人，兼职就业人数减少了</span></span>2.25<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万人。参与率稳定在</span></span>66.2%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，但女性参与率略有下降了</span></span>0.1%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%9A%E4%B8%8D%E8%B6%B3.md#1">就业不足</a></span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">有工作但需要更多工作时间的澳大利亚人</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">增加了</span></span>0.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，达到</span></span>7.9%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
