<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳Keystart住房贷款人收入上限新规延期12个月</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月25日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者彭妮澳洲珀斯报导）日前，麦高恩政府宣布，在</span></span>2019-2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年财政年度临时放宽的</span></span>Keystart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">住房贷款申请人收入上限新规，将延期</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个月，旨在帮助更多的西澳人有资格购买<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E8%B4%9F%E6%8B%85%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B.md#1">可负担房屋</a>。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">最近，麦高恩（</span></span>Mark McGowan<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）政府公布了</span></span>2019-2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年州政府预算案，其中一项计划为政府将零时放宽中低收入贷款机构</span></span>Keystart Home Loans<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（该机构由政府所有）的借款人资格标准</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">自</span></span>2019<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>7<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日起，政府上调该住房贷款申请人的收入限额，原定于</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>30<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日结束，但现在已延长至</span></span>2022<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>6<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>30<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">日。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">自</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>12<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月以来，麦高恩政府已批准将房贷计划</span></span>Keystart2021-22<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年度的借款限额提高</span></span>3.62<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元。此次增加使</span></span>Keystart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的总借款限额达到</span></span>58<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">亿澳元，并使</span></span>Keystart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">能够在</span></span>2021-22<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年获得大约</span></span>2,700<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">笔额外贷款。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">上个财政年，</span></span>Keystart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">帮助</span></span>3,977<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">户符合条件的家庭购买了<ahref="https://github.com/ojaqxe3952/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E8%B4%9F%E6%8B%85%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B.md#1">可负担房屋</a>，这是自</span></span>2009-10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">财政年以来的最高水平。其中首次购房者贷款占</span></span>80%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">大约</span></span>2900<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">户家庭还能够从</span></span>Keystart<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">过渡到传统贷方或偿还贷款，这是购房过程中的重要一步。这是自</span></span>2015-16<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">财政年以来最多的一次。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">中低收入贷款机构</span></span>Keystart Home Loans<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">成立于</span></span>1989<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年，该机构由政府所有，是全澳公认的同类计划中最成功的。自成立以来，它已帮助超过</span></span>11.8<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万中低收入的西澳人实现了购房梦。</span></span></p>
