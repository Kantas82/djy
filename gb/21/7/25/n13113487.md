<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>露营用品旧变新 简易上漆教学超实用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113551-828-0075-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>将露营用品全部上漆成为同一种颜色，不只自己看了美观又好像新买的一样。（尖端出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9C%B2%E8%90%A5.md#1">露营</a>用品变旧了怎么办？其实只要重新<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8A%E6%BC%86.md#1">上漆</a>也能一直使用下去。有一阵子我非常喜欢红色，能够上漆的用品几乎都涂上红色。但我现在爱上了橄榄绿，因此有些用品的颜色就变成了绿色。将用品涂上颜色之后，能够呈现出统一感。但是，上漆的诀窍就是不要急躁，慢慢地反复上漆。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">用具</span></h2>
<p>中性洗剂<br />
砂纸（240~320号）<br />
脱脂剂<br />
抹布<br />
亮光漆或香蕉水<br />
纸胶带<br />
广告纸<br />
吹风机</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h2>
<p>要<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8A%E6%BC%86.md#1">上漆</a>的用品（<ahref="https://github.com/plpwfo313/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9C%B2%E8%90%A5.md#1">露营</a>锅的盖子）<br />
耐热涂料（喷漆）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113560-T0A9334.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113560-T0A9334-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113560" class="wp-caption-text">上漆后的露营锅。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">进行准备作业</span></h3>
<p>1.这次要来为露营锅的盖子上漆。首先以中性洗剂或清洁剂将脏污的地方清洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113552" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113552-P3060.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113552-P3060-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113552" class="wp-caption-text">以中性洗剂或清洁剂将脏污的地方清洗干净。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将把手拆下来，以砂纸将整个锅盖磨粗一点，以便让涂料能够渗入锅盖的表面。</p>
<p>3.将脱脂剂喷在抹布上，然后擦拭整个锅盖，将蜡与油分清除干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113535-828-0054.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113535-828-0054-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113535" class="wp-caption-text">将脱脂剂喷在抹布上。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.最后以抹布沾上亮光漆或香蕉水擦拭整个锅盖，再将锅盖晾干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113536-828-0057.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113536-828-0057-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113536" class="wp-caption-text">最后以抹布沾上亮光漆或香蕉水擦拭整个锅盖。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将纸胶带贴在没有要上漆的部分，要慢慢地贴，以免纸胶带贴到锅盖的边缘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113537-828-0061.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113537-828-0061-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113537" class="wp-caption-text">纸胶带贴在没有要上漆的部分。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.内侧也要贴上纸张。可将锅盖放在空罐上面，这样作业起来会比较方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113538-828-0064.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113538-828-0064-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113538" class="wp-caption-text">可将锅盖放在空罐上面，这样作业起来会比较方便。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">开始上漆</span></h3>
<p>7.从锅盖的内侧开始喷耐热涂，分数次反复地喷一层薄薄的耐热涂料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113555-828-0065.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113555-828-0065-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113555" class="wp-caption-text">从锅盖的内侧开始喷耐热涂。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将耐热涂料慢慢地喷在锅盖的正面。这时要注意喷漆的喷嘴有无阻塞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113556-828-0067.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113556-828-0067-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113556" class="wp-caption-text">将耐热涂料慢慢地喷在锅盖的正面。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.反复将整个锅盖喷上3次漆左右，只要表面平整即可不用再喷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113547" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113547-828-0070.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113547-828-0070-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113547" class="wp-caption-text">反复将整个锅盖喷上3次喷漆左右的状态。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">进行烘干作业</span></h3>
<p>10.以吹风机将喷漆吹干。喷漆会慢慢形成“皱纹涂装”也可以让喷漆自然晾干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113549" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113549-828-0071.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113549-828-0071-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113549" class="wp-caption-text">喷漆会慢慢形成“皱纹涂装”。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.在喷漆完全干燥以前（八分干燥～九分干燥）将纸胶带撕下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13113550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13113550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113550-828-0073.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13113550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13113550-828-0073-450x244.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13113550" class="wp-caption-text">在喷漆完全干燥以前将纸胶带撕下来。（尖端出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.喷漆全干之后，将把手装回去，上漆作业大功告成。此时要确认蒸气孔是否阻塞。</p>
<h4>备注：若不喜欢涂料的气味，只要浸泡热水就能消除气味。＠</h4>
