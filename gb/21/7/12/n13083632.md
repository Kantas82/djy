<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秉持妈祖精神 庆安宫捐行动车及医疗器材</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083634-506544-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>庆安宫捐行动车及医疗器材。（基隆市立医院提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年07月12日讯】（大纪元记者陈秀媛台湾基隆报导）基隆市立医院院长王慧珺与<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%AE%89%E5%AE%AB.md#1">庆安宫</a>主任委员童永，12日下午举行“长照社区整合服务中心—<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%8C%E5%8A%A8%E8%BD%A6.md#1">行动车</a>”、门诊部“升降平台”及眼科“超音波白内障乳化仪”等<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%BB%E7%96%97%E5%99%A8%E6%9D%90.md#1">医疗器材</a>捐赠仪式。</p>
<p>王慧珺表示，基隆信仰中心“<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E5%AE%89%E5%AE%AB.md#1">庆安宫</a>基隆妈”秉持妈祖救苦救难精神，庇佑苍天众生，于疫情严峻医疗量能紧张时期，捐赠“长照社区整合服务中心—<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%8C%E5%8A%A8%E8%BD%A6.md#1">行动车</a>”、门诊部“升降平台”及眼科“超音波白内障乳化仪”等<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%BB%E7%96%97%E5%99%A8%E6%9D%90.md#1">医疗器材</a>，深感谢意及感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13083635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13083635" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083635-506545-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13083635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083635-506545-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13083635" class="wp-caption-text">长照社区整合服务中心—行动车。（基隆市立医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市议员童子玮说，市立医院日益进步，特别在疫情期间，全力发挥医院的量能，协助筛检及疫苗施打，使命必达，令人敬佩！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13083636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13083636" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083636-506546-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13083636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083636-506546-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13083636" class="wp-caption-text">眼科“超音波白内障乳化仪”。（基隆市立医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基隆市立医院自2018年3月开办“社区整合服务中心”，提供需要长期照顾的民众、照顾者及家属多项资源连结专业的服务。有了庆安宫捐赠的“行动服务车”及医疗器材，可以将基隆妈慈悲救苦的大愿化为行动，提供市民更便利迅速优质的服务！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13083637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13083637" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083637-506547-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13083637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13083637-506547-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13083637" class="wp-caption-text">门诊部“升降平台”。（基隆市立医院提供）</figcaption></figure>
