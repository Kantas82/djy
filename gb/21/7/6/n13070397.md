<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>独立日庆典 南加民众向英雄致敬</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070517-IMG_1359-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>7月4日，安纳罕山社区独立日游行起点，两辆消防车用长臂高高地举起了美国国旗。（李梅／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月06日讯】（大纪元记者李梅美国加州橙县报导）封闭一年多后，<ahref="https://github.com/tngshp365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E5%8A%A0%E6%B0%91%E4%BC%97.md#1">南加民众</a>在7月4日纷纷走出家门，参与游行、音乐会、烧烤聚会、放烟花等各种活动，开心庆祝美国第245个独立日。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13070531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070531-IMG_1362.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070531-IMG_1362-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070531" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日安纳罕山社区独立日游行，盛装的民众在警队游行队伍中间。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070577" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070577-IMG_1360.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070577-IMG_1360-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070577" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，独立日游行，阿纳海姆山市的警队游行队伍。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070578-IMG_1361.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070578-IMG_1361-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070578" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日民众观看独立日游行。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070580" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070580" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070580-IMG_1368.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070580-IMG_1368-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070580" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，阿纳海姆山市独立日游行队伍。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>橙县<ahref="https://github.com/tngshp365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E7%BA%B3%E7%BD%95%E5%B8%82%E5%AE%89%E7%BA%B3%E7%BD%95%E5%B1%B1.md#1">安纳罕市安纳罕山</a>（Anaheim Hills）的<ahref="https://github.com/tngshp365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">庆祝活动</a>由社区委员会主持，从4日早7点开始举行跑步和步行比赛，然后是宠物狗装饰展、大游行，下午在佩拉尔塔峡谷公园（Peralta Canyon Park）有音乐会及烧烤活动，直到晚上放烟花。</p>
<p>组委会号召民众穿上爱国服装、带上旗帜参加比赛与游行。当天不止是选手，居民特别是很多小孩也穿上了红、白、蓝三色组合的节日盛装。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13070534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070534-IMG_1385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070534-IMG_1385-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070534" class="wp-caption-text">安纳罕山社区的游行队伍向居民送礼物。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070538-IMG_1415.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070538-IMG_1415-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070538" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，阿纳海姆山市独立日游行。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午1点，大游行从峡谷高中开始，沿着圣安娜峡谷路行进到桉树公园（Eucalyptus Park），全程约1.2英里，很多居民兴高采烈地坐在路旁等待。今年的游行以“向现役和退伍军人致敬”为主题，队伍中有很多退伍军人的车辆，还有警车、消防车等，很有气势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13070544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070544-IMG_1407.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070544-IMG_1407-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070544" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，安纳罕山社区独立日游行。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070574-IMG_1356.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070574-IMG_1356-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070574" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日独立日游行，南加州阿纳海姆山市的警队游行队伍。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070582-IMG_1373.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070582-IMG_1373-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070582" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，阿纳海姆山市独立日游行的童子军队伍。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070596-IMG_1392.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070596-IMG_1392-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070596" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，阿纳海姆山市独立日游行队伍。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070585" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070585-IMG_1374.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070585-IMG_1374-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070585" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，游行队伍向居民送礼物。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070589" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070589-IMG_1376.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070589-IMG_1376-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070589" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，游行队伍向居民送礼物。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居民安迪（Andy）表示：“我喜欢看那种几十人的大队列或大乐队的行进，不过，今年能举办这个游行活动已经很好了。”</p>
<p>临近的约巴琳达（Yorba Linda）市也有<ahref="https://github.com/tngshp365/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">庆祝活动</a>，居民自带音响播放着爱国歌曲。居民霍姆斯（Sharon Holmes）女士表示，自己“我最开心双手挥动美国国旗”。</p>
<p>居民弗罗兰（Steven Froland）双手高举一面大国旗，在车库前开心挥舞。他说：“今天是美国独立自由的日子，这一天很重要；我们民众不希望被政府所控制，权力在于人民。”他还说，自己去报名服役时，越战已结束，“我愿意为美国而战”。</p>
<p>晚8点左右，佩拉尔塔峡谷公园的大草坪上坐满了人，大家边观赏音乐会，边等待看烟花。附近居民则在自家草地上邀集亲友开派对，边烧烤、玩投掷游戏，边看烟花，每当投中一个包，还大喊一声“自由”（Freedom）；也有人举杯向路人高喊：“干杯！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13070550" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070550-IMG_1481.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070550" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070550-IMG_1481-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070550" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，在佩拉尔塔公园（Peralta Park）的烟火秀。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13070557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13070557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070557-IMG_1495.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13070557" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13070557-IMG_1495-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13070557" class="wp-caption-text">7月4日，在佩拉尔塔公园（Peralta Park）的烟火秀。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园附近的公路旁也停了长长的车队，人们拿出折叠椅坐在车旁等待放烟花。一位87岁的女士很开心女儿带她前来，来自施放烟花城市的一个家庭，还用童车推来了1岁的女儿。</p>
<p>受邀嘉宾、橙县检察官托德·斯皮策（Todd Spitzer）在放烟花前的致辞中，号召民众理解并尊重美国自由的最大象征——美国国旗，并感谢保护民众自由、美好生活的现役军人，以及阿纳海姆山市的“第一响应者”——警队和消防员。“让我们互相尊重，捍卫自由。”他说。</p>
<p>组委会还在草地旁竖立了该市六位军人烈士的照片和简介，不少居民前来向他们致敬。</p>
<p>1776年7月4日，北美大陆会议正式通过了《独立宣言》，北美13个殖民地联合起来宣布独立于大英帝国，成为一个独立自由的国家。自1776年以来，人们每年都庆祝7月4日，但直到1870年，它才正式成为联邦假日。◇</p>
