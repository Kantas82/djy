<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>包心菜，包裹着最纯朴的医病关系</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061118-503741-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>包心菜故事1。（中医四物所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年07月01日讯】我家是<ahref="https://github.com/rskgqd324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>诊所，也许因为地点离市中心较远，来这里找我爸看诊的患者，很多是隔壁邻居，跟我爸妈互动都非常的热络，这可能就是所谓乡下人的<ahref="https://github.com/rskgqd324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E6%83%85.md#1">热情</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13061118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13061118" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061118-503741.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13061118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061118-503741-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13061118" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rskgqd324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E5%BF%83%E8%8F%9C.md#1">包心菜</a>故事1。（<ahref="https://github.com/rskgqd324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13061121" aria-describedby="caption-13061121" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061121-503742-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061121-503742-450x450.jpg" alt="包心菜故事2。" width="499" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13061121" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rskgqd324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E5%BF%83%E8%8F%9C.md#1">包心菜</a>故事2。（中医四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13061122" aria-describedby="caption-13061122" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061122-503743-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061122-503743-450x450.jpg" alt="包心菜故事3。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13061122" class="wp-caption-text">包心菜故事3。（中医四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们常常会拿自家栽种的蔬菜水果或是去市场采买还会“顺便”买一些食物帮我们家加菜，在我的角度来看，很多患者都是我非常熟悉对我相当亲切的叔叔阿姨，他们会隔三差五往家里送很多很棒的食材，常让我妈很不好意思，对我妈来说看诊只是我爸的本分，没道理一直接受别人的好处，而那些叔叔阿姨，总是反复的说多亏我爸能够帮他们解除病痛疾患，所以才能够有好的身体去从事那些体力活，所以老想跟我们家分享。</p>
<figure id="13061127" aria-describedby="caption-13061127" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061127-503903-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061127-503903-450x450.jpg" alt="包心菜故事4。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13061127" class="wp-caption-text">包心菜故事4。（中医四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13061124" aria-describedby="caption-13061124" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061124-503744-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061124-503744-450x450.jpg" alt="包心菜故事5。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13061124" class="wp-caption-text">包心菜故事5。（中医四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在这种医病关系良好的环境长大的我，看到前阵子攻击医护的新闻感到震惊又难过生气，除了对肇事者的态度感到愤怒外，也对受伤的医护感到不舍、惋惜，但这时候除了谴责做错事的人外，还想跟大家分享我家的一点小事，希望这看似很微不足道的温暖可以星火燎原，让大家对台湾大多数的人还抱有信心，希望人跟人之间善的能量不会因为疫情就无法继续传播。</p>
<figure id="13061126" aria-describedby="caption-13061126" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061126-503745-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13061126-503745-450x450.jpg" alt="包心菜故事6。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13061126" class="wp-caption-text">包心菜故事6。（中医四物所提供）</figcaption></figure>
