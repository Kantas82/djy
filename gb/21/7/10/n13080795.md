<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西南部房产日趋受欢迎 丹麦镇浏览量居首</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月10日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者</span><span lang="zh-TW">杨新云</span><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">西澳房地产</a>协会</span></span>(REIWA)<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的数据显示，西澳丹麦（</span></span>Denmark<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）小镇的房产备受向往安静清幽的生活的买家的欢迎。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">从年初到六月底，在<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">西澳房地产</a>协会网站</span><span lang="zh-TW">——</span></span>reiwa.com<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">搜索丹麦小镇房产的浏览量为</span></span>25,098<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次，几乎是<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E6%A0%BC%E4%B8%BD%E7%89%B9%E6%B2%B3.md#1">玛格丽特河</a>（</span></span>Margaret River<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）房产浏览量（</span></span>13,233<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次）的两倍。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">但是，这个安静的乡村小镇似乎还没有超越<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E6%A0%BC%E4%B8%BD%E7%89%B9%E6%B2%B3.md#1">玛格丽特河</a>、邓斯堡（</span></span>Dunsborough<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）或巴瑟尔顿（</span></span>Busselton<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">），成为那些想远离喧嚣城市生活而搬到一个更加安静和放松的海边小镇的人们的理想之地。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在这六个月里，丹麦有</span></span>83<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">宗房产交易，使该镇房产价格今年增长了</span></span>15.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">玛格丽特河的人口是丹麦镇的三倍，其房产销售量几乎是丹麦镇的三倍，今年上半年有</span></span>237<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">套房屋易手，促成了</span></span>12.4%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的年度价格上涨。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">邓斯堡的房产的网上浏览量为</span></span>16,817<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次，在同期录得</span></span>265<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">笔交易，今年价格上涨</span></span>9.8%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳西南部的其它地区的房产浏览量依次为：</span></span>Busselton<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>15,956<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次）、</span></span>Bunbury<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>13,246<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次）和</span></span>Albany<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>12,965<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">次）。</span></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
