<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东奥7·23：韩女子射箭夺前三 20岁安山破奥纪录</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110458-content_GettyImages-1234120118-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东奥今（7月23日）继续举行第三天赛事，韩女选手于射箭场上大放光芒。图为20岁的韩国代表安山。(ADEK BERRY/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p style="text-align: left;">【大纪元2021年07月23日讯】（大纪元记者黄家熙香港报导）东京<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E8%BF%90.md#1">奥运</a>今天（23日）进入第三天赛事，于当地时间早上九时，一共64名选手参与女子<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%84%E7%AE%AD.md#1">射箭</a>项目，赛事于<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E4%B9%8B%E5%B2%9B%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">梦之岛公园</a>排名赛会场进行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13110467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13110467" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110467-805f51a71006b07d8d658a2b953876d7.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13110467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110467-805f51a71006b07d8d658a2b953876d7-600x391.png" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13110467" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E8%BF%90.md#1">奥运</a>女子个人<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%84%E7%AE%AD.md#1">射箭</a>比赛历年赛果。（来源：奥运会官方网站／大纪元制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>结果由韩国代表霸占前三位，年仅20岁的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%B1%B1.md#1">安山</a>以680分获最高排名，破1996年由乌克兰选手Herasymenko所创的673分奥运纪录。安山一共射出了36支10分箭，当中有10支正中红心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13110460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13110460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110460-content_GettyImages-1330122845.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13110460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110460-content_GettyImages-1330122845-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13110460" class="wp-caption-text">东奥今（7月23日）继续举行第三天赛事，韩女选手于射箭场上大放光芒。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13110461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13110461" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110461-content_GettyImages-1330132631.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13110461" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110461-content_GettyImages-1330132631-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13110461" class="wp-caption-text">东奥今（7月23日）继续举行第三天赛事，韩国代表霸占前三位，于射箭场上大放光芒。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另俩名韩国选手张敏熙与姜彩瑛分别得分677与675，均破奥运纪录，而姜彩瑛乃现今世界纪录（692分）保持者。韩国以外得分最高的为墨西哥选手Valencia（674分），而日本代表山内梓则排名第七（665分，个人最佳成绩）、中国杨晓蕾排名十四（654分）、中华台北林佳恩排行二十一（651分，赛季最佳成绩）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13110459" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13110459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110459-content_GettyImages-1234120488.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13110459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110459-content_GettyImages-1234120488-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13110459" class="wp-caption-text">东奥今（7月23日）继续举行第三天赛事，韩女选手于射箭场上大放光芒。图为现今世界纪录保持者、韩国代表姜彩瑛。(ADEK BERRY/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛中每人总共发72箭，满分720。明天（24日）将进行首届登场的混合团体赛，并将产生第一面射箭金牌。</p>
<p>由于东京天气炎热，俄罗斯选手Gomboeva（630分，第四十五位）在排名赛结束后疑因太热中暑昏倒，幸而并无大碍。据俄箭队成员Perova（664分，第八位）透露，当时自己正在与队友议论赛果，岂料回头一看Gomboeva忽然昏倒在地。@</p>
<figure id="attachment_13110462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13110462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110462-content_GettyImages-1330140373.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13110462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13110462-content_GettyImages-1330140373-600x386.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13110462" class="wp-caption-text">由于东京天气炎热，俄罗斯选手Gomboeva（630分，第四十五位）在排名赛结束后疑因太热中暑昏倒。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
