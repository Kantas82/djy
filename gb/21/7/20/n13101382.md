<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七月的加东四省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（1）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101396-20210720-canada_helen_chou_105-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>佩姬湾小渔村。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>都说七月的<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">加东</a>四省美出天际。两个年纪加起来超过100岁的闺蜜临时起意，决定扔下娃与另一半，亲自去看一看。两个人花了半天时间在书店里翻看资料，再上网查询电话预约，几小时后一场说走就走的<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E9%A9%BE.md#1">自驾</a>游行程表就出炉了。不过我们可不是《末路狂花》里的赛尔玛和路易丝哦。我们是奔向快乐与远方的潇洒俏大妈。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">加东</a>四省是指<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">加拿大</a>在大西洋海岸的四个省：新斯科舍(Nova Scotia)，新不伦瑞克(New Brunswick)，<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BE%B7%E5%8D%8E%E7%8E%8B%E5%AD%90%E5%B2%9B.md#1">爱德华王子岛</a>(Prince Edward Island，简称PEI)，和<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0%E4%B8%8E%E6%8B%89%E5%B8%83%E6%8B%89%E5%A4%9A.md#1">纽芬兰与拉布拉多</a>(Newfoundland and Labrador)。这四个省是加拿大面积最小的四个省，并且前三个省的总面积都没有纽芬兰与拉布拉多的大。相对加拿大其它省份来说，这四省较落后贫困，但自然环境优美，基本都是原汁原味的，少有人工雕琢。这也是极吸引我们的地方。</p>
<p>我们前一天从多伦多飞到新斯科舍省的省会城市哈利法克斯(Halifax)。这是<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7.md#1">加拿大</a>唯一在大西洋的不冻港，气候适宜，是加拿大第二温暖的城市。那里有许多值得一游的景点。我们因为要赶去纽芬兰的轮渡，所以没有进城游玩。</p>
<p>今天一大早我们开车去哈利法克斯城外西南方40多公里的一个小渔村佩姬湾。别看是个小渔村，佩姬湾名气很响，景色壮观秀丽。那里的灯塔是一个著名的景点，加拿大许多风景明信片上都有它的照片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101389-20210720-canada_helen_chou_102.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101389-20210720-canada_helen_chou_102-450x639.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="639" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101389" class="wp-caption-text">佩姬湾灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场看的确很有气势。灯塔建于1914年，坐落在一大片花岗岩上，气势雄伟。这些巨大的花岗岩是由4亿多年的冰川漂砾形成的，然后被庞大的冰块顶出海面，再飘洋过海，冰块溶化后留在了陆地上。漫长的岁月里，风刀在岩石上刻下了一道道深沟，海水又将岩石表面冲刷的光滑平整。一步千万年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101394" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101394" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101394-20210720-canada_helen_chou_103.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101394" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101394-20210720-canada_helen_chou_103-450x618.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="618" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101394" class="wp-caption-text">捕鱼用的绳索与漂浮物。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佩姬湾尽量保持原生态，渔村风味极浓。很多房屋看上去很有岁月感，但维修的不错。色彩各异，但看上去又很协调。可以想像在夕阳的余晖里是多么美的一幅画。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101395-20210720-canada_helen_chou_104.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13101395" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101395-20210720-canada_helen_chou_104-600x433.jpg" alt="加拿大, 佩姬湾" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101395" class="wp-caption-text">佩姬湾小渔村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13101396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101396-20210720-canada_helen_chou_105.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13101396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101396-20210720-canada_helen_chou_105-600x406.jpg" alt="加拿大, 佩姬湾" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101396" class="wp-caption-text">佩姬湾小渔村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13101398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101398-20210720-canada_helen_chou_106.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101398-20210720-canada_helen_chou_106-450x579.jpg" alt="加拿大，教堂" width="450" b="579" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101398" class="wp-caption-text">圣约翰圣公会教堂(St. John&#8217;s Anglican Church)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是佩姬湾唯一的一座教堂，木匠哥特式的圣约翰圣公会教堂，建于1934年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101399" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101399-20210720-canada_helen_chou_107.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13101399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101399-20210720-canada_helen_chou_107-450x326.jpg" alt="加拿大, 佩姬湾" width="598" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101399" class="wp-caption-text">佩姬湾小渔村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果不是赶时间，我是极愿意坐在这椅子上，面朝大海，看云卷云舒，看潮涨潮落，看金乌西坠，看玉兔东升……</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101404-20210720-canada_helen_chou_108.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101404-20210720-canada_helen_chou_108-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大, 佩姬湾" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101404" class="wp-caption-text">野花爬满坡的佩姬湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13101406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101406" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101406-20210720-canada_helen_chou_109.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101406-20210720-canada_helen_chou_109-450x275.jpg" alt="加拿大, 佩姬湾" width="450" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101406" class="wp-caption-text">野花爬满坡的佩姬湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>或者赤着脚，在野花丛中走过，或躺在绿草上闻一闻边上野花的香味……</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101409-20210720-canada_helen_chou_110.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101409-20210720-canada_helen_chou_110-450x633.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="633" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101409" class="wp-caption-text">吹苏格兰风笛的老帅哥。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>或者听听老帅哥吹一曲《绿袖子》。</p>
<p>可是我们还得往下一站赶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101410-20210720-canada_helen_chou_111.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101410-20210720-canada_helen_chou_111-450x625.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="625" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101410" class="wp-caption-text">长须鲸的颚骨。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>按出发前的行程安排，我们还要去佩姬湾往西一小时左右的世界遗产渔港小镇卢嫩堡(Lunenburg)看看。说实话有点失望。除了上面照片上那个长须鲸(fin whale)颚骨外，其它都乏善可陈。</p>
<p>倒是途中经过的另一个小镇马宏湾(Mahone Bay)更合我心意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101412-20210720-canada_helen_chou_112.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101412-20210720-canada_helen_chou_112-450x616.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101412" class="wp-caption-text">马宏湾街边小屋。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>时间允许的话，很想在这样的小屋里过一夜，哪怕就坐下来喝杯咖啡都满足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101413-20210720-canada_helen_chou_113.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101413-20210720-canada_helen_chou_113-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101413" class="wp-caption-text">马宏湾街边的老爷车。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我甚至动念想开着这辆拉风的老爷车继续我们的<ahref="https://github.com/smhdla347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E9%A9%BE.md#1">自驾</a>游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13101416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101416" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101416-20210720-canada_helen_chou_114.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101416-20210720-canada_helen_chou_114-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101416" class="wp-caption-text">精致的白镴器皿。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13101422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13101422" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101422-20210720-canada_helen_chou_115.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13101422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13101422-20210720-canada_helen_chou_115-450x572.jpg" alt="加拿大" width="450" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13101422" class="wp-caption-text">精致的白镴器皿。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>镇上一家手工艺品小店里的白镴器皿也让我爱不释手。可是一想旅途刚开始，还是先悠着点吧。否则一路买买买，行李都没地方放了。</p>
<p>斗争了半天空着手离开了马宏湾，一路向东横跨新斯科舍岛，5个半小时后到达了港口小镇悉尼(Sydney)。对，是悉尼！不过不是澳洲那个悉尼。这是新斯科舍省东岸的港口小镇，明天我们就将在此坐船去纽芬兰。</p>
