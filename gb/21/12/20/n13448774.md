<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>普通平底锅就能做的“圣诞树松饼 ”亲子同乐！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448788-P.74-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>以杉树为灵感而诞生的圣诞树松饼！（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong><ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">松饼</a>不难做、步骤也简单，但要做得外型立体漂亮、口感松软细腻，才是厉害！学会“基础美式松饼”与“舒芙蕾松饼”两种基本面糊作法后，只要稍微改变一下材料，就能宛如魔法般变出各种风味的松饼。<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣诞节</a>即将到来，跟孩子在家做<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">圣诞树松饼</a>，增添过节氛围吧！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">圣诞树松饼</a></span></h2>
<p>以杉树为灵感而诞生的圣诞树<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">松饼</a>！如果只是用加了抹茶粉的面糊烤出绿色松饼，那就太普通了，因此我刻意把绿色做在打发鲜奶油上。松饼、水果、绿色鲜奶油，妆点出满满的耶诞氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448788-P.74.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13448788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448788-P.74-450x616.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448788" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p><ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a> <u>????????????????????????????????????????????? </u>?8颗</p>

<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>前置准备</strong></span></p>
<p>?低筋面粉、无铝泡打粉、上白糖混合后过筛。<br />
?鸡蛋、鲜奶、无糖原味优格在开始制作前10分钟，从冰箱取出备用。<br />
?水果充分洗净后拭干水汽。<br />
?鲜奶油在使用前都先放在冰箱冷藏。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h3>
<p>1.取一颗<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a>不切蒂头，直接对半直切（装饰用），其他草莓切除蒂头后，直切成四等分。黄桃切成约1cm大小的丁状。水果切好后封保鲜膜，放冰箱冷藏降温。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448789" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448789-P.75-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448789-P.75-1-450x269.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="359" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448789" class="wp-caption-text">水果除了顶端装饰的以外，切成小块会比较好夹进去。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.煎松饼。参考以下2个步骤，做出从第一片到第六片，面积越来越小的六片松饼。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>?</strong><strong>混合面糊</strong></span></p>
<p>1）将混合过筛的低筋面粉、无铝泡打粉、上白糖，放入大的调理盆中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448782" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448782" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448782-P.72-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448782-P.72-1-450x517.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448782" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2）在另一个调理盆中，放入鸡蛋、鲜奶、无糖原味优格后，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448783" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448783-P.72-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448783-P.72-2-450x515.jpg" alt="" width="348" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448783" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3）将2的液态材料分次少量倒入1的盆中混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448784" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448784-P.72-3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448784-P.72-3-450x598.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448784" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4）搅拌到质地均匀、没有粉末颗粒感即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448785" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448785-P.72-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448785-P.72-4-450x553.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448785" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>?</strong><strong>煎松饼</strong></span></p>
<p>1）开小火或是设定温度160～170℃，在预热好的平底锅中倒入直径约12cm 的面糊（约全部的五分之一），煎到表面冒出小凹洞后翻面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448786" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448786-P.72-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448786-P.72-5-450x553.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448786" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2）维持小火或160～170℃续煎3～4分钟后，用竹签戳刺确认熟度，没有沾黏面糊即可取出，再煎剩下的五片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448787" style="width: 347px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448787-P.72-6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448787-P.72-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="347" b="463" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448787" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.制作抹茶打发鲜奶油。在调理盆中放入鲜奶油、细砂糖、抹茶粉①，用电动搅拌器打发到举起搅拌棒时，鲜奶油前端有小尖角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13448790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13448790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448790-P.75-2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13448790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13448790-P.75-2.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="528" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13448790" class="wp-caption-text">以杉树为灵感的绿色鲜奶油，是带有些许苦味的大人口味。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将抹茶打发鲜奶油装入装有花嘴的挤花袋中。</p>
<p>5..将最大片的松饼放到盘上，摆上切小块的草莓、黄桃后，挤上抹茶打发鲜奶油。</p>
<p>6..接着叠放第二大片的松饼，一样放上草莓、黄桃，挤上抹茶打发鲜奶油。然后依照同样方式，依序叠上其他四片松饼，最上层的松饼最小片。</p>
<p>7.在松饼顶端放上1个有蒂头的切半草莓和3颗蓝莓，其他放到盘上装饰。</p>
<p>8.插入圣诞装饰，接着在整体撒上抹茶粉②就完成了。⊙</p>
