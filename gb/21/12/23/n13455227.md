<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>岁末祈平安 台东县多处特色耶诞树点灯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455229-533533-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东县政府耶诞树前设置TAITUNG，方便游客拍照打卡。（台东县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>报导）一年一度的耶诞节即将来临，台东县从市区到乡镇多处<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%B6%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91.md#1">耶诞树</a>纷纷点亮，在冬日里展现温暖的耶诞气氛。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">台东</a>县政府广场设置粉红<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%B6%E8%AF%9E%E6%A0%91.md#1">耶诞树</a>，广场周边设置梦幻光廊隧道，还有TAITUNG立体字样，方便游客拍照打卡。伴随着温暖又轻快的耶诞歌曲，2022年2月28日前天天浪漫点灯，希望减缓疫情带来的焦虑，期盼明年美好平安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13455230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455230-533534.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455230-533534-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455230" class="wp-caption-text">台东县政府广场周边设置梦幻光廊隧道。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13455231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455231-533535.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455231-533535-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455231" class="wp-caption-text">台东县政府前的粉红耶诞树。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东客家学堂今年首次推出圣诞艺术装置，有别于西洋风，客家学堂以浓浓客家风灯笼打造隧道光廊，搭配可爱麋鹿和萤火虫灯，到2021年2月20日，天天浪漫点灯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13455232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455232-533536.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455232-533536-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455232" class="wp-caption-text">台东客家学堂的圣诞艺术装置。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13455234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455234-533538.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455234-533538-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455234" class="wp-caption-text">台东客家学堂的客家风灯笼隧道光廊。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>具有浓浓原住民风味的工艺圣诞树则在台东波浪屋前点亮，圣诞树运用枯木作主干，代表原住民族对于这片土地的情感，外层衬托的七条染布象征台东7大原民族群，染布素材均使用台东常见的植物染色而成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13455236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455236-533568.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455236-533568-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455236" class="wp-caption-text">台东波浪屋前的原住民工艺圣诞树。（台东县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台东县延平乡公所近日在公所旁竖起高达15公尺的圣诞树，夜空中闪烁的灯光，展现浓浓的圣诞节氛围。乡公所表示，全球各地受到疫情冲击，人们充满焦虑，矗立在乡间的这棵耶诞树，希望带给民众喜乐、祝福与温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13455235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13455235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455235-533539.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13455235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13455235-533539-600x458.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13455235" class="wp-caption-text">台东县延平乡公所旁高达15公尺的圣诞树。（延平乡公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
