<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【泰式料理】九层塔鸡肉绿咖喱 香气浓郁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430562-curry-vert-poulet-250-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>九层塔鸡肉绿咖喱搭配糯米饭一起吃更好吃！（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：南亚风味的菜肴总是让人联想起炙热的阳光和热情的人们，尤其是在疫情期间特别让人向往，因为人和人之间的联系频率减少，到户外的机会也随之降低。现在，“宅”在家就可以把温暖和温馨带进来，自己动手下厨做“<ahref="https://github.com/ovcnhe396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%BC%8F%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">泰式料理</a>”一点都不困难。<ahref="https://github.com/ovcnhe396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1.md#1">绿咖喱</a>、椰奶等入菜就很有泰国味，而且只要4样食材就可以做出来一道菜来，非常简单方便！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">九层塔鸡肉<ahref="https://github.com/ovcnhe396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1.md#1">绿咖喱</a></span></h2>
<p>份量：4人份<br />
准备时间：5分钟<br />
烹调时间：20分钟</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430571" style="width: 283px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430571-ingredients-curry-vert-poulet-258.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13430571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430571-ingredients-curry-vert-poulet-258-450x495.jpg" alt="" width="283" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430571" class="wp-caption-text">想要让食物有泰国味，加入一点绿咖喱酱吧！辛辣芳香却浓郁的说不出什么味道，就是这样让人难以捉摸的好吃。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>鸡柳条 400公克<br />
绿咖喱酱 1汤匙<br />
椰奶或椰浆 450毫升<br />
九层塔 10小节</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430570" style="width: 289px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430570-ingredients-curry-vert-poulet-255.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13430570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430570-ingredients-curry-vert-poulet-255-450x409.jpg" alt="" width="289" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430570" class="wp-caption-text">九层塔准备10小节。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将鸡柳条切块。</p>
<p>2. 在一个厚底锅里加热绿咖喱酱，接着倒入椰奶煮滚，再将火关小，之后放入鸡肉块。将锅盖半盖上后，让它以小火炖煮15～18分钟，炖煮时加入一半份量的九层塔。</p>
<p>3. 起锅前加入另外一半份量的九层塔，并尽早食用。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13426175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13426175" style="width: 120px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426175-eb8f32c01c11a03dae4ca6a52fc130dc.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13426175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426175-eb8f32c01c11a03dae4ca6a52fc130dc-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="120" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13426175" class="wp-caption-text">《4样食材做泰国菜》书封。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
