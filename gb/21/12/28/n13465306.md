<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州必去二十大景点介绍（三）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465795-143789-56-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Mornington Peninsula。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月29日讯】（大纪元记者沈诗昂墨尔本编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>素有“花园之州”的美誉，这里不仅有壮丽的自然景观，也有许多著名的人文<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。如果玩遍以下这二十个<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%83%9C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">旅游胜地</a>，相信大家一定会对这座美丽的花园之州有个非常深入的了解。</p>
<p><strong>11、格兰屏山区（The Grampians）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13465727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13465727" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465727-Grampians.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13465727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465727-Grampians-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13465727" class="wp-caption-text">The Grampians。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>格兰屏山区位于<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>西北部，距离墨尔本约三个小时车程。您可以从Halls Gap启程，一路领略格兰屏连绵起伏的<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E8%84%89.md#1">山脉</a>，欣赏澳洲土着的岩石壁画，品味山区美味新鲜的农产品以及屡获大奖的葡萄酒。</p>
<p><strong>12、吉普斯兰（Gippsland）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13465747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13465747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465747-2364311f50ebb8946ce9f8c22758eca0.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13465747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465747-2364311f50ebb8946ce9f8c22758eca0-600x418.png" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13465747" class="wp-caption-text">Gippsland。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吉普斯兰是维州东部一片辽阔、安静的土地。这里有未遭破坏的<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>、天然的湖泊、绵延起伏的农田、古朴典雅的海滨城镇、依山而建的村庄，以及纯朴好客的居民。</p>
<p><strong>13、莫宁顿半岛（Mornington Peninsula）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13465762" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13465762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465762-146895-56.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13465762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465762-146895-56-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13465762" class="wp-caption-text">Mornington Peninsula。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在田园诗般的莫宁顿半岛，有许多值得体验的事情——海边漫步、游泳潜水、逛展览馆、打高尔夫球、泡温泉、品美酒、尝佳肴，从墨尔本开车到莫宁顿半岛只需一小时三十分钟。</p>
<p><strong>14、奥特韦斯（The Otways）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13465767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13465767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465767-bf893a75a51d8d06a1a42ded434788b1.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-13465767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465767-bf893a75a51d8d06a1a42ded434788b1-600x448.png" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13465767" class="wp-caption-text">Erskine Falls。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奥特韦斯是大洋路上的必经之地，有些人喜欢在游完大洋路后，从这里绕回墨尔本。这里有大片的热带雨林和灌木丛，厄斯金瀑布（Erskine Falls）是许多游客流连忘返的<ahref="https://github.com/qqvukm3983/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>，值得一看。</p>
<p><strong>15、吉朗和贝拉林半岛（Geelong and the Bellarine Peninsula）</strong></p>
<p>吉朗如今已是一座文化氛围浓厚的小城，距离墨尔本仅一小时车程。当地有很多知名的餐厅、展览馆和精品店。沿着吉朗继续往南开三十分钟的车，就来到了贝拉林半岛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13465785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13465785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465785-geelong-waterfront_gb_r_149233_1150x863.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13465785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13465785-geelong-waterfront_gb_r_149233_1150x863-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13465785" class="wp-caption-text">Geelong。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贝拉林半岛拥有迷人的海湾、诱人的美食，大片的葡萄园和世界一流的高尔夫球场。</p>
