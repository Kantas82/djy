<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—闲来看青山（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13464408-fc44b6b5d1a7a75c3f2491c131319159-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—闲来看青山（彩墨）。70×70cm。（局部）。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%B2%E6%9D%A5%E7%9C%8B%E9%9D%92%E5%B1%B1.md#1">闲来看青山</a><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</h2>
<p>画画绝不能受“规范”限制，这点和书法有很大的不同。有一个写书法的人每次联展都写一张很大的草书“畅怀”，写来写去，永远都在畅怀。但画画的人如果展相同或类似的作品，马上有人会指责他：“毫无创意”。</p>
<p>尤有甚者，书法多了一点、少了一撇都不行，如草书“中”，在左下边少了这么一提，就变成“巾”字了，这算是写错字了，写错字是不被允许的。画画不同喔，多画一只飞鸟，少画一艘船，甚至少了一座山都没关系，自由自在，不必管它什么“规范”的存在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13464408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13464408" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13464408-fc44b6b5d1a7a75c3f2491c131319159.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13464408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13464408-fc44b6b5d1a7a75c3f2491c131319159.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="577" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13464408" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%868.md#1">徐明义画集8</a>—<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%B2%E6%9D%A5%E7%9C%8B%E9%9D%92%E5%B1%B1.md#1">闲来看青山</a><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。70×70cm。（图片来源：徐明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Looking at mountain at leisure/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Painting must not be restricted by &#8220;norm&#8221;, that very differs from calligraphy. There is a calligrapher who usually writes a big paper in cursive style with joyful at the joint exhibition, always joyfully writing and writing.? However, if the painter who draws the same or similar article, he will be appointed out immediately and accused &#8220;no innovation&#8221;.</p>
<p>Even worse accusation, a little bit more or a little bit less in writing calligraphy is not allowed, for instance, the cursive style &#8220;中 Chung&#8221; less a bit in the lower left, it becomes a &#8220;巾towel&#8221;, so this is a typo, which is not allowed. Drawing a paint is different, a bird more, a less boat or even a less mountain that does not matter, leisurely and carefree, no &#8220;norms&#8221; exists.@</p>
