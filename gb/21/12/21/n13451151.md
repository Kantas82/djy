<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拜访圣诞老人之家 不用到北极！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451168-01_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-9cea50-1024x683-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>圣诞老人的家：斜坡屋顶、大烟囱和原木风格。（Zillow提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月22日讯】文：李欧?大纪元<br />
圣诞节又将到来！我们都知道<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA.md#1">圣诞老人</a>住在<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E6%9E%81.md#1">北极</a>，但有人看过圣诞老人的家吗？我想应该没人见过吧！很有趣的是，房地产线上公司Zillow为了满足大家的心愿，把圣诞老人之家“出卖”了！你猜它值多少钱？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13451171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451171-02_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-c7d321-1024x683.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13451171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451171-02_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-c7d321-1024x683-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451171" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%80%81%E4%BA%BA.md#1">圣诞老人</a>的客厅。（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在上Zillow Listing就能入屋观赏，不过圣诞老人之家只能看不能卖。而且，因为<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%86%A0%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">新冠病毒</a>的缘故，我们发现圣诞老人之家多了几样不同以往的物品，例如：体温计、手部消毒液；还有为了消磨时光的拼图、脚踏健身车；圣诞老人还多了一台笔记本电脑，当然是用来视讯了；不意外，克劳斯夫人也爱上了烘焙饼干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13451172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451172-03_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-820d30-1024x683.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-13451172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451172-03_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-820d30-1024x683-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451172" class="wp-caption-text">专属克劳斯夫人的美食厨房。（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13451173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451173-04_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-d50ddd-1024x683.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13451173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451173-04_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-d50ddd-1024x683-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451173" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老人的工作室。（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从Zillow Listing上看到，圣诞老人之家坐落在25英亩的北国田园风光中，包含小<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E7%81%B5.md#1">精灵</a>住宅社区、最先进的玩具制造设施、可容纳全天候雪橇的车库和可容纳 8只<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AF%E9%B9%BF.md#1">驯鹿</a>的马厩，以及红鼻子公司！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13451174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451174-05_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-6fb70c-1024x683.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13451174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451174-05_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-6fb70c-1024x683-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451174" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老人的卧室（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>资料显示，圣诞老人之家，建于1800年代，充满古老的魅力，但由于最近进行了翻新，所以提供了现代化设施。在客厅里有一个落地式河岩壁炉，用来烤栗子；美食厨房是面包师的梦想，拥有12种不同饼干设置的烤箱，并且饼干可直接从烤箱送到隔壁的餐桌上。</p>
<p>同时，Zillow也公布了<ahref="https://github.com/ykcpbx327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E7%81%B5.md#1">精灵</a>住所的样貌，指出没有一个精灵的家是一模一样的，除了都是可爱的小房子，每户精灵都自己的室内风格，甚至有一个喜爱中国风的精灵。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_13451175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451175-06_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-c19fbe-1024x683.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13451175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451175-06_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-c19fbe-1024x683-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451175" class="wp-caption-text">精灵之家。（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_13451177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451177-07_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-13c186-1024x683.jpeg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451177-07_2021_Santas-House-Zillow-Listing_1085x724-13c186-1024x683-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-13451177" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451177" class="wp-caption-text">有一位精灵爱好中国风家居。（Zillow提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>根据资料，圣诞老人之家被估值：103万1,401美元，而且从2020年疫情之后开始飙涨。附近没有相似可以比较的房屋。#</p>
<figure id="attachment_13451163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13451163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451163-41cfcda3c5726273e3b1232d6f3ef36d.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13451163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13451163-41cfcda3c5726273e3b1232d6f3ef36d-450x713.png" alt="" width="450" b="713" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13451163" class="wp-caption-text">圣诞老人之家小档案。资料来源：Zillow。（大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
