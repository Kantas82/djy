CCP uses its power to control news media and social media to censor any dissenting voices for speech freedom and democracy。CCP deliberately made vague laws and makes use of its law enforcement and jurisdiction to arrest and detail dissents and commit brutal physical persecution。<br />
CCP installed surveillance cameras everywhere to track everyone and applies real-time spy on text messages，phone calls，and social chat software such as Wechat and QQ to track any things threatening its ruling。All the above measures are for one thing that is reinforcing CCP&#8217;s dictatoril ruling and strike freedom of speech and democracy。One more disgusting thing is CCP uses state-owned mouse-piece medias to indoctrine and brainwash chinese people with positive and bright picture of CCP。In addition，CCP is brainwashing young chinese by enforcing relevant eduction of CCP starting from elementary school through College and Universtity。<br />
I feel disgusted by CCP&#8217;s gross hypocrisy，corruption and brutality。Even if I am in the U.S. I am afraid of speaking out to unveil the evil side of CCP because my parents，silbings and extended families are still in China and I am worried CCP will persecute my families。So，In here I anonymously announce I withdraw from evil CCP to express my support for freedom and democracy and protests against dictatorial and dark regime。<br />
