<h6>2022 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月04日讯】Trailblazer车系以往是用在中型SUV上，在北美Trailblazer SUV只在2001年到2008年之间，在之后便停产了（在其他国家仍继续生产），而在2020年Chevrolet却将Trailblazer应用在新紧凑型Crossover之上。这种使用旧有名字运用在不同车系也不是第一次，上一款是Chevrolet Equinox。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d1q5zi9q4mpi9f.cloudfront.net/H9uCBD1Kp"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sxvshi319/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/sxvshi319/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/t91FHeltt0c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a><br />
新Chevrolet Trailblazer车型细小，全车尺寸为长度173.7吋、宽度71.2吋、高度65.7吋。采用GM VSS-F底盘，离地距为8吋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416032-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_06-e1638578399835.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416032-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416032" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Chevrolet Trailblazer RS。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">测试车是Trailblazer RS型号，车身是上黑下白Two-Tone双色，并且装有车顶架，整体就是运动型，RS更独有网状前进风口栏栅，以及车尾左右双排气喉，而且有镀铬排气口端。头灯位于日间行车灯（DRL）之下，近方形头灯就像雾灯一样，而真正的雾灯隐藏于防撞器下方。RS装有四个旋风形银黑两色合金轮圈，配225/55R18轮胎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416034" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416034-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_11-e1638578420229.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416034-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416034" class="wp-caption-text">日间行车灯。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416035" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416035-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_12-e1638578430686.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416035-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416035" class="wp-caption-text">近方形头灯。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">引擎分为两种，1.2升直列三缸引擎，最大马力137匹，扭矩162磅呎，只用在LS及LT前驱版本；另一款是1.3升直列三缸引擎，最大马力155匹，扭矩174磅呎，应用在其他型号，尤其是AWD型号。变速箱方面，1.2升引擎会配备CVT变速箱，而1.3升引擎就会配备9速自动变速箱。Trailblazer RS AWD版内有AWD按键，汽车启动自动启动AWD，驾驶者可手动解除，只以前驱行车，进一步减少油耗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416037-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_23-e1638578456507.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416037-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416037" class="wp-caption-text">1.3升直列三缸引擎。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416033-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_10-e1638578410603.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416033-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416033" class="wp-caption-text">车顶行李架。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">内装方面，Trailblazer备有三幅式方向盘，上方有各种按键，除了有音响操作及巡航功能之外，也有四方向按键，用以控制仪表板上的资讯显示。RS及ACTIV型号更有电热功能，而LT则是加选项目。中控台标配7吋触屏，但可升级到8吋，内置4G/LTE热点功能，可支援Apple?Carplay及Android Auto，而且是可Wireless无线连接，另外也有Amazon Alexa。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416042-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_32-e1638578366298.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416042-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_32-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416042" class="wp-caption-text">这辆小车在载运时更有优势。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416039-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_27-e1638578483428.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416039-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416039" class="wp-caption-text">8吋触屏。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">中控台底部装有无线充电板，而在充电板后方有数个按键，其中一个可将Trailblazer标配的自动引擎熄火功能关闭，另一个是格子旗标志的按键，其实就是Sport模式。Trailblazer也备有前座双杯座及电子手刹车，而且是标配装置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416041-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_30-e1638578505678.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416041-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416041" class="wp-caption-text">底部装有无线充电板。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13416040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416040-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_29-e1638578494278.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416040-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416040" class="wp-caption-text">9速自动变速杆。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">为了令这辆小车在载运时更有优势，Trailblazer的前乘客座位是可完全折平，可载运最长达到8.5呎的东西。后方则可分为40/60折叠，最大载货量为54.4立方呎。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">最令人惊讶的是Trailblazer虽然只有一副小容积引擎，但加速绝不可小观，反应极快，在1,600转时已可达最大扭矩，9速自动变速变档时间也快，而且对减少耗油有大帮助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13416038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13416038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416038-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_26-e1638578469106.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13416038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13416038-2022_Chevrolet_Trailblazer_RS_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13416038" class="wp-caption-text">仪表板。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Trailblazer的四个轮胎接近汽车的四个角落，所以行车也很稳定，转向灵活。整体来说，Trailblazer是非常出色的小Crossover，但仍可找到两个小问题，一为后车门开启角度较小，出入不够方便，另外就是部分组件之间的间隙不一致，相比于Trailblazer其他出众的地方，也就一值一提了！◇</p>
