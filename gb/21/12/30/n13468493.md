<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州必去二十大景点介绍（四）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Lorne。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月30日讯】（大纪元记者赵琼玉墨尔本编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>素有“花园之州”的美誉，这里不仅有壮丽的自然景观，也有许多著名的人文<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。如果玩遍以下这二十个<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%83%9C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">旅游胜地</a>，相信大家一定会对这座美丽的花园之州有个深入的了解。</p>
<p><strong>16、亚拉谷（Yarra Valley）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13468511" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13468511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468511-Yarra-Valley.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13468511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468511-Yarra-Valley-600x366.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13468511" class="wp-caption-text">Yarra Valley。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚拉谷是<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>最著名、最古老的葡萄酒产区，拥有许多世界一流的酒庄。这里远山如黛、古木参天、蓝天碧水、风景不俗。</p>
<p>清晨，您可以乘坐热气球，一览亚拉谷的怡人景色。另外，还可以到当地农庄采摘和品尝新鲜的农产品。</p>
<p><strong>17、马利（Mallee）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13468517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13468517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468517-australia-gccc7ade9a_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13468517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468517-australia-gccc7ade9a_1920-600x451.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13468517" class="wp-caption-text">谷仓<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A3%81%E7%94%BB.md#1">壁画</a>。（agmclellan/Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>维州西部的马利地区是维州的农业中心，来这里游玩的游客不多。不过这里的Silo Art Trail谷仓<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A3%81%E7%94%BB.md#1">壁画</a>可谓维州最大的户外画廊，很有特色。如果您喜欢田野风光和壁画艺术，不妨来此一游。</p>
<p><strong>18、冲浪海岸（Surf Coast）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13468519" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13468519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468519-Torquay.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13468519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468519-Torquay-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13468519" class="wp-caption-text">Torquay。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冲浪海岸位于墨尔本西南75公里处，沿岸有几个著名小镇，如托基（Torquay）、安格尔希（Anglesea）、艾利入海口（Airey&#8217;s Inlet）和洛恩（Lorne）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12648473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12648473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12648473" class="wp-caption-text">Lorne。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>洛恩比较适合住宿，因为那里有一些很棒的餐厅。大洋路的起始地就位于安格尔希附近。</p>
<p><strong>19、维州高原（High Country）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13468524" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13468524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468524-High-Country-mountain-biking.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13468524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13468524-High-Country-mountain-biking-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13468524" class="wp-caption-text">High Country骑行。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>喜欢公路旅行的朋友可以到维州高原游玩，从墨尔本开车出发，途经一些美丽的小镇，四个小时后您就会来到维州高原区。</p>
<p>冬天，这里是滑雪者的天堂；夏天，这里是爬山或骑马的好地方。</p>
<p><strong>20、希尔斯维尔（Healesville）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12645181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12645181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/128105-56-e1608908138738.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/128105-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12645181" class="wp-caption-text">Healesville小镇的Healesville Sanctuary动物保护区。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>希尔斯维尔盛产葡萄酒，风景十分优美，距离墨尔本仅一个半小时车程。当地有一些著名酒庄和餐厅，热爱美酒美食的人一定不虚此行。</p>
<p>喜欢看袋鼠和考拉等<ahref="https://github.com/ycbkmj370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>的朋友还可以去希尔斯维尔自然保护区游玩。</p>
