<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州必去二十大景点介绍（一）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13461887-the-twelve-apostles-g1b0d79db7_1920-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>十二使徒岩（现在只剩下八个）是维州大洋路（Great Ocean Road）上最著名的景点，距离墨尔本大约四小时车程。（SeverS74/Pixabay）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月27日讯】（大纪元记者沈诗昂墨尔本编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>素有“花园之州”的美誉，这里不仅有壮丽的自然景观，也有许多著名的人文<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。如果玩遍以下这二十个<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%83%9C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">旅游胜地</a>，相信大家一定会对这座美丽的花园之州有更深入的了解。</p>
<p><strong>1、戴尔斯福德（Daylesford）<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89.md#1">温泉</a>和赫本斯普林斯（Hepburn Springs）温泉</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12639071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12639071" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/133036-56-e1608684811224.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12639071" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/133036-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12639071" class="wp-caption-text">Daylesford。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>共有141处天然<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B3%89.md#1">温泉</a>，其中距离墨尔本大约一个半小时车程的戴尔斯福德温泉和赫本斯普林斯温泉非常受澳洲人欢迎，这里不仅有设备齐全的水疗中心，住宿也很方便。</p>
<p><strong>2、布莱特（Bright）小镇</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12637884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bright-e1608633824364.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bright-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637884" class="wp-caption-text">Bright。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>布莱特是Victorian Alps山脚下一座风景如画的小镇，距离墨尔本大约三小时四十分钟的车程。闲暇之余，来到这里爬山、骑自行车、泛舟，品尝一下当地的葡萄酒和啤酒，会让您身心彻底放松。</p>
<p><strong>3、仙女港（Port Fairy）和十二使徒岩（Twelve Apostles）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12637835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Great-Ocean-Road-3-e1608632009874.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Great-Ocean-Road-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637835" class="wp-caption-text">Great Ocean Road。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>十二使徒岩（现在只剩下八个）是维州大洋路（Great Ocean Road）上最著名的<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>，距离墨尔本大约四小时车程。继续往前开一小时二十分钟，就到了大洋路的终点站——仙女港。在这个美丽的海滨小镇，人们不仅可以品尝到美食，还可能在海边看到海豹、鲸鱼和海豚。</p>
<p><strong>4、墨累河（Murray River）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12637830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Rutherglen-e1608631640118.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Rutherglen-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637830" class="wp-caption-text">Rutherglen。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从维州东北部的大分水岭（Great Dividing Range）出发，开车沿着墨累河行驶，一直可以开到南澳。沿途的美景会让人忘却旅途的疲惫。墨累河沿岸有一些小镇，其中Rutherglen地区的葡萄酒闻名遐迩。</p>
<p><strong>5、威尔逊海角国家公园（Wilson&#8217;s Promontory National Park）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12637839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Wilsons-Promontory.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Wilsons-Promontory-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637839" class="wp-caption-text">Wilsons Promontory。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>威尔逊海角国家公园位于维州最南端，距离墨尔本大约两小时三十分钟车程。这里有大片的热带雨林、一望无际的海滩、花岗岩构成的山脉，以及大量的野生动物，是一个放松身心的好去处。</p>
