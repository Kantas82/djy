<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州必去二十大景点介绍（二）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13463909-145985-56-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Phillip Island。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年12月28日讯】（大纪元记者沈诗昂墨尔本编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/iehdxr350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>素有“花园之州”的美誉，这里不仅有壮丽的自然景观，也有许多著名的人文<ahref="https://github.com/iehdxr350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。如果玩遍以下这二十个<ahref="https://github.com/iehdxr350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8%E8%83%9C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">旅游胜地</a>，相信大家一定会对这座美丽的花园之州有个非常深入的了解。</p>
<h2>6、菲利普岛（Phillip Island）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12639083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12639083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/146023-56-e1608685397248.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12639083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/146023-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12639083" class="wp-caption-text">Phillip Island。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菲利普岛距离墨尔本只有两个小时的车程，在这里可爱的小企鹅每天都会游到<ahref="https://github.com/iehdxr350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>上来。幸运的话，人们还可以看到海豹。如果想在这里停留一晚，可以选择在考斯（Cowes）镇过夜，不过这里比较冷，要记得带件厚一点的衣服。</p>
<h2>7、Great Alpine Road公路</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13463905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13463905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13463905-Original__9625728_TVIC_Gippsland_Lakes_Coastal_Park_n1ifrfo_1600X1200.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13463905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13463905-Original__9625728_TVIC_Gippsland_Lakes_Coastal_Park_n1ifrfo_1600X1200-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13463905" class="wp-caption-text">Gippsland lake。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从<ahref="https://github.com/iehdxr350/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>东北部的Wangaratta出发，沿着Great Alpine Road公路行使，一路上的风景美不胜收，特别是Gippsland Lake湖，更是美得令人惊艳。沿途您还会经过一些葡萄园和酒庄，最后一直就可开到海边。</p>
<h2>8、大洋路（Great Ocean Road）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13463881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13463881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13463881-twelve-apostles-g8e0e9b7d0_1920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13463881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13463881-twelve-apostles-g8e0e9b7d0_1920-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13463881" class="wp-caption-text">大洋路。（ThierryBEUVE/Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大洋路是世界上景色最优美的沿海公路之一，沿途壮丽的景色令人赞叹，每年都吸引着大量的游客前来观赏。大洋路的起点位于Anglesea附近，终点至仙女港，全程近三百公里，沿途有许多美丽的小镇。</p>
<h2>9、本迪戈（Bendigo）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bendigo-1-e1608631773576.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Bendigo-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637801" class="wp-caption-text">Bendigo。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本迪戈位于维州中部，它曾经是世界上最富有的一个城镇。自1850年代的淘金热以来，本迪戈一直吸引着来自世界各地的人们。这里有许多维多利亚年代的建筑，充满怀旧气息。同时，市中心喧闹的酒吧和餐厅又让这座城市充满了活力。</p>
<h2>10、丹德农山区（The Dandenongs）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12637869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12637869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Dandenong.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12637869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/Dandenong-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12637869" class="wp-caption-text">Dandenong Ranges。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果不想花很多时间开车，那就到丹德农山区玩一玩吧。虽然距离墨尔本只有一小时十五分钟的车程，但来到这里仿佛置身于世外桃源。小朋友们喜欢的普芬比利蒸汽小火车（Puffing Billy Railway）就在这一带。</p>
