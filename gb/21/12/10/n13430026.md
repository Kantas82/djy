<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>神韵小商品高级珠宝系列 诠释传统文化之美</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430097-shenyunshangpin-600x400.png" />
<h6>神韵小商品全新珠宝系列，揉合传统理念与创意，设计出具有传统韵味的首饰。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="http://ShenYunShop.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">神韵小商品</a><ahref="http://ShenYunShop.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Shen Yun Shop</a>）推出“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E8%88%9E%E4%BB%99%E9%9F%B5.md#1">凤舞仙韵</a>”和“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E7%BC%98%E5%A6%82%E6%84%8F.md#1">圆缘如意</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E7%BA%A7%E7%8F%A0%E5%AE%9D.md#1">高级珠宝</a>系列，创作理念根植于传统。设计师将传统文化和时尚风格巧妙融合，以非凡的创意演绎传统文化之美，创作出既具有传统韵味又时尚经典的珠宝首饰。每件饰品透过背后的意涵与美好寓意，传颂正统、纯粹的传统美学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430054" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430054-image013.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13430054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430054-image013-300x212.jpg" alt="" width="445" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430054" class="wp-caption-text">有着传统韵味设计感的系列，18k 黄金，镶嵌稀有的天然翠绿宝石——沙弗莱石和日本Akoya 珍珠。<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5%E5%B0%8F%E5%95%86%E5%93%81.md#1">神韵小商品</a>“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E7%BC%98%E5%A6%82%E6%84%8F.md#1">圆缘如意</a>珠宝系列</span></strong></h2>
<p>有着传统韵味设计感的系列，选用18k 黄金制作，镶嵌稀有的天然翠绿宝石——沙弗莱石和日本Akoya珍珠。在绿宝石当中，沙弗莱石是祖母绿之外最受追捧的。珍珠与绿宝石的搭配，使得宝石独特的色泽与珍珠的饱满光泽交相辉映。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430055" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430055-image015.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430055-image015-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430055" class="wp-caption-text">传统的金色如意云纹，中心镶嵌绿色宝石，吊坠则是一颗饱满圆润的珍珠。<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A5%9E%E9%9F%B5%E5%B0%8F%E5%95%86%E5%93%81.md#1">神韵小商品</a>“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">圆缘如意项链</span></strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13430124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430124" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430124-137753a5600ef539e3e6dc2d351bdc06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430124-137753a5600ef539e3e6dc2d351bdc06-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430124" class="wp-caption-text">不论是正式的场合还是日常穿搭，这款项链戴在颈项格外耀眼，呈现出淡雅的古典美。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<p>设计灵感来自神韵节目，象征圆满如意，将幸运传递给每一个人。以优雅温柔的镂空线条，打造出传统的金色如意云纹。中心迷人的绿色宝石，闪耀璀璨光芒，吊坠则是一颗饱满圆润的梨形珍珠，静静地放射出珍珠独有的纯洁光华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430060" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430060-image019.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430060" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430060-image019-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430060" class="wp-caption-text">金色如意云纹造型，绿宝石镶嵌其中，日本Akoya 珍珠缀在耳垂部位。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">圆缘如意耳环</span></strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13430128" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430128" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430128-01d7a0fff9c5ffac10f3ae59f10f92be.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430128-01d7a0fff9c5ffac10f3ae59f10f92be-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430128" class="wp-caption-text">圆缘如意耳环，既优雅又华丽。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这款耳环以精致巧思取胜， 金色如意云纹的造型，圆润洁白的日本Akoya珍珠缀在耳垂部位，清澈透明的绿色宝石镶嵌在镂空的缀饰上，明亮耀眼。充满几何美感的线条与闪耀的宝石相映衬，珠翠之光华在耳畔熠熠生辉，彰显低调细腻的奢华感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430110" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430110-aa9c7303f25691e77ec13f7bf75594b4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430110-aa9c7303f25691e77ec13f7bf75594b4-450x293.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430110" class="wp-caption-text">蓝宝石和海蓝宝石镶嵌在18k 白金中，设计灵感源自神韵节目《宝蓝凤舞》。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E8%88%9E%E4%BB%99%E9%9F%B5.md#1">凤舞仙韵</a>珠宝系列</span></strong></h2>
<p>蓝宝石和海蓝宝石镶嵌在18k 白金中， 设计灵感源自神韵节目《宝蓝凤舞》，通过传统图形元素与铺镶动人的珠宝设计，捕捉凤凰的妩媚之美，精致线型与精湛工艺，尽显凤凰的灵活生气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430067-image005.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430067-image005-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430067" class="wp-caption-text">18k 白金嵌以蓝宝石和海蓝宝石，设计灵感源自神韵节目《宝蓝凤舞》。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">凤舞仙韵项链</span></strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13430114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430114" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430114-1899a685a08ac3169a4e5de83b151eba.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430114-1899a685a08ac3169a4e5de83b151eba-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430114" class="wp-caption-text">18k 白金的长链，蓝宝石和钻石镶嵌其中，这款项链挂在颈项，举手投足间展现出典雅的气质。。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<p>18k白金的长链，蓝宝石和钻石镶嵌其中，璀璨却不张扬。设计师将“凤凰结”以柔和的椭圆形设计呈现，以美钻勾勒出优雅的线条，凸显项链的优美轮廓。在宝石的运用上，以蓝宝石为主体，静谧的蓝色宝石宛如凤眼，一泓湛蓝完美演绎出灵动之美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13430069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430069-image009.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430069-image009-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430069" class="wp-caption-text">采用蓝宝石和钻石相结合的方式设计而成。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #0000ff;">凤舞仙韵耳环</span></strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13430120" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13430120" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430120-9f716ddd9dff2c1f50ab9535eaf30efc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13430120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13430120-9f716ddd9dff2c1f50ab9535eaf30efc-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13430120" class="wp-caption-text">充满古典气息的耳环，用来搭配日常装扮，或是迷人晚装，都能衬托出您与众不同的气质。（神韵小商品“Shen Yun Shop”）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这款耳环采用蓝宝石和钻石相结合的方式设计而成，臻于细节，柔美的造型精致刻划了凤凰的形态，也凸显出耳环的古典对称美。耳垂部分镶嵌圆形钻石，闪耀动人光芒，中心同样镶嵌一颗深邃的蓝色宝石， 璀璨夺目的光芒，令造型精美奢华，尽显优雅非凡气质。</p>
