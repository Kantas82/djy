<h6>2022 Nissan Pathfinder Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月25日讯】HO！HO！HO！不论你称它圣诞节，又或是耶诞节，又或是节日，你应该都会喜欢圣诞老人，假如我是圣诞老人的话，会否以Nissan Pathfinder代替鹿车呢？</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://dfxrfb9070id6.cloudfront.net/M8p21CPot"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wtnrng3147/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/syeOmpYH2ZY" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">要代替鹿车的首项要点是容量要大，才可以放入放满礼物的大袋，第五代Pathfinder的内部总容量由上一代车型的154.0立方呎增加到164.6立方呎，新的“桥式”前中控台下面增加了13.4升的存储空间，用来放置小礼物也不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459036-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_155-e1640410206347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459036-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_155-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459036" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Nissan Pathfinder Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">外貌同样重要，红色是最配合喜气洋洋的圣诞节日气氛，所以这辆红色的Pathfinder最适合不过。而且2022年的第五代Nissan Pathfinder是全新设计的，巨大的前脸使用了V-Motion设计，放上鹿鼻装饰也很可爱。C型的大灯和浮动车顶是新Pathfinder的特式，细长的日间行车灯，有如上扬的眉毛，和大灯都是LED灯。LED尾灯比旧款更细长，尾闸设计粗犷，闸面上有巨大镀铬的Pathfinder徽章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459037-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_163-e1640410218745.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459037-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_163-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459037" class="wp-caption-text">尾闸设计粗犷。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13459038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459038-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_167-e1640410232994.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459038-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_167-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459038" class="wp-caption-text">浮动车顶。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">要载着这么多这么重的礼物，当然要有相当气力，2022年的Pathfinder配备了284匹马力的3.5升直喷V6引擎，扭矩为259磅呎，配置全新9速自动变速箱，输出顺滑、强劲、直接，用来代替6只驯鹿再好不过。这款全新的变速箱也有助于提高燃油经济性和驾驶舒适性，而在切线或出弯时也表现佳。新Pathfinder具有6,000磅最大拖力，可拖拉船只、ATV、拖车等，当然拖拉雪橇也可以。另外，拖车摇摆控制是标准配置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459039-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_172-e1640410241375.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459039-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_172-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459039" class="wp-caption-text">3.5升直喷V6引擎。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">要跨越田野、郊外派送礼物，Pathfinder具备的全新智能四驱系统，非常有用。有七种不同的地形模式选择，包括标准、雪地、沙地、泥地、运动、经济和牵引，在各种路面也可适应。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459041-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_176-e1640410260183.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459041" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459041-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_176-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459041" class="wp-caption-text">全新9速自动变速箱。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">内饰采用了新材料和开放式设计，12.3吋数码仪表板可提供各种资讯，也可从两种仪表设计互换。10.8吋的平视显示器，让我可专注道路。中控台的9吋彩色触摸屏，内置了NissanConnect系统，有Wi-Fi热点、无线Apple CarPlay及Android Auto连接、无线手机充电板、13个扬声器的Bose高级音响等，另外，导航系统和环视监视器，比以前分辨率更高、图像可视角度更广。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459043" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459043-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_187-e1640410284671.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459043" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459043-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_187-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459043" class="wp-caption-text">内饰采用了新材料和开放式设计。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">为了避免碰撞而影响送礼物，Pathfinder装有“安全盾牌360”安全系统，包括带行人探测的自动紧急制动、盲点警告、后方交叉交通警报、车道偏离警告、远光灯辅助和后方自动制动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459040-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_173-e1640410251415.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459040-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_173-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459040" class="wp-caption-text">“安全盾牌360”安全系统。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">标配8人座位，第二排可配EZ FLEX一键式座椅，座椅可以向前倾斜，让乘客可更容易地进出第三排。第三排座椅可60/40分离式折叠，带着数个小精灵帮手又可有空间放礼物，提供了最大的灵活性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459042" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459042" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459042-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_182-e1640410274161.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459042" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459042-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_182-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459042" class="wp-caption-text">EZ FLEX一键式座椅。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新的双齿轮电动转向系统，结合更宽的255/50R20轮胎，提供更稳定、快速的反应，转向角率提高了40%，也减少了转向力度。前悬挂使用了新连接系统，加强了滚动刚度28%，后悬挂则采用多连杆设计，并更新了减震器和阻尼器，使后滚动刚度增强14%。ProPILOT辅助系统结合了转向辅助和智能巡航控制，在繁忙的市内和高速公路上都能自动加速、制动和转向。有了这些设备，大大减少圣诞老人驾驶时的疲累。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13459044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13459044" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459044-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_194-e1640410294169.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13459044" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13459044-2022_Nissan_Pathfinder_194-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13459044" class="wp-caption-text">12.3吋数码仪表板。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">想想也不错，当你们在圣诞节时看到门外停泊了一辆红色Pathfinder，要快点准备牛奶饼干了，不然就没有礼物呀！◇</p>
