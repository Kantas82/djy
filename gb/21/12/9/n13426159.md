<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>异国料理DIY 4食材做泰式凤梨猪肉红咖喱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426176-curry-de-porc-ananas-320-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>4种食材就可以上桌一道泰皇泰式料理—凤梨猪肉红咖喱，简单异国菜不用花钱上馆子，自己在家就可以做。（山岳文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：不用到泰国也可以品尝到<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泰国菜</a>，不用花钱上馆子也可以吃到精致异国菜，在家就可以做出一道经典的泰式料理。谈起泰国菜就会勾起多样食物的记忆，浓郁的椰奶香、辛辣甜咸的咖喱味和清爽微酸带甜的青柠檬，多变、美味、颜值高的泰国菜是很多饕客的最爱。烹调<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">泰国料理</a>似乎有门槛，事实不然，只要简单的4种食材就可以上桌！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8%E7%8C%AA%E8%82%89%E7%BA%A2%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1.md#1">凤梨猪肉红咖喱</a></span></h2>
<p>份量：4人份<br />
准备时间：10分钟<br />
烹调时间：48分钟</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>新鲜凤梨 200公克（如果没有新鲜凤梨，也可以用凤梨罐头代替。）<br />
红咖喱酱 2汤匙<br />
梅花猪肉 500公克<br />
椰奶 400毫升</p>
<figure id="attachment_13426177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13426177" style="width: 273px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426177-ingredients-curry-porc-ananas-452.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13426177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426177-ingredients-curry-porc-ananas-452-450x495.jpg" alt="" width="273" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13426177" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜凤梨（也可以用凤梨罐头取代）。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将凤梨切成小丁。</p>
<p>2. 将一个西式或中式炒锅加热，并在锅中放入红咖喱酱拌炒约2分钟。加入去骨切块的梅花猪肉以及凤梨丁。继续拌炒1分钟，接着倒入椰奶后稍微搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13426180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13426180" style="width: 278px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426180-ingredients-curry-porc-ananas-451.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13426180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426180-ingredients-curry-porc-ananas-451-450x495.jpg" alt="" width="278" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13426180" class="wp-caption-text">在锅中拌入红咖喱酱拌炒。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加盖并以小火炖煮45分钟。</p>
<p>4. 煮好后盛盘并趁早食用。⊙</p>
<figure id="attachment_13426175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13426175" style="width: 120px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426175-eb8f32c01c11a03dae4ca6a52fc130dc.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13426175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13426175-eb8f32c01c11a03dae4ca6a52fc130dc-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="120" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13426175" class="wp-caption-text">《4样食材做<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD%E8%8F%9C.md#1">泰国菜</a>》书封。（山岳文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
