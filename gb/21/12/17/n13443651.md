<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2021/22时尚九大潮流趋势（下）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443654-e5cb7156ea8d0e63cca044ace7ee1eb6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月18日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）很多人都说，2022年会是时尚领域非比寻常的一年。为什么呢？因为一些新的时尚概念得到品牌和大众的认可，而流行了起来，比如倡导永续时尚、无残忍时尚、主张采用植物性皮革、慢时尚的定制服装，推行合作消费的二手服饰、回收服装等，都给2022年带来时尚的变革，让大家拭目企盼。</p>
<p>回首2021年，流行的超大西装、卫衣与西装叠穿、波西米亚风在2022冬季依旧流行，撞色拼搭、大款夹克、<ahref="https://github.com/zwerhs3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%88%E7%BB%87.md#1">针织</a>连衣裙装、全身图标穿搭、流苏服饰仍然位于潮流趋势的前列；高筒靴、拖拉机靴和厚底乐福鞋也将持续受到欢迎。</p>
<h2>5.厚底乐福鞋</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13443735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443735" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443735-GettyImages-1198862560.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443735-GettyImages-1198862560-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443735" class="wp-caption-text">带帽衫外套螺纹大衣，水洗黑色牛仔裤，黑色厚底乐福鞋。米兰时装周秀场。（Tullio M. Puglia/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果觉得靴子太约束，凉鞋太冻脚，高跟鞋走路又太累，那么建议你穿厚实的乐福鞋。拥有正式感的皮鞋外型加入厚底元素，除了舒适、易搭，更显新潮、率性，能够展现与以往秋冬截然不同的崭新形象。凭借整体经典的外观，这双鞋就不会被时尚主流淘汰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443916-GettyImages-1357666544.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443916-GettyImages-1357666544-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443916" class="wp-caption-text">一身黑色，搭上胸前花团锦簇着香奈儿图标，黑色漆皮乐福鞋与丝绒裤子的光泽更显这款穿搭华贵高雅。12月7日举办2021/2022香奈儿专业艺术时装周秀场，位于巴黎香奈儿新总部大厦Le 19M。 (Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>业界推荐：</strong>乐福鞋是平底鞋中最流行的一款。可以搭配中长裙、长裤，也可搭配短裙、短裤；在搭配高筒棉袜时，可营造学院派的风格。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>选择漆皮乐福鞋，配上仿鳄鱼皮的纯色皮包，打造现代、酷炫的“明星”造型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443730-GettyImages-1302042084.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443730-GettyImages-1302042084-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443730" class="wp-caption-text">厚实的黑色乐福鞋，搭配白色面外套、黑高领、黑裤，温暖协调。2021“单飞”纽约时装周秀场。（Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Flying Solo）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>6.学院风穿搭</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13443722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443722-GettyImages-509594830.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443722-GettyImages-509594830-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443722" class="wp-caption-text">短款格子上装，黑色大围巾，细格西裤，经典、优雅的深色学院风。纽约时装周秀场。（Neilson Barnard/Getty Images For Marissa Webb）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学院风在新冠疫情（又称中共疫情）封锁期间大幅发展， 2022 年将继续流行。在社交媒体对学校和时尚怀旧情绪的推动下，学院风强调教育、学习，因此具有“学术/学院”风格，包括深色学院风、浅色学院风和灰系学院风三种。</p>
<p><strong>风格特点：</strong>剪裁俐落、风格复古、儒雅精致。</p>
<p>格纹应该算是学院风的经典代表，不管是方形小格子还是菱格纹，都能给人正式但又不过于严肃的谦谦学子气质，格子颜色与大小会营造不同的效果，浅格子给人轻松阳光、青春向上的感觉﹔深格子则显得沉稳成熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443903" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443903-GettyImages-1327130953.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13443903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443903-GettyImages-1327130953-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="596" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443903" class="wp-caption-text">浅粉色上装，白衬衣，粉色千鸟格短裙，同色小方包，清新淡雅、柔和明亮的轻学院风。巴黎时装周2021/2022（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images For Dior）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>学院风百搭易穿，格裙、格子衬衣、毛背心、蝴蝶结恤衫、小方包，都是此风格的特色及必备单品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443897" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443897" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443897-GettyImages-1304200313.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443897-GettyImages-1304200313-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443897" class="wp-caption-text">学院风与波西米亚风混搭。米兰时装周2021/22秋冬时装秀场。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13443754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443754" style="width: 594px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443754-GettyImages-1327264370.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13443754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443754-GettyImages-1327264370-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="594" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443754" class="wp-caption-text">亮丽清新文雅的格裙装。巴黎时装周2021/2022（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images For Dior）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学院风从古典希腊文学、艺术、建筑中汲取灵感。可选深色调的厚重<ahref="https://github.com/zwerhs3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%88%E7%BB%87.md#1">针织</a>衫来打造深色学院风；轻学院风浅色的穿搭营造一种带有清新积极气氛的美学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443796-GettyImages-1353594860.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443796-GettyImages-1353594860-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443796" class="wp-caption-text">细格大西服裙装。墨尔本2021时装周秀场。（Daniel Pockett/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13443900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443900" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443900-GettyImages-1198236480.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443900-GettyImages-1198236480-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443900" class="wp-caption-text">典型的暗学院风。意大利佛罗伦萨97届Pitti Immagine Uomo 正是时装秀场。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>7.阔腿裤</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13443851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443851" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443851-GettyImages-1357667718.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443851-GettyImages-1357667718-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443851" class="wp-caption-text">呢格套装，外套黑色对襟毛衣，层叠项坠，下装阔腿裤。优雅、大气。2021/2022香奈儿专业艺术时装周秀场。 (Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>阔腿裤再次流行吹起复古风。阔腿裤有易搭配、经典不过时的气场，很多人都认为它适合任何腿型，其实，阔腿裤如果搭配不当容易显胖。</p>
<p><strong>穿搭原则：</strong>用剪裁或合身的上衣来平衡阔腿裤，既宽松舒适，还能修身塑型，不过最好是搭配短款上衣，看起较不会显得邋遢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443833" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443833-GettyImages-1327333960.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443833-GettyImages-1327333960-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443833" class="wp-caption-text">天鹅绒黑色短款小西装配丝绸阔腿裤，像月光下秋湖光与影的跳跃与流动。光彩照人，折射极致的品味。巴黎时装周2021/2022高定时装秀场。（Kristy Sparow/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13443842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443842" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443842-GettyImages-1327334047.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443842-GettyImages-1327334047-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443842" class="wp-caption-text">短款上装配阔腿裤。2021/2022 巴黎时装周高定时装秀场。 （Photo by Kristy Sparow/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，材质决定阔腿裤的穿着效果。双腿匀称偏瘦的人，穿薄且垂坠的面料会比厚重面料更显瘦；若是腿部赘肉较多，偏厚且挺括的裤子能美化腿型。而选择较高腰线的阔腿裤，在视觉上可拉伸腿部长度，调整身材比例。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>A型、且质感挺括的阔腿裤，可有效藏匿赘肉，修饰腿部线条，视觉上腿部更笔直、显瘦。无论胖瘦及腿型都百搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443866" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443866-GettyImages-1354596587.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443866-GettyImages-1354596587-450x709.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="709" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443866" class="wp-caption-text">腰部合身、裤脚宽大的A型阔腿裤有良好的修身效果。墨尔本时装秀场。（Quinn Rooney/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>

<h2>8.针织/<ahref="https://github.com/zwerhs3885/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%A9%E9%92%88%E8%83%8C%E5%BF%83.md#1">钩针背心</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13443790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443790-GettyImages-1340116895.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443790-GettyImages-1340116895-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443790" class="wp-caption-text">红果毛衣马甲搭白衬衣、牛仔裤，活泼可爱的造型。柏林“关于你”时装周2021秋冬时装秀。（Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在现代就穿搭而言，传统男士正装持续影响流行趋势，除了大西装外套外，针织、钩针也是打造时尚造型的必备单品。特别在秋冬季，针织背心不仅保暖，还可叠穿春夏装束，既有层次感，还能瞬间穿搭出经典的学院风格。</p>
<p><strong>流行趋势：</strong>2021/2022冬季最抢眼的针织背心，并非纯色款式，而是有着几何图案的款式，如千鸟纹、菱格纹、棋盘格纹、亮片图案更为吸睛。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>日常穿搭时，可将针织衫叠穿在衣服上，并搭配牛仔裤，还可以把宽松长版的针织背心当成洋装穿，可内搭衬衣、下配过膝靴或者超高筒靴。</p>

<h2>9.休闲风衣</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13443781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443781-GettyImages-1304178335.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443781-GettyImages-1304178335-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443781" class="wp-caption-text">风衣在百变百搭中，可打造着各类造型。2021/2022米兰秋冬时装周秀场。（Stefania M. D&#8217;Alessandro/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>风衣是一件四季皆宜的单品，几十年来成为职场女性首选外套，尤其秋冬季节，更是防雨挡风、保暖经典穿搭，是当下潮人的最爱。风衣在百变百搭中，变换着各类造型，特别是用休闲卫衣来与风衣叠穿，正是这个季节最受欢迎的混搭风。另外，风衣也非常适合刮风下雨天穿着。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443784" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443784-GettyImages-1340116914.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443784-GettyImages-1340116914-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443784" class="wp-caption-text">深军绿色风衣，休闲帅气。柏林“关于你”时装周2021秋冬时装秀。（Sebastian Reuter/Getty Images for ABOUT YOU）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13443837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443837" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443837-GettyImages-1345417710.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13443837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443837-GettyImages-1345417710-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443837" class="wp-caption-text">经典色风衣，内配同色系深驼色衣裤，协调又凸显女性柔美线条。韩国汉城时装周2022SS秀场。（Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>业界建议：</strong>如果你想与众不同，可选一件仿皮风衣，内搭螺纹羊毛高领衫，再搭配花裙和高跟靴，整体气场瞬间扬升。当然，风衣也非常适合搭配羊绒高领和拖拉机靴，外观时尚又不失帅气。</p>
<p><strong>特别推荐：</strong>风衣+卫衣的叠穿组合既飒又酷，时尚且温暖；可以下配牛仔裤，依据不同场合搭配靴子、高跟鞋或者运动鞋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13443856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13443856" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443856-GettyImages-1354615786.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13443856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13443856-GettyImages-1354615786-450x668.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="668" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13443856" class="wp-caption-text">竖条纹米色大衣，配同色短裤，墨镜，咖啡色衬衣，打造休闲俊酷的装扮。2021墨尔本时装周闭幕秀场。（Quinn Rooney/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
