<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【无鱼坊的摄影心视界】花开一品圣诞红</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460761-532616_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>圣诞红叶红如火庆圣诞，艳冠群芳蔚奇观。（图／郑清海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>叶红如火庆圣诞</strong><br />
<strong>艳冠群芳蔚奇观</strong><br />
<strong>喜气洋洋称一品</strong><br />
<strong>乳汁微毒莫乱沾</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13460764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13460764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460764-532618_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13460764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460764-532618_medium-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13460764" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yomtaa338/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E7%BA%A2.md#1">圣诞红</a>Euphorbia pulcherrima大戟科。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong><ahref="https://github.com/yomtaa338/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E7%BA%A2.md#1">圣诞红</a>Euphorbia pulcherrima大戟科</strong></h3>
<p>又名为一品红、圣诞花。植株0.6~3公尺，深绿色叶长可达16公分。最顶层的叶是火红色、红色或白色的，因此经常被误会为花朵。花呈聚伞花序排列，雌雄花生在同一总苞内，雌花单一位于众多雄花的中央，有的花序只开雄花。总苞外有一、二明显的腺体/蜜槽，黄色，二唇裂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13460765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13460765" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460765-532615_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13460765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460765-532615_medium-600x905.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="905" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13460765" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13460766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13460766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460766-532614_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13460766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460766-532614_medium-600x903.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="903" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13460766" class="wp-caption-text">喜气洋洋称一品，乳汁微毒莫乱沾。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>常流传着错误的观念，认为圣诞红有剧毒，但已被证明是假的，它没有任何部分对人类或动物构成危险。来源是因大部分大戟属植物均有毒性的而造成误解。只是乳汁碰到皮肤会产生红肿发炎，误食会有呕吐、喉咙痛、腹泻等症状，还是要小心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13460767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13460767" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460767-532617_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13460767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13460767-532617_medium-600x799.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="799" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13460767" class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相传16世纪时的墨西哥，有一个在圣诞夜流浪在路边的小女孩，因为太穷没有奉献给基督的礼物，她被一个天使启发搜集草籽种在教堂边上，这些草突然绽放出红色的“花朵”，即圣诞红，被认为是奇迹。</p>
