<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩国MMA颁奖礼明日登场 MyMusic直播免费看</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414532-211203014545100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾大哥大MyMusic将独家直播韩国2021 MMA（Melon Music Awards）颁奖典礼。（MyMusic提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月03日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心综合报导）韩国乐坛年度盛事Melon Music Awards 2021（简称MMA）颁奖典礼将于明日（4日）下午5点举行，台湾大哥大MyMusic将独家直播。</p>
<p>今年将有许多人气歌手参加MMA颁奖典礼，包含睽违四年再次出席该活动的IU（李知恩），以及音源表现亮眼的Heize、新秀李茂珍、Trot歌手任英雄（林英雄）、人气男团THE BOYZ、TOMORROW X TOGETHER、ENHYPEN，综艺限定组合MSG Wannabe，还有STAYC、Brave Girls等人气偶像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13414534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13414534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414534-211203014925100707.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13414534" title="IU" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414534-211203014925100707-600x600.jpg" alt="IU" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13414534" class="wp-caption-text">韩国人气歌手IU（李知恩）时隔四年出席MMA（Melon Music Awards）颁奖典礼。（MyMusic提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为让全台的K-pop粉丝随时掌握MMA颁奖典礼的最新资讯，MyMusic特别推出“KPOP盛典必听攻略”，网罗入围名单、卡司看点、韩流追星族Podcaster专访等第一手消息，并汇整“MMA 2021入围名单”、“2021 MAMA入围名单”与“韩国TMA音乐奖得奖名单”等歌单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13414535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13414535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414535-211203014549100707.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13414535" title="Heize" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414535-211203014549100707-600x600.jpg" alt="Heize" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13414535" class="wp-caption-text">音源成绩亮眼的韩国歌手Heize将出席韩国2021 MMA（Melon Music Awards）颁奖典礼。（MyMusic提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了举办全台唯一专属包厅直播派对，MyMusic还加码推出“韩迷应援点听活动”，即日起到12月20日，点听韩语歌曲次数最多的前100名会员，即有机会抽中Samsonite Red与防弹少年团（BTS）联名推出的独家限量行李箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13414536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13414536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414536-211203014547100707.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13414536" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13414536-211203014547100707-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13414536" class="wp-caption-text">MyMusic推出“韩迷应援点听活动”抽防弹少年团（BTS）独家联名的限量行李箱活动。（MyMusic提供）</figcaption></figure>
