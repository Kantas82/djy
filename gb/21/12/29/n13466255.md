<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>SM娱乐发行冬季专辑 前后辈跨团合作受注目</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466571-211229053534100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>BoA宝儿（左）与EXO成员KAI参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年12月29日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心综合报导）SM娱乐继2011年后睽违10年再度发行SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》，集结了旗下艺人安七炫、<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/boa%E5%AE%9D%E5%84%BF.md#1">BoA宝儿</a>、东方神起、SUPER JUNIOR、少女时代小分队Girls&#8217; Generation-Oh!GG、SHINee、KAI、Red Velvet、NCT与aespa参与。除收录团体新歌外，还有许多意想不到的跨团合作歌曲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466565-211229052250100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466565" title="Red Velvet" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466565-211229052250100707-600x248.jpg" alt="Red Velvet" width="600" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466565" class="wp-caption-text">Red Velvet参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此次专辑收录的两首主打歌《Hope from KWANGYA》与《Dreams Come True》是翻唱SM娱乐旗下元组偶像H.O.T和S.E.S于1998年所发行的经典歌曲。《Hope from KWANGYA》为大合唱曲，原曲原先就以SMTOWN代表歌曲而备受喜爱，在今年元旦举办的家族演唱会上已有表演过，本次由原曲创作人安七炫担任歌唱指导，前后辈的合作别具意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466554" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466554-211229052235100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466554" title="KANGTA" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466554-211229052235100707.jpg" alt="KANGTA" width="428" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466554" class="wp-caption-text">安七炫（KANGTA）参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外一个受瞩目的前后辈合作是已抢先公开、由<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/boa%E5%AE%9D%E5%84%BF.md#1">BoA宝儿</a>监制、aespa翻唱的《Dreams Come True》，该曲特别请到S.E.S成员Bada亲自传授唱歌秘诀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466555-211229052224100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466555" title="aespa" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466555-211229052224100707-600x250.jpg" alt="aespa" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466555" class="wp-caption-text">aespa参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>令人惊喜的是，于2018年组成并发行一张专辑后至今没有以新作回归乐坛的少女时代小分队Oh!GG推出了新歌《Melody》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466556-211229052245100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466556" title="SNSD" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466556-211229052245100707-600x248.jpg" alt="SNSD" width="600" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466556" class="wp-caption-text">少女时代小分队Girls&#8217; Generation-Oh!GG参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，东方神起和SUPER JUNIOR更是久违的推出合作新曲《Magical》，是首以“我们的友情是命定与奇迹”为主题的轻快流行歌曲，也是两团继2005年推出《Show Me Your Love》后，睽违16年的新歌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466557" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466557-211229052247100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466557" title="SUPER JUNIOR" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466557-211229052247100707-600x248.jpg" alt="SUPER JUNIOR" width="600" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466557" class="wp-caption-text">SUPER JUNIOR参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>SMTOWN本次推出的冬季专辑包含双主打歌《Dreams Come True》与《Hope from KWANGYA》，还有东方神起和少女时代小分队Girls&#8217; Generation-Oh!GG的新歌《DINNER》与《Melody》以及东方神起与SUPER JUNIOR的合作曲《Magical》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466558-211229052240100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466558" title="TVXQ" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466558-211229052240100707-600x263.jpg" alt="TVXQ" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466558" class="wp-caption-text">东方神起参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>专辑还收录圭贤、温流和泰一演唱的《Ordinary Day》，还有泰容、JENO、HENDERY、扬扬、GISELLE合作的《ZOO》；SUNNY、廷祐、仁俊诠释的《Goodbye》以及YERI、楷灿、辰乐、志晟、NINGNING翻唱的《Snow Dream 2021》和演奏曲《The Promise of H.O.T. (Jazz Ver.)》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466560-211229052232100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466560" title="NCT" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466560-211229052232100707-600x236.jpg" alt="NCT" width="600" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466560" class="wp-caption-text">NCT参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>睽违十年、有着SM娱乐旗下歌手多样化合作的SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》共收录10首歌曲，专辑12月27日与韩国同步在台数位发行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13466566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13466566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466566-211229052237100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13466566" title="SHINee" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/12/id13466566-211229052237100707-600x248.jpg" alt="SHINee" width="600" b="248" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13466566" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee参与SMTOWN冬季专辑《2021 Winter SMTOWN : SMCU EXPRESS》。（avex taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，SM娱乐继今年1月1日举办“SMTOWN LIVE “Culture Humanity&#8221;”线上演唱会后，还将于2022年1月1日再度举办免费线上演唱会。SM娱乐的最新企划活动“Girls On Top（GOT）”中由BoA宝儿、少女时代的太妍与孝渊、Red Velvet的瑟琪及Wendy、aespa的Karina和Winter组成的“GOT the beat”，将在这场“SMTOWN LIVE 2022：SMCU EXPRESS＠KWANGYA”演唱会中带来新歌《Step Back》的特别舞台演出。</p>
