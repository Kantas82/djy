<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>借问秋色何处寻 玩家首推指湖区</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261332-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_125-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋季最美的风景不外乎红叶加瀑布了。纽约州西北角的指湖区，原意“五道瀑布小溪”的维斯考艾瀑布(Wiscoy Falls)群。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>在纽约州的西北边藏着一块风景优美的风水宝地。这块宝地从东到西有80英里，从北到南50英里，中间有长长短短11条像手指一样的淡水湖，所以称为<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%87%E6%B9%96%E5%8C%BA.md#1">指湖区</a>（Finger Lakes Region）。这里有约25个<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9E%E7%AB%8B%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">州立公园</a>，光<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">瀑布</a>就有50多条。一年四季风景如画，但<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E5%8F%B6.md#1">红叶</a>加瀑布的<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%AD%A3.md#1">秋季</a>绝对是最美的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261168" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261168-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_102.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13261168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261168-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_102-600x528.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="451" b="397" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261168" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%87%E6%B9%96%E5%8C%BA.md#1">指湖区</a>地图。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天就给大家看看秋色里的指湖区<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">瀑布</a>有多美。其中还有一条藏在深闺中，知道的人肯定不多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261278-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_103.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13261278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261278-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_103-450x300.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261278" class="wp-caption-text">塔翰瑙克瀑布(Taughannock Falls)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>落差215英尺的塔翰瑙克瀑布（Taughannock Falls）座落在最长的手指湖，卡优嘎湖（Cayuga Lake）的西南端，是落基山脉往东最高的单层瀑布。Taughannock是印第安语，意思是树林里的大瀑布。以瀑布为名的<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9E%E7%AB%8B%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">州立公园</a>离康奈尔大学（Cornell University）不远。</p>
<p>看这瀑布最方便的方法就是开车到瀑布对面的观景台，停车走几步路就到。如上图。</p>
<p>从215英尺冲下来的瀑布在耸立400英尺的峡谷里开出一条小溪，蜿蜒流入卡优嘎湖。夏天枯水季可以直接在河床上走到瀑布脚下。秋天时踩着树叶，牵着狗狗，沿着步道一路走到瀑布跟前，绝对是一步一景，人间至美。</p>

<p>离塔翰瑙克不远就是罗伯特.川门（Robert Treman）州立公园。那里有大大小小12个瀑布。最有名的就是115英尺高的路西法瀑布（Lucifer Falls）和下游的低瀑布（Lower Falls）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261286" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261286-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_108.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13261286" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261286-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_108-450x257.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="601" b="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261286" class="wp-caption-text">路西法瀑布(Lucifer Falls)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>连公园门口的无名小瀑布都那么美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261315-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_113.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13261315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261315-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_113-600x400.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261315" class="wp-caption-text">川门公园里的无名瀑布。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三内卡湖（Seneca Lake）是手指湖中最大最深的一条。它的最南端有个著名的瓦金斯狭谷（Watkins Glen）州立公园。公园里有大大小小19道瀑布，最有名的就是下图的彩虹瀑布（Rainbow Falls），春夏之际树木郁郁葱葱时尤为漂亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261316-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_114.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13261316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261316-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_114-600x400.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261316" class="wp-caption-text">瓦金斯狭谷（Watkins Glen）州立公园里最有名的彩虹瀑布(Rainbow Falls)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公园入口处那道小瀑布像栖息的白孔雀，十分优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13261318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13261318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261318-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_115.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13261318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13261318-20210925_water_fall_helen_chou_115-600x902.jpg" alt="纽约, 指湖区" width="600" b="902" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13261318" class="wp-caption-text">公园入口处的小瀑布。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
