<h6>2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年09月11日讯】Subaru的Crosstrek已进入第二代产品周期一段时间，这款车在北美曾经称为XV?Crosstrek，后来除去了XV，只称为Crosstrek，奇怪的是，在亚洲欧洲却相反，此车只用XV这名称。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d3malt9gyhsmb.cloudfront.net/K9vZL2LNn"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/oqjwys331/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/oqjwys331/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/2JWcomK0WhA" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">无论称为XV也好，Crosstrek也好，都是由Impreza而来，所以当Impreza改款的时候，Crosstrek也同样改款。自2017年推出新设计，至今已有数年，2021年款也是经过小改良，外貌上最大不同是车前下方的防撞器（保险杆）加大了，以及新雾灯设计，进风口栏栅也有更改。另外，今年新加入了Sport版本，于Limited及Premium两版本中间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225501" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225501" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225501-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_04-e1631320377755.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225501" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225501-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225501" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">2021?Crosstrek的Sport及Limited均配备了182匹马力的2.5升四缸水平对向引擎（Boxer Engine），而Base和Premium只会配备2升引擎，所以Sport及Limited在动力性能有了显着的分别。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225505" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225505-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_19-e1631320428965.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225505-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225505" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">此2.5升四缸Boxer Engine在2019年Forester上首次使用，可发出最大182匹马力和176磅呎扭矩，搭配Lineartronic?CVT变速箱，EPA估计的耗油量可达到27mpg（城市）和34mpg（ 高速公路）。Crosstrek?Base?和?Premium?继续使用2升四缸Boxer引擎，可提供152匹马力和145磅呎的扭矩，标准配备6速手动变速箱或可升级至Lineartronic?CVT。CVT的耗油量约为28mpg（城市）及33mpg（高速公路），而6速手动耗油量约为22mpg（城市）及29mpg（高速公路）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225504" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225504-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_18-e1631320417313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225504-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225504" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13225502" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225502" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225502-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_09-e1631320395626.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225502" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225502-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_09-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225502" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">配备?CVT的Crosstrek均具有斜坡下降控制（HDC）的?X-Mode，可在低摩擦和越野条件下增强性能。另外，SI-Drive动力总成性能管理系统，让驾驶者可选择在“智能”和“运动”两种行车模式 ，“智能”模式加速柔和，有助节省燃油，而“运动”模式则强调加速性能。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225509-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_25-e1631320478812.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225509-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">屡获殊荣的Subaru?EyeSight驾驶员辅助技术，此系统可协助高级自适应巡航控制，并包括自动预碰撞制动、预碰撞油门管理、车道偏离预防、引导车辆启动警报、自动刹车等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225508" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225508" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225508-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_23-e1631320468210.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225508" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225508-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225508" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">2021?Crosstrek配备了大量标准功能，包括恒温控制、Subaru?Starlink及6.5吋触屏多媒体系统，可支援Android Auto和Apple CarPlay；前座有自动升降功能的电动车窗；60/40分体式折叠后排座椅；电动门锁和后视镜；多功能显示器；智能钥匙； 带发动机防盗器的安全系统。中央仪表板以及前后门板上均采用黑色碳纤维纹饰面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225507" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225507" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225507-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_22-e1631320458496.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225507-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225507" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">?2021?年?Crosstrek?的所有型号都有7个安全气囊：驾驶员和前排乘客正面安全气囊、侧帘式安全气囊和侧骨盆/躯干安全气囊，以及驾驶员膝部安全气囊。Crosstrek?连续六年（2015-2020?年）被评为公路安全保险协会?(IIHS) TOP SAFETY PICK+。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225510" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225510-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_34-e1631320487432.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225510-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_34-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225510" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">最重要的是Crosstrek标准内置Symmetric AWD四驱系统，在测试期间，无论在市内或高速公路，驾驶都很顺畅，没有明显阻力；在海滩上又使人更有信心，不会陷入困境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13225512" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13225512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225512-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_17-e1631320725979.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13225512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13225512-2021_Subaru_Crosstrek_Ltd_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13225512" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Subaru Crosstrek Limited。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Crosstek的重心低，高速过弯时车身稳定，车体也很扎实。然而，测试的Limited版本已配置2.5升引擎，但在加速上仍不够理想，期望将来加上2.5升涡轮增压引擎，那么此车会更加好玩！</p>
