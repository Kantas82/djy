<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本反封锁游行第三天 战争纪念馆前警民对峙</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252559-GettyImages-1235415787-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2021年9月22日，墨尔本反封锁游行抗议者与警察在战争纪念馆前对峙。（WILLIAM WEST/AFP via Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年09月22日讯】（大纪元记者天睿综合报导）9月22日（周三），墨尔本连续第三天爆发反封锁与反强制接种疫苗的示威游行。</p>
<p>当天早上，穿着防暴装备的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F.md#1">维州警察</a>在墨尔本市区巡逻，驱散抗议人群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13252562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252562-GettyImages-1235414928.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252562-GettyImages-1235414928-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252562" class="wp-caption-text">大批墨尔本防暴警察在街上巡逻，驱散抗议人群。（CON CHRONIS/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13252587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252587-GettyImages-1235415161.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252587-GettyImages-1235415161-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252587" class="wp-caption-text">一名示威者举着牌子，上面写着：我来这里不是为了暴力，而是为了选择。（ CON CHRONIS/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中午时分，抗议者聚集在战争纪念馆（Shrine Of Remembrance）前，与警方形成对峙局面。</p>
<p>抗议者们坐在战争纪念馆的台阶上唱澳洲国歌，还有人用扩音器呼吁警方不要再阻止人们抗议，这名男子说，抗议和游行是人权，警察仍然有机会去做他们该做的事情。“我们在这里是和平的，我们没有武器…&#8230;我们需要你们，就像你们需要我们一样。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13252573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252573-GettyImages-1235415967.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252573-GettyImages-1235415967-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252573" class="wp-caption-text">墨尔本战争纪念馆台阶上的抗议者们正在对警察喊话。（WILLIAM WEST/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方包围了战争纪念馆门前的抗议人群。数小时后，警察开始驱散战争抗议人群。一名不愿意离开的女子被警察强行拖走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13252609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252609-GettyImages-1235414511.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252609-GettyImages-1235414511-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252609" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F.md#1">维州警察</a>正在向一名抗议者喷辣椒喷雾。（CON CHRONIS/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据《先驱太阳报》报导，警方从周三早晨开始抓捕抗议者。位于伊丽莎白街（Elizabeth St）的建筑业、林业、矿业与能源工会（CFMEU）总部大楼外，警方在盘问每一个经过这栋建筑的行人。上午10点之前，已经有3人被捕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13252577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252577" style="width: 606px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252577-GettyImages-1235414642.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252577-GettyImages-1235414642-606x400.jpg" alt="" width="606" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252577" class="wp-caption-text">维州警察正在拘捕一名女性抗议者。（CON CHRONIS/AFP via Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>工会大楼外已经连续两天成为爆发冲突的抗议现场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13252623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13252623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252623-GettyImages-1341589002.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-13252623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13252623-GettyImages-1341589002-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13252623" class="wp-caption-text">9月21日，媒体曝光墨尔本建筑工地发生自杀事件后，一名建筑工人试图将一束鲜花放在工会大楼外。（Darrian Traynor/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周二，数千人在墨尔本市中心示威游行。维州警方表示周二逮捕了62人，期间有三名警察受伤。◇</p>
