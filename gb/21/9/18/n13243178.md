<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【烟熏料理】不用烤炉 在家DIY烟熏棒棒腿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243228-23231065534d38742c5bdd555d54bfca-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>不用柴火慢熏，在家也可以简单ＤＩＹ烟熏棒棒腿。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9F%E7%86%8F.md#1">烟熏</a>料理似乎是高门槛的厨艺，而且需要柴火慢熏和烟熏器具等，让人听了就望之却步。事实上，我们都想的太难了，只要有一只铸铁锅，在家就可以轻松自制。烟熏食物还有一个优点，冷热都好吃，冷冻冷藏都好保存，作为<ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%83%83%E8%8F%9C.md#1">开胃菜</a>、配菜、副菜都是精致佳肴，而且熏亮的食物表层和独特香气，让人看了就食指大动。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9F%E7%86%8F.md#1">烟熏</a><ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E6%A3%92%E8%85%BF.md#1">棒棒腿</a></span></h2>
<p>胡椒的香气浓郁，非常适合下酒的一道菜色。做好保存备用，随时都可以吃的方便料理。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">建议锅具尺寸</span></h3>
<p>20cm staub</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">保存方式</span></h3>
<p>烟熏料理放凉了吃，加热再吃都很美味，冷藏可以保存2～3天，冷冻则可以保存约2个星期，一次制作大量些，可以随时拿来当作常备菜使用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（２人份）</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E6%A3%92%E8%85%BF.md#1">棒棒腿</a>&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;４只（约２５０g）<br />
盐&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１／２小匙<br />
黑胡椒&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..适量<br />
烟熏木屑&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;２０g<br />
砂糖（白砂糖等）&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. ２小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">准备</span></h3>
<p>撒盐于棒棒腿上，放入密封袋内保存于冷藏室内２～３个小时。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 锅内铺上铝箔纸，放入砂糖与烟熏木屑，稍稍拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243203-5b7fd99258b8acc45adb90965716b35e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243203-5b7fd99258b8acc45adb90965716b35e-450x593.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243203" class="wp-caption-text">锅内铺上铝箔纸，放入砂糖与烟熏木屑，稍稍拌匀。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 使用厨房纸巾将棒棒腿的水分拭干，将黑胡椒平均地撒于肉上。把<ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡肉</a>平放在烘焙纸上，连同烘焙纸整个放入步骤１的锅内。盖上锅盖，稍微保留一个小缝，转中火加热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243204-e7683b8bbc211b2786744bc4b410f0d0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243204-e7683b8bbc211b2786744bc4b410f0d0-450x593.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243204" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/pybnyd3634/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡肉</a>平放在烘焙纸上，连同烘焙纸整个放入步骤１的锅内。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 开始微微散发热气之后，将锅盖确实盖紧，加热约１０分钟。途中，从锅盖的隙缝散发出水蒸气后，再将火调小一点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243205-01784949cf2ec11854c898068bf1d88c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243205-01784949cf2ec11854c898068bf1d88c-450x593.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243205" class="wp-caption-text">开始微微散发热气之后，将锅盖确实盖紧，加热约１０分钟。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 迅速地打开锅盖，确认锅中状况后，再尽速盖上锅盖。如果冒出的烟十分旺盛，请将火候调小。如果烟呈现缓缓飘出的状态，维持一样的火力，持续加热约10分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243223" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243223-4ad747bd90366d27273acd91837124b1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243223-4ad747bd90366d27273acd91837124b1-450x290.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243223" class="wp-caption-text">迅速地打开锅盖，确认锅中状况后，再尽速盖上锅盖。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13243222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243222" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243222-c2b5deb9fc3848df7965f69d078f4b5f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243222-c2b5deb9fc3848df7965f69d078f4b5f-450x297.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243222" class="wp-caption-text">如果冒出的烟十分旺盛，请将火候调小。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 当鸡肉呈现焦褐色后，熄火，盖上锅盖再放置约２０ ～３０分钟（使用余热焖煮）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243224" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243224-1dcca1564624651f4a6e7df16b8b4488.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243224-1dcca1564624651f4a6e7df16b8b4488-450x593.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243224" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉呈现焦褐色后，熄火，盖上锅盖再放置约２０ ～３０分钟。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放凉之后，连同肉汁一起放入密封袋内，移至冷藏库内冷却。比起刚起锅时，稍微放置一阵子再品尝会更美味。静置于冷藏库内一个晚上的话，味道会更加入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243225" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243225-ccc22dbed36239dd0d2299f91ceb18af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243225-ccc22dbed36239dd0d2299f91ceb18af-450x593.jpg" alt="烟熏料理" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243225" class="wp-caption-text">放凉之后，连同肉汁一起放入密封袋内，移至冷藏库内冷却。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">烟熏料理的注意事项</span></h3>
<p>★ 打开锅盖时，烟雾与味道会飘逸而出（比较一般的锅具来说烟雾较少）。由于烟熏的味道会较为浓厚，在室内烹调时，请留意不要让气味影响到邻居。</p>
<p>★ 锅具温度会变得十分高温，请小心不要烫伤。</p>
<p>★ 衣服容易沾染上烟雾的臭味。</p>
<p>★ 锅具内侧与锅盖容易沾附黑色烟渍，可以先使用湿的报纸或保鲜膜轻轻地将脏污去除，再使用海绵与中性洗剂清洗。依照这个方式，比较不会弄脏海绵。</p>
<p>★ 只要确实地清洗锅内，就可以将烟熏的味道去除。</p>
