<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【大人料理】啤酒炖牛肉 独特口味软嫩好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237732-bf591df937e434bc6bff2ce5e7b23e83-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天气渐凉，暖胃的季节又到了，带点苦味的“啤酒炖煮牛肉”是一款大人味料理，有着啤酒香气和软嫩口感的牛肉。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【编者按】“红酒<ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炖牛肉</a>”是一道常见西式家常菜，那么你是否也听过“台式<ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92.md#1">啤酒</a>炖牛肉”这一道菜呢？啤酒在高温炖煮下酒精会挥发掉，留下的是淡淡的啤酒香，同时啤酒酵素在炖煮牛肉的过程中渐渐渗入，牛肉上桌时咬起来非常软嫩好入口。整体的风味带点苦，但是汤品上的<ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89.md#1">香草</a>和啤酒香，混合出台式、西式料理的独特口味，不违和却异常好食。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92.md#1">啤酒</a><ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">炖牛肉</a></span></h2>
<p>这一道汤品藉由啤酒的碳酸让肉变得软嫩。将意大利香芹撕碎后大量撒在料理上，<ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8D%89.md#1">香草</a>的香气与色泽为料理增添韵味。吃起来是带有微微苦涩，大人味的料理。也可以将面包沾汤汁，一起享用。</p>
<p><strong>建议锅具尺寸：20cm</strong></p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（４人份）</span></h3>
<p>牛肉块（咖喱用）&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;３００g<br />
洋葱&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..２颗<br />
红萝卜&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１条<br />
蘑菇&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１包<br />
意大利香芹&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..适量<br />
啤酒&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.１罐（３５０ml）<br />
奶油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;２０g<br />
橄榄油&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１大匙<br />
盐①&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１／２小匙<br />
盐②&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..１小匙<br />
黑胡椒（依照个人喜好）&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将洋葱沿着纤维垂直的方向切成薄片，红萝卜则切成５cm长、１.５cm厚的棒状，蘑菇对半切。撒盐①于牛肉上。</p>
<p>2. 锅内倒入橄榄油，转中火加热，放入洋葱拌炒直到变成透明状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13237730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13237730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237730-4fc915c946096fd3d069656ad5b7fb31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13237730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237730-4fc915c946096fd3d069656ad5b7fb31-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13237730" class="wp-caption-text">锅内倒入橄榄油，转中火加热，放入洋葱拌炒直到变成透明状。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 放入牛肉、红萝卜、蘑菇、啤酒与盐②。煮到沸腾后上盖，转微火并持续加热约１个小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13237731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13237731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237731-e5041acf45ae6186e98c51a35372d950.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13237731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237731-e5041acf45ae6186e98c51a35372d950-450x288.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13237731" class="wp-caption-text">锅内放入牛肉、红萝卜、蘑菇、啤酒与盐。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 熄火后，不要取下锅盖直到变成常温（使用余热焖煮）。</p>
<p>5. 再次转中火加热，沸腾后加入奶油，熄火。依照个人喜好撒上现磨的黑胡椒、撕碎的意大利香芹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13237759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13237759" style="width: 124px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237759-ffcb808a3d273ea95e4c654f712a0d19.png"><img class=" wp-image-13237759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13237759-ffcb808a3d273ea95e4c654f712a0d19-450x546.png" alt="" width="124" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13237759" class="wp-caption-text">《Staub<ahref="https://github.com/vuwsmg331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%B8%E9%93%81%E9%94%85.md#1">铸铁锅</a>料理全书》书封。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
