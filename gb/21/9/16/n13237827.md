<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【烧烤料理】鲷鱼盐釜烧锁住鲜味的秘诀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243160-fa1e3b1b08f0e2a811a3a8890aa9b587-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>盐釜烧料理：在锅内要布满着盐才放入鲷鱼或其他肉类。（幸福文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>日本东北特有的料理法“<ahref="https://github.com/xfzpul3051/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E9%87%9C%E7%83%A7.md#1">盐釜烧</a>”，是一道适合在派对或是特别日子里出现的菜色。遍布锅内的盐分，让味道彻底地入味。如果食材的鱼身较大，可以去尾后再放入。若是食材符合锅子的大小，同样的作法亦可将鱼类更换为猪五花或是猪肉块。料理完毕覆盖食材的厚盐较为坚硬，敲开时请留意盐块飞散。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243165" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243165-9269a7140f1106bffa16ca9192f2baa4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243165-9269a7140f1106bffa16ca9192f2baa4-450x319.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243165" class="wp-caption-text">日本东北特有料理法“<ahref="https://github.com/xfzpul3051/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E9%87%9C%E7%83%A7.md#1">盐釜烧</a>”。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鲷鱼盐釜烧</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">建议锅具尺寸</span></h3>
<p>24cm <ahref="https://github.com/xfzpul3051/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%93%81%E9%93%B8%E9%94%85.md#1">铁铸锅</a>，水滴形锅或圆锅的大小皆适合。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（４人份）</span></h3>
<p>鲷鱼※&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;１尾（约３００g）<br />
蛋白&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..２颗份<br />
低筋面粉&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;３０g<br />
粗盐&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;５００g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 烤箱预热２２０°C。在调理碗里放入蛋白，使用打蛋器打发蛋白直到稍稍冒泡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243158-31d9f0cdde6f4f88fa95e031539e5f1a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243158-31d9f0cdde6f4f88fa95e031539e5f1a-450x433.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243158" class="wp-caption-text">在调理碗里放入蛋白，使用打蛋器打发蛋白直到稍稍冒泡。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 放入盐与低筋面粉，使用硅胶锅铲将食材搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243159-1ffc09cda8d721bc4cad4887b3944759.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243159-1ffc09cda8d721bc4cad4887b3944759-450x433.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243159" class="wp-caption-text">放入盐与低筋面粉，使用硅胶锅铲将食材搅拌均匀。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 锅内铺上烘焙纸，将步骤２一半的份量铺在锅内，再放入鲷鱼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243160" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243160-fa1e3b1b08f0e2a811a3a8890aa9b587.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243160-fa1e3b1b08f0e2a811a3a8890aa9b587-450x433.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243160" class="wp-caption-text">锅内铺上烘焙纸，将步骤２一半的份量铺在锅内，再放入鲷鱼。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 放入步骤２剩下的食材，稍稍调整形状。不要盖上盖子，使用烤箱烤约３０分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243162" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243162-38f3366c119ff75d36dd4b32f8a45baf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243162-38f3366c119ff75d36dd4b32f8a45baf-450x433.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243162" class="wp-caption-text">不要盖上盖子，使用烤箱烤约３０分钟。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 从烤箱取出锅子，使用锅铲或杆面棍等器具将盐釜（覆盖的厚盐）敲开即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13243163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243163-6dbf76ff8ee4a281a0ae0e9ef96fa361.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243163-6dbf76ff8ee4a281a0ae0e9ef96fa361-450x433.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243163" class="wp-caption-text">从烤箱取出锅子。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13243164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13243164" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243164-c7f4f62a09d096e511f2700f121744fd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13243164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13243164-c7f4f62a09d096e511f2700f121744fd-450x614.jpg" alt="盐釜烧" width="450" b="614" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13243164" class="wp-caption-text">使用锅铲或杆面棍等器具将盐釜（覆盖的厚盐）敲开即可。（幸福文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【备注】</span></h3>
<p>※ 鲷鱼的体长约２３～２５cm，事先去掉鳞片、内脏、鱼鳃。可以请店家代为处理。</p>
