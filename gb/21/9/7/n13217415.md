<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>善用质感发饰 轻松完成韩剧造型</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217422-510974-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>随兴马尾x无害公主头。（POYA宝雅提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年09月08日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）韩剧经常成为人们的话题，其中引发高度关注的除了衣着彩妆，就是女性角色的<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E5%9E%8B.md#1">发型</a>，想成功塑造变化丰富又有质感的发型，POYA宝雅推荐多款日韩发饰，打造人气发型，展现活力与自信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13217417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13217417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217417-510969.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13217417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217417-510969-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13217417" class="wp-caption-text">毛巾发圈不咬发丝，简单好固定。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>气质低<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%B0%BE.md#1">马尾</a>造型：发圈</strong></p>
<figure id="13217424" aria-describedby="caption-13217424" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217424-510976-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217424-510976-450x450.jpg" alt="发圈不咬发丝，简单好固定。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217424" class="wp-caption-text">发圈不咬发丝，简单好固定。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13217428" aria-describedby="caption-13217428" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217428-510979-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217428-510979-450x450.jpg" alt="大肠圈还能配戴在手腕上当装饰。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217428" class="wp-caption-text">大肠圈还能配戴在手腕上当装饰。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许多人习惯2到3天才洗一次头发，不想被人发现的秘密小<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E5%9E%8B.md#1">发型</a>，便是以围绕方式绑起韩式低<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%B0%BE.md#1">马尾</a>或丸子头，简单又充满随意感。不咬发丝，简单好固定，一分钟就可完成，轻松搞定。</p>
<p><strong>马尾的小秘诀：大肠圈发束</strong></p>
<figure id="13217425" aria-describedby="caption-13217425" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217425-510977-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217425-510977-450x600.jpg" alt="唯美的缎丝材质大肠圈。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217425" class="wp-caption-text">唯美的缎丝材质大肠圈。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13217418" aria-describedby="caption-13217418" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217418-510970-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217418-510970-450x600.jpg" alt="近期时髦度激增的大肠圈。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217418" class="wp-caption-text">近期时髦度激增的大肠圈。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>使用一般黑色发圈绑发有时过于平凡，可以透过一些蓬松造型发束做变化，抓起发丝也能变成公主头造型。近期时髦度激增的大肠圈，搭配唯美的缎丝材质，不论是马尾还是公主头，都能给人一种温柔精致感。也很推荐佩戴在手腕上，随着行走摆动，成为饰品，更显特色。</p>
<p><strong>高雅又甜美：金属线型鲨鱼夹</strong></p>
<figure id="13217421" aria-describedby="caption-13217421" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217421-510973-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217421-510973-450x600.jpg" alt="金属线型鲨鱼夹气质优雅。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217421" class="wp-caption-text">金属线型鲨鱼夹气质优雅。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13217426" aria-describedby="caption-13217426" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217426-510978-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217426-510978-450x600.jpg" alt="鲨鱼夹无论长短发都能轻松驾驭。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217426" class="wp-caption-text">鲨鱼夹无论长短发都能轻松驾驭。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论长短发都能轻松驾驭，单纯将略长浏海勾在耳后稍显无聊，建议利用鲨鱼抓夹增添一点造型，利用侧边夹发将偏长的浏海随意夹起，看起来精神有活力。金属色系甜美造型，不仅多了一些俏皮感，还能利用不同的穿搭来搭配，创造出独一无二的风格。</p>
<p><strong>韩妞必备：珍珠系发饰</strong></p>
<figure id="13217420" aria-describedby="caption-13217420" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217420-510972-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217420-510972-450x450.jpg" alt="珍珠钻抓夹。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217420" class="wp-caption-text">珍珠钻抓夹。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13217423" aria-describedby="caption-13217423" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217423-510975-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217423-510975-450x450.jpg" alt="高贵甜美的珍珠抓夹。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217423" class="wp-caption-text">高贵甜美的珍珠抓夹。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>珍珠系发饰不同于传统塑胶鲨鱼夹，让你升级成气质系小姐姐，今年流行元素金属线条再搭配小珍珠点缀，只需几秒钟，造型度大大提升。鲨鱼夹稳固又便利，随意夹起都有种知性美感，也让工作效率瞬间提升上来，不会造成头发的负担，轻松又美丽。</p>
<p><strong>戴上你的美丽：发带、发夹</strong></p>
<figure id="13217419" aria-describedby="caption-13217419" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217419-510971-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13217419-510971-450x600.jpg" alt="发带能瞬间提升穿搭造型感。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13217419" class="wp-caption-text">发带能瞬间提升穿搭造型感。（POYA宝雅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>发带及发夹也是能瞬间提升穿搭质感的小方法，寻找材质、颜色不一样且精致的款式，低调的散发着温柔的氛围，让整个人性格更显柔和与知性。◇</p>
