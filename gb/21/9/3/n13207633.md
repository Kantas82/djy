<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大温8月房屋销售速度放缓 价格同比上涨13.2％</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年09月03日讯】（大纪元记者<span style="font-weight: 400;">曹一存</span>加拿<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%B8%A9.md#1">大温</a>哥华编译报导）<span style="font-weight: 400;">根据<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%B8%A9.md#1">大温</a>地产局（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">REBGV</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）最新公布的数据，大温地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房屋销售</a>速度</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">明显</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">放缓，新房源挂牌量同比大幅下降，但<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC.md#1">价格</a>仍在持续上涨。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">数据显示，该地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">房屋销售</a>总量为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,152</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，比去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,047</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，销售量比近</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的平均销售量高出约</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>平均上市35天售出</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大温地区所有住宅物业的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">MLS</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">房屋<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC.md#1">价格</a>指数综合基准价格目前为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">117.66</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元。比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月增长了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13.2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比微增</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%B1%8B.md#1">独立屋</a>的挂牌房源达到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3,736</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，销量为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">945</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,095</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套独立屋销量减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，平均上市</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">35</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天售出。。独立屋的基准价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,807,100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比增幅为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，公寓房的挂牌房源达到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4,156</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，销售量为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,631</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,332</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套相比，增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">22.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，平均上市</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">28</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天售出。公寓物业的基准价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">735,100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的联排别墅的挂牌房源为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,113 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，销售总量为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">576</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">620</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套相比，减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.1%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，平均上市</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">26</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">天售出。基准价格为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">952,600</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">16.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，与</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.3%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h2>市场<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%9B%E5%BA%94%E4%B8%8D%E8%B6%B3.md#1">供应不足</a> 价格仍走高</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">整个夏季大温地区的购房者一直很活跃，但自</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月以来，待售房屋供应量一直在稳步下降。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4,032</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套新房源上市，比去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5,813</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套减少</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">30.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4,377</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">大温地产局的经济学家基思．斯图尔特（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Keith Stewart</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）说：“这些数字表明，住房供应已经成为市场面临的最大问题之一。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份比预期的要繁忙，上市房屋数量没有跟上需求步伐，市场<ahref="https://github.com/wzyhai300/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%9B%E5%BA%94%E4%B8%8D%E8%B6%B3.md#1">供应不足</a>。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">目前大温地区</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">MLS</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">系统上挂牌销售房屋总数为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9,005</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12,803</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">29.7%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2021</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9,850</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">套减少了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">斯图尔特说：“为了缓解价格压力，改善购房选择，市场需丰富待售房屋供应。住房负担能力一直是联邦选举中的关键问题，我们鼓励各政党专注于解决方案，为今天和未来的购房者创造更多样化的住房选择。”◇</span></p>
