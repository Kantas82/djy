<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—姑婆芋下（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211063-8ad2267a5283f7bda3179dcd9f3b83e8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—姑婆芋下（彩墨）。69×68cm。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/hraqrb3904/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%91%E5%A9%86%E8%8A%8B%E4%B8%8B.md#1">姑婆芋下</a><ahref="https://github.com/hraqrb3904/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</h2>
<p>古人说：“画有常理无常法”，意思是说画画没有一定的方法，不要有规范，想怎么画就怎么画，工细整齐或逸笔草草；大红大绿或渗淡灰暗都不计较，任君挥洒。唯一不变的就是不能有违常理。</p>
<p>譬如说有人画茄子，把它吊挂在宛如丝瓜藤蔓的末端，这是不对的。又如有人画螃蟹，在螃蟹身上画上鱼鳞，那更是贻笑大方了，显示他观察写生的能力不足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13211063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13211063" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211063-8ad2267a5283f7bda3179dcd9f3b83e8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13211063" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13211063-8ad2267a5283f7bda3179dcd9f3b83e8.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="576" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13211063" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/hraqrb3904/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%86.md#1">徐明义画集</a>8—<ahref="https://github.com/hraqrb3904/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%91%E5%A9%86%E8%8A%8B%E4%B8%8B.md#1">姑婆芋下</a><ahref="https://github.com/hraqrb3904/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。69×68cm。（图片来源：徐明义提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Under alocasia odora? ink and color painting</h2>
<p>The ancients said: &#8220;The painting has conventions but no permanent rules,&#8221; means that no certain method to paint, no need regulation and specification, paint whatever you want to paint, fine and neat drawing or randomly scribble; it does not matter if it is bright red and green or gloomy gray, do whatever you want to do. The only constant is that you can’t go against conventions.</p>
<p>For example, someone paint eggplant but hangs it at the end of the loofah, so this is wrong. If someone draw a crab with scales on, it is even more ridiculous, showing his incapable of sketch observation.#</p>
