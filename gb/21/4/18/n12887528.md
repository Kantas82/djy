<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：巴萨4：0大胜毕巴 勇夺西班牙国王杯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887577-GettyImages-1313017377-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月18日讯】（大纪元记者李熔石综合报导）当地时间4月17日，2020/21赛季西班牙<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%9D%AF.md#1">国王杯</a>决赛中，<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%A1%9E%E7%BD%97%E9%82%A3.md#1">巴塞罗那</a>以4：0大胜<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E5%B0%94%E5%B7%B4%E9%84%82%E7%AB%9E%E6%8A%80.md#1">毕尔巴鄂竞技</a>，第31次赢得国王杯冠军。这是<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%90%A8.md#1">巴萨</a>最近两年来赢得的第一个冠军奖杯，也是科曼（Ronald Koeman）出任巴萨主帅后获得的首冠。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%90%A8.md#1">巴萨</a>终结721天“冠军荒”</h2>
<p>本赛季，两队已经交手过三次。在联赛中，巴萨以3：2和2：1双杀对手；在西超杯决赛中，毕巴通过加时赛以3：2逆转巴萨，笑到了最后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887572" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887572" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887572-GettyImages-1312998801.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887572" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887572-GettyImages-1312998801.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="574" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887572" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%9D%AF.md#1">国王杯</a>决赛在塞维利亚奥林匹克球场举行。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场，巴萨控球率达到了惊人86%，但未能撕开毕巴的防线，双方战成0：0。下半场，场上形势风云变幻。巴萨在8分钟里连入3球，“杀死”了比赛悬念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887573-GettyImages-1313006213.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887573-GettyImages-1313006213.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887573" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%A1%9E%E7%BD%97%E9%82%A3.md#1">巴塞罗那</a>以4：0大胜<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E5%B0%94%E5%B7%B4%E9%84%82%E7%AB%9E%E6%8A%80.md#1">毕尔巴鄂竞技</a>夺冠。图为现场的巴萨球迷。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12887568" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887568" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887568-GettyImages-1232365066.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887568" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887568-GettyImages-1232365066.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887568" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。图为现场的毕尔巴鄂竞技球迷。(CRISTINA QUICLER/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第60分钟，德容右路传中，无人防守的格列兹曼轻松推射入得手。第63分钟，阿尔巴左路传中，德容头球破门；第68分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E8%A5%BF.md#1">梅西</a>和德容踢墙配合后，切入禁区再下一城。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887570" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887570-GettyImages-1232366730.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887570-GettyImages-1232366730.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887570" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。两队球员争顶瞬间。(CRISTINA QUICLER/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12887569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887569-GettyImages-1232365697.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887569-GettyImages-1232365697.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887569" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。<ahref="https://github.com/vdisel398/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E8%A5%BF.md#1">梅西</a>（左）在比赛中。(CRISTINA QUICLER/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第72分钟，梅西接阿尔巴左路传中，劲射破门，完成“梅开二度”。最终，巴萨4：0大胜毕巴，夺得国王杯，终结了球队721天的“冠军荒”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887575" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887575-GettyImages-1313016376.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887575-GettyImages-1313016376.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="444" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887575" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。图为巴萨球员赛后庆祝夺冠。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>梅西作为巴萨队长首捧奖杯</h2>
<p>大胜毕巴夺冠之后，巴萨球员捧杯时显得异常兴奋，包括了梅西和皮克等那些老将。这对于见惯了大场面的巴萨球员来讲，让人颇感意外。因为在重建期夺冠，对于巴萨而言是个意外惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887578-GettyImages-1313017397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887578-GettyImages-1313017397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="471" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887578" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然对梅西来讲，这是他在巴萨赢得的第35个冠军奖杯，是队中获得奖杯最多的球员，皮克和布斯克茨也拿到了个人的30个；而对另外15名巴萨球员来讲，这是他们在巴萨的第一个奖杯。要知道，巴萨上一次夺冠还要追溯到2018/19赛季的西甲联赛，当时他们提前3轮夺得了西甲冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887576-GettyImages-1313017324.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887576-GettyImages-1313017324.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="519" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887576" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。这是梅西作为巴萨队长第一次捧起冠军奖杯。(Fran Santiago/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实，巴萨本届国王杯之旅，也走得相当惊险。在前四轮比赛，巴萨踢了三场加时赛才得以晋级，其中两次是终场前打进了“绝平球”。对于巴萨小将们来讲，这一路走来，让他们收获了更多的信心，这才是最重要的。</p>
<p>对梅西来说，这个冠军也很特别。这是他作为巴萨队长赢得的第一个冠军奖杯。而巴萨主席拉波尔塔（Joan Laporta）则认为，这个冠军对梅西留队很有帮助。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12887571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12887571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887571-GettyImages-1232366901.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12887571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12887571-GettyImages-1232366901.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="468" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12887571" class="wp-caption-text">2020/21赛季西班牙国王杯决赛，巴塞罗那以4：0大胜毕尔巴鄂竞技夺冠。图为巴萨主席拉波尔塔（左）和主帅科曼庆祝夺冠。(CRISTINA QUICLER/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>梅西与巴萨的合同本赛季结束后到期，阿根廷球星是否会继续留在巴萨，现在还未可知。据西班牙媒体分析，梅西的好友拉波尔塔当选巴萨新主席后，他留队的可能性越来越大。</p>
