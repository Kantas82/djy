<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日式地瓜羊羹作法 古早味百吃不厌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853899-P.82-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>手作日式甜点：地瓜羊羹。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>风味朴实的<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a><ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E7%BE%B9.md#1">羊羹</a>，让人百吃不厌。据说在古早时代，地瓜羊羹是相当受欢迎的点心，常被视为高价羊羮的替代品。制作地瓜泥时，不只是将地瓜压碎成泥而已，还会利用细筛网过筛，入口的口感非常地滑顺，风味十分特别。</p>
<p>◆材料（14cm×11cm×4.5cm的塑形盘）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a>（去皮后的重量） 400g<br />
上白糖 90～100g<br />
盐 少量<br />
炒过的黑芝麻 适量</p>
<p>◆前置准备<br />
?上白糖事先过筛备用。<br />
?取2张烘焙纸，以十字交叠的方式铺入塑形盘中。<br />
?双层蒸笼的下层要先加水，上层则铺上干的纱布，要事先将蒸笼准备好以便随时可用。</p>
<p>◆作法<br />
1. 削掉地瓜外皮后，切成厚度2公分大小的块状，泡水约15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853900" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853900-P.83-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853900-P.83-1-300x244.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853900" class="wp-caption-text">作法1。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 接着把地瓜的水分擦干，放入蒸笼，开大火蒸10～15分钟，若用竹签能扎得进去就表示蒸熟了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853901" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853901-P.83-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853901-P.83-2-300x243.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853901" class="wp-caption-text">作法2。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将地瓜趁热移入碗中，加入上白糖与盐，用研磨棒或压泥器将碗中的地瓜捣碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853902" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853902-P.83-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853902-P.83-3-300x287.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853902" class="wp-caption-text">作法3。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊上白糖可以依地瓜的甜度，调整使用分量。</p>
<p>4. 在钢盆上铺一块沾过水拧干的纱布，然后放上细筛网，将步骤3的地瓜泥趁热过筛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853903" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853903-P.83-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853903-P.83-4-300x286.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853903" class="wp-caption-text">作法4。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 隔着纱布将步骤4的地瓜泥充分揉捏成一整块。</p>
<div class="mceTemp"></div>
<figure id="attachment_12853904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853904" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853904-P.83-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853904-P.83-5-300x242.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853904" class="wp-caption-text">作法4。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将步骤5的地瓜泥分三次填入塑形盘内，每次约1／3。每次填入时要用手平均地压紧地瓜泥，以去除内部的空气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853905" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853905-P.83-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853905" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853905-P.83-6-300x242.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853905" class="wp-caption-text">作法6。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 将所有的地瓜泥填入后，用塑形盘的隔板之类的物品将表面压平，并铺上烘焙纸以避免干燥，接着再包上一层保鲜膜并放入冰箱冷藏。<br />
＊动作要快，必须趁地瓜泥尚温热时完成所有的步骤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853906" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853906-P.83-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853906-P.83-7-300x201.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853906" class="wp-caption-text">作法7。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 放入冰箱1～2小时，确定完全定型后取出，依个人喜好切成合适的大小，撒上黑芝麻做装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853907" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853907-P.83-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853907-P.83-8-300x200.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 地瓜羊羹" width="300" b="200" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853907" class="wp-caption-text">作法8。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
