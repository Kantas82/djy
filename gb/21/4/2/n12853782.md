<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不再是买回家吃的点心 自己作日式胡桃馒头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853784-P.58-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>手作日式甜点：胡桃馒头。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小小圆圆的烤<ahref="https://github.com/ldbjnk371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%92%E5%A4%B4.md#1">馒头</a>，是在面团中加入奶油烘烤而成的。无论是使用红豆内馅或是其他口味的内馅制作，都是非常好吃的美味点心。在吃之前猜测里面是哪一种馅料，也是享用这道点心的乐趣之一。</p>
<p>◆材料（约20个）<br />
蛋液 40g<br />
上白糖 60g<br />
无盐奶油 25g<br />
小苏打粉 1g<br />
低筋面粉（面团用） 120g<br />
低筋面粉（手粉用） 适量<br />
胡桃蛋黄豆沙馅 520g<br />
装饰用胡桃 适量</p>
<p>◆前置准备<br />
?在烤盘铺上烘焙纸备用。<br />
?烤箱事先预热至180℃。<br />
?低筋面粉等制作面团须使用到的各种粉类都要事先过筛备用。<br />
?小苏打粉以少许的水溶解备用。<br />
?将胡桃蛋黄豆沙馅均分为1个26g的球状内馅备用。<br />
?装饰用的胡桃事先切碎备用。</p>
<p>◆作法<br />
1. 将蛋液倒入钢盆内，加入上白糖并充分搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853786" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853786-P.59-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853786-P.59-2-300x288.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853786" class="wp-caption-text">作法1 （图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将奶油切成小块使其更易融化，然后倒入钢盆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853787" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853787-P.59-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853787-P.59-3-300x288.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853787" class="wp-caption-text">作法2 。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 稍稍隔水加热直到上白糖与奶油融化为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853788" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853788-P.59-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853788-P.59-4-300x287.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853788" class="wp-caption-text">作法3。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊上白糖若没有充分溶解在蛋液中，烘烤完的成品会发生上色不均的状况，所以务必要使其充分溶解。</p>
<p>4. 步骤3的奶油蛋液放凉后，加入小苏打水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853789" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853789-P.59-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853789-P.59-5-300x282.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853789" class="wp-caption-text">作法4。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 倒入低筋面粉，用刮刀将面粉与蛋液充分搅拌成面团。接着用保鲜膜包起来，放入冰箱冷藏30分钟左右，让面团松弛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853790" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853790-P.59-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853790-P.59-6-300x283.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853790" class="wp-caption-text">作法5-1。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12853791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853791" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853791-P.59-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853791-P.59-7-300x289.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853791" class="wp-caption-text">作法5-2。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊由于面团中含有奶油，会使面团较为黏手。若面团太黏，不但会使后续处理困难，也可能会在处理过程中加入过多的手粉。</p>
<p>6. 将手粉用的低筋面粉过筛后撒在平盘上，然后在盘子上揉捏面团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853792" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853792-P.59-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853792-P.59-8-300x290.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853792" class="wp-caption-text">作法6。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 将步骤6做好的面团对折，然后一边调整面团的硬度一边充分地揉捏，直到面团变得柔软滑顺为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853793" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853793-P.60-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853793-P.60-1-300x285.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853793" class="wp-caption-text">作法7。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 将步骤7的面团一边测量重量，一边分成20个小面团（1个重13g）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853794" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853794-P.60-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853794-P.60-2-300x243.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853794" class="wp-caption-text">作法8。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 在手上沾些面粉，将一个面团放在左手掌心，接着把面团压平成圆形面皮，再用毛刷将上面多余的粉末刷掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853795" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853795-P.60-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853795" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853795-P.60-3-300x286.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853795" class="wp-caption-text">作法9。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 将先前准备好的内馅球放在面皮中央，轻轻地把手掌合起包住内馅球，接着用面皮将内馅整个包裹起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853796" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853796-P.60-4.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853796-P.60-4-300x285.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853796" class="wp-caption-text">作法10。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 用指尖捏住面团开口处将之闭合后，再用双手轻轻地调整好形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853797" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853797-P.60-5.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853797-P.60-5-300x286.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="286" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853797" class="wp-caption-text">作法11。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12. 将所有半成品表面上多余的粉末以毛刷刷掉，接着间隔排入事先备好的烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853798" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853798-P.60-6.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853798-P.60-6-300x285.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853798" class="wp-caption-text">作法12。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊若面团沾上多余的粉末，烤好的成品会有上色不均的情形。</p>
<p>13. 在每个面团正中央放上一片装饰用的胡桃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853799" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853799-P.60-7.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853799-P.60-7-300x284.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853799" class="wp-caption-text">作法13。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14. 用喷雾瓶将水雾均匀地喷撒在半成品的表面使其湿润。放入事先预热至180℃的烤箱下层烘烤约15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853800" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853800-P.60-8.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853800-P.60-8-300x285.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853800" class="wp-caption-text">作法14。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊烘烤的时间会因烤箱的机种而异，因此为避免发生上色不均的情况，烘烤时请以15分钟为基准，并视烘烤的状况调整面团的位置，或是将已上色的成品取出烤箱。</p>
<p>15. 烘烤完成之后移至网架上放凉即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853801" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853801-P.60-9.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853801-P.60-9-300x285.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853801" class="wp-caption-text">作法15。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>＊刚烤好的成品口感粗糙，静置一日后口感会较为湿润。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">胡桃蛋黄豆沙馅</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12853817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853817" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853817-P.18-1.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853817-P.18-1-300x214.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="214" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853817" class="wp-caption-text">胡桃蛋黄豆沙馅。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在蛋黄豆沙馅加入胡桃，为馅料增添口感与香气</p>
<p>◆材料（成品约400g）<br />
蛋黄豆沙馅 330g<br />
细砂糖 25g<br />
胡桃 30g<br />
水麦芽 25g<br />
水 80cc～</p>
<p>◆前置准备<br />
＊将胡桃放入预热至150℃的烤箱烘烤到飘出香味后，将胡桃切碎成方便拌入馅料的大小。</p>
<p>◆作法<br />
1. 先在锅中加水煮到沸腾，再放入细砂糖搅拌使其溶解，最后拌入蛋黄豆沙馅熬煮。<br />
＊若太硬不易搅拌，就稍微加一点水，让馅料软化到较容易搅拌的状态。</p>
<p>2. 将馅料熬煮到用刮刀从锅中舀起后，落下时会像山一样地层层堆叠，即为正确的硬度（图a）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853818" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853818" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853818-P.18-2.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853818" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853818-P.18-2-300x200.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="200" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853818" class="wp-caption-text">图a 若用刮刀从锅中舀起后，落下时会像山一样地层层堆叠，即为正确的硬度。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 加入胡桃碎（图b）后继续搅拌，接下来关掉炉火，拌入水麦芽，利用锅中的余热和馅料搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12853819" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12853819" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853819-P.18-3.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-12853819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12853819-P.18-3-300x203.jpg" alt="手作日式甜点, 馒头" width="300" b="203" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12853819" class="wp-caption-text">图b 胡桃若切得太大块，会影响口感，所以要切成适当的大小最佳。（图/苹果屋出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 把馅料压到锅边和沾在锅边煮到干硬的豆沙充分拌匀后，分成数小堆置于平盘上放凉。<br />
＊若馅料仍旧很软，可以将豆泥压到锅边静置一段时间，使水分蒸发。</p>
