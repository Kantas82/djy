<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（2）作客野生动物家园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876678-20210413_africa_helen_chou_206-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坦桑尼亚的塔兰吉雷(Tarangire)国家公园的斑马。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/21/4/6/n12861186.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">走进非洲（1）作客野生动物家园</a></p>
<p>长颈鹿撒完狗粮回家了，我们也要前往今晚的住宿点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876682" style="width: 352px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876682-20210413_africa_helen_chou_202.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876682-20210413_africa_helen_chou_202.jpg" alt="非洲" width="352" b="469" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876682" class="wp-caption-text">塔兰吉雷公园内的Sopa Lodge渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今晚我们就住在塔兰吉雷公园内的Sopa Lodge Resort。第一眼就喜欢上了这极具非洲风味的小楼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876681" style="width: 1125px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876681-20210413_africa_helen_chou_203.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876681-20210413_africa_helen_chou_203.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1125" b="844" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876681" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村的餐厅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在房间内稍事休息后就前往餐厅吃晚餐。渡假村里的餐厅也极有特色。不过更吸引我的是餐厅外的景色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876680" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876680" style="width: 1125px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876680-20210413_africa_helen_chou_204.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876680-20210413_africa_helen_chou_204.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1125" b="558" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876680" class="wp-caption-text">非洲大陆的黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此时太阳已经西下。放眼望去，近处的灌木丛与远处的猴面包树上一片金色。我顾不得吃饭，静等人生第一次在非洲大陆的日落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876679" style="width: 1153px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876679-20210413_africa_helen_chou_205.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876679-20210413_africa_helen_chou_205.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1153" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876679" class="wp-caption-text">日落非洲大陆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太阳正落在塔兰吉雷坡(Tarangire Hill)上。第一次用相机纪录到了非洲大陆的日落，没有“大漠孤烟直，长河落日圆”的气势，不过也很满足了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876678" style="width: 1146px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876678-20210413_africa_helen_chou_206.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876678-20210413_africa_helen_chou_206.jpg" alt="非洲, 斑马" width="1146" b="765" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876678" class="wp-caption-text">坦桑尼亚的塔兰吉雷(Tarangire)国家公园的<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%91%E9%A9%AC.md#1">斑马</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一夜好眠。第二天一大早出发继续热身赛。先是如愿以偿的看到了一群正在吃早饭的<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%91%E9%A9%AC.md#1">斑马</a>。看着这黑白分明圆滚滚的斑马PP我也是醉了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876677" style="width: 1183px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876677-20210413_africa_helen_chou_207.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876677" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876677-20210413_africa_helen_chou_207.jpg" alt="非洲, 狼" width="1183" b="787" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876677" class="wp-caption-text">黑背胡狼(Black backed jackal)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876676" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876676-20210413_africa_helen_chou_208.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876676-20210413_africa_helen_chou_208.jpg" alt="非洲, 鸵鸟" width="350" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876676" class="wp-caption-text">鸵鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876675" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876675" style="width: 1129px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876675-20210413_africa_helen_chou_209.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876675-20210413_africa_helen_chou_209.jpg" alt="非, 鸵鸟" width="1129" b="780" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876675" class="wp-caption-text">鸵鸟两公婆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876674" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876674-20210413_africa_helen_chou_210.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876674-20210413_africa_helen_chou_210.jpg" alt="非洲, 老鹰" width="351" b="480" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876674" class="wp-caption-text">老鹰。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了斑马，一路上还看到在岔路口匆匆而过的黑背胡狼，在车前不惊不慌过马路的鸵鸟及在林中悠闲散步的鸵鸟两公婆。我们的车子还惊起了躲在草丛中的老鹰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876673" style="width: 1146px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876673-20210413_africa_helen_chou_211.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876673-20210413_africa_helen_chou_211.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1146" b="765" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876673" class="wp-caption-text">水羚(Waterbuck)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876672" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876672-20210413_africa_helen_chou_212.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876672-20210413_africa_helen_chou_212.jpg" alt="非洲" width="350" b="485" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876672" class="wp-caption-text">水羚(Waterbuck)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>出公园的路上还看到了颜值颇高的水羚。导游跟我说：“你等它转身时一定要再拍一张。”水羚大概听懂了，一转身，我也乐了：你的PP果然很QQ。</p>
<p>出了公园，车子一过马路就到曼雅拉湖国家公园（Lake Manyara National Park）。顾名思义，这里主要是观赏水上动物与鸟类。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876671" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876671" style="width: 1125px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876671-20210413_africa_helen_chou_213.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876671-20210413_africa_helen_chou_213.jpg" alt="非洲, 鹈鹕" width="1125" b="749" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876671" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%88%E9%B9%95.md#1">鹈鹕</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876670" style="width: 1125px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876670-20210413_africa_helen_chou_214.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876670-20210413_africa_helen_chou_214.jpg" alt="非洲, 鹈鹕" width="1125" b="751" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876670" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B9%88%E9%B9%95.md#1">鹈鹕</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>湖面上最引人注目的是一群表演水上芭蕾的鹈鹕。不知是谁领头，一群鹈鹕扎猛子入水，湖面上只见一对对翅膀和肥PP。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876669" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876669-20210413_africa_helen_chou_215.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876669-20210413_africa_helen_chou_215.jpg" alt="非洲" width="350" b="461" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876669" class="wp-caption-text">长脚雉鸻(African Jacana)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在公园里看到了十几种以前没见过的鸟类。印象最深的是一种叫“长脚雉鸻”的大脚鸟，当地人把它叫做“耶稣鸟”(Jesus bird)，因为它那双大脚能在水上行走，人们觉得很神奇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876668" style="width: 1108px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876668-20210413_africa_helen_chou_216.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876668-20210413_africa_helen_chou_216.jpg" alt="非洲水牛" width="1108" b="745" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876668" class="wp-caption-text">非洲<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E7%89%9B.md#1">水牛</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12876667" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876667" style="width: 1119px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876667-20210413_africa_helen_chou_217.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12876667" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876667-20210413_africa_helen_chou_217.jpg" alt="非洲水牛" width="1119" b="791" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876667" class="wp-caption-text">非洲<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E7%89%9B.md#1">水牛</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>突然间游客纷纷往车边跑，导游也让我们上车。原来是水牛慢慢踱步过来了。到底是非洲五大猛兽之一，霸气侧漏的水牛一出场，只靠一个眼神就把人们乖乖的逼回车上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12876666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12876666" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876666-20210413_africa_helen_chou_218.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12876666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12876666-20210413_africa_helen_chou_218.jpg" alt="非洲, 蓝猴" width="350" b="524" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12876666" class="wp-caption-text">蓝猴。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车子开过一片小树林，导游指着树上说看蓝猴。怎么看都不蓝啊，叫灰猴更适合，身上的毛是深深浅浅50度灰。</p>
