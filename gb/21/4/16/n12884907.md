<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉义市校外会 办理热力青春反毒探索营活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884910-493485-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	在活动期间，春晖志工一起投入其中，用关怀温暖孩子的心，使莘莘学子勇于拒绝毒品的诱惑。（嘉义市校外会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年04月16日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）为使<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E7%94%9F.md#1">学生</a>提升自我胆识、应变能力、激发潜能、肯定自己及培养开放与尊重的态度，教育部嘉义市联络处暨嘉义市学生校外生活辅导会（以下简称<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%A4%96%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校外会</a>），由军训督导樊立平上校带领教官同仁、春晖志工团队及<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E7%94%9F.md#1">学生</a>共计30人，假南投日月潭青年活动中心，规划为期3日的“热力青春、<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E6%AF%92.md#1">反毒</a>探索营”活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12884912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884912-493486.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12884912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884912-493486-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884912" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E6%AF%92.md#1">反毒</a>探索营第二天活动。（嘉义市<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%A4%96%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校外会</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>樊立平表示，此次活动目的为增强学生对药物滥用防制的认知及能力培育，藉由各校反毒种子师资学生，以各项探索活动及挑战自我的方式，培养面对挫折、超越自我、迈向目标的能力，并藉由“做中学”体验式学习，让学生在做中了解、体验生命的可贵，认识<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E5%93%81.md#1">毒品</a>及对自身的危害。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12884914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884914-493487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12884914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884914-493487-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884914" class="wp-caption-text">赏鲸船学习信赖与协作。（嘉义市校外会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>春晖志工林三元表示，学生藉由破冰活动开始人际关系拓展，在课程中学会尊重、沟通、协调、创意激发，进而增进胆识应变及勇于尝试面对挑战；在活动期间，春晖志工一起投入其中，用关怀温暖孩子的心，使莘莘学子勇于拒绝<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E5%93%81.md#1">毒品</a>的诱惑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12884915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884915-493488.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12884915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884915-493488-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884915" class="wp-caption-text">操舟体验。（嘉义市校外会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉义高商庄同学表示，参与此次活动我跟我同学都很兴奋，除了有骑脚踏车环湖、还有水上活动，最让我们兴奋的是，还能让我们学习和他人一起交流，虽然只有短短三天，但我想这将会是个不错且难忘的体验！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12884917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884917-493491.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12884917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884917-493491-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884917" class="wp-caption-text">骑脚踏车环湖。（嘉义市校外会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12884916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884916-493490.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12884916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884916-493490-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884916" class="wp-caption-text">骑脚踏车环湖。（嘉义市校外会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>规划挑战极限、设计自我突破的活动课程，活动项目中包含“高低空设施挑战”、“团队自力造筏”及“人际关系沟通技巧”等，使学生了解正确药物使用观念、防制校园霸凌事件、遵守各项安全规则及尊重性别之间的差异性，进而形塑正确的价值观及人生观，更期许这群反毒小尖兵继续为校园安全传递正向力量！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12884918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12884918" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884918-493492.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12884918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12884918-493492-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12884918" class="wp-caption-text">高索课程。（嘉义市校外会提供）</figcaption></figure>
