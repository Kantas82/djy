<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（1）作客野生动物家园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861209-20210406_africa_helen_chou_101-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>坦桑尼亚的塔兰吉雷(Tarangire)国家公园的大象群。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>老话说的对，凡好事总得多磨。第一次去<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>，因飞机机械故障，误点再误点再误点，最终航班取消。折腾一夜之后总算换了一家航空公司再次起飞，比原定计划整整晚了24小时才踏上非洲大陆。飞机在坦桑尼亚的乞力马扎罗国际机场（Kilimanjaro International Airport）降落已是当地时间的半夜。司机把我们带到位于德鲁提(Duluti)河畔的渡假村后，我撑着拍下房间的照片，掀起蚊帐倒头就睡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861208" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861208" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861208-20210406_africa_helen_chou_102.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861208-20210406_africa_helen_chou_102.jpg" alt="非洲" width="355" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861208" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>第一夜。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几小时后，迷迷糊糊在手机闹钟声中醒来。隐约记得已在非洲，一声欢呼从床上跳下。一番洗漱后前往餐厅吃早饭，顺便走走看看渡假村的风景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861207" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861207" style="width: 1114px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861207-20210406_africa_helen_chou_103.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861207-20210406_africa_helen_chou_103.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1114" b="770" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861207" class="wp-caption-text">德鲁提河畔的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昨晚瞎灯黑火根本看不清周围，现在一看还真不错。四周一片绿油油的，空气也是湿漉漉的。与想像中的非洲有点不一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861206" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861206" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861206-20210406_africa_helen_chou_104.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861206-20210406_africa_helen_chou_104.jpg" alt="非洲" width="355" b="473" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861206" class="wp-caption-text">德鲁提河畔的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12861205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861205" style="width: 356px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861205-20210406_africa_helen_chou_105.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861205-20210406_africa_helen_chou_105.jpg" alt="非洲" width="356" b="499" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861205" class="wp-caption-text">颇具欧洲风格的渡假村餐厅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12861204" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861204" style="width: 357px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861204-20210406_africa_helen_chou_106.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861204-20210406_africa_helen_chou_106.jpg" alt="非洲" width="357" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861204" class="wp-caption-text">非洲的第一顿早餐。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一杯咖啡，一杯果汁下肚后满血复活了。早餐后我们就要启程开始为期一周的Safari（专用名词，即在非洲大陆移动，进行狩猎或观赏野生动物的一种海外旅行方式。）。当然我们只观赏，不杀生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861203" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861203-20210406_africa_helen_chou_107.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861203-20210406_africa_helen_chou_107.jpg" alt="非洲" width="355" b="487" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861203" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村服务员。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>渡假村见不到搬运行李的推车。服务员一人顶一个行李箱在前面带路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861202" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861202" style="width: 356px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861202-20210406_africa_helen_chou_108.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861202-20210406_africa_helen_chou_108.jpg" alt="非洲" width="356" b="477" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861202" class="wp-caption-text">Safari专用越野车。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在接下来的一周里，这辆车和司机将带着我们驰骋在坦桑尼亚的原野上追逐动物们的踪迹，近距离观察它们在自然界中是如何生活的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861201" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861201" style="width: 357px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861201-20210406_africa_helen_chou_109.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861201-20210406_africa_helen_chou_109.jpg" alt="非洲" width="357" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861201" class="wp-caption-text">阿鲁沙市中心。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们昨晚住宿的渡假村位于坦桑尼亚的阿鲁沙(Arusha)市，离乞力马扎罗国际机场50公里左右。阿鲁沙有“非洲的日内瓦”之称，是Safari之都。座落在市中心的这个钟塔标志着从开罗到开普敦的非洲之中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861200" style="width: 1084px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861200-20210406_africa_helen_chou_110.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861200-20210406_africa_helen_chou_110.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1084" b="762" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861200" class="wp-caption-text">阿鲁沙的交通。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>街上也到处可见头上顶着大大小小各色各样的物品的行人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861199" style="width: 1084px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861199-20210406_africa_helen_chou_111.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861199-20210406_africa_helen_chou_111.jpg" alt="非洲" width="1084" b="813" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861199" class="wp-caption-text">塔兰吉雷国家公园(Tarangire National Park)的猴面包树。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天是我们Safari之行的热身赛，在塔兰吉雷国家公园看动物。该公园是坦桑尼亚的第六大保护区。它的名称来源于塔兰吉雷河。这条河从公园内蜿蜒穿过，在旱季时是保护区内动物们唯一的饮水来源。该国家公园以<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%B1%A1.md#1">大象</a>、猴面包树和非洲岩蟒著称。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861198" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861198" style="width: 356px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861198-20210406_africa_helen_chou_112.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861198-20210406_africa_helen_chou_112.jpg" alt="非洲" width="356" b="503" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861198" class="wp-caption-text">从公园内蜿蜒而过的塔兰吉雷河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12861197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861197" style="width: 1028px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861197-20210406_africa_helen_chou_113.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861197-20210406_africa_helen_chou_113.jpg" alt="非洲, 大象" width="1028" b="668" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861197" class="wp-caption-text">塔兰吉雷国家公园内的象群。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导游说，公园内有600多头非洲象。我们这天起码看到了一半。它们每天只睡6小时，其余18个小时就是吃吃喝喝，再喝喝吃吃，中间拉拉撒撒，再撒撒拉拉。这也太悠闲了吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861196" style="width: 1029px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861196-20210406_africa_helen_chou_114.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861196-20210406_africa_helen_chou_114.jpg" alt="非洲, 大象" width="1029" b="681" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861196" class="wp-caption-text">象宝宝与象妈妈。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导游说这头象宝宝才三个月大。它步履蹒跚的跟在象妈妈后面呆萌呆萌的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861195" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861195" style="width: 995px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861195-20210406_africa_helen_chou_115.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861195-20210406_africa_helen_chou_115.jpg" alt="非洲, 黑面猴" width="995" b="685" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861195" class="wp-caption-text">黑面猴。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12861194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861194" style="width: 995px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861194-20210406_africa_helen_chou_116.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12861194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861194-20210406_africa_helen_chou_116.jpg" alt="非洲" width="995" b="663" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861194" class="wp-caption-text">黑面猴。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑面猴真是脸漆黑，可能比包公的脸还黑吧。可等它华丽一转身，却看到它的蛋蛋是粉蓝粉蓝的喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12861193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12861193" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861193-20210406_africa_helen_chou_117.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12861193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12861193-20210406_africa_helen_chou_117.jpg" alt="非洲" width="355" b="533" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12861193" class="wp-caption-text">栗头丽椋鸟(Superb Starling) 。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只鸟的羽毛蓝的也太亮眼了，名字也好听：栗头丽椋鸟，是东非常见的一种鸟。</p>
