<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JYD7U"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/chxaej3386/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/chxaej3386/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台北报导）台湾<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>县<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a><ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">樱花季</a>，清明连假期间大批游客涌入，阿里山工作站统计4月2日有超过4千人入园。</p>
<p>台湾林务局<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>林区管理处表示，清明连假来<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>国家森林游乐区赏花正是时候，随着气温回暖，盛开的染井吉野樱、高砂樱、八重红枝垂、木兰、茶花、垂丝海棠、玉山杜鹃，将森林点缀的花团锦簇。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12857150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857150-2104040022262378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12857150" title="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857150-2104040022262378-600x338.jpg" alt="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857150" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">樱花季</a>，生态观察家暨摄影师苏家弘拍摄蒸汽桧木主题列车行驶樱花树间的美景。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12857147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857147-2104040022282378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12857147" title="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857147-2104040022282378-600x338.jpg" alt="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857147" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山樱花季，生态观察家暨摄影师苏家弘拍摄蒸汽桧木主题列车行驶樱花树间的美景。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>生态观察家暨摄影师苏家弘表示，林务局阿里山林业铁路及文化资产管理处（林铁处）为响应樱花季，有推出阿里山<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6.md#1">小火车</a>桧木车厢主题列车，到对高岳<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%A8%B1.md#1">赏樱</a>之旅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12857159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12857159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857159-2104040022302378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12857159" title="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12857159-2104040022302378-600x338.jpg" alt="台湾阿里山樱花季 清明连假游客涌入" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12857159" class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山樱花季，生态观察家暨摄影师苏家弘拍摄蒸汽桧木主题列车行驶樱花树间的美景。（苏家弘提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>苏家弘说，游客要拍摄阿里山“火车版樱花”，可以在阿里山派出所旁边一个弯角的地方，或是到对高岳车站。日前他有搭林铁处的阿里山<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%A8%B1.md#1">赏樱</a>之旅主题列车，到对高岳拍摄蒸气列车行驶樱花树间的美景和蒸汽火车表演，看到游客都非常兴奋欢呼。</p>
