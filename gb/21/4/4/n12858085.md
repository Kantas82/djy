<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：迈阿密网赛 胡尔卡奇创历史 巴蒂卫冕</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858185-GettyImages-1310792582-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>迈阿密网球公开赛男单决赛中，24岁的胡尔卡奇夺得了波兰史上首个ATP大师赛男单冠军。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月05日讯】（大纪元记者王也知综合报导）上周末，2021美国迈阿密网球公开赛中，24岁的<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8D%A1%E5%A5%87.md#1">胡尔卡奇</a>（Hubert Hurkacz）创造了历史，直落两盘击败19岁意大利新星<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E7%BA%B3.md#1">辛纳</a>（Jannik Sinner），夺得波兰史上首个ATP大师赛男单冠军；女单决赛中，因加拿大名将<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%BE%B7%E8%8E%B1%E6%96%AF%E5%BA%93.md#1">安德莱斯库</a>（Bianca Andreescu）比赛中扭伤脚踝退赛，世界第一、24岁澳洲好手<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%92%82.md#1">巴蒂</a>（Ashleigh Barty）轻松卫冕。</p>
<h2>波兰选手首夺大师赛男单冠军</h2>
<p>在迈阿密网赛历史上，不满20岁打进男单决赛的选手此前只有三人，分别是德约科维奇、纳达尔和阿加西，本届比赛，意大利小将<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E7%BA%B3.md#1">辛纳</a>淘汰西班牙老将阿古特后，成为了第四位；他也是自2005年马德里大师赛纳达尔之后，闯进ATP1000级别男单决赛最年轻的球手。而<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8D%A1%E5%A5%87.md#1">胡尔卡奇</a>则是职业生涯首次在一项赛事中接连击败世界排名前十选手——希腊好手西西帕斯和俄罗斯名将卢布列夫，首登大师赛决赛舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858186" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858186-GettyImages-1310794650.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858186" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858186-GettyImages-1310794650.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858186" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛男单决赛中，24岁的胡尔卡奇直落两盘击败意大利新星辛纳，夺得冠军。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12858188" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858188-GettyImages-1310809713.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858188-GettyImages-1310809713.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858188" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛男单决赛中，19岁意大利新星辛纳获得亚军。(Matthew Stockman/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>胡尔卡奇和辛纳之前从未交过手，不过两人在今年曾一起合作出战过男双比赛。首盘，胡尔卡奇很快上手，取得3-0开局。之后，辛纳逐渐适应，在第5局回破，并在第11局再度破发，以6-5反超。但在接下来的发球胜盘局中，小将辛纳突然失去手感，连丢4分，让胡尔卡奇将比赛拖入“抢七”。</p>
<p>“抢七”中，经验更加老道的胡尔卡奇，一直占据主动，以7-4先声夺人。次盘，胡尔卡奇越打越好，连续破发取得4-0领先。辛纳在第5局艰难保发后，试图发起反击，但为时已晚。最终，胡尔卡奇以6-4再下一城，笑到最后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858187" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858187" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858187-GettyImages-1310794925.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858187-GettyImages-1310794925.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858187" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛男单决赛中，胡尔卡奇成为波兰网球史上首位赢得大师赛单打冠军的球员。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这场胜利，让胡尔卡奇成为波兰网球史上首位赢得大师赛单打冠军的球员；他也是继2005年巴黎大师赛的伯蒂奇之后，首位夺得大师赛男单桂冠世界排名三十开外的球员。在最新世界排名中，胡尔卡奇的排名将达到个人历史新高——第16位；而19岁小将辛纳的排名也将升至第22位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858190" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858190-GettyImages-1310811481.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858190-GettyImages-1310811481.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858190" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛男单决赛中，24岁的胡尔卡奇（右）直落两盘击败19岁意大利新星辛纳，夺得波兰史上首个ATP大师赛男单冠军。(Matthew Stockman/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>收“退赛礼” <ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%92%82.md#1">巴蒂</a>成功卫冕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12858179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858179" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858179-GettyImages-1310634494.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858179-GettyImages-1310634494.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858179" class="wp-caption-text">2021迈阿密网球公开赛女单决赛中，世界第一、24岁澳洲名将巴蒂成功卫冕女单冠军。(Matthew Stockman/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>女单决赛是巴蒂和<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E5%BE%B7%E8%8E%B1%E6%96%AF%E5%BA%93.md#1">安德莱斯库</a>之间的首次交手。世界排名第一的巴蒂自2020年澳网后，进行了长达11个月休战，但在最新排名规则下，澳洲人保住了榜首位置；而安德莱斯库在2019年获得美网女单冠军后，2020年因伤同样缺席了几乎整个赛季。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858181" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858181-GettyImages-1310635527.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858181" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858181-GettyImages-1310635527.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858181" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛女单决赛中，成功卫冕女单冠军的澳洲名将巴蒂击球瞬间。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>两人在晋级决赛的过程中，都有一些惊险的经历，前法网冠军巴蒂挽救过赛点；而安德莱斯库则连续经历了四场三盘大战，比赛用时比巴蒂多出了将近3个小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858182" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858182-GettyImages-1310635595.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858182-GettyImages-1310635595.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="1001" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858182" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛女单决赛中，获得女单亚军的加拿大名将安德莱斯库击球瞬间。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>首盘，巴蒂快速进入状态，连续破发；相比之下，安德莱斯库的状态明显不佳。结果，巴蒂以6-3拿下首盘。次盘，安德莱斯库因双误送掉开盘的发球局。第3局，加拿大人不慎跌倒，再次遭到破发。局间休息时，她要求理疗师对其右脚脚踝进行了处理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858178" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858178-GettyImages-1310633677.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858178-GettyImages-1310633677.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="364" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858178" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛女单决赛中，加拿大名将安德莱斯库扭伤脚踝瞬间。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12858180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858180" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858180-GettyImages-1310634534.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858180-GettyImages-1310634534.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="370" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858180" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛女单决赛中，加拿大名将安德莱斯库脚踝受伤，中途退赛。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>之后，安德莱斯库又坚持了一局，最终含泪无奈退赛。这样，头号种子巴蒂以6-3/4-0成功卫冕女单冠军。这是巴蒂在职业生涯首次实现卫冕，同时也是她赢得的第二个WTA1000级别赛事冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858184" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858184-GettyImages-1310636215.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12858184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858184-GettyImages-1310636215.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="852" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858184" class="wp-caption-text">迈阿密网球公开赛，世界第一巴蒂成功卫冕女单冠军。(Michael Reaves/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
