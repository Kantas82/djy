<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：F1伊莫拉站 维斯塔潘夺冠 小汉获亚军</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889327-GettyImages-1232382184-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>F1伊莫拉站，红牛车手维斯塔潘赢得个人本赛季首胜。(BRYN LENNON/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月20日讯】（大纪元记者王也知综合报导）上周末，F1第二站——意大利大奖赛在著名的伊莫拉赛道举行。红牛车手<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E6%96%AF%E5%A1%94%E6%BD%98.md#1">维斯塔潘</a>（Max Verstappen）赛季首次登顶；杆位起步的<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E8%B5%9B%E5%BE%B7%E6%96%AF.md#1">梅赛德斯</a>车手<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%AF%86%E5%B0%94.md#1">汉密尔</a>顿（Lewis Hamilton）位居第二；<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%88%E5%87%AF%E4%BC%A6.md#1">迈凯伦</a>英国车手诺里斯（Lando Norris）排名第三。</p>
<p>伊莫拉地处意大利北部，这是一条以狭窄著称的经典赛道。1994年，拉森博格（Ratzenberger）驾驶的赛车在排位赛以超过300公里的速度，撞上了水泥墙，这位即将退役的奥地利车手因伤重去世。第二天，巴西车王塞纳（Ayrton Senna）也撞上了防护墙，命丧伊莫拉。</p>
<p>此后，这条赛道的许多危险性弯角被重新设计，高速弯也被改成了低速弯。现役车手中，只有阿隆索和<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E5%AF%86%E5%B0%94.md#1">汉密尔</a>顿有过在伊莫拉夺冠的经历。而从前五排之外发车的车手还从未率先冲线过。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12889338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889338-GettyImages-1313136796.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889338-GettyImages-1313136796.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889338" class="wp-caption-text">F1<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%8A%E8%8E%AB%E6%8B%89%E7%AB%99.md#1">伊莫拉站</a>，红牛车手<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E6%96%AF%E5%A1%94%E6%BD%98.md#1">维斯塔潘</a>。(Bryn Lennon/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12889330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889330" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889330-GettyImages-1313112561.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889330-GettyImages-1313112561.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889330" class="wp-caption-text">F1<ahref="https://github.com/xdgivf3848/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%8A%E8%8E%AB%E6%8B%89%E7%AB%99.md#1">伊莫拉站</a>比赛中，红牛车手维斯塔潘（右）与汉密尔顿竞争瞬间。(Lars Baron/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在排位赛，汉密尔顿夺得杆位，红牛车手佩雷兹和维斯塔潘分列第二和第三位。正赛在雨后湿滑的路面上展开。起步阶段，汉密尔顿和维斯塔潘发生了擦碰，前者的赛车前翼受损，后者升至首位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12889331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889331" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889331-GettyImages-1313118899.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889331-GettyImages-1313118899.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889331" class="wp-caption-text">F1伊莫拉站，因事故出现，赛会派出了安全车。(Bryn Lennon/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一圈跑完，因赛道上发生了事故，安全车出动。比赛恢复后，维斯塔潘接连刷新了几个最快单圈，领先达到5秒。之后，小汉也不示弱，逐渐逼近对手，将差距缩小到1.3秒。第28圈开始，两人相继进站换胎。出站后，维斯塔潘仍处在领先位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12889333" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889333" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889333-GettyImages-1313119402.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889333-GettyImages-1313119402.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="408" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889333" class="wp-caption-text">F1伊莫拉站，维斯塔潘的红牛赛车在维修站进行换胎瞬间。(Mark Thompson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12889325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889325" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889325-GettyImages-1232380982.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889325-GettyImages-1232380982.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889325" class="wp-caption-text">F1伊莫拉站，勒克莱尔驾驶的法拉利赛车。( MIGUEL MEDINA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12889324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889324" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889324-GettyImages-1232380090.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889324-GettyImages-1232380090.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="410" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889324" class="wp-caption-text">F1伊莫拉站，维斯塔潘驾驶的红牛赛车。(MIGUEL MEDINA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第31圈，赛道上风云突变，汉密尔顿在超越慢车时，不慎冲进缓冲区，撞上了防护墙，赛车前鼻翼严重受损，他不得不回维修通道更换，出站后，他的排名落到第9位。</p>
<p>就在同时，小汉的队友博塔斯在跟拉塞尔争抢位置时，发生严重碰撞，大量碎片散落在赛道上，赛会直接出示“红旗”暂停了比赛。半小时后，比赛恢复。汉密尔顿开始上演了超车好戏。他从第九位开始一路追赶，直到第60圈，他升至第二。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12889335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12889335" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889335-GettyImages-1313126125.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12889335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12889335-GettyImages-1313126125.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12889335" class="wp-caption-text">F1伊莫拉站，维斯塔潘驾驶的红牛赛车率先冲线瞬间。(Mark Thompson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终经过63圈争夺，维斯塔潘率先冲线，成为第16位在伊莫拉赛道登上最高颁奖台的车手，汉密尔顿排名第二，迈凯轮车手诺里斯位列第三。勒克莱尔、小塞恩斯、里卡多、斯特罗尔、加斯利、莱科宁和奥康分别排在第四至第十位，都有相应积分入账。</p>
