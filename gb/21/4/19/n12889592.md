<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（3）非洲农家乐与塞伦盖蒂野生动物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890444-20210419_africa_helen_chou_301-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>接一篇：走进<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>（2）<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/21/4/13/n12876643.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">走进非洲（2）作客野生动物家园</a></p>
<p class="title">导游给我们准备的惊喜是什么呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890443" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890443" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890443-20210419_africa_helen_chou_302.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890443" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890443-20210419_africa_helen_chou_302-450x600.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890443" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>农家乐。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>非洲农家乐！这的确是出乎意料的欣喜。我每到一个新的地方总喜欢看看当地人的生活，尝尝他们喜欢吃的食物。还别说，午餐的菜式不少，味道也不错。主要是还有米饭。一碗饭下肚，胃一下妥贴了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890442-20210419_africa_helen_chou_303.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890442" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890442-20210419_africa_helen_chou_303-450x573.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="573" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890442" class="wp-caption-text">非洲的啤酒。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当地出产的啤酒也必须喝一瓶，反正不是我开车！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890441" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890441" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890441-20210419_africa_helen_chou_304.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890441" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890441-20210419_africa_helen_chou_304-450x600.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890441" class="wp-caption-text">曼雅拉湖畔的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酒足饭饱后导游把我们带到了今晚的住宿地 – 又一个及具非洲风格的渡假村。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890440" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890440-20210419_africa_helen_chou_305.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890440-20210419_africa_helen_chou_305-450x600.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890440" class="wp-caption-text">曼雅拉湖畔的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12890438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890438" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890438-20210419_africa_helen_chou_306.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890438-20210419_africa_helen_chou_306-450x266.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="266" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890438" class="wp-caption-text">晨曦中的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12890437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890437" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890437-20210419_africa_helen_chou_307.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890437-20210419_africa_helen_chou_307-450x616.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890437" class="wp-caption-text">晨曦中的渡假村。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一夜休整后，第二天起个大早。晨曦中的渡假村宁静美丽。露台上望远镜所指的方向就是我们昨天去的曼雅拉湖国家公园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890436-20210419_africa_helen_chou_308.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890436-20210419_africa_helen_chou_308-450x316.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890436" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村餐厅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早餐后我们就将进入坦桑尼亚的大型<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>区，<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%9E%E4%BC%A6%E7%9B%96%E8%92%82.md#1">塞伦盖蒂</a>国家公园(Serengeti National Park)。这是我们这次Safari的重头戏。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%9E%E4%BC%A6%E7%9B%96%E8%92%82.md#1">塞伦盖蒂</a>”是当地著名的游牧民族马赛人对这一地区的称呼，意思是“无尽的平原”。1951年成为坦桑尼亚第一座国家公园。每年该公园都会出现超过150万只角马和约25万头斑马的大规模迁徙。1981年被列入联合国教科文组织世界遗产名录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890435" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890435-20210419_africa_helen_chou_309.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890435-20210419_africa_helen_chou_309-450x322.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890435" class="wp-caption-text">塞伦盖蒂国家公园入口。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12890433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890433-20210419_africa_helen_chou_310.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890433-20210419_africa_helen_chou_310-450x210.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="210" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890433" class="wp-caption-text">蓝天白云之下的角马群(Wildebeest)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一进公园大门，眼前即是一望无际的平原。导游指着远方几个小黑点说：“角马来了！”</p>
<p>我极目远眺，也只看到几个蚂蚁大小的黑点在地平线上移动。几分钟后黑点变成了一条黑线，再接着一群角马就出现在了眼前。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890432" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890432" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890432-20210419_africa_helen_chou_311.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890432-20210419_africa_helen_chou_311-450x300.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890432" class="wp-caption-text">打盹的狮子。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车子继续往公园内开去。一会儿导游指着一堆草丛说：“看狮子！”原来是一头母狮子在打盹。</p>
<p>几分钟后导游把车子往左一拐，上了条小路，取出望远镜对我们说要试试运气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890431-20210419_africa_helen_chou_312.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890431-20210419_africa_helen_chou_312-450x300.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890431" class="wp-caption-text">打哈欠的金钱豹。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们运气果然不错。顺着导游所指的方向，我用长焦镜对着草丛中的一点一对焦，就看到一只张着血盆大口打哈欠的金钱豹。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890430-20210419_africa_helen_chou_313.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890430-20210419_africa_helen_chou_313-450x300.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890430" class="wp-caption-text">猎豹(Cheetah)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>离金钱豹不远，我们还遇到了两只拦路虎，应该说是拦路豹更准确。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890429-20210419_africa_helen_chou_314.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890429-20210419_africa_helen_chou_314-450x305.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890429" class="wp-caption-text">猎豹(Cheetah)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12890428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890428-20210419_africa_helen_chou_315.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890428-20210419_africa_helen_chou_315-450x691.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="691" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890428" class="wp-caption-text">优雅冷漠的猎豹(Cheetah)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猎豹是陆地上跑的最快的动物，速度可达每小时70英里。见我们的车子过来，它们慢慢的踱到路边的草丛里，摆出各种姿势让我们拍照。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890427" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890427-20210419_africa_helen_chou_316.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890427-20210419_africa_helen_chou_316-450x607.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="607" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890427" class="wp-caption-text">猎豹(Cheetah)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后它们起身往草丛深处走去，其中一只还转头冲着我们呲牙咧嘴，看我们敢不敢跟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12890426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890426-20210419_africa_helen_chou_317.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890426-20210419_africa_helen_chou_317-450x214.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="214" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890426" class="wp-caption-text">捕猎中的猎豹。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12890425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12890425" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890425-20210419_africa_helen_chou_318.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12890425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12890425-20210419_africa_helen_chou_318-450x169.jpg" alt="非洲, 猎豹" width="450" b="169" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12890425" class="wp-caption-text">捕猎中的猎豹。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接下来看到的一幕就有点惊心动魄了。两只猎豹冲着不远处的一群斑马和角马跑去，惊的这群个子比它们大一倍的食草动物四下躲闪，马背上的鸟也一飞冲天。</p>
<p>导游兴奋的告诉我们，这是猎豹试图冲散外围防护捕捉被护在里面的小斑马小角马。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
