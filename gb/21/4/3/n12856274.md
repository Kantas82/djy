<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全澳房产过户量涨幅 西澳夺魁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856283-535-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>数据显示，2月西澳的房产过户量录得50%的同比增长。图为珀斯一房产待售。（林文责/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月03日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者杨新云澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">根据房地产过户数字平台</span></span>PEXA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的数据显示，</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月西澳的房产过户量录得</span></span>50%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的同比增长。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据商业新闻网报导，</span></span>PEXA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">研究发现，在</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月和</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月期间，西澳有超过</span></span>1.5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万笔房产过户，比</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年同期多出</span></span>5000<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">笔，此前中共病毒爆发（又称</span></span>COVID19<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）对房市造成影响。</span></span></p>
<p>PEXA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">高级研究经理</span></span>Mike Gill<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">表示，总体而言，澳洲房产市场表现强劲，<ahref="https://github.com/yezrrt3460/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">西澳房地产</a>销售量的提升有多重因素所致。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月，在全澳有</span></span>60,508<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">笔房产过户，同比增长约</span></span>28%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，这一趋势是继</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份的房市强劲表现与</span></span>COVID19<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">之后的显着复苏之后”，</span></span>Gill<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">说，“西澳房产销售量的增长出现在</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年中期即全澳实施边境封锁之后，这得益于该州在控制病毒传播方面的成功。”</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份房产过户量同比增长居全澳之首，其次是南澳，同比增长</span></span>35%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，昆士兰州（增长</span></span>34%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）、新州（增长</span></span>28%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）和维州（增长</span></span>14%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“从房产过户的领先指标如新房源与销售量等来看，我们预计这种增长趋势将持续到</span></span>3<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月份及以后，</span></span>2021<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年的过户量已经远远高于</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年。”</span></span>Gill说<span lang="zh-CN">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">责任编辑：周鑫</span></span></p>
