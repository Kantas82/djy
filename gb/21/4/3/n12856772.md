<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：德甲法甲“榜首之争”拜仁和里尔胜出</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856841-GettyImages-1310633358-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德甲第27轮，拜仁客场1：0险胜莱比锡。拜仁门将诺伊尔高接低挡，力保球门不失。(Alexander Hassenstein/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月04日讯】（大纪元记者李熔石综合报导）当地时间4月3日，<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E7%94%B2.md#1">德甲</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E7%94%B2.md#1">法甲</a>两大联赛同时上演了“天王山之战”。德甲“领头羊”<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E4%BB%81.md#1">拜仁</a>慕尼黑在客场1：0险胜联赛排名第二的<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B1%E6%AF%94%E9%94%A1.md#1">莱比锡</a>RB，将领先优势扩大到7分，德甲冠军奖盘已近在咫尺。在法甲一同领跑的<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E%E5%9C%A3%E6%97%A5%E8%80%B3%E6%9B%BC.md#1">巴黎圣日耳曼</a>和里尔直接交手，结果里尔在客场1：0获胜，以3分优势，力压对手登顶。比赛中，内马尔因故意推人被罚下场，巴黎可谓输球又输人。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E4%BB%81.md#1">拜仁</a>客场险胜<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B1%E6%AF%94%E9%94%A1.md#1">莱比锡</a>七分领跑</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E7%94%B2.md#1">德甲</a>本轮开赛前，拜仁26战，积61分，以4分优势领跑德甲，莱比锡排在第二。本场两队直接对话将决定赛季争冠的大致走势。如果莱比锡想冲击冠军，就必须要赢下这场“六分之战”；如果输球，分差将扩大为7分，逆转机会将变得非常渺茫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856833" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856833-GettyImages-1232089089.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856833-GettyImages-1232089089.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="385" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856833" class="wp-caption-text">德甲第27轮，拜仁客场1：0险胜莱比锡。图为莱比锡红牛主场（the Red Bull Arena stadium）。(ALEXANDER HASSENSTEIN/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>两队实力确实有不小的差距，但对莱比锡来讲，好消息是，已为拜仁本赛季联赛打进35球的波兰前锋莱万多夫斯基因伤缺阵，爆冷获胜可能性还是有的。没想到让出一个莱万，莱比锡面对德甲霸主仍显得无能为力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856839-GettyImages-1310621379.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856839-GettyImages-1310621379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856839" class="wp-caption-text">德甲第27轮，拜仁客场1：0险胜莱比锡。图为双方球员拼抢瞬间。(Photo by Alexander Hassenstein/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场第37分钟，拜仁由穆勒策动进攻，他助攻队友格雷茨卡（Leon Goretzka）禁区内爆射得手。拜仁取得1：0领先。这粒进球刷新了拜仁队史各项赛事连续取得进球场次纪录——62场；而穆勒则收获了个人联赛第15次助攻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856840" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856840-GettyImages-1310631624.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856840-GettyImages-1310631624.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856840" class="wp-caption-text">德甲第27轮，凭借格雷茨卡（左）打进唯一入球，拜仁在客场1：0险胜莱比锡。图为他同队友穆勒庆祝进球。(Alexander Hassenstein/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12856838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856838" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856838-GettyImages-1232091713.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856838-GettyImages-1232091713.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="511" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856838" class="wp-caption-text">德甲第27轮，拜仁客场1：0险胜莱比锡。穆勒贡献了个人联赛第15次助攻。(ALEXANDER HASSENSTEIN/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>易边再战，莱比锡放手一搏，主帅纳格尔斯曼（Julian Nagelsmann）先后将替补席上三名前锋全部换上场，这给拜仁施加了不小的压力。但面对对手下半场高达两位数的射门，拜仁门将诺伊尔（Manuel Neuer）高接低挡，力保城门不失。最终，拜仁在客场1：0笑到最后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856836" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856836-GettyImages-1232089682.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856836-GettyImages-1232089682.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856836" class="wp-caption-text">德甲第27轮，拜仁客场1：0险胜莱比锡。图为双方球员拼抢瞬间。( ANNEGRET HILSE/POOL/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本轮过后，拜仁积64分，以7分优势领跑，联赛仅剩下7轮，冠军已在向他们招手；莱比锡积57分，排名第二；沃尔夫斯堡主场一球小胜保级球队科隆后，积54分，位居第三；法兰克福本轮在客场2：1战胜了多特蒙德，以50分，稳居第四，而积43分的多特距离“前四”已达7分，“争四”前景变得不妙。</p>
<h2>内马尔染红 大巴黎输球又输人</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12856830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856830-GettyImages-1232086087.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856830-GettyImages-1232086087.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856830" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E7%94%B2.md#1">法甲</a>第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败<ahref="https://github.com/loyxaz397/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E%E5%9C%A3%E6%97%A5%E8%80%B3%E6%9B%BC.md#1">巴黎圣日耳曼</a>。图为大巴黎的主场——王子公园体育场（the Parc des Princes Stadium）。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>法甲30轮比赛过后，巴黎和里尔同积63分，前者仅以净胜球优势排在首位。本轮两队直接交手，巴黎排出姆巴佩（Kylian Mbappe）、内马尔（Neymar）、迪马利亚（Angel Di Maria）的豪华攻击线。比赛开始后，巴黎进攻犀利，内马尔和姆巴佩接连威胁对方球门，但均无功而返，反倒让客队先拔头筹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856832" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856832-GettyImages-1232088831.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856832-GettyImages-1232088831.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="497" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856832" class="wp-caption-text">法甲第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败巴黎圣日耳曼。比赛中，内马尔（中）是对方防守球员主要针对的对象。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第19分钟，里尔打出反击，伊科内右路传中，乔纳森?戴维（Jonathan David）劲射破门，助里尔取得1：0领先。这位21岁加拿大前锋近期状态非常火爆，11场比赛已打进8球；不过，进球后不久，戴维就因伤退场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856834" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856834-GettyImages-1232089194.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856834-GettyImages-1232089194.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856834" class="wp-caption-text">法甲第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败巴黎圣日耳曼。图为巴黎22岁球星姆巴佩（右）在比赛中。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>取得领先后，里尔全力防守，表现相当坚韧，巴黎的进攻屡屡受阻。下半场刚开场，内马尔反击中被推倒，巴西人起身后与对手发生冲突，并掌掴对手，吃到了一张黄牌。比赛的火药味越来越浓，双方冲突不断。久攻不下，巴黎球员情绪变得急躁；而里尔则伺机反击，若不是门将纳瓦斯（Keylor Navas）神勇扑救，巴黎很可能会丢第二球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856831-GettyImages-1232086354.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856831-GettyImages-1232086354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856831" class="wp-caption-text">法甲第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败巴黎圣日耳曼。图为巴黎阿根廷球星迪马利亚（Angel Di Maria）。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>比赛临近尾声，内马尔和对手贾洛在边线处对抗，后者阻挡前者快发边线球。在对抗中，内马尔倒地，他起身后做出了一个匪夷所思的动作，一把将对手推倒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856835-GettyImages-1232089602.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856835-GettyImages-1232089602.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856835" class="wp-caption-text">法甲第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败巴黎圣日耳曼。图为主裁判给了内马尔（右）第二张黄牌，将其罚出场。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴西人此举，立马引发了双方冲突，主裁判迅速做出反应，给了内马尔一张黄牌，这样他两黄变一红，被罚出场，这彻底断送了巴黎扳平的希望；而贾洛也收到一张黄牌。数据显示，内马尔在法甲已4次被罚下，同期只有其他两人吃到过4张或者4张以上红牌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12856837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12856837" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856837-GettyImages-1232090171.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12856837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12856837-GettyImages-1232090171.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12856837" class="wp-caption-text">法甲第31轮，里尔在客场1：0击败巴黎圣日耳曼，以3分优势力压对手登顶。图为比赛最后时刻，巴黎门将纳瓦斯（中）到对手门前争顶头球瞬间。(FRANCK FIFE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终，里尔1：0获胜，25年来首次在客场战胜大巴黎，以3分优势，力压对手领跑法甲。此前，人们普遍认为，在欧洲五大联赛中，大巴黎登顶法甲是最没有悬念的。但本赛季，巴黎似乎陷入了危机，这已是他们本赛季在法甲输掉的第5个主场，而在此前7年，他们在主场也仅输了4场联赛。</p>
