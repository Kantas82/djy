<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】扇贝蜜瓜草莓沙拉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871067-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>清凉一下～扇贝蜜瓜草莓沙拉。（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a>季节来到了尾声，不赶快抓紧品尝非常可惜，但是单吃还是淋上炼乳吃，是不是了无新意？没有关系，面对迎来的炎炎夏日，让草莓入菜，甜甜酸酸的滋味马上消暑气！</p>
<p>搭配上低热量高蛋白的海鲜贝类，只能说是绝佳的菜肴了，如果你在减肥，更不要错过这样的健康美食，保证让你大口吃也没负担，吃得满足。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%87%E8%B4%9D.md#1">扇贝</a>蜜瓜<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E8%8E%93.md#1">草莓</a>沙拉</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%89%87%E8%B4%9D.md#1">扇贝</a>12安士（Scallops 12 oz）<br />
蜜瓜10安士（Melon 10 oz）<br />
草莓8粒（Strawberry 8 pieces）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%8E%93.md#1">黑莓</a>16粒（Blackberry 16 pieces）<br />
熟通心粉6安士（Cooked Penne ziti Rigate 6 oz）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871049" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871049-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871049" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871049-1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871049" class="wp-caption-text">草莓8粒、<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E8%8E%93.md#1">黑莓</a>16粒（图／<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12871054" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871054" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871054-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871054" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871054-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871054" class="wp-caption-text">通心粉6安士，煮熟。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味沙拉酱】</span></h3>
<p>美乃滋半杯（Mayonnaise 1/2 C）<br />
甜炼奶1茶匙（Sweetened Condensed Milk 1t）<br />
柠檬汁1茶匙（Lemon Juice 1t）拌匀备用</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871051" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871051-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871051-2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871051" class="wp-caption-text">美乃滋、甜炼奶、柠檬汁。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 扇贝先用水煮熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871052-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871052-3-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871052" class="wp-caption-text">扇贝先用水煮熟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 锅中放少许油，扇贝煎至金黄后取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871053" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871053-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871053-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871053" class="wp-caption-text">锅中放少许油，扇贝煎至金黄后取出备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 通心粉煮熟后放凉备用。<br />
4. 草莓切开边，蜜瓜切粒。在一个大碗内放入所有材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12871062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12871062" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871062-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12871062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12871062-6-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12871062" class="wp-caption-text">蜜瓜切粒（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将调好的沙拉酱放入拌匀后，上碟便可享用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h3>
<p>若然想吃冰凉一点，可以将沙拉先放入冰箱冷冻30分钟即可。◇</p>

<p>姓名：梁志生<br />
厨龄：40余年<br />
经历：<br />
1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />
2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />
3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />
