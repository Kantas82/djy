<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】椰青蒸蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852432-f88969e70b55564e412eba08fa390892-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道椰青蒸蛋，散发着奇特诱人的芬香。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>椰子是典型的热带水果，既美味可口又养生保健。椰子的功效与作用有很多，无论是椰子汁还是椰肉，都含有非常丰富的营养成分。果实越成熟，所含的蛋白质和脂肪越多，这是其它水果难以比拟的。</p>
<p>椰子肉富含人体所需的18种氨基酸，还含有钙、锌、锰、铁、维生素C等营养元素，能够有效地补充人体的营养成分，提高机体的抗病能力。</p>
<p>椰汁含有丰富的钾、镁等矿物质，其成分与细胞内液相似，可纠正脱水和电解质紊乱，达到利尿消肿的功效。</p>
<p>椰汁还含有生长激素、维生素和大量的人体必需的微量元素，经常饮用，能补充细胞内液，扩充血容量，滋润皮肤，尤其适合女性享用。</p>
<p>枸杞是驰名中外的名贵中药材，药食同源的历史悠久，早在《神农本草经》中就被列为上品，称其“久服轻身不老、耐寒暑”。枸杞子中含有多种氨基酸，并含有甜菜碱、玉蜀黍黄素、酸浆果红素等特殊营养成分。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E9%9D%92%E8%92%B8%E8%9B%8B.md#1">椰青蒸蛋</a>，散发着奇特诱人的芬香，自然的椰子和牛奶的香气使普通的蒸蛋提升为又香又滑的美味，以枸杞做配料，不仅在色彩上画龙点睛，而且更加养生滋补。</p>
<h2>【<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8E%A8%E5%AF%BB%E5%91%B3.md#1">罗厨寻味</a>】<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E9%9D%92%E8%92%B8%E8%9B%8B.md#1">椰青蒸蛋</a></h2>
<p>主料：椰青2个、鸡蛋3个</p>
<p>配料：鲜奶4盎司、枸杞</p>
<p>调料：蜂蜜4汤匙（根据个人喜好添加）</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将椰青顶部开口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852396" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852396-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852396-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852396" class="wp-caption-text">将椰青顶部开口。<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将椰子水倒入盆中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852398" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852398-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852398" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852398-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852398" class="wp-caption-text">将椰子水倒入盆中。<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将椰子顶部切开形成带盖的容器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852401-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852401-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852401" class="wp-caption-text">将椰子顶部切开形成带盖的容器。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将椰子水、鲜奶、枸杞和椰青放入蒸箱蒸15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852403-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852403-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852403" class="wp-caption-text">将椰子水、鲜奶、枸杞和椰青放入蒸箱蒸15分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将鲜奶倒入蛋液中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852408-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852408-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852408" class="wp-caption-text">将鲜奶倒入蛋液中。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 放入椰青水后一起打匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852412-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852412-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852412" class="wp-caption-text">放入椰青水后一起打匀。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 将蛋奶倒入椰青内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852415-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852415-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852415" class="wp-caption-text">将蛋奶倒入椰青内。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 加盖后放入蒸箱蒸约30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852416" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852416-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852416-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852416" class="wp-caption-text">加盖后放入蒸箱蒸约30分钟。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 蛋完全凝固后取出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852419" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852419-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852419-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852419" class="wp-caption-text">蛋完全凝固后取出。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 加入几颗枸杞子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852421" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852421-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852421-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852421" class="wp-caption-text">加入几颗枸杞子。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 最后添加蜂蜜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12852422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12852422" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852422-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12852422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12852422-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12852422" class="wp-caption-text">最后添加蜂蜜。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
