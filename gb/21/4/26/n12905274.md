<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>世华妇女企协纽约分会 李艳翠当选第三届会长</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905283-148645-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>张俊裕主持监交印信仪式，李艳翠（右）从卸任会长叶周森娣手上接棒，正式出任会长。（黄小堂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月26日讯】（大纪元记者黄小堂美国<ahref="https://github.com/sxzqkx306/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/sxzqkx306/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%81%E7%AE%A1%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">世界华人工商妇女企管协会</a>大纽约分会24日举办2021年新旧会长交接典礼，在驻纽约台北经文处副处长张俊裕的监交下，第二届会长叶周森娣正式交棒给新当选的第三届会长李艳翠；纽约华侨文教服务中心主任陈永丰、驻美投资贸易服务处主任丁伟、纽约市议员顾雅明等将近50位嘉宾出席了典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12905278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905278-148640.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905278-148640-600x261.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905278" class="wp-caption-text">图为出席<ahref="https://github.com/sxzqkx306/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E5%95%86%E5%A6%87%E5%A5%B3%E4%BC%81%E7%AE%A1%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">世界华人工商妇女企管协会</a>大<ahref="https://github.com/sxzqkx306/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>分会“2021年新旧会长交接典礼”的全体嘉宾合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12905279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905279" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905279-148641.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905279-148641-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905279" class="wp-caption-text">世界华人工商妇女企管协会大纽约分会会员合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12905280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905280-148646.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905280-148646-600x307.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905280" class="wp-caption-text">纽约华侨文教服务中心主任陈永丰（右六），向世界华人工商妇女企管协会大纽约分会第三届理事颁发当选证书。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卸任会长叶周森娣致词介绍，2019年4月接棒会长职务，与社区举办多场慈善活动。2020年1月召开会员大会时，会员近倍数成长，为大纽约华裔工商妇女提供优质的交流平台。2020年疫情爆发后，大纽约分会在支援防疫行动上不落人后，配合社区服务机构，捐赠医疗物资予所需单位。</p>
<p>新任李艳翠致词表示，她从事文化与医疗相关产业，而纽约是世界经济贸易枢纽，人文荟萃，加上美国政府对于少数族裔与妇女企业有很多鼓励发展的法案，将来分会有很大的发展空间，将尽自己在美国商场上的经验与知识，结合姐妹们的力量与智慧，协助海外的华人妇女一起发展经济贸易，提升妇女的地位而努力，如同总会宗旨“心怀世界，掌握脉动、建立网络、发展经贸”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12905276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905276-148638.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12905276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905276-148638-600x713.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905276" class="wp-caption-text">世界华人工商妇女企管协会大纽约分会第三届会长李艳翠致词。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处副处长张俊裕主持了印信交接、责任炼传承、致赠水晶及当选聘书等仪式；文教服务中心主任陈永丰则代表侨委会委员长童振源，分别向叶周森娣和李艳翠致赠感谢状和贺状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12905282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905282-148644.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905282-148644-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905282" class="wp-caption-text">驻纽约台北经文处副处长张俊裕（左），向世界华人工商妇女企管协会大纽约分会新任会长李艳翠（中）致赠水晶。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12905281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905281-148643.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905281" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905281-148643-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905281" class="wp-caption-text">驻纽约台北经文处副处长张俊裕，向世界华人工商妇女企管协会大纽约分会卸任会长颁发责任炼传承。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12905277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12905277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905277-148639.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12905277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12905277-148639-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12905277" class="wp-caption-text">纽约华侨文教服务中心主任陈永丰（中）代表侨委会委员长童振源，向叶周森娣（左）和李艳翠（右）致赠感谢状和贺状。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天出席典礼的嘉宾还包括松柏爱心会会长王玉英、纽约市皇后区环保局局长Tommy Lin和国会议员孟昭文代表黄敏仪等。◇</p>
