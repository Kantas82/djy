<h6>过百民众在麻州勒星顿镇的公园里庆祝爱国者日。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年04月21日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8B%92%E6%98%9F%E9%A1%BF.md#1">勒星顿</a>镇报导）4月19日是美国一年一度的<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD%E8%80%85%E6%97%A5.md#1">爱国者日</a>。在打响<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E6%88%98%E4%BA%89.md#1">美国独立战争</a>第一枪的<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>勒星顿镇(Lexington)，上百名民众与家人和朋友欢快庆祝这一节日。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894440" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894440-DSC1734.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894440" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894440-DSC1734-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894440" class="wp-caption-text">与会者很恭敬地在聆听国歌演唱。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8B%92%E6%98%9F%E9%A1%BF.md#1">勒星顿</a>镇海斯汀公园(Hastings Park)飘荡着欢快的音乐，四周装点着爱国旗帜和标语。在温暖的阳光下，许多孩子在草地上玩耍。集会者们唱国歌，宣读效忠誓词，还以弹唱和朗诵诗歌的形式表达他们的爱国热情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894438-DSC1738.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894438-DSC1738-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894438" class="wp-caption-text">集会者吉他弹唱，表达爱国热情。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“作为美国人，我很自豪。相信这里的每一位爱国者都有同样的感觉。所以才会有这么多人来。”<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>广播节目主持、副州长竞选人坎贝尔(Rayla Campbell)说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894428-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.54.03-AM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894428-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.54.03-AM-600x404.png" alt="" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894428" class="wp-caption-text">麻州广播节目主持、副州长竞选人坎贝尔（Rayla Campbell）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>麻州居民林肯(Grace Lincoln)认为，爱国意味着支持美国宪法、法律和秩序。“这是这个国家的根基。我们重视这些。我们还相信上帝，上帝保佑美国。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894435-DSC1743.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894435-DSC1743-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894435" class="wp-caption-text">麻州居民林肯（Grace Lincoln）（左）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>受访者还批评延长的防疫封锁措施和政府对人们生活的过度控制。“你要站起来说，受够了封锁，受够了商店关门，限制客流，受够了口罩指令。孩子们需要回到学校，我们需要自由。”坎贝尔说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894434" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894434-DSC1721.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894434-DSC1721-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894434" class="wp-caption-text">在<ahref="https://github.com/mkpdrk3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD%E8%80%85%E6%97%A5.md#1">爱国者日</a>，勒星顿镇街道上插着国旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12894433" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894433-DSC1722.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894433-DSC1722-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894433" class="wp-caption-text">在爱国者日，勒星顿镇图书馆外挂了巨大的国旗。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有受访者表示，这样的控制措施，是社会主义和共产主义制度的一种表现。他们担心社会主义和共产主义对美国的渗透日益加深。来自越南的移民凯莉(Bao Chau Kelley)担心，美国的社会主义力量也会推进言论管制。“阅读宪法，在你还有机会做的时候，实践每一件事。因为在共产主义国家，他们教育你不要思考政治，不要讨论政治，最重要的是不要实践政治。”她说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894425-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.56.01-AM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894425-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.56.01-AM-600x366.png" alt="" width="600" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894425" class="wp-caption-text">来自越南的移民凯莉（Bao Chau Kelley）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>退伍军人联合会弗拉明翰(Framingham)929站指挥官桑图里安(Ingrid Centurion)说，美国人应团结起来对抗共产主义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12894430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12894430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894430-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.53.40-AM.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-12894430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12894430-Screen-Shot-2021-04-20-at-12.53.40-AM-600x414.png" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12894430" class="wp-caption-text">退伍军人会弗拉明翰（Framingham）929站指挥官桑图里安（Ingrid Centurion）。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我像所有退伍军人兄弟姐妹一样重视自由。我们必须团结。你都看到了社会主义和共产主义在美国的兴起。缺少言论自由。这是我为什么来这里。过去所有失去生命的士兵和所有战争，都不是徒劳的。他们的成功才让你享有你的自由。”她说。◇</p>
