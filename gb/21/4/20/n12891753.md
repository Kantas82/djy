<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>健康无负担 软Q消暑的日式黑糖蕨饼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891815-ff7348b14df930ebcec5f6d2af618ff4-448x400.jpg" />
<h6>黑糖蕨饼消暑软Q，是一款夏季适合食用的甜点。（四块玉文创提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>时间即将来到夏季，清凉消暑的甜点可以缓和对天气的不适感，多喝凉水对身体未必有益处，来一盘软Q清爽的<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%95%A8%E9%A5%BC.md#1">蕨饼</a>，健康无负担又美味，而且一点都不难做。这一款改良式的蕨饼使用<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%97%95.md#1">莲藕</a>泥和<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C%E7%B2%89.md#1">地瓜粉</a>，因为蕨粉在某些地方不容易购买，但是做出来的口感和香气一点都不走调。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E8%97%95.md#1">莲藕</a><ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%95%A8%E9%A5%BC.md#1">蕨饼</a></span></h2>
<p>● 创作者：美虹厨房─朱美虹</p>
<p>莲藕有黏性，它的口感和香气，让我想到日本的蕨饼，感觉拿来当甜点会很适合。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料 (2人份)</span></h3>
<p>莲藕200g，<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C%E7%B2%89.md#1">地瓜粉</a>100g，白糖50g，黑糖蜜100cc，黄豆粉适量，水300cc。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 先将100g的莲藕磨成泥状，100g的莲藕切成小丁烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12891816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12891816" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891816-78cae2d2805d2146388ce9dd584d6eb0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12891816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891816-78cae2d2805d2146388ce9dd584d6eb0-450x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12891816" class="wp-caption-text">莲藕切成小丁烫熟备用。（四块玉文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将地瓜粉、水、糖以及作法1所有材料一起拌匀。</p>
<p>3. 将作法2放进铁锅中用中火加热并一面搅拌，待稍微开始变透明时，即转成小火并快速搅拌，等到全部都熟透变成透明时即可关火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12891817" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12891817" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891817-47209eaec9de6c8cdf0e9d8c0d3fe9a8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12891817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891817-47209eaec9de6c8cdf0e9d8c0d3fe9a8-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12891817" class="wp-caption-text">等到混合物全部熟透变成透明时即可关火。（四块玉文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将作法3冰镇后沥干水分，放在盘中倒入黑糖蜜及黄豆粉即可享用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">● TIPS</span></h3>
<p>1. 为了要有口感，一部分磨成泥，一部分则切丁。</p>
<p>2. 地瓜粉很重要，会增添Q劲。</p>
<p>3. 在锅里要用勺子不断的搅拌。</p>
<p>4. 冰镇会让口感更好。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">★主厨好友真心话</span></h3>
<p>法兰：也可以加蒟蒻粉试试看。</p>
<p>淑倩：可以尝试加糯<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E7%B2%89.md#1">米粉</a>或是米粉，口感会更Q一点。</p>
<p>秋慧：可以将莲藕先蜜过，会更有甜味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12891764" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12891764" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891764-9227c490f280f6bb35bd94b924f74996.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12891764" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12891764-9227c490f280f6bb35bd94b924f74996-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12891764" class="wp-caption-text">《蔬食餐桌》书封。（四块玉文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
