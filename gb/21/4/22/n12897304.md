<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>网红点心DIY 黑金奶黄流沙包柔软香Q</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897373-6dc3cdeb963d37a0998dfdf20bec888d-456x400.jpg" />
<h6>撕开包子会流出热呼呼的奶黄浆，一口咬下在嘴里暖暖的，超级幸福。（橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：午后小点和饭后甜点的最佳选择之一莫过于<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E9%BB%84%E6%B5%81%E6%B2%99%E5%8C%85.md#1">奶黄流沙包</a>了。暖呼呼的流沙内馅，从嘴里暖到胃里，再加上咸甜的口味非常好入口，柔软的面体本身更是一种温柔的享用。现在不用买冷冻食品，想吃的话下厨多做几个冰在冰箱冷冻，要吃时解冻蒸热就可以了，而且还可以当作早餐。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">黑金<ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E9%BB%84%E6%B5%81%E6%B2%99%E5%8C%85.md#1">奶黄流沙包</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>4个</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E7%82%AD%E9%9D%A2%E5%9B%A2.md#1">竹炭面团</a>（黑）?????120g<br />
奶黄流沙馅 ??????? 4 个<br />
食用金色色膏 ??????????10g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h3>【分割造型】</h3>
<p>1. 黑面团压揉至光滑，再分成4个（每个30g），滚圆。</p>
<p>2. 黑面团微压扁，再擀成直径7cm圆形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897386-c7d3b62ba82fcf4e417b530c82415b8b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897386-c7d3b62ba82fcf4e417b530c82415b8b-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897386" class="wp-caption-text">黑面团微压扁，再擀成直径7cm圆形。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 每片黑面皮包入1个奶黄流沙馅，收口捏紧成球形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897387-794478f8fe08538bddcf446dca9a4f3c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897387-794478f8fe08538bddcf446dca9a4f3c-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897387" class="wp-caption-text">每片黑面皮包入1个奶黄流沙馅，收口捏紧成球形。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【发酵】</h3>
<p>4. 每个包子面团放在不沾纸，发酵30分钟至原来1.6倍大。</p>
<h3>【蒸熟】</h3>
<p>5. 待蒸笼锅子水煮滚，包子放在饭巾上方，上层锅与锅盖间插入木筷子。</p>
<p>6. 以大火蒸到产生蒸气，转中火续蒸17分钟即关火，不开盖焖2分钟。</p>
<p>7. 再慢慢平移锅盖，立即取出流沙包子放在凉架，待凉。</p>
<h3>【后制】</h3>
<p>8. 在放凉的包子表面刷上食用金色色膏即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897388" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897388-c0241da71a67c54c0f2fa8b2169cb40b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897388" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897388-c0241da71a67c54c0f2fa8b2169cb40b-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897388" class="wp-caption-text">在放凉的包子表面刷上食用金色色膏即可。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 奶黄流沙馅遇到温暖的环境容易融化，所以等要包的时候再从冷冻库取出。</p>
<p>2. 奶黄流沙包趁有热度时食用才容易流沙，食用时多小心勿大口咬，以免烫口。</p>
<p>3. 食用金色色膏主原料为食用金粉所调制，可到烘焙材料行购买，若没有也可省略。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">? <ahref="https://github.com/dltxxv330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%B9%E7%82%AD%E9%9D%A2%E5%9B%A2.md#1">竹炭面团</a></span></h2>
<p>颜色：黑／重量：约500g</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897356" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897356" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897356-100a9ff7f43ae4b11c88dc4ece98bc07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897356-100a9ff7f43ae4b11c88dc4ece98bc07-450x554.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="554" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897356" class="wp-caption-text">竹炭面团。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>A<br />
中筋面粉???? 150g<br />
低筋面粉???? 150g<br />
竹炭粉?????? 3g<br />
细砂糖?????? 30g<br />
沙拉油?????? 10g</p>
<p>B<br />
水??????? 150g<br />
高糖速溶酵母??? 2g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h3>【准备】</h3>
<p>1.中筋面粉、低筋面粉混合后过筛。</p>
<h3>【搅拌】</h3>
<p>2. 材料B放入搅拌缸，拌匀至酵母溶解。</p>
<p>3. 再加入全部材料A。</p>
<p>4. 先以低速搅拌1分钟。</p>
<p>5. 再以中速搅拌5分钟至不黏缸的团状。</p>
<h3>【排气】</h3>
<p>6. 面团移至桌面，进行排气后收圆。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 若家中没有搅拌机，可以手揉方式。<br />
2. 经过排气步骤可让产品组织更细致。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">? 奶黄流沙馅</span></h2>
<p>重量：约370g<br />
保存：冷藏NO／冷冻 1个月</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897352-a547994701b70c4f13315f28ab4c7db9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897352-a547994701b70c4f13315f28ab4c7db9-450x552.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="552" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897352" class="wp-caption-text">奶黄流沙馅 。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h3>奶黄馅（10个）</h3>
<p>A<br />
咸蛋黄??????2个</p>
<p>B<br />
无盐奶油????? 24g<br />
椰浆??????? 40g<br />
细砂糖?????? 40g</p>
<p>C<br />
起司粉?????? 24g<br />
全蛋??????? 40g<br />
浓缩鲜奶????? 60g</p>
<p>D<br />
全脂奶粉????? 30g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">流沙馅（10个）</span></h3>
<p>A<br />
咸蛋黄??????2 个</p>
<p>B<br />
起司粉?????? 15g<br />
树薯粉?????? 15g<br />
细砂糖?????? 50g<br />
水???????? 20g<br />
无盐奶油????? 30g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h3>【准备】</h3>
<p>1.奶油放室温变软，4个咸蛋黄淋上少许米酒。</p>
<p>2.以大火蒸5分钟至熟，放凉后切碎。</p>
<h3>【奶黄馅】</h3>
<p>3.材料B放入调理盆，搅拌均匀。</p>
<p>4.加入材料C，拌匀。</p>
<p>5.再加入全脂奶粉，拌匀至无粉粒。</p>
<p>6.接着倒入汤锅，以小火加热至浓稠，关火。</p>
<p>7.再放入一半份量咸蛋黄碎，拌匀即为奶黄馅。</p>
<p>8.用粗孔筛网过筛奶黄馅，并滤除杂质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897353" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897353-2c6238d458511c5789eb64fe21593884.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897353-2c6238d458511c5789eb64fe21593884-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897353" class="wp-caption-text">用粗孔筛网过筛奶黄馅，并滤除杂质。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.盖上保鲜膜，立即冷冻至凉。</p>
<p>10.再分成每个20g，立即冷冻至微硬。</p>
<h3>【流沙馅】</h3>
<p>11.材料B放入汤锅，以小火加热并拌匀。</p>
<p>12.加热至冒小泡泡。</p>
<p>13.再放入另一半份量咸蛋黄碎，拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897355" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897355-6518b721d90a1d12c22e8caa0a916cdd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897355-6518b721d90a1d12c22e8caa0a916cdd-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897355" class="wp-caption-text">再放入另一半份量咸蛋黄碎，拌匀。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14.不用过筛，盖上保鲜膜立即放入冰箱，冷冻至凉。</p>
<p>15.再分成每个15g，立即冷冻至微硬。</p>
<h3>【组合】</h3>
<p>16.每份奶黄馅包覆1份流沙馅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897371-44b04a84282195a20f1ad25f18e504b2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897371-44b04a84282195a20f1ad25f18e504b2-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897371" class="wp-caption-text">每份奶黄馅包覆1份流沙馅。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>17.捏合后排入盘中，依序完成后冷冻备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897372" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897372-570196207f5714bc3fd6069aa8fd743f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897372" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897372-570196207f5714bc3fd6069aa8fd743f-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897372" class="wp-caption-text">捏合后排入盘中，依序完成后冷冻备用。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 奶黄馅和流沙馅烹煮过程会流失较多水分，所以重量有些影响。完成的奶黄馅需过筛，组织才会细致。</p>
<p>2. 咸蛋黄也可用烤箱烤熟，以160℃烤约5分钟。</p>
<p>3. 流沙馅必须完全被奶黄馅包覆，在接下来的蒸制过程才能避免爆馅。</p>
<p>4. 树薯粉是由树薯块根磨制而成的粉类，和地瓜粉、太白粉不一样（多为木薯磨制而成），购买时必须留意成分标示为“树薯粉”。</p>
