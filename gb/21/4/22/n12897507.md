<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗狗罹癌更致命！自制5道营养健康的抗癌料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/dog_s-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>单单让狗狗吃狗饲料还不够，预防狗狗罹癌，不妨自制符合狗狗健康的抗癌料理。（ShutterStock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>（前言）<ahref="https://github.com/gjvrso3175/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗的癌症，比人类的癌症更致命！十岁以上的高龄犬，50％以上有罹癌风险，大部分会死于癌症。只喂<ahref="https://github.com/gjvrso3175/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E9%A5%B2%E6%96%99.md#1">狗饲料</a>，会让你的<ahref="https://github.com/gjvrso3175/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗</a>狗成为得到癌症的高危险群！日本权威兽医须崎恭彦，教你运用平价食材做出比市售饲料营养、健康又好吃的狗狗防癌料理，让狗狗吃对食物、拥有健全的免疫机能；还能预防罹癌、防止肿瘤扩大、调理虚弱体质；也能帮助你陪伴生病的狗狗一起愉快生活，不再手足无措！</p>
<p>以下介绍5道天天都能吃的抗癌食谱：</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897558" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897558-762f0a2b818c629ebea22ebe54b7adee.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897558-762f0a2b818c629ebea22ebe54b7adee-450x664.jpg" alt="狗, 料理" width="450" b="664" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897558" class="wp-caption-text">鲑鱼纸包烧。（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>鲑鱼纸包烧</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12897561" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897561" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897561-20210422_dog_food_p34_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897561" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897561-20210422_dog_food_p34_02-450x301.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897561" class="wp-caption-text">鲑鱼纸包烧材料。（瑞丽美人国际媒体／大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>作法</strong></h3>
<p>1.烤盘纸裁成30cm长，放在烤盘上备用。<br />
2.鲑鱼及所有蔬菜食材切成狗狗容易吞咽的大小。<br />
3.将步骤2的食材和煮熟的白米饭放在烤盘纸上，淋上溶于水中的味噌，将烤盘纸封好。<br />
4.放进烤箱，以200度烤15分钟。</p>
<h3><strong>抗癌重点</strong></h3>
<p>鲑鱼含有大量的抗氧化物质—虾青素，即使经过加热也不会流失，因此可和其他食材连同汤汁一起摄取。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897579-2c20786ac7e736c3600ff1b8863db4e1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897579-2c20786ac7e736c3600ff1b8863db4e1-450x561.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="561" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897579" class="wp-caption-text">黑醋炒鸡肉冻豆腐。（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>黑醋炒鸡肉冻豆腐</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12897586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897586-20210422_dog_food_p34_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897586-20210422_dog_food_p34_04-450x302.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897586" class="wp-caption-text">黑醋炒鸡肉冻豆腐材料。（瑞丽美人国际媒体／大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<p>※以上分量均以重量10kg之狗狗的一餐进食量而制定。</p>
<h3><strong>作法</strong></h3>
<p>1.除调味料以外的食材全部切成狗狗容易吞咽的大小。<br />
2.芝麻油倒入平底锅加热，放入步骤1的食材拌炒至熟软。<br />
3.起锅前淋上黑醋即完成。</p>
<h3><strong>抗癌重点</strong></h3>
<p>鸡肉和冻豆腐是补充蛋白质的良好来源，如果狗狗不喜欢黑醋口味，可省略不用。这道料理还可以另外添加深色蔬菜，抗癌效果更佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897587" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897587-20210422_dog_food_P35_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897587-20210422_dog_food_P35_05-450x301.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897587" class="wp-caption-text">秋刀鱼蔬菜烩饭。（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>秋刀鱼蔬菜烩饭</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12897588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897588-20210422_dog_food_p35_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897588-20210422_dog_food_p35_06-450x390.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897588" class="wp-caption-text">秋刀鱼蔬菜烩饭材料。（瑞丽美人国际媒体／大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>作法</strong></h3>
<p>1.昆布加水浸泡至软化备用。<br />
2.秋刀鱼烤熟后，去骨去刺，剥下鱼肉备用。<br />
3.将泡软的昆布和其他蔬菜切成狗狗容易吞咽的大小。<br />
4.橄榄油倒入平底锅加热，放入鱼肉及所有蔬菜拌炒。<br />
5.将煮好的糙米饭、豆浆加入焖煮一下后盛入碗中，铺上无盐茅屋起司即完成。</p>
<h3><strong>抗癌重点</strong></h3>
<p>鱼种可以依季节变换，也可用肉类替代。请多用色彩鲜艳的蔬菜搭配，让食物更添美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897589-20210422_dog_food_P36_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897589-20210422_dog_food_P36_07-450x314.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897589" class="wp-caption-text">猪五花肉焖炒南瓜。（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>猪五花肉焖炒南瓜</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12897591" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897591" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897591-20210422_dog_food_p36_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897591-20210422_dog_food_p36_08-450x380.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897591" class="wp-caption-text">猪五花肉焖炒南瓜材料。（瑞丽美人国际媒体／大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>作法</strong></h3>
<p>1.将所有食材切成狗狗容易吞咽的大小。<br />
2.橄榄油倒入平底锅加热，放入切好的所有食材拌炒。<br />
3.加入适量的水后加上盖子，焖煮至食材全部熟透即可。</p>
<h3>抗癌重点</h3>
<p>当爱犬没有食欲时，应将饮食重点放在“甜味→能量→脂肪→热量、蛋白质→维持肌肉”此顺序来供应食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12897592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897592-20210422_dog_food_P37_09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897592" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897592-20210422_dog_food_P37_09-450x314.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897592" class="wp-caption-text">山药泥汤饭。（瑞丽美人国际媒体提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>山药泥汤饭</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12897595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12897595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897595-319e04524fb45ac1acb680287fbab49b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12897595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12897595-319e04524fb45ac1acb680287fbab49b-450x427.jpg" alt="狗料理" width="450" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12897595" class="wp-caption-text">山药泥汤饭材料。（瑞丽美人国际媒体／大纪元制表）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong>作法</strong></h3>
<p>1.将A食材全部切成狗狗容易吞咽的大小。<br />
2.鲑鱼烤熟后去骨、剥下鱼肉；大蒜去皮，磨泥备用。<br />
3.山药磨成泥状，与蛋及豆腐均放入碗里混合均匀。<br />
4.平底锅放入高汤、蒜泥及切好的A食材焖煮至熟透，加入鲑鱼及步骤3的食材之后，再煮一下即可盛起。</p>
<h3><strong>抗癌重点</strong></h3>
<p>为避免狗狗因吞咽困难而导致肺炎，请善用容易咀嚼的食材，关于这点不妨请教常为爱犬看诊的医生。</p>
