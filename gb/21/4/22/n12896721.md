<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作狮子头包子 咸甜好滋味令人食指大动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896839-af3d40442047e83b7989e7bc1bc1546a-458x400.jpg" />
<h6>咸甜好滋味的红白狮子头包。（橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #008000;"><span style="color: #000000;">小编语：<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E5%AD%90.md#1">包子</a>和馒头是很多人喜欢的日常面食，下厨动手做减少了人工添加物和色素，一样可以做出令人食指大动的食品，这次透过中式面点专家麒文的分享，带领我们包出咸甜好滋味的<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E5%A4%B4.md#1">狮子头</a>馅的<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E5%AD%90.md#1">包子</a>。</span><br />
</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">红白<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E5%A4%B4.md#1">狮子头</a>包</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>4个</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>红麹面团（红）?????120g<br />
基本面团（白）?????120g<br />
狮子头馅????????? 4个</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">【分割造型】</span></h3>
<p>1. 红面团、白面团分别压揉至光滑。</p>
<p>2. 两色面团微压扁，分别擀成长20×宽12cm长方形。</p>
<p>3. 白<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%9A%AE.md#1">面皮</a>抹水后叠上红面皮，再擀至长25cm，收口处擀薄。</p>
<p>4. 整片<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%9A%AE.md#1">面皮</a>抹少许水，由下往上卷起成圆柱，并黏合收口处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12896860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896860-b2067571e23adc0472c81414415d5d57.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896860-b2067571e23adc0472c81414415d5d57-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896860" class="wp-caption-text">整片面皮抹少许水，由下往上卷起成圆柱，并黏合收口处。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 松弛3分钟，左右两端修齐后切成4段。</p>
<p>6. 螺旋面朝上后擀成直径10cm圆片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12896861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896861-ca0393260101c90037c9fd4d325c1cb4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896861-ca0393260101c90037c9fd4d325c1cb4-450x297.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896861" class="wp-caption-text">螺旋面朝上后擀成直径10cm圆片。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 再包入狮子头馅，用手虎口捏合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12896862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896862-013db7060178d4cb3b378ec7c0be8e87.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896862-013db7060178d4cb3b378ec7c0be8e87-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896862" class="wp-caption-text">再包入狮子头馅，用手虎口捏合。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 接着塑成圆形，依序完成另外3个。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12896863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896863-da14bf5bbd7f69a6b53c414a25c30697.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896863-da14bf5bbd7f69a6b53c414a25c30697-450x310.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="310" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896863" class="wp-caption-text">接着塑成圆形，依序完成另外3个。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">【发酵】</span></h3>
<p>9. 包子面团放在不沾纸，发酵30分钟至原来1.6倍大。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">【蒸熟】</span></h3>
<p>10. 待<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E7%AC%BC.md#1">蒸笼</a>锅子水煮滚，包子放在饭巾上方，上层锅与锅盖间插入木筷子。</p>
<p>11. 以大火蒸到产生蒸气，转中火续蒸17分钟即关火，不开盖并且焖2分钟。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 收口处擀薄目的可避免面皮太厚。</p>
<p>2. 面团松弛3分钟让<ahref="https://github.com/xfymyx3585/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%AD%8B.md#1">面筋</a>软化，方便后续擀圆片更好操作。</p>
<p>3. 圆形包也可塑成椭圆形，或是两色面团揉合大理石纹路再分割4份包裹。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">? 基本面团 </span></h2>
<h3>颜色：白／重量：约500g</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12896864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896864-a5f69e683f6a07de7eeb16b39914b322.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896864-a5f69e683f6a07de7eeb16b39914b322-450x458.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="458" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896864" class="wp-caption-text">基本面团。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>A<br />
中筋面粉???? 150g<br />
低筋面粉???? 150g<br />
细砂糖?????? 30g<br />
沙拉油?????? 10g</p>
<p>B<br />
水??????? 150g<br />
高糖速溶酵母??? 2g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<h3>【准备】</h3>
<p>1. 中筋面粉、低筋面粉混合后过筛。</p>
<h3>【搅拌】</h3>
<p>2 . 材料B放入搅拌缸，拌匀至酵母溶解。</p>
<p>3. 再加入全部材料A。</p>
<p>4. 先以低速搅拌1分钟。</p>
<p>5. 再以中速搅拌5分钟至不黏缸的团状。</p>
<h3>【排气】</h3>
<p>6. 面团移至桌面，进行排气后收圆。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 若家中没有搅拌机，可以手揉方式。<br />
2. 经过排气步骤可让产品组织更细致。</p>

<h2><span style="color: #008000;">? 狮子头馅</span></h2>
<p>重量：约400g</p>
<p>保存：冷藏 2 天／冷冻 5 天</p>
<figure id="attachment_12896865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12896865" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896865-f2828ca999cc1919f54809b234f44f08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12896865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12896865-f2828ca999cc1919f54809b234f44f08-450x516.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="516" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12896865" class="wp-caption-text">狮子头馅。（橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>A<br />
猪绞肉????? 250g<br />
大白菜????? 100g<br />
干香菇?????? 10g</p>
<p>B<br />
葱末??????? 20g<br />
蒜末??????? 10g<br />
姜末??????? 10g</p>
<p>C<br />
盐???????? 5g<br />
白胡椒粉????? 3g<br />
酱油??????? 10g<br />
香油??????? 5g<br />
细砂糖?????? 3g<br />
水???????? 20g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 猪绞肉多剁几次让绞肉更细碎；干香菇泡水后沥干，切小丁，备用。</p>
<p>2. 将大白菜切约1.5cm小片，加入额外5g盐，抓拌均匀，放置5分钟待出水后挤干。</p>
<p>3. 猪绞肉加入盐，抓拌出黏性，再加入材料C，搅拌均匀，接着放入香菇、大白菜及材料B拌匀，再分成8份（每份50g），沾少许水搓圆。</p>
<p>4. 肉球放入180℃油锅（油量盖过肉球），油炸至定型，捞起后放凉即可使用，或分装冷冻备用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">陈老师叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1. 大白菜加盐脱水、狮子头油炸皆导致水分流失一些，所以成品重量比生材料轻。</p>
<p>2. 猪绞肉的肥瘦比例3：7 做出来的狮子头口感最佳，勿全部使用瘦肉或肥肉。</p>
<p>3. 肉球可改清蒸方式取代油炸，中火蒸至定型即可。</p>
