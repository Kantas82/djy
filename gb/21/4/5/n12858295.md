<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>怀念的家乡味 法式可可玛德莲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858300-4-c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>可可玛德莲用栗子模型做出，超级像栗子。（图／橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>一种简单纯朴的家乡味总是让人难以忘怀，就像台湾早期社会的鸡蛋糕，只需要面粉和糖搅拌混合，然后面粉糊倒入小鸡形状的烤盘内，几分钟就可以出炉了！在法国也有这种经典的蛋糕，<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E5%BE%B7%E8%8E%B2.md#1">玛德莲</a>就是这种纯粹的滋味，如果想变化口味，可以加一点<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF.md#1">可可</a>粉或者是抹茶粉，就会有不同风味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a><ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF.md#1">可可</a><ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%9B%E5%BE%B7%E8%8E%B2.md#1">玛德莲</a></span></h2>
<p>面糊添加迷人的<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E9%85%92.md#1">咖啡酒</a>与可可粉， 沾上脆脆的<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%8A%AE%E8%84%86%E7%89%87.md#1">巴芮脆片</a>，乍看很像<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%97%E5%AD%90.md#1">栗子</a>啊！</p>
<p><strong>份 量</strong>：3 盘（6连栗子模）<br />
<strong>烤 温</strong>：上火190℃ / 下火190℃（ 单火190℃）<br />
<strong>赏味期</strong>：室温3 天/ 冷藏7 天</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料 </span></h3>
<h3>? 面糊</h3>
<p>? 全蛋 130 克<br />
细砂糖 100 克<br />
Kahlua 卡鲁瓦<ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1%E9%85%92.md#1">咖啡酒</a> 20 克<br />
动物性鲜奶油 25 克<br />
蜂蜜 20 克</p>
<p>? 低筋面粉 155 克<br />
泡打粉 5 克<br />
无糖可可粉 20 克<br />
杏仁粉 20 克</p>
<p>? 无盐奶油 150 克<br />
? 熟栗子（切半）18 个</p>
<h3>? 装饰</h3>
<p>? 苦甜巧克力（非调温）50 克<br />
? <ahref="https://github.com/nfnkod3022/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E8%8A%AE%E8%84%86%E7%89%87.md#1">巴芮脆片</a> 50 克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 材料Ａ全蛋、细砂糖放入钢盆，拌匀至无颗粒。</p>
<p>2 再加咖啡酒、动物性鲜奶油 和蜂蜜。</p>
<p>3 继续用打蛋器拌匀。</p>
<p>4 材料Ｂ过筛后加入作法３，拌匀至无粉状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858297-50c15f19cc38c0052c9d2bddfebf725a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858297-50c15f19cc38c0052c9d2bddfebf725a-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858297" class="wp-caption-text">低筋面粉、泡打粉、无糖可可粉、杏仁粉过筛加入作法３，拌匀至无粉状。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 材料Ｃ放入小汤锅，转小火煮至完全融化，再倒入作法４，边倒边拌匀。</p>
<p>6 接着装入套平口花嘴的挤花袋，冷藏松弛20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858299" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858299" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858299-4020d856ad6260707d5842226faa9539.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858299-4020d856ad6260707d5842226faa9539-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858299" class="wp-caption-text">装入套平口花嘴的挤花袋。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">烘烤</span></h3>
<p>7 将面糊挤入模具凹槽，在面糊中心填入栗子，接着挤上面糊至８分满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858342-fcfa03d38cb3a017dd59e3ba78664867.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858342-fcfa03d38cb3a017dd59e3ba78664867-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858342" class="wp-caption-text">将面糊挤入模具凹槽，在面糊中心填入栗子，接着挤上面糊至８分满。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8 放入烤箱中层，烘烤10 ～12 分钟至熟。</p>
<p>9 趁热小心脱模后放凉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858343-a367badbbe88377eab8eb71f1b6299d5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858343-a367badbbe88377eab8eb71f1b6299d5-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858343" class="wp-caption-text">趁热小心脱模后放凉。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">装饰</span></h3>
<p>10 苦甜巧克力以小火隔水加热融化。</p>
<p>11 巴芮脆片装入塑胶袋擀碎， 再倒入调理碗。</p>
<p>12 栗子可可玛德莲钝端（底部） 1/4处沾裹适量苦甜巧克力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858344-ac58e0e38831eaf3e9b87193a534c2f3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858344-ac58e0e38831eaf3e9b87193a534c2f3-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858344" class="wp-caption-text">栗子可可玛德莲钝端（底部） 1/4处沾裹适量苦甜巧克力（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13 再沾上巴芮脆片，放入冰箱冷藏10分钟凝固即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858346-85dc96d2078927765ee57bdb4b6da2f2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858346-85dc96d2078927765ee57bdb4b6da2f2-450x300.jpg" alt="玛德莲" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858346" class="wp-caption-text">再沾上巴芮脆片，放入冰箱冷藏10分钟凝固即可。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">小叮咛</span></h2>
<p>＊使用的６连栗子模，它的模具为长30×宽20×高2cm。</p>
<p>＊融化好的奶油液倒入面糊，边倒边拌更容易拌均匀。</p>
<p>＊熟栗子可到便利商店或超市购买，勿买生的栗子。</p>
<p>＊烘焙材料行可买到酥脆口感的巴芮脆片。</p>
