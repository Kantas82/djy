<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>当磅蛋糕遇上酥菠萝 多层次口感更上一层</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858420-13-d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>酥菠萝起司磅蛋糕，口感绵密厚实（图／橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：吃下去的每一口都扎实，每一口都是蓬松奶香味。<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A3%85%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">磅蛋糕</a>以奶油为基底，加入<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B3%8A.md#1">面糊</a>和鸡蛋做出浓郁的<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>体，让嗅觉和味觉都有馥郁的感受。介绍的这一款磅蛋糕添加了酥菠萝，让口感更多层次变化，是一道绵密又厚实的<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">酥菠萝<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a><ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A3%85%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">磅蛋糕</a></span></h2>
<p>原味磅<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a><ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B3%8A.md#1">面糊</a>加奶油<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8.md#1">起司</a>、分蛋作法，让这道<ahref="https://github.com/eydeib3500/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>兼具绵密与厚实口感。</p>
<p>份 量：1 个磅蛋糕模（长17.5 × 宽8.5 × 高7cm）<br />
烤 温：上火190℃ / 下火180℃（单火185℃）<br />
赏味期：冷藏4天</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h3>· 面糊</h3>
<p>Ａ<br />
奶油起司85克<br />
无盐奶油85克<br />
糖粉85克</p>
<p>Ｂ<br />
低筋面粉90克<br />
玉米粉10克<br />
泡打粉2克</p>
<p>Ｃ 蛋黄2个<br />
Ｄ 柠檬皮屑＋汁1/4个<br />
Ｅ 蛋白2个、细砂糖40克</p>
<p><span style="color: #000080;">? 酥菠萝 1份 （作法如下所示）</span><br />
<span style="color: #000080;"> ? 起司香堤鲜奶油 1份（作法如下所示）</span><br />
? 装饰Ａ糖粉 20克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 材料Ａ奶油起司、奶油回软后放入搅拌缸，打发至变成乳白色蓬松状。</p>
<p>2 再加入材料Ｃ拌匀，可看到光泽的蛋糊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858421" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858421-4d4c9b96d5edf549f4cd0f1dca4ab9fd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858421-4d4c9b96d5edf549f4cd0f1dca4ab9fd-450x300.jpg" alt="磅蛋糕" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858421" class="wp-caption-text">奶油起司、无盐奶油、糖粉打发至变成乳白色蓬松状，加入蛋黄拌匀，可看到光泽的蛋糊。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 接着加入过筛的材料B、材料D，拌匀。</p>
<p>4 材料Ｅ打发至湿性偏硬性发泡（蛋白霜尖端呈鹰勾嘴状）。</p>
<p>5 蛋白霜分次加入作法３，用刮刀拌匀，即看到起司面糊呈蓬松浓稠状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858422" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858422-6e5bceac13177c693569c9fb536283b3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858422-6e5bceac13177c693569c9fb536283b3-450x300.jpg" alt="磅蛋糕" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858422" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白霜分次加入作法３，用刮刀拌匀，即看到起司面糊呈蓬松浓稠状态。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">烘烤</span></h3>
<p>6 面糊倒入模具，用硅胶刮刀 抹平。</p>
<p>7 均匀铺上酥菠萝，放入烤箱中层烤40 ～ 45分钟至熟，趁热撕开烘焙纸，放凉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858426-a720e2c0bab6b65ae35071aeea1749b4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858426-a720e2c0bab6b65ae35071aeea1749b4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858426" class="wp-caption-text">均匀铺上酥菠萝，放入烤箱中层烤40 ～ 45分钟至熟，趁热撕开烘焙纸，放凉。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">组合装饰</span></h3>
<p>8 蛋糕横切３片，中间２层抹适量起司香堤鲜奶油，均匀筛上糖粉即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12858427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12858427" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858427-e5ea8e9ad8d1613c5c6e66254f8bee07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12858427" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12858427-e5ea8e9ad8d1613c5c6e66254f8bee07-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12858427" class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕横切３片，中间２层抹适量起司香堤鲜奶油，均匀筛上糖粉即可。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">小叮咛</span></h3>
<p>＊上火的温度比较高，主要让蛋糕表面的酥菠萝着色深一点；下火温度较低， 是尽量不让蛋糕表面爆开。</p>
<p>＊由于面糊表面有酥菠萝，所以烘烤中途不需要划１刀。</p>
<p>＊依据原味磅蛋糕制法改成分蛋法制作，并透过蛋白打发提升起司磅蛋糕的口感层次，是１款兼具轻乳酪蛋糕绵密与奶油磅蛋糕厚实的磅蛋糕。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">酥菠萝</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>A.无盐奶油25克、糖粉25克<br />
B.杏仁粉45克、低筋面粉45克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1.回软的无盐奶油与糖粉放入搅拌缸（或钢盆），加过筛的材料Ｂ。<br />
2.搅拌均匀成细颗粒即可。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">小叮咛</span></h3>
<p>1.搅拌成细颗粒即停止，若搅拌过度，则会拌成面团状。<br />
2.用不完的酥菠萝可冷藏7天、冷冻1个月。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">起司香堤鲜奶油</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>A.牛奶35克、吉利丁片1/2片<br />
B.白巧克力（调温）45克、奶油起司80克<br />
C.动物性鲜奶油85克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 牛奶转小火煮沸，吉利丁片泡入冰水待软。<br />
2. 稍微挤干水分的吉利丁片加入牛奶，拌匀备用。<br />
3. 白巧克力以小火隔水加热融化，与作法2材料拌匀。<br />
4. 回软的奶油起司加入作法3，用打蛋器充分拌匀。<br />
5. 材料Ｃ打发至有明显纹路（7分发），加入作法4，换硅胶刮刀拌匀。</p>
