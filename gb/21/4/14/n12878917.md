<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温哥华数千Airbnb房东转向长期租赁</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年04月14日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者罗晟加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>对</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Airbnb</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">等<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E6%9C%9F%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md#1">短期租赁</a>的监管年度报告显示，数千名房东已将短期出租屋转为<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%9C%9F%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md#1">长期租赁</a>。</span></p>
<div style="width: 100%; height: 170px; margin-bottom: 20px; border-radius: 10px; overflow: hidden;"><a style="width: 100%; height: 170px;" src="https://player.captivate.fm/episode/f805ea1f-6e1b-4ecf-a16d-924f245bf3a3" width="300" b="150" frameborder="no" scrolling="no" seamless=""></a></div>
<p><strong>（听更多新闻请至<ahref="https://canadanews.captivate.fm/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">“听纪元”</a>平台）</strong><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">数据显示，2020年12月底挂牌出租的短租屋的数量为3,123间，比上一年同期的5,713间减少了2,590间。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">温哥华市府在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2018</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年引入了对<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E6%9C%9F%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md#1">短期租赁</a>的监管，以提高温哥华的<ahref="https://github.com/qwxkbn3052/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%95%BF%E6%9C%9F%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81.md#1">长期租赁</a>房屋库存。报告称，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年市府发放了超过</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1.25</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万张长期租赁的营业执照，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2019</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">297</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">张。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">报告说，尽管COVID</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">-19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和去年短期租赁活动骤减，但仍有人继续经营这类租赁，表明这些住宿设施将成为未来城市不可或缺的一部分。◇</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
