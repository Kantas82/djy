<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>女星沐浴诀窍 香氛纾压撩心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880913-493098-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。（开丽娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年04月15日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）女星<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E8%88%92%E6%B6%B5.md#1">梁舒涵</a>近来忙于拍摄偶像剧《废财闯天关2》，日前被厂商相中清新可人的气质与旗下<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%90%E6%B5%B4.md#1">沐浴</a>乳调性相符，故受邀担任产品最新一季代言人，希望透过梁舒涵的代言推广最新“撩心系列”香氛沐浴乳，她也分享了自己的沐浴保养习惯。</p>
<figure id="12880918" aria-describedby="caption-12880918" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880918-493103-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880918-493103-450x300.jpg" alt="梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12880918" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E8%88%92%E6%B6%B5.md#1">梁舒涵</a>偏爱小苍兰香氛<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%90%E6%B5%B4.md#1">沐浴</a>乳。（开丽娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12880917" aria-describedby="caption-12880917" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880917-493102-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880917-493102-450x300.jpg" alt="梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12880917" class="wp-caption-text">梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。（开丽娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12880914" aria-describedby="caption-12880914" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880914-493099-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880914-493099-450x675.jpg" alt="梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12880914" class="wp-caption-text">梁舒涵偏爱小苍兰香氛沐浴乳。（开丽娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首次代言沐浴乳的梁舒涵，被问及平常的沐浴习惯，以及如何保养的撇步？她说：“平时<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%97%E6%BE%A1.md#1">洗澡</a>多半淋浴，而且是分区块慢慢洗，洗完也都一定上乳液，不同季节也会更换不同乳液，有时候还会用面膜敷身体。算是对身体肌肤照顾得很周到。”她偏爱小苍兰香氛，觉得很细致柔滑。</p>
<p>第一次接到代言广告的梁舒涵，厂商不仅端出6位数字代言酬劳。她穿上特别设计的气球装，一看就有泡泡沐浴的感觉，拍摄时挑战从1层楼高的钢管一下滑落至浴缸，拍摄过程整整耗费20个小时。还有全新广告歌曲也是量身打造，让梁舒涵尝试自己演唱，让她笑称：“拍一支广告送一支MV”。◇</p>
<figure id="12880916" aria-describedby="caption-12880916" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880916-493101-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12880916-493101-450x300.jpg" alt="梁舒涵淋浴完一定会上身体乳。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12880916" class="wp-caption-text">梁舒涵淋浴完一定会上身体乳。（开丽娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
