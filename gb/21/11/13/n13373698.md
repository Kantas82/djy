<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寒冬裙装穿搭 5大建议让你时髦又保暖</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373710-11111-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>秋冬季节，裙装依然可以绽放光彩，时尚美丽，不再“冻人”。（Getty Images／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年11月14日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）三国时期诗人曹植曾作诗讲述梦遇伏羲之女洛神的情境，特别描述到洛神服饰绮艳绝世——身披明丽的罗衣、下着薄雾般长裙，带着幽兰的清香，在山谷边徜徉……而这份绝尘美丽的女神“裙装”装扮，让诗人情思缱绻，因而写下了千古流芳的诗文。</p>
<p>两千年后的今天，裙装以无可比拟的迷人和高贵，在这个裤装盛行的时代依然享有无可替代的地位，成为胶囊衣橱的必备单品。各国的第一夫人在重大场合几乎全线裙装现身，裙装的贵族地位可见一斑。</p>
<p>在民间，裙装也比裤装多几分娇柔和妩媚，总能展示万种风情的“女人味”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373722-GettyImages-80995014-450x656.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13373722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373722-GettyImages-80995014-450x656.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="656" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373722" class="wp-caption-text">伊万卡·川普身着晚礼服，现身纽约大都会博物馆艺术服饰学院周年庆典。（Stephen Lovekin/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在北方，天已转凉，但裙装只要搭配得当，美丽与温暖均可兼得。除了常规的秋冬皮裙、毛衣裙，在本季裙装有哪些流行的穿搭？又有哪些需要注意？就请您随我继续一起往下看吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373723-GettyImages-461814344-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13373723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373723-GettyImages-461814344-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373723" class="wp-caption-text">黑色皮毛外套，裙装，同色细腰带、紧身裤袜。高贵典雅。柏林时装周。（Peter Michael Dills/Getty Images for Lena Hoschek）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>建议1：叠穿夏装裙子</h2>
<p>很多人的衣橱是按季节分类的。<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%86%AC%E8%A3%99%E8%A3%85.md#1">秋冬裙装</a>需要厚实一些，才更保暖。但冬天还是可以穿亮丽飘逸的裙子的。如果你正有款心宜的夏裙不忍束之高阁，也可混搭各季单品，比如夏裙配秋冬外套，可穿连裤袜、配长靴，就可以确保美丽的同时，不再“冻人”。</p>
<h3>皮夹克/大衣+裙子</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13373732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373732" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373732-shutterstock_1485834563-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13373732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373732-shutterstock_1485834563-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373732" class="wp-caption-text">黑皮夹克，黑色蕾丝纱裙，黑长靴，墨镜，凭海临风，美丽前卫。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夏裙的色彩一般会更显亮丽，为适合秋冬季节穿着，需整体调整色彩的平衡。比如穿浅色夏装，可配深色夹克，尤其深色牛仔夹克/皮夹克，非常适合搭配夏装。</p>
<h3>大开衫+裙子</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13373728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373728" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373728-shutterstock_327014693-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13373728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373728-shutterstock_327014693-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="601" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373728" class="wp-caption-text">灰色毛衣开衫，酒红色塔裙，黑色礼帽。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一个非常适合秋冬的穿搭，就是穿超大开衫，可以敞口，系上细腰带，外边还可套上一件外套，再搭配皮靴，打造可爱而知性的装扮。</p>
<p>小Tip： 在寒冷地区，可选有厚重感且垂坠的裙子，也可选比较立挺的西装面料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373734-shutterstock_1891959472-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13373734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373734-shutterstock_1891959472-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373734" class="wp-caption-text">西装材质的裙子，米色仿毛大衣，红飘带上衣，驼色长裙，黑短靴，有品有温度。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>建议2：秋冬选择碎花裙</h2>
<p>印花是常见的时尚元素之一，在裙装中常年可见。秋冬最好选择小碎花图案的裙装，显得柔美精致，会给深色穿搭增添活泼和俏皮感，而不宜选大块的抽象图案。当然，暖色调印花更具有秋冬的浪漫情怀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373725" style="width: 404px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373725-GettyImages-509593000-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13373725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373725-GettyImages-509593000-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="404" b="606" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373725" class="wp-caption-text">灰黑碎花短裙，灰色仿毛套头背心，黑长靴，黑色打底裤。立体感时尚感自然流露。纽约时装周。（Neilson Barnard/Getty Images For Marissa Webb）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋冬流行三款碎花连衣裙：长裙、迷你裙和带有蓬松/褶皱袖的连衣裙。</p>
<h3>深色外套+碎花长裙/短裙</h3>
<p>这是在过去几季中一直很有影响力的流行趋势。与深色外套互搭可形成鲜明对比。整体造型时，还可用珍珠首饰或水晶吊坠项链搭配。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373730-shutterstock_1246701661-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13373730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373730-shutterstock_1246701661-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373730" class="wp-caption-text">小碎花长裙，牛仔服，过膝长靴，贝雷帽，粉色皮包，太阳镜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小Tip：颜色可帮助营造个性氛围。要展示你的温柔活泼，可选米色、裸粉色等淡雅色系的裙装，不仅减龄，还能柔化寒冷；要打造熟女的独立和知性可选奶茶色、莫兰蒂蓝色和驼色等。</p>
<h2>建议3：裙装可搭配毛衣</h2>
<p>毛衣、毛裙都是秋冬季节不可或缺的单品，羊毛/羊绒的观感、手感都会让人觉得温暖亲和。半长毛衣可以与裙子配搭，短小款可提高腰线，拉伸腿部长度，调整身材比例；较长款也可扎上细腰带，显示腰线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373729" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373729-shutterstock_540845026-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13373729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373729-shutterstock_540845026-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373729" class="wp-caption-text">灰裙，灰羊毛衫扎入裙中，显示高腰线。协调柔和。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽量选择织纹细密的毛衣，比如羊绒毛衣，更为亲肤保暖，贴身显瘦；纯色能够彰显温柔雅致。</p>
<h3>白毛衣+半长裙+靴子</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13373731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373731" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373731-shutterstock_1265568532-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13373731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373731-shutterstock_1265568532-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373731" class="wp-caption-text">白毛衣，动物图案裙，褐色牛仔短靴，同色皮手包。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小Tip：半身裙不管什么质地，A字裙是永不会出错的选择，既修饰身材，又能营造韩系氛围，减龄效果好。毛衣裙则可外搭西装或皮夹克，也是不错的选择。</p>
<h2>建议4：流行带图案紧身裤袜</h2>
<p>秋冬的裙装离不了紧身裤袜，特别是羊绒质地的，一层薄薄的紧身裤袜，不仅可以保持身形，增加了时尚元素，还能有效保暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373724-GettyImages-461828962-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-13373724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373724-GettyImages-461828962-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373724" class="wp-caption-text">灰色紧身裤袜衬托一身的温暖柔和的“春“的气息。柏林时装周。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for Marc Cain）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>经典黑紧身裤袜+黑裙装</h3>
<p>这种从头“黑”到脚的穿搭，永远不过时。想作大胆尝试，可选与裙装为对比色的紧身裤袜，打造色彩碰撞的感觉。</p>
<h3>休闲装+黑色圆点紧身裤袜/渔网袜+连衣裙</h3>
<p>带图案的紧身裤可瞬间改变连衣裙的风格，让你霎那间别致起来。黑色圆点图案具法式元素，可搭配大多数连衣裙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13373848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13373848" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373848-shutterstock_2055267566.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13373848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13373848-shutterstock_2055267566-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13373848" class="wp-caption-text">米色风衣，黑短裙，圆点黑紧身裤袜，黑贝雷帽，及膝黑皮靴。 （Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小Tip：如果连衣裙图案繁复，最好穿素色紧身裤。在秋冬季节，可以尝试复古风格，在身上添加不同色彩，但尽量保持服装单色。另外，紧身裤袜比连裤丝袜织得要厚密，透光、不透光的都有，选择多元；打底裤厚但不透光，长度仅到脚踝。</p>
<h2>建议5：搭配长靴</h2>
