<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>adidas Brand Center信义概念店登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365192-526560-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>信义adidas Brand Center打造台北首座3D裸视墙。（品牌提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年11月09日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）全台首间adidas Brand Center信义品牌概念店11月9日举行开幕记者会，首度公开全新Team adidas黄金阵容，包含鞍马王子李智凯、网坛传奇<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A2%E5%BD%A6%E5%8B%8B.md#1">卢彦勋</a>、台湾最速男<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E4%BF%8A%E7%80%9A.md#1">杨俊瀚</a>、跨栏一哥<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%9D%B0.md#1">陈杰</a>、射击甜心吴佳颖与adidas Originals代表潮流R&amp;B女星吴卓源，众星齐聚展现“挑战不可能，看见更多可能”的精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13365191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13365191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365191-526559.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13365191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365191-526559-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13365191" class="wp-caption-text">全新Team adidas黄金阵容包含李智凯、<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A2%E5%BD%A6%E5%8B%8B.md#1">卢彦勋</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E4%BF%8A%E7%80%9A.md#1">杨俊瀚</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/fjmocf3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%9D%B0.md#1">陈杰</a>、吴佳颖与女星吴卓源。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13365197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13365197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365197-526563-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13365197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365197-526563-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13365197" class="wp-caption-text">全新信义Brand Center占地555坪，同步全球囊括品牌齐下全系列最新商品。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Brand Center为adidas于世界各国主要城市所设立的最高规格品牌概念店，不仅拥有丰富齐全的商品，更特别引进城市当地特色与在地艺术创作，注入独树一格创作能量。</p>
<p>全新信义Brand Center占地555坪，同步全球囊括品牌齐下全系列最新商品。为庆贺品牌在台开启新纪元，本次信义adidas Brand Center以台北首座3D裸视墙、台湾原创艺术装置、全台首座客制体验Maker Lab创作研究室，可体验台湾专属刺绣图案，展现国际概念店规格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13365199" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13365199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365199-526564-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13365199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365199-526564-1-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="365" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13365199" class="wp-caption-text">adizero独卖限量PRIME X鞋款，挑战速度极限。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13365200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13365200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365200-526565-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13365200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365200-526565-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13365200" class="wp-caption-text">首座Maker Lab创作研究室在台登场，提供原创客制化服务体验。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不仅如此，该店更独步全台抢先上市新升级革命跑鞋adizero与梦幻潮流时尚单品限定独卖，包含独家限量首卖adizero PRIME X鞋款、破纪录冠军跑鞋款adizero adios Pro 2及厚底碳纤轻量训练鞋款adizero Boston 10，引领运动热爱者体验超越自我速限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13365196" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13365196" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365196-526562-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13365196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13365196-526562-1-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13365196" class="wp-caption-text">艺术鬼才李霁打造环保装置艺术，呼应品牌对于永续蔚蓝的承诺。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
