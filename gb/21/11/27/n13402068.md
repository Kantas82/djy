<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>只用一张纸折出方形礼物盒 送礼心意满满</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402390-P.47-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>想要送的礼物用什么包装好呢？本文教你用一张纸折出方形礼物盒，给礼物加值。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>想要送的礼物用什么包装好呢？本书教你只要“一张漂亮的纸”就能做出实用又精巧的<ahref="https://github.com/ecpdgp301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E7%89%A9%E7%9B%92.md#1">礼物盒</a>，给礼物加值，最适合在特别的日子传递满满心意。文末附有视频连结可以观看制作方法。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">附盖方形<ahref="https://github.com/ecpdgp301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E7%89%A9%E7%9B%92.md#1">礼物盒</a></span></h2>
<p>可向两侧打开的方形纸盒，不仅开口大、方便拿取物品，而且用一张纸就能折出来，是不是很令人意外呢？挑一张有美丽花纹的纸张来折，细细感受这款作品的魅力吧！</p>
<p><strong>〈准备材料〉</strong><br />
一张正方形纸张</p>
<p><strong>〈示范纸张大小〉</strong><br />
150×150mm</p>
<p><strong>〈完成尺寸〉</strong><br />
约45×40×33mm</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1.将纸张背面朝上，上下对折后打开。（谷折）</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402335" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402335-P.47-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402335-P.47-2-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402335" class="wp-caption-text">1.将纸张背面朝上，上下对折后打开。（谷折）（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.接着左右对折后打开（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402336-P.47-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402336-P.47-3-450x319.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402336" class="wp-caption-text">2.接着左右对折后打开（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将四个角沿着虚线，对准中心点折（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402337-P.47-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402337-P.47-4-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402337" class="wp-caption-text">3.将四个角沿着虚线，对准中心点折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.如图折好后，旋转45度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402338" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402338-P.47-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402338-P.47-5-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402338" class="wp-caption-text">4.如图折好后，旋转45度。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将其中一个三角形的角对准上面的边，轻轻压一下留下标记即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402339-P.47-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402339-P.47-6-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402339" class="wp-caption-text">5.将其中一个三角形的角对准上面的边，轻轻压一下留下标记即可。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.如图将角对准步骤5留下的标记，沿着虚线往上折（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402340-P.48-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402340-P.48-1-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402340" class="wp-caption-text">6.如图将角对准步骤5留下的标记，沿着虚线往上折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7-1.将四方形下边沿虚线往上折（谷折），折至对齐步骤6折出的三角形的底边，接着打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402341-P.48-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402341-P.48-2-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402341" class="wp-caption-text">7-1 将四方形下边沿虚线往上折（谷折），折至对齐步骤6折出的三角形的底边，接着打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7-2.折的时候，务必先用手指压住纸张再往上折，以避免内侧的纸错位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402342-P.48-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402342-P.48-3-450x319.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402342" class="wp-caption-text">7-2 折的时候，务必先用手指压住纸张再往上折，以避免内侧的纸错位。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.打开时会发现里面已经折出了一个三角形，现在将上面的小三角形翻下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402343-P.48-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402343-P.48-4-450x319.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402343" class="wp-caption-text">8打开时会发现里面已经折出了一个三角形，现在将上面的小三角形翻下来。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.将四方形的右侧边，对准虚线（对齐下方白色三角形的右边端点）往左折（谷折）后打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402344-P.48-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402344-P.48-5-450x319.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402344" class="wp-caption-text">9将四方形的右侧边，对准虚线（对齐下方白色三角形的右边端点）往左折（谷折）后打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.将四方形剩下的两个边也照样沿虚线对折（谷折）后打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402345" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402345-P.48-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402345-P.48-6-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402345" class="wp-caption-text">10.将四方形剩下的两个边也照样沿虚线对折（谷折）后打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11.全部折完之后，将步骤5中做了标记的三角形往上打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402346" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402346-P.48-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402346-P.48-7-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402346" class="wp-caption-text">11.全部折完之后，将步骤5中做了标记的三角形往上打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.沿着图中折痕（虚线）往下折叠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402347" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402347-P.48-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402347-P.48-8-450x320.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="320" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402347" class="wp-caption-text">12.沿着图中折痕（虚线）往下折叠。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>13.如图左右预留3mm的宽度、沿虚线往上折（谷折）。注意若使用材质较厚的纸张时，需再多错开一点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402348" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402348-P.48-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402348-P.48-9-450x306.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402348" class="wp-caption-text">13.如图左右预留3mm的宽度、沿虚线往上折（谷折）。注意若使用材质较厚的纸张时，需再多错开一点。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>14.将上下都打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402349" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402349-P.48-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402349-P.48-10-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402349" class="wp-caption-text">14.将上下都打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>15.将●对准●、○对准○，沿着箭头方向由下往上凹（不要折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402350" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402350-P.48-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402350-P.48-11-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402350" class="wp-caption-text">15.将●对准●、○对准○，沿着箭头方向由下往上凹（不要折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16-1.两边的●与○都对准之后，将前面那片沿虚线往下折（谷折）至对齐后面那片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402351" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402351-P.48-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402351-P.48-12-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402351" class="wp-caption-text">16-1 两边的●与○都对准之后，将前面那片沿虚线往下折（谷折）至对齐后面那片。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16 &#8211; 2.折往自己这侧，将前后的三角形折成相同大小，就能确实盖紧盒盖，再把上下都打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402352-P.49-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402352-P.49-1-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402352" class="wp-caption-text">16 &#8211; 2 折往自己这侧，将前后的三角形折成相同大小，就能确实盖紧盒盖，再把上下都打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>17.将步骤13与16 -1做出的折痕沿虚线反折成谷折线。</p>
