<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作衬衫造型礼物袋 送礼更有诚意</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402442-P.51-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在送礼的日子里，不妨亲自制作衬衫造型的礼物袋，礼物更有加分效果。（苹果屋提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>编者按：</strong>明明很想尝试手作，却总是卡在“需要的材料太多、好像很麻烦”吗？本文教你只要“一张漂亮的纸”做出衬衫<ahref="https://github.com/xbyzgo3148/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%A0%E5%9E%8B.md#1">造型</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/xbyzgo3148/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E7%89%A9%E8%A2%8B.md#1">礼物袋</a>，最适合在特别的日子传递满满心意。这是专属于大人的美感体验，除了可以唤起儿时<ahref="https://github.com/xbyzgo3148/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%98%E7%BA%B8.md#1">折纸</a>那份纯粹的快乐，你也会体会到，原来纸张远比我们想像中更强韧。文末附有视频连结可以观看制作方法。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">衬衫<ahref="https://github.com/xbyzgo3148/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%A0%E5%9E%8B.md#1">造型</a><ahref="https://github.com/xbyzgo3148/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E7%89%A9%E8%A2%8B.md#1">礼物袋</a></span></h2>
<p>使用一张A4纸就能完成，若选择带有金属光泽或图样典雅的纸张，会更能衬托这款礼物袋的质感。</p>
<p><strong>〈准备材料〉</strong><br />
一张长宽比为1.4：1的纸张<br />
<strong>〈示范纸张大小〉</strong><br />
A4（210×297mm）<br />
<strong>〈完成尺寸〉</strong><br />
约115×110×35mm</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h2>
<p>1.将纸张的正面朝上，沿着虚线对折（谷折）后打开、翻面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402400-P.51-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402400-P.51-2-450x320.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="320" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402400" class="wp-caption-text">1.将纸张的正面朝上，沿着虚线对折（谷折）后打开、翻面。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.在纸张左侧边1/2的位置，轻轻折一下留下标记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402401-P.51-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402401-P.51-3-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402401" class="wp-caption-text">2.在纸张左侧边1/2的位置，轻轻折一下留下标记。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.接着在左侧边1/4的位置，轻轻折一下留下标记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402402-P.51-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402402-P.51-4-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402402" class="wp-caption-text">3.接着在左侧边1/4的位置，轻轻折一下留下标记。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将左上角对准步骤3 留下的标记（★），轻轻折一下留下标记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402403-P.51-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402403-P.51-5-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402403" class="wp-caption-text">4. 将左上角对准步骤3 留下的标记（★），轻轻折一下留下标记。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将纸张下边对准步骤4留下的标记，沿虚线往上折（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402404" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402404-P.51-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402404-P.51-6-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402404" class="wp-caption-text">5.将纸张下边对准步骤4留下的标记，沿虚线往上折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.将纸张上边对准步骤3留下的标记（★），沿着虚线往下折（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402405-P.52-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402405-P.52-1-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402405" class="wp-caption-text">6.将纸张上边对准步骤3留下的标记（★），沿着虚线往下折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.确认上、下两片不是对齐的，接着将全部打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402406" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402406-P.52-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402406-P.52-2-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402406" class="wp-caption-text">7.确认上、下两片不是对齐的，接着将全部打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将●对准●、○对准○，沿虚线往箭头方向对折（谷折）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402407" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402407" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402407-P.52-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402407-P.52-3-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402407" class="wp-caption-text">8.将●对准●、○对准○，沿虚线往箭头方向对折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>10.将步骤9打开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13402409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402409-P.52-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402409-P.52-5-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402409" class="wp-caption-text">10.将步骤9打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402410-P.52-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402410-P.52-6-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402410" class="wp-caption-text">11.将右侧边对准步骤9做出的折痕，沿虚线往左折（谷折）过去，再打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402411-P.52-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402411-P.52-7-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402411" class="wp-caption-text">12.可以看到纸张中央有两条折痕，左为山折线，右为谷折线。顺着折痕折起。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402412-P.52-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402412-P.52-8-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402412" class="wp-caption-text">13.将●对准●、○对准○，沿虚线往箭头方向对折（谷折）。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402413" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402413" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402413-P.52-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402413-P.52-9-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402413" class="wp-caption-text">14.将步骤13、纸张中心处都打开。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402414" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402414-P.52-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402414-P.52-10-450x312.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402414" class="wp-caption-text">15.下方沿着折痕（虚线）往上折。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_13402415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13402415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402415-P.52-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13402415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13402415-P.52-11-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13402415" class="wp-caption-text">16-1 将标示有★的边对准另一个★的边，沿虚线往下折，顺势立起。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
