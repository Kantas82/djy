<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>舒适又有型 达成秋季时髦穿搭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13346692-523095-1.jpg" />
<h6>西装混搭背心与束口裤。（rather提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年11月02日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）天气变凉，随着秋意来临，除了必需换上长袖保暖，如何让穿搭既轻松又有型也是女孩们重视的课题。</p>
<p>秋冬来临之际，叠加多层衣物的同时难免让身型看起来臃肿。设计师品牌rather强调不费力美学，推荐简单而不失质感的穿搭妙法，轻松应付各个场合。</p>
<h2>不费力穿搭技巧一：ALL IN ONE轻薄洋装</h2>
<figure id="13345799" aria-describedby="caption-13345799" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345799-523094-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345799-523094-450x600.jpg" alt="不对称领假两件洋装。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13345799" class="wp-caption-text">不对称领假两件洋装。（rather提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>说到不费力穿搭，洋装是许多人的首选。特殊领型五分袖绑带洋装有双层假两件，让美眉可以系上腰带收腰，亦可以打开当俐落的洋葱式罩衫，拥有all-in one的多种变化。</p>
<figure id="13345795" aria-describedby="caption-13345795" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345795-523090-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345795-523090-450x675.jpg" alt="西装混搭V领针织小可爱。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13345795" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%A3%85.md#1">西装</a>混搭V领针织小可爱。（rather提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13345796" aria-describedby="caption-13345796" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345796-523091-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345796-523091-450x675.jpg" alt="西装混搭V领针织小可爱。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13345796" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%A3%85.md#1">西装</a>混搭V领针织小可爱。（rather提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>不费力穿搭技巧二：极简黑显时尚</h2>
<p>一般来说，黑色单品是最万用、百搭不出错的选择，然而在配搭上也需要一些小技巧，才能更显时尚好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13345794" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13345794" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345794-523089.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13345794" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345794-523089-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13345794" class="wp-caption-text">全身极简黑，显瘦又时尚。（rather提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13345798" aria-describedby="caption-13345798" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345798-523093-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345798-523093-450x600.jpg" alt="条纹绑带衬衫洋装。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13345798" class="wp-caption-text">条纹绑带衬衫洋装。（rather／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13345797" aria-describedby="caption-13345797" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345797-523092-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13345797-523092-450x600.jpg" alt="压线绑带休闲西装外套搭配渔夫帽。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13345797" class="wp-caption-text">压线绑带休闲西装外套搭配渔夫帽。（rather提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>条纹绑带衬衫洋装在秋季可以当有个性的洋装单穿，或当罩衫做休闲的搭配。而压线绑带休闲西装外套则是运用不易皱、好打理的面料，配上撞色压线及绑带，呈现俐落随兴感，提高日常实穿度，推翻人们对于西装厚重闷热的刻板印象。</p>
<h2>不费力穿搭技巧三：成套西装混搭</h2>
<p>成套西装在今年成为许多女性衣柜里的常胜军，选择微oversize的舒适版型，能使成套西装搭配起来更为容易。像是搭配运动上衣、率性牛仔裤都有不同的味道。◇</p>
