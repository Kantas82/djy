<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自制饼干这样保存 久放也如新鲜出炉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407145-shutterstock_1925573429-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>源自苏格兰的黄油苏饼干。保存方法正确就能让你更久享用最爱的美味。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年11月30日讯】（大纪元记者李平综合报导）很多人都喜欢自制<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>、饼干，除了享受DIY的乐趣，因其更为健康、新鲜，自制饼干尤其受到青睐。当然自己动手，随时都可以举个人喜好变化任何口味和外形。漂亮诱人的自制饼干也是很好的礼物，特别在聚会和节日派对上，更能营造温馨气氛、表达浓浓爱意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407111" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407111-shutterstock_1903140142.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407111-shutterstock_1903140142-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407111" class="wp-caption-text">你相信这是饼干吗？这分明是充满浓浓情谊的爱心。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407169" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407169-shutterstock_504228274.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407169-shutterstock_504228274-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407169" class="wp-caption-text">节日派对上品种繁多的饼干，总能让人眼睛一亮、兴致盎然。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>可正因为自制饼干零添加，所以保质期也相对短。怎样能较长时地<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a>、还能有新鲜出炉的口感呢？让我们一起来了解。</p>
<h2>饼干能<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%98%E5%82%A8.md#1">存储</a>多久？</h2>
<p>根据饼干的馅料和配料的不同，<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a>时间长短不一，一般情况下，曲奇饼在室温下能存放三天。室温在41-78华氏度（5-26摄氏度）左右为宜。但如果你<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%98%E5%82%A8.md#1">存储</a>得当，在冷冻柜中可保存长达6个月。饼干的存储有哪些必须注意的呢？</p>
<h2>存储饼干有哪些须知？</h2>
<h3>须知一：存储前，饼干必须完全冷却。</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13407105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407105-shutterstock_116566225.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407105-shutterstock_116566225-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407105" class="wp-caption-text">冷却架上新鲜出炉的花生酱饼干。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407108-shutterstock_1027373275.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407108-shutterstock_1027373275-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407108" class="wp-caption-text">诱人的曲奇黄油饼干。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>制作饼干有时是很繁复的，为了不让你的努力白费，冷却是不能省的。可置于烤盘中，也可以放到冷却架上冷却。否则残存在饼干内的热量会发生冷凝，水分会存留在容器中，很容易使饼干变质，</p>
<h3>须知二：不同类型的饼干，须分开保存</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13407109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407109-shutterstock_1096009013.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407109-shutterstock_1096009013-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407109" class="wp-caption-text">不同类型饼干密闭在同一容器中会串味，最好分开保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407106" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407106-shutterstock_246630661.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407106-shutterstock_246630661-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407106" class="wp-caption-text">心形巧克力饼存放在玻璃罐中。不同口味的饼干也要分开保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不同类型的饼干有不同的储存要求。比如干脆的饼干与松软型饼干、奶油奶酪饼干的存储方式就完全不同，不能一同存放，还容易串味，影响口感。</p>
<p>软饼干需要密封保存，脆饼干不一定严格密封，封盖保存即可；可存放与自封袋中，但不要置于厨房台面，否则饼干容易随厨房升温而受潮、变质。</p>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>使用玻璃罐存储固然美观，但大部分饼干罐都没有密封条，饼干容易受潮。可选用带密封条的罐子，也可以将饼干放入自封袋中，再置于罐中存储。</p>
<h3>须知三：用烤盘纸分层存放饼干</h3>
<p>在制作保存饼干过程中，最不幸的就是饼干在存放过程中粘在一起，全部辛苦就会付诸东流！为了避免这种“悲剧”发生，建议分层保存饼干时要以烤盘纸或者蜡纸分隔开。然后，再依据饼干的类型或于常温保存，或放<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E7%AE%B1.md#1">冰箱</a>保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407172" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407172-shutterstock_1918951067.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13407172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407172-shutterstock_1918951067-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407172" class="wp-caption-text">以油纸分隔的布朗尼饼。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407107" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407107-shutterstock_568023187.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407107-shutterstock_568023187-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407107" class="wp-caption-text">以蜡纸分开马卡龙饼，能更好保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>常温保存时，最好将饼干置于阴凉处，比如储物架后部，避开阳光直射。</p>
<p>既然大家已经对存储饼干有了基本的了解，我们就具体讲一下不同类型饼干的存放方法。</p>
<h3>1. 大块条状饼干/饼干棒</h3>
<p>最好的存储方法就是在原烤盘中冷却后，就在原烤盘存放，然后拿锡纸包裹，放入大的自封袋中保存。如果饼干中含有易腐烂配方，比如有奶油、奶酪涂层，需要及时放入<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E7%AE%B1.md#1">冰箱</a>保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407141-shutterstock_394255516.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407141-shutterstock_394255516-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407141" class="wp-caption-text">大块的布朗迪饼切块后冷却，再放入储存盒中保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>如果无法在原来的烤盘中存放，可以将整块切片，再以<ahref="https://github.com/lpwfcx330/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E5%9F%B9%E6%B2%B9%E7%BA%B8.md#1">烘培油纸</a>分隔，放入密封容器，放入冰箱保存。这种方法可让大块条饼干保鲜长达两周。</p>
<h3>2.??? 酥脆饼干的存储</h3>
<p>这部分饼干含水分比较少，储存时间最长。防潮是关键，暴露在空气中吸收湿气变软，所以最好装入密闭容器中，也可同时放方糖吸附潮气。正确密封保存能保存一个月。</p>
<p>薄脆的饼干不能放在冷藏室，容易变软，影响口感；如果短期内不吃，可考虑放入冷冻室保存。比较耐磨的饼干，像黄油酥饼、巧克力碎曲奇饼可以直接分层存放，无需加油纸分隔；不耐摩擦的饼干，可考虑用油纸、保鲜膜，或者锡箔纸隔开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407104-shutterstock_115593577.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407104-shutterstock_115593577-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407104" class="wp-caption-text">颇有节日气氛的彩色饼干，可以放在一起存储。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407110" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407110-shutterstock_1851731758.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407110-shutterstock_1851731758-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407110" class="wp-caption-text">花形黄油曲奇饼加坚果可加隔层保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13407170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407170-shutterstock_661622035-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407170-shutterstock_661622035-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407170" class="wp-caption-text">经典的巧克力曲奇饼。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>如果脆酥饼干受潮变软，可以在300华氏度哦烤箱中烘培5分钟。</p>
<h3>3.??? 较软曲奇饼的保存</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13407171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407171-shutterstock_1890473869.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407171-shutterstock_1890473869-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407171" class="wp-caption-text">看着就想吃的巧克力布朗尼饼干。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>较软曲奇饼干包括布朗尼饼、加糖霜的饼干，保质期相对短，储存的关键是尽量减少空气流动，这样就能保持饼干的水分。如果饼干过多地暴露在空气中，其淀粉分子就会结晶，并吸收水分，很快饼干就失去新鲜度。为了防止粘连，应该单层存放在密闭容器中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407142-shutterstock_702328486.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407142-shutterstock_702328486-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407142" class="wp-caption-text">加了糖霜的曲奇饼保存时间相对短。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>如果软性的饼干变硬饼干，可以放一片面包在容器中，面包中释放的水分能够充分使饼干软化，恢复先前的口感。</p>
<h3>4.???? 有馅料饼干/乳酪饼干的存储</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13407113" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407113" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407113-shutterstock_1984558103.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407113-shutterstock_1984558103-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407113" class="wp-caption-text">带奶油装饰的曲奇饼需要放到冰箱冷藏室保存。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有馅料的饼乾和奶油、乳酪饼干，储存时间都相对而言比较短，3天左右。所以最好放在冰箱冷藏室中存放，大概能保鲜5～7天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13407149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13407149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407149-shutterstock_1990484609.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13407149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13407149-shutterstock_1990484609-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13407149" class="wp-caption-text">类似凤梨酥这样带馅料的饼干存储时间也相对短。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>温馨提示：</strong>如果短期不吃，带馅饼干最好放到冰箱的冷冻室内存放起来，食用前自然解冻就可以了。但乳酪类饼干不宜冷冻，否则乳酪会呈渣状。</p>
<h2>如何保存饼干面团</h2>
<p>当然也可以在冰箱中保存自己揉制的饼干面团，随吃随用。冷藏可保存一周，冷冻保存达三个月。做法是，把面团分成小球，放在铺有烧烤纸的烤盘中，冷冻一小时直至变硬。然后移至密封容器中保存。</p>
<p>用时无需特别解冻，当然也可在前一晚放入冷藏室。</p>
