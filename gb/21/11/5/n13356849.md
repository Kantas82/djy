<h6>2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年11月06日讯】Honda CR-V虽在2017年已推出新第五代产品，可是Hybrid版却在2020年推出Facelift版本的时候，才跟着推出市场，测试车是2022年款式Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring版。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://d1ntfewljph5k2.cloudfront.net/S9aNaxv2c"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wtnrng3147/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/w1GOe15nrZY" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">CR-V是北美SUV／CUV的第一位，自有其优点，首先，外形上Honda CR-V Hybrid设计简洁，日间行车灯是采用LED灯，头灯是由多个LED组成，同时雾灯也是由5个LED组成（Touring版）。刹车灯同样是使用了LED，新CR-V的尾灯设计仍是依照传统，从D柱伸延至车顶上，也是由第一代CR-V延续来的特式，但这一代不是单纯的直条形，而是倒“T”形设计。CR-V Hybrid版与汽油版最大分别是Hybrid版在车两侧有“Hybrid”徽章。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356853-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_06-e1636155149786.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356853-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356853" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Honda CR-V Hybrid的尺寸跟汽油版没有分别，都是182.1吋长、73吋宽及66.5吋高，内部空间也基本上一样，载客空间为102.9立方呎，比对手RAV4 Hybrid的98.9立方呎为大。但是，Hybrid版的行李舱空间只有33.2立方呎，因为电池组及控制系统于行李舱地板下，所以比汽油版的39.2立方呎为小，就算是放倒后排后可升至68.7立方呎，也是比不上汽油版的75.8立方呎，对载货空间有要求的人要留意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356854-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_07-e1636155161744.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356854-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356854" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13356855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356855-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_15-e1636155170799.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356855-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356855" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新CR-V使用了全新的ACE车体结构，并且大大增加了超高强度热冲压钢（Ultra-high strength hot-stamp steel），以及超高强度钢材（Ultra-high strength steel）的使用，而且部分位置的点焊距离由40毫米减少至20毫米，也说是增加了点焊的数量，所以新CR-V的车体更坚固。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356857-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_22-e1636155204704.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356857-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356857" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">动力系统方面，CR-V Hybrid使用先进的双马达混合动力系统，与Accord Hybrid一样，将两个电动马达与2.0升16气门双凸轮轴引擎结合在一起，热效率超过40%。Hybrid在运行时不需要传统的自动变速器连接，可以达到近似纯电动车的平滑无缝的动力传输。系统总马力为212匹，峰值扭矩232磅呎，标配Lithium锂电池。CR-V Hybrid版的耗油量约为城市40MPG及公路35MPG，在城市的耗油量比汽油版的27MPG优胜不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356860-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_27-e1636155232450.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356860-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_27-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356860" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">CR-V Hybrid的变速箱采用eCVT无段变速箱，由于CVT可在运转当中换档，从而节省了转档期间的动力流失，从而节省汽油。CR-V Hybrid装置了全轮驱动（Real Time All-Wheel Drive），当牵引力较低时，电子离合器会启动，动力由电动马达发送到后轮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356862-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_28-e1636155181678.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356862-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356862" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Touring版CR-V是车系中的最高级版本，所以内装也比较豪华，最明显是Touring版的7吋中控台触控式萤幕，内置系统可支援Apple CarPlay及Android Auto，CR-V Touring配备了330W的9个扬声器，笔者最喜欢其木纹内饰，有如身在豪华汽车内。由于变速杆前移到中控台中下位置，并使用了电子手刹车，所以中控台底部有空间装上手机无线充电板，而且杯座也可左右平排。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356856-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_20-e1636155192864.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356856-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356856" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">驾驶席是12方向电动调节座椅，并有两组位置记忆，前座乘客也有4方向电动调节，并各有三段电热。三幅式方向盘上，有各种功能按键，如音量、语音控制、定速巡航等，同时也有加热功能。Paddle Shift跟一般汽车不同，并不是用作控制变速箱，而是控制电力回收刹车强度，在最强时在下坡路也不用使用刹车也可维持车速。仪表板中间是一个彩屏，用以显示车速及各种资讯，两旁则是的油缸存量及油温显示。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356858-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_24-e1636155213101.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356858-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356858" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13356859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356859-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_25-e1636155223956.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356859-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356859" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">尾闸是电动开关的，并且在车底加装了感应器，可“踢脚”方式开关；电动尾闸也可自定开启高度，首先需手动把尾闸开启至理想高度，跟着接着尾闸按键约3秒，听到两下响声便成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13356861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13356861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356861-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_37-e1636155243278.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13356861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13356861-2022_Honda_CRV_Hybrid_37-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13356861" class="wp-caption-text">2022 Honda CR-V Hybrid Touring。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">第五代Honda CR-V既有Honda的出色引擎及造车工艺，也备有各种先进设备，Facelift后外貌也有了新鲜感！CR-V Hybrid与Toyota RAV4 Hybrid虽同是Hybrid汽车，但是机械结构完全不同，然而CR-V的耗油量却不输RAV4 Hybrid呢！◇</p>
