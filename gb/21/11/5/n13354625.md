<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大蒜保存对了吗？3招长久存放 不怕变黑变质</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354794-garlic2_704737405-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大蒜如何挑选和保存？(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%92%9C.md#1">大蒜</a>是最常见的调味食物之一。在做菜前剥蒜时，有时会发现大蒜不新鲜，甚至发黑了，怎样才能<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%91%E9%80%89.md#1">挑选</a>新鲜、水分饱满的大蒜，并且长久<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a>呢？</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;"><ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%91%E9%80%89.md#1">挑选</a>好<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%92%9C.md#1">大蒜</a>的方法</span></h2>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E5%A4%B4.md#1">蒜头</a>：</strong></p>
<p>●?用手拿起来时感觉沉甸甸的。<br />
●?瓣数要少不要多，结构扎实且看起来肥硕。<br />
●?带有较强的独特辣味。<br />
●?避免选择已经冒芽，或是开始腐烂的大蒜。</p>
<p><strong>蒜仁：</strong></p>
<p>●?选择饱满且末端较尖的。<br />
●?根部较窄的部位较软，且散发亮嫩绿色。<br />
●?没有变色或碰撞的痕迹，带有较强的辣味与香味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">简单三招，让大蒜长久<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98.md#1">保存</a></span></h2>
<p><strong>一、蒜仁的<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E8%97%8F.md#1">冷藏</a>保存法</strong></p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>砂糖、厨房纸巾、密封容器</p>
<p>① 在密封容器中倒入1 公分高的砂糖，然后再铺上厨房纸巾。砂糖与厨房纸巾可以吸收长时间存放所产生的水分，防止蒜仁泡烂。</p>
<p>② 将蒜仁放到步骤①上，再盖上厨房纸巾，最后盖上盖子<ahref="https://github.com/edihvs3186/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E8%97%8F.md#1">冷藏</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13354741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13354741" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354741-1027-0662-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13354741" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354741-1027-0662-600x400.jpg" alt="蒜仁的冷藏保存法。（联经提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13354741" class="wp-caption-text">蒜仁的冷藏保存法。（联经提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>二、整颗大蒜的室温保存法</strong></p>
<p>① 将大蒜铺平在报纸或竹篮上，放在阴凉通风处风干。新鲜的大蒜需放一周，已经过干燥处理的大蒜可以立刻收起来，或放置一天之后再收起来。（新鲜大蒜水分较多，应该摊平干燥后再收起来，才能放比较久。）</p>
<p>② 将大蒜的芯去除，用报纸包起后放在阴凉处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13354747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13354747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354747-1027-06622-600x264.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13354747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354747-1027-06622-600x264.jpg" alt="整颗大蒜的室温保存法。（联经提供）" width="600" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13354747" class="wp-caption-text">整颗大蒜的室温保存法。（联经提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>三、整颗大蒜的冷藏保存法</strong></p>
<p>① 将大蒜铺平在报纸或竹篮上，放在阴凉通风处风干。新鲜的大蒜需放一周，已经过干燥处理的大蒜可立刻收起来，或放置一天之后再收起来。</p>
<p>② 在密封容器中，依报纸、大蒜、报纸、大蒜的顺序层层堆叠，再盖上盖子后以冷藏保存。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13354748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13354748" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354748-1027-066222-600x264.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13354748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13354748-1027-066222-600x264.jpg" alt="整颗大蒜的冷藏保存法。（联经提供）" width="600" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13354748" class="wp-caption-text">整颗大蒜的冷藏保存法。（联经提供）</figcaption></figure>
