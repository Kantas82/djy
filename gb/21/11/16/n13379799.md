<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>复古红棕色唇膏 增添知性高级感</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13380640-525894-1.jpg" />
<h6>赤陶是红砖般的复古色调。（品牌提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年11月17日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）随着秋季气温下降，彩妆也换上深邃浓郁的氛围，唇彩换上赤陶色Terracotta与酒棕色Bordeaux Brown，兼具成熟色彩与柔和气质，宛若浸染秋色的棕红，呈现出绽放红晕透亮的美丽双唇。</p>
<figure id="13379804" aria-describedby="caption-13379804" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379804-525893-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379804-525893-450x338.jpeg" alt="赤陶是红砖般的复古色调。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379804" class="wp-caption-text">赤陶是红砖般的复古色调。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最近这几季时尚界流行复古色调，从眼影到指甲油、服装等，尤其到了秋冬，复古色是不少女孩的选择。以“花嫁唇膏”美名在开架市场占有一席之地的日本彩妆品牌OPERA，今年秋冬也突破过去日系甜美的形象，首度推出两款棕色系唇膏，展现与过往截然不同的成熟氛围。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13379802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13379802" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379802-525891.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13379802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379802-525891-450x300.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13379802" class="wp-caption-text">赤陶是红砖般的复古色调。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新色#09赤陶，是红砖般的复古色调，薄擦透出的红陶光泽不只衬出白皙肤色，更为整体妆容增添大人感的高贵优雅；厚涂则能完美彰显红陶色的迷人质感，显色却又不失深邃。</p>
<figure id="13379803" aria-describedby="caption-13379803" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379803-525892-450x300.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379803-525892-450x300.jpeg" alt="酒棕宛如浸泡过波尔多红酒的迷人色泽。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379803" class="wp-caption-text">酒棕宛如浸泡过波尔多红酒的迷人色泽。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而#10酒棕则宛如浸泡过波尔多红酒，揉合迷人的紫色调，看似成熟的深棕色膏体，上唇后却透出红宝石般的红润色泽，为双唇增添时尚秋色。</p>
<figure id="13379806" aria-describedby="caption-13379806" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379806-525895-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379806-525895-450x450.jpg" alt="酒棕宛如浸泡过波尔多红酒的迷人色泽。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379806" class="wp-caption-text">酒棕宛如浸泡过波尔多红酒的迷人色泽。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>打造持久水光唇 拿下口罩也有好气色</h2>
<p>每每拿下口罩的瞬间，总担心毫无血色的双唇流露出病恹恹的气息吗？想要长时间维持美丽妆容，让唇膏不沾口罩也有小秘诀：</p>
<figure id="13379815" aria-describedby="caption-13379815" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379815-525901-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379815-525901-450x300.jpg" alt="想要长时间维持美丽妆容，让唇膏不沾口罩也有小秘诀。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379815" class="wp-caption-text">想要长时间维持美丽妆容，让唇膏不沾口罩也有小秘诀。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13379811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13379811" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379811-525899.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13379811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379811-525899-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13379811" class="wp-caption-text">想要长时间维持美丽妆容，让唇膏不沾口罩也有小秘诀。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Step1.将唇膏均匀涂抹于双唇上，来回涂抹2～3次。</p>
<p>Step2.抿唇使唇膏均匀融合。选择添加润泽精华油的唇膏，在上妆的同时可修护并美化唇纹，保持双唇柔嫩饱满。</p>
<figure id="13379807" aria-describedby="caption-13379807" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379807-525896-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379807-525896-450x300.jpg" alt="将唇膏均匀涂抹于双唇上，来回涂抹2～3次。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379807" class="wp-caption-text">将唇膏均匀涂抹于双唇上，来回涂抹2～3次。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13379809" aria-describedby="caption-13379809" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379809-525897-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13379809-525897-450x300.jpg" alt="将唇膏均匀涂抹于双唇上，来回涂抹2～3次。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13379809" class="wp-caption-text">将唇膏均匀涂抹于双唇上，来回涂抹2～3次。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Step3.用面纸轻轻按压双唇，抹掉多余唇膏与油分，轻薄又持色的水嫩双唇就完成了。◇</p>
