<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园仙草花节紫色浪漫盛开 慰劳幕后英雄</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405549-529645-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>现场农创市集特别邀请桃园农村社区、青年及严选农村好物参与“农创市集”展售。（桃园市农业局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年11月29日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）2021桃园<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E8%8D%89%E8%8A%B1%E8%8A%82.md#1">仙草花节</a>自20日开展至今已突破50万人次，桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>29日在市议员周玉琴、涂权吉、李家兴及杨梅区公所区长邱世源陪同下，视察桃园仙草花节活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13405548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13405548" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405548-529644.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13405548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405548-529644-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13405548" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市长<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>视察桃园<ahref="https://github.com/sxvshi319/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%99%E8%8D%89%E8%8A%B1%E8%8A%82.md#1">仙草花节</a>活动。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑文灿表示，活动以“愈风仙境”为主题打造5.2公顷的紫色仙境，共种植超过12.5万株的仙草花，2.7公顷的波斯菊花海，经上周冷锋过境，仙草花越冷越开花，现场已是盛开紫爆花海，民众热烈回响，本活动除了由桃园市政府农业局主办，更动员市府各局处分工执行，也感谢地方巡守队以及环保志工合作，协助仙草花节治安、交通及环整治会场的清洁整理，成功创造出具有口碑及高品质的活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13405551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13405551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405551-529646.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13405551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405551-529646-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13405551" class="wp-caption-text">桃园仙草花节紫色浪漫盛开。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动进入最后一周参观机会，周末另有桃园优质团队“风雅颂”领军，以美妙的旋律徜徉花海中，也有疗愈频率为主题，用温暖而低频的手碟、颂钵…等乐器，将音律回荡在花田间，请大家把握观赏时机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13405552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13405552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405552-529647.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13405552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405552-529647-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13405552" class="wp-caption-text">桃园仙草花节紫色浪漫盛开。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>副秘书长邱俊铭表示，现场农创市集特别邀请桃园农村社区、青年及严选农村好物参与“农创市集”展售，共同展现推动农村再生成果；杨梅区农会研发出仙草饼、仙草冻、仙草茶、仙草蛋卷等多元产品，以及今年研发的仙草吐司、仙草QQ饼等新产品。此外，也邀请桃园市杨梅区秀才休闲农业发展协会，主打蜂蜜产品，可作为蜂蜜抹酱抹面包，也可泡成蜂蜜水饮用。各式仙草特色产品及桃园农产品，让大家买得满满。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13405553" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13405553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405553-529648.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13405553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405553-529648-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13405553" class="wp-caption-text">桃园仙草花节紫色浪漫盛开。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13405554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13405554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405554-529649.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13405554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13405554-529649-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13405554" class="wp-caption-text">桃园仙草花节紫色浪漫盛开。（桃园市农业局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>农业局长郭承泉表示，因应活动人潮，周边部分有交通管制，建议民众多多利用大众运输工具以及搭乘活动免费接驳专车抵达会场或是提早规划平日假期，避开周末假日人潮，并做好防疫准备，规划一场梦幻的疗愈之旅！保护自己也保护他人。◇</p>
