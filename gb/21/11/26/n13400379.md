<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>西澳Tambourah Metals勘探公司的项目盘点</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年11月26日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">【工商报导】西澳新型金镍勘探公司</span></span>Tambourah Metals Ltd <span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（</span></span>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">目前收购了西澳的两个黄金项目和两个镍项目，同时在澳大利亚证券交易所（</span></span>ASX<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">上市并获得了</span></span>800<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元的融资。在本期报导中，</span></span>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司向读者重点介绍以上四个项目。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13400393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13400393" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400393-76cdad4720af529da030292edbc47f77.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13400393" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13400393-76cdad4720af529da030292edbc47f77-600x856.jpg" alt="图为该公司项目的地理分布图片。（商家提供）" width="450" b="642" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13400393" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/prvlql3569/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E5%8B%98%E6%8E%A2.md#1">西澳勘探</a>公司Tambourah Metals Ltd收购了西澳的两个黄金项目和两个镍项目。图为该公司项目的地理分布图片。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Tambourah<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">金矿项目</span></span></h2>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">位于西澳皮尔巴拉（</span></span>Pilbara<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）东部地区</span></span>Tambourah<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的采金区域，大约在黑德兰港（</span></span>Port Hedland<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">和纽曼（</span></span>Newman<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">之间。皮尔巴拉东部地区</span></span>Marble Bar<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的西南</span></span>85<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里处。</span></span></p>
<p>Tambourah<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">金矿项目已经获得一份</span><span lang="zh-TW">勘查许可证（</span></span>Exploration Licence<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）和四份探矿许可证（</span></span>Prospecting Licences<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）。矿权总面积为</span></span>696<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷，它具有巨大的潜在金矿化前景。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该项目的勘探程度非常低，</span></span>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司计划在未来</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年内进行全面的系统勘探。</span></span>Tambourah<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">采矿中心的黄金总产量为</span></span>163.2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公斤。</span></span></p>
<h2>Cheela<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">黄金项目（</span></span>Cheela Gold Project<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></span></h2>
<p>Cheela<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">黄金项目（</span></span>Cheela Gold Project<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">位于西澳</span></span>Pilbara<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">地区，在</span></span>Paraburdoo<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西北偏西处约</span></span>50<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里。</span></span>Karratha<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">主要矿业区域服务中心位于项目西北偏北约</span></span>250<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里处。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该项目获得两个勘探许可证（</span></span>E08/2889<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>E08/3053<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">。占地面积约</span></span>38158<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷，横跨项目的最大距离约为西北</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">东南方向</span></span>70<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里，东北</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">西南方向</span></span>20<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在</span></span>Cheela<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">断层沿和</span></span>Nanjilgardy<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">断层沿总长度约</span></span>2.5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里的区域里，（经过勘探）已经发现了显着的金矿化表现。根据在</span></span>Cheela<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">地区及其周围的钻孔的勘探中发现的显着黄金异常表现，</span></span>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司认为此地区值得进一步跟踪与深入钻探。因此，该公司制定了几个已确定的钻井目标和异常（地质、地球物理和地球化学）需要进行地面勘查。</span></span></p>
<h2>Julimar<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">镍铜项目（</span></span>Julimar North Project<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></span></h2>
<p>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司在</span></span>Julimar Nth-Jimperding<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">拥有大量土地。</span></span>Julimar<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的</span></span>Ni-Cu-PGE<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（镍</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">铜为主的硫化物矿床）项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">位于西澳</span></span>Wheatbelt<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">地区。</span></span></p>
<p>Julimar<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">镍铜项目也获得两个勘探许可证和两个正在申请待批的勘探许可证，占地面积</span></span>50,828<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷（</span></span>Tambourah Metals<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">占</span></span>80%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></span>Chalice Mining Limited<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司最近在附近发现了</span></span>Julimar PGE-Ni-Cu-Co<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">矿床，使该地区成为一个新的</span></span>Ni-Cu-PGE<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">硫化物矿床。在</span></span>Julimar<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的发现是澳大利亚的第一个重大钯金（</span></span>Pd-Au<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）发现，并且正在迅速成为重要的“绿色金属”战略矿床。</span></span></p>
<p>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">将成为这个新兴矿产领域最大的勘探者之一。公司近期收到了一份新的详细的航磁数据和重力数据。在</span></span>Julimar North<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的</span></span>Bolgart East<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目，经过考察确定了潜在目标区域可能存在矿化物的主要地层。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">在</span></span>Julimar North<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的</span></span>Gonneville<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目通过钻一个离散的移动环路电磁与高振幅航磁异常相关的异常表现中，发现了被镁铁超镁铁质侵入（</span></span>Chalice Mining<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span><span lang="zh-TW">的岩石矿化物</span></span>Ni-Cu-PGE<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。这些矿化石具有很强的导电性，在电磁数据中异常显着。对于未来的规划，</span></span>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">正在对现有的地球物理勘探数据进行勘探绘制，以确定超镁铁质岩石的含量。</span></span></p>
<h2>Achilles<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目（</span></span>Achilles Gold, Nickel and PGE Project<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></span></h2>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳东北部金矿区的</span></span>Achilles<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目位于</span></span>Laverton<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以北</span></span>200<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里，</span></span>Wiluna<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以东约</span></span>190<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该项目获得了两个勘探许可证，占地面积</span></span>22,654<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷。</span></span>Achills<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目位于</span></span>Gerry Well<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">绿岩带，毗邻南部的</span></span>Collurabbie<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目，该项目拥有一系列</span></span>Ni-Cu-PGE<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（镍铜为主的硫化物）发现。</span></span>Gerry Well<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">绿岩带以南位于</span></span>Duketon<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">绿岩带，</span></span>Moolart Well<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">金矿位于该项目以南约</span></span>90<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公里处。</span></span></p>
<p>2006<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年，</span></span>Creasy<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">集团在该项目的南部进行了一次地面电磁测量。电磁探测产生了</span></span>20<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个异常目标。通过在这之前的矿化超镁铁质内钻探，确定了</span></span>Ni-PGE-Cu<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">硫化物的潜在目标区域。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">根据先前的勘探工作表明，该项目区域具有镍铜铂锗和金矿化的前景，值得后续工作。现有几个已确认的异常表现，需要地质、地球物理和地球化学等额外的工作。</span></p>
<p>TMB<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">最近的一项研究发现，在南部毗邻的</span></span>Collurabbie<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目区域，可能存在类似的</span></span>Ni-Cu-PGE<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和金矿化表现。现场调查和取样将有助于确定钻井测试目标。后续钻井还将进一步测试项目邻近地区的异常多元素结果。</span></span></p>
