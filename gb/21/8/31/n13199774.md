<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加东自驾游：果冻豆房子和纽芬兰最古老的灯塔</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199805-20210831_canada_helen_chou_606-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>加东自驾游，今天来到纽芬兰省府圣约翰市，街上就是有名的果冻豆房子。(Jellybean House)。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/21/8/23/n13181471.md#1">七月的加东四省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（5）</a></p>
<p>一早顶着大雨开了2个半小时，到达<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0.md#1">纽芬兰</a>的省府<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E7%BA%A6%E7%BF%B0%E5%B8%82.md#1">圣约翰市</a>，顿时感觉一脚踏进了大城市，回归文明。头等大事就是找一家好餐馆大吃一顿！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199787" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199787-20210831_canada_helen_chou_602.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13199787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199787-20210831_canada_helen_chou_602-600x901.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199787" class="wp-caption-text">颇具爱尔兰风的餐馆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199789" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199789" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199789-20210831_canada_helen_chou_603.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199789-20210831_canada_helen_chou_603-600x450.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199789" class="wp-caption-text">颇具爱尔兰风的餐馆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家名叫“凯尔特炉”(Celtic Hearth)的餐馆还不错。炉边烤着火，听着恩雅的歌，吃着当地有名的啤酒面炸鳕鱼，幸福感瞬间爆表。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199797-20210831_canada_helen_chou_604.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199797-20210831_canada_helen_chou_604-600x438.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199797" class="wp-caption-text">当地有名的啤酒面炸鳕鱼。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>说起炸鳕鱼，其实一路走来已经吃了很多顿了，但是还没有吃腻。无他，唯新鲜尔！每家的炸法不一样，外面裹的那层面极有讲究。传统的做法是裹一层湿面炸，口感厚重。用啤酒面炸就松脆多了。据说这种做法就是从<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0.md#1">纽芬兰</a>省府传出的。</p>
<p>饱餐一顿后就在街上走走，消食看风景。下图靠左的那个红房子就是我们刚刚吃饭的餐馆Celtic Hearth。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199800-20210831_canada_helen_chou_605.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199800-20210831_canada_helen_chou_605-600x437.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199800" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E7%BA%A6%E7%BF%B0%E5%B8%82.md#1">圣约翰市</a>街景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199805-20210831_canada_helen_chou_606.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199805-20210831_canada_helen_chou_606-600x424.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199805" class="wp-caption-text">圣约翰市有名的<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E5%86%BB%E8%B1%86%E6%88%BF%E5%AD%90.md#1">果冻豆房子</a>(Jellybean House)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>圣约翰市的一大亮点就是Jellybean House<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E5%86%BB%E8%B1%86%E6%88%BF%E5%AD%90.md#1">果冻豆房子</a>）。除了最出名的果冻豆街(Jellybean Row)，在街上随便走走就能看到五彩缤纷的房子，为阴雨天增色不少。我们权当这是餐后甜点了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199810-20210831_canada_helen_chou_607.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199810-20210831_canada_helen_chou_607-600x435.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199810" class="wp-caption-text">圣约翰市有名的果冻豆房子(Jellybean House)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看完果冻豆我们直接开车上了信号山(Signal Hill)俯瞰海港。因其战略位置，信号山从17世纪开始就成为一座炮台，现在是加拿大国家历史遗迹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199826" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199826-20210831_canada_helen_chou_608.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13199826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199826-20210831_canada_helen_chou_608-450x600.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199826" class="wp-caption-text">信号山(Signal Hill)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199834" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199834-20210831_canada_helen_chou_609.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199834-20210831_canada_helen_chou_609-600x810.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="810" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199834" class="wp-caption-text">炮台窗口下的海港。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199837-20210831_canada_helen_chou_610.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199837-20210831_canada_helen_chou_610-600x358.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199837" class="wp-caption-text">信号山上俯瞰圣约翰港口。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从信号山上还可以看到一排排新建的果冻豆房子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199839-20210831_canada_helen_chou_611.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199839-20210831_canada_helen_chou_611-600x399.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199839" class="wp-caption-text">信号山上俯瞰新建的果冻豆房子。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199840" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199840-20210831_canada_helen_chou_612.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13199840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199840-20210831_canada_helen_chou_612-450x600.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199840" class="wp-caption-text">著名的纽芬兰犬。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在信号山上我们还邂逅了一条名犬：纽芬兰犬(Newfoundland Dog)。它们最初是当地渔民的工作犬，体型巨大，聪明、力量惊人、性格冷静和忠诚，号称“巨人绅士”。不过我没看出它的绅士风范，倒是觉得有点憨。</p>
<p>临下山时天空开始放晴，阳光下的信号山野花满山坡，好美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199843" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199843-20210831_canada_helen_chou_613.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13199843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199843-20210831_canada_helen_chou_613-450x600.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199843" class="wp-caption-text">野花满山坡的信号山。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199846" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199846-20210831_canada_helen_chou_614.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13199846" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199846-20210831_canada_helen_chou_614-450x605.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="605" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199846" class="wp-caption-text">信号山上的指路牌。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些指向世界各地的指路牌在提示着下一站的方向。</p>
<p>从信号山下来往东南半小时就是加拿大与北美的最东点：Cape Spear（矛岬）。这里有纽芬兰现存的最古老的<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AF%E5%A1%94.md#1">灯塔</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199848-20210831_canada_helen_chou_615.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199848-20210831_canada_helen_chou_615-600x403.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199848" class="wp-caption-text">矛岬的<ahref="https://github.com/dmdccu385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AF%E5%A1%94.md#1">灯塔</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13199850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199850" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199850-20210831_canada_helen_chou_616.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13199850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199850-20210831_canada_helen_chou_616-450x600.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199850" class="wp-caption-text">矛岬的灯塔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几位当地人跟我们说，坐在这些临海的岩石上可以近距离看鲸鱼。我们等了很久都没有看到一条。后来灯塔的工作人员跑过来叫我们去灯塔的另一面，说在那里发现了好几条鲸鱼。我们前前后后大概看到了十几条，可惜都在远处喷水，连水面都不肯浮出。</p>
<p>今天的最后一站是Fort Amherst（爱姆赫斯特堡）。这里是圣约翰港口的进出口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13199855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13199855" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199855-20210831_canada_helen_chou_617.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13199855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13199855-20210831_canada_helen_chou_617-600x439.jpg" alt="加东自驾游" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13199855" class="wp-caption-text">爱姆赫斯特堡。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
