<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>兰道夫中秋庆典 盛况空前</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201219-DSC03723-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>月下荷塘草屋的布景吸引游客。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年09月01日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B0%E9%81%93%E5%A4%AB.md#1">兰道夫</a>镇报导）一排排红色和绿色的灯笼下人声鼎沸，络绎不绝的游客走进灯笼装饰的大门，驻足街道上的各式摊贩，而后来到街道尽头的巨大“月球”下拍照。这是8月27日和28日的第二届兰道夫镇（Randolph）<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">中秋节</a>庆典活动现场的热闹气氛。</p>
<p>这个庆典开始于2019年，由<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E8%A3%94.md#1">越南裔</a>社区主办。去年由于疫情，这个庆典改为了较小的室内活动。而今年，众多的游客、摊贩和表演者们又回到<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%B0%E9%81%93%E5%A4%AB.md#1">兰道夫</a>镇南部公园（South Park）共庆佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201226-DSC03682.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201226-DSC03682-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201226" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚，众多游客排队等待进场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201216-DSC03690.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201216-DSC03690-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201216" class="wp-caption-text">夜幕降临，众多游客来到庆典现场。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相比两年前的第一届庆典，今年虽然摊位和演出节目有所减少，但游客规模显着增加。尤其到了傍晚之后，煎饼、烧烤、甘蔗汁等小吃摊位前面都排起了长队。在隔壁的儿童游乐区，许多孩子在充气滑梯上爬上爬下玩耍。游乐区旁边是代表中秋主题的巨大圆月。今年主办方还布置了草屋与荷塘，更显古典风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201217-DSC03702.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201217-DSC03702-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201217" class="wp-caption-text">兰道夫镇<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82.md#1">中秋节</a>庆典中的小吃摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201222-DSC03764.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201222-DSC03764-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201222" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E8%A3%94.md#1">越南裔</a>童子军带来的棉花糖摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201225-DSC03787.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201225-DSC03787-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201225" class="wp-caption-text">越南文书法摊位。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>舞台区域是气氛最热闹的地方。这两天的傍晚之后，各种流行歌曲、舞蹈和民俗艺术轮番在舞台上演出。28日晚压轴的华林功夫舞狮则是最能点燃观众热情的节目。五头彩狮在锣鼓声中翻腾起舞，又穿行观众席中献瑞，吸引了数百观众聚集围观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201223-DSC03869.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201223-DSC03869-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201223" class="wp-caption-text">华林武馆带来压轴舞狮表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201224-DSC03920.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201224-DSC03920-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201224" class="wp-caption-text">华林武馆带来压轴功夫表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13201221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201221-DSC03780.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201221-DSC03780-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201221" class="wp-caption-text">庆典中的文艺表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>兰道夫镇经理霍华德（Brian P. Howard）及多名镇议员和官员都到场同乐。霍华德谈到，他在第一天的傍晚6点半来到人山人海的节庆现场，体会到人们对这次活动和亚裔文化的热情。他说，当主办方开始与镇政府商讨这次活动时，相关官员立刻同意为活动提供停车位。霍华德表示很高兴为这次庆典提供支持，同时也感谢Q Smile牙医诊所和其他主办人员付出辛劳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13201220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13201220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201220-DSC03743.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13201220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13201220-DSC03743-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13201220" class="wp-caption-text">兰道夫镇的嘉宾到场祝贺，左6为镇经理Brian P. Howard。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Q Smile诊所的John Lu回顾说，反复不定的疫情让他们难以决策。所以，一直到两个月之前<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>在6月才完全解封），他们才最终决定恢复室外庆典，并开始做相关准备。</p>
<p>庆典活动执行主任Lynn Nguyen说，在经历了疫情的一年之后，希望这次的中秋节能为社区带来希望与活力。“疫情将会结束，我们将重新出来，麻州会重新开放，一切都会回归正常。”她说。</p>
<p>主办人之一Brian Win说，他希望分享越南裔的文化，也希望通过举办庆典来鼓励其它族裔分享各自的文化。他期待看到这个年度中秋节庆典在未来继续扩大规模。◇</p>
