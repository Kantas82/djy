<h2>推荐Evoke Towns 的五大理由:</h2>
<h2>【理由一】：四通八达的地理位置</h2>
<p>Evoke Towns位于Bathurst Street和Rutherford Road(16街），处于Vaughan市高级住宅核心区，交通便利，与高速公路400和407相连。公共交通也十分发达，驾车数分钟可以到达Maple Go Station和Rutherford Go Station，VMC地铁站等等，对于没有车的上班族和学生来说十分便捷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13178976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13178976" style="width: 900px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178976-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.12.22-PM.png"><img class=" wp-image-13178976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178976-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.12.22-PM.png" alt="" width="900" b="615" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13178976" class="wp-caption-text">四通八达的地理位置（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>距离西边的400号高速只有4公里，去更西边427高速公路也就是8公里。如上班在多伦多的北约克，东边的士嘉堡和西边的密西沙加市，即使在高峰时间开车最多40分钟。有了这几条高速公路，使得住户驱车前往GTA各个角落都极其便利。</p>
<h2>【理由二】：<strong>优越的教育资源</strong></h2>
<p>Evoke Towns周边拥有顶尖的教育资源，私立高中UMCA Rich Tree Academy, Delano Academy还有Vilanova College都是不错的私校选择，Seneca学院和约克大学也是加拿大知名学府，在教育的每个阶段都能为你提供最好的保障。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13178981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13178981" style="width: 773px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178981-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.32.18-PM.png"><img class=" wp-image-13178981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178981-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.32.18-PM.png" alt="" width="773" b="514" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13178981" class="wp-caption-text">优越的教育资源（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【理由三】：<strong>完善的周边设施</strong></h2>
<p>居住在Evoke Towns，只需要 驾车2分钟，便可以到达附近的Longo‘s超市，Shoppers Drug Mart，CIBC，Scotiabank以及各式各类的餐厅。驾车10分钟便可到达Vaughan Mills购物商场和Hillcrest shopping centre，对于购物达人来说在家附近就能满足逛街需求。Lego乐园和Reptilia动物园也是空闲时带孩子外出的好去处。高尔夫球爱好者一定不能错过的Eagles Nest高尔夫俱乐部，开车仅需5分钟即可到达。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-13178983 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178983-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.36.51-PM.png" alt="" width="834" b="706" /></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13179904-Screen-Shot-2021-08-22-at-11.15.05-AM.png"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-13179904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13179904-Screen-Shot-2021-08-22-at-11.15.05-AM.png" alt="" width="626" b="50" /></a></p>
<p>Patterson社区西边3公里是大多地区最大的市内商场Vaughan Mills Mall。西北面是安大略最大的游乐场Canada Wonderland。居民享受得天独厚的便利，乐趣和新型社区的阳光。这里的商业特别发达，几乎每个主要道路的交叉口都有大型Plaza，里面各种商业设施应有尽有，饭店、咖啡厅比比皆是。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13178989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13178989" style="width: 824px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178989-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.42.28-PM.png"><img class=" wp-image-13178989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178989-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.42.28-PM.png" alt="" width="824" b="457" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13178989" class="wp-caption-text">Canada Wonderland<strong>（商家提供）</strong></figcaption></figure>
<h2>【理由四】：<strong>独一无二的绿色环境</strong></h2>
<p>Vaughan市拥有超过800公顷的绿化面积，230个以上的运动场和200个以上的游乐场，Evoke Modern Town附近有多个公园如Marc Santi、枫树自然保护区等等，让你既能感受到城市的现代与便捷又能感受到大自然的宁静和谐，充分体验户外活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13178992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13178992" style="width: 844px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178992-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.45.09-PM.png"><img class=" wp-image-13178992" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13178992-Screen-Shot-2021-08-21-at-10.45.09-PM.png" alt="" width="844" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13178992" class="wp-caption-text">享受大自然的宁静和谐<strong>（商家提供）</strong></figcaption></figure>
