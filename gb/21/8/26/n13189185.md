<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>樱花树饭卷：将春天的幸福包进饭卷里</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189313-20210826_rice_roll_P.88-1_13-600x400.jpg" />
<h6> 吃到春天的幸福：樱花树饭卷。（台湾广厦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【编者按】每年三、四月间，世界各地都有樱花盛开的盛况，淡淡的带有梦幻般美感的樱花令人陶醉！似乎有了樱花，人们才有期待春天花开的心情。现在，让我们将樱花包进<ahref="https://github.com/gwrihu3930/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%AD%E5%8D%B7.md#1">饭卷</a>里，让樱花伴随我们天天都像春天赏樱一样的好心情。</p>
<p>会煮白饭就会做！制作造型<ahref="https://github.com/gwrihu3930/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%AD%E5%8D%B7.md#1">饭卷</a>，就像玩乐高般有趣！不需要厨艺技巧，也不用拘泥工具材料，用酱油、花椰菜等手边食材将白饭染成缤纷色彩，以海苔片做出轮廓线条，不同形状的食材拼接组合，只要了解基本制作原理，自由变换食材、排列方式，就可以做出自己独创的饭卷造型！</p>
<p>本文介绍的<ahref="https://github.com/gwrihu3930/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1%E6%A0%91%E9%A5%AD%E5%8D%B7.md#1">樱花树饭卷</a>，以粉红色饭和巴西里，让灿烂的樱花纷纷绽放！感受春天的幸福气息。</p>
<h3>准备材料</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13189253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189253" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189253-20210826_rice_roll_P.88-2_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13189253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189253-20210826_rice_roll_P.88-2_02-600x245.jpg" alt="饭" width="600" b="245" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189253" class="wp-caption-text">米饭的准备。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饭（白色） 180g<br />
饭（粉红色） 150g</p>
<h3>海苔</h3>
<figure id="attachment_13189249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189249" style="width: 422px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189249-20210826_rice_roll_P.88-3_01.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189249-20210826_rice_roll_P.88-3_01.jpg" alt="海苔" width="422" b="234" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189249" class="wp-caption-text">海苔的分配。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>内部材料</h3>
<p>煮瓠瓜干<br />
5×10公分 1片<br />
4×10公分 1片<br />
＊煮瓠瓜干也可以换成菜脯、笋干等其他食材。</p>
<p>装饰材料<br />
巴西里碎 少许<br />
腌萝卜片（粉红色） 少许</p>
<h3>局部造型</h3>
<p>1. 树干</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189256-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-1_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13189256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189256-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-1_03-600x217.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="217" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189256" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将5×10公分的煮瓠瓜干放在2/3片海苔中间，左右往内包起来。4×10公分的瓠瓜干则用1/2片海苔包起来。<br />
组合</p>
<p>2. 连接1片和1/2片海苔后，其中一侧保留5公分，其他均匀铺上120g白饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189257" style="width: 403px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189257-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-2_04.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189257-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-2_04.jpg" alt="" width="403" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189257" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将2份30g白饭分别堆成3公分宽，放到白饭中间，间隔1公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189259" style="width: 396px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189259-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-3_05.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189259-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-3_05.jpg" alt="" width="396" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189259" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将2份30g粉红色饭放到同样的位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189261" style="width: 402px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189261-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-4_06.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189261-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-4_06.jpg" alt="" width="402" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189261" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 在饭的中间放煮上步骤1的5公分瓠瓜干，做成?的形状，旁边直立放4公分煮瓠瓜干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189278" style="width: 397px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189278-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-5_07.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189278-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-5_07.jpg" alt="饭卷" width="397" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189278" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 5公分煮瓠瓜干上平铺40g粉红饭，4公分煮瓠瓜干上平铺20g粉红饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189284" style="width: 402px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189284-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-6_08.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189284-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-6_08.jpg" alt="" width="402" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189284" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 在上方堆30g粉红色饭，做成半圆形的弧状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189291" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189291" style="width: 396px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189291-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-7_09.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189291-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-7_09.jpg" alt="" width="396" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189291" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 单手握起竹帘，另一手稍微按压调整形状，再以竹帘将海苔和饭包起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189303" style="width: 403px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189303-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-8_10.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189303-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-8_10.jpg" alt="" width="403" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189303" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 用手将饭卷两端的饭压实，稍微调整形状后切成4块。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189306" style="width: 397px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189306-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-9_11.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189306-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-9_11.jpg" alt="" width="397" b="287" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189306" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>装饰</h3>
<p>10. 将巴西里碎铺在下方，做出草地。并用花型模具压腌萝卜片，做出樱花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13189309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13189309" style="width: 402px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189309-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-10_12.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-13189309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13189309-20210826_rice_roll_P.89-10_12.jpg" alt="" width="402" b="291" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13189309" class="wp-caption-text">（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
