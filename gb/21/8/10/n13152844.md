<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七月的加东四省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（4）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152853-20210810_canada_helen_chou_404-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西溪塘峡湾(Western Brook Pond Fjord)的无敌风景。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文 ：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/21/8/4/n13137470.md#1">七月的加东四省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（3）</a></p>
<p>如果你问我：<ahref="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF%E8%8E%AB%E6%81%A9%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">格罗斯莫恩公园</a>最美的景点在哪里？我会毫不犹豫的告诉你：在<ahref="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BA%AA%E5%A1%98%E5%B3%A1%E6%B9%BE.md#1">西溪塘峡湾</a>（Western Brook Pond Fjord）！</p>
<p>如果你问我：<ahref="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF%E8%8E%AB%E6%81%A9%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">格罗斯莫恩公园</a>最难到达的景点在哪里？我也会毫不犹豫的告诉你：在<ahref="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BA%AA%E5%A1%98%E5%B3%A1%E6%B9%BE.md#1">西溪塘峡湾</a>！</p>
<p>更多的人问我：上面那张照片太精彩！哪里拍的到？我立马告诉你：在西溪塘峡湾！</p>
<p>看到这里，大概有不少人开始摩拳擦掌，准备行程了！我再告诉你：那里是美，但要爬上去太难了！它不像其它徒步登山有徒步道可用，全程几乎是要四肢并用的。要会像猴子那样挂到树枝上跳过水塘，也要像狗狗一样从树枝下穿过，还要像青蛙一样吸在岩石上翻过去。难就难吧，还贵！每个人要付近300加元预约参加有专业导游带队的徒步登山队才能上去。</p>
<p>但是，“不到长城非好汉”呀。我们一定下<ahref="https://github.com/liezrg346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C.md#1">加东</a>行程就在网上预约了这项徒步登山活动。昨天又徒步8英里预热了一下，晚上都早早的睡下养精蓄锐。然而，人生处处有“惊喜”。今天一早才发现，我的登山鞋坏了！附近又没有任何商店可以买到鞋，哪怕能买到也不能穿着新鞋进行这样强度与难度的徒步登山活动呀。我是真伤心了。</p>
<p>可是再伤心也没办法，只能和闺蜜两人兵分两路，她按原计划徒步登山从上往下看西溪塘，我改坐游船从下往上看峡湾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152849-20210810_canada_helen_chou_402.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152849-20210810_canada_helen_chou_402-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152849" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾(Western Brook Pond Fjord)的无敌风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这几张无价无敌的照片是闺蜜徒步18公里山路再加坐快艇60公里水路，然后几乎全程四肢并用爬上1500多英尺高的山峰，来回8个多小时才拍到的，再被我哭着喊着求来的！</p>
<p>其实，闺蜜参加的徒步登山还不是最难的。还有一项更有挑战的徒步登山，需要在野地里过3到4夜，全程35公里。这要求参加者有一定的专业徒步登山的经验了，是要通过考试的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152851-20210810_canada_helen_chou_403.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152851-20210810_canada_helen_chou_403-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152851" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾(Western Brook Pond Fjord)的无敌风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13152853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152853-20210810_canada_helen_chou_404.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152853-20210810_canada_helen_chou_404-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152853" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾(Western Brook Pond Fjord)的无敌风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>西溪，也称西布鲁克(Western Brook)，是一条长达30公里的峡湾。峡湾里的水全部来自于冰川融水。冰川融化时水自身的重量压沉了下面的土壤，切断了峡湾的入海口，形成了一条封闭的峡湾。这就是西溪塘峡湾名字的来源。</p>
<p>相对闺蜜的高难度徒步，我的坐船行程就享受多啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152856-20210810_canada_helen_chou_405.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152856-20210810_canada_helen_chou_405-600x427.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152856" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾游船码头。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>和闺蜜在停车场分手后，我一个人沿着宽宽平平的步道走了大约45分钟，一路看风景，就到了游船码头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152858-20210810_canada_helen_chou_406.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152858-20210810_canada_helen_chou_406-600x390.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152858" class="wp-caption-text">水上看西溪塘峡湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13152860" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152860" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152860-20210810_canada_helen_chou_407.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152860-20210810_canada_helen_chou_407-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152860" class="wp-caption-text">水上看西溪塘峡湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13152862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152862" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152862-20210810_canada_helen_chou_408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152862-20210810_canada_helen_chou_408-600x450.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152862" class="wp-caption-text">水上看西溪塘峡湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水上看西溪塘峡湾没有从山顶上看那么惊心动魄，但也够美仑美奂了。游船来回2小时，对于想偷懒看风景的人来说是非常值得的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152939" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152939-20210810_canada_helen_chou_409.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13152939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152939-20210810_canada_helen_chou_409-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152939" class="wp-caption-text">载歌载舞的游客。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>航程快结束时船长放起了纽芬兰民歌。纽芬兰的音乐与爱尔兰音乐非常相似，欢快明亮。两把刀叉，一个响铃，船上的气氛一下子热烈起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152945-20210810_canada_helen_chou_410.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13152945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152945-20210810_canada_helen_chou_410-450x600.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152945" class="wp-caption-text">载歌载舞的游客与船长。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这位穿红外衣的老绅士就是我们的船长。他用两把勺子就能打出欢快的节奏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152947-20210810_canada_helen_chou_411.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13152947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152947-20210810_canada_helen_chou_411-450x337.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152947" class="wp-caption-text">载歌载舞的游客。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小朋友们也不甘落后。欢快的音乐，热烈的气氛，驱散了乌云带来的阴冷，我们的航程也接近尾声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152948" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152948-20210810_canada_helen_chou_412.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13152948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152948-20210810_canada_helen_chou_412-450x254.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="601" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152948" class="wp-caption-text">乌云下的西溪塘峡湾。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了等还在辛苦爬山的闺蜜，我一个人开着车在西溪塘外慢慢兜风。没想到沿路的风光也令人流连忘返。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13152951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152951-20210810_canada_helen_chou_413.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152951-20210810_canada_helen_chou_413-600x293.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152951" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾外的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13152952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13152952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152952-20210810_canada_helen_chou_414.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13152952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13152952-20210810_canada_helen_chou_414-600x414.jpg" alt="加拿大, 西溪塘峡湾" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13152952" class="wp-caption-text">西溪塘峡湾外的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
