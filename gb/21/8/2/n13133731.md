<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏日解封 出游包款成缤纷亮点</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133736-507637-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>东奥限量款手提肩背包，为选手应援。（Kipling提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年08月03日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）全民居家防疫两个月，终于迎来各场域适度开放的解封阶段。比利时品牌Kipling夏季必备包款从随身小包到旅行袋一应俱全，兼具好搭外型与机能设计，方便备妥防疫用品，让外出不仅放松安心，也能成为夏季<ahref="https://github.com/rhhfmo347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>焦点。</p>
<p><strong>户外野餐露营：</strong></p>
<figure id="13133734" aria-describedby="caption-13133734" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133734-507635-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133734-507635-450x300.jpg" alt="天堂蓝云朵色ACTIVE系列随装随拿轻装上阵。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13133734" class="wp-caption-text">天堂蓝云朵色ACTIVE系列随装随拿轻装上阵。（Kipling提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>夏日向往户外活动，手绘棕榈树印花系列带出夏季氛围，以配件包、后<ahref="https://github.com/rhhfmo347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%8C%E5%8C%85.md#1">背包</a>及行李袋为主，无论是躺在草皮上野餐、露营，都能为轻旅行增添更多活力。</p>
<p><strong>全家踏青戏水：</strong></p>
<figure id="13133735" aria-describedby="caption-13133735" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133735-507636-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133735-507636-450x300.jpg" alt="清新薄荷绿系列利用拼接网点的材质打造，适合户外活动。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13133735" class="wp-caption-text">清新薄荷绿系列利用拼接网点的材质打造，适合户外活动。（Kipling提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>清新薄荷绿系列利用拼接网点材质与防水材料制成，既能防泼水又能减少脏污，最适合一家大小出门踏青或露营、探索大自然的奥秘。</p>
<p><strong>随身物品繁多：</strong></p>
<p>若是习惯带很多物品出门的人，可使用轻巧大容量的包包，天堂蓝云朵色系列与限定清新牛油果绿，拥有多种尺寸，随装随拿轻装上阵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13133733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13133733" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133733-507634.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13133733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13133733-507634-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13133733" class="wp-caption-text">东奥限量款手提肩<ahref="https://github.com/rhhfmo347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%8C%E5%8C%85.md#1">背包</a>，为选手应援。（Kipling提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，为迎接东京奥运，Kipling也推出限量纪念款“童趣猴运动涂鸦手提肩背包MY TOTE BAG”，一起为选手应援。◇</p>
