<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>穿出你的T-shirt时尚 舒适又不邋遢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164771-GettyImages-651036704-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>春夏爱马仕男装秀，由浅灰与深蓝组合而成的细横条纹T-shirt，显得大方时尚。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年08月16日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）你的衣柜中满是T-shirt（<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/t%E6%81%A4.md#1">T恤</a>），却找不到好看的搭配方法吗？拿出万用牛仔裤，却又厌倦T-shirt搭牛仔裤的一成不变吗？又或者你抗拒穿T-shirt出门，觉得怎么搭怎么邋遢、像是在穿睡衣？</p>
<p>无论你对T-shirt抱有如何复杂的情感，T-shirt可是从未离开<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>界，简约、舒适的自然生活感更是饱受疫情摧残的我们所渴望的，所以放宽心拥抱T-shirt的慵懒吧！就让它轻抚你的皮肤，在2021夏天，自信地穿出属于你的T-shirt时尚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164772-GettyImages-1173241225.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164772-GettyImages-1173241225-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164772" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周中，澳洲女装品牌以白T-shirt和水彩画花短裙的组合，展现知性优雅的态度。（Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images for Fashion Palette)）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白色T-shirt最是常见，实穿好搭，干干净净的同时也是最安静的陪衬，能让搭配的裤、裙、配件等尽情展现。</p>
<p>购买白T时务必货比三家，因为它简单，所以版型和布料就相对显眼，也更加重要。像是过于薄透的白T，不仅易显内衣痕、多洗几遍版型可能会松垮，若是洗出领口荷叶边，还会给人不修边幅的感受；而具有一定挺度和份量的布料，会让你穿着时更加时髦有型喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164712-GettyImages-807456848.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164712-GettyImages-807456848-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164712" class="wp-caption-text">法国模特薇欧莉特（Violette Marie d&#8217;Urso）出席香奈儿高订秀，简约黑色T-shirt搭碎花高腰裤，散发不简单的俐落可爱。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相比于白色T-shirt的轻盈，黑T-shirt就沈静许多，且依然素雅。</p>
<p>穿着时可以考虑搭配一件设计繁复的下着，因为上身的黑Ｔ压着整体造型，所以平常习惯一身素的人也不必担心太过花俏喔！另外也可以尝试多种质感混搭，像是皮裙、皮包、皮靴等和黑Ｔ组合都能呈现出不错的效果，相当帅气，黑色的深沈和皮质的反光也形成很棒的对比落差。</p>
<p>配饰则可以考虑金属制品，链条背带、首饰、腰带扣环等在黑色的衬托下都将更加闪耀迷人！但要注意别一股脑儿的把所有金属饰品都往身上戴，一不小心可会显得俗气恼人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164769-GettyImages-1297475178.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164769-GettyImages-1297475178-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164769" class="wp-caption-text">2021柏林时装周，灰绿色宽大T-shirt与牛仔破裤搭配展现满满街头率性风。（Andreas Rentz/Getty Images for MBFW）</figcaption></figure>
<p>印花T-shirt多了几分个性，不同图样的选择，恰恰展现个人风格。若是版型宽大，可以搭配牛仔裤和帆布鞋，有着满满的街头感，凉快透气又无拘无束。</p>
<p>值得注意的是，许多人喜欢印有外文的款式，似乎显得自己更文艺一些，但下手前最好拿出google翻译确认下到底上头写了些什么，免得不小心穿去不适当的场合，反而闹了笑话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164767" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164767-GettyImages-57152575.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164767-GettyImages-57152575-450x801.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="801" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164767" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周中，Taverniti So Jeans品牌呈现贴身T-shirt与牛仔造型，简单却富有细节质感。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13164770" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164770" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164770-GettyImages-179591240.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164770-GettyImages-179591240-450x649.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="649" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164770" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯·奔驰时装周，西班牙品牌Desigual 以印花T-shirt与热裤搭配，兼具凉快与女人味。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛仔与T-shirt的搭配，满街都是，要怎么穿出区别？除了衣料质感，做工也很重要，富有细节的造型，能成就超越旁人的素雅品味。至于大面积、满版印花图腾则相当引人注目，落落大方，搭配简单的下身出门也不嫌无聊，洋溢着活力的夏日风情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164768" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164768-GettyImages-958675162.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164768-GettyImages-958675162-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164768" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周中，澳洲女装品牌C/Meo Collective以暖黄T-shirt与细肩带裙的结合，展现夏日风情。（Stefan Gosatti/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了单穿，T-shirt也适合当作内搭，选择稍微合身些的版型，外搭细肩带裙、吊带裤／裙，在夏天也能轻松打造层次感，有着青春活泼的气息。当你只是要到附近买东西，懒得思考搭配时，这种穿搭绝对是快速方便不邋遢的首选！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13164771" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164771-GettyImages-651036704.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164771-GettyImages-651036704-450x729.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="729" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164771" class="wp-caption-text">春夏爱马仕男装秀，细横条纹T-shirt与锥型裤搭配，显得大方<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13164705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13164705" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164705-GettyImages-1281956785.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13164705" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13164705-GettyImages-1281956785-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13164705" class="wp-caption-text">意大利女演员卡洛塔?纳多里（Carlotta Natoli），身着条纹T-shirt，展现简单却自信的气质。（Stefania M. D&#8217;Alessandro/Getty Images for RFF）</figcaption></figure>
<p>条纹设计可谓服装造型经典元素之一，在T-shirt上使用自然也合适，简约又俐落，单穿或做为内搭都十分好看。</p>
<p>条纹粗细会给人不同印象，粗而宽的条纹大体来说有明显的色块感，带来休闲、帅气、可爱的感受；细密条纹则相较细致、内敛，也能搭配西装裤和西装外套打造商务休闲装，用途相当广泛喔！</p>
<p>看了以上介绍，是否有为你增添一两个搭配小灵感？快打开你的衣柜，穿出属于你的T-shirt时尚吧！</p>
