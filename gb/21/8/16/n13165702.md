<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—秋声（彩墨）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13165704-a153658df918f98a55727f7d05bf8ddc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>困知勉行—徐明义画集8—秋声（彩墨）。局部。（图片来源：徐明义提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%A3%B0.md#1">秋声</a>（<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）</h2>
<p>米开兰基罗说过：“绘画是音乐、是旋律，只有天才才能理解其复杂性。”——依我看，米氏前半句说对了，后半句我并不全然认同。</p>
<p>绘画必须要有律动感，像音乐一样，有旋律、有休止、空白——这是公认的了。至于说只有天才才能理解其复杂性，这未免就显得“玄虚”了。——绘画不外乎“经营位置”、“随类赋彩”、“应物象形”……这些话古人早已说过，已然是颠扑不破的真理了，并不是只有天才才能懂的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13165704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13165704" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13165704-a153658df918f98a55727f7d05bf8ddc.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13165704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13165704-a153658df918f98a55727f7d05bf8ddc.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="481" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13165704" class="wp-caption-text">困知勉行—<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89%E7%94%BB%E9%9B%868.md#1">徐明义画集8</a>—<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%A3%B0.md#1">秋声</a>（<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A2%A8.md#1">彩墨</a>）。65×76cm。（图片来源：<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%98%8E%E4%B9%89.md#1">徐明义</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>Autumn sound/ink and color painting</h2>
<p>Michelangelo said: &#8220;Painting is music, melody, and only genius understand its complexity.&#8221; &#8211; From my point of view, the first half of Michelangelo’s saying is right but I do not entirely recognize the latter half.</p>
<p>Painting must be with rhythmic, like music, it must be with melodies and pause &#8211; it is accredited. With regard to the saying “only genius understand its complexity” this absolutely becomes a &#8220;mystery&#8221; &#8211; Painting is nothing more than &#8220;Management position&#8221;, &#8220;Assortment with colors, &#8220;Pictorial phenomena&#8221;.</p>
<p>The ancients said these all long time ago and it has been an irrefutable truth, not only the genius understand.@</p>
