<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超人气烘培老师教绝活 起司面包轻松出炉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197898-300ce349036025caad34ce8a0942d6c7-600x400.png" />
<h6>面包烤成金黄色泽的花式造型，不管是一口咬下，还是撕成一块一块放入嘴里，都能享受美味和乐趣。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">起司面包</a>”一向是<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>店的热销款，这也是在<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E5%9F%B9.md#1">烘培</a>圈有20年资历的吴介甫老师的最爱，他说：“因为我实在太喜欢起司了，想要享受满满起司的口感，多次调整配方的成品，就是这款‘双倍起司面包’。”</p>
<p>吴介甫是<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E5%9F%B9.md#1">烘培</a>教室的人气讲师，他将自己在各大<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>店学到的绝活倾囊相授，毫无保留的将诀窍提点给学生，希望每一个人在家也能做出美味面包。虽说烘培面包复杂容易失败，但是只要跟着吴老师的制作步骤，就能轻松简易的让面包出炉了！</p>
<p>这一款“汤种界的双倍<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">起司面包</a>”，柔软面包体包裹着浓郁乳酪丁，上头撒上乳酪丝，一口咬下就有双重起司的风味。面包烤成金黄色泽的花式造型，不管是一口咬下，还是撕成一块一块放入嘴里，都能享受美味和乐趣。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">双倍起司面包</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">材料（4个）</span></h3>
<h4>【主面团】</h4>
<p>高筋面粉 200g<br />
细糖 24g<br />
盐 4g<br />
干酵母 2g<br />
冰水 90g<br />
鲜奶 40g<br />
汤种面团 20g<br />
无盐发酵奶油 20g</p>
<h4>【装蒜馅】</h4>
<p>乳酪丁 15g<br />
披萨乳酪丝 适量<br />
海苔粉 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">作法</span></h3>
<h4>【A 制作面团】</h4>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">1. 混合材料</span></p>
<p>将汤种、奶油以外的所有材料（高筋面粉、细糖、盐、干酵母、冰水、鲜奶）放入搅拌钢盆中，慢速拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197919" style="width: 347px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197919-00652fcc9b2b57a12d13efc59536b4de.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197919-00652fcc9b2b57a12d13efc59536b4de.png" alt="面包" width="347" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197919" class="wp-caption-text">将汤种、奶油以外的所有材料放入搅拌钢盆中，慢速拌匀。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">2. 加入汤种面团 （等搅拌中的面团成团再加入汤种面团口感会更好！）</span></p>
<p>拌匀后加入汤种，中速打至可以拉出薄膜（约8分筋）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197917" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197917-4c6b1f4fc1afe35512d637c4a2cebd75.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197917-4c6b1f4fc1afe35512d637c4a2cebd75.png" alt="面包" width="348" b="274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197917" class="wp-caption-text">面团可拉出薄膜的状态。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">3. 加入奶油</span></p>
<p>加入无盐发酵奶油，慢速拌匀后转中速，打至拉出的薄膜边缘无锯齿状（约9分筋）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197920-a755639042869d85003ba0ba7d6cd972.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197920-a755639042869d85003ba0ba7d6cd972-450x148.png" alt="面包" width="450" b="148" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197920" class="wp-caption-text">面团拉出的薄膜边缘无锯齿状。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">4. 滚圆</span></p>
<p>将面团整圆，放上烤盘稍微拍平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197922" style="width: 358px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197922-f515417a80042012342b5cd2e08fe6ec.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197922-f515417a80042012342b5cd2e08fe6ec.png" alt="" width="358" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197922" class="wp-caption-text">将面团整圆。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13197923" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197923" style="width: 352px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197923-7329f25041363a964dde34ec4202c519.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197923-7329f25041363a964dde34ec4202c519.png" alt="" width="352" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197923" class="wp-caption-text">面团放上烤盘稍微拍平。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">5. 基本发酵</span></p>
<p>放入发酵箱（温度30度，湿度75度）发酵，也可以盖上湿布或保鲜膜放于室温发酵。发酵40分钟后，将面团先拍成长方形，以三等份折起。重新拍平后，再继续发酵20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197936" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197936-a8a431f1aaf4a406fd2979f195558c92.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197936-a8a431f1aaf4a406fd2979f195558c92.png" alt="面包" width="452" b="154" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197936" class="wp-caption-text">面团先拍成长方形，以三等份折起。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13197937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197937" style="width: 327px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197937-4de2ef6fa4d23003fae0d569d6694ea2.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197937-4de2ef6fa4d23003fae0d569d6694ea2.png" alt="面包" width="327" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197937" class="wp-caption-text">面团重新拍平后，再继续发酵20分钟。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">6. 分割整圆</span></p>
<p>将面团分成60g一份，滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197942-bc6bdeafdcf59b7965f5c8d2feb5a41b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13197942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197942-bc6bdeafdcf59b7965f5c8d2feb5a41b-450x139.png" alt="" width="450" b="139" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197942" class="wp-caption-text">面团分成60g一份，滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">7. 中间发酵</span></p>
<p>将整圆好的小面团放上烤盘并保持距离，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度30度，湿度75度）发酵30分钟。</p>
<h4>【Ｂ整形包馅】</h4>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">8. 杆平面团</span></p>
<p>将面团稍微拉长、擀开，将靠近自己一侧的面团用手指压平固定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197946" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197946-03ff5be781a0019b7d14eb8f61470df2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13197946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197946-03ff5be781a0019b7d14eb8f61470df2-450x143.png" alt="" width="450" b="143" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197946" class="wp-caption-text">面团稍微拉长、擀开。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13197947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197947" style="width: 335px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197947-e54ea4913eddf3377314a081446bf35e.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197947-e54ea4913eddf3377314a081446bf35e.png" alt="" width="335" b="237" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197947" class="wp-caption-text">面团靠近自己一侧的面团用手指压平固定。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">9. 包入馅料</span></p>
<p>放上乳酪丁，从前向后卷成长条，再用双手将两端搓尖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197967" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197967-c36f8952807af020c22f14ab81292440.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13197967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197967-c36f8952807af020c22f14ab81292440-450x144.png" alt="" width="450" b="144" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197967" class="wp-caption-text">放上乳酪丁，从前向后卷成长条。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13197968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197968-4829712f0d1ed79d9f79db99690f0160.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13197968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197968-4829712f0d1ed79d9f79db99690f0160-450x143.png" alt="" width="450" b="143" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197968" class="wp-caption-text">用双手将面团两端搓尖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">10. 整形</span></p>
<p>将面团沾上切碎的乳酪丝，用小刀轻轻切至一半高度，不要完全切断，抓住两端弯成弧形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197972" style="width: 345px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197972-2e67dcdd707895cf89b25b071f8b4de9.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197972-2e67dcdd707895cf89b25b071f8b4de9.png" alt="" width="345" b="234" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197972" class="wp-caption-text">将面团沾上切碎的乳酪丝。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13197973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197973-6d59b9d83c7b57a21e0762b3cd665a12.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13197973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197973-6d59b9d83c7b57a21e0762b3cd665a12-450x151.png" alt="" width="450" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197973" class="wp-caption-text">用小刀轻轻切面团至一半高度，不要完全切断，抓住两端弯成弧形。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h4>【Ｃ烘烤】</h4>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">11. 最后发酵</span></p>
<p>将包好的面团放上烤盘，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度34~36度，湿度75度）最后发酵60～70分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13197980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13197980" style="width: 330px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197980-9affe0c6d499e87091d70eefd7486b5c.png"><img class=" wp-image-13197980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13197980-9affe0c6d499e87091d70eefd7486b5c.png" alt="" width="330" b="219" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13197980" class="wp-caption-text">将包好的面团放上烤盘，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度34~36度，湿度75度）最后发酵60～70分钟。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">12. 烘烤</span></p>
<p>烤箱预热至上火200度，下火200度，烘烤14～16分钟。出炉后撒上海苔粉装饰即完成。</p>

<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>制作汤种面团</strong></span></h2>
<p>“汤种”对台湾人来说想必是一点都不陌生，街坊的面包店里应该都有贩卖“<ahref="https://github.com/zeqdou3554/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A4%E7%A7%8D%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">汤种面包</a>”，吃起来特别柔软美味，可说是风靡一时的热门品项。其实汤种就是用沸水煮过的面种，用汤种法做出来的面包，具有湿润、柔软的口感，并有着良好的保水性，即使放了一段时间，面包依然能保有水分，吃起来有弹性。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;">Tips：制作好的汤种面团可冷冻保存约一年，使用前再退冰软化。</span></p>
