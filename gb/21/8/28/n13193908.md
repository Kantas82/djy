<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超人气烘培老师教绝活 软Q的“熏鸡面包”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202963-ed09812ee07b397c40ec59ff7db8f7ce-393x400.png" />
<h6>简单好制作的熏鸡汤种面包！（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>咸甜柔软的<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>大人小孩都喜欢，尤其是内馅包裹熏鸡配料的面包，更是面包店的销售常胜军。肺炎疫情期间，不便外出，如果想在家<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E5%9F%B9.md#1">烘培</a>面包又害怕失败，不妨参考资深烘培老师吴介甫的食谱，让你做出“与众不同”的<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%8F%E9%B8%A1%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">熏鸡面包</a>，从里到外看得美味、吃得满意。</p>
<p>此款<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%8F%E9%B8%A1%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">熏鸡面包</a>外型四四方方，从<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a>体中间为起点裂开后蔓延至面包边，看起来特别，作法却不困难。吴介甫说：“其实整形手法意外简单！不过，俗话说魔鬼藏在细节里，在包馅造型时，还是要留意必须将边边角角都仔细封起，才不会让馅料在烘烤期间冲出面团，流得一塌糊涂喔。”</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">火焰熏鸡面包</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">材料（4个）</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【主面团】</strong></span></p>
<p>高筋面粉 200g<br />
细糖 24g<br />
盐 4g<br />
干酵母 2g<br />
水 90g<br />
鲜奶 40g<br />
汤种 20g<br />
无盐发酵奶油 20g</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【配料】</strong></span></p>
<p>辣味熏鸡肉 30g<br />
起士片1片<br />
匈牙利红椒粉 适量<br />
沙拉酱 适量<br />
海苔粉 适量</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【A制作面团】</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">1. 制作主面团</span></p>
<p>请参考?<strong><span style="color: #800080;"><a style="color: #800080;" href=><a href="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/21/8/30/n13197774.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">超人气烘培老师教绝活 起司面包轻松出炉</a></span></strong>?作法 1~5</p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">2. 分割面团</span></p>
<p>将面团分成100g一份，滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202894" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202894-404419dc8232819021fc6d933551ded6.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202894-404419dc8232819021fc6d933551ded6.png" alt="" width="299" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202894" class="wp-caption-text">将面团分成100g一份，滚圆。 （采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13202895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202895" style="width: 293px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202895-c5eba90d0414a4e9b468849ea8f56645.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202895-c5eba90d0414a4e9b468849ea8f56645.png" alt="" width="293" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202895" class="wp-caption-text">将面团滚圆后捏起面团底部收合。 （采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">3. 中间发酵</span></p>
<p>将整圆好的小面团放上烤盘并保持距离，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度30度，湿度75度）发酵30分钟。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【B整形包馅】</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">4. 杆平面团</span></p>
<p>将面团轻轻拍平，用擀面棍杆开后，以手指整成四边形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202932-b732feb4d33b0a592119377317187379.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13202932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202932-b732feb4d33b0a592119377317187379-450x95.png" alt="" width="450" b="95" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202932" class="wp-caption-text">将面团轻轻拍平，用擀面棍杆开后，以手指整成四边形。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">5. 放入馅料</span></p>
<p>在面团中间铺上约30g辣味熏鸡肉与一片起司片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202935" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202935-f00403978a5f0dd1098e8e66c967f6f7.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202935-f00403978a5f0dd1098e8e66c967f6f7.png" alt="" width="299" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202935" class="wp-caption-text">在面团中间铺上约30g辣味熏鸡肉。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13202936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202936" style="width: 303px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202936-d2b6b06f6e26cb5ae9cf8df325df6bba.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202936-d2b6b06f6e26cb5ae9cf8df325df6bba.png" alt="" width="303" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202936" class="wp-caption-text">在面团中间铺上约30g辣味熏鸡肉与一片起司片。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">6. 包馅</span></p>
<p>抓起四个角叠向面团中间包起馅料。包起后，用手指捏紧四个边角，避免馅料溢出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202944" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202944-6b617c88c38c2ee85e13b8dc8a1716b7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13202944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202944-6b617c88c38c2ee85e13b8dc8a1716b7-450x145.png" alt="" width="450" b="145" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202944" class="wp-caption-text">抓起四个角叠向面团中间包起馅料。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13202945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202945" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202945-b6a95dd4f4b2a3a1e1b8a4b75a03bc1f.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13202945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202945-b6a95dd4f4b2a3a1e1b8a4b75a03bc1f-450x145.png" alt="" width="450" b="145" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202945" class="wp-caption-text">包起后，用手指捏紧四个边角，避免馅料溢出。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">7. 装饰</span></p>
<p>面团平整的一面沾上匈牙利红椒粉，翻面后，再用小刀轻轻划开十字装饰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202952" style="width: 308px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202952-7646055c3e732b935b11fbd30ad4920a.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202952-7646055c3e732b935b11fbd30ad4920a.png" alt="" width="308" b="204" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202952" class="wp-caption-text">面团平整的一面沾上匈牙利红椒粉。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13202953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202953" style="width: 290px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202953-0569b273b74020f6607b49dac4b6bd27.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202953-0569b273b74020f6607b49dac4b6bd27.png" alt="" width="290" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202953" class="wp-caption-text">面团翻面后，再用小刀轻轻划开十字装饰。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>Tips 划刀装饰时，动作必需轻柔，避免让馅料溢出。</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">8. 最后发酵</span></p>
<p>将面团放上烤盘，室温静置或放入发酵箱（温度34~36度，湿度75度）最后发酵60～70分钟。</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【烘烤】</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">9. 烤前装饰</span></p>
<p>沿着十字刀痕，挤上沙拉酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202961" style="width: 297px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202961-05b7f467faeae0e0f43ff3e84dabcecd.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202961-05b7f467faeae0e0f43ff3e84dabcecd.png" alt="" width="297" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202961" class="wp-caption-text">沿着十字刀痕，挤上沙拉酱。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">10. 烘烤</span></p>
<p>烤箱预热至上火200度，下火200度，烘烤14～16分钟后，在中心撒上海苔粉装饰即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13202962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13202962" style="width: 290px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202962-8be4c91b8282f45effceb93aa0270f6b.png"><img class=" wp-image-13202962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13202962-8be4c91b8282f45effceb93aa0270f6b.png" alt="" width="290" b="202" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13202962" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱烘烤14～16分钟，在中心撒上海苔粉装饰即完成。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>制作汤种面团</strong></span></h2>
<p>“汤种”对台湾人来说想必是一点都不陌生，街坊的面包店里应该都有贩卖“<ahref="https://github.com/iwaaux328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A4%E7%A7%8D%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">汤种面包</a>”，吃起来特别柔软美味，可说是风靡一时的热门品项。其实汤种就是用沸水煮过的面种，用汤种法做出来的面包，具有湿润、柔软的口感，并有着良好的保水性，即使放了一段时间，面包依然能保有水分，吃起来有弹性。</p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;">Tips：制作好的汤种面团可冷冻保存约一年，使用前再退冰软化。</span></p>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">材料</span></h3>
<p>高筋面粉50g<br />
细糖 5g<br />
煮沸水 50g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;">作法</span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">1. 混合材料</span></p>
<p>将高筋面粉与糖分别测量好并混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13198026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198026" style="width: 295px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198026-ff523afcc96ff613877988ad0164a1a2.png"><img class=" wp-image-13198026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198026-ff523afcc96ff613877988ad0164a1a2.png" alt="" width="295" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198026" class="wp-caption-text">将高筋面粉与糖分别测量好并混合。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff;">2. 沸水煮拌</span></p>
<p>将水煮沸后，关火，放入面粉与糖，轻轻快速搅拌至成团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13198029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13198029" style="width: 305px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198029-c1a78526c21def456d54c6c2c78ab798.png"><img class=" wp-image-13198029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13198029-c1a78526c21def456d54c6c2c78ab798.png" alt="" width="305" b="201" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13198029" class="wp-caption-text">将水煮沸后，关火，放入面粉与糖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
