<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东奥8.4 女子滑板碗池赛 日本队包揽冠亚军</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138361-content_GettyImages-1332252518-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（从左至右）：12岁的日本银牌得主开心那（ Kokona Hiraki）、19岁的日本金牌得主四十住樱（Sakura Yosozumi）和13岁英国铜牌得主Sky Brown。(Ezra Shaw/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年08月04日讯】（大纪元记者黄家熙报导）本届<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%A5%A5.md#1">东奥</a>首次举办奥运<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%91%E6%9D%BF.md#1">滑板</a>项目，<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/z%E4%B8%96%E4%BB%A3.md#1">Z世代</a>称霸，今天（8月4日）约中午时分经一轮激烈争夺后，由19岁、出生于磐田市的日本选手四十住樱（Sakura Yosozumi）夺得奥运会首面<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A2%97%E6%B1%A0%E8%B5%9B.md#1">碗池赛</a>金牌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13138367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13138367" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138367-content_GettyImages-1332255542-02.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13138367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138367-content_GettyImages-1332255542-02-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13138367" class="wp-caption-text">本届<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E5%A5%A5.md#1">东奥</a>首次举办奥运<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%91%E6%9D%BF.md#1">滑板</a>项目，今天（4日）经一轮激烈争夺后，由日本选手四十住樱夺得<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A2%97%E6%B1%A0%E8%B5%9B.md#1">碗池赛</a>金牌。(Ezra Shaw/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>与<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%BC%8F%E8%B5%9B.md#1">街式赛</a>不同的是，碗池赛的场地像只碗一般，内里布满不同曲线及弧面，各选手要在这样复杂的碗池形场地中，做出各种腾空、旋转与跳跃姿势。</p>
<p>于首轮（共三轮）比赛中，四十住樱及12岁的日本银牌得主开心那（Kokona Hiraki）等滑手即献上540度转体、垂直方向旋转360度等女子赛罕见的高难度动作，而四十住樱于首轮即获60.09全场最高分数，奠定金牌地位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13138373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13138373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138373-content_GettyImages-13322549480-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13138373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138373-content_GettyImages-13322549480-03-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13138373" class="wp-caption-text">12岁的日本银牌得主开心那（Kokona Hiraki）。(Ezra Shaw/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三、四位之争异常激烈，13岁英国选手Sky Brown在首轮空中旅转后落地不稳而摔，次轮再现同样失误，直至第三轮才以绝伦姿态完成动作，全场鼓掌叫好。世界排名第一的冈本碧优（Misugu Okamoto）在首、次轮皆犯严重失误，到了尾轮当一切顺利向前、表现近乎完美之际，突然在最后一个动作失误，与奖牌擦身而过。</p>
<p>较早前，13岁的西矢椛和22岁的堀米雄斗分别赢得女子、男子街板冠军，今天<ahref="https://github.com/ffngue315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/z%E4%B8%96%E4%BB%A3.md#1">Z世代</a>再次酷炫东奥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13138378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13138378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138378-content_GettyImages-1234441461-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13138378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138378-content_GettyImages-1234441461-04-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13138378" class="wp-caption-text">13岁英国选手Sky Brown（左）和19岁的日本选手四十住樱（Sakura Yosozumi）。(LOIC VENANCE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13138379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13138379" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138379-content_GettyImages-1234441397-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13138379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13138379-content_GettyImages-1234441397-05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13138379" class="wp-caption-text">本届东奥首次举办奥运滑板项目，今天（4日）经一轮激烈争夺后，由日本选手四十住樱夺得碗池赛金牌。(LOIC VENANCE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
