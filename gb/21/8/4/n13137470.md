<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七月的加东四省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（3）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137548-20210804_canada_helen_chou_310-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>洛基港附近的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/21/7/27/n13118704.md#1">七月的加东4省美出天际 潇洒自驾走一圈（2）</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_13137473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href=><a href="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/21/7/27/n13118704.md#1"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137473-20210804_canada_helen_chou_302-600x275.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137473" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/21/7/27/n13118704.md#1">晨雾缭绕的格罗斯莫恩公园。（图片/周海伦提供）</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>一清早，晨雾缭绕的公园里几乎没什么过往车辆，笔直的马路似乎是要通往天尽头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137474" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137474-20210804_canada_helen_chou_303.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13137474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137474-20210804_canada_helen_chou_303-600x392.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="602" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137474" class="wp-caption-text">晨雾缭绕的格罗斯莫恩公园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13137475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137475-20210804_canada_helen_chou_304.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137475-20210804_canada_helen_chou_304-600x450.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137475" class="wp-caption-text">晨雾缭绕的格罗斯莫恩公园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两个人停停开开拍拍，慢慢享受宁静的大自然。今天要去两个景点看看。一个是<ahref="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%8C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">桌地</a>（Tableland），另一个是绿花园（Green Garden），都在公园内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137478" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137478-20210804_canada_helen_chou_305.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137478-20210804_canada_helen_chou_305-600x363.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137478" class="wp-caption-text">通往<ahref="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%8C%E5%9C%B0.md#1">桌地</a>的公路。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>五亿年前，两个大陆板块相撞，把<ahref="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%B9%94.md#1">地幔</a>从地壳下挤到地面上，形成了阿巴拉契亚山脉(Appalachian Mountains)及被称为Pangea的超大陆，也叫“盘古大陆”。经过四亿多年的风化，高山变平地，又把地幔显露出来了，极其独特少见。桌地就是这么几个极其光秃秃的橘黄色荒山，与周边的青山绿水形成了强烈的反差。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137535-20210804_canada_helen_chou_306.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13137535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137535-20210804_canada_helen_chou_306-450x600.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137535" class="wp-caption-text">五亿年前的<ahref="https://github.com/mczpij310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%B9%94.md#1">地幔</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本来藏在地球深层的地幔，被挤到地表，又被四亿多年的风风雨雨冲刷走了压在头上的大山，露出了真面目。我忍不住去摸摸石头表面的纹路，无法想像五亿年就这么从指尖流过去了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137537-20210804_canada_helen_chou_307.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13137537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137537-20210804_canada_helen_chou_307-450x600.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137537" class="wp-caption-text">桌地的地貌。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再荒凉的不毛之地，时间久了，也会有生命迹象。你看，石头缝里不也长出了野草，开出了野花？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137538-20210804_canada_helen_chou_308.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13137538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137538-20210804_canada_helen_chou_308-450x600.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137538" class="wp-caption-text">圣劳伦斯海湾的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桌地附近有一条通往绿花园(Green Garden)的步道弯弯曲曲、上上下下的步道带着你离开荒凉的桌地，一直下到面向北大西洋圣劳伦斯海湾(Gulf of St. Lawrence)的海边。这里是火山喷发后形成的，土地肥沃，绿草如茵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137539-20210804_canada_helen_chou_309.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137539-20210804_canada_helen_chou_309-600x450.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137539" class="wp-caption-text">大西洋边的绿花园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>步道来回有八英里左右，海拔270米。我走了近一万九千步。爬到那个小山头上好像能够摸到蓝天白云。但是说实话呢，这里的风景与昨天走几步路就能看到的风景比差多了。权当是为了明天的高强度爬山做预热吧。</p>
<p>从绿花园回来在洛基港找了家路边餐厅吃午饭。没想到他们家居然有油炸鳕鱼脸肉！吃客们都知道，鱼身上哪块肉最好吃？鱼脸肉！可惜他们除了油炸就不知道还有别的更美味的做法。给我口炒锅，一把葱姜，我整一盘爆炒鱼脸肉让你吃得放不下！唉！没吃爽，连照片都忘了拍。</p>
<p>但附近的风景还是极美的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137548-20210804_canada_helen_chou_310.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137548-20210804_canada_helen_chou_310-600x450.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137548" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港附近的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13137551" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137551" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137551-20210804_canada_helen_chou_311.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137551-20210804_canada_helen_chou_311-600x386.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137551" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港附近的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13137552" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137552" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137552-20210804_canada_helen_chou_312.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137552" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137552-20210804_canada_helen_chou_312-600x338.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137552" class="wp-caption-text">洛基港附近的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13137554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137554-20210804_canada_helen_chou_313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137554-20210804_canada_helen_chou_313-600x381.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="381" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137554" class="wp-caption-text">梦幻般的洛基港黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄昏来临了，整个小镇蒙上了一层梦幻般的橙色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137556-20210804_canada_helen_chou_314.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137556-20210804_canada_helen_chou_314-600x273.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137556" class="wp-caption-text">梦幻般的洛基港黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13137558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137558-20210804_canada_helen_chou_315.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13137558" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137558-20210804_canada_helen_chou_315-600x280.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="600" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137558" class="wp-caption-text">日落时分。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>回到诺里斯点小镇，正好赶上落日。没有火烧云，但也耐看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13137560" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13137560" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137560-20210804_canada_helen_chou_316.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13137560" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13137560-20210804_canada_helen_chou_316-450x624.jpg" alt="加东, 周海伦" width="450" b="624" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13137560" class="wp-caption-text">二万五千年的冰川水酿的啤酒。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
