<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本首家独一无二手抓海鲜餐馆开张</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052347-IMG_2239-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Kickin Inn是澳洲一家独一无二的手抓海鲜餐馆，供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠。（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月28日讯】<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn</a>是澳洲一家独一无二的手抓海鲜餐馆，已发展成拥有八家的连锁店。现在悉尼和墨尔本都能品尝到独特风味的(K)ajun海鲜，以及体验其独特的用餐方式。<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn</a>供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052353" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w1oDniQ3_Pg"><img class=" wp-image-13052353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052353-New64388_Kickin_Inn_Logo_BlackBG-.png" alt="" width="450" b="636" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052353" class="wp-caption-text">Kickin Inn是澳洲一家独一无二的手抓海鲜餐馆，供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ravi和Sami两位合伙人都是有远见的有抱负的企业家，梦想是在澳大利亚开设100家餐厅，建立与众不同的品牌海鲜餐厅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052348" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052348" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052348-IMG_2399.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052348" class="wp-caption-text">Kickin Inn供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠，让人感觉十分新鲜、吃得开心。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn</a>的“Kajun”风格配料是用55种香料合成、组成了5种口味的酱汁，适合于多种海鲜烹制，包括新鲜龙虾、青口、小章鱼、海湾蟹、帝王蟹和整条新鲜大虾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052349" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052349-IMG_2643.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052349" class="wp-caption-text">Kickin Inn供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠，让人感觉十分新鲜、吃得开心。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn Kajun Seafood</a>海鲜餐馆吃海鲜，服务生会将龙虾、螃蟹、大虾、青口、蛤蚧、八爪鱼等海鲜什锦连同酱汁一同放在铺有整张大白纸的餐桌上，没有盘子，不用刀叉，顾客穿上围兜，戴上手套或直接用手，就这么开始开心豪放吃海鲜。这种独特的进餐方式，不仅让人感觉十分新鲜、吃得开心，而且感觉吃得心满意足又尽兴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052346" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052346-IMG_0734-2.jpg" alt="" width="499" b="477" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052346" class="wp-caption-text">Kickin Inn供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠，让人感觉十分新鲜、吃得开心。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13052351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052351" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052351-IMG_2763.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="1004" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052351" class="wp-caption-text">Kickin Inn是澳洲一家独一无二的手抓海鲜餐馆，供应优质的海鲜新鲜且实惠。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>到<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn Kajun Seafood</a>海鲜餐馆进餐通常有四个步骤：</h3>
<p>第一步：选择你最喜欢的海鲜。可选择的海鲜有带壳整虾、去壳虾、海蜇、虾尾、花蟹、泥蟹、龙虾，蛤蚧、小龙虾、盲曹、巴尔曼龙虾、八爪鱼、青口和阿拉斯加帝王蟹等。半只龙虾仅$28.95；</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052341" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052341" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052341-01.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="187" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052341" class="wp-caption-text">第一步：选择你最喜欢的海鲜。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二步：选择你喜欢的酱汁。五种酱汁有：Cajun、大蒜Cajun、大蒜黄油、柠檬胡椒和沙邦（Sha-Bang）及前四种酱的总混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052342" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052342-02.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="120" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052342" class="wp-caption-text">第二步：选择你喜欢的酱汁。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>什么是Cajun？Cajun是以美国中南部的路易斯安那州（Louisiana）新奥尔良市为代表的一种独特口味。而Cajun食品大多以洋葱、辣椒和芹菜组成的，加上大量的香料，又可以达到疯狂辣的程度，Cajun也就成为一种特有的风味。</p>
<p>第三步骤：选择烹调和辣味的程度。<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn KajunSeafood</a>餐馆的口味有六种选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052343" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052343-03.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="101" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052343" class="wp-caption-text">第三步骤：选择烹调和辣味的程度。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>* 普通的口味“Orignial”：适合品尝到海鲜的浓郁本色，如螃蟹、巴尔曼龙虾等；<br />
* 温和口味“Mild”：能感觉到一点点辣味；<br />
* 辣味“Spicey”：能承受住的辣度，但还没有到强辣的辣度；<br />
* “Inferno”：辣味的程度达到像要歌唱的程度，辣得口舌不停抖动；<br />
* 第五种口味：辣到如同“汽笛长鸣”，大汗淋漓；<br />
* 最最辣的是第六种——“Seriously Hot”：超级辣口、辣心，能承受的了的，想必只有超强忍耐力的极少数人能享用。</p>
<p>第四步骤：选择主食配食。有法式面包、牛肉香肠、土豆、玉米、意面和米饭，以调剂口味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052344" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052344" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052344-04.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="175" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052344" class="wp-caption-text">第四步骤：选择主食配食。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在墨尔本港最近开业的<ahref="https://www.kickininn.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kickin Inn</a>，餐厅占据了海湾建筑的两层楼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13052354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052354" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052354-Opening-specials.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="640" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052354" class="wp-caption-text">Port Melbourne——Kickin Inn Seafood新开张有特价。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13052355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13052355" style="width: 349px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.kickininn.com.au"><img class=" wp-image-13052355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13052355-PortMelbourne.jpeg" alt="" width="349" b="1379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13052355" class="wp-caption-text">在墨尔本港最近开业的Kickin Inn餐厅占据了海湾建筑的两层楼。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
