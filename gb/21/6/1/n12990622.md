<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【组图】呆萌的雁宝宝～嬉戏照曝光超疗愈</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990642-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_07-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>列队行进的雁宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>那天，我正在湖边芦苇丛中拍天鹅，突然听到一阵阵急促的“嘶嘶”声，就像有谁在压低了嗓音嘶吼一样。我一转身，只看到芦苇丛中有一只正在孵育宝宝的雁妈怒目相向，喉咙里发出一声声的警告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990627" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990627" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990627-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990627-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_02-600x407.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990627" class="wp-caption-text">孵育宝宝的雁妈。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>啊呀，我一不小心踩到雷区了。雁爸雁妈孵育宝宝时对一切外来生物都极其戒备，生怕威胁到它们与宝宝。雁妈无法起身，只能冲我嘶吼。还好雁爸不在，否则它就会更着脖子冲上来咬我的。赶紧撤！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990630" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990630-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990630-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_03-600x414.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990630" class="wp-caption-text">孵育宝宝的雁妈。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看着我蹑手蹑脚的离去，雁妈还不放心，抬起头继续盯着，直到我退到安全地带才罢休。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990631" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990631-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990631-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_04-600x399.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990631" class="wp-caption-text">带着孩儿们出游的雁妈。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过了一段时间回到芦苇荡，雁妈雁爸带着已经能蹒跚学步的<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%81%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">雁宝宝</a>们四处觅食戏耍了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990632" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990632-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12990632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990632-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_05-450x609.jpg" alt="雁子" width="450" b="609" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990632" class="wp-caption-text">呆萌的<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%81%E5%AE%9D%E5%AE%9D.md#1">雁宝宝</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>给俺来个特写吧！俺萌不萌？呆不呆？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990635-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12990635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990635-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_06-450x585.jpg" alt="雁子" width="450" b="585" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990635" class="wp-caption-text">呆萌的雁宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>毛茸茸的雁宝宝比它们的爹妈可爱多了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990642-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_07.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12990642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990642-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_07-450x317.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990642" class="wp-caption-text">列队行进的雁宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不知是谁在喊口令，四只雁宝宝迈着整齐的步伐，“一、二、一！”向水边走去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990646-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990646-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_08-600x396.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990646" class="wp-caption-text">喝水嬉戏的雁宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>短脖子的雁宝宝也想“曲项向天歌”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990648" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990648-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_09.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12990648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990648-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_09-600x829.jpg" alt="雁子" width="453" b="627" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990648" class="wp-caption-text">我只是想喝口水而已。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12990649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990649-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12990649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990649-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_10-450x580.jpg" alt="雁子" width="450" b="580" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990649" class="wp-caption-text">哥俩好。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12990651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990651-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_11.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12990651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990651-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_11-450x312.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990651" class="wp-caption-text">排队回家。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水也喝了，玩也玩了，雁爸雁妈一声令下，雁宝宝们排着整齐的队伍游泳回家了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12990654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990654" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990654-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990654-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_12-600x374.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990654" class="wp-caption-text">秋水长天中的大雁。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12990657" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12990657" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990657-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12990657" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12990657-20210601_swallow_helen_chou_13-600x401.jpg" alt="雁子" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12990657" class="wp-caption-text">秋水长天中的大雁。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>别看雁宝宝们现在只能摇摇摆摆的在陆地上走，在浅水里游，秋天的时候它们肯定能像雁爸雁妈一样在天空中自由飞翔了！</p>
