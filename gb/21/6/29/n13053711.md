<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>WFH视讯会议妆容 打造有神大眼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053714-501112-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>今年主打自然空气感眉妆。（dejavu提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）近期许多OL居家上班兼防疫，碰到<ahref="https://github.com/ixmwwk3204/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A7%86%E8%AE%AF%E4%BC%9A%E8%AE%AE.md#1">视讯会议</a>时，要如何打造出明亮有神的妆感呢？日本<ahref="https://github.com/ixmwwk3204/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E5%A6%86.md#1">眼妆</a>品牌KISSME睽违5年推出抗晕眼线液笔，介绍3种眼妆，让目光温柔有神，连线2021东京关键字“展现自身独特魅力”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13053713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053713-501111.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053713-501111-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053713" class="wp-caption-text">逆刷加上顺刷，刷出根根分明的毛流线条。（dejavu提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近一年疫情影响下，口罩<ahref="https://github.com/ixmwwk3204/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A6%86%E5%AE%B9.md#1">妆容</a>强调持久、抗晕的<ahref="https://github.com/ixmwwk3204/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E5%A6%86.md#1">眼妆</a>。品牌观察现代女性渴望展现不同性格的魅力目光，可藉由“浓郁深黑”展现工作女强人的俐落犀利眼神；“知性深棕”为眼神增添自然优雅气质，为五官自然增添深邃感；“温柔浅棕”自然融入眼尾，若隐若现中透露日系女孩的无辜眼神。</p>
<p>现代女生注重精致美丽，对眼妆要求不再只是从无到有，更追求从完整<ahref="https://github.com/ixmwwk3204/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A6%86%E5%AE%B9.md#1">妆容</a>到“完美妆容”。眼尾毫厘间的差距决定了眼神天壤地别的差异，真正的行家更懂得雕琢眼妆，借此强化自身五官优势，善用好的眼线笔可以让美丽一次到位。</p>
<p>疫情之下长期佩戴口罩影响妆容需求，当五官只剩双眸时，若担心使用深黑色眼线显得锐利，也可选择咖啡色系，能使目光更柔和亲切，也与东方人肤色相衬，刻画出理想的形象。</p>
<h2>女强人气势? 沉稳浓黑猫眼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13053722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053722-503204.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053722-503204-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053722" class="wp-caption-text">女强人气势 沉稳浓黑猫眼。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>－眼尾上勾是态度，浓黑的黑色眼线撑住你的高冷气势</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13053716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053716" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053716-503199.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053716-503199-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053716" class="wp-caption-text">女强人气势 沉稳浓黑猫眼。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>１使用低彩度、雾面眼影进行全眼窝打底。</p>
<p>２使用深一阶的大地色系加强眼尾。</p>
<p>３眼尾加粗、加宽、加上明显的上扬角度，强化眼神。</p>
<p>４接着将眼头及眼尾的眼线衔接起来，若是新手建议可分段填。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13053723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053723-503205.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053723" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053723-503205-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053723" class="wp-caption-text">女强人气势 沉稳浓黑猫眼。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>小技巧：</strong></p>
<p>眼尾上勾的长度视每人眼睛大小而定，适合高级感妆容上勾长度约为眼睛长度的1/5?; 酷炫魅惑妆容上勾长度约为眼睛长度的1/4~1/3?。</p>
<h2>气质满分? 深邃知性棕眼眸</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13053724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053724" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053724-503206.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053724-503206-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053724" class="wp-caption-text">气质满分 深邃知性棕眼眸。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>－彷若天生的立体轮廓，深棕眼线描绘细眼线，让气质从双眸蔓延</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13053720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053720-503202.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053720-503202-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053720" class="wp-caption-text">气质满分 深邃知性棕眼眸。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>１沿着睫毛根部，将睫毛缝隙填满，外眼线控制在约0.1公分宽度。</p>
<p>２若张开眼睛后还看得到睫毛下面有一条白色的线，则需要填补内眼线。</p>
<p>３填补内眼线前，使用棉花棒将内眼睑边缘水分、油脂拭干后，就可使用眼线液笔填补睫毛根部空白处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13053718" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053718-503200.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053718-503200-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053718" class="wp-caption-text">气质满分 深邃知性棕眼眸。（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>小技巧：</strong></p>
<p>内双或泡泡眼的女生若想透过眼线加强眼神，建议只需强调眼尾眼线；而前段眼线仅需细细填补睫毛根部，就能自然放大双眼。</p>
<h2>日系女孩? 无辜狗狗眼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_13053721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053721-503203.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053721-503203-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053721" class="wp-caption-text">日系女孩 无辜狗狗眼。　（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>－又圆又大的双眼无辜惹人怜，浅棕眼线画出你的姻缘线</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_13053719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053719-503201.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053719-503201-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053719" class="wp-caption-text">日系女孩 无辜狗狗眼。　（KISSME提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>１就眼型向下延伸、画出自然的下拉式眼线，避免眼线低于眼头显得无精打采。</p>
<p>２若是长眼女生想让眼神变可爱，可于眼线中段靠近眼球处稍微加粗，前后段眼线画较细，成圆弧的形状。使整体眼型看起来更像小狗，充满圆滚滚的无害感。</p>
<p>３除了眼线之外，眼尾1/3处可用浅棕色眼影将上、下外眼角连接晕染，使眼神更显朦胧无辜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13053715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13053715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053715-503198.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13053715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13053715-503198-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13053715" class="wp-caption-text">日系女孩 无辜狗狗眼。　（KISSME／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>小技巧：</strong></p>
<p>若要使眼睛有戴放大片的效果，可以在下眼线黑眼球处的下方，填补睫毛缝隙，瞬间眼睛放大也会变得更圆更无辜喔。</p>
<h2>眉妆柔和轻盈新质感</h2>
<p>此外，另一个日系美妆品牌dejavu观察，市面上眉笔、眉粉及眉彩膏，都能配合脸型或发色调整眉型及眉色，但距离“真正自然眉彩”还是有一段距离，因此推出新品主打自然空气感眉妆。</p>
<p>品牌从形状、颜色、质感三大要素来改善有别于以往的眉彩彩妆，使用极细3ｍｍ螺旋锥型刷头，贴合每一根眉毛刷出根根分明的毛流。并建议依照个人发色来选择适合自己的眉彩膏：灰棕、深棕、浅棕，是提升整体妆容精致度的最佳选择。</p>
<p>戴好口罩做好防疫之下，仍能轻松打造时下最受欢迎的野生眉，即使WFH也能让眉毛散发动人光彩。</p>
<p>１将眉毛梳顺。</p>
<p>２沾取适当的使用量，包覆每根毛发、覆盖原有眉色。</p>
<p>３逆刷加上顺刷，刷出根根分明的毛流线条。◇</p>
