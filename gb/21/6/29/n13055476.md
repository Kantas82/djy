<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉市经济弱势学童午餐券提高至70元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055478-504705-526x400.png" />
<h6>嘉义市针对弱势学童，暑期午餐不中断。（嘉义市政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月29日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎报导）为持续照顾经济弱势学生，嘉义市政府于今年7月5日开始，将原有<ahref="https://github.com/mjhpml342/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90%E5%88%B8.md#1">午餐券</a>金额由每餐50元调整至每餐70元，透过提高午餐券金额让学生的选择更加多元化。</p>
<figure id="13055479" aria-describedby="caption-13055479" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055479-504706-450x600.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055479-504706-450x600.jpeg" alt="50元餐劵样式_7-11新港坪门市提供。" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055479" class="wp-caption-text">50元餐劵样式_7-11新港坪门市提供。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学校午餐一直是嘉义市市长黄敏惠推动教育政策的重要目标之一，自民国100年开始，嘉义市已经是台湾第一个全面自设午餐厨房、自办学童午餐的城市。</p>
<figure id="13055480" aria-describedby="caption-13055480" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055480-504707-450x600.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055480-504707-450x600.jpeg" alt="70元餐劵样式_7-11新港坪门市提供。" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055480" class="wp-caption-text">70元餐劵样式_7-11新港坪门市提供。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠表示，暑假即将来临，如何让弱势家庭的孩子在这期间也能均衡营养用餐是当务之急，她责成教育处评估因应物价状况，提高暑假的午餐金额，虽然会增加约新台币230万元的支出，但为照顾经济<ahref="https://github.com/mjhpml342/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B1%E5%8A%BF%E5%AD%A6%E7%AB%A5.md#1">弱势学童</a>，让孩子能安心上学，都是值得的。目前预估约有3056名学童受惠，市府透过资源的挹注，让嘉义市的学童们都能幸福用餐，营造一个无后顾之忧、快乐学习的环境。</p>
<figure id="13055481" aria-describedby="caption-13055481" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055481-504708-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055481-504708-450x337.jpeg" alt="50元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。" width="500" b="374" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055481" class="wp-caption-text">50元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教育处处长林立生表示，凡就读嘉义市国民中小学，领有低收入户、中低收入户证明者以及家庭突发因素无力支付午餐费、经导师家庭访视认定家庭经济困难者，都可向学校提出申请。除此之外，学生未来在暑假期间，无论是否到校，都有午餐补助，只要未到校学生持<ahref="https://github.com/mjhpml342/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90%E5%88%B8.md#1">午餐券</a>至学校附近合作商家即可兑换餐食，让孩子在暑假期间午餐不中断。</p>
<figure id="13055483" aria-describedby="caption-13055483" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055483-504710-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055483-504710-450x337.jpeg" alt="70元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055483" class="wp-caption-text">70元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13055482" aria-describedby="caption-13055482" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055482-504709-450x337.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055482-504709-450x337.jpeg" alt="70元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055482" class="wp-caption-text">70元餐劵样式(1)_全家兴安门市提供。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13055484" aria-describedby="caption-13055484" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055484-504711-450x311.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13055484-504711-450x311.jpeg" alt="午餐券。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13055484" class="wp-caption-text">午餐券。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
