<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>减肥好食兼顾营养 焗烤鸡柳食谱大公开</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034276-16c6670ef88199fff8ab892e35d291a7-600x400.png" />
<h6>焗烤番茄鸡柳。（积木文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：在家也能做出好吃即时的<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E9%A4%90.md#1">西餐</a>，只要30分钟就完成的“<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%97%E7%83%A4.md#1">焗烤</a>番茄<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E6%9F%B3.md#1">鸡柳</a>”，不需要开火，就放心交给烤箱工作就行了！在等待食物煮熟的时间，也可以同步准备其他的菜肴，真的非常省时省力。</strong></p>
<p><strong>当<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%84%97%E7%83%A4.md#1">焗烤</a>出炉时，香喷喷的气味就让人食指大动，加上金黄色焦酥的表层、冒烟牵丝的起司、柔顺滑口的酸甜内馅，怎么不会是孩子和大人们的最爱呢？而且，不用怀疑，一边吃还在一边施行<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a>计划，因为这里推荐的就是一道低脂高蛋白的食谱，营养和美味同时兼顾。</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">焗烤番茄<ahref="https://github.com/fgzxus3327/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E6%9F%B3.md#1">鸡柳</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料 2人份</span></h3>
<p>鸡柳．4 条<br />
盐．1 小匙<br />
洋葱．1/2 颗<br />
番茄．1/2 颗<br />
面包粉．1 大匙<br />
盐．1 小匙<br />
黑胡椒粉．1 小匙<br />
橄榄油．1 大匙<br />
起司丝．2 大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 ?鸡柳表面撒盐（分量外），再抹一层橄榄油（分量外）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013279-333.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013279-333-450x296.png" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013279" class="wp-caption-text">鸡柳表面撒盐。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 番茄和洋葱切丁，放入烤盘，加入橄榄油、面包粉、 盐和黑胡椒粉拌匀铺平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013280" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013280-444.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013280-444-450x290.png" alt="" width="450" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013280" class="wp-caption-text">番茄和洋葱切丁，放入烤盘，加入橄榄油、面包粉、 盐和黑胡椒粉拌匀铺平。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13013282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013282-555.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013282" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013282-555-450x292.png" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013282" class="wp-caption-text">材料拌匀铺平。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 将作法①的鸡柳均匀平铺在作法②的烤盘中间，上面撒一层起司丝，盖上锡箔纸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013283" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013283-666.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013283-666-450x293.png" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013283" class="wp-caption-text">将作法①的鸡柳均匀平铺在作法②的烤盘中间，上面撒一层起司丝。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13013284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013284-777.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013284-777-450x293.png" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013284" class="wp-caption-text">盖上锡箔纸。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 放入已预热230度的烤箱，烤10分钟。<br />
5 将锡箔纸取出，继续烤5～7分钟即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013285-888.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013285" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013285-888-450x283.png" alt="" width="450" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013285" class="wp-caption-text">锡箔纸取出，继续烤5～7分钟即完成。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13013036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013036" style="width: 106px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013036-1a902f792c7aa08f47892b497c0ec291.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13013036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013036-1a902f792c7aa08f47892b497c0ec291-450x640.jpg" alt="" width="106" b="151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013036" class="wp-caption-text">《宜料理?鸡胸肉》书封。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
