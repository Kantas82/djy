<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>3道鸡绞肉料理 沙拉炒饭凉拌菜一次学</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013089-8-600x400.png" />
<h6>鸡肉樱花虾炒饭。（积木文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：“今天要吃什么？”孩子和家人可能会问一下今天的菜色，但是身为掌厨的人有时候真的煮到山穷水尽，连自己也不知道要煮什么才好，有时也会担心煎蛋、炒菜，家人没有食欲。尤其是肉类，一直电锅炖肉、炒肉片的，都吃腻味了。在此为您推荐三道“<ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%BB%9E%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡绞肉</a>”变化料理，可以一次买入大量鸡绞肉，分次煮出不同菜肴，值得学起来！</strong></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鸡肉<ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a><ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">沙拉</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13013034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013034-63798c2d7ba00dcd3cfbc9adae6b45b9.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013034-63798c2d7ba00dcd3cfbc9adae6b45b9-450x576.png" alt="" width="450" b="576" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013034" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉<ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E7%93%9C.md#1">地瓜</a><ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">沙拉</a>。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（2人份）</span></h3>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E7%BB%9E%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡绞肉</a>．100 公克<br />
水煮蛋．1 颗<br />
栉瓜．50 公克<br />
地瓜．150 公克<br />
蒜末．1 小匙<br />
盐．2 小匙<br />
糖．1/2 小匙<br />
奶油．20 公克</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 地瓜洗净后削皮，切成块状，放入电锅蒸熟。<br />
2 栉瓜切薄片，平底锅加热，加油，将栉瓜放入煎到微焦卷曲，取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013030-2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013030-2-450x288.png" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013030" class="wp-caption-text">将栉瓜放入煎到微焦卷曲，取出备用。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">3 鸡绞肉放入作法②的锅中，用锅铲将肉压散分开，加入蒜末和盐拌炒，炒熟即可取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013031-3.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013031-3-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013031" class="wp-caption-text">鸡绞肉放入作法②的锅中，用锅铲将肉压散分开，加入蒜末和盐拌炒，炒熟即可取出备用。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">4 水煮蛋切碎。<br />
5 将作法①蒸好的地瓜取出，趁热加入盐、糖和奶油， 压成泥状，加入作法②的栉瓜、作法③的鸡肉末、作法④的水煮蛋，拌匀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013032-4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013032-4-450x297.png" alt="" width="450" b="297" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013032" class="wp-caption-text">将作法①蒸好的地瓜取出，趁热加入盐、糖和奶油， 压成泥状。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13013033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013033-5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013033-5-450x293.png" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013033" class="wp-caption-text">加入作法②的栉瓜、作法③的鸡肉末、作法④的水煮蛋，拌匀即完成。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">Point</span></h3>
<p>地瓜蒸熟后请注意是否有很多水分，水分太多要先沥除。 地瓜种类多，用任何品种都可以，台湾近几年种的台农57 号黄地瓜非常香甜好吃，推荐大家试试。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13013029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013029-1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013029-1-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013029" class="wp-caption-text">地瓜蒸熟后请注意是否有很多水分，水分太多要先沥除。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E6%8B%8C.md#1">凉拌</a>味噌鸡肉末</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13013080" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013080" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013080-7.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013080-7-450x420.png" alt="" width="450" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013080" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E6%8B%8C.md#1">凉拌</a>味噌鸡肉末。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料（ 2人份）</span></h3>
<p>鸡绞肉．100 公克<br />
姜末．1 小匙</p>
<p>A<br />
味噌．1/2 大匙<br />
米酒．1/2 大匙<br />
二砂糖．1 小匙<br />
白胡椒粉．1 小匙</p>
<p>番茄．1 颗<br />
洋葱．1 颗<br />
九层塔．1 把<br />
橄榄油．1 大匙<br />
黑胡椒粉．1 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 锅子加热，加油，放入鸡绞肉，用锅铲将肉压平压散，不要让鸡绞肉黏在一起，放入姜末和［A］拌炒， 将肉炒熟后取出备用。<br />
2 番茄和洋葱洗净后切小丁，九层塔洗净后擦干切碎。<br />
3 作法②放入盘中，加入橄榄油和黑胡椒粉拌匀。<br />
4 将作法①的肉末放在作法③上，稍微拌一下即完成。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">鸡肉樱花虾<ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13013089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13013089" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013089-8.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13013089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13013089-8-450x313.png" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13013089" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉樱花虾<ahref="https://github.com/rraias314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>。（积木文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料 （2人份）</span></h3>
<p>鸡绞肉．80 公克<br />
蒜末．5 公克<br />
白饭．2 碗<br />
蛋．2 颗<br />
樱花虾．10 公克<br />
葱末．1 大匙<br />
盐．2 小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 锅子加热，加油，蛋打匀后放入，炒到七分熟后取出。<br />
2 鸡绞肉放入作法①的锅中，再加点油，用锅铲将肉压平压散，让肉不会黏在一起，放入蒜末和盐将肉炒熟。<br />
3 白饭放入作法②的锅中，和肉末炒匀，加入作法①的蛋和樱花虾拌炒均匀，起锅前撒入葱末炒匀即完 成。</p>
