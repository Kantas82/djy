<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【视频】国旗日大游行 昆士市盛况空前</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020295-DSC2727-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>麻州昆士市拥有全美最早的国旗日游行之一。（黄剑宇/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-838383cf-69d5-4e77-8e8b-a28df0b0d88d" data-id="player-838383cf-69d5-4e77-8e8b-a28df0b0d88d"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/838383cf-69d5-4e77-8e8b-a28df0b0d88d?r=16x9&amp;d=110&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F21%2F6%2F14%2Fn13020325.md#1"></a>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月14日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨、黄剑宇<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%A3%AB%E5%B8%82.md#1">昆士市</a>报导）6月14日是美国<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%97%97%E6%97%A5.md#1">国旗日</a>（Flag Day）。在2位美国总统的故乡<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%A3%AB%E5%B8%82.md#1">昆士市</a>（Quincy），人们挥别了疫情的苦闷，于6月12日举办了上万人的盛大<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>，来<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D.md#1">庆祝</a>“第70届国旗日和烟花汇演”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13020297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020297-DSC2731.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020297-DSC2731-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020297" class="wp-caption-text">帅气的警局骑士们为<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>队伍开路。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020305-DSC2810.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020305-DSC2810-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020305" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍中的国旗队。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>周六傍晚7点，盛大的队伍伴随着鼓乐从昆士市政府出发，穿过汉考克大道。游行队伍由警车带领，后面跟随着政府官员、退伍军人、童子军、各式乐队、昆士亚协（QARI）等几十个方阵。整个队伍花了近40分钟才全部从市政府广场出发。</p>
<p>“昆士市恢复了，几乎恢复正常了。”带着孩子观看游行的墨菲（Steve Murphy）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13020303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020303" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020303-DSC2799.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020303-DSC2799-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020303" class="wp-caption-text">游行队伍中的乐团。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020302-DSC2769.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020302-DSC2769-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020302" class="wp-caption-text">众多孩子们参加游行庆典。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天参与游行的政要包括麻州联邦众议员林奇（Stephen Lynch）、麻州众议员陈德基（Tackey Chan）、昆士市长寇克（Thomas Koch）、市议会议长梁秀婷（Nina Liang）等等。在游行庆典之后，晚上9点，昆士的海边还燃放了烟花。</p>
<p>“这是昆士市一年里最盛大的活动之一。对整个社区来说，能共同出来<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D.md#1">庆祝</a>，是非常重要的。今天真是完美，天气非常好。可以看到很多人已经出来了，我们都很兴奋。”梁秀婷说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13020298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020298-DSC2736.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020298-DSC2736-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020298" class="wp-caption-text">昆士市长Thomas Koch（左）、国会议员Stephen Lynch（中）以及昆士市议会议长梁秀婷（Nina Liang）等官员参与游行。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020380-DSC02223.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020380-DSC02223-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020380" class="wp-caption-text">昆士市议会议长梁秀婷和当地民选官员的方阵。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020308-DSC2842.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020308-DSC2842-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020308" class="wp-caption-text">军队卡车参与游行。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昆士市的<ahref="https://github.com/pputke380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%97%97%E6%97%A5.md#1">国旗日</a>游行由现任市长的父亲Richard Koch创办，从1952年开始举办，也是全美最早的国旗日游行之一。这是当地一年中最盛大的活动之一。即使在去年疫情严重的时期，当地也举办了汽车游行，来庆祝这个节日。</p>
<p>每年担任游行领队的罗斯维尔（Sam Rounseville）说，这是他扮演“Uncle Sam”参加游行的第30年。“这次将会是史上最盛大的国旗日游行，棒极了！”他感慨道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13020310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020310-DSC02213.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020310-DSC02213-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020310" class="wp-caption-text">Sam Rounseville（左三）每年在国旗日游行中扮演“Uncle Sam”，至今已有30年了。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020306-DSC2824.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020306-DSC2824-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020306" class="wp-caption-text">游行参与者驾驶着外型古典的汽车。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市长寇克说，“国旗日”是让整个城市团结一致的活动。“这提醒大家，我们都在一面国旗之下，这是个伟大的国家，我们要庆祝这件事。”他说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13020309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020309-DSC02234.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020309-DSC02234-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020309" class="wp-caption-text">高中生们的游行队伍出发。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13020307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13020307" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020307-DSC2826.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13020307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13020307-DSC2826-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13020307" class="wp-caption-text">穿着美国早期服饰的礼枪队。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过了疫情肆虐的一年，今年的国旗日游行比过去几年都更加的盛大。这对参与者们而言也有着特别的意义。“由于疫情，今年的活动是尤其重要的。人们需要开心一下，需要社交活动，这是个非常好的活动。”领队车成员莱恩（Harry Ryan）说。◇</p>
