<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>MLB柯尔好投被中继砸锅 皇家逆转洋基</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13042327-503854-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽约洋基王牌柯尔。（Elsa/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月23日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）纽约<ahref="https://github.com/phvhkd3594/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E5%9F%BA.md#1">洋基</a>王牌<ahref="https://github.com/phvhkd3594/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E5%B0%94.md#1">柯尔</a>（Gerrit Cole）23日对上堪萨斯皇家，缴出7局失2分优质先发，眼看本季第9胜即将落袋，无奈<ahref="https://github.com/phvhkd3594/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E5%9F%BA.md#1">洋基</a>中继王罗艾西加（Jonathan Loaisiga）失常，0.2局狂丢4分，搞砸柯尔胜投，最后洋基5：6遭皇家逆转获胜。</p>
<figure id="13042329" aria-describedby="caption-13042329" style="width: 340px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13042329-503856-450x590.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13042329-503856-450x590.jpg" alt="纽约洋基王牌柯尔。" width="340" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13042329" class="wp-caption-text">纽约洋基王牌<ahref="https://github.com/phvhkd3594/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E5%B0%94.md#1">柯尔</a>。（Elsa/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="13042328" aria-describedby="caption-13042328" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13042328-503855-450x316.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13042328-503855-450x316.jpg" alt="纽约洋基王牌柯尔。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-13042328" class="wp-caption-text">纽约洋基王牌柯尔。（Elsa/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>柯尔主投7局，四缝线直球、曲球、滑球交互搭配，只被敲出3支安打，送出6张老K，失掉2分，以胜利候选人之姿退场。八局上罗艾西加登板，没想到他拿手的滑球失灵，接连被击出安打失掉4分，被皇家以6：3反超。</p>
<p>洋基八局下用全垒打追回2分，九局下打线遭皇家终结者霍兰德（Greg Holland）封锁，无奈吞败，柯尔即使缴出好投，也只能与本季第9胜擦肩而过。◇</p>
