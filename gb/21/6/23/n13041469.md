<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>异国美食轻松做 “安茹白乳酪蛋糕”超好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041499-20200820-_Z1A0142-500x400.jpg" />
<h6>安茹白乳酪蛋糕（crEmet d'Anjou）。（台湾东贩提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>小编语：远赴至法国蓝带学习糕点的高石纪子，运用法式烘培的温暖手感，制作出温润丝滑、入口即化的美味“安茹白<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B3%E9%85%AA%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">乳酪蛋糕</a>”</strong></span><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>。现在，在家防疫的你也可以做<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%82%E5%9B%BD%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">异国美食</a>，而且非常简单，只要大略地搅拌食材就会完成！别忘了，要享用前淋上自己喜爱的果酱，酸酸甜甜融合著松软<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>，一口接一口，很难停下来！</strong></span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">安茹白<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B3%E9%85%AA%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">乳酪蛋糕</a>（crEmet d&#8217;Anjou）</span></h2>
<p>当做餐后甜点等刚刚好，法国安茹地区制作的糕点。<br />
因为只需要大略搅拌即可，作法非常简单。<br />
请淋上自己喜爱的水果酱汁再享用吧！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料和前置作业</span></h3>
<p><strong>容量160?的烤盅</strong> 3个份</p>
<p><strong>原味优格（无糖）??</strong>400g</p>
<p>→将网筛叠放在钵盆中，里面铺上厨房纸巾，倒入优格之后覆盖保鲜膜，在冷藏室放置一个晚上，沥除水分。变成200g 就OK了。如果不足的话，将沥除的水分加进去，调整重量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13041474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041474-20200727-_Z1A6472.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041474-20200727-_Z1A6472-450x673.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="673" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041474" class="wp-caption-text">网筛叠放在钵盆中，里面铺上厨房纸巾，倒入优格之后覆盖保鲜膜，在冷藏室放置一个晚上，沥除水分。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13041475" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041475-20200728-_Z1A6569.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041475-20200728-_Z1A6569-450x673.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="673" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041475" class="wp-caption-text">沥除水分的原味优格从400g约减至200g。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>鲜奶油霜</strong></p>
<p>鲜奶油（乳脂肪含量45％）? 100g<br />
细砂糖? 15g</p>
<p><strong>个人喜好的水果酱汁? 适量</strong></p>
<p>＊将干净的纱布裁剪成大约25 ㎝的四方形，铺在烤盅里面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13041484" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041484" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041484-20200728-_Z1A6572.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041484-20200728-_Z1A6572-450x673.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="673" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041484" class="wp-caption-text">将干净的纱布裁剪成大约25 ㎝的四方形，铺在烤盅里面。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将优格放入钵盆中，以橡皮刮刀搅拌，使硬度均一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13041486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041486-20200728-_Z1A6604.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041486-20200728-_Z1A6604-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041486" class="wp-caption-text">将优格放入钵盆中，以橡皮刮刀搅拌，使硬度均一。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 制作鲜奶油霜。将鲜奶油和细砂糖放入另一个钵盆中，钵盆的底部垫着冰水，用手持式电动搅拌器以高速打发1 分钟左右，打发至与优格差不多相同的硬度就OK了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13041485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041485" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041485-20200728-_Z1A6585.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041485-20200728-_Z1A6585-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041485" class="wp-caption-text">用手持式电动搅拌器以高速打发1 分钟左右，打发至与优格差不多相同的硬度就OK了。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将2的鲜奶油霜分成3次左右加入1的钵盆中，每次加入时都要以打蛋器大幅度搅拌5 次左右。最后一边以单手转动钵盆，一边以橡皮刮刀从底部大幅度地翻拌，整体翻拌5次左右。</p>
<p>4. 将3均分填入烤盅中，提起纱布，轻轻扭转之后藏入旁边，然后覆盖保鲜膜。放在冷藏室中冷却2 小时以上，要品尝的时候添加个人喜好的水果酱汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13041497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041497" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041497-20200728-_Z1A6617.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041497-20200728-_Z1A6617-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041497" class="wp-caption-text">将奶油霜均分填入烤盅中，提起纱布，轻轻扭转。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13041498" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13041498" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041498-20200728-_Z1A6619.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13041498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13041498-20200728-_Z1A6619-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13041498" class="wp-caption-text">纱布轻轻扭转之后藏入旁边，然后覆盖保鲜膜。（台湾东贩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>· 去除优格所含的水分，把优格变成较硬的口感。味道会变得更浓厚。<br />
· 纱布可以在药局等处购买。<br />
· 稍微不留神就会立刻变得太硬，请留意。<br />
· 呈现滑顺的一体感时就OK了。<br />
· 藉由冷藏使状态稳定下来。保持这样的状态可以冷藏保存2天左右。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">POINT</span></h3>
<p>◎ 不使用圆形模具，而是以烤盅制作。<br />
◎ 沥干优格水分的作业，请预先在前一天做好备用。</p>
