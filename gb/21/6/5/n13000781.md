<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（8）维多利亚瀑布～不速之客凑热闹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001075-20210605_africa_helen_chou_801-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>跨越赞比西河连接津巴布韦和赞比亚两国的维多利亚瀑布大桥。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/jokqkt383/djy/blob/master/gb/21/5/22/n12967632.md#1">走进非洲（7）维多利亚瀑布</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000784" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000784-20210605_africa_helen_chou_802.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000784-20210605_africa_helen_chou_802-600x450.jpg" alt="走进非洲" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000784" class="wp-caption-text">津巴布韦的第一顿早餐。（图片/<ahref="https://github.com/jokqkt383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E4%BC%A6.md#1">周海伦</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上慢悠悠的吃完简单美味的早餐后就步行前往赞比亚境内看瀑布。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000785" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000785-20210605_africa_helen_chou_803.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000785-20210605_africa_helen_chou_803-600x389.jpg" alt="走进非洲" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000785" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jokqkt383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">维多利亚瀑布</a>大桥。（图片/<ahref="https://github.com/jokqkt383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E6%B5%B7%E4%BC%A6.md#1">周海伦</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>横跨赞比西河的<ahref="https://github.com/jokqkt383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">维多利亚瀑布</a>大桥于1905年4月落成，全长250米。桥的北端是津巴布韦，南端是赞比亚。这与在尼亚加拉瀑布边横跨尼亚加拉河，连接美加两国的彩虹桥(Rainbow Bridge)有点相似。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000787" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000787-20210605_africa_helen_chou_804.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000787-20210605_africa_helen_chou_804-600x437.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000787" class="wp-caption-text">维多利亚瀑布地貌示意图。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过了桥就是维多利亚瀑布在赞比亚境内的部分，莫西奥图尼亚国家公园（Mosi-Oa-Tunya National Park），意思是“像雷霆般轰轰作响的烟雾。”</p>
<p>今天在赞比亚境内我更深刻的领会了这“雷霆般轰轰作响的烟雾”有多厉害，穿了两件雨衣还是像从水里捞出来一样。相机完全不能拿出来用，拍照全靠手机。很多观景点只能听听轰鸣的水声，一片白茫茫雨雾。要看清层层叠叠的峡谷只能是在枯水季节了。</p>
<p>幸运的是，一路有彩虹相随。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000788" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000788-20210605_africa_helen_chou_805.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000788-20210605_africa_helen_chou_805-600x389.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000788" class="wp-caption-text">赞比亚境内的维多利亚瀑布。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13000790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000790" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000790-20210605_africa_helen_chou_806.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000790-20210605_africa_helen_chou_806-600x450.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000790" class="wp-caption-text">瀑布观景台。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13000791" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000791-20210605_africa_helen_chou_807.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000791-20210605_africa_helen_chou_807-600x306.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000791" class="wp-caption-text">维多利亚瀑布大桥与彩虹。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13000792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000792-20210605_africa_helen_chou_808.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13000792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000792-20210605_africa_helen_chou_808-600x800.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000792" class="wp-caption-text">刀锋桥(Knife Edge Bridge)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刀锋桥是公园里一个有名的景点，是最佳观赏东瀑布的地点，离赞比西河水面420英尺。没有雨衣是无法过桥的，除非你想变成落汤鸡。要么就是在枯水季过桥。不过我过桥时两件雨衣都挡不住如倾盆大雨般的瀑布水。放眼望去，除了水帘还是水帘，根本看不清东瀑布的真面目。</p>
<p>与刀锋桥平行的另一条步道叫“摄影家步道”(Photographic Trail)，可以一直走到维多利亚瀑布大桥边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000793-20210605_africa_helen_chou_809.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000793-20210605_africa_helen_chou_809-600x394.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000793" class="wp-caption-text">从“摄影家步道”回望刀锋桥。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一直走到步道头，也就是维多利亚瀑布大桥边上，就是另一个相当震撼的景点 – “沸腾的锅”(Boiling Pot)。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000798-20210605_africa_helen_chou_810.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000798-20210605_africa_helen_chou_810-600x415.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000798" class="wp-caption-text">“摄影家步道”尽头。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13000801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000801-20210605_africa_helen_chou_811.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000801-20210605_africa_helen_chou_811-600x424.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000801" class="wp-caption-text">“沸腾的锅”景点。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在那里，奔腾而下的瀑布水挤过狭窄的峡谷口一涌而出，一浪接着一浪翻滚着往前冲，真像一大锅刚烧开的水！站在那里，耳边是轰轰的雷鸣般的水声，眼前是汹涌澎湃的水流与漫天的水雾，极为壮观。</p>
<p>尽管浑身湿透，鞋子里叽叽咕咕的都是水，心情异常满足。</p>
<p>回到渡假村大家饥肠辘辘，火速换下衣服就跑去餐厅大吃一顿。前后两天被赞比西河水淋成落汤鸡，总得让口、胃也享受一下赞比西河水酿的啤酒吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000802" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000802-20210605_africa_helen_chou_812.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13000802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000802-20210605_africa_helen_chou_812-450x600.jpg" alt="非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000802" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村内的午餐。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>午餐还没吃完，四个人就开始商量起来晚饭去哪里吃。</p>
<p>最后大家一致决定，晚餐就去津巴布韦最古老、最豪华的维多利亚瀑布大酒店！跟大自然打了这么久的交道，必须要接一接地气，感受一下现代文明的舒适。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000805" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000805-20210605_africa_helen_chou_813.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13000805" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000805-20210605_africa_helen_chou_813-600x412.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布大酒店" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000805" class="wp-caption-text">维多利亚瀑布大酒店。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>维多利亚瀑布大酒店建于1904年，是爱德华时代风格的世界级五星级酒店。酒店的位置得天独厚，从酒店的大草坪可以直接看到瀑布与大桥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000806" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000806" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000806-20210605_africa_helen_chou_814.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13000806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000806-20210605_africa_helen_chou_814-450x600.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布大酒店" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000806" class="wp-caption-text">维多利亚瀑布大酒店。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>酒店外面风景优美，内部装修考究，极具英伦风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13000813" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13000813" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000813-20210605_africa_helen_chou_818.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13000813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13000813-20210605_africa_helen_chou_818-450x600.jpg" alt="维多利亚瀑布大酒店" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13000813" class="wp-caption-text">维多利亚瀑布大酒店餐厅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
