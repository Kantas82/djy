<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（9）开普敦的桌山与酒庄</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034466-20210620_africa_helen_chou_904-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/21/6/5/n13000781.md#1">走进非洲（8）维多利亚瀑布～不速之客凑热闹</a></p>
<p>告别了美丽的维多利亚瀑布，我们来到了此次非洲行的最后一站：<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E9%9D%9E.md#1">南非</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E6%99%AE%E6%95%A6.md#1">开普敦</a>。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E6%99%AE%E6%95%A6.md#1">开普敦</a>因其自然与地理环境被称为世界最美丽的城市之一。市区有被誉为“上帝的餐桌”的桌山（Table Mountain），山脚下有科斯滕布什国家植物园（Kirstenbosch National Botanical Gardens）。开普敦半岛的顶端是印度洋与大西洋的交汇点好望角（Cape of Good Hope），中间还有一个<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E9%9D%9E.md#1">南非</a>企鹅生态保护区。</p>
<p>然而，近年来，这个非洲南部最大的城市因治安、毒品、爱滋病等问题令不少人望而生畏。相对来讲，开普敦半岛的旅游区还算安全，但我们在出发前、到达后都多次被告诫，千万不要独自离开旅游区。我们人生地不熟，不想一不小心踩过线发生意外，索性雇了一位当地人作司机加导游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034464" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034464-20210620_africa_helen_chou_902.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034464-20210620_africa_helen_chou_902-600x450.jpg" alt="开普敦" width="601" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034464" class="wp-caption-text">乘缆车上桌山。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了开普敦不上桌山等于没到过开普敦。桌山于2011年被评为世界新七大奇观之一。为避免上山下山两次排长队，我们事先在网上买好票。这就有点考人品了。因为山上时晴时阴，如果正好碰上云彩给“上帝的餐桌”铺上桌布，那山脚下的景色就什么都看不到了。</p>
<p>当然，我们的人品总是没话说的。早上在万里晴空下乘着360度旋转的缆车上到山顶，壮观的景色一览无余。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034465-20210620_africa_helen_chou_903.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034465-20210620_africa_helen_chou_903-600x196.jpg" alt="开普敦, 桌山" width="600" b="196" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034465" class="wp-caption-text">桌山山顶的风光。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山脚下就是有着350万人口的南非立法首都开普敦。右边是魔鬼峰（Devil’s Peak），左边是狮子头（Lion’s Head）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034466" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034466-20210620_africa_helen_chou_904.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034466-20210620_africa_helen_chou_904-600x450.jpg" alt="开普敦, 狮子头, 桌山" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034466" class="wp-caption-text">狮子头（Lion’s Head）。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13034467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034467" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034467-20210620_africa_helen_chou_905.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034467-20210620_africa_helen_chou_905-600x377.jpg" alt="开普敦" width="600" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034467" class="wp-caption-text">开普敦的海岸线。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13034469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034469-20210620_africa_helen_chou_906.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034469-20210620_africa_helen_chou_906-600x378.jpg" alt="开普敦" width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034469" class="wp-caption-text">会当凌绝顶。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13034470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034470" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034470-20210620_africa_helen_chou_907.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034470-20210620_africa_helen_chou_907-600x800.jpg" alt="开普敦, 桌山" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034470" class="wp-caption-text">被当地人称为dassie的蹄兔。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桌山上除了无敌的景色外还有许多特殊的动植物，其中最有名的是一种被当地人称为dassie的蹄兔，它们外型像兔子，圆滚滚的身子，耳朵与尾巴都是短短小小的。山顶上专门有牌子警告游人不要投喂蹄兔，它们会咬人的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034474-20210620_africa_helen_chou_908.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034474-20210620_africa_helen_chou_908-600x415.jpg" alt="开普敦, 桌山" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034474" class="wp-caption-text">徒步登山者。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了乘坐缆车，户外活动爱好者们还可以选择徒步登山。我们在山顶时就看到这两位年轻人身手矫健的一路攀爬，最后利索的翻身上了山顶的岩石。</p>
<p>从桌山上下来，司机带着我们前往城外有名的斯泰伦博斯镇（Stellenbosch）。该镇位于开普敦东面50公里，当年是欧洲移民开普敦后第二大聚集地。斯泰伦博斯大学就位于此地。这是个真正依山傍水的美丽城镇，沿着依尔斯特河（Eerste River）坐落在斯泰伦博斯山脚下。大量的欧洲移民加上当地肥沃的土地及完美的气候，使得斯泰伦博斯地区成了南非葡萄酒业的中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034483-20210620_africa_helen_chou_909.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034483-20210620_africa_helen_chou_909-600x358.jpg" alt="开普敦, 酒庄" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034483" class="wp-caption-text">Meerlust<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E5%BA%84.md#1">酒庄</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们去的第一家Meerlust<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E5%BA%84.md#1">酒庄</a>，一进大门，迎面就是一条橡树与棕榈树相间的大道，十分有气势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034485" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034485-20210620_africa_helen_chou_910.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034485-20210620_africa_helen_chou_910-600x423.jpg" alt="Meerlust酒庄" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034485" class="wp-caption-text">Meerlust酒庄。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13034486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034486" style="width: 391px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034486-20210620_africa_helen_chou_911.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034486-20210620_africa_helen_chou_911-450x541.jpg" alt="酒庄" width="391" b="470" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034486" class="wp-caption-text">老上海风味的广告牌。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>居然在酒庄内看到了上个世纪三、四十年代旧上海的广告牌，颇感亲切。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034487" style="width: 390px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034487-20210620_africa_helen_chou_912.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034487-20210620_africa_helen_chou_912-450x600.jpg" alt="酒" width="390" b="520" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034487" class="wp-caption-text">品酒。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>几乎每家酒庄都提供品酒活动。他们家这款Pinot Noir口感不错，层次分明。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034489" style="width: 605px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034489-20210620_africa_helen_chou_913.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034489-20210620_africa_helen_chou_913-450x265.jpg" alt="Vredenheim酒庄" width="605" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034489" class="wp-caption-text">Vredenheim酒庄。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第二家酒庄Vredenheim格局上明显上了几个档次。整个庄园不仅像公园，还像动物园。里面有白狮子，还有老虎（非洲大陆是没有野生老虎的），梅花鹿及开普斑马。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034490" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034490-20210620_africa_helen_chou_914.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034490-20210620_africa_helen_chou_914-600x800.jpg" alt="酒庄" width="451" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034490" class="wp-caption-text">动物园般的酒庄。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13034491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034491" style="width: 391px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034491-20210620_africa_helen_chou_915.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034491" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034491-20210620_africa_helen_chou_915-600x800.jpg" alt="酒" width="391" b="521" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034491" class="wp-caption-text">酒庄的酒。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过与其规模与风景相比，这家的酒品尝下来好像没有第一家的好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034492" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034492-20210620_africa_helen_chou_916.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13034492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034492-20210620_africa_helen_chou_916-600x367.jpg" alt="Neethlingshof酒庄" width="602" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034492" class="wp-caption-text">Neethlingshof酒庄。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三家Neethlingshof酒庄可以用7个字形容：景美饭香葡萄甜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13034493" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13034493" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034493-20210620_africa_helen_chou_917.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13034493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13034493-20210620_africa_helen_chou_917-600x433.jpg" alt="Neethlingshof酒庄" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13034493" class="wp-caption-text">Neethlingshof酒庄。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>司机告诉我们，三家酒庄中这家的饭最好吃，而他们家最好吃的是用南非特产的无须鳕鱼(hake)做的炸鱼（fish fry）。</p>
