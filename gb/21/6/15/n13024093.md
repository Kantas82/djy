<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【视频】波士顿明慧学校重传统文化 新址办结业式</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023841-DSC2655-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>明慧学校本学年结业，校长刘珊玲向孩子们赠送奖状。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-23f7a27d-24f2-4f9a-8d02-74f452b7b9a6" data-id="player-23f7a27d-24f2-4f9a-8d02-74f452b7b9a6"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/23f7a27d-24f2-4f9a-8d02-74f452b7b9a6?r=16x9&amp;d=156&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F21%2F6%2F15%2Fn13024093.md#1"></a>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月15日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨剑桥市报导）6月12日，<ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a><ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>在新校址举办2020-2021学年结业典礼，师生们以表演的形式为家长和来宾们展示这一年中学到的<ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87.md#1">中文</a>和中华<ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>。校长刘珊玲说，希望通过中华传统文化的教育，让孩子们培养良好的品行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13023843" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023843" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023843-DSC2670.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023843-DSC2670-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023843" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">明慧学校</a>的学生唱<ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87.md#1">中文</a>歌。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在当天的结业仪式中，孩子们唱中文歌，背诵中文篇目如《弟子规》；也有孩子盛装出场，为大家表演了中国舞蹈。教授舞蹈的慧慧老师也为师生、家长们展示了她扎实的中国舞基础。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13023839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023839" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023839-DSC2634.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023839-DSC2634-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023839" class="wp-caption-text">慧慧老师表演舞蹈技巧。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13023844" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023844" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023844-DSC02211.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023844" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023844-DSC02211-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023844" class="wp-caption-text">明慧学校的学生表演舞蹈。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了孩子们之外，母语不是中文的家长们也用中文背诵文章，展现她们这一年来学习中文的成果。</p>
<p>在孩子们的学年颁奖典礼之后，师生家长们还一起吃粽子，庆祝6月14日端午节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13023838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023838-DSC2614.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023838-DSC2614-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023838" class="wp-caption-text">家长们观看孩子们的表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A3%AB%E9%A1%BF.md#1">波士顿</a>明慧学校校长刘珊玲介绍，明慧学校的中文教育采用正体汉字和注音符号，同时非常注重中华<ahref="https://github.com/frzfvh3644/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A0%E7%BB%9F%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96.md#1">传统文化</a>的熏陶。</p>
<p>在过去一学年里，孩子们除了学习文法和才艺之外，还背诵《三字经》、《弟子规》等经典篇目，以及体会尊敬长辈的传统礼仪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13023840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023840-DSC2651.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023840-DSC2651-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023840" class="wp-caption-text">校长刘珊玲（右）赠送奖状，学生鞠躬感谢。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“小孩子从《三字经》或《弟子规》那里学了一些做人的道理，培养一些中国传统的道德观念。希望孩子做一个有品行道德的小孩。”刘珊玲说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13023837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13023837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023837-DSC2611.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13023837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13023837-DSC2611-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13023837" class="wp-caption-text">越南裔妈妈Huyen Aguyen背诵中文篇目。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>越南裔妈妈Huyen Aguyen说，中华传统文化不仅仅是中国的文化，而有着更深刻的内涵。她说，她送儿子来明慧学校，希望儿子学习传统的理念，将来能成为一个高尚的人。◇</p>
