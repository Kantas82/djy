<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>把缤纷色彩穿上身 清爽穿搭洋溢夏日风情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12996537-GettyImages-958238974-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，澳洲时装品牌 Lee Mathews，微带米色的白底洋装，洋溢着夏日风情的缤纷花儿一朵朵绽放于上，清浅的粉、黄、绿相搭不冲突。（Lisa Maree Williams/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月10日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）进入芒种时节，时序已入仲夏，是该拿出凉快洋装、背心的时候了！但是炎炎夏季到底适合怎样的服装来搭配呢？</p>
<p>除了款式，<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>美学上还有个不容忽视，却时常被低估的要点：颜色。有些人会觉得考虑颜色很麻烦，或是不太敢挑战鲜艳<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>，因此总是围绕着黑与白、米棕、咖啡等基本色系。这些基本色彩当然有其魅力，不仅百搭又耐看，但其实这个世界还有极其广大的部分你尚未探索，所以就让我们踏进这个明亮缤纷的花花世界吧。</p>
<p>那些对鲜艳<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>感到害怕的人们不用担心，本篇将重点介绍清爽的夏日<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>，既洋溢着夏日风情又百搭，让我们一起把清凉的视觉感受穿上身，至于华丽灿烂色调的选择，就留到以后再介绍了！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #333399;">奶油白</span></h2>
<p>“白”这种看似简单的颜色，其实在衣着上大多不是纯白，不像上班正装穿的白衬衫，较常见的是根据不同亮度与微微的色调偏向，细分出来的许多不同白，如象牙白、珍珠白、奶油白等，而这些微微偏黄、偏裸的米白色系，其实比纯白更不易落入显黑陷阱、更能衬托亚洲人的肤色。</p>
<p>说到米白，就不能不提到亚麻材质，透气、凉快、轻薄易干、挺度又很够，完全是炎炎夏日万中选一的布料，随便一搭就很有度假的风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001240" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001240-GettyImages-1321292410.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001240-GettyImages-1321292410-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 澳洲" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001240" class="wp-caption-text">澳大利亚<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E8%A3%85%E5%91%A8.md#1">时装周</a>，澳洲品牌 Anna Quan 度假系列走秀。（Brendon Thorne/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>条纹和蕾丝也是白色常见的变化之一，这里提供一个小秘招：穿着条纹衣时，下半身可选择与条纹颜色相近的色彩做搭配，会让整体造型更加融合。</p>
<p>蕾丝外套则是夏日特有装饰性衣物，内搭简单的背心，外头罩上雕花蕾丝外套，能稍微遮蔽皮肤，不那么裸露却能保有最大程度的凉爽，同时搭出层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001238" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001238" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001238-GettyImages-606182994.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001238" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001238-GettyImages-606182994-450x670.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 伦敦" width="450" b="670" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001238" class="wp-caption-text">伦敦<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E8%A3%85%E5%91%A8.md#1">时装周</a>，Ones To Watch 秀。（Eamonn M. McCormack/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13001239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001239" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001239-GettyImages-1321048475.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001239-GettyImages-1321048475-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 澳洲" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001239" class="wp-caption-text">澳大利亚时装周，澳洲奢华时装品牌 Oroton 度假系列走秀。（Stefan Gosatti/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #333399;">薄荷绿</span></h2>
<p>一般对绿色穿搭的印象，可能多是秋冬季的军绿、墨绿大衣，但其实夏天也有专属的绿。像薄荷绿、湖水绿、蒂芬尼绿&#8230;&#8230;这些有如水流般清澈的绿色，就像一股清凉的微风，为你缓解夏日的酷暑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001235-GettyImages-1229072359.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001235-GettyImages-1229072359-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 土耳其" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001235" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，土耳其设计师品牌 Mehtap Elaidi 走秀。（Ferhat Zupcevic/Getty Images for IHKIB）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绿色属于冷色调，适合皮肤较为暗沉的人，能够平衡视觉效果；但对于皮肤过于白皙，且因血管等因素而微带青色的人，穿着这类型绿色可能反而显气色差、更加苍白。</p>
<p>轻浅的绿给人干净、明亮的感受，因此适合搭配同样清亮的色彩，像是白、蓝、黄以至浅棕色等。而和绿色相对的粉色也值得尝试，只要拿捏好轻重，这两个颜色组合也可能达到冲突却亮丽的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001236-GettyImages-1272805153.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001236-GettyImages-1272805153-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 意大利" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001236" class="wp-caption-text">2021 罗马时装周，意大利服饰 Casa Preti 品牌走秀。（Ernesto S. <h2><span style="color: #333399;">薰衣草紫</span></h2>
<p>自2018年 Pantone 宣布当年代表色为“紫外光（ULTRA VIOLET）”，紫色受穿搭潮人的欢迎程度可说是与日俱增。而说到夏日紫，首选自然是淡淡的、偏蓝色调的薰衣草紫，与微带粉色调的丁香紫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001266" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001266-GettyImages-1276601858.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001266-GettyImages-1276601858-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 意大利" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001266" class="wp-caption-text">米兰时装周，意大利品牌 Marco Rambaldi 走秀。（Pietro S. D&#8217;Aprano/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为一个带有神秘、梦幻、轻奢意象的色彩，紫色穿搭门槛稍微高了一些，但也不须太紧张！</p>
<p>首先可以试试简单好上手的全身单色系穿搭法，暂时先省掉颜色互搭考量的麻烦，从不同比例蓝红混合出的紫色中，找出最适合于自己皮肤的紫，你就赢在紫色穿搭起跑点了！而两种不同色调的紫相搭，也是一种极具美感与冲突的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001263-GettyImages-844980984.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001263-GettyImages-844980984-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 美国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001263" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，美国时装品牌 Tibi。（JP Yim/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>简单的颜色搭配，建议从基本色如米白、奶油白、浅卡其等，能充分展现浅紫色的浪漫与与甜美。另外，也可搭配牛仔裤，由于蓝色本来就是组成紫色的原色之一，因此绝对不会突兀，也能减弱一些紫色的贵气，增添一股年轻率性的<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>感。</p>
<p>若有余力，搭配一些简单的珠宝、首饰，点缀金银的光泽和紫色轻奢的意象也能搭配的很好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001265" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001265-GettyImages-1173013207.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001265-GettyImages-1173013207-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 法国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001265" class="wp-caption-text">法国奢侈品牌 Longchamp 春夏系列服装展示。（JP Yim/Getty Images for Longchamp）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13001268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001268-GettyImages-1282078475.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001268-GettyImages-1282078475-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 韩国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001268" class="wp-caption-text">首尔时装周，韩国时装品牌 LIE。（Justin Shin/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #333399;">清浅黄</span></h2>
<p>讲了这么多，终于轮到春夏必备色之一，闪耀着阳光热情的黄色！热爱鲜艳亮丽，或是习惯低调穿着的人们都相当适合。也许你会担心黄色显黑、显黄，但其实黄色反而能衬出一股温柔的好气色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001255-GettyImages-1172805417.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001255-GettyImages-1172805417-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 韩国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001255" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，韩国女装品牌 LEYII 走秀。（Mike Coppola/Getty Images for NYFW: The Shows）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13002679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13002679" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13002679-GettyImages-470188461.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13002679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13002679-GettyImages-470188461-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 伦敦" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13002679" class="wp-caption-text">伦敦时装周，Ashley Williams 品牌走秀。（Tristan Fewings/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>淡淡的浅黄色适合不喜欢张扬的人，柔和的色调有着春日温柔宁静的感受，做为点缀或是直接变成造型中的主色都很恰当，搭配白、黑等色系就相当具有<ahref="https://github.com/vmhdwv328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>感。</p>
<p>还有，蓝黄相搭的概念不仅能运用在设计中，例如稍微饱和些的黄，特别适合搭配牛仔裤，撞色冲突感显得特别有活力与精神，时髦又简单。</p>

<h2>橘/鲑鱼粉</h2>
<p>说起橘色，在平日穿搭中，是一个很让人避之的颜色，总感觉太过鲜艳，不实穿。但其实橘色没你想像的那么难以驾驭，反而能轻松搭出好气色与独特个人风格！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001259-GettyImages-51290269.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001259-GettyImages-51290269-450x760.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 土耳其" width="450" b="760" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001259" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，土耳其设计师品牌 Atil Kutoglu 走秀。（Fernanda Calfat/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最简单的莫过于单一色系穿搭了，一旦习惯之后，可以搭上浅色系衣物，像是米白、浅褐等，黑色也不错，就是重了点；另外也可搭配万年不败的经典牛仔裤，没有黄蓝相搭那么抢眼，却多了一分沉静，但造型依旧是活泼生动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001260" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001260-GettyImages-186016454.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001260-GettyImages-186016454-450x675.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 乔治亚" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001260" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周，乔治亚设计师品牌“GOGA NIKABADZE”。（Andreas Rentz/Getty Images for Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Russia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鲑鱼粉是个相当特殊的颜色，虽然名字中有“粉”，但却有些偏橙红色，只是更加柔和，与人体肤色更加接近，因此十分好搭，是值得尝试的夏日清淡色彩之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13001261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13001261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001261-GettyImages-602247000.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13001261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13001261-GettyImages-602247000-450x676.jpg" alt="时装周, 时尚, 裙, 美国" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13001261" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，美国高级时装品牌 A Detacher。（Fernanda Calfat/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鲑鱼粉自带甜美气息，却不如粉红色那样粘腻，有着清新脱俗的内敛，能相搭的颜色也非常多，如黑白、大地色系、牛仔蓝等，无论是上班、出门游玩都很适合。</p>
