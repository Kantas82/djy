<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【视频】响应全国反对仇恨亚裔 波士顿数百人集会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994147-DSC2588-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>亚裔青年献唱自创的反对歧视歌曲。（刘景烨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-da9e58cb-fee8-41c5-97b2-fb1d16b4d1ae" data-id="player-da9e58cb-fee8-41c5-97b2-fb1d16b4d1ae"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/da9e58cb-fee8-41c5-97b2-fb1d16b4d1ae?r=16x9&amp;d=184&&api=7&autostart=true&mute=false&logo=true&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F21%2F6%2F2%2Fn12994178.md#1"></a>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）5月31日是“全国团结反对仇恨<ahref="https://github.com/snxhgu316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>集会日（National Day of Solidarity against AAPI Hate）”。当天下午，波士顿同源会（CACA）在公共公园（Boston Common）主办集会，获多位当地政要支持，及数百民众参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994141-DSC2440.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994141" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994141-DSC2440-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994141" class="wp-caption-text">数百民众参与反对仇恨<ahref="https://github.com/snxhgu316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">亚裔</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们今天在此，团结一致，以共同的声音强调，我们是美国人。”波士顿同源会会长陈清（Ai Cheng）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994139-DSC2412.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994139-DSC2412-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994139" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿同源会会长陈清欢迎来宾。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与会者谴责针对亚裔的仇恨犯罪行为，以及对亚裔的<ahref="https://github.com/snxhgu316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A7%E8%A7%86.md#1">歧视</a>态度。集会现场周围的草坪上插着美国国旗和反对仇视亚裔的标语，许多参与者都穿着“Stop Asian Hate（停止仇恨亚裔）”黑色衣服。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994145" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994145-DSC2551.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994145-DSC2551-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994145" class="wp-caption-text">与会民众<ahref="https://github.com/snxhgu316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%AF%B9%E4%BB%87%E6%81%A8%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md#1">反对仇恨亚裔</a>。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在锣鼓声中，人们首先看到南派武馆带来的舞狮表演。而后，华裔青年学生领唱美国国歌，退伍少将陈绍章（William S. Chen）领读效忠誓词。一群亚裔青年还献唱了原创歌曲，表达采取行动反对<ahref="https://github.com/snxhgu316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A7%E8%A7%86.md#1">歧视</a>的意愿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994138-DSC2393.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994138-DSC2393-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994138" class="wp-caption-text">南派武馆带来舞狮表演。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994137-DSC2376.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994137-DSC2376-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994137" class="wp-caption-text">退伍少将陈绍章（左）领读效忠誓词。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“从《排华法案》，到集中关押日裔美国人，再到性化亚裔女性的文化，歧视的火焰在我们国家燃烧得太久了。我们不会让这种事在波士顿持续。”波士顿代理市长珍妮（Kim Janey）说。她呼吁人们一同发声，反对仇恨和暴力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994144" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994144-DSC2479.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994144-DSC2479-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994144" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿代理市长珍妮（中间灰衣女士）向同源会赠予褒奖。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天集会的一件象征物品是黄色的哨子。美国华裔传统基金会共同创办人李徐慕莲说，黄色在美国历史上曾代表仇外情绪，而现在，主办方希望以黄色哨子来象征人们对歧视的反抗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994142-DSC2483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994142-DSC2483-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994142" class="wp-caption-text">美国华裔传统基金会共同创办人李徐慕莲。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“这个黄色哨子是自卫和团结的象征，象征我们共同反对历史上的歧视，以及仇视亚裔的暴力。”李徐慕莲说。她与代理市长珍妮和众多来宾一同吹响了哨子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994140-DSC2426.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994140-DSC2426-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994140" class="wp-caption-text">左起：昭伦公所主席谢如键、波士顿市议员费连、阮氏公所主席阮鸿灿。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12994149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994149-DSC2522.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994149-DSC2522-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994149" class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员吴弭及多位公职候选人与会支持。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天参与集会的嘉宾还包括波士顿市议员吴弭（Michelle Wu）、费连（Ed Flynn），麻州众议员瓦娜·霍华德（Vanna Howard），以及多位市长候选人和市议员候选人。集会主持人是波士顿律师琳达·尚宾（Linda Champion）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12994148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12994148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994148-DSC01887.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12994148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12994148-DSC01887-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12994148" class="wp-caption-text">余风彩堂美东总长余文博参与反歧视集会。（刘景烨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>纽英崙传统侨社也积极支持反对仇视亚裔的行动。当天到场的侨社代表包括波士顿同源会前会长李卫新（Wilson Lee）、余风彩堂美东总长余文博、阮氏公所主席阮鸿灿、昭伦公所主席谢如键等等。</p>
<p>余文博表示，他来到这里反对歧视，因为这关乎着他与所有亚裔美国人的子孙后代。◇</p>
