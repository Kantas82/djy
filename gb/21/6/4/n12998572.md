<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>北市大豪雨 文山区淹水至轿车一半高</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998588-20210604PHO0081l-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月04日讯】受梅雨锋面及台风彩云外围环流影响，水利署针对<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%B1%B1%E5%8C%BA.md#1">文山区</a>、信义区发布<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%B9%E6%B0%B4.md#1">淹水</a>一级警戒。<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%87%E5%B1%B1%E5%8C%BA.md#1">文山区</a>部分路段淹水已至轿车的一半高，南港区南深陆闸门水位已达警戒关闭。</p>
<p>受梅雨锋面及台风彩云外围环流影响，中央气象局今天发布大雨及<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%AA%E9%9B%A8.md#1">豪雨</a>特报，水利署也针对<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a>文山区、信义区、新北市新店区发布<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%B9%E6%B0%B4.md#1">淹水</a>一级警戒，<ahref="https://github.com/nzljjq3117/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82.md#1">台北市</a>大安区则是淹水二级警戒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12998582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12998582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998582-20210604PHO0082l.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12998582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998582-20210604PHO0082l-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12998582" class="wp-caption-text">图为信义区永吉路淹水情形，民众涉水牵机车。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台北市政府表示，中午12时起北市即开始有持续性的强对流发展，市中心有瞬间强降雨，文山、大安及信义区时雨量均超过100毫米，大安及信义区最大10分钟雨量均超过30毫米。</p>
<p>据北市府提供照片显示，文山区木栅路四段159巷演水已达半辆轿车高度、居民涉水牵机车，也有民众在黄泞积水中拿扫把要清理。</p>
<p>此外北市消防局、南湖大桥也有淹水。</p>
<p>北市府表示，灾情整理中，目前测得最大累积雨量为大安区福州山站127.5毫米。水利署已发布南港区淹水一级警戒及松山区一级警戒。南港区南深陆闸门因为<br />
逼近警戒水位，已关闭。</p>
<p>北市府表示，市灾害应变中心一级开设，消防局等29个单位立即派员进驻，各区灾害应变中心同时成立。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12998601" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12998601" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998601-20210604PHO0083l.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12998601" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998601-20210604PHO0083l-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12998601" class="wp-caption-text">忠孝东路出现积水情形，机车骑士小心闪避。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12998606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12998606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998606-805865.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12998606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id12998606-805865-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12998606" class="wp-caption-text">图为台北市六张犁信安街淹水的情况。（北市府提供）</figcaption></figure>
