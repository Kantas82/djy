<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清凉一夏  短裤穿搭你的身材适合哪款裤型？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062131-GettyImages-481477106-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>属于夏日的短裤造型。Ｔshirt 配牛仔短裤、罗马凉鞋，搭出时尚感毫不费力。（Juan Naharro Gimenez/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月10日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）艳阳高照，连风都是热的，走在街上总是汗流浃背，到底该穿什么才凉快？裙子或宽裤都不错，但可别忘了还有夏季专属的<ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E8%A3%A4.md#1">短裤</a>。迷你短裤、单车裤、五分裤，短裤款式如何穿搭出夏日<ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>感呢？快来一探究竟吧！</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E8%A3%A4.md#1">短裤</a></h2>
<p>如果你拥有一双羡煞旁人、匀称笔直的美腿，那裤长不及大腿一半的短裤正是属于你的单品，能恰如其分的展现身材优势。白上衣＋牛仔短裤向来是不会出错的选择，令人联想到夏日奔放自由的大海，搭配黑色短靴显得俏皮可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062127" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062127" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062127-GettyImages-179483695.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062127-GettyImages-179483695-450x676.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 影展" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062127" class="wp-caption-text">第70届威尼斯影展，以色列女星 Yuval Scharf。（Gareth Cattermole/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>花花迷你短裤充满夏日风情，搭配同花色配件，像是包包、发带、领巾等，能使<ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%A0%E5%9E%8B.md#1">造型</a>更完整，看起来也显得年轻活泼。如果想穿迷你短裤又觉得有点害羞，试试绑一件衬衫在腰间，让衬衫稍微遮掩一些皮肤，依旧能享受迷你短裤的特点。</p>
<p>短裤要穿出高质感，有两点需要留意：一是千万别挑选会露出屁股的过短款式﹔二是注意裤款的整洁度，若是已经起了毛球或退色的短裤，最好还是别穿出门，以免坏了形象又贻笑大方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062124" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062124" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062124-GettyImages-51299296.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062124-GettyImages-51299296-450x739.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 短裤, 纽约" width="450" b="739" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062124" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，美国品牌 Milly 春夏高订系列。（Fernanda Calfat/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一般短裤长度落在大腿中段上下，通常呈现微微伞状，能隐藏大腿内侧的肉，同时修饰臀型。裤管不贴腿的设计，能让大腿线条呈现纤细的视觉效果，是一款不挑人又好穿的版型。</p>
<p>一扫冬日的暗沈，清爽有朝气的夏日黄短裤亮眼又柔和，搭配白上衣和帆布鞋，<ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%80%A0%E5%9E%8B.md#1">造型</a>轻松休闲，就算不习惯鲜艳色彩的人也能尝试。如果觉得太单调的话，可以配戴一条浮夸的项链点缀，让整体穿搭更有<ahref="https://github.com/pgrwgc317/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13068411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13068411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13068411-GettyImages-457246248.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13068411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13068411-GettyImages-457246248-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 短裤" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13068411" class="wp-caption-text">梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周。（ Andreas Rentz/Getty Images for IMG）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">＃背心＋短裤</span></h3>
<p>夏季必备经典穿搭，出游绝对少不了这套，高腰设计的裤款，衬托出美腿显得更加修长。此款造型建议加件薄衬衫作为外套，在外头可以遮阳防晒，进冷气房时也能缓冲温度变化，才不会感冒喔！</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062139" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062139-GettyImages-1321459280.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062139-GettyImages-1321459280-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 澳洲, 短裤" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062139" class="wp-caption-text">2021 澳大利亚时装周，澳洲品牌 Aqua Blu 度假系列。（Stefan Gosatti/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">＃衬衫＋短裤</span></h3>
<p>属于常见的组合，但除了将上衣扎好扎满的普通穿法，试试让下?外露，再以腰带束紧，打造出优雅知性的造型。而强调腰身的做法，也能调节身型比例，无论高挑或娇小的女孩都适合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062126-GettyImages-74810659.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062126-GettyImages-74810659-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 美国" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062126" class="wp-caption-text">美国服装品牌 Tommy Hilfiger 春夏时装秀。（Gaye Gerard/Getty Images for Tommy Hilfiger）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">＃西装短裤</span></h3>
<p>套装穿搭法一改西装制式的感受，或许不适合职场穿着，但以日常休闲时尚而言，绝对让你跳脱路人等级。</p>
<p>初次尝试此款搭法，可以从容易上手的单色系套装开始，衬衫和外套选用色彩微饱和度差异的做出层次感，搭配同为裸色系的配件与鞋子，在艳阳之下，柔和清新的鲑鱼粉色像是一股清凉微风，甜而不腻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062138" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062138-GettyImages-1321421339.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062138-GettyImages-1321421339-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 澳洲, 短裤" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062138" class="wp-caption-text">2021 澳大利亚时装周，澳洲品牌 MAARA 度假系列。（Stefan Gosatti/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没有西装裤也想搭出西装感？翻出衣柜中的西装外套与同色系短裤，图样不一致也没有关系，更能显出悠闲感。另外，考虑反折内搭衬衫袖口，或是戴上夏日必备的墨镜，营造率性惬意的个人风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062134" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062134-GettyImages-1176442658.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062134-GettyImages-1176442658-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 米兰" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062134" class="wp-caption-text">2020春夏米兰时装周街头穿搭。（Edward Berthelot/Getty Images for Ermanno Scervino）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>单车裤</h2>
<p>单车裤强调的概念是“随时都能跳上一旁的单车，去到任何地方”，相当随性自由，是近几年热卖的复古流行款。这种裤型能收紧大腿肉，使双腿显得笔直紧实，当然，贴腿设计可能对梨形身材不大友善，但若能搭配长版上衣，或是以长版外套多层次穿搭营造上宽下窄的视觉效果，达到完美修饰效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062133" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062133-GettyImages-1172644166.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062133" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062133-GettyImages-1172644166-450x790.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 纽约" width="450" b="790" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062133" class="wp-caption-text">2020春夏纽约时装周街头穿搭。（Arturo Holmes/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也可以试试变化款，单车裤造型的牛仔裤少了几分运动感，却因其布料硬挺，使造型更加俐落，搭配合身上衣，时髦有型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13067863" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067863-GettyImages-1277633200.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13067863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067863-GettyImages-1277633200-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 法国, 短裤" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067863" class="wp-caption-text">2021 春夏巴黎时装周，法国时尚品牌 Balmain。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>五分裤</h2>
<p>长度至膝盖上下的五分裤，正是小男孩风经典单品。宽大裤管巧妙地隐藏了大腿肉，修饰身形，同时有着凉快透气的效用，夏天穿着也不闷热。</p>
<p>若担心五分视觉效果显得腿短的话，可以试试搭配跟鞋，在延伸腿长的同时，和小男孩风的中性感相撞，能迸发出强烈的独特风格。另外，面对宽松剪裁的单品，可以用皮带强调腰身，看起来会比较有精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13067886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13067886" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067886-GettyImages-1176442335.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13067886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13067886-GettyImages-1176442335-450x675.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装, 米兰, 短裤" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13067886" class="wp-caption-text">纽约名媛 Olivia Palermo 出席2020 春夏米兰时装周。（Edward Berthelot/Getty Images for Ermanno Scervino）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全身浅色系的装扮，配上裤管微微收紧的五分裤和高跟凉鞋，一改小男孩风的街头感，转为带有自信与优雅的都会时尚，适合上班族穿着。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062123" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062123-GettyImages-2509447.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062123-GettyImages-2509447-450x657.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 纽约" width="450" b="657" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062123" class="wp-caption-text">春夏纽约时装周，纽约设计师 Behnaz Sarafpour 品牌。（Myrna Suarez/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然，你也可以抛下一切线条、身形的顾虑，拥抱最舒适的自己，选择上下身都宽松的五分裤棉质套装，居家休闲适穿，外出搭配墨镜、鲜艳的小包也能展露时尚态度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13062130" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13062130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062130-GettyImages-460032356.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13062130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13062130-GettyImages-460032356-450x660.jpg" alt="时尚, 时装周, 衬衫, 纽约" width="450" b="660" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13062130" class="wp-caption-text">Savannah White身着英国品牌 Asos 夏日套装。（Daniel Zuchnik/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
