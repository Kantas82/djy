<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【组图】谁在后院偷吃鸟儿们的口粮？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019091-20210613_bird_helen_chou_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>冬日后院的小鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>一年四季，<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E9%99%A2.md#1">后院</a><ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>来鸟往。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019034" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019034" style="width: 461px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019034-20210613_bird_helen_chou_02.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019034-20210613_bird_helen_chou_02-600x800.jpg" alt="鸟" width="461" b="615" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019034" class="wp-caption-text">爱美的小<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F.md#1">鸟</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有各种各样大大小小的鸟。有些是漂亮又爱美的小鸟，如美洲<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%BF%85%E9%9B%80.md#1">金翅雀</a>（American goldfinch）和<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%BE%8E%E7%BA%A2%E9%9B%80.md#1">北美红雀</a>（Northern cardinal）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019036" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019036-20210613_bird_helen_chou_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019036-20210613_bird_helen_chou_03-600x450.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019036" class="wp-caption-text">爱捉虫的小鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有些是长得美又能捉虫的啄木鸟，如绒啄木鸟（Downy woodpecker）和北扑翅?（Northern flicker）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019040" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019040-20210613_bird_helen_chou_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13019040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019040-20210613_bird_helen_chou_04-450x579.jpg" alt="鸟" width="450" b="579" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019040" class="wp-caption-text">红尾鵟(Red-tailed hawk)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>甚至还会从天而降一只鹰，如上图的红尾鵟（它的故事要另外讲）。</p>
<p>几年前在<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8E%E9%99%A2.md#1">后院</a>挂了几只喂鸟器（bird feeder），这下就更热闹了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019048" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019048-20210613_bird_helen_chou_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019048" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019048-20210613_bird_helen_chou_05-600x442.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019048" class="wp-caption-text">吃瓜子能手<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%97%E7%BE%8E%E7%BA%A2%E9%9B%80.md#1">北美红雀</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019050" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019050" style="width: 461px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019050-20210613_bird_helen_chou_06.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019050-20210613_bird_helen_chou_06-600x792.jpg" alt="鸟" width="461" b="609" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019050" class="wp-caption-text">吃瓜子能手<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%BF%85%E9%9B%80.md#1">金翅雀</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019051-20210613_bird_helen_chou_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019051-20210613_bird_helen_chou_07-600x393.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019051" class="wp-caption-text">吃瓜子能手黑顶山雀(Black-capped chickadee)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我也是第一次知道鸟儿们吃瓜子居然是吐壳的，速度还比我快！真是“高手在鸟间。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019055" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019055-20210613_bird_helen_chou_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019055-20210613_bird_helen_chou_08-600x405.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019055" class="wp-caption-text">和平相处的小鸟们。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019057-20210613_bird_helen_chou_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019057-20210613_bird_helen_chou_09-600x437.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019057" class="wp-caption-text">争食的小鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019059" style="width: 461px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019059-20210613_bird_helen_chou_10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019059" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019059-20210613_bird_helen_chou_10-600x781.jpg" alt="鸟" width="461" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019059" class="wp-caption-text">愤怒的小鸟。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大多数时间小鸟们能和平相处，各自埋头苦吃。但有时一言不合也能立刻剑拔弩张，鸟飞雀跳的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019062" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019062" style="width: 461px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019062-20210613_bird_helen_chou_11.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019062" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019062-20210613_bird_helen_chou_11-600x795.jpg" alt="鸟" width="461" b="611" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019062" class="wp-caption-text">金翅雀两公婆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019065" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019065-20210613_bird_helen_chou_12.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019065" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019065-20210613_bird_helen_chou_12-600x800.jpg" alt="鸟" width="462" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019065" class="wp-caption-text">嬉戏的黑顶山雀。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有时喂鸟器成了两公婆谈恋爱的地方，有时又成了小山雀嬉戏的游乐场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019066" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019066" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019066-20210613_bird_helen_chou_13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019066" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019066-20210613_bird_helen_chou_13-600x407.jpg" alt="鸟" width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019066" class="wp-caption-text">吃相霸道的雌红雀。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019067" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019067-20210613_bird_helen_chou_14.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13019067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019067-20210613_bird_helen_chou_14-600x600.jpg" alt="鸟" width="460" b="460" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019067" class="wp-caption-text">懂得感恩的小山雀。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小鸟们多数吃完了就扬长而去，等饿了再飞回来。不过这只小山雀却极懂礼貌，吃完了冲主人一鞠躬。</p>
<p>渐渐的就发现，鸟儿们的口粮消耗得越来越快，几乎每天都要添加。但鸟的数量不见多呀。难道有人在和鸟儿们抢口粮？</p>
<p>我在窗户里盯了两天，捉到了两个偷吃的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019068" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019068" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019068-20210613_bird_helen_chou_15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019068" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019068-20210613_bird_helen_chou_15-600x424.jpg" alt="松鼠" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019068" class="wp-caption-text">偷吃鸟食的<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0.md#1">松鼠</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13019069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019069" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019069-20210613_bird_helen_chou_16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13019069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019069-20210613_bird_helen_chou_16-600x404.jpg" alt="松鼠" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019069" class="wp-caption-text">偷吃的<ahref="https://github.com/jdbuew3581/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%BC%A0.md#1">松鼠</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只小松鼠先是像跳远运动员一样从树枝上蹦到喂鸟器上，一看无从下嘴，马上跳到另一棵树上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019070-20210613_bird_helen_chou_17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13019070" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019070-20210613_bird_helen_chou_17-450x617.jpg" alt="松鼠" width="450" b="617" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019070" class="wp-caption-text">偷吃的松鼠。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只喂鸟器里的东西能吃到，但角度有点刁钻，怎么办？小松鼠也是天赋异禀，居然可以大头朝下吃饭，也不怕呛到鼻子里？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019073" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019073" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019073-20210613_bird_helen_chou_18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13019073" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019073-20210613_bird_helen_chou_18-450x629.jpg" alt="松鼠" width="450" b="629" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019073" class="wp-caption-text">偷吃的松鼠。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身体居然能拉成一直线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13019074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13019074" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019074-20210613_bird_helen_chou_19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13019074" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13019074-20210613_bird_helen_chou_19-450x567.jpg" alt="松鼠" width="450" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13019074" class="wp-caption-text">偷吃的松鼠？（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>它这算是在谢我吗？</p>
