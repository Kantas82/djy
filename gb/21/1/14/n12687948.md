<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA哈登、杜兰特与欧文 建构篮网三巨头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/479470-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>杜兰特，图为资料照。（Sarah Stier/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月14日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）季前高喊“卖我”，还闹出延迟球队报到事件，最近还放“状况无法修复”重话来批判球队，休士顿火箭球星<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E7%99%BB.md#1">哈登</a>（James Harden）终于得偿所愿，将与前队友<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%9C%E5%85%B0%E7%89%B9.md#1">杜兰特</a>（Kevin Durant）和明星控卫<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E6%96%87.md#1">欧文</a>（Kyrie Irving），在布鲁克林<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%BD%91.md#1">篮网</a>形成三巨头。</p>
<figure id="12687952" aria-describedby="caption-12687952" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/479472-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/479472-450x300.jpg" alt="哈登，图为资料照。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12687952" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%88%E7%99%BB.md#1">哈登</a>，图为资料照。（Carmen Mandato/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12687951" aria-describedby="caption-12687951" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/479471-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/479471-450x675.jpg" alt="厄文，图为资料照。" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12687951" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E6%96%87.md#1">欧文</a>，图为资料照。（Sarah Stier/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次牵扯到四方交易。火箭获得欧拉迪波（Victor Oladipo）、库鲁斯（Rodions Kurucs）及艾克森（Dante Exum）等好手，这还不够，<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AF%AE%E7%BD%91.md#1">篮网</a>2022、2024、2024年未保护首轮签与2021、2023、2025与2027首轮交换权，加上克里夫兰骑士2022年首轮签和印地安纳步行者2轮选秀权。</p>
<p>篮网则获得哈登及骑士2轮选秀权，步行者取得篮网勒佛特（Caris LeVert）与2轮选秀权，骑士则取得篮网艾伦（Jarrett Allen）与普林斯（Taurean Prince）。</p>
