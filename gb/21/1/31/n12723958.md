<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寒冬保暖也可兼顾时尚 纽约时装周街头穿搭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205787795-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）寒冬依旧，出门总想多穿几件衣服，一不小心就把自己捆成肉粽，到底该如何是好？相较于时装伸展台上难以亲近的氛围，场外潮流达人的穿着更能成为日常<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>的参考。</p>
<p>本篇特别观察纽约时装周场外人士造型，归纳出绒毛大衣、皮衣、西装外套三种<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E6%9A%96.md#1">保暖</a>方式以及两种色彩建议，让你在穿得暖的同时，走出<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>的一片天！</p>
<h2>绒毛大衣</h2>
<p>以深浅不一的棕色调做全身搭配，是相当适合<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E5%A4%A9.md#1">冬天</a>的色系。咖啡高腰皮革锥形裤帅气俐落，这种裤型特色是大腿处微宽，至裤脚微收紧，没有多余皱褶，能使双腿看起来笔直。外搭微带光泽感的绒毛外套，横向线条让造型多了层次；下摆落在大腿中段，但袖长略短于手臂，提拉视觉身高。搭配杏色柏金包，整体装扮低调、富有质感与细节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12723960" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/GettyImages-1205279641.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12723960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/GettyImages-1205279641-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Britney Wilson被摄于2020纽约时装周街头。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>异质料、款式衣物搭配，让单色系也不嫌无聊。白色毛衣外穿一件西装外套，硬挺材质下搭配高腰运动风锥型裤，裤脚收进纯白短靴，融合休闲与正式感；最外罩上柔软的米白绒毛外套，同时以围巾包住后脑，丰富整体造型兼具<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BF%9D%E6%9A%96.md#1">保暖</a>功效，灰色手拿包同样以菱纹绒毛覆盖。一身灰白混搭，仿佛<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E5%A4%A9.md#1">冬天</a>绵绵白雪，纯净无瑕。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724131" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205134092.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724131" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205134092-450x669.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="669" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>飘逸的连身长裙与厚实的绒毛长版外套也是不错的组合，棕熊般的短绒毛给人温暖、安心的感受。镂空设计的短靴和身上“连成一片”的衣物形成对比，相当醒目，成功营造成熟、温柔的淑女风范。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1199077822-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724130" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1199077822-1-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Zinnia Bukhari身着Zara系列<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>，于2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（David Dee Delgado/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>皮衣</h2>
<p>除了绒毛大衣外，皮衣也是冬天百搭小帮手之一。帅气之余，依版型和颜色选择的不同，能呈现出成熟、可爱等各种相异面貌！</p>
<p>跳脱传统风衣质料，皮风衣更添几分干练、时髦，防风功力亦是不在话下。微微反光的皮面与酒红色搭配，白、金色腰带成为全身视觉重点，整体低调却不容忽视。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724122" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205839307.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205839307-450x615.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="615" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>皮外套和皮裙也能同时搭配。微带棕色调的橘色抢眼却不会太高调，深一个色阶的酒红色跟鞋也恰如其分的撑住上身重量。微微澎起的袖缘刚刚好修饰了肩膀线条；顺着内搭白衬衫的扣子往下到单排扣Ａ字皮裙，延伸视觉身高；裙子同样没有太多装饰，以剪裁设计为重，让皮革静静地散发自己的光彩，搭配复古马鞍包，散发一股随性浪漫的女性韵味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724119" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205787841.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205787841-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>西装外套</h2>
<p>格纹西装外套属于常见款式，比起素色西装添了一抹休闲；若再内搭牛仔吊带七分裤，让自由奔放和内敛拘束相互冲撞，更稀释了西装的正式感；微微反光的绒面深蓝短靴与造型特殊的圆筒包也相当引人注目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724115" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205133956.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205133956-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然，西装外套也能呈现出柔和的风貌。相较于大格纹，小格子显得细致、优美；落肩设计一改传统西装俐落的印象，大大的柔化了肩部线条；没有多余装饰的奶茶色洋装以同色腰带系出精神。高贵优雅的都会风格，适合上班族日常穿搭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724684" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205787795.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205787795-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>介绍完三种保暖又<ahref="https://github.com/taioys3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>的外套搭配后，让我们来看看两种隐藏在纽约时尚穿搭客中的色彩建议！</p>
<h2>粉蓝糖果色</h2>
<p>每到冬天，街上总是一片灰白冷暗的色调，这时如果想从背景中脱颖而出，在你下一次结账前，不妨考虑一下糖果色系的衣裳！</p>
<p>深粉红皮制长版风衣，内搭圆点浅蓝洋装，样式简单，却和周遭人群宛若在不同世界；突出的色彩与皮衣质料更添强势感，用胸针将领巾别在胸前的巧思相当独特，很好的平衡了两种强烈色彩间的关系。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724683" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205842606.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205842606-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Mitsuki Kimura带着Coach手拿包，于2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将基本色和糖果色互搭也相当耐看。全套西装内搭高领毛衣，配上高跟凉鞋，最普通常见的选择，因为长裤颜色的不同而有了天壤之别的转变；多了些俏皮、少了点无趣。这套装扮还有个独到之处：全身虽然以毫无相关的灰、粉、蓝组合，但“明度”相近，因此看起来意外的协调。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724682" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205067435.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205067435-450x601.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="601" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Leonie Hanne，提着Fendi手袋，于2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同样是粉、蓝的组合，但柔软的布料能使色彩相对柔和。针织开襟衫与长裙是相当典型的女性穿搭，鹅黄色的包包和鞋子提亮了全身色彩，整体风格温柔可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12724681" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205149348.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205149348-450x645.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="645" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Actress Ava Michelle Cota，于2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>渐层</h2>
<p>如果你不喜欢那样的缤纷，却依旧渴望比身旁的人更突出一些些，渐层也是值得尝试的一种方案！</p>
<p>从深色头发开始一路向下，高雅的连身中长裙从奶茶色、鹅黄色最后过渡到如雪般纯白的中筒靴，流畅的渐层让人无法移开视线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12728575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205153010.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12728575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/GettyImages-1205153010-450x675.jpg" alt="纽约, 时装周, 时尚, 穿搭" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020纽约时装周 场外街拍。（Donell Woodson/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
