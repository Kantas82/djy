<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澎恰恰跨足网路综艺节目 近期狂接通告曝光高</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432131487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>澎恰恰（前排左5）跨足网路主持大型综艺节目，与艺人王彩桦（前排左6）携手主持初一特别节目。图为群星合照。（AMM亚洲心动娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年01月29日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬报导）网路新节目《<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%BD%AC%E4%B9%BE%E5%9D%A4%E5%A5%BD%E8%BF%90%E5%88%B0.md#1">牛转乾坤好运到</a>》，找来台湾知名主持人<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%8E%E6%81%B0%E6%81%B0.md#1">澎恰恰</a>与艺人<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%BD%A9%E6%A1%A6.md#1">王彩桦</a>，带领群星重现90年代超年味综艺，以笑料短剧进攻初一特别节目。</p>
<p>节目一开场，就以艺人排排站方式表演受访。<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%BD%A9%E6%A1%A6.md#1">王彩桦</a>猛攻<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%8E%E6%81%B0%E6%81%B0.md#1">澎恰恰</a>欠债狂接通告，逗得现场哈哈大笑，也让澎恰恰哭笑不得，两人重现过去的经典节目风格，加上艺人与模仿巨星同台较劲，演唱经典歌曲，营造出巨星登台的热闹新年气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12720391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12720391" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432151487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12720391" title="澎恰恰、王彩桦" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432151487.jpg" alt="澎恰恰、王彩桦" width="453" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12720391" class="wp-caption-text">澎恰恰（左）与王彩桦携手主持新春综艺节目，陪大家过初一。<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/amm%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E5%BF%83%E5%8A%A8%E5%A8%B1%E4%B9%90.md#1">AMM亚洲心动娱乐</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一单元“群星贺新年”除了有两位主持人外，还找来选秀节目《DD52》出道的女团“PINK FUN”与歌手金志遥、梁一贞、邵大伦，加上模仿秀走红的“小阿妹”简语卉、“小伍佰”黄上，一同庆牛年。金志遥、梁一贞与邵大伦也分别带来郭富城、萧亚轩、萧煌奇的模仿秀，维妙维肖效果十足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12720398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12720398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432171487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12720398" title="PINK_FUN" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432171487-600x338.jpg" alt="PINK_FUN" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12720398" class="wp-caption-text">从选秀节目《DD52》出道的台湾女团PINK_FUN。<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/amm%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E5%BF%83%E5%8A%A8%E5%A8%B1%E4%B9%90.md#1">AMM亚洲心动娱乐</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>节目特地选在2月12日大年初一晚间8点播出，除了艺人与模仿巨星排排站的“群星贺新年”，也有笑料短剧“双喜临门好预兆”与“不好公民一家”，前者由澎恰恰、王彩桦、白云、黄靖伦、早川小百合演出；后者则由新加坡名导梁志强带领王雷、刘玲玲、莫小玲、RICO等接力演出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12720379" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12720379" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432201487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12720379" title="澎恰恰、王彩桦、白云" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2101290432201487.jpg" alt="澎恰恰、王彩桦、白云" width="456" b="513" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12720379" class="wp-caption-text">澎恰恰、王彩桦、白云合演笑料短剧。（AMM亚洲心动娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后康康、荒山亮、阎奕格接力开唱“HAPPY牛YEAR演唱会”。观众可在AMM亚洲心动娱乐脸书粉专观看，并下载AMMapp，线上直播抽红包，边看边拿压岁钱。</p>
