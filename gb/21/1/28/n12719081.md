<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】皇汤海参</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/832828535a4cbf1c90faa08b047b9d18-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道皇汤海参，不仅美味可口，而且滋补养生。（普照荟苑餐厅提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>海参自古以来就是传统滋补品，被很多老年人、病人、孕妇等视为最有营养的食品之一。现代科学研究证明，海参含有超过50种对人体生理活动有益的营养成分。</p>
<p>由于海参的蛋白质含量极其高，其中的胶原蛋白也十分丰富，可与阿胶、鹿角胶相媲美，不仅能补血，还能延缓机体衰老，让肌肤润滑，消除皱纹和面部色斑，并且海参中的脂肪含量低，不会导致肥胖。</p>
<p>海参中的角蛋白能增强红骨髓细胞的造血功能，丰富的天然活性钙容易被人体吸收，食用后可以生血养血，且可促进钙质吸收，补充人体所需钙质。</p>
<p>海参中含有18种氨基酸，包括牛磺酸、胶原蛋白、硫酸软骨、多肽、维生素和钙、铁等微量元素，这些营养元素有助于增强细胞活性、提高人体免疫力。</p>
<p>另外，海参中还含有DHA，孕妇食用海参有助于胎儿大脑发育，所以备孕期的女性和已经怀孕的女性可适量食用。</p>
<p>由于海参本身的香气和味道单薄，但质地爽口软滑，<ahref="https://github.com/zmvsty371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>处理海参一般以焖、煮等为主，配以高汤、上汤煮成的汤羹或清炒小菜烹调，有时也配排骨、冬菇以小砂锅（煲仔）处理。即使日常汤面也可以加入食用。鲁菜一般用葱爆炒，葱爆海参就是一道经典菜肴。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/zmvsty371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E6%B1%A4%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">皇汤海参</a>，不仅美味可口，而且滋补养生，清甜可口的鸡汤和红萝卜的原汁被海参所吸收，使得海参更加鲜美，口感清香弹口。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/zmvsty371/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%87%E6%B1%A4%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">皇汤海参</a></h2>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/87c91fed0b6c5e92b6199d2168dc0747.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/87c91fed0b6c5e92b6199d2168dc0747-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>主料：海参一条</p>
<p>配料：姜、葱、红萝卜、油菜</p>
<p>调料：油、盐、胡椒粉、料酒、糖、料酒、鸡汤、生粉水</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 热油锅将姜、葱爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/01-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/01-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 锅内放入鸡汤后，煮沸。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/02-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/02-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 加入料酒、盐、糖、胡椒粉等调料。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/03-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/03-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 放入浸好发好的海参，浸煮几分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/04-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/04-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 将蒸好的红萝卜片原汁打烂。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/05-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/05-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 将红萝卜汁倒入浓鸡汤中煮香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/06-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/06-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 添加少许盐调味。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/07-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/07-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 加入生粉水3汤匙勾芡。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/08-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/08-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 将汤注入海参盆中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/09-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/09-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 慢火蒸3分钟即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11. 成品海参味美无比，口感清香弹口。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12719115" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-8-450x271.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="271" /></a></p>
