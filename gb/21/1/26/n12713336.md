<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2021春夏浏海趋势 女明星范本发型</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480158-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>金裕贞八字浏海。（friDay影音提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）迎接2021年春季，许多女星们纷纷换上了<ahref="https://github.com/unrhwi3015/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%8F%E6%B5%B7.md#1">浏海</a><ahref="https://github.com/unrhwi3015/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%91%E5%9E%8B.md#1">发型</a>，尤其以眉毛的“水平浏海”与变化款，是近期最热门的造型。</p>
<p><strong>氧气<ahref="https://github.com/unrhwi3015/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%8F%E6%B5%B7.md#1">浏海</a></strong></p>
<p>有别于空气浏海的轻薄发量，厚度大约在空气浏海跟妹妹头齐浏海之间，任何脸型都适合，搭配度也高。</p>
<p><strong>眉上浏海</strong></p>
<figure id="12713342" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480161-450x300.jpg" alt="峮峮眉上浏海。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">峮峮眉上浏海。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12713343" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480162-450x675.jpg" alt="峮峮眉上浏海。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">峮峮眉上浏海。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>眉上浏海在2021春夏重新吹起潮流，以眉上1公分的长度最受到日韩女生的喜爱，显得俏丽且年轻了不少。</p>
<p><strong>八字浏海</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12713338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480157.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12713338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480157-450x326.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="326" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金裕贞八字浏海。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>八字浏海不管中分或旁分都可以，可以达到修饰脸型的效果，戴发箍或是绑公主头都好看。</p>
<p><strong>旁分浏海</strong></p>
<figure id="12713341" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480160-450x630.jpg" alt="太妍旁分浏海。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/unrhwi3015/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E5%A6%8D.md#1">太妍</a>旁分浏海。（环球唱片提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论以上哪一种浏海，只要长长都可以做旁分造型，有修饰肉脸的效果。</p>
<p><strong>卷卷浏海</strong></p>
<figure id="12713340" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/480159-450x686.jpg" alt="采子卷卷浏海。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">采子卷卷浏海。（小林眼镜提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>轻盈的浏海卷上饱满有弹性的卷度，让脸蛋更有立体感，也能显出造型感。◇</p>
