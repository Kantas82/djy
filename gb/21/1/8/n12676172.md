<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】炒石斑鱼球</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8361dd0aff5cf8cbd3cb654d4bfcfb3a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道炒石斑鱼球，不仅美味可口、营养丰富，而且色、香、味、形俱全。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>石斑鱼营养丰富，肉质细嫩洁白，类似鸡肉，素有“海鸡肉”之称。石斑鱼又是一种低脂肪、高蛋白的上等食用鱼，被港澳地区推为中国四大名鱼之一，也是喜庆宴会中不可缺少的海鲜主角。</p>
<p>石斑鱼含有铁、钙、磷、镁、硒、钾、胶质、OMEGA-3脂肪酸等营养素，OMEGA-3脂肪酸中的EPA与DHA可阻止血液凝结、减少血管收缩及降低三酸甘油酯等，EPA对血管壁的壁内层细胞有调节作用，是抗血管硬化的重要元素之一，而DHA则是大脑皮质中细胞膜的成分，是负责传递讯息的重要物质。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC%E7%90%83.md#1">炒石斑鱼球</a>，选用蜜豆、马蹄、百合、红椒、黄椒做配料，不仅美味可口、营养丰富，而且色、香、味、形俱全。</p>
<p>百合含独特的生物碱，止咳效果明显，还可以增加肺脏内血液的灌流量，改善肺部功能。 <sup>?</sup>马蹄，又叫荸荠，含水分多，肉质甜嫩，不但营养丰富而且有极高的药用价值。《本草纲目》载马蹄能“消渴、温中益气、下丹石、消风毒、除胸中实热”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E7%9F%B3%E6%96%91%E9%B1%BC%E7%90%83.md#1">炒石斑鱼球</a>属于一道功夫小炒，对于厨师而言也是一道难以驾驭的菜式，要炒出一碟完美的斑球，对厨艺要求较高。</p>
<p>罗厨强调，鱼肉炒起来不散是这道菜的烹饪关键，要用盐、生粉及极少量的蛋白将鱼肉腌制10分钟，先入油锅炸三分熟，然后煎至七分熟定形，这样炒出来的鱼球才会完整。</p>
<h2>炒石斑鱼球</h2>
<p>主料：石斑鱼</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/b8e77e08336a9467f6d5dab323b4cb19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/b8e77e08336a9467f6d5dab323b4cb19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：蜜豆、姜片、马蹄、百合、红椒、黄椒</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/04f59646a8367aac9d8b76337a931bb2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676187" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/04f59646a8367aac9d8b76337a931bb2-450x279.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a></p>
<p>调料：海盐、砂糖、生抽、胡椒粉、生粉水</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 石斑洗净沥干水分后去皮切肉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676188" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 用盐、蛋清1个、生粉1茶匙腌制10分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676189" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 将蜜豆过油捞起沥干油分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676190" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 再把鱼球过油至三成熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 再将石斑球两面煎香至七成熟捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 将小姜片炒香后再放鲜马蹄片、红椒角炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 再放入蜜豆、百合、黄椒件炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 放海盐、砂糖一同炒香至闻到食材香气后捞出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 利用镬底余油，放入生抽、清水、盐、糖、胡椒粉、生粉水调成芡汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 将石斑球及配料放入镬中炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11. 包尾油半汤匙即可装入黄椒盅内。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12676199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
