<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【简单做蛋糕卷】香葱肉松卷 咸甜奶香好滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8d98f03c7662689ecfa220f2d37cc4ed-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>柔软咸甜的青葱肉松蛋糕卷，下午茶或早餐都很适合。（图／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>吃腻了甜味<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>吗？来试试这一款<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%B8%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">咸蛋糕</a>吧！绝对让你惊喜连连！拿来当早餐或<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E5%8D%88%E8%8C%B6.md#1">下午茶</a>，都很适合。品尝的每一口充满了奶香、蛋香、青葱香、<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">肉松</a>咸香，在味蕾中的甜咸香气，保证让你回味无穷。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">青葱<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%89%E6%9D%BE.md#1">肉松</a>卷卷</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>
<p>39.5cm长，1卷</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h4>原味<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>体</h4>
<p><strong>A ?蛋黄面糊</strong><br />
热水－28g<br />
细砂糖－5g<br />
沙拉油－28g<br />
低筋面粉－45g<br />
泡打粉－0.5g<br />
蛋黄－60g</p>
<p><strong>B ?蛋白霜</strong><br />
蛋白－110g<br />
细砂糖－45g<br />
干燥青葱末－适量</p>
<h4>内馅</h4>
<p>沙拉酱－适量<br />
肉松－适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将热水、沙拉油与细砂糖放入钢盆中搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7091c94093dd06dfe6b59568ec54010b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7091c94093dd06dfe6b59568ec54010b-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714300" class="wp-caption-text">热水、沙拉油与细砂糖放入钢盆中搅拌均匀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 加入过筛的低筋面粉、玉米粉与泡打粉搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/f284ed812bd6b9a25e2c962ecf3a508c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/f284ed812bd6b9a25e2c962ecf3a508c-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714301" class="wp-caption-text">加入过筛的低筋面粉、玉米粉与泡打粉搅拌均匀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再加入蛋黄拌匀即为蛋黄面糊。<br />
4. 另取一锅，将蛋白放入钢盆中，打至粗泡沫。<br />
5. 分次加入细砂糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10da7132d3c8f3b05f3d25cf947239c8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10da7132d3c8f3b05f3d25cf947239c8-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714304" class="wp-caption-text">分次加入细砂糖。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将蛋白打至勾起有鸟嘴状，即为蛋白霜。<br />
7. 取1/3份量的蛋白霜加入蛋黄糊中拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/50f307da64117bba1c6f25669422fa7c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/50f307da64117bba1c6f25669422fa7c-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714305" class="wp-caption-text">取1/3份量的蛋白霜加入蛋黄糊中拌匀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 再倒回剩余蛋白霜中，用刮刀轻轻搅拌均匀。<br />
9. 加入适量青葱末，轻轻搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/ceb67eb0721e0de1b32d39e13529884e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/ceb67eb0721e0de1b32d39e13529884e-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714307" class="wp-caption-text">加入适量青葱末，轻轻搅拌均匀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 将面糊倒入铺有白报纸烤盘中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/d78cd62336223c845c60aebde4dd2547.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/d78cd62336223c845c60aebde4dd2547-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714316" class="wp-caption-text">将面糊倒入铺有白报纸烤盘中。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 用软刮板将面糊表面轻轻抹平。<br />
12. 烤箱预热后，烤时，盘轻敲一下，再将烤盘放入上火 190℃，下火170℃的烤箱中烘烤。<br />
13. 烘烤至12分钟时，取出烤盘调头，再放回烤箱。<br />
14. 烘烤约6分钟后取出，重摔一下烤盘释出热气。<br />
15. 迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/15f7f85770e1e0b07abf093b77bc2674.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/15f7f85770e1e0b07abf093b77bc2674-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714317" class="wp-caption-text">迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>16. 先覆盖一张新白报纸。<br />
17. 翻面将<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>纸膜撕除。<br />
18. 翻回烘烤上色面，抹上薄薄的沙拉酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714318" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/498b55455d1933f7723f789b666dbb23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/498b55455d1933f7723f789b666dbb23-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714318" class="wp-caption-text">抹上薄薄的沙拉酱。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>19. 然后在长边先轻划三刀。<br />
20. 均匀铺上厚厚一层肉松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/36c96c85df8a527b408cf46178a03279.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/36c96c85df8a527b408cf46178a03279-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714319" class="wp-caption-text">铺上厚厚一层肉松，将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>21. 将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下。<br />
22. 轻轻拉起，先往下压一下。<br />
23. 一边卷起擀面棍，一边顺势将<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>起。<br />
24. 将擀面棍往回推，让蛋糕体更紧实。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714320" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6c1a0c302f9e5e6e74a89e742c702ab5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12714320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6c1a0c302f9e5e6e74a89e742c702ab5-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714320" class="wp-caption-text">一边卷起擀面棍，一边顺势将蛋糕卷起。最后要将擀面棍往回推，让蛋糕体更紧实。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>26. 两端扭紧，放入冰箱冷藏约1小时定型即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12714332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12714332" style="width: 125px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2dca9e35145163663ffd81ce3bb58406.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12714332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2dca9e35145163663ffd81ce3bb58406-450x540.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="125" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12714332" class="wp-caption-text">《卷！卷！卷！蛋糕卷的百变造型与风味》书封。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
