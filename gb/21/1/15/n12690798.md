<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】美极虾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/e9d1115008fe9516f32d866c778e076d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道美极虾，白虾肉质爽滑，加上美极特殊的酱油味道以及鲜辣椒的鲜辣味，使这道菜风味十足。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>白虾富含蛋白质，是蛋白质含量很高的食品之一，也是营养均衡的蛋白质，其蛋白质含量是鱼、蛋、奶的几倍至几十倍。</p>
<p>白虾含有甘氨酸，这种氨基酸的含量越高，虾的甜味就越高。白虾中的牛磺酸能够降低人体血压和胆固醇，所以在预防代谢综合症方面有一定疗效。</p>
<p>白虾中含有丰富的微量元素锌和镁，锌可改善人因缺锌所引起的味觉障碍、生长障碍、皮肤不适等病症。镁对心脏活动具有重要的调节作用，能很好地保护心血管系统。它可降低血清胆固醇值、防止动脉硬化，同时还能扩张冠状动脉，有利于预防高血压及心肌梗塞。</p>
<p>白虾有很多做法，有辣的、有甜的、有香脆的、有麻辣的等等。<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%9E%81%E8%99%BE.md#1">美极虾</a>是一道经典的美味小炒，这道菜的主要食材就是选用白虾，调料包括盐、糖、美极生抽等。简单的食材可以将鲜虾的美味发挥得淋漓尽致。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/vdbisv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E6%9E%81%E8%99%BE.md#1">美极虾</a>，白虾肉质爽滑，加上美极特殊的酱油味道以及鲜辣椒的鲜辣味，使这道菜风味十足。鲜辣椒圈的加入堪称点睛之笔，成功去掉了白虾的土腥味。</p>
<h2>美极虾</h2>
<p>主料：白虾</p>
<p>配料：青椒、姜片、葱白</p>
<p>调料：盐、糖、香油、老抽、美极酱油、生粉水</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将白虾洗净后放入开水中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/baa7b753901afcb05805e471dbb05e19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690806" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/baa7b753901afcb05805e471dbb05e19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 在开水中放入些许盐。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-18-450x270.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="270" /></a></p>
<p>3. 待白虾略微变色即可捞起沥干。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690809" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 在煮熟的虾上撒2汤匙生粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 热油锅将虾煎至金红色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 将姜片、辣椒圈、葱段放入锅中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 用美极酱油、糖和水调芡汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 倒入调好的芡汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 轻轻翻炒白虾。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 翻炒后盛盘。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11. 成品菜肴色香味俱全。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12690824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
