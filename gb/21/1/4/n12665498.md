<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7684-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月04日讯】（大纪元特约记者陆洋报导）<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B0%E7%94%B0.md#1">丰田</a>Yaris Cross是Yaris衍生车型，是丰田公司推出的首款5座小型城市SUV跨界车型，它采用了丰田第四代先进的混合动力技术，是丰田家族最小的SUV。其主要的竞争对手来自马自达CX-3、本田HR-V等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7668-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7668-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665522" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B0%E7%94%B0.md#1">丰田</a>家族<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%80%E5%B0%8Fsuv.md#1">最小SUV</a>——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yaris Cross外型采用全新的设计。娇小的车头搭配一款细小的进气格栅，五边型的大灯组，凹型线条的引擎盖，内嵌式的雾灯，虽然每一项都很简洁，但是组合在一起确能给人一种活力四射的感觉。车身一圈的底部和车轮拱都采用黑色塑料材质，双层轮眉设计的车轮拱使得侧面层次更加的丰富，车子的尾部使用了目前流行的贯穿式尾灯形成一字型，辨识度也非常之高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross8747-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross8747-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665525" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%80%E5%B0%8Fsuv.md#1">最小SUV</a>——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丰田Yaris Cross的内饰和Yaris基本一致。入门级GX配备16英寸合金轮毂、卤素大灯、按钮启动和无钥匙进入，并且标配Apple CarPlay和Android Auto功能的7.0英寸多媒体触摸屏。</p>
<p>中档GXL保留了16英寸合金轮毂，增加了LED大灯和后窗隐私玻璃，安全功能包括盲点监控和全景摄像头。</p>
<p>顶级的Urban车型采用更大的18英寸铝合金轮毂，加热的前排座椅以及驾驶员电动调节装置，抬头显示器和带脚踏传感器的电动后挡板，以及和GXL相同的盲点监控与全景监控。全系标配六个扬声器，八个SRS安全气囊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665514" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross0831-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross0831-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665514" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yaris Cross共分三个等级，分别为入门级GX、中档GXL和顶级Urban。每种型号都可以选择汽油前轮驱动，混合动力前轮驱动或混合动力四轮驱动。混合动力版油耗仅为百公里3.8升，顶级配置Urban Hydrid四轮驱动百公里油耗5.2升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7482-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7482-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665521" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yaris Cross基于TNGA-B平台打造，所有二驱车型均采用前麦克弗森支柱和后扭力梁。引擎依然搭载2020款Yaris 的1.5升自然吸气三缸发动机，产生最大功率为88kW，峰值扭矩为145Nm，混合动力版产生最大功率为85kW，峰值扭矩为141Nm。传动系统匹配Direct Shift CVT无极变速箱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665518" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7190-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7190-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665518" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yaris Cross车身尺寸长4,180毫米、宽1,765毫米、高1,590毫米。在车身尺寸上比Yaris车型长240毫米、宽70毫米、高85毫米。后排的设计具有最大的灵活性，可以40/20/40比例放倒后排座椅，使每个单独的部分都可以平放折叠以增加后行李箱空间。2WD车型后备箱空间为390升，混合动力的后备箱空间为314升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665520" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665520" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7206-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665520" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7206-450x675.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="401" b="606" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665520" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安全配置有碰撞前安全系统、道路标志辅助、主动巡航控制、交叉路口转弯辅助、车道追踪辅助。丰田采用了最先进的内置通信设备、SOS紧急电话、自动碰撞通知和被盗车辆追踪，如遇交通事故，无法使用电话，车辆自动的系统可以通知丰田紧急救援。遇到车辆被盗，Toyota Stolen Vehicle Tracking也可以协助警察帮助找寻车辆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12665517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12665517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7168-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12665517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2021ToyotaYarisCross7168-450x300.jpg" alt="2021款YARIS CROSS" width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12665517" class="wp-caption-text">丰田家族最小SUV——2021款YARIS CROSS。（Toyota提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Yaris Cross的价格从基本型GX$26,990，到顶级规格Urban Hybrid AWD$37,990不等。</p>
