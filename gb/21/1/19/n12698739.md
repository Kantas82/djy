<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去年12月温哥华租金位居全国榜首</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月20日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者曹一存加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">根据<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%B1%8B%E7%A7%9F%E8%B5%81%E7%BD%91.md#1">房屋租赁网</a>站</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">最新公布的数据，尽管</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2020</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>的住房租金同比下降，但已超过<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>，跃居全国之首。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">数据显示，温哥华一居室<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%9D%87%E7%A7%9F%E9%87%91.md#1">平均租金</a>为每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1855</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2019</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年下降了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。两居室为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2,635</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，同比下降了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">尽管租金有所下降，但温哥华的平均月租金在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">35</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个城市中仍是最高的。这是自</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和咨询公司</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bullpen Research and Consulting</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">两年多前开始公布这类数据报告以来，<ahref="https://github.com/ntdfku385/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>第一次退出榜首。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的首席执行官</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Matt Danison</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在一份新闻稿中说，“在许多城市，由于疫情的影响，租金已经降到了用去年相同的租金可以租到多一个房间的套房。”</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">其他上榜的大温城市包括：</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">列治文（第</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名）：一居室平均每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1730</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">本拿比</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">第</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：一居室平均每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,676</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">新西敏</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">第</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：一居室平均每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,511</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里 (</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">第</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">19</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">名</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：一居室平均每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,403</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">虽然北温没有被列入榜单，但</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rentals.ca</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">数据显示，该市一居室的租金平均为每月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1871</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">◇</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span></p>
