<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“真善忍”之感悟</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/200619032819100093-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>莲花（王嘉益／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>真 —— 真人得道以升天<br />
真心?真意?真诚?真情<br />
至真求真理<br />
在谎言的沙漠上播种真理<br />
真理的种子就会将沙漠变成绿洲<br />
将真理的火种撒向黑暗<br />
黑暗就会被点燃</p>
<p>善 —— 上善若水?柔爱之心<br />
善心?善良?善意?善举<br />
至善做善人<br />
污泥中之纯洁凌波仙子<br />
莲花的芳香会驱除污秽<br />
将善的福音播向尘世<br />
尘埃会被荡涤干净</p>
<p>忍 —— 心上一把刀?能之最<br />
忍耐?忍让?忍辱?忍受<br />
容天下难容之人<br />
忍天下难忍之事<br />
忍?绝不是逃离回避 ——<br />
是可忍?孰不可忍<br />
该出手时就出手<br />
与爱同行?与正义同舟</p>
<p>至真?至善?至忍 —— 至美<br />
上帝是永恒的美<br />
无法用言辞形容之美<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/dcvmnq377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%9F%E5%96%84%E5%BF%8D.md#1">真善忍</a>?为人之本?为人之道<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/dcvmnq377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%9F%E5%96%84%E5%BF%8D.md#1">真善忍</a>?人性之美<br />
唯真善忍可拯救人类<br />
唯真善忍可带来光明之未来</p>
<p><strong>Ode to &#8220;Truthfulness &#8211; Compassion – Forbearance&#8221;</strong><br />
By Yiyan Han (c) 2020-12-29 (first completed on 2018-01-07)</p>
<p>Truthfulness includes<br />
&#8220;honesty, veracity, sincerity, no deceit, trustworthiness<br />
genuineness, candidness, frankness, openness, forthrightness&#8221;<br />
Be true to always seek the truth<br />
speak the truth, the whole truth and nothing but the truth<br />
Once the truth is sown on the deserted land of lies<br />
the seeds of truth will turn the desert into an oasis<br />
Once the kindling of truth is sprinkled into the darkness<br />
the darkness will burn out in seconds</p>
<p>Compassion includes<br />
&#8220;kindness, big-hearted, goodwill, benignity, benevolence<br />
thoughtfulness, decency, public-spirited, social conscience<br />
consideration, charity, altruism, humanity, philanthropy, goodness&#8221;<br />
Be kind to be a good person<br />
The lotus flower rises from the sludge of water<br />
and her aromatic fragrance expels the foul odour<br />
When the seeds of kindness are sprinkled into the earth<br />
the dirty dust of humanity will turn into the fertile land of conscience</p>
<p>Forbearance includes<br />
&#8220;tolerance, patience, resignation, fortitude, stoicism, endurance<br />
long-suffering, leniency, clemency, indulgence&#8221;<br />
Be tolerant to those hard to be tolerated<br />
To endure those hard to be bearable<br />
Yet one should not run away from challenges:<br />
When devil attacks one should fight back<br />
and must always seek justice with a loving heart</p>
<p>To the limit of truthfulness &#8211; compassion &#8211; forbearance<br />
is to stretch our hands to the Beauty, and the eternal Beauty<br />
the eternal God that we cannot describe Him in words<br />
The truthfulness &#8211; compassion &#8211; forbearance<br />
is what a human should be a human<br />
the beauty of humanity, the only salvation of the world<br />
and the only hope for us to have a bright future</p>
<p>责任编辑：林芳宇</p>
