Being wise, we are truly humble.<br />　　6.把贪念转为满足，把满足化作慈悲。<br />　　Transform greed into contentment, and contentment into compassion.<br />　　7.只要以理来转事，不要拿事来转理。<br />　　We must carry out our tasks according to principles, not let our principles be compromised by our tasks.<br />　　8.整体的美，在于个体的修养。<br />　　The beauty of a group lies in the refinement of its individuals.<br />　　9.一个人的修养、气质，均在行、住、坐、卧四威仪中自然地显露出来。<br />　　A refined disposition is naturally expressed in the way a person walks, lives, sits, and sleeps.<br />　　10.面容动作、言谈举止合宜得体，都是从日常生活中修养忍辱得来。<br />　　It is through the daily cultivation of tolerance and humility that we become refined in demeanor and conduct.<br />　　11.欣赏他人，即是庄严自己。<br />　　To appreciate others is to dignify ourself.<br />　　12.人有正确的信仰，在人生旅途走的路，就不会有差错。<br />　　When one has true faith, one will not go wrong on the journey of life.<br />　　13.人生在世，但求一心正念，心正气盛，心开运通。<br />　　We must practice right mindfulness. The right state of mind strengthens us; and a happy mind attracts good fortune.<br />　　14. 舍得、舍得，能舍才能得。<br />　　Let go of all attachment, only then will you receive.<br />　　15.付出劳力又欢喜，便叫做“喜舍”。<br />　　To give with joy is to help others with a happy mood.<br />　　16.人应该相信自己，但是不可执著。<br />　　Believe in yourself, but do not be attached to your own point of view.<br />　　17.无信与迷信二者，宁愿“无信”，也不要“迷信”; 信必须智信。<br />　　Atheism is better than superstition. Faith must be guided by wisdom.<br />　　18.人有正确的信仰，在人生旅途走的路，就不会有差错。<br />　　When one has true faith, one will not go wrong on the journey of life.<br />　　19.人生在世，但求一心正念，心正气盛，心开运通。<br />　　We must practice right mindfulness. The right state of mind strengthens us; and a happy mind attracts good fortune.<br />　　20. 每天都是生命中的一张白纸，每一个人、每一件事都是一篇生动的文章。<br />　　Every single day of our life is like a blank page in our diary. Every person we meet, every event we participate in, is a lively essay.<br />　　</p> <p>（慈济亚特兰大供稿）<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
