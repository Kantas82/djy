<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：“艺想天开─张彩杏水墨创作展”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2014年04月02日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾台北报导）“艺想天开─张彩杏<ahref="https://github.com/hlsodg3891/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%A2%A8.md#1">水墨</a>创作展”即日起至4月6日止在国父纪念馆翠亨艺廊隆重推出，张彩杏创作题材多为生活中周遭的情境与景色，用多种不同题材来呈现，随时捕捉生命情感的意象。</p>
<p>张彩杏自小爱绘画，早期以彩墨画花鸟为主，后期则多山水画，曾参加国内外各种联展及多次个展，社会公益义卖展，获全国书画美术才艺金牌奖，亚洲国画银牌奖，交通美展特优，中央机关美展佳作，台北市劳工美展第一名，台湾区金轮奖第四名，东南亚综合艺术展优秀赏。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5713172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5713172" style="width: 366px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404010320242378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5713172" class="wp-caption-text">东岸风情。（张彩杏）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5713177" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5713177" style="width: 462px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404010320292378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5713177" class="wp-caption-text">焦点。（张彩杏）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>张彩杏说，当沉浸在艺术的创作里，在不断的探求与摸索中，偶有新意象的作品呈现，心中之喜悦无法言喻。自然的美是取之不尽的创作泉源，发现美、透过笔墨的符号，在绘画里表达自然的形与色是创作理念，也是她将要传达的意念，希望将艺术达到臻至完美的境界。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5713184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5713184" style="width: 509px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404010320352378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5713184" class="wp-caption-text">源。（张彩杏）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5713190" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5713190" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404010320412378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5713190" class="wp-caption-text">山外青山青。（张彩杏）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>主办单位表示，绘画是来自心灵的表达，艺术家将其观赏到万物的视觉效果，选择性的化为绘画情绪，将自我个性及对描写对象的情感用艺术来诠释，继而创造出令人感动的作品。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5713197" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5713197" style="width: 549px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404010320482378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5713197" class="wp-caption-text">飞瀑动青山。（张彩杏）</figcaption></figure><br />
