<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：卡尔加里牛仔节掠影</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2014年07月09日讯】（大纪元记者罗宾加拿大<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a>报导）102届卡尔加里<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>在7月的第一个星期五（7月4日）如期举行了。卡城牛仔节闻名全球，是当今全球最大的户外表演活动，据报导，每年过百万游客观赏牛仔节盛况，甚至有些游客万里迢迢从澳大利亚来的。</p>
<p>周日恰逢<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E4%BB%94%E8%8A%82.md#1">牛仔节</a>家庭日，记者随着清晨的人流来到了牛仔节公园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5746637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746637" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407090935532695-600x384.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746637" class="wp-caption-text">图：这里是牛仔节的开端，留个影吧！</figcaption></figure>
<p>早晨7点50到达牛仔公园的南门，得到了工作人员派发的的牛仔节免费早餐券——一看排号已到了12,119名了，每天只有头20,000名才有机会享到此优惠的哦。所以要想体验美誉远播的牛仔节免费早餐，就得早早的起床，正所谓“早鸟得早食”。</p>
<p>继续往北走，看到等待早餐的游客已经排起了数百米的3列长龙，原来要见识这特色饮食文化，还需要耐心等待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5746646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407091244302695-600x360.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746646" class="wp-caption-text">图：清晨短暂的小雨丝毫没有动摇游客们的信心，食客长龙中的人们依然兴致勃勃地翘首以盼Grandstand大厅提供的早餐。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746655" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746655" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081311452695-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746655" class="wp-caption-text">图：终于见到牛仔节万人免费早餐了，漂亮的PAN CAKE外加一大块煎牛肉饼，还有一杯任选饮料！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081312532695-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746663" class="wp-caption-text">图：牛仔节的热闹果真名不虚传，游人太多了，真是人山人海。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746673" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746673" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081313372695-600x390.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746673" class="wp-caption-text">图：“吃喝玩乐”，吃排到第一，很远就闻到了烤火鸡的特别香味；循香而至，烤得黄黄的火鸡腿只只都还渗着油滴。</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的农场展览是牛仔节的一个特色内容，原来以为就是一般的粮食作物外加牛羊介绍这些内容罢了。没想到走进农业展览厅，里面也是人流川流不息，甚至摩肩接踵——这里还是小孩们的乐园，能亲眼见到甚至摸摸各种农场动物。</p>
<p>一个华人小朋友说，“我喜欢这里的动物，很有趣，摸着他们感觉很亲热。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_5746682" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081315532695-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746682" class="wp-caption-text">图：天天喝牛奶，原来牛奶是这样来的呀。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746690" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081318092695-600x323.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746690" class="wp-caption-text">图：马马，让我摸摸你！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081317102695-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746699" class="wp-caption-text">图：抱出一只只羊给孩子们演示怎样剪羊毛的。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是测量“马力”的车，由两人组成，测量共同创造出多大的拉力。据主办方介绍，2012的牛仔节中由Jesse和Sam团队拉出来了13,400磅的纪录，不过两人的体重共达5,475磅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5746707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081320192695-600x379.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746707" class="wp-caption-text">图：父子俩共展身手，拉，拉，拉！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746715" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081319082695-600x509.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746715" class="wp-caption-text">图：我也来试一试，看我的力气有多大。加油，加油！98磅！！！</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746723" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746723" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081321412695-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746723" class="wp-caption-text">图：各式各样的游戏奖品开足了马力吸引着游人。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5746733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081323352695-600x431.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746733" class="wp-caption-text">图：游乐场的运动游戏在9点左右运转了。旋转高空吊挂上孩子们体验空中飞人的感觉。</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛仔竞技共六项，每项入围20人，经过9天的角逐，前10名进入最后一天的“周日大决战”，冠军奖金10万元，六项十天总奖金高达200万元，不愧为地球上最昂贵的牛仔竞技大赛，难怪来自世界各地的牛仔精英每逢<ahref="https://github.com/epckcw3537/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A1%E5%B0%94%E5%8A%A0%E9%87%8C.md#1">卡尔加里</a>牛仔节，便像圣诞节临近的小孩子一样兴奋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5746743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5746743" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/07/1407081309482695-600x432.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5746743" class="wp-caption-text">图：牛仔竞技中间的小丑的大枪打出的礼物击中一名幸运观众。</figcaption></figure>
