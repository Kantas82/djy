<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>月兔躺机堡 赏月视野无死角</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>台湾“2014桃园地景艺术节”即日起至14日止在海军桃园基地展出，由于展览期间跨越中秋节，展品“<ahref="https://github.com/zwrhjx328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E5%85%94.md#1">月兔</a>”大受欢迎，参观人潮络绎不绝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5763663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5763663" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409040714512378-600x347.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5763663" class="wp-caption-text">2014年桃园地景艺术节登场，荷兰籍艺术家霍夫曼的作品“<ahref="https://github.com/zwrhjx328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E5%85%94.md#1">月兔</a>”在空中摄影所呈现的模样。（桃园县政府文化局）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“月兔”是黄色小鸭创作者荷兰艺术家霍夫曼特别制作，采取环保可回收再利用的纤维纸，防水防尘，在长度上也做过特别的精算及考量，远观果然栩栩如生。不少民众为了与月兔拍照远道而来，带动地方观光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5766329" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5766329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409030534382378-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5766329" class="wp-caption-text">“月兔”兔毛用防水纸做成，在风吹拂下可感受到轻飘与柔软。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>25公尺的月兔躺在海军桃园基地的机堡上，以放松的大字型慵懒的仰躺着，据说是在一边思考着生命、一边做着白日梦。以这个姿态赏中秋月真是一览无遗、毫无视野死角，月兔凝望玉兔，成就一段佳话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5766305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5766305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409020853092378-600x367.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5766305" class="wp-caption-text">“月兔”躺在机堡上（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>相较于之前的黄色小鸭，想和“月兔”合影，需要多花点心思，不然很容易拍不全月兔的全貌，只拍到局部的白色兔身，萌度瞬间减弱。多试几个角度、多拍几张都是不留下遗憾的好窍门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5772981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5772981" style="width: 413px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409080546581456.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5772981" class="wp-caption-text">想和“月兔”全身合影需要花点心思。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5772989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5772989" style="width: 402px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409080546441456.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5772989" class="wp-caption-text">民众和“月兔”合影，留下难得回忆。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>展出时间延长到晚间8时，安排了夜间艺文表演，民众可以看到月兔从白天的纯白无瑕，蜕变成灯光照射下的“夜间紫兔”，与月亮相映成趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5763676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5763676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409070015302378-600x397.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5763676" class="wp-caption-text">“月兔”入夜后在灯光照明下变成“夜间紫兔”(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年桃园地景艺术节共展出27件气势磅礡的巨作，并规划2个文创市集、4个艺术行动、6场竞赛体验、7个静态展览、8个艺术工作坊等精彩活动。@*</p>
