<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：2014国际粉彩画家邀请展画作精选</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2014年05月10日讯】由台湾<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E5%BD%A9.md#1">粉彩</a>画协会主办的 “2014国际粉彩<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%BB%E5%AE%B6.md#1">画家</a>邀请展”，即日起至5月25日在国立中正纪念堂管理处三楼志清厅举行，展出台湾、美国、法国、西班牙等17个国家、90余幅知名<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E5%BD%A9.md#1">粉彩</a>画家的精彩<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%BB%E4%BD%9C.md#1">画作</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5729396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729396" style="width: 428px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091544122378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729396" class="wp-caption-text">张哲雄-玫瑰花铺-68x89cm。（台湾<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E5%BD%A9%E7%94%BB.md#1">粉彩画</a>协会）</figcaption></figure>
<p> <B><ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%89%E5%BD%A9%E7%94%BB.md#1">粉彩画</a>艺术 </B></p>
<p>台湾粉彩画协会官网介绍 “粉彩画艺术”，粉彩是一种最为古老的绘画素材，远古时代的人类先民，曾在洞窟里留下一些壁画，当时尚无绘画材料的制作，而是以大自然的矿物质色料拿来作画，便属最早期的粉彩画了。粉彩艺术的创作，近30年来在欧美相当的盛行，一般西洋的<ahref="https://github.com/mpuazy3528/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%BB%E5%AE%B6.md#1">画家</a>，皆懂得以粉彩来作画。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5729403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729403" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091544302378-600x457.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729403" class="wp-caption-text">冯英-丽莎Lisa-70x84cm。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5729410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729410" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091543312378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729410" class="wp-caption-text">Rafael Cabo Cagigas-活跃Lively-70x85cm。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>全球已成立的粉彩画协会，目前已超过百个相关的粉彩艺术组织，每年皆举办国际公开大赛。欧美重要的美术馆皆收藏有丰富的粉彩作品。美国粉彩画协会的文献中，特别指，“粉彩色料在显微镜下，宛如被切割的钻石，它晶莹璀璨、光彩四射、令人着迷。&#8230;&#8230;当今世上，还没有一种绘画颜料，能像粉彩这么恒久不变。”</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5729414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729414" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091542552378-600x412.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729414" class="wp-caption-text">Aurelio Rodringuez Lopez-来自地中海From The Mediterranean-62x82cm。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5729423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091545222378-600x426.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729423" class="wp-caption-text">斋藤共永-冬之道-43x60cm。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>主办单位表示，此次展出的作品丰富，无论质与量都颇具规模，皆是最近各国重要的粉彩比赛得奖及入选的佳作，并在中正艺廊视听室举行粉彩画论坛活动，探讨世界各地粉彩画发展的现况及未来的展望。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5729429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729429" style="width: 457px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091543012378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729429" class="wp-caption-text">Maria Dolores Gil-纺织品Textures-79x59cm。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5729437" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5729437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405091543132378-600x448.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5729437" class="wp-caption-text">Marlene Wiedenbaum-莉娜的茶壶Lena&#8217;s Teapot-40.5&#215;48.3。（台湾粉彩画协会）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>台湾粉彩画协会为配合这次国际粉彩展，同时开设多项研习课程，包括旅美粉彩大师张哲雄教授，及法国粉彩协会前会长sylvie cabal，还有旅美华裔粉彩人像画家逢英女士来台，于协会画室亲自示范教学，协会鼓励有兴趣的粉彩画爱好者踊跃上网报名参加。</p>
<p>（责编：林诗远）</p>
