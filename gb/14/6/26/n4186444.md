<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：传神之难在目 吴秀丽水墨经典展</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2014年06月26日讯】 “传神之难在目-吴秀丽<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%A2%A8.md#1">水墨</a>个展”即日起至7月2日止，在台湾国立<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>经典展出。工笔<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%BB%E5%AE%B6.md#1">画家</a>吴秀丽，擅长人物、花鸟、动物等精细作品，画风华丽却不失典雅脱俗，纯熟的传统技法中又溶入鲜明的时代精神，展现出画家的深厚功底和不凡的独特韵味。</p>
<p>“传神之难在目-吴秀丽<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%A2%A8.md#1">水墨</a>个展”展览的特色为作者突破传统工笔画法，进而融入西画的重要元素-焦点透视，并结合传统的散点透视。人物和动物着重刻划眼神和表情，尤其在“猫”一系列作品中展现传神灵动之姿，花鸟画则在突显天籁隽永和深远静谧的意境。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5741799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235202378-600x496.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741799" class="wp-caption-text">吴秀丽-《美与思》。<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5741808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235292378-600x419.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741808" class="wp-caption-text">吴秀丽-《高高的白桦林》。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>吴秀丽生于中西文化交汇的上海，父亲却是道地的台湾新竹县北埔人。1947年，一家人被国民党征调到中国，直到1988年才落叶归根返回台湾，她与家人也于1994年陆续回台，定居于新竹县竹东镇。吴秀丽5岁开始学画，专攻素描及速写长达13年，1989年毕业于中国美术大学南京分校国画系。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5741816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741816" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251236232378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741816" class="wp-caption-text">“传神之难在目-吴秀丽水墨个展”海报。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5741822" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741822" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235502378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741822" class="wp-caption-text">“传神之难在目-吴秀丽水墨个展”海报。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>“传神之难在目”语出自苏东坡，意指眼睛是最能传达被绘者的神韵，但也是最难以描绘的部分。自古以来，<ahref="https://github.com/auytan380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%BB%E5%AE%B6.md#1">画家</a>若能捕捉住目光流转的瞬间，则代表画家的功力相当深厚。该次展出的作品完整呈现作者近40年艺术创作生涯与艺术探索的结晶，创作时间跨度从1980年人物画《千里共婵娟》的发表延伸至2014年《天地缘-吉祥如意福寿玉璧组》最新创作完成。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5741824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741824" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251236042378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741824" class="wp-caption-text">“传神之难在目-吴秀丽水墨个展”海报。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5741833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235132378-600x334.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741833" class="wp-caption-text">吴秀丽水墨个展(三折页)。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>吴秀丽的新作《天地缘》绽放出浓烈的人文情怀及开拓进取的精神，老题新作则将单纯的花鸟画融入思想性，并置放于历史文化、民俗文化、艺术文化的交汇点上陶冶、溶铸。综观其花鸟画创作，涵具思想性且富于艺术价值，可谓将花鸟画的境界导引出创新的思路与境界。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5741841" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235082378-600x1050.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741841" class="wp-caption-text">吴秀丽邀请卡。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5741852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5741852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1406251235042378-600x1050.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5741852" class="wp-caption-text">吴秀丽邀请卡。（国父纪念馆）</figcaption></figure></p>
