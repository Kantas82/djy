<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大陆民众冲破高压封锁纪念六四 倒共呼声高</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>

<p><B>冲破中共<ahref="https://github.com/ofczvx399/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%81%E9%94%81.md#1">封锁</a>重回天安门</B></p>
