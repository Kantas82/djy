<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台国父纪念馆展出辛亥名人翰墨展</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2014年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾台北报导）推翻中国数千年君主专制，开辟民主共和的<ahref="https://github.com/zztuup3178/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E4%BA%A5%E9%9D%A9%E5%91%BD.md#1">辛亥革命</a>，容易让人联想到无数革命志士勇武的豪侠之气，而辛亥革命领导人和参加者不少都深受中华传统文化薰染，富有风雅情怀。台湾国立<ahref="https://github.com/zztuup3178/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>推出“剑胆琴心——辛亥名人翰墨展”，展期至12月21日止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5803033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803033" style="width: 321px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081600282378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803033" class="wp-caption-text">中华民国国父<ahref="https://github.com/zztuup3178/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E4%B8%AD%E5%B1%B1.md#1">孙中山</a>题《福寿》。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“剑胆琴心——辛亥名人翰墨展”39幅书画作品，引领观众注意到辛亥名人狭义表象背后的儒雅风度。中华民国国父<ahref="https://github.com/zztuup3178/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E4%B8%AD%E5%B1%B1.md#1">孙中山</a>先生的“博爱”思想是悲天悯人的情怀；其“天下为公”思想具有世界大同的理想，这两种思想也是他一生书写最多的墨宝。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5803039" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081546212378-600x284.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803039" class="wp-caption-text">黎元洪为黄申芗之母题《得天独厚》横批。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803046" style="width: 397px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081549002378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803046" class="wp-caption-text">图上为谭延闿致李翊东函，下为熊十力为何自新夫人提墓志铭。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803058" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081534392378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803058" class="wp-caption-text">曹亚伯诗画折扇。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>中华民国开国元勋黄兴先生书法得苏东坡、颜真卿笔意，自成一体，表达了他反清革命之志。宋教仁先生的书法存世较少，其“存诚自不妄语始，定性惟治怒字难”是一幅书法、意蕴俱佳的修身联语。辛亥殉难的大将军吴禄贞所作录昆阳诗的条幅，可想见其儒雅之状。首义都督黎元洪所书“得天独厚”，字如其人，厚实端正，得颜体书法精妙。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5803061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803061" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081534162378-600x327.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803061" class="wp-caption-text">张培梅墨书横幅。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803074" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803074" style="width: 380px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081534072378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803074" class="wp-caption-text">居正为展余题五言对联。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803083" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803083" style="width: 348px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081624202378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803083" class="wp-caption-text">汤化龙为炳堃题字对联《供石略存稽古意 养花都是爱才心 》。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>展品中尚有一代草圣于右任的“顶天立地 继往开来”对联，谭延闿草书王羲之十七帖四条屏，首义志士、书法家喻育之“楚天极目”横批。除书法外，还有武昌首义志士、画家蒋兰圃的松鹤图，武昌首义志士黄申芗的墨竹图，彭养光、曹亚伯所书扇面及向岩尺牍等艺术形式。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803089" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803089" style="width: 372px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081543122378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803089" class="wp-caption-text">黄侃给妹婿温楚珩题对联《北堂萱草常茂 南国桂树不凋》。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5803095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5803095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1412081540022378.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5803095" class="wp-caption-text">台湾国立<ahref="https://github.com/zztuup3178/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%88%B6%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E9%A6%86.md#1">国父纪念馆</a>2楼东、西文化艺廊隆重推出“剑胆琴心——辛亥名人翰墨展”，展期至12月21日止。（钟元翻摄/大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
