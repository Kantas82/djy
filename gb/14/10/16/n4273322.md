<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这8种水果不要空腹吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1408282154512483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水果要何时吃才正确？有些水果吃错时间反倒会影响我们的健康。(fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2014年10月16日讯】（大纪元记者李熙综合报导）为了养生美容，营养专家剖析饭前吃<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>，有助维生素吸收，且能控制食量；也有认为饭后再吃，不伤胃又可帮助消化。究竟要何时吃才正确？注意，有些水果吃错时间反倒会影响我们的健康，以下这8种水果最好不要空腹吃。</p>
<p><b>凤梨</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786456" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1406110853102483.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786456" title="凤梨。（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1406110853102483.jpg" alt="凤梨。（fotolia）" width="456" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786456" class="wp-caption-text">凤梨。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>凤梨的好处很多，它含有很强的凤梨酵素，能帮助肉类消化和吸收蛋白质，抗发炎的特性甚至比类固醇药物还要好，还能有效缓解关节疼痛和胃食道逆流不适的症状。但由于其蛋白分解酵素强，适合在饭后食用，若在饭前或两餐间吃的话会使胃壁受伤，不过不是每个人都适合吃，胃溃疡的患者不能吃。</p>
<p><b><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E6%A5%82.md#1">山楂</a></b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1409031705372483-600x402.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786464" title="山楂（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1409031705372483-600x402.jpg" alt="山楂（fotolia）" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786464" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E6%A5%82.md#1">山楂</a>（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山楂的酸味具行气消食作用，但若空腹食用，不仅耗气，且会增加饥饿感或产生胃痛。山楂含有大量的有机酸、果酸等，空腹食用，会使胃酸猛增，对胃黏膜造成不良刺激，使胃发胀满、泛酸。有胃炎、胃溃疡的人，更不适合空腹吃山楂。</p>
<p><b>香蕉</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1302130434042382-600x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786477" title="香蕉（Fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1302130434042382-600x600.jpg" alt="香蕉（Fotolia）" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786477" class="wp-caption-text">香蕉（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>香蕉含有大量的镁元素，如果空腹大量吃香蕉，会使血液中含镁量骤然升高，并且造成人体血液内镁与钙的比例失调，影响心血管收缩，造成心脏不适。早餐若空腹进食“大量”香蕉可能导致不适。</p>
<p>香蕉正确的方法为“吃定量的香蕉，且边吃边喝水”，利用其丰富的纤维素润滑肠胃，排除宿便。</p>
<p><b>番茄</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/140912133454100311-600x490.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786490" title="番茄（Fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/140912133454100311-600x490.jpg" alt="番茄（Fotolia）" width="600" b="490" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786490" class="wp-caption-text">番茄（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>番茄因含有大量的果胶、柿胶酚、可溶性收敛剂等成分，容易与胃酸发生反应，凝结成不易溶解的块状物，这些硬块可将胃的出口幽门堵塞，使胃里的压力升高，造成急性胃扩张而导致胃胀痛。</p>
<p><b><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E5%AD%90.md#1">橘子</a></b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5782729" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5782729" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407241410262525-600x542.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5782729" title="橘子含抗氧化剂成分，其含量名列所有柑橘类水果之首。（Fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407241410262525-600x542.jpg" alt="橘子含抗氧化剂成分，其含量名列所有柑橘类水果之首。（Fotolia）" width="600" b="542" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5782729" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E5%AD%90.md#1">橘子</a>含抗氧化剂成分，其含量名列所有柑橘类<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>之首。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>橘子汁液含有大量糖分和有机酸，空腹时吃橘子，会刺激胃黏膜，使胃胀泛酸。最好别空腹吃橘子。</p>
<p><b>甘蔗和荔枝</b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407041111512483-600x406.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786506" title="荔枝(fotolia)" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407041111512483-600x406.jpg" alt="荔枝(fotolia)" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786506" class="wp-caption-text">荔枝(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>甘蔗和荔枝因糖分含量较高，空腹食用最好不要过量，否则体内会因突然渗入过量高糖分而发生“高渗性昏迷”。</p>
<p><b><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%BF%E5%AD%90.md#1">柿子</a></b></p>
<figure id="attachment_5786521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5786521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1408081401112483-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-5786521" title="柿子（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1408081401112483-600x400.jpg" alt="柿子（fotolia）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5786521" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%BF%E5%AD%90.md#1">柿子</a>（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同番茄一样，柿子含有柿胶酚、果胶、鞣酸和鞣红素等物质，具很强的收敛作用，胃空时遇到较强的胃酸，易和胃酸结合成难以溶解的硬块，小硬块可随粪便排泄，但若结成大硬块，就易引起中医所称的“柿石症”。空腹食用容易引起恶心、呕吐等症状，严重的会造成胃溃疡甚至胃穿孔，最好在饭后食用。</p>
<p>此外，柿子含有单宁酸，会与铁结合，阻碍铁的吸收，患有缺铁性贫血的人不宜吃柿子。**</p>
