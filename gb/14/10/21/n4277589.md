<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：青海湖出现“九龙吸水”奇观</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2014年10月21日讯】（大纪元记者杨一帆综合报导）10月20日上午，<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%B5%B7%E6%B9%96.md#1">青海湖</a>出现9条“<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%90%B8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">龙吸水</a>”奇观，场面壮观。</p>
<p>据大陆《青海日报》报导，10月20日9时50分左右，<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%B5%B7%E6%B9%96.md#1">青海湖</a>海心山北侧先后出现9条大大小小“<ahref="https://github.com/zhhqsq3413/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%90%B8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">龙吸水</a>”奇观。白色水柱形成漩涡状，从天空直挂到湖面，景区游轮上的工作人员和游客目睹了这一“九龙吸水”奇观。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5789242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789242" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211122332436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789242" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5789245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789245" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211123042436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789245" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5789255" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789255" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211123572436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789255" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5789261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789261" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211124122436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789261" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>游轮上的工作人员称，两条间隔约千米形态壮观的“龙吸水”同时出现，观者惊叹。最初看到的两条“龙吸水”，一条较清晰，一条较模糊，较模糊的“龙吸水”从湖上慢慢消失时，在“龙吸水”消失的同一方向，再次出现一条清晰的“龙吸水”，此后不时有“龙吸水”继续再现，40多分钟内，海心山北侧共出现9条“龙吸水”。这些“龙吸水”有的呈纯白色，有的颜色较黑，场面壮观。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5789264" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789264" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211122502436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789264" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5789272" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789272" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211123162436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789272" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5789278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5789278" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1410211123432436.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5789278" class="wp-caption-text">10月20日上午，青海湖出现9条“龙吸水”奇观，场面壮观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>目击者们发现，“龙吸水”连接到湖面时，水柱像喷泉一样，往外喷射水滴。</p>
<p>中国传统文化信奉天人合一，网民们纷纷好奇：这“九龙吸水”奇观意味着什么？九龙戏水，何解？一些网民说：蛟化龙，天劫……这是天显异相，不是福就是祸。</p>
