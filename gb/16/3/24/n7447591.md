<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台阿里山樱花绽放 “樱王”盛开吸睛</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年03月24日讯】<span lang="zh-TW">台湾<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>花季</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;">10</span><span lang="zh-TW">日登场，<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B1%B1.md#1">阿里山</a>森林游乐区有大量游客涌入，位于阿里山工作站的“<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E7%8E%8B.md#1">樱王</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E9%87%8E%E6%A8%B1.md#1">吉野樱</a>近日满开，虽然天空飘着雨，仍吸引游客拍照留念。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">据</span>阿里山国家风景区官网介绍，每逢初春，满山遍野的<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>在阿里山区形成一片壮观的花海，让阿里山春天的樱花季成为一年一度不可错过的赏花盛事，吸引无数游客前来观赏。主要的花种包括山樱花、<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%89%E9%87%8E%E6%A8%B1.md#1">吉野樱</a>与八重樱等花种。在<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>树下漫步留影，真是无比浪漫的赏花雅趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7447603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603241401042378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7447603 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603241401042378-450x292.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山花季10日登场，位于阿里山工作站的“<ahref="https://github.com/dxvcno324/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E7%8E%8B.md#1">樱王</a>”吉野樱几乎满开，20日天空虽然飘着雨，仍吸引大量游客拍照留念。（嘉义林管处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7447628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603211236142378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7447628 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603211236142378-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿里山花季主角吉野樱，预估3月下旬才会绽放，被视为阿里山花季盛开指标的“樱王”，19日绽放7成左右。虽然飘着雨，仍有大量游客前往赏樱。（民众张素绫提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">在阿里山森林游乐区内以吉野樱数量最为庞大，多达一千九百多株。今年台湾冬季较冷，黄历新年前，阿里山还下过一场雪，因此阿里山花季主角吉野樱也开得比往年迟，</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;">3</span><span lang="zh-TW">月下旬才绽放，被视为阿里山花季盛开指标的“樱王”已经满开。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7447631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603241434212378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7447631 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603241434212378-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾阿里山花季10日登场，图为位于阿里山工作站的“樱王”吉野樱开4、5成左右时的照片。（阿里山国家森林游乐区）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7447635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603211244202378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7447635 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603211244202378-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿里山花季期间，阿里山公路沿线有多种樱花可观赏，阿里山公路近55公里处（隙顶路段）、私人栽种的数十株吉野樱，现在几乎满开，美得让人赞叹。</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="zh-TW">嘉义林管处表示，阿里山是台湾率先大规模栽植日本樱花的地方，最老植株是</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;">1903</span><span lang="zh-TW">年引进栽植，树龄已有</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;">110</span><span lang="zh-TW">多年，使得阿里山成为台湾的赏樱胜地。这棵“樱王”位于林务局阿里山工作站前，虽然是“老欉”（老树），但还是很会开花。 </span></p>
