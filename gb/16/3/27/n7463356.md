<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>乐与猫狗为伍 邵庭与法比欧带宠物走秀</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011571487-e1459089497315-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宠物沟通师杜莉德（左二）解读宠物的心声。左为主持人邵庭，右为法比欧。（中天提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年03月27日讯】台湾女星<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E5%BA%AD.md#1">邵庭</a>昨（26）日与法国男模<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%AF%94%E6%AC%A7.md#1">法比欧</a>分别带着猫狗等宠物上台走秀，为一起搭档的新节目宣传，不过动物们一上台后状况百出，逗得现场众人爆笑连连。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7463374" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011311487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7463374" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011311487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="373" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《宠物大联萌》主持人<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E5%BA%AD.md#1">邵庭</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%AF%94%E6%AC%A7.md#1">法比欧</a>。（中天提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邵庭和法比欧热爱小动物，平常也会在粉丝团上晒出和爱犬的合照，吸引许多粉丝关注。<span style="line-height: 1.5;">邵庭表示主持新节目《宠物大联萌》根本是天堂，但每次录完影回到家中就会被两只爱犬Uni和Una（双污）闻透透，笑称“它们觉得被背叛”，爱犬的反应让邵庭哭笑不得，直呼要跪着进门才行。</span></p>
<p>来自法国的型男模特儿法比欧走起台步更是驾轻就熟，连金刚鹦鹉也在他手臂上相当温驯，法比欧忍不住大赞它们“超专业”。现场也请来“宠物沟通师”杜莉德老师解读宠物们的心声，没想到西藏獒犬竟认为它和法比欧差不多帅，法比欧笑言下次也应该请老师解读一下自己的爱犬Luna平时都在想什么。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7463376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011521487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7463376" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011521487-600x587.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="440" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">象龟与邵庭走秀暴冲。（中天提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7463377" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011471487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7463377" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011471487-600x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">邵庭和宠物在台上走秀。（中天提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7463379" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011421487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7463379" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603271011421487-600x490.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="368" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法比欧和宠物走秀有一套。（中天提供）</figcaption></figure>
