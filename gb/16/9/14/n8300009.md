<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中秋疏运开跑 国道夜间免收费</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/219769-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中秋连假国道全线恢复夜间免收费，连假期间每晚11时至隔日清晨6时，皆可享此优惠。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年09月14日讯】（大纪元记者张原彰台湾台北报导）<span class="s1"><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md#1">中秋</a><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9E%E5%81%87.md#1">连假</a>到来，昨(14)日晚间11时起，<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%81%93.md#1">国道</a>全线恢复夜间免收费，<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%9E%E5%81%87.md#1">连假</a>期间每晚11时至隔日清晨6时，皆可享此优惠，高公局表示，今日</span><span class="s2"><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E9%81%93.md#1">国道</a>实施之交通疏导措施，包括国</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s2">南下</span><span class="s1">(</span><span class="s2">内湖至头份</span><span class="s1">)</span><span class="s2">、国</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">南下</span><span class="s1">(</span><span class="s2">木栅至香山</span><span class="s1">)</span><span class="s2">及国</span><span class="s1">5</span><span class="s2">南下</span><span class="s1">(</span><span class="s2">南港至坪林</span><span class="s1">)</span><span class="s2">高乘载管制、国</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s2">平镇系统交流道南下入口封闭、单一费率、差别费率、匝道仪控及开放路肩等</span><span class="s1">措施。</span></p>
<p><span class="s1">受台风影响，昨日晚间国道车流量较预估低，高公局表示，</span><span class="s2">截至</span><span class="s1">18</span><span class="s2">时，除国</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s2">北上林口至五股路段</span><span class="s1">，因</span><span class="s2">下班车潮</span><span class="s1">较</span><span class="s2">壅塞外，国道整体行车顺畅，截至下午</span><span class="s1">17</span><span class="s2">时，国道全线交通量为</span><span class="s1">47</span><span class="s2">百万车公里，</span><span class="s1">台</span><span class="s2">北高雄双向尖峰时段旅行时间均可维持在</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">小时内。</span></p>
<p><span class="s1">15日</span><span class="s2">预期有返乡及出游车潮，预估国道全线交通量为</span><span class="s1">105~125</span><span class="s2">百万车公里</span><span class="s1">，高公局表示，</span><span class="s2">上午车多路段，将出现在国</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s2">南下杨梅至头份系统、王田至员林、国</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s2">北上仁德系统至永康、圆山至汐止系统、国</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">南下新台五路至</span><span class="s3">南港系统、土城</span><span class="s2">至</span><span class="s3">关西、新竹系统至香山、乌日至雾峰、中兴系统至南投服务区、关庙服务区至燕巢系统、</span><span class="s2">国</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">北上安坑至</span><span class="s3">南港系统</span><span class="s2">及国</span><span class="s1">5</span><span class="s2">南下</span><span class="s3">南港</span><span class="s2">至</span><span class="s3">坪林</span><span class="s2">等路段。</span></p>
