<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>AirPods耳机未卖 配套挂绳现身淘宝</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月15日讯】苹果AirPods无线<ahref="https://github.com/mvnqfd3062/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%B3%E6%9C%BA.md#1">耳机</a>10月才上市，虽然价格不便宜，但已点燃果粉热情。由于外界预期AirPods容易丢失，随即在网上亮相各种防耳机丢失的挂绳、耳环等配件。大陆淘宝也随影随形，开始售出AirPods模型，外型酷似真AirPods。网民嘲讽：“<ahref="https://github.com/mvnqfd3062/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E5%AF%A8.md#1">山寨</a>版又出来捣乱了。”</p>
<p>苹果公司表示，AirPods取代了传统的3.5mm<ahref="https://github.com/mvnqfd3062/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%B3%E6%9C%BA.md#1">耳机</a>接口，AirPods不会轻易脱落，甚至跑步、听摇滚都没事，如果一对AirPods耳机丢了其中一个，苹果将会允许你单独购买这一个，而不必购买完整的一对。</p>
<p>但有人还是认为，由于AirPods并未预备任何安全措施，难以让人放心。尽管AirPods仍未发售，但淘宝网上已出现了各种AirPods防丢神器，如：防丢耳环、防丢挂绳等。防丢耳环是把耳机绑定在耳环，然后再把耳环套在耳朵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8302179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8302179" style="width: 232px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/119872dd707ac566ff29b647cdfaac63.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8302179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/119872dd707ac566ff29b647cdfaac63.jpg" alt="防丢耳环。（网络图片）" width="232" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8302179" class="wp-caption-text">防丢耳环。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有一款设计成女士专用的耳环，质料是金属，使用者要在耳垂打孔，把AirPods勾在耳环、耳环再勾在耳朵上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8302180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8302180" style="width: 440px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/7a23367fjw1f7n3l7fx24j20t70kugob.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8302180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/7a23367fjw1f7n3l7fx24j20t70kugob.jpg" alt="防丢耳环。（网络图片）" width="440" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8302180" class="wp-caption-text">防丢耳环。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外也有各种AirPods防丢绳，把耳机绑在绳子上、再挂在颈项上，价格不等，视乎质料而定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8302270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8302270" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/42c8e1aee808ada3591dd29f3722db03-450x182.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8302270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/42c8e1aee808ada3591dd29f3722db03-450x182.jpg" alt="防丢挂绳。（网络图片）" width="450" b="182" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8302270" class="wp-caption-text">防丢挂绳。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同时，大陆淘宝店竟有售卖AirPods模型，售价5元人民币，外型与真AirPods非常酷似。网民嘲讽：“<ahref="https://github.com/mvnqfd3062/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E5%AF%A8.md#1">山寨</a>版又出来捣乱了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8302173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8302173" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/c931d82f25cfe2f51a4976286ea1c7ce-450x279.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8302173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/c931d82f25cfe2f51a4976286ea1c7ce-450x279.jpg" alt="淘宝上出现AirPods模型，每一双卖5元，外型酷似真AirPods非常。（网络图片）" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8302173" class="wp-caption-text">淘宝上出现AirPods模型，卖5元人民币，外型酷似真AirPods非常。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苹果CEO 库克（Tim Cook）在接受《早安美国》的采访时说，自从他使用AirPods以来耳机还没有脱落过，库克称自己在跑步机上跑步时也使用无线耳机。库克认为，因为线是有重量的，会将耳机从耳朵中扯出来。有用户认为，丢失AirPods只是时间问题。</p>
