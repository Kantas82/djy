<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：《机械姬》林中宅 原为挪威山水酒店</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月22日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云编译报导）在获奥斯卡最佳视效奖的2015年电影《<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E6%A2%B0%E5%A7%AC.md#1">机械姬</a>》（Ex Machina，又译：人造意识、智能叛侣）中，神经质的高科技亿万富翁在林中豪宅内制造了一个具有人工智能的人形机器人。现实生活中，这座位于<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%AA%E5%A8%81.md#1">挪威</a>瓦尔河谷（Valldal）的豪宅并不是私宅，而是一座设计和景致都很奇特的山水式酒店。</p>
<p>在群山环绕、有瓦尔河流过的乡村里，看到设计现代的旅店<ahref="http://www.juvet.com/the-juvet-hotel/the-hotel" target="_blank">Juvet Landscape Hotel</a>，难免感到兀然，不过，入住Juvet的体验却与周围的自然环境完全分不开。从下面的图片，可以大致领略入住这座房价200美元一晚的酒店是怎样的感觉。</p>
<p>说是酒店，其实Juvet是散布在林中的9个木制客舱，据老板斯林宁（Knut Slinning）向媒体介绍，每个舱内可住两位客人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746552669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324277 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746552669-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324277" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>要去其中一些客舱，客人需走过急流上的这道钢索围栏的小桥。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324279" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747082669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324279 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747082669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324279" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每个客舱面积仅250平方英尺（约23平米），内设卧室、书房、起居室和浴室。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746272669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324282 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746272669-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324282" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>空间不大，客人却可在开阔的视野中享受美景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324290" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746352669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324290 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746352669-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324290" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了卫生间和卧室外，舱内每间客房都有落地玻璃窗，这些玻璃幕墙朝向不同方向，以最大限度地提高私密性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324289" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746322669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324289 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746322669-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324289" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下面是其中一间起居室，这是《<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%BA%E6%A2%B0%E5%A7%AC.md#1">机械姬</a>》男主角——计算机程序员迦勒?史密斯（Caleb Smith）和“科学怪人”内森?贝特曼（Nathan Bateman）博士第一次坐下聊天的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324294" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746382669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324294 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746382669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324294" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当两人和“机械姬”Iva说话，后者站在四面是透明玻璃的一个房间里。但现实中，所有的内墙玻璃都是磨砂的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324295" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746482669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324295 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746482669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324295" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>酒店浴室的色彩也比电影中的明快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324303" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746582669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324303 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746582669-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324303" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>室内装饰的其余部分相当低调，采用了混凝土和再生木材等天然材料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747052669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324305 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747052669-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324305" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于<ahref="https://github.com/acnndw3533/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%AA%E5%A8%81.md#1">挪威</a>西部的温度在冬季可降至冰点以下，每个客舱都有暖气和壁炉供客人取暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324301" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324301" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746452669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324301 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746452669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324301" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有一个公共舱，作为餐厅供所有客人使用，和电影中的功能一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747012669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324308 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211747012669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324308" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这座悬舱外40平米的木制露台上有一个热水浴盆，可以边泡澡，边俯瞰瓦尔河。影片中，贝特曼在浴盆边安了一个拳击袋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746412669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324309 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746412669-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324309" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>建筑师詹森和斯科特文（Jensen &amp; Skodvin）希望让酒店的设计与大自然融为一体。Juvet的大窗户确实实现了这一点，给客人带来独一无二的山水体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8324313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746512669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324313 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211746512669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324313" class="wp-caption-text">（Juvet Landscape Hotel官网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8324276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8324276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211752472669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8324276 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609211752472669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8324276" class="wp-caption-text">挪威山水酒店Juvet Landscape Hotel。(Fredrik Linge /Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
