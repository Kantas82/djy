<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>菜贩子教你15个买菜窍门</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/03/1212251027282382-e1473050444445.jpg" />
<h6>你会买菜吗？买菜也有窍门。(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月05日讯】在都市中长大的孩子，对蔬果的认识大部分人仅止于外观和名称，至于哪种甜？哪种新鲜？这种选购的诀窍，则显得很贫乏。今天就让<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%9C%E8%B4%A9.md#1">菜贩</a>子来教你买菜的窍门。</p>
<p><strong>以下整理15</strong><strong>种菜的选购窍门：</strong></p>
<p><strong>1.</strong><strong>马铃薯（<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E8%B1%86.md#1">土豆</a>）</strong></p>
<p>选购马铃薯时，尽量选圆的，没有破皮的，而且皮一定要干爽，泡过水的马铃薯，不易保存，口感也较差。</p>
<p>如果发现马铃薯外皮变绿，表示有毒生物碱存在，哪怕是很浅的绿色都不要食用。长出嫩芽的马铃薯也同样含有毒素，不宜食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268238" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1505022245352483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268238 size-medium" title="土豆（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1505022245352483-450x298.jpg" alt="土豆（fotolia）" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%9F%E8%B1%86.md#1">土豆</a>（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2.</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a></strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>的“眼睛”是判断老嫩的标准，眼睛越大，表示茄子越嫩，眼睛越小，表示越老。茄子的眼睛在茄子的萼片与果实连接的地方，有一白色略带淡绿色的带状环。</p>
<p>此外，用手握嫩茄子会有粘滞感，发硬的茄子是老茄子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1606091512302483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268277 size-medium" title="Long Eggplants isolated on white" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1606091512302483-450x295.jpg" alt="Long Eggplants isolated on white" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">茄子（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3.</strong><strong>辣椒</strong></p>
<p>辣椒品种有多种，尖辣椒较辣，果肉越薄，辣味越重。圆椒多为甜椒，果肉越厚越甜脆。半辣味椒则介于两者之间。</p>
<p>论营养，红椒营养成分比青椒来得高，但口感不若青椒脆嫩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/101015233351.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268284 size-medium" title="布碌仑植物园智利辣椒节" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/101015233351-450x338.jpg" alt="布碌仑植物园智利辣椒节" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">造型奇特的红﹑ 黄﹑绿﹑紫﹑青色辣椒。（大纪元图片库）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4.</strong><strong>黄瓜</strong></p>
<p>选黄瓜最好是带花的（花冠残存于脐部），瓜面要有光泽，且新鲜的瓜条是硬的，失水后就会变软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6491044" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/140912133442100311.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6491044 size-medium" title="黄瓜白色背景" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/140912133442100311-450x300.jpg" alt="黄瓜白色背景" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄瓜（大纪元图片库）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5.</strong><strong>山药</strong></p>
<p>选购山药，块茎的表皮是挑选的重点。选表皮光洁无异常斑点的，因为表皮有任何异常斑点，就表示已经感染病虫害。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5811190" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1411161939452483-e1460685696527.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5811190 size-medium" title="山药(fotolia)" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1411161939452483-450x300.jpg" alt="山药(fotolia)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">山药（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6.</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a></strong></p>
<p>当水果吃的<ahref="https://github.com/rlilro332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84.md#1">番茄</a>要选粉红色的，酸味淡；要料理用的则选大红色，这种番茄味浓郁，可增添料理风味。青番茄以及有“青肩”（果蒂部青色）的番茄，营养较低，而且含的番茄苷有毒性，建议不要买。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7467475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603202340431848.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7467475 size-medium" title="番茄" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603202340431848-450x300.jpg" alt="番茄" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">番茄 （孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7.</strong><strong>冬瓜</strong></p>
<p>一般生鲜超市卖的冬瓜都是切片的，可以用手指压冬瓜果肉，挑肉质致密的，这种冬瓜口感好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5715723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1211301215022382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5715723 size-medium" title="Wax gourd" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1211301215022382-450x265.jpg" alt="Wax gourd" width="450" b="265" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冬瓜（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>8.</strong><strong>萝卜</strong></p>
<p>选购萝卜，以根形圆整、表皮光滑为优，因为皮光的往往肉也较细，且掂在手里要沉甸甸的，中型偏小的萝卜比较紧密。叶色浓绿的不要买，说明生长期间干旱缺水，生长迟缓，粗纤维多，吃起来口感较差。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1605241524352483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268321 size-medium" title="三个白萝卜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1605241524352483-450x300.jpg" alt="三个白萝卜" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白萝卜（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>9.</strong><strong>芹菜</strong></p>
<p>新鲜芹菜叶身是平直的，叶子尖端翘起、叶子软，甚至发黄起銹斑的，表示已经不新鲜了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5848174" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1501181143372483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5848174 size-medium" title="新鲜的芹菜(fotolia)" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1501181143372483-450x300.jpg" alt="新鲜的芹菜(fotolia)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新鲜的芹菜(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>10.</strong><strong>冬笋</strong></p>
<p>新鲜的冬笋，它的壳会包的很紧。如果笋壳张开翘起，还有一股硫磺气味，表明它可能是硫磺熏过的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268328" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1603202340581848.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268328 size-medium" title="冬笋" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1603202340581848-450x300.jpg" alt="冬笋" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冬笋 （孙明国/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>11.</strong><strong>青菜</strong></p>
<p>青菜要看菜株（叶子）高矮，短的品质好，长的品质差，纤维多。叶柄颜色淡绿的青梗，味道浓郁，叶柄颜色近似白色的叫白梗，味道则较清淡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1209110632312382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268338 size-medium" title="bok choy" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1209110632312382-450x300.jpg" alt="bok choy" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白菜（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>12.</strong><strong>韭菜</strong></p>
<p>韭菜有宽叶韭和窄叶韭。宽叶韭香味清淡，窄叶韭香味较浓郁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8268342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1503301332142483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268342 size-medium" title="韭菜（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1503301332142483-450x298.jpg" alt="韭菜（fotolia）" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韭菜（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>13.</strong><strong>花菜</strong></p>
<p>选购花菜时，以花球周边未散开的最好，花球要洁白微黄、无异色、无毛花的为佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7293452" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/0912031414031848.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7293452 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/0912031414031848-450x484.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="484" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西兰花 (:明国 / 大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>14.</strong><strong>卷心菜</strong></p>
<p>卷心菜叶球要坚硬紧实，松散的表示包心不紧，叶球坚实，但顶部隆起，表示球内开始挑薹，食用风味变差，不要买。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5809063" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1411132218122483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5809063 size-medium" title="卷心菜（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1411132218122483-450x299.jpg" alt="卷心菜（fotolia）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卷心菜（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>15.</strong><strong>豆芽</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7738094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1107281112071839.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7738094 size-medium" title="糖醋豆芽菜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1107281112071839-450x300.jpg" alt="糖醋豆芽菜" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豆芽菜（江柏逸/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>选豆芽时，先闻闻有没有氨味，再看看有没有须根，如果发现有氨味和无须根的，可能是化学豆芽，建议不要买。@*</p>
