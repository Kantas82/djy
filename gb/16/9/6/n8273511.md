<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿帕奇直升机陷农田 奇努克飞来轻松解困</h2>
<a href="https://dgk92ykceobaf.cloudfront.net/N8aCqZctg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月06日讯】（大纪元记者张秉开编译报导）近日，美国一架<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%B8%95%E5%A5%87.md#1">阿帕奇</a><ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B4%E5%8D%87%E6%9C%BA.md#1">直升机</a>因故障在一片水稻田内紧急降落，之后这架王牌直升机被<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E5%8A%AA%E5%85%8B.md#1">奇努克</a>运输<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B4%E5%8D%87%E6%9C%BA.md#1">直升机</a>运走。</p>
<p>据《飞行家》（Aviationist）9月5日报导，8月30日，美国德州国民警卫队的一架AH-64D型<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/15/6/24/n4464257.md#1">阿帕奇</a>（Apache）攻击直升机发生故障，不得不紧急降落于休斯敦东部48公里（30英里）的一块水稻田内。</p>
<p>幸运的是，紧急降落非常成功，无人员伤亡。但是，这架发生故障的战地王牌攻击性直升机无法自行修复，只能等待救护队的到来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8273582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8273582" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-apache-04-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8273582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-apache-04-450x300.jpg" alt="发生事故的阿帕奇直升机。（视频截图）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8273582" class="wp-caption-text">发生事故的<ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%B8%95%E5%A5%87.md#1">阿帕奇</a>直升机。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8273583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8273583" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-apache-05.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-8273583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-apache-05.jpg" alt="奇努克准备起吊阿帕奇直升机。（视频截图）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8273583" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E5%8A%AA%E5%85%8B.md#1">奇努克</a>准备起吊阿帕奇直升机。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两天后，一架CH-47奇努克运输直升机飞来，用钢索将阿帕奇从田间吊起，运到26公里（16英里）外的德州贝敦机场（Baytown Airport）。</p>
<p>阿帕奇是世界著名的单旋翼攻击直升机，机身长约18米、高约4米，旋翼直径约15米，自重约5吨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6005375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6005375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/05/1105262103421680-450x313.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-6005375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/05/1105262103421680-450x313.jpg" alt="阿帕奇武装直升机。(Photo credit should read JUNG YEON-JE/AFP/Getty Images)" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6005375" class="wp-caption-text">阿帕奇武装直升机。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>1989年AH-64阿帕奇直升机投入实战以来，在巴拿马战役、海湾战争、阿富汗战争中发挥出极强的战斗力，曾摧毁几百辆伊拉克装甲车。</p>
<p>AH-64的最高时速293公里，升限6,400米，可以挂载AGM-114地狱火导弹、AIM-92刺针导弹、AGM-122佩枪式反辐射导弹、AIM-9响尾蛇导弹等武器。</p>
<p><a width="640" b="360" src=""></a><a href="https://dgk92ykceobaf.cloudfront.net/N8aCqZctg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tqbjzq3185/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tqbjzq3185/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/hL2dqzkGthE?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></a></p>
<p>据报导，对于吊载负荷可达11.8吨（2.6万磅）的奇努克运输机来说，运走阿帕奇是个轻松的任务。</p>
<p>奇努克是波音公司制造的双旋翼直升机，机身长约30米、高6米，旋翼直径约18米，具有全天候飞行能力，可在恶劣高温、高原气候下完成运输任务，具备空中加油能力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8273595" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8273595" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/CH-47FChinook-01-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8273595" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/CH-47FChinook-01-450x253.jpg" alt="奇努克直升机机队。（视频截图）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8273595" class="wp-caption-text">奇努克直升机机队。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8273596" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8273596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/CH-47FChinook-03-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8273596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/CH-47FChinook-03-450x253.jpg" alt="奇努克直升机。（视频截图）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8273596" class="wp-caption-text">奇努克直升机。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奇努克也能吊运其它飞机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8273563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8273563" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-A-7KCorsair-II-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8273563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/Chinook-A-7KCorsair-II-450x300.jpg" alt="奇努克运走A-7K海盗二式攻击机（Corsair II）（视频截图）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8273563" class="wp-caption-text">奇努克运走A-7K海盗二式攻击机（Corsair II）（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
