<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“女人我最大2016美妆大赏”颁奖 今晚登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609121003581487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>浩角翔起担任美妆大赏主持。（联意制作提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月12日讯】“<ahref="https://github.com/hxdsjt3959/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E4%BA%BA%E6%88%91%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7.md#1">女人我最大</a><ahref="https://github.com/hxdsjt3959/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2016%E7%BE%8E%E5%A6%86%E5%A4%A7%E8%B5%8F.md#1">2016美妆大赏</a>颁奖典礼”迈入第四年，颁奖典礼首度移师TVBS电视台棚内举行，也让平日隐身幕后的美妆品牌们，化身一日主角。典礼由“<ahref="https://github.com/hxdsjt3959/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E8%A7%92%E7%BF%94%E8%B5%B7.md#1">浩角翔起</a>”担纲主持，并邀请台湾女星曾佩慈与韩国新生代团体INX为活动献唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8291741" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291741" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821411487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8291741" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821411487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291741" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hxdsjt3959/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%A9%E8%A7%92%E7%BF%94%E8%B5%B7.md#1">浩角翔起</a>担任美妆大赏主持。（联意制作提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼主持人“浩角翔起”一首《万人迷》的劲歌热舞现身，正呼应了女人自信不仅在外表，内在的自我提升才更加令人着迷不已。</p>
<p>曾为星光歌手、目前正拍摄TVBS的原创概念剧《在一起，就好》的女主角－曾沛慈带来新歌演出当天，同剧中的男主角钟承翰也前来为女主角打气，意外为典礼增添温暖亮点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8291777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291777" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821451487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8291777" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821451487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291777" class="wp-caption-text">曾沛慈（左）与钟承翰正拍摄新戏《在一起》联袂出席颁奖。（联意制作提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来自韩国新生代团体INX更将美妆大赏列为台湾演出第一站，从手持玫瑰花对着台下女性观众深情演唱《小幸运》，再到台上展现韩团独有的唱跳功力，落落大方的表现令人印象深刻，成了全场瞩目焦点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8291780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821551487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8291780" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821551487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291780" class="wp-caption-text">韩国新生代团体INX深情演唱《小幸运》。（联意制作提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8291783" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291783" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821501487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8291783" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120821501487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291783" class="wp-caption-text">韩国新生代团体INX深情演唱《小幸运》。（联意制作提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年颁奖礼，《<ahref="https://github.com/hxdsjt3959/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E4%BA%BA%E6%88%91%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7.md#1">女人我最大</a>》支柱蓝心湄因事缺席，但也特地录制一段影片送给观众和品牌盟友，表示这13个年头，正因为观众的反应及回馈，才有更多发掘美丽新事物的动力，节目即将迈入第14年，她将持续带领观众和节目一起成长。</p>
<p>今晚（12日）将由彩妆师凯文、保养达人小布、美妆暖男Daniel、造型达人佑群、彩妆新锐Vincent、发妆设计师Tony一同为获奖品牌颁奖，为产品再度加分。今晚活动将由台湾与大陆多家网路媒体同步直播，而完整名单可详见节目官网。</p>
