<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>戈登美食啤酒屋Dan Gordon&#8217;s</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月12日讯】听到这一家餐厅的名字，大家应该都可以想到?Gordon Biersch，在硅谷也算是一个鼎鼎大名的自制<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92%E5%B1%8B.md#1">啤酒屋</a>，谁也忘不了它的几座招牌大啤酒桶，本店酿制、如假包换。今年换汤不换药，重新包装，再度登场。</p>
<p>餐厅全部精心装修，而且菜单也重新设计，已经不再是一个美式<ahref="https://github.com/vtmwhj347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%95%A4%E9%85%92%E5%B1%8B.md#1">啤酒屋</a>，而是一个美食啤酒。所以经由Eater International?评定及推荐，也算是2016?年硅谷15家新开张最夯餐厅之一。</p>
<p>餐厅的气氛十分大气及友善，高大的空间着实是为了配合那一座啤酒酿造桶。不管你要不要，先上一盘咸甜辣爆米花（免费），吃了再说！全新设计的菜单真的是佳肴美食，样样都想尝一口，先从小的来吧！</p>
<p>Share，其实就是前菜，我很喜欢吃朝鲜蓟（Artichoke），所以首先看到这一项烟熏烤朝鲜蓟（Catroville Smoked &#038; Grilled Artichoke），沾上特制酱料，再滴点柠檬汁，确实是美味无比，原来这一道菜可以用烤的，今天学了一招。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/01_Catroville-smoked-Grilled-artichoke.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291064"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/01_Catroville-smoked-Grilled-artichoke-450x338.jpg" alt="烟熏烤朝鲜蓟（Catroville Smoked &amp; Grilled Artichoke）。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291064" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291064" class="wp-caption-text">烟熏烤朝鲜蓟（Catroville Smoked &#038; Grilled Artichoke）。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>面包搭配烟熏三文鱼酱及牛油（Fresh Smoked NZ ORA King Salmon Rillettes）：这一道是十分特别的法国料理，熏过的三文鱼，经过处理后用一层厚厚的牛油保鲜，放在罐内密封，再冷藏。所以当罐子打开之后上面有一层厚厚的硬硬的牛油，牛油除去后底下就是光鲜亮丽的熏三文鱼酱。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291082" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291082" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/02_Fresh-smoked-NZ-ORA-king-salmon-Rillettes.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291082"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/02_Fresh-smoked-NZ-ORA-king-salmon-Rillettes-450x338.jpg" alt="。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291082" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291082" class="wp-caption-text">。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>牛油有三文鱼的味道，三文鱼也有牛油的香味，面包可以涂牛油吃，也可以涂三文鱼酱吃，美味无比。没有想到在美国啤酒屋还能够尝到法国料理，也算是惊艳吧！</p>
<p>烤花椰菜（Happy boy farms Roasted Coastal Cauliflower）：这一道菜有水准！先把花菜烤熟，再加上乾红梅、橘子片、柠檬片，样样都好吃。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291088" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/03_Happy-boy-farms-roasted-coastal-Cauliflower.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291088"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/03_Happy-boy-farms-roasted-coastal-Cauliflower-450x338.jpg" alt="烤花椰菜（Happy boy farms Roasted Coastal Cauliflower）。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291088" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291088" class="wp-caption-text">烤花椰菜（Happy boy farms Roasted Coastal Cauliflower）。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>烧烤辣汤（BBQ Ends Chili Soup）：这一道汤是重要的美国文化，又浓又辣，一碗吃下去，就好像吃完一大碗饭那么饱。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291092" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/04_BBQ-ends-chili-soup.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291092"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/04_BBQ-ends-chili-soup-450x338.jpg" alt="烧烤辣汤（BBQ Ends Chili Soup）。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291092" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291092" class="wp-caption-text">烧烤辣汤（BBQ Ends Chili Soup）。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>吃了这么多前菜，已经饱得差不多了，但是老远跑来不吃吃本店之宝，最有名的“拉猪肉”怎么能走呢？</p>
<p>拉猪肉（Coleman Ranch Pulled Pork Shoulder）：“拉猪肉”是花了12小时烹调，慢火料理而成，旁边配着大蒜薯条，伴着啤酒下肚，美食真要命！<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/05_Coleman-ranch-pulled-pork-shoulder.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291098"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/05_Coleman-ranch-pulled-pork-shoulder-450x338.jpg" alt="拉猪肉（Coleman Ranch Pulled Pork Shoulder）。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291098" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291098" class="wp-caption-text">拉猪肉（Coleman Ranch Pulled Pork Shoulder）。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>来到啤酒屋没有不喝啤酒的道理，样样都想喝，可是一杯这么大，一个人也喝不完。幸好有啤酒品酒单，于是我们选了7小杯试酒：6种德国啤酒以及1种美国啤酒。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8291103" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8291103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/06_sampler.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-8291103"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/06_sampler-450x338.jpg" alt="7种试饮啤酒。（大葱摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8291103" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8291103" class="wp-caption-text">7种试饮啤酒。（大葱摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>德国啤酒巴伐利亚风格（Gordon Biersch &#8211; German/Bavarian style）：<br />
1、Pilsner<br />
2、Golden Export<br />
3、Hefeweizen<br />
4、Dunkelweizen<br />
5、Maibock<br />
6、Blonde Bock<br />
美国啤酒（Dan Gordon’s &#8211; American style）<br />
7、Wildcide</p>
<p>感觉上啤酒很香，但是不烈，再加上吃了那么多食物，所以也没有觉得醉醺醺的感觉。这是一个感觉非常轻松的啤酒屋，大伙儿来这里十个八个都不嫌多，吃多吃少没关系，便宜够大碗，肯定饱着走人。</p>
