<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>毕书尽等13组歌手接力 一块为公益而唱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120346581487-400x400.jpg" />
<h6>毕书尽参与2016“CoCo Love一块发现爱★917台庆公益演唱会”。（红心字会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月12日讯】2016“CoCo Love一块发现爱★917台庆公益演唱会”10日晚上7点于台北ATT SHOW BOX举行，邀请TOP1男子汉、TPI CoCo Girls、朱俐静、池端玲名、艾怡良、吴思贤、李玉玺、<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BD%B3%E8%96%87.md#1">李佳薇</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>、陈彦允、廖文强、魏如昀（以上按姓氏笔画排列）等13组歌手轮番上阵献唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8291021" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120346251487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8291021" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609120346251487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小乐吴思贤参与2016“CoCo Love一块发现爱★917台庆公益演唱会”。（<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E5%BF%83%E5%AD%97%E4%BC%9A.md#1">红心字会</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当晚演唱会由甫入围金钟奖“戏剧节目新进演员奖”的吴思贤（小乐）揭开序幕。“老鹰家族”歌手“Bii”<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>、李玉玺、陈彦允与朱俐静各自带来拿手歌曲；创作歌手<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%91%A8%E5%85%B4%E5%93%B2.md#1">周兴哲</a>与艾怡良则献唱擅长的情歌；<ahref="https://github.com/boyqsg391/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BD%B3%E8%96%87.md#1">李佳薇</a>则带来充满爆发力的经典K歌。</p>
