<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>梁咏琪新歌温暖励志 郑元畅谢依霖相挺演出</h2>
<a href="https://d2cenl0crtmzsc.cloudfront.net/K7gDhqA2a"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/srzsma3440/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/srzsma3440/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>梁咏琪（中）新歌《陪伴》温暖励志，郑元畅（右）与谢依霖（左）相挺演出。（得意智文创提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月01日讯】有鉴于前阵子香港有许多中、小学生自杀的案件，<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%92%8F%E7%90%AA.md#1">梁咏琪</a>特别推出温暖励志的新歌《陪伴》，由善于关怀社会议题的金奖新锐导演柯汶利执导，并邀请<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E5%85%83%E7%95%85.md#1">郑元畅</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BE%9D%E9%9C%96.md#1">谢依霖</a>友情跨刀演出。MV中呈现三个因陪伴而获得救赎的故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8257530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8257530" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259361487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8257530" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259361487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8257530" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%92%8F%E7%90%AA.md#1">梁咏琪</a>（中）新歌《陪伴》温暖励志，<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E5%85%83%E7%95%85.md#1">郑元畅</a>（右）与<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E4%BE%9D%E9%9C%96.md#1">谢依霖</a>（左）相挺演出。（得意智文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑元畅饰演因意外丧妻而悲伤的深情男子，最终发现妻子的爱并不因逝世而消失；谢依霖则饰演被丈夫家暴 ，但结局选择相信自己迈向光明的家庭主妇。而Gigi演出一位女老师，温柔陪伴着压力过大的学生找到自己。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8257533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8257533" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259311487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8257533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259311487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8257533" class="wp-caption-text">梁咏琪新歌《陪伴》MV画面。图为郑元畅。（得意智文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MV 选角之初，Gigi第一个想到由阳光暖男代表的郑元畅演出失去挚爱的深情男；谢依霖则是在实境综艺节目其自然不做作的表现让Gigi印象深刻，虽然MV剧情设定三人分饰不交错的故事平行线，但Gigi仍趁着空档前往探班表达感谢之意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8257540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8257540" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259401487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8257540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259401487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8257540" class="wp-caption-text">梁咏琪新歌《陪伴》MV画面。图为谢依霖。（得意智文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8257541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8257541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259561487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8257541" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609010259561487-600x411.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8257541" class="wp-caption-text">梁咏琪（右）新歌《陪伴》MV画面。（得意智文创提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尤其Gigi到台湾后迷上FB直播功能，探班时不忘打开直播带粉丝们即时目睹现场，粉丝们不仅开心抢先看到三人现身于同一画面，更得知是为拍摄Gigi《你不是一个人》MV，都感到相当兴奋。</p>
<p>曾合作过的Gigi与郑元畅在镜头前话叙家常，郑元畅更称Gigi是他的“微博启蒙老师”；谢依霖与郑元畅则因为合作大陆综艺节目《非凡搭档》结缘；只是三人同处镜头前，Gigi和郑元畅两长人身高，让较娇小的谢依霖大呼“镜头只拍到我头皮了啦！”惹来笑声不断。</p>
