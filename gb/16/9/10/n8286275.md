<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>广东湛江海面惊现“龙吸水”奇观</h2>
<a href="https://d3a3umoypakbtm.cloudfront.net/K5iCKYXum"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/falcnk3242/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/falcnk3242/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月10日讯】（大纪元记者洪宁报导）9月7日中午，广东湛江观海长廊海面突然出现时间长达十多分钟的海上<ahref="https://github.com/falcnk3242/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99.md#1">龙</a>卷风，白色水柱形成漩涡状，从天空直挂到湖面。民间称此天象为“<ahref="https://github.com/falcnk3242/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%90%B8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">龙吸水</a>”。</p>
<p>白色水柱形成漩涡状，从天空直挂到湖面。很多游客目睹了这一奇景，并纷纷用手机和照相机记录，奇特的景观让人迷醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8286288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8286288" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1-54-e1473505157779.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8286288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1-54-e1473505157779.jpg" alt="“龙吸水”奇观。（网络图片）" width="300" b="485" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8286288" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/falcnk3242/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99.md#1">龙</a>吸水”奇观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8286292" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8286292" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/3-25-e1473505207956.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8286292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/3-25-e1473505207956.jpg" alt="“龙吸水”奇观。（网络图片）" width="300" b="537" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8286292" class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="https://github.com/falcnk3242/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%90%B8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">龙吸水</a>”奇观。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网民将现场拍的视频上传网络，引发热议：“好壮观！”“大自然奇观万物生好美！”“这真是龙在吸水吗？”</p>
<p>还有网民表示，自己见过真龙。</p>
<p>“怎扰i”说：“我记得那是小时候下午2点多的时候，那天全黑完了，还刮着大风，天上有几条龙在那飞来飞去。”</p>
