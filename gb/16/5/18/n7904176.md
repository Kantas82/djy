<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>清除毒素 4种食物非吃不可</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年05月18日讯】（大纪元记者张孟平报导）你清楚自己身体内藏有多少<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E7%B4%A0.md#1">毒素</a>吗？根据美国疾病控制和预防中心的统计资料显示，每个人体内循环着140多种有毒化学物质。这么多的毒素，要如何快速从体内排除？</p>
<p>美国《医学日报》介绍几种有解毒功能的食物，能有效清除全身的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%92%E7%B4%A0.md#1">毒素</a>。</p>
<p><strong>苹果?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_6912796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6912796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/0912191321421892.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6912796 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/0912191321421892-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6912796" class="wp-caption-text">苹果（连震黎/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新鲜的水果经消化后会使血液呈碱性，有助体内毒素的代谢，并从尿液排出。苹果含有丰富的果胶，这种可溶性膳食纤维，能避免食物在肠内腐化，所含的半乳糖醛酸能从人体系统中去除食品添加剂和金属残留物，是很好的肠道<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E6%AF%92.md#1">排毒</a>水果。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%8F%9C.md#1">甜菜</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_5829798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5829798" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1411091454172483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5829798 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1411091454172483-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5829798" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%8F%9C.md#1">甜菜</a>和甜菜根汁(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>甜菜含有丰富的甜菜碱，具有抑制血中脂肪、协助肝脏细胞再生与解毒的功能，是血液净化器，有助于红血球的恢复与再生，同时提供人体新鲜的氧气。甜菜对于肝脏的毒素，如黄疸、肝炎、食物中毒、腹泻和呕吐等胆汁类疾病都有缓解作用。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C.md#1">姜</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7409168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7409168" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1502152201162483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7409168 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1502152201162483-450x331.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7409168" class="wp-caption-text">生<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C.md#1">姜</a>茶。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>生姜中的姜辣素能刺激消化道，帮助排出宿便、排出毒素，启动机体新陈代谢。姜辣素还有抗血小板凝集和抗发炎的功效，能预防血栓的发生，并促进血液循环。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1.md#1">洋葱</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7906046" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7906046" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605061540512483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7906046 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605061540512483-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7906046" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1.md#1">洋葱</a>沙拉。.(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>洋葱能吸收污染食品中的砷、镉、铅、汞、锡等有毒金属元素。所含的硒能和汞结合排出体外：所含的硫化物，也就是洋葱的气味成分，可使肝脏的解毒酵素充分发挥作用；所含的檞黄素可结合铝等毒素排出体外，与谷胱甘肽都有促进脂肪代谢的功效。</p>
<p>每个人身上都藏有毒素，学习如何调整生活作息时间、培养正确的饮食习惯、维持好心情以及多运动等，都能帮助体内各器官组织<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E6%AF%92.md#1">排毒</a>，慢慢恢复原有的身体机能。</p>
