<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“AOA”健美形象回归 雪炫等成员曝新照</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040255331487-e1462345112615-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国女团AOA成员雪炫（由左至右顺时针）、珉娥、酉奈。（华纳／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月04日讯】韩国女团“AOA”（智珉、草娥、<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%89%E5%A5%88.md#1">酉奈</a>、惠晶、<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%89%E5%A8%A5.md#1">珉娥</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E7%82%AB.md#1">雪炫</a>、澯美）最近变身为海上救援队的形象，宣告5月16日携第四张迷你专辑《Good Luck》回归。新专辑的封面照和MV已在关岛完成拍摄，AOA将展示变身海上救援队员的迷人风貌。</p>
<p>“AOA”以《迷你裙》的OL装扮、《Like a Cat 》的猫女，以及《怦然心动》的曲棍球选手等不同风格获得了高人气，尤其去年的夏日热曲《怦然心动》在火热的女团热风中展现出独特的存在感，时隔11个月发表的新专辑再次诠释《怦然心动》，将“AOA”独特充满活力的健康美充分表现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7801224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7801224" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040233541487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7801224" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040233541487-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7801224" class="wp-caption-text">韩国女团AOA成员<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E7%82%AB.md#1">雪炫</a>。（华纳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7801227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7801227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040230431487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7801227" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040230431487-600x365.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7801227" class="wp-caption-text">韩国女团AOA成员<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%89%E5%A5%88.md#1">酉奈</a>。（华纳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的第四张迷你专辑《Good Luck》将推出“WEEK”和“WEEKEND”两种版本，将通过展现“AOA”健康及专业的海上救援队形象的“WEEK”，以及享受周末活泼形象的“WEEKEND”两种版本，可以看到团员们多样化的表现。</p>
<p>日前“AOA”所属经纪公司FNC Entertainment透过官方预告网站率先公开了成员雪炫三人在《Good Luck》专辑“WEEK”版本风格的照片，照片中成员们以亮眼的海边为背景，红色的泳衣和白色热裤展现了气场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7801231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7801231" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040230461487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7801231" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040230461487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7801231" class="wp-caption-text">韩国女团AOA成员<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%89%E5%A8%A5.md#1">珉娥</a>。（华纳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外四名成员智珉、草娥、惠晶、澯美的“WEEK”风格照片，4日也将在官方预告网站公开，“AOA”全员正在加紧5月16日回归的准备。新辑中，海上救援队形象的回归预告，已经引起歌迷们关注跟期待，而AOA韩语迷你4辑《Good Luck》即将在台发行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7801746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7801746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040530491487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7801746" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040530491487-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7801746" class="wp-caption-text">韩国女团AOA成员澯美。（华纳提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7801747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7801747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040533381487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-7801747" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605040533381487-600x429.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7801747" class="wp-caption-text">韩国女团AOA成员智珉。（华纳提供）</figcaption></figure>
