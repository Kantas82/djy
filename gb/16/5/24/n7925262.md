<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：荷兰水乡羊角村的小桥流水人家</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者韩婕编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E5%85%B0.md#1">荷兰</a><ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%A7%92%E6%9D%91.md#1">羊角村</a>（Giethoorn）又称绿色<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E5%B0%BC%E6%96%AF.md#1">威尼斯</a>，也就是乡村版的<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B9%A1.md#1">水乡</a>，如同古早中国的南方水城，交通靠水运。位于<span id="result_box" class="short_text" lang="zh-TW"><span class="">艾瑟尔</span></span>（Overijssel）省，距省会<span id="result_box" class="short_text" lang="zh-TW"><span class="">斯滕韦克</span></span>（Steenwijk）西南五公里远的<span id="result_box" class="short_text" lang="zh-TW">德<span class="">威登</span></span>自然保护区（De Wieden）<span id="result_box" class="short_text" lang="zh-TW"></span>内。</p>
<p>冰河时期，<span id="result_box" class="short_text" lang="zh-TW">德<span class="">威登</span></span>正好位在两个冰碛带之间，地势低洼造成土壤贫瘠且泥炭沼泽遍布，除了芦苇与薹属植物外，唯一的资源则是地底下的泥煤。居民为了生计以挖泥媒为生，不断开凿的结果，将土地挖掘成一道道的狭窄沟渠。后因运输需求，将沟渠拓宽，而扩大成运河交通网。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7925423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7925423" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr01-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7925423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr01-450x338.jpg" alt="荷兰羊角村" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7925423" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E5%85%B0.md#1">荷兰</a><ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%A7%92%E6%9D%91.md#1">羊角村</a>的小桥流水人家（Bert Knot / 维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7925474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7925474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr05-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7925474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr05-450x338.jpg" alt="荷兰羊角村,水乡" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7925474" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰羊角村的小桥流水人家（Bobil / 维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7925477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr03-450x249.jpg" alt="Giethoorn_Netherlands_flckr03" width="450" b="249" /></p>
<p>羊角村曾经是一个没有道路的地区，但自从1958年荷兰制片人在此拍了一个喜剧片后，声名大噪，村里增加了许多自行车道。现在只剩老城区仍以水路为主。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7925481" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7925481" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn-bridge-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-7925481 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn-bridge-300x400.jpg" alt="荷兰羊角村,水乡" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7925481" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰羊角村（giethoorn官网）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7925483" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7925483" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Bridge_in_Giethoorn-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-7925483 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Bridge_in_Giethoorn-300x400.jpg" alt="荷兰羊角村,水乡" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7925483" class="wp-caption-text">荷兰羊角村（CrazyPhunk / 维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然，羊角村出名后许多观光客到访，但旅游业对当地的传统影响不大。这里也有“北方<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E5%B0%BC%E6%96%AF.md#1">威尼斯</a>”、“荷兰威尼斯”的称号，长约7公里小村庄有超过180座拱桥，所以要“路游”这座世外桃园般的村庄也可行。</p>
<p>它是如此的宁静，漂亮的芦苇屋顶，没有吵杂的商业氛围，顶多是几声鸭叫或鸟叫，连运河里行走的船也都是“静音的”。</p>
<p>村里几乎家家户户都是芦苇屋顶，门前的花园、绿树、垂柳、拱桥，令人心旷神怡。</p>
<p>老天常常与人开玩笑，当初的挖媒工人无钱盖砖瓦房，家家户户只好就地取材，用芦苇建房舍，如今芦苇的价格为砖瓦的几十倍，住砖瓦房的人反成为穷人。不过，现今的“穷人”也是高收入所得人家，原先的挖媒工人已搬离现今的豪宅区了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7925480" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7925480" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn-boat-sailing-450x269.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7925480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/Giethoorn-boat-sailing-450x269.jpg" alt="荷兰羊角村,水乡" width="450" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7925480" class="wp-caption-text">扬帆于荷兰羊角村村外的湖泊上（giethoorn官网）</figcaption></figure>
<p>明明是<ahref="https://github.com/yzovrq360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B9%A1.md#1">水乡</a>，为何会称为羊角村呢？原来在13世纪时，挖媒工人曾在这里挖出许多因第10世纪洪水残留的野山羊角，因此村庄就以羊角命名。</p>
<p>想享受中国民初的小桥流水人家气氛，可到此一游，享受静谧的乡村美景。事实上，这里也是中国游客的最爱，村庄中仅有2,600多居民，但每年却有15万到20万的中国游客到访。</p>
