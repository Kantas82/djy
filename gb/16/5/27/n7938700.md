<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>意外的艺术：连艺术家也会惊叹的生活之美</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年05月28日讯】对你来说艺术是什么呢？其实<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB.md#1">生活</a>中处处都见得到艺术的身影，一些艺术就躲在你我身边，甚至就在不经意间呢！这种偶然的艺术才更显弥足珍贵，来看看下面的几张照片，你一定也会为之赞叹的。</p>
<p>1.还未搅拌的颜料桶，竟然美得似<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E7%A9%BA.md#1">星空</a>、似瀑布，也似<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%B4%8B.md#1">海洋</a>。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-7946276 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-11-450x381.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="381" /></p>
<p>2.不小心掉落的一滴墨汁，竟然形成了公鹿的<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E5%BD%B1.md#1">剪影</a>。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-1-450x546.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-1" width="450" b="546" /></p>
<p>3.喝掉最后一口咖啡，杯底竟然出现了一片森林。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-4-450x600.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-4" width="450" b="600" /></p>
<p>4.倒掉用完的颜料后，水池中出现了奇景。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-9-450x338.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-9" width="450" b="338" /></p>
<p>5.正在调颜料，突然看到了山峰，也有点像富士山耶。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-5-450x600.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-5" width="450" b="600" /></p>
<p>6.狗狗，你是不是选错了坐下的位置？还是说你是故意的……</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946271" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-7-450x371.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-7" width="450" b="371" /></p>
<p>7.不知道这是什么？其实这是一间底特律的旧车厂汽车油漆的抛面层。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946272" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-10-450x507.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-10" width="450" b="507" /></p>
<p>8.色彩的花朵，<ahref="https://github.com/trdaqu3782/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%96.md#1">意外</a>地惊艳了时光。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-2-450x338.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-2" width="450" b="338" /></p>
<p>9.洗车时的泡沫，竟然呈现出了泡泡糖般的色彩。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-6-450x660.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-6" width="450" b="660" /></p>
<p>10.颜料洒在了地板上，这这这……我要把它直接拿去艺术馆。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7946268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/examples-of-accidental-art-8-450x600.jpg" alt="examples-of-accidental-art-8" width="450" b="600" /></p>
<p>11.运送涂料的车翻了，竟然形成了一幅画！好吧，司机一定没什么心情欣赏这个。</p>
