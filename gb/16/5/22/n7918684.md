<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>广东暴雨200年一遇 致55万人受灾8死4失踪</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月22日讯】20日到21日，广东省遭遇极端罕见的<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%BA%E9%99%8D%E9%9B%A8.md#1">强降雨</a>袭击，重灾区茂名市信宜市（茂名市下辖县级市）成为本次强降雨的“雨窝”，６小时雨量达429．5毫米，官方称此次强降雨“超200年一遇”。目前，茂名市出现的特大<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%B4%E9%9B%A8.md#1">暴雨</a>已造成23个镇55万多人受灾，其中8人死亡、4人失踪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7918975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7918975" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/850d285egw1f4497xh4tuj20i80i80w2-300x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-7918975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/850d285egw1f4497xh4tuj20i80i80w2-300x300.jpg" alt="广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7918975" class="wp-caption-text">广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%BA%E9%99%8D%E9%9B%A8.md#1">强降雨</a>袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>广东省防总称，5月20日凌晨到21日傍晚，广东出现了今年以来最强降水，粤北、粤西和珠三角市县普降<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9A%B4%E9%9B%A8.md#1">暴雨</a>到大暴雨，茂名、阳江和深圳的部分地方还出现了特大暴雨。重灾区茂名信宜市成为本次强降雨的“雨窝”，6小时雨量达429.5毫米，超200年一遇，刷新了日雨量历史极值，强降雨还引发市内山洪暴发，<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E6%B0%B4.md#1">洪水</a>漫堤，造成严重灾情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7918957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7918957" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zka3dj20j60cmmyd-300x197.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-7918957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zka3dj20j60cmmyd-300x197.jpg" alt="广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）" width="300" b="197" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7918957" class="wp-caption-text">广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E6%B0%B4.md#1">洪水</a>造成信宜山塘水库多处受损，公路交通多处中断，镇隆境内的一座桥梁坍塌。市区内涝严重，部分街道水深至齐腰部，甚至淹没轿车车顶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7918961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7918961" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zbywaj20de0hwjsu-300x401.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-7918961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zbywaj20de0hwjsu-300x401.jpg" alt="广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）" width="300" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7918961" class="wp-caption-text">广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据茂名市官方初步统计，今次强降雨造成茂名市23个镇，其中信宜19个镇、高州4个镇共55万多人受灾，其中信宜542,000人、高州15,421人受灾；死亡8人、失踪4人，全部为信宜居民。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7918962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7918962" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zvffgj20j60ef75e-300x226.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-7918962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zvffgj20j60ef75e-300x226.jpg" alt="广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）" width="300" b="226" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7918962" class="wp-caption-text">广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据报导，截至21日晚，广东本次强降雨过程基本结束。同时，广西、江西也遭受暴雨袭击，房屋倒塌、农田受淹，江西受灾人数亦有28万余人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7918958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7918958" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zguc2j20j60crmy1-300x200.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-small wp-image-7918958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/60718250jw1f43r1zguc2j20j60crmy1-300x200.jpg" alt="广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）" width="300" b="200" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7918958" class="wp-caption-text">广东省近日遭遇极端罕见的强降雨袭击，茂名市信宜市成重灾区。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，海南目前却有13个市县局部出现不同程度的旱情。#</p>
