<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>黄美珍术后首献“声” 为公益活动站台</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080112372560-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>歌手黄美珍5月7日为伊甸公益活动站台演出。（伊甸基金会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月08日讯】歌手<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E7%BE%8E%E7%8F%8D.md#1">黄美珍</a>昨（7日）晚担任伊甸公益大使，在母亲节前夕为慢飞天使（发展迟缓儿）献“声”祝福。这也是黄美珍在听力受损手术后的第一场公益演出，她现场演唱两首歌，清亮动人的歌声完全不受手术影响，让台下民众听得如痴如醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7873019" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7873019" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080112442560.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7873019 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080112442560-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7873019" class="wp-caption-text">歌手<ahref="https://github.com/mfycig3823/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E7%BE%8E%E7%8F%8D.md#1">黄美珍</a>出席在伊甸公益活动时表示，听力在手术后已经逐渐恢复。（伊甸基金会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自选秀节目《超级星光大道》出道的黄美珍，23年来饱受因耳压不平衡而晕眩、抽痛，耳朵还会发炎、流脓血的痛苦，多年来她隐瞒家人、好友，直到与丈夫张瑞哲被民众直击前往医院，她才透露因儿时玩水意外导致左耳几乎失聪，多年来听力仅剩2成。黄美珍藏在心里的秘密才曝光。</p>
<p>黄美珍上（4）月进行手术，术后在耳后留下5公分伤疤，麻药消退后，她痛得崩溃大哭，不过因为看到隔壁病房的“八仙尘爆”伤患艰难的复健、换药过程，让黄美珍坚强起来，并在住院期间不断鼓励女病患，为她祷告、打气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7873020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7873020" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080113002560.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7873020 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080113002560-300x478.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="478" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7873020" class="wp-caption-text">黄美珍4月中旬进行“左侧鼓室成形”手术，4月24日在脸书向大家报告“开刀顺利”。（翻摄自黄美珍脸书）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7873021" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7873021" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080113042560.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7873021 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080113042560-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7873021" class="wp-caption-text">黄美珍在脸书向大家报告“开刀顺利”。（翻摄自黄美珍脸书）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昨晚是黄美珍手术后的第一场公益演出，现场演唱两首歌，以实际行动支持台湾身处于医疗、教育、生活资源贫瘠的偏乡发展迟缓儿，能够获得足够疗育资源，以免错过疗育时机。她清亮动人的歌声完全不受手术影响，让台下民众听得如痴如醉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7873022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7873022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080112512560.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7873022 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605080112512560-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7873022" class="wp-caption-text">孩子献花给妈妈。（伊甸基金会提供）</figcaption></figure>
