<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>罗东不老棒球队成立  老少棒友谊赛开打</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195421_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗东不老棒球队与竹林少棒队合影。 （谢月琴／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年05月03日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E4%B8%9C.md#1">罗东</a>镇于5月3日假竹林国小棒球场<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%90%E7%AB%8B.md#1">成立</a>“老懂”<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E8%80%81%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83%E9%98%9F.md#1">不老棒球队</a>，并首次与竹林国小少棒队进行<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8B%E8%B0%8A%E8%B5%9B.md#1">友谊赛</a>。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E4%B8%9C.md#1">罗东</a>镇今年在弘道老人福利基金会的协助下，<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%90%E7%AB%8B.md#1">成立</a>“老懂”<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8D%E8%80%81%E6%A3%92%E7%90%83%E9%98%9F.md#1">不老棒球队</a>，在大家的奔走努力下，邀集社区43名65岁以上长者参与，长者年纪加总超过千岁。他们固定在罗东垒球场集训练习。</p>
<p>弘道基金会主任钟智馨表示：不老棒球队在全国共有13支，其中宜兰就占了6支，宜兰虽起步较慢，但动作很快，每年两支球队，真不错。希望宜兰好山好水外，也要“好健康”！</p>
<figure id="attachment_7798537" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7798537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195422_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7798537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195422_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="罗东不老棒球队成立，林姿妙镇长授旗。（谢月琴／大纪元） " width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7798537" class="wp-caption-text">罗东不老棒球队成立，林姿妙镇长授旗。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天罗东“老懂”不老棒球队成军大会上，首先由弘道基金会主任钟智馨赠送头盔，球具给老懂不老棒球队，罗东镇长林姿妙主持授旗仪式，随后由林姿妙镇长担任投手，许俊仁镇民代表担任捕手，罗东镇民代表会主席杨振益担任打击手，举行简单隆重的开球典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7798538" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7798538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195423_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7798538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195423_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="弘道基金会致赠球具后老少棒队合影。（谢月琴／大纪元） " width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7798538" class="wp-caption-text">弘道基金会致赠球具后<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E5%B0%91%E6%A3%92.md#1">老少棒</a>队合影。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>罗东镇“老懂”不老棒球队，成军后首次与竹林国小少棒队举行<ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8B%E8%B0%8A%E8%B5%9B.md#1">友谊赛</a>，光是暖身，就消耗老棒不少体力，为了这一天，长者可是特地抽空练习，全身酸痛，但个个很开心，体力当然比不上竹林小将，这一仗打的是斗志，打的是老少之间对棒球的热爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7798539" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7798539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195425_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7798539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/195425_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="老少棒队友谊赛开打。（谢月琴／大纪元） " width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7798539" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/huihjg380/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E5%B0%91%E6%A3%92.md#1">老少棒</a>队友谊赛开打。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
