<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>紫藤花开 日本名花紫雾盎然</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6832-e1462251708981-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>紫藤开花！爱花人赞为世界美景之一。（可容／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年05月03日讯】<span style="font-size: large;"><ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E5%90%8D%E8%8A%B1.md#1">日本名花</a>紫</span><span style="font-size: large;">藤开花受到许多人青睐，爱花人赞为世界美景之一。邻接日本东京都的琦玉县春日部市的“<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%B2%9B%E4%B9%8B%E8%97%A4.md#1">牛岛之藤</a>”园中四月底、五月初已经开满了紫色的<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%97%A4.md#1">紫藤</a>花。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: large;">“<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%B2%9B%E4%B9%8B%E8%97%A4.md#1">牛岛之藤</a>”位在春日部的牛岛，这里以一千二百岁的<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%97%A4.md#1">紫藤</a>木闻名。</span><span style="font-family: Liberation Serif,serif;"><span style="font-size: large;"><span lang="en-US">1955</span></span></span><span style="font-size: large;">年被日本政府列为国家指定的特别天然纪念保存木。暮春时节，园内约二千平方米的藤架上，开满紫色的紫藤花。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7797067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797067 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6842-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6842" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛岛之藤是日本琦玉县的紫藤名园（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797068 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6823-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6823" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”园中一千二百岁的<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%97%A4%E6%9C%A8.md#1">紫藤木</a>。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797090" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797090 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6831-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6831" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">紫藤花紫雾盎然。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: large;">“牛岛之藤”早期是个古寺院，以古典的日本庭院为原形，庭院中有中岛、池、流水的布置。园中花木以紫藤为主，有一棵一千二百岁的<ahref="https://github.com/tedesu344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B4%AB%E8%97%A4%E6%9C%A8.md#1">紫藤木</a>，和几棵几百岁的紫藤木。仅仅七、八棵的紫藤木一开花，花团串串，满溢二千平<span id="transmark"></span>方米的庭园。枝条上紫蝶儿般的花朵数不胜数宛如紫雾，舞动暮春。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7797091" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797091 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6833-450x600.jpg" alt="IMG_6833" width="450" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">紫藤花舞动春天紫色一帘幽梦。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797092" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797092 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6847-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6847" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">紫藤花可爱的花形如蝶。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797094" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797094 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6871-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6871" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">暮春时节，紫藤花邀游人舞动春天。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797093 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6867-450x600.jpg" alt="IMG_6867" width="450" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">紫藤花，舞动春天最后的炫彩。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797095 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6881-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6881" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花棚是紫藤花的舞台。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797084 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6869-450x600.jpg" alt="“牛岛之藤”紫藤花舞动春天。（可容／大纪元）" width="450" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”紫藤花舞动春天。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: large;">园中还有菖蒲园、五、六百岁的红松。另外来自富士山的熔岩、奇石的造景，构成“牛岛之藤”优雅的逸趣。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7797130" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797130 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6907-450x338.jpg" alt="“牛岛之藤”古典的日本庭院。（可容／大纪元）" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”古雅的日本庭院，五百岁红松一木擎天。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797134" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797134 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6780-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”的中岛。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797135" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797135 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6887-450x338.jpg" alt="富士山熔岩" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取自富士山的熔岩造景。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797138" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797138 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6895-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6895" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">五百岁红松。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: large;">紫藤花在日本关东、九州数量硕大的花开景致，已经吸引全世界追寻美景者的注目。开花时节落在二十四节气的谷雨后、立夏前。紫藤花开表示春天近尾声了！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7797150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797150 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6919-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6919" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”大门口。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7797147" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797147 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6942-450x338.jpg" alt="IMG_6942" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小小“牛岛之藤”车站前墙壁彩绘。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: large;"> ＊“牛岛之藤”位于琦玉县春日部市牛岛</span><span style="font-family: Liberation Serif,serif;"><span style="font-size: large;"><span lang="en-US">786</span></span></span><span style="font-size: large;">，从新宿、池袋等地可搭乘日本国铁（</span><span style="font-family: Liberation Serif,serif;"><span style="font-size: large;"><span lang="en-US">JR</span></span></span><span style="font-size: large;">）湘南新宿线，在“藤之牛岛”（<span lang="ja-JP">ふじのうしじま</span>）站下车，步行</span><span style="font-family: Liberation Serif,serif;"><span style="font-size: large;"><span lang="en-US">10</span></span></span><span style="font-size: large;">分可达。藤之季节，沿途都有告示板导引。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_7797096" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-7797096 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/IMG_6882-450x600.jpg" alt=" “牛岛之藤”紫藤花季。" width="450" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“牛岛之藤”紫藤花季，春天的结语。（可容／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
