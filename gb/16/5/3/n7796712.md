<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽约女子专门偷邮包 偷窃全程被拍</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年05月03日讯】（大纪元特约记者安心<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>报导）近日在纽约市布碌崙班森贺新卓越大道，70到80街范围内，出现了专门<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E7%AA%83.md#1">偷窃</a>邮件<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E8%A3%B9.md#1">包裹</a>的窃贼。一位受害人家中的摄像头拍到了窃贼作案的全过程。只见一名<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%90.md#1">女子</a>嘴里叼着烟卷，装作若无其事的样子走到一户住家门前，抓起一个有着梅西百货标志的包裹，塞进随身的挎包里，然后大步流星的逃走。</p>
<figure id="7796718" aria-describedby="caption-7796718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/d4d0f1eed9eff7f79b87d0b246a7682d-450x671.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/d4d0f1eed9eff7f79b87d0b246a7682d-450x671.jpg" alt="出现在班森贺的形迹可疑男子。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-7796718" class="wp-caption-text">出现在班森贺的形迹可疑男子。(NYPD)</figcaption></figure>
<p>社区组织“守护班森贺”（Watch Bensonhurst）怀疑这名<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%90.md#1">女子</a>在跟踪快递公司的卡车，并趁房主白天不在家的时候，偷盗<ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8C%85%E8%A3%B9.md#1">包裹</a>。因此他们贴出了中、英以及西班牙语的告示，希望邻里看到此人立即拨打911报警。</p>
<figure id="7796715" aria-describedby="caption-7796715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/91c9b17afec634b6168ee75b2a67ca78-450x651.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/91c9b17afec634b6168ee75b2a67ca78-450x651.jpg" alt="偷窃民众门前包裹的女贼。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-7796715" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/aizshe3672/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%B7%E7%AA%83.md#1">偷窃</a>民众门前包裹的女贼。(安心／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在另一张告示里，他们提醒民众要注意一名男子，因为该男子曾在下午和傍晚时分，无故2次按了一位当地住户的门铃，查看是否有人在家，形迹可疑。这名男子在62分局和66分局都有案底，所以“守护班森贺”组织呼吁民众看到此人也要报警，以维护社区的安全。◇</p>
<figure id="7796714" aria-describedby="caption-7796714" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ce2ea4c812b9cc534bf95c54c4799d77-450x605.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/ce2ea4c812b9cc534bf95c54c4799d77-450x605.jpg" alt="出现在班森贺的形迹可疑男子。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-7796714" class="wp-caption-text">出现在班森贺的形迹可疑男子。(安心／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
