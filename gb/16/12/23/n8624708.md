<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>多伦多台湾同乡青藤会2016年终会员大会暨圣诞联欢</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年12月24日讯】<span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">台湾</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">同乡青藤会于<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">2016</span></span>年<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">12</span></span>冃<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">17</span></span>日<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">（</span></span>星期六<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">）</span></span>中午，在驻<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>台北经济文化办事处文化中心举办“<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">2016</span></span>年终会员大会暨<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%81%94%E6%AC%A2.md#1">圣诞联欢</a>会”，莅临的佳宾有驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处处长徐咏梅，与文化中心主任李叔玲及各界人士有一百多人。</span></span></p>
<figure style="width: 643px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://mail.epochtimes.com/cgi-bin/webmail/login/canada_editor@epochtimes.com.authvchkpw/4C5A400ECF6581AC83E7D3BB644BA3C3/1482521831?folder=INBOX&amp;form=fetch&amp;pos=9&amp;mimeid=1.2" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="shrinkToFit" src="https://mail.epochtimes.com/cgi-bin/webmail/login/canada_editor@epochtimes.com.authvchkpw/4C5A400ECF6581AC83E7D3BB644BA3C3/1482521831?folder=INBOX&amp;form=fetch&amp;pos=9&amp;mimeid=1.2" width="643" b="280" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%90%8C%E4%B9%A1%E9%9D%92%E8%97%A4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾同乡青藤会</a>庆祝圣诞佳节，驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处处长徐咏梅（右6）、文化中心主任李叔玲（右8）与庄信雄会长（右7）及现任委员合照。（梁清祥提供文图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%81%94%E6%AC%A2.md#1">圣诞联欢</a>会由张瑟玲宣布开始<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">，</span></span>全体唱会歌。庄信雄会长致词，来宾致词后，庆生吃蛋糕<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">。</span></span>参加者还可拿到一个红包，令现场气氛喜气洋洋<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">。</span></span>圣诞老人的出现，更増添圣诞佳节的热闹。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">节目的安排多采多姿。有<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">RITA</span></span>舞蹈团的表演、欣赏<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">2016</span></span>年回顾幻灯片、青藤长者演唱老歌、李紫贺表演扇舞、翁智衡夫妇双人舞蹈<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">，</span></span>歌唱表演还有王裕辉夫妇、陈森隆夫妇、李秀兰、刘康融。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">多伦多<ahref="https://github.com/ychojm359/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%90%8C%E4%B9%A1%E9%9D%92%E8%97%A4%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台湾同乡青藤会</a>委员会任期到年底<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">，庄</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000; font-size: medium;">信雄</span>会长</span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">颁感谢状给现任委员<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">，</span></span>感谢委员为青藤会的付出及贡献<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">，</span></span>并介绍下一任新的委员：陈森隆、苏雪玉、林妙真、张理邦、彭瑞贞、许秋源、林望鄕、陈光男、林清泰、陈雪娇、陈诗文。</span></span></p>
