<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法式新奥尔良灵魂食物</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年12月24日讯】什么是“<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B5%E9%AD%82%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">灵魂食物</a>”？第一次听到时觉得有点不着边际，但是会吃的人一定知道它的意思。百合也是由吃货王带到此一圣宝地，才发现自己实在吃得太少，吃得太少，更要奋发吃出一条“血”路（编注：会不会太吓人啊！百合）。</p>
<p>如果说是“灵魂音乐”大概大家都懂得是什么意思，以此类推，“<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%B5%E9%AD%82%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">灵魂食物</a>”也就是由新奥尔良（New Orleans），路易斯安那州，那里传来的食物。大厨的名字就和店名是一样的?Brenda。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8627922" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627922" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/08_Brendas-French-Soul-Food.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8627922 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/08_Brendas-French-Soul-Food-300x354.jpg" alt="餐厅内部的壁画。（百合摄）" width="300" b="354" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627922" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅内部的壁画。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Brenda从小在新奥尔良长大，常常跑到自家后面的水池里面抓小龙虾（Crawfish）。抓虾、抓蟹、抓鱼，都是她的本事。29?岁的她由新奥尔良搬到旧金山，立即成为主厨，一直到现在拥有了自己的餐厅Brenda&#8217;s French Soul Food。</p>
<p>来到餐厅时才11点，但是已经大排长龙，原来这里最有名的就是早午餐（Brunch），大家饱餐一顿，今天可以省了一餐。看到菜单上的头一道菜我就十分好奇，什么是Beignet（法文：油炸圈饼）？于是叫了一份综合的?Sampler，包含各式各样都有的口味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8627925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627925" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01_Beignet-Flight-Beignets.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8627925 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/01_Beignet-Flight-Beignets-300x225.jpg" alt="各式口味的法式油炸圈饼（Beignet Flight Beignets）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627925" class="wp-caption-text">各式口味的法式油炸圈饼（Beignet Flight Beignets）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8627926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627926" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02_Ghirardelli-Chocolate-Beignet.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-8627926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/02_Ghirardelli-Chocolate-Beignet-300x183.jpg" alt="巧克力法式油炸圈饼（Ghirardelli Chocolate Beignet）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="183" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627926" class="wp-caption-text">巧克力法式油炸圈饼（Ghirardelli Chocolate Beignet）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Beignet味道有点像甜甜圈，但Beignet?不是圆圈形的，而是里面都填满了好料的丸形。送来了四种口味：小龙虾、巧克力、苹果和阳春。我说不出来喜欢哪一种，咸咸甜甜的都好吃，只是挺饱人的。</p>
<p>主餐叫了起士虾（Shrimp &amp; Grits）和酥脆五花肉（Pork Belly Crisp），两样都好吃，但是都十分有份量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8627929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627929" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/04_Shrimp-Grits.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-8627929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/04_Shrimp-Grits-300x225.jpg" alt="起士虾（Shrimp &amp; Grits）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627929" class="wp-caption-text">起士虾（Shrimp &amp; Grits）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8627927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8627927" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/03_Pork-Belly-Crisp.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-8627927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/03_Pork-Belly-Crisp-300x225.jpg" alt="酥脆五花肉（Pork Belly Crisp）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8627927" class="wp-caption-text">酥脆五花肉（Pork Belly Crisp）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外看到许多人点各式各样的班尼迪克蛋（Eggs Benedict）以及稀饭（或称汤饭），相信也是本店名菜，我点了搭配炸鸡的班尼迪克蛋（Eggs Benedict with Fried Chicken）和鸡肉香肠佐秋葵稀饭（Cup of Chicken, Sausage and Okra Gumbo）。</p>
<p>有空到旧金山来不妨尝试一下不同的食物，来这里千万不要想到减肥，不然会很失望的！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8628345" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8628345" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/05_Eggs-Benedict-with-Fried-Chicken.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-8628345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/05_Eggs-Benedict-with-Fried-Chicken-300x225.jpg" alt="搭配炸鸡的班尼迪克蛋（Eggs Benedict with Fried Chicken）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8628345" class="wp-caption-text">搭配炸鸡的班尼迪克蛋（Eggs Benedict with Fried Chicken）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8628351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8628351" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/06_Cup-of-Chicken-Sausage-and-Okra-Gumbo.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8628351 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/06_Cup-of-Chicken-Sausage-and-Okra-Gumbo-300x169.jpg" alt="鸡肉香肠佐秋葵稀饭（Cup of Chicken, Sausage and Okra Gumbo）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="169" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8628351" class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉香肠佐秋葵稀饭（Cup of Chicken, Sausage and Okra Gumbo）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
