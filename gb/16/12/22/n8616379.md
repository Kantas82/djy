<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>越夜越美丽的居食酒屋</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年12月22日讯】第一次走进这家餐厅，越夜越热闹，有点错觉好似置身于台北的居酒屋，或者是东京？座位坐的满满的，大家饮酒作乐，说话一点也不压低嗓门，就差没有划拳劝酒。小小的一间餐厅，出来的菜式可是一流。</p>
<p>我们叫了许多特别的菜，除了生鱼片，还有海胆、蛤蜊汤、扬出豆腐（Agedashi Tofu）、鸭胸肉、烤鱼下巴、鱿鱼夹饭、盐烤鲭鱼。每样菜都好吃，客人源源不断地进来，像是越夜越美丽的夜生活。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8616416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616416" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_001.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_001-300x400.jpg" alt="份量超大的海胆，搭配紫苏叶及紫菜。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8616416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616416" class="wp-caption-text">份量超大的海胆，搭配紫苏叶及紫菜。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8616417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616417" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_002.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_002-300x225.jpg" alt="鲜甜的蛤蜊汤。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8616417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616417" class="wp-caption-text">鲜甜的蛤蜊汤。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这里有各式各样的<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%B8%85%E9%85%92.md#1">日本清酒</a>，也有超大号的绿茶冰淇淋。另外还有两人小雅座，可以在里面自己罗曼蒂克一番，享受醇酒、美食以及不被打搅的情调。</p>
<p>其中几道我要特别介绍，他给的海胆又多又大方，旁边配上紫苏叶及紫菜，包起来吃美味无比，一点也没有鱼腥味。</p>
<p>蛤蜊汤很有台湾海港吃海鲜的味道，生鱼片新鲜自是不在话下。扬出豆腐炸得外脆内嫩，调料也做得恰到好处。</p>
<p>烤鱼下巴可以吃到肉质的鲜美，不像有些店好像是用剩鱼来做的。听说他的饭团也好吃，只是这一次吃的实在太饱了，要等下回才能来吃他的饭团。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8616421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616421" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_003.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_003-300x225.jpg" alt="肉质鲜美的烤鱼下巴。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8616421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616421" class="wp-caption-text">肉质鲜美的烤鱼下巴。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8616422" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616422" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_006.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_006-300x225.jpg" alt="鸭胸肉。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8616422" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616422" class="wp-caption-text">鸭胸肉。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_8616425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616425" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_004.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_004-300x225.jpg" alt="扬出豆腐。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8616425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616425" class="wp-caption-text">扬出豆腐。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8616428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616428" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_005.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_005-300x225.jpg" alt="鳗鱼饭。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8616428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616428" class="wp-caption-text">鳗鱼饭。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>平常餐期去这家餐厅都是要等上半个小时以上，越晚去反而越容易进入餐厅。如果你是夜猫子，晚上不怕吵，或是喜欢吃宵夜，这是一个最佳去处。和三两好友，夜里喝两杯，点上三、五道菜，我觉得是物超所值。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8616436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616436" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/Sweet-rice-cakes-mochi-with-green-tea-ice-cream.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/Sweet-rice-cakes-mochi-with-green-tea-ice-cream-300x224.jpg" alt="超大号的绿茶冰淇淋。（百合摄）" width="300" b="224" class="size-small wp-image-8616436" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616436" class="wp-caption-text">超大号的绿茶冰淇淋。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8616438" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8616438" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_008.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/tanto_008-300x400.jpg" alt="日本饮食文化特有的隐私座位，两人小雅座。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8616438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8616438" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E9%A5%AE%E9%A3%9F.md#1">日本饮食</a>文化特有的隐私座位，两人小雅座。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
