<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拳王阿里激励人心的10句名言</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606040430001758-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>阿里（左）于1964年击败重量级世界冠军利斯顿，登上新一代拳王宝座。（大纪元资料室）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年06月11日讯】（大纪元记者李小奕编译报导）前世界重量级<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%B3%E7%8E%8B.md">拳王</a><a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C.md">阿里</a>（Muhammad Ali）本月初在休克中过世，享年?74岁（1942—2016）。这位以“飘如蝴蝶、刺如蜜蜂”独特战术纵横拳坛30年的职业拳手也是个信仰坚定的社会活动家，他一生积极从事人权、宗教与慈善事务，广受世人爱戴。他还说过许多为人津津乐道并传诵的至理<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E8%A8%80.md">名言</a>。</p>
<p>本文摘录<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C.md">阿里</a>10则鼓舞人心的<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E8%A8%80.md">名言</a>，从中我们或许能够略窥这位传奇人物成功背后的精神。</p>
<p><strong>1. 谈成功之道</strong></p>
<p><strong>?</strong>如果发霉面包能萃取出盘尼西林（抗生素），那么人通过锻炼，也必能有所<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%90%E5%B0%B1.md">成就</a>。</p>
<p>（If they can make penicillin out of moldy bread, they can sure make something out of you.）</p>
<p><strong>?2. </strong><strong>谈年纪</strong></p>
<p>一个人到了50岁，如果世界观还和20岁时相同，那么他的人生就已浪费了30年。</p>
<p>（A man who views the world the same at 50 as he did at 20 has wasted 30 years of his life.）</p>
<p><strong>3. 谈坚韧</strong></p>
<p>折腾你的不是你爬的那座山，而是你鞋子里的小石头。</p>
<p>（It isn’t the mountains ahead to climb that wear you out; it’s the pebble in your shoe.）</p>
<p><strong>4. 谈准备</strong></p>
<p>我脑海里想像得出来、内心相信的东西，就有办法实现。</p>
<p>（If my mind can conceive it, and my heart can believe it—then I can achieve it.）</p>
<p><strong>5. 谈高尔夫</strong></p>
<p>我是最厉害的高尔夫球手，我只不过还没去玩而已。</p>
<p>（I&#8217;m the best. I just haven&#8217;t played yet.）</p>
<p><strong>6. 谈谦卑</strong></p>
<p>在家里我是个好人：但我不想给别人知道。我发现，人太谦卑就行之不远。</p>
<p>（At home I am a nice guy: but I don’t want the world to know. Humble people, I’ve found, don’t get very far.）</p>
<p><strong>7. 谈自身<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%90%E5%B0%B1.md">成就</a></strong></p>
<p>我和鳄鱼搏斗过，我跟鲸鱼打过架，我擒拿过闪电，我还把雷抓去坐牢。</p>
<p>（I’ve wrestled with alligators; I’ve tussled with a whale; I done handcuffed lightning; and throw thunder in jail.）</p>
<p><strong>8. 谈胜利</strong></p>
<p>意志必定强过技能。</p>
<p>（The will must be stronger than the skill.）</p>
<p><strong>9. 谈偿还</strong></p>
<p>你杀我的狗，你最好把你的猫藏好。</p>
<p>（You kill my dog, you better hide your cat.）</p>
<p><strong>10. 谈梦想</strong></p>
<p>就算你只是梦想击败我，你最好还是清醒过来跟我道歉。</p>
<p>（If you even dream of beating me you&#8217;d better wake up and apologize.）＃</p>
