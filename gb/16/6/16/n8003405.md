<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《今晚你想要点什么》20首歌唱出20则故事</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160207331487-e1466057455360-600x396.jpg" />
<h6>公视单元剧《今晚你想要点什么》剧照。（公视／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年06月16日讯】每一首歌都有一个故事，每一个有故事的人，都有一首无可取代的歌，今晚，你想点什么。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8003747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003747" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030251487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003747" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030251487-600x362.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="272" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003747" class="wp-caption-text">剧组大合照。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公视推出全新单元连续剧《<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%8A%E6%99%9A%E4%BD%A0%E6%83%B3%E8%A6%81%E7%82%B9%E4%BB%80%E4%B9%88.md#1">今晚你想要点什么</a>》，由<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E7%83%88.md#1">杨烈</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E7%90%BC%E5%A7%BF.md#1">张琼姿</a>领衔主演，邀请<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%9C%9D%E4%BA%AE.md#1">黄朝亮</a>导演、<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%85%8C%E5%9D%A4.md#1">林煌坤</a>剧本编辑，搜罗脍炙人口的国台语经典歌曲，蔡琴《最后一夜》、《恰似你的温柔》、凤飞飞《开始或结束》、《祝你幸福》等，希望用20首歌诉说20个人生故事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8003748" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003748" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030371487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003748" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030371487-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003748" class="wp-caption-text">公视单元剧《<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%8A%E6%99%9A%E4%BD%A0%E6%83%B3%E8%A6%81%E7%82%B9%E4%BB%80%E4%B9%88.md#1">今晚你想要点什么</a>》中，<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E7%90%BC%E5%A7%BF.md#1">张琼姿</a>饰演卡拉OK店老板娘“May姐”。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8003769" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030141487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003769" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030141487-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003769" class="wp-caption-text">《今晚你想要点什么》剧照，图为<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E7%83%88.md#1">杨烈</a>饰演厨师。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《今晚你想要点什么》场景在卡拉OK店，张琼姿饰演温暖热情的老板娘“May姐”，常用歌声安抚客人；杨烈饰演内敛话少的神秘厨师“阿万师”，犀利的眼神与坚毅的表情露出十足剧力，两人有段谜一般的黄昏爱恋。而在确认接演后，张琼姿锻炼歌喉，苦练凤飞飞《开始或结束》、台语歌《碎心恋》等多首动听歌曲，被女儿形容“妈妈连洗澡都在练唱”。杨烈则纳闷，自己是个能歌唱的歌手，却演着“沉默是金的厨师，他笑说“差点被误认是个哑巴”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8003772" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030191487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003772" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030191487-600x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="238" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003772" class="wp-caption-text">《今晚你想要点什么》主演合照，图为杨烈（左起）、林若亚、黄婕菲、张琼姿、丁宁、林小楼。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>各集主角网罗丁宁、林若亚、王凯、林小楼、何瑷芸、李佳豫、吴政迪等一线演员诠释，也邀来地球、黄婕菲、方大韦、黄瑄、黄荻钧、钟其翰、元六、胡鸿达、蓝一萍、洪瑞霞、胡利等演出。另外制作人郑绣瑛邀请到刘林、孙情等资深艺人参演。剧中有国台语老歌新曲，有本省、外省的老中青三代演员，制作人郑秀英形容“资深带年轻，象征演艺圈不分族群、大团结”；导演<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%9C%9D%E4%BA%AE.md#1">黄朝亮</a>也说：“资深的很专业，年轻的很敬业。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8003774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030431487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003774" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030431487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003774" class="wp-caption-text">知名作词人<ahref="https://github.com/gmzozr3243/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%85%8C%E5%9D%A4.md#1">林煌坤</a>担纲音乐顾问，为公视单元连续剧《今晚你想要点什么》站台。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>60年代红极一时的作词人林煌坤，曾为凤飞飞、邓丽君等人谱写多首名曲，耳熟能详的《祝你幸福》，以人生境遇入词的他说：“每一个人都是一则故事。”也提到“即使我们不在这世界，它还会人间流传，原因就是它带给人们温暖”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8003777" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8003777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030301487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8003777" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606160030301487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8003777" class="wp-caption-text">图为导演黄朝亮。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>导演黄朝亮难得接拍连续剧，以电影规格打造。拍摄时遇到大寒流，5度C冷天拍摄学生制服、吃锉冰，更从拍摄现场直接赶到记者会。他说：“我们想证明影视环境再恶劣，还是可以作出好东西。”该剧于本周六（18日）起，将于晚间9至11时播出。</p>
