<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>与简宏霖高温拍戏摔不停 林可彤晒成黑美人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240758451487-e1466769718650-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾女星林可彤资料照。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年06月24日讯】台湾演员<ahref="https://github.com/jjhjmf3968/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%80%E5%AE%8F%E9%9C%96.md#1">简宏霖</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/jjhjmf3968/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%8F%AF%E5%BD%A4.md#1">林可彤</a>最近拍摄新八点华剧《<ahref="https://github.com/jjhjmf3968/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%91%E7%9A%84%E6%9E%81%E5%93%81%E7%94%B7%E5%8F%8B.md#1">我的极品男友</a>》，日前冒着35度高温下“二人三脚”跑步，<ahref="https://github.com/jjhjmf3968/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E5%8F%AF%E5%BD%A4.md#1">林可彤</a>半途摔倒硬是被<ahref="https://github.com/jjhjmf3968/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%80%E5%AE%8F%E9%9C%96.md#1">简宏霖</a>拖着跑，一天晒下来皮肤更加黝黑，相形之下，简宏霖就更像“小白脸”了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8032928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8032928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749391487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8032928" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749391487-600x409.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8032928" class="wp-caption-text">简宏霖（右）与林可彤最近拍摄新八点华剧，日前冒着35度高温下“二人三脚”跑步，图为剧照。（三立、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>剧中，简宏霖演活幼稚总经理，而他与饰演秘书的林可彤不断上演互杠桥段，每集观众光看他们两人斗法就大呼过瘾。日前他与林可彤拍摄一场在职教育训练的剧情，简宏霖与林可彤跑“两人三脚”来培养职场默契。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8032931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8032931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749351487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8032931" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749351487-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8032931" class="wp-caption-text">林可彤半途摔倒硬是被简宏霖拖着跑，一天晒下来皮肤更加黝黑，图为剧照。（三立、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为此，两人顶着35度烈阳限时竞走，晒了三、四个小时大太阳，来回跑了不下50次，中途林可彤摔了好多次，还被简宏霖拖着跑，原本应该吃 NG的画面，但那些画面太有效果，导演硬是不喊卡继续拍。过程中，简宏霖还关心地问林可彤说：“如果姐姐跑不动我来扛着你跑。”展现贴心的一面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8032934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8032934" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749301487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8032934" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749301487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="419" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8032934" class="wp-caption-text">剧中简宏霖与林可彤两人不断互杠外，中途林可彤摔了好多次，图为剧照。（三立、东森提供）</dd>
<dd class="wp-caption-dd"></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8032937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8032937" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749251487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8032937" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606240749251487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8032937" class="wp-caption-text">林可彤半途摔倒硬是被简宏霖拖着跑，图为剧照。（三立、东森提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只是本来皮肤黝黑的林可彤，曝晒了一上午之后更是黑得发亮，她笑说这下简宏霖就更像“小白脸”了。而林可彤不愧平时都有在健身，拍摄中能够持续不断地来回跑，连简宏霖都佩服她的耐力，只是差点被太阳烤焦的两人还是希望待在冷气房拍戏就好。</p>
