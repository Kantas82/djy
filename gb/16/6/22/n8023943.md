<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>朴信惠金来沅新剧收视开红 完胜同档韩剧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220429261487-e1466584775535-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《Doctors》海报，图为金来沅（右）与朴信惠。（FNC ENTERTAINMENT提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年06月22日讯】韩国KBS2的电视剧《Beautiful Mind》和SBS的《Doctors》同在本月20日开播，根据国内尼尔森公布统计数据显示，由<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E4%BF%A1%E6%83%A0.md#1">朴信惠</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%9D%A5%E6%B2%85.md#1">金来沅</a>主演的《Doctors》一、二集分别以12.9%及14.2%的收视率，摘下月火剧该时段的收视冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8024004" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8024004" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220333011487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8024004" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220333011487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8024004" class="wp-caption-text">《Doctors》海报，图为<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%9D%A5%E6%B2%85.md#1">金来沅</a>（右）与<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E4%BF%A1%E6%83%A0.md#1">朴信惠</a>。（FNC ENTERTAINMENT提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据韩媒报导，由朴信惠和金来沅合演的新韩剧《Doctors》，讲述坐过牢的女流氓刘惠静经刑满获释后发愤图强，成为女医生的故事。首集一开始，朴信惠饰演的“刘惠静”已是一名女医生，在一场医院与黑帮的打斗戏中，功夫了得，让粉丝直呼“帅呆了”。</p>
<p>这次朴信惠一改柔弱形象，上演全武行，甚至徒手将人过肩摔，俐落的身手背后其实是下足苦功。报导说，导演透露朴信惠拍摄动作戏时，坚持不用替身，对于朴的敬业表现大为赞赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8024002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8024002" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220333051487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8024002" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220333051487-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="449" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8024002" class="wp-caption-text">《Doctors》剧照，图为朴信惠。（FNC ENTERTAINMENT提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，在KBS 2TV开播的《Beautiful Mind》收视率虽然不如预期，位居第3名，但剧中主演<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E4%B8%B9.md#1">朴素丹</a>的表现也获得不少网友的好评。而在同档韩剧中，MBC的《怪物》昨天（21日）的收视率为11.1%，排名第2名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8024008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8024008" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220338461487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8024008" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606220338461487.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="429" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8024008" class="wp-caption-text">翻摄《Beautiful Mind》海报，图为<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/rguhiq3241/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E4%B8%B9.md#1">朴素丹</a>。（全宇／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
