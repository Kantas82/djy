<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年轻主厨返乡开店 留住家传怀旧美食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280803482357-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>看了令人食指大动的招牌“蚵仔煎”。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年06月28日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）斗六市是<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%91%E6%9E%97%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">云林美食</a>小吃的集散地，最近在永安路又出现一家美食餐馆“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%A1%E9%BC%8E%E7%AB%99.md#1">御鼎站</a>”，馆内布置有蓑衣、旧裁缝车、秤锤、怀旧电影海报等等，充满浓浓的古早味，一走进餐馆，有如走入60年代的时光隧道。尤其年轻帅气的主厨<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E9%9F%A1%E4%BB%81.md#1">陈韡仁</a>，为了照顾妈妈，放弃大饭店高薪，返乡开店的故事更令人感动。</p>
<p>这家怀旧餐馆以“蚵仔煎”为招牌，做法颠覆传统，以铁板烧式取代传统闷煮式，且内馅丰富，尤其是附加的酱汁，味道非常独特，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E9%9F%A1%E4%BB%81.md#1">陈韡仁</a>说是祖辈流传下来的8种秘方调制而成的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8045126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8045126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280805222357.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8045126" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280805222357-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8045126" class="wp-caption-text">陈韡仁为了照顾妈妈，放弃大饭店高薪返乡开店。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丼饭做法也很创新，主厨陈韡仁不但丰富丼饭菜色，而且米饭粒粒分明，让丼饭更具怀旧便当味；锅烧面也突破传统做法，增加内涵，让这一锅面更有料。此外，包括炸物、蒲烧鳗，也各具特色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8045125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8045125" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280808062357.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8045125" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280808062357-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8045125" class="wp-caption-text">蒲烧鲷鱼丼饭。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>曾任职大饭店的陈韡仁说，他只是想留住陈家几代以来的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%80%E6%97%A7%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">怀旧美食</a>，而店内布置则是他祖父辈留下的收藏品，希望让来店内用餐的旧雨新知忆当年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8045121" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8045121" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280809562357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8045121 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280809562357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8045121" class="wp-caption-text">馆内珍藏的蓑衣。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8045123" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8045123" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280808312357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8045123 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/1606280808312357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8045123" class="wp-caption-text">馆内布置了许多怀旧的收藏品。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈韡仁说，小时候常跟着爸妈开着小发财车到处卖蚵仔煎、卤肉饭、猪血汤及臭豆腐等，印象最深刻的是有一次妈妈背着他做生意，突然下起雨来，不但妈妈被淋湿了，自己也湿了。流动摊贩虽然没有房租的压力，但遇到刮风下雨就没办法做生意，因此从小他就希望将来能开一家店，让爸妈不用那么辛苦做生意。</p>
<p>前往光顾的议员周秀月，最喜欢店内的蒲烧鳗鱼丼饭，她说，这道<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%80%E6%97%A7%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">怀旧美食</a>，让她重拾孩提时美好回忆，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%A1%E9%BC%8E%E7%AB%99.md#1">御鼎站</a>的美食“真赞”。</p>
<p>得知为了照顾老妈，青年返乡开店的故事，喜欢美食小吃的县长李进勇也表示肯定，他说有空会去吃美食怀旧，鼓励漂鸟<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%94%E4%B9%A1%E5%88%9B%E4%B8%9A.md#1">返乡创业</a>。</p>
