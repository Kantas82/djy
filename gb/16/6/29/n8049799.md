<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏天来了  最适合公路旅行的8款车</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年06月30日讯】今年的汽油价格虽然比去年涨了些，但和2年多前的价格相比，还是很便宜了，今年也是个开车<ahref="https://github.com/atrttf3643/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C.md#1">公路旅行</a>的好时机。《Autoweek》杂志精挑细选了8种适合公路旅行的车款，让你能安全、舒适、享受地实现多年的梦想。</p>
<h2>Mazda MX-5 Miata<br />
MSRP: $25,735</h2>
<figure id="attachment_8049824" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049824" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/MX5Club_2016_003_0-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8049824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/MX5Club_2016_003_0-450x253.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda MX-5 Miata.（Mazda）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049824" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda MX-5 Miata.（Mazda）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然不适合家庭<ahref="https://github.com/atrttf3643/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E8%B7%AF%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C.md#1">公路旅行</a>，但却是两人旅行的绝妙车款。操驾乐趣仍然是Miata的最大特点，“人马一体”的驾驭感受，带给你更多的乐趣，而且也不用花那么多的钱。打开音响，忘记州际公路的柏油路吧。</p>
<h2>Jaguar XF<br />
MSRP: $51,900</h2>
<figure id="attachment_8049826" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049826" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu2_2016-Jaguar-XF-S-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8049826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu2_2016-Jaguar-XF-S-450x299.jpg" alt="2016 Jaguar XF-S。（Jaguar）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049826" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Jaguar XF-S。（Jaguar）</figcaption></figure>
<p>捷豹（Jaguar）第二代的XF轿车堪称是最受消费者喜爱的车款，和上一代相比，车重大幅减轻，双涡轮增压的V6引擎，输出强大的340或380马力。带给你安静、舒适的巡航，带着淡淡的英国绅士魅力。</p>
<h2>Cadillac CTS-V<br />
MSRP: $84,990</h2>
<figure id="attachment_8049829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049829" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu3_2016-cadillac-cts-v-450x275.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8049829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu3_2016-cadillac-cts-v-450x275.jpg" alt="2016 Cadillac CTS。（Cadillac）" width="450" b="275" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049829" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Cadillac CTS。（Cadillac）</figcaption></figure>
<p>CTS-V 的定风翼、尾翼和扰流板等运动套件的设计，彰显了这款车的运动基因。6.2 升 V8 增压发动机，最大功率达到 640hp。宽阔的室内空间，加上Bose的音响、4G LTE无线网络让人更享受旅途的舒适。</p>
<h2>Toyota Prius<br />
MSRP: $24,495</h2>
<p><figure id="attachment_8049833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049833" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu4_2016-toyota-prius-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu4_2016-toyota-prius-450x300.jpg" alt="2016 Toyota Prius。（Toyota）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8049833" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049833" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Toyota Prius。（Toyota）</figcaption></figure><br />
普锐斯（Prius）多年来一直是省油的代名词，也是混合动力车的佼佼者。如何想既省钱又玩得更远，Prius是不二的选择，既少加油，又安静。现在的Prius里外都愈来愈新潮，已摆脱了传统、务实的印象。</p>
<h2>Ford Mustang<br />
MSRP: $25,045</h2>
<p><figure id="attachment_8049836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu5_fordmustang2016-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu5_fordmustang2016-450x253.jpg" alt="2016 Ford Mustang。（Ford）" width="450" b="253" class="size-medium wp-image-8049836" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049836" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Mustang。（Ford）</figcaption></figure><br />
经典的美式肌肉车款Ford Mustang，既有EcoBoost涡轮增压引擎，又有自然进气的选择，以FR前置引擎后轮驱动的传统方式，回应给驾驶者满满地驾驭乐趣。这匹小马，在阳光下熠熠生辉，让你的旅行更精彩，更刺激！</p>
<h2>Kia Optima<br />
MSRP: $22,665</h2>
<p><figure id="attachment_8049838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049838" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu6_2016-kia-optima-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu6_2016-kia-optima-450x300.jpg" alt="2016 Kia Optima。（Kia）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-8049838" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049838" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Kia Optima。（Kia）</figcaption></figure><br />
Kia Optima虽然不是最令人兴奋的车款，也不是最畅销的，但可以说是市场上最好的中型轿车之一。完善的主被动安全防护配备、豪华、安静的车内空间、语音命令技术、真皮座椅意味着放松、舒适的旅行。</p>
<h2>Honda Accord<br />
MSRP: $22,925</h2>
<p><figure id="attachment_8049839" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8049839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu7_2016-Honda-Accord-VS-450x244.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/Tu7_2016-Honda-Accord-VS-450x244.jpg" alt="2016 Honda Accord。（Honda）" width="450" b="244" class="size-medium wp-image-8049839" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8049839" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Honda Accord。（Honda）</figcaption></figure><br />
与Kia Optima相似，Honda Accord不是令人兴奋的轿车，但却是长寿的、最畅销中型轿车了。随着不断地改进，现在内外都显得更时尚，信息娱乐系统也支持 Android Auto和Apple CarPlay，让你在旅途中不会寂寞！</p>
