<a href="https://d3siwajeg0khti.cloudfront.net/D3fm9jW8t"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/eahkqg304/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/eahkqg304/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者张雅云综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%9D%E5%B8%8C%E7%89%B9%E6%96%AF%E5%8A%A0%E7%99%BB%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">贝希特斯加登国家公园</a>内森林茂密，溪流潺潺，山花遍野，湖泊如镜，恍若世外桃源，是德国人所夸耀的风景胜地。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%9D%E5%B8%8C%E7%89%B9%E6%96%AF%E5%8A%A0%E7%99%BB%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">贝希特斯加登国家公园</a>(Nationalpark Berchtesgaden)建立于1978年，位于德国的东南角，距离德国与奥地利的边境不远，是德国境内阿尔卑斯山地区最早的保护区之一。</p>
<p>贝希特斯加登被阿尔卑斯山所环抱，公园内均为天然景观，其中包括德国最干净和最美丽的<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%B9%96.md#1">国王湖</a>(K?nigssee)和德国的第二高峰<ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%93%A6%E8%8C%A8%E6%9B%BC%E5%B1%B1.md#1">瓦茨曼山</a>(Watzmann)。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E7%8E%8B%E6%B9%96.md#1">国王湖</a>由冰川形成，长7.7千米，最宽处约1.7千米，最深处190米，为德国最深的湖泊，湖水呈现翡翠绿色，清澈见底，水质达到饮用水标准，只有电动船、手划船和脚踏船才被允许在湖中航行。圣巴尔多禄茂教堂(St.Bartholoma)是国王湖的标志，坐落在国王湖西岸中段半岛上，教堂以其特别造型而出名，红色圆穹顶呈洋葱型，礼拜堂半圆形后殿分成对称的三瓣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8392755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8392755" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1310081235531758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8392755 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1310081235531758-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8392755" class="wp-caption-text">德国国王湖湖水呈现翡翠绿色，清澈见底。（徐焕宗／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8392859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8392859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1310090047521758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8392859 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1310090047521758-450x338.jpg" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8392859" class="wp-caption-text">圣巴尔多禄茂教堂是国王湖的标志，教堂以其特别造型而出名。（徐焕宗／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eahkqg304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%93%A6%E8%8C%A8%E6%9B%BC%E5%B1%B1.md#1">瓦茨曼山</a>最高海拔为2,713米，高耸入云，峰雄伟壮观，无论从哪个角度观望，都有震撼心灵的磅礴气势。<span class="irc_su" dir="ltr">Kehlsteinhaus)</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8393026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8393026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130100071758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8393026 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130100071758-450x253.jpg" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8393026" class="wp-caption-text">德国的第二高峰瓦茨曼山。（Dirk Schmidt／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8393099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8393099" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130122241758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8393099 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130122241758-450x338.jpg" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8393099" class="wp-caption-text">贝希特斯加登公园内。（Ond?ej ?vá?ek／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贝希特斯加登一年四季景色各异。春天，满山满谷野花怒放，一片新绿；夏天，蓝天白云，绿水清波，划艇游泳，激流冲浪，是避暑天堂；秋天，是一年里风光最美好的季节，漫山红叶，满坡牛羊，烟岚缈缈。冬天，则白雪皑皑，是高山滑雪的圣地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8393028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8393028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130056161758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8393028 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610130056161758-450x253.jpg" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8393028" class="wp-caption-text">贝希特斯加登公园野花盛开。（Daniel Van Gravel／维基百科）</figcaption></figure>
