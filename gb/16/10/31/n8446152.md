<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本杯德比日 第四代驯马师传承胜利神话</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/60bdb904f9c18e160b15ff16f4f5f1ee-e1477968273226-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>MELBOURNE, AUSTRALIA &#8211; OCTOBER 29:  Trainer James Cummings and Glyn Schofield pose with trophy after Prized Icon won Race 7, AAMi Victoria Derby on Derby Day at Flemington Racecourse on October 29, 2016 in Melbourne, Australia.  (Photo by Vince Caligiuri/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年10月31日讯】（大纪元记者杰妮澳洲墨尔本综合报导）2016年10月29日，墨尔本杯<ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%9B%E9%A9%AC.md#1">赛马</a>嘉年华<ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E6%AF%94%E6%97%A5.md#1">德比日</a>大赛（the AAMI Victoria Derby）于弗莱明顿（Flemington）<ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%9B%E9%A9%AC.md#1">赛马</a>场完美谢幕，9万名观众现场观看了令人热血沸腾的比赛。赛驹Prized Icon在最后的二百米突出重围，获得冠军。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8446232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8446232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610310342382759-450x308.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8446232 size-medium" title="93161216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610310342382759-450x308.jpg" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8446232" class="wp-caption-text">2016年10月29日，Prized Icon在最后的二百米突出重围，获得<ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC%E6%9D%AF.md#1">墨尔本杯</a><ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E6%AF%94%E6%97%A5.md#1">德比日</a>赛马冠军。（Quinn Rooney/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Prized Icon所取得的骄人成绩为Cummings驯马王朝继续书写胜利的传奇。其驯马师、28岁的James Cummings继曾祖父、祖父、父亲之后，成为Cummings家族第4代冠军驯马师。</p>
<p>Cummings说：“Prize Icon并不是侥幸获胜，它之前进行过大量的训练，我非常高兴地看到它今天所展示出的实力。”</p>
<p>Prize Icon在最后的二百米时，超过了赛前最受关注的赛驹Sacred Elixir ，赢得了150万澳元的奖金。</p>
<p>骑手Glyn Schofield也是喜不自禁，他说：“这场比赛赢得很轻松。”</p>
<p>维州赛马俱乐部首席执行官拉文（Simon Love）兴奋地说： “今天的赛马日真是太完美了，人们玩得也非常开心。周二的阿联酋航空墨尔本杯赛马日（Emirates Melbourne Cup Day）一定会非常精彩。”</p>
<p>在本届墨尔本杯赛马嘉年华的第一天，还有许多其它的亮点。Flying Artie在赢得库莫大奖赛（Coolmore Stud）胜利后被誉为澳洲最佳雄马，Oceanographer 在获取雷克萨斯大奖赛（Lexus Stakes）后成为<ahref="https://github.com/aredbs3394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC%E6%9D%AF.md#1">墨尔本杯</a>（Melbourne Cup）的热门赛驹。</p>
<p>德比赛马节是澳洲历史最悠久的经典大赛，从社会名流到普通民众，各界人士都被深深吸引。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8446236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8446236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610310343342759-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8446236 size-medium" title="93164954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1610310343342759-450x299.jpg" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8446236" class="wp-caption-text">网坛名将休伊特夫妇（Lleyton Hewitt、 Bec Hewitt）参加墨尔本杯德比日盛事。（Scott Barbour/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>明星厨师Curtis Stone、网坛名将Lleyton Hewitt、名模Nicole Trunfio以及演员/记者Maria Menounos、著名电影导演演员伊斯特伍德（Clint Eastwood）的儿子、电影明星Scott等大牌明星都出现了在观众席上。普通观众也十分尽兴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8448672" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8448672" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/19b2d5d69dd4a45fc1da620cecb0bc1c-e1477969960428.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8448672 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/19b2d5d69dd4a45fc1da620cecb0bc1c-e1477969960428.jpg" alt="MELBOURNE, AUSTRALIA - OCTOBER 29: Myer Fashions on the Field Women's Racewear competition winner Gunita Kenina (C) and Runners Up Stacey Hanley and Tanya Lazarou pose in the Myer Fashion On The Field Marquee on Victoria Derby Day at Flemington Racecourse on October 29, 2016 in Melbourne, Australia. (Photo by Daniel Pockett/Getty Images for the VRC)" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8448672" class="wp-caption-text">2016年10月29日，Gunita Kenina（中）赢得迈雅（Myer）时尚女装选美大赛冠军。（Daniel Pockett/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>警方对赛马节当天观众的表现整体上表示满意。St John Ambulance 救护车接走了一些观众，大多是因为醉酒或脱水的原因，但没有人住院。</p>
