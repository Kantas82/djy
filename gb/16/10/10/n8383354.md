<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Black sheep Brasserie黑羊小酒馆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/04_Blacksheep-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>黑羊小酒馆的餐厅内部。（百合提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年10月10日讯】这一家黑羊小酒馆餐厅来头不小，有几位大厨掌舵。并且这几位大厨在硅谷早已是名声响亮，开店多年。各位吃货如果这一家吃的不过瘾，还有好几家可以去，百合将他们其他姐妹餐厅名字、地址以及网站都已熟记如下。</p>
<p>Don Durante曾经主理过Saratoga的Le Mouton Noir法国餐厅，又经营过Santa Clara的Birk&#8217;s餐厅，相信十之八、九的硅谷人都耳熟能详这一家老店。另外在 Mountain View也开了Cascal；以及Los Gatos的Cin-Cin Win Bar &amp; Restaurant。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8383853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8383853" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/Blacksheep-collection-of-wine.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8383853 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/Blacksheep-collection-of-wine-300x450.jpg" alt="黑羊小酒馆的吧台。（百合提供）" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8383853" class="wp-caption-text">黑羊小酒馆的吧台。（百合提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Don Durante有了一辈子的经验，又再与多位伙伴合作经营这一家黑羊小酒馆，想必不用再背书，来了就点，点了就吃，一切就是如此的顺理成章，佳肴美馔，享受就是！</p>
<p>百合今天点的菜肴都是比较特别的，先来一道冷盘，鸭肝慕丝（Duck Liver Mousse）；再上一道小牛排配法式炸鱼（Veal Sweetbreads a La Meunière）；最后来道澳洲羊排（Dijon Crusted Rack of Australia Lamb）；甜点叫的是橄榄油蛋糕（Olive Oil Cake）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8383855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8383855" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/06_Duck-liver-mousse.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8383855 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/06_Duck-liver-mousse-450x338.jpg" alt="鸭肝慕丝（Duck Liver Mousse）。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8383855" class="wp-caption-text">鸭肝慕丝（Duck Liver Mousse）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p><figure id="attachment_8383856" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8383856" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/Dijon-crusted-rack-of-Australia-lamb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8383856 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/Dijon-crusted-rack-of-Australia-lamb-300x400.jpg" alt="澳洲羊排（Dijon Crusted Rack of Australia Lamb）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8383856" class="wp-caption-text">澳洲羊排（Dijon Crusted Rack of Australia Lamb）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_8383861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8383861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/e86a612cd9695b9293d9f0206c379835.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/e86a612cd9695b9293d9f0206c379835-450x338.jpg" alt="法式炸鱼（Veal Sweetbreads a La Meunière）。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8383861" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8383861" class="wp-caption-text">法式炸鱼（Veal Sweetbreads a La Meunière）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<figure id="attachment_8383858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8383858" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/06_Olive-oil-cake.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8383858 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/06_Olive-oil-cake-450x338.jpg" alt="橄榄油蛋糕（Olive Oil Cake）。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8383858" class="wp-caption-text">橄榄油蛋糕（Olive Oil Cake）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
