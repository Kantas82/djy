<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大熊猫见大雪欣喜若狂 满地打滚</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年10月27日讯】（大纪元记者陈默报导）10月25日晚（周二），哈尔滨亚布力迎来一场<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9B%AA.md#1">大雪</a>，仅一个晚上降雪量就达四五厘米厚，乐坏了来自四川的大<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%8A%E7%8C%AB.md#1">熊猫</a>佑佑。</p>
<p>网上传的视频和图片中，佑佑在雪地里刨洞欣喜若狗，前滚翻三周就像个体操胖王子，雪中来回奔跑尽情撒欢，荡完秋千又爬上三米高的凉亭欣赏雪景，快乐的心情无以表达，一改往日在四川的文静状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8436554" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8436554" style="width: 337px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1477528209454.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8436554 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1477528209454.jpg" alt="1477528209454" width="337" b="234" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8436554" class="wp-caption-text">佑佑雪中来回奔跑尽情撒欢，浑身是雪。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佑佑于2008年出生于四川，今年9岁，相当与人类的27岁，是一雄性大<ahref="https://github.com/chxaej3386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%86%8A%E7%8C%AB.md#1">熊猫</a>。平时是一位慵懒宁静的美男子，他的最爱就是睡觉。</p>
<p>今年的7月5日，与一雌性大熊猫“思嘉”一起乔迁至黑龙江亚布力熊猫馆定居，经过十几天的适应后，18日正式与公众见面。入住亚布力时，身体健康状况良好。</p>
<p>在老家四川，佑佑也能见到雪，但是南方的雪怎能与北方柔软厚实的雪质相比。四川的雪存留时间极短，而哈尔滨的雪柔软似绵，厚实如长绒地毯，让热爱睡觉的佑佑冲动！顷刻之间文静的美男子就变了样，冲上雪地就开始翻滚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8436555" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8436555" style="width: 314px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323640465624.gif" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8436555 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323640465624.gif" alt="w020161027323640465624" width="314" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8436555" class="wp-caption-text">佑佑激动得冲上雪地就开始翻滚。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8436556" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8436556" style="width: 314px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323641281366.gif" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8436556 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323641281366.gif" alt="w020161027323641281366" width="314" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8436556" class="wp-caption-text">佑佑激动得冲上雪地翻滚，使劲刨雪。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>佑佑的雪地憨态，引发网友的热议：“估计在四川比较热，一下子冷了很舒服吧”；“一年四季穿着皮草就等这一天！”；“‘南方熊’第一次见到下雪的真实反应”等等。也如网友戏称：“可以回老家跟同伴们吹嘘一辈子了！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8436558" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8436558" style="width: 314px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323642053821.gif" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8436558 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/W020161027323642053821.gif" alt="w020161027323642053821" width="314" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8436558" class="wp-caption-text">佑佑荡完秋千又爬上三米高的凉亭欣赏雪景。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8436652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8436652" style="width: 441px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1477528209944.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8436652 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1477528209944.jpg" alt="1477528209944" width="441" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8436652" class="wp-caption-text">佑佑悠然自得地荡秋千，观赏雪景。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冬天来了，佑佑和他的伙伴每天会吃更多的食物，以保障有充沛的体力度过哈尔滨寒冷的冬季。</p>
