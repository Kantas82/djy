<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>眼下长皱纹可能是脾肾虚 喝这碗粥有效！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/1501162132121778-e1476434230626-450x400.jpg" />
<h6>《本草纲目》记载：“薏苡仁粥，除湿热，利肠胃。”（Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年10月14日讯】（大纪元记者李熙综合报导）黑眼圈几乎成为年轻人的标志了，再严重点的还会有眼袋出现！“<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%84%BE%E8%99%9A.md#1">脾虚</a>眼袋大，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%BE%E8%99%9A.md#1">肾虚</a>眼袋黑。”<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">眼睛</a>下方有<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%B1%E7%BA%B9.md#1">皱纹</a>，眼睑发青或发黑，双目无神，是肾气不足。眼睑发亮并鼓起则是<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%84%BE%E8%99%9A.md#1">脾虚</a>。想要去除眼袋和黑眼圈，首先必须调节作息，还要注意均衡饮食，尤其是加强补脾肾。</p>
<p>中医讲肝主目，<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">眼睛</a>归肝管，眼周围<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9A%B1%E7%BA%B9.md#1">皱纹</a>跟肝有直接关系。中医五轮学说，将眼睛分为眼白归肺管，黑珠子归肝管，瞳仁归肾管，上下眼睑归脾管，眼睛还需要肾精的濡润。</p>
<p><strong>黑眼圈</strong></p>
<p>肾五行属水，对应黑色。肾精亏虚的人两眼缺少精气的滋润，由于眼睑处皮肤非常薄，肾的黑色就会浮在眼脸上，也就是黑眼圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5767936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5767936" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409021133041778.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5767936 size-small" title="Sleepy woman holding a cup of coffee, falling asleep" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409021133041778-300x368.jpg" alt="Sleepy woman holding a cup of coffee, falling asleep" width="300" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5767936" class="wp-caption-text">漂亮素颜是许多女性梦寐以求的事，而黑眼圈是素颜最大的天敌。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>眼袋</strong></p>
<p>脾胃较虚弱的人，脾不能把体内的水湿代谢出去，皮肤和肌肉营养供给不足，皮肤便出现松弛无弹性，导致眼睑下垂形成眼袋，这种眼下皱纹是亮的。</p>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E8%8D%AF.md#1">山药</a>茯苓莲子粥可同时补脾<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%82%BE%E8%99%9A.md#1">肾虚</a></strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E8%8D%AF.md#1">山药</a>茯苓莲子粥是补肝补脾肾、去眼周围皱纹最有效之粥品。</p>
<p><strong>山药</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8397296" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8397296" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1605161425212483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8397296 size-medium" title="山药" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1605161425212483-450x300.jpg" alt="山药" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8397296" class="wp-caption-text">山药（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>山药，性平微温、味甘无毒，入脾、肺、肾三经，为和缓平稳之补品。山药，既可以脾肾双补，还可以补肺补气，因此有“神仙之药”美称。</p>
<p>山药的药用功效有，益气补脾、滋补作用、助消化、补虚劳益气力，并有缓泻袪痰等作用，久食会使皮肤白嫩细腻又光滑。</p>
<p><strong>莲子</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8060833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060833" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1605042314222483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8060833 size-medium" title="lotus seeds and cord with clipping path（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1605042314222483-450x301.jpg" alt="lotus seeds and cord with clipping path（fotolia）" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060833" class="wp-caption-text">连子（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>莲子，性平、味甘涩，入脾、肾经。不仅脾肾双补，还能养心安神帮助睡眠。</p>
<p>本草纲目记载，莲子有“交心肾，厚肠胃，固精气，强筋骨，补虚损，利耳目，除寒湿”等功能，被认为有补脾止泻、清心养神益肾，还有延缓衰老的作用。</p>
<p><strong>茯苓</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_6669232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6669232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/01/1301240707461778.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6669232 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/01/1301240707461778-450x300.jpg" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6669232" class="wp-caption-text">茯苓（大纪元图片库）</figcaption></figure>
<p>茯苓，性平、味甘淡，入心、肺、脾经。具有利水渗湿、益脾和胃、宁心安神功用。</p>
<p>茯苓被称为“四时神药”，功效非常广泛，不分四季，将它与各种药物配伍，不管寒、温、风、湿等疾病，都能发挥其独特功效。在粥中加入茯苓可以促进山药、莲子的吸收。</p>
<p><strong>山药茯苓莲子粥作法</strong></p>
<p>山药、茯苓、莲子按1：1：1的比例煮，茯苓和莲子浸泡一晚再跟山药一起煮粥。@*</p>
