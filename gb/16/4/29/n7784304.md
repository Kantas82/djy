<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>SpaceX公司飞船欲2018年登陆火星</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月29日讯】<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者夏君编译报导</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">）</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">周三（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;">4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span>27<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">日）﹐位于霍桑（</span></span></span></span>Hawthorne<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）的<ahref="https://github.com/rqtcae304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%AA%E5%A4%A9%E8%88%AA%E7%A9%BA%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8.md#1">航天航空公司</a></span></span></span></span>SpaceX<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">宣布﹐他们计</span></span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">划</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">于</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;">2018<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年向火星发送</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">一</span></span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">艘该公司的</span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/rqtcae304/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E9%A3%9E%E8%88%B9.md#1">龙飞船</a></span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">（</span></span></span></span>Dragon spaceship?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;">）。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">这一使命旨在测试将大型荷载物在火星上登陆所需的技术﹐这是为未来实现载人登陆火星和在这颗红色星球上实现可能的殖民计<span lang="zh-CN">画</span>的关键一步。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">该公司已经完成了龙</span></span>2<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">号航天飞船的测试任务。据该公司创始人兼首席执行官马斯克（</span></span>Elon Musk<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）在推特上说﹐龙</span></span>2<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">号航天飞船是设计用来在太阳系任何地方都能登陆的飞船。不过﹐该飞船并不是设计用来将宇航员送上火星的飞船，因为它里面的空间仅有一辆越野车</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">那么大﹐若宇航员坐进去做超越月球的更长旅行会不舒服。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">该公司尚未公布更多的关于火星任务的细节。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">目前﹐</span></span>SpaceX<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">公司上下仍沉浸在火箭在海上成功回收的喜悦中。</span></span>4<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>8<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">日﹐该公司将一<span lang="zh-CN">艘</span>龙航天飞船发射<span lang="zh-CN">到</span>国际太空站后﹐其发射<span lang="zh-CN">的</span>猎鹰火箭（</span></span>Falcon rocket<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）成功在大西洋海域一艘无人船上降落。之前﹐该公司曾尝试过数次海上火箭回收﹐但都以失败告终。海上回收所需的燃料更少﹐费用更低﹐是节约航天航空成本的重要一步。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">去年</span></span>12<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">月份﹐</span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">猎鹰火箭之一</span></span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">开创了回收火箭的历史，</span></span></span>在佛罗里达州卡纳维海角（</span></span>Cape Canaveral<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）陆地降落成功</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3"></span>﹐由此证明价值数百万美元的火箭可以回收和重用﹐而不必再像以前那样在大气层或在海洋中燃烧并消失。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">与波音公司一样﹐</span></span>SpaceX<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">公司是一家承揽美国国家航空航天局（</span></span>NASA<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）合同﹐为填补太空梭项目退役后留下的空白﹐开发可以运送宇航员到国际太空站的航天飞船的公司。自太空梭项目于</span></span>2011<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年结束以来﹐美国一直依赖俄罗斯的飞船将宇航员送上太空站 。◇</span></span></span></p>
