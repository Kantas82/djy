<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>约书亚树公园去年访客200万 周边消费近亿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1511051017371973-600x400-e1461900564610.jpg" />
<h6>加利福尼亚州东南部约书亚树国家公园（Joshua Tree National Park），这里是独特的约书亚树的主要栖息地。（戴兵/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月29日讯】<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张纯之编译报导）据<ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A6%E4%B9%A6%E4%BA%9A%E6%A0%91%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">约书亚树国家公园</a>（</span></span>Joshua Tree National Park<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）服务部最新报告，</span></span>2015<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年，有超过</span></span>200<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">万人拜访该公园。这些参观者在公园附近社区<ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E8%B4%B9.md#1">消费</a>了</span></span>9600<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">万美元，支持了</span></span>1341<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">个工作，<span lang="zh-CN">累计</span>为当地带来超过</span></span>1.28<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">亿美元<span lang="zh-CN">的</span><ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E%E6%94%B6%E7%9B%8A.md#1">经济收益</a>。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">公园经理哈罗（</span></span>Christian Harlow<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说，今年会比去年更忙。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">公园服务部报导说，</span></span>2014<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年，有</span></span>160<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">万人拜访该公园，<ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%88%E8%B4%B9.md#1">消费</a>约</span></span>6300<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">万美元。国家公园保护协会加州沙漠和国家野生动物计划主任拉姆弗洛姆（</span></span>David Lamfrom<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说，参观人数呈指数级增加。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">约书亚树商会（</span></span>Joshua Tree Chamber of Commerce<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）主任</span></span>Marcus Motamedi<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">说：“餐馆业一直快速增长，我们没有能力服务所有的访客。”他说，访客发现住宿和餐饮都很缺乏。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">为应对这个情况，</span></span>29<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">商会棕榈分会媒体顾问</span></span>Scott Clinkscales<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">说，该会正在帮助会员更好运用社交媒体掌握游客量。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">不过并非所有旅游增长效应都是积极的，比如涂鸦、故意破坏等，一直是公园官员们担心的。公园管理者</span></span>David Smith<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">说，本月有</span></span>4<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">名</span></span>20<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">多岁来自附近社区的年轻人因涉嫌污损公共财产而被捕。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">2<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">月，公园东南部的克里城堡舱和金矿（</span></span>Carey’s Castle cabin and gold mine<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）以及</span></span>El Sid<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">金矿（</span></span>El Sid gold mine<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）由于矿石及其它物品被盗而关闭。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">公园官员说，近年来，岩石喷涂、人为破坏以及艺术品被盗等情况一直在增加。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">该报告还分析了全美国家公园附近的消费结构，结果显示最大消费来自住宿，约占</span></span>31.1%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">，其次是餐饮占</span></span>20.2%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">，然后是汽油占</span></span>11.8%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">。◇</span></span></span></p>
