<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拉面达人评2016年版“史上十大美味方便面”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月20日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云编译报导）人称“拉面达人”的美国美食家汉斯?利内什（Hans Lienesch）18日在个人博客“拉面评家”（theramenrater.com）上发布了2016年版“史上十大美味方便面”排行榜。本年度榜单上，马来西亚产品占去四席，台湾的曾拌面排在第九。</p>
<p>利内什在文中透露，去年他发文品评了357种新品方便面，去泰国参观了方便面公司，近日则刚刚发表了第2,000篇“面评”。这促使他从迄今品尝过的2004种泡面中评选出了2016新版的Top 10榜单。</p>
<p>他也再次强调，自己的品鉴是以原产品为基础。佐食的配料是在尝了汤头、面条和原来的料包后添加的。</p>
<p><strong>10. MAMA牌椰奶味方便面（缅甸）</strong></p>
<p>MAMA Instant Noodles Coconut Milk Flavour</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571938" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330532669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571938 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330532669-450x260.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571938" class="wp-caption-text">MAMA牌椰奶味方便面（缅甸）(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>拉面达人说他今年早些时候收到MAMA方便面公司样本的大箱子，为这种杯面的独特品味感到惊艳，而袋装也同样不同凡响。面条浸在椰子鸡肉味的甜美汤头中，有迷人的口感。而这是缅甸泡面第一次被他选入前十榜单。</p>
<p>佐食配料：煮鸡蛋、焗鸡、香菜和烤甜洋葱。</p>
<p><strong>9. 曾拌面，青葱拌酱与椒麻拌酱（台湾）</strong></p>
<p>Zeng Noodles Scallion With Sichuan Pepper Flavor</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571937" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330562669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571937 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330562669-450x247.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571937" class="wp-caption-text">曾拌面，青葱拌酱与椒麻拌酱(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>拉面达人表示，自己今年有机会品尝了不少台湾新品，曾拌面脱颖而出。宽展的面条口感Q弹。酱包量足，不多不少正好把面条裹住，而不是太少；且四川椒麻与其它口味保持了完美平衡。花椒如同薄荷带来清爽感。</p>
<p>佐食配料：中式香肠，煸香的葱蒜。</p>
<p><strong>8. Prima<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%B3%E9%A3%9F%E9%9D%A2.md#1">佳食面</a>，奶油鸡汤风味（斯里兰卡/新加坡）</strong></p>
<p>Prima Juzz’s Mee Creamy Chicken Flavour – Sri Lanka / Singapore</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330582669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571936 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191330582669-450x254.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571936" class="wp-caption-text">Prima<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%B3%E9%A3%9F%E9%9D%A2.md#1">佳食面</a>，奶油鸡汤风味。(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>拉面达人表示自己一直偏爱奶油鸡味方便面，他的第一篇“面评”就是奶油鸡味面。这款面条厚厚宽宽——比较像台湾面，但更柔软。肉汤比市售奶油鸡汤更厚重，加进的植物蛋白使之更鲜美。这款面是斯里兰卡为新加坡市场生产的。</p>
<p>佐食配料：煮鸡蛋，拌合约翰尼鸡肉调味料（Johnny’s Chicken Seasoning）和激辛调味料（BonCabe Level 10）烤制的鸡块（华氏375度烤45分钟）。</p>
<p><strong>7. 五谷道场番茄牛腩紫薯面（中国）</strong></p>
<p>Wugudaochang Tomato Beef Brisket Flavor Purple Sweet Potato Noodles</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331012669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571934 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331012669-450x216.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="216" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571934" class="wp-caption-text">五谷道场番茄牛腩紫薯面(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这款面让拉面达人惊讶：紫薯面不但很醒目，质量也非常高。面条的规格与其它品种类似，但很有嚼劲。番茄牛肉味汤头味道和质感俱佳。最棒的部分是大块的新鲜番茄和牛肉，值得细细品味。这是中国面条首次登上拉面达人的年度Top 10。</p>
<p>佐食配料：香菜。</p>
<p><strong>6. MyKuali槟城白<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">咖喱面</a>（地道风味，马来西亚）</strong></p>
<p>MyKuali Penang White Curry Noodle Authentic Taste</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571933" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331042669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571933 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331042669-450x267.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571933" class="wp-caption-text">MyKuali槟城白<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">咖喱面</a>(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这款面最早吸引了拉面达人开始关注马拉西亚方便面：经典的汤头，完美的面条口感。新的“地道风味”更加浓郁、辛辣，故而连续第三年进入年度Top 10。</p>
<p>佐食配料：Salad Cosmo牌绿豆芽，薄荷，炸豆腐（Tau Pok），Dodo牌鸟鱼丸，甜洋葱。</p>
<p><strong>5. 百胜厨新加坡咖喱全麦拉面（新加坡）</strong></p>
<p>Prima Taste Singapore Curry Wholegrain La Mian</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331062669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571926 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331062669-450x256.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571926" class="wp-caption-text">百胜厨新加坡咖喱全麦拉面(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>拉面达人钟爱这款咖喱拉面已有不少年头：面条宽厚耐嚼，汤味浓郁，配上酱包和和椰子粉尤其出彩。创新之处在于面条已经升级为用全麦面粉制作，更接近手工品质，口感亲切。</p>
<p>佐食配料：香菜，Salad Cosmo牌绿豆芽，炸豆腐，Dodo牌鸟鱼丸。</p>
<p><strong>4. MyKuali槟城红<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E8%8D%AB%E5%8A%9F.md#1">冬荫功</a>（酸辣味）汤面（地道风味，马来西亚）</strong></p>
<p>MyKuali Penang Red Tom Yum Goong Noodle Authentic Taste</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571921" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331092669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571921 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331092669-450x277.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571921" class="wp-caption-text">MyKuali槟城红<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E8%8D%AB%E5%8A%9F.md#1">冬荫功</a>（酸辣味）汤面(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这是MyKuali新出的“地道风味”系列的另一款。同样，面条耐嚼，汤头是浓郁的冬荫功味：在虾、柠檬草、番茄和辣味基础上新加入了菇香。这款面是第二年上榜。</p>
<p>佐食配料：香菜，Salad Cosmo牌绿豆芽，虾。</p>
<p><strong>3. 佳珍娘惹咖喱叻沙面（改进风味，马来西亚）</strong></p>
<p>CarJEN Nyonya Curry Laksa Improved Taste</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571920" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331122669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571920 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331122669-450x266.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="266" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571920" class="wp-caption-text">佳珍娘惹咖喱叻沙面（改进风味）(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>面条品质和质感俱佳，浓郁的咖喱汤头用椰子奶油粉来增稠（而不是用非奶制品奶精），还加入了马来参巴香辣酱（sambal），使得改进配方味道更丰富、更有冲击力。这也是这款面第二年上榜。<br />
佐食配料：Salad Cosmo牌绿豆芽，炸豆腐。</p>
<p><strong>2. MyKuali槟城辣虾汤面（地道风味，马来西亚）</strong></p>
<p>MyKuali Penang Spicy Prawn Soup Noodle Authentic Taste</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331142669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571918 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331142669-450x278.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571918" class="wp-caption-text">MyKuali槟城辣虾汤面(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这款面从去年的第七位来到亚军位置，原因即在于“地道风味”的改良。面条一如既往地棒——粗而有咬劲。虾味汤头有甜甜的浓香。更重要的是配加了炸洋葱料包——不是粉末状，而是有模有样。美丽。这也是这款面第二年进入Top 10。新增沙拉科斯莫绿豆芽，香菜，煮鸡蛋，叉烧和虾。</p>
<p>佐食配料：Salad Cosmo牌绿豆芽，香菜，煮鸡蛋，烧烤猪肉，虾。</p>
<p><strong>1. 百胜厨新加坡叻沙全麦拉面（新加坡）</strong></p>
<p>Prima Taste Singapore Laksa Whole grain La Mian</p>
<figure id="attachment_7571913" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7571913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331172669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7571913 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604191331172669-450x258.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7571913" class="wp-caption-text">百胜厨新加坡叻沙全麦拉面(courtsy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>在今年Top 10榜单坐上头把交椅的是拉面达人宝爱很久的老品种的改良版：全麦面条品质更佳，奶油色汤上漂着的油滴如同一粒粒红宝石，具有赏心悦目的美。浓浓的辣汤老少咸宜。拉面达人特别提到，如果没有这款全麦面，原来的叻沙拉面和咖喱拉面品种也应上榜。</p>
<p>佐食配料：Dodo牌鱼丸，鱿鱼卷。@*</p>
