<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>研究：青少年睡眠不足易酒驾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1506091322552700-e1460158554102.jpg" />
<h6>睡眠少的高中生更容易做出酒驾、开车发短信、乘坐喝酒者驾驶的车辆，以及不扣安全带等危险行为。图为开车途中使用手机。（薛文/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月09日讯】<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张纯之编译报导）最新研究发现，和睡足</span></span>9<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">个小时的高中生相比，睡眠少的高中生更容易做出<ahref="https://github.com/pussbx395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E9%A9%BE.md#1">酒驾</a>、开车发短信、乘坐喝酒者驾驶的车辆，以及不扣安全带等危险行为。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">从</span></span>2007<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年到</span></span>2013<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年，疾病控制预防中心研究者调查了超过</span></span>5<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">万</span></span>9-12<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">年级的青少年。调查者列出了一系列危险行为，询问学生们在过去</span></span>30<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">天里有没有做出其中一种行为。还问他们的平均睡眠时间等问题。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">国家睡眠基金说，</span></span>14-17<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的青少年每晚应当睡足</span></span>8-10<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">个小时。新调查报告的作者也说，长期睡眠短缺可能会导致判断力下降等。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">但是，调查结果显示，大部分年轻人都没有睡足。只有不到</span></span>30%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的青少年每晚平均睡眠在</span></span>8-9<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时，大约</span></span>30%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的每晚睡眠</span></span>7<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时，</span></span>22%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的平均睡</span></span>6<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时，还有</span></span>10.5%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的人平均睡</span></span>5<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时，<span lang="zh-CN">仅</span>有</span></span>1.8%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的<ahref="https://github.com/pussbx395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E7%9D%A1%E7%9C%A0.md#1">青少年睡眠</a><span lang="zh-CN">达或</span>超过</span></span>10<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">报告说，相比每晚平均睡足</span></span>9<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时的少年，睡眠平均</span></span>6<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时的青年人有</span></span>84%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">说过去</span></span>30<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">天他们有喝<ahref="https://github.com/pussbx395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E9%A9%BE.md#1">酒驾</a>车行为，</span></span>92%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">说他们有过乘车不扣安全带。还有</span></span>42%<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">的少年说他们乘坐了喝酒司机驾驶的车。而每晚睡眠</span></span>5<span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时以下的青少年，做出以上危险行为的比例是休息良好的青少年的两倍还多。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">尽管缺少睡眠容易导致危险行为，但是调查显示太多睡眠也容易导致青少年的危险行为。在每晚平均睡眠</span></span></span></span>10<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">小时以上的青少年中，做出上面危险行为的比例也比正常睡足青少年更高。研究者说，抑郁可能是导致这些青年做出冒险行为比例较高的最好解释。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">另外，报告显示，睡眠不足的女孩子比睡眠不足的男孩子更多（为</span></span></span></span>71%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">对</span></span></span></span>66.4%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">）。</span></span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">而</span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">亚裔学生</span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">是</span></span><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">各族裔学生中睡</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">眠不足</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">最高的，</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">比例</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">达</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;">75.7%</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: PMingLiU;"><span lang="zh-TW">?◇</span></span></span></span></span></p>
