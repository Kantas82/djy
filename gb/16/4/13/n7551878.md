<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蔬菜生而不平等！盘点最适合沙拉的绿叶菜TOP10</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>就<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">沙拉</a>配菜而言，绿叶<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>并非“生而平等”，其营养成分和营养价值都有很大差别。美媒“商业内幕”（Business Insider）利用美国疾病控制与预防中心（CDC）2014年发布的“超级食品”（powerhouse foods）榜单，结合蔬菜的营养成分分析，刊文罗列出了适合拌沙拉的营养<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%8F%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">绿叶菜</a>TOP10排行榜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">盘点最适合拌<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">沙拉</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%8F%B6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">绿叶菜</a>TOP10</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>10. 球形生菜（卷心莴苣，Iceberg lettuce）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_5795904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5795904" style="width: 447px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1301100155312382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5795904 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/11/1301100155312382-e1520745926481.jpg" alt="" width="447" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5795904" class="wp-caption-text">生菜。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>球形生菜是最没营养的绿色<ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E8%8F%9C.md#1">蔬菜</a>之一。事实上，美国炸鸡连锁餐厅“福来鸡”（Chick-Fil-A）甚至禁用生菜，原因就是营养成分不足。每杯生菜大约只提供人体每日所维生素A的7%、所需维生素C的3%。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：10</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>9. 芝麻菜（Arugula）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131438042669.png"><img class="wp-image-7551904 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131438042669-450x337.png" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551904" class="wp-caption-text">芝麻菜。(SweetOnVeg/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>芝麻菜味道独特，但营养含量不高。它是维生素A和K的来源，但缺乏其它营养成分。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：6</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>8. 羽衣甘蓝（Kale）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7534868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7534868" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1407310032482483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7534868 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1407310032482483-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7534868" class="wp-caption-text">羽衣甘蓝叶。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>颇受欢迎的羽衣甘蓝，维生素A、C和K含量较高，维生素K对血液凝结尤为重要。但它也有个小小缺点：热量较其它蔬菜高（其实还是非常低），意味着其营养密度比较低。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：33</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>7. 紫莴苣（Radicchio）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551907" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435532669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551907 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435532669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551907" class="wp-caption-text">紫莴苣。(Wiki Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫莴苣是菊苣家族的成员，它也含有维生素K，可以100%满足人体日常所需。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：9</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>6. 苦苣（苦菊，Endive）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551912" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435502669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551912 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435502669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551912" class="wp-caption-text">苦苣。(Lablascovegmenu/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>苦苣也是一种菊苣，富含维生素K，每杯苦苣可满足20%的人体所需维生素A。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：8</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>5. 长叶生菜（罗马生菜，Romaine）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435462669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551928 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435462669-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551928" class="wp-caption-text">长叶生菜。(liz west/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>长叶生菜跻身美国疾病预防控制中心发布的超级食品前十榜单，原因是食用它可降低罹患慢性病的风险。长叶生菜的维生素A含量特别高，一杯生菜含人体日需维生素A的81%，还含有B族维生素。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：8</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong> 4. 花叶生菜（叶用莴苣，Leaf lettuce）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551930" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435432669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551930 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435432669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551930" class="wp-caption-text">花叶生菜(Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>它是生菜家族中最有营养的品种，热量低，富含钾及维生素A和K。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：5<br />
<strong><br />
<span style="color: #800000;">3. <ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">菠菜</a>（Spinach）</span></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7551931" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551931" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435392669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551931 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435392669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551931" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">菠菜</a>。(Ccharmon/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>菠菜作为蔬果沙拉常用的配菜，凭借其全面的营养价值进入前三甲。它含有多种维生素和营养物质，特别是钾和铁，对调节红血球和血压很重要。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：7</p>
<h3><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>2. 瑞士甜菜（莙荙菜，Chard）</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551940" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131509302669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551940 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131509302669-450x246.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551940" class="wp-caption-text">瑞士甜菜。(eflon/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>瑞士甜菜红色的菜梗很好辨识，其低热量、高营养使之成为顶级食品之一。甜菜的维生素K含量比任何绿叶菜都高，一杯即含人体日需维生素K的300%。甜菜含镁也相当多，镁对于维持肌肉和神经健康、控制血糖、调节血压都很重要。</p>
<h3>每杯卡路里：7<br />
<strong><br />
<span style="color: #800000;">1. <ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B4%8B%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西洋菜</a>（Watercress）</span></strong></h3>
<figure id="attachment_7551942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7551942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435352669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7551942 size-medium" src="//i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604131435352669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7551942" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jrmdsc346/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B4%8B%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西洋菜</a>。(Wendell Smith/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>顶着小圆叶的西洋菜，被美国疾病控制与预防中心评为最顶级食物。它的维生素A、C和钾含量都非常高，热量则极低。西洋菜很适合家庭种植，常吃可预防2型糖尿病。</p>
<p>每杯卡路里：4</p>
