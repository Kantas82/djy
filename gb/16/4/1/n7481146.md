<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>整洁的家更健康 老外这样整理家居</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/100617083213100445-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>早上起床将床铺整理干净，床不是用来堆积衣物的。（摄影：auris/Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月01日讯】（大纪元记者明月报导）窗明几净的居家环境让人心情开朗、身体健康；反之，肮脏凌乱的环境让人心情烦躁、身体不适。下面我们就来看看老外如何整里<ahref="https://github.com/pwazrt366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B1%85.md#1">家居</a>。</p>
<p><strong>整理床铺</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7481966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7481966" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1312041014041750.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7481966 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1312041014041750-300x414.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="414" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7481966" class="wp-caption-text">每天起床，把床铺收拾整齐。（李贤珍/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每天起床，把床铺收拾整齐。床是用来睡觉的，不是堆放杂物的地方，把书本放回书架上，衣服吊在衣架上。如果把它们都堆在床上，不但不<ahref="https://github.com/pwazrt366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%B4%E6%B4%81.md#1">整洁</a>，积灰了还影响健康。</p>
<p><strong>用完随手整理</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482058" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1311251521532483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482058 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1311251521532483-300x282.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="282" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482058" class="wp-caption-text">梳洗后东西放回原位，保持脸盆的<ahref="https://github.com/pwazrt366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%B4%E6%B4%81.md#1">整洁</a>。（大纪元资料室）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刷牙后，把盥洗盆的水擦干；梳妆后，把护肤品盖上，放回原位；做完饭后，把灶台清理干净。总之，每次办完事情顺手处理，很快就能整理干净。如果用完东西随处摆放，东西就会越摆越乱，积累灰尘，到时就要花大力气清理了。</p>
<p><strong>善用记事本</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_6696061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696061" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304021137342184.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6696061 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304021137342184-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696061" class="wp-caption-text">善用记事本记录生活中的大小事。(图 :photos.com)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有些人习惯随手在信封背后或小贴纸上记电话号码、写灵感等，其实这是混乱的开始。写完随手一扔，东一张西一张的，把家里弄得杂乱不说，等要用到时却经常找不到。应该学习把这类零碎资料记在记事本或手机里，既节省空间，又不容易弄丢。</p>
<p><strong>别囤东西</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482125" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482125" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1303011046341528.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482125 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1303011046341528-300x368.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="368" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482125" class="wp-caption-text">日常用品需要多少买多少。Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>日常用品最好是需要多少买多少，别看到便宜就买，结果囤了一堆，不仅占用空间，还浪费钱，因为等到要用时却发现已经过期了。再者，杂物越多，打扫时花的时间就越长。</p>
<p><strong>丢弃不用的物品</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482118" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482118" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1209180934192382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482118 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1209180934192382-300x330.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="330" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482118" class="wp-caption-text">家中杂物太多，是该丢弃的时候了。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>家中杂物太多，是丢弃的时候了。日本杂物管理咨询师山下英子在其著作《断舍离》一书中说在决定东西要留还是要舍时，要问的问题是：“现在的我需要这件东西吗？”那些“总有一天会用到”、“丢了很可惜”的东西，绝大部分都可以不要。</p>
<p>美国整理专家史卡夫指出，许多人把不用的东西都留着，心想要用到时就不必再买，省时省钱。可是，在真的需要用到这些东西时，却怎么也找不到，因为它们早已被一样又一样舍不得丢的东西淹没了。根据他的经验，如果你已经一年没用过这样东西，你大概就不需要、可以丢了。</p>
<p><strong>别乱扔衣服</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482163" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1404091044582483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482163 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1404091044582483-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482163" class="wp-caption-text">脏衣服换下来之后，应该放到洗衣篮或洗衣机里，别随手扔在沙发、椅子或地板上。</figcaption></figure>
<p>每次把脏衣服换下来之后，应该放到洗衣篮或洗衣机里，别随手扔在沙发、椅子或地板上，因为这只会让你花更多的时间去收拾。如果衣服还要再穿，就把它吊在架子上，既不皱，又通风。</p>
<p><strong>吃完饭就洗碗</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482172" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1503061544262483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482172 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1503061544262483-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="225" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482172" class="wp-caption-text">吃完饭就洗碗。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用过的盘碗要尽快洗干净、擦干，放进碗柜或消毒柜里。有些人习惯吃完饭继续看电视，把碗堆个一两天才洗。这样很不卫生，而且堆多了看着都累，更不想洗了。</p>
<p><strong>勤换洗碗布</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_5922076" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5922076" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/09/100929131820.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5922076 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/09/100929131820-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5922076" class="wp-caption-text">勤换洗碗布。（photos.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沾满了油污和食物残渣的桌布、洗碗布（或洗碗海绵）是包括大肠杆菌在内的很多病菌的温床。建议准备两三块布轮着使用，而且每天用完后都用消毒剂清洗、晾干。食物的清洁和烹饪用具的干净对人体的健康都很重要。</p>
<p><strong>把毛巾晾起来</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482179" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1406190815422483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482179 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1406190815422483-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482179" class="wp-caption-text">干净的白毛巾挂在衣架上（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用完面巾和毛巾之后要晾起来，把窗子打开，让它通风，不要随手扔在洗手盆、沙发或椅子上，潮湿的棉质品很容易发霉，有异味，也不利于健康。天气晴朗时，最好晒在室外。面巾和毛巾要定期换洗，别等到油腻了、长黑斑了才拿去洗。</p>
<p><strong>每天倒垃圾</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7482185" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7482185" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1409282131022483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7482185 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1409282131022483-300x300.jpg" alt="每天倒垃圾。（Fotolia)" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7482185" class="wp-caption-text">垃圾桶（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不是每个人都有每天倒垃圾的习惯，但这却是很重要的。厨房的垃圾，如剩菜、果皮、虾壳、鱼骨之类的，很容易发臭、滋生细菌，会引来蚂蚁、苍蝇、蟑螂、老鼠等。尤其是养了宠物，更要每天倒垃圾，否则就麻烦了。</p>
<p>其实，只要养成好习惯，每天仅花一点时间，就能把这些小细节做好，使<ahref="https://github.com/pwazrt366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B1%85.md#1">家居</a>更整洁，生活更健康。住在干净整洁的环境里，我们也会感觉心情舒畅，幸福感倍增。</p>
