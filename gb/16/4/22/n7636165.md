<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>干旱中 加州水利部再放松供水</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月23日讯】<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">（大纪元记者夏君编译报导）周四（</span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span>21<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">日）﹐<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E%E6%B0%B4%E5%88%A9%E8%B5%84%E6%BA%90%E9%83%A8.md#1">加州水利资源部</a>（</span></span>Department of Water Resources<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）官员说﹐由于今年春季风暴带来的雨水几乎填满了州内的主要水库﹐</span></span></span><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">所以加州今年将再提供比此前三次中每次都多的水供应量。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E%E6%B0%B4%E5%88%A9%E8%B5%84%E6%BA%90%E9%83%A8.md#1">加州水利资源部</a>宣布﹐那些为加州近</span></span>100<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">万亩农田和</span></span>4000<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">万居民供水的水区将收到他们所要求的供水量的</span></span>60%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">官员说，</span></span>2<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月份加州大部地区仍处于<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E6%97%B1%E7%8A%B6%E6%80%81.md#1">干旱状态</a>﹐</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月份的风暴给北加州带来了很多<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E9%87%8F.md#1">雨量</a>，但州里一直敦促居民继续<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%B4.md#1">节水</a>，因为目前尚不清楚明年冬天雨季会带来多少<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E9%87%8F.md#1">雨量</a>。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">加州水利资源部主任科文（</span></span>Mark Cowin<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）在一份声明中说﹐<ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%B4.md#1">节水</a>是保障供水的最可靠和最简单的方式。每个人都需要养成节水和明智用水的好习惯。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">加州已连续</span></span>5<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年干旱，但从去年</span></span>12<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月份开始﹐供水分配却已连续四次调涨。而在</span></span>2014<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年，加州水利资源部仅为水区客户提供他们所要求的供水量的</span></span>5%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">北加州今春获得的雨量比圣华金谷和南加州多得多，圣华金谷和南加州水库的水位仍很低。官员说，加州要想摆脱干旱还需几年的时间。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">现在﹐全州居民仍被要求比干旱前节水至少</span></span>20%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。◇</span></span></span></p>
