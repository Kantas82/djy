<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>邻家酒屋般的法式大餐Daniel</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月18日讯】(大纪元记者李乐儿综合报导)?Daniel是一家位于<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%BC%E5%93%88%E9%A1%BF.md#1">曼哈顿</a>上东区的<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>二星餐厅，晚餐价格极其优惠，传统三道菜（前菜，主菜，甜点）只要＄125。Daniel的服务生素质极高，是人们公认的最温馨的米其林餐厅 。低调的Daniel并没有顶级<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">法国餐厅</a>的高傲，反而更像隐藏在<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6.md#1">纽约</a>巷弄里的邻家<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%92%E5%B1%8B.md#1">酒屋</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237616" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1ab-DANIEL-Dining-Room-by-Eric-Laignel-600x442.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8237616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1ab-DANIEL-Dining-Room-by-Eric-Laignel-600x442.jpg" alt="DANIEL 的晚宴厅 (图 Eric-Laignel)" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237616" class="wp-caption-text">DANIEL 的晚宴厅 (图 Eric-Laignel)</figcaption></figure>
<p>很多人评价说：在Daniel的就餐经历是他们此生吃过最完美的一餐，他们甚至想不出任何缺点，也不懂Daniel为何会在<ahref="https://github.com/oyagpl3054/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>二星和三星间起伏。跟其他餐厅一样高档的美食，却有超出顾客预期的体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237618" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DANIEL_SkyBox2012_22_b_by-T.Schauer-600x334.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8237618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/DANIEL_SkyBox2012_22_b_by-T.Schauer-600x334.jpg" alt="DANIEL SkyBox厅 (图 T.Schauer)" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237618" class="wp-caption-text">DANIEL SkyBox厅 (图 T.Schauer)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Daniel有着非常漂亮的晚餐大厅，由美国知名室内设计师Adam D. Tihany所设计，古典吊灯和柱子，配上优雅的花草装饰，让人不禁想起电影泰坦尼克号中奢华的晚宴大厅。甚至很多人会选择在这里举办婚礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-Miel-SigneBirck-600x810.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8237641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-Miel-SigneBirck-600x810.jpg" alt="Daniel Miel (图 Signe Birck)" width="600" b="810" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237641" class="wp-caption-text">Daniel Miel (图 Signe Birck)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8237622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237622" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-PetitsPois-SigneBirck-600x599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8237622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-PetitsPois-SigneBirck-600x599.jpg" alt="Daniel Petits Pois (图 Signe Birck)" width="600" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237622" class="wp-caption-text">Daniel Petits Pois (图 Signe Birck)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Daniel的前菜非常美味，还曾有顾客反馈前菜会把主菜的风头全部抢走。不过他们的主菜也极具特色，在一道料理中有两种或两种以上的味觉呈现，根据食材不同的位置做出“求同存异”的美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-Canard-SigneBirck-600x820.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8237639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Daniel-Canard-SigneBirck-600x820.jpg" alt="Daniel Canard (图 Signe Birck)" width="600" b="820" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237639" class="wp-caption-text">Daniel Canard (图 Signe Birck)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8237636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237636" style="width: 675px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/White-Cosmo-vertical_NF.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8237636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/White-Cosmo-vertical_NF.jpg" alt="Daniel White Cosmo (图 Daniel 提供)" width="675" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237636" class="wp-caption-text">Daniel White Cosmo (图 Daniel 提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Daniel还有一个特色的花瓣冰酒White Cosmopolitan是最受欢迎的调酒之一，中间是一个带有花瓣的冰块，花香和白蔓越莓的味道酸甜爽口，让人唇齿留香。</p>
<p>地址：60 E 65th St, New York, NY 10065</p>
<p>网址：<ahref="http://www.danielnyc.com">www.danielnyc.com</a></p>
