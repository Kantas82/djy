<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>米其林三星法餐 Jean Georges</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120725_dine_064_1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>落地玻璃，举目可见中央公园，让用餐时的心情份外愉快 ( Jean Georges 提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月18日讯】(大纪元记者李乐儿综合报导) ?位于纽约<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%A4%AE%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">中央公园</a>附近的Jean Georges餐厅在经历了几十年的风霜之后依然骄傲的保持着<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>三星的头衔。它的主厨Jean-Georges Vongerichten作为世界上最负盛名的<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%A8%E5%B8%88.md#1">厨师</a>之一，完美的结合不同的食材口味与亚欧风情，因其不断迸发的创意，与新颖独到的烹饪方法，被称为“法国现代烹饪界的奇葩”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_086_1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8237728 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_086_1-600x400.jpg" alt="主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 亲自制作的炭烧乳鸽及配菜 Char grilled squab, crushed sweet peas and nasturtium vinaigrette. (Jean Georges 提供|photo by Francesco Tonelli )" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237728" class="wp-caption-text">主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 亲自制作的炭烧乳鸽及配菜 Char grilled squab, crushed sweet peas and nasturtium vinaigrette. (Jean Georges 提供 photo by Francesco Tonelli )</figcaption></figure>
<p>在美剧“欲望都市”中，主角Samantha说：“看一个男人的权势和地位，就要看他在Jean Georges吃饭需要不需要定位”。这也说明了Jean Georges在餐饮界尊贵的地位。他的预订难度指数为4星，仅接受提前1个月的电话预定或Open Table。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_094_1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8237732 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_094_1-600x400.jpg" alt="黑海鲈鱼由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges | photo by Francesco Tonelli )" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237732" class="wp-caption-text">黑海鲈鱼由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges|photo by Francesco Tonelli )</figcaption></figure>
<p>出生在法国的Jean Georges生长在一个热爱美食的家庭。经过了法国南部的烹饪大师Louis Outhier的锤炼之后，他曾到曼谷，新加坡和香港工作和旅行，这让他爱上了富有异国情调的亚洲风味。从此，法式菜肴在他的手中发生了质的变化。他将传统的法式与东方料理进行了融合，创造出了新颖的“新式法餐”。他不仅将传统浓郁的奶油肉汤底改良为清新的蔬菜汁、水果香味和清汤，还大量加强香料的运用，营造出强烈的异域风味。他的举动不仅让众多主厨争相模仿，也让餐厅的顾客时常会有出乎意料的惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_132_1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8237747 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_132_1-600x400.jpg" alt="焦糖扇贝由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges|photo by Francesco Tonelli )" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237747" class="wp-caption-text">焦糖扇贝由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges|photo by Francesco Tonelli )</figcaption></figure>
<p>Jean Georges开业三个月的时候，29岁的他就获得“New York Times”四星好评，入选由James Beard Foundation颁发的最佳新餐厅和最杰出<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%A8%E5%B8%88.md#1">厨师</a>大奖。而后更是一举拿下<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>三星,全球前50餐厅等多项大奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8237766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8237766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_121_1-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8237766 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/20120709_jgr_121_1-600x400.jpg" alt="黄鳍金枪鱼由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges|photo by Francesco Tonelli )" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8237766" class="wp-caption-text">黄鳍金枪鱼由主厨 Jean Georges 和主厨 Gregory Brainin 准备 (图 Jean Georges|photo by Francesco Tonelli )</figcaption></figure>
<p>Jean Georges的主餐区是由Thomas Juul-Hansen设计的大片落地窗。透过落地窗就可以观赏到喧闹的<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%BC%E5%93%88%E9%A1%BF.md#1">曼哈顿</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/mepkcs322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%A4%AE%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">中央公园</a>。金边的餐盘和银质刀具，让每位食客都感受到“帝王”般的待遇。</p>
<p>除了常规菜单，Jean Georges还有不同食材的季节性菜单。</p>
