<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波可诺山 特拉华河区自然之旅</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月18日讯】在纽约周边旅游，可以选择自驾去有2个小时车程的<ahref="https://github.com/hhttpp3765/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%8F%AF%E8%AF%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">波可诺山</a>风景区。波可诺山（POCONO MOUNTAINS）有着真正壮观的景色，这里有18个风格各异的度假庄园、11个原始公园、150个湖泊、270公里的河流和30多条<ahref="https://github.com/hhttpp3765/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">瀑布</a>。穿梭于树木繁茂的山丘和谷地，惝徉于绿荫蔽日的林地和湖畔，欣赏幽静而又壮观的瀑布与深潭，尽情体味回归自然的乐趣。这里还有美国排名第一的度假庄园和美东地区最大的水上乐园。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8211901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8211901 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/big-mountain-view2-450x178.jpg" alt="big mountain view2" width="450" b="178" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">想要计划纽约周边游，千万不要忘记景色壮观的<ahref="https://github.com/hhttpp3765/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%8F%AF%E8%AF%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">波可诺山</a>（POCONO MOUNTAINS）。（www.poconomountains.com）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>纽约周边旅游推荐：Delaware River Region?一日游路线</h3>
<p>? 在特拉华河上划独木舟或橡皮艇，享受特拉华州中部的宁静的自然风光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8211905" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8211905 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/86efa561ac19c4ee991ba8d17fe8cd30-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（www.poconomountains.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>? 小径徒步旅行，沿途欣赏密集的<ahref="https://github.com/hhttpp3765/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%80%91%E5%B8%83.md#1">瀑布</a>群，体验大自然赋予的独特景观：这里既有Resica瀑布，Winona瀑布，也有具“宾夕法尼亚的尼亚加拉”之称的Bushkill瀑布，Dingmans瀑布， Silverthread瀑布，Raymondskill瀑布，Deer Leap瀑布等8条瀑布。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8211904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8211904 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/Quiet-Valley-Living-Historical-Farm-450x338.jpg" alt="Quiet Valley Living Historical Farm" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（www.poconomountains.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>?参观波可诺印第安人博物馆（Pocono Indian Museum），可在周边小古董店、手工店或本土印第安人店铺闲逛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8211898" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8211898 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/04_-229-450x300.jpg" alt="04_ (229)" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（www.poconomountains.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>? 参观波可诺环境教育中心（PEEC），这里经常举办自然讲座活动，向人们介绍山区丰富的动植物种类。资深导游可提供团队讲解服务，带你探索当地鹰群等鸟类，让人们详细了解这里的自然生态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8211899" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8211899 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/8-9-450x300.jpg" alt="8" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（www.poconomountains.com）</figcaption></figure>
