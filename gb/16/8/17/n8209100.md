<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>什么饮料可以比咖啡更提神？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月17日讯】（大纪元记者李熙综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>、茶饮、手摇杯饮料是现代人生活中的好伙伴，工作时手边一杯饮料会有份幸福感觉，工作起来更带劲！不过，市售咖啡多添加了砂糖、人工甘味剂，并不是很好的健康选择。有几种食物和饮品不仅可以<ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%90%E7%A5%9E.md#1">提神</a>醒脑、消除疲劳，更有瘦身、抗老等功效，更适合女性朋友。</p>
<p><strong>1.苹果醋</strong></p>
<p>苹果醋含有消除疲劳的有机酸和氨基酸；果胶能促进肠胃的消化，有减肥作用。加了蜂蜜的苹果醋能够促进代谢功能恢复，从而消除疲劳，<ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8F%90%E7%A5%9E.md#1">提神</a>醒脑。蜂蜜中的营养成分相当丰富，有多种维生素、叶酸、泛酸、烟碱和矿物质，每天早上喝一杯，可以摄取到大量的营养。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8057056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8057056" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607011336072669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8057056 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607011336072669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8057056" class="wp-caption-text">苹果醋。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2.奇亚籽</strong></p>
<p>奇异籽近年来深受欧美健康人士的青睐，被称为超级食物的奇亚籽，含有8种体内无法自行合成的必须氨基酸，矿物质含量也很高，还具有蛋白质、维生素，亚麻仁油等营养成分，其中omega3脂肪酸含量相当丰富，不仅能预防疲劳、活化<ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%84%91.md#1">大脑</a>，还具有抗老功效。因膳食纤维含量高，泡水后可膨胀10倍，十分具有饱足感，是减肥者很好的饮品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5766371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5766371" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408272139401680.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5766371 size-medium" title="20080507-AMX-LIFE_NTR-CHIASEEDS_SA" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1408272139401680-450x326.jpg" alt="20080507-AMX-LIFE_NTR-CHIASEEDS_SA" width="450" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5766371" class="wp-caption-text">奇亚籽（Chia seeds）。 (Photo by Scott Lorenzo/Sacramento Bee/MCT via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3.</strong><strong>绿叶蔬菜</strong></p>
<p>绿叶蔬菜的维生素B含量相当丰富，早打成蔬果汁作为早餐，能够提高能量。铁质、镁、维生素B群、维生素C等营养素，都能提神醒脑，只要你吃蔬菜、水果、瘦肉，就很难会缺乏B群。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8200852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8200852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608141649442669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8200852 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608141649442669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8200852" class="wp-caption-text">蔬果汁。(Magdanatka/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4.</strong><strong>胡椒薄荷</strong></p>
<p>胡椒薄荷在帮助消化、消炎与提神醒脑方面效果颇佳。研究发现，胡椒薄荷的香气具有提高注意力、减少疲劳的效果。早上喝一杯胡椒薄荷香草茶，其提神醒脑效果更胜<ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8209691" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8209691" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1606021507592483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8209691 size-medium" title="Cups of tea with mint leaves." src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1606021507592483-450x279.jpg" alt="Cups of tea with mint leaves." width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8209691" class="wp-caption-text">薄荷茶。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5.</strong><strong>蛋</strong></p>
<p>鸡蛋中的蛋白质和脂肪能提供持续平稳的能量，有帮助提神的效果，是最适合做为早餐的食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7725983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7725983" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1106090131101012.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7725983 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/07/1106090131101012-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7725983" class="wp-caption-text">煎蛋。（大纪元资料图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6.</strong><strong>水</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_5863527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5863527" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1501071438352483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5863527 size-medium" title="年轻商人坐在办公室的桌子拿着杯子喝水（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1501071438352483-450x311.jpg" alt="年轻商人坐在办公室的桌子拿着杯子喝水（fotolia）" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5863527" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/kmveae3628/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%96%9D%E6%B0%B4.md#1">喝水</a>提神。（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上刚起床时，为身体补充足够水分，能促使身体恢复正常机能，也是最简单的醒脑方法。</p>
<p>“疲倦是最高的脱水信号”，因此，水可说是最佳的提神饮料。@*</p>
