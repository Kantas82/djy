<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：在德国应品尝的29道美食（上）</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月06日讯】德国人的饮食可能不太清淡，却是货真价实的好东西。虽然各色香肠（wurst）令人眼花缭乱，还是有很多其它美食可选（多数不适合素食者），比如淋肉汁的肉丸、各种制法的土豆和各式甜点。下面就为您逐一呈现。</p>
<p><strong>1. 巴伐利亚白香肠（Weisswurst）尽管外表相当朴实无华，却是当地人早餐的主食。吃之前别忘记剥皮。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612322669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173352 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612322669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173352" class="wp-caption-text">(Alper ?u?un/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 如果你正想饱餐一顿，炸肉排（schnitzel）是最佳选择，就是裹面包屑油炸的肉排，有鸡肉排和猪肉排，不过最常见也最值得品尝的还是小牛肉排。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173354" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051620232669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173354 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051620232669-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173354" class="wp-caption-text">(Manuel Menal/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 德国苹果馅饼（apfelstrudel）外皮酥脆，里面是带着热气的苹果馅和糖粉，食用时要淋上香草冰淇淋或香草酱。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173358" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612252669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173358 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612252669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173358" class="wp-caption-text">(Oliver Hallmann/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 最适合配香肠和炸肉排一起吃的是土豆沙拉（kartoffelsalat）。与美国不同，德国土豆沙拉是用醋和芥末拌的。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173360" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051626512669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173360 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051626512669-450x345.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="345" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173360" class="wp-caption-text">(Oxfordian Kissuth, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5. Frittatensuppe 是浸着咸味鸡蛋煎饼条的牛肉清汤。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051629172669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173362 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051629172669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173362" class="wp-caption-text">(Rkolarsky, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 德国几乎每家面包店都出售大号的椒盐卷饼（bretzel，也称蝴蝶脆饼）。饼里面超软，外皮很脆，不同于美国同类食品（pretzel）的坚硬口感。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173365" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612212669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173365 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612212669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173365" class="wp-caption-text">(Spidysg/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7. 黑森林樱桃果仁蛋糕（Schwarzw?lder kirschtorte）是德国最精致的蛋糕，名字来自其产地黑森林地区，一层层的巧克力蛋糕上饰有奶油和樱桃。</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612172669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173368 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612172669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p><strong>8. 德国最流行的街食是咖喱香肠（Currywurst），为经过汆烫再油煎的切块香肠浇上咖喱番茄酱再撒上咖喱粉。</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612042669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173372 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051612042669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p><strong>9. 微咸的德国鸡蛋面疙瘩（sp?tzle）可以单吃，也可以与肉汁或奶酪佐餐。虽然常常作为配菜，但硬奶酪酱面疙瘩（k?sesp?tzle）配烘烤洋葱则是一道流行主食。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051615202669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173374 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051615202669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173374" class="wp-caption-text">(Christian Allinger/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>10. 从4月到6月下旬，你会发现所有菜单上都有芦笋。时令白芦笋可以说是德国人最爱吃的蔬菜。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8173376" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8173376" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051615262669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8173376 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608051615262669-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8173376" class="wp-caption-text">(Walter Sch?rer/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
