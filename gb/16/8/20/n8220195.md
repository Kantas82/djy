<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：人性化的SYNC 2016 Ford Focus</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2016_Ford_Focus_01-e1471673227881.jpg" />
<h6>2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月20日讯】在众多的小型汽车中，Ford Focus可算是提供最多变化的一辆，先可分为四门房车Sedan或揭背式Hatchback两种，而又有低耗油的一升引擎版。要纯电力的，Focus Electric也可满足您。喜欢跑车的，Focus除了有ST版之外，今年也有终极RS版在北美推出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220216" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159082224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220216 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159082224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220216" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>而今周测试的，回归基本，是一辆装置了2升自然吸气直列四缸引擎的Focus Titanium，最高马力输出160匹，峰值扭矩146磅呎，引擎压缩比为12：1，Ford此引擎为Flexfuel设计，可使用普通汽油又或E85汽油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220218" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159292224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220218 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159292224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220218" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8220225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220225" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159192224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220225 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159192224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220225" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据不同型号，有不同的变速箱选择，总计有三种，5速手动变速箱、6速PowerShift自动变速箱及6速PowerShift自动变速箱加上SelectShift手动升降系统。测<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%95%E8%BD%A6.md#1">试车</a>为Titanium版，已装备了6速PowerShift自动变速箱加上SelectShift手动升降系统。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220220" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159132224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220220 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159132224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220220" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>测<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%95%E8%BD%A6.md#1">试车</a>采取深蓝色车身颜色，再加上各种镀铬的饰边，前进风口栏栅也是使用了镀铬凸出了线条，整辆车给人的感觉也高级了不少。Focus Titanium标配17吋轮圈轮胎，而测试车升级至18吋合金轮圈轮胎，为Focus增加了帅气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220221" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159262224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220221 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159262224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220221" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>2016年Ford Focus均装有SYNC或SYNC with MyFord系统，又或可升级至SYNC 3系统，而高级版如Titanium均已标配SYNC 3系统。SYNC 3拥有更大的屏幕，运算更快，并已包含导航系统，以及更高级的语音系统，可辨认出更人性化的语句，例如：“Call Mom〈致电母亲〉”，而且可使用语音控制如恒温系统等。SYNC 3可连接WIFI并为SYNC 3系统更新。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220222" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159232224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220222 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159232224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220222" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然Focus〈测试车〉也配备了“自动泊车系统〈Active Park Assist〉”，但Focus的就只可以进行“平行泊车〈Parallel Parking〉，并没有垂直泊车（Perpendicular Parking）的选择。至于后视镜头，已经是所有Focus的标配装置，对不少人来说可以更安全倒车了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8220226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8220226" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159322224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8220226 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608200159322224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8220226" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Ford Focus。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>Focus房车版的行李箱，因为有一部分被后备车胎所占用了，所以容量只有373.8升，比起同级车四百多升的容量都要小，平常使用问题应该不大，只是要出外旅游的话，可否放进两个行李箱，那真要出发前认真量度一下了！◇</p>

责任编辑：钟义云</p>
