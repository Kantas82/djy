<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许淑净凯旋返乡 云县民热烈欢迎</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300938422357-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>云林县长李进勇（中）称赞许淑净（右3）是云林之光，并颁赠印有“台湾之光”的匾额。（廖素贞／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年08月30日讯】（大纪元记者廖素贞台湾云林报导）为欢迎<ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%A5%E8%BF%90.md#1">奥运</a><ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%BE%E9%87%8D.md#1">举重</a><ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%89%8C.md#1">金牌</a>国手<ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E6%B7%91%E5%87%80.md#1">许淑净</a>凯旋返乡，云林县政府30日特举办“台湾之光 云林女儿”为许淑净喝采庆功游街活动，上午9时，许淑净凯旋返乡的车队出现在县府广场时，就受到民众及县府团队的热烈欢迎，高喊“许淑净回来了！云林的女儿回来了。”</p>
<p>云林县长李进勇率队迎接<ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B8%E6%B7%91%E5%87%80.md#1">许淑净</a>的归来，并召开记者会为许淑净披上“云林之光”红彩带，并颁赠“台湾之光”匾额后，搭上许淑净的车队，进入斗六市中心，经莿桐、西螺、二崙、<ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C.md#1">崙背</a>等地，沿途受到民众夹道欢迎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8251593" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8251593" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300941472357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251593 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300941472357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8251593" class="wp-caption-text">县长李进勇率县府团队迎接许淑净凯旋返乡。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8251597" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8251597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300936552357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251597 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300936552357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8251597" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B4%99%E8%83%8C.md#1">崙背</a>乡民热情欢迎许淑净凯旋归国。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>崙背是许淑净的故乡，当车队抵达崙背乡公所时，受到乡民热烈的欢迎，崙背乡长李永茂还特别致赠许淑净价值5万元的<ahref="https://github.com/tstusp3875/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%89%8C.md#1">金牌</a>一座，在乡民的热情接待后，车队随即往崙背国中，一进校门，全校500多位师生列队欢迎，“淑净学姊我爱你”的欢呼声不断，场面温馨感人。校长曾丽珠特别致赠了贴心的礼物和崙背名产，感谢许淑净为国争光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8251590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8251590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300950292357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251590 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300950292357-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8251590" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国中校长曾丽珠率领全校师生欢迎校友许淑净凯旋返乡。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8251602" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8251602" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300935392357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251602 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300935392357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8251602" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国中曾丽珠与许淑净及蔡温义教练合影。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8251603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8251603" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300934022357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251603 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608300934022357-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8251603" class="wp-caption-text">崙背国中全校师生500多人列队欢迎校友许淑净凯旋返乡。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车队最后抵达许淑净的老家崙背乡的草湖村拱后宫，<span class="inverse">震耳</span>欲聋的鞭炮声，响彻云霄，向妈祖烧香祭拜后回到老家已下午1时，许家席开60桌宴请亲友，大家都与有荣焉，场面热闹。</p>
