<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2016全球最辣泡面前十 韩国面占六席</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_001.jpg" />
<h6>全球最辣泡面榜单，韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒拉面连续第五年登顶。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月10日讯】人称“<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拉面</a>达人”的美国美食家汉斯?利内什（Hans Lienesch）已经为2,100多种速食面写过食评。近日他在个人博客“拉面评家”<ahref="http://www.theramenrater.com/top-ten-lists/all-time-spiciest/top-ten-spiciest-2016/" target="_blank">theramenrater.com</a>）上发布了2016年版“十大<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3%E5%91%B3.md#1">辣味</a><ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B9%E4%BE%BF%E9%9D%A2.md#1">方便面</a>”榜单。本年度榜单上，韩国产品独占六席，韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拉面</a>连续第五年居于榜首。</p>
<p><strong>10. 农心韩式担担面</strong><br />
（Nongshim Jinjja Jinjja）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185313" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_009-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8185313 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_009-450x257.jpg" alt="农心韩式担担面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185313" class="wp-caption-text">农心韩式担担面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管美国还买不到，农心韩式担担面又回到了韩国市场。调味料有猪肉、花生、黑芝麻等，<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3%E5%91%B3.md#1">辣味</a>劲道。</p>
<p><strong>9. 今麦郎辣煌尚辣子鸡面</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8185312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_008-450x261.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_008-450x261.jpg" alt="今麦郎辣煌尚辣子鸡面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="261" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185312" class="wp-caption-text">今麦郎辣煌尚辣子鸡面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>博主评价其“非常非常辣，配有一大包粗辣椒面”。</p>
<p><strong>8. 韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒海鲜<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">泡面</a></strong><br />
（emart Dare You! Habanero Jjamppong）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185311" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_007-450x242.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_007-450x242.jpg" alt="韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒海鲜泡面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="242" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185311" class="wp-caption-text">韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒海鲜<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">泡面</a>。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这款粗粗的拉面搭配海鲜清汤和大量碎海鲜，风味极辣——斯科维尔辣度单位（SHU）高达1,960。</p>
<p><strong>7. 马来西亚MyKuali槟城白咖喱面</strong><br />
（MyKuali Penang White Curry Noodle）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/7-1-450x270.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/7-1-450x270.jpg" alt="马来西亚MyKuali槟城白咖喱面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185306" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚MyKuali槟城白咖喱面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>MyKuali的新配方为已经很经典的槟城白咖喱面面增加了辣味。汤汁浓稠，还配了大量的蒜。</p>
<p><strong>6. 印度Ching秘辣蒜香速食面</strong><br />
（Ching&#8217;s Secret Hot Garlic Instant Noodles）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185310" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_005-450x202.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_005-450x202.jpg" alt="印度Ching秘辣蒜香速食面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="202" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185310" class="wp-caption-text">印度Ching秘辣蒜香速食面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>拉面达人在印度当地的市场发现了这款辣面，出于对大蒜的喜爱而买来一尝，没想到不是预期中的咸奶油风味，而是辣味生猛、带有蒜香。</p>
<p><strong>5. 马来西亚Mi E-Zee咖喱面</strong><br />
（Mi E-Zee Perisa Kari Mi Segera）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185305" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/5-11-450x262.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/5-11-450x262.jpg" alt="马来西亚Mi E-Zee咖喱面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="262" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185305" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚Mi E-Zee咖喱面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>面条丰满有弹性，咖喱味道更足，配上辣椒香料包，把博主辣得在客厅里走来走去合不上嘴。</p>
<p><strong>4. 韩国八道极地麻辣汤面</strong><br />
（Paldo Teumsae Ramyun）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_004-450x252.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_004-450x252.jpg" alt="韩国八道极地麻辣汤面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185309" class="wp-caption-text">韩国八道极地麻辣汤面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这款面是连续第四年上榜。极地（Teumsae）在韩国有餐厅，菜品非常辣。</p>
<p><strong>3. 韩国三阳辣鸡炒面</strong><br />
（Samyang Foods Buldak Bokkeummyeon）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_003-450x215.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_003-450x215.jpg" alt="韩国三阳辣鸡炒面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="215" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185308" class="wp-caption-text">韩国三阳辣鸡炒面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在YouTube频道，比赛吃这款面的视频不下几十个。拉面达人评道：“这一款确实极辣。不过，这款干吃泡面还不仅是辣味凶猛，味道也很好。宽宽的面与辣油等配料融合得很好。”</p>
<p><strong>2. 韩国八道辣子炒章鱼拌面</strong><br />
（Paldo Bulnak Bokkummyun Spicy Fried Octopus Ramyun）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185304" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2-42-450x244.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2-42-450x244.jpg" alt="韩国八道辣子炒章鱼拌面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185304" class="wp-caption-text">韩国八道辣子炒章鱼拌面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>拉面达人写道：“看到包装上那只小章鱼脸上的笑容吗？它在笑看食客吃到这款超辣拌面时汗流浃背的模样。这可不是开玩笑。准备好灭火器吧！”</p>
<p><strong>1. 韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒拉面</strong><br />
（emart Dare You! Habanero Ramyun）</p>
<figure id="attachment_8185307" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8185307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_001-450x260.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8185307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/2015_8_3_tts_001-450x260.jpg" alt="韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒拉面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8185307" class="wp-caption-text">韩国emart哈瓦那辣椒拉面。(Courtesy of Hans Lienesch)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这款巨辣的泡面连续第五年登上辣面榜单冠军宝座。虽然设计有所变化，博主还是建议看好黑色包装上面的警告标识：右上角的哈瓦那辣椒（俗称墨西哥辣椒）、温度计和尖叫的人……道理很简单：这一款可不能小看，绝对是“狂辣”一级的；汤头也不仅仅是辣嘴。“你有试过舔烛苗吗？我向你保证，这一款面条更加热辣！”</p>
