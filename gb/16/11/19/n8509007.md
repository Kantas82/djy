<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大厨的果园厨房Orchard City Kitchen</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月20日讯】这一家新开的餐厅，位于Campbell的Pruneyard，是Hobee&#8217;s的旧址。这是一家已评定为<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>的平价餐厅，如果你还记得Hobee长什么样，现在已经完全的脱胎换骨。</p>
<p>以前的Hobee黑黑暗暗，是一家传统式有名的早餐店，到了周末早上都是大排长龙。现在这家餐厅换了大门大窗，采光极好。改成开放式厨房，可以看到许多位厨师在里面忙着烧菜，感觉上比从前的餐厅大很多。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8509043" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509043" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Open-kitchen-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Open-kitchen-1-450x338.jpg" alt="Orchard City Kitchen的开放式厨房。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8509043" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509043" class="wp-caption-text">Orchard City Kitchen的开放式厨房。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>这一家最有名的是餐厅的大厨，大厨是亚历山大牛排馆（Alexanda Steak House）的创店厨师，他出来开了这一家餐厅，里面全新式的菜单，虽说是美国餐厅，但是可以看到许多东方味。</p>
<p>我们的前菜叫了蟹肉玉米泥（Crab &#038; Corn Dumping）里面有少许辣味，感觉十分特别。另外点了珍宝蟹沙拉（Dungeness Crab Louie salad）清新脱俗，吃得到蔬菜的香鲜。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8509044" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509044" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/02_Dungeness-Crab-Louie-salad-2.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/02_Dungeness-Crab-Louie-salad-2-300x400.jpg" alt="前菜珍宝蟹沙拉（Dungeness Crab Louie salad）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8509044" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509044" class="wp-caption-text">前菜珍宝蟹沙拉（Dungeness Crab Louie salad）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8509040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509040" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/03_Korean-fried-chicken-sandwich-2.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/03_Korean-fried-chicken-sandwich-2-300x400.jpg" alt="韩国炸鸡三明治（Korean fried chicken sandwich）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8509040" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509040" class="wp-caption-text">韩国炸鸡<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%8E%E6%B2%BB.md#1">三明治</a>（Korean fried chicken sandwich）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>再试试看有名的韩国炸鸡，不过却做成了韩国炸鸡<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%8E%E6%B2%BB.md#1">三明治</a>（Korean fried chicken sandwich）真的不输给韩国人。猪肉三明治（Hot Pokin）是各式各样的猪肉产品做成的一道三明治，有香肠、火腿，将几种不同猪肉堆叠起来，别有一番风味。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8509047" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509047" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/06_Hot-Pokin-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/06_Hot-Pokin-1-300x225.jpg" alt="猪肉三明治（Hot Pokin）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8509047" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509047" class="wp-caption-text">猪肉三明治（Hot Pokin）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8509048" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509048" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/07_Panna-Cotta-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/07_Panna-Cotta-1-300x400.jpg" alt="甜点奶冻（Panna Cotta）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8509048" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509048" class="wp-caption-text">甜点奶冻（Panna Cotta）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>最后来个甜点，这个奶冻（Panna Cotta）看起来不怎么样，吃起来真的不一样。Alexanda Steak House出来的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md#1">米其林</a>厨师，硬是有两把刷子。</p>
<p>Orchard City Kitchen坐落于Campbell的闹区，旁边就是电影院，有一个小院子，可以在外面喝酒、吃美食。全店重新装修，黑白相间，落落大方，简单、时尚、低调奢华。就连厕所都很有品味，大厨一出马，即便是开个小店也看得出质量与水准。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8509052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509052" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Pork-tacos.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Pork-tacos-300x374.jpg" alt="猪肉玉米饼（Pork tacos）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="374" class="size-small wp-image-8509052" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509052" class="wp-caption-text">猪肉玉米饼（Pork tacos）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8509053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8509053" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Smoked-Duck-Breast-with-blood-orange-lemon-balm-carrot-cocoa-vinaigrette.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Smoked-Duck-Breast-with-blood-orange-lemon-balm-carrot-cocoa-vinaigrette-300x225.jpg" alt="烟熏鸭胸肉（Smoked Duck Breast）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="225" class="size-small wp-image-8509053" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8509053" class="wp-caption-text">烟熏鸭胸肉（Smoked Duck Breast）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
