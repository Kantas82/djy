<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2016 Meet Taipei 创新创业展现台湾新能量</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者李月爱台湾台北报导）2016Meet Taipei创业展于11月17日开展。有3大舞台、6大展区、280家展位、吸引了11个国际合作城市、60位讲者及评审参与、87组团队Demo、促成52组媒合场次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8508351" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8508351" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/cbfdcad69e2dec137664a7a3c1d48c57-450x256.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8508351 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/cbfdcad69e2dec137664a7a3c1d48c57-450x256.jpg" width="450" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8508351" class="wp-caption-text">展场开放民众免费参观与体验2016创新创业佳年华会。（李月爱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次展会的4大场论坛 是由多位讲者轮番上阵，多方探讨创业可能性，包括女性创业、旅游创业、国际市场等创新趋势。其中Wheelys智慧咖啡单车，北欧极简设计风的车身非常吸晴，才短短几个月就吸引一千多人报名加盟行列，台湾负责人NOAH YEH说：“目前公司会先求质再求量，所以还得多方审核。”</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_8508352" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8508352" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/75c896000de5b1fa2f6bad311f6fd6d7-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8508352 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/75c896000de5b1fa2f6bad311f6fd6d7-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8508352" class="wp-caption-text">Time Space创辨人庄正维“做自已真正相信的事”。（李月爱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_8508353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8508353" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/7405048d646327c70d70546943cf1bc8-300x527.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8508353 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/7405048d646327c70d70546943cf1bc8-300x527.jpg" width="300" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8508353" class="wp-caption-text">uMeal优膳粮张焕基执行长，粮身您的健康，走过改善的每一步。（李月爱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除此之外，本次活动还吸引许多新世代团队加入。Time Space创办人庄正维谈到创立咖啡平台是“做自已真正相信的事”，希望能让大家喝到真正健康的咖啡而且还要帮助更多咖啡创业者，透过Time Space平台共同成长。<ahref="https://github.com/elibqk3256/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%8E%E5%B8%82%E5%B0%8F%E5%86%9C.md#1">城市小农</a>执行长廖子瑄语重心长的提到，当初创办的初衷是想能帮助更多小农，但这条路走的很辛苦，希望政府单位能多予协助。uMeal优膳粮张焕基执行长，是因为姑姑在医院接受好多年的治疗，厌倦平淡的饮食，让他升起一股念头，想帮助更多像姑姑一样的人，结合了营养师与饭店主厨的专业共同打造更温暖、美好的健康生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8508355" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8508355" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/fc20a337b0f9b539011377c61e933012-300x527.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8508355 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/fc20a337b0f9b539011377c61e933012-300x527.jpg" width="300" b="527" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8508355" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/elibqk3256/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%8E%E5%B8%82%E5%B0%8F%E5%86%9C.md#1">城市小农</a>执行长廖子瑄（右）希望能帮助小农。（李月爱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的Meet Taipei能量十足，成果丰富。3天的展会中可以看到多项重要计划成果，特别是为呈现育成20年的培育成果，经济部中小企业处于会场中规划育成20主题专馆及荣耀育成表扬等活动，展场也开放民众免费参观与体验2016创新创业佳年华会。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_8508354" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8508354" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/79003e6c12b8413a6b7c1a3fb08da3c3-450x256.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8508354 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/79003e6c12b8413a6b7c1a3fb08da3c3-450x256.jpg" width="450" b="256" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8508354" class="wp-caption-text">北欧极简设计风的咖啡车身非常吸晴 &amp; Wheelys 台湾负责人NOAH YEH。（李月爱／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
