<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>时尚编辑教你穿搭秘技！让你爱上平底鞋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1512222329102483-600x400-e1478702412640.jpg" />
<h6>连身裙加上平底鞋的造型很悠闲。（Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月11日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/qmsvrr394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%BA%95%E9%9E%8B.md#1">平底鞋</a>百搭又时尚，遇到正式场合或临时开会，实在不想穿<ahref="https://github.com/qmsvrr394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E8%B7%9F%E9%9E%8B.md#1">高跟鞋</a>，那么平底包鞋绝对是好选择。</p>
<p><strong>百慕达短裤怎么穿搭<ahref="https://github.com/qmsvrr394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%BA%95%E9%9E%8B.md#1">平底鞋</a>？</strong></p>
<p>如果你很满意自己的小腿，但不那么喜欢自己的大腿，那么百慕达短裤（Bermuda shorts）就是个好选择。我常去海边，所以很喜欢这种悠闲的造型。但夏天时，合身版本的百慕达短裤也很适合穿去上班哦！</p>
<p><strong>参加喜宴怎么穿搭平底鞋？</strong></p>
<p>参加喜宴时，人们常常会一时昏头穿错衣服。好比说穿上猫跟鞋（Kitten heels，低跟鞋）搭配直筒连身裙，但这些单品你日常生活中根本连碰都不碰。图中的照片，是我参加哥哥婚礼时的打扮。上半身我配上了同花色的上衣，完完全全是我的风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477465" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-1-7-e1478702683797.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8477465 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-1-7-e1478702683797.jpg" alt="短裤品牌Topshop；凉鞋品牌Birkenstock。（商周出版社提供）" width="299" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477465" class="wp-caption-text">短裤品牌Topshop；凉鞋品牌Birkenstock。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8477470" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477470" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-6-1-e1478702830123.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477470" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-6-1-e1478702830123.jpg" alt="长裤品牌Bimba Y Lola；僧侣鞋品牌Northern Cobbler。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477470" class="wp-caption-text">长裤品牌Bimba Y Lola；僧侣鞋品牌Northern Cobbler。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>滑板鞋怎么穿搭？</strong></p>
<p>我喜欢把运动鞋拿来搭配一些时尚的单品，以免自己看起来真的像是要上健身房似的。像是图中，我穿了一件西装裤来搭配滑板鞋。此外，我也喜欢穿上铅笔裙配上慢跑鞋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477467" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-3-4-e1478702884757.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-3-4-e1478702884757.jpg" alt="长裤品牌H&amp;M；滑板鞋品牌Essentiel Antwerp。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477467" class="wp-caption-text">长裤品牌H&amp;M；滑板鞋品牌Essentiel Antwerp。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>上班怎么穿搭平底鞋？</strong></p>
<p>豹纹平底鞋是非常值得投资的上班鞋款。就算是周末，你也可以配上一条牛仔裤外出。像是图中的尖头豹纹平底鞋，就可以配上一条铅笔裙或是A 字裙，端看自己适合哪一种。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477468" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-4-3-e1478702939767.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-4-3-e1478702939767.jpg" alt="裙子品牌Baukjen；吸烟鞋品牌Russell &amp; Bromley。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477468" class="wp-caption-text">裙子品牌Baukjen；吸烟鞋品牌Russell &amp; Bromley。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>穿踝靴时如何秀出一双美腿？</strong></p>
<p>重点在于踝靴跟双腿之间的相对高度。像我个头小，就得买一双非常低筒的踝靴，露出很大一截腿，这样一来比例才会正确。如果你身形高?，可以试试下半身搭配较长的裙裤，再配上筒身较高的靴子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477469" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477469" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-5-2-e1478702998165.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-5-2-e1478702998165.jpg" alt="短裤品牌Vintage；沙漠靴品牌Penelope Chilvers。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477469" class="wp-caption-text">短裤品牌Vintage；沙漠靴品牌Penelope Chilvers。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>如何穿平底鞋担任伴娘？</strong></p>
<p>我替好友黛丝担任伴娘时，穿的正是图中这副打扮。另外两位伴娘不但高身兆，而且还穿了双<ahref="https://github.com/qmsvrr394/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E8%B7%9F%E9%9E%8B.md#1">高跟鞋</a>。虽然当我跟那两位伴娘走上红毯时，雕花鞋先生一度还把我错认为孩童，但我真的很满意这个造型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477466" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477466" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-2-5-e1478703049573.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-2-5-e1478703049573.jpg" alt="洋装品牌Marilyn Moore；鞋子品牌Diane von Furstenberg。（商周出版社提供）" width="299" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477466" class="wp-caption-text">洋装品牌Marilyn Moore；鞋子品牌Diane von Furstenberg。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>穿平底鞋时，裤管怎么折？</strong></p>
<p>穿平底鞋时，反折裤管真的有画龙点睛之效！露出脚踝；或者当你穿踝靴时，可以露出小腿，能让双脚更显修长。</p>
<p>牛仔裤的长度顿时成为造型成败关键。我自己通常会稍微改短一点，或是把裤管反折两次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477472" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-8-1-e1478703110432.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-8-1-e1478703110432.jpg" alt="灯芯绒裤品牌Donna Ida；狩猎靴品牌Penelope Chilvers。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477472" class="wp-caption-text">灯芯绒裤品牌Donna Ida；狩猎靴品牌Penelope Chilvers。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>穿平底鞋时，袜子怎么搭？</strong></p>
<p>如果天冷时，当然不可能继续露出脚踝，所以应该买几双好袜子以备不时之需。我很爱圆点图样的袜子，或者像图中这双经典菱格纹袜。偶尔可以把牛仔裤反折，好好秀一下你的袜子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8477471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8477471" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-7-1-e1478703156736.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8477471" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1-7-1-e1478703156736.jpg" alt="牛仔裤品牌MiH Jeans；便士乐福鞋品牌Hobbs NW3。（商周出版社提供）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8477471" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔裤品牌MiH Jeans；便士乐福鞋品牌Hobbs NW3。（商周出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
