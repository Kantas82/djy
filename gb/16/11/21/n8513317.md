<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>圣诞节送礼八大科技产品供你选</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者凌妃报导）圣诞假期即将来临，许多人会采购商品作为圣诞节礼物，作为送礼或是自用。科技产品通常也是人们考虑的品项之一，假如你计划以科技产品作为节日礼物，本文推荐八种科技礼物清单，供您参考。</p>
<h4>音乐爱好者：<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8A%BD%E5%96%87%E5%8F%AD.md#1">蓝芽喇叭</a></h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513375" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Anker-450x351.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8513375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Anker-450x351.jpg" alt="Anker SoundCore Sport XL可携式蓝芽喇叭。（Anker官网截图）" width="450" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513375" class="wp-caption-text">Anker SoundCore Sport XL可携式<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8A%BD%E5%96%87%E5%8F%AD.md#1">蓝芽喇叭</a>。（Anker官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想将音乐大声放出来吗？也许蓝芽喇叭是一个不错的选择，连接桌上型电脑、智能手机或是平板电脑之后，立即享有真实立体的音效。Anker SoundCore Sport XL可携式蓝芽喇叭售价是59.99美元。</p>
<h4>音乐爱好者：<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8A%BD%E8%80%B3%E6%9C%BA.md#1">蓝芽耳机</a></h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513381" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1MORE-iBFree-450x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513381 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/1MORE-iBFree-450x339.jpg" alt="（1MORE官网截图）" width="450" b="339" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513381" class="wp-caption-text">1MORE iBFree入耳式<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%93%9D%E8%8A%BD%E8%80%B3%E6%9C%BA.md#1">蓝芽耳机</a>。（1MORE官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蓝芽耳机能满足你对聆听音乐的需求，即使体验音乐时，也不会错失重要的来电，1MORE iBFree入耳式蓝芽耳机可以在听音乐和通话间相互转换，售价是59.99美元。</p>
<h4>影音娱乐爱好者：电视机上盒</h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513385" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Amazon-Fire-TV-450x278.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513385 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Amazon-Fire-TV-450x278.jpg" alt="（Amazon官网截图）" width="450" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513385" class="wp-caption-text">Amazon Fire TV。（Amazon官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不想再受制于有线电视每个月的高额费率吗？想自由选择自己喜欢的影片看吗？Amazon Fire TV不仅拥有7,000个App，包括热门的影音平台Hulu与Netflix，串流品质提升，还可搭配摇杆变身游戏机，满足客厅娱乐。Amazon Fire TV机上盒的售价是89.99美元。</p>
<h4>想建置<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%B1%85.md#1">智能家居</a>：智能家居设备</h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513392" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Amazon-Echo-Dot-450x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513392 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Amazon-Echo-Dot-450x366.jpg" alt="（Amazon官网截图）" width="450" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513392" class="wp-caption-text">Amazon Echo Dot体积小巧，可以放在房间的任何一个角落。（Amazon官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Echo是亚马逊（Amazon）语音上网装置，透过Alexa语音命令方式来阅读新闻、查看天气或点播歌曲等，用户还可利用Alexa操控<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%B1%85.md#1">智能家居</a>装置。Amazon Echo Dot体积小巧，可以放在房间的任何一个角落，售价是49.99美元。</p>
<h4>想建置智能家居：<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD%E6%8F%92%E5%BA%A7.md#1">智能插座</a></h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513396" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/TP-Link-Smart-Plug-450x273.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513396 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/TP-Link-Smart-Plug-450x273.jpg" alt="（TP-Link官网截图）" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513396" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD%E6%8F%92%E5%BA%A7.md#1">智能插座</a>可以让家电变智能化。（TP-Link官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>智能插座可以让家电变智能化，只要下载TP-Link Smart Plug的App，然后将家电插到智能插座上，就可以变成智能家电了。用户只要以App或是语音操控亚马逊Echo，就可以轻易开关家电。TP-Link Smart Plug的价格为54.99美元。</p>
<h4>喜爱阅读者：电子书</h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Kindle-E-Reader-450x462.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8513410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/Kindle-E-Reader-450x462.jpg" alt="Kindle E-Reader。（Amazon官网截图）" width="450" b="462" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513410" class="wp-caption-text">Kindle E-Reader。（Amazon官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>旅行时阅读电子书是一件称充满乐趣的事，亚马逊Kindle E-Reader方便阅读，即使在夜间阅读，眼睛也不会过于疲劳。它的长效电池寿命可达数周，Kindle E-Reader售价是99.99美元。</p>
<h4>怀旧者：<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%B0%E4%BD%8D%E7%9B%B8%E6%A1%86.md#1">数位相框</a></h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513416" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513416" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/NIX-Advance-450x357.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513416 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/NIX-Advance-450x357.jpg" alt="（NIX官网截图）" width="450" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513416" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%B0%E4%BD%8D%E7%9B%B8%E6%A1%86.md#1">数位相框</a>。（NIX官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>想要一进房间，过去美好的回忆都在数位相框里自动呈现吗？离开房间之后，数位相框还能自动关闭吗？试试这款NIX Advance 8英寸高解析度数位相框，售价是69.99美元。</p>
<h4>未来主义者：虚拟实境头戴装置</h4>
<figure id="attachment_8513421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8513421" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/D-scope-Pro-Google-Cardboard-Kit-450x336.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8513421 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/D-scope-Pro-Google-Cardboard-Kit-450x336.jpg" alt="（Amazon官网截图）" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8513421" class="wp-caption-text">Google Cardboard是低成本的虚拟实境。（Amazon官网截图）</figcaption></figure>
