<h6>直升机俯瞰悉尼，超值体验，不容错过！（Adrenaline.com.au提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年11月14日讯】悉尼，澳大利亚东南沿岸的璀璨明珠。十年前，还是留学生的我，一步踏入悉尼，还不知自己能否驾驭这座城市的生活。住久了，便渐渐的爱上了她。总有朋友问我，“为什么喜欢待在悉尼？”因为——“悉尼是个很美的都市！” 往往会被追问：“悉尼哪里景色最美？哪里最好玩？”标准答案：探索悉尼歌剧院、攀登悉尼海港大桥，扬帆悉 尼海港，跳上达令港喷气快艇，到史蒂芬港滑沙，穿越蓝山峡谷，跳伞、赏鲸，或者……乘坐直升机从空中观赏悉尼！ 以全新的方式领略悉尼风光，这庞大而迷人的城市有着太多美妙而独特的视角等待你的发掘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490592" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/2-49-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8490592 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/2-49-450x253.jpg" alt="2" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490592" class="wp-caption-text">下面一起跟着我的镜头，完整的感受一下悉尼的直升机之旅吧！（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<p>下面一起跟着我的镜头，完整的感受一下悉尼的直升机之旅吧！</p>
<p><a src=""></a><a href="https://dwpf34pf0p4a8.cloudfront.net/K2oq3heJb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/csxjhx3714/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/csxjhx3714/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/z_D7PSH0SkQ" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>伴随着直升机推杆启动器的缓缓推动，直升飞机徐徐从悉尼西南区的Bankstown机场起航，沿着 Parramatta River向悉尼市区飞行…… 随着直升机海拔高度的攀昇，整个悉尼的美景尽收眼底。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490579" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/3-1-450x298.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/3-1-450x298.png" alt="直升机上看悉尼，美景尽收眼底（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490579" class="wp-caption-text">直升机上看悉尼，美景尽收眼底（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<p>途经Gladesville Bridge，这座横跨悉尼海湾的大桥始建于1964年，是Gladesville的地标，如果选择徒步旅行，也是个不错的路线哟！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490590" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490590" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/4-450x300.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490590" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/4-450x300.png" alt="空中拍摄的Gladesville地标Gladesville Bridge（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490590" class="wp-caption-text">空中拍摄的Gladesville地标Gladesville Bridge（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<p>穿过城区，飞过闪耀的Parramatta River水域， 最先映入眼帘的是全世界最宽的大铁桥－悉尼海港大桥。据称，这座耗资1350万澳元于30年代建成的大桥由约600万颗铆钉固定，桥拱的跨度为503米，最高处距离海面134米。整个悉尼大桥桥身长度（包括引桥）1149米，从海面到桥面高58.5米，从海面到桥顶高达134米，万吨巨轮可以从桥下通过，被誉为世界上最高的钢铁拱桥。其宽阔的车道连接着悉尼商业中心和北岸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490583" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/5-19-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/5-19-450x300.jpg" alt="映入眼帘的是全世界最宽的大铁桥－悉尼海港大桥（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490583" class="wp-caption-text">映入眼帘的是全世界最宽的大铁桥－悉尼海港大桥（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8490620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490620" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/5-450x298.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8490620 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/5-450x298.png" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490620" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼海港大桥被誉为世界上最高的钢铁拱桥（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<p>直升飞机近距离飞过大桥后，到达悉尼歌剧院——宛如蓝色海港上镶嵌的大贝壳，是慕名而来的各地游客的留影胜地，这一幅画面多年来一直令人们叹为观止，从未老去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490621" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/6-450x299.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/6-450x299.png" alt="悉尼歌剧院——宛如蓝色海港上镶嵌的大贝壳（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490621" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼歌剧院——宛如蓝色海港上镶嵌的大贝壳（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果说悉尼“天生丽质”，那其中一个理由肯定是缘于那些令人叫绝的壮丽海滩！直升机缓缓飞过悉尼名区Mosman，Spit Bridge，Manly海滩和Curl Curl海滩，当然也会看到风景如画的东部城区海岸线包括Watsons Bay、Rose Bay、Coogee海滩和世界闻名的Bondi海滩等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490589" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490589" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/12-9-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/12-9-450x300.jpg" alt="悉尼令人叫绝的壮丽海滩（摄／Mr Lok）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490589" class="wp-caption-text">悉尼令人叫绝的壮丽海滩（摄影／Mr Lok）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坐在直升机内，距离水面仅有150米，可以通过独特视角俯瞰修建在砂石悬崖上的房子，还可以居高临下观赏悉尼的豪宅。随后直升机开始返程，途中顺便将悉尼奥林匹克公园，时尚新区Rhodes和 Wentworth Point的美景一网打尽！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8490584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490584" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/7-12-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/7-12-450x253.jpg" alt="透过独特视角俯瞰悉尼海边独特的建筑（摄／Mr Lok）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490584" class="wp-caption-text">透过独特视角俯瞰悉尼海边独特的建筑（摄影／Mr Lok）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8490585" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490585" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/8-13-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/8-13-450x300.jpg" alt="时尚新区Rhodes和 Wentworth Point的美景（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490585" class="wp-caption-text">时尚新区Rhodes和 Wentworth Point的美景（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8490586" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8490586" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/9-14-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8490586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/9-14-450x300.jpg" alt="空中俯瞰悉尼奥林匹克运动场馆（摄／Mr Lok)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8490586" class="wp-caption-text">空中俯瞰悉尼奥林匹克运动场馆（摄影／Mr Lok)</figcaption></figure>
