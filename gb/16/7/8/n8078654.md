	<p>【大纪元2016年07月08日讯】（大纪元记者罗东平综合报导）英国男子里奇（Rich McCor）是一名<ahref="https://github.com/dxiqxf361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1.md#1">摄影</a>师，同时也一名<ahref="https://github.com/dxiqxf361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%89%AA%E7%BA%B8.md#1">剪纸</a>达人，他巧妙的将自己的这两项特长结合起来，“玩”遍世界各地的著名旅游<ahref="https://github.com/dxiqxf361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E6%A0%87.md#1">地标</a>。他创作的一张张创意十足的艺术作品，不仅让人眼睛为之一亮，也把网友们逗乐了。</p>

<p>果真如此，里奇“玩”完了<ahref="https://github.com/dxiqxf361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A6%E6%95%A6.md#1">伦敦</a>之后一发不可收拾，他的触角开始伸向了世界各地。不仅欧洲大陆的<ahref="https://github.com/dxiqxf361/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E6%A0%87.md#1">地标</a>几乎都增添了他别出心裁的创意，香港、中国南京、法国瓦勒迪泽尔、纽约、拉斯维加斯都留下了他的足迹。</p>

<p>里奇一边旅行，一边创作，他也将作品张贴在社交网社Instagram上与网友分享，至今为止，他的账号已经有超过16万名的粉丝。里奇说，这是我独特的看世界方式，我也很庆幸别人能喜欢我这种别样的视角。</p>

<p><a style="color: #000; font-family: Arial,sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: 17px; text-decoration: none; word-wrap: break-word;" href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BGAC8LYJFZ1/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">When Frank Lloyd Wright was commissioned by Solomon Guggenheim to design a museum for his art collection, he went a bit over the top with some of his ideas. His original concepts included a super fast glass-tube elevator that would whisk visitors up to the top of the building in moments &amp; an automatic vacuum system in the lobby to constantly clean the dirt brought in by visitors.
