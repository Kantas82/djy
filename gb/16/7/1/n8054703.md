<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蔡英文首度出访过境洛杉矶 超人气</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/d92d6958d26aa3034b466f072a76f54f-e1467353314765-450x400.jpg" />
<h6>身着黑色西装的蔡英文，在众多安保簇拥下与侨民握手，亲切微笑。（苏湘岚/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月01日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW">（大纪元</span><span lang="ZH-TW">记者苏湘岚<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%B0%91%E5%9B%BD%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F.md#1">中华民国总统</a>蔡英文出访台湾友邦巴拿马、巴拉圭，</span>6<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>30<span lang="ZH-TW">日在行程尾声<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%87%E5%A2%83.md#1">过境</a><ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>，受到当地侨民热烈欢迎。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8054743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai10-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8054743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai10-450x253.jpg" alt="傍晚5点半左右，蔡英文抵达环球希尔顿酒店。图为酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054743" class="wp-caption-text">傍晚5点半左右，蔡英文抵达环球希尔顿酒店。图为酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">当日蔡英文专机降落洛杉矶时，美国在台协会主席薄瑞光（</span>Raymond Burghardt<span lang="ZH-TW">）登上飞机，迎接蔡总统，两人握手寒暄。联邦众议员罗伊斯（</span>Ed Royce<span lang="ZH-TW">）等人也赶到机场欢迎。之后罗伊斯等政要和蔡英文乘坐同一辆专车，于傍晚</span>5<span lang="ZH-TW">点半左右，抵达环球希尔顿酒店。身着黑色西装的蔡英文，在众多安保簇拥下与侨民握手，向媒体微笑。酒店门口，手举台美国旗、站在印有各侨团名称横幅后面的民众已经等待多时，看到蔡英文终于现身，兴奋不已，发出欢呼。据</span><span lang="ZH-TW">现</span><span lang="ZH-TW">场工作人员说，参加晚宴的侨胞有</span>860<span lang="ZH-TW">人，因场地有限，无法再加人，但已经比前总统马英九今年</span>3<span lang="ZH-TW">月到访的侨宴人数超过一倍。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8054820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054820" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/2016-06-3016.20.46-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8054820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/2016-06-3016.20.46-450x338.jpg" alt="酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054820" class="wp-caption-text">希尔顿酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8054804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054804" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsia5-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8054804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsia5-450x253.jpg" alt="环球希尔顿酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054804" class="wp-caption-text">环球希尔顿酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8054873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai9-1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8054873 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="tsai9" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054873" class="wp-caption-text">环球希尔顿酒店门口四周欢迎的民众。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8054875" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054875" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai14-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8054875 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsai14-450x253.jpg" alt="tsai14" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054875" class="wp-caption-text">蔡英文在环球希尔顿酒店门口。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8054876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/2016-06-30-19.09.08-450x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8054876 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/2016-06-30-19.09.08-450x600.jpg" alt="2016-06-30 19.09.08" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054876" class="wp-caption-text">美国会议员罗伊斯和刘云平在环球希尔顿酒店陪同蔡英文。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">除罗伊斯外，华裔联邦议员刘云平、美国国会众议院外交委员会资深众议员谢尔曼（</span>Brad Sherman<span lang="ZH-TW">）也到酒店欢迎蔡英文。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">侨民<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E6%A0%91%E4%BA%BA.md#1">郭树人</a>特意身着苏格兰裙来欢迎总统。他说：“小时候是空军子弟，那时小小的心愿就是见到三军统帅。”他称赞蔡英文的外交风格如其形象，都很平实：“以前大家对凯子外交都有诟病。蔡英文的外交很实际。形象平实，深得我心。我觉得这是台湾新里程的开始。”</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8054753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8054753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsia3-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8054753 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/tsia3-450x253.jpg" alt="侨胞郭树人特意身着苏格兰裙欢迎台湾总统蔡英文。（苏湘岚/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8054753" class="wp-caption-text">侨胞<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%AD%E6%A0%91%E4%BA%BA.md#1">郭树人</a>特意身着苏格兰裙欢迎台湾总统蔡英文。（苏湘岚/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">这是蔡总统“<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E7%BF%94%E4%B8%93%E6%A1%88.md#1">英翔专案</a>”的最后一站，她在洛杉矶停留不到</span>24<span lang="ZH-TW">小时，当晚与侨胞代表晚宴，无其它公开活动，在美低调<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%87%E5%A2%83.md#1">过境</a>，次日（星期五）中午专机就将启程，飞回台湾。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">蔡总统</span>24<span lang="ZH-TW">日展开“<ahref="https://github.com/sbrpux352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E7%BF%94%E4%B8%93%E6%A1%88.md#1">英翔专案</a>”，这是她上任后首度出访行程，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">自</span>6<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>24<span lang="ZH-TW">日到</span>7<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>2<span lang="ZH-TW">日搭乘华航专机，出访友邦巴拿马，出席巴拿马运河河道拓宽与巨型船闸工程竣工启用仪式，并顺访巴拉圭；访问团去程过境美国迈阿密、回程过境美国洛杉矶，全程</span>9<span lang="ZH-TW">天</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">夜。</span><span lang="ZH-TW">◇ #<br />
