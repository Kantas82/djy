<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧足联：葡萄牙夺冠有5个原因</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545972002-594x400.jpg" />
<h6>葡萄牙队在决赛中以1:0胜出法国队，夺得本届欧洲足球锦标赛的冠军。  (Matthias Hangst/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月11日讯】（大纪元记者林南报导）7月10日，全球瞩目的第十五届<ahref="https://github.com/aiuxfq3515/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83%E9%94%A6%E6%A0%87%E8%B5%9B.md#1">欧洲足球锦标赛</a>在法国落幕。葡萄牙队在决赛中以1:0战胜东道主法国队，捧走德劳内杯。而赛前预测有71%胜算可能的夺冠大热门法国队却饮憾败北，屈居亚军。</p>
<p>欧足联网站在比赛结束后推出对葡萄牙取得最后胜利的分析，从<ahref="https://github.com/aiuxfq3515/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c%E7%BD%97.md#1">C罗</a>提前离场，到埃德尔攻入意想不到的绝杀球，共有5个原因。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8087284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545972046-450x273.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8087284 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545972046-450x273.jpg" alt="PARIS, FRANCE - JULY 10: Portugal players and staffs celebrate after their 1-0 win against France in the UEFA EURO 2016 Final match between Portugal and France at Stade de France on July 10, 2016 in Paris, France. (Photo by Alex Livesey/Getty Images)" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087284" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙队在决赛中以1:0胜出法国队，夺得本届<ahref="https://github.com/aiuxfq3515/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2%E8%B6%B3%E7%90%83%E9%94%A6%E6%A0%87%E8%B5%9B.md#1">欧洲足球锦标赛</a>的冠军。 ( Alex Livesey/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>1、<ahref="https://github.com/aiuxfq3515/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c%E7%BD%97.md#1">C罗</a>提前退场 迫使葡萄牙改变战术</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8087331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545876990-450x335.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8087331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545876990-450x335.jpg" alt="葡萄牙队队长C罗在上半场受伤退赛。 ( Dan Mullan/Getty Images)" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087331" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙队队长C罗在上半场受伤退赛。 ( Dan Mullan/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>C罗上半场伤退后，替补的夸雷斯马无法有效地在锋线上进攻，渐渐地，几乎所有的葡萄牙球员都退入本方半场防守。葡萄牙的防守反击战术似乎奏效，看起来要靠点球大战一决胜负。</p>
<p>在埃德尔上场前，葡萄牙没有出现一个公认的前锋，尽管纳尼和马里奥越来越多地突破抢攻。</p>
<p><strong>2、法国被抑制住了</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8087426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087426" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/contained-450x280.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8087426 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/contained-450x280.jpg" alt="PARIS, FRANCE - JULY 10: Blaise Matuidi (C) and France players show their dejection after their team's 0-1 defeat in the UEFA EURO 2016 Final match between Portugal and France at Stade de France on July 10, 2016 in Paris, France. (Photo by Alex Livesey/Getty Images)" width="450" b="280" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087426" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙地毯式防守令法国前锋破门无术。(Alex Livesey/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在对决爱尔兰，随后对阵冰岛和德国的比赛中，法国队创造了辉煌的战绩。然而，法国却无法突破顽强的葡萄牙后卫防线。葡萄牙用地毯式防守在很大程度上扼杀了法国前锋格里兹曼和吉鲁的发挥，同时也令帕耶被提前换下场。而帕耶的替补科曼扬言要破门，但优秀的葡萄牙左后卫格雷罗很快将他封住。<br />
<strong>3、门将表现神勇</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8087428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/doorkeeper-450x285.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8087428 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/doorkeeper-450x285.jpg" alt="MARSEILLE, FRANCE - JUNE 30: Rui Patricio of Portugal saves a penalty by Jakub Blaszczykowski of Poland at the penalty shootout during the UEFA EURO 2016 quarter final match between Poland and Portugal at Stade Velodrome on June 30, 2016 in Marseille, France. (Photo by Lars Baron/Getty Images)" width="450" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087428" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙门将帕特里西奥在10日的决赛中表现神勇。(Lars Baron/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，自1980年以来，欧洲还没有一位守门员像葡萄牙的帕特里西奥这样，在正规比赛时间内救了这么多个球。比如，帕特里西奥挡住了法国西索科上下半场的两次进攻。在这次欧锦赛中他在最后298分钟保持不败战绩。</p>
<p><strong>4、葡萄牙在加时赛中运气佳</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8087440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087440" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/lucky-portugal-450x307.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8087440 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/lucky-portugal-450x307.jpg" alt="during the UEFA EURO 2016 Final match between Portugal and France at Stade de France on July 10, 2016 in Paris, France." width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087440" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙在加时赛中运气佳。（Michael Regan/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在2016年欧洲足球锦标赛中共有五场比赛拖至加时。在额外的30分钟比赛内，只有2个进球，而这2个球都是由葡萄牙射入的，并且均是替补球员建功。</p>
<p>第一个球是夸雷斯马在对阵克罗地亚的小组赛中在第117分钟射中的。而这一次在法兰西体育场，法国不停轰炸葡萄牙大门，进行了18次射门，相比之下，葡萄牙只有9次射门，但葡萄牙依旧挺立不倒，而且最终还在最后关键时刻踢进一球，把奖杯捧回家了。</p>
<p><strong>5、替补前锋埃德尔把握了绝佳时机</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8087308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8087308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545972264-1-450x292.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8087308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/GettyImages-545972264-1-450x292.jpg" alt="葡萄牙替补前锋埃德尔把握住了绝佳机会。（Mike Hewitt/Getty Images)" width="450" b="292" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8087308" class="wp-caption-text">葡萄牙替补前锋埃德尔把握住了绝佳机会。（Mike Hewitt/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>埃德尔之前从未为葡萄牙打进过如此漂亮的球。事实上，他在参加过的28场国际比赛中只射中过3次。葡萄牙教练桑托斯在79分钟时将中场桑切斯换掉，改由埃德尔上场，并将阵型从4-4-2切换到4-3-3。埃德尔的前锋位置迫使法国的斯切尔尼和乌姆蒂蒂到后面防守，而埃德尔把握机会，并躲过法国中后卫的阻挡，以一记惊人的低射破门 。#</p>
