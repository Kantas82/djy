<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><ahref="https://github.com/qfpowy340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B.md#1">鸡蛋</a>吃不完 冷冻更保鲜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_5837766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5837766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/03/1408191000232483-e1468214606472.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5837766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/03/1408191000232483-e1468214606472.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5837766" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qfpowy340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B.md#1">鸡蛋</a>营养价值可媲美肉类，一天一颗蛋，是人体最好的营养补给。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>【大纪元2016年07月11日讯】鸡蛋是家庭常备食材，也是家人每天补充蛋白质的重要来源之一，入菜、烘焙，正餐、点心都适宜。因为用量较大，有时不免多买了些，尤其在夏天天气炎热，食物不易保存，鸡蛋一时吃不完，除了冷藏，其实也可以冷冻保存，保鲜可长达3个月。</p>
<p><strong>冷藏蛋</strong></p>
<p>营养专家总是建议，鸡蛋一定要煮熟再吃，是因为沙门氏杆菌在蛋壳形成前就已经存在鸡蛋里面，而且存放室温下的鸡蛋，沙门氏杆菌繁殖相当迅速，烹调时处理不当，就容易造成食物中毒。</p>
<p>因此，买回来未经清洗灭菌鸡蛋，最好先清洗干净再放冰箱，储存在4℃以下的冷藏室，才能避免细菌增长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8086972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8086972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/120506212205100088.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8086972 size-medium" title="鸡蛋" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/120506212205100088-450x301.jpg" alt="鸡蛋" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8086972" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋 （大纪元图片库）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><ahref="https://github.com/qfpowy340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E5%86%BB%E8%9B%8B.md#1">冷冻蛋</a></strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_6726373" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6726373" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/06/1306241812501538.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6726373 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/06/1306241812501538-300x453.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6726373" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋去壳（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸡蛋原来也可以冷冻？是不是颠覆了传统观念？<ahref="https://github.com/qfpowy340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E5%86%BB%E8%9B%8B.md#1">冷冻蛋</a>要去壳，放在耐冻袋里冷冻，保存期限可长达3个月。</p>
<p>保存时先将全蛋打到碗里，稍微搅散，然后倒进可密封的耐冻袋或保鲜盒里。或将蛋白蛋黄分开保存，蛋黄需加一小撮盐或糖（视用途而定），可减缓冷冻凝胶现象，同样要搅拌一下，蛋白可直接冷冻不需事先处理。记住在冷冻袋或保鲜盒外面标记全蛋、蛋白或蛋黄的数量，以方便取用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511614" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511614" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1508241411472273.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6511614 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1508241411472273-450x421.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511614" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/qfpowy340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">鸡蛋料理</a>（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>烹煮前先取出冷冻蛋放冷藏室解冻，然后马上使用。冷冻蛋可用在烘蛋、煎蛋、松饼、蛋饼、炒饭或其他烘焙点心上，临时需用随时可派上用场。</p>
