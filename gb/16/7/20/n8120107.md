<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>洛杉矶240万加仑污水溢出 致长滩海滩关闭</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月21日讯】（<span lang="ZH-TW">大纪元记者夏君编译报导</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">周</span><span lang="ZH-TW">一（</span>7<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>18<span lang="ZH-TW">日）下午</span>2<span lang="ZH-TW">点﹐<ahref="https://github.com/qsdads389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>地区的下水<ahref="https://github.com/qsdads389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%A1%E9%81%93%E7%A0%B4%E8%A3%82.md#1">管道破裂</a>﹐导致</span>240<span lang="ZH-TW">万加仑的污水流入了<ahref="https://github.com/qsdads389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>河﹐并迫使长滩</span>与<span lang="ZH-TW">海豹滩</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的所有</span><span lang="ZH-TW">海滩</span><span lang="ZH-TW">都</span><span lang="ZH-TW">关闭</span><span lang="ZH-TW">了。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN">洛杉矶公共工程部发言人戈麦斯（</span>Paul Gomez<span lang="ZH-CN">）说﹐<ahref="https://github.com/qsdads389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AE%A1%E9%81%93%E7%A0%B4%E8%A3%82.md#1">管道破裂</a>处位于洛杉矶的</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN">街至波义耳高地的米森路（</span>Mission Road in Boyle Heights<span lang="ZH-CN">）。</span><span lang="ZH-TW">这次污水泄漏是由于</span>5<span lang="ZH-TW">英尺</span><span lang="ZH-TW">下水道管的上方破裂了﹐导致碎片堵塞了管道﹐污水</span><span lang="ZH-TW">流</span><span lang="ZH-TW">溢</span><span lang="ZH-TW">而</span><span lang="ZH-TW">出</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">并</span><span lang="ZH-TW">流向了洛杉矶河和长滩地区。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">洛杉矶河长达</span>51<span lang="ZH-TW">英里﹐从圣莫</span><span lang="ZH-TW">妮</span><span lang="ZH-TW">卡和圣苏珊娜山区（</span>Santa Susana mountains<span lang="ZH-TW">）蜿蜒延伸﹐穿过大洛杉矶地区﹐直至长滩。</span><span lang="ZH-TW">所幸</span><span lang="ZH-TW">维修人员通宵达旦进行抢修，当晚</span>10<span lang="ZH-TW">点</span><span lang="ZH-TW">就</span><span lang="ZH-TW">止住泄漏</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，估计水</span><span class="shorttext"><span lang="ZH-TW">泵</span></span><span class="shorttext"><span lang="ZH-TW">抽拦了原可能流入洛杉矶河的</span></span><span class="shorttext">75<span lang="ZH-TW">万加仑污水。不过，</span></span><span lang="ZH-TW">他们还要继续</span><span lang="ZH-TW">进行必要的疏导和维修</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，预估周四晚能完工</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3"></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">据洛市环境卫生部助理主任哈格哈里尔（</span>Adel Hagekhalil<span lang="ZH-TW">）说﹐从林肯高地泄漏出来的污水</span><span lang="ZH-TW">都已</span><span lang="ZH-TW">改道或分流</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW"><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3"><span lang="ZH-TW">周二﹐环卫工人已建</span><span lang="ZH-TW">好</span><span lang="ZH-TW">一个</span><span lang="ZH-TW">永久</span><span lang="ZH-TW">疏导系统，使污水绕过旧管道分流过去。</span></span>目前</span><span lang="ZH-TW">不清楚是什么导致了下水道管的破裂，但那些管道建于</span>1929<span lang="ZH-TW">年。</span><span lang="ZH-TW">他</span><span lang="ZH-TW">说﹐随着管道的老化，必须要作必要的修理或替换。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">长滩市环境卫生局经理克尔（</span>Nelson Kerr<span lang="ZH-TW">）说﹐</span><span lang="ZH-TW">正在</span><span lang="ZH-TW">测试海洋水样，以确定此次污水泄漏是否给公众带来了任何健康风险</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">长滩<ahref="https://github.com/qsdads389/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9%E5%85%B3%E9%97%AD.md#1">海滩关闭</a>的决定是长滩市卫生局常务局长陶诺博士（</span>Dr. Mauro Torno<span lang="ZH-TW">）作出的。他说﹐要等到海洋水样测试表明水是安全的才会重新开放海滩﹐允许人们下海游泳。长滩官员们告诫公众﹐要避开污水溢出处附近的水坑</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，尚未处理的污水可能导至眼耳与喉咙的炎症</span><span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">据哈格哈里尔说﹐这次的污水溢出量相比于</span>1998<span lang="ZH-TW">年那次还不算太多，</span>1998<span lang="ZH-TW">年受厄尔尼诺风暴影响﹐共有超过</span>3000<span lang="ZH-TW">万加仑的污水溢出了。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">他说</span><span lang="ZH-TW">：</span><span lang="ZH-TW">“自那之后的</span>15<span lang="ZH-TW">年</span><span lang="ZH-TW">里</span><span lang="ZH-TW">﹐洛杉矶地区还未发生过大的污水溢出事件。这些年来﹐我们一直做得很好。这次只是一个意外。现在﹐我们还在对此事进行着调查。</span><span lang="ZH-CN">”</span><span lang="ZH-CN">◇</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">责任编辑：</span><span lang="ZH-TW">方平</span></p>
