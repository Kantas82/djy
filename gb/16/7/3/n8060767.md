<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白歆惠不惜扮丑 与林美秀上演职场喜剧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254091487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白歆惠与林美秀在新片中上演职场上的双姝对决。（华联提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月03日讯】电影《<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE%E5%A5%87%E5%A7%AC.md#1">销售奇姬</a>》是台湾亿万票房监制叶如芬2016年带来的最新职场喜剧，邀请双姝<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%AD%86%E6%83%A0.md#1">白歆惠</a>（白白）与<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%BE%8E%E7%A7%80.md#1">林美秀</a>同场较劲，在职场上精彩交锋，片商在今（3）日发布的电影前导预告与前导海报中一览无遗。而白白更为了这次演出不惜扮丑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8060870" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060870" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254151487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8060870" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254151487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060870" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%AD%86%E6%83%A0.md#1">白歆惠</a>饰演的职场鲁蛇陈夙芬，是个光鲜亮丽的行销人员，总是以“夙夜匪懈的夙”来介绍自己的名字。（华联提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由白歆惠饰演的陈夙芬，是个光鲜亮丽的行销人员，总是以“夙夜匪懈的夙”来介绍自己的名字，也表现出夙芬拼命积极的个性，虽然拼命却老是出包，这次她得罪大客户被炒了鱿鱼，让她一心想重生再起。</p>
<p>片中，白歆惠为重返职场使出浑身解数，为了跟美秀姐在电影中展现的地板旋转舞蹈PK，白白使出高难度下腰迎战；而亿万票房“吉祥物”——<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%97%E7%BE%8E%E7%A7%80.md#1">林美秀</a>饰演的职场女王则为面对菜鸟的反扑，展现的精彩叫卖能力也不容小觑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8060892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060892" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254211487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8060892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254211487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060892" class="wp-caption-text">林美秀饰演的职场女王美秀，只要出声就是轰动全场，说学逗唱样样精通。（华联提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8060891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060891" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254271487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8060891" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254271487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060891" class="wp-caption-text">电影《<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE%E5%A5%87%E5%A7%AC.md#1">销售奇姬</a>》剧照，图为白歆惠为扮演的职场角色不计形象扮丑。（华联提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白歆惠这次为出演该片牺牲扮丑，为饰演工作失利、生活潦倒的陈夙芬，穿着掉线的毛衣，还顶着花脸到处跑吓坏路人，原来她是挫折碰壁哭花了妆。除了扮丑，白白与林美秀的双姝对战也是火药味十足。</p>
<p>监制叶如芬说：<span lang="ZH-CN">“</span>《销售奇姬》<ahref=" https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=goLC3FDTWbU&amp;feature=youtu.be ">预告片</a>）是职场喜剧类型电影，特别请白白跟美秀两人共同演出，两人合体真的非常有效果！”电影将于9月2日在台上映。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8060887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060887" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254321487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8060887" title="销售奇姬前导人物海报-白歆惠-B" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254321487.jpg" alt="销售奇姬前导人物海报-白歆惠-B" width="300" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060887" class="wp-caption-text">电影《销售奇姬》前导海报，图为白歆惠扮演的职场女王。（华联提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8060890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8060890" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254371487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-8060890" title="销售奇姬前导人物海报-林美秀-B" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1607030254371487.jpg" alt="销售奇姬前导人物海报-林美秀-B" width="300" b="428" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8060890" class="wp-caption-text">电影《销售奇姬》前导海报，图为林美秀扮演的职场女王。（华联提供）</figcaption></figure>
