<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>尹相铉大婚 李钟硕与李宝英到场祝福</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年02月09日讯】韩国人气男星<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%92%9F%E7%A1%95.md#1">李钟硕</a>刚在大陆广州举办粉丝见面会后，昨天（8日）便风尘仆仆赶往参加曾合作出演《<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AC%E8%A7%81%E4%BD%A0%E7%9A%84%E5%A3%B0%E9%9F%B3.md#1">听见你的声音</a>》的伙伴<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B9%E7%9B%B8%E9%93%89.md#1">尹相铉</a>（又译：<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B9%E5%B0%9A%E8%B4%A4.md#1">尹尚贤</a>）的婚礼，同时该剧女主角<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%AE%9D%E8%8B%B1.md#1">李宝英</a>已有孕在身，也开心出席祝福。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5830464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5830464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502090338211487-600x514.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5830464" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%92%9F%E7%A1%95.md#1">李钟硕</a>在广州开唱。（wellmadeyedang提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>甫结束电视剧《Pinocchio》拍摄的李钟硕，日前为感谢粉丝的支持，自2月7日起以中国大陆广州为起点，展开2015亚洲巡回粉丝见面会。刚结束广州粉丝会的李钟硕翌日随即盛装在韩国参加好友<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B9%E7%9B%B8%E9%93%89.md#1">尹相铉</a>的婚礼。</p>
<p>虽然尹相铉与歌手女友MayBee的婚礼并不对外公开，但还是有婚礼现场照片被释出。一样在《<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AC%E8%A7%81%E4%BD%A0%E7%9A%84%E5%A3%B0%E9%9F%B3.md#1">听见你的声音</a>》中演出的崔星俊于8日晚间释出一张同剧演员的合照，并贴文说：“祝相铉哥结婚快乐、百年好合。”难得与同剧演员相聚，也让许多粉丝直呼怀念，除了大赞<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%AE%9D%E8%8B%B1.md#1">李宝英</a>怀孕后一样漂亮，也恭喜这对新人“新婚快乐”。</p>
