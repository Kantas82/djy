<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>伊林尾牙办趴 名模艺人盛装拼场</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年02月05日讯】伊林娱乐于昨晚（4日）举办尾牙主题派对，公司旗下名模、艺人皆盛装打扮出席，值得一提的是，现场充满甜蜜氛围，情侣档<ahref="https://github.com/muamfc3297/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E6%B7%91%E8%87%BB.md#1">蔡淑臻</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/muamfc3297/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%B2%9B%E6%97%AD.md#1">李沛旭</a>以及日前才公开婚讯的夫妻档<ahref="https://github.com/muamfc3297/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E7%A7%89%E8%B0%9A.md#1">余秉谚</a>与模特儿娇妻王依蕾，都喜气洋洋首度在媒体前公开合体放闪。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5835901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5835901" style="width: 363px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/02/1502050335521487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5835901" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）王依蕾、<ahref="https://github.com/muamfc3297/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E7%A7%89%E8%B0%9A.md#1">余秉谚</a>。（伊林娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
