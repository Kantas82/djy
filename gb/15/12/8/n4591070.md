<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拉马尔领先斯威夫特 格莱美入围 11项</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年12月08日讯】（大纪元记者程动言综合报导）第58届<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E.md#1">格莱美</a>音乐奖公布入围名单，28岁的饶舌歌手肯德里克?拉马尔（Kendrick Lamar）入围11奖，美国“乡村小公主”<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%8B%92%E2%80%A7%E6%96%AF%E5%A8%81%E5%A4%AB%E7%89%B9.md#1">泰勒?斯威夫特</a>（Taylor Swift）入围年度唱片、专辑、歌曲等7项大奖提名，另一位饶手歌手威肯（The Weekend）也同样入围7奖。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6534239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6534239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/1512080451311487-600x414.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6534239" class="wp-caption-text">SYDNEY, AUSTRALIA &#8211; NOVEMBER 28:Taylor Swift performs during her &#8216;1989&#8217; World Tour at ANZ Stadium on November 28, 2015 in Sydney, Australia.(Photo by Mark Metcalfe/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>由威兹?哈利法（Wiz Khalifa）和CP查理（Charlie Puth）合作的《速度与激情7》（Furious 7）的主题曲《See You Again》则入围年度最佳单曲奖、最佳流行重唱／团体演出、影视作品类最佳歌曲。</p>
<p>今年由常胜军艾丽西亚?凯斯（Alicia Keys）宣布入围名单，<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E.md#1">格莱美</a>奖主席尼尔?波特诺（Neil Botero）表示：“音乐是一种国际共通语言，我非常高兴能看到这样的艺术活力，体现在今年的入围名单中。艺术家以令人兴奋的方式将音乐推到极限，今年是音乐非常强劲的一年”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6534250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6534250" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/1512080452581487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6534250" class="wp-caption-text">NEW YORK, NY &#8211; OCTOBER 05:Singer-songwriter Alicia Keys attends the Harlem School of the Arts 50th anniversary kickoff at The Plaza on October 5, 2015 in New York City.(Photo by Michael Loccisano/Getty Images for Harlem School of the Arts)</figcaption></figure>
<p>主办单位也将于未来几周，陆续公布表演嘉宾名单。格莱美奖预定明年2月15日在洛杉矶颁奖。</p>
