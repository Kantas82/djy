<a href="https://d3siwajeg0khti.cloudfront.net/V3ytgLwPr"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ogfijm369/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ogfijm369/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年07月09日讯】（大纪元记者张小清编译报导）人称“<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拉面</a>达人”（The Ramen Rater）的美国知名博主汉斯?利内什（Hans Lienesch），近期在其<ahref="http://www.theramenrater.com/" target=“_blank">拉面评级网站</a>发布三个排行榜。其中，2015年“全球美味杯面”由韩式年糕<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">泡面</a>拔得头筹，台湾品牌“满汉大餐”连续两年上榜；马来西亚有四个品牌登上“全球美味快熟米粉”榜单，其中一款还拿下冠军；同期发布的还有2015年“最不推荐泡面”排行榜。</p>
<p>自2011年开始，汉斯?利内什不时在网站发布美味<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E9%9D%A2.md#1">泡面</a>排行榜，榜单按国家、风味、原料、包装等分门别类，成为全球同好的参考；热爱泡面的他，每天都少不了品尝一款面，几年来已撰写了1,700篇图文并茂的泡面评论。</p>
<p>曾有读者质疑其榜单的公正，汉斯说明，产品都是按照说明烹制，所有评价都是在添加个性化配料之前进行；而他自认只是个“喜欢吃面条的家伙”，从新版榜单也可看出，他对咖喱风味真的很偏爱。</p>
<p>今年的碗面评比，日本有三个品牌（日清、Acecook 和Maruchan）四款面进入前十榜单，韩国入围三款，加拿大、台湾、马来西亚各入选一款。台湾“满汉大餐”椒麻火鳗面从去年的第七位来到第五位；说到该品牌更有名的红烧牛肉面，汉斯则表示因为买不到，所以还未能品尝。</p>

<p>以下就是“<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拉面</a>达人”博客2015年版最佳杯面和快熟米粉榜单。</p>
<p><h2>◇ 全球十大美味杯面排行榜（2015年版）</h2>
<p><h2>1. 韩国Choripdong Hurricane韩式年糕泡面</h2>
<p>（Choripdong Hurricane Rice Cake Topokki）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880868" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880868" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902332669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880868" class="wp-caption-text">韩国Choripdong Hurricane韩式年糕泡面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：红色汤头香浓辛辣，偏甜；这款不但有汤面，竟然还有年糕，难怪喜爱韩式泡面的汉斯说，这是他吃过的最好吃的大碗泡面。</p>
<p><h2>2. 日本日清拉王“酱油口味拉面”</h2>
<p>（Nissin Raoh Rich Soy Sauce Flavor）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880872" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880872" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902362669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880872" class="wp-caption-text">日本日清Raoh“酱油口味拉面”（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：汉斯称之为可在烛光下享用的一款泡面：里面的猪肉相当不错，汤头跟面条搭得很好。不过，各式各样的日本泡面也让汉斯感到难分轩轾，不禁商请热心网友再多给他寄一些来鉴定。</p>
<p><h2>3. 加拿大Plats Du Chef Cuisine Adventures“鸡肉汤<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B3%E7%B2%89.md#1">河粉</a>”</h2>
<p>（Plats Du Chef Cuisine Adventures Chicken Ph? Soup）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880877" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902392669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880877" class="wp-caption-text">加拿大Plats Du Chef Cuisine Adventures“鸡肉汤<ahref="https://github.com/ogfijm369/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B3%E7%B2%89.md#1">河粉</a>”（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：汉斯坦言对各式加拿大泡面“犹存恐惧”，但这款真的是个例外：居然吃得到鸡肉块，肉汤也非常可口。</p>
<p><h2>4. 韩国八道炸酱大碗面</h2>
<p>（Paldo Jjajangmen Chajang Noodle King Bowl）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880882" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902422669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880882" class="wp-caption-text">韩国八道炸酱大碗面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：面条粗而耐嚼，炸酱十分美味。</p>
<p><h2>5. 台湾“满汉大餐”椒麻火鳗面</h2>
<p>（Unif Man Han Feast Sichuan Chili Eel Flavor Instant Noodle）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880886" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902452669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880886" class="wp-caption-text">台湾“满汉大餐”椒麻火鳗面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：这款面已经第二次上榜，比去年上升两位。面条很宽，质感很好，且不太硬；面汤香辣，还有鳗鱼！汉斯表示自己也很愿意品尝“满汉大餐”其它风味的泡面，包括红烧牛肉面，只是没有买到。</p>
<p><h2>6. 日本Acecook“担担面”</h2>
<p>（Acecook Comet Mori Yukino Tan-tan Men）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880893" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902482669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880893" class="wp-caption-text">日本Acecook“担担面”（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：面条很宽，口感甚佳；猪肉风味的汤头浓稠辛辣，红油很足。蔬菜和其它辅料都加工考究。</p>
<p><h2>7. 韩国八道大碗海鲜炒码面</h2>
<p>（Paldo Jjamppong Seafood Noodle King Bowl）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880898" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880898" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902512669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880898" class="wp-caption-text">韩国八道大碗海鲜炒码面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：又粗又劲道的面条，与浓稠的汤头搭配得很好，海鲜风味的汤微微甜辣。</p>
<p><h2>8. 日本Maruchan奶油玉米盐味大碗拉面</h2>
<p>（Maruchan Big Cup Ramen Corn, Salt &#038; Butter Flavor）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880902" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902542669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880902" class="wp-caption-text">日本Maruchan奶油玉米盐味大碗拉面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：不但有碎玉米，还有一小块黄油，十分独特。</p>
<p><h2>9. 日本日清Spa王“明太子意大利面”</h2>
<p>（Nissin King Spa Oh Tarako Spaghetti）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880907" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081902572669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880907" class="wp-caption-text">日本日清“明太子意大利面”（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />一款干面。明太鱼籽（Tarako，俗称明太子）是日本面食常用的配料，味道非常好，面条恰到好处，而上面铺的烤紫菜也很棒。</p>
<p><h2>10. 马来西亚好味碗装槟城白咖喱面</h2>
<p>（Hao Way Penang White Curry Instant Bowl Noodles）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880911" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081903002669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880911" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚好味碗装槟城白咖喱面（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：汉斯自谓“咖喱控”——特别着迷的是马来西亚咖喱。这一款面条粗而劲道，白咖喱风味浓郁。</p>
<p><h2>◇ 全球十大美味快熟米线排行榜（2015年版）</h2>
<p><h2>1. 马来西亚MyOri槟城绿咖喱米粉</h2>
<p>（MyOri MalaysiaPenang Green Curry Rice Vermicelli）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880918" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081903032669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880918" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚MyOri槟城绿咖喱米粉（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：米粉很好，而绿咖喱味则堪称完美：味道厚重丰富，辣味刚好，还带甜头。汉斯说，如果你喜欢绿咖喱，绝对会喜欢这一款。</p>
<p><h2>2. 泰国Mama冬炎（冬阴功）虾汤米粉</h2>
<p>（Mama Instant Rice Noodle Soup Spicy Shrimp Flavour）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880925" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081903062669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880925" class="wp-caption-text">泰国Mama冬炎（冬阴功）虾汤米粉（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：米粉非常好——宽且易嚼。汤头相当不错：辛辣，有香茅和虾的风味，还有些甜。配料里也有一只小虾，加工得很好。</p>
<p><h2>3. 马来西亚阿来槟城白咖喱米粉</h2>
<p>（Ah Lai Penang Favourite White Curry Rice Vermicelli）</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5880926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5880926" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1507081903092669.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5880926" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚阿来槟城白咖喱米粉（Courtesy of Hans Lienesch）</figcaption></figure><br />点评：比起该品牌的白咖喱面，白咖喱米粉更让汉斯喜爱，他直言这不寻常，也评说，汤头浓香的咖喱味令他很愉悦。</p>
<p><h2>4. 马来西亚妈咪金牌厨师全新秘方手制咖喱米粉</h2>
