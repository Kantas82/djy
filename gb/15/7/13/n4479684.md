<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>减少体臭 夏天少吃这5种食物</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2015年07月13日讯】夏天容易流汗，体味相对会比较重，饮食、贺尔蒙也会影响体味，如吃一些含挥发性硫化物的食物，洋葱、大蒜、芦笋等，其所含的挥发性硫化物通过毛孔渗出会加重体味，自己感觉既不清爽，也会影响人际关系。</p>
<p>避免加重体臭，以下这5种食物，夏天尽量少吃：</p>
<p><h2>酒</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5891095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5891095" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1506151350402483-600x480.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5891095" class="wp-caption-text">（fotolia）</figcaption></figure><br />有学者认为喝酒会使体臭加剧。大量喝酒时，酒精代谢物质会从毛孔渗出，此时身体味道会特别难闻。 </p>
<p><h2>芦笋</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5891101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5891101" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1506131832232133.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5891101" class="wp-caption-text">（pixelio.de）</figcaption></figure><br />研究发现，芦笋中的硫醇经过体内消化酶的分解后，会释放出气味较浓的化学物质。这些物质会随着汗液或尿液排出。</p>
<p><h2>咖啡</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5891200" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5891200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1506080844521778-600x402.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5891200" class="wp-caption-text">（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure><br />咖啡利尿，过多饮用会导致口渴，口腔干燥容易滋生细菌，产生口臭。而且喝咖啡也会让汗腺分泌更旺盛，导致体味增加。</p>
<p><h2>咖喱和孜然</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5891215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5891215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1412031011081839-600x415.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5891215" class="wp-caption-text">(家和／大纪元)</figcaption></figure><br />咖喱和孜然这些食物味道较重，其气味会滞留体内，几天都挥之不去，再慢慢通过毛孔散发，而使汗水产生异味。</p>
<p><h2>红肉</h2>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5891232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5891232" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/07/1012270747141528-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5891232" class="wp-caption-text">（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure><br />有证据显示，吃大量红肉的人体味会更加浓烈。美国一项研究发现，不吃肉的人体味更具吸引力，更让人愉快。因肉食在消化道中消化慢，会导致呼吸、排汗及排便臭味加重。**</p>
