<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欢庆中秋 毕书尽等多组歌手新竹开唱</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年09月26日讯】台湾昨晚（26日）在新丰红树林举办“2015新竹中秋晚会”，邀请Bii<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>、Popu Lady、<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%80%E5%85%B0%E7%8E%9B%E9%9B%85.md#1">秀兰玛雅</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%A9.md#1">陈大天</a>、“八三么”乐团等多组艺人轮番登台献唱，与民众一起欢度中秋佳节。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6507992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6507992" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509251638061487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6507992" class="wp-caption-text">Popu_Lady。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>由Popu Lady率先开唱，带来《恋爱元气弹》、《MORE》等招牌歌，以甜美动感舞步揭开序幕；接着李唯枫、<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%80%E5%85%B0%E7%8E%9B%E9%9B%85.md#1">秀兰玛雅</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%A4%A7%E5%A4%A9.md#1">陈大天</a>等演唱多首好歌；受到年轻人欢迎的八三么乐团也热唱《东区东区》等劲歌炒热全场，为中秋佳节增添热闹气息。</p>
<p>最后由Bii<ahref="https://github.com/nbrsvv347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%95%E4%B9%A6%E5%B0%BD.md#1">毕书尽</a>担任压轴，一出场便以三立周五华剧《料理高校生》插曲《Love More》，以深情嗓音迅速掳获在场观众的心，此外，还会演唱新歌《Action Go》嗨翻全场，现场也和粉丝互动频频，在台上的一举一动皆吸引粉丝的目光。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6508002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6508002" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509251637581487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6508002" class="wp-caption-text">毕书尽。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>除了昨晚人气歌手热力演唱外，新竹县县长邱镜淳现场也抽出首奖汽车大礼助兴，而主办方新竹县政府在中秋连假期间（9月26日至28日）还有规划免费红树林导览、街头艺人演出，周边活动丰富多元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6508008" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6508008" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509251637501487.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6508008" class="wp-caption-text">秀兰玛雅。（三立提供）</figcaption></figure>
