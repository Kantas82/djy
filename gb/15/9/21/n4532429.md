<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日本小学生午餐时间做什么</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/Jv0vS"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/upuqsh2796/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://git.io/JesJV">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2015年09月21日讯】在<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC.md">日本</a>基本每个学校都提供校餐，通常包括主食、两菜一汤和牛乳，食物搭配都有严格规定，小学生的热量会控制一餐在七百卡路里上下。而日本学生的<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md">午餐</a>，不仅仅是饮食的<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md">教育</a>，还有不浪费食物、环保、打扫卫生以及感恩等教育。</p>
<p>这是YouTube网上一个关于<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC.md">日本</a>小学生中<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E9%A4%90.md">午餐</a>的过程。视频的开头有一句该学校校长的话：“这45分钟午餐时间，也像教授数学一样，被视为一个<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md">教育</a>的时间。”</p>
<p>每个学生都要轮流帮忙分菜，分菜时要带白色的配膳服，头上戴帽子，把头发放进帽子中，还要带好口罩。</p>
<p>班级负责的同学会检查所有同学是否都有好好洗手，确定后，他们就跟着老师一起去厨房取本班级的饭菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507483" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210344012525-600x335.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="335" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了厨房，在领取饭菜前要先向厨房里的阿姨说：“谢谢你们给我们准备的食物。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507498" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507498" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210344472525-600x322.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="322" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507515" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210344242525-600x322.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="322" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>同学们都是在自己的教室里吃午餐，当食物拿班里，会分配不同的同学做不同的工作。他今天负责分发牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507534" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210345122525-600x327.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="327" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们负责分饭、菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507559" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507559" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210345362525-600x333.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>分发完毕后，如果有剩下的，同学们会以剪刀石头布来定，谁可以获得多出的食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507581" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210346522525-600x331.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="331" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在开始吃饭前，大家都会说：“谢谢你们分发食物给我们。”吃完饭后，还会说：“谢谢今天好吃的午餐。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507606" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210346022525-600x329.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="329" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>班级负责的同学会宣读当天的营养食物。“今天我们吃的马铃薯是六年级的同学在学校农场种植的。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507633" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507633" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210346282525-600x331.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="331" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饭后，每个人都要把牛奶盒拆开、清洗、放干后，送到垃圾回收站。从小培养垃圾分类、再循环的习惯。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507659" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210347182525-600x330.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="330" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507686" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210348062525-600x333.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507714" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210348312525-600x319.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="319" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每个小朋友饭后都要刷牙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507738" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507738" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210347422525-600x327.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="327" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>负责的值日生还要将餐具送回厨房。食物都吃得很干净，没有人浪费食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507764" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210348542525-600x327.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="327" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每天学生都有20分钟时间要做大扫除。日本的学校从小学开始就逐渐培养孩子的们责任感和勤劳，所以除了普通的班级扫除，连走廊都会让孩子们擦的干干净净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507783" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507783" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210349272525-600x321.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="321" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507806" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210349502525-600x326.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="326" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507829" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210350112525-600x328.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="328" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6507851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507851" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210350322525-600x328.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="328" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>教职人员的办公室或者是储藏室，孩子们也会认真打扫干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507878" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210351172525-600x331.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="331" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>学校的厕所，也是学生们需要清理的地方。厕所可以说是一个学校里最脏的地方，可是交给学生们打扫，他们就知道在使用厕所的时候一定要保持洁净，起码不要给别人添麻烦，这也是教养的体现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-large wp-image-6507901" title="（视频截图）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509210350542525-600x333.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="600" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当然，扫除不可以少了体育馆，在扫除中，孩子们逐渐养成了，珍惜使用和合作的重要性。</p>
