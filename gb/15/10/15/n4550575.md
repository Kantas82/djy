<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>明星炫技：唐嫣双手背后合掌 乔恩锁骨放蛋</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年10月15日讯】近日，演员<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AB%A3.md#1">唐嫣</a>在微博晒出一张“背身双手合十”的高难动作照，引来网友赞叹。其实，有不少明星都曾在网上“炫技”：陈乔恩曾为网友示范“锁骨放鸡蛋”及“锁骨托硬币”；还有<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%B0%8F%E7%92%90.md#1">李小璐</a>的“酒窝夹笔盖”；<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%86%B0%E5%86%B0.md#1">李冰冰</a>曾在船上劈“一字马”为自己庆生……明星的种种“绝技”真是让人大开眼界。</p>
<p><h2><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%94%90%E5%AB%A3.md#1">唐嫣</a>背身双手合掌</h2>
<p>12日晚，唐嫣在微博晒出一张反手背后合掌的照片，看上去还十分轻松。她留言“要胖的节奏”，但网友看完后留言说唐嫣并不胖，“你这是在秀你很瘦”。因为不久前海清也分享过这样的高难动作照，有人预测：“这个新技能要火了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511380" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141437171693.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511380" class="wp-caption-text">唐嫣背身双手合掌。（唐嫣微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2>陈乔恩锁骨放鸡蛋</h2>
<p>今年6月，陈乔恩通过微博晒出多张示范自己“锁骨放鸡蛋”的照片，并留言：“不就是放2颗鸡蛋嘛。”此外，她还示范过“锁骨托硬币”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511386" style="width: 315px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141445501693.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511386" class="wp-caption-text">陈乔恩锁骨放鸡蛋。（陈乔恩微博截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%B0%8F%E7%92%90.md#1">李小璐</a>酒窝夹笔盖</h2>
<p>6月19日下午，李小璐晒出一张自拍拼图，其中有她为众人示范的“酒窝夹笔盖”技能的照片，十分俏皮可爱。她还自侃：“#酒窝夹笔盖# #自拍是种病#。”看到照片的粉丝纷纷称赞小璐厉害。 </p>
<figure id="attachment_6511393" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511393" style="width: 395px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141452261693.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511393" class="wp-caption-text">李小璐酒窝夹笔盖。（李小璐微博截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E4%BF%AA.md#1">孙俪</a>练高难普拉提</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E4%BF%AA.md#1">孙俪</a>也曾于今年5月在微博晒出一组练习普拉提的动作照，还留言：“普拉提象是肌肉、骨骼的自愈疗法，很爽值得尝试，为了健康，动起来。”照片中，孙俪在教练的指导下做出高难度“悬空”动作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511401" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511401" style="width: 555px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141450431693.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511401" class="wp-caption-text">孙俪练高难普拉提。（孙俪微博截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%A7%80%E8%B4%A4.md#1">金秀贤</a>爬杆摆造型</h2>
<p>今年3月，韩国男星<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%A7%80%E8%B4%A4.md#1">金秀贤</a>通过微博晒出爬高杆搞怪照。照片中的他爬在一根高高的杆子上，还摆出“猴子探路”的造型向远处眺望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141447201693-600x600.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511411" class="wp-caption-text">金秀贤爬杆摆“猴子探路”造型。（金秀贤微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p><h2><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%86%B0%E5%86%B0.md#1">李冰冰</a>劈“一字马”庆生</h2>
<p>李冰冰于2月时曾在微博晒出她在游艇上劈“一字马”、练瑜伽等动作照为自己庆生，并留言： “一不留神又过生日啦，看着这么蓝蓝的天，心情倍儿好！真想跳一段Let it go、Let it go！愿望：八十岁的时候还能劈个一字马！”**</p>
<figure id="attachment_6511417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6511417" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510141449061693.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6511417" class="wp-caption-text">李冰冰劈“一字马”庆生。（李冰冰微博截图）</figcaption></figure>
