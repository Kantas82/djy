<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：AT-3殉职飞官公祭  马英九吊唁</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元10月12日报导】（中央社高雄12日电）<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/at-3%E6%95%99%E7%BB%83%E6%9C%BA.md#1">AT-3教练机</a>殉职<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E5%AE%98.md#1">飞官</a>王劲钧、黄俊荣今天在冈山空军官校举行联合<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%A5%AD.md#1">公祭</a>，总统马英九南下吊唁并慰问家属，场面肃穆哀荣。</p>
<p>马总统上午由国防部长高广圻、参谋总长严德发陪同到灵堂吊唁并慰问遗眷，黄父一度伤心哀痛哭倒在马总统身上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6507071" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510120147171758-600x508.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6507071" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/at-3%E6%95%99%E7%BB%83%E6%9C%BA.md#1">AT-3教练机</a>殉职<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9E%E5%AE%98.md#1">飞官</a>王劲钧、黄俊荣12日在冈山空军官<br />
校举行联合<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E7%A5%AD.md#1">公祭</a>，总统马英九（前右）南下吊唁并慰问<br />
家属，黄父哭倒在总统身上。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公祭现场播放两名殉职飞官生前生活照影片，家属触景伤情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6507086" style="width: 339px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510120147071758.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6507086" class="wp-caption-text">AT-3教练机殉职飞官王劲钧、黄俊荣12日在冈山空军官<br />
校举行联合公祭，现场播放两人生前生活照影片，令家<br />
属触景伤情，十分催泪。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>马总统离去后，高广圻代表总统颁授两名飞官旌忠状、追晋奖章，并主持灵柩覆旗仪式。王、黄两飞官分别追晋一级为中校与上尉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6507098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6507098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510120141331758-600x366.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6507098" class="wp-caption-text">AT-3教练机殉职飞官王劲钧、黄俊荣12日在冈山空军官<br />
校举行联合公祭，空军以隆重军礼送两名殉职飞官最后<br />
一程。图为覆旗仪式。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公祭典礼结束后，在宪兵鸣枪下起灵，空军袍泽列队以隆重军礼送两名殉职飞官最后一程，4架AT-3型教练机同时在现场上空冲场，向两名殉职飞官致敬。</p>

	<figure id="attachment_6507151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6507151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/10/1510120140381758-600x339.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6507151" class="wp-caption-text">AT-3教练机殉职飞官王劲钧、黄俊荣12日在冈山空军官校举行联合公祭，公祭后，空军以隆重军礼相送，4架AT-3型教练机在现场上空冲场，向两名殉职飞官致敬。（中央社）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>两名殉职飞官骨灰随后移灵台北，将暂厝新北碧潭空军公墓。</p>
