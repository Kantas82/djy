<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】烧汁杂菌炒牛柳粒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282006192768-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（许心如/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年08月29日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨<ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>先生，本周为大家带来──烧汁杂菌炒牛柳粒。</p>
<p><h2>材料: </h2>
<p>肉眼牛柳：8oz<br />丝瓜：12oz<br />有机杂菌菇：8oz<br />姜,干葱,蒜：各少许<br />红萝卜花：8片</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005202768-600x400.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500237" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2>调味料: </h2>
<p>日本烧汁：1/4杯<br />美极酱油：少许<br />水：3汤匙<br />粟粉（玉米粉）：1/4茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005282768-600x400.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500259" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.牛柳切粗粒，用少许生抽、糖、胡椒粉腌15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005352768-600x435.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="435"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500278" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.丝瓜去皮后切块；杂菌菇切去尾段少许，撕散备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500299" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005402768-600x412.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="412"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.热镬下油，把牛柳粒炒至7成熟盛起；用一半姜、蒜、盐炒熟丝瓜，盛在碟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005482768-600x377.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="377"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500321" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再爆香余下姜、干葱、蒜、红萝卜花，放进杂菌翻炒一会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500340" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282005562768-600x424.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="424"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500340" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5把牛柳粒回镬，调味汁液拌匀，边倒入边快手炒匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282006032768-600x432.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="432"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500362" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.随后将牛柳、杂菌菇铺在丝瓜上，美味的烧汁杂菌炒牛柳粒就完成了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6500384" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508282006112768-600x403.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="403"
	class="size-large wp-image-6500384" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?多种有机杂菌菇，在如safeway西人超市多有贩售，若没有，也可使用多款菇类代替。<br />?杂菌菇很快熟不能炒太久，这样才能保持口感及营养；烧汁可在超市买到。</p>
<p>若对<ahref="https://github.com/ejszrb3140/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E8%B0%B1.md#1">食谱</a>制作步骤有疑问，可寄E-mail至大纪元美食组信箱：food.djy@gmail.com询问食谱相关问题。</p>
