<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：蓝天之微 2016 Mazda CX-3</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221603542224-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年08月23日讯】近年Mazda不断推陈出新，每每有令人惊喜的产品推出，新Mazda3在外型在动力也是上乘的产品，再以成功的要素再加上升高了的车身及四驱系统，变身成为全新的CX-3，再开拓微型(紧凑型)SUV/CUV的市场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604102224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604102224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494259" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>SKYACTIV技术是Mazda自家开发的，包括底盘、变速箱及引擎等。并先后已用在CX-5、Mazda6及Mazda3上，顺理成章的，在CX-3上也是以SKYACTIV技术打造出来。</p>
<p>CX-3装备了2升SKYACTIV-G汽油引擎，其最大输出马力146匹，最大扭矩为146磅呎，压缩比13：1，搭配6速自动SKYACTIV-Drive变速箱，附有手动+/-设计，而GT版CX-3另加上方向盘转档拨片(Paddle Shift)。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494273" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604332224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604332224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494273" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494273" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>GX及GS标配前轮驱动系统，可另外升级至四驱系统，而GT另是标配四驱系统，这类SUV/CUV的四驱系统，一般行车只会以前轮驱动，当监察系统侦察到有轮胎打滑时，才会将动力分配至后轮，使汽车能从失控边缘走回来，从新令轮胎获得抓着力。</p>
<p>其实CX-3的吸引人之处，仍是它的外型设计，延续了Mazda的KODO“动魂”设计风格，充满动感的车身设计，车侧的线条设计可媲美欧洲汽车，而车头尾也是极为优美，只是车头设计跟Mazda3太相似，没有了新鲜感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494289" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604292224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604292224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494289" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>那么，可能作为消费者的您，又会升起一个疑问，作为消费者，应该如何从CX-3及Mazda3 Sport(五门揭背版)之间作取舍？</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494300" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604262224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604262224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494300" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6494314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604182224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604182224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494314" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>先以尺寸作比较，CX-3的长宽高为168.3 x 69.6 x 60.9吋，而Mazda3 Sport则为175.6 x 70.7 x 57.3 吋，Mazda3的内部空间也比较大，而且Mazda3 Sport除了2升引擎之外，另有2.5升引擎选择，需要真正“激动”派的应该选择Mazda3。但是CX-3却提供了Mazda3所没有的四驱系统，对于常常“单人匹马”出动的人士，又要独自面对冬日的挑战，CX-3就应该更为适合您！</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604222224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604222224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494324" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一方面，对于Mazda2在加拿大迟迟未有更新，而在美国更不引入Mazda2，却同时推出CX-3，看来以CX-3取代Mazda2的说法，并不是空穴来风，看回Mazda2在去年北美的销量，约为14,000 辆，相比起Mazda3超过10万辆的数量也就真的差太多，Mazda对此车的回应，又会否跟CX-7的处理方法一样呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_6494336" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6494336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604152224-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/08/1508221604152224-600x400.jpg" alt="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" title="2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6494336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6494336" class="wp-caption-text">2016 Mazda CX-3。(李奥/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而Mazda CX-3的推出，造成最直接影响的将会是Nissan Juke，此外Honda也刚推出HR-V，另一方面，MINI也快将更新它的Countryman，再加上市场上的Buick Encore、Chevrolet Trax及Mitsubishi RVR(Outlander Sport)等，那么，明年的紧凑型SUV/CUV的战线，也将会由这两个新挑战者所带起，希望各消费者也能得益，形成厂商及消费者双赢局面吧！◇#</p>
<p>文：李奥</p>
