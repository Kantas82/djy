<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语（二十）</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>新闻出处：http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/big5/aReadArticle.jsp?id=27786</p>
<p><center><ahref=http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/clips/ebrief/04262005.ram>在线观看 </a><ahref= http://media.ntdtv.com/ebrief/04262005.rm>下载观看</a></center></p>
<p>Since The Epoch Times newspaper published “The Nine Commentaries on The Communist Party” in November, the rate of those voluntarily quitting the CCP is increasing daily. <br />自从大纪元时报在去年11月刊出“九评共产党”，志愿退出共产党的人数每日俱增。</p>
<p>【讲解】Since+S+V+O, S+V+SC. 这是由Since所引导的副词子句，加上主要子句。<br />Published=issued</p>
<p>Websites, phone lines, and drop-in service centers have been set up by The Epoch Times to facilitate those who wish to publicly renounce their membership in any of the CCP’s organizations. <br />大纪元时报设下网站、电话、退党服务中心，以方便凡是希望公开放弃共产党党籍及相关组织会籍的民众。</p>
<p>【讲解】1. drop-in: 有顺道的意思。这是说有许多大陆人到美国观光或商务，那么就顺道至退党服务中心办理退党。2. to facilitate: 使方便</p>
<p>The total number of withdrawals from the CCP, Communist Youth League, and Young Pioneers has now exceeded one million, with 20,000 withdrawal submissions every day. <br />从共产党、共青团、少先队退出的总数，现在已经超过100万人次，并且每天还持续有2万人次提出退党退团。</p>
<p>【讲解】submission: 递交，递送。是submit的名词。通常记者用submit与tender这两个动词表示提出、递交之意，特别是下对上的提出，你看sub-是under的意思，-mit 是to send之意。</p>
<p>Rally speakers representing eleven different human rights and pro-democracy groups all shared a positive vision of hope for a new day in China. <br />集会发言人代表11个不同人权与支持民主的团体，共同分享对中国新页的希望愿景。</p>
<p>【讲解】猜猜看这句唯一的动词在那儿？答案是shared。主词是Rally speakers，后面是以分词词组作为名词的后位修饰。全句只是S+V+O的结构，但为什么会这么长呢？是因为主词与受词都作了后位修饰的缘故。核心结构则是Rally speakers shared the positive vision. </p>
<p>John Patrick from The China Support Network called the day “a harbinger of change.” He said“China will never be the same after today,” he advocates for “a revolution without war, a peaceful non-violent revolution- with no more killing!” <br />中国支援网路的约翰派翠克称这一天是“改变的前兆”。他说：“中国在今天之后将不再是一样了”，他呼吁一项“没有战争的革命，和平非暴力的革命，不再有杀戮。”</p>
<p>【讲解】a harbinger of change= an omen of change, 改变的前兆。</p>
<p>Some of the speakers shared openly the years of suffering they experienced under the CCP’s regime. <br />一些发言人公开分享了他们在共产党政权下所经历的苦难。</p>
<p>【讲解】这是一个主要子句+名词子句的结构。引导名词子句的That，作为受词可以省略。</p>
<p>Xu Wenli, a pioneer for democracy in China, spoke openly of his belief in a free China, a belief for which he has paid dearly. <br />吴维利，中国的民主先驱，公开说到他对一个自由中国的信仰，一个他已经为之付出许多的信仰。</p>
<p>【讲解】dearly=very much。To speak of= to note, 提到。</p>
<p>He was imprisoned in China for 17 years after expressing the same simple belief for freedom in his homeland. <br />在表答这个相同单纯的、对自由的信仰后，他在中国被关了17年。</p>
<p>【讲解】主要子句很简短，就是He was imprisoned. 后面跟了五个介系词词组，分别说明地点、时间与原因。（本则新闻待续）@、<br /><font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
