<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩国民众同庆“世界法轮大法日”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051539471695-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元5月15日讯】（大纪元记者文龙韩国报导）5月13日﹐来自韩国首尔﹑大田﹑安山﹑水原﹑仁川等地的法轮功学员欢聚在仁寺洞露天广场﹐庆祝第六届世界<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%95%E6%97%A5.md#1">法轮大法日</a>和法轮大法传世13周年。</p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051531291695-600x379.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051531291695-600x379.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214319" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214319" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051532581695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051532581695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214231" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p>下午六点﹐庆祝活动正式开始﹐这次庆祝活动包括游行﹑诗朗诵﹑器乐演奏﹑歌舞表演﹑腰鼓队等﹐演出内容丰富多彩。</p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051537021695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051537021695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214139" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214139" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p>演出开始之前﹐节目主持人向大家宣布﹕在广大市民的声援和各社会团体的全力营救下﹐韩国公民郭炳镐先生的妻子﹑法轮功学员高成女﹐今天已经走出了监狱的铁门﹐现在正走在回家的路上。在场观众听到了高成女获释的消息后﹐掌声﹑叫好声响成一片。</p>
<p>节目主持人以这个喜讯为开场白﹐更增加了今天这个节日的欢乐气氛。演出分两个部分﹐在第一部分的演出中﹐身着民族服装的歌手们的一首《为你而来》﹐声情并茂﹐气势磅礡﹔而器乐独奏仿彿又把人们带入了如诗如画的恬美境界。</p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051529101695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051529101695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214389" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p>节目中场﹐大屏幕播放了国会议员辛鹤用先生发来的贺辞和录像﹐辛鹤用议员表示与大家同庆同贺第六届世界<ahref="https://github.com/zkoiap363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%A4%A7%E6%B3%95%E6%97%A5.md#1">法轮大法日</a>﹐同时表示﹐法轮大法从中国传向世界﹐遗憾的是又在中国遭到镇压﹐希望中国对法轮功的这场迫害早日结束﹐还给人民信仰的自由。</p>
<p>第二部分的节目主持﹑韩国演员徐甲淑女士告诉大家﹕我修炼法轮功的时间不长﹐但是我知道如何以“真善忍”的法理要求自己﹐做一个好人。</p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214452" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051528221695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051528221695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214452" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214522" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051527041695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051527041695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214522" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214522" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p><center><br />
<figure id="attachment_7214604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7214604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051525561695-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/05/5051525561695-600x400.jpg" alt="（大纪元）" title="（大纪元）" class="size-large wp-image-7214604" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7214604" class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure></center></p>
<p>在这部分的演出中﹐明慧学校的孩子们﹑大唐舞﹑腰鼓阵的精彩表演﹐逐渐把气氛推向高潮﹐观众人山人海。有位中国朋友感慨的说﹕“说实话﹐在法轮功刚开始遭到打压的时候﹐我对法轮功也是抱有偏见的﹐可是﹐看到法轮功顶着这么残酷的打压一步步的走了过来﹐发展到了整个世界﹐真的使我感到非常的震撼﹐中共对法轮功的打压算是把中共自己引向了绝路”。</p>
<p>节目最后﹐人们纷纷点亮了盏盏蜡烛﹐伴着凄婉的乐曲﹐主持人讲述了法轮大法洪传世界﹐以及面对中共残暴的打压﹐法轮功学员走过的艰难历程。有着亲身经历的法轮功学员和许多善良的观众﹐面对此情此景流下了泪水。</p>
<p>烛光下﹐泪水中﹐人们悼念着中国大陆被迫害致死的法轮功修炼者﹐企盼着这场对法轮功的迫害早日结束。</p>
<p>据悉﹐在韩国安山﹑大邱等地也先后举行了相应的庆祝活动。@<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
