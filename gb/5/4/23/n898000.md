<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>流行美语 第120课</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元4月23日讯】<center><ahref=http://www.freexinwen.com/download.asp?file=/chinese/s_pa/pa120.mp3>MP3下载</a></center></p> <p>今天Larry和李华要到体育馆去和几个朋友打篮球。李华会学到两个常用语：stick to something和give something a shot.</p> <p>(Ambience sound in gym)</p> <p>LL: Hey, Li Hua, are you looking forward to today&#8217;s game?</p> <p>LH: 嗨，其实我的篮球打得不怎么样。我倒想试试其他的运动。</p> <p>LL: You&#8217;ll get better. You&#8217;ve only been playing basketball for a few weeks. You have to stick to something if you want to become good at it.</p> <p>LH: 对，我才打了几个星期的篮球！可是你怎么说要我stick to something. Stick不是黏的意思？这跟打篮球有什么关系啊？</p> <p>LL: To &#8220;stick to something&#8221; means that you continue to do it and don&#8217;t give up.</p> <p>LH: 噢！我懂了，stick to something就是坚持不懈，继续做某件事。那不就像我们中文里说的“持之以恒”嘛！ 你的意思是，要是我想打好篮球，就得不断地练习咯！</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s right. You&#8217;ve stuck to practicing English for a long time, and now your English is much better. Right?</p> <p>LH: 你说的对，我学英文倒是尽量的多说，不断地练习。对了，Larry, 那stick to something除了运动，学语言，我还可以用在哪些地方呢？</p> <p>LL: Well, a person can stick to a decision, too. If I decide to learn Chinese, and I stick to my decision, that means I don&#8217;t change my mind or give up.</p> <p>LH: 噢，如果一个人坚持自己的决定，也可以说stick to one&#8217;s decision。那一个立场不坚定，经常flip-flop的人可就不能这么说咯！</p> <p>LL: That&#8217;s right. Very good use of the term flip-flop, by the way.</p> <p>LH: 谢谢。不过有时候要坚持做一件事还真不容易。就好比打篮球，我已经不知道我是不是真的喜欢打篮球了。</p> <p>LL: Remember how hard English was at first? But you stuck to it and now it&#8217;s much easier, right?</p> <p>LH: 好象是这样。你觉得打篮球也会像学英文一样，只要我坚持练习，就会越来越容易吗？</p> <p>LL: I do. You&#8217;ll see. So, have you decided to play in today&#8217;s game after all?</p> <p>LH: 好吧，我来试试看，也许就象你说的，多练习，我就能打好球！</p> <p>******<br />(Sound of dribbling basketball)</p> <p>LH: Larry, 那个篮球架子太远了，我肯定投不进！</p> <p>LL: Come on, Li Hua, give it a shot. It&#8217;s okay if you miss.</p> <p>LH: Larry，我是在投球耶！你怎么叫我给它一枪？&#8221;Give it a shot&#8221; 那是什么意思啊？</p> <p>LL: To give something a shot is to go ahead and try something, even if you don&#8217;t expect to be successful.</p> <p>LH: 嗨，我还以为give it a shot是开枪呢！原来，give it a shot就是试一试，不管会不会成功。好吧。那我就投喽！可是我一定投不进啦！</p> <p>(Sound of basketball into the basket)</p> <p>LL: Hey, look, you made it! That&#8217;s three more points for our team! See, it never hurts to give something a shot.</p> <p>LH: 哇！太棒了！我居然投进了！对了，Larry, give it a shot我是不是还能用在其他什么地方？</p> <p>LL: Just about anything. For instance, I&#8217;d like to give sailing a shot, even though I&#8217;ve never sailed a boat before in my life.</p> <p>LH: 你想学开船啊！我倒想试试开飞机呢！</p> <p>LL: Hey, slow down. We all know what your driving is like. I don&#8217;t think it&#8217;s a good idea for you to fly a plane.</p> <p>LH: 嘿！我的开车技术有什么不好？再说，刚才是你告诉我，不管结果如何，都应该去试一试的啊！</p> <p>LL: Maybe if you stick to driving for a while and get better at it, then you can give flying a shot.</p> <p>LH: 哎呀，其实我也是说说而已啦。我根本就没钱去上飞行课。不过如果有机会，我会去试试的！</p> <p>今天李华学到两个常用语。一个是stick to something.就是坚持不懈，继续做某件事。第二个常用语是give it a shot. 这是试一试的意思，哪怕你认为不会成功，但也可以一试。 </p> <p>(转载自《美国之音》)<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
