<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：只要不长大</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />回到从前<br />只要可以回到从前<br />只要不长大……</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />常时黏着妈妈撒娇<br />甚至皮到她手足无措<br />让她展现对我的溺爱</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />用哭笑作工具<br />赚取父母亲人的焦急和安心<br />左右他们心中的阴晴风雨</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />打着赤脚在乡间到处乱跑<br />无忧无虑地玩各种游戏：<br />打陀螺、玩玻璃弹珠、相扑……</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />随意到田野里<br />欣赏众鸟和昆虫们的舞蹈和音乐会<br />抓蟋蟀相斗，挖蚯蚓钓鱼钓青蛙……</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />大口大口呼吸新鲜空气<br />跳进清澈的江河里游泳、抓鱼虾<br />不必去面对各种污染之害</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />纵声大笑，有话就说<br />真诚大胆地坦露肺腑之言<br />享受无知、“童言无忌”之乐</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />懵懵懂懂，迷迷糊糊<br />不知病痛、药物到底有多苦<br />日复一日、年过一年地过生活……</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />不计毁誉，躲过大人世界的<br />许多机巧、算计、诡诈<br />过自由自在、天真无邪的日子</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />免受任何压力和伤害<br />包括课业、身体、人际关系……<br />包括生活、金钱、恶势力……</p> <p>只要不长大，我便可以<br />尽情地唱“只要我长大”<br />让前面成长的空间更大更宽广<br />不必在私下祈求“只要不长大”</p> <p><b>If I Do Not Grow Up</b>Hsu ChiCheng</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Go back to the past<br />If I can go back to the past<br />If I do not grow up …</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Act as a spoiled child before Mom<br />Even she may feel quite at a loss at my pestering<br />Her to show doting love to me</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Use willful crying and laughing as the means<br />To gain care and solicitude from parents<br />To control rain or sunshine in their heart</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Run about barefooted in the countryside<br />Play games free from any worry and care: <br />Play peg-tops, play glass beads, and sumo …</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Go to the field as I like<br />To enjoy the dancing and singing of birds and insects<br />To catch crickets for fighting, to dig up earthworms and to angle fishes and frogs …</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Freely breathe in fresh air<br />I can jump into the limpid river to swim and to catch fish and shrimps<br />No need to face up to various pollutions</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Laugh heartily, say what I think<br />And can speak words from the bottom of my heart<br />To enjoy free and innocent pleasure</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Live in muddleheadedness<br />Unaware of disease and the bitterness of medicine<br />Day after day, year after year …</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Be regardless of loss or gain, shun calculation<br />And trickery of the mature world<br />To live a carefree and innocent life</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Be free from any pressure and harm<br />Including homework, body, and interpersonal relationship …<br />Including life, money, and vicious power …</p> <p>If I do not grow up, I can<br />Sing If I Grow Up with abandon<br />Let there be more space for my growth<br />No need to pray in private for If I Do Not Grow Up</p> <p>@</p>
