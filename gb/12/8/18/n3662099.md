<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：半天仔翩飞着</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>半天仔翩飞着 <br />边翩飞边歌唱，边歌唱边翩飞 <br />一只，一只，又一只…… <br />冉冉上升，冉冉飘下 <br />在那广阔的田野 <br />在那广阔的牧场 <br />那里生长着很多农作物、杂草和树木 <br />生长着很多泥土和草木的芳香 <br />生长着平静和安详 <br />农人们便在那里辛勤地工作着 <br />牛羊便在那里嚼着草 <br />半天仔翩飞着 <br />冉冉上升，冉冉飘下 <br />边歌唱边翩飞，边翩飞边歌唱 <br />一只，一只，又一只…… <br />在我的童年 <br />在那有着广阔田野和牧场的我的童年 <br />在那生长着农作物、杂草和树木的我的童年 <br />在那生长着泥土和草木芳香的我的童年 <br />在那生长着平静和安详的我的童年 <br />在那农人辛勤地工作的我的童年 <br />在那牛羊嚼着草的我的童年 <br />啊，半天仔翩飞着 <br />一只，一只，又一只…… <br />冉冉上升，冉冉飘下 <br />边歌唱边翩飞，边翩飞边歌唱 <br />在那遥远的广阔的田野 <br />在那遥远的广阔的牧场 <br />在那遥远的甜美的我的童年 </p> <p>注：半天仔又称半天鸟，即台湾小云雀。 </p> <p><b>The Skylark Is Wheeling</b>Hsu ChiCheng </p> <p>The skylark is wheeling <br />Wheeling and singing, singing and wheeling <br />One, two, three, and four … <br />Slowly ascending, slowly descending <br />In the open field <br />In the open pasture <br />Which is rich with a lot of crops, weeds, and trees <br />With a wealth of fragrance of mud, trees and grasses <br />With peace and tranquility <br />And farmers are diligent of their work <br />Cattle and sheep are grazing there <br />The skylark is wheeling <br />Slowly ascending, slowly descending <br />Singing and wheeling, wheeling and singing <br />One, two, three, and four … <br />In my childhood <br />In my childhood where there are open field and pasture <br />In my childhood where there are crops, weeds, and trees <br />In my childhood where there are fragrant mud, trees and grasses <br />In my childhood where there are peace and tranquility <br />In my childhood where farmers are working assiduously <br />In my childhood where cattle and sheep are grazing <br />Oh, the skylark is wheeling <br />One, two, three, and four … <br />Slowly ascending, slowly descending <br />Singing and wheeling, wheeling and singing <br />In the distant open field <br />In the distant open pasture <br />In my distant and sweet childhood @</p> <p>Remark: the skylark here is a particular kind called Bantianzai, native of Taiwan. </p> <p>
