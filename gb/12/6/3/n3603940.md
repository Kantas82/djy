<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：对不起 老师</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>老师，请别生气<br />我没说你长得像魔鬼<br />我没说你兔唇、塌鼻子、小眼睛<br />我只是说你很丑</p> <p>对不起，老师<br />我不是故意的</p> <p>其实你有很吸引人的脸孔<br />其实你长得孔武健壮<br />其实你常常穿着整洁<br />可是我还是要说你很丑</p> <p>对不起，老师<br />我不是故意的</p> <p>如果你多付出一些些爱心<br />如果你多露出一些些笑容<br />如果你说话稍为轻声细语<br />老师，我就会说——你很美</p> <p><b>I’m Sorry, My Teacher</b>Hsu ChiCheng</p> <p>I’m sorry, my teacher<br />I haven’t said that you look like a devil<br />Or that you were harelips, flat nose and small eyes<br />Yet I will just say you seem very ugly</p> <p>I’m sorry, my teacher<br />I didn’t do it on purpose</p> <p>In fact, your face is very beautiful<br />You are strong and in good health<br />And you are always neatly dressed<br />Yet I will still say you seem very ugly</p> <p>I’m sorry, my teacher<br />I didn’t do it on purpose</p> <p>If you show much loving care for your students<br />If much smile appears on your face<br />If you speak more soft and gently<br />I will say—you are very beautiful, my teacher<br />@</p>
