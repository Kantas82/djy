<p>“Avatar,” “Titanic” Film Director Eyes Co-Producing Movies in China<br />阿凡达&#038;铁达尼号导演 看在中国合制电影</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/playlistembed_691786_0.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻	</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Eye</B>： [&#8216;ɑ?] v. 注视<br />2.	<B>Censorship</B>： [s&#8217;?ns??,?p] n. 审查<br />3.	<B>Incentive </B>：[?nˋs?nt?v] n. 动机；诱因<br />4.	<B>Blockbuster</B>： [ˋblɑk?b?st?] n. 电影巨片<br />5.	<B>Grossing</B>： [gr&#8217;os??] adj. 卖座的<br />6.	<B>Pocket </B>：[ˋpɑk?t] v. 赚进<br />7.	<B>Fraction</B>： [ˋfr?k??n] n. 小部分<br />8.	<B>Premium</B>： [ˋprim??m] adj. 优质的<br />9.	<B>Exempt</B>： [?gˋz?mpt] v. 免除<br />10.	<B>Piracy </B>：[ˋpa?r?s?] n. 盗版行为<br />11.	<B>Anodyne</B>： [ˋ?noda?n] n. 止痛剂<br />12.	<B>Pirate</B>： [ˋpa?r?t] v. 盗版</p>
<p>&#8220;Avatar&#8221; and &#8220;Titanic&#8221; film director James Cameron said Sunday he’s considering the opportunity for co-producing movies in China.<br />“阿凡达”和“铁达尼号”导演詹姆斯?卡麦隆周日表示，他正在考虑在中国合作制作电影的机会。</p>
<p>The 57-year-old Canadian-born director was in Beijing for a film festival. <B>Censorship</B> and other restrictions are concerns he says need to be addressed before he would move forward. <br />57岁加拿大出生的导演卡麦隆，当时正出席北京的一个电影节。他表示，在他开始行动之前，媒体监控和其他限制是他担忧需要解决的事情。</p>
<p>[James Cameron, Film Director]: <br />&#8220;I am here to explore the idea of a co-production, find out what restrictions need to be met, you know, find out what content, guidelines need to be met, and find out what the economic<B> incentives </B>are, and I will weigh them all out.&#8221;<br />电影导演詹姆斯?卡麦隆说：“我来这里探索合作生产电影的想法，找出必须满足什么限制，如你所知，找出需要符合的内容标准、准则，并找出经济诱因是什么，我会衡量这些全部要素。”</p>
<p>&#8220;Avatar&#8221;—Cameron&#8217;s <B>blockbuster </B>movie &#8211; was the highest-<B>grossing </B>movie in China in 2010. It made $85.6 million US dollars in 15 days.<br />卡麦隆的电影大片“阿凡达”，是2010年中国最卖座的电影。它在15天卖出 8,560万美元。</p>
<p>Hollywood movies<B> pocketed </B>44 percent of the article content begin -->.6 billion in sales that year &#8211; even though they form a tiny<B> fraction</B> of movies screened in China.<br />在那年16亿美元的销售额中，好莱坞电影赚足44％，- 尽管他们只是在中国放映的电影的一小部分。</p>
<p>The Chinese regime vice president Xi Jinping&#8217;s February visit to the United States has helped to finalize a deal for the import of 14 <B>premium</B> format films. Only 20 foreign films per year are allowed in China. But the new deal <B>exempts </B>formats like IMAX and 3D films. <br />中国副主席习近平2月的访美行程，已完成14部优质格式电影的进口交易。在中国每年只允许进口20部外国电影。但新的政策不适用像IMAX和3D电影的格式。</p>
<p>Cameron said the restrictions will ultimately be unnecessary.<br />卡麦隆说，这些限制最终将是多此一举。</p>
<p>[James Cameron, Film Director]: <br />&#8220;I think that, you know, the government restrictions are protecting the Chinese film industry, the mainland Chinese film industry. That may be valuable to them in the short term. I think eventually it is not going to be necessary, just like it hasn&#8217;t been necessary in most of the territories out there.&#8221;<br />电影导演詹姆斯?卡麦隆说：“你知道的，我的想法是，政府限制是要保护中国电影产业、中国大陆电影产业。就短期来讲，那对他们可能有其价值。但我觉得最后那会是多此一举，就像其它大部分国家一样，那是没有必要的。”</p>
<p>Cameron said 3-D technology could solve widespread<B> piracy</B> issue.<br />卡麦隆说，3D技术可以解决普遍存在的盗版问题。</p>
<p>[James Cameron, Film Director]: <br />&#8220;I have always said that 3D is an <B>anodyne </B>to the piracy problem, because you can <B>pirate</B> a 3D movie, but you cannot pirate the 3D experience of that movie. (It’s) not technically possible.&#8221;<br />电影导演詹姆斯?卡麦隆说：“我一直在说，3D是盗版问题的止痛药，因为你可以盗版一部3D电影，但你却不能盗版那部电影的3D体验。 （这）不是技术上可行的。”</p>
<p>Despite restrictions and censorship issues, Hollywood is eyeing China &#8211; as China’s growing middle class spends more of their money in theatres and less on pirated movies.<br />尽管有限制和审查的问题，随着中国不断壮大的中产阶级，能花费更多的钱在电影院，而非盗版电影，好莱坞正盯上了中国。</p>
<p>The next &#8220;Iron Man&#8221; film will be co-produced in China. This is a joint agreement between Walt Disney Co, Marvel Studios, and DMG Entertainment.<br />下一部“钢铁人”电影将在中国合作生产。这是华特?迪斯尼公司、惊奇制片厂，和DMG娱乐之间的联合协议。</p>
<p>Disney also hopes to collaborate with the Chinese Ministry of Culture and Tencent Holdings to promote the animation industry in China.<br />迪斯尼也希望与中国的文化部和腾讯控股合作，促进中国的动画产业。<br />＠＊</p>
