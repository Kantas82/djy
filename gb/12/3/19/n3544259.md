<p>Jeremy Lin Focused on Knicks Season, Staying Grounded<br />林书豪聚焦尼克队球季，休战中</p>
<p><a width="420" b="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/6JQYVauylSY" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p>1.	Sensation ：[s?nˋse??n] n. a mental feeling, especially a state of excited feeling. 轰动、热潮<br />2.	spotlight： [ˋspɑt?la?t] n. the area of immediate or conspicuous public attention焦点人事<br />3.	court： [kort] n. 球场<br />4.	trademark： [ˋtred?mɑrk] a distinctive mark or feature particularly characteristic of or identified with a person or thing. 商标、特色<br />5.	pick-and-roll: 挡切（篮球术语，指进攻方做掩护球员的战术）<br />6.	consecutive： [k?nˋs?kj?t?v] adj. following one another in uninterrupted succession or order 连续的</p>
<p>Basketball’s latest sensation, Jeremy Lin, was further thrust into the spotlight on the weekend at the BBVA Rising Star Challenge game during the NBA&#8217;s All-Star Weekend in Orlando.<br />蓝球最新的轰动人物林书豪，周末在奥兰多NBA全明星赛的BBVA新秀挑战赛中，进一步成为焦点人物。</p>
<p>During a recent interview, Lin thanked his fans in China, and said he plans to visit there during the summer.<br />在最近一次访问中，林书豪感谢他在中国的球迷，并说他计划在夏天时去那儿访问。</p>
<p>For now, he said, his focus is on helping his team, the New York Knicks, stay focused on the court so that they can win games.<br />现在，他说他的焦点放在帮助他的团队，纽约尼克队，聚焦在球场，为了他们能赢得球赛。</p>
<p>Lin said he intends to rest a lot during the All-Star break to get ready for the second half of the season.<br />林书豪说，他打算在全明星赛后多休息一会，以准备下半段的球季。</p>
<p>[Jeremy Lin, New York Knicks Point Guard]:<br />&#8220;I&#8217;ll be able to get ready for second half of the season. I make sure I get away when I need to get away, spend time with family and friends, make sure I read the Bible and remind myself, you know, what I&#8217;m living for, what I&#8217;m playing for. And then once I get that rest, once I get away, I can feel energized and get right back into it and continue working and getting better.&#8221; <br />纽约尼克队后卫林书豪说：“我要能准备下半段球季。我确信要离开一下当我必须逃脱一下，与家人与朋友们共度时光，当然一定要读圣经，并且提醒自已，你知道的，我就是为这活着的，就是为这打球的。然后一旦我得到休息，一旦我走开了，我就能感到精力充沛，然后回来投入，继续更好的工作。”</p>
<p>Earlier this month, Lin became a hero in New York, helping the Knicks win seven consecutive games and sparking &#8220;Linsanity,&#8221; which has spread across the globe.<br />本月稍早，林书豪在纽约成为英雄，帮尼克队连续赢得7场比赛，并引发散播到全球的“林来疯”热潮。</p>
<p>The 23-year-old Harvard graduate’s sensational plays include his trademark pick-and-roll, which won the hearts of fans in America and Asia.<br />这位23岁的哈佛毕业生轰动的比赛，包括他的特色战法“挡切球” ，赢得了美国与亚洲的球迷的心。<br />***<br />【篮球常用术语】<br />1.	dribble：运球<br />2.	dunk：灌篮 <br />3.	walking：带球走步<br />bank shot：擦板球<br />4.	fast break：快攻 <br />point guard：控球后卫<br />5.	coach：教练<br />6.	referee：裁判 <br />7.	center：中锋<br />8.	forward：前锋<br />9.	starter：先发球员<br />10.	bench player：板凳（后备）球员<br />11.	guest team：客队 <br />12.	home court：主场 <br />13.	home court advantage：主场优势<br />14.	winning streak：连胜场数<br />15.	foul：犯规 <br />foul out：犯满离场，“毕业”<br />16.	technical foul：技术犯规<br />17.	foul strategy：犯规战术<br />timeout：暂停<br />18.	overtime：延长赛<br />19.	one-one-one defense：人盯人防守 <br />20.	free throw：罚球<br />@*</p>
