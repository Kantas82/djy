<p>The world&#8217;s oldest man with his youngest great, great, grandchild. <br />世界上最老的男人和他最年轻的曾曾孙子。</p>
<p>At 115, Jiroemon Kimura is the third oldest man ever<B> recorded</B>.<br />Jiroemon Kimura 115岁，是第三位创纪录最年老的男子。</p>
<p>Born on April 19th, 1897 he was<B> around</B> for the first flight by the Wright brothers, he was 64-years-old when Yuri Gagarin first flew into space and he was already in his nineties for the birth of the <B>Worldwide Web</B>. <br />他出生于1897年4月19日，约在莱特兄弟首次飞航时。加加林首次飞进太空时，他64岁。网际网路誔生时，他已90岁。</p>
<p>And his secret to <B>longevity</B>?<br />他长寿的秘诀是什么呢？</p>
<p>[Jiroemon Kimura, 115-Year-Old Kyoto<B> Resident</B>]:<br />&#8220;I don&#8217;t know exactly &#8230; maybe it&#8217;s all <B>thanks to</B> the sun above me. I am always looking up<B> towards </B>the sky, that is how I am.&#8221;<br />115岁京都居民Jiroemon Kimura说：<br />“我并不是很清楚。或许都要归功于头顶上的天吧！我常仰望天空，那就是如何达到的原因吧。”</p>
<p>Kimura says he <B>keeps </B>his mind<B> active</B> by <B>learning</B> new things.<br />木村说，他藉由学习新事物，保持思维活跃。</p>
<p>[Jiroemon Kimura, 115-Year-Old Kyoto Resident]: <br />&#8220;&#8216;Thank you very much. You are very kind man. I&#8217;ve been learning to speak in English.&#8221;<br />115岁京都居民Jiroemon Kimura说：<br />“非常谢谢你。你是很善良的人。我一直在学习讲英语。”</p>
<p>The <B>centenarian</B> has 14 grandchildren, 25 great grandchildren and 13 great, great grandchildren.<br />这名百岁老人有14个孙子，25个曾孙和13个曾曾孙。</p>
<p>That&#8217;s <B>quite </B>a family.</p>
<p>那真是相当大的一个家庭。<br />@*</p>
