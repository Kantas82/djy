<p>World&#8217;s Largest Car Show Kicks Off in Beijing<br />世界最大车展在北京揭幕</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/playlistembed_698432_0.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻	</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Kick off </B>:ph. 开始<br />2.	<B>Record</B>: [ˋr?k?d] adj. 空前的；创纪录的<br />3.	<B>Wild about</B>: ph. 热切的；渴望的<br />4.	<B>Cash in on</B>: ph. 利用；赚钱<br />5.	<B>Versatility </B>: [?v?s?ˋt?l?t?] n. 通用性；多用途<br />6.	<B>Congestion</B>: [k?nˋd??st??n] n. 拥塞<br />7.	<B>Soar </B>: [sor] v. 猛增<br />8.	<B>Premier</B>: [ˋprim??] n. 首位<br />9.	<B>Take off</B>: ph. 起飞<br />10.	<B>Prospect</B>: [ˋprɑsp?kt] n. 前景<br />11.	<B>Tier</B>: [t?r] n. 阶层<br />12.	<B>Metropolitan </B>: [?m?tr?ˋpɑl?tn] adj. 大都市的</p>
<p>The annual Beijing International Automotive Exhibition kicked off on Monday (April 23). A<B> record</B>number of over one thousand vehicles competing for the attention of the world&#8217;s biggest auto market.<br />一年一度的北京国际汽车展览会在上周一（4月23日）拉开序幕。破纪录地，有超过一千辆汽车，在这个世界最大的汽车市场竞相较劲。</p>
<p>Although China&#8217;s economic growth is slowing, the automobile industry is still hopeful. Wealthy Chinese are<B> wild about</B>SUVs, luxury cars, and in the future, many brands hope also green vehicles. <br />虽然中国的经济成长正在减缓，汽车工业依然充满前景。有钱的中国人非常热衷休旅车和豪华轿车，而且在未来，许多品牌期待有环保汽车。</p>
<p>Lamborghini, hoping to <B>cash in on</B>the Chinese taste for bigger cars, for the first time presented the Urus &#8211; a luxury SUV concept car.<br />蓝宝基尼，针对中国人喜好大车的品味，首次推出乌鲁斯 &#8211; 豪华休旅概念车。</p>
<p>Ford is a late-comer to the Chinese market, but is hopeful for its future in the country.<br />福特是比较晚进军中国市场的，但对于在该国的未来充满希望。</p>
<p>Ford Asia chief, Joe Hinrichs, presented four brand new Ford SUVs on Sunday (April22). He was confident about the cars&#8217; success.<br />福特亚洲区总裁韩瑞麒，周日（4月22日）提出四款全新的福特休旅车。他对这四款汽车能否成功充满信心。</p>
<p>[Joe Hinrichs, Ford Asia Chief]: <br />&#8220;SUV is one of the fastest growing segments in China, some people think 2.5 million units were sold last year. We&#8217;ve done some research on it, a lot of people are buying an SUV for their second vehicle, when they buy a second vehicle, to have <B>versatility </B>, and have that flexibility with the vehicle. Also people like the higher seating point, they like to see over what&#8217;s going on in front of them, there&#8217;s a lot of traffic, a lot of <B>congestion </B>, so they can see what&#8217;s going on ahead of them,&#8221;<br />福特亚洲区总裁韩瑞麒说：“休旅车是中国成长最快的部分之一，有些人觉得去年共卖出250万台。我们已经做了一些关于它的研究，很多人第二部车都选购休旅车，他们购买第二部车，考虑的是通用性和车子的灵活性。还有人喜欢较高的座位点，他们喜欢掌握前面发生的事情，很多车流量、很多交通阻塞，所以他们可以看到前面发生了什么情况。”</p>
<p>The number of SUVs Chinese consumers bought<B> soared</B>to 2.1 million last year. An increase of 25 percent, making for almost 12 percent of total vehicle sales, according to J.D. Power and LMC Automotive.<br />根据JD Power和LMC汽车，购买休旅车的中国消费者去年猛增到210万人，增加了25％，将近汽车总销量的12％。</p>
<p>While other brands like Rolls Royce, Ferrari and Maserati also introduced flashy new models, German car-maker Mercedes-Benz held a world<B> premier</B>for its new A-Class “Quad Coupe.”<br />其他品牌，如劳斯莱斯、法拉利和玛莎拉蒂，还介绍了闪亮的新车型。德国汽车制造商宾士，则率先推出新A级的世界最大的“四双门跑车。”</p>
<p>Car sales in China increased by 5 percent in 2011, the slowest pace since the nation&#8217;s car culture took off </B> at the turn of the century.<br />在2011年，汽车销售在中国增长了5％，但这是中国世纪交替后，汽车文化起飞以来最缓慢的步伐。</p>
<p>Industry observers say<B> prospects</B>for 2012 are expected to improve. This is thanks to automobile demand in lower <B>tier</B>cities, which are catching up with major <B>metropolitan</B>areas.<br />产业观察家表示，2012年预计前景将会改善。这是基于二线城市正赶上大城市地区的汽车需求。<br />@*</p>
