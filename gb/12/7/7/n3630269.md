<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩国B.A.P组合大马开唱各显才华</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2012年07月07日讯】（大纪元记者高飞马来西亚吉隆坡报导）韩国男子组合B.A.P意即Best（最好的）、Absolute（纯粹的）、Perfect（完美的）由容国、力灿、大贤、永才、钟业及准烘（Zelo）六名成员组成，才出道5个月，推出两张迷你专辑，就展开“B.A.P 1st亚洲巡回showcase”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6602449" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080038452145-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602449" class="wp-caption-text">韩国男子组合B.A.P，7月6日在马来西亚举行一场小型演唱会。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>昨日（6日）的马来西亚小型演唱会，B.A.P入乡随俗用马来话“Apa Khabar”（你好吗）， “Saya sayang kamu”（我爱你），用华语说“ 大家好吗，我好想你们”等等哄粉丝。</p>
<p>6位成员以4首劲歌热舞成功带动现场气氛，帅气的白马王子造型献唱2首抒情歌， 除了连同演出6首歌之外，每位成员皆有个人独秀或分组的表演机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6602464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080038262145-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602464" class="wp-caption-text">B.A.P以劲歌热舞带动现场气氛。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6602479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080043492145-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602479" class="wp-caption-text">B.A.P以帅气的白马王子造型献唱2首抒情歌。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>容国和大贤合唱《I Remember》；准烘脚踏滑板出场演唱《Never Give Up》随后来个空手翻；钟业的组曲舞蹈出乎预料，先模仿一小部分Michael Jackson招牌舞步带来惊喜，最后更大胆的扯开上衣露胸肌；力灿秀一小段吉他并与永才和大贤载歌载舞带来Justin Bieber的《BABY》 。</p>
<p>B.A.P在当晚的表演各显才华，粉丝们也十分热情，全程呼喊尖叫连连回报偶像。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6602491" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602491" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080039092145-600x391.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602491" class="wp-caption-text">容国和大贤合唱《I Remember》。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6602506" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080039272145-600x951.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602506" class="wp-caption-text">准烘脚踏滑板出场演唱《Never Give Up》。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6602525" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080040342145-600x949.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602525" class="wp-caption-text">钟业模仿一小部分Michael Jackson招牌舞步。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6602543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6602543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/07/1207080043142145-600x417.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6602543" class="wp-caption-text">力灿与永才和大贤载歌载舞带来Justin Bieber的《BABY》。（摄影：杨晓慧/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
