<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：铁达尼沉船届百年</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Titanic Centennial Approaching<br />铁达尼沉船届百年</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/gb/showaflvembed689384.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B> Centennial</B>: [s?nt&#8217;?ni?l] adj. 百年纪念<br />2. <B>Doom </B>: [d&#8217;um] v. 死亡<br />3. <B> Liner</B>: [l&#8217;ɑ?n?] n. 定期船<br />4. <B>Tout</B>: [t&#8217;ɑ?t] v. 招徕<br />5. <B> Iceberg</B>: [&#8216;ɑ?sb?g] n. 冰山<br />6. <B>Split </B>: [spl&#8217;?t] v. 裂开<br />7. <B>maiden voyage </B>:ph. 处女航、首航<br />8. <B> starboard </B>: [st&#8217;ɑrb?d] adj. 右侧；右舷<br />9. <B> bow</B>: [b&#8217;ɑ?] n. 船首<br />10. <B> stern</B>: [st&#8217;?n] n. 船尾<br />11. <B> snap </B>: [sn&#8217;?p] v. 拉断；咬断<br />12. <B> lifeboat </B>: [l&#8217;ɑ?fb,ot] n. 救生艇<br />13. <B> distress</B>: [d?str&#8217;?s] n. 悲痛；危难<br />14. <B> ocean floor </B>:ph. 海底<br />15. <B> wreckage</B>: [r&#8217;?k?d?] n. 残骸<br />16. <B> expedition</B>: [,?ksp?d&#8217;???n] n. 考察；探险<br />17. <B> remains</B>: [r?m&#8217;enz] n. 残余；废墟<br />18. <B> blockbuster </B>: [bl&#8217;ɑkb,?st?] n. 电影巨片<br />19. <B>grossing</B>: [gr&#8217;os??] adj. 卖座的</p>
<p>April 15th will mark the centennial of the day the Titanic sank, an event that <B>doomed</B> 1,517 of its passengers and crew.<br />4月15日是铁达尼号沉船事件第一百年，该事件中有1517名乘客和船员丧生。</p>
<p>The RMS Titanic was an Olympic class ocean <B>liner</B>, built to be the ultimate level of luxury for ocean travel. <B>Touted</B>to be ‘unsinkable,’ the ship famously hit an<B> iceberg</B>that ripped open holes in some watertight compartments. Two hours and forty minutes later, the ship <B>split </B> in two and sank.<br />铁达尼号是奥林匹克级的远洋邮轮，属海洋航旅最豪华级别。该邮轮吹捧的是“不会沉”，但它却因撞上冰山，导致水密舱室破洞而著名。两小时四十分钟后，船一分为二，沉入海中。</p>
<p>The Titanic was performing its <B>maiden voyage</B>from Southampton, England, to New York City. It had been designed with a 2435 passenger capacity, and its sixteen remotely-sealable watertight compartments led to its ‘unsinkable’ claim.<br />当时是铁达尼号从英国南安普敦至纽约市的首航。它设计有2435名载客量，以及16个远程的密封水密舱室，这也是它“不会沉”的保证。</p>
<p>Four days into its journey, the Titanic struck an iceberg on the<B> starboard </B> side. It opened holes in at least five of the compartments. As they filled with water, the<B> bow</B>sank deeper, and water spilled into other compartments.<br />旅程中的第四天，铁达尼号右舷撞上了冰山。至少有五个水密舱室撞破了洞。随着舱室进满了水，船头下沉更深，水也进到了其他舱室。</p>
<p>The water-filled bow sank into the ocean, lifting the<B> stern</B>into the air. This would <B>snap</B>the boat into two.<br />进水的船头沉入大海，将船尾提升至空中。这让船咬断为二。</p>
<p>Approximately 711 survivors were rescued from its 20<B> lifeboats</B>. These lifeboats had a maximum capacity of 1,178 people, far fewer than the total aboard the ship, and more than those saved.<br />其20艘救生艇大约救出711名幸存者。这些救生艇只有1178名载客量，远远少于船上的载客量总数，而且也超过救出来的人数。</p>
<p>The RMS Carpathia, answering the Titanic’s <B>distress</B>calls, rescued those in lifeboats. They arrived in New York three days later.<br />卡帕提亚号，收到铁达尼号的求救信号，救出在救生艇上的人。三天后，他们抵达纽约。</p>
<p>The ship lay undisturbed on the<B> ocean floor</B>until 1985, when discovered by a Franco-American team. The <B>wreckage</B>split bow and stern, laying 600 meters apart about 3,700 meters below sea level.<br />船舶在海底原封不动，直到1985年，被一组法美团队发现。残骸分为船头和船尾两部分，相距600公尺，位于海平面3700公尺下。</p>
<p>There are still regular<B> expeditions</B>to the Titanic. Researchers note that iron-eating bacteria have been disintegrating the <B>remains</B>, which will soon collapse.<br />至今还有定期的铁达尼号探险。研究人员指出，食铁的细菌一直在解体遗骸，将很快让铁达尼号瓦解。</p>
<p>The events of the Titanic inspired James Cameron’s <B>blockbuster </B> Titanic, starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslett. Released in 1997, the film is the second highest <B>grossing </B> movie of all time, earning over 1.9 billion dollars worldwide. 2009&#8217;s Avatar, also by James Cameron, has earned over 2.7 billion.<br />铁达尼号事件启发了詹姆斯?卡梅隆的大片－由李奥纳多笛卡皮欧和凯蒂温丝勒主演的“铁达尼号”。该片于1997年发行，是史上第二卖座的电影，全球有超过19亿美元的收入。2009年，也是由詹姆斯?卡梅隆执导的“阿凡达”，也赚进超过27亿元。</p>
<p>Titanic was recently released in 3D for the 100th anniversary.<br />铁达尼号最近也推出了3D立体电影版，以纪念事件100周年。<br />@*</p>
