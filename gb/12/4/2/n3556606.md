<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>谷歌推出搞笑8位元地图 似电玩画面</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2012年04月02日讯】（大纪元记者陈俊村综合报导）为庆祝愚人节，向来相当“应景”的<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%B7%E6%AD%8C.md#1">谷歌</a>公司宣布，该公司推出一款专为任天堂红白机设计的卡匣，可供使用者查询谷歌<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9B%BE.md#1">地图</a>的内容，不过此地图是以8位元呈现，犹如电玩《勇者斗恶龙》的画面；该公司另外还推出网路版8位元地图，可供消费者在购买卡匣前试用。</p>
<p>这款卡匣具备拨号功能供连线上网，使用者可藉由输入文字查询<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9B%BE.md#1">地图</a>，也可透过麦克风进行语音搜寻。万一画面不清晰，使用者可对着磁带吹气、将灰尘吹走，或能解决这个接触不良的问题。</p>
<p><a width="560" b="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/rznYifPHxDg?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></a><br />（视频：<ahref="https://github.com/zpfgng326/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%B7%E6%AD%8C.md#1">谷歌</a>新推出的8位元地图卡匣）</p>
<p>至于网路版8位元地图，使用者只要到谷歌网页<ahref="http://maps.google.com/?t=8">http://maps.google.com/?t=8</a>以一般谷歌地图的查询方式进行查询，就可以找到各地的8位元地图。值得注意的是，这个8位元地图里，不但有<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=NYC&#038;hl=en&#038;ll=40.687863,-74.039998&#038;spn=0.01946,0.044031&#038;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&#038;sspn=41.496446,90.175781&#038;hnear=New+York&#038;t=8&#038;z=15">纽约自由女神像</a>、<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Taipei+101,+Sinyi+District,+Taiwan&#038;hl=en&#038;sll=19.993998,110.742188&#038;sspn=19.470747,36.628418&#038;oq=taipe&#038;t=8&#038;hq=Taipei+101,+Sinyi+District,+Taiwan&#038;z=15">台北一零一</a>、<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Sydney+Opera+House,+Macquarie+Street,+Sydney,+New+South+Wales,+Australia&#038;hl=en&#038;sll=40.740494,-73.988028&#038;sspn=0.019444,0.044031&#038;oq=Sydney+Opera&#038;t=8&#038;hq=Sydney+Opera+House,+Macquarie+Street,+Sydney,+New+South+Wales,+Australia&#038;z=15">悉尼歌剧院</a>等著名地标，连<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Madrid,+Spain&#038;hl=en&#038;ll=40.407353,-3.75947&#038;spn=0.041828,0.063171&#038;sll=42.682435,0.021973&#038;sspn=10.335508,16.171875&#038;oq=madrid&#038;hnear=Madrid,+Community+of+Madrid,+Madrid,+Spain&#038;t=8&#038;z=14">熊猫</a>、<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=A82+Inverness,+Highland,+UK&#038;hl=en&#038;ll=57.332776,-4.421911&#038;spn=0.035348,0.081453&#038;sll=57.146129,-4.677815&#038;sspn=0.004441,0.010182&#038;t=8&#038;hnear=A82,+United+Kingdom&#038;z=14">尼斯湖水怪</a>、甚至<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=roswell,+NM&#038;hl=en&#038;ll=33.400384,-104.496031&#038;spn=0.02293,0.031586&#038;sll=0,0&#038;sspn=158.009823,333.632812&#038;t=8&#038;hnear=Roswell,+Chaves,+New+Mexico&#038;z=15">外星人</a>也找得到。当然，<ahref=" http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Google+Headquarters,+Amphitheatre+Parkway,+Mountain+View,+CA&#038;hl=en&#038;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&#038;sspn=52.550571,83.408203&#038;oq=google+head&#038;t=8&#038;hq=Google+Headquarters,+Amphitheatre+Parkway,+Mountain+View,+CA&#038;z=15">谷歌总部</a>也是不可少的景点。</p>
