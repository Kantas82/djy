<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“澳”妙无穷 尽在嘉年华</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2012年04月09日讯】2012年悉尼皇家复活节展（Sydney Royal Easter Show）将于2012年4月5日（周四）到18日（周三）在澳洲悉尼市中心的奥林匹克公园（Sydney Olympic Park）举办。该展览由新南威尔斯州（纽省）皇家农业学会（Royal Agricultural Society of NSW， 简称RAS）于190年前开办，是南半球最大的嘉年华盛会，每年吸引超过90万名游客慕名前往。</p>
<p>在这你可以看到都市里不常见的各种动物、农产品，同时也能欣赏到精彩纷呈的娱乐表演。展览汇集了14,000只动物、300个奇趣包、400家食品店──这是一个能真正感受澳洲文化与生活方式的绝佳机会。</p>
<p>文/王莉莉图片/RAS提供</p>
<p><B>娱乐表演首选</B></p>
<p>“我的娱乐首选是新的表演奇观（Showtime Spectacular）──该节目在以澳洲为主题的表演场进行，由著名的乡村音乐歌手James Blundell主持。表演每晚6时30分开始，开场节目是澳洲唯一的牛仔竞技表演（Sydney Royal Rodeo Series），新配备的大萤幕和顶尖的声光系统更是让此次赛事精彩异常。”皇家复活节展总经理Michael Collins先生说。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578342" style="width: 309px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091155152120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578342" class="wp-caption-text">牛仔竞技表演</figcaption></figure><br />晚会还有独具“澳洲味”的烟花音乐会（Colours of the Outback）。观众将开始一段惊艳、奇妙的烟花之旅，欣赏火红的烟火和绚丽的景象，并有澳洲最潮的新秀顶级乐队伴奏，观众还可以欣赏到澳洲原住民的传统乐器迪吉里杜管（didgeridoo）的独特音效。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578346" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091153552120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578346" class="wp-caption-text">独具“澳洲味”的烟花音乐会</figcaption></figure><br />而历年受欢迎的节目均将在2012年回归，包括经典的伐木和锯木竞赛（Woodchopping and Sawing Competition）、抽鞭锦标赛（Whipcracking Championships）和丰富的街头游行表演等。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578353" style="width: 490px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091151292120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578353" class="wp-caption-text">经典的伐木和锯木竞赛</figcaption></figure><br /><B>2012展会大游行（Grand Parade 2012）</B><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578358" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091152112120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578358" class="wp-caption-text">农展会上的大游行</figcaption></figure><br />没有比这更具有农展会像征意义了！参加大游行的都是百里挑一、动物比赛或其它赛事中的获胜者，如牛、狗、绵羊、山羊、骏马以及伐木比赛的获胜者等等。而悉尼皇家复活节展的各项比赛（Sydney Royal Easter Show Competitions）也是纽省皇家农业协会（Royal Agricultural Society of NSW）用以考虑和促进澳洲农业发展水准的重要赛事。千万不要错过这个欣赏澳洲农业最佳成就的机会！<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578364" style="width: 532px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091152342120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578364" class="wp-caption-text">获奖的乳牛与农夫</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578367" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091154352120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578367" class="wp-caption-text">获奖的山羊与农夫</figcaption></figure><br /><B>互动农场体验</B></p>
<p>在Sunday Telegraph Animal Walk节目中，您将穿过一条小道，中途经过14,000只大小和形状各异的动物；也可在Dairy Farmers Farmyard Nursery喂养小动物，并观看牧羊狗如何把羊赶进羊圈的表演。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578371" style="width: 561px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091212062120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578371" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友在农场与小动物互动。</figcaption></figure><br />到Dairy Farmers Milking Barn观看挤牛奶、剪羊毛，或与动物馆的农民们道声澳洲式的问候：G’day，亲身体验一下“城市遇到乡村”（City Meets Country）的奇妙感受。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578381" style="width: 470px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091151412120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578381" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友在给小牛喂食。<br /></figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578385" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578385" style="width: 265px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091151572120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578385" class="wp-caption-text">牧羊狗赶羊表演</figcaption></figure> <br /><B>美食爱好者</B></p>
<p>2012年展览上有多种美食。享用展览上最受欢迎的传统食物，比如棉花糖和多层三文治，或尝试更多美食，在Woolworths Fresh Food Dome边走边品尝或在崭新的Food and Wine Garden休息。您也可以品尝纽省最好的红酒，菜肴则是由Caf□ NSW用当地原料烹饪的西班牙小吃，自然也少不了澳洲特色BBQ烤肉。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578391" style="width: 360px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091153282120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578391" class="wp-caption-text">在比赛中获奖的蛋糕</figcaption></figure><br />不要错过具有1百多年历史的District Exhibits，它展出了澳洲五大农作物种植区的获奖农产品，同时还要把1万多个不同的水果、蔬菜和谷物很有创意地拼成一个“豪华大拼盘”。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578402" style="width: 512px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091151152120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578402" class="wp-caption-text">由1万多个不同水果、蔬菜和谷物拼成的“豪华大拼盘”<br /></figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578409" style="width: 282px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091154482120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578409" class="wp-caption-text">在这里您可以品尝到纽省最好的红酒。</figcaption></figure><br /><B>过山车 &#038; 嘉年华</B></p>
<p>该展览是南半球最大的嘉年华，有将近60种最大最酷的过山车。嘉年华在本次展览有两个区域，一个是专为年长寻求刺激者准备的可口可乐嘉年华，一个是专为有幼童的家庭准备的儿童嘉年华。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578419" style="width: 544px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091213262120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578419" class="wp-caption-text">精彩的电单车特技表演</figcaption></figure><br />儿童嘉年华里有全新的超级滑道（Super Slide），还新加了世界独轮车冠军表演及火焰杂耍喜剧表演。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578425" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091155042120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578425" class="wp-caption-text">儿童嘉年华中的卡通表演<br /></figcaption></figure><br /><B>奇趣包</B></p>
<p>奇趣包始于1百多年前，是专为复活节展设计的。奇趣包是装有小玩意、商品、糖果和美食等独特的复活节主题包，其价格不一，有些奇趣包起价1澳元。</p>
<p>自那时起奇趣包已经历了漫长的历史；孩子们现在可以从大量他们最喜爱的电视剧和电影中作出选择，比如朵拉大冒险（Dora the Explorer）、反斗奇兵3（Toy Story 3）和欢乐合唱团（Glee）。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578427" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578427" style="width: 319px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091154232120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578427" class="wp-caption-text">手提奇趣包的小朋友</figcaption></figure><br />2012年的展览有342种奇趣包可以选择，不论你是朱古力狂、体育迷或是恶作剧者，你都能找到完全符合你个性和预算的奇趣包。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578436" style="width: 318px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091154072120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578436" class="wp-caption-text">出售服饰用品的商贩</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578442" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578442" style="width: 284px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091211522120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578442" class="wp-caption-text">精美的手工制品</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6578444" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6578444" style="width: 357px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204091153422120.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6578444" class="wp-caption-text">色彩鲜艳的羊毛毛线</figcaption></figure><br /><B>小贴士</B></p>
<p>今年展会推出iPhone免费Apps下载，有展会地图、活动介绍、时间表、奇趣包、购物指南等功能。</p>
<p>晚会看秀有史以来的第一次可以花钱预定座位，好处是不用为占位而浪费时间。但每晚可预定的座位数量有限，票价为每个座位10澳元，家庭套票是32澳元（2个成人 + 2个小孩）。</p>
<p>展会设有特卖场，可以买到具有鲜明澳洲特色的服饰、手工制品、化妆品等，而澳洲标志名牌R.M. Williams也都有不少折扣。</p>
