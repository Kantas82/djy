<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：神韵在柏林 “振奋人心 了不起”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Shen Yun in Berlin – “Inspiring and Fantastic”<br />神韵在柏林 &#8211; “振奋人心 了不起”</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed682871.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>by all accounts</B>: ph.From all reports available, from what everyone is saying据说<br />2.	<B>emotional</B>: [?ˋmo??n!] adj. readily or excessively affected by emotion感动的<br />3.	<B>resonate</B>: [ˋr?z??net] v. to be understood or receive a sympathetic response共呜、共振<br />4.	<B>exude</B>: [?gˋzjud] v. to make apparent by mood or behavior散发、渗出<br />5.	<B>spiritual</B>: [ˋsp?r?t???l] adj. relating to the spirit or soul and not to physical nature or matter精神上的、心灵的<br />6.	<B>reference</B>: [ˋr?f?r?ns] n. the relation between a word, phrase, or symbol and the object or idea to which it refers 关系、关联<br />7.	<B>divine</B>: [d?ˋva?n] adj.of, relating to, or characterizing God神的、神性的<br />8.	<B>reflect</B>: [r?ˋfl?kt]v. to think, meditate, or ponder反省、思考<br />9.	<B>vivid</B>: [ˋv?v?d]adj. strikingly bright or intense鲜艳的、生动的<br />10.	<B>scenography</B>: [siˋnɑgr?f?]n. the art of portraying objects or scenes in perspective透视法的<br />11.	<B>combination</B>: [?kɑmb?ˋne??n]n. a number of things combined结合(体)<br />12.	<B>transition</B>: [tr?nˋz???n]v. to make a transition过渡、转变</p>
<p>Shen Yun Performing Arts continue their European tour, with a second show in Berlin on Saturday.<br />上周六，神韵艺术团继续他们的欧洲巡演，在柏林演出第二场。</p>
<p>And <B>by all accounts</B>, the audience found the performance highly enjoyable.<br />据说，观众们都觉得表演令人感到高度享受。</p>
<p>[Hans Maronna, Entrepreneur]:<br />“The evening was wonderful. This is the first time I&#8217;ve seen the show. I had heard of it, but to experience it &#8211; you feel like you&#8217;re in a fairy tale movie. For me, it was incredibly <B>emotional</B>.”<br />企业家Hans Maronna说：<br />“这夜晚非常美好。我第一次看这出表演。有听说过，但要体验，你会感觉自己就像在童话电影里。对我来说，这是极为感动的。”</p>
<p>Some in the audience say they <B>resonated</B> with a spiritual depth the show <B>exuded</B>.<br />某些在场观众说，他们与节目所散发出的深层崇高的精神深度，产生共鸣。</p>
<p>[Thorsten Wittke, Radio Programming Director]:<br />“I myself am a very<B> spiritual</B> person, and the <B>references</B> to the <B>divine </B>were not particularly foreign to me. I felt my inner life very well <B>reflected</B> through it. This was not that new to me also &#8211; I felt there was a certain familiarity there, indeed.”<br />[电台主编Thorsten Wittke说：<br />“我自己是一个很有灵性的人，与神有关的我不会特别陌生。我感觉自己内在生命，经此表演而有很好的内省。对我来而言，这也不是那么新的。我觉得，的确有某种程度的熟悉。”</p>
<p>This artist was inspired by the <B>vivid</B> use of color in <B>scenographic </B>design.<br />这位艺术家受到在透视设计上的鲜艳色彩所启发。</p>
<p>[René Melzer, Artist &#038; Graphic Designer]:<br />“I found the colors very inspiring, especially in the costumes, but also the whole combination with the music and the dance. This was amazing, I have to say. It was really the<B> combination </B>of it all. Every time it was a completely new scenery. The colors also changed the mood every time, and one could feel this inside. I feel it in myself and now I would like to paint and express that.” <br />艺术家及平面设计师René Melzer说：<br />“我发现颜色非常令人振奋与启发，尤其在服装上，还有音乐与舞蹈整体结合上。我必须说，令人惊奇。它的确是所有的结合。每一次都是完全新的场景。每一次颜色也改变了心情，人们可以感受到它的内在。我感觉它在我心中，现在我想画出来表达。”</p>
<p>Digital and animated projections of the scenery complemented the dance on stage.<br />数位和动态的场景投影，增添舞台上的舞蹈呈现。</p>
<p>[Karl-Heinz Drescher, Entrepreneur]:<br />“The whole composition impressed me very much. The way all the incredible dancers combined together. And I have never seen anything like the stage scenery here.”<br />企业家Karl-Heinz Drescher说：<br />“整场的乐曲让我印象非常深刻。还有所有这些不可思议的舞蹈演员，结合在一起的方式。我从未看过，像这里这样的舞台场景。”</p>
<p>[Sigrun Dierkes, Retired Doctor] :<br />&#8220;Lovely images in the background scenography. Very nice how it <B>transitioned </B>from people dancing in front of the screen to them sort of jumping into the image.&#8221;<br />退休医师Sigrun Dierkes说：<br />“舞台背景的图像很美。如何让舞蹈演员从图像中跳到银幕前，或从银幕前跳回背景图像，安排的非常好。”</p>
<p> [Karl-Heinz Drescher, Entrepreneur]:<br />“This was a fantastic experience for me. Even just in terms of the optical and acoustic effects. I can recommend this show to everybody.”<br />企业家Karl-Heinz Drescher说：“对我来说，这是个很棒的体验。即使就视觉或听觉的效果而言。我会向每个人推荐这个表演。”</p>
