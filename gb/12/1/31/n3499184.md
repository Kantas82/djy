<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：跳蚤市场里的新年旧玩具</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Old Toys for the New Year Seen at the &#8220;Flea Market&#8221;<br />跳蚤市场里的新年旧玩具</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed647885.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Flea market </B>:ph. 跳蚤市场<br />2.	<B>Abound </B>: [?ˋba?nd] v. 大量存在<br />3.	<B>Collector</B>: [k?ˋl?kt?] n. 收藏家<br />4.	<B>Among other</B>: ph. 除了…以外<br />5.	<B>Antique</B>: [?nˋtik] n. 古董；古物<br />6.	<B>Vintage </B>: [ˋv?nt?d?] adj. 古老的；古色古香的<br />7.	<B>Brooch </B>: [brot?] n. 女用胸针<br />8.	<B>Spruce</B>: [sprus] n. 云杉<br />9.	<B>Papier-mache</B>: [ˋpep?m?ˋ?e] n. 混凝纸浆<br />10.	<B>Kaleidoscope</B>: [k?ˋla?d??skop] n. 万花筒<br />11.	<B>Wool</B>: [w?l] n. 羊毛<br />12.	<B>Retro </B>: [ˋr?tro] adj. 复古的</p>
<p>For residents of Moscow, new stories about old things <B>abound </B>at the 23rd Flea Market Fair in Moscow.<br />莫斯科举行的第23届跳蚤市场大会，对于莫斯科居民，比比皆是旧事物的新故事。</p>
<p>Russian and European <B>collectors</B>have brought Christmas goods here, <B>among other antiques</B>.<br />俄罗斯和欧洲的收藏家们带来的除了古董，还有耶诞商品。</p>
<p>The author of the project presented her own collection of <B>vintage brooches </B>in the form of Christmas trees.<br />这个专案的作者，将她自己收藏的古董胸针用耶诞树造型呈现。</p>
<p>Today a<B> spruce</B> is a welcome guest at the New Year holiday. <br />今天，云杉是新年假期的迎宾客人。</p>
<p>Officially the first Christmas tree was first seen in Moscow only in 1936. <br />第一株耶诞树仅在1936年在莫斯科第一次正式被看到。</p>
<p>The Russian version of Santa Claus, Ded Moroz appeared there.<br />俄罗斯版本的耶诞老人，迪德莫罗兹出现在那里。</p>
<p>Before another winter character Jack Frost was known in Russia, and his figure was on greeting cards at the beginning of the 20th century.<br />之前，另一个在俄罗斯的知名冬季角色是杰克弗罗斯特，20世纪初的贺卡上都是他的身影。</p>
<p>People started to celebrate the New Year everywhere in the 1960s. <br />在60年代，四处的人们开始庆祝新年。</p>
<p>They decorated Christmas trees and hurried to make festive costumes and masks like this made of <B>papier-mache</B>.<br />他们装饰耶诞树，急忙制作节庆的服装，以及戴上像这种混凝纸浆做的面具。</p>
<p>[Participant in Exhibition]:<br />&#8220;Every year there were the same masks and children were familiar with them, because they come from generation to another generation.”<br />参展民众说：“每年都有相同的面具，孩子们也很熟悉，因为他们自一代传一代。”</p>
<p>In addition to vintage objects, there is also the newest invention, a Xenia 3D-<B>kaleidoscope</B>.<br />除了古老物件之外，也有最新的发明－森雅3D万花筒。</p>
<p>Visitors can see toys made of paper, glass, <B>wool</B>, plastic fantastic characters, festive lights and other New Year&#8217;s <B>retro</B> objects.<br />参观民众可以看到用纸、玻璃、羊毛、塑胶制的梦幻人物、节庆灯和其他新年的复古物件制作的玩具。<br />@*</p>
