<p>Chinese New Year Festival Travel Rush Begins<br />中国新年春运大潮开始</p>

<p>本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br /><B>ahead of </B>:phr. 在&#8230;之前<br /><B>reunite</B>: [?rijuˋna?t] v. 使重聚<br /><B>rush</B>: [r??] n. 热潮<br /><B>account for</B>: v.phr. 占~% <br /><B>roughly</B>: [ˋr?fl?] adv. 粗略地<br /><B>cope with</B>: phr. 对付; 处理</p>
<p>China&#8217;s Spring Festival travel season began on Sunday, pushing the limits of the country&#8217;s public transport system.<br />中国的春运从本周日开始，将其公共运输系统推到极限。</p>
<p>Hundreds of millions of people working and studying away from their hometowns are returning home to<B> reunite </B>with family members.<br />好几亿远离家乡工作和求学的人们，将要返回家乡与亲人团聚。</p>
<p>The <B>rush</B> began 15 days<B> ahead of</B> the Chinese Lunar New Year, and is expected to last until the middle of February.<br />返乡交通的高峰从农历春节前的15天开始，预计持续到二月中旬。</p>
<p>Nearly 3.2 billion passenger trips are expected during this year&#8217;s Spring Festival season, a nine percent increase over last year. Given that China&#8217;s population is around 1.3 billion, these numbers indicate that nearly everyone in the country will travel twice.<br />春节期间大约有近32亿人次的客流，比去年增加了9%。中国现有约13亿人口，这些数字显示，几乎每个人会出行2次。</p>
<p>It is estimated that around 2.8 billion trips will be made by bus, which <B>accounts for roughly </B>90 percent of the total number of passenger trips.<br />据估计，约28亿旅次是由巴士运载，占了整个客运数量的90%。</p>
<p><B>To cope with</B> the rising demand, 840 thousand buses are now running on roads, with over 2.6 million departures every day.<br />为了应付不断上升的客流，84万辆巴士上路运行，每天发车260多万次。</p>
<p>One of Beijing&#8217;s biggest transportation hubs, the Liuliqiao Long Distance Bus Station, is already experiencing a huge transportation rush.<br />北京最大的长途巴士站之一六里桥站，已经出现巨大的运输潮。</p>
<p>[Tian Fan, Liuliqiao Long Distance Bus Station]:<br />&#8220;Ten thousand passengers departed from this station on Sunday alone.&#8221;<br />六里桥长途巴士站的田方说：“星期天一天就有上万名旅客从这里出发。”</p>
<p>The station expects over 20 thousand passengers a day, as it gets closer to Lunar New Year&#8217;s Eve, just 15 days away.<br />预计农历新年除夕前的15日，每天都会有2万多名旅客涌向车站。<br />@*</p>
