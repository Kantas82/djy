<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：耶诞节礼日柏林市民赛鹅减重</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Berliners Jog Off the Christmas Pounds in Boxing Day Goose Run<br />耶诞节礼日柏林市民赛鹅减重</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/showaflvembed644152.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Pound</B>: [pa?nd] n. 磅重<br />2.	<B>Boxing day</B>: ph. 节礼日（耶诞节次日）<br />3.	<B>Get rid of </B>:ph. 摆脱<br />4.	<B>Pull together</B>: ph. 同心协力<br />5.	<B>Filling</B>: [&#8216;f?l??] adj. 充满的<br />6.	<B>Stuffed</B>: [st&#8217;?ft] adj. 填料的<br />7.	<B>Spur on</B>: ph. 鼓舞；激励<br />8.	<B>Dieter</B>: [d&#8217;it?] n. 节食者<br />9.	<B>Turn out </B>:ph. 出动<br />10. <B>	Seasonal</B>: [s&#8217;iz?n?l] adj. 季节性的<br />11.	<B>Mulled wine</B>: ph. 热香料酒<br />12.	<B>Festive</B>: [&#8216;f?st?v] adj. 节庆的</p>
<p>The Boxing Day goose race has become a firm tradition in the German capital and this year is no exception.<br />节礼日赛鹅已成为德国首都柏林的固定传统，今年也不例外。</p>
<p>Usually organized by members of Berlin&#8217;s sports club, Charlottenburg (SCC), this year&#8217;s 13th run to <B>get rid of</B> the extra pounds from the festive eating was <B>pull together</B> by Berlin marathon enthusiast, Bernd Huebner.<br />通常由柏林运动俱乐部的夏洛滕堡（SCC）成员举行，今年第13届的路跑，是由柏林马拉松爱好者贝恩德许布纳召集，目的是摆脱因节庆饮食增加的多余体重。</p>
<p>Germans traditionally eat a very <B>filling </B>and rich roast goose at Christmas and for extra motivation the race is led by St. Nick himself, holding a <B>stuffed </B>goose to <B>spur</B> the <B>dieters on</B>.<br />传统上德国人在耶诞节会吃一只非常饱满又丰富的烤鹅。此外，这项比赛有一名耶诞老人抱着一只填充鹅领头，鼓励减肥者一起来路跑。</p>
<p>Some 280 participants <B>turned out</B> for the run through West Berlin&#8217;s Grunewald forest.<br />约有 280名参加者出动，路跑穿越西柏林的古耐沃德森林。</p>
<p>Despite the effort to lose the <B>seasonal </B>pounds, cake and <B>mulled wine </B>were available in the woods at the halfway mark.<br />尽管为了季节性减肥多努力，在树林半路标志处，还是有蛋糕和热香料酒可以享用。</p>
<p>Dressed in <B>festive</B> Christmas hats and outfits the runners didn&#8217;t seem to mind being so sporting so soon after Christmas.<br />路跑者们身着节庆风耶诞帽和服装，但似乎并不介意耶诞节才过没多久就这么的运动。</p>
<p>[Bernd Huebner, Run Organizer]:<br />&#8220;After 5 kilometers I felt great and after the roast goose its a way to relax and is to be seen as a fun run. I know almost everyone here and some of them I only see on Boxing Day. It&#8217;s just fun and that&#8217;s how it should be.&#8221;<br />路跑召集人贝恩德许布纳说：“跑了5公里后我感觉很棒，我觉得这是吃完烤鹅后一种放松的方式，也是一个有趣的路跑活动。我几乎认识在场每个人，其中有些人在节礼日认识。这就是这么好玩，这活动也应该这样。”<br />@*</p>
