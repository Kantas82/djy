<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：美浓－美浓美意情浓</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2012年02月08日讯】（大纪元记者黄振茂李晴玳台湾高雄报导）有人说，美浓之美，不在于精雕细琢，而是浑然天成的恬静与亲切。</p>
<p>源出于南方六堆客庄的美浓小镇，三面倚山，南临悠悠的荖浓溪，坐拥山水的钟灵毓秀，勤朴的美浓居民，世代竟孕育出无数俊彦英才。美浓风光之美、人情之醇、文物之丰，乃至博士之多，都是闪耀全台的特色，值得行家前来探访、细细品味！</p>
<figure id="attachment_6555218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202080649021758-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555218" class="wp-caption-text">美浓皇历新年前后处处可见的花海，与田园农庄相映成趣。（摄影: 黄振茂 / 大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6555235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202081154371758-600x407.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555235" class="wp-caption-text">美浓中正湖凉亭与群山写照。（大纪元档案照片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>美浓拓垦始于乾隆年间，历经二百多年岁月，今日小镇风貌是创新与怀旧并呈。走访永安老街，寻访当年的烟业大户，废置的伙房烟楼隐约透露出历史的沧桑；巡礼“东门楼”与“敬字亭”，不由让人缅怀先民捍卫家园、教育子弟的苦心孤诣；此外，钟理和纪念馆，美浓客家文物馆、竹子门发电厂，也是造访美浓必不可错过的景点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6555248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555248" style="width: 405px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202081201291758.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555248" class="wp-caption-text">美浓百年历史古迹敬字亭物馆烟楼式建筑。（大纪元档案照片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6555253" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555253" style="width: 427px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1108120409302477.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555253" class="wp-caption-text">美浓重要古迹东门楼。（大纪元档案照片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6555263" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202080648121758-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555263" class="wp-caption-text">台湾第一座发电厂──美浓竹子门发电厂，至今仍在运转，堪称“活古迹”。电厂排放的水用以灌溉美浓平原，孕育出精致好吃的“美浓米”。（摄影: 黄振茂 / 大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6555276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1202081158451758-600x416.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555276" class="wp-caption-text">美浓客家文物馆烟楼式建筑。（大纪元档案照片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>行脚美浓，您若不期然迎来满眼的翠绿、或遍地的缤纷，且请放慢脚步，随心赏玩！走累了，不妨歇脚品鉴客庄的文物、美食──美浓雅致的陶窑与纸伞，有口皆碑；浓郁的擂茶芳香保证让您难忘！此外，若要享用美浓小吃，粄条和猪脚是必尝的美味，炒猪肠、福菜、野莲等客家小菜也绝不可少！</p>
<figure id="attachment_6555288" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6555288" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/02/1108200809472188-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6555288" class="wp-caption-text">美浓区客家特色农作野莲逐渐成为高雄市，目前种植面积近100公顷。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“台湾十大观光小城选拔”活动，美浓成为唯一入围的客家小镇，第二阶段投票暂居第二，高雄市客委会主委古秀妃，特以美浓女儿身份推荐故乡的“好山好水、好男好女”，她说，美浓淳厚的人情为旅客添增源源不断温暖。◇</p>
