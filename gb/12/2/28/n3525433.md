<p>Celebrities React to Whitney Houston&#8217;s Death<br />名人回应惠妮休斯顿之死</p>
<p><a width='470px' height='380px' src='http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/gb/showaflvembed663808.md#1' frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></a><br />本则影音新闻出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>Celebrity</B>: [s?l&#8217;?br?ti] n. 名人<br />2.	<B>Sadden</B>:[s&#8217;?d?n] v. 使悲痛<br />3.	<B>Mentor </B>: [m&#8217;?nt,?r] n. 良师益友<br />4.	<B>Mogul</B>:[m&#8217;og?l] n. 显要人物<br />5.	<B>Scores of</B>:ph. 许多；几十<br />6.	<B>Swarm</B>:[sw&#8217;?rm] v. 群集<br />7.	<B>Helicopter</B>:[h&#8217;?l?k,ɑpt?] n. 直升机<br />8.	<B>Personnel </B>: [p,?s?n&#8217;?l] n. 人事；全体人员<br />9.	<B>Pronounce </B>: [pr?n&#8217;ɑ?ns] v. 宣布；宣告</p>
<p>Celebrities at a pre-Grammy Awards party were shocked and<B> saddened</B> on Saturday by the news that singer Whitney Houston had died.<br />周六，在葛莱美奖预备舞会上的名人，都对惠妮休斯顿过世的消息感到震惊和悲痛。</p>
<p>The pop superstar died on the eve of the Grammy Awards in Los Angeles at the same hotel where her <B>mentor</B>, record <B>mogul </B> Clive Davis, was holding an annual pre-event party featuring <B>scores of </B> music industry celebrities.<br />这位流行乐坛超级巨星，于葛莱美奖举办前夕在洛杉矶过世。她的良师益友－唱片巨头克莱夫?戴维斯也在同一间饭店。当时他正在举办年度预备舞会，来了许多音乐界知名人士。</p>
<p>A dramatic scene unfolded at the Beverly Hilton hotel as guests arriving for the party expressed their shock at her death, while reporters <B>swarmed </B> the hotel, fans gathered outside to light candles in her memory and <B>helicopters </B>hovered overhead.<br />戏剧性的一幕展现在比佛利希尔顿饭店：抵达舞会的客人们表达他们的震惊，同时记者群集饭店、粉丝们聚集在饭店外点蜡烛悼念、直升机在上空盘旋。</p>
<p>Beverly Hills police said they were called to the Beverly Hilton at around 3:43 p.m. local time, and fire department <B>personnel</B>who were already at the location responded immediately.<br />比佛利山庄的警方说，当地时间下午3:43左右接获比佛利希尔顿饭店来电，消防部门人员已在现场并做出立即反应。</p>
<p>Houston was in her fourth-floor room but was unresponsive to CPR, and she was <B>pronounced</B>dead at 3:55 p.m.<br />休斯顿当时在她四楼的客房，心肺复苏术已无反应，她在下午3:55被宣告死亡。</p>
