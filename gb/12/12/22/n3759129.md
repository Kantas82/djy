<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宜居台中 乐活山城</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2012年12月22日讯】</p>
<p> <b> 新社花海踏青 紫色故乡寻梦</b> </p>
<p>时序进入秋冬农田休耕期，早期利用大片绿肥花草打造的花海节，如今已蜕变成大台中年度盛会，新社“国际花毯节”扬名海外。<br />当微风吹拂双颊，暖暖阳光、青草芬芳沁入心扉，亲子、情侣穿梭万紫千红花田间，紫色故乡为一群寻梦人带来幸福的春天。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220449132599-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660890" class="wp-caption-text">新社花海吸引旅人目光。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p> <b> 谷关温泉流淌 泰雅原音传唱 </b></p>
<p>自山麓间涌出、终年不竭的谷关温泉，流淌出清澈透蒙带弱碱的碳酸泉水，暖了身子，也纾解紧绷已久的心灵。<br />到松鹤、里冷部落走一趟吧! 原乡、原音响彻山谷、精湛的泰雅工艺品，让您有了买回家的冲动。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660902" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220531352599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660902" class="wp-caption-text">谷关温泉。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660907" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660907" style="width: 530px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220452562599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660907" class="wp-caption-text">疯泰雅部落、泡谷关温泉。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p> <b>新丁粄节闹元宵 东势客家筑山城 </b> </p>
<p>形如月弯、水源充沛、日夜温差大，克勤克俭的客家族群，在400公尺以上的海拔，成就东势“水果王国”，大量的高接梨、柑桔、甜柿、葡萄，成为大雪山下一颗闪亮的明珠。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220534302599-600x398.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660912" class="wp-caption-text">东势采果乐。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure><br />流传百年，全台独有的“新丁粄节”，如今节庆也有了新花样，新灯踩街、丢炮城及客家美食，在乍暖还寒的元宵夜，香客涌入，远到的日本鸟取县太鼓团也来祝贺，为庆典制造高潮。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660919" style="width: 299px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220532522599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660919" class="wp-caption-text">新丁粄节元宵庆典。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p> <b> 石冈铁马道 水坝绿生态 </b> </p>
<p>石冈水源丰沛、气候良好，石冈水坝自然景观丰富，水域周遭林间，大量鹭科水鸟聚集繁殖，赏鸟人士趋之若鹜。<br />废弃铁道改建自行车专用道的东丰绿廊，夏日穿越林间，无比畅快，回程买个草本阿嬷药浴包洗涤一下。<br /> <br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660925" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660925" style="width: 330px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220538452599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660925" class="wp-caption-text">东丰铁马道由废弃铁道改建。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p> <b><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660935" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220539332599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660935" class="wp-caption-text">单车穿越东丰绿廊，畅快无比。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure>大坑步道登山趣 漫山萤河蝶飞舞</b> </p>
<p>大坑10条登山步道，有老少皆宜及挑战登顶的选择，你要哪一条？大汗淋漓后，下山有土鸡城、烤乳猪庭园、甜品、饮料任君选择。<br />完整的山林地形，留住台湾猕猴，复育区也找回萤火虫、群蝶飞舞，美不胜收。◇<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660940" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220535262599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660940" class="wp-caption-text">大坑共有10条登山步道。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660944" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220536272599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660944" class="wp-caption-text">大坑步道沿途可见凤蝶飞舞。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_6660947" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6660947" style="width: 272px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/12/1212220537142599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6660947" class="wp-caption-text">大坑猕猴。（图：台中市观光旅游局提供）</figcaption></figure><br />(责任编辑：丘叔)</p>
