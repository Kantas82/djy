<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金马57贵宾专属礼曝光 24项严选商品荣耀入围者</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414131487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第57届金马奖今年的24项专属客制贵宾礼品于日前曝光。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年10月02日讯】第57届<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>颁奖典礼将于11月21日举行，众所瞩目的“金马贵宾礼品”，近年已是电影人在入围名单之外最期待的金马大事，更曾有<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A5%E5%9B%B4%E8%80%85.md#1">入围者</a>表示：“不在乎有没有得奖，只希望每年都可入围拿到礼品！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447639" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414281487-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447639" class="wp-caption-text">本届<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>星光大道主持人杨千霈（左）、林鹤轩于日前共同宣布今年的24项专属客制贵宾礼品，为所有<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%A5%E5%9B%B4%E8%80%85.md#1">入围者</a>喝采。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日前（9月30日）金马执委会邀请本届星光大道主持人杨千霈、林鹤轩共同宣布今年的24项专属客制贵宾礼品，为所有入围者喝采。今年金马贵宾礼品以“生活”为主题，严选众品牌打造极具质感商品。</p>
<p>除了先前曝光的客制金色限量款大同联名电锅、迷你纪念电锅置物盒，以及CSD中卫口罩礼盒等影人心中的梦幻逸品，还有第六年打造专属瓶身的海洋深层气泡水、泡泡骚手机气囊支架、台电文创以旧公文纸环保再生的扩香石笔记本，以及著名钢笔品牌的联名玫金钢笔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447640" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414241487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12447640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414241487.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447640" class="wp-caption-text">台湾特有种五木精油香气礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447683" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446071487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12447683" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446071487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="677" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447683" class="wp-caption-text">CSD中卫联名口罩礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447646" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414171487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12447646" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414171487.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="681" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447646" class="wp-caption-text">JINS金马联名墨镜。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>考量入围者在外拍戏需求，金马也贴心准备来自美国的HYDY联名两用随行保温杯、日本品牌JINS联名墨镜、文青指定plain-me旅行小包及托特包。还有肌肤香氛品牌KP女王香氛的花灿光耀香氛洗沐组、星光保养礼盒等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447684" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446031487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447684" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446031487-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447684" class="wp-caption-text">星光保养礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414201487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414201487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447643" class="wp-caption-text">花灿光耀香氛礼盒组。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，还有疫情之下，更针对环境防护推出多款商品，包含肯园“疗愈之岛”台湾特有种五木精油香气礼盒、大稻埕品牌大春炼皂的耀净金马皂礼盒、ible Airvida联名穿戴式空气清净机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446111487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447686" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446111487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447686" class="wp-caption-text">品牌联名穿戴式空气清净机。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832531487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447874" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832531487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447874" class="wp-caption-text">大春炼皂的耀净金马皂礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>食品部分则有带起台湾“黑糖鲜奶”风潮的联名黑糖礼盒、有机食品专家的芝麻礼盒、超夯团购的客制爆米花，以及来自台中的8结蛋卷，行政院农委会与彰化县埔盐乡大有社区合作的金碳稻礼盒，还有老协珍熬鸡精。此外，陪所有人一起长大的零食“乖乖”也加入联名行列，一连推出两款商品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832491487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447876" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832491487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447876" class="wp-caption-text">农村好物金碳稻礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446151487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447687" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446151487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447687" class="wp-caption-text">老协珍联名熬鸡精。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年除了金马奖入围者可以获得全套贵宾品，部分商品也将在品牌通路贩售，11月4日起更可直接在指定的便利商店购买，让更多人搜藏<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC57.md#1">金马57</a>的限量联名商品，共享盛会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447877" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832441487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12447877" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020832441487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="678" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447877" class="wp-caption-text">米森金粹芝麻礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第57届金马奖颁奖典礼将于11月21日在台北国父纪念馆举行。</p>
