<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>争睹巨星风采 白嘉莉艺术创作展轰动新竹县</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4ec0a95083e4d107ac1689d96978a466-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白嘉莉（右）和新竹县长杨文科合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年10月02日讯】（大纪元记者赖月贵台湾新竹报导）拥有最美丽主持人称号的<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E5%98%89%E8%8E%89.md#1">白嘉莉</a>，也是1970年代许多人心目中的偶像，日前回台举办艺术创作巡回展，继台中、台北之后，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9%E5%8E%BF.md#1">新竹县</a>是第3站，从今年4月起网路就疯传着白嘉莉要在新竹县办展的消息，可说是未演先轰动。</p>
<p>10月2日记者会上宾客云集，热情的民众几乎挤满了竹县文化局美术馆的展览厅，大家争相一睹明星风采，虽经岁月流逝，<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E5%98%89%E8%8E%89.md#1">白嘉莉</a>本人却风采依旧、依然闪耀亮眼，被热情民众包围着。白嘉莉表示，人亲、土亲、故乡更亲，看到民众这样的热情让她很感动，虽然前年她最爱的丈夫过世了，但回到台湾让她觉得很温暖不会孤单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8f773ecfb04d03f2290d8cee3a88e71d-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8f773ecfb04d03f2290d8cee3a88e71d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447799" class="wp-caption-text">白嘉莉（中）和<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E7%AB%B9%E5%8E%BF.md#1">新竹县</a>长<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%96%87%E7%A7%91.md#1">杨文科</a>（左）接受媒体采访。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新竹县长<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%96%87%E7%A7%91.md#1">杨文科</a>特地出席记者会，开心的表示，驰名国际，拥有最美丽主持人头衔的白嘉莉，曾主持多样各式节目，如群星会、银河旋宫……等，出版过5张唱片专辑，也曾参与影剧演出，真是多才多艺，是当年许多人的偶像，今天很高兴能邀请到她来新竹县办展，展览期间适逢国庆日，也将特别邀她在双十国庆当天，参加县府广场所举办的升旗典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447800" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447800" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1f3dafd93f1d477f5b1c851cce4519c5-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447800" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1f3dafd93f1d477f5b1c851cce4519c5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447800" class="wp-caption-text">参观民众和美丽的作品合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白嘉莉婚后旅居海外，闲暇寄情绘画40多年，先后曾蒙张大千、吴冠中、丁绍光等各界大师的指导，作品丰富多元，从水墨到水彩，进入压克力油画，开创多元的创作风格，也有绢印版画、青花瓷器及贝瓷等文创作品，展出场面很盛大，包下了美术馆一楼的全部展览室。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447801" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/aeaf1b5839acef5fe68d956706cb658f-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/aeaf1b5839acef5fe68d956706cb658f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447801" class="wp-caption-text">现场展出的陶瓷作品美丽吸睛。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新竹县文化局长田昭容表示，“白嘉莉-另一个春天<ahref="https://github.com/ncmsaz3871/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%88%9B%E4%BD%9C%E5%B1%95.md#1">艺术创作展</a>”于即日起至10月18日盛大展出，本次策展概念是“生命盛开，艺术绽放”，用来彰显白嘉莉对艺术追求的自在与饱满，透过作品传达生命深度与丰沛内涵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447802" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447802" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/32581e7c5ad05d3ab49e8555ff2a8809-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/32581e7c5ad05d3ab49e8555ff2a8809-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447802" class="wp-caption-text">现场展出的陶瓷作品美丽吸睛。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次展览除了白嘉莉累积40多年之艺术创作之外，也展出昔日精彩怀旧照片，还特别提供现场预购白嘉莉《回眸》自传、《另一个春天》画册及2021月历等，让粉丝重温1970年代这位巨星的翩翩风采。此外，白嘉莉于展出期间，每周末下午3点会在美术馆举办签书会，民众可以一睹巨星风采并与她合影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12447803" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447803" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4ace1716e329fd43982eb40feaf12340-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447803" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4ace1716e329fd43982eb40feaf12340-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447803" class="wp-caption-text">现场展出美丽的压克力油画。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12447804" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12447804" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7d39f5e2f2603e81548dbf4a4c1929f1-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-12447804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7d39f5e2f2603e81548dbf4a4c1929f1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12447804" class="wp-caption-text">参观民众和美丽的绢印版画作品合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
