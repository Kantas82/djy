<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】葡汁烩鲜鱿鱼 香浓馥郁特色粤菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/13-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天气变冷时，千万不能错过“葡汁烩鲜鱿鱼”这一道粤菜，味道浓郁又好下饭。如果不吃海鲜，单纯葡汁杂菜也很够味！（图／梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编按：</strong>“葡”汁是和蔬菜混合的食用汁液，材料取用港澳独有食材调酱，并非来自“葡萄牙”。所以，如果有机会去一趟葡萄牙，你可能吃不到这一道家常菜。“葡菜”特色重视酱料，会将多种类的食材混合在酱汁中，味道带点辣，不注重卖相摆盘，这种料理方法和饮食文化，有人说是因为在澳门的葡萄牙人后代表现出热情、乐于分享的性格。</p>
<p>葡汁美食味道偏香浓馥郁，黄姜、咖喱、椰奶都是不可或缺的调味料。天气变冷时，葡汁杂菜或是葡汁烩海鲜就相对受欢迎，味道浓郁好下饭、又可以暖身子。现在，如果料理时不会调酱，也不用伤脑筋，市面上已有贩售葡汁罐头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463642" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-3-450x338.jpg" alt="葡汁" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463642" class="wp-caption-text">葡汁烩海鲜，有菜有肉一锅满足家人的胃，此道料理味道浓郁又营养充足，是秋冬常见家常菜。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/wdyzca3145/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>

鲜<ahref="https://github.com/wdyzca3145/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BF%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱿鱼</a>1磅</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463628" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463628" class="wp-caption-text">准备食材：马铃薯、洋葱、<ahref="https://github.com/wdyzca3145/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0%E8%8A%B1.md#1">西兰花</a>、鲜<ahref="https://github.com/wdyzca3145/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BF%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鱿鱼</a>。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/wdyzca3145/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>

黄姜粉1茶匙<br />
糖1汤匙<br />

<figure id="attachment_12463630" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463630" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463630" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463630" class="wp-caption-text">制作“葡汁”材料，鸡汤、椰奶、面粉、糖、牛油、咖喱粉、黄姜粉。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 马铃薯去皮切小块，洋葱切小块。西兰花切成小朵，用姜汁酒烫过后捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463632" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463632" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463632" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-5-450x338.jpg" alt="西兰花" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463632" class="wp-caption-text">西兰花切成小朵，用姜汁酒烫过后捞出备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 鱿鱼从肚中间剪开去除内脏及软骨，撕去外面的鱿鱼衣，在内面切上十字花纹，再切成小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463634" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463634" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-3-450x338.jpg" alt="鱿鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463634" class="wp-caption-text">鱿鱼从肚中间剪开去除内脏及软骨，撕去外面的鱿鱼衣，在内面切上十字花纹，再切成小块备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鱿鱼用姜汁酒烫30秒后，即捞出沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-2-450x338.jpg" alt="鱿鱼" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463635" class="wp-caption-text">鱿鱼用姜汁酒烫30秒后，即捞出沥干。 （图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热镬下油2汤匙，先将马铃薯煎至金黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463636" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463636" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e2588a218b27f68f9a1eb4cf92af0252.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e2588a218b27f68f9a1eb4cf92af0252-450x338.jpg" alt="马铃薯" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463636" class="wp-caption-text">热镬下油2汤匙，先将马铃薯煎至金黄。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 接着加入洋葱炒至微黄后捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-2-450x338.jpg" alt="洋葱" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463638" class="wp-caption-text">加入洋葱炒至微黄后捞出备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 锅中加入面粉和牛油炒香成糊状，再加入咖喱粉黄姜粉拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463639" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-2-450x338.jpg" alt="咖喱粉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463639" class="wp-caption-text">锅中加入面粉和牛油炒香成糊状，再加入咖喱粉、黄姜粉拌匀。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着加入鸡汤、糖和椰奶，放回马铃薯和洋葱，煮5分钟左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463640" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-2-450x338.jpg" alt="鸡汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463640" class="wp-caption-text">加入鸡汤、糖和椰奶，放回马铃薯和洋葱，煮5分钟左右。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 最后加入西兰花和鲜鱿煮约2分钟，便可上碟食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12463641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12463641" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12463641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12463641" class="wp-caption-text">加入西兰花和鲜鱿煮约2分钟，便可上碟食用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
