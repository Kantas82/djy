<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【在家咖啡厅】手作外酥内软“奶油蜜糖吐司”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/26028a1f673d89a2a448df1431357dee-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>早餐和下午茶的口袋名单“蜜糖吐司”，口感外酥内软令人回味再三，吐司上面还可以装饰水果和鲜奶油，营养加分。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%9C%E7%B3%96%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">蜜糖吐司</a>是最近人气高涨的早午餐菜色，早上将香甜可口的蜜糖吐司烤过，外酥内软的口感令人回味再三。蜜糖吐司的外型讨喜、滋味诱人，十分适合在悠闲的周末享用，也是招待客人非常体面的<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E5%BF%83.md#1">点心</a>。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料</span></h3>
<p>厚片吐司1个<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a> 2?汤匙<br />
蜂蜜 2?汤匙<br />
糖1／2杯</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">选择性材料</span></h3>
<p>糖粉 0.5 汤匙<br />
肉桂粉少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h3>
<p>1.把吐司切成厚片。<br />
(把2到3片普通土司叠在一起也OK！也可买整条吐司，请店家切取需要的大小。)</p>
<figure id="attachment_12477831" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12477831" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b5b44126f52fdcfff336cf8d01f6d58f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b5b44126f52fdcfff336cf8d01f6d58f-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12477831" class="wp-caption-text">取一个厚片土司，也可以将很多片薄片吐司叠在一起。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.<ahref="https://github.com/imabdi3119/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a>放在室温下融化。在距离吐司边缘1cm的地方用刀子划X，在隙缝处挤入蜂蜜。<br />
(使用装在压挤式容器里的蜂蜜较方便！)</p>
<figure id="attachment_12477832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12477832" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/878b56b527164c0388b585c29bc2a53d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/878b56b527164c0388b585c29bc2a53d-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12477832" class="wp-caption-text">奶油放在室温下融化。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12477833" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12477833" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/121a92fee505fcbdbe64dc4b9fc8e0f9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/121a92fee505fcbdbe64dc4b9fc8e0f9-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12477833" class="wp-caption-text">在距离吐司边缘1cm的地方用刀子划X，在隙缝处挤入蜂蜜。奶油放在室温下融化。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.在吐司表面涂上奶油，最后再涂一层糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12477834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12477834" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ca6e23aec1ea460c16ea22278fc8b3d3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ca6e23aec1ea460c16ea22278fc8b3d3-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12477834" class="wp-caption-text">在吐司表面涂上奶油，最后再涂一层糖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.烤箱以200℃预热，放入吐司烤5～10分，最后洒上糖粉和肉桂粉。<br />
（可以用水果或鲜奶油装饰，美味令人难以抗拒！）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12477835" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12477835" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/0d3eb2b317936037e92cfb6862a1b40e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/0d3eb2b317936037e92cfb6862a1b40e-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12477835" class="wp-caption-text">烤箱以200℃预热，放入吐司烤5～10分。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
