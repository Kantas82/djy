<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>集集石虎号彩绘列车首航 纪念套票同步发行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/548266336d2921a225216c46b934397c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>集集石虎号彩绘列车首航仪式。（集集镇公所提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者林萌骞台湾南投报导）生态保育与大众运输结合，台铁<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%86%E9%9B%86%E6%94%AF%E7%BA%BF.md#1">集集支线</a>新<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E7%BB%98%E5%88%97%E8%BD%A6.md#1">彩绘列车</a>“集集<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E8%99%8E.md#1">石虎</a>号”20日在二水火车站亮相，该列车至110年3月底前，每月15日以固定班次行驶，<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E5%A5%97%E7%A5%A8.md#1">纪念套票</a>也在现场同步发行。</p>
<p>下午首航的“集集<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E8%99%8E.md#1">石虎</a>号”列车，是由特生中心与台铁合作，将<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%86%E9%9B%86%E6%94%AF%E7%BA%BF.md#1">集集支线</a>4节车厢分别以墨绿、靛青、茶褐、棕红色为基底，彩绘上石虎、山羌、白鼻心、台湾野猪、台湾野兔、竹鸡、赤腹松鼠和莫氏树蛙等野生动物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11958142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11958142" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/fb64d2ca85c1b21d126e696a1b18ec53-450x364.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11958142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/fb64d2ca85c1b21d126e696a1b18ec53-450x364.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="364" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11958142" class="wp-caption-text">台铁集集支线新<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E7%BB%98%E5%88%97%E8%BD%A6.md#1">彩绘列车</a>“集集石虎号”20日在二水火车站亮相。（集集镇公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>车厢内还有国产材石虎木雕，伴随着旅客到沿途的里山小镇漫游，放慢生活步调，带着好心情，认识里山生物的安心生活网络。</p>
<p>此外，活动现场还有春鱼工作室《濒临绝种团RESCUTE》的成员“石虎15号”coser，陪伴来宾搭乘首航列车并互动拍照，“集集石虎号”<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%AA%E5%BF%B5%E5%A5%97%E7%A5%A8.md#1">纪念套票</a>则于上午在二水车站同步限量发行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11958143" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11958143" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8d6e1d03dc0a1ac327d395e7f6ae7171-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11958143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8d6e1d03dc0a1ac327d395e7f6ae7171-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11958143" class="wp-caption-text">集集镇长陈纪衡﹙右﹚与春鱼工作室《濒临绝种团RESCUTE》的成员“石虎15号”coser﹙左﹚在集集石虎号彩绘列车旁合影。（集集镇公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>集集镇长陈纪衡表示，集集镇是石虎的重要栖地之一，加上研究单位特生中心也位在镇内，使得集集镇获得外界给予“石虎娘家”的美誉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11958144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11958144" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d83a1a60ae247b116b20bc61494ef57a-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11958144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d83a1a60ae247b116b20bc61494ef57a-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11958144" class="wp-caption-text">集集镇长陈纪衡﹙左﹚手持集集石虎号”纪念套票在彩绘列车旁合影。（集集镇公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>镇公所过去曾推出寻找石虎的活动，在镇内布置超过30件石虎相关作品，近期也向观光局争取到守护者石虎花灯，正在隘寮牛湖田驿站进行典藏展出，近几年，镇公所更与特生中心、镇内业者合作，期盼推动石虎生态旅游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11958146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11958146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4c17dac469e51b3b0ce88f4dfa60c246-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-11958146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4c17dac469e51b3b0ce88f4dfa60c246-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11958146" class="wp-caption-text">集集石虎号车厢内的国产材石虎木雕。（集集镇公所提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈纪衡欢迎生态迷、石虎迷、铁道迷把握这一波的机会，搭乘集集石虎列车、国立集集美术馆列车，来一趟寻找石虎的旅程，到集集探险。</p>
<p>特生中心则希望透过本次生态保育与大众运输的结合，让更多民众接触与了解里山生物样貌、生态习性与生存环境，进而支持生态保育研究与行动，了解政府推动国土生态绿网计划的重要性，强化国人对生活周遭环境的关心。</p>
