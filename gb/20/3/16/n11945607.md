<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【武汉肺炎】3月16日最新消息</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月17日讯】3月16日武汉肺炎（又称新型冠状病毒，COVID-19）最新消息。</p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1·3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">16</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，总理特鲁多（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Justin Trudeau</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）宣布，加拿大将对非加拿大公民或永久居民的人关闭边境，但美国公民例外。从星期三开始，国际航班（除从美国和墨西哥入境）将仅限于加拿大的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个机场，包括卡尔加里，多伦多，温哥华，和蒙特利尔。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">2·亚省首席医学官肖欣（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Deena Hinshaw</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）博士出现感冒症状，现开始自我隔离。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">3·加拿大首席公共卫生官谭（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Theresa Tam</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）博士建议不要有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人以上的聚集。全国目前有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">407</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例确诊的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">病例。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4·3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">15</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，省长康尼（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Jason Kenney</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）宣布，过去</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">24</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">小时内，亚省有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">17</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例新的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">病例，病例总数达到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">56</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">5·全省所有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">K-12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">和高校课程均被取消，所有有执照的托儿设施，校外看护计划和学前教育计划都将关闭，直至另行通知。礼拜堂聚会不超过</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">250</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">6·卡尔加里市已宣布进入紧急状态。</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CEMA</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">负责人桑普森（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Tom Sampson</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）说，所有城市拥有的健身设施，游泳池，竞技场和公共图书馆都将关闭。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">7·卡尔加里</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Co-op</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">表示，他们将为受</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">影响的人们提供免费护理服务包，至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">15</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">8·Enmax</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">电力公司宣布对未付费者暂停断电，直至另行通知。并为那些受</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">影响的人增加了灵活的付款方式。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">9·Tim Hortons</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">与星巴克（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Starbucks</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）将于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">17</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日起，在大多数地点关闭座位。</span></p>
<h3>最新取消</h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">1·加拿大落基山脉的度假胜地将从</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">15</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日开始暂停滑雪胜地运营。纳基斯卡（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Nakiska</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">），费妮（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Fernie</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">），金伯利（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Kimberley</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）和踢马（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Kicking Horse</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）滑雪场将关闭。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">2·卡尔加里动物园，遗产公园和卡尔加里堡从今天开始暂时关闭。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">3·卡城</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">WinSport</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">的滑雪场和单板滑雪山以及</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Markin MacPhail</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">中心（包括比赛培训中心和竞技场）暂停运营。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">4·原定于四月举行的卡尔加里地下电影节（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">The Calgary Underground Film Festival</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）将被推迟。</span></p>
