<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲“侏儒”水果树 孩子也会种</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/PlantNet-Plum-trees-1200x800-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（PlantNet? 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月28日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>墨尔本工商讯）每个澳洲人家的后院，都能种上一棵“侏儒”<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E6%A0%91.md#1">水果树</a>！Backyard Beauties?系列水果树，是由澳洲公司PlantNet<sup>?</sup>为家庭园艺爱好者精心研发的<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E6%A0%91.md#1">水果树</a>种。</p>
<p>在园艺空间相对有限的环境下，这样的水果树设计诞生了，它们因此也被称为<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E4%BE%8F%E5%84%92%E2%80%9D%E6%A0%91.md#1">“侏儒”树</a>，也就是指，较小的树型却能结出正常大小的果实。它们深受<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>家庭园艺爱好者的喜爱，具有非凡的价值。这样的树种，花费更少的人工照料，却能让您每个季节收获优质的水果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11982248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11982248" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/PlantNet-favourites-new-varities.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11982248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/PlantNet-favourites-new-varities-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11982248" class="wp-caption-text">（PlantNet<sup>?</sup>?提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Backyard Beauties?系列水果树，不仅在树型大小方面具有优势， 这意味着它们既可以在地面上生长，也可以通过<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>种植，此外，<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E4%BE%8F%E5%84%92%E2%80%9D%E6%A0%91.md#1">“侏儒”树</a>在水果品质方面也是无可匹敌的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11982243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11982243" style="width: 260px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e16a9a6d73478d86f5ca2f2eab2eb9f1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11982243" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e16a9a6d73478d86f5ca2f2eab2eb9f1.jpg" alt="" width="260" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11982243" class="wp-caption-text">当您在当地的苗圃购物时，请留意上图所示的Backyard Beauties?商标。（PlantNet<sup>?</sup> 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Backyard Beauties?系列中第一批品种包括Super Dwarf Sunset Peach?和Super Dwarf Sunset Sunset Nectarine?。将超级侏儒桃树和超级侏儒油桃树纳入Backyard Beauties?首发品种中的主要原因之一，是因为它们适合澳大利亚的大多数园艺爱好者种植，包括<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A9%E5%AD%90.md#1">孩子</a>。紧接着，Backyard Beauties?推出了苹果树Pinkabelle<sup>?</sup>和Leprechaun? Apples。最新的品种包括人气超高的蓝莓Blueberry Burst<sup>?</sup>和Blueberry Kisses<sup>?</sup>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11982245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11982245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/AdobeStock_155512600-Fresno.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11982245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/AdobeStock_155512600-Fresno-600x392.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11982245" class="wp-caption-text">（PlantNet<sup>?</sup>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11982246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11982246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/AdobeStock_24866223-Dwarf-Royal-Gem.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11982246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/AdobeStock_24866223-Dwarf-Royal-Gem-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11982246" class="wp-caption-text">（PlantNet<sup>?</sup> 提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Backyard Beauties?系列的水果树将在种植后的前12个月内完全生长，一旦开始结果，其生长将显着减慢，因此所有树木培养都应在前12个月内完成。在整个季节中，由于所有品种会在不同时间段成熟，因此您不需要在整个夏季/秋季再从水果店购买蓝莓、苹果、梨、油桃、桃子或杏子了。</p>
<p>我们希望帮助您改善园艺体验并收获丰盛的果实，也希望您对自己购买的植物充满信心，相信它们物有所值。</p>
