<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>主厨也逛Costco！ 7种他们会买的食物</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年03月06日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者陈霆编译报导）你也许会以为，专业的<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%BB%E5%8E%A8.md#1">主厨</a>多半都在肉铺、精品杂货店或者农夫市集等处购买食材，但其实，他们跟我们一样，也常会光顾那些连锁的量贩卖场。在众多卖场中，以大量购买为主要消费模式的</span><span class="s2"> Costco</span><span class="s1">，其实深受<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%BB%E5%8E%A8.md#1">主厨</a>们的喜爱和推荐。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Costco </span><span class="s1">受到青睐的原因，绝对不只是商品的价格便宜，更是因为此处所贩售的食材品质具有一定的水平。下面就让我们一起来了解，专业的主厨们到底都到</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">买些什么东西吧！</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>各种肉类</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11918871" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11918871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-71611422.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11918871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-71611422-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11918871" class="wp-caption-text">许多主厨都很习惯在 Costco 购买肉品，例如牛排、猪肉排等。对一般人来说，在 Costco 能够买到具有餐厅水平，但是价格又可亲的各种肉类。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">许多主厨都很习惯在</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">购买肉品，例如牛排、猪肉排等。而且，</span><span class="s2">Costco </span><span class="s1">有时候也会提供一些特殊的顶级肉，像是和牛（</span><span class="s2">Waygu beef</span><span class="s1">）和顶级的上盖肉牛排（</span><span class="s2">Spinalis Steak</span><span class="s1">）。</span><span class="s2">Costco </span><span class="s1">肉品部门的切肉技术也有一定的水平，对一般人来说，在</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">能够买到具有餐厅水平，但是价格又可亲的各种肉类。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">主持烹饪节目“</span><span class="s2">Bits &amp; Pieces</span><span class="s1">”的主厨朱莉</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">尤恩（</span><span class="s2">Julie Yoon</span><span class="s1">）也经常到</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">购买猪肉排，她会把猪排一块块用保鲜膜包好，再用</span><span class="s2">Zip</span><span class="s1">夹链袋装起来，冷冻在冰箱，需要时再解冻使用。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>冷冻海鲜</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11918882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11918882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Fotolia_68369178_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11918882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Fotolia_68369178_Subaion_L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11918882" class="wp-caption-text">主厨们建议，可以购买体积小的海鲜，例如虾仁和扇贝（scallops ）。扇贝可用奶油简单煎香，整个过程通常只需4～5分钟的时间。（Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">冷冻海鲜可以长时间储存，买回家不需要马上煮完。主厨们建议，可以购买体积小的海鲜，例如虾仁和扇贝（</span><span class="s2">scallops </span><span class="s1">），因为这类海鲜解冻快，需要煮菜时，短时间就能解冻完成。以虾仁为例，将冷冻的虾仁连袋子放在冷水中约</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">分钟，就可以初步解冻一部分虾仁。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">“冷冻的扇贝太棒了！它不仅美味口感好，而且可以存放在冰箱中，需要时随时可以用！”餐厅“</span><span class="s2">Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen</span><span class="s1">”的主厨艾米</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">阿拉尔孔（</span><span class="s2">Amy Alarcon</span><span class="s1">）说。而且，烹调扇贝也十分简单，可以用奶油简单煎香，扇贝通常只需烹调</span><span class="s2">4</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">分钟，对忙碌的人来说，是很方便的菜色。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>烤鸡</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11918877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11918877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-53078399.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11918877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-53078399-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11918877" class="wp-caption-text">烤鸡可说是 Costco 最受欢迎的熟食之一，烤鸡货架前总是排着长长的人群等着购买香喷喷的鸡肉。有趣的是，主厨们也很常购买 Costco 的烤鸡。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">烤鸡可说是</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">最受欢迎的熟食之一，烤鸡货架前总是排着长长的人群等着购买香喷喷的鸡肉。有趣的是，主厨们也很常购买</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">的烤鸡。“当我跟家人一起去逛</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">时，我们总是会拿一只烤鸡。它虽然不是最高级的，但它非常便宜，而且调味良好。”餐厅“</span><span class="s2">Bar Boulud</span><span class="s1">”的行政主厨迪特</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">萨米恩（</span><span class="s2">Dieter Samijn</span><span class="s1">）说。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>橄榄油与酪梨油</b></span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">Costco</span><span class="s3">有几款价格实惠的油品，这些品质良好但价格优惠的油也受到主厨的肯定。《</span><span class="s1">Salt, Fat, Acid, Heat: Mastering the Elements of Good Cooking</span><span class="s3">》的作者沙明</span><span class="s1">·</span><span class="s3">诺斯拉特（</span><span class="s1">Samin Nosrat</span><span class="s3">）也推荐过</span><span class="s1"> Kirkland </span><span class="s3">的橄榄油，她表示，这款橄榄油美味又便宜，且杂质少，适合所有需要固定烹调餐点的人。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">送餐网站“</span><span class="s2">Mind Body Fork</span><span class="s1">”的创始人黛比</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">李（</span><span class="s2">Debbie Lee</span><span class="s1">）则喜欢</span><span class="s2"> Costco </span><span class="s1">贩卖的酪梨油，“（酪梨油）的价格真是令人难以置信，而且这种中性且万用的油品，通常可用于调制沙拉酱，或者用来油炸。”黛比</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">李说。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>坚果和健康零食</b></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在忙碌的生活中，坚果和健康零食可以让我们补充体力，有时候工作到很晚想吃宵夜，吃这类食物也比吃一般的零嘴还要好得多！在</span><span class="s2"> Costco</span><span class="s1">，你可以买到杏仁、胡桃、腰果和核桃等坚果，有的已经调味，有的则是无调味且需要自行烘培的，主厨们十分推荐这些坚果产品。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另外，主厨尼可</span><span class="s2">·</span><span class="s1">帕拉诺莫斯（</span><span class="s2">Niko Paranomos</span><span class="s1">）也推荐了</span><span class="s2"> Inno Specialty Foods </span><span class="s1">有机南瓜奇亚籽椰子脆块这款健康零食，他表示自己虽然身为厨师，但忙碌的生活让他很少在家做饭，这款健康的零食可以帮助他补充体力，并确保自己摄取足够的营养。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>蕃茄罐头</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11918889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11918889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Fotolia_57613768_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11918889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Fotolia_57613768_Subaion_L-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11918889" class="wp-caption-text">如果你想替你做的浓汤、炖菜或炖饭增添味道的层次，可以像主厨们一样使用costco的蕃茄罐头。蕃茄罐头比新鲜蕃茄更能增添浓郁的滋味。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Costco </span><span class="s1">的蕃茄罐头是不少餐厅的备品，因为在制作炖菜时，使用蕃茄罐头可以做出更浓醇的风味。如果你想替你做的浓汤、炖菜或炖饭增添味道的层次，可以像主厨们一样使用蕃茄罐头。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s3"><b>枫糖浆</b></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Kirkland</span><span class="s1">出品的枫糖浆具有一定品质，且价格实惠。主厨建议，枫糖浆除了可以作为甜味剂外，也可以用来涂在烤肉上，增加烤肉的色泽。◇</span></p>
