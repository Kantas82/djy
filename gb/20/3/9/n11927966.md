<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】腊味炒荷兰豆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/a39cbd410e93434a83f95e6d7d79f5b6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨的“腊味炒荷兰豆”，甜脆的荷兰豆搭配浓醇的腊肉，腊肉的韧劲、荷兰豆的爽脆，咸中带甜，甜中带脆，味道好，口感好，做法还简单。您不想试一试吗？（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>腊肉是中国腌肉的一种，主要流行于四川、湖南和广东一带，在南方其它地区也有制作。</p>
<p>在中国“腊”制食物的名称最早见于最古老的文献之一《易经》：“晞于阳而炀于火，曰腊肉。”据记载，早在两千多年前，张鲁称汉宁王，兵败南下走巴中，途经汉中红庙塘时，汉中人用上等腊肉招待过他；又传，清光绪二十六年，慈禧太后携光绪皇帝避难西安，陕南地方官吏曾进贡腊肉御用，慈禧食后，赞不绝口。</p>
<p>好的腊肉，表里一致，煮熟切成片，透明发亮，色泽鲜艳，黄里透红，吃起来味道醇香，肥不腻口，瘦不塞牙，不仅风味独特，而且具有开胃、祛寒、消食等功效。</p>
<p>荷兰豆，原产地中海沿岸及亚洲西部，和所有豆类食物一样都是营养十分丰富的食材：含有优质蛋白质，可以提高机体的抗病能力和康复能力；富含胡萝卜素，可防止人体致癌物质的合成；富含粗纤维，可以促进胃肠蠕动，防止便秘。而且荷兰豆也是铁的上好来源。此外，荷兰豆还含有一种其特有的植物凝集素、止杈素及赤霉素A20等，具有抗菌消炎、增强人体新陈代谢的功效。</p>
<p>荷兰豆嫩荚质脆清香，炒着吃味道特别棒，尤其搭配上自家做的腊味，色泽青翠、口感脆嫩，特别开胃。</p>
<p>罗厨这道“<ahref="https://github.com/qkhlv2383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8A%E5%91%B3%E7%82%92%E8%8D%B7%E5%85%B0%E8%B1%86.md">腊味炒荷兰豆</a>”，甜脆的荷兰豆搭配浓醇的腊肉，腊肉的韧劲、荷兰豆的爽脆，咸中带甜，甜中带脆，味道好、口感好，做法还简单。您不想试一试吗？</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/qkhlv2383/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%8A%E5%91%B3%E7%82%92%E8%8D%B7%E5%85%B0%E8%B1%86.md">腊味炒荷兰豆</a></h2>
<p>主料：腊味6两、荷兰豆2磅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/89e624d5147a10e0d276f96907848e05.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11932998" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/89e624d5147a10e0d276f96907848e05-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4150e2d0de82d50579e2620710c30180.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11932999" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4150e2d0de82d50579e2620710c30180-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：蒜头2颗、葱段1汤匙、姜片0.5汤匙。<br />
调料：生抽、蚝油、白酒、白糖。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 荷兰豆择掉两头去筋洗净。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933001" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-11-450x293.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><br />
2. 把腊肉用热水洗干净，去掉底部肉皮。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933002" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将去皮腊肉切成薄片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933003" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 猛火阴油将腊味肉片用蒜片、姜片、葱粒爆炒至香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933004" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 加入生抽、蚝油、白酒、白糖等调料，加葱段爆香后盛出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 利用锅底余油爆炒荷兰豆。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933007" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加约2汤匙水、适量盐、香油适量，快速炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 荷兰豆炒好后捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933010" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 再利用锅底余油放入葱白段炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-1-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><br />
10. 加入荷兰豆、腊味，快速炒香即可起锅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10-2-450x292.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="292" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴味道鲜明、层次清晰，腊味甘香，荷兰豆爽脆。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11933016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
