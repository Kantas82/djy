<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【蔬食烘焙】吃不出来是素食～番茄罗勒司康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蕃茄罗勒司康（如何出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>番茄酸甜的滋味搭配微辣的罗勒，能创造出丰富的口感与香味。</p>
<p>番茄含有丰富的抗氧化物番茄红素，可以预防癌症、老化，加热煮熟后能够提升番茄红素的吸收率，所以</p>
<p>做成司康来吃，可说是能摄取到大量营养的绝佳方式。</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">食材－8个份</span></strong></h2>
<p>小番茄 200 克</p>
<p>乾罗勒 6 克</p>
<p>植物油 20 克</p>
<p>全麦<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a> 250 克</p>
<p>泡打粉 3 克</p>
<p>糖浆 10 克</p>
<p>盐 2 克</p>
<p>手粉（全麦<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a>）、装饰用豆浆适量</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">事前准备</span></strong></h2>
<p>将小番茄与罗勒叶洗干净，再把水擦干。</p>
<p>面团放入烤箱前，先以 180 度 C 预热 20 分钟。</p>
<p>在烤盘上铺<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E7%84%99.md#1">烘焙</a>纸或烤盘布。</p>
<p>先把要涂抹在面团上的豆浆及硅胶刷准备好。</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">制作面团</span></strong></h2>
<p>1-1 先用细网眼的筛网将全麦面粉筛入调理盆中，再倒入植物油，用手拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271786" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271786" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_05.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_05.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="199" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271786" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1-2 将洗净、擦干的小番茄切成四分之一，再跟泡打粉、罗勒、糖浆、盐一起倒入步骤 1-1 的调理盆中，以刮刀轻轻搅拌至看不见粉末颗粒，再用手揉成团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271787" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271787" style="width: 261px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_06.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_06.jpg" alt="" width="261" b="174" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271787" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">醒面</span></strong></h2>
<p>2-1 在工作桌上洒一点手粉，并将步骤 1-2 的面团放到工作桌上，捏成扁圆形后装进容器里，盖上盖子放入冰箱休息 30 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271793" style="width: 424px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_08.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_08.jpg" alt="" width="424" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271793" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2-2 将冰箱里的面团拿出来放到工作桌上，以刮刀切成 8 等分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12271792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_07-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271792" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">烘烤</span></strong></h2>
<p>3-1 将切成 8 等分的面团，以一定间隔放在烤盘上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271796" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271796" style="width: 355px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_09.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_09.jpg" alt="" width="355" b="237" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271796" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-2 用刷子在面团表面涂抹少量豆浆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271797" style="width: 368px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_10.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_10.jpg" alt="" width="368" b="246" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271797" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-3 放入以 180 度 C 预热好的烤箱里烤 20 至 30 分钟，再把烤好的司康取出、放到冷却网上散热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12271801" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12271801" style="width: 361px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_12.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12271801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2020_sally_food_12.jpg" alt="" width="361" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12271801" class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
