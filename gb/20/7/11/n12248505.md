<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>关闭4月后 迪斯尼城区重新开放</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_72532-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>7月9日迪斯尼城区（Downtown）重新开放，游客都戴着口罩，并保持安全距离。（李梅/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年07月11日讯】（大纪元记者李梅橙县报导）在因“<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>”（武汉肺炎）疫情关闭四个月后，7月9日<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%AA%E6%96%AF%E5%B0%BC%E5%9F%8E%E5%8C%BA.md#1">迪斯尼城区</a>（Downtown）<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E6%96%B0%E5%BC%80%E6%94%BE.md#1">重新开放</a>，许多人早上10点开门前就在检测门外排队，其中还有从拉斯维加斯来的。</p>
<p>迪斯尼公司规划了入门的路线，游客们须先经过体温和安全检查才能入内，里面多处标志提醒人们保持安全距离和戴口罩，路旁还有洗手液站台，也提醒人们至少要洗手20秒钟。<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%AA%E6%96%AF%E5%B0%BC%E5%9F%8E%E5%8C%BA.md#1">迪斯尼城区</a>第一天重开，许多穿着不同服装的工作人员分别负责安全检查、消毒、疏导和协助人们排队购物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12248527" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7247.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248527" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7247-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248527" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼市区（Downtown）重开第一天，人们先要测体温，然后等待安检。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12248536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_72512.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_72512-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248536" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼城区（Downtown）<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E6%96%B0%E5%BC%80%E6%94%BE.md#1">重新开放</a>的第一天，游客络绎不绝。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12248541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7267.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7267-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248541" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼城区（Downtown）重开第一天，La Brea面包店还没有开。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12248548" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248548" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7280.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248548" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7280-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248548" class="wp-caption-text">手工冰激凌店门庭若市。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小男孩布雷特利特怀里抱着一个玩偶，他妈妈说，进商店或买吃的要排队等待，所以他们逛了快三个小时，“人虽然多，但不拥挤”。</p>
<p>迪斯尼城区很多商家开店了，大部分都设计从一个门进，然后走另一个门出；另外，室外的饮食区也很大。星巴克咖啡店的地上画着清晰的折线和脚印，一个门是排队买单，另一个门是排队拿咖啡，以便人们最大限度地保持距离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12248566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248566" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_3113.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248566" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_3113-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248566" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼城区（Downtown）的一个室外就餐区。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12248569" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_3120.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_3120-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248569" class="wp-caption-text">JAZZ Kitchen 门前的迎客先生塑像。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不止是孩子们喜欢来迪斯尼，两位西裔的中年妇女也结伴来买印有迪斯尼65周年字样的服装；工作人员介绍说：“还有米妮耳朵、迪斯尼图案的口罩以及65周年的收藏品。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12248532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7254.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7254-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248532" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼商店重开第一天，在出售纪念65周年的收藏品。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>乐高（LEGO）景点的门口也有不少人排队，用乐高积木搭的造型很醒目，经理告诉记者，作品“五个女孩”是用了62万多片积木拼出的，“美女和野兽”大概用了一百多万块，而从屋顶到地下的“骑士战恶龙”造型则是用2百多万片拼出来的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12248515" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7356.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_7356-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248515" class="wp-caption-text">用乐高积木搭的造型很醒目，“五个女孩”是用了62万多片积木拼出的。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管迪斯尼乐园和冒险公园都没开放，人们还是走到两个公园的入口处，向里张望或照张像。迪斯尼的动画电影和主题公园深受人们喜爱，迪斯尼电影音乐也让人们兴奋，年轻人随着音乐的节奏在晃动着。游客爱丽莎有年票，以前每个月都会和朋友来几次，有时自己一个人也会来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12248562" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12248562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_73642.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12248562" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/IMG_73642-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12248562" class="wp-caption-text">迪斯尼城区（Downtown）的装饰墙画。（李梅/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近来，<ahref="https://github.com/jegvhw372/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>和周围几个县检测出的总感染人数超过了2万人，且有快速上升趋势，迪斯尼乐园和冒险公园已推迟7月19日的重开计划。一位售货员认为：“虽然天气很热，但大家都很自觉地保持距离、戴着口罩，还是比较安全的。”当和一位商店经理谈到最近的困难时期时，经理反驳说：“这是个好时候，因为我们重新开放了。”◇</p>
