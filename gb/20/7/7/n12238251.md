<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作千张馄饨 好吃内馅 酥脆外皮</h2>
<a href="https://dtfavhq5d1ym2.cloudfront.net/V3uUBabHn"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tnpikr314/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/tnpikr314/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>气炸千张馄饨，减油80%不减酥脆口感，健康又美味。（图／Ｃ2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%83%E5%BC%A0.md#1">千张</a>”是什么？它可是现在最夯的<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8E%E7%B3%96.md#1">低糖</a>圣品，是传统的薄豆腐皮，用来取代面皮可以增添豆香，提高蛋白质、减少碳水化合物的摄取。“千张<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A6%84%E9%A5%A8.md#1">馄饨</a>”市面上也是热卖品，但是价格偏贵，虽然是减糖低脂的料理，但是要省荷包的您不妨自己动手包馄饨；再运用气炸的方式，减油80%不减酥脆口感，健康又美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12240588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12240588" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/shutterstock_1701228280.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12240588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/shutterstock_1701228280-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12240588" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%83%E5%BC%A0.md#1">千张</a>是什么？其实就是薄豆腐皮。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h3>
<p>绞肉200g<br />
洋葱适量<br />
香菜1把</p>
<figure id="attachment_10451261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10451261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10451261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/Untitled-4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10451261" class="wp-caption-text">香菜能排出体内重金属。它有独特香味、也是一种草药。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【调味料】</span></h3>
<p>酱油1小匙<br />
绍兴酒1小匙<br />
香油1小匙<br />
白胡椒适量<br />
蒜泥35g<br />
糖1小匙<br />
盐适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 将香菜切碎，洋葱切丁备用。<br />
2. 取一碗，将绞肉、蒜末、白胡椒、绍兴、糖、香油、盐、酱油抓匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12238256" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12238256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4030e025cfc1a4ba745ee031d4fc2d0e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12238256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4030e025cfc1a4ba745ee031d4fc2d0e-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12238256" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 再拌入步骤1拌匀备用。<br />
4. 取千张皮，将料填入，包紧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12238257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12238257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/41ea5f375f2974174a157f751983824b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12238257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/41ea5f375f2974174a157f751983824b-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12238257" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 入气炸锅，180度8分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12238258" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12238258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d775dbfa2107a67ff1c727db2bfd81a9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12238258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d775dbfa2107a67ff1c727db2bfd81a9-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12238258" class="wp-caption-text">（图／Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
