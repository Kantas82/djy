<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>慈善机构WE花60万美元请美国顾问为哪般</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年07月31日讯】<span class="s2">（大纪元记者周行多伦多报导）</span><span class="s1">WE Charity</span><span class="s2">（简称</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">）去年花了数十万美元，在美国聘请政治咨询公司。而且</span><span class="s1">,</span><span class="s2">这正好与加拿大一家媒体对</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">做调查性报导的时间相吻合。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">《环球邮报》的报导称，根据美国国税局（</span><span class="s1">IRS</span><span class="s2">）的备案，截至</span><span class="s1">2019</span><span class="s2">年</span><span class="s1">8</span><span class="s2">月</span><span class="s1">31</span><span class="s2">日的财政年度，</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">在美国分支机构向</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">家咨询公司支付了共计</span><span class="s1">605,853</span><span class="s2">美元的费用。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">其中的</span><span class="s1">130,000</span><span class="s2">美元付给了</span><span class="s1">Firehouse Strategies</span><span class="s2">，该公司是由</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s2">名在</span><span class="s1">2018</span><span class="s2">年支持鲁比奥（</span><span class="s1">Marco Rubio</span><span class="s2">）总统竞选活动的人创立，他们是：当时的竞选经理沙利文（</span><span class="s1">Terry Sullivan</span><span class="s2">）、传讯总监科南特（</span><span class="s1">Alex Conant</span><span class="s2">）及战略首席顾问霍利（</span><span class="s1">Will Holley</span><span class="s2">）。按该公司网站介绍，其业务是“提供全方位服务的公共事务公司，致力于提供有针对性的说服运动”。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">该公司的合伙人特里尔（</span><span class="s1">Matt Terrill</span><span class="s2">）称，他们的工作重点是支持</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">的</span><span class="s1">WE Day</span><span class="s2">活动，尤其是在美国主要地区的活动，包括华盛顿特区。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">据《环球邮报》报导，两名来自与</span><span class="s1">Firehouse Strategies</span><span class="s2">创办者同一政治阵营的人士，在</span><span class="s1">2018</span><span class="s2">年和</span><span class="s1">2019</span><span class="s2">年撰写了一系列文章，试图贬低加拿大新闻媒体</span><span class="s1">Canadaland</span><span class="s2">，该媒体在过去</span><span class="s1">2</span><span class="s2">年，针对</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">的项目发表了调查性报导，主要关注童工及</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">在海外的发展问题。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">去年，在</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">与</span><span class="s1">Firehouse</span><span class="s2">的合同曝光之前，</span><span class="s1">Canadaland</span><span class="s2">曾质疑为什么美国同一个阵营的顾问撰写文章攻击该新闻媒体，并质疑那可能是有组织的行动。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">与另外</span><span class="s1">2</span><span class="s2">家咨询公司的交易是，向</span><span class="s1">202 Strategies</span><span class="s2">支付了</span><span class="s1">297,570</span><span class="s2">美元，该公司在其网站上称，其业务是制定政治、媒体和传播策略；向</span><span class="s1">David Baum and Associates</span><span class="s2">支付了</span><span class="s1">178,283</span><span class="s2">美元，</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">是这家公司的一名长期顾客。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">《环邮》的报导称，</span><span class="s1">WE</span><span class="s2">没有回答他们为何要聘请这些顾问公司的问题。不过，</span><span class="s1">Firehouse</span><span class="s2">的创办者之一沙利文先生在</span><span class="s1">2019</span><span class="s2">年</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s2">月的播客中，说到一种宣传策略，就是先弄清对手，然后写文章贬低他们。</span></p>
<p class="p2"><span class="s2">Canadaland</span><span class="s3">的出版商布朗（</span><span class="s2">Jesse Brown</span><span class="s3">）表示，他想知道</span><span class="s2">Firehouse</span><span class="s3">是否对</span><span class="s2">Canadaland</span><span class="s3">采取了这些战术。“</span><span class="s2">Canadaland</span><span class="s3">遭隐蔽攻击的方式，正是</span><span class="s2">Firehouse Strategies</span><span class="s3">籍以出名的行动方式”。</span><span class="s2">WE</span><span class="s3">需要说明，它为何用慈善资金来聘用这样的公司。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">责任编辑：岳怡</span></p>
