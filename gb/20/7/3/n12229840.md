<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哪个才是我阿嬷？ 台3万网友笑翻按赞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/qqq5756015847137_4646032361848899866_o-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嘉义网友分享两位阿嬷撞衫照，笑翻网友。（爆废公社二馆）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年07月03日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89.md#1">嘉义</a>一名女子在脸书发文，日前载<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%AC%B7.md#1">阿嬷</a>出门买东西，当时阿嬷向另一位年长女性问路，意外发现两人<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%92%9E%E8%A1%AB.md#1">撞衫</a>又撞脸，她笑翻发文后吸引网友留言，才知道两位阿嬷的孙子还是同学！一天内就吸引逾3万网友按赞。</p>
<p>女网友在脸书社团“<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%86%E5%BA%9F%E5%85%AC%E7%A4%BE.md#1">爆废公社</a>二馆”发文，她29日载<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%AC%B7.md#1">阿嬷</a>出门买东西，“我要笑死了，我经历了什么，载阿嬷出来买东西，然后随便找一个路边的阿嬷问个路，结果她们在报路讲话讲到一半，我车停下来才突然发现她们<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%92%9E%E8%A1%AB.md#1">撞衫</a>。”</p>
<p>从照片中可见，两位阿嬷头发灰白，身上穿着同款的紫色菱格无袖上衣。女网友笑说：“然后她们也没发现，我突然大笑跟她们说她们才发现。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12229930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12229930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/rrr1_1045755972513808_8681300101450353480_o.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12229930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/rrr1_1045755972513808_8681300101450353480_o-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12229930" class="wp-caption-text">两位阿嬷穿着同款的紫色上衣。<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%86%E5%BA%9F%E5%85%AC%E7%A4%BE.md#1">爆废公社</a>二馆）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更巧的是，该名女网友将两位阿嬷撞衫的照片上传后，钓出一名网友立刻留言认亲说：“左边是我阿嬷。”原来，另一位阿嬷的孙子，竟然是发文女网友的姊姊的国中同学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12230017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12230017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/photo_2020-07-03_17-17-58.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12230017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/photo_2020-07-03_17-17-58-600x1074.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1074" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12230017" class="wp-caption-text">（爆废公社二馆）</figcaption></figure>
<p>众多网友纷纷直呼两位阿嬷仿佛是<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E8%83%9E%E8%83%8E.md#1">双胞胎</a>，“上天注定的缘分，在一起在一起”、“世界真是太小了”、“这是阿嬷界的时尚款”、“没想到这么妙的事情，发生在我们这的菜市场”、“欸欸欸欸这件我婆婆也有”，短短一天就吸引超过3万网友涌入按赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12230029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12230029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/photo_2020-07-03_17-18-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12230029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/photo_2020-07-03_17-18-06-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12230029" class="wp-caption-text">（爆废公社二馆）</figcaption></figure>
