<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】豆豉鲮鱼油麦菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/c1177efa38741b69e630911de5de3e18-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%B1%89%E9%B2%AE%E9%B1%BC%E6%B2%B9%E9%BA%A6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">豆豉鲮鱼油麦菜</a>是用豆豉鲮鱼、油麦菜制作的一道色、香、味俱全的传统粤菜。</p>
<p>油麦菜含有大量维生素A、B1、B2、钙、铁、蛋白质、脂肪等营养成分，具有降低胆固醇、治疗神经衰弱、清燥润肺、化痰止咳等功效，是一种低热量、高营养的蔬菜，有“凤尾”之称。</p>
<p>鲮鱼肉质细嫩、味道鲜美，富含丰富的蛋白质、维生素A、钙、镁、硒等营养元素，具有益气血、健筋骨、通小便之功效。鲮鱼味美，但鱼刺多，通常被制成鱼丸或罐头食品，“豆豉鲮鱼”就是常见的鲮鱼罐头食品。</p>
<p>豆豉是中国传统发酵豆制品，鲜美可口、香气独特，含有丰富的蛋白质、多种氨基酸及多种矿物质和维生素等营养物质。在日本传统制法的豆豉，叫做“寺纳豆”或“盐辛纳豆”，是日本医学界“推荐食品”之第一名。</p>
<p>中医学认为豆豉性平，可治感冒头痛、胸闷烦呕、伤寒寒热及食物中毒等病症。</p>
<p>关于豆豉，历史上还有这样一个传说。留名千古的《滕王阁序》完成后，连日宴请，王勃前来辞行。适逢主人阎都督浑身发冷，骨节酸痛，胸中烦闷，咳喘不已，经十多位名医诊治，都主张以麻黄为君药。谁知，他坚决不同意，并说：“麻黄峻利之药，岂能乱用，况且我已年迈，汗出津少，用发汗之药，就同釜底添薪，不可，不可！”</p>
<p>王勃便拿出自己刚从河旁老翁处买来的豆豉，劝其服下，阎都督连服三天，果真见效：汗出喘止，胸闷顿减，几天后就痊愈了。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%B1%89%E9%B2%AE%E9%B1%BC%E6%B2%B9%E9%BA%A6%E8%8F%9C.md#1">豆豉鲮鱼油麦菜</a></h2>
<p>主料：油麦菜5棵、豆豉2汤匙，鲮鱼2条切小块。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/26419dfeaa07c605643b2ad03c972468.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/26419dfeaa07c605643b2ad03c972468-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a></p>
<p>配料：姜末、蒜末、红辣椒末、葱白、葱叶。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/f66bbdd6c92105a02b094d62a4730915.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/f66bbdd6c92105a02b094d62a4730915-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><br />
调料：生抽、蚝油、香油、自制红油、蜂蜜、白芝麻、辣椒酱、老抽、盐、糖。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 姜、蒜切末，红辣椒切粒，葱白、葱叶切段后装盘。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001014" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-7-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><br />
2. 将以上配料用热油淋至香浓。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 放入蜂蜜、豆豉、鲮鱼和自制红油。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 放入蜂蜜、辣椒酱和白芝麻。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 将所有调料充分搅拌调制成酱料。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 开水中放生抽、盐和糖。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 把已洗净的5棵油麦菜放入开水中烫熟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将油麦菜捞出切断。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将切段后的油麦菜整齐摆放在盘中。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 淋上调好的酱料。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴鲜辣开胃，爽脆味美。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
