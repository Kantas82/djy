<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】 动感牛肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/b397312e5c88979c5b1afbffcc5da535-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>牛肉的营养价值很高，自古就有“牛肉补气，功同黄芪”之说。牛肉性味甘平、味道鲜美，还享有“肉中骄子”的美称。</p>
<p>牛肉蛋白质含量高，而且包含全部种类的氨基酸，各种氨基酸的比例与人体蛋白质中各种氨基酸的比例基本一致，其中所含的肌氨酸比任何食物都高。</p>
<p>牛肉的脂肪含量很低，但它却是低脂的亚油酸的来源，还是潜在的抗氧化剂。牛肉还含有矿物质和维他命B群，包括烟酸、维生素B1和核黄素。牛肉还是人体每天所需要的铁质的最佳来源。</p>
<p>胡萝卜含有大量胡萝卜素、纤维素等，同时还含有木质素，可抗过敏、抗氧化，提高人体免疫力，延缓衰老。西芹性凉、味甘，可以清热解毒、养血补虚，是很好的减肥食品。洋葱不仅富含丰富的营养素，更有两种特殊的营养物质——槲皮素和前列腺素A，可杀菌、抗毒，维护心血管健康，预防癌症。</p>
<p>胡萝卜、洋葱和西芹三种食材的组合就是天然的味精。胡萝卜可以让汤汁变红，洋葱若是先炒至焦糖色，也可以上色，甚至可以提香、提味，而西芹用来熬汤，可以祛除肉里面的腥味。</p>
<p>这道<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E6%84%9F%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">动感牛肉</a>是一款风味家常菜，香浓爽脆，略带酒香和黑胡椒的香辣，铁板的热量令汤汁更香浓，同时铁板的保温作用也令牛肉和配料本身的味道更突出。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E6%84%9F%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">动感牛肉</a></h2>
<p>主料：牛肉2磅。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/0f2bae0c5bc8e3eb302b54381bb62979.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/0f2bae0c5bc8e3eb302b54381bb62979-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：胡萝卜、西芹、红葱头、甜豆、姜、葱。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/b5b8061f9caaf0da5681760170bb01bc.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/b5b8061f9caaf0da5681760170bb01bc-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料：木鱼花（柴鱼片）、生抽、生粉、蚝油、老抽、白酒。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 先将牛肉洗净沥干，顺横纹切成薄片。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 放入生抽、生粉腌制，再加入沙拉油拌匀待用。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001107" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 姜、蒜切片，葱切丁，甜豆去根，红葱头切丁，胡萝卜、芹菜切粒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 将胡萝卜丁、西芹丁、甜豆用热水分别烫过，五成熟即可，捞起。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 猛火阴油，把腌过半小时的牛肉片猛火煸炒，四成熟即取出。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001113" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 利用锅底余油，将姜片、葱丁、蒜片、红葱头炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001116" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 放入黑胡椒粉、老抽，炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 放入牛肉，添加蚝油、老抽、白酒爆香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 放入热水烫过的红萝卜粒、西芹粒、甜豆，一起炒香即可。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 将炒好的菜肴放在热的铁板之上。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001121" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 最后撒上木鱼花。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12001122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
