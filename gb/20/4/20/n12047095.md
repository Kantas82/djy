<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】腊味煲仔饭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/05ab7df540a2a17fc7d56d3d362d83d1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美味的煲仔饭色、香、味俱全。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“煲仔饭”源自广州，是以砂锅作为器皿煮米饭，而广东称“砂锅”为“煲仔”，故称“煲仔饭”。煲仔饭的历史渊源可以追溯到两千多年前的中原地区，据《礼记注疏》等书记载，“周代八珍”中的第一珍、第二珍和煲仔饭做法一样，只不过改用黄米作原料罢了，可见在当时是很名贵的。</p>
<p>制作煲仔饭，通常把淘好的米放入煲中，量好水量，加盖，把米饭煲至七成熟时加入配料，再转用慢火煲熟。</p>
<p>用瓦做成的“煲”，在火候控制方面比较灵活，煲出来的饭也较为香口，齿间留香，回味无穷。吃煲仔饭时，记得尝尝饭焦的味道，又香又脆，把热气的饭倒在汤里泡一会儿再吃一样可口。</p>
<p>煲仔饭的用料灵活多样，可因时、因地而异。罗厨制作的这道腊味煲仔饭，选用腊肠、带壳鲜虾和香菇等为配料，兼顾美味和营养。米饭煮好后，腊味与鲜虾的香气和营养完全融入其中。</p>
<p>腊肠放在米饭上煮熟后， 充满腊味气味的油脂便流出来，使米饭带有独特的香味。香菇是低热量、高蛋白、高纤维食物，其所含糖类比米或薯类等少。香菇还含有多量优质氨基酸，其它维生素类也颇多，尤其维生素D的前体麦角固醇含量颇多。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，煮糯米饭时，米与水的比例是3:2，要大火煮开后，然后慢火煮熟。当听到米饭发出吱吱的声音时，就快好了，因为这时水快干了。添加配料时，鲜虾要放在最上面，当闻到饭的焦香和腊味的香气时，就可以开盖了。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%A3%E5%91%B3%E7%85%B2%E4%BB%94%E9%A5%AD.md#1">辣味煲仔饭</a></h2>
<p>主料：腊肠3根、糯米3杯。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/794637940eccd93ad8bb5aeac3e494a1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/794637940eccd93ad8bb5aeac3e494a1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6b1c823e741843f5b3338f2b4a7f4633.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047135" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6b1c823e741843f5b3338f2b4a7f4633-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：带壳鲜虾、元葱、香菇、姜丝、葱段、葱粒。</p>
<p>调料：美极鲜酱、蚝油、白酒、生抽。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将3根腊肠切小片备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-57.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-57-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 元葱切小片，姜切小片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-41.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047137" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-41-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 糯米洗净泡2小时后煮开，然后慢火煮15分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-36.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-36-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 将香菇、元葱等配料用美极鲜酱腌制。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 再放入1汤匙白酒和半汤匙蚝油搅拌均匀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 将带壳鲜虾用生抽腌制。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047143" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 将腌好的配料放在蒸熟的糯米饭上面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将6个鲜虾放在食材的最上面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 加盖慢火煮5分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 闻到香气后开盖放入葱粒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-22.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12047150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-22-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 美味的煲仔饭色、香、味俱全。</p>
