<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>6个超简单动作练核心肌群 在家就能做</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>核心训练是非常重要的训练。6个简单动作训练核心肌群，在家徒手就能做。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>【编者按】</strong>在<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%A5%E8%BA%AB.md#1">健身</a><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a>中，<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E5%BF%83%E8%AE%AD%E7%BB%83.md#1">核心训练</a>是非常重要的训练。<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E5%BF%83%E8%82%8C%E7%BE%A4.md#1">核心肌群</a>力量足，能帮助身体找回重心，改善不正确姿势，还可减去腹部的赘肉。A级体能教练马歇尔．道尔（Marcel Doll）教你6个简单的核心训练动作，在家徒手就能做！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">6个超简单的<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E5%BF%83%E8%AE%AD%E7%BB%83.md#1">核心训练</a>动作，在家就能做</span></h2>
<h3>一、四足跪姿抬手</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065753" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-111.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-111-600x230.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之一：四足跪姿抬手。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065753" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之一：四足跪姿抬手。（商周出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 身体呈四足跪姿，双手在肩膀下方。膝盖在臀部后方，脚尖踩稳。</p>
<p>2. 左右手轮流往上抬，感受上半身拉伸。举起的手拇指朝上。维持这个姿势5秒。</p>
<h3>二、平板超人</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-222.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-222-600x230.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之二：平板超人。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065754" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之二：平板超人。（商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 双手双脚与肩膀同宽撑住身体。骨盆与上半身、大腿呈一直线。</p>
<p>2. 将对角的一只手与一只脚抬离地面。维持这个姿势，然后换边。</p>
<h3>三、<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E5%BC%8F.md#1">棒式</a></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065755" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-333.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-333-600x230.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之三：棒式。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065755" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之三：<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A3%92%E5%BC%8F.md#1">棒式</a>。（商周出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前臂撑地，手肘在肩膀下方，骨盆与上半身和大腿呈一直线。绷紧<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E5%BF%83%E8%82%8C%E7%BE%A4.md#1">核心肌群</a>，让脊柱保持稳定。维持这个姿势。</p>
<h3>四、棒式抬腿</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-4-600x168.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之四：棒式抬腿。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="168" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065697" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之四：棒式抬腿。（商周出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用前臂支撑身体，手肘位置在肩膀正下方。骨盆与上半身和大腿呈一直线。左右脚轮流抬离地面。抬起后，维持姿势2至3秒再换边。</p>
<h3>五、滑板登山者</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-5-600x230.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之五：滑板登山者。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065698" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之五：滑板登山者。（商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 撑地姿势，双手与肩膀同宽，双脚与髋关节同宽。两脚下方各垫一块小毛巾。手腕位置在肩关节正下方，收紧核心与臀部，全身呈一直线。</p>
<p>2. 轮流不断地将左右膝盖拉往胸部方向。</p>
<h3>六、俯卧游泳</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12065699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12065699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12065699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Core-training-szbook-6-600x230.jpg" alt="核心训练动作之六：俯卧游泳。（商周出版提供）" width="600" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12065699" class="wp-caption-text">核心训练动作之六：俯卧游泳。（商周出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1. 身体俯卧，手臂向前伸直过头。</p>
<p>2. 轮流抬起一只手和对侧的脚。抬起的手脚都要离开地面。</p>
