<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】炒鱼面配雪菜牛肉～湖北特色小吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>炒鱼面是传统的湖北小吃，搭配雪菜牛肉香气四溢，口感脆嫩增添酸咸滋味，入口回味无穷。（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>【小编语】</h3>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>”源自湖北省云梦县的特产，制作方法是采用面粉、草鱼等鱼肉混制而成的食品，在超市或商场可以买到急冻包，烹调方式相当多元可以炒、炸、煮；因为营养易消化，有长寿面的美誉。“炒<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8F%9C.md#1">雪菜</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>”本身是一道家常菜；雪菜是一年四季都送欢迎的蔬菜，具有通肠润便的作用，可以单独炒成脆嫩菜肴，也可以腌渍成酸咸味的常备食材。</p>
<p>这一道“炒<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>配<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E8%8F%9C.md#1">雪菜</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>”分成两部分烹煮，炒鱼面和炒雪菜牛肉，如果没有鱼面单是雪菜牛肉也非常下饭，很促进食欲。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027531" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-17-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027531" class="wp-caption-text">炒鱼面配雪菜牛肉（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>急冻鱼面 1包<br />
雪菜粒 4oz<br />
牛绞肉 6oz<br />
红洋葱粒 oz<br />
姜蒜粒 少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-36.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12027158 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-36-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027158" class="wp-caption-text">材料：牛绞肉、雪菜粒、姜蒜粒、红洋葱粒 （图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【腌料】</h3>
<p>生抽、糖、胡椒粉各适量<br />
粟粉加少许水拌匀</p>
<h3>【调味】</h3>
<p>蚝油1茶匙<br />
糖1茶匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉1茶匙开水勾芡用<br />
水1杯</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-28-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027159" class="wp-caption-text">调味：粟粉用开水勾芡、麻油、胡椒粉、糖、蚝油。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 雪菜先用清水浸约15分钟，洗干净捞出雪菜，沥干水分备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027157" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027157" class="wp-caption-text">雪菜先用清水浸约15分钟（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将所有腌料混合搅拌，牛绞肉加入混合的腌料腌约10分钟备用，最后加少许生油拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027172" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/73950476e4c29dc6b0959c2f13b469c5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/73950476e4c29dc6b0959c2f13b469c5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027172" class="wp-caption-text">牛绞肉加入混合的腌料腌约10分钟，最后加少许生油拌匀。 （图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 热镬下油，先把腌过的牛绞肉炒至七成熟，再加入姜蒜和洋葱粒炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027173" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027173" class="wp-caption-text">牛绞肉炒至七成熟，再加入姜、蒜和洋葱粒炒香。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着放入雪菜炒匀后，加入清水及调味料煮滚，用粟粉水勾芡后盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027182" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027182" class="wp-caption-text">放入雪菜炒匀后，加入清水及调味料煮滚，再用粟粉水勾芡后盛出备用。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 鱼面用滚水煮约2分钟，捞出沥干。热镬下油，把鱼面放进翻炒至有少许金黄色，加入一茶匙生抽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027183" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027183" class="wp-caption-text">鱼面先用滚水煮约2分钟，沥干后再热镬下油翻炒至少许金黄色，最后加一茶匙生抽。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将鱼面炒香之后盛在碟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027184" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027184" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027184" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-7-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027184" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 把之前煮好的雪菜牛肉淋在鱼面上便可食用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027170" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12027170" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027170" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-7-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12027170" class="wp-caption-text">把雪菜牛肉淋在鱼面就完成了！（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
