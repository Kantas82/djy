<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>5种适合养在厨房的植物 不只净化空气 还吸油烟</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/af98a72e652675470ec28065178f2251-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>厨房摆着绿色盆栽不仅美观还有净化空气的作用。（Riverview Homes,Ine, Wikimedia commons）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年04月14日讯】（大纪元记者嫣华综合报导）居家杂志中一些感觉很舒爽的厨房，经常可以看到其中妆点着美丽的绿色植物。原来这不仅仅是为了良好的视觉效果而已，绿色植栽带来的好处可能是我们不曾注意到的。</p>
<p>厨房负责一家人的生计，特别是东方人（亚洲人）的厨房，因为烹饪习惯的关系，更是容易制造很多油烟。而有些绿色植栽却有吸收油烟、清洁剂等有害化学物质的作用，以下介绍5种适合养在厨房的绿色植物。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1.?</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%B8%E6%98%A5%E8%97%A4.md#1">常春藤</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12029659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029659" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/aea18d76a953ac55a3291146c2efda73.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/aea18d76a953ac55a3291146c2efda73-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029659" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%B8%E6%98%A5%E8%97%A4.md#1">常春藤</a><ahref="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Hedera_helix_Leaves_3008px.jpg">Derek Ramsey Ram-Man, Wikimedia commons</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>常春藤（Ivy Green 学名：Hedera helix），是常春藤的一种常绿攀援植物。在欧美地区，常春藤常见于攀爬在围墙、老建筑物墙面上，做为绿墙之用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12029664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/fd91b98e5f787e8d22510e2cb332e073.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/fd91b98e5f787e8d22510e2cb332e073-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029664" class="wp-caption-text">攀爬在墙面上的常春藤。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秀气娇小的常春藤叶片可吸收如甲醛等有害物质，还能去除霉菌，能有效净化室内空气。</p>
<p>? 请务必注意：常春藤为五加科属有毒性植物，其果实、种子和叶子都有毒，汁液有可能引发皮肤过敏；孩童误食会引起腹痛、腹泻等症状，种植于室内最好放置在儿童和竉物碰触不到的地方，手不小心碰触植栽时也请务必洗手。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2.?</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%B9%A4%E8%8A%8B.md#1">白鹤芋</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12029669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029669" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4eefada86e2f941ab899ada76f907995.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4eefada86e2f941ab899ada76f907995-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029669" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E9%B9%A4%E8%8A%8B.md#1">白鹤芋</a>盆栽。（Jerzy Opiola, Wikimedia commons）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白鹤芋(Spathiphyllum kochii)为天南星科白鹤某属下的一种，多年生草本植物，又称白掌，白鹤芋原产于中南美洲等热带雨林中。白鹤芋花色洁白，叶片翠绿，清新幽雅的姿态使它成为普受喜爱的观赏植物，常做为盆栽观赏。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12029674" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029674" style="width: 576px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7005e5f19192796dbd262d2b3d928983.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12029674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7005e5f19192796dbd262d2b3d928983.jpg" alt="" width="576" b="768" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029674" class="wp-caption-text">白鹤芋的花序。<ahref="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:%E7%99%BD%E9%B6%B4%E8%8A%8B_20190730184305.jpg">Ping an Chang, Wikimedia commons</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>适应性强的白鹤芋好种、好养，适合室内盆栽，它具有过滤室内废气的功能，如氨气、丙酮、苯和甲醛都有一定功效。</p>
<p>? 请务必注意：天南星科植物都具有毒性，不可误食，最好放置在儿童和竉物碰触不到的地方，手不小心碰触植栽时也请务必洗手。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3.?</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E8%8D%9F.md#1">芦荟</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12029676" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029676" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/919afa4cbb88a3f6c948208341bdba89.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/919afa4cbb88a3f6c948208341bdba89-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029676" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%A6%E8%8D%9F.md#1">芦荟</a>。（Pikrepo）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芦荟（学名：Aloe）很多人因为它的农业和医药用途而种植，也由于它很容易栽种，又具观赏植物，广受大众喜爱。叶片中的芦荟胶对皮肤具有保湿、抗老、减少发炎的作用。另外，芦荟也能够吸收空气中的甲醛等有毒物质。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>4.?</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E8%90%9D.md#1">绿萝</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12029681" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2294db0a8f7784c234a4ac42f91c48a2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2294db0a8f7784c234a4ac42f91c48a2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029681" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E8%90%9D.md#1">绿萝</a>。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绿萝（Golden pothos学名：Aloe）是相当容易栽种的植物，也很容易照顾，甚至养在水瓶里也可以活得很好。美国园艺学会期刊曾刊登在一项研究中发现，绿萝有很好的吸收臭氧的作用；它也可以吸收空气中的甲醛，对净化空气起到良好作用。</p>
<h3><strong>5.?</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E7%AC%BC%E8%8D%89.md#1">猪笼草</a> </strong></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12029683" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12029683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5b17ad4c3244674b26500e83893e39c2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12029683" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5b17ad4c3244674b26500e83893e39c2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12029683" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ybgvzf3286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E7%AC%BC%E8%8D%89.md#1">猪笼草</a>。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>猪笼草（学名：Nepenthes）最让人们耳熟能详的是它具有捕食昆虫的功能，厨房中五味俱全，难免吸引很多小昆虫聚集，放一盆猪笼草应该是不错的选择；不过听说它不太好照顾，想要种植的话，需要事先了解一下它生长习性。</p>
