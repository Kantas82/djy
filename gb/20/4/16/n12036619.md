<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩瑜感谢护理人员 笑曝叫外送胖了3公斤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004161012461470-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩瑜2020年4月16日在台北出席代言新品发表与签约记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年04月16日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）女星<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E7%91%9C.md#1">韩瑜</a>今天（16日）出席代言记者会，她笑说最近为了<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>宅在家，常叫<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%96%E9%80%81.md#1">外送</a>美食、逛网购，窝在沙发上导致胖了3公斤。而为了感谢全国的护理人员，她与代言品牌也捐赠美容饮品与保湿喷雾等千瓶防疫用品，祈求这次扩及全球的“中共病毒”（武汉肺炎）疫情能尽快平息。</p>
<p>久未公开露面，<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E7%91%9C.md#1">韩瑜</a>以好气色亮相，她表示过往总是忙碌的拍戏，这段时间给自己一小段时间让心灵沉淀，以轻松慢活的态度生活。</p>
<p>驻颜有术的韩瑜聊起自己如何保养的方法，就是重饮食均衡、运动保持好的体态，节制生活习惯，并表示：“真的不能什么都不做啦，要对抗时间跟地心引力很不简单。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12036722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12036722" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004161012431470-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004161012431470-ss1.jpg" alt="韩瑜" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12036722" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12036722" class="wp-caption-text">韩瑜感谢全国的护理人员。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12036717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12036717" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160454162122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160454162122-ss1.jpg" alt="韩瑜" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12036717" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12036717" class="wp-caption-text">韩瑜以好气色亮相记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩瑜也希望疫情能尽快退去，想约好友们去喜欢的餐厅尽情享美食。她最近因为鲜少出门、饮食多叫外卖，还每天窝在沙发，自曝胖了3公斤；她这次拍摄新的代言广告前，还赶紧瘦身。</p>
<p>目前韩瑜也为新戏筹备中，她还希望未来能演出不同于以往的反派角色，给自己一个挑战，她不担心被粉丝骂，“因为只敢在戏中耍坏！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12036724" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12036724" style="width: 396px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160453412122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160453412122-ss1.jpg" alt="韩瑜" width="450" b="595" class="size-full wp-image-12036724" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12036724" class="wp-caption-text">韩瑜希望疫情能尽快退去。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12036726" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12036726" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160454002122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2004160454002122-ss1.jpg" alt="韩瑜" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12036726" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12036726" class="wp-caption-text">韩瑜自曝宅在家<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>胖了3公斤。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
