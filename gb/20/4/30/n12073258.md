<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李玖哲宅在日本家 穿防疫装备外出为岳父母买菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1712140706592384-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>歌手李玖哲资料照。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年04月30日讯】（大纪元记者杨逸帆报导）近来因“中共病毒”（武汉肺炎）疫情几乎都待在日本家中的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%8E%96%E5%93%B2.md#1">李玖哲</a>（Nicky Lee），虽然上周才现身在《宅在家SP音乐祭 Vol.1 powered by 17LIVE》；但是谈到这段日子里，Nicky除了在家中健身、汲取音乐创作灵感外，还不时下厨担任“煮夫”，在朋友们眼中是非常“宠妻”的人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12073330" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12073330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-002.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12073330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-002-600x901.jpg" alt="李玖哲" width="450" b="901" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12073330" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E7%8E%96%E5%93%B2.md#1">李玖哲</a>在家中健身。（由你娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Nicky表示前些日子因为作品、演唱会忙碌得不可开交，但这阵子他学会安排自己难得空闲的时间，除了每日在家健身及创作，也让他增加了不少与妻子<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B8%E9%A9%AC%E8%8C%9C.md#1">相马茜</a>相处的机会。</p>
<p>十分“疼妻”的Nicky，因为担心家人外出会有接触病毒的危机，担纲起家中外出采买的重责大任，每天口罩、帽子全副武装，更因为担心搭电梯会增加风险，每天提着大包小包爬10几层楼梯。</p>
<p>此外，Nicky也忧心年纪已长的岳父、岳母外出接触人群会有危险，也常将买好的菜放在岳父母家门前，相当贴心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12073321" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12073321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-001.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12073321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-001-600x800.jpeg" alt="李玖哲" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12073321" class="wp-caption-text">李玖哲外出的<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E7%96%AB.md#1">防疫</a>穿着。（由你娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12073322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12073322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-004.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12073322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-004-600x1238.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="1238" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12073322" class="wp-caption-text">李玖哲将买好的东西放在岳父母家门前。（由你娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>日文不好的Nicky，也常因语言不通闹笑话，妻子<ahref="https://github.com/zogmns3550/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%B8%E9%A9%AC%E8%8C%9C.md#1">相马茜</a>透露Nicky因为只看得懂日文的汉字，所以常常买了一堆用不到的东西回家，像只要看到路边有人排队限购清洁或民生用品，他便不由分说地加入队伍；Nicky尤其对“%数”特别敏感，曾经买过“99%纯水湿纸巾”、“99.99%除菌厕所清洁剂”等用品，兴高采烈带回家后却让妻子好气又好笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12073324" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12073324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12073324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-005-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12073324" class="wp-caption-text">日本当地的排队人潮。（由你娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12073325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12073325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-006.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12073325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/20200430-Nicky-Lee-006-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12073325" class="wp-caption-text">李玖哲采买回家的战利品。（由你娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妻子也在一旁用照片悄悄记录下Nicky每天外出的状态，自然且有趣的日常让大家看了都忍不住会心一笑。而有一阵子未与乐迷们见面的Nicky，也表示希望歌迷们保重身体，相信很快之后就能与粉丝们见面。</p>
