<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>因中共病毒亚省3月失去117,000个工作</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/termination-110302_1280-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（Gerd Altmann / Pixabay）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年04月10日讯】（<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">记者陈安综合报导）</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">9</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日（周四），加拿大统计局发布数据，亚省</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月份损失了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">117,100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个工作岗位，全国失去超过</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">100</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万个岗位。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">这些新数据反映了中共病毒（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）对经济造成的损失。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省失业全国第四</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">这是亚省有记录以来最严重的单月变化。该省的总体失业率从</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月份的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">7.2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％升至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月份的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">8.7</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％，为全国第四高的省份。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月失去的岗位中，全职工作</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">43,500</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个，占</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">37%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，兼职工作</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">73,600</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个，占</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">63%</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。与</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2019</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">年</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月同期比较，就业人数减少了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">118,400</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，失业人数增加</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">38,900</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">与去年同期相比，私营部门失业人数为</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">95,300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。公共部门</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25,300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，而自雇就业增加</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2,200</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">全省失业集中在住宿和食品服务（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">-27.9</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％）、信息、文化和娱乐（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">-25.8</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％）以及批发和零售贸易（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">-8.1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％）等方面。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁以上的人，就业人数下降了约</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">由于许多年轻人在服务业工作，因此，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁以下的年轻人，特别是年轻妇女的失业更为显着。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">根据加拿大统计局的数据，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月份，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁以下工作人口跌至大约</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">23.1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万，下降了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">18.9</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％。年轻女工下降幅度更大，达到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">23.4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％，大约</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">34,000</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">本周初，亚省省长康尼表示，他预计亚省的失业率将达到至少</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％，相当于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万失业者。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">目前尚不清楚联邦政府的工资补贴计划会产生什么影响。联邦政府的工资补贴计划为雇主提供了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">75</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％的工资补贴，以鼓励他们留住或重新雇用员工。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例如，总部位于卡尔加里的西捷航空（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">WestJet</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）本周表示，它将使用工资补贴计划重新雇用本月初被解雇的近</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">6,400</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名工人。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">全国“最黑暗的季度”</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">全国平均失业水平从</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">5.6</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％升至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">7.8</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">加拿大皇家银行表示，这可能是加拿大经济“有史以来最黑暗的一个季度”。“由于本季度国内生产总值下降和失业率达到前所未有的水平，可能很难发现一线希望。”“但是到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2021</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">年，低利率和被压抑的需求将使增长恢复正常。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">加拿大统计局重新调整了一些计算方法，分为失业人数和“不在劳动力中”的人数，以更好地衡量</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">对就业市场的影响。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">在全国范围内，失业人数在</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月之间增加至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">413,000</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，这几乎全部是由<ahref="https://github.com/yiebnr363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B4%E6%97%B6%E8%A3%81%E5%91%98.md#1">临时裁员</a>导致的，这意味着工人预计在</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">6</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个月内回来工作。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">完全没有工作的人数增加至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">130</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万，而不到正常工作时间的一半的人增加至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">80</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万。上个月约有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">310</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万加拿大人失业或工作时间减少。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">加拿大统计局警告说，整周缺勤而无薪的人数（经季节性调整后达到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">55.8</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％），“可能表示未来失业。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该机构表示，“预计</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月份出现的就业突然下降将对未来几个月的加拿大经济表现产生重大影响。”这些变化都可以归因于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">大流行。</span></p>
