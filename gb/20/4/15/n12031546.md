<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>女性万用穴道！3招按摩 改善更年期、经期症状</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Menopause2_1313449220-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>女性生理痛、经期不顺等经期症状，可通过按摩三阴交穴来改善。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E9%98%B4%E4%BA%A4%E7%A9%B4.md#1">三阴交穴</a>是“<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7.md#1">女性</a>万用穴道”，能改善生理痛、<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%9C%9F.md#1">经期</a>不顺等经期<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%97%87%E7%8A%B6.md#1">症状</a>，还可以缓解<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%B4%E5%B9%B4%E6%9C%9F.md#1">更年期</a>问题。平时怎样按摩三阴交穴？</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">有效应付生理期等妇女病，是<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7.md#1">女性</a>的万用穴道</span></h2>
<p>位于小腿肚的<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E9%98%B4%E4%BA%A4%E7%A9%B4.md#1">三阴交穴</a>，顾名思义，为三条阴经络的相交处。</p>
<p>这是属于生殖系统疾病的穴道，以女性来说，与生理不顺、子宫内膜异位及<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9B%B4%E5%B9%B4%E6%9C%9F.md#1">更年期</a>等妇女病<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%97%87%E7%8A%B6.md#1">症状</a>息息相关，更被称为“妇科三阴交”，对于女性相当重要。</p>
<p>另外，此穴道也能改善食欲不振、腹泻、消化不良及胀气等肠胃症状。主要以指压和按摩刺激双脚的穴道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12031581" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12031581" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12031581 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-2-600x264.jpg" alt="位于小腿肚的三阴交穴，为三条阴经络的相交处，可改善经期和更年期症状。（和平国际）" width="600" b="264" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12031581" class="wp-caption-text">位于小腿肚的三阴交穴，为三条阴经络的相交处，可改善<ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%9C%9F.md#1">经期</a>和更年期症状。（和平国际）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>三阴交穴主要功效：</strong>生理痛、生理不顺、手脚冰冷、胃痛、腹泻和更年期症状等。</p>
<p><strong>穴道位置：</strong>小腿内侧，距离脚踝往上约四指（食指至小指）宽处。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">三阴交穴的按摩方法</span></h2>
<p><strong>1. 拇指指压</strong></p>
<p>坐在地板脚往前伸，将拇指放在穴道上。前1、2、3 拍加压，后4、5、6 拍放松。</p>
<p>另一侧重复一样的动作，各进行五次。</p>
<p><strong>2. 温热风加温</strong></p>
<p>在距离肌肤约5 公分处，将吹风机朝着穴道中心，以温热风吹约10 秒后拿开。反复进行三次，另一侧重复一样的动作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12031587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12031587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12031587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-22-600x263.jpg" alt="三阴交穴的按摩方法1和2：拇指指压和吹风机加温。（和平国际提供）" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12031587" class="wp-caption-text">三阴交穴的按摩方法1和2：拇指指压和吹风机加温。（和平国际）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 穴道伸展</strong></p>
<p>呈坐姿，脚掌贴合，两手的拇指分别放在双脚的穴道上。</p>
<p>前1、2、3 拍吐气的同时手指施压、身体向前弯；后4、5、6 拍边吸气边恢复原姿势。像是有重物压着身体般，进行五～六次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12031594" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12031594" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-223.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12031594" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/sanyinjiaoxue32-223-600x430.jpg" alt="三阴交穴的按摩方法3：穴道伸展。（和平国际提供）" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12031594" class="wp-caption-text">三阴交穴的按摩方法3：穴道伸展。（和平国际）</figcaption></figure>
