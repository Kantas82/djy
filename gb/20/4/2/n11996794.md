<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>面对居家隔离 乐当城市农夫 (下)：8种好收成的盆栽作物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce2998fe7e7_ttl7day8gW_shutterstock_619176701-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>随着中共病毒（武汉肺炎）的大流行，孩子们纷纷停课，家人们也团聚在一起，生活的各方各面都被迫暂缓下来。这时，很适合重新触摸泥土，让园艺疗愈你的身心。（shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年04月02日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元记者陈霆综合报导）随着中共病毒（武汉肺炎）的大流行，孩子们纷纷停课，家人们也团聚在一起，生活的各方各面都被迫暂缓下来。这时，很适合重新触摸泥土，让园艺疗愈你的身心。下次出门购买生活用品时，不妨就买些花盆与种子，尝试当一个<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9F%8E%E5%B8%82%E5%86%9C%E5%A4%AB.md#1">城市农夫</a>吧！</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在居家隔离的期间，你不妨先种种看下列这些生长季节短的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E7%94%A8%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">食用植物</a>，说不定将会培养出人生的新兴趣。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>1. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>菜苗（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Microgreens</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11996814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11996814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce2998c80cf_ttl7dayCVp_shutterstock_1224642301.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11996814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce2998c80cf_ttl7dayCVp_shutterstock_1224642301-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11996814" class="wp-caption-text">菜苗（又称芽菜）是种子发芽后，随即收成的绿色蔬菜。它们有细腻的质地、独特的风味与丰富的营养，而且收成期很短，在播种后10～20天内即可收成。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">菜苗（又称芽菜）是种子发芽后，随即收成的绿色蔬菜。它们有细腻的质地、独特的风味与丰富的营养，而且收成期很短，在播种后</span><span class="s3">10</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">20</span><span class="s1">天内即可收成，相当适合初学者，某些品种还可以收成不只一次。园艺店或网路上，有很多专用于菜苗栽培的种子，品种有很多选择，如：向日葵、萝卜、芝麻菜、生菜、玉米、甜菜、荞麦、小麦草等等。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>2. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>各式生菜（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Salad leaves</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11996816" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11996816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29986e34f_ttl7daypOW_shutterstock_206942437.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11996816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29986e34f_ttl7daypOW_shutterstock_206942437-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11996816" class="wp-caption-text">各种沙拉中常见的生菜，如：芝麻菜、萝蔓莴苣等，都可用<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>种植。可使用宽幅 20～40 公分的长形花槽，或6、7吋的花盆种植，<span class="s1">种下数周后就可以开始采收。</span>（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">各种沙拉中常见的生菜，如：芝麻菜、萝蔓莴苣等，都可用<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9B%86%E6%A0%BD.md#1">盆栽</a>种植。可使用宽幅</span><span class="s3"> 20</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">40 </span><span class="s1">公分的长形花槽，或</span><span class="s3">6</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s3">7</span><span class="s1">吋的花盆，弄湿土壤后将种子排成一排，相距约一英寸，盖上一层薄薄的覆土，并用保鲜膜或覆盖花盆。等发芽后，再移到光线充足的南向窗台。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">种下数周后就可以开始采收，将生长茂盛密集的部分摘下来，不仅有助通风，也能让下层的叶片继续生长。约</span><span class="s3">10</span><span class="s1">天可用液肥施肥一次，若管理得宜，每周都可采收，直到生长季节结束。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>3. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>嫩羽衣甘蓝（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Baby Kale</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11996828" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11996828" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce299a63abf_ttl7day47T_shutterstock_1406681876.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11996828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce299a63abf_ttl7day47T_shutterstock_1406681876-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11996828" class="wp-caption-text">嫩羽衣甘蓝有着丰富的营养，并能预防多种疾病，近年来被视为蔬菜界的超级食品。嫩羽衣甘蓝很适合用盆栽种植，从播种开始，在30天内即可收成。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">嫩羽衣甘蓝有着丰富的营养，并能预防多种疾病，近年来被视为蔬菜界的超级食品。嫩羽衣甘蓝很适合用盆栽种植，从播种开始，在</span><span class="s3">30</span><span class="s1">天内即可收成。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">播种时，可将种子埋在约</span><span class="s3">1/4</span><span class="s1">英寸的深度，保持土壤潮湿，</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">5</span><span class="s1">天后就会发芽。在充足的阳光下，大约一个月叶子就会长到四至五英寸高，这时可以从根部上方一英寸左右剪下，底部还会继续生长。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>4. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>芜菁（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Turnips</b></span><span class="s2"><b>，又称大头菜）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11996829" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11996829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29992ce17_ttl7dayks1_shutterstock_733782385.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11996829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29992ce17_ttl7dayks1_shutterstock_733782385-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11996829" class="wp-caption-text">芜菁又被称作大头菜。因为生长期短，容易栽培，适合种在阳台的盆栽里。种植时先在盆器底部铺上粗砂砾，再加入排水良好的土壤。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">芜菁又被称作大头菜。因为生长期短，容易栽培，适合种在阳台的盆栽里。种植时先在盆器底部铺上粗砂砾，再加入排水良好的土壤。播种时适合的深度为</span><span class="s3">1/2</span><span class="s1">英寸，株间相距至少</span><span class="s3">4</span><span class="s1">英寸。放在阳光充足的窗台上，每天浇水一次，大约</span><span class="s3">40</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">50</span><span class="s1">天后即可采收，根与叶都可以食用。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>5. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>萝卜（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Radishes</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11996830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11996830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29996258f_ttl7dayMkp_shutterstock_1010435131.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11996830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1601dce29996258f_ttl7dayMkp_shutterstock_1010435131-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11996830" class="wp-caption-text">种植萝卜时，土质要够疏松，株间距离不要太近，以免影响根系生长。萝卜的需水量比一般蔬菜高，记得定时浇水。另外，可以适度添加磷肥、钾肥。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">许多不同种类的萝卜，都在</span><span class="s3">30</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">60</span><span class="s1">天之内就可以收成。在挑选时，可以选择短根系的品种，比较适合在室内种植。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">种植萝卜时，土质要够疏松，株间距离不要太近，以免影响根系生长。萝卜的需水量比一般蔬菜高，记得定时浇水。另外，可以适度添加磷肥、钾肥。等到萝卜可采摘时，根部会从土壤中露出来，这时从地面上拔起来即可。除了根，萝卜的叶子也可用来煮汤。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>6. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>香草（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Herbs</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11919934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11919934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/15f9abee20ef6733_ttl7dayMcH_shutterstock_157046342.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11919934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/15f9abee20ef6733_ttl7dayMcH_shutterstock_157046342-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11919934" class="wp-caption-text">大多香草都喜欢明亮的阳光与排水良好的土壤，只要避免过度浇水，多数香草都可以在你家里茂盛的生长。采收香草时，记得从顶端修剪，让底部强壮的茎长成稳固的基础。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">薄荷、虾夷葱（</span><span class="s3">chives</span><span class="s1">）、迷迭香、百里香等香草，都很适合作为盆栽。大多香草都喜欢明亮的阳光与排水良好的土壤，只要避免过度浇水，多数香草都可以在你家里茂盛的生长。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">采收香草时，记得从顶端修剪，让底部强壮的茎长成稳固的基础。每次可从顶端减去</span><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">英寸的枝叶，剪过以后香草会分支，变得更丛生茂密，长出更多的叶子。修剪时，切勿剪去超过三分之一的枝条，以免影响<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9.md#1">植物</a>的生长。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>7. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>香葱（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Scallions</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_5851731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5851731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1405120807102483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-5851731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/04/1405120807102483-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5851731" class="wp-caption-text">葱可以用鳞茎来种植。从超市将葱买回来后，只要留下2～3英寸左右带根的葱白，将它种在盆栽里，它就会继续生长，长出新的葱绿供你使用。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">葱可以用鳞茎来种植。从超市将葱买回来后，只要留下</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">～</span><span class="s3">3</span><span class="s1">英寸左右带根的葱白，将它种在盆栽里，它就会继续生长，长出新的葱绿供你使用。如果栽植条件良好，大约</span><span class="s3">20</span><span class="s1">天左右，就会长成市售青葱的长度。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1"><b>8. </b></span><span class="s2"><b>红苋菜（</b></span><span class="s1"><b>Red Amaranth</b></span><span class="s2"><b>）</b></span></p>
