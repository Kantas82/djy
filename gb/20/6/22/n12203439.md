<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南洋酪梨打抛饭 微辣咸甜促食欲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1c698267499bbf2ca996bad0c4f6f6d1-600x400.png" />
<h6>南洋风味酪梨打抛饭（图：高宝出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>充满香草风味的南洋<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>饭，煎炒时记得让酪梨吸收鸡肉的美味，真想跟啤酒一起吃个过瘾！</p>
<p>【材料】（2人份）</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>?1颗<br />
茄子?1根<br />
红、黄彩椒?各1/2颗<br />
鸡腿肉（或是鸡绞肉）?250g<br />
饭?2碗<br />
蛋?2颗</p>
<p>【A】</p>
<p>鱼露?2大匙<br />
蚝油?2大匙<br />
蒜头（磨泥）?1/4小匙<br />
姜（磨泥）?1/4小匙<br />
酱油?1大匙</p>
<p>【其它】</p>
<p>料理酒?5大匙<br />
卡宴辣椒粉（或一味辣椒粉）?1/4大匙<br />
罗勒叶、沙拉油?各适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203453" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12203453" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1385731064.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12203453" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1385731064-450x338.jpg" alt="卡宴辣椒" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12203453" class="wp-caption-text">卡宴辣椒（Cayenne Pepper）外观细长、尾端偏尖，磨成粉状呈现亮红色，是有明显辣度的香料；在墨西哥、印度料理中广泛被使用，适合面食、炒菜、炖煮食物等，可以增添菜色的风味与层次。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【作法】</p>
<p>1. 酪梨、茄子、彩椒切成1cm的丁状。鸡肉切细丁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203447" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12203447" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_136637633.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12203447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_136637633-450x298.jpg" alt="彩椒" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12203447" class="wp-caption-text">彩椒要切成丁状似乎不是一刀完成的工作。首先，要把蒂头切除，再由上而下把彩椒纵剖两半，然后取出内部种籽、纤维，再顺着纵向切成条状，最后把条状切成丁状。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 平底锅内加入稍多的沙拉油，打入蛋，煎荷包蛋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11213332" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11213332" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/friedegg-042501.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11213332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/friedegg-042501-450x300.jpg" alt="看到蛋白开始凝固时，可在鸡蛋上洒2小勺热水，马上盖上锅盖。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11213332" class="wp-caption-text">平底锅中的沙拉油不够，煎蛋时容易黏着锅底。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 用中火炒鸡腿肉。加入酒，酒精挥发后加入【A】、茄子、彩椒翻炒。</p>
<p>4. 蔬菜熟了之后就加入酪梨后简单翻炒后关火，加入手撕的罗勒叶后搅拌混合。</p>
<p>5. 将容器盛入饭，放上步骤4的鸡肉，上头摆上荷包蛋。洒上卡宴辣椒，可以在蛋黄的部分依个人喜好加上鱼露（材料外）。</p>
<p>（网站专文）</p>
