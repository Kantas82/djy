<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽房地产疫情下强劲增长 全国平均价格下降</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/GettyImages-57632048-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月03日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（ 大纪元记者安琪<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>编译报导 ）今年 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，房产销售网站 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">realestate.co.nz </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">收到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8324 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">份来自全国各地的新<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E6%BA%90.md#1">房源</a>，仅比去年 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。这与 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份仅</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2962 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">处新<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E6%BA%90.md#1">房源</a>相比有了实质性的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%9E%E9%95%BF.md#1">增长</a>，当时<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>全国处于 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">级封锁状态。这表明<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">市场</a>活动可能在冬季回归正常。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">皇后镇湖区 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( Queenstown &#8211; Lakes District ) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">的增幅最大， </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的新房源较去年</span><b>同期</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%9E%E9%95%BF.md#1">增长</a>了 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">21.8 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">％。在新西兰其他地区，新上市房源的数量差别很大，西海岸新房源较上年同期增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，而惠灵顿</span><b>比</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">上年同期下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">24.3 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">? </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">与一年前相比，奥克兰的新上市房源数量下降了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">12.5 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，坎特伯雷 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( Canterbury ) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">下降了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4.4 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md#1">市场</a>上的房屋总数也在反弹，截至</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月底，在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">realestate.co.nz </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">网站上共有 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20,940 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">处房屋可供出售，这是自去年 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月以来的最高数量。</span><b>但是</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">，与去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月相比，数字仍下降</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">19.1 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份全国所有地区的总房源库存水平都</span><b>比去年低</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">，除了中部奥塔哥湖 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( Central Otago &#8211; Lakes ) </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">较上年同期增长</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 13 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，奥塔哥地区</span><b>也比</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">上年同期增长</span><b>了</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">2 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份全国平均房屋大幅回落，从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的创纪录</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> 87 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4886 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元</span><b>下降</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">72</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9002</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">( </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">跌幅 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">16.1 % )</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，但仍</span><b>比</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">去年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月份上涨了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10 %</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
