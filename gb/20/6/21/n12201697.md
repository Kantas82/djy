<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>去嘉义市北香湖公园看上帝的金戒指</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/e42a38a742d3ced7fb710c3248553199-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在嘉义市北香湖公园看上帝的金戒指。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月21日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%B8%82.md#1">嘉义市</a>长<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E6%83%A0.md#1">黄敏惠</a>、台北市长柯文哲、名导魏德圣共赏“上帝金戒指”，被誉为“上帝金戒指”的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E7%8E%AF%E9%A3%9F.md#1">日环食</a>于621<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E8%87%B3%E6%97%A5%E5%85%A8%E7%A8%8B.md#1">夏至日全程</a>、明亮的展现在嘉义市北香湖公园的天空，看完全程的民众都感动不已，有的甚至说人生值得了，因为下一次要再看到此奇景要等195年，也就是下两世或三世人了。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E7%8E%AF%E9%A3%9F.md#1">日环食</a>现身<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E4%B9%89%E5%B8%82.md#1">嘉义市</a>上空，不仅天文迷嗨翻、更吸引大批民众前往北香湖公园共赏日环食，整个嘉义市市区主要道路挤满车潮，管制的友爱路更挤满观日人潮，2020嘉义市日环食实在太有吸引力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201700" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a44394d9be2fa904ae733f920d06e9a0-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12201700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a44394d9be2fa904ae733f920d06e9a0-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201700" class="wp-caption-text">F16战机冲场。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%95%8F%E6%83%A0.md#1">黄敏惠</a>表示，为迎接这一刻到来已经准备两年了，市府团队“超前部署、全心全意准备”，要让日环食活动富含教育意义，同时邀请台北市长柯文哲与台北市政府团队，柯文哲夫人陈佩琪也一同前来，还有与嘉义市有着深厚缘分的魏德圣导演，来“嘉”共赏世纪天文秀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201957" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201957" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446224.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12201957" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446224-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201957" class="wp-caption-text">最有代表性的月球画面。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12201967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201967" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446233.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12201967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446233-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201967" class="wp-caption-text">教授解说观天文奇景的注意事项。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位处环食带中心线的嘉义市，为绝佳观测地点。许多民众不畏高温炎热天气，挤爆文化与友爱路，携家带眷前往北香湖公园，上午听取天文讲堂，下午静待进入日环食时间后，拿起观日滤镜扇，抬头仰望天空，欣赏16点14分仅一分钟的食甚，现场惊呼声连连；接着由5架F-16战机，以大雁队形通过北香湖公园上方，为日环食活动再带起一波高潮；黄敏惠观看完食甚后，感动不已。柯文哲市长也说，这是最佳的城市体验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201702" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/cb73cb47a23d3d5f81d8235e85b8a2bb-450x292.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12201702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/cb73cb47a23d3d5f81d8235e85b8a2bb-450x292.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201702" class="wp-caption-text">刚开始进入日环食的画面。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不仅北香湖人潮挤爆，嘉义火车站、森林之歌、嘉义制材所，只要有大片绿地的地方，都挤满了人潮，大家带着观日圆扇或观日设备，以舒适野餐的方式，期待日环食的到来。嘉义市真的是到处可以观赏日环食的所在，更吸引了全国满满人潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201703" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/edbd8e0329a046934b17ad5425db4f92-450x302.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12201703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/edbd8e0329a046934b17ad5425db4f92-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="336" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201703" class="wp-caption-text">日环食就像上帝的金戒指一样的令在场感动不已。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄敏惠感谢柯文哲带着台北市政府团队，南下嘉义市共襄盛举，“日环食”是很有教育意义，开启了人们对于宇宙探索与天文的奥秘。同时嘉义市与台北市在今天满满的行程中，有更深入的市政交流，期盼未来一起携手合作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201704" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/859797e834aa511825ec517f5f81d3ee-450x348.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12201704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/859797e834aa511825ec517f5f81d3ee-450x348.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201704" class="wp-caption-text">渐渐离开之后。（嘉义市政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台北市长柯文哲、夫人陈佩琪以及台北市政府，今天参访了乐龄健康训练场、火车站前广场、嘉义制材所及北香湖公园等，嘉义市充满历史人文底蕴，真正来过看过，更能体验嘉义市不仅市民热情友善，更是处处有美景、随手有美食的城市。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201958" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446228.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12201958" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446228-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201958" class="wp-caption-text">贵宾聚精会神的看日环食。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>将嘉农拼战日本甲子园故事，以KANO电影跃上国际舞台的导演魏德圣透露，自己第一次追天文景观，觉得是个特别经验，才知道原来这么多人热衷，今天中午的高铁车厢都爆满，嘉义市发挥团结一心的正向能量，把所有人聚集在一起，非常厉害。导演魏德圣几乎从头到尾都仔细观看着日环食的过程，他说“太阳、宇宙真的很神奇，真的很好看。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12201959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12201959" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446229.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12201959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/446229-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12201959" class="wp-caption-text">导演魏德圣几乎从头到尾都仔细观看着日环食的过程，他说“太阳、宇宙真的很神奇，真的很好看。”（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
