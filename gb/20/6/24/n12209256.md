<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嘉县能源教育知识决赛现场30位PK盟主出炉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/757249589f19943cba6c24c475f3f148-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	水上国小陈秉轩、简嘉禧和杨承邦荣获节电小霸王。（嘉义县政府提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月24日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">水上国小</a>札实的能源知识教育远近驰名，也成就了本次的荣耀。嘉义县环保局6月24日在235环境教育绿能生活馆举行“嘉义县学龄孩童<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%BD%E6%BA%90%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">能源教育</a>知识竞赛”决赛PK活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209259" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8ed1d33a210c899f1656b6a880a671ce.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12209259 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8ed1d33a210c899f1656b6a880a671ce-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209259" class="wp-caption-text">学童获得节电小尖兵。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过一番激烈竞争的PK淘汰赛，最后由<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E4%B8%8A%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%8F.md#1">水上国小</a>江宜霈同学脱颖而出，拿下最大奖“节电小博士”，水上国小黄珮祯及周家萱荣获节电小神童，水上国小陈秉轩、简嘉禧和杨承邦荣获节电小霸王，还有30位学童获得节电小尖兵。</p>
<p>环保局表示，能源知识竞赛有232名学童参赛，首先以线上初赛挑战后，取优胜30名学童进入现场决赛，最后以竞赛方式，让学童积极学习能源知识及关心节能议题，将它应用在日常生活中，实践环境教育。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209261" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3bdc2440eb8e05ab5c0f34f0d1c87c1a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12209261 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3bdc2440eb8e05ab5c0f34f0d1c87c1a-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209261" class="wp-caption-text">环保局表示，能源知识竞赛有232名学童参赛，首先以线上初赛挑战后，取优胜30名学童进入现场决赛。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>环保局长张根穆指出，能源知识竞赛活动因疫情改用线上测验进行初赛，参赛的学童在指定答题期间可随时进行挑战，提升参与的便利性。秉持对<ahref="https://github.com/hxcaik307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%BD%E6%BA%90%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2.md#1">能源教育</a>深耕的精神，竞赛结束后活动网站(http://cypowergame.green99.com.tw/)将持续开放让民众体验作答，提供亲子同乐多元学习节能新知，欢迎民众多加利用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12209262" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/afb8ddae8b292187c1a4d3a1c070f1d3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12209262 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/afb8ddae8b292187c1a4d3a1c070f1d3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12209262" class="wp-caption-text">学童获得节电小尖兵。（嘉义县政府提供）</figcaption></figure>
