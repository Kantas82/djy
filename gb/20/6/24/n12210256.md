<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】红焖鸭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/790901f4896a96787c37011867e78dfc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这道菜的特点是：色泽红润，酥烂软嫩，香味浓醇。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鸭肉一直是华人餐桌上的上乘肴馔，不仅是各种美味名菜的主要原料，也是人们进补的优良食品。</p>
<p>中医认为，鸭子吃的食物多为水生物，故其肉性味甘、寒，入肺胃肾经，有滋补、养胃、补肾、除痨热骨蒸、消水肿、止热痢、止咳化痰等作用。</p>
<p>凡体内有热的人适宜食鸭肉，体质虚弱、食欲不振、发热、大便干燥和水肿的人食之更为有益。民间还传说，鸭是肺结核病人的“圣药”。</p>
<p>鸭肉的营养价值很高，蛋白质含量比畜肉高得多。而鸭肉的脂肪、碳水化合物含量适中，特别是脂肪均匀地分布于全身组织中。鸭肉中的脂肪酸主要是不饱和脂肪酸和低碳饱和脂肪酸，而且饱和脂肪酸、单不饱和脂肪酸、多不饱和脂肪酸的比例接近理想值，其化学成分近似橄榄油。</p>
<p>红焖，是中国传统的烹饪方法之一，是将加工好的原料经焯水或过油后，放入锅中加调味品，盖上锅盖后，旺火烧沸转中火焖，直至原料酥烂成菜。调味料以红色调味品为主，比如酱油、糖色、老抽、甜面酱等，这道菜的特点是：色泽红润，酥烂软嫩，香味浓醇。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%84%96%E9%B8%AD.md#1">红焖鸭</a>，选用超大的生铁锅，而且是用最古老的烹调方法——烧木柴，成品菜肴鲜、甜、软滑……满满的家乡味，希望您有口福能够品尝到罗厨亲自制作的这道香浓美味。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，制作这道菜时要用高度白酒，这样可以去除鸭肉的腥味。另外，每一个调味料都不可少，海盐调味、冰糖和味、十三香增味，收汁时要让鸭肉充分吸收汤汁。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%84%96%E9%B8%AD.md#1">红焖鸭</a></h2>
<p>主料：鸭子 1只</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/f3ecca4f40415128324a257fe5f82f2f.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/f3ecca4f40415128324a257fe5f82f2f-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a></p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱段、香菇、蒜瓣、大料</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b0a72738cbf4312c7f9ae53888b610d6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b0a72738cbf4312c7f9ae53888b610d6-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a></p>
<p>调料：美极酱油、白酒、胡椒粉、蜂蜜、海盐、冰糖、十三香、生粉</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将刮干净的鸭子切成小块后沥干水分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-33.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-33-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 将浸发好的香菇切开两半。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3. 猛火阴油后将姜片、蒜头爆香捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 把香菇爆香捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 利用锅底余油把鸭块爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6. 把鸭子捞出沥干水分和油分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7. 猛火阴油把鸭子和香菇炒香，放入姜片和蒜再炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8. 加入美极酱油、白酒、大料、胡椒粉和蜂蜜炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9. 放入3碗肉汤，盖上木盖，慢火焖25分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10. 加入海盐、冰糖、十三香、生粉水，然后收汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11. 最后放入葱段装盘。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12210320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
