<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>以柔克刚的杰作─落基山约翰斯顿峡谷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20170902-AdobeStock-Johnston-Canyon-Lower-Falls-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在约翰斯顿峡谷感受水滴石穿的惊世之作。(Adobe Stock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月28日讯】“在约翰斯顿峡谷，有一种力量可以劈山移石，它的名字叫——水。”的确，在加拿大亚省落基山的约翰斯顿峡谷（Johnston Canyon），水是这里的主宰。峥嵘伟岸的高山无法规划水的流向，相反，柔弱清凉的流水决定岩石是走是留。去约翰斯顿峡谷吧，感受水的惊世之作！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210639" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-stream.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210639 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-stream-600x450.jpg" alt="Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park，Alberta Travel" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210639" class="wp-caption-text">在约翰斯顿峡谷，水是这里的主宰，决定岩石是走是留。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>展现自然之力</h3>
<p>到过加拿大的朋友可能听过这样一个说法，加拿大一年有两个季节：冬季和非冬季。之所以这样说，是因为大多数地区冬季过于寒冷漫长，而春夏秋几乎没有什么过渡。虽然不能用它来概括所有的地方，然而用来形容<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1%E8%84%89.md#1">落基山脉</a>倒是颇为贴切。</p>
<p>年轻的约翰斯顿峡谷坐落于<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%AD%E8%8A%99%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">班芙国家公园</a>，距美丽的班芙镇只有17公里的山路，是展示自然伟力和时间的好地方。流水在石灰岩上尽情雕刻，一路冲刷，仅用了八千年的时间就将峡谷加深了一倍！通往峡谷瀑布的小路是落基山最繁忙的步道，络绎不绝的人群挤入狭窄的悬阶，其独特的山径和幽谷的确值得一游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210692" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-0203.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210692 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-0203-600x400.jpg" alt="Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park，Alberta Travel" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210692" class="wp-caption-text">左图：微风拂幽谷，清泉石上流；右图：悬阶直插入壁。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">水流永远冰凉</h3>
<p>山谷里水声潺潺，春季，峭壁上依然挂着悬冰，夏季来时又消失于无形。由于水流来自消融的冰雪，因此这里的水永远冰凉彻骨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210700" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210700" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-04-450x600.jpg" alt="Cliff with ice on, Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210700" class="wp-caption-text">春季，峭壁上依然挂着悬冰。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谷中大大小小的瀑布众多，渐行1.1公里，就到了低瀑(Lower Falls)。瀑高10米，下方有一水流冲积出的深潭，飞沫折射出小小的彩虹，在阳光下时隐时现。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210710" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-05-Lower-Falls.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210710 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-05-Lower-Falls-450x654.jpg" alt="Lower Falls, Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park" width="450" b="654" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210710" class="wp-caption-text">低瀑（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再走1.6公里，行至高瀑(Upper Falls)，水声雷动，却不见其形，隐藏于山林巨石之间，在特定的位置才能观其全貌，颇有些“犹抱琵琶半遮面”的韵味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210714" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-05-Upper-Falls.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-05-Upper-Falls-450x642.jpg" alt="Upper Falls, Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park" width="450" b="642" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210714" class="wp-caption-text">高瀑（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>石灰岩与鸟巢</h3>
<p>一路走来，你已经看到了融冰冲刷岩壁的证据，眼前潮湿光滑的石壁是石灰藻理想的栖息地。这种原始的植物有助于石灰岩形成石灰石晶体，晶体不断积累生长成石灰岩。在两千年前的罗马，石灰岩被广泛用于建筑，斗兽场就是著名的范例。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210740" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-0708.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12210740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-0708-600x394.jpg" alt="Limestone, Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210740" class="wp-caption-text">左图：壁立的石灰岩；右图：灰岩上细流淙淙。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>约翰斯顿峡谷还是亚省著名的鸟类繁殖地，仔细观察，你会发现很多鸟巢安置在峭壁的岩洞之内，每年都会吸引很多鸟类研究人员慕名前来。山谷里的小动物也早已被人喂熟了，经常发生飞鸟从人手中抢食的事件。也有些游客逗弄花栗鼠时被咬伤，山有山规，还是遵守为妙。</p>
<h3>墨池变化多端</h3>
<p>如果你还有体力，可以去距离高瀑3.2公里的墨池（Inkpots）体验山谷另一神奇之处。在这无边无际的森林里放缓脚步，放慢呼吸，让云杉、松树散发的郁香拂面，让微醺的暖风滑过你的指尖，感受大自然丰厚的馈赠，心怀感激。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210760" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12210760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20090517-SUNNY-Johnston-Canyon-09-600x450.jpg" alt=" Johnston Canyon, Banff, Banff National Park" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210760" class="wp-caption-text">沙粒化作峥嵘。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<p>山路并不难走，及至墨池，眼前豁然开朗，你已置身于峡谷的深处了。墨池实际上是五个蓝绿色的泉水池，终年水温4度，根据出水的快慢忽蓝忽绿，变化多端。池中细沙翻舞，忽而成圈，忽而消失，为墨池平添了许多趣味。“行至水穷处，坐看云起时。”此刻，你的心情也会象山谷里的野风恣意，凡事顿消呢。</p>
