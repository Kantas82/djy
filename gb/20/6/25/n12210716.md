<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】海鲜猪骨汤鱼面 懒人料理口袋名单</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海鲜猪骨汤鱼面（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong><span style="color: #000000;">【小编按】</span></strong><span style="color: #000000;">有时候在家懒得下厨，又没有头绪要煮什么，也不想要叫外卖，怎么办？来一道快速、省时又丰盛营养的料理，可以满足自己的饥饿感。找一找冰箱冷藏（冻）的食材，比方鱼饼、虾、火锅料，或是常备食材如腌渍食品、速食面等，就可以轻松烹调。这一道“海鲜<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E9%AA%A8.md#1">猪骨</a>汤<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>”烹饪起来智慧又省力，就算是料理新手也不用担心，学起来！当您需要来一份“懒人料理”食谱，就能派上用场！</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>6安士（Fish Noodles 6 oz）<br />
日本鱼饼2片（Steamed Fish Cake 3 slices）<br />
日本烧鱼饼条2条（Yaki Fish Cake Stick 2 strips）<br />
虾3只（Shrimp 3 pieces）<br />
腌渍竹笋 1.5安士（Pickled Bamboo Shoots 1.5 oz）<br />
芽菜1.5安士（Mung Bean Sprouts 1.5 oz）<br />
葱花少许（Green onion a little）<br />
紫菜2片（Seaweed 2 pieces）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E9%AA%A8.md#1">猪骨</a>汤酱1包 1.06安士（Tonkotsu Ramen Soup Paste 1.06 oz）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210745" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210745" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-34.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210745" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-34-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210745" class="wp-caption-text">鱼面、芽菜、葱花、海苔片（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12210747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-25-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210747" class="wp-caption-text">腌渍竹笋、虾、烧鱼饼条、鱼饼（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12210749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210749" class="wp-caption-text">方便又好用的猪骨汤酱包（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 猪骨汤包先用两杯水煮滚备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-17-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210752" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 煮滚一锅开水，鱼面煮3分钟后捞出，放进大汤碗内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5c4f847ec29365fc09562b57f0f63ab1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5c4f847ec29365fc09562b57f0f63ab1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210754" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 芽菜用滚水烫过后捞出，放在面上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a2deea0495ef3914100b4eccb064ef23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a2deea0495ef3914100b4eccb064ef23-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210756" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 接着把各样海鲜用滚水烫1分钟左右，同样捞出摆放在鱼面上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12210757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-10-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210757" class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 放上竹笋，淋上猪骨汤，最后再放上葱花及紫菜装饰，便可享用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>
<p>1. 在日本超市内很容易找到各式各样的食材来做汤面，这种猪骨汤酱非常方便，好用又美味！<br />
2. 这次的食谱是用鱼面搭配猪骨汤，鱼面的口感比乌龙面更弹牙爽滑！</p>
