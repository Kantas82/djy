<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中坜警分局廉政反贪-“想抓你”  毛毛小孩版</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9b893167f8e9b3ac14a57cbff6888421-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>妇女志工中队涂中队长秀祯商借重量级汪星人“妞妞”独挑大梁。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月25日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）因应端午佳节到来，桃园市政府警察局政风室与中坜分局合作拍摄一部由毛小孩所主演的<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%89%E6%94%BF.md#1">廉政</a><ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC%E7%9F%AD%E7%89%87.md#1">宣导短片</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210857" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/ab1b6695973fc36cf45e55ee616995c0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210857 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/ab1b6695973fc36cf45e55ee616995c0-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210857" class="wp-caption-text">饰演认真热血巡逻警察“陈韵妞”，让人称好。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C%E8%AD%A6%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">中坜警分局</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了拍这部片，中坜分局向分局妇女志工中队涂中队长秀祯商借重量级汪星人“妞妞”独挑大梁，饰演认真热血巡逻警察“陈韵妞”；请到素有短腿界隋棠之称的喵星人“小芝麻”饰演喝酒开车上路的醉汉“李子喵”。片中陈韵妞拒绝酒后驾车醉汉李子喵的“罐罐关说”，展现警方<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BB%89%E6%94%BF.md#1">廉政</a>反贪及严正执法决心！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6baaedf54e6577deffc8b3d196335b1c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210859 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6baaedf54e6577deffc8b3d196335b1c-600x329.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210859" class="wp-caption-text">素有短腿界隋棠之称的喵星人“小芝麻”饰演喝酒开车上路的醉汉“李子喵”。（桃园市<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C%E8%AD%A6%E5%88%86%E5%B1%80.md#1">中坜警分局</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然拍片过程中毛孩子一度不受控制，但拍出的成果却让人很满意，不仅结合电视剧“想见你”及反贪廉洁等元素，让政风宣导更有趣且吸睛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12210861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12210861" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/434a937cf999712c6b081122814342c8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12210861 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/434a937cf999712c6b081122814342c8-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12210861" class="wp-caption-text">桃园市政府警察局政风室与中坜分局合作拍摄一部由毛小孩所主演的廉政<ahref="https://github.com/qscbje3555/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E5%AF%BC%E7%9F%AD%E7%89%87.md#1">宣导短片</a>。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>端午佳节人团圆，为中坜地区治安及交通平稳顺畅，中坜警分局长刘印宫表示，坜警同仁仍日夜不停歇，用心守护大家，于连假期间仍积极净化辖区治安，并再次重申公务机关“不送礼、不收礼、不赴（邀）宴、拒受不当请托关说”等廉政规定，展现坜警严正执法及革除陋习之决心。◇</p>
