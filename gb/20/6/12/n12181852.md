<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】泡椒蒸鱼头鱼尾</h2>

<h6>（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鱼是公认的健康食物，也是华人餐桌上的主菜之一。鱼肉含有丰富的蛋白质、氨基酸、脂肪、鱼油、维生素B群、维生素A、D、E、碘、钾等各种丰富的营养素。</p>
<p>鱼头除了含鱼肉的全部营养外，还含有鱼肉所缺乏的卵磷脂。鱼鳃下边的肉呈透明的胶状，里面富含胶原蛋白，能够对抗人体老化及修补身体细胞组织。</p>
<p>鱼头还含丰富的不饱和脂肪酸，它对大脑的发育尤为重要，可使大脑细胞异常活跃，故使推理、判断力得到极大增强，因此，常吃鱼头不仅可以健脑，而且还可延缓脑力衰退。</p>
<p>有人爱吃鱼头，也有人认为鱼尾才是最好吃的，鱼尾的摆动使得这里的肉最嫩， 尾部的鱼翅吃起来也最有味。</p>
<p>泡椒鱼头鱼尾，以鱼头鱼尾的“味鲜”和泡椒的“辣”为一体。火辣辣的红剁椒覆盖着白嫩嫩的鱼肉，热气腾腾、香气四溢。</p>
<p>罗厨认为，鱼头鱼尾鱼骨架最适合于蒸，因为蒸制的方法可以使鱼肉的鲜香最大限度地保留在肉质之内。另外，带骨的鱼肉最滑、最嫩、最鲜美，加上吸收丰富的蒜汁、葱汁后，味道更浓郁，再配上鲜辣清甜的自制剁椒，又恰到好处地渗入到鱼肉当中，使这道本来就完美的佳肴略带野味，令人胃口大增。</p>
<h2><strong><a href="https://github.com/whu295/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E6%A4%92%E8%92%B8%E9%B1%BC%E5%A4%B4%E9%B1%BC%E5%B0%BE.md">泡椒蒸鱼头鱼尾</a></strong></h2>
<p>主料：比目鱼头、鱼尾、鱼骨架<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/bbd343d382f7e9cd5ede76340b0c208e.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/bbd343d382f7e9cd5ede76340b0c208e-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><br />
配料：蒜头、绿叶葱<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/69cbcf6981ed3d1461331d17d8053d7b.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/69cbcf6981ed3d1461331d17d8053d7b-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><br />
调料：盐、生粉、泡椒、生抽</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将比目鱼的鱼头、鱼尾洗干净，沥干水分。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-21.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-21-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将鱼头和鱼尾切成大小均匀的鱼块，整齐摆放入盘中。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 鱼块上撒生粉。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 鱼块上淋3汤匙泡椒。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 放入蒸箱蒸8～9分钟。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 将3颗蒜头拍烂切碎。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-6-450x280.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="280" /></a><br />
7. 将一根绿叶葱切成小粒。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-3-450x288.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="288" /></a><br />
8. 取出蒸好的鱼，加入蒜粒和葱粒。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-3-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><br />
9. 在蒸好的鱼块上淋热油。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 最后，淋上生抽2汤匙。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清香鲜美、酸辣爽口。<br />
<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12181886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
