<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>闯红灯机车汽车碰撞  中坜警开罚吁守交通号志</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/06715d54126be5ee2353317b8f2bf803-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中坜区中华路一段与兴仁路口闯红灯机车汽车碰撞。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）局)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月05日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）上午10时33分许，63岁机车驾驶叶男于<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C.md#1">中坜</a>区中华路一段与兴仁路口，自兴仁路<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%AF%E7%BA%A2%E7%81%AF.md#1">闯红灯</a>至中华路一段往<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9D%9C.md#1">中坜</a>方向车道时，与40岁自小客车驾驶古女发生交通事故，所幸车毁人没事，中坜警方处新台币1800至5400元以下罚缓，并请用路人行车时遵守<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E5%8F%B7%E5%BF%97.md#1">交通号志</a>，切勿违规驾驶，以维护行车安全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12162929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12162929" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/58178d357ef0e483081b9828c8fc6bed.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12162929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/58178d357ef0e483081b9828c8fc6bed-450x353.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="353" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12162929" class="wp-caption-text">中坜区中华路一段与兴仁路口<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%97%AF%E7%BA%A2%E7%81%AF.md#1">闯红灯</a>机车汽车碰撞。（桃园市中坜警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E7%A5%B8.md#1">车祸</a>发生后，中坜警方针对双方驾驶酒测值为0，警方也呼吁违反<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%A4%E9%80%9A%E5%8F%B7%E5%BF%97.md#1">交通号志</a>者，依道交管理处罚条例可处新台币1800至5400元以下罚缓，请用路人行车时遵守交通号志，切勿违规驾驶，以维护行车安全。◇</p>
