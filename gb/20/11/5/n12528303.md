<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】红烧划水</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/17f3d3be93e368ce9d293997d3952a09-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道红烧划水，色泽明亮、卤汁稠浓、肥糯油润、肉滑鲜嫩。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E5%88%92%E6%B0%B4.md#1">红烧划水</a>是安徽传统名菜。“划水”也称“甩水”，是鱼尾的一种俗称，是指鱼的尾部连尾鳍的一段。由于经常运动，这个部位的肉特别嫩滑。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E5%88%92%E6%B0%B4.md#1">红烧划水</a>的烹饪技巧以红烧为主，口味属于咸鲜味。这道菜工艺十分讲究，鱼尾留肉最好不少于10厘米，剖斩鱼肉时不要切断鱼尾，以保持菜形的完整美观。烹饪时，须经多次颠翻而保证鱼尾不断，难度很高。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道红烧划水是鲩鱼系列菜肴之一。一条完整的鲩鱼，鱼肉做鱼肉酿秋葵和姜葱水浸鲩鱼，鱼头做宽粉炖鱼头，鱼尾做红烧划水，剩下的鱼骨做椒盐鱼骨。整条鱼一点也不浪费，每道菜都美味可口、极富特色，堪称道道经典。</p>
<p>这道红烧划水以香菇和五花肉为配料。香菇是高蛋白、低脂肪的菌类食物，香菇的水提取物可以清除体内的过氧化氢。香菇菌盖部分含有双链接构的核糖核酸，进入人体后会产生具有抗癌作用的干扰素。</p>
<p>五花肉含有多种脂肪酸，能提供极高的热量，并且含有蛋白质、B族维生素、维生素E、维生素A、钙、铁、磷、硒等营养元素。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道红烧划水，色泽明亮、卤汁稠浓、肥糯油润、肉滑鲜嫩。罗厨强调，制作这道菜掌握火候很关键，另外高汤也要适量，汤多不易收浓，汤少则主料不易烧透入味。</p>
<h2>红烧划水</h2>
<p>主料：鲩鱼鱼尾一条、五花肉、虾<br />
配料：蒜、姜片、葱段、香菇、香菜<br />
调料：盐、生抽、生粉、胡椒粉、老抽、料酒、高汤、糖、蚝油、白酒</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鱼尾顺尾方向切一刀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 用生抽、生粉腌鱼尾待用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 把姜片爆香后加五花肉炒出油。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528346" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 再把香菇、虾放入爆炒至香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 煎鱼至双面焦香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 把配料一起倒入煎香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528350" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 然后放入葱段。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 添加料酒、生抽、糖和蚝油。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 加盖慢火烧10分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 开盖后加一点白酒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528356" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 摆盘后用原汁勾芡并洒上葱花。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12528358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
