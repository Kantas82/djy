<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吃甜食不会胖？浓郁奶香咸派的料理秘密</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3ed545471fce85b4b3350bd59ec594cf-600x400.png" />
<h6>洋葱培根起司咸派，内馅咸香搭配滑嫩蛋香，非常对味好吃！（图／橘子文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：想要瘦身却禁不起吃点心的欲望吗？时下流行的<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8E%E7%B3%96.md#1">低糖</a>食谱可以满足你的口腹之欲，却不会热量爆表！而且不只是甜点，连<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">咸派</a>都可以征服，关键就在酥脆的<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%BC%E7%9A%AE.md#1">饼皮</a>是用杏仁粉、赤藻糖醇来取代面粉及砂糖，让碳水化合物大大降低。</p>
<p>除了派皮之外，内馅浓郁奶香、咸甜不腻，就算当主食也很适合。如果喜欢甜派口味，只要调整赤藻糖醇和海盐的比例就行了！一份食谱除了控制热量、咸甜味自选，而且不用准备繁复的烹饪工具，只要有<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%83%A4%E7%AE%B1.md#1">小烤箱</a>就可以完成这一道美食，非常简易好上手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12504903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12504903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_431273479.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12504903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_431273479-450x300.jpg" alt="早餐, 高蛋白, 蛋饼, 法式咸派" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12504903" class="wp-caption-text">想来一点欧美乡村风的餐点吗？菠菜法式<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">咸派</a>很适合。（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">洋葱培根起司咸派</span></h2>
<p><b>咸香馅料与滑嫩蛋香的完美结合</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>烤箱温度</strong>：180℃</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>烘烤时间</strong>：30～35分钟</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>使用模具</strong>：6吋派模（以下有派皮作法）</span></p>
<p><strong>份量</strong>：1个</p>
<p><strong>营养成分</strong>：糖53.70克、碳水化合物58.40克、膳食纤维4.70克、糖22.81克、蛋白质73.38</p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;"><strong>奶油糊材料</strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">鸡蛋2颗</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">鲜奶油120克</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">奶油乳酪80克</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>饰顶</strong></span><br />
</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">帕玛森起司丝适量</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>馅料</strong></span><br />
</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">培根 100克<br />
胡椒粉 2克<br />
洋葱 100克<br />
彩椒 50克<br />
盐 2克</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1. 培根切碎、洋葱切碎、彩椒切丁备用。</span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #003366;"><br />
<span style="color: #000000;">? ? TIPS 培根可自行替换成喜欢的肉类。</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #003366;"><br />
<span style="color: #000000;">2. 先将培根炒香，再放入洋葱、彩椒，加入胡椒、盐调味，炒熟备用。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12582635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12582635" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/36b4671b619c8417b3fa190e7645e8bf.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12582635" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/36b4671b619c8417b3fa190e7645e8bf-450x267.png" alt="彩椒" width="450" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12582635" class="wp-caption-text">先将培根炒香，再放入洋葱、彩椒，加入胡椒、盐调味（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #003366;"> <span style="color: #000000;"> 3. 鲜奶油与奶油乳酪，隔水加热融化。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12582582" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12582582" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/a8198c2d16e4ccf444acdf9c0a98c891.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12582582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/a8198c2d16e4ccf444acdf9c0a98c891-450x213.jpg" alt="隔水加热鲜奶油" width="450" b="213" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12582582" class="wp-caption-text">鲜奶油与奶油乳酪，隔水加热融化。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #003366;"> <span style="color: #000000;"> 4. 冷却后加入鸡蛋，拌匀成奶油糊。</span><br />
<span style="color: #000000;"> 5. 培根肉填入派皮，表面撒上帕玛森起司丝。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_12582631" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12582631" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/630be0566055985cf560fd4d5c139831.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12582631" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/630be0566055985cf560fd4d5c139831-450x328.png" alt="帕玛森起司丝" width="450" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12582631" class="wp-caption-text">培根肉填入派皮，表面撒上帕玛森起司丝，倒入奶油糊约9分满。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #003366;"> <span style="color: #000000;"> 6. 倒入奶油糊约9分满，放入烤箱180℃烘烤30</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">～</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">35分钟即完成。</span></span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><b>6吋派皮制作</b></span></h2>
<p>香酥的派皮，与各种馅料都百搭。</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>使用模具</strong>：6吋活动派模</span></p>
<p><strong>份量</strong>：1个</p>
<p><strong>营养成分</strong>：糖25.12克、碳水化合物25.12克、膳食纤维0克、糖6.79克、蛋白质34.53克。</p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">蛋黄 1颗</span><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">冷藏无盐奶油 70克</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;"><strong>粉料</strong></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">杏仁粉 130克<br />
赤藻糖 10克<br />
海盐 1克</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 奶油捏成玉米粒状，加入所有粉料，指腹搓成颗粒粉状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12572436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12572436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fee31d8afdf40684a286ac688936deab.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12572436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fee31d8afdf40684a286ac688936deab-450x304.png" alt="粉料" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12572436" class="wp-caption-text">加入所有粉料，指腹搓成颗粒粉状。（图／ 橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 加入蛋黄，手搓成面团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12572431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12572431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/c3aac8ee6cc92ae1a963c393448421be.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12572431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/c3aac8ee6cc92ae1a963c393448421be-450x311.png" alt="面团" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12572431" class="wp-caption-text">加入蛋黄，手搓成面团。（图／ 橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 按压入派模中，由边绕圈填满，表面用叉子插小洞备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12572428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12572428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6d3373f45e1054fd307bbfb1639c4936.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12572428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6d3373f45e1054fd307bbfb1639c4936-450x341.png" alt="派皮" width="450" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12572428" class="wp-caption-text">派皮表面用叉子插小洞备用。（图／ 橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #0000ff;">贴心提醒</span></h3>
<p>1. 避免手温过高造成奶油融化，制作的派皮会不酥脆。<br />
2. 可依不同食谱状况，先烤熟派皮或同时与馅料一起烤。<br />
3. 调整赤藻糖与海盐的比例，可以做成甜派或咸派。</p>
