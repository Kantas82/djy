<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金马57群星耀红毯 小镁大霈白礼服优雅脱俗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210713471487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第57届金马奖于11月21日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，除了得奖名单揭晓，星光红毯也是外界关注焦点。图为桂纶镁（左）与李霈瑜（大霈）。（中央社／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年11月21日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）第57届金马奖21日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，优雅清丽的白色系礼服成为本届大势美色，包括金马影后<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%82%E7%BA%B6%E9%95%81.md#1">桂纶镁</a>（小镁）、<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%AC%A3%E9%A2%96.md#1">谢欣颖</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%9C%88%E7%91%9C.md#1">李霈瑜</a>（大霈）等都穿上白色系礼服，散发不同的优雅风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12565936" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12565936" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210716591487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12565936" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210716591487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="644" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12565936" class="wp-caption-text">第57届金马奖颁奖典礼21日晚间7时起在台北登场，以电影《腿》入围最佳女主角的<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%82%E7%BA%B6%E9%95%81.md#1">桂纶镁</a>身穿一袭纯白礼服亮相红毯。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桂纶镁穿着白色纱质礼服，散发知性脱俗的氛围；<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E6%AC%A3%E9%A2%96.md#1">谢欣颖</a>以一袭白色镶缀珠宝百褶礼服亮相，洋溢甜美复古的风采；大霈选择银白马甲风礼服，闪耀动人又高雅；星光大道主持人杨千霈的白色礼服则镶缀水钻与流苏，典雅秀丽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12565937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12565937" style="width: 458px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210636521487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12565937" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210636521487.jpg" alt="" width="458" b="622" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12565937" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC57.md#1">金马57</a>星光红毯21日傍晚在台北国父纪念馆展开，演员谢欣颖今年以电影《怪胎》入围影后，身穿白色长裙开心现身红毯。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12565938" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12565938" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210710371487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12565938" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210710371487.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12565938" class="wp-caption-text">第57届金马奖颁奖典礼21日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，以电影《消失的情人节》入围最佳女主角的<ahref="https://github.com/yacvxo3623/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E9%9C%88%E7%91%9C.md#1">李霈瑜</a>身穿白色礼服气质典雅，开心登上红毯。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李心洁挑选刺绣花朵纱质礼服，宛如把成熟甜美的秋季花园穿上身；徐若瑄一袭抢眼桃红垫肩礼服，明艳照人；颁奖嘉宾郭书瑶穿着粉黑拼接礼服，甜美融合个性感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12565939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12565939" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210702421487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12565939" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210702421487.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="637" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12565939" class="wp-caption-text">第57届金马奖颁奖典礼于11月21日晚间在台北国父纪念馆举行，星光大道抢先登场，图为颁奖嘉宾的李心洁盛装出席走红毯。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12565941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12565941" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210705491487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12565941" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2011210705491487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="649" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12565941" class="wp-caption-text">第57届金马奖星光大道21日傍晚登场，艺人徐若瑄一身桃红色深V开衩礼服十分抢镜。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
