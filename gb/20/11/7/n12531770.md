<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚马逊实体店落户尔湾 有3种购物方式</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0493-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为亚马逊店的蔬菜和水果区域。（李梅／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年11月07日讯】（大纪元记者李梅橙县报导）10月底，<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E9%80%8A%E5%AE%9E%E4%BD%93%E5%BA%97.md#1">亚马逊实体店</a>（Amazon Fresh）在橙县尔湾市的The Market Place购物区域开张了，刚开始时很难从地图上找到其位置，这两天已经标注的很清楚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531840" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0507.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0507-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531840" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E9%80%8A.md#1">亚马逊</a>在橙县尔湾的实体店。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12531823" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0497.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0497-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531823" class="wp-caption-text">图为<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E9%80%8A.md#1">亚马逊</a>店的蔬菜区域。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12531848" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0479.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0479-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531848" class="wp-caption-text">亚马逊店里的饮料和食品柜。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12531886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0502.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0502-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531886" class="wp-caption-text">亚马逊店里的鲜花区域。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_12531894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_04992.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_04992-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531894" class="wp-caption-text">亚马逊店里的鲜花。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>桑尼（Sanny）11月6日是第一次去该店，她说感觉很好，里面的空间很大，东西也很多，可能是新店，比普通超市的人少。营业时间从早上7:00至晚上10:00。</p>
<h2>到<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E9%80%8A%E5%AE%9E%E4%BD%93%E5%BA%97.md#1">亚马逊实体店</a>，有三种<ahref="https://github.com/kobfyi3602/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F.md#1">购物方式</a>：</h2>
<p>拉丁裔阿郎索（音译）坐着轮椅车进来购物，她觉得和在常规的超市购物一样，挺方便的。阿郎索说是侄子告诉她来这个店的；还有一位华裔女士，也是她的孩子先知道后告诉她来的。这或许是年轻人更喜欢亚马逊这种网上购物，从而早得知的信息。</p>
<p>第二种就是使用亚马逊的卡购物，可以减少等待付款的时间。桑尼说：“你需要使用亚马逊专门的购物车，每拿一件商品就输一下编码，那个车可以根据重量来判定拿商品时是否扫码了，而且有亚马逊的卡（连着信用卡）就不必在柜台等着支付了，就从信用卡直接扣了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531881" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531881" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0486.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0486-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531881" class="wp-caption-text">亚马逊店里的提货和退货处。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5日晚，记者还看到几位顾客在等候这种购物车，可能大多数人还需要询问一下怎么使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0474.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12531850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/IMG_0474-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531850" class="wp-caption-text">亚马逊的可以扫码的购物车。（李梅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>记者问店里的员工，如果有人没带卡怎么办？那位员工说，你可以下载Amazon的应用，用手机扫一下二维码就行。</p>
<p>第三种是网上购物，店里面有好几位亚马逊的员工都推着亚马逊专用的购物车，记者开始以为他们在快速上货，一问才知是为网上购物的人们捡货。据了解，如果顾客购买一定金额的商品或有免运输卡，那还是挺划算和方便的。</p>
<p>店里的东西和一般美式超市类似，也有现做出的美式食品，不过只能打包带走。</p>
<p>这里还有亚马逊的提货和退货处，但对商品的尺寸有限制。尺寸要求内的，凡是在网上和店里购买的商品都可以在这里退货，也可选择在这里提货。如果你住在附近，可以来购物时顺便把商品提走。◇</p>
