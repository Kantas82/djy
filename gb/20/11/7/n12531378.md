<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不用排队！在家做香甜松软“蜂蜜奶香吐司”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/0a32618823029132fa3a9db594d57c32-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>口感绵密，奶香中有淡淡的甜味是蜂蜜吐司迷人的地方。（图／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>在家烘培<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">吐司</a>要零失败，配方、作法都很重要，太困难的作法让人无法理解，没有手把手的食谱图更无法明白。现在不用伤脑筋，这一份烘培图说可以满足你的需求，步骤仔细又简易。<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a><ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">牛奶吐司</a>是老少皆宜的主食、点心，平常有空做一些起来，冷藏保存可以放4~5天，健康、卫生又方便！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>牛奶小<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">吐司</a></span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">牛奶吐司</a>，是单吃或搭配各种食材都很好吃的吐司。</p>
<p>这款面团使用蜂蜜代替细砂糖，而蜂蜜口味可以选用自己喜欢的，例如甜度跟香气都最浓郁的龙眼蜜，或者尾韵带着花香的<ahref="https://github.com/thmxpb3749/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%94%E6%9E%9D%E8%9C%9C.md#1">荔枝蜜</a>，打造出自己的特色吐司。</p>
<p>另外，在吐司面团中添加蜂蜜，能让吐司的口感更湿润，也让烤色更好看。</p>
<p>份量 ?: 4条/每条150g</p>
<p>模具 ?: 银色水果条模 4个</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">前置作业?</span></h3>
<p>将银色水果条模内部均匀涂上奶油备用。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<h4><strong>面团?A</strong></h4>
<p>高筋面粉 295g<br />
低筋面粉 35g<br />
盐 6g<br />
奶粉 10g<br />
酵母粉 3.3g<br />
水 100g<br />
牛奶 115g<br />
蜂蜜 33g</p>
<p><strong>B</strong></p>
<p>奶油 26g</p>
<p><strong>表面装饰</strong></p>
<p>奶油 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法 Steps</span></h3>
<p>1、将A材料倒入钢盆中，搅拌至面团表面光滑有扩展的状态，再加入奶油，搅拌至面团有薄膜后，基本发酵60分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad2281c2e7fec407d9fd5a97e886dc6c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531411 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad2281c2e7fec407d9fd5a97e886dc6c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531411" class="wp-caption-text">将A材料倒入钢盆中，搅拌至面团表面光滑有扩展的状态，再加入B奶油搅拌。（图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2、将面团分割成12个（每个50g），分别滚圆后，进行中间发酵15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/31fc2992da382ae6e144ed4fb7e74a92.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531409 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/31fc2992da382ae6e144ed4fb7e74a92-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531409" class="wp-caption-text">将面团分别滚圆后，进行中间发酵15分钟。 （图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3、将面团开成长方形，左右两边往中间折并用手掌根轻压排气。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531410" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4a697722f017f991978f903e23f3c659.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531410 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4a697722f017f991978f903e23f3c659-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531410" class="wp-caption-text">用手掌根轻压排气面团。（图文／日日幸福）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4、由上往下卷，最后将面团收口，放入烤模型中，做最后发酵30～40分钟，或发酵至满模。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531431" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5884743eadac82abea42b837c7665ef6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531431 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5884743eadac82abea42b837c7665ef6-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531431" class="wp-caption-text">由上往下卷，最后将面团收口。（图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5、烤箱预热190℃，放入烤箱烤20分钟，烤好后取出并在面团表面刷上奶油即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12531408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12531408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11879e20b8e118bfd32b4316242e54b8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531408 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11879e20b8e118bfd32b4316242e54b8-450x298.jpg" alt="吐司" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12531408" class="wp-caption-text">在烤好的吐司上方涂抹奶油。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
