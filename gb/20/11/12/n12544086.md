<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【电锅料理】一次出2道菜～韩式排骨、南瓜烧肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3b325e78105ba1b7185d18444b8c7171-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一锅到底，韩式排骨在家就可以做，简单又省时。（图／日日幸福提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>是不是常常觉得在厨房手忙脚乱却没办法煮一桌好菜？现在，不用油烟、大火烹饪、注意炉火，只要一个按键就有2份营养不油腻的特色菜上桌，准备好食材、调味料和一只电锅就行了！当然，还需要一份好的食谱，这样才是真正零失败的省时美味料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12411988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12411988" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/pumpkin-benefit-healthy-recipe-1-hnw-health11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12411988" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/pumpkin-benefit-healthy-recipe-1-hnw-health11-450x260.jpg" alt="南瓜减肥吃法：吃南瓜减肥，最简单的方法是用电锅蒸南瓜。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12411988" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>最简单的料理方法，就是“用电锅”蒸南瓜！（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">韩式<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">排骨</a>丶黑糖<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>烧鸡肉（份量2-3人）</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">菜色技巧说明</span></h3>
<p>1、肋骨可先腌渍起来冰在冰箱，要料理时在拿出来使用喔，是不是很方便！<br />
2、南瓜外皮洗刷干净，营养美味不流失喔。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">韩式<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8.md#1">排骨</a></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>猪肋排3只<br />
蒜末10g<br />
姜末10g<br />
洋葱丝30g<br />
香油1大匙<br />
香菜10g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料</span></h3>
<p>米酒2大匙<br />
素蚝油2大匙<br />
韩式辣酱1大匙<br />
白胡椒粉1小匙<br />
糖1茶匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">黑糖南瓜烧鸡肉</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12551193" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12551193" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1ac98095b016f2fbd262a4f31ddf0bec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12551193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1ac98095b016f2fbd262a4f31ddf0bec-450x403.jpg" alt="南瓜" width="450" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12551193" class="wp-caption-text">黑糖南瓜烧鸡肉，一点都不困难的料理。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>鸡翅3只<br />
南瓜块100g<br />
中姜片10片<br />
红枣6颗<br />
香油1大匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">调味料</span></h3>
<p>米酒2大匙<br />
酱油膏2大匙<br />
黑糖1大匙<br />
白胡椒粉1小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>
<p>1、先将猪肋排用叉子叉洞，在取调味料1丶蒜末丶姜末丶洋葱丝丶香油抓匀，放在蒸盘上备用。<br />
2、鸡翅画刀和姜片一起用香油煎香，再和南瓜丶红枣放入蒸盘，再下调味料2拌匀。<br />
3、电锅加2杯水，在放入蒸架和2道菜，在盖上电锅盖按按钮蒸煮即可，最好撒上香菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12551194" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12551194" style="width: 115px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8319305ade7e8113584963c6965e3c7b.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12551194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8319305ade7e8113584963c6965e3c7b-450x586.jpg" alt="书封" width="115" b="150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12551194" class="wp-caption-text">《料理神手吴秉承的万用电锅食谱》书封。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>本文摘自：《<ahref="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010827721?sloc=main" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">料理神手吴秉承的万用电锅食谱：一锅出两菜、低温烹调、减糖料理，电锅一次搞定！同场加映五辛蔬食料理，美味更健康！</a>》，日日幸福提供。</h3>
