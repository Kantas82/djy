<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【圈妈烹饪笔记】低糖萝卜糕～瘦18公斤的食谱之一</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/c7d8d90f03f17d541b3aa22e1fcc6a1b-305x400.jpg" />
<h6>生活化的食谱，让你轻松上桌～低糖萝卜糕（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这道<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8E%E7%B3%96.md#1">低糖</a><ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C%E7%B3%95.md#1">萝卜糕</a>也可以自行变换食谱，比方虾米、肉末等不同食材。蒸好后可以直接食用，品尝萝卜原味香气，不管冷热都好吃。或是煎到表面微焦，蘸上蒜末酱油，滋味更甚。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>白萝卜 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 250g<br />
干香菇 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 5 ～ 8g<br />
橄榄油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 10g<br />
鹅油香葱 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 20g<br />
白胡椒粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 1g<br />
玫瑰盐 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 2g<br />
开水 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..80ml</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料A】</span></h2>
<p>烘焙杏仁粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 100g<br />
洋车前子细粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 10g<br />
中型鸡蛋 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 2 颗<br />
开水 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;..80ml</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1 将白萝卜切成丝，干香菇泡发后切碎。<br />
2 橄榄油热锅，放入香菇爆香一下，再加入萝卜丝拌炒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P55-3.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340441 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P55-3-450x344.png" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><br />
3 加入鹅油香葱、白胡椒粉、玫瑰盐继续拌炒，再加入开水，煮至萝卜丝软化。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P55-4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340442 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P55-4-450x347.png" alt="" width="450" b="347" /></a></p>
<p>4 等待萝卜丝软化的空档，将材料A 拌匀备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340445 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-1-450x323.png" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a></p>
<p>5 取一个600ml 长型模具，在四周和底部刷上橄榄油（材料分量外）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340446 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-2-450x324.png" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a></p>
<p>6 将步骤4 拌匀的材料，加入煮熟的步骤3 中，翻炒均匀至呈现收汁黏稠状即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-3.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340447 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-3-450x324.png" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340448 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-4-450x323.png" alt="" width="450" b="323" /></a></p>
<p>7 将萝卜糊填入模具中，用力压密实。将模具放入电锅内，外锅加入1 杯水，蒸煮30分钟。将竹签插入<ahref="https://github.com/tnpikr314/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C%E7%B3%95.md#1">萝卜糕</a>中央，取出无沾黏即代表已煮熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340449 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-5-450x324.png" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a></p>
<p>8 冷却后即可脱模，直接切片食用，或是再煎至外皮酥脆都很美味。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-6.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12340451 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/P56-6-450x324.png" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a></p>
