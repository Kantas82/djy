<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南加州侨团与侨领赴洛侨中心拜会新主任</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/ced901084628725d5c3ccaf3045ac62f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>南加州中文学校联合会拜会洛侨中心。（洛侨中心）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年08月06日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>华侨文教服务中心主任<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E7%9A%93%E9%92%A7.md#1">张皓钧</a>于7月份赴任履新，因考量南加州地区<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>（COVID-19）疫情严峻，遂以简单精致的会谈形式，于<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E4%BE%A8%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">洛侨中心</a>与各侨团、侨领晤叙，就侨务政策以及各社团未来发展方向广泛交换意见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12310300" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12310300" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2be38aa9c2a20a7c039577c4a7c8e48c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12310300" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2be38aa9c2a20a7c039577c4a7c8e48c-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12310300" class="wp-caption-text">南加州大专院校联合会拜会<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E4%BE%A8%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">洛侨中心</a>。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12310302" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12310302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/18943914c3bdc5eb4830ad96205de91d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12310302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/18943914c3bdc5eb4830ad96205de91d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12310302" class="wp-caption-text">世华南加分会拜会洛侨中心。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E7%9A%93%E9%92%A7.md#1">张皓钧</a>表示，因应时代变迁以及疫情影响，侨务委员会持续求新求变，侨务委员会童（振源）委员长积极推动以新科技扩大服务侨胞、促进侨胞与台湾各领域的连结与发展，期许侨务委员会扮演杠杆支点，运用台湾优势结合全球侨胞力量，并透过单一窗口与服务平台，汇聚侨胞力量。</p>
<p>此外，张皓钧赞许南加州以及亚利桑那地区侨界能量充沛，各侨团与商会组织过去几个月来积极筹募防疫物资，协助主流社群、第一线医疗警消人员、学区、弱势团体等单位，推动国民外交，并将传统集会活动改以线上方式举办，维持联系密度、分享新知。海外青年志工（FASCA）与各侨校亦在短期内积极转型，办理线上培训与华语文教育，创意无限。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12310309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12310309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/b1d582a3f9784f64613475f7feb72f5e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12310309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/b1d582a3f9784f64613475f7feb72f5e-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12310309" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>侨界急难救助协会拜会洛侨中心。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12310312" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12310312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/364ebb99270938f65337d36070e22efa.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12310312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/364ebb99270938f65337d36070e22efa-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12310312" class="wp-caption-text">世华大洛杉矶分会拜会洛侨中心。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12310313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12310313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3d9a12b89ae51ddbd409446afad33dd6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12310313" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3d9a12b89ae51ddbd409446afad33dd6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12310313" class="wp-caption-text">加台联拜会洛侨中心。（洛侨中心）</figcaption></figure>
<p>各侨团及侨领除了表达对侨务政策的关心，对于洛侨中心各项具体活动规划也提出宝贵建议，双方以务实的态度，共同研究在疫情与后疫情时期如何共同推展侨务工作，促进侨社永续传承。◇</p>
