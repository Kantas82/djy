<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>协助台湾人  侨界急难救助协会办第二届年会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/169396c9a830cbe8955b55c18f5eb9e6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>王金智（左二）、林慧绮（右一）分别连任大纽约区侨界急难救助协会理事长和会长，经文处处长徐俪文代表侨务委员长吴新兴（右二）向两位颁发奖状。左一为纽约华侨文教中心主任黄正杰。（黄小堂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月10日讯】（大纪元记者黄小堂美国纽约报导）<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6%E5%8C%BA%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C%E6%80%A5%E9%9A%BE%E6%95%91%E5%8A%A9%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">大纽约区侨界急难救助协会</a>9日晚在法拉盛举办第二届年会暨理事长会长就职典礼，王金智及林慧绮分别连任理事长和会长。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857418" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857418" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b4cd0e05773a42f7d8927447c838a1fc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b4cd0e05773a42f7d8927447c838a1fc-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857418" class="wp-caption-text">嘉宾们合影，左起：市议员顾雅明、州参议员史塔文斯基、经文处处长徐俪文、纽约中华总商会董事长于金山。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>驻纽约经济文化办事处处长徐俪文前来祝贺，她致词表示，美东地区有四个急难救助协会，从点到面、四面八方皆有该会的成员协助台湾人，希望彼此合作，将今天工作做得更好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6cae23925362963f32062e392ccd68af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6cae23925362963f32062e392ccd68af-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857417" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E7%BA%BD%E7%BA%A6%E5%8C%BA%E4%BE%A8%E7%95%8C%E6%80%A5%E9%9A%BE%E6%95%91%E5%8A%A9%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">大纽约区侨界急难救助协会</a>所有理监事和组长们为大家献唱“你是我兄弟”。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11857412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/20f34c529732c208ecf53f4628e3f6d5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/20f34c529732c208ecf53f4628e3f6d5-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857412" class="wp-caption-text">图为参加大纽约区侨界急难救助协会第二届年会的嘉宾合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>理事长王金智介绍，该会2017年5月在侨务委员长吴新兴的协助下成立，两年来办理海外侨胞和留学生的急难救助个案超一百多件，快速、有效地联系和沟通，并作出即时支援和救助。会长和各组长也做了诸多关怀和探访的案例，“在92个急难救助协会中首屈一指”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857414" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/f69e21a3da62a76257e1ff0ac382afb6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/f69e21a3da62a76257e1ff0ac382afb6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857414" class="wp-caption-text">州参议员史塔文斯基（中）向理事长王金智（左）和会长林慧绮颁发褒奖。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>会长林慧绮提到，该会在去年举办多场服务当地社区的活动，例如为社区两百多户家庭安装烟雾警报器、举办“个人防灾及急难救助紧急应变计划”讲座。她将于今年四月也将代表协会，回台湾参加急难救助的第一届年会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857415" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2a61dd7d4a3649b2771c06426fb07fc8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2a61dd7d4a3649b2771c06426fb07fc8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857415" class="wp-caption-text">关怀组组长蒋珊珊分享急难救助的个案和经验。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关怀组组长蒋珊珊则分享，协会帮助一位在纽约旅游时发生急性肠胃炎的27岁台湾女子，她没有投保旅游险，在人生地不熟的纽约要就医怎么办？所幸她在协会的多位理事和经文处的协助下，顺利至医院就医，并顺利继续纽约的旅游行程。 ◇</p>
