<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法拉盛美食节聚人气  拉抬小商家生意</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/0f29a17769f3209fa25254da79a5039a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>民众排队凭票券领取各式亚裔美食。（黄小堂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月10日讯】（大纪元记者黄小堂美国纽约报导）<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B%E5%95%86%E6%94%B9%E5%8C%BA.md#1">法拉盛商改区</a>（Flushing BID）和市小商业服务局（SBS）合作于8日举办“2020<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">美食节</a>”，吸引了男女老少逾400位民众前来品尝超过千份美食。商改区余钿崧表示，对<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>（新冠病毒）疫情的心理影响已造成法拉盛商家生意下滑，冀望这个皇后区最大的亚裔美食节能重振并带动当地经济。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857424" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4bd132885f04231b3c65e7eee9c8a0bc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4bd132885f04231b3c65e7eee9c8a0bc-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857424" class="wp-caption-text">民众排队凭票券领取各式亚裔美食。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11857425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857425" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/e8ccc1e797e61dbe8abd1a98d59bf7b3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/e8ccc1e797e61dbe8abd1a98d59bf7b3-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857425" class="wp-caption-text">民众排队凭票券领取“好想吃台湾小吃”的炒米粉、咸酥鸡等美食。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上活动还没开始，已经有不少民众至喜来登大酒店排队，民众可凭票券品尝从台湾、日本和汇集中国大江南北的亚裔美食，包括红豆饼、水果茶、盐酥鸡、珍珠奶茶、凉皮、煎饼、肉夹馍、炸鱿鱼、卤味、摩摩喳喳、芋头糕、寿司等小吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857429" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857429" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2ecc4001e7c45088a3d0dbbc402ca23e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2ecc4001e7c45088a3d0dbbc402ca23e-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857429" class="wp-caption-text">民众排队凭票券领取“开心红豆饼”车轮饼甜点。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当天参与活动商家包括一芳台湾水果茶、好想吃台湾小吃、开心红豆饼、老洛阳凉皮、潼关肉夹馍、Fresh Sushi、川霸王、马来西亚美食、Eggcellent舒芙蕾、头壹号杂粮脆皮煎饼、真姜乐公司等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857430" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857430" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2c884cba9520ed567335dd9fbbea05e8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857430" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2c884cba9520ed567335dd9fbbea05e8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857430" class="wp-caption-text">华裔民众积极参加<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E8%8A%82.md#1">美食节</a>，品尝台湾、日本和汇集中国大江南北的亚裔美食。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>商改区执行主任余钿崧介绍，<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a>疫情（武汉肺炎）造成社区商家的生意影响，各行各业皆努力降低疫情的冲击，并透过不同渠道吸引平常较少接触的顾客上门消费；他说在一开始发放票券时就发觉民众索取票券踊跃，资讯亭和网路两个渠道合计发放逾600张，在目前武汉肺炎影响下，还有四百多位民众踊跃参加支持，感到美食节活动办得非常成功。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11857428" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11857428" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c45393d80c45638ce9d72d3a693cc632.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11857428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c45393d80c45638ce9d72d3a693cc632-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11857428" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B%E5%95%86%E6%94%B9%E5%8C%BA.md#1">法拉盛商改区</a>8日举办“2020法拉盛美食节”。图为全体嘉宾合影。（黄小堂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动现场各个摊位前都排满了民众，商家准备的餐点在一个小时后就销售一空。市议员顾雅明表示，虽然目前纽约市没有新冠病毒的确诊病例，预防之道可从最基本的措施做起：多洗手、遮挡咳嗽和喷嚏、有状况即时致电医护人员等，“鼓励大家消费”。◇</p>
