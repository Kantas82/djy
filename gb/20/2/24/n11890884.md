<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>义气狗狗陪小主人罚站 暖举让爸妈瞬间消气</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b7114905e7e2dbde74aa2a3f3dea1d41-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>裴顿喜欢与小戴腻在一起。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月24日讯】（大纪元记者赖依琴报导）来自<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>加州<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%BA%E7%93%A6%E5%85%8B.md#1">诺瓦克</a>（Norwalk）的小男孩裴顿（Peyton）与忠心的<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>小戴（Dash）是一对有福同享、有难同当的好<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%84%E5%BC%9F.md#1">兄弟</a>。日前，裴顿因为跟姊姊打闹而被罚站，狗狗小戴看到兄弟被处罚，便默默的靠过去，裴顿也把手搭在小戴身上，狗狗义气相挺的陪伴小主人直到“重获自由”，这一幕让妈妈看到直呼：“这画面可爱到我无法继续生气！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890886" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5e5639843bedc2d762ef4beb94f422a6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890886 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5e5639843bedc2d762ef4beb94f422a6-450x605.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="605" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890886" class="wp-caption-text">当小主人被罚站时，小戴默默地靠在他身边陪伴着他。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小时候被爸妈惩罚时，如果有人陪在身旁安慰，心里肯定会好受一些！裴顿因为跟姊姊发生争吵，爸妈便决定让他去“面壁思过”，当裴顿站在墙边时，与他一起长大的<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>小戴便靠了过去，蹲坐在他的旁边陪伴着他，裴顿也将手搭在小戴身上，一人一狗依偎在墙边。</p>
<p>裴顿的妈妈吉莉安（Jillian Marie Smith）说：“当看到裴顿把手臂环绕着小戴时，这画面可爱到我无法继续生气。”她接着说：“我想，当裴顿在接受惩罚时，小戴知道‘裴顿需要我’。”当然，因为义气相挺的小戴，也让裴顿的惩罚得以提早结束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890887" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9d3d2edd6c22b3e1f622be12661f7392.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890887 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9d3d2edd6c22b3e1f622be12661f7392-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890887" class="wp-caption-text">裴顿从小跟小戴一起长大。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890889" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/00626f6e55f5b8740ce7b9dd00bdaf33.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890889 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/00626f6e55f5b8740ce7b9dd00bdaf33-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890889" class="wp-caption-text">裴顿妈妈说，时常可以看到他们俩腻在一起。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小戴是一只英国獒犬，从裴顿还是三个月的婴儿时，就陪伴着他到现在。裴顿妈妈告诉《大纪元》，当裴顿要出门时，他会对小戴说：“没事的，我很快就会回了，别难过。”而当裴顿回家时也会马上对小戴说：“<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%84%E5%BC%9F.md#1">兄弟</a>，我回来了～～我好想你啊！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/29d645f49164af13bfc94e48e5c115b0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11890891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/29d645f49164af13bfc94e48e5c115b0-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890891" class="wp-caption-text">裴顿喜欢与小戴玩各种游戏。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890895" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ec98327c2137b1126438c485f3076271.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890895 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ec98327c2137b1126438c485f3076271-450x571.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="571" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890895" class="wp-caption-text">一日，当裴顿一直玩电动时，小戴就吃醋了，想要裴顿陪它玩。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890896" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1e2597a83ea317e5e1536f184a3cf586.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890896 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1e2597a83ea317e5e1536f184a3cf586-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890896" class="wp-caption-text">小戴想找裴顿一起玩。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fjillian.meadows.7%2Fvideos%2F3343384179010056%2F&amp;show_text=0&amp;width=267" width="267" b="476" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">▲裴顿专注在游乐器上，小戴在旁边“搔扰”他，希望男孩停下来！</p>
<p>裴顿妈妈说，儿子告诉她，小戴是他最棒的“伙伴”，妈妈非常开心在他们的家庭中，有小戴这么甜蜜的存在，她说：“小戴为这个家庭带来了许多欢乐，尤其是对裴顿而言更是如此”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11890894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890894" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2152280627dca889501518c2b2abc8d5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890894 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2152280627dca889501518c2b2abc8d5-450x724.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="724" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890894" class="wp-caption-text">不只跟小戴玩乐，裴顿也很照顾它，天冷时试着帮它加衣服。（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11890892" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11890892" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b4714a8363d2f0535e0d353e25b3470f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11890892 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b4714a8363d2f0535e0d353e25b3470f-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11890892" class="wp-caption-text">出门时，裴顿会对小戴说：“没事的，我很快就会回来，别难过。”（Jillian Marie Smith 授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
