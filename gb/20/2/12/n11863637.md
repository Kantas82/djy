<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】鲍鱼羊肉四物汤 居家常备药膳</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲍鱼羊肉四物汤。（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></strong><br />
急冻墨西哥<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%8D%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲍鱼</a>一只（约4份3磅）<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>1磅半<br />
姜片3块<br />
四物补虚方一包（70克）<br />
水8杯<br />
盐适量<br />
花雕酒适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_11863696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863696" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-13-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11863696 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼,羊肉,姜片" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863696" class="wp-caption-text">急冻墨西哥<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%8D%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲍鱼</a>一只、<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8A%E8%82%89.md#1">羊肉</a>1磅半、姜片3块。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11863652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-17.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11863652 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-17-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,四物补虚汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863652" class="wp-caption-text">四物补虚方成分包含当归、川芎、熟地、白芍、黄芪、桂枝、党参、杜仲、黑枣和枸杞，共70克。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span><br />
1.鲍鱼解冻后，用毛刷刷洗干净，剪去鲍鱼内脏，一个切开成两块。用姜汁酒烫过后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11863670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863670" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11863670 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,鲍鱼,羊肉" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863670" class="wp-caption-text">鲍鱼解冻后，用毛刷刷洗干净，剪去鲍鱼内脏，一个切开成两块。用姜汁酒烫过后备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.羊肉用姜汁酒烫过后，再清洗干净备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11863659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11863659 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,四物补虚汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863659" class="wp-caption-text">羊肉用姜汁酒烫过后，再清洗干净备用。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11863663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863663" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11863663 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,羊肉,鲍鱼,四物补虚汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863663" class="wp-caption-text">加入所有材料，大约炖4小时左右，之后加少许盐和花雕酒。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.慢炖锅内放入煮滚的8碗水，加入所有材料，盖上锅盖，大约炖4小时左右，之后加少许盐和花雕酒，便可食用。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></strong><br />
? 养生药膳汤煮好后，可放置数小时或隔夜，重新用炉火加热再食用，味道会更入味，养生效果更佳。（中药材忌用微波炉加热）</p>
<p>? 四物补虚方成分包含当归、川芎、熟地、白芍、黄芪、桂枝、党参、杜仲、黑枣和枸杞，共70克。若是素食者，可用素料取代荤肉，熬煮时加1茶匙苦茶油，以确保脂溶性成分有效溶出。</p>
<p>? 四物补虚方除了活血和补血之外，还可以补虚，比一般<ahref="https://github.com/kpdqbw323/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9B%E7%89%A9%E6%B1%A4.md#1">四物汤</a>更适合体质虚弱，容易感冒、腰酸背痛和常感疲劳者食用，是适合居家常备的药膳。调补养生，四季皆宜。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11863651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11863651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11863651 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,四物补虚汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11863651" class="wp-caption-text">四物补虚方除了活血和补血之外，还可以补虚，是适合居家常备的药膳。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
