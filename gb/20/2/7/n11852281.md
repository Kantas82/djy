<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】年菜系列之六：煎酿鲈鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/cec423e1789f7fbf2a5bc03f987e8372-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨做的“煎酿鲈鱼”，肉馅吸收鱼汁鲜味，鱼肉吸收五花肉的鲜美，外面甘香酥脆，里面香滑可口。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>鲈鱼营养丰富、味道鲜美，古淞江鲈鱼、长江鲥鱼、黄河鲤鱼和巢湖银鱼被誉为“四大名鱼”，历代赞誉松江鲈美味的诗词歌赋、传说颇多。《三国演义》中“左慈掷杯戏曹操”的故事，说明鲈鱼早在三国时期已闻名天下。据传说，吴人献松江鲈于炀帝，炀帝品尝后，大加赞赏，称其为“金齑玉脍，东南佳味也”。时至清代，鲈鱼被康熙、乾隆品尝后，被誉为“江南第一名菜”，其名声更为远扬。</p>
<p>历代本草多记载：鲈鱼对人体有良好的滋补作用，具有补肝肾、益脾胃以及化痰止咳之功效。</p>
<p>据分析，鲈鱼自身含蛋白质、脂肪、钙、磷、铁、铜、VA、VB1、VB2等成分。鱼皮又有丰富胶质，因此能补充营养、调理伤口、保健身体。由于鲈鱼血中含有较多的铜元素，因此食用鲈鱼，对脾胃虚弱、食少体倦或气血不足、脾虚水肿、肝肾不足、筋骨不健、胎动不安等症状有良好的治疗作用。此外，鲈鱼肉中含有丰富的铁元素，如食用时配以醋、酱油或柠檬，更容易消化吸收。</p>
<p>鱼是喜庆宴客出现的高档鱼。罗厨的这道“<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%85%BF%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md">煎酿鲈鱼</a>”，肉馅吸收鱼汁鲜味，鱼肉吸收五花肉的鲜美，外面甘香酥脆，里面香滑可口，更会让您的餐桌平添一道亮丽、喜庆的色彩。</p>
<p>但是，罗厨提醒您，鲈鱼的肝不能食用，在食用前切记祛除干净。另外，患有皮肤病或疮肿者忌食鲈鱼。鲈鱼忌与牛羊油、奶酪和中药荆芥同食。</p>
<h2><a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E9%85%BF%E9%B2%88%E9%B1%BC.md">煎酿鲈鱼</a></h2>
<p>主料：鲈鱼1条约1.5磅、五花肉7两。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c7943226e7f00c20e6b28b70e3416416.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c7943226e7f00c20e6b28b70e3416416-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：葱1根切粒、姜末2汤匙、香菇4切粒、腊肉粒2汤匙、香菜粒1汤匙。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/a6073cc00eb9794988c5af1b7ae9bb84.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/a6073cc00eb9794988c5af1b7ae9bb84-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>调料：盐1茶匙、糖0.5茶匙、胡椒粉0.5茶匙、蚝油1汤匙、生粉1.5汤匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 鲈鱼洗净，去掉主骨和鱼腩及鱼翅的骨。<a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 五花肉剁成粒。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 添加葱、姜、香菇等配料。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 添加盐、糖、胡椒粉等调料。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 将所有食材搅拌均匀，打至起胶。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加水4汤匙，再打至起胶。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 在鱼身和内部洒入少许胡椒粉和1/3茶匙盐。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将五花肉馅酿入鱼肚中，归为鱼形。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 鱼身上再撒上1汤匙生粉。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>10. 猛火阴油，将撒好生粉的鱼放入锅中。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 将鲈鱼慢火煎熟，一般15分钟即可。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11852323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
