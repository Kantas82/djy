<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】五柳炸蛋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2ba689e6c165fd61c7abb88b8bbc7ae4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨烹制的“五柳炸蛋”，外脆里嫩，甜酸可口，香脆嫩滑的鸡蛋中融入了略带柠檬汁的奇特酸果味。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>传统粤菜“<a href="https://github.com/xgqfwa2071/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%9F%B3%E7%82%B8%E8%9B%8B.md">五柳炸蛋</a>”，老广东人很熟悉，大热天去茶餐厅，一份五柳炸蛋、一份青菜、一盅炖汤，简简单单，美味可口，好不惬意。据说饭馆里是十几个鸡蛋一起下锅，声若响雷，故称“炸蛋”。而“五柳”也容易让人想到五柳先生陶渊明。</p>
<p>不过民间普遍认为<a href="https://github.com/xgqfwa2071/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%94%E6%9F%B3%E7%82%B8%E8%9B%8B.md">五柳炸蛋</a>与“五柳先生”并没有什么关系，而是源自于五柳菜，由红萝卜、荞头、大肉姜、青瓜等腌制而成，因五种材料状如柳条而得名。</p>
<p>据说，广东地区除了五柳炸蛋，还有名菜五柳鱼。五柳鱼的来源，一说是唐代诗人杜甫发明的。一日杜甫宴请好友，烹制鱼的过程中杜甫在鱼上加了一些四川泡菜，因为鱼上的腌菜丝细长如柳，而杜甫又很崇敬陶渊明，便给它取名为“五柳鱼”。也有说是苏东坡发明的五柳鱼，为了使鱼快熟，苏东坡在鱼身上划了五刀交错，恰似柳叶，故名五柳鱼。</p>
<p>罗厨烹制的“五柳炸蛋”，外脆里嫩，甜酸可口，香脆嫩滑的鸡蛋中融入了略带柠檬汁的奇特酸果味，深受喜爱。</p>
<p>罗厨提醒您，自己在家炸鸡蛋时，应先打一只鸡蛋入碗，再倒入沸油中炸制；碗内不能有水分，否则鸡蛋倒入油中后，会溅油花烫伤人。另外，炸鸡蛋时油不要少放，油多才能够将鸡蛋炸得外皮焦脆，然后吸收了酸甜汁后会变得很好吃。</p>
<h2>五柳炸蛋</h2>
<p>主料：鸡蛋6枚。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1873902958f007ea939af6a5b4254904.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1873902958f007ea939af6a5b4254904-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：红葱头1颗切丝、黄椒1颗切丝、姜块切丝（1汤匙）、小番茄3颗切丝、葱叶切丝（1汤匙）、柠檬3个。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b059a30a370a1516f5c25f2451793d62.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/b059a30a370a1516f5c25f2451793d62-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料：海盐1茶匙、白醋2～3汤匙、冰糖3块。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 取6个小碗盛装鸡蛋。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将鸡蛋分别打入6个小碗中。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861882" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用1茶匙的盐平均撒入6个碗中。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 用八成油温将6个蛋炸至外脆金黄。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 鸡蛋炸至里面刚刚熟，捞起后沥干油分。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 用冰糖、姜丝（几条）、水10盎司熬制汁水。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 取3颗柠檬的汁、白醋2～3汤匙放入锅内，制作酸甜汁。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将配料分别洒在6个炸好的鸡蛋上。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 在炸好的鸡蛋上淋自制的甜酸汁。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 成品五柳炸蛋外面金黄酥香，里面软嫩多汁。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11861892" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
