<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】姜黄冬粉蟹煲?绵密弹牙 鲜味十足</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>姜黄冬粉蟹煲（图／梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【小编语】</span></strong>俗话说：“春捂秋冻、不生杂病。”初春乍暖还寒，昼夜温差大，气候不稳定，别忘适时增减衣服！</p>
<p>这个时节海象相对冬季稳定，市场旬味仍新鲜十足，料理上增添辛辣食材（<ahref="https://github.com/ibfsyb3725/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E9%BB%84.md#1">姜黄</a>、辣椒、洋葱、青葱…）能缓和海鲜的寒性，亦补充优质蛋白质增添抵抗力。天气稍寒，可用滚烫的瓦煲盛装食材，保留美味佳肴的温度，温暖身子。</p>
<p>这一道<ahref="https://github.com/ibfsyb3725/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E9%BB%84.md#1">姜黄</a><ahref="https://github.com/ibfsyb3725/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E7%B2%89.md#1">冬粉</a>蟹煲，透过梁厨食谱仔细说明、图片搭配，在家下厨也不会手忙脚乱，挽起袖子，动手做看看吧！</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></strong><br />
大蟹1只约2 磅（Fresh Crab 1 piece 2 lbs）<br />
粉丝2扎（Bean Vermicelli 2 pieces）<br />
土芹、蒜头、干葱、洋葱、红椒各少许（切细粒）（Chinese cerely, garlic, shallot, onion, chili pepper）<br />
芫荽、葱各少量（Green onion &amp; Celantro a little）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866302" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-18.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11866302 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大蟹1只约2 磅（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></strong><br />
黄姜粉1/2汤匙（Turmeric powder 1/2 T）<br />
椰奶4汤匙（Coconut milk 4 T）<br />
盐1/2茶匙（Salt ? t）<br />
糖1茶匙（Sugar 1 t）<br />
鸡汤1杯（Chicken broth 1 C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866305" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【其他材料】</span></strong><br />
生粉、绍兴酒、油各适量</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【做法】</span></strong><br />
1. 大蟹处理好后洗净，切好备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866306" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大蟹处理好后洗净，切好备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 粉丝用冷水浸软。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-10-450x600.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粉丝用冷水浸软。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 浸好的粉丝捞起后，用热水加一点老抽调色浸泡约2分钟，捞起备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-7-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浸好的粉丝捞起后，用热水加一点老抽调色浸泡约2分钟，捞起备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 土芹、干葱、蒜头、红椒、洋葱等料头切细碎后备用。瓦煲放在炉火上烧热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866311" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">土芹、干葱、蒜头、红椒、洋葱等料头切细碎后备用。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 螃蟹先洒上少许生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866312" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">螃蟹先洒上少许生粉。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 热镬下油1汤匙，先放料头爆香，再放入蟹块炒至变红色，加黄姜粉再炒一会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866296" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热镬下油1汤匙，先放料头爆香，再放入蟹块炒至变红色，加黄姜粉再炒一会。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 接着加入少量绍酒、鸡汤以及剩下的调味料，盖上盖闷约2分钟。加入粉丝，兜匀炒至收干汁液。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">接着加入少量绍酒、鸡汤以及剩下的调味料，盖上盖闷约2分钟。加入粉丝，兜匀炒至收干汁液。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 洒上土芹、芫荽和葱，装入已经烧热的瓦煲内，煲滚后菜肴便可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866294" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【梁师傅小贴士】<br />
1.?粉丝用台湾<ahref="https://github.com/ibfsyb3725/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E7%B2%89.md#1">冬粉</a>较爽口，不会过于黏稠。先用冷水浸软再用热水浸泡至软滑，加老抽是为了让粉丝颜色变得金黄好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866300" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/12-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11866300 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/12-2-450x467.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="467" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾冬粉较爽口，煮起来不会过于黏稠。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.??这次使用的螃蟹为加州大蟹，又称作珍宝蟹（Dungeness Crab），现在是吃这种螃蟹的最佳季节，肥大有肉，鲜味十足。在这道菜中，粉丝能索取蟹的鲜味， 非常好吃，加上用滚烫的瓦煲盛装，是冷天的最佳吃法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11866307" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/13-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11866307" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/13-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食, 姜黄冬粉蟹煲" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这次使用的螃蟹为加州大蟹，肥大有肉，鲜味十足。（图／梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.??姜黄性温，且姜黄的块根有解郁破瘀、利胆退黄和通经等功效。姜黄中的姜黄素可抑制肿瘤细胞增长，也具抗氧化功用。姜黄素并可加速皮肤伤口愈合，减少疤痕形成。◇</p>
