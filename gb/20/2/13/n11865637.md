<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>萧敬腾与温贞菱联手 演绎日系轻装流行风</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130152231487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年02月13日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A7%E6%95%AC%E8%85%BE.md#1">萧敬腾</a><ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%81%E8%90%A7.md#1">老萧</a>）与<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E8%B4%9E%E8%8F%B1.md#1">温贞菱</a><ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B8%A9.md#1">温温</a>）近来同获日本知名服装品牌之邀，联手演绎春夏最新三大流行风格，其中以工装休闲风为2020年春夏最火的风格，除了对于细节上的讲究外，宽松剪裁更是必备元素之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865679" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865679" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204361487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865679" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204361487.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="646" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865679" class="wp-caption-text">全新2020春夏系列，<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A7%E6%95%AC%E8%85%BE.md#1">萧敬腾</a>演绎工装休闲穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>身为品牌代言人，萧敬腾选择内搭圆领T恤，外罩最新款的宽版衬衫或水洗工作外套，多口袋设计赋予实用机能，下身搭配古着风宽版工作裤或是刷破牛仔裤，无论是大地配色或者丹宁色系，都完美诠释了工装精神，俐落又不失层次感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865678" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865678" style="width: 463px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205211487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11865678" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205211487.jpg" alt="" width="463" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865678" class="wp-caption-text">萧敬腾演绎工装休闲穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文青女星<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E8%B4%9E%E8%8F%B1.md#1">温贞菱</a>也亲身示范难得的中性风格，温贞菱选择了色彩柔和的开领上衣搭配EZY牛仔裤，呈现出帅气中带点女性独有的细腻，不一样的气质却同样的吸睛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865689" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204531487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865689" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204531487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="586" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865689" class="wp-caption-text">温贞菱演绎工装休闲穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外两人也诠释了本季春夏流行的“机能时尚”以及“活力色彩”的流行风。</p>
<p>平常穿搭大胆的萧敬腾，利用自己最爱的“亮色系”搭配机能单品展现不一样的机能时尚，不论是活力四射的哈密瓜橙，还是春意盎然的青草绿，私底下很爱打篮球的他，搭上抗UV连帽外套，清爽舒适还防晒，下身则搭配束口裤或卡其短裤，相当活力阳光，收工后直接就能上球场，让他穿得有型，动得时尚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865677" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865677" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205141487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865677" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205141487.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="582" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865677" class="wp-caption-text">萧敬腾演绎机能时尚穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而温贞菱为了保持拍戏时的完美体态，私底下也是运动爱好者，她的机能时尚穿搭小秘诀就是贴身的机能商品，像是坦克背心搭配高腰DRY弹性打褶宽?裙，或者是圆领T恤搭配九分裤，外罩一件流行色系的宽版或连帽外套，不只运动，任何场合皆可展现动感自信。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865700" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204451487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865700" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204451487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="585" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865700" class="wp-caption-text">温贞菱机能时尚穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>发挥时尚与色彩混搭功力</h2>
<p>萧敬腾造型时尚多变，勇于尝试各种色彩跟混搭技巧，他不藏私大力推荐两件POLO衫的叠穿技巧，将经典的POLO衫穿出时尚味，神秘紫红撞色沉稳深灰，下身搭配新品长裤，打造不一样的绅士品格，另一个LOOK则选择了粉紫色调的T恤单品搭配卡其短裤，长度是日本时下最受欢迎的膝上10公分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865702" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205001487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865702" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130205001487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="624" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865702" class="wp-caption-text">萧敬腾秀出活力色彩穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>粉彩色轻松展现自信优雅</h2>
<p>气质出众温贞菱则展现两套粉彩LOOK，温柔奶茶色亚麻混纺A字洋装搭配鹅黄的抗UV SUPIMA COTTON V领开襟外套，撞色更添丰富层次，另一套则以薄荷绿宽版外套搭配同色调雪纺细褶裤裙，整体清透飘逸，轻松展现自信与优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11865704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11865704" style="width: 459px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204291487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11865704" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2002130204291487.jpg" alt="" width="459" b="636" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11865704" class="wp-caption-text">温贞菱演绎活力色彩穿搭。（台湾优衣库&amp; 先势公关提供）</figcaption></figure>
