<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>做出居酒屋风格的胡椒虾 吹风机也派上用场</h2>
<a href="https://d2b68ib2p4x8t6.cloudfront.net/S9sbkRqYf"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/crgjqw3751/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>好吃的下酒菜，不能错过“胡椒虾”！（Ｃ2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>这几天秋老虎发威，昼夜温差大，白天的酷热和夜晚的凉风成了明显对比。晚间在户外摆几张小凳子、桌子就可以和朋友小酌一杯，好不惬意。这时下酒菜特别重要，不妨来试做这一道简单、快速、风味迷人的<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E6%A4%92%E8%99%BE.md#1">胡椒虾</a>。</p>
<p>把家里的吹风机拿来做料理，就是做出好吃<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E6%A4%92%E8%99%BE.md#1">胡椒虾</a>的诀窍，因为需要把外层酱汁收干。因此热炒后用吹风机马上吹干，可以让胡椒粉附着在<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE.md#1">虾</a>子的表面，更加入味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【腌<ahref="https://github.com/crgjqw3751/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE.md#1">虾</a>食材】</span></h2>
<p>草虾 12只<br />
白胡椒粉适量<br />
盐适量<br />
香油适量<br />
米酒适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味料食材】</span></h2>
<p>蒜头碎 适量<br />
辣椒碎1根<br />
葱花适量<br />
糖少许<br />
盐巴 1大匙<br />
白胡椒粉大量<br />
黑胡椒粉适量<br />
香油 少许</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 首先将白虾去须、开背、去肠泥，下适量盐、白胡椒、香油搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6c572674724715162668a71e5b57d1b3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6c572674724715162668a71e5b57d1b3-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413389" class="wp-caption-text">白虾去须、开背、去肠泥，下适量盐、白胡椒、香油搅拌均匀。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 取一锅开大火下油，放入虾子煎至双面稍微上色，下蒜头碎、辣椒碎略炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413392" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/34ce6009c2f0349b2f1235206a8dabe4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/34ce6009c2f0349b2f1235206a8dabe4-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413392" class="wp-caption-text">取一锅开大火下油，放入虾子，加盖为闷煎至双面稍微上色。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 下盐、白胡椒、黑胡椒翻炒均匀，起锅前下香油、葱花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413390" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/911dd622765f3938907a65fbd2a22efa.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/911dd622765f3938907a65fbd2a22efa-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413390" class="wp-caption-text">虾子熟时下盐、白胡椒、黑胡椒翻炒均匀。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12413391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413391" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8dbce4cd9c4c7fa6d27085dbafd644af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8dbce4cd9c4c7fa6d27085dbafd644af-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413391" class="wp-caption-text">起锅前下香油、葱花。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 最后用吹风机将虾子吹干即可。</p>
