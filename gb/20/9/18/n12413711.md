<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】创意粤菜“花枝浆煎酿茄子”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>粤菜“心太软”食谱～花枝浆煎酿茄子（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>每年9月至隔年3月是<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>盛产的季节，可惜很多人是不敢吃茄子的！这一道港式餐馆的经典创意菜“花枝浆煎酿茄子”，风味深邃、逆转茄子料理的命运，让很多人趋之若鹜，很想尝尝看！</p>
<p>一口咬下“软而不烂”的<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>让很多人放了心，搭配酿在茄子里的Ｑ弹花枝浆，口感有咬劲却不需要用力咀嚼，相当于粤菜的“心太软”食谱；烹饪过程茄子炸、回锅煮的时间必须注意才能掌握好口感，其中调味酱汁更是整道菜的关键，咸甜够味的酱汁突出混搭新吃法的特色滋味。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>花枝浆 10安士（Cuttlefish paste 10 oz）<br />
日本茄子两条约10安士（Japan Eggplant 10 oz）<br />
四季豆10安士（Green Bean 10 oz）<br />
葱花少许（Green onion）<br />
姜蓉少许（Minced ginger）<br />
胡椒粉少许（pepper）<br />
麻油少许（sesame oil）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></h2>
<p>味噌汁2汤匙（Miso sauce 2 T）<br />
水1杯（Water 1 C）<br />
糖半茶匙（Sugar ? t）<br />
麻油少许（Sesame oil ）<br />
粟粉1汤匙开水勾芡用（Cornstarch solution 1 T）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413728" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413728" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-28-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413728" class="wp-caption-text">味噌汁、葱花及姜蓉、粟粉<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1.花枝浆拌入葱花、姜蓉、胡椒粉和麻油，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-19-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413730" class="wp-caption-text">花枝浆拌入葱花、姜蓉、胡椒粉和麻油，搅拌均匀<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.接着用汤匙挖出小粒花枝浆备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413731" class="wp-caption-text">用汤匙挖出小粒花枝浆。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.茄子斜切三刀，不要切断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413732" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413732" class="wp-caption-text">茄子斜切三刀，不要切断。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.在切好的茄子上洒少许生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413733" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413733" class="wp-caption-text">切好的茄子上洒少许生粉。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.酿入花枝浆，抹平滑后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413734" class="wp-caption-text">酿入花枝浆，抹平滑后备用。 （梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.四季豆切除头尾，洗干净后用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413735" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413735" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413735" class="wp-caption-text">四季豆切除头尾。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.热锅后下2杯油，油烧滚后放入茄子，放入时花枝浆的部分朝下，炸1分钟，再反转两面炸30秒，盛出后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413736" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413736" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413736" class="wp-caption-text">热锅后下2杯油，油烧滚后放入茄子。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.锅中剩下少许油，爆香姜、蒜蓉和味噌汁，再加入水和糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413737" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413737" class="wp-caption-text">锅中剩下少许油，爆香姜、蒜蓉和味噌汁，再加入水和糖。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.将茄子放回锅中，中火煮一分半钟，最后用粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12413738" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12413738" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12413738" class="wp-caption-text">将茄子放回锅中，中火煮一分半钟，最后用粟粉水勾芡（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10.茄子放在碟中央，四季豆围在旁边即完成。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>
<p>选购花枝浆时，可选择台湾制造的。其囗感爽脆嫩滑，用来酿蔬菜类最适合。</p>
