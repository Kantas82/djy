<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>顾及台湾资深艺人健康 中秋为防疫改赠礼券</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/202009-ttv-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年09月24日讯】（大纪元记者杨逸帆报导）由台湾电视台举办的“109年关怀演艺人员中秋联欢餐会”，因“中共病毒”（武汉肺炎）疫情尚未完全解除，为顾及演艺前辈们的身体健康，改赠二千元（台币）<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E5%88%B8.md#1">礼券</a>，取代原规划数百人参与的<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E9%A4%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中秋餐会</a>，以感念资深艺人付出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E5%88%B8.md#1">礼券</a>于9月23日、24日上午10点至下午4点在台视大厅发放，预计发送400份礼券。现场包括<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%9D%BE%E5%8B%87.md#1">陈松勇</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%AB%E5%AD%90%E4%BA%91.md#1">卫子云</a>、张复健、池秋美、丁强、李景光、刘引商等人皆现身活动领取。</p>
<p>身为基金会董事的资深演员<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%86%E4%B8%80%E9%BE%99.md#1">陆一龙</a>特别呼吁好友们多保重：“希望大家一天比一天更健康，保持好状态，朋友时常联络，健康平安最重要。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12427904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12427904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-1-2020-9-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12427904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-1-2020-9-23-600x908.jpg" alt="陆一龙" width="450" b="908" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12427904" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%86%E4%B8%80%E9%BE%99.md#1">陆一龙</a>到活动现场领取礼券。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近80岁的<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%9D%BE%E5%8B%87.md#1">陈松勇</a>坐轮椅出席，身体依旧硬朗，说话中气十足，他不改幽默性格打趣表示：“上次端午节因外籍看护没看信箱，错过领券时间，这次我特别早来。”</p>
<p>陈松勇、<ahref="https://github.com/jqoits3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%AB%E5%AD%90%E4%BA%91.md#1">卫子云</a>在领取礼券后，促膝闲聊，陈松勇感性表示：“虽然平时大家没有联络，但每次聚餐我都会去，每次都有点伤感，因会发现又少几个人，也知道自己很幸福。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12427915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12427915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-2-2020-9-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12427915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-2-2020-9-23-600x883.jpg" alt="卫子云、陈松勇" width="450" b="883" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12427915" class="wp-caption-text">卫子云、陈松勇出席活动现场。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12427916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12427916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-3-2020-9-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12427916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-3-2020-9-23-600x840.jpg" alt="卫子云、陈松勇" width="450" b="840" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12427916" class="wp-caption-text">近80岁的陈松勇坐轮椅出席，与卫子云闲聊。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卫子云依旧维持很好的身材，他说：“这次因疫情取消餐叙，虽少了团聚味道，不过还是很谢谢文化部和基金会，算是一份心意。”</p>
<p>演员唐文杰也表示：“感受到长官们关心，心中有一股暖流经过，每年都会趁这机会看老朋友、老前辈，看到大家都健康，很替他们开心。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12427909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12427909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-1-2020-9-24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12427909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/ttv-1-2020-9-24-600x939.jpg" alt="唐文杰" width="450" b="939" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12427909" class="wp-caption-text">唐文杰到活动现场领取礼券。（台视提供）</figcaption></figure>
