<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【快炒野菜】爽脆杨桃豆 香气十足冷热都好吃</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52887556-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>热炒野菜杨桃豆，加入菜脯和小鱼干，爽脆口感又营养，非常下饭。（秀兰妈咪提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>营养满满的“<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A1%83%E8%B1%86.md#1">杨桃豆</a>”除了有10多种氨基酸，丰富蛋白质，膳食纤维、钙质都高于黄豆2倍，种子也能榨油，再加上对环境适应力佳，几乎没有病虫害，不需要喷洒农药，对于重视养生的人属于极品菜单。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12404543" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52715523.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-12404543 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52715523-450x338.jpeg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404543" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A8%E6%A1%83%E8%B1%86.md#1">杨桃豆</a>是台湾原住民常见菜肴。（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杨桃豆（翼豆）在台湾市面上不多见，因为这是原住民部落常见的菜肴，东南亚地区也比较常见，目前台湾东部就广泛被种植。这种菜肴料理上非常方便，口感爽脆犹如山苏，不论是热炒、汆烫、凉拌沙拉都相当美味，就算在冰箱冷藏也不失脆度，很适合忙碌的家庭当作冷藏<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%B8%E5%A4%87%E8%8F%9C.md#1">常备菜</a>。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>热炒杨桃豆</strong></span></h2>
<p>杨桃豆本身有点涩味，所以热炒的过程会多次闷煮，让水蒸气软化豆子。如果想要用汆烫的方式，可以先煮一锅热水放入杨桃豆，30秒后捞起放入冰水中冰镇保持脆度，再加上ＸＯ酱做凉拌菜也相当好吃。</p>
<p>这一道食谱我们不加食盐，让特色<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E8%8F%9C.md#1">野菜</a>更升级，食材上运用“<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%9C%E8%84%AF.md#1">菜脯</a>和小鱼干”，而最后加入鱼露、辣椒来小火收汁更是这一道菜的美味灵魂。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>杨桃豆 250公克<br />
吻仔鱼（或香煎小鱼干）20公克<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/xsumwp3307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8F%9C%E8%84%AF.md#1">菜脯</a> 30公克（先泡水2~3分钟去咸味）<br />
辣椒 一小条、切成圈状<br />
橄榄油 1大匙<br />
水 50公克<br />
鱼露 1大匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_12404541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871175.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-12404541 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871175-450x337.jpeg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404541" class="wp-caption-text">翼豆（杨桃豆）、吻仔鱼、菜脯、辣椒（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1.翼豆先泡水冲洗，切头去尾，切成薄片状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12404542" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871174.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-12404542 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871174-450x338.jpeg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404542" class="wp-caption-text">杨桃豆先切去头尾，再横切薄片。（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12404544" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404544" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871173.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-12404544 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52871173-450x338.jpeg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404544" class="wp-caption-text">翼豆切成薄片状热炒起来口感比较脆、好入口，若是喜欢扎实口感可以切成段状。（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.冷锅下橄榄油1大匙，开中火等锅子升温放入吻仔鱼、菜脯爆香。<br />
3.放入杨桃豆拌炒几下，盖锅闷煮1分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12404545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404545" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52887557.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-12404545 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52887557-450x337.jpeg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404545" class="wp-caption-text">杨桃豆拌炒几下后就盖锅闷煮。（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.打开锅盖加入50公克的水，拌炒几下，转小火闷半分钟。<br />
5.打开锅盖连续翻炒半分钟，加入鱼露1大匙、辣椒，等微微收汁就可以装盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12404567" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12404567" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52887555.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12404567 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/S__52887555-450x337.jpg" alt="杨桃豆" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12404567" class="wp-caption-text">杨桃豆搭配辣椒、菜脯、吻仔鱼，爽脆口感有咸辣，错觉中还有几分客家小炒的滋味。（秀兰妈咪提供）</figcaption></figure>
