<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>AKB48 Team TP首办台北签唱会 铁粉热情应援</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050419501487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会。图为16位团员全员出动至板桥车站某购物中心2楼活动地点合影。（好言娱乐提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年01月05日讯】女团AKB48 Team TP发行第三张单曲《<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%8B%E8%A7%81%E5%A4%95%E9%98%B3%E4%BA%86%E5%90%97.md#1">看见夕阳了吗</a>？》成绩不俗，出道两年来首办签唱会，16位团员全员出动至活动现场板桥车站某购物中心2楼，而第一场签唱会大成功，队长诗雅说：“粉丝们都很热情，来了很多人，超开心的！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11769462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050419561487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769462" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050419561487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769462" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会。图为现场粉丝等待签名。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于是首次签唱会，团员<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BD%B3%E4%BF%90.md#1">李佳俐</a>因为太过兴奋，提前抢先上台，发现后面15位团员还在台下，惊觉自己“大抢拍”，惊呼一声之后，赶快走下台，台下歌迷都觉得她迷糊的样子很可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11769468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050405391487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769468" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050405391487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769468" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会，左起为成员<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E4%BD%B3%E4%BF%90.md#1">李佳俐</a>、蔡亚恩、刘洁明。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李佳俐说：“听到主持人说欢迎AKB48 Team TP 时我就走上去了，结果到定位后才发现原来还有一段介绍词啊！后来就转头跑回去找成员们！顿时觉得好糗啊！”</p>
<p>AKB48 Team TP对于自己的签名都有特别设计，<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%BC%E8%BF%AA%E7%90%A6.md#1">冼迪琦</a>则分享她的签名会写到自己的绰号和画画的关系，所以要花很多时间。“另外，我有很多粉丝都在下班后马上到达现场，也会关心他们的工作或学业考试。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11769465" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355091487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769465" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355091487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769465" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会，左起为成员<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%BC%E8%BF%AA%E7%90%A6.md#1">冼迪琦</a>、林倢、张羽翎。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>队长诗雅说，以前就常常看其他演艺前辈办签唱会的画面，所以一直很憧憬，没有想像过自己竟然也可以实现这个画面，感受粉丝们的热情之余，她也对于二月份的台中、高雄巡回签唱场次向粉丝喊话，“希望也可以看到大家”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11769463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355271487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769463" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355271487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769463" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会，左起为成员陈诗雅、潘姿怡。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刚结束日本NHK红白大赛录影的人气团员<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%93%81%E6%B6%B5.md#1">邱品涵</a>说：“歌迷都会跟我说有看红白的播出有在电视机前为我加油，觉得好感动，有特别练签名的速度，担心会签不完。”而当天现场预计签了1,200张单曲，签唱会花了五小时结束。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11769471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769471" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355221487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769471" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050355221487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769471" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会，左起为成员<ahref="https://github.com/mgisno386/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%93%81%E6%B6%B5.md#1">邱品涵</a>、蔡伊柔。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11769474" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11769474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050416281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11769474" title="AKB48 Team TP" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2001050416281487-600x400.jpg" alt="AKB48 Team TP" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11769474" class="wp-caption-text">AKB48 Team TP出道两年来首办签唱会，左起为成林于馨、刘晓晴、刘语晴。（好言娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>
