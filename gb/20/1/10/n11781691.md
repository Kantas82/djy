<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>舞台剧《阿铭上菜》初一登场温哥华</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0454-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月10日讯】由<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>采风乐坊编排的<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E5%8F%B0%E5%89%A7.md#1">舞台剧</a>《<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%93%AD%E4%B8%8A%E8%8F%9C.md#1">阿铭上菜</a>》，将于1月25日，黄历大年<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%9D%E4%B8%80.md#1">初一</a>，在<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>伊丽莎白女王剧院登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781739" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0618.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781739" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0618-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781739" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E5%8F%B0%E5%89%A7.md#1">舞台剧</a>《<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%93%AD%E4%B8%8A%E8%8F%9C.md#1">阿铭上菜</a>》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据采风乐坊的介绍，《阿铭上菜》将以传统吹打乐为主体，表现当今<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>办桌的欢快热闹，再现普通台湾人的饮食生活，具有独特的台湾味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781743" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9483.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781743" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9483-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781743" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11781763" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781763" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0257.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0257-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781763" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>传统上，在台湾，一年到头举凡祈求风调雨顺、国泰民安的斋醮宴、以及宴请好兄弟而举行的普渡宴，乃至生命礼俗中的?婚丧喜庆?、新居?入厝?、?弥月?庆生?、?谢师?饯行?、?尾牙?春酒?、?庙会?庆典?、 地方选举等，都会举行办桌，宴请宾客，一同共餐庆祝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781746" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9378.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9378-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781746" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“办桌”(闽南语发音)，指的是办好饭桌上的料理，让客人饱餐一顿。办桌通常是以在马路边搭?棚架?、?寺庙?广场?、?学校?操场?或地方民众活动中心作为场地，搭起?炉灶?与备菜的桌台，由?总铺师?负责决定与主导整个宴会的菜色与流程，现场烹煮食物，之后上桌，由活动或是庆典参与者共同享用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2683.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2683-450x309.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781753" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11781754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9385.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_9385-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781754" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>类舞台剧《阿铭上菜》中，演员们用锅、碗、 瓢、盆、炉灶、柴火和桌椅，敲敲打打的声响,表现台湾办桌热闹的味道与文化；以传统吹打乐的方式生动地表现总铺师们，从备料到打菜到上菜等等诸多有趣的过程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781749" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781749" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2883.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2883-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781749" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>《阿铭上菜》将融入剧场诸多元素，以类舞台剧的表演型态呈现当代思维，以此丰富传统乐器表演的演出型式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2685.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF2685-450x586.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="586" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781757" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11781758" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781758" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF3142.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781758" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/DSCF3142-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781758" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11781759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11781759" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0540.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11781759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/YDG_0540-450x300.jpg" alt="舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11781759" class="wp-caption-text">舞台剧《阿铭上菜》剧照。（采风乐坊提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于台北市的采风乐坊1991年成立，致力于保存台湾传统音乐文化，积极参与各类型节目的创作、改编、相互结合演出，希望在全世界都能看见台湾的艺术文化。1月25日在<ahref="https://github.com/bolgor305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>伊丽莎白女王剧院演出的《阿铭上菜》，是由LunarFest与采风乐坊合作，在北美巡回演出的首站。</p>
