<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>5种营养果昔 帮你做体内大扫除</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/smoothie_1549270583-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>在早晨做一杯营养助消化的果昔，帮助你开启一天的精神力，给体内做大扫除。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span class="s1">经过假期的美食与大餐，你是否厌倦了油腻的大鱼大肉，开始怀念简单干净的食物？在这个时候，多补充一些健康的<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C.md#1">蔬果</a>，帮体内做个大扫除，才能恢复苗条的体态与活力。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在新年的开始，早晨打一杯营养助消化而色彩鲜亮的<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E6%98%94.md#1">果昔</a>，帮助你开启一天的精神力。自己制作果昔时，记得别添加太多<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E5%88%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">精制糖</a>，才能享用食材本身的天然味道。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #188638;"><strong><span class="s2">1. </span><span class="s1">莓果橙汁生姜<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%9C%E6%98%94.md#1">果昔</a></span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11759236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11759236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_1421431727.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11759236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_1421431727-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11759236" class="wp-caption-text">姜可抑制食欲并减少体内发炎，莓果含有大量的抗氧化剂与维生素C，有利于促进<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%B6%E5%8E%9F%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD.md#1">胶原蛋白</a>合成。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><strong><span class="s1">食材：</span></strong><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">杯橙汁、</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">根中型香蕉、</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">块新鲜生姜去皮、</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">杯冷冻什锦莓果。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">生姜含有姜辣素</span><span class="s1">和姜烯酚</span><span class="s1">，可抑制食欲、助消化并减少体内发炎，帮助维持健康的体重。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">草莓、蓝莓、覆盆莓等莓果，都有大量的抗氧化剂，特别是富含维生素</span><span class="s2">C</span><span class="s1">，有利于促进<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%83%B6%E5%8E%9F%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD.md#1">胶原蛋白</a>合成。草莓和香蕉也含有丰富的钾，可以帮助消除水肿。</span></p>
<h2 class="p4"><span style="color: #188638;"><strong><span class="s3">2. </span><span class="s1">芒果<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E4%BA%9A%E7%B1%BD.md#1">奇亚籽</a>果昔</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11759240" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11759240" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_420882919.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11759240" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_420882919-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11759240" class="wp-caption-text">营养学家说，芒果富含维生素C和维生素A，以及铁、铜和丰富的钾，对身体很有帮助，可使人精力充沛。<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E4%BA%9A%E7%B1%BD.md#1">奇亚籽</a>中的膳食纤维，也可以增加饱足感。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p4"><strong><span class="s1">食材：</span></strong><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">杯无糖杏仁奶、</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">汤匙奇亚籽、</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">杯冷冻芒果、</span><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">根中型香蕉。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">芒果不仅有浓郁的热带风味，也有傲人的营养价值。它</span><span class="s1">富含维生素</span><span class="s3">C</span><span class="s1">和维生素</span><span class="s3">A</span><span class="s1">，以及铁、铜和丰富的钾。适量食用芒果，不仅不会增胖，更可使人保持精力充沛。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">同时，奇亚籽中的膳食纤维，可以增加长时间的饱足感，避免在下一餐中过度进食。两汤匙的奇亚籽，所提供的膳食纤维几乎是一天所需的一半。同时，奇亚籽也有丰富的</span><span class="s3">omega-3</span><span class="s1">脂肪酸，以及钙、锰、镁、磷等矿物质。</span><span class="s3"><br />
</span></p>
<h2 class="p4"><span style="color: #188638;"><strong><span class="s3">3. </span><span class="s1">花生香蕉肉桂果昔</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11759246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11759246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_472916476.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11759246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_472916476-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11759246" class="wp-caption-text">香蕉可以取代<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%BE%E5%88%B6%E7%B3%96.md#1">精制糖</a>，增加营养与膳食纤维的摄取。有助于维持体重、控制血压。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p4"><strong><span class="s1">食材：</span></strong><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">杯牛奶、</span><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">根冷冻香蕉、</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">汤匙花生酱、1/2茶匙肉桂、</span><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">茶匙蜂蜜、谷麦（</span><span class="s3">granola</span><span class="s1">）适量（装饰用）。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">香蕉拥有十足的香气与甜度，几乎可以取代精制糖；吃香蕉还能并帮助消化、控制食欲，同时也是膳食纤维、钾、镁的良好来源，有助于维持体重、控制血压与调节神经系统。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">中医认为肉桂能温中散寒、活血、健胃，有帮助消化、抑制细菌的功效。近年来，肉桂抗发炎的作用，也渐渐为人所知，也有部分研究指出，肉桂粉有助于控制血糖。如果喜欢肉桂的香气，不妨用花生香蕉肉桂果昔作为明天的早餐吧！</span></p>
<h2 class="p4"><span style="color: #188638;"><span class="s3"><strong>4. </strong></span><strong><span class="s1">凤梨菠菜果昔</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11759249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11759249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_629427680.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11759249" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_629427680-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11759249" class="wp-caption-text">菠菜可帮助控制血糖，降低罹癌风险，并改善骨骼健康等。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p4"><strong><span class="s1">食材：</span></strong><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">杯冷冻凤梨块、</span><span class="s3">2</span><span class="s1">杯菠菜、半颗酪梨、</span><span class="s3">1</span><span class="s1">杯水。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">菠菜是可以煮熟，也可以生吃的蔬菜。只要清洗干净，确保没有虫卵，很适合加入早餐果昔之中，让营养更加分。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">菠菜中的纤维可预防便秘，并含有丰富的类胡萝卜素、维生素C、维生素</span><span class="s3">K</span><span class="s1">、叶酸，以及铁、钙等矿物质。可以帮助控制血糖，降低罹患癌症的风险，改善骨骼健康等。再加上凤梨与酪梨可让果昔更美味，并提供一个上午的能量。</span></p>
<h2 class="p4"><span style="color: #188638;"><strong><span class="s3">5. </span><span class="s1">蓝莓香蕉燕麦果昔</span></strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11759251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11759251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_523854742.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11759251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/shutterstock_523854742-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11759251" class="wp-caption-text">莓果有天然甜味与香气，热量却不高，并可补充大量的营养。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><strong><span class="s1">食材：</span></strong><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">杯不加糖杏仁奶或燕麦奶、</span><span class="s2">1.5</span><span class="s1">杯冷冻蓝莓、</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">根香蕉、</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">汤匙燕麦片或</span><span class="s1">亚麻籽。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">各种莓果对控制体重都有帮助，因为它们的脂肪和热量含量不高。根据美国农业部食物资料库，100克</span><span class="s1">蓝莓只有57</span><span class="s1">大卡的热量，但却有丰富的维生素</span><span class="s3">C</span><span class="s1">、维生素<span class="s3">K、</span>锰</span><span class="s1">和膳食纤维。一些研究也指出，莓类或许可以控制血压与对抗发炎。</span></p>
<p class="p4"><span class="s1">燕麦片与亚麻籽还能提供额外的膳食纤维、抗氧化物与好的脂肪，对心血管保健与减重有积极的作用，适合正在进行饮食控制的人们。</span></p>
