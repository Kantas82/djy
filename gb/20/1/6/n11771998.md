<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>活动预告：悉尼好事围2020黄历新年庆典</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月06日讯】1月18日（周六），<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC.md#1">悉尼</a>华人大区<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%BD%E4%BA%8B%E5%9B%B4.md#1">好事围</a>（Hurstville）将举行盛大庆典，迎接<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">黄历新年</a>的到来。精彩的表演、热闹的集市、多彩的活动，伴您欢欢喜喜过大年！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772012 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-02.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热闹的节日步行街。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>花车巡游，热闹新年</h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%BD%E4%BA%8B%E5%9B%B4.md#1">好事围</a>Forest Road将迎来金鼠年<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">黄历新年</a>庆典。盛大的花车巡游将于上午10点开始：打头阵的“柔功门”将带来传统的<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md#1">舞狮</a>点睛仪式，并为观众献上精彩的舞狮、<ahref="https://github.com/ixpzix312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E9%BE%99.md#1">舞龙</a>表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772013" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772013 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-03-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热闹的节日步行街。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
<p>您将欣赏到一系列光彩夺目的花车，包括寓意吉祥的中国龙，以及去年大受欢迎的充气生肖动物。人气卡通Hello Kitty也将前来助兴，为大家送上新年祝福哦！</p>
<p>预计当天的活动将吸引超过5万名居民及游客参加。该活动已成功举办多届，见证了乔治河市政府（Georges River Council）对当地社区及商业的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772014 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-04.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">和可爱的生肖动物合照吧！（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>精彩表演，新秀对决</h2>
<p>当天的大舞台将安排丰富多彩的表演，包括来自多个本地社区团体的演出，以及歌唱、舞蹈和乐器表演。街头上还有走高跷、杂技表演和生肖人偶，让大小朋友目不暇接。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772015 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-05.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">翩翩起舞的古代仕女。（燕楠／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11772016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772016 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-06.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粤剧表演。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11772017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772017 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-07.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">街头高跷表演。（安平雅／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年舞台节目的一大新亮点，是中午12点40分上演的《中国好声音》澳洲海选决赛：多位歌唱好手将一决高下，您将现场见证新星的诞生。这是澳洲最大规模的中文歌唱比赛，获胜者将直接入围《中国好声音》节目总决赛，赢得全球电视亮相机会！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772018 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-08-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《中国好声音》澳洲海选决赛将在节庆中上演。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>趣味活动 特色街市</h2>
<p>写下您的新年愿望，挂到许愿树上；带孩子到马戏团工作坊，让孩子尝试玩杂耍和呼啦圈；Forest Road上的停车场将改造为儿童游乐区，设有波波池、滑梯及攀爬设施。家长们可在休息区小憩放松，让孩子们玩个痛快！</p>
<p>此外，还有怀卡托打鼓表演及工作坊，以及由画家Chris Yee现场展示壁画绘制，还有折纸、画脸及伞艺等活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11772021" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772021 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-09-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美食摊位。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
<p>热闹的节日街市将设有各式各样的摊位，包括中西小吃及各式精美工艺品，让您流连忘返。带上全家大小，一起来好事围庆祝黄历新年吧！</p>
<p>更多详情，请访问<span style="color: #333399;"><strong><a style="color: #333399;" href="http://www.georgesriver.nsw.gov.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.georgesriver.nsw.gov.au</a></strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11772023" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11772023 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200106-Chi-Jin-Hurstville-10-300x465.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="465" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">本次活动赞助商。（Georges River Council）</figcaption></figure>
