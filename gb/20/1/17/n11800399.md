<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年节新亮点三星花海 爱无界限猫鼠一家亲</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423116-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>年节新亮点三星花海。（郭千华／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月17日讯】（大纪元记者郭千华台湾宜兰报导）金鼠年新亮点<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F.md#1">三星</a><ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E6%B5%B7.md#1">花海</a>，去年圣诞节推出23.5米全国最高耶诞树，结合宜兰监狱17件<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%BB%E8%8D%89%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">稻草艺术</a>竞赛得奖作品，成为三星花海最吸情的要角，从17日起展出至元宵节为止。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F.md#1">三星</a>乡乡长李志镛表示，除了丰富的<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A8%BB%E8%8D%89%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">稻草艺术</a>装置，还委托狱方制作<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%A0%E6%9D%A5%E5%AE%9D.md#1">鼠来宝</a>生肖主题<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%81%AF.md#1">花灯</a>迎接新年，金鼠手捧金元宝，造型可爱，模样讨喜，象征好运滚滚来，幸福富贵数不完。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11800499" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423113.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11800499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423113-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%A0%E6%9D%A5%E5%AE%9D.md#1">鼠来宝</a>主题<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%81%AF.md#1">花灯</a>。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>诚挚欢迎国内外人士来参访三星，徜徉<ahref="https://github.com/ndbhcw3425/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E6%B5%B7.md#1">花海</a>意境，感受热闹温馨又色彩缤纷的年节氛围，与鼠来宝同欢，喜迎春贺新年，更是来打卡的新亮点，愿你将这份喜气美好分享到世界每个角落。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11800503" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423111.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11800503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423111-653x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="307" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">23.5米全国最高圣诞树花灯。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰监狱秘书王恩先导览谈到，这次展出的17件作品皆由收容人制作，是狱方举办的稻草装置竞赛得奖作品，最最吸情的是冠军作品－－爱无界限，作者编织猫与鼠依偎凝眸、和平共处的甜蜜意象，期盼大家相亲相爱一家亲，消弭隔阂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11800500" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423114.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11800500" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423114-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宜兰监狱王恩先秘书向三星乡长导览“爱无界限”作品。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11800498" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423112.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11800498" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/423112-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“爱无界限”猫鼠一家亲。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>王恩先分享说，还有其它古早农村的草垺、力阿卡（人力手拉车）、大灶与维妙维肖的勺子等，以及可爱造型的龙猫、猪宝宝、鼠宝宝，尚有神龙摆尾与祥狮献瑞，甚至连台北101、漫威超级英雄钢铁人等，都是众人巧手下的作品，稻草艺术栩栩如生且饶富特色，将改变受刑人刻板印象，透过反省改变，让世界变得更美好，散播喜气洋洋。</p>
