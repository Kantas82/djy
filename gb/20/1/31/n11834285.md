<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾会馆新春团拜  祝乡亲健康平安</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/e1db7929545abde1cecfed67af18d6f5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾会馆举行新春团拜会，祝福乡亲侨胞在新的一年健康、平安。（林丹／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者林丹美国纽约报导）纽约<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BC%9A%E9%A6%86.md">台湾会馆</a>30日举行新春团拜，会馆内热闹非凡，“国泰民安福永绵，风和日丽春常在”的春联高悬，醒狮、祝福、<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E5%B9%B4.md">拜年</a>、品尝家乡美食一环接一环，祝乡亲侨胞在新的一年健康、平安。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834290" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2813acbbf59651b29b9ba2c388371c1f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834290" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2813acbbf59651b29b9ba2c388371c1f-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">进门前先用免洗洗手液洗手、发口罩。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年因新型冠状病毒瘟疫在全球扩散，<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BC%9A%E9%A6%86.md">台湾会馆</a>的团拜也与往年不一样──大家见面时自觉不握手、不拥抱、不Kiss，改为抱拳问安；宾客入门时，也先以免洗的酒精洗手液洗手、每人发一个口罩。</p>
<p>在团拜开始前，舞狮队带来喜庆双狮献瑞，两只红狮子上下起舞，锣鼓声声。理事长方秀蓉以“鼠”字的谐音，送出一大串吉祥话，祝福乡亲“好运非你莫属”、“有情人终成眷属”、“幸福数不尽”……</p>
<p>徐俪文给乡亲<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8B%9C%E5%B9%B4.md">拜年</a>时表示，本月台湾成功完成总统大选，大选获国际高度关注，台湾也向世界展示成熟的民主素养，这是台湾的骄傲，台湾的民主在每一次选举中得到巩固。新的一年也有挑战，希望大家在团结中战胜困难。</p>
<p>纽约华侨文教服务中心主任黄正杰祝福乡亲有“鼠（数）不完的健康，鼠（数）不完的财富”。</p>
<p>之后侨团负责人、乡亲双手抱拳，互相拜年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834291" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1e21bdd94962e2cce2dd9b680e8f6402.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834291" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1e21bdd94962e2cce2dd9b680e8f6402-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">进门前先用免洗洗手液洗手、发口罩。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11834287" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9d1947d89e402f3b09f69d9062149f6c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834287" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9d1947d89e402f3b09f69d9062149f6c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新任台湾会馆老人中心主任邵顺秀给大家煮了红豆汤圆。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾会馆准备了丰富的美食给大家品尝，有卤味、油饭、米粉、油鸡、葱油鸡、叉烧等，即将上任的台湾会馆老人中心主任邵顺秀熬制了一大锅红豆汤圆，祝大家在新的一年甜甜蜜蜜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4cd81efc669002ce228d35d0a0b0077b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834288" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4cd81efc669002ce228d35d0a0b0077b-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">市议员顾雅明祝福台湾移民黄历新年快乐。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11834292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c554e8c9cedcd34109ebbb8482b2b821.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c554e8c9cedcd34109ebbb8482b2b821-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布朗士区市议员Fernando Cabrera祝福台湾移民黄历新年快乐。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11834295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/43efe33a5600fbbc24a1952316f3ce85.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11834295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/43efe33a5600fbbc24a1952316f3ce85-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纽约州众议员布朗斯坦祝福台湾移民黄历新年快乐。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经文处副处长张俊裕、台湾会馆创会理事长陈隆丰、州众议员布朗斯坦（Ed Braunstein）、市议员顾雅明、布朗士市议员Fernando Cabrera等亦出席，向台湾乡亲祝贺黄历新年。◇</p>
