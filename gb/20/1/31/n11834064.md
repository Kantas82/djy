<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：怒蜂 2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月31日讯】Dodge品牌的双门跑车，除了Viper（已停产）之外，就是大众化的Challenger，而Challenger又有多种不同马力的型号，这周测试的是Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834103" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_03-e1580441836321.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834103" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Dodge在1970年开发第一代Challenger，以“挑战”同级对手Ford Mustang及Chevrolet Camaro，推出4年后，即1974年中停产。而第2代的Challenger在相隔4年后问世，但这只是Mitsubishi?（三菱）Galant Lambda的美洲版，服役5年。直至2008年2月份，Chrysler在芝加哥车展上展出了Challenger，并在同年5月开始生产。自2008年起推出市场的第三代Dodge Challenger，外型十年几乎不变，只是在不同时段推出不同的引擎搭配，像拥有808匹的2018年Challenger Demon版，并会装备不同的悬挂系统及刹车等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834104" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_05-e1580441843257.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_05-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">保持粗犷巨大的美式车款，市场上已是极少见，所以Dodge Challenger拥有一批喜爱美式汽车的车迷，而且Challenger的动力有不同选择，迎合不同需要的消费者们。引擎却只有3.6升V6、5.7升V8 HEMI，392 V8 SRT以及顶级型号使用的6.2升V8 SRT HEMI机械增压（Supercharged）引擎，前三款引擎马力分别为305匹、375匹及485匹，而6.2升机械增压引擎在Hellcat上的输出为717匹，Hellcat Redeye则有797匹，为消费者提供不同动力的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_11-e1580441867935.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834108" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">测试车是使用392 V8 SRT HEMI MDS引擎，因为是搭配了TorqueFlite 8速自动变速箱，MDS是为了节省耗油而设。392代表392立方吋，约为6.4升，输出485匹马力及475磅呎扭矩，起步冲击力很强，在运续多天下雨的湿滑路面上，难以发挥它的五成功力。就算是在高速公路上试图探索它的潜能，但由于车多及车速限制关系，仍只感有力不能尽发，就如一匹骏马不能在无尽草原上奔跑。装置了8速自动变速箱，日常使用非常方便，但缺少了手动的乐趣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_16-e1580441888331.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834110" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Scat Widebody套装包括四个加阔轮拱、四个20&#215;11吋Devil&#8217;s铝合金锻造轮圈、四个305／35ZR20四季跑车轮胎、前Brembo六活塞刹车及刹车碟，以及可变阻尼悬挂系统等，务求令392引擎全力输出时，汽车有能力将力量消化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_10-e1580441860550.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834106" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">虽然Challenger有五个座位，但后排进出都要先把前座推前，所以Challenger仍是适合两人家庭；尾厢的容量超乎想像，由于车身阔度大，可放入一些大型东西，整个尾箱容量为16.2立方呎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834111" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834111" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">方向盘后方设有Paddle Shift拨片，可作升与降档之用，但是它跟其他汽车的Paddle Shift不一样，它只有看见的部分才是，下方则是音响的按键。变速杆顶部采用横向设计，事实上也颇顺手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_13-e1580441877610.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">车内是集高科技与传统于一身，测试车装备了8.4吋轻触式萤幕，内里运行Uconnect 4系统，可兼容Apple CarPlay或Android Auto两种，后视镜头也是基本配备。此外，R/T Scat Pack Widebody标配了276瓦扩音机，并有6个高级扬声器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834112" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Dodge特别为R/T Scat Pack设计了一个“Angry Bee”徽章，除了贴在在车身两旁之外，在车内前座椅的椅背上也可见这只“<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%92%E8%9C%82.md">怒蜂</a>”呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11834105" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_07-e1580441850786.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11834105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2019_Dodge_Challenger_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Dodge Challenger R/T Scat Pack Widebody。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
