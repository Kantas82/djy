<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>除夕夜 应景的年菜佳肴庆团圆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c66579a7745f6aa9603bc17ad1d9fd4f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中国新年，应景的年菜佳肴，庆团圆。图为过年美食：新式年糕。（袁玫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年01月25日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E5%A4%95.md#1">除夕</a>之夜，阖家团圆，到处充满了温馨的年的味道；初一拜年，互道恭喜，这就是中国人最重视的节日黄历新年。而在海外的华人也越来越重视这个传统，过年准备应景年菜，难得一见的亲朋好友同聚一堂欢度佳节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11819328" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11819328" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9948b6eea33bc3e2d5df9b84e5aaaeea.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11819328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9948b6eea33bc3e2d5df9b84e5aaaeea-600x736.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="613" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11819328" class="wp-caption-text">中国新年，应景的<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C%E4%BD%B3%E8%82%B4.md#1">年菜佳肴</a>，庆团圆。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自古，过年食物菜肴都有它的含义：吃年糕年年高，大朋友年年高升，小朋友快高长大。年糕除有传统的黄年糕、红豆年糕、黑糖年糕、红枣年糕、萝卜糕等等外，还有许多创意年糕，酥皮年糕就是其中一种，脆香的酥皮，涂上一层蛋黄液，松脆外皮将黑糖年糕、红豆年糕裹在里边，外面再洒上黑芝麻，一口咬下奶香、蛋香，品尝年糕香润、层次分明不同的美味，令人惊喜，齿颊留香，难以忘怀。</p>
<p>还有，鱼是年夜饭不能缺少的年菜，象征年年有余，无论各种烹调方式都是以全鱼呈现，但不能吃完，喻其年年丰盛有余。在<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>被评为米其林一星的天普市那家小馆是正黄旗官府北方菜，菜色多样精致，服务好；店内的紫茄鳟鱼，道地的北方菜。鳟鱼鲜嫩，片片茄子铺在其上，是非常惊艳的绝配。</p>
<p>另外，过年人们家里总会摆上一盆橘子，橙色鲜艳的外皮，象征大吉大利、吉祥好运。吃发糕，有“ 发财”的意思，热腾腾刚出炉的发糕，松软诱人，无法抗拒的一定要吃一口。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11819334" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11819334" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/602d1b11b2aca9065f66cf1ab196de1e.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11819334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/602d1b11b2aca9065f66cf1ab196de1e-600x665.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="554" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11819334" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜水果：橘子。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11819322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11819322" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/002ed87685744e819aab80d5e0b69fa9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11819322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/002ed87685744e819aab80d5e0b69fa9-600x591.jpg" alt="" width="498" b="491" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11819322" class="wp-caption-text">中国新年，应景的<ahref="https://github.com/srzsma3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C%E4%BD%B3%E8%82%B4.md#1">年菜佳肴</a>，庆团圆。图为发糕。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
