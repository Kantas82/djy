<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>经典年菜料理 (1) ～软嫩多汁的红烧肉</h2>
<a href="https://db1uzmukbk5lb.cloudfront.net/T2ePTSV1o"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cufnqn370/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/cufnqn370/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>好媳妇就要学会这一道经典<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">年菜</a>料理，入口即化、软嫩多汁的<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E8%82%89.md#1">红烧肉</a>。让人忍不住饭一口接一口～<br />
炒糖色让<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E8%82%89.md#1">红烧肉</a>油亮亮吃起来香甜不死咸，带有淡淡的焦糖香，加入话梅更解腻。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【食材】</strong></span><br />
五花肉600g<br />
姜片5片<br />
马铃薯2颗<br />
酸梅1颗</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【调味料食材】</strong></span><br />
酱油50cc<br />
绍兴酒20ml<br />
冰糖20g<br />
花椒少许<br />
八角1~2个颗<br />
水200cc</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span><br />
1.先起油锅爆香香料，再猪肉放剪至焦香，取出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11805408" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11805408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/94826a3b76d785a4d29b681349c8505f.png"><img class="wp-image-11805408 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/94826a3b76d785a4d29b681349c8505f-450x252.png" alt="红烧肉,C2食光" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11805408" class="wp-caption-text">先起油锅爆香香料<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11805402" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11805402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3884c7fad00a50adb96d327dd7371ec0.png"><img class="wp-image-11805402 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3884c7fad00a50adb96d327dd7371ec0-450x254.png" alt="红烧肉,C2食光" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11805402" class="wp-caption-text">将猪肉放剪至焦香<ahref="https://github.com/cufnqn370/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11805403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11805403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5150a7e6a09b19d22c46553ef0c9cbcc.png"><img class="wp-image-11805403 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5150a7e6a09b19d22c46553ef0c9cbcc-450x254.png" alt="红烧肉,C2食光" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11805403" class="wp-caption-text">红烧肉（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.放入冰糖炒糖色，之后再放入猪肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11805406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11805406" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b44f01e6c90bf31ef40e89993ae3ea22.png"><img class="wp-image-11805406 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b44f01e6c90bf31ef40e89993ae3ea22-450x254.png" alt="" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11805406" class="wp-caption-text">在油锅中放入冰糖炒糖色（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.放入姜片，加酱油与水、酸梅煮开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11805409" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11805409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c29e84d15c88633f00e1a3f2beb22fa4.png"><img class="wp-image-11805409 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c29e84d15c88633f00e1a3f2beb22fa4-450x253.png" alt="红烧肉,C2食光" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11805409" class="wp-caption-text">放入姜片，加酱油与水、酸梅煮开。（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.放入马铃薯煮至水滚，盖上锅盖转小火煮45分钟后，开盖收汁。</p>
