<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2020屏东热博开幕  苏揆邀请民众屏东走春</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6a0a77feb69f8beecf693250bb55053b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020屏东热带农业博览会18日热闹登场，行政院长苏贞昌莅临开幕。 （简惠敏／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月18日讯】（大纪元记者简惠敏台湾屏东报导）2020屏东热带农业博览会18日热闹登场，行政院长<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E8%B4%9E%E6%98%8C.md#1">苏贞昌</a>在屏东县长潘孟安陪同下体验超人气明星OPEN家族彩绘稻田，揭开为期22天的<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E7%83%AD%E5%8D%9A.md#1">屏东热博</a>活动。苏邀请全国民众新年期间到屏东，见证进步中的屏东农业结合科技、艺术展现的深度与厚度。</p>
<p>县长潘孟安表示，屏东是农业大县，是全台花卉、蔬果及畜渔产重要生产基地，为发挥农业软实力，县府以农试所育成彩稻品种，种出不同造型的彩稻图腾，并将园区融入以花卉、蔬果、稻米、水果、杂粮等农作为元素，谱出美丽可食地景图案，每年不同主题的<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C%E7%83%AD%E5%8D%9A.md#1">屏东热博</a>吸引造访游客屡创新高。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11802808" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/dd0c655f9b785caa4d5b2989292f3a97.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802808 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/dd0c655f9b785caa4d5b2989292f3a97-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802808" class="wp-caption-text">屏东热博超人气明星OPEN家族彩绘稻田。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>潘孟安感谢农委会协助在热博园区成立屏东物产馆，明年完成设立后将成为全县农渔畜产品交易中心。他强调，今年热博园区26大亮点主题，不仅好逛、好玩，更是优质伴手礼展示基地，观迎大家一起前进屏东，过好年、买好物、吃好食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11802809" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/f468d3568998d101a290bbd3e704d2db.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802809 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/f468d3568998d101a290bbd3e704d2db-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802809" class="wp-caption-text">迎春花海。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11802810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/a5bfcd5c44b34c9645dc1ab724b6da82.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802810 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/a5bfcd5c44b34c9645dc1ab724b6da82-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802810" class="wp-caption-text">迎春花海。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>行政院长<ahref="https://github.com/equmdn325/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%8F%E8%B4%9E%E6%98%8C.md#1">苏贞昌</a>肯定潘孟安是最会行销的首长，从台湾灯会到结合屏东在地特色农业与地景艺术的热带农业博览会，成功打响“我屏东、我骄傲”，他表示，屏东热带农业博览会举办多年，农渔畜产品质优良，屏东农业的转型，令人耳目一新，邀请全国民众新年期间一定要来屏东热博一游。</p>
<p>今年热博园区共设置26大亮点主题，包括浪漫缤纷入口花海，花园里有各式草花拼凑成的穿山甲、朱鹂、海龟、梅花鹿等图样，花海中的树屋杉林馆及竹梦馆，介绍国产材加工、文创商品及全台种类最多彩瓜廊道。入园就送OPEN小将限量纸帽子、提袋，还有OPEN家族热闹大游。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11802811" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5befc099d67acb043bd880fa6d26104a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802811 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5befc099d67acb043bd880fa6d26104a-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802811" class="wp-caption-text">梦幻彩瓜廊道。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11802812" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/e6770a0e74a70908b89a84eaf2e5a79f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802812 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/e6770a0e74a70908b89a84eaf2e5a79f-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802812" class="wp-caption-text">树屋杉林馆。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，还有魔法凤梨主题乐园、泡泡彩鱼区、鸭力解放馆、玫瑰馆、客家产业馆、泡泡彩鱼区、青草特作、国产杂粮、地景艺术、农村再生、好农好物市集等，天天都有采果乐，亲子同游、户外教学、情侣约会、团聚旅游，让你打卡拍照。</p>
<p>县府表示，今年前来热带农业博览会，可优先下载i-Ping屏东智慧导览农城app，园区特色介绍及优质农特产商家一手掌握，还有优惠卷可下载，让你行囊满满，尽收屏东伴手礼。外地民众请上屏东热博官网https://2020ptae.tw查询，内有丰富的热博游程排定，想报名周间活动则请随时关注屏东农好脸书。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11802815" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8657a9019fba12aa0029b8ce357883a1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802815 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8657a9019fba12aa0029b8ce357883a1-600x377.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="377" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802815" class="wp-caption-text">农村再生、好农好物市集。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11802814" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11802814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/fc5243e75e27638b87061093c4245b23.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11802814 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/fc5243e75e27638b87061093c4245b23-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11802814" class="wp-caption-text">农村再生、好农好物市集。（简惠敏／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2020屏东热带农业博览会活动从18日起至2月9日止，除1月24日除夕休园外，每日开园时间为早上9点到下午5点，免收入园门票，另屏东火车站有接驳车前往园区，提供民众搭乘。</p>
