<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】牛排粒伊面</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7bd146bd2db4b7c6489fda43556c9a04-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>摆在盘子上的牛排粒伊面，在红椒和青椒的陪衬下，颜色亮丽，香浓四溢。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>伊面，又称“伊府面”，台湾叫做“意面”，是一种油炸的蛋面。伊面是中国著名面食之一，也是粤菜经典主食，因在民间广泛地流传与改良，传至世界各地。</p>
<p>相传伊面为清代官员伊秉绶发明。因他为官“有政声”，加上书法成就颇高，故与他交游的文人墨客不少。为此伊府上常常是宾客接踵而至，就连负责准备宴饮的厨师也跟着忙碌。</p>
<p>伊秉绶为了给家中的厨师减轻负担，命人把面粉和鸡蛋掺水和匀，擀成面条状后卷曲成团，晾干后再下油锅炸至金黄色捞起。这样的鸡蛋面条因含水量极低，可保存较长时间不变质。客人一到，便可随时取用。食用时只需用开水一冲，加上炒制好的香菇、瘦肉等佐料即可。后来为纪念伊秉绶，此种面食得名“伊府面”。</p>
<p>牛肉中富含丰富蛋白质，氨基酸组成比猪肉更能贴近人体的需要，能提高机体抗病能力，对生长发育及术后、病后调养的人在补充失血、修复组织等方面特别适宜。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%8E%92%E7%B2%92%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">牛排粒伊面</a>，极富特色，口味与众不同。煮开的全蛋伊面要两面过油，而且牛排粒要经过几次煎炸，首先中火过油，淋干油分后，再次入锅煎香， 然后依次加入白酒、生抽、牛油、蚝油、黑胡椒等调料，蜂蜜为这道菜增加了甜蜜与幸福的滋味。</p>
<p>摆在盘子上的<ahref="https://github.com/bsaxfv3321/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%8E%92%E7%B2%92%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">牛排粒伊面</a>，在红椒和青椒的陪衬下，颜色亮丽，香浓四溢。伊面外脆里滑，吸入了黑椒牛油汁、牛排汁，香浓之余略带蜂蜜的香甜味。</p>
<h2>牛排粒伊面</h2>
<p>主料：牛排2磅，伊面1块（煮开）。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/01ebef2454f642c7555f315221575a70.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12109613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/01ebef2454f642c7555f315221575a70.jpg" alt="" width="377" b="231" /></a></p>
<p>配料：青椒、红椒。</p>
<p>调料：奶油、蜂蜜、蚝油、美极酱油、黑胡椒、盐。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 牛排切粒，用老抽腌制。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-30.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-30-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将牛排粒用中温火过油浸至五成熟，捞起淋干油分。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用锅底余油将伊面两面煎香。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 捞起淋干油分，放在碟子上。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109619" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 再利用锅底余油将半熟的牛排粒煎香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加入生抽、白酒各半汤匙，炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加入牛油、清鸡汤和黑胡椒等调料。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 添加蜂蜜、蚝油、美极酱油、盐等调料。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 添加青椒和红椒丁，焖香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 把炒香的牛排丁放在伊面上面。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品的牛排伊面香气四溢。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12109627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
