<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>桃园妇产科名医徐鸿洋 义居堂 ─ 宋瓷展</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/a15ee7df0d7d95f97fc02014837272d1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宋 磁州窑红绿彩婴儿枕。（桃园市文化局提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年05月06日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>报导）桃园市文化局3楼第二展览室自即日起至5月24日展出义居堂─<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E7%93%B7.md#1">宋瓷</a>展，展出者徐鸿洋医师是桃园妇产科名医，闲暇时以鉴赏和<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%94%B6%E8%97%8F%E6%96%87%E7%89%A9.md#1">收藏文物</a>为乐，并以“义居堂”为名号。展览以其收藏的宋瓷为主，展出约70件藏品。徐鸿洋说：“收藏是个人学习，个人玩赏，提高文化素养的事。收藏使自己与美零距离，收藏是深度的欣赏”，透过展览和民众一起分享和品味经典文物。</p>
<figure id="12086956" aria-describedby="caption-12086956" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/aac36642c24dddab10b7b73029d54e81-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/aac36642c24dddab10b7b73029d54e81-450x450.jpg" alt="北宋 磁州窑黑剔花玉壶春瓶。." width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12086956" class="wp-caption-text">北宋磁州窑黑剔花玉壶春瓶。<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E5%9B%AD.md#1">桃园</a>市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E7%93%B7.md#1">宋瓷</a>造型简洁、优美，恰当的比例和尺度，这些特质影响徐鸿洋对美感的鉴赏与对瓷器艺术的研究。</p>
<figure id="12086957" aria-describedby="caption-12086957" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/e82a3bb789582ed0fddec58681b3b3bc-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/e82a3bb789582ed0fddec58681b3b3bc-450x450.jpg" alt="宋定窑婴儿枕。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12086957" class="wp-caption-text">宋定窑婴儿枕。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>经过数十年不间断地收藏和研究各国博物馆瓷器记录，因此累积了古玩鉴赏的广度与深度，犹如修行般安住、自在，也借此修心养性。</p>
<figure id="12086959" aria-describedby="caption-12086959" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7e61907058fbeed4a7019642a9ef9f72-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7e61907058fbeed4a7019642a9ef9f72-450x337.jpg" alt="北宋越窑青瓷双狮枕。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12086959" class="wp-caption-text">北宋越窑青瓷双狮枕。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“义居堂─宋瓷展”，让文物之美，自然流露历史的容颜，以最亲近市民的角度与距离，诉说一场瓷器之美的故事。</p>
<figure id="12086958" aria-describedby="caption-12086958" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/0e962993d2a0694e7b9047ca3d7229ed-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/0e962993d2a0694e7b9047ca3d7229ed-450x338.jpg" alt="宋青瓷茶盏托。" width="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12086958" class="wp-caption-text">宋青瓷茶盏托。（桃园市文化局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>徐鸿洋邀请来宾欣赏。除本次展出的藏品外，还有更多的砚台、鎏金佛、各式骨董家具等珍藏与民众共赏。徐鸿洋希望透过展览，建立更宏观文物史观并推广文化艺术品味，提升文物鉴赏力，让所有的桃园市民零距离接触，认识古文物的精妙悠远气蕴。◇</p>
