<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>与邵雨薇演绎千年之恋 贺军翔拍戏自备额温枪</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318272122-ss1-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（左起）贺军翔、邵雨薇5月6日在台北出席台视、三立新备档周日偶像剧《天巡者》媒体现场采访。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年05月06日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%86%9B%E7%BF%94.md#1">贺军翔</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E9%9B%A8%E8%96%87.md#1">邵雨薇</a>6日首度为新戏《<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%B7%A1%E8%80%85.md#1">天巡者</a>》曝光古装造型，最近拍摄正逢“中共病毒”（武汉肺炎）疫情，当演员不能戴口罩，让贺军翔特别紧张，担心把病毒带回家影响家人，自带额温枪到片场检测体温。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087975" style="width: 449px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318432122-ss1-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12087975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318432122-ss1-1.jpg" alt="邵雨薇、贺军翔" width="449" b="526" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087975" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%82%B5%E9%9B%A8%E8%96%87.md#1">邵雨薇</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E5%86%9B%E7%BF%94.md#1">贺军翔</a>曝光新戏《<ahref="https://github.com/jqegmt3431/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A9%E5%B7%A1%E8%80%85.md#1">天巡者</a>》古装造型。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>邵雨薇笑说“我从来没被量过，他都是（额温枪）量自己”，还说片场只要有人咳嗽，贺军翔就会马上“弹出去”，闪得远远的。</p>
<p>两人在剧中上演钟馗与孟婆的千年宿命之恋，贺军翔赞美邵雨薇的古装造型很有灵气。聊起合作默契好，邵雨薇笑说因为两人都是幼稚的人，在片场时常打闹、互亏对方而变熟识。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319472122-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12087928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319472122-ss1.jpg" alt="邵雨薇" width="450" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087928" class="wp-caption-text">邵雨薇。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319362122-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12087927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319362122-ss1.jpg" alt="邵雨薇" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087927" class="wp-caption-text">邵雨薇。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087924" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319302122-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12087924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060319302122-ss1.jpg" alt="贺军翔" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087924" class="wp-caption-text">贺军翔。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318462122-ss1-1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12087973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318462122-ss1-1.jpg" alt="贺军翔、邵雨薇" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087973" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）贺军翔、邵雨薇。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>贺军翔笑说，邵雨薇是笑点非常低的人，常因一句台词就NG笑场，例如他有次将台词“棋逢敌手”讲成“骑冯迪索”，逗得邵雨薇频频笑场，被冠上“笑场王”的封号。</p>
<p>邵雨薇表示，年轻时都看贺军翔演出的偶像剧，没想到两人有机会合作拍戏，开玩笑说：“你可是偶像剧的鼻祖！原来你这么年轻才36岁！”还说：“要不然你女儿也来客串！”</p>
<p>当被问到“是否想让3岁的女儿美宝客串或拍广告当童星？”时，贺军翔表示，女儿热爱表演，在家常跳舞唱歌，还会自己演打电话桥段，但目前希望好好呵护她、不要让她太早接触复杂的世界，“怕她不受控，叫她演戏有点难，因为她看到人群会怕生。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12087906" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318332122-ss1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2005060318332122-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="贺军翔、邵雨薇" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12087906" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）贺军翔、邵雨薇5月6日在台北出席《天巡者》媒体现场采访。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
