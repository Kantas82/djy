<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】青瓜炒鲜虾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/644eefea3001d24883e0231817c1023a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨今天烹制的这道青瓜炒鲜虾，不仅清脆爽口、香气自然，而且味道鲜美，香而不咸。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>中国人认为， 人的饮食之道，离不开“自然”。中国古人因为深悟饮食之道，强调饮食“道法自然”的原理，通过高超的烹调技术、火候、刀工、调料等反映出菜的本味。这些体现的都是做菜的真功夫。</p>
<p>罗厨一直坚信：“原汁原味才是最好的味道。”因此，为了保证食材原味，他烹饪时基本不用人工调味料，而是根据不同食材的特点研究出加工烹调的最佳方法，同时选用自己慢火熬制的高汤来调味，精心的制作加上多年练就的功夫，令他做出的每道菜都会给客人带来惊喜。</p>
<p>罗厨今天烹制的这道<ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E7%93%9C%E7%82%92%E9%B2%9C%E8%99%BE.md#1">青瓜炒鲜虾</a>，不仅清脆爽口、香气自然，而且味道鲜美，香而不咸。</p>
<p>青瓜也称黄瓜、胡瓜、刺瓜，是夏季主要蔬菜之一。中医认为，青瓜味甘、性凉，可除热、利尿、解毒。</p>
<p>青瓜富含维生素、蛋白质、尼克酸，多吃可以美容护肤、清新口气。同时，青瓜对肾脏的保护具有一定的作用。青瓜含水量极高，且含丙醇二酸，可抑制糖类转化为脂肪，常被视为减肥食品。</p>
<p>虾肉富含蛋白质和钙质，Q弹嫩滑，虾壳又富含营养。所以制作这道菜时，罗厨没有选用虾仁，而是特别选用了无头带壳的鲜虾。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，制作菜肴时，要将鲜虾很充分地爆炒，使虾的味道充分地被青瓜吸收，被炒好的、比较干爽的鲜虾，反过来又能吸收更多的青瓜汁，使鲜虾又增加了自然的甜味。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/ecesbp3561/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E7%93%9C%E7%82%92%E9%B2%9C%E8%99%BE.md#1">青瓜炒鲜虾</a></h2>
<p>主料：青瓜2磅、带壳无头虾1磅。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9e5caa48b69c7f8c2840284361f5cd23.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129633" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9e5caa48b69c7f8c2840284361f5cd23-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：蒜头、红葱头、香菇。<br />
调料：盐、生抽、蚝油、生粉水。</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将青瓜洗干净后，切开去瓤。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-37.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1-37-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 青瓜切厚片，鲜虾洗干净后晾干水分。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 猛火阴油将香菇爆香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129639" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/3-19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 放入拍碎的蒜头，炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/4-15-450x273.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="273" /></a><br />
5. 放入鲜虾猛火爆炒至虾的香味很浓。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加入2汤匙生抽。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/6-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加入青瓜一起爆炒，加盖焗20秒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/7-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 加入盐、蚝油。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/8-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 加入红葱头一起爆炒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 炒至香气扑鼻后，放入生粉水。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-27.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/10-27-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清脆爽口，味道鲜美。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12129650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/11-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
