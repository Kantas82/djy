<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>辣椒酱内藏价值三亿元冰毒 悉尼两男被起诉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18025-100475325_ABF-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新州警方在从美国空运来的768瓶斯里拉查辣椒酱（Sriracha Hot Sauce）的瓶子中发现了约400公斤冰毒。（ABF）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年05月26日讯】（大纪元记者肖婕<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>悉尼编译报导）悉尼两名男子涉嫌卷入一起在辣椒酱中藏匿价值3亿多澳元<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%AF%92.md#1">冰毒</a>的案子。他们已被逮捕并遭到指控。</p>
<p>据九号新闻网报导，去年10月，新州警方在从美国空运来的768瓶斯里拉查辣椒酱（Sriracha Hot Sauce）的瓶子中发现了约400公斤<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%AF%92.md#1">冰毒</a>，估计黑市价值超过3亿澳元。已有7名涉案人被捕。</p>
<p>上周五，警方逮捕了两名年龄分别为21岁和50岁的男子。警方说他们与一个洗钱和<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%B0%E7%A7%81%E6%AF%92%E5%93%81.md#1">走私毒品</a>的调查有关。</p>
<p>21岁的格兰维尔（Granville）区男子<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7.md#1">被控</a>故意处理犯罪所得，他于周二在中央地方法庭出庭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12137317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12137317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18024-100799141_ABF.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12137317 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18024-100799141_ABF-450x258.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12137317" class="wp-caption-text">在从美国空运来的768瓶斯里拉查辣椒酱（Sriracha Hot Sauce）的瓶子中发现约400公斤冰毒。（ABF）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12137316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12137316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18023-99107250_NSW.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12137316 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18023-99107250_NSW-450x591.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="591" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12137316" class="wp-caption-text">缴获的现金（新州警方）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12137314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12137314" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18020-98425525_NSW.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12137314 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18020-98425525_NSW-450x593.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="593" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12137314" class="wp-caption-text">嫌疑人（新州警方）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12137315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12137315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18021-98851694_NSW.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12137315 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/2020-05-26-18021-98851694_NSW-450x590.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="590" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12137315" class="wp-caption-text">嫌疑人（新州警方）</figcaption></figure>
<p>50岁的男子在格兰维尔附近被捕，警方在他的汽车里查获一台点钞机、一部手机和大量现金，包括6.3万澳元、1000欧元和1000美元。</p>
<p>警方还在汽车后备箱的一个冷藏袋里缴获了50万澳元现金，并在搜查悉尼圣苏西（Sans Souci）区的一个住宅时发现了相关文件。</p>
<p>50岁男子<ahref="https://github.com/mixtwp303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7.md#1">被控</a>两项故意处理犯罪所得罪，他将于周三在伯伍德（Burwood）地方法庭受审。警方将在法庭上指控他为一个涉及毒品走私的有组织犯罪集团洗钱。</p>
