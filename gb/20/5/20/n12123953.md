<h6>夏天到了！就是要来一碗“透心凉”的凉面。（C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%8B%E5%A4%8F.md#1">立夏</a>节气</span></strong></h2>
<p>时序的滚轮来到了五月，正式进入了夏季第一个节气“<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%8B%E5%A4%8F.md#1">立夏</a>”。这时气温炎热，人们体温上升，新陈代谢也相较旺盛，情绪上就容易浮躁不安。因此，补充足量的水分，避免脱水、中暑。</p>
<p>中医有个概念：冬病夏治，在夏日增加“阳气”避免秋冬有皮肤过敏等病兆出现。而夏天在五行中属火，对应五脏属“心”，因此多以<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%BB%E5%BF%83.md#1">养心</a>食物为佳，可以多吃一些红色食材补血、入心、养气。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #003366;">立夏建议食材 ｜红萝卜、番茄、枸杞、红椒、黄瓜、芦笋</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12126962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12126962" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/daa246eb9d6e42af5f0cda16443a21a3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12126962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/daa246eb9d6e42af5f0cda16443a21a3-450x253.jpg" alt="立夏,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12126962" class="wp-caption-text">立夏节气（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">日式<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">凉面</a>（中华<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E9%9D%A2.md#1">冷面</a>）</span></h2>
<p>夏天到了！就是要来一碗“透心凉”的<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">凉面</a>。怎么能不安抚一下受热的身躯呢？只要准备好食材，各种时蔬都可以尝试当作配料，一一摆盘在<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B7%E9%9D%A2.md#1">冷面</a>上面缤纷美丽，看了都会食欲大开。别忘了凉面的灵魂是“酱汁”，可以根据自己的口味调整调味料和比例，或是尝试一下食谱提供的作法。</p>
<p>尽管阳光不留情面的照映地面上的人们，空气弥漫着一股热呼呼又黏腻的湿气，但是只要吃下爽口开胃的凉菜料理，就会暑气全消又照顾营养喔！</p>
<p>说起日式凉面的由来，在日本被称为“中华冷面（冷やし中华）”，因为是由中华料理变化而来的，也是日本人夏季相当喜爱的美食之一。</p>
<h2>【食材】</h2>
<p>油面1把<br />
蛋2颗<br />
小黄瓜1条<br />
火腿2~3片<br />
蕃茄几片<br />
海苔丝适量</p>
<h2>【酱汁食材】</h2>
<p>日式酱油50cc<br />
清酒10cc<br />
味醂10cc<br />
柴鱼片5g<br />
糖20g</p>
<h2>【酱汁做法】</h2>
<p>将酱油、糖煮开后加入清酒、味醂之后放入柴鱼片，关火浸泡至放凉，过滤后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12126971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12126971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9b396269cdb04263e19605a71d4344f3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12126971 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/9b396269cdb04263e19605a71d4344f3-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12126971" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【面作法】</h2>
<p>1. 锅热后倒入蛋液，之后盖上锅盖闷一下，翻面后再微煎一下出锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12126969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12126969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1df347ad469a0085c378b68edd187521.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12126969 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/1df347ad469a0085c378b68edd187521-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12126969" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将蛋皮，黄瓜、火腿切丝备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12126970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12126970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/84ffba8210d7f757a46482f8e043521e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12126970 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/84ffba8210d7f757a46482f8e043521e-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12126970" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蕃茄切片。<br />
4. 水滚后下面条后，捞出泡冰水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12126972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12126972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5cdd209e14f536e4c1597aea5882b4a5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12126972 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5cdd209e14f536e4c1597aea5882b4a5-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12126972" class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
