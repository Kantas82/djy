<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】冬令进补 药膳莲子海参鸡煲汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1850068597__1_-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>又到了冬令进补时刻！搜集美味健康的药膳鸡汤食谱是必要的。（图／shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语</strong>：冬令进补的时间来了，还在烦恼什么<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%85%B2%E6%B1%A4.md#1">煲汤</a>老少皆宜吗？相信“港式炖汤”会是很好的选择，善用中药材，肉质选用低脂高蛋白的童子鸡，熬出的鸡汤少油腻，却有满满的丰富氨基酸，对于身体细胞修补很有益处，而且只要有炖锅或是电锅就可以完成，非常简单！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619030" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619030" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">莲子</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%AD%90%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">童子鸡汤</a>。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">莲子</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%AD%90%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">童子鸡汤</a>?</span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p>淮山 1.5 oz<br />
杞子 1 oz<br />
茨实 1.5 oz<br />
莲子 1 oz<br />
百合 1 oz<br />
冬菇 5个<br />
无花果 4粒<br />
童子鸡 1只（约 1.5磅）<br />
湿海参 1.5磅<br />
陈皮 1块<br />
姜 3片<br />
水 16碗</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619023" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619023" class="wp-caption-text">鸡汤炖煮食材。（图／<ahref="https://github.com/hcyfcy393/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12619022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619022" class="wp-caption-text">准备童子鸡、湿海参、姜片、陈皮。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 所有材料用水洗干净后，浸泡 20分钟后备用。<br />
2. 童子鸡去皮去除脂肪，切成四大块，用姜汁酒烫过，再清洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619024" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619024" class="wp-caption-text">童子鸡去皮去除脂肪，切成四大块，用姜汁酒烫过，再清洗干净。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 海参切块，用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619025" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619025" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-11-450x388.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619025" class="wp-caption-text">海参切块，用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 汤煲内放入16碗水，把海参和杞子外的所有材料都放入，用大火煮滚15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619026" class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入16碗水，把海参和杞子外的所有材料都放入，用大火煮滚15分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 接着转为小火再多炖2个小时，然后加入海参和杞子续煮20分钟，便可食用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12619027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12619027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12619027" class="wp-caption-text">加入海参和杞子续煮20分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
