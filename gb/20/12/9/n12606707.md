<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>想吃怕变胖？低糖燕麦布朗尼香甜解嘴馋</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1414338914-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>用燕麦粉取代面粉的布朗尼蛋糕，对麸质过敏的人也适用。（图／shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>小编语：美式小点“<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E6%9C%97%E5%B0%BC.md#1">布朗尼</a>”是大人、小孩的共同最爱，浓郁口感有<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF%E7%B2%89.md#1">可可粉</a>的香甜，加入切碎坚果更是有嚼劲。但是也有人说高热量布朗尼不健康，今天让我们调整一下食谱，让<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E9%BA%A6.md#1">燕麦</a>取代面粉、甜菊糖取代蔗糖，做一道无麸质、低糖、水果香甜的<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E6%9C%97%E5%B0%BC.md#1">布朗尼</a>吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5939d283abfae304b516f357a8edb099.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5939d283abfae304b516f357a8edb099-450x540.jpg" alt="布朗尼" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606774" class="wp-caption-text">布朗尼是欧美大人小孩的共同最爱，在节庆或日常都很常食用的点心。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>天然甜味 “<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E9%BA%A6.md#1">燕麦</a>布朗尼”</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>
<p>燕麦粉 25g<br />
无糖<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF%E7%B2%89.md#1">可可粉</a> 15g<br />
甜菊糖 15g<br />
泡打粉 1/8 小匙<br />
黑巧克力 15g<br />
切碎的<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E6%9E%A3.md#1">椰枣</a>（或<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%BF%E9%A5%BC.md#1">柿饼</a>）20g<br />
低脂牛奶 2大匙＋3大匙<br />
鸡蛋 1颗<br />
葡萄籽油 15g<br />
香草精 1/8小匙</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1 将黑巧克力、切碎的<ahref="https://github.com/qezyik366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A4%B0%E6%9E%A3.md#1">椰枣</a>、低脂牛奶2大匙放入耐热容器中混合，微波加热30 秒，进行2 ～ 3 次，让材料变软且好压碎。</p>
<p>* 微波加热时需注意煮沸后会溅出，因此最好使用稍大一点的耐热容器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606751" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606751" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/681f69f0204b5abcae9b860bbcefd700.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/681f69f0204b5abcae9b860bbcefd700.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606751" class="wp-caption-text">取一耐热容器，放入黑巧克力碎片、切碎的椰枣。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2 用叉子压碎并拌匀后，静置冷却。</p>
<p>* 如果没有完全冷却的话，可能会让步骤4 中的鸡蛋变熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606752" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606752" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6be89a686e21f68e639fd5ecc3048dde.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6be89a686e21f68e639fd5ecc3048dde.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606752" class="wp-caption-text">用叉子压碎并拌匀后，静置冷却。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3 将燕麦粉、可可粉、甜菊糖、泡打粉一起过筛。</p>
<p>* 烤箱预热170 度。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606773" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606773" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/260281a793a83dcd6072c1778a0aa346.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/260281a793a83dcd6072c1778a0aa346.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606773" class="wp-caption-text">将燕麦粉、可可粉、甜菊糖、泡打粉一起过筛。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4 取另一个碗，放入鸡蛋、葡萄籽油、低脂牛奶3大匙、香草精拌匀，再加入2和3，用刮勺切拌混合，直到没有飞粉为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606753" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1a690d4406e1f2f70f971e53c67864c0.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1a690d4406e1f2f70f971e53c67864c0.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606753" class="wp-caption-text">取另一个碗，放入鸡蛋、葡萄籽油、低脂牛奶3大匙、香草精拌匀，再加入作法2和3的食材。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5 将两个12×7×3cm 长方形蛋糕模铺上烘培纸，倒入面糊并铺平，为了消除面糊间的气泡，可将蛋糕模大力敲打桌面2 ～3 次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12606754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606754" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3e4be4d027d243c9837c530a21189080.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3e4be4d027d243c9837c530a21189080.jpg" alt="燕麦布朗尼" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606754" class="wp-caption-text">取两个12×7×3cm 长方形蛋糕模铺上烘培纸，倒入面糊并铺平。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6 放入预热170 度的烤箱中，烤约20 ～ 25 分钟，用筷子插入中间的部分，不会沾上面糊即可。</p>
<p>* 也可以在烤之前，放上切碎的坚果类、巧克力饼干、巧克力碎片等。<strong>?@</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12606755" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12606755" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/04da282a4b0bcca0a1b2cfa0dcd62b36.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12606755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/04da282a4b0bcca0a1b2cfa0dcd62b36.jpg" alt="布朗尼" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12606755" class="wp-caption-text">烘烤前可以在面团上面放入切碎坚果类、巧克力饼干、巧克力碎片。（图／高宝书版）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12590820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12590820" style="width: 102px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eefe36f2e9e4dd38e14287c6fc5d660e-e1606912703904.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12590820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eefe36f2e9e4dd38e14287c6fc5d660e-450x659.jpg" alt="燕麦" width="102" b="149" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12590820" class="wp-caption-text">《好想吃喔！燕麦的美味新吃法》书封。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
