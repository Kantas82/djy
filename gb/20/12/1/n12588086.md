<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>那个眨眼的瞬间</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/just-a-wink-of-an-eye-600-600x329.jpg" />
<h6>（图片由韩亦言提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>昨天夜里?我见到了你<br />
就在那一条沿河的街上<br />
你瞅见我?我的眼睛<br />
也看着你的方向！</p>
<p>锁在一起的?是你我的双眼<br />
你的眼睛缤纷异彩<br />
呵?是街上的路灯<br />
让它放出绚丽的光芒！</p>
<p>尽管?你的诱人<br />
嘴唇紧闭着?可你<br />
动人的眼睛<br />
说出万语千言……！</p>
<p>那是一个完美的夜晚<br />
天空?熠熠星光<br />
你我?目光一对接<br />
瞬间?就夺走了我的心！</p>
<p>呵?在那样迷人的时刻<br />
你我的心?热切地渴望<br />
美美的一个吻<br />
万般恋欲的井喷<br />
就在那个一眨眼的瞬间！</p>
<p>你的感情?是那样的强和烈<br />
你的秋波?激荡我的心潮澎和湃<br />
你我?双双坠入情海<br />
这种莫名的冲动<br />
就在那个一眨眼的瞬间！</p>
<p>你我?这次的相遇是缘<br />
命中注定 不可避免<br />
呵 情感的大潮<br />
紧紧地拥抱着你我<br />
向神秘莫测的尽头冲去！</p>
<p>注：<br />

娟-奥斯廷夫人的原英文诗可查阅下面网页<br />

<p><strong>Just A Wink Of An Eye</strong></p>
<p>Last night I saw you<br />
On the waterfront<br />
You glanced at me<br />
And I looked your way!</p>
<p>Our eyes locked together<br />
And your eyes shifted colors<br />
As they caught the light<br />
Installed at the waterfront!</p>
<p>Though your alluring lips<br />
Were sealed<br />
Yet those lovely eyes spoke<br />
A thousand words&#8230;!</p>
<p>The night was perfect<br />
With star studded sky<br />
You winked at me<br />
And snatched my heart right away!</p>
<p>It was a wonderful moment<br />
Our hearts craved for a kiss<br />
With the bliss<br />
Everything happened<br />
In just a wink of an eye!</p>
<p>You were utterly crazy<br />
Your glance made me crazy<br />
We were both swept away<br />
By this undefined ecstasy<br />
In just a wink of an eye!</p>
<p>We soared the inevitable<br />
We could not resist<br />
The high tides of emotion<br />
It knocked us down<br />
To the very end!</p>
<p>Rose Marie Juan-Austin, Missouri, USA<br />
