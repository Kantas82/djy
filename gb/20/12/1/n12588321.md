<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>瘦肚子运动跟着做 腹肌上下左右都练到</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/belly-thin-1129-wg-health11.jpg" />
<h6>想要让腹部瘦得平坦有型，重训增肌绝对是必要的。（World Gym Blog提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong>怎样才能让小腹赘肉消失、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B9%E8%82%8C.md#1">腹肌</a>凸出？跟着世界健身俱乐部（Wolrd Gym）的教练一起做<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%82%9A%E5%AD%90.md#1">瘦肚子</a><ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a>，训练上腹、下腹、侧腹和核心肌群，全方位减<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B9%E9%83%A8%E8%84%82%E8%82%AA.md#1">腹部脂肪</a>。</span></p>
<p>为什么乖乖做<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B9%E8%82%8C.md#1">腹肌</a>训练，还是见不到腹肌的身影呢？因为腹肌被脂肪盖住了，也就是你的体脂率不够低。基本上女生体脂率在20％以下；男生在15％以下，腹部线条就会开始显现出来啰！想要<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%82%9A%E5%AD%90.md#1">瘦肚子</a>除了控制饮食减少<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%85%B9%E9%83%A8%E8%84%82%E8%82%AA.md#1">腹部脂肪</a>以外，通过4个瘦肚子动作，持之以恒的做，帮你全方位紧致小腹。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;">4个全方位瘦肚子<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a></span></h2>
<h3>1. 摸膝卷腹：训练上腹</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12588524" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1a96157266d165798c798cdc01781951.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-large wp-image-12588524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1a96157266d165798c798cdc01781951-600x336.gif" alt="瘦肚子运动1：摸膝卷腹，训练上腹。（World Gym提供）" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瘦肚子运动1：摸膝卷腹，训练上腹。<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #188638;"><a style="color: #188638; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1a96157266d165798c798cdc01781951.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击看动图。</a></span></span>（World Gym提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>动作：</strong></p>
<p>采仰躺姿，双脚屈膝，膝盖和脚尖成一直线，双手往前伸直轻碰大腿。</p>
<p>吸气预备，吐气时，将感受放在腹部上，卷起身体，抬起肩膀和上背，让双手可以碰到膝盖，再慢慢回到起始位置，重复动作。</p>
<h3>2. 抬脚卷腹：训练下腹</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12588526" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f098e7839b6146fb331a9672d064c105.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-large wp-image-12588526" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f098e7839b6146fb331a9672d064c105-600x336.gif" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瘦肚子运动2：抬脚卷腹，训练下腹。<span style="color: #188638;"><a style="color: #188638;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f098e7839b6146fb331a9672d064c105.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击看动图。</a></span>（World Gym提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>动作：</strong></p>
<p>采仰躺姿，双手放在身体两侧，双腿并拢，稍微抬离地面。</p>
<p>吸气预备，将感受放在下腹部，吐气时抬起双腿，尽量让臀部也离开地面，再慢慢回到起始位置，重复动作。</p>
<h3>3. 屈膝钟摆：训练侧腹</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12588530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258ca21c3600317bf48c63cc5dfea43.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-large wp-image-12588530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258ca21c3600317bf48c63cc5dfea43-600x336.gif" alt="瘦肚子运动3：屈膝钟摆：训练侧腹。（World Gym提供）" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瘦肚子运动3：屈膝钟摆，训练侧腹。<span style="color: #188638;"><a style="color: #188638;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258ca21c3600317bf48c63cc5dfea43.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击看动图。</a></span>（World Gym提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>动作：</strong></p>
<p>采仰躺姿，双手向身体两侧打开，手掌贴地帮助身体稳定。</p>
<p>双脚屈膝并拢，并抬起双腿，吸气预备，吐气时将感受放在腹部两侧，慢慢让双脚轮流往左侧和右侧移动。</p>
<h3>4. 空中踢腿：核心训练</h3>
<figure id="attachment_12588534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/56120638cf220c5095049ccaf279de93.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="size-large wp-image-12588534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/56120638cf220c5095049ccaf279de93-600x336.gif" alt="瘦肚子运动4：空中踢腿：核心训练。（World Gym提供）" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瘦肚子运动4：空中踢腿，核心训练。<span style="color: #188638;"><a style="color: #188638;" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/56120638cf220c5095049ccaf279de93.gif" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击看动图。</a></span>（World Gym提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>动作：</strong></p>
<p>采仰躺姿，双手抱头但不给予太大压力。</p>
<p>抬起双脚让大腿和小腿都离开地面，交替上下移动，重复动作，过程中记得保持自然的呼吸节奏，不憋气喔！</p>
<p>最后跟大家厘清一个观念，想要瘦肚子绝对不是狂做腹部训练就有用的，必须搭配全身性的燃脂运动和饮食控制，才能达到减脂显腹效果。</p>
