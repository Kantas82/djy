<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>饮食失调水肿 自我改善按摩8部位 顺便消橘皮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_1412672537-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>按摩脚底反射区的穴位可以改善食欲不振、体重过重。(图／Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #188638;"><strong>【编者按】</strong><ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%AE%E9%A3%9F.md#1">饮食</a>不规律，太能吃或吃不下，都会让你变胖或过瘦；也有些人虽然吃得不多，却依然<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%82%BF.md#1">水肿</a>发胖、甚至长<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E7%9A%AE.md#1">橘皮</a>组织。用精油<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">按摩</a>手脚8大部位，帮你调节饮食和食欲、消除水肿橘皮。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #188638;"><ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A5%AE%E9%A3%9F.md#1">饮食</a>不规律导致过胖或过瘦</span></h2>
<p>长期的饮食不规律是非常不可轻忽的情况，因为它容易导致过度饥饿或体重过重，有时也会因此营养不良。反射区<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">按摩</a>（如下图）可以改善这种状况，但也要注意饮食，必要时，也可以寻求心理咨商师的协助。</p>
<p>建议搭配精油：薰衣草精油。</p>
<p><strong>● 脚底反射区的按摩部位：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12611234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12611234" style="width: 504px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone1-cs-health11.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12611234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone1-cs-health11.jpg" alt="改善食欲不佳或过盛的穴位指压，图为脚底示意图。（采实文化提供）" width="504" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12611234" class="wp-caption-text">改善食欲不佳或过盛的穴位指压，图为脚底示意图。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 手心反射区的按摩部位：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12611235" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12611235" style="width: 507px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone2-cs-health11.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-12611235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone2-cs-health11.jpg" alt="改善食欲不佳或过盛的穴位指压，图为手心示意图。（采实文化提供）" width="507" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12611235" class="wp-caption-text">改善食欲不佳或过盛的穴位指压，图为手心示意图。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">食欲不振</span></h2>
<p>如果是因为身体过于虚弱、患病或是在术后恢复期时食欲不佳，可以按压位于胃经上的这个穴位。</p>
<p><strong>● 脚背胃经上的穴位：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12611248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12611248" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone3-cs-health11-600x379.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12611248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone3-cs-health11-600x379.jpg" alt="按压位于胃经上的这个穴位，可以缓解大病初愈的食欲不振。（采实文化提供）" width="503" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12611248" class="wp-caption-text">按压位于胃经上的这个穴位，可以缓解大病初愈的食欲不振。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%8C%E9%A3%9F%E7%97%87.md#1">厌食症</a>是严重的饮食失调，需要接受正规住院治疗。不进食的话，该怎么活下去呢！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">不是吃胖的怎么瘦？<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E7%9A%AE.md#1">橘皮</a>与下肢<ahref="https://github.com/ubsbdl387/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E8%82%BF.md#1">水肿</a>按摩法</span></h2>
<p>活动肌肉，特别是水下运动，如水中律动、水中跑步、水中飞轮，是消除橘皮最有效的方式，搭配局部反射区按摩疗法能帮助身体排除废物。按压穴位建议搭配丝柏精油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12467139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12467139" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1413295310.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12467139" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1413295310.jpg" alt="丝柏" width="500" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12467139" class="wp-caption-text">丝柏精油香味如雨后森林般的清新木味，对于想初次尝试精油的人是很好的选择。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 脚底、手心反射区的按摩部位：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12611279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12611279" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone5-cs-health11.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-12611279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone5-cs-health11.jpg" alt="橘皮与下肢水肿时，按摩图上的脚底、手心反射区穴位。（采实文化提供）" width="499" b="535" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12611279" class="wp-caption-text">橘皮与下肢水肿时，按摩图上的脚底、手心反射区穴位。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>● 脚背、手背反射区的按摩部位：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12611314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12611314" style="width: 422px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone6-cs-health11.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-12611314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/diet-reflection-zone6-cs-health11.jpg" alt="橘皮与下肢水肿时，按摩图上的脚背、手背反射区穴位。（采实文化提供）" width="422" b="543" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12611314" class="wp-caption-text">橘皮与下肢水肿时，按摩图上的脚背、手背反射区穴位。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>把四根手指摆在脚背上，从脚踝开始滑向脚背中央。想像自己是个园丁，用耙子爬梳这个区域。请人帮忙的话，会更容易操作。</p>
