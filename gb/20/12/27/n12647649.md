<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自由的孩子</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1127mother-and-daughter-600-600x394.jpg" />
<h6>（母亲和女儿，图片来自维基公共领域，韩亦言提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #000080;">题记：为了自由的孩子，更为了孩子的自由</span></p>
<p>哦 亲爱的宝贝快快长大<br />
妈妈要告诉你一句话<br />
“你妈妈的言论被监控过”<br />
这曾经发生在我们民主的国家</p>
<p>哦 亲爱的宝贝不用害怕<br />
我们生活在民有民治民享的国家<br />
人权和<ahref="https://github.com/ssdabc3483/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A8%80%E8%AE%BA%E8%87%AA%E7%94%B1.md#1">言论自由</a>是普世价值<br />
谁也不可违背美国的宪法</p>
<p>注：受恺莱．麦克恩莱在2020年10月15日所发推文的启发。她的推文说“有一天，我会告诉她什么是(言论)审查，而且为了发声，她不得不昧着良心删除妈妈的推文！”</p>
<p><strong>Free Child</strong><br />
&#8211; To all freedom loving mothers<br />
By Yiyan Han(c), 2020-10-19</p>
<p>Oh please grow up fast my sweetheart<br />
I want to tell you Mommy&#8217;s tweet<br />
was once grossly censored<br />
on our free democratic land</p>
<p>Oh my darling baby don&#8217;t be fearful<br />
as our country is of, by, and for the people<br />
Human rights and free speech are universal values<br />
that the American constitution guarantees</p>
<p>Note: This poem is inspired by Kayleigh McEnany&#8217;s tweet on 15 October 2020 &#8211; “One day l will explain to her what censorship is and why she had to unjustly delete Mommy&#8217;s tweet in order to speak!”</p>
