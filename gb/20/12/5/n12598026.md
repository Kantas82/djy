<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车太铉作客《露营》 朴素丹假哭骗倒综艺前辈</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050650111487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>演员朴素丹最近参与主持全新韩国综艺节目《感性露营》的录制。图为资料照。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年12月05日讯】（大纪元马维芬报导）全新韩国综艺节目《<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%80%A7%E9%9C%B2%E8%90%A5.md#1">感性露营</a>》是五个想要旅行的女明星们在韩国名胜场地，以异国感性享受两天一夜的实境综艺，由知名主持人朴娜敕、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%8B%B1%E7%BE%8E.md#1">安英美</a>，携手女团偶像“Apink”<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E5%A8%9C%E6%81%A9.md#1">孙娜恩</a>、“MAMAMOO”颂乐和演员<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E4%B8%B9.md#1">朴素丹</a>共同主持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653061487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598038" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653061487-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598038" class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%80%A7%E9%9C%B2%E8%90%A5.md#1">感性露营</a>》前往海边录制。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最新一集来宾邀请到演员<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%A4%AA%E9%93%89.md#1">车太铉</a>作客，一行人到海边玩冲浪，但<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B4%E7%B4%A0%E4%B8%B9.md#1">朴素丹</a>因为身体不适，唯独她没下水，没想到此举却引来<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E5%A4%AA%E9%93%89.md#1">车太铉</a>不满、数落她“不合群”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598037" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598037" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653121487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598037" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653121487-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598037" class="wp-caption-text">车太铉现身《感性露营》外景，当来宾参与录制。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他还质问“连怕水的<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E8%8B%B1%E7%BE%8E.md#1">安英美</a>前辈都下去了，你身体有不舒服成这样吗”，让朴素丹委屈哽咽，但争论发展到最后又出现惊喜反转。</p>
<p>车太铉认为冲浪应该要全体一起参与才有意思，不断撂话责怪朴素丹缺席，连颂乐都看不下去，问说“哥你为什么一直针对她”，现场气氛几度尴尬，朴娜敕则在旁试图缓和气氛，但车太铉仍显得耿耿于怀。</p>
<p>正当陷入僵局之际，朴娜敕打开汽车后车厢，看到飞出的“Happy Birthday”气球，才揭晓一切都是“生日整人计划”，让寿星朴娜敕和安英美又惊又喜，朴素丹装出差点被骂哭的样子，也让众人盛赞“果真是奥斯卡电影的演技”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653181487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598040" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653181487-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598040" class="wp-caption-text">车太铉（左起）、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AD%99%E5%A8%9C%E6%81%A9.md#1">孙娜恩</a>、安英美、朴娜敕、颂乐到海边冲浪。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12598041" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598041" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653091487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598041" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2012050653091487-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598041" class="wp-caption-text">众人帮朴娜敕（右一）、安英美（右三）庆生。（friDay影音提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，众人到海边冲浪时，以苗条身材著名的孙娜恩穿上冲浪连身泳衣现身，她也在推特上大方分享花絮照<ahref="https://twitter.com/Apink_2011/status/1334822258167095297/photo/2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点看</a>），她也在冲浪时展现运动细胞，成功掌握冲浪诀窍、大秀乘风破浪英姿，让网友直呼“又美又帅”。</p>
<p>车太铉还在节目中大跳20年前，他身为唱跳歌手的夯曲，节目里播出画面，只见他身穿西装与戴黑色墨镜，舞蹈动作俐落，受到少女粉丝热爱，让一起露营的众人陷入回忆杀，网友打趣说：“这是爸爸（指车太铉）还是唱跳歌手的往日时光。”朴素丹现场看到车太铉舞蹈，直喊：“好酷！”</p>
<p>《感性露营》每周六于friDay影音同步跟播，而下一集节目，将回顾众人童年时期的怀旧回忆，邀来高大帅气的演员郭时旸做客。</p>
