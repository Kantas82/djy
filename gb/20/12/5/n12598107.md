<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作蜡烛香氛 为自己留一段纾压疗愈时光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_top_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花朵装饰烛台。（图/朵琳出版）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/yrwdct367/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%A1%E7%83%9B.md#1">蜡烛</a>对许多人来说或许只是精致的摆设，但当你享受火光＆香气围绕的疗愈时光，体验到蜡烛创作的多样与创意性时，你会发现原来蜡烛也可以这么贴近生活！只要学会基础，再搭配详细步骤进行，你也能创造出如同艺术品般细致、充满情调的蜡烛。无论是自我收藏装饰或赠与他人，都会是一份充满心意、温度的礼品。这样的小巧美好，只要动手就能体会！</p>
<p>本文介绍花朵装饰烛台，推荐给喜爱手作，想在生活里增添小浪漫，提升生活质感氛围的您！</p>
<h2>花朵装饰烛台</h2>
<p>组合了两种种类的干燥花和玻璃纱的装饰烛台。让烛台与火焰的高度同高，使火光与素材交织，让观者在跨越作品看到火光时，也能被火光所倾迷。</p>
<p>【材料】</p>
<p>● 干燥花??不同大小的2种（蜡菊、千日红）<br />
● 玻璃纱缎带??2m<br />
● 耐热容器??80ml（直径70mm×高45mm×底部直<br />
径50mm）1个<br />
● 石粉黏土??200g<br />
● 压克力颜料??珠光系、白色、与干燥花同色系的颜色<br />
※ 花朵使用相近色，花瓣也准备类似的两种干燥花。<br />
※ 缎带选择玻璃纱等，较薄又带有透明感的材质。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598694" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598694" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_material_01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598694" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_material_01-450x307.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598694" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台使用的材料。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【道具】</p>
<p>60号砂纸（偏粗）、120号砂纸（一般粗度）、240号<br />
砂纸（偏细）／木工专用接着剂（或热熔枪）／吹风<br />
机／竹签／剪刀／小盘子／平笔／镊子<br />
※ 吹风机用来凝固木工专用接着剂，以固定干燥花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598695" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_material_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598695" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_material_02-450x306.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598695" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台使用的道具。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【作法】</p>
<p>1.将黏土延展成圆棒状，圈起容器 之后将两端结合固定。黏土干 了之后会缩水，做出来的圆圈直 径约15～20mm，待黏土干了之 后，还要空出装饰物与容器的距 离约5mm，黏土圈可以做得比容 器直径大两圈左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598112" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598112" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598112" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_01-450x307.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598112" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法1。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.待黏土固定之后，以砂纸修整整体形状。从颗粒较粗的开始使用到一般粗度、细小颗粒的砂纸，使黏土表面变得光滑。放置在平面上，确认是否会摇晃不稳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598119" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598119" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598119" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_02-450x305.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598119" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法2。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.在圆圈上用缎带打死结（打两次 结）牢牢绑住。一个缎带结使用 200mm，一整圈使用约20～23个 缎带结，分段剪开后绑上。打结 时将结尽可能拉小，每段缎带结 的间隔约5mm。一个一个绑在圆 圈上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598698" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_03-450x306.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598698" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法3。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将干燥花以外的部位剪去。准 备各式各样从小的花苞到最大 35mm的花朵。干燥花的花瓣容 易折损，使用时要注意力道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_04-450x308.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598701" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法4。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将步骤4准备的花朵分成五堆， 一堆里面放入各种大小的花，使 其有所变化。小的花朵不足时， 可以拨去大朵花的花瓣来充用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598702" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_05-450x306.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598702" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法5。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.用接着剂在步骤3完成的圆圈周 围黏上花朵。由正上方确认整体 轮廓，将装饰物分五处分配，花 朵负责在各范围之内遮住缎带打 结的地方黏上圆圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598708" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_06-450x307.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598708" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法6。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.从大朵的花开始黏起。不要使花 朵面朝下。将容器放在圆圈内， 为了不让周围塌陷，用镊子在圆 圈与容器间塞进小的花朵。稍微 裂开的花也可以用其他没有裂开 的角度朝外，黏上圆圈。缎带不 够的时候可以另外增加上去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598710" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_07-450x306.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598710" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法7。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将缎带剪短。将缎带尾端朝不同 角度及方向斜斜剪去，使其和圆 形花朵产生对比。为了不让缎带 引火燃烧，长度不可以超出烛台 高度。下半部也要修整得可以看 得到圆圈的形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598712" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_08-450x307.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598712" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法8。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9.在花瓣上涂上亮点。调和比例一 半的珠光白和与花色相似的颜 料，薄薄地涂在会照到亮光的花瓣上或想要特别加以强调的地方 即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12598716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12598716" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12598716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/flower_candle_procudure_09-450x307.jpg" alt="书摘, 烛台" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12598716" class="wp-caption-text">花朵装饰烛台作法9。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
