<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>苗可丽叫卖变见面会 小薰婚期“几年后考虑”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474675-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>苗可丽面对客人要求合照、表演骂人，可说是成了小型粉丝见面会。　（LINE TV提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月16日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E5%8F%AF%E4%B8%BD.md#1">苗可丽</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%98%87%E8%B1%AA.md#1">温昇豪</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E6%9C%89%E4%BC%A6.md#1">柯有伦</a>、“<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%96%B0.md#1">小薰</a>”黄瀞怡、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E7%81%BF%E5%BE%97.md#1">蔡灿得</a>、张书伟7位明星16日出席实境秀《今天谁来店》记者会，分享到老店叫卖趣事，苗可丽面对客人要求合照、表演骂人，可说是成了小型粉丝见面会；小薰被问到婚期则透露“几年后才会考虑”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12624563" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624563" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474673.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624563" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474673-600x760.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="760" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624563" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8B%97%E5%8F%AF%E4%B8%BD.md#1">苗可丽</a>面对客人时，成了小型粉丝见面会。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>实境秀《今天谁来店》邀请7位明星走访7间老店，协助老店转型再行销，充满人情味与传承意义。包含苗可丽走访“平溪古早味面茶”、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E8%96%B0.md#1">小薰</a>走访“德安堂中药行”、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%98%87%E8%B1%AA.md#1">温昇豪</a>走访“阿娇柑仔店”、<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E6%9C%89%E4%BC%A6.md#1">柯有伦</a>走访“南投喷水餐厅”、王彩桦走访“新竹稳捷机车行”、张书伟走访“南投添兴窑”，而刚加入任务计划的<ahref="https://github.com/axjxqy377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E7%81%BF%E5%BE%97.md#1">蔡灿得</a>则透露想走一趟影院风。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12624579" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624579" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474688.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474688-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624579" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）柯有伦、张书伟、“小薰”黄瀞怡、苗可丽、蔡灿得、温昇豪。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12624578" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624578" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474687.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474687-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624578" class="wp-caption-text">（左起）柯有伦、张书伟、“小薰”黄瀞怡、苗可丽、蔡灿得、温昇豪。　（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>众人在记者会现场分享拍摄过程中印象深刻的事，金钟视后苗可丽在大街上叫卖“古早味面茶”吸引民众围观，不少客人要求合照、录影，甚至表演骂人，可以说是成了小型粉丝见面会，也让她大呼“做生意真不简单”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12624573" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624573" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474682.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624573" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474682-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624573" class="wp-caption-text">小薰在中药行变成“吃货”。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12624571" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624571" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474681.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624571" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474681-600x814.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="814" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624571" class="wp-caption-text">小薰在中药行变成“吃货”。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小薰在中药行变成“吃货”，每个都想用嘴巴尝，吓的老板娘赶快阻止。她制作养身甜点时，食材喷飞、料理机器解体，差点成了灾难现场。与郑人硕交往的她被问到婚期，回应说：“几年后才会考虑。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12624576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624576" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474685.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474685-600x389.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624576" class="wp-caption-text">温昇豪叫卖在短短5分钟就达标。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12624577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624577" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474686.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474686-600x662.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="662" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624577" class="wp-caption-text">温昇豪叫卖在短短5分钟就达标。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温昇豪逛传统菜市场不亦乐乎，根本快忘记任务。看到很多摊子，包含外星刀疗法、精品街试穿衣服首饰、鸡肉摊、牛肉摊试吃等，每家店都让他眼睛一亮。他挑战当外送员一点也不别扭，就连叫卖也在短短5分钟就达标。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12624575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624575" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474684.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474684-600x672.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="672" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624575" class="wp-caption-text">柯有伦PK餐厅老板获胜。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12624574" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474683.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624574" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474683-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624574" class="wp-caption-text">柯有伦PK餐厅老板获胜。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12624570" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12624570" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474680.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12624570" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/474680-600x559.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="559" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12624570" class="wp-caption-text">柯有伦PK餐厅老板获胜。（LINE TV提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>柯有伦挑战学习三道招牌料理，还要让路人盲测是否比老板做的好吃，所幸他对于自己的厨艺相当有信心，PK老板获胜。</p>
<p>张书伟学习拉陶器，笑说：“有师傅帮助有80分，我自己做可能只有30分。”蔡灿得刚加入节目计划，期待能走一趟怀旧影院，“我人生很多重要的决定都在电影院，陪伴你的就是放映师。”</p>
