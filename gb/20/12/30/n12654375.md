<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>滋补温润小甜点 杏仁豆腐简单易做</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/ea0c4633756599497d1e854ad9e70b1a-600x400.png" />
<h6>杏仁豆腐用牛奶盒制作一点都不困难！（图／高宝书版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>手工<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E4%BB%81%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">杏仁豆腐</a>清香软嫩，美味的秘诀就在食材的选择。而且，杏仁豆腐滋补温润、Q软弹牙，在冬天也是不容错过的可口小点。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>直接用<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6.md#1">牛奶</a>盒做的<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E4%BB%81%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">杏仁豆腐</a></strong></span></h2>
<p>每个人从小到大的梦想！装满整个<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6.md#1">牛奶</a>盒的大分量杏仁豆腐。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E4%BB%81%E9%9C%9C.md#1">杏仁霜</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">鲜奶油</a>正是美味的秘诀。可以在进口食品专卖店或是烘焙材料行买到<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8F%E4%BB%81%E9%9C%9C.md#1">杏仁霜</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/ea0c4633756599497d1e854ad9e70b1a.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/ea0c4633756599497d1e854ad9e70b1a-450x646.png" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="646" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛奶盒装做的杏仁豆腐，完成时打开就像拆礼物！（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料　</span></h3>
<p>1公升牛奶盒1个（一份）<br />
A：杏仁霜?9大匙 / 砂糖?6大匙<br />
牛奶（使用森永制菓好喝牛乳）?700cc<br />
吉利丁粉? 15g<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">鲜奶油</a>?200cc</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<p>1.在1公升牛奶盒全新未开封的状态下，倒出300cc，让盒中只剩下会用到的700cc牛奶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654407" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/be942d29d092aea7f4e00244b8710bd2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/be942d29d092aea7f4e00244b8710bd2-450x335.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在1公升牛奶盒全新未开封的状态下，倒出300cc。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.把700cc里的500cc牛奶倒入耐热玻璃碗中，加入材料Ａ，以打蛋器搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/049098270b8b54d5c24a05a644c9de5b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/049098270b8b54d5c24a05a644c9de5b-450x333.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">耐热玻璃碗中倒入500cc牛奶，加入材料搅拌均匀。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.以微波炉加热3分～3分30秒，将材料拌匀至溶解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1390571e7a53a5e94e506619808dcee6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1390571e7a53a5e94e506619808dcee6-450x331.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">微波炉至材料拌匀溶解。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.趁热加入吉利丁粉并快速搅拌，使其完全溶解。</p>
<p><strong>※POINT</strong></p>
<p>为了方便从盒中取出，完成的<ahref="https://github.com/dahstt3965/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E4%B8%81.md#1">布丁</a>口感会偏硬。若是想要绵滑一点的口感，可以调整吉利丁粉的用量。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/28309b7d8ab8f84142de8acf74901e58.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/28309b7d8ab8f84142de8acf74901e58-450x327.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="327" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入吉利丁粉并快速搅拌至完全溶解。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.一边用滤茶器过滤，一边将步骤4的杏仁豆腐液倒入装有200cc牛奶的牛奶盒中，并加入鲜奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/16192aafc33236837344dd7747032fa2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/16192aafc33236837344dd7747032fa2-450x331.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一边用滤茶器过滤，一边将步骤4的杏仁豆腐液倒入装有200cc牛奶的牛奶盒中，并加入鲜奶油。（图文／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.用手将封口密合压紧，上下摇晃，使全体充分混合。接着放入冰箱冷藏5～6小时，使其冷却凝固。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654412" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6235030a904dd6ab8a5005d4cab82b9b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6235030a904dd6ab8a5005d4cab82b9b-450x327.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="327" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用手将封口密合压紧，上下摇晃，使全体充分混合。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
