<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Q弹珍珠粉圆 2种材料轻松自己做</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1f67982b056be65719e45eb79cbb059d-600x400.png" />
<h6>用太白粉和黑糖做的珍珠弹牙Ｑ嫩，又没有食物添加物，吃了非常放心！（图／高宝书版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>小编语：</strong>“台湾之光”的<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md#1">珍珠</a>粉圆在全球的饮料店相当风行，Ｑ弹的口感连阿兜仔（台语：外国人）也很喜爱，加在奶茶里更是浑然天成的好喝、美妙。现在自己下厨做，少了食品添加剂，吃了也会放心。而且，只要2种食材和水就可以做成了，非常简单容易！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">用<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E7%99%BD%E7%B2%89.md#1">太白粉</a>做Q弹<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8F%8D%E7%8F%A0.md#1">珍珠</a></span></h2>
<p>用<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E7%99%BD%E7%B2%89.md#1">太白粉</a>做出来的珍珠，咬起来Ｑ韧又<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B9%E7%89%99.md#1">弹牙</a>，正是珍珠会有的口感。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料　1人份</span></h3>
<p>A：<ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E7%B3%96.md#1">黑糖</a>? 10g / 水?20cc<br />
太白粉?20g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">步骤</span></h3>
<p>1.将材料A放入锅子中，以橡胶刮刀搅拌后，用大火煮至沸腾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/79068d027f36c8a9628557ee11d8d1bf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/79068d027f36c8a9628557ee11d8d1bf-450x369.jpg" alt="高宝" width="450" b="369" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/svfrxl307/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%91%E7%B3%96.md#1">黑糖</a>、水倒入锅子中，以橡胶刮刀搅拌后，用大火煮至沸腾。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.关火，使其稍微冷却。加入太白粉，尽快拌匀至没有结块的光滑糊状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/a9da22ba69822bec0abbb2a80bf81feb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/a9da22ba69822bec0abbb2a80bf81feb-450x365.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="365" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">关火，使其稍微冷却。加入太白粉，尽快拌匀。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.等面糊成团后取出，使其形成棒状，切细，搓圆成红豆大小。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5c4bf58956c5f0653abe87697630deff.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5c4bf58956c5f0653abe87697630deff-450x362.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面糊成团后取出，使其形成棒状，切细，搓圆成红豆大小。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将水煮至沸腾，加入步骤3搓好的珍珠面团，轻轻搅拌，避免面团黏在一起。等珍珠都浮起来后，转小火煮约20分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/ebccea692c65bfc90671b30078258821.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/ebccea692c65bfc90671b30078258821-450x359.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="359" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">水煮至沸腾，加入步骤3搓好的珍珠面团，轻轻搅拌。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将煮好的珍珠用漏勺捞起，沥干水分。放入碗中后，以冷水快速冷却。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12654032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/99e574a339103d4796f3ec528c20b878.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12654032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/99e574a339103d4796f3ec528c20b878-450x362.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮好的珍珠用漏勺捞起，沥干水分。放入碗中后，以冷水快速冷却。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>◎ MEMO</h3>
<p>这份食谱做出来的珍珠不适合放太久， 煮好后请尽快享用。</p>
