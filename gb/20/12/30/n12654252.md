<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吴宗宪怨圣诞节没交换礼物 吴姗儒安抚老爸</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477181-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>吴姗儒出席家电发表会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月30日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%A7%97%E5%84%92.md#1">吴姗儒</a>（Sandy）今年担任桃园<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%A8%E5%B9%B4.md#1">跨年</a>晚会主持人，她30日出席家电记者会，笑着分享：“衣服不嫌多。”不过父亲<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%AE%97%E5%AE%AA.md#1">吴宗宪</a><ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%AA%E5%93%A5.md#1">宪哥</a>）日前吐苦水没被邀玩交换礼物，Sandy表示考量父亲工作忙所以没约，送飞吻安抚“Love You，嗯嘛！”</p>
<figure id="12654271" aria-describedby="caption-12654271" style="width: 352px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477189-450x639.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477189-450x639.jpg" alt="吴姗儒出席家电发表会。" width="352" b="500" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12654271" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%A7%97%E5%84%92.md#1">吴姗儒</a>出席家电发表会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴姗儒手上有多档节目，副业也经营有成，今年初更发表新书。她受邀出席家电活动，笑称目标是“内外兼具”的女人，无论出席任何场合都能完美登场。</p>
<figure id="12654268" aria-describedby="caption-12654268" style="width: 367px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477187-450x611.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477187-450x611.jpg" alt="吴姗儒担任桃园跨年晚会主持人。" width="367" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12654268" class="wp-caption-text">吴姗儒担任桃园<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B7%A8%E5%B9%B4.md#1">跨年</a>晚会主持人。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为准备桃园跨年晚会这场大型活动，吴姗儒经挑选各种洋装及配件，笑说：“衣服不嫌多！刚好给我一个好机会购买新衣。”她也爆料因为爸爸衣服总是乱塞而产生皱折、沾染雪茄味，都分辨得出是哪家KTV。</p>
<figure id="12654262" aria-describedby="caption-12654262" style="width: 353px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477185-450x638.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477185-450x638.jpg" alt="吴姗儒担任桃园跨年晚会主持人。" width="353" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12654262" class="wp-caption-text">吴姗儒担任桃园跨年晚会主持人。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E5%AE%97%E5%AE%AA.md#1">吴宗宪</a>日前向媒体吐苦水，家人在圣诞节有玩交换礼物，自己却没被邀约，“我不要再当一个尽责的父亲了，我父亲的工作就到今天为止了。”</p>
<p>对此，吴姗儒笑着解释，家人都知道父亲在圣诞节当天排满行程，不想刺激他所以没有先说，不过事后有安抚他，现在与爸爸已经没事了。她向镜头送飞吻：“<ahref="https://github.com/pyolwc3608/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%AA%E5%93%A5.md#1">宪哥</a>爱你喔！Love You，嗯嘛！”</p>
<figure id="12654260" aria-describedby="caption-12654260" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477184-450x660.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/477184-450x660.jpg" alt="吴姗儒担任桃园跨年晚会主持人。" width="351" b="515" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12654260" class="wp-caption-text">吴姗儒担任桃园跨年晚会主持人。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴姗儒也苦笑说，自己准备了电烤炉来当圣诞节交换礼物，却抽到弟弟送的杯子，觉得很“杯”剧。</p>
