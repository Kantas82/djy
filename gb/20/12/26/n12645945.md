<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州20处最美海滩精选（下）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Gibson-Steps-Beach-2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为澳洲维州Gibson Steps海滩。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月26日讯】（大纪元记者李宿驿<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）澳大利亚维多利亚州从来就不缺漂亮的<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>，这里就为您介绍Escape网站列出的维州最美20处海滩。</p>
<h2>GIBSON STEPS</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/148303-56-e1609126361635.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648454" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/148303-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Gibson Steps Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648528" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Gibson-Steps-Beach-1-e1609128516500.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Gibson-Steps-Beach-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Gibson Steps Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里是维州最宏伟的<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>了，位于大洋路旁。最出名的是矗立在海面上的高大石灰岩。</p>
<p>大部分人都不知道这里可以游泳。从70米高的悬崖顶端向下走86级台阶，就到了沙滩。</p>
<h2>LAKE TYERS BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lake-Tyers-beach-1-e1609126526944.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648459" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lake-Tyers-beach-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前往Lake Tyers beach的步道。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648465" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lake-Tyers-beach.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lake-Tyers-beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Lake Tyers beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Lake Tyers地区有丰富的活动，学冲浪、捕虾、钓鱼和抓螃蟹，还可以去丛林里漫步、驾船出海或者在隔离区里游泳。</p>
<p>来到这里，一定要到当地的饭馆里吃点东西，到海滨小镇转转。</p>
<p>附近还有个Ninety Mile Beach，也是不错的去处。</p>
<h2>NINETY MILE BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648468" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Ninety-Mile-Beach-e1609126686259.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Ninety-Mile-Beach-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ninety Mile Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Ninety-Mile-Beach-1-e1609128075839.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Ninety-Mile-Beach-1-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ninety Mile Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>90英里长的Ninety Mile Beach把Bass Strait和Gippsland Lakes分隔开。</p>
<p>黄沙依托着岩石绵延不绝，使这里成为了<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>第三长的海滩。</p>
<p>人们在这里游泳、钓鱼、步行观光、到Woodside和Seaspray的海滨小镇游览。</p>
<p>海滩的另一边，是静谧的Gippsland Lakes，放舟湖面，与沙滩另一边的大海相比，风景大不同。</p>
<h2>LORNE</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Lorne。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne-1-e1609126871661.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Lorne-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Lorne。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蓝绿色的水面上，可以游泳、冲浪；岸边的沙滩上，则可以野餐。Lorne就是这样一个地方，特别适合全家出游。</p>
<p>附近的Great Otway National Park，可供徒步旅行。</p>
<p>人们在Lorne码头上可以垂钓，梭鱼、沙鮻鱼、池鱼轮番上钩。</p>
<p>五月到九月间，天气变冷的时候，还能看到海上鲸鱼出没。</p>
<h2>LOCH ARD GORGE</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Loch-Ard-Gorge-e1609127333227.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648483" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Loch-Ard-Gorge-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Loch Ard Gorge。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>十二门徒往西，驾车3分钟，就到了Loch Ard Gorge。</p>
<p>蓝绿的海水、崎岖的丛林，这里有多处<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。</p>
<p>与前方波浪翻腾的海岸相比，这里相对安静。</p>
<p>这处海滩是以遇难船只的幸存者名字命名的。1878年Loch Ard被冲上了这片海滩，幸好他本人安然无恙。</p>
<h2>PORTARLINGTON</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648493" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Portarlington-1-e1609127541347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Portarlington-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Portarlington。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Portarlington-e1609127602155.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Portarlington-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Portarlington。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Port Phillip Bay的波涛柔美，这处海滩适合全家出游。</p>
<p>Portarlington与Indented Head相邻，这里有BBQ和野餐的地方，还有一处码头和大型露营地。附近还有些葡萄酒庄。</p>
<h2>INDENTED HEAD</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648499" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Indented-Head-e1609127723824.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648499" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Indented-Head-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Indented Head。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Indented Head坐落于Portarlington和St Leonards之间，海水慵懒地拍打着沙滩，是家庭度假出游的天堂。</p>
<p>这里有三处海边露营地，不同的季节可以有不同的选择。</p>
<p>1925年，一艘明轮蒸汽机船在附近海域沉没，沉船的残骸也成为了当地的一处<ahref="https://github.com/kdttuo305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。</p>
<h2>TORQUAY BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Torquay-beach-e1609127833956.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Torquay-beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Torquay beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Torquay Beach是冲浪者的天堂，无论你是新手还是专业冲浪者。</p>
<p>海滩南面的Cosy Corner和北边的Fisherman’s Beach海水却很平静，是游泳的好地方。</p>
<p>在镇上的餐馆里点上一份新鲜的鱼薯，没有比这更惬意的了。</p>
<h2>YCW BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648505" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/YCW-Beach-e1609127963494.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/YCW-Beach-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YCW Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>YCW Beach位于Phillip Island的南岸，有400米长。</p>
<p>这里没有配置救生员，不建议下水游泳，而冲浪在这里备受欢迎。</p>
<p>人们还会在海滩漫步、钓鱼，去到岩石上天然形成的水池里探索一番。</p>
<h2>RYE BAY BEACH</h2>
<p>Rye Bay Beach位于Port Phillip Bay的南岸，海面平静。</p>
<p>人们在码头上垂钓，孩子们噗通跃入海中。沙滩旁有BBQ和野餐的设施。</p>
