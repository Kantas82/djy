<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>维州20处最美海滩精选（上）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Bells-Beach-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Bells Beach是维州最著名的海滩。（Visit Victoria提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月26日讯】（大纪元记者李宿驿墨尔本编译报导）澳大利亚维多利亚州从来就不缺漂亮的<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>，这里就为您介绍Escape网站列出的<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>最美20处<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>。</p>
<h2>BELLS BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Bells-Beach-e1609123862556.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Bells-Beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Bells Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为Rip Curl世界冲浪巡回赛的比赛地点，Bells Beach是<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E5%B7%9E.md#1">维州</a>最著名的海滩。湛蓝的海水卷起巨大的海浪，向沙滩周边的悬崖峭壁涌来。</p>
<p>站在岸边悬崖顶端俯瞰，整个海滩好像一个天然的圆形剧场。</p>
<p>由于这里的海浪太大，只适合经验丰富的冲浪者，初学者尽量不要尝试。附近还有多处<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。</p>
<h2>PORTSEA</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/131825-56-e1609124290539.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/131825-56-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Portsea。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/131823-56-e1609124356751.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/131823-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Portsea。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在夏季，人们喜欢去莫宁顿半岛的Portsea海滩。沙丘上矗立着巨大的岩石，海水里的暗礁若隐若现，垂钓者在一旁耐心地守候。</p>
<p>人们从码头上纵身一跃，跳到海水里畅游，你还会看到更远处，冲浪者在波涛里起伏。</p>
<h2>FORREST CAVES BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133367-56-e1609124468788.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648396" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133367-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Forrest Caves Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133366-56-e1609124674697.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648406" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133366-56-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Forrest Caves Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Forrest Caves Beach位于Port Phillip Island的东南，当潮水从绵长的沙滩和巨大的岩石上退去，许多洞穴会显露出来。</p>
<p>这里不但受到冲浪者的青睐，也是垂钓者和徒步<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C.md#1">旅行</a>者的挚爱。</p>
<p>附近有停车场和厕所等公共设施。由于没有救生员，游泳之前请三思。</p>
<h2>APOLLO BAY</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/116582-56-e1609124804743.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/116582-56-600x359.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Apollo Bay。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Apollo Bay是一处适合游泳的海滩，同时也受到冲浪者和垂钓者的欢迎。</p>
<p>海滩旁有一处草地，非常适合野餐。附近有不错的餐馆，在这里住宿的选择也很多。</p>
<p>附近还有Otway Ranges森林，有很多适合徒步<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E8%A1%8C.md#1">旅行</a>的山路。</p>
<h2>REFUGE COVE</h2>
<p>Refuge Cove位于Wilsons Promontory的东部，风景如画的Refuge Cove只能通过Sealers Cove或者Little Waterloo Bay步行才能够到达。</p>
<p>在这里你可以感受一下丛林生活，只需要支付一点点露营的费用，就可以使用这里的基本设施了，包括饮用水和厕所。</p>
<p>清晨，一个猛子扎进清澈的海水中，顿时神清气爽。</p>
<h2>KITTY MILLER BAY</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648416" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133372-56-e1609125050561.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/133372-56-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Kitty Miller Bay。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这处美丽的海滩位于Phillip Island的南岸，山石环抱，海面平缓。各种海洋生物在礁石里生长。</p>
<p>这里既适合潜水，也适合沿着港湾漫步。不远处还能见到1906年遇难的船只SS Speke号的残骸。</p>
<h2>JOHANNA BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Johanna-Beach-e1609125174693.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Johanna-Beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Johanna Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Johanna Beach是南大洋号称“沉船港湾”上最危险的海滩。这里不适合游泳，但是颇受冲浪者和垂钓者的喜爱。</p>
<p>在海滩上既能够野餐也可以漫步，还可以在沙丘上露营。</p>
<p>Johanna Beach Lookout是值得一去的地方，海滩岩石上天然形成的水坑是这里的一大特色。</p>
<h2>CAPE PATERSON</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Cape-Paterson-Bay-Beach-e1609125280824.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Cape-Paterson-Bay-Beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Cape Paterson Bay Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位于墨尔本南部的Cape Paterson有巨石上天然形成的游泳池，还有两处海滩：Cape Paterson Surf Beach（又称First Surf）和Cape Paterson Bay Beach。</p>
<p>Bay Beach的海浪温和，但是Surf Beach则截然相反，非常适合冲浪者和喜欢在大浪里畅游的人们。</p>
<p>沙滩上有救生员，垂钓者们也喜欢在这里下饵。</p>
<h2>BRIGHTON BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Brighton-Beach.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Brighton-Beach-600x402.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="402" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Brighton Beach上的小木屋。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>提到Brighton Beach，就会想起海滩上五颜六色的小木屋，这些兴建于1900年代的木屋，让这里成为了维州最有特色的<ahref="https://github.com/jeclfs332/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AF%E7%82%B9.md#1">景点</a>。</p>
<p>这里波涛柔曼，非常适合全家老小到访。</p>
<p>来钓鱼的人也不少，附近还有一处Holloway Beach。</p>
<h2>FAIRHAVEN BEACH</h2>
<figure id="attachment_12648448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Fairhaven-Beach.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Fairhaven-Beach-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Fairhaven Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12648449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Fairhaven-Beach-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/Fairhaven-Beach-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Fairhaven Beach。（Visit Victoria提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Fairhaven Beach位于大洋路上，有6公里长，这里有两个冲浪俱乐部、一个救生俱乐部。附近到处都是适合游泳的地方，立式桨板和垂钓在这里也特别受欢迎。</p>
<p>海滩从崎岖的Otway Ranges的丛林里延伸出来，早已成为了度假圣地。</p>
<p>在海边的森林探险一番也是不错的选择，附近的景点有Cape Otway Lightstation和Otway Fly Treetop Adventures。</p>
