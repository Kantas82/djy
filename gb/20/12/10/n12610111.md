<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作圆珠烛台 享受宁静的烛光浪漫</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_cover_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白色圆珠的烛台。（图/朵琳出版）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>原来<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9C%A1%E7%83%9B.md#1">蜡烛</a>也可以这么贴近生活！只要学会基础，再搭配详细步骤进行，你也能创造出如同艺术品般细致、充满情调的蜡烛。无论是自我收藏装饰或赠与他人，都会是一份充满心意、温度的礼品。这样的小巧美好，只要动手就能体会！本文介绍非常简单好做的白色圆珠<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">烛台</a>和黑色<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E7%8F%A0%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">圆珠烛台</a>。</p>
<h2>白色圆珠的<ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">烛台</a>（Summer Morning）</h2>
<p>【材料】</p>
<p>雾面圆形白珠??直径2mm 6个、直径4mm 8个、直径6mm 8～10个、<br />
直径8mm 4～5个<br />
● 耐热容器??100ml（直径75mm×高58mm）1个<br />
● 细线??130cm<br />
● 粗毛线??180cm<br />
※ 作为烛台的耐热玻璃容器若杯口过窄，会因为燃烧所产生的二氧化碳无法消散而导致火焰熄<br />
灭，也有可能过度囤积热能导致玻璃破裂，所以尽可能选择宽口的容器来使用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610146" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_-material_01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_-material_01-450x303.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610146" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/dwbyin3339/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%86%E7%8F%A0%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">圆珠烛台</a>的材料。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【圆珠素材的选用方法】</p>
<p>● 不要使用圆角珠，需选用圆形且不会反射光的串珠材料。</p>
<p>● 圆珠的存在感如以下顺序增强：透明＜半透明＜不透明。选用透明度较高的串 珠，数量就需多准备一些。</p>
<p>【道具】</p>
<p>竹签／剪刀／木工专用接着剂／棉花棒／镊子／文镇／铝箔纸杯（接着剂用）／ 夹子</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610147" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_-material_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_-material_02-450x305.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610147" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台的道具。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【制作时注意】</p>
<p>● 整体的比重分配，下方为毛线，上方以圆珠为多。</p>
<p>● 接着剂不要黏在线或毛线与圆珠上，需直接黏在耐热容器上。</p>
<p>● 使用接着剂时以竹签取沾少量涂在黏贴面上。</p>
<p>【作法】</p>
<p>1.在耐热容器的侧面下方开始黏贴毛线。用竹签沾取少量接着剂涂抹于容器上，再取毛线往上面贴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610148" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_01.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_01-450x303.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610148" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法1。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.越往上方贴去，毛线之间的间隔便可取得越宽。一圈大约用接着剂固定四处即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_02-450x304.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610149" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法2。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.环绕到上方结束后，将最后一端黏贴固定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_03-450x308.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610150" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法3。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将细线一边先留下100mm后，开始 串珠。从小的圆珠开始串，再慢慢使用较大的圆珠。有时也可以突然穿插不同大小的圆珠。如果是使用不透明的黑色圆珠，搭配大颗圆珠时的间距要缩短一些。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610151" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_04-450x306.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610151" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法4。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将步骤4的串珠从底部算起15mm 的位置，在步骤3完成的杯上开始旋转（相当于毛线第三圈和第 四圈中间的位置）。涂抹接着剂于容器上，仅用来黏贴固定圆珠，线的部分不需要用接着剂来固定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610152" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_05-450x303.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610152" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法5。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.第一圈黏上最小的圆珠，约三个 左右的数量。越往上黏数量越多，圆珠的大小也越大。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610153" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610153" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_06-450x307.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610153" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法6。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.以竹签沾取接着剂，穿过第一个圆珠和最后一个圆珠的孔洞，来固定线头，使线头不会滑出圆珠外。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610154" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_07-450x304.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610154" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法7。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.在接着剂凝固之前，以夹子固定丝线。最后再剪掉第一个和最后 一个圆珠外的丝线即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12610155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12610155" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12610155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/candle_white_black_08-450x307.jpg" alt="烛台, 圆珠" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12610155" class="wp-caption-text">圆珠烛台作法8。（图/朵琳出版）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>黑色圆珠的烛台（Autumn Sundown）</h2>

● 耐热容器??100ml（直径75mm×高58mm）1个<br />
