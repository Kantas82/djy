<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>味蕾畅游南洋 圣诞打卡好吃好拍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473731-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>XL天使红虾痛风面。（笠正整合行销提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年12月11日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）现在虽然因为疫情无法出国，还是可以品尝异国美味，让“舌尖伪出国”。信义远百“FoodOpera食代馆 ”<ahref="https://github.com/gpquka3758/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>广场，汇集新马、港台、日韩等美食名店，不仅让人置身异国风情装潢，更因应<ahref="https://github.com/gpquka3758/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>节推出5折起的优惠，相当适合与三五好友<ahref="https://github.com/gpquka3758/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%81%9A%E9%A4%90.md#1">聚餐</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12612319" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12612319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473736.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12612319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473736-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12612319" class="wp-caption-text">“学记港式茶餐厅”招牌鲜虾云吞弹牙，汤头齿颊留香；还有艇仔粥绵密暖胃。（笠正整合行销提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“FoodOpera食代馆 ”囊扩亚洲驰名餐饮品牌，从新加坡名店、正宗南洋料理、日式与韩式料理通通有，也是全台第一间采用南洋峇峇娘惹风格地砖及当地莫兰迪色系墙面，呈现新加坡Katong区特有建筑物与用餐气氛，拥有约250坪空间、310个座位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12612314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12612314" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473732.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12612314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473732-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12612314" class="wp-caption-text">全台第一间采用南洋地砖及莫兰迪色系墙面的<ahref="https://github.com/gpquka3758/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>广场，呈现新加坡Katong区特有建筑物与用餐气氛。（笠正整合行销提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>学记港式茶餐厅</strong></p>
<p>拥有半世纪历史的学记粥面，是香港“元朗十大必吃”食肆之一。招牌鲜虾云吞面?，秘制鲜虾云吞粒粒爽口弹牙，独家加入“大地鱼末”，搭配金华火腿骨熬制6小时而成的汤头，风味一绝；还有加入猪皮、鱿鱼等熬煮的艇仔粥，绵密暖胃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12612317" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12612317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473735.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12612317" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473735-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12612317" class="wp-caption-text">“三巡”以新加坡文华东方酒店的配方制作海南鸡，荣获多项奖项。（笠正整合行销提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>三巡海南鸡饭</strong></p>
<p>“三巡”荣获多项美食奖项，以新加坡文华东方酒店海南鸡饭的配方，以传统手法及上等鸡肉制作海南鸡，肉质滑嫩鲜美，加上自家秘制酱料与鸡油香米饭，以及搭配当归熬煮的清鸡上汤以及辣味沾酱，爽口美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12612316" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12612316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473734.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12612316" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473734-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12612316" class="wp-caption-text">TOAST BOX土司工坊可品尝新加坡吐司小吃与南洋风咖啡。（笠正整合行销提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>TOAST BOX土司工坊</strong></p>
<p>新加坡道地南洋咖啡室，能轻松品尝经典小吃，每杯香浓南洋咖啡更蕴含着当地传统饮食文化传统与心思，招牌美食包括星洲叻沙、小鱼椰浆饭、香浓的海南咖喱等，令人齿颊留香。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_12612315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12612315" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473733.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12612315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/473733-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12612315" class="wp-caption-text">透明隔间厨房让饕客饱览“烹饪秀”。（笠正整合行销提供）</figcaption></figure>
