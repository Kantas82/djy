<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Party最靓 新靴款秀出圣诞风华</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2013年12月20日讯】（大纪元记者杨存菁台湾采访报导）无论是长<ahref="https://github.com/htawlw362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%B4.md#1">靴</a>、短靴、踝靴，穿对比例，就能让你成为圣诞PARTY中的闪耀之星，岁末，让时尚达人刘大强，帮你‘靴’出令人惊艳的完美造型！</p>
<p>秋冬时尚吹起优雅复古风，让各式<ahref="https://github.com/htawlw362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%B4.md#1">靴</a>款也走向粗跟的复古设计，对女性来说不但行走更方便，许多巧思也藏在鞋跟细节中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5679070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679070" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312160722562178-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679070" class="wp-caption-text">踝靴搭配短裙、短裤也能显得下身比例修长。黑色镂空洋装 Club Monaco，踝靴 Stuart Weitzman，黑色皮包 Coach。（摄影／林仕杰　Model／Wendy）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年鞋跟设计带有些许建筑结构的视觉感及趣味性，金属材质被广泛运用在鞋面及鞋跟中，例如铆钉、星星或拉链等元素，呈现出有个性的中性美。 </p>
<figure id="attachment_5679077" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679077" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312160723222178-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679077" class="wp-caption-text">豹纹铅笔裙+鹿皮踝靴，显得典雅端庄。桃红丝衬衫 Le souk，豹纹铅笔裙 Club Monaco，踝靴 Stuart Weitzman， 咖啡色皮包 Coach。（摄影／林仕杰　Model／Amber）</figcaption></figure>
<p><B>优雅裸靴 最夯</B></p>
<p>高至脚踝的踝靴，搭配优雅的铅笔裙，就是今年的流行重点，但踝靴却像是挑人的骄骄女，较适合小腿纤细的女性。</p>
<p>穿搭建议：让腿跟踝靴融为一体，例如：穿黑色丝袜、紧身裤或踩脚裤，较能拉长并延伸腿部线条；或是穿着复古A-line宽版 <br />个性短筒 帅气</p>
<figure id="attachment_5679087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679087" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312160723362178-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679087" class="wp-caption-text">穿着黑色丝袜让下半身视觉自然延长，搭配豹纹踝靴，优雅中带点小狂野！金葱上衣，黑色金花洋装 Club Monaco，豹纹踝靴 Stuart Weitzman。（摄影／林仕杰　Model／Wendy）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随性穿着搭配短筒靴，就能呈现个性化的街头风！</p>
<p>穿搭建议：无论是帅气的格纹裤+皮衣，或是宽版的针织洋装+黑丝袜，随意穿着搭配短筒靴，就能呈现街头风的个性气息！</p>
<figure id="attachment_5679098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679098" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312160723152178-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679098" class="wp-caption-text">上宽下窄原则，让腿部激瘦，更现下身修长。黄色印花上衣Club Monaco，蓝色灯心绒紧腿裤Le souk ，深蓝翻领靴Stuart Weitzman。（摄影／林仕杰　Model／Wendy）</figcaption></figure>
<p><B>百搭长靴 经典</B></p>
<p>长靴是秋冬必备单品，无论搭配裤、裙，不论长短，都是经典不败款。</p>
<p>穿搭进阶：不妨尝试今年最IN的铅笔裙或复古洋装，搭配OVERSIZE大外套都能展现秋冬最时尚风范！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_5679108" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312160723282178-600x386.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679108" class="wp-caption-text">时尚造型师刘大强（摄影／林仕杰）</figcaption></figure>
