<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2013西澳珀斯圣诞大游行</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2013年12月13日讯】（大纪元记者林文责澳洲珀斯报导）12月7日，南半球最孤独的城市珀斯举办了西澳最大规模的圣诞游行。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5679006" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110425272491-600x419.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679006" class="wp-caption-text">三只小猪盖新房&#8230;&#8230;“莴苣，莴苣，放下你的长发，让我上去！”（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5679015" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110424582491-600x403.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679015" class="wp-caption-text">“谁在啃我的糖果屋？”（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5679020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110424282491-600x405.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679020" class="wp-caption-text">火车托马斯也是孩子们的至爱。（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>在炎炎夏日的澳洲西部，超过15万人带着家人孩子聚集到市中心，参加这一年一度的欢乐聚会。许多人从下午三点就开始到市区站踞观看的有利位置，哪怕游行要四个小时后才开始。</p>
<p>
	<figure id="attachment_5679028" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110421442491-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679028" class="wp-caption-text">长灯笼和舞扇仙女引导着长长的金色游行方阵。（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5679038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110422152491-600x406.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679038" class="wp-caption-text">法轮大法腰鼓队及九人舞动的金龙为观众带来浓浓的中国风情，同时也让众多华人观众感到兴奋和骄傲。（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>超过2千多人自各社区和不同团体的游行队伍参加了一个半小时的表演游行。法轮大法团体作为唯一华人团体也参加了游行，在远离其他城市的孤独珀斯，为当地观众带去了中国韵味的欢乐和法轮大法的美好。真善忍大型灯笼、腰鼓阵和舞动的金龙让众多观众眼前一亮，更让在场的众多华人感到亲切和骄傲。 </p>

	<figure id="attachment_5679056" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5679056" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/12/1312110423342491-600x418.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5679056" class="wp-caption-text">鉴定的锡兵走在队伍中，很多孩子在老远就认出来了。（林文责／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
