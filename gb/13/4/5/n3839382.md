<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>春水悠悠花盛放 东京樱花见花情</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>由冬入春，寒暖渐次，春天的脚步一阵紧似一阵。听说日本东京气象厅预报，2013年<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>前线提早一周到来。上野公园的“花见”樱花祭还没准备好……，赏花人，也可能来不及上路……</p>
<p>春到东京，街头、川边、园里如入定般的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>木，一夕乍然苏醒，老干着新花，一个星期的时间，给尽人间生命的丰华。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6696780" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696780" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042357581456-600x800.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696780" class="wp-caption-text">川边公园樱花盛开</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6696795" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042357521456-600x459.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696795" class="wp-caption-text">樱花树下母子情</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6696810" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042357301456-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696810" class="wp-caption-text">袅然垂枝樱</figcaption></figure>
<p>“数大便是美”对樱花来说，不是一种无视的轻忽，而是一种虔敬的赞美。樱花木的生命，一年潜藏，一瞬急放，面对这样的生命样貌，你怎能轻忽无视？你怎能不屏息赞叹？</p>
<figure id="attachment_6696825" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042358061456-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696825" class="wp-caption-text">石神井川“樱花见”花情</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6696836" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696836" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042357411456-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696836" class="wp-caption-text">石神井川“樱花见”花情</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6696850" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6696850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/04/1304042358151456-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6696850" class="wp-caption-text">石神井川“樱花见”花情</figcaption></figure>
<p>东京赏樱花的名所很多，而更多的是街头、川边、小园里，无所不在的、随心拾掇的“美遇”，融入人群生活中，溶入一吸一呼间。石神井川是穿流东京的小川之一，也是“樱花见”的自然美域之一。在樱花季里，两岸还有岸边公园的樱花木急放，宛然梦里花间集，纤纤然、盈盈然、盎盎然，满溢着纯真出尘之美，涤荡成住人间一年的尘埃，轻解重锁人身一心的执著，让人赞叹纯真、浩叹壮美，忘了“我”，然后，再出发。</p>
<p>拂去一身乱，转身看天地……<br />春水悠悠花盛放，春风闲闲马蹄疾。<br />匆匆人间莫香忘，捎住人生好花景。</p>
<p>（成稿３月２２日东京樱花满开前日）＠*</p>
