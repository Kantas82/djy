<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：葡京大赌场</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>夕阳像一只受惊吓的夹尾狗<br />被夜驱逐追赶，快速逸去<br />从世界各地聚拢来的赌客们<br />便因心中贪婪的鼓动，纷纷<br />钻进那只大蝙蝠的双翼下（注一）</p> <p>一钻进那只大蝙蝠的双翼下<br />赌客们便像走进了一个大迷宫<br />为阴谋诡计的烟所蒙<br />被诈骗构陷的雾所障<br />吸了毒，喝了迷魂汤似的<br />再怎么走都走不出来<br />再怎么找都找不到出路<br />双脚陷在烂泥中，越陷越深<br />即使输掉了最后一条裤子<br />仍要把自己的生命当筹码押下去<br />深恐那座大桥建造不起来（注二）<br />深恐澳门的繁荣无法撑持下去（注三）</p> <p>归根究底，都是那夜惹的祸<br />如果不是他驱走夕阳，让黑暗<br />渐渐深浓，染黑熊猫的双眼<br />赌客们怎么会因心中贪婪的鼓动<br />纷纷钻进那只大蝙蝠的双翼下？</p> <p>注一：澳门葡京大赌场建筑正像一只展翅的大蝙蝠。赌客们从门口进入，正如钻进它的双翼下。<br />注二：此桥顶有名，曰友谊大桥，据传系澳门政府以所收赌税建造，而赌客以台湾人最多，当地又有台湾人所建造的说法。<br />注三：澳门的繁荣靠赌场撑持，即使回归后，赌场照开不误。<br />注四：熊猫的双眼周边全黑。赌客敖夜后，双眼周边尤其是眼袋亦黑，当地戏称为熊猫的双眼。</p> <p> <b>Macao Gambling House</b>　 Hsu ChiCheng</p> <p>The setting sun looks like a scared dog<br />Fleeing with its tail behind its legsbeing driven by night<br />Enticed by money<br />Gamblers gathering together from all corners of the world<br />Sneak into the wings of the big bat one after another*1</p> <p>Getting into the wings of the big bat<br />Gamblers seem to have got into a maze<br />Shrouded in the smoke of tricks<br />Enveloped in the mist set up with fraud<br />As if being taken in by someone’s flattery<br />They are powerless to get out<br />They are unable to find a way out<br />They have sunk deeper and deeper in the mire<br />Even his only trousers is lost at gambling<br />He will use his life as a chip<br />Lest that big bridge fails in building up*2<br />Lest Macao doesn’t keep prosperous</p> <p>In a word, all troubles are invited by the night<br />Suppose it didn’t drive the setting sun away<br />And let it get darker and darker like a panda’s eyes<br />How come gamblers enticed by money<br />Sneak into wings of the big bat one after another?<br />*1. The building of Macao Gambling House looks like a big bat. Gamblers enter from the gate just like sneaking into its wings.<br />*2. That big bridge is called Friendship Bridge. It is said that Macao government built it with the tax dues levied from Nacao Gambling House. Most of gamblers are Taiwaners. So a wording has been handed down that the bridge built by Taiwaners.<br />*3. The prosperity of Macao relies the tax of the Gambling House, even after its return the Gambling House still open.<br />*4. Gamblers’ orbits are black look like panda’s after they have gambled for whole the night or even several nights.@</p>
