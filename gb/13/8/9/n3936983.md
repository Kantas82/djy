<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>七夕情人节 银色情侣档穿搭</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2013年08月10日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E5%A4%95.md#1">七夕</a>情人节到来，这象征了远距离、爱情长跑仍不散的感情，来看看演艺圈中有哪些银色情侣或夫妻档，他们又是怎么穿的吧！</p>
<p><b>台湾</b>　</p>
<p>陈建州&#038;范玮琪</p>
<p>爱情长跑的这组高个儿档，出席正式场合，黑人多半是黑色西装，范范则是小洋装；私下则是素色T恤搭牛仔裤的休闲运动风最常见。而且范范喜欢背黑色中性款的大包包，黑人就经常拿她的包包来用，情侣衣服难互穿，包包两人共背就很实用。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738705" style="width: 477px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082328102599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738705" class="wp-caption-text">陈建州&#038;范玮琪。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%9D%8E%E4%BB%81.md#1">李李仁</a>&#038;陶晶莹</p>
<p>这组姐弟恋差了5岁，也是演艺界的才子佳人，育有一子一女。<ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E6%9D%8E%E4%BB%81.md#1">李李仁</a>爱家形象深植人心，照顾小孩当然是穿短裤、T恤最轻松方便，但他会搭配一双名牌的休闲鞋、名表，衬托品味。陶子则是喜欢有个性的服装，再以独特配件画龙点睛。<br />[[5]]<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738713" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082315242599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738713" class="wp-caption-text">李李仁＆陶晶莹（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />阮经天&#038;许玮甯</p>
<p>双名模不管是身高或架式都高人一等，阮经天除了西装，穿运动休闲服更有刚毅爽朗的男人味，但他活泼的个性，有时也会搭个抢眼图案、亮面料子来突显潮流感。许玮甯则是爱鞋狂，她笑说自己品味两极化，喜爱穿极简的服装，却爱看奢华的设计。<br />[[8]]<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082320092599-600x775.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738719" class="wp-caption-text">阮经天&#038;许玮甯。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p> 胡志强&#038;邵晓玲</p>
<p>台中市长胡志强现身，不是西装就是休闲衬衫；夫人邵晓玲比较有看头，温柔婉约的熟女风韵，像是粉红亮缎套装、深红皮草针织衫、金色抓皱礼服、白色长裙套装，想当贵妇的人，就是这个味。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738731" style="width: 353px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082324032599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738731" class="wp-caption-text">胡志强&#038;邵晓玲。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><b>韩国</b>　</p>
<p>Rain&#038;金泰熙</p>
<p>一个是南韩“头号师奶杀手”的舞者兼歌手，一个是南韩“第一天然美女”的名演员，这组银色情侣档超引人注目。两人拍照时常以热情的红色亮相，出席正式场合则是偏爱休闲商务风，无论是黑白配还是整套黑，两人都诠释得很得体。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738734" style="width: 576px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082257102599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738734" class="wp-caption-text">Rain&#038;金泰熙。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><b>日本</b>　</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A0%BA%E9%9B%85%E4%BA%BA.md#1">堺雅人</a>&#038;菅野美穂</p>
<p>相较于有个性的韩国人穿搭大胆、亮眼；日本人就显得正经、端庄多了。体现在服装上，<ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A0%BA%E9%9B%85%E4%BA%BA.md#1">堺雅人</a>穿衣服挑选窄领、细条纹、温和不强势的灰色调；菅野美穂则是粉色针织衫、纱质蓬裙、小碎花洋装，显得温柔甜美。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738742" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082255072599-600x449.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738742" class="wp-caption-text">堺雅人&#038;菅野美穗。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><b>香港</b>　</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E6%9C%9D%E4%BC%9F.md#1">梁朝伟</a>&#038;<ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%98%89%E7%8E%B2.md#1">刘嘉玲</a></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E6%9C%9D%E4%BC%9F.md#1">梁朝伟</a>电力超强，兼具成熟和绅士的熟男风范，他喜爱舒服、合身的料子与剪裁，以及中西合璧的现代男装；<ahref="https://github.com/imdmks3166/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%98%89%E7%8E%B2.md#1">刘嘉玲</a>也毫不逊色，单色长礼服，无论是黑、白、红、蓝还是渐层，都能衬托出高雅、气势的熟女风韵。　</p>
<p><b>好莱坞</b>　</p>
<p>布莱德彼特（Brad Pitt）&#038;安洁莉纳裘莉 （Angelina Jolie）</p>
<p>布莱德彼特走红毯偏爱Tom Ford西装，私下则是休闲随性风格，例如西装搭配T恤，或是衬衫随性搭配造型眼镜、皮鞋、布鞋。裘莉偏爱单色、有张力的时尚，例如驼色风衣搭皮靴、白色西服裤装、 黑色高衩礼服、一抹红肩的不对称白色礼服称霸红毯。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738753" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738753" style="width: 416px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082303002599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738753" class="wp-caption-text">布莱德彼特&#038;安洁莉纳裘莉（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br /><b>法义</b>　</p>
<p>文森卡索（Vincent Cassel）&#038;莫妮卡贝鲁奇（Monica Bellucci） 　</p>
<p>法国影帝文森卡索穿衣走黑白极简风，却有慵懒、高雅、成熟却又带点神秘的法国男性气质；意大利艳星莫妮卡贝鲁奇，穿着也像个性一样大胆浪漫，她走红毯总是大方展现姣好身材，就连巴黎美爵MATZO PARIS也特别推出莫妮卡贝鲁奇同名订制包。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6738759" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6738759" style="width: 409px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308082301582599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6738759" class="wp-caption-text">文森卡索&#038;莫妮卡贝鲁奇（Getty Images)</figcaption></figure><br /><b>夫妻爱侣相处之道：</b></p>
