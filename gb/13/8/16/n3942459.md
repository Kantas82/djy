<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>早秋时尚关键字：冷艳高贵 神秘又奢华</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2013年08月16日讯】盛夏接连破高温，早秋来报到！让我们来看看今年早秋关键字，包括浓厚的英伦格纹风、Oversize外套、复古蕾丝、不败动物纹等，潮流越来越倾向颓废浪漫、都市冷艳风两大派系。早秋暑意未消，也可先从包包、鞋子、丝巾等配件着手。</p>
<p><b><font color="#800080">1.颓废苏格兰</font></b></p>
<p>Chanel今年早秋Métiers d&#8217;Art工坊系列飘荡浓郁的异国情调，回顾香奈儿女士对苏格兰的情怀，格纹盛放在针织软呢、紧身裙、连身衣、洋装、披风及配饰上，男装则以贵族们户外游猎服为主，Karl Lagerfeld称呼这季主题为“野蛮的浪漫”。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734404" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734404" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160501192599-600x265.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734404" class="wp-caption-text">Chanel苏格兰风情。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><b><font color="#800080">2.蕾丝迷情</font></b></p>
<p>春夏流行裸透蕾丝，秋冬则以奢华风当道。Louis Vuitton将蕾丝与黑色皮革应用于缇花织物、光泽缎面裙饰以蕾丝、缇花外套覆盖蕾丝、运动衫裙毛衣裙融入蕾丝元素。还有蕾丝花边面纱、天鹅绒缎带饰边、貂皮毛领和灰色铆钉鞋跟，神秘奢华。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734414" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734414" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160502052599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734414" class="wp-caption-text">Louis Vuitton蕾丝迷情。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><b><font color="#800080">3.危险动物</font></b></p>
<p>Gucci早秋强调致命女人香，合身及膝裙以宽版腰带系出纤细腰线，V领、圆领露出锁骨，并在各式皮革、貂皮、雪纺纱上缀以豹纹印花，搭配极细跟的玛莉珍高跟鞋，危险妩媚。</p>
<p>Burberry Prorsum招牌的风衣以亮色蛇皮压纹、牛奶纹、长颈鹿纹等全新亮相。男装还有心形印花卡通俄罗斯红场卫兵印花，混搭绅士与趣味。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734421" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734421" style="width: 444px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160500542599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734421" class="wp-caption-text">Burberry Prorsum俏皮动物纹。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><b><font color="#800080">4.内敛俐落</font></b></p>
<p>Dior剪裁俐落的千岛格洋装、蝴蝶结，以灰色为基调，营造出低调沉稳的优雅；材质上则第一次创新选用羊毛单宁布料，缀以经典玫瑰花元素，更贴近女性生活形象。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734425" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160501442599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734425" class="wp-caption-text">Dior内敛千鸟纹。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure><br /><b><font color="#800080">5.神秘斗篷</font></b></p>
<p>奢华宫廷风的Valentino将雪纺纱、皮革、铆钉与喀什米尔混搭，象牙白、暗夜黑、洋红、裸色或深蓝，高贵气派。长短斗篷端庄含蓄，搭配精巧的蕾丝晚装、金属铆钉包，奢华抢眼。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734434" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160502262599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734434" class="wp-caption-text">Valentino高贵斗篷。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p><b><font color="#800080">6.Oversize</font></b></p>
<p>延续春夏流行宽松衣服后，蚕茧轮廓的Oversize超大外套继续在早秋走红。例如Bottega Veneta强调轮廓和剪裁，演绎一系列复古优雅的女装，宽袖茧形大衣搭配高腰线、笔直飘逸的宽版长裤，展现隽永黑白、简洁俐落的设计。◇<br />
	<figure id="attachment_6734440" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6734440" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/08/1308160500352599.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6734440" class="wp-caption-text">Bottega Veneta俐落轮廓。（网路图片）</figcaption></figure></p>
