<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：巴黎之昔日与今天</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2013年03月24日讯】浪漫之都巴黎也是一座古城，从中世纪发展到今天，城市里的许许多多大街小巷依然保留昔日的风貌。以下让我们跟随19世纪法国摄影师Charles Marville的镜头，领略巴黎之昔日与今天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6686974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6686974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324102427100574-600x422.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6686974" class="wp-caption-text">昔日的巴黎市政府。由摄影师Charles Marville摄于1852年，现珍藏于卡那瓦雷博物馆（Musée Carnavalet）。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6686987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6686987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324102541100574-600x318.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6686987" class="wp-caption-text">今天，巴黎市政府成为全欧洲最大的一座市政办公建筑物。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6686996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6686996" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324102651100574-600x449.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6686996" class="wp-caption-text">昔日的6区雷恩路（Rue de Rennes）<br />
由摄影师Charles Marville摄于1877年。雷恩路位于圣日耳曼德佩（Saint-Germain-des-Pr□s）广场附近，自1866年建成的，街道以布列塔尼大区的雷恩市来命名，是因为当时该条街正好通向巴黎蒙帕纳斯火车站，而火车站的列车则是通往布列塔尼的。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687011" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324102809100574-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687011" class="wp-caption-text">后来，雷恩街被延长至塞纳河边，并建造了一座呈X型的桥。雷恩街附近有著名的法兰西学会（Institut de France）。今天，雷恩街两旁依然保留有古老建筑的特色，风景优美，街上有不少古董商店。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324102917100574-600x569.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687022" class="wp-caption-text">昔日的巴黎政治大学街道，由摄影师Charles Marville摄于1866年。该街名叫la rue Saint Guillaume，位处圣日耳曼德佩广场和拉丁区这两个最古老的中世纪巴黎地带。（Paris.fr）<br /></figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687029" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324103004100574-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687029" class="wp-caption-text">今天的la rue Saint Guillaume。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687038" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324103049100574-600x343.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687038" class="wp-caption-text">今天，久负盛名的巴黎政治大学在校学生有上千多名。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687052" style="width: 456px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324103130100574.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687052" class="wp-caption-text">林荫大道圣米歇尔大街。由摄影师Charles Marville摄于1877年。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687058" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324103219100574-600x331.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687058" class="wp-caption-text">今天的圣米歇尔大街。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_6687063" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6687063" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/03/130324103256100574-600x445.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6687063" class="wp-caption-text">1868年的香榭丽舍公园一角。（Paris.fr）</figcaption></figure>
