<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【水彩行家】福山意象</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>已经无法计算几次到福山植物园了，对这个北台湾最著名的森林中的植物园，个人有特殊的喜爱，这里除了有植物种类的展示之外，创园之初有计划的约束参观人数和时间 造就园区的生态特色，成为台湾最佳典范，在这里写生成为我的最爱之一。<br /> <br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663972" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311060831262030-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663972" class="wp-caption-text">福山植物园 (摄影／<ahref="https://github.com/cbduwl3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E4%B8%9C%E6%A0%87.md#1">洪东标</a>)</figcaption></figure><br />福山位于宜兰县和新北市的交界，但必须从宜兰进入，早先五号国道未通车之前去一趟福山颇为费时费劲，现在则成为大台北的后花园了，一年超过200天的雨天，让这里的森林中到处可见附生着各种苔癣和蕨类植物，亚热带雨林中的植物园特征非常明显。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663978" style="width: 592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311070901241454.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663978" class="wp-caption-text">二、园中蕨类</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663983" style="width: 544px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311070904181454.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663983" class="wp-caption-text">五、水生池加上森林的绿意</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311070908261454-600x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663988" class="wp-caption-text">四、哈盆溪</figcaption></figure><br />我以全开300g的arches<ahref="https://github.com/cbduwl3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E5%BD%A9.md#1">水彩</a>纸用湿裱法让纸张平贴在画板上〈湿裱法请参阅第八期水彩资讯杂志〉，开始构图，为了在有限的画面中同时展现最多的元素，组合的过程参阅了将近20张拍自于福山植物园的照片，所以画面并不是园区中的一个特定的角度，而是一个组合的意象，其中以哈盆溪和水生池作为主要的景观代表加上森林郁郁苍苍的绿意，和林中及草坡间隐藏的动物组合一幅完整的画作。<br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311060815492030-600x423.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663995" class="wp-caption-text">1</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5663999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5663999" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311060822142030-600x429.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5663999" class="wp-caption-text">2</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5664005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5664005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311060822012030-600x421.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5664005" class="wp-caption-text">3</figcaption></figure><br />
	<figure id="attachment_5664012" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5664012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/11/1311060816012030-600x416.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5664012" class="wp-caption-text">4</figcaption></figure><br />主要技法：为了表现湿度和阴凉的特征，我以灰蓝和灰绿色作为这幅画作中色彩的主调，技法以大渲染法铺陈底色，再以重叠法刻划植物的特征和细节。<br />
