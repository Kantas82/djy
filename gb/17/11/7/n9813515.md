<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>严惩分心驾驶省府大幅涨罚金</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Message-600x349.jpg" />
<h6>图：省府宣布进一步加强打击分心驾驶，明年3月1日起，三年内重犯司机的罚款提高至2000元。（加通社）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年11月07日讯】（<span lang="ZH-TW">记者余天白温哥华编译报导）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">周一（</span><span lang="EN-US">11</span><span lang="ZH-TW">月</span><span lang="EN-US">6</span><span lang="ZH-TW">日），<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>省府宣布将使用手机等<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%86%E5%BF%83%E9%A9%BE%E9%A9%B6.md#1">分心驾驶</a>行为定为“高危驾驶行为”，并从明年</span><span lang="EN-US">3</span><span lang="ZH-TW">月</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span lang="ZH-TW">日起大幅上涨相关保险罚金。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">目前，一年内收到两张<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%86%E5%BF%83%E9%A9%BE%E9%A9%B6.md#1">分心驾驶</a>罚单的司机需缴纳包括罚单罚金、车险罚金在内的</span><span lang="EN-US">1,256</span><span lang="ZH-TW">元<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%9A%E6%AC%BE.md#1">罚款</a>。明年三月起，在三年以内收到过两张分心驾驶罚单的司机将需缴纳</span><span lang="EN-US">2,000</span><span lang="ZH-TW">元<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BD%9A%E6%AC%BE.md#1">罚款</a>。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">此外，若司机一年内出现多次分心驾驶，省府将对司机进行审查并发出为期</span><span lang="EN-US">3</span><span lang="ZH-TW">个月至</span><span lang="EN-US">12</span><span lang="ZH-TW">个月不等的驾驶禁令。而持学习驾照的司机只要出现一次分心驾驶，其驾照就将被彻底吊销。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">省检察长尹大卫（</span><span lang="EN-US">David Eby</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）表示，分心驾驶和酒后驾驶、过分超速同样严重。“采取措施增加（道路）安全、惩罚危险的行为对所有<ahref="https://github.com/jjdlwc348/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>人都有利。这是我们朝正确的方向又走了一步。”他说。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">根据</span><span lang="EN-US">ICBC</span><span lang="ZH-TW">数据，在本省所有因车祸丧生的案件中，有超过四分之一与分心驾驶有关，对应的死亡人数为</span><span lang="EN-US">78</span><span lang="ZH-TW">人</span><span lang="EN-US">/</span><span lang="ZH-TW">年。目前省内约有</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span lang="ZH-TW">万</span><span lang="EN-US">2000</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名司机在过去三年内收到过多张分心驾驶罚单。新规实行后，</span><span lang="EN-US">ICBC</span><span lang="ZH-TW">从该司机群体中所获的罚款收入将从当前的每年</span><span lang="EN-US">200</span><span lang="ZH-TW">万元增至</span><span lang="EN-US">500</span><span lang="ZH-TW">万元。</span><span lang="EN-US">◇</span></p>
