<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>奥莉薇小姐的厨房：蚝油焖冬瓜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/496e89de1d90a28d7e3b588be6d336ee-e1511338071595-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蚝油焖冬瓜(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年11月22日讯】抗癌/化疗餐：蚝油焖<ahref="https://github.com/wvhxis384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E7%93%9C.md#1">冬瓜</a></p>
<p>明明是夏天生产的瓜，为何叫做“<ahref="https://github.com/wvhxis384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%AC%E7%93%9C.md#1">冬瓜</a>”呢？因为在它成熟的时候，表面上会产生一层白色粉状物，就像冬天的白霜一样，故名“冬瓜”。据《本草纲目》记载，冬瓜的疗效有：消暑解毒、治痱子、治水肿、止渴等功效。有些癌症患者伴有水肿症状，或有小便不顺的问题，此时可试试冬瓜料理，因冬瓜的钠含量很低，利尿功效好。一旦小便顺畅，患者的水肿就容易消除了。</p>
<p>今天奥莉薇小姐要介绍的是“蚝油焖冬瓜”，它是一道极简易的<ahref="https://github.com/wvhxis384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B8%B8%E8%8F%9C.md#1">家常菜</a>，食材又很容易取得，真的是“煮得轻松、吃得愉快”的一道抗癌佳肴喔！</p>
<p><strong>【防癌示范料理：蚝油焖冬瓜】</strong></p>
<p>◎材料：姜丝少许。冬瓜300克。蚝油5大匙。砂糖少许。水少许。</p>
<p>◎作法：</p>
<p>1.冬瓜洗净，去皮切小块备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880497" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880497" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/5aaba4dda72ac248fe3b61ec73ecf85f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880497" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/5aaba4dda72ac248fe3b61ec73ecf85f-600x969.jpg" alt="冬瓜洗净，去皮切小块备用。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="969" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880497" class="wp-caption-text">冬瓜洗净，去皮切小块备用。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.锅中放少许油，把姜丝和冬瓜炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880513" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880513" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/9ef8c880f38d6c0e6346fb9ec0226585.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/9ef8c880f38d6c0e6346fb9ec0226585-600x715.jpg" alt="锅中放少许油，把姜丝和冬瓜炒香。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="715" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880513" class="wp-caption-text">锅中放少许油，把姜丝和冬瓜炒香。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.锅中加入蚝油和糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880517" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/fc83534ecea2ca0df2174c14392cade9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/fc83534ecea2ca0df2174c14392cade9-600x433.jpg" alt="锅中加入蚝油和糖。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="433" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880517" class="wp-caption-text">锅中加入蚝油和糖。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9880536" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/6bdbb8531aeeec0d8b716758cb0b0452.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/6bdbb8531aeeec0d8b716758cb0b0452-600x694.jpg" alt="锅中加入蚝油和糖。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="694" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880536" class="wp-caption-text">锅中加入蚝油和糖。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.炒到冬瓜有点上色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880540" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/108674a890e304d27aef73291a3577a9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/108674a890e304d27aef73291a3577a9-600x952.jpg" alt="炒到冬瓜有点上色。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="952" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880540" class="wp-caption-text">炒到冬瓜有点上色。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.倒入水，稍微淹过冬瓜即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880541" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/560efe3a9ea41a1e62af79cf23a6d49c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/560efe3a9ea41a1e62af79cf23a6d49c-600x1067.jpg" alt="倒入水，稍微淹过冬瓜即可。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880541" class="wp-caption-text">倒入水，稍微淹过冬瓜即可。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.盖上锅盖，焖煮到冬瓜变软即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9880546" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9880546" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/40a6de7caeb3b0d9827034e0faa7f783.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9880546" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/40a6de7caeb3b0d9827034e0faa7f783-600x1067.jpg" alt="盖上锅盖，焖煮到冬瓜变软即完成。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)" width="600" b="1067" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9880546" class="wp-caption-text">盖上锅盖，焖煮到冬瓜变软即完成。(图：奥莉薇小姐/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>后记：冬瓜本身是凉性食物，所以对于体质寒凉的人来说，吃多了可能会有腹泻的情形，所以这类人别吃太多。平时我都会在烹调时，加入姜片和冬瓜一起煮，以达到寒热平衡的功效。@</p>
