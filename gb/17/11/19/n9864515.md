<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>ATP年终赛爆冷 葛芬淘汰费德勒闯决赛</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">【大纪元2017年11月19日讯】（大纪元记者尚上礼综合报导）<span lang="EN-US">ATP</span><ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E7%BB%88%E8%B5%9B.md#1">年终赛</a><span lang="EN-US">4</span>强战大爆冷门，比利时名将<ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8A%AC.md#1">葛芬</a>（<span lang="EN-US">David Goffin</span>）以<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">4</span>逆转封王呼声最高的<ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Roger Federer</span>），将在决赛与迪米特洛夫（<span lang="EN-US">Grigor Dimitrov</span>）争王。</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">男网世排第<span lang="EN-US">8</span>的<ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8A%AC.md#1">葛芬</a>，在分组赛最后一场击败提姆（<span lang="EN-US">Dominic Thiem</span>），才惊险拿下最后<span lang="EN-US">1</span>张晋级<span lang="EN-US">4</span>强门票。先前与<ahref="https://github.com/sbgbmd396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%BE%B7%E5%8B%92.md#1">费德勒</a><span lang="EN-US">6</span>度交手都输球，且才赢过两盘，几乎无人看好他能晋级决赛。</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">首盘费德勒也仅花<span lang="EN-US">33</span>分钟就以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>胜出。不过次盘靠着在第<span lang="EN-US">7</span>局的唯一成攻破发，以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>扳平后，信心大增，决胜盘又在第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>局率先破发，以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">4</span>拿下，粉碎费德勒在本赛事<span lang="EN-US">7</span>度封王的希望。赛后葛芬说：“我在第一盘的时候就开始感觉到击球的状况愈来愈好，因此我打得更有攻击性。”</p>
<p>而另一场单打<span lang="EN-US">4</span>强赛由迪米特洛夫以<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>击败萨克（<span lang="EN-US">Jack Sock</span>），两人将在决赛碰头。之前葛芬首场比赛击败纳达尔（<span lang="EN-US">Rafael Nadal</span>）后，隔天对上迪米特洛夫（<span lang="EN-US">Grigor Dimitrov</span>）的比赛遭到横扫。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
