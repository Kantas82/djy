<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗讨食3年 每次不吃完“打包”就跑 这天女子决定跟踪</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/Aki-151932-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德索萨每次都帮它把食物袋捆好，方便它“打包”带走。她越来越好奇，终于有一天，她决定跟着狗狗，看它到底在给谁带饭。（视频截图／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年11月10日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）这只生活在垃圾场的巴西狗狗本可以独享给它的饭菜，但每次它都只吃一半，剩下的叼走，<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%86%E4%BA%AB.md">分享</a>给各种让人想不到的动物伙伴！爱狗人跟踪它6公里、发现它的<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E7%A7%81.md">无私</a>举动后，拍摄了一支影片，感动众多网友。</p>
<p>母狗莉莉卡（Lilica）被主人遗弃在了巴西圣卡洛斯的一座垃圾场。虽然那里很破败，但它很快就交到了很多朋友，包括<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%81%E6%B5%AA%E7%8B%97.md">流浪狗</a>、猫咪和鸡只全家，让它感到非常温暖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824344" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-DOGAASA.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824344" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-DOGAASA-450x350.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog11-450x249.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后来，莉莉卡生下了8只小狗，拥有了一个大家庭。</p>
<p>垃圾场老板尼勒·安东尼奥（Neile V?nia Ant?nio）说，为了喂养幼崽，莉莉卡别无选择，只好外出乞食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824328" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog1-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog1-2-450x249.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>莉莉卡越走越远，就是在漆黑的夜里，也还要冒生命危险穿过公路和高速路。</p>
<p>安托尼奥说：“一开始，它只带食物给小狗们吃，后来，也开始给垃圾场的其它动物带食物。”</p>
<p>8只小狗被收养后，它妈妈般的举动并未停止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824334" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog7-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog7-2-450x251.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="251" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog2-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog2-1-450x282.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="282" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三年来，莉莉卡每天都要走4英里（6.4公里）的夜路去见喂它的人——露西亚·德索萨（Lucia Helena de Souza）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog6-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog6-2-450x244.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="244" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每天晚上，德索萨都在自家附近的一片空场等待莉莉卡的出现。她说：“因为有它，我不去旅行，去哪里都不会待太久。我知道它依赖我，这是我对它的承诺，也是它对我的承诺——因为它每天都来。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824338" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog10-450x252.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“一天，它吃着吃着就停下来了，叼起敞开的食物袋就跑了；边跑，食物边从袋中往下掉。”德索萨回忆道，“于是第二天它吃完后我就系好了包，它叼起来了。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog3-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog3-1-450x248.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="248" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog16-450x249.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从那以后，德索萨每次都帮它把食物袋捆好，方便它“打包”带走。她越来越好奇，终于有一天，她决定跟着狗狗，看它到底在给谁带饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog8-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog8-1-450x270.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="270" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog12-450x267.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="267" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>追踪莉莉卡一路回到垃圾场后，德索萨终于发现了答案。</p>
<p>令人难以置信的是，这只<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E7%A7%81.md">无私</a>的狗儿是带给垃圾场的一众动物朋友，其中包括：一只狗，一只猫，一头骡子，还有一群鸡，它们轮流把脑袋伸进莉莉卡的食物袋！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824332" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog5-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog5-2-450x273.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog9-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog9-1-450x247.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="247" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9824342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog14-450x262.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="262" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>垃圾场老板安托尼奥感慨说：“我们作为人类都很少与他人<a href="https://github.com/upuqsh2796/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%88%86%E4%BA%AB.md">分享</a>，而动物却不吝于分享，给我们上了一课。”</p>
<p>讲述莉莉卡故事的一支影片已经被播放900多万次，狗狗的无私让许多网友称叹。</p>
<p>“人类与狗因为美德与爱聚在了一起。看这支影片，我流泪，我微笑，我也大笑、叹气，真是百感交集。”一位YouTube用户写道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9824341" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9824341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171109NTD-Dog13-450x269.jpg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="269" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一位则说：“如果每个人都以这种精神待人处事，将可以改变这个世界。”</p>
<p>感谢莉莉卡给我们上了关于“无私分享”的重要一课！</p>
