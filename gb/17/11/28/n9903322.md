<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：灯</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1602131600292483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Classic lamp shines white light（Fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>当白天依循着大自然的规律，被时间推拥着，走向命运必然的归宿，黑暗便以其绵密的细网，遮覆住世界，使夜漆黑不堪；如果再加上无星无月，夜更是黑越越的，让人不辨天地了。此时，灯显得多么重要呀！<br />
灯和光，形如骨之与肉，只要有灯，光明便永在。<br />
黑暗，予人一种茫然、困惑、恐惧、不祥的感觉。没有人喜欢黑暗，也没有人不喜欢光明。“希望有一盏灯呀！”在黑暗中，这是人们心中的最大想望。此时，果真出现一盏灯，便有如跋涉在无尽的沙漠中，渴极欲饮，忽见绿洲甘泉，有如迷失在荒山野林中，饿极欲食，忽见饭菜，有如连续一周不眠不休，困极欲睡，倦极欲休，忽然获得睡觉休息的机会，有如在困顿失望中，忽然获得一丝解决的端倪，其喜悦之情，怎可计量？<br />
“啊，有灯了！”<br />
“啊，光明在望了！”<br />
路灯，伫立路旁，以其光芒为手指，指引人们回家的路，指引人们走向光明大道……。<br />
灯塔，伫立海边，以其闪光为讯号，指引茫茫大海中的船舶，使不致迷失，航抵目的地……。<br />
每一个人的心中，也都要有一盏灯，引导着他，走向光明大道，锦绣前程……。</p>
<p> <strong>The Lamp</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng<br />
When day is pushed by time according to the rule of nature, walks to the inevitable termination of fate, dark will cover the world with its fine and dense net, make night to be pitch-black; if it’s no moon and star, it will more black, let us can’t distinguish all things of the world. It appears that how importance is the lamp.<br />
Lamp and light like bone and flesh, light will exist forever only there is a lamp.<br />
Dark gives us the feeling of at a loss, bewildered, fear, unlucky. Nobody likes dark, nobody dislikes light too. “Wishing there is a lamp!” This is the biggest wishing of everybody while in the dark. If there truly appears a lamp, it will like a person trudging in the unlimited desert, extremely hoping for drink, views suddenly the oasis and spring of sweet well, like lose his way in barren mountain and wild forest, want to have food when hungry extremely, meets food suddenly, likes he no sleep and rest a week successively for a week, sleepy extremely to want to sleep, tired extremely to want to rest, obtains an opportunity to sleep and rest, likes in the lose and fatigued, obtains a little clue for solution suddenly, how to measure the pleasure?<br />
“Oh, it is the lamp!”<br />
“Light is hopping!”<br />
Street lamp, stands by the roadside, points and leads the road for back home, points and leads everybody to go on the bright road…<br />
Light house, stands on sea bank, with the shining lamp, points and leads the boat on the vast sea, gets no lose, sails to termination…<br />
In the heart of everybody, ought to have a lamp, leads him to go on brilliant road, beautiful future…@</p>
