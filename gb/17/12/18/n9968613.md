<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>融到心坎里的手工冰淇淋</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年12月18日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
每次开车经过这里都会看到一个招牌“手工冰淇淋Goodies Homemade Ice Cream”，百合超爱吃冰淇淋，今天进来一试，林林总总不下二十多种，许多连名字见都没见过，于是大胆尝试新口味卡布奇诺杏仁碎片（Cappuccino Almond Crunch）。我只能说好吃，真的好吃！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9968696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9968696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/01_IMG_9510.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9968696" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/01_IMG_9510-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9968696" class="wp-caption-text">各种口味的冰淇淋。<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%86%B0%E6%B7%87%E6%B7%8B.md#1">湾区冰淇淋</a>Goodies Homemade Ice Cream，百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>店面清爽又干净，以<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%86%B0%E6%B7%87%E6%B7%8B.md#1">湾区冰淇淋</a>店来讲算是蛮大一间的。和老板聊聊，是一对印度夫妇，到洛杉矶上了课，学到了制作冰淇淋的奥秘和诀窍，于是开了仅此一家、别无分号的湾区手工冰淇淋店。他说大部分的冰淇淋店卖的冰淇淋里面都有很多空气，但是他的是实实在在的，所以吃起来才如此美味和满足！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9968699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9968699" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/02_IMG_9508.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9968699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/02_IMG_9508-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9968699" class="wp-caption-text">各种口味的<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B3%E8%84%82%E8%BD%AF%E7%B3%96.md#1">乳脂软糖</a>（Fudge）。<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了软硬各色冰淇淋此外，他们也自己做<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B9%B3%E8%84%82%E8%BD%AF%E7%B3%96.md#1">乳脂软糖</a>（Fudge）及爆米花（Pop Corn），另外还有冰酸奶、手工巧克力、奶昔、圣代、焦糖苹果、冰沙、刨冰、巧克力蘸冷冻香蕉、冷冻芝士蛋糕、浓缩咖啡饮料、<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%B7%87%E6%B7%8B%E5%B8%95%E5%B0%BC%E5%B0%BC.md#1">冰淇淋帕尼尼</a>（这倒是第一次听过）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9968701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9968701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/03_IMG_9511.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9968701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/03_IMG_9511-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9968701" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%B7%87%E6%B7%8B%E5%B8%95%E5%B0%BC%E5%B0%BC.md#1">冰淇淋帕尼尼</a>（Ice Cream Panini）。<ahref="https://github.com/eizauy3553/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小店干干净净，就座落于Meridian &amp; San Carlos两条大街的交界处，十分容易找。如果有空经过，不妨到此尝尝看（冬天吃冰也很有风味，或是说吃甜食避寒）！就算是没空去，到他的网站看看，包你口水流得满地都是！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9968702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9968702" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/04_IMG_9516.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-9968702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/04_IMG_9516-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9968702" class="wp-caption-text">多种口味的爆米花。（旧金山吃货百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9968708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9968708" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/05_IMG_9514.jpg"><img class="size-small wp-image-9968708" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/05_IMG_9514-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9968708" class="wp-caption-text">手工冰淇淋。（旧金山吃货百合摄）</figcaption></figure>
