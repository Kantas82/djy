<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这座湖暗藏玄机 让所有潜入湖里的人通通都不想离开！</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JORuw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rspjap308/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/rspjap308/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月05日讯】（大纪元记者李思静综合报导）不知道大家有没有过<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BD%9C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">潜水</a>的经验，那真是一种非常舒服又自在的感觉，尤其看到海底那壮观的画面和围绕在身边的海洋生物，那种感觉真的很特别。</p>
<p>不过今天要带大家看的是一座看起来相当美丽的湖泊，它不仅外观看起来翠绿漂亮，就连湖里都让人为之惊艳喔！</p>
<p><center><a title="Palau_2008030818_4734" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/86265563@N00/2346940479" data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true"><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2273/2346940479_34b12cc388_b.jpg" alt="Palau_2008030818_4734" width="600" b="400" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center>这座湖就叫做<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%AF%8D.md#1">水母</a>湖（Ongeim Tketau）。它位于帛琉洛克群岛的埃尔马尔克岛，本为海洋的一部分，后来由于地壳运动，周围的海床升高，逐渐与外海隔绝。</p>
<p><center><a title="Palau_2008030818_4737" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/luxtonnerre/2346941293/in/photostream/" data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true"><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2332/2346941293_23eec0606f_z.jpg" alt="Palau_2008030818_4737" width="600" b="400" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center><ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%AF%8D.md#1">水母</a>湖是闻名于世的<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BD%9C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">潜水</a>胜地，令人惊艳的地方就在于湖中有大量的无毒水母。</p>
<p><center><a title="Palau_2008030818_p1020192" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/luxtonnerre/2347158407/in/photostream/" data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true"><img src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3152/2347158407_4cc24281e4_z.jpg" alt="Palau_2008030818_p1020192" width="600" b="400" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center>据说这座湖里的无毒水母甚至高达1,300万只，而且比起其它有毒水母而言，这些水母相当温和。</p>
<p>当它们划过人类皮肤上时几乎是没有感觉的。</p>
<p><center><a title="Palau_2008030818_p1020183" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/luxtonnerre/2347987430/in/photostream/" data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true"><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2366/2347987430_7e9221e279_z.jpg" alt="Palau_2008030818_p1020183" width="600" b="400" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center><br />
2014年一名叫做娜娜(Nana)的女孩曾在此湖浮潜，拍下了湖底美丽又漂亮的画面。</p>
<p>只见水母圆的像水晶球，缓缓地落在她的手上，画面真的很梦幻呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9926375" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9926375" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171205-SIJING-PARROT0006.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9926375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171205-SIJING-PARROT0006-600x352.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="352" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9926375" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%AF%8D%E6%B9%96.md#1">水母湖</a>中的水母。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>至于为什么这些水母没有毒？有这么一个说法。</p>
<p>当<ahref="https://github.com/rspjap308/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%AF%8D%E6%B9%96.md#1">水母湖</a>形成之后，由于湖中的水质与养分的改变，使得水母的天敌消失，而水母的毒刺也渐渐退化，因此毒性也就跟着消失了。</p>
<p>密密麻麻的水母，看起来是不是很壮观呢？</p>
<p><center><a title="Palau_2008030818_p1020195" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/86265563@N00/2347988698" data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true"><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2312/2347988698_1141a09599_z.jpg" alt="Palau_2008030818_p1020195" width="600" b="400" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></center>这些无毒水母看起来就像是穿了一件华丽的衣服呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9926374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9926374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171205-SIJING-PARROT0005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9926374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171205-SIJING-PARROT0005-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9926374" class="wp-caption-text">水母湖中的水母。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在就来看看在湖中实际潜水的样子吧！</p>
