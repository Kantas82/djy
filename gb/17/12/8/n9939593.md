<h6>圣诞老人小摆件。（林乐予／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月09日讯】（大纪元记者林乐予美国<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C%E5%85%B0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">马里兰州</a>报导）相传数百年前，在德国埃尔茨地区（Erzgebirge），最后一班的矿工们安全返回地面后，会把探照灯放在一起组成半圆形，照亮矿井的出口，象征一天的工作顺利结束。从两百五十年前，在家中摆放拱门<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">烛台</a>（Schwibbogen）也成为了德国人家中的节庆传统。在<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%B0%94%E7%9A%84%E6%91%A9.md#1">巴尔的摩</a>的<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>市集，您有机会看到德国匠人巧手制作的拱门<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%83%9B%E5%8F%B0.md#1">烛台</a>工艺品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9939663" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939663" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1936.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939663" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1936-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939663" class="wp-caption-text">传统的德国拱门烛台。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9939664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939664" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1943.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1943-450x362.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939664" class="wp-caption-text">传统的德国拱门烛台。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据统计，作为<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E.md#1">圣诞</a>村的起源地，德国有超过两千五百个<ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E5%B8%82%E9%9B%86.md#1">圣诞市集</a>，世界上历史最悠久的圣诞市集可追溯至1434年的德勒斯登（Dresden）圣诞市集。在美国，德国的圣诞传统也格外受到欢迎，在节日季，许多美国城市都有热闹的德国市集。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9939654" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939654" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1920.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939654" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1920-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939654" class="wp-caption-text">精美的手工艺品摆件。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9939722" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1959.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1959-450x600.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939722" class="wp-caption-text">精美的手工艺品摆件。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9939662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939662" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1927.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1927-450x447.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="447" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939662" class="wp-caption-text">漂亮的圣诞树挂件。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E5%B0%94%E7%9A%84%E6%91%A9.md#1">巴尔的摩</a><ahref="https://github.com/nynade343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E5%B8%82%E9%9B%86.md#1">圣诞市集</a>（Christmas Village in Baltimore）位于繁华的内港（Inner Harbor）地区，已经举办了五年时间。有超过五十家小商品摊位，既有从德国专程前来的摊主，也有马里兰当地的手艺工匠。</p>
<p>最具看点的当数K?the Wohlfahrt公司的特别展区——这里有各式各样的圣诞树挂件、精美的拱门烛台、旋转烛台（the pyramid）和摆饰，是由德国乡村手艺人延用承传百年的传统方法手制完成。</p>
<p>旋转烛台也是一件颇有特色的工艺摆件：点燃蜡烛，顶端的扇页开始慢慢旋转，营造出光影交织的温馨效果。据说在两百年前，只有富裕的德国家庭才能买得起圣诞树，埃尔茨地区的能工巧匠们设计出了圣诞树的替代品——用木棍支起架子，装饰上节日主题的小工艺品和蜡烛，这种温馨怡人的旋转烛台由此诞生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9939669" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939669" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1948.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1948-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939669" class="wp-caption-text">温馨怡人的旋转烛台。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9939725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9939725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1945.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9939725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_1945-450x624.jpeg" alt="" width="450" b="624" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9939725" class="wp-caption-text">温馨怡人的旋转烛台。（林乐予／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴尔的摩圣诞市集的公关负责人、来自德国的安娜．霍菲尔德（Anna Hohfeld）表示，除了工艺品以外，这里还可以买到德国原汁原味的节日食品和酒，并有丰富的现场演出，是体验德国圣诞气氛的好地方。</p>
