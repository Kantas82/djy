<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>袋熊妈妈丧命轮下 育婴袋中宝宝获救</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年12月14日讯】（大纪元记者郑煦婷<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>墨尔本编译报导）近日，在澳洲维州的Harcourt地区，一只被撞死的<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>妈妈横尸路中央，好心人在将它移走时发现育婴袋中有动静，最终救出了一只可爱的袋熊宝宝，下面这个视频记录下宝宝获救的全过程，看完超暖心！</p>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2F175726775812588%2Fvideos%2F1729595690425681%2F&amp;show_text=0&amp;width=267" width="267" b="476" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>据9号台新闻（9 News）报导，当天，洛克斯（Paul Rocks）开车经过Harcourt地区时，发现路中央躺着一只<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A2%8B%E7%86%8A.md#1">袋熊</a>。“我走到它旁边，发现已经死掉了，但突然，它鼓出的肚子有动静，我觉得非常奇怪。”</p>
<p>然后，洛克斯看到了两只耳朵和一只脚探出来，就意识到育婴袋里有袋熊宝宝，于是联系了<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>紧急<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>机构。</p>
<p>这只袋熊宝宝获救后住在一个庇护所内，得到工作人员全天候的悉心照顾。它被冠以昵称“Harcourt来的小哈蒂（Hattie）”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9956721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.29.58-pm.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9956721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.29.58-pm-450x299.png" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">获救的袋熊宝宝。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9956722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.26.30-pm.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9956722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.26.30-pm-450x301.png" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">获救的袋熊宝宝。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9956726" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.27.39-pm.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9956726" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Screen-Shot-2017-12-14-at-6.27.39-pm-450x321.png" alt="" width="450" b="321" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">获救的袋熊宝宝。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">救援</a>人员摩根（Neil Morgan）说，哈蒂的情况很好，庇护所会再饲养它两年，等到大一些再将其放生。</p>
<p>摩根希望，哈蒂的故事能提醒更多的司机在不小心撞到动物后，停下来检查一下，必要时要联系<ahref="https://github.com/iptuzu337/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9.md#1">野生动物</a>救援机构。</p>
