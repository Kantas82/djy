<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>钟铉出殡 KEY与太妍泪崩 东海凝望至最后一刻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171017061487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>SHINee钟铉出殡，众星送行，泰民（左排一）、Key（左排二）与利特（右排一)、银赫（右排二）及东海（右排三）等人负责擡棺，面色凝重。 (Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月21日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）SHINee成员<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%9F%E9%93%89.md#1">钟铉</a>18日轻生离世，于今（21日）晨台湾时间8点于首尔峨山医院葬礼场出殡。担任主祭人的SHINee成员们面容憔悴，珉豪捧着牌位咬牙忍住情绪，而KEY则是忍不住情绪，倚靠<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B5%81.md#1">温流</a>的肩膀痛哭。东海则是凝望着灵车至最后一刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790007" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171035371487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10790007" title="98869802" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171035371487-600x400.jpg" alt="98869802" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790007" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee队友珉豪（左）低头捧着牌位，左二为<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%92%9F%E9%93%89.md#1">钟铉</a>的姊姊捧着遗照。 (Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>出殡仪式由钟铉的姊姊捧遗照，而SHINee成员珉豪负责捧钟铉的牌位，SHINee的<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%B5%81.md#1">温流</a>、KEY与泰民则是与Super Junior的利特、银赫与东海一同抬棺。而一行人将棺木送上灵车后，原先与温流牵着手、尽量保持平静的SHINee成员KEY，却忍不住悲从中来，靠在温流肩上痛哭。而温流也给予KEY拥抱及安慰，场面让人鼻酸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171039051487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10790013" title="98869796" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171039051487-600x400.jpg" alt="98869796" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790013" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee钟铉出殡，众星送行，与钟铉情同姊弟的“<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">少女时代</a>”太妍（中）泪崩。 (Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而昨晚于IG发长文悼钟铉的<ahref="https://github.com/miondt3036/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">少女时代</a>队长太妍，亦于送行现场痛哭。身旁的润娥（允儿）搂着太妍的肩膀给予安慰，太妍仍是皱紧眉头手摀着嘴望着灵车悲伤痛哭，润娥及徐玄也哭成泪人儿。而孝渊、秀英和俞利亦于一旁拭泪，Super Junior的始源的泪水在眼眶里打转。安七炫则是面色凝重地望着灵车。而钟铉生前疼爱的师妹Red Velvet成员Yeri也涕泣不止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790017" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171017021487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10790017" title="98869798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171017021487-600x400.jpg" alt="98869798" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790017" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee钟铉出殡，众星送行，图后排右二为崔始源。(Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在将钟铉棺木送上车、灵车准备出发时，为钟铉送行的至亲好友们准备回灵堂，但负责抬棺的Super Junior东海仍皱着眉悲伤的望着灵车，貌似是想目送钟铉到最后一刻。现场亦有众多粉丝哭着为钟铉送行，场面哀戚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10790027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10790027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171046151487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10790027" title="AFP_VC3VO" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1810171046151487-600x404.jpg" alt="AFP_VC3VO" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10790027" class="wp-caption-text">SHINee钟铉出殡，现场粉丝哭着送行。(JUNG YEON-JE/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
