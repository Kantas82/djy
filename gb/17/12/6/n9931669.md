<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：忍冬花</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1410012139102483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Sprig of honeysuckle with white flowers and green leaves isolated on white background (fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>

<p>忍耐着，坚强忍耐着，咬紧牙关地忍耐着…。<br />
忍耐着，要忍耐多久？要有多少坚强的忍耐力？<br />
忍冬花，那么细瘦，那么娇小，那么纤柔，那么金枝玉叶，被称为金针花，能够忍耐得起吗？<br />
冬是暴君，是恶魔，天生具有暴力倾向、虐待狂，总露出狰狞的面目，以风霜雨雪为武器，到处奔逐，到处横行，发出呼呼碰碰声，欺压万物，要令万物萧条，百花凋落，万叶枯萎……。<br />
再细瘦，再娇小，再纤细，再金枝玉叶，被称金针花，她还是得忍耐，并且忍耐得起！<br />
谁说忍耐不起？<br />
他一定忍耐得起的！即使过后，身上留有泪的酸味，他还是会坚强地忍耐下去，而且忍耐得起。<br />
忍冬，忍冬，忍过冬天以后，会是怎样？<br />
忍过冬天以后，当然就是春天了。<br />
是的，忍过冬天以后，春天一到，便会复苏、滋长、蓬勃、开花……。<br />
只是，现在仍是冬天，她仍需忍耐：<br />
再细瘦，再娇小，再纤弱，再金枝玉叶，既然在冬天里，就需忍耐，坚强地忍耐，咬紧牙关地忍耐……。<br />
再细瘦，再娇小，再纤弱，再金枝玉叶，既然在冬天里，就需忍耐，再久也需忍耐，再苦也需忍耐……。</p>
<p><strong> The Honeysuckle</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Bears, bears firmly, grit teeth to bear…<br />
Bears, how long will she bear? How much is the strong bear force need?<br />
Honeysuckle, thin likes that, tiny likes that, slander likes that, “He’s a woman, she is a man” likes that, since it’s called honeysuckle, can it be bearable?<br />
Winter is a tyrant, is a devil, posted the incline of violence inborn, mad in ill-treatment, often shows the ferocious face, arms with wind, frost, rain and snow, goes around everywhere, runs amuck everywhere, issues the sound of whir and boom, bullies all the thing, will make all the thing desolate, all the flower fall, all the leave wither…<br />
What is more thin, more tiny, more slander, more “He’s a woman, she is a man”, who is called honeysuckle, she must bear, and to be bear!<br />
Who says she can’t bear?<br />
She certainly bearable! Even after it passed, the sour tears are left, she will bear firmly still, and will bearable.<br />
Bear the winter, bear the winter, what will she be after borne the winter?<br />
After the winter is passed, it of course is spring.<br />
Yes, bears after winter, when spring arrived, recovery , growing, vigor, bloom will be come…<br />
Only it’s still winter, she must bear:<br />
What is more thin, more tiny, more slander, more “He’s a woman, she is a man”, since it’s winter, she must bear, bear firmly, grit teeth to bear…<br />
Only it’s still winter, she must bear: What is more thin, more tiny, more slander, more “He’s a woman, she is a man”, since it’s winter, she must bear, how long it need also to bear, how long it need to bear…@</p>
