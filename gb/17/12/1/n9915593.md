<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花前月下 独揽旧金山美景</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/ISe838fze4dthl1000000000-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>旧金山豪宅352 Cumberland Street有一间可观赏美景的日光屋。（Zillow）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月02日讯】（大纪元记者李欧旧金山汇编）向往住进旧金山吗？那就不要错过这里变幻多端的天际线，高楼、海景、树林、街景、塔楼，不同的位置都有各自独特的风景画面，使人心旷神怡！本周挑选4套价位不同的豪宅，从它们的角度一窥旧金山多采风貌。</p>
<p>一、352 Cumberland Street,<br />
San Francisco, CA 94114</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917223" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.17.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9917223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.17-450x249.png" alt="" width="450" b="249" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917223" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E7%9C%8B%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区看房</a>：352 Cumberland Street, San Francisco, CA 94114。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个独特的家坐落在Dolores Heights最令人垂涎的街区之一，并提供从各个楼层观赏市中心天际线的壮观景色。该物业包括一个开放式的客厅、餐厅和厨房，还有一个大型中庭日光浴空间、屋顶甲板和花园。</p>
<p>这是2层楼建筑，有一个2卧2卫的住宅单位，另有2个独立的Studio位于建筑物的前面，每个都有自己的入口，各有单独的厨房和浴室，可用于出租或作为办公室或客人套房。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/01_IS2r98yirs1lhl1000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/01_IS2r98yirs1lhl1000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917224" class="wp-caption-text">木片外墙的典雅造型。（Zillow）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9917225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/02_ISme5kx5jclqhl1000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/02_ISme5kx5jclqhl1000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917225" class="wp-caption-text">一览<ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%A4%A9%E9%99%85%E7%BA%BF.md#1">旧金山天际线</a>的日光浴空间。（Zillow）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二、2528 Union Street,<br />
San Francisco, CA 94123</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.32.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9917227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.32-450x247.png" alt="" width="450" b="247" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917227" class="wp-caption-text">湾区买房：2528 Union Street, San Francisco, CA 94123。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是一处现代式新豪宅，地面上有3层楼，车库在底部楼层。从阳台甲板可观赏美丽的海湾景色，甚至眺望金门大桥。室内的装潢典雅，以白色基调为主，房间宽广大气，从地板到灯具，选材不凡。</p>
<p>此住宅于2017年9月曾售出1200万美元。这个房子的租金Zillow预估约为每月$28,695。2017年物业税为$52,398。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/03_ISmuddqz792x9r0000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/03_ISmuddqz792x9r0000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917228" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E8%B1%AA%E5%AE%85.md#1">旧金山豪宅</a>，底层的后花园空间。（Zillow）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9917230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917230" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/04_IS2r12czhqk29r0000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/04_IS2r12czhqk29r0000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917230" class="wp-caption-text">在阳台远望海湾与金门大桥。（Zillow）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三、1210 Lombard Street,<br />
San Francisco, CA 94109</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917231" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.48.png"><img class="wp-image-9917231 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.48-450x239.png" alt="" width="450" b="239" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917231" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BAopen-house.md#1">湾区Open House</a>：1210 Lombard Street, San Francisco, CA 94109。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是位在西边Russian Hill的双户型豪宅，此物业位在上层，能看到Marina一直延伸到金门大桥的景色。客厅宽大明亮，还有一个燃气壁炉，直接与餐厅相通；两间卧室也都有明亮的采光。</p>
<p>屋后还有茂盛的绿色植物花园，底部有水池造景，并透过不同楼层的阳台甲板，欣赏不一样的风景线。此建筑另有下层独立空间，门号1212。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917233" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/05_159063283-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/05_159063283-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917233" class="wp-caption-text">双户型住宅，此物业为上层单位。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9917234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/06_159063283-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917234" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/06_159063283-7-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917234" class="wp-caption-text">可远眺海港区的阳台甲板。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure>
<p>四、2860 Greenwich Street,<br />
San Francisco, CA 94123</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917236" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.56.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9917236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.10.56-450x235.png" alt="" width="450" b="235" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917236" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E8%B1%AA%E5%AE%85.md#1">旧金山豪宅</a>：1210 Lombard Street, San Francisco, CA 94109。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个是由Jeff Schlarb设计工作室重新打造的房子，紧挨着Presidi东边大门，靠近Lyon街。室内有超大的5千多平方呎，在顶楼有一间“天空休息室”，看着天海相连，享受旧金山美景。</p>
<p>经由著名设计师打造，散发着当代优雅的设计，内外皆以白色为主，构建纯白的梦幻色调，从厨房到卫浴皆彰显设计师的一贯理念。一街之隔即是Presidi的树林景致，在大城市享受自然竟如此轻松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9917239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9917239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/07_174797853-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9917239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/07_174797853-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9917239" class="wp-caption-text">在顶楼的“天空休息室”享受<ahref="https://github.com/nqqppy374/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%A4%A9%E9%99%85%E7%BA%BF.md#1">旧金山天际线</a>。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure>
