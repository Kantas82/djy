<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>在悉尼Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅享受德国大餐,过瘾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-07-600x392.jpg" />
<h6>Kaiser Stub'n餐厅著名的德国猪脚配德国酸菜（Kaiser Stub'n餐厅提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年12月20日讯】在北悉尼Terrey Hills有一家餐厅KaiserStub&#8217;n，以经营德国餐为主。欧式的建筑，德国的风格装饰摆设令食客犹如身处异国他乡。Robert既是老板又是主厨，二十三年前，由他一手创建了这个餐厅。在这几十年里，经历了荣与乐、苦与累的体验，给顾客们带来了无尽的享受和快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974271" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974271" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-06.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9974271 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-06-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974271" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅老板兼主厨Robert（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Robert是一位经验丰富的专业厨师，早期在奥地利学习烹调，先后在奥地利、瑞士、澳大利亚等国的许多餐厅担任主厨。1994年他得到了一个机会，创建一个全新的、属于自己的餐厅，Robert把他毕生心血倾注在这座餐厅里。功夫不负有心人，1997年餐厅获得了新州饮食协会颁发的最佳餐厅奖—“European Restaurant of the Year”。在接下来的1998、1999年、2004年和2010年，餐厅又斩获多项大奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9974268 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-03-450x329.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974268" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅招牌菜烤鸭（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅有许多受华人喜爱的德国餐，比如著名的<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E5%9B%BD%E7%8C%AA%E8%84%9A.md#1">德国猪脚</a>“Pork Knuckle”，又称德国咸猪手，其实准确的说应该是猪肘。这道菜还配有<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E5%9B%BD%E9%85%B8%E8%8F%9C.md#1">德国酸菜</a>“Sauerkraut”和Bread Dumpling。Robert的招牌菜烤鸭也颇受欢迎，<ahref="https://github.com/rapdkl384/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E5%9B%BD%E9%A6%99%E8%82%A0.md#1">德国香肠</a>当然也是不可不尝的美食。如果有喜欢炸鸡排的朋友，维也纳风味的炸鸡排也是值得一试的选择。品尝这些美食的时候可千万不要忘记德国啤酒，这可是德国菜不可或缺的一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974270" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9974270 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-05-450x262.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="262" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974270" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅烤煎饺（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kaiser Stub&#8217;n售卖各种各样的德国啤酒。大口吃猪脚、香肠，大口饮着略带苦味的啤酒，肉香，啤酒香四溢，好不过瘾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974269" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974269" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-04.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9974269 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-04-300x399.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974269" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅大虾牛油果色拉（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Robert 还有几道出色的甜品，例如：Cream Brulee with Mascarpone，Mousse and Tia Maria Sauce以及Apple Strudel served with Homemake Cinnamon Ice Cream等。坐在异乡风情的餐厅里，品尝Robert出品的美食，一定会有一番别样的风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974267" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974267" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9974267 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-02-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974267" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅内部的欧式风格装饰（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从悉尼市中心驾车前往只需35分钟，沿Mona Vale Road行驶到Terrey Hills的Church Pont出口驶出，进入Mc Carrs Road，Kaiser Stub&#8217;n就在您路口的右侧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9974266" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9974266" style="width: 571px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-01.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9974266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171220-yichun-rst-01.jpg" alt="" width="571" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9974266" class="wp-caption-text">Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅（Kaiser Stub&#8217;n餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Kaiser Stub&#8217;n营业时间：</p>
<p>周二至周日晚餐：18:00-24:00；周日午餐：12:00-14:30</p>
