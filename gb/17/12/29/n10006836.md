<p>问：如果牙齿掉了，没有补上会怎样？</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>纽约<ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E8%B0%A2%E6%82%A8%E7%89%99%E7%A7%91%E8%AF%8A%E6%89%80.md#1">谢谢您牙科诊所</a>院长</strong><ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E8%95%B4%E7%A0%94.md#1">徐蕴研</a>：</span></h2>
<p>一般情况下当你牙齿掉了，牙医都建议补上——除了智慧牙通常不需要补。</p>
<p>如果牙齿掉了不补，那么在缺少牙齿的位置，牙骨会下降、收缩，牙肉也会跟着一直降。旁边的牙齿也会跟着歪向<ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%BA%E7%89%99.md#1">缺牙</a>的地方，比如后面的牙齿会往前推，前面的牙齿会向后倒；上下对应的牙齿也会受到影响，比如下牙缺了，对应的上牙就会向下生长，比邻牙多出一块。</p>
<p>特别是后面的大牙不能空缺。如果我们没有大牙，会影响吃东西、影响咬合。前面的牙齿缺失，也会影响到美观。</p>
<p>如果没有牙齿，人的面貌看上去也会显老。因为脸颊会向里收缩，皱纹也会增多。</p>
<p>补牙齿的方法有三种：</p>
<p>1. 植牙（通过手术，将人造牙根植入牙床骨，再套上<ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%87%E7%89%99.md#1">假牙</a>）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10006928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10006928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/yachi1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10006928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/yachi1-600x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="179" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10006928" class="wp-caption-text">植牙。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 固定式<ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%87%E7%89%99.md#1">假牙</a>/牙桥（利用粘合剂固定在缺齿附近的牙齿上）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007221" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/yach.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10007221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/yach-600x318.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="267" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007221" class="wp-caption-text">固定式假牙（牙桥）。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 活动式假牙（部分活动假牙适用于<ahref="https://github.com/fslmcq3362/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BC%BA%E7%89%99.md#1">缺牙</a>不多的人，通常是利用金属钩，将假牙挂在旁边的牙齿上；全口活动假牙则适用于缺失大部分牙齿的人）</p>
<figure id="attachment_10006930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10006930" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/yachi2.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10006930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/yachi2-600x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="178" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10006930" class="wp-caption-text">活动式假牙。(<span style="color: #808080;"><a style="color: #808080;" href="https://pxhere.com/en/photo/776977">Pxhere</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
