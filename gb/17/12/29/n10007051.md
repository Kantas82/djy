<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：宁静一刻 2017 Ford Focus Electric</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732292224-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月30日讯】虽然电动车的市占率仍不高，但随着不同品牌的电动车投入市场，以及快速充电基建的落成，相信不久在道路上行驶的电动车会越来越多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007070" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732202224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007070 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732202224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007070" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>这周测试的Ford Focus Electric是一辆纯电动车，即是如果电力耗尽便会完全停下来，不会像Plug-in Hybrid般有引擎作为后备动力源。但是也因此比Plug-in Hybrid轻，如Ford Focus Electric的车重只有1,643公斤，比C-Max Energi的1,750公斤轻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007095" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007095" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732152224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007095 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732152224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007095" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>Ford的Focus车系内，包括汽油版Focus、Focus ST及电动版Focus Electric，然而各具特式，一般人会选择Focus汽油版，而爱开快车的人必定钟爱Focus ST，而热爱环保的就会选择Focus Electric。三款车采用相同底盘与车身，为了令人容易区分它们，Ford为每一款车设计了不同的进风口栏栅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007096" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732022224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007096 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732022224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007096" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>Focus Electric由置于车头的永久磁力马达所驱动，此马达输出为143匹马力，扭矩为184磅呎，以单速变速箱将动力传递至前轮，Focus Electric的最高时速84mph，续航力约为76英里，即约为120公里，然而，无论在何时开启空调，显示续航力马上减少至60%，如120公里便可减至72公里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007098" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007098" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732132224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007098 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732132224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007098" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>内置电池是由LG化工制造的Lithium-ion锂离子电池，储电量23kW/h，可使用240V或120V充电，如以240V充电，只需2.5小时便可完全充满，如以120V进行充电，则需20小时才行！但是请留意，Focus Electric的电池是收藏在车尾，所以占据了部分行李舱空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732102224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007101 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732102224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007101" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>在实际驾驶Focus Electric的时候，整个过程就有如玩游戏一样，均速到达目的地为最好、最为省电，开太快固然会减少行车里程数，而开太慢，车内设备如空调等也在不断耗用电能，也会令行车里程有影响。同样，刹车时绝不可太急速，因为Focus Electric装备了能源回收系统Regenerate Brake，在较远距离开始刹车，会容易将多些动能转为电能，储存回电池内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007104" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732052224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007104 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732052224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007104" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于是电动车的关系，Focus Electric停顿时非常宁静，就算是在行驶时，也只有一点马达的高频声音，相反的是Focus Electric的隔音一般，车外杂音、路噪风噪也传入了车内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10007107" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10007107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732072224.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10007107 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1712291732072224-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10007107" class="wp-caption-text">2017 Ford Focus Electric。〈李奥/大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，Focus Electric的车主也可利用MyFord Mobile Apps，遥远控制Focus Electric，如开关车门、启动汽车、查找汽车位置、检视充电情况等。同时，车主更可预先设定空调开启时间及温度，在炎夏及寒冷天气最为实用。</p>
<p>至于价钱方面，Focus Electric由$33,199加元起，比Focus ST全装备还要贵上一点，但是，各地区政府对于这些节能车都有不同的补助，可节省数千块钱的！◇</p>
