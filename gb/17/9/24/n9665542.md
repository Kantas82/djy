<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>牙科知识人人须知（629）“我已经戒烟了”</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>病人配合对于治疗成功何其重要，这个案例能说明这点。吸烟是个坏习惯，<ahref="https://github.com/wlfpjr3170/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E7%83%9F.md#1">戒烟</a>却不容易。</p>
<p>何先生52岁，是为瘾君子，牙周炎严重，三个上门牙摇摇欲坠，流脓（图一至三：6,7,9），流脓处插入橡皮尖，拍摄X光片，确认病牙。总之，这三个牙齿必须拔除，然后即刻植牙，加以修复，但是事先必须<ahref="https://github.com/wlfpjr3170/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E7%83%9F.md#1">戒烟</a>或者减少吸烟。何先生选择后者，结果手术顺利：植入三个植牙（四、五）。立即装上三个临时牙冠（图六），一周后脓肿消失。何先生非常高兴，但是他偶尔还会抽烟。术后一个半月，其中一个临时牙冠脱落（图九），重新粘上，顺便拍摄X光片（图七、八），显示植骨（^）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9665545" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9665545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_01-Sep.-24-17.57-e1506294242586.gif"><img class="size-large wp-image-9665545" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_01-Sep.-24-17.57-600x170.gif" alt="图1至图4" width="600" b="170" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9665545" class="wp-caption-text">????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????? 图1至图4</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实这些骨粉来自他自己骨头，我们植牙钻洞时，收集骨粉，最后把骨粉放在最需要的地方，而且自身骨粉最安全，愈合最好。必要时，加用异体骨或者人工骨粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9665547" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9665547" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_02-Sep.-24-17.57-e1506294337484.gif"><img class="size-large wp-image-9665547" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_02-Sep.-24-17.57-600x168.gif" alt="图5至图8" width="600" b="168" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9665547" class="wp-caption-text">???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????? 图5至图8</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于何先生工作忙，家里事多，还回国看妈妈，三个临时牙冠一戴便是半年，最后其中一个牙冠又丢失，不得不回诊所。复查X光片显示骨粉好像已经与原地骨头愈合！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9665549" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9665549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_03-Sep.-24-17.58-e1506294517513.gif"><img class="size-large wp-image-9665549" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/ScreenHunter_03-Sep.-24-17.58-600x211.gif" alt=" 图9至图11" width="600" b="211" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9665549" class="wp-caption-text">???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????? 图9至图11</figcaption></figure>
<p>让我们吃惊的是：他说他已经戒烟一个月零一周。他伤口愈合得这么好可能与他身体以及毅力有关。</p>
