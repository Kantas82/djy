<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本中位房价在世界各地都能买什么？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年09月28日讯】（大纪元记者宋清宁澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/ukhsfz302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）随着墨尔本住房价格的飙涨，你有没有想过抛开一切，移居海外？这不能怪你，因为现在墨尔本<ahref="https://github.com/ukhsfz302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">房地产</a>市场竞争确实很激烈，短期内也不会有所改善。</p>
<p>目前，<ahref="https://github.com/ukhsfz302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>的房屋<ahref="https://github.com/ukhsfz302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E4%BD%8D%E4%BB%B7.md#1">中位价</a>是865,712澳元，这笔钱在世界各地都能买什么样的房子呢？以下是都门（Domain）新闻网的发现：</p>
<h2>美国纽约曼哈顿</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679005" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679005" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/220-Madison-Avenue-New-York-.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/220-Madison-Avenue-New-York--450x253.jpg" alt="220 Madison Avenue, New York（Stribling） " width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679005" class="wp-caption-text">220 Madison Avenue, New York（Stribling）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：一卧一卫公寓</p>
<p>价格：68.5万美元（约86万澳元）</p>
<p>在麦迪逊大道上找一处栖身之地肯定不会便宜。但这间公寓实在是不错，有明亮的窗户，有屋顶平台可以眺望帝国大厦，还有24小时的门卫服务。</p>
<h2>法国南部比利牛斯</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679013" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679013" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Beaumont-de-Lomagne-Midi-Pyrenees-France-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Beaumont-de-Lomagne-Midi-Pyrenees-France--450x253.png" alt="Beaumont-de-Lomagne, Midi-Pyrenees, France（Lonagne-Immobilier）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679013" class="wp-caption-text">Beaumont-de-Lomagne, Midi-Pyrenees, France（Lonagne-Immobilier）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：5居室农舍</p>
<p>价格：57.5万欧元（约86.3万澳元）</p>
<p>这座恬静宜人的农舍充满了古朴的气息，又能带来现代化的舒适感。这座房子坐落在3.8公顷的土地上，被花园和鲜花环抱。房子还附有一个独立的客用公寓。</p>
<h2>日本东京港区</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679016" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/501-Grand-Maison-Shirokane-Minato.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/501-Grand-Maison-Shirokane-Minato-450x253.png" alt="501 Grand Maison Shirokane, Minato（Asian Property Inc）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679016" class="wp-caption-text">501 Grand Maison Shirokane, Minato（Asian Property Inc）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：两卧一卫公寓</p>
<p>价格：7680万日元（约86.3万澳元）</p>
<p>东京的中心地带自然房价不菲。这间漂亮的公寓十分宽敞，还附有一点庭院区域。走路4分钟就能到达邻近的火车站。</p>
<h2>葡萄牙阿尔加维法鲁</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679020" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679020" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Carvoeiro-Carvoeiro-Algarve-Portugal-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Carvoeiro-Carvoeiro-Algarve-Portugal--450x253.png" alt="Carvoeiro Carvoeiro, Algarve, Portugal（EDL real estate）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679020" class="wp-caption-text">Carvoeiro Carvoeiro, Algarve, Portugal（EDL real estate）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：三卧两卫别墅</p>
<p>价格：58万欧元（约86.9万澳元）</p>
<p>住在这里，每天都像是在度假。这座别墅有多个露天平台，你可以在泳池中畅游，也可以漫步900米到海滩。这个摩洛哥风格的建筑绚丽且奢华，室内设备和装修也很精致。</p>
<h2>英国西约克郡克莱克西顿</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679022" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Drub-Lane-Cleckheaton-West-Yorkshire-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Drub-Lane-Cleckheaton-West-Yorkshire--450x253.png" alt="Drub Lane, Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire（Reeds Rains）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679022" class="wp-caption-text">Drub Lane, Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire（Reeds Rains）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：五居室大房子</p>
<p>价格：50万英镑起价（约85万澳元）</p>
<p>到英国的乡间隐居，享受精美的下午茶。这栋大房子被静谧的花园环抱，还带有私人阳光露台和暖房。这里离利兹不远。</p>
<h2>南非豪登省丹佛恩（Dainfern）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679023" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679023" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Dainfern-Gauteng-South-Africa-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Dainfern-Gauteng-South-Africa--450x253.png" alt="Dainfern, Gauteng, South Africa（Century 21）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679023" class="wp-caption-text">Dainfern, Gauteng, South Africa（Century 21）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：四卧三卫豪宅</p>
<p>价格：890南非兰特（约83.9万澳元）</p>
<p>这栋豪宅的车库里可以停三辆车。设有一个酒吧、台球室、步入式衣柜、双面壁炉、一个游泳池和佣人房（如果你计划雇人的话）。</p>
<h2>新西兰卡皮蒂海岸</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679031" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Ratanui-Road-Paraparaumu-Kapiti-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/Ratanui-Road-Paraparaumu-Kapiti--450x253.png" alt="Ratanui Road, Paraparaumu, Kapiti（Tommy’s）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679031" class="wp-caption-text">Ratanui Road, Paraparaumu, Kapiti（Tommy’s）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：四卧两卫湖景房</p>
<p>价格：94.5万新西兰元（约86.3万澳元）</p>
<p>为什么不住在湖边或海边呢？这栋房子和周围的环境融为一体。卡皮蒂海岸位于惠灵顿以北，开车很方便。</p>
<h2>阿联酋迪拜</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679032" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/District-9E-Jumeirah-Village-Triangle-Dubai-.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/District-9E-Jumeirah-Village-Triangle-Dubai--450x253.png" alt="District 9E, Jumeirah Village Triangle, Dubai（Espace Real Estate）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679032" class="wp-caption-text">District 9E, Jumeirah Village Triangle, Dubai（Espace Real Estate）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：三卧三卫联排别墅</p>
<p>价格：245万阿联酋迪拉姆（约83.5万澳元）</p>
<p>这栋别墅有一个私人游泳池、三个卧室和一个开放的娱乐空间。在这个以超现代建筑和摩天大楼著称的城市里，这栋房子算是回归了基础。</p>
<h2>美国得克萨斯州蒙哥马利</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9679033" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9679033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/78-Creekwood-Montgomery-Texas-.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9679033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/78-Creekwood-Montgomery-Texas--450x253.jpg" alt="78 Creekwood Montgomery, Texas（Mariner Realty）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9679033" class="wp-caption-text">78 Creekwood Montgomery, Texas（Mariner Realty）</figcaption></figure>
<p>房型：四卧四卫湖景房</p>
<p>价格：69万美元（约86.4万澳元）</p>
<p>如果你想找个地方停船，这里是不二之选。喷气滑水艇船坞和私人码头给这座大房子锦上添花。</p>
