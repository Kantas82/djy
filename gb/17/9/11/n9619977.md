<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>MLB史特拉斯堡8局无失分 国民国联东区封王</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年09月11日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E7%89%B9%E6%8B%89%E6%96%AF%E5%A0%A1.md#1">史特拉斯堡</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Stephen Strasburg</span>）的火球，完全压制费城人打线，主投<span lang="EN-US">8</span>局仅被敲<span lang="EN-US">2</span>安无失分，帮助华盛顿<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B0%91.md#1">国民</a>以<span lang="EN-US">3</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>力克费城人，<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%B0%91.md#1">国民</a>以<span lang="EN-US">88</span>胜<span lang="EN-US">55</span>败的成绩，登上国联东区王座，在国联率先挺进季后赛。</p>
<p>前<span lang="EN-US">5</span>局费城人的菜鸟里夫利（<span lang="EN-US">Ben Lively</span>），与<ahref="https://github.com/fzhmuf3171/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B2%E7%89%B9%E6%8B%89%E6%96%AF%E5%A0%A1.md#1">史特拉斯堡</a>上演投手战。不过国民强打透纳（<span lang="EN-US">Trea Turner</span>）先在六局下敲出三垒安打，桑奇士（<span lang="EN-US">Adrian Sanchez</span>）也击出安打，罗夫莱斯（<span lang="EN-US">Victor Robles</span>）再补上二垒安打，八局下透纳轰出阳春炮，国民拉出<span lang="EN-US">3</span>分差距。</p>
<p>国民麦德森（<span lang="EN-US">Ryan Madson</span>）把守最后一关，没想到一上场就挨了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>支安打，即时拿下两人出局后，却又被敲出带有<span lang="EN-US">2</span>分打点安打，让赛事顿时紧张起来。幸好运气还是站在国民这一边，解决霍斯金斯（<span lang="EN-US">Rhys Hoskins</span>）后，国民全队冲出球场，享受胜利的一刻。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
