<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加拿大安省中华总会馆70周年庆</h2>

<p>【大纪元2017年06月30日讯】<span style="color: #000000;">今年是<ahref="https://github.com/wbtnxs3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E5%AE%89%E7%9C%81%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E6%80%BB%E4%BC%9A%E9%A6%86.md#1">加拿大安省中华总会馆</a>成立<span lang="EN-CA">70</span></span><span style="color: #000000;">周年庆，会馆于</span><span lang="EN-CA"><span style="color: #000000;">2017</span></span><span style="color: #000000;">年</span><span lang="EN-CA"><span style="color: #000000;">6</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><wbr />月</span><span lang="EN-CA"><span style="color: #000000;">24</span></span><span style="color: #000000;">日（星期六）在帝豪名宴大酒楼举办庆祝会暨</span><span lang="EN-CA"><span style="color: #000000;">2017</span></span><span style="color: #000000;">年的梅花春<wbr />宴。</span></p>
