<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>接住安以轩捧花 陈乔恩惊喜：要先找到男友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060357461487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>陈乔恩资料照。（安以轩工作室提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月06日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E4%BB%A5%E8%BD%A9.md#1">安以轩</a>在夏威夷时间5日下午5点举行婚礼仪式，在200位亲友的见证下，她与澳门集团CEO陈荣炼交换婚戒，完成浪漫婚礼。最后新娘<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%A7%E8%8A%B1.md#1">捧花</a>则由姊妹淘中的<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E4%B9%94%E6%81%A9.md#1">陈乔恩</a>幸运接住，当下她十分惊喜，笑说要先找到男朋友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9231664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9231664" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060349211487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9231664" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060349211487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9231664" class="wp-caption-text">在夏威夷的婚礼上，<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%89%E4%BB%A5%E8%BD%A9.md#1">安以轩</a>（右）与新郎交换婚戒后，当众亲吻，完成婚礼仪式。（安以轩工作室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安以轩的这场婚礼选在一望无际的的蓝色大海边，现场布置成浪漫的希腊风情，有大竖琴摆饰，也有昵称小叮当的夫婿拥有的任意门。</p>
<p>一开始，由新郎和伴郎团先亮相，接着<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E4%B9%94%E6%81%A9.md#1">陈乔恩</a>、刘品言、白歆惠、Linda、夏于乔等10位伴娘团入场，安以轩身穿法国时装设计师量身打造的唯美白纱礼服。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9231989" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9231989" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531141487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9231989" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531141487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9231989" class="wp-caption-text">婚礼一开始，由新郎和伴郎团先亮相。（安以轩工作室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9231993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9231993" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531161487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9231993" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531161487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9231993" class="wp-caption-text">图为伴郎陈小春与伴娘夏于乔。（安以轩工作室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了这件婚纱，安以轩远赴巴黎三次定装，设计师专门为她设计长三米宽两米半的白色拖尾裙，腰间还有象征完美结合的镶钻蝴蝶结，行走起来就像着一只美丽的蝴蝶漫步飞舞在白云上，现场宾客无不赞叹美爆了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9231997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9231997" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060349231487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9231997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060349231487-600x900.jpg" width="350" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9231997" class="wp-caption-text">安以轩身穿的婚纱，是法国设计师专门为她设计长三米宽两米半的白色拖尾裙，腰间还有象征完美结合的镶钻蝴蝶结，相当别致典雅。（安以轩工作室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>安以轩的钻戒也十分抢眼，由名家设计以象征祝福的礼物意念为出发点，两条飘逸的细致线条相互缠绕出戒指的优雅身形，围绕而上衬托着10克拉白钻主石，小心呵护着新人的爱，并于戒脚处系上俐落而优雅的蝴蝶结。象征坚贞的爱情。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9231998" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9231998" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531191487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9231998" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1706060531191487.jpg" alt="" width="501" b="333" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9231998" class="wp-caption-text">安以轩的钻戒由名家设计，以象征祝福的礼物意念为出发点，衬托着10克拉白钻主石，小心呵护着新人的爱。（安以轩工作室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后来到单身女孩最期待的<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8D%A7%E8%8A%B1.md#1">捧花</a>时间，由新娘安以轩丢出手上捧花。主持人纳豆问她最想丢给谁，她回答一切都是缘分，去年她就是接到<ahref="https://github.com/owcgqx3850/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">弦子</a>的捧花，今年步入礼堂。结果捧花竟然由伴娘团的陈乔恩接到。</p>
<p>陈乔恩完全没想到自己会接到捧花，当接到捧花后，她开心地笑着说“要先找到男朋友”。</p>
