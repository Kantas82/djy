<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法网女单爆冷　卫冕冠军穆古露莎16强止步</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/GettyImages-692208830-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>穆古露莎。 (Photo credit should read GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">【大纪元2017年06月05日讯】（大纪元记者尚上礼综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E7%BD%91.md#1">法网</a><ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%8D%95.md#1">女单</a>第<span lang="EN-US">4</span>轮再爆冷，力求卫冕的<ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%86%E5%8F%A4%E9%9C%B2%E8%8E%8E.md#1">穆古露莎</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Garbine Muguruza</span>）遭地主选手美拉德诺维奇（<span lang="EN-US">Kristina Mladenovic</span>）以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>狙击成功。同日瑞士好手巴辛斯基（<span lang="EN-US">Timea Bacsinszky</span>）也以<span lang="EN-US">5</span>：<span lang="EN-US">7</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>，拍倒第<span lang="EN-US">10</span>种子<ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%A8%81.md#1">大威</a>廉丝（<span lang="EN-US">Venus Williams</span>）。</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm"><ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%86%E5%8F%A4%E9%9C%B2%E8%8E%8E.md#1">穆古露莎</a>坦言面对地主选手压力很大，尽管第<span lang="EN-US">13</span>种子美拉德诺维奇出现<span lang="EN-US">16</span>次双发失误，仍以<span lang="EN-US">1</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>悬殊比数奉送首盘，第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>盘恢复手感后，却又在决胜盘不敌法国选手的完美演出。赛后记者会上，她表示观众的敌意造成些影响：“我可以理解地主球迷不会让我好过，但有点太超过了，我不是来这里树立敌人，我喜欢在这里打球，不过感觉很不好。”</p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm"><ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%A8%81.md#1">大威</a>首盘以<span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11.5pt; color: black;">7</span><span style="font-size: 11.5pt; color: black;">：<span lang="EN-US">5</span>逆转拿下。次盘</span>巴辛斯基<span style="font-size: 11.5pt; color: black;">就不再失手，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>领先后，连续两次破发得手，以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">2</span>扳回一城。决胜盘巴辛斯基完全进入状态，开局取得<span lang="EN-US">4</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>巨幅领先，最终也以<span lang="EN-US">6</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>轻松拿下。</span></p>
<p class="canvas-atomcanvas-textmb10emmb0--smmt08em--sm">高位种子纷纷淘汰的情况下，目前<ahref="https://github.com/okmvia343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%8D%95.md#1">女单</a>第<span lang="EN-US">4</span>种子哈蕾普（<span lang="EN-US">.Simona Halep</span>）夺冠呼声最高，芳龄<span lang="EN-US">24</span>的美拉德诺维奇（<span lang="EN-US">Kristina Mladenovic</span>）也成了口袋名单。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
