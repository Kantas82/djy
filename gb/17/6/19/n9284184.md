<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【英国秘境】10个超棒沙滩，玩水去！</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>美好的夏天已到，拦不住，我要下海玩水去啦！英国是个岛，按说随便指个方向，一直走下去就能到海边。然而……</p>
<p>到了海跟前也许你就发现了，英国的海滩呐，有些是大礁石滩，有些地方海水并没有那么干净，有些还暗潮汹涌的，实在不忍心放自己跳下去……那么，以下提到的海滩，很多都是只有当地最懂门道的人才知道的哦！沙子细腻、海水清澈、风光旖旎——挑个离你近的，该出发啦！</p>
<h2><strong>康沃尔郡：</strong><strong>天涯海角（</strong><strong>Land&#8217;s End</strong><strong>）附近的</strong><strong>Porthcurno</strong><strong>沙滩</strong></h2>
<p><strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_9284236" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284236" style="width: 551px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/8e3aa734a224f3c177802a1fa3499499-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/8e3aa734a224f3c177802a1fa3499499-450x299.jpg" alt="Porthcurno，天涯海角附近的湛蓝之水。（Adam Gibbard / visitcornwall.com）" width="551" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284236" class="wp-caption-text">Porthcurno，天涯海角附近的湛蓝之水。（Adam Gibbard / visitcornwall.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>藏在南康沃尔郡(South Cornwall)有名的室外米纳克剧场（Minack Theatre）悬崖之下，有一片康沃尔郡最美的海湾。漏斗状的沙滩夹在布满青苔的花岗岩悬崖之间，很容易找到，而且沙子洁白又细腻，非常受大家欢迎。落潮后，在湛蓝的海水环绕中可以悠哉散步到临近的几个海湾和海滩，也可以在Treryn Dinas古老的悬崖石堡上坐一坐，静静地看海。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong>：康沃尔奶油茶、咖啡和美味简餐，就在米纳克剧场咖啡店里。<br />
<strong>住</strong>：旅馆The Old Coastguard Hotel位于Mousehole，也提供宽敞的酒吧和餐厅，可俯瞰大海与棕榈树花园的美景。<br />
<strong>行：</strong>Porthcurno, Cornwall TR19 6JX</p>
<h2><strong>德文郡：</strong><strong>黑潭沙滩</strong><strong>Blackpool Sands</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284233" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284233" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/00c0a100f49207adbb0a92917f302713-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/00c0a100f49207adbb0a92917f302713-450x338.jpg" alt="Blackpool Sands，海水清澈碧蓝，四周绿荫环绕。（blackpoolsands.co.uk）" width="550" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284233" class="wp-caption-text">Blackpool Sands，海水清澈碧蓝，四周绿荫环绕。（blackpoolsands.co.uk）</figcaption></figure>
<p>位处南德文郡(South Devon)达特茅斯（Dartmouth）西南三英里外，这个新月形的宁静海湾树木繁茂，山丘环绕。虽然沙子不算细腻，但海水清澈碧蓝，是个下海游泳的极棒去处。可别忘了带上皮划艇和桨板哦！</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>就在海滩上有金星咖啡厅（The Venus Café），可以吃到鲜美的德文郡螃蟹、面包棒还有沙拉。<br />
<strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>Strete Barton House,这座时尚的B＆B旅馆位于达特茅斯附近的一座16世纪庄园内，邮编TQ6 0RU。<br />
<strong>行：</strong>Dartmouth, Blackpool TQ6 0RG</p>
<h2><strong>怀特岛：康普顿湾</strong><strong>Compton Bay</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284229" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/564f0b81473e03d7ab5d6e475162fadd-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/564f0b81473e03d7ab5d6e475162fadd-450x338.jpg" alt="Compton Bay，看看白崖，划划白浪，捡块化石，开心。（isleofwight.com）" width="550" b="413" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284229" class="wp-caption-text">Compton Bay，看看白崖，划划白浪，捡块化石，开心。（isleofwight.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>怀特岛(Isle of Wight)上这个从未遭受破坏的海滨之地，就在英吉利海峡与西怀特（West Wight）之间。落潮时向南漫步至布鲁克湾（Brook Bay），也许在海滩上你能发现古老的恐龙遗迹或在沧桑的白崖上发现一些化石。在停车场停好车后，可以从悬崖上险峻的木制栈道前往海边。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>淡水湾（Freshwater Bay）Dimbola Lodge酒店的咖啡厅，座落在一个迷人的摄影博物馆，供应茶和午餐。<br />
<strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>靠近海滩有康普顿农场宿营车和露营（Compton Farm Caravan and Camping）。<br />
<strong>行：</strong>Compton Beach，PO30 4HB</p>
<h2><strong>肯特郡：</strong><strong>植物湾</strong><strong>Botany Bay</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284228" style="width: 551px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/d229d7b224c64ba726eff0eeb1feaba6-450x450.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/d229d7b224c64ba726eff0eeb1feaba6-450x450.jpg" alt="Botany Bay，离伦敦不远，能看到很多奇特的海边白崖。（Carla Passino / womanandhome.com）" width="551" b="551" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284228" class="wp-caption-text">Botany Bay，离伦敦不远，能看到很多奇特的海边白崖。（Carla Passino / womanandhome.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是位于肯特郡(Kent)Broadstairs海滩中最偏僻的地方，也是最漂亮的——660英尺长的沙滩被白色的崖壁包围。碧浪、白崖、岩石池，下水游泳很安全。在落潮时，可以步行到肯特最好的冲浪海滩Joss湾（Joss Bay）。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>Bessie&#8217;s Tea Parlour茶室和莫雷利冰淇淋（Morelli&#8217;s Gelato）都是受欢迎的选择。<br />
<strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>位于马盖特（Margate）的新月维多利亚酒店（Crescent Victoria Hotel），有复古的氛围和美妙海景的独特风格的客房。<br />
<strong>行：</strong>Botany Rd, Broadstairs CT10 3LG</p>
<h2><strong>约克郡：桑森德</strong><strong>Sandsend</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/7fe19c1fc47051a91c158ba2ef2c23f9-450x183.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284225" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/7fe19c1fc47051a91c158ba2ef2c23f9-450x183.jpg" alt="Sandsend，美丽的海滨小镇就在海滩旁边。（sandsendbaycottages.co.uk）" width="600" b="244" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284225" class="wp-caption-text">Sandsend，美丽的海滨小镇就在海滩旁边。（sandsendbaycottages.co.uk）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在约克郡(Yorkshire)惠特比（Whitby）宽阔的海滩与草地悬崖前，桑森德这一段海滩与附近的海滩相比更安静，也更漂亮。孩子们可以安全地在沙滩上戏耍，欣赏海浪划过沙滩，野鸭蹒跚穿过迷人的Sandsend村庄绿地，落潮后这里又成了一个寻觅化石的好地方。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>沙滩附近有一家The Woodlands，是个可爱的咖啡馆餐厅，但周一不营业。</p>
<p><strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>旅店The Porthole是由一座19世纪地堡改建而成，建在悬崖上，有能够俯瞰大海的私人露台。</p>
<p><strong>行：</strong>The Lane, Sandsend, Whitby YO21 3SY</p>
<h2><strong>诺森伯兰郡：</strong><strong>班堡</strong><strong>Bamburgh</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284224" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/c7a3e7be9aa6717074d753c1c79d3241-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284224" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/c7a3e7be9aa6717074d753c1c79d3241-450x300.jpg" alt="Bamburgh，海滩与古堡长相厮守的独特情怀，你看到了吗？（Donsimon / Depositphotos）" width="550" b="366" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284224" class="wp-caption-text">Bamburgh，海滩与古堡长相厮守的独特情怀，你看到了吗？（Donsimon / Depositphotos）</figcaption></figure>
<p>站在诺森伯兰郡(Northumberland)的班伯城堡（Bamburgh Castle）远眺这个美丽的天然海岸线，海水清澈、沙滩面积巨大，一直延伸到三英里之外的Seahouses。天气晴朗的时候，还可以看到远处的林迪斯法恩圣岛（Lindisfarne）和法恩群岛（Farne Islands）。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>老船旅馆（The Old Ship Inn, Seahouses）享有宽阔大气的海景景观，也有鲜美的海鲜供应。<br />
<strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>在北桑德兰附近的Seahouses，旅馆圣库斯伯特（St. Cuthbert&#8217;s House）从前是个有200年历史的优雅的小教堂。<br />
<strong>行：</strong>Bamburgh Beach, Northumberland NE69 7DF</p>
<h2><strong>兰开夏郡：</strong><strong>福姆比</strong><strong>Formby</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284222" style="width: 552px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/5a9e3449d1920635e5f7d06ed0c753bd-450x256.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284222" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/5a9e3449d1920635e5f7d06ed0c753bd-450x256.jpg" alt="Formby，兰开夏郡的巨大沙滩，还有，那到不了的天边。（visitsouthport.com）" width="552" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284222" class="wp-caption-text">Formby，兰开夏郡的巨大沙滩，还有，那到不了的天边。（visitsouthport.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>兰开夏郡(Lancashire)的这个巨大沙丘被列为特殊科学兴趣的场所（Special Scientific Interest），站在顶部可以看到坎布里亚山脉（Cumbrian mountains）的景色，天气晴朗时甚至能看到黑潭塔（Blackpool Tower）。在这个广阔的海滩上有条小路穿过松树林（是红松鼠保护区），还可以看到史前人类和动物的脚印。在这里看日落也是很壮观的。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>准备食物，选个沙丘，来场海边野餐也是很不错的。</p>
<p><strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>附近有湾树屋（Bay Tree House）B &amp; B。</p>
<p><strong>行：</strong>91 Victoria Rd, Formby, Liverpool L37 1LJ</p>
<h2><strong>苏格兰东海岸，</strong><strong>鲁南湾</strong><strong>Lunan Bay</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284220" style="width: 551px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/463e5f4e50717d1c396abfd66a93cf7a-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/463e5f4e50717d1c396abfd66a93cf7a-450x300.jpg" alt="Lunan Bay，苏格兰海滩与古老的红城堡。（visitscotland.com）" width="551" b="367" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284220" class="wp-caption-text">Lunan Bay，苏格兰海滩与古老的红城堡。（visitscotland.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是在苏格兰东海岸(East coast of Scotland)安格斯（Angus）的海岸线上两英里的沙丘海滨，看看附近沧桑的12世纪红城堡（Red Castle），格外壮观。城堡粉红色的砂岩色调与下面海滩上的红色悬崖和奇特的岩层颜色正好匹配。这个沙滩是观鸟的好地方，也很受冲浪者和骑马人的欢迎。不过下海游泳要小心，因为可能会碰到比较强的水流。</p>
<p><strong>吃</strong><strong>:</strong>在因弗基勒（Inverkeilor）附近有家Gordon&#8217;s Restaurant餐厅，也有客房供游客居住，含早餐。海滩上没有餐厅，但可以野餐。</p>
<p><strong>住</strong><strong>:</strong>在鲁南湾附近海岸的14世纪砂岩堡垒Ethie Castle，是苏格兰最古老的有人居住的城堡之一，也是最有格调的B＆B旅馆之一。</p>
<p><strong>行：</strong>Lunan Bay, Montrose DD10 9TG</p>
<h2><strong>北爱尔兰之北，</strong><strong>Portstewart Strand</strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9284217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9284217" style="width: 550px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/886c307a6a2feb2f02e4a666cb6b3620-450x307.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-9284217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/886c307a6a2feb2f02e4a666cb6b3620-450x307.jpg" alt="Portstewart Strand，北爱尔兰之北，巨人之路附近，粗犷礁石伴着细腻沙滩，光着脚丫踩浪花。（discovernorthernireland.com）" width="550" b="375" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9284217" class="wp-caption-text">Portstewart Strand，北爱尔兰之北，巨人之路附近，粗犷礁石伴着细腻沙滩，光着脚丫踩浪花。（discovernorthernireland.com）</figcaption></figure>

<strong>行：</strong>118 Strand Rd, Portstewart BT55 7PG</p>
