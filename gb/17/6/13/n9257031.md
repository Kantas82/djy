<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽约蛋糕展 诠释“百老汇”经典戏剧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1eea6764994c9d12fd15237acee80cb5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《美女与野兽》造型蛋糕。 (兰青／大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年06月13日讯】（大纪元特约记者兰青纽约报导）在周日（6月11日）结束的第五届纽约<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>展上，来自世界各地甜点师的蛋糕设计令人目不暇给，唯妙唯肖的诠释了经典<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E8%80%81%E6%B1%87.md#1">百老汇</a>戏剧。</p>
<figure id="9257044" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/01a0a4a1a8c9a9b114edc1ac6806c6c6-450x656.jpg" alt="百老汇主题蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BE%E8%80%81%E6%B1%87.md#1">百老汇</a>主题<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9257042" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/50f916b8d4ef66ad89f3d470c147fd3e-450x300.jpg" alt="百老汇主题蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百老汇主题蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9257041" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/f6d39b5ef85160c3d0fbcf7f3cf43d35-450x573.jpg" alt="百老汇主题蛋糕“Wicked”。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百老汇主题蛋糕“Wicked”。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9257040" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1b58f851b5775ce1d498b9346f3df74d-450x745.jpg" alt="《狮子王》主题蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E7%8E%8B.md#1">狮子王</a>》主题蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9257039" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/c67d045c8f60e19c597c7720e2986770-450x654.jpg" alt="《歌剧魅影》造型蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《歌剧魅影》造型蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9257038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/663fcb5c1c295c64255a96d7099e5eba-450x687.jpg" alt="《芝加哥》造型蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《芝加哥》造型蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的纽约蛋糕展主题是“百老汇”，各种百老汇戏剧主题的蛋糕设计，争奇斗艳。其中有不少蛋糕师以《<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90%E7%8E%8B.md#1">狮子王</a>》为题，制作出的造型蛋糕，把《狮子王》诠释得惟妙惟肖，让参观民众叹为观止。</p>
<figure id="9257037" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/a7999fbae9c86201ab591c477ca23ffd-450x675.jpg" alt="《美女与野兽》造型蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%B3%E4%B8%8E%E9%87%8E%E5%85%BD.md#1">美女与野兽</a>》造型蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>看到这个蛋糕，是经典百老汇剧《<ahref="https://github.com/weyerd3881/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%B3%E4%B8%8E%E9%87%8E%E5%85%BD.md#1">美女与野兽</a>》的主题，从城堡到玫瑰，很多装饰细致入微，人物也是栩栩如生，各有特点，让人们一眼就看出是百老汇剧。</p>
<figure id="9257036" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/f06a511d7093b574d56f7f0fddd2c0ce-450x661.jpg" alt="百老汇主题蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百老汇主题蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有民众看到这样设计精致的蛋糕，感到完全是艺术品，美到舍不得去吃。参观民众Mark说：“如果让我切这样的蛋糕，我会感觉不好，我不想破坏这样的艺术品。”另一位民众Corinne说，有人能做出这么精致的蛋糕，把热情投入到蛋糕制作中，真的很棒。</p>
<figure id="9257035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/85f21974ff7d22006c5c227ddab61f5d-450x660.jpg" alt="《美女与野兽》造型蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《美女与野兽》造型蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在纽约蛋糕展上，除了让很多人留连忘返的蛋糕设计之外，还有各种相关的丰富活动，包括设计评奖、烘培教学和甜点装饰课程等。◇</p>
<figure id="9257034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/4e4911b643fd67169b9ea2bd65cf883a-450x702.jpg" alt="Wicked造型蛋糕。" width="450" b="auto" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Wicked造型蛋糕。(兰青／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
