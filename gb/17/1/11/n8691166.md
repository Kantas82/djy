<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浮潜巧遇受困河豚 解救却意外目睹真爱</h2>
<a href="https://dl1ez95kskssm.cloudfront.net/E4mT3ez6g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hcmkav320/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/hcmkav320/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月11日讯】“秀恩爱”可说是很多情侣的强项，但爱情这件事要是发生在<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C.md#1">动物世界</a>里，那相随相依的爱情可能会让人更惊讶。</p>
<p>一名男子，和家人<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BD%9C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">潜水</a>时，巧遇一只受困在废弃渔网的<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B3%E8%B1%9A.md#1">河豚</a>，当他利用碎酒瓶企图割断鱼网解救河豚时，却意外目睹一段动人的真爱，让不少网民看了都称赞不已！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10089462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10089462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10089462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-07-600x325.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10089462" class="wp-caption-text">男子<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BD%9C%E6%B0%B4.md#1">潜水</a>时，巧遇受困在废弃渔网的<ahref="https://github.com/hcmkav320/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B3%E8%B1%9A.md#1">河豚</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当时河豚就像这样卡在透明渔网中无法脱身，但守候在一旁的同伴却没有离开，反而一直不停的围绕在它身边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10089463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10089463" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10089463" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-08-600x326.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10089463" class="wp-caption-text">另一只河豚不但没离开，还一直不停的围绕在它身边，守候着它。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就连男子来解救时也没有因为人类出现而吓跑，反而让人在这对特别的河豚身上看见了满满的真爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10089460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10089460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10089460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-06-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10089460" class="wp-caption-text">河豚为了守候同伴，看到人类出现丝毫不惧怕。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而当网子解开后，这只河豚依然紧紧相随，温暖的画面也让救援男子感到相当暖心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10089464" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10089464" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10089464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/20180126-SIJING-09-600x329.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10089464" class="wp-caption-text">两只河豚获救后仍紧紧相随。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
