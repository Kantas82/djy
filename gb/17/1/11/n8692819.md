<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>到旧金山喝一碗忘忧海鲜汤</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月11日讯】吃货今天又回到旧金山，Woodhouse Fish Co.这家餐厅已经来过两次仍旧念念不忘。在记忆中这一家的<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">海鲜汤</a>，是我吃过最好吃的。几次来都是吃午餐，这次顺便看看左右邻舍点的都是些什么菜。?</p>
<p>这家<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">旧金山美食</a>，Woodhouse Fish Co.在旧金山有两家分店，座落在 Fillmore 街上的这一家是我比较喜欢去的。Lower Pacific Heights 有个小坡慢慢走上去，逛逛橱窗，看看街上的行人，一顿中饭吃下来，倒是十分写意。</p>
<p>看到旁边一对老夫妇，两人都是叫着一模一样的菜，我轻声问他们这道菜真的这么好吃吗？他们说两个人都爱这盘菜，这一道菜确实在别家店没有见过的，这一盘丰盛的朝鲜蓟（Stuffed Artichoke）确实做得漂亮，尝起来也不错。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8699342" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8699342" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/01_Stuffed-artichoke.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/01_Stuffed-artichoke-450x600.jpg" alt="丰盛的朝鲜蓟（Stuffed Artichoke）。（百合摄）" width="450" b="600" class="size-medium wp-image-8699342" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8699342" class="wp-caption-text">丰盛的朝鲜蓟（Stuffed Artichoke）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>我们另外叫了大碗的义式炖<ahref="https://github.com/umgvlm3440/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">海鲜汤</a>（Cioppino），鲜美的汤汁内加上各式各样的生猛海鲜，喝下一口，哇！天地之间只有我和那碗汤！什么都不想。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8699347" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8699347" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Cioppino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Cioppino-450x338.jpg" alt="义式炖海鲜汤（Cioppino）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8699347" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8699347" class="wp-caption-text">义式炖海鲜汤（Cioppino）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>另外又叫了一盘炸鱼玉米饼（Fish Taco），鱼肉新鲜，咬起来觉得好像刚才还在活泼乱跳的样子。几道菜下来其实是蛮饱的，但是不叫个甜点好像这餐没吃完。想想巧克力慕斯（Chocolate Mousse）不过就是空气打泡吧，叫来了之后发现这个气泡好像蛮扎实的，份量又足真是有点吃不下了。<br />
<figure id="attachment_8699348" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8699348" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/03_Fish-taco.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/03_Fish-taco-450x338.jpg" alt="炸鱼玉米饼（Fish Taco）。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8699348" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8699348" class="wp-caption-text">炸鱼玉米饼（Fish Taco）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_8699349" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8699349" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/04_Chocolate-Mousse.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/04_Chocolate-Mousse-300x400.jpg" alt="巧克力慕斯（Chocolate Mousse）。（百合摄）" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-8699349" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8699349" class="wp-caption-text">巧克力慕斯（Chocolate Mousse）。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>涨涨饱饱的肚子正好到外面坡上走一走，午后艳阳高照，闹了个五臓庙开心，精神气爽！看到墙上写着，星期二特价，生蚝一个一元。喜欢吃生蚝的吃货，别忘了这一天来！<br />
<figure id="attachment_8699350" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8699350" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/05_Woodhouse-Fish-Co.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/05_Woodhouse-Fish-Co-450x338.jpg" alt="餐厅内部，我喜欢顺便看看邻桌都吃些什么。（百合摄）" width="450" b="338" class="size-medium wp-image-8699350" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8699350" class="wp-caption-text">餐厅内部，我喜欢顺便看看邻桌都吃些什么。（百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
