<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李冰冰送新年祝福 晒“一飞冲天”瑜伽照</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/libingbing-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大陆李冰冰资料照。(Andreas Rentz/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月02日讯】在众明星纷纷送旧迎新、祝福大家2017年快乐之际，<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%86%B0%E5%86%B0.md#1">李冰冰</a>也没闲着。昨（1）日零点，她晒出一组在舞蹈房带着小外甥练<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9C%E4%BC%BD.md#1">瑜伽</a>的温馨照，并留言说：“来个‘一飞冲天’，祝大家新年快乐！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8655365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8655365" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9zo3m9evj21jk15onpd-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8655365 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9zo3m9evj21jk15onpd-450x338.jpg" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8655365" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%86%B0%E5%86%B0.md#1">李冰冰</a>祝福大家新年“来个一飞冲天”。（李冰冰微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在2017年第一天零点时刻，李冰冰晒出与小外甥一起做<ahref="https://github.com/skwgxt3375/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%91%9C%E4%BC%BD.md#1">瑜伽</a>的照片，庆祝新年的到来。穿着白色背心的李冰冰笑容满面，与外甥开心地不时挑战高难度瑜伽动作。</p>
<p>她附言道：“带着小外甥去健身房，那是锻练基本靠笑，健身基本靠闹，正经点儿来个‘一飞冲天’，祝大家新年快乐！”</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8655369 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9znxitb7j215p1jkhdt-300x400.jpg" alt="47145978ly1fb9znxitb7j215p1jkhdt" width="300" b="400" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-8655374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9zob8jnwj21jk15ou0x-450x338.jpg" alt="47145978ly1fb9zob8jnwj21jk15ou0x" width="450" b="338" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-8655378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9zo5eagfj21jk113hdt-450x300.jpg" alt="47145978ly1fb9zo5eagfj21jk113hdt" width="450" b="300" /></p>
<figure id="attachment_8655380" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8655380" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9znyzqzej21jk113hdt-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8655380 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/47145978ly1fb9znyzqzej21jk113hdt-450x300.jpg" alt="47145978ly1fb9znyzqzej21jk113hdt" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8655380" class="wp-caption-text">李冰冰带小外甥练瑜伽，挑战高难度动作。（李冰冰微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李冰冰六岁的小外甥是妹妹李雪的儿子。据陆媒报导，李冰冰出道之初，李雪便开始担任她的经纪人，李冰冰取得的所有成就离不开妹妹的帮助。李冰冰拍戏之初，因经验少，不敢承认自己是专业演员，李雪便鼓励她：“干一行就要爱一行、精通一行，虽然万事开头难，但自信心绝对不可少。”由于姐妹扶持，李冰冰逐渐步入事业佳境。</p>
<figure id="attachment_5728086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5728086" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405151713591693.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5728086 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/05/1405151713591693-300x400.jpg" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5728086" class="wp-caption-text">李冰冰姐妹合影。（李冰冰微博）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而李冰冰也非常疼爱小外甥，曾表示他是自己的精神食粮。2014年，李雪又生下女儿，在外甥女周岁宴上，李冰冰也豪气放话：“以后外甥女每年过生日都要送她一份嫁妆当礼物。”</p>
