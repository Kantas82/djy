<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>78岁广州女教师 从洗脑班回家后离奇死亡</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月06日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">据明慧网</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">日报导，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">78</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">岁的<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BF%E5%B7%9E.md#1">广州</a>退休小学教师，<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>学员许慧珠女士，一个人独居，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2016</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月下旬再次被发现失踪，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">8</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月上旬被发现在家中已离世多日。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">经多方打听，这次“失踪”，许慧珠仍然是被中共绑架到黄埔<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%97%E8%84%91%E7%8F%AD.md#1">洗脑班</a>，该洗脑班是由中共专职迫害<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.md#1">法轮功</a>的非法机构“</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">610</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">办公室”操控，对外称“黄埔区<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%80%9D%E6%83%B3%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E7%8F%AD.md#1">思想教育学习班</a>”。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">报导说，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2016</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">11</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月，天河区法轮功学员武扬珍女士因为起诉江泽民被绑架到黄埔<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%97%E8%84%91%E7%8F%AD.md#1">洗脑班</a>，洗脑班人员徐少奇指着一张床，十分得意嚣张地说：许慧珠当时睡的就是这张床，她从这里回家几天就死了。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">72</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">岁的武扬珍女士，遭黄埔洗脑班人员徐少奇连续</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">小时罚站和捆绑导致眼睛失明。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">此前，原<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BF%E5%B7%9E.md#1">广州</a>师范学院（现合并为广州大学）的数学系副教授，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">41</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">岁的法轮功学员李晓今女士被黄埔洗脑班迫害致死，当时她被绑架至此黑窝还不到</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">24</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">小时。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">许慧珠在黄埔洗脑班受到了什么样的暴力酷刑导致她离世，还有待进一步核实。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">家住广州市天河区六运二街小区的许慧珠，因为坚持修炼法轮功，在过去十几年中，曾多次被绑架和非法拘禁。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">1996</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年夏天，许慧珠在广州天河体育中心炼功点开始修炼法轮功。广州的夏天很炎热，但当时许慧珠怕冷，穿着厚衣服和棉鞋，说脚很痛。修炼法轮功后，许慧珠的身体很快恢复健康。她家住在六楼，没有电梯，许慧珠说她感觉上楼很轻松。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">1999</span><span lang="ZH-TW"><span style="color: #000000;">年中共开始迫害法轮功以后，许慧珠也多次遭到绑架。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2001</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年，许慧珠被绑架到恶名昭著的广州黄埔洗脑班。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2004</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月中旬，许慧珠因发放法轮功真相资料，被天河南派出所绑架。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2012</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">9</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月中旬，许慧珠被发现失踪，亲属前去探望，才发现家中被抄，私人物品如电脑、印表机等被非法抄走。后来得知许慧珠被非法拘禁在广州市洗脑班。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2015</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月，许慧珠以自己修炼法轮功后亲身受益，以及因为坚持信仰而多次被迫害的事实向最高检察院和最高法院邮寄了《刑事控告书》，起诉迫害法轮功的元凶江泽民。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2015</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">9</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">日上午，许慧珠被天河南派出所骗去办医保卡，被暴力绑架到黄埔洗脑班。许慧珠的手臂被掐青了，绑架者用有水的矿泉水瓶子打她，还威胁她说不签字就要活摘她的器官。随后许慧珠被非法抄家。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2015</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">日，许惠珠被放回家，她被折磨得消瘦了很多，每天还有三个人跟踪她。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2016</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年新年过后，许慧珠又被天河南派出所绑架到黄埔洗脑班，被迫害得出现严重高血压症状。</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月，许慧珠从洗脑班出来。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">因为起诉江泽民，</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2016</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月下旬，许慧珠再次被非法抓捕，劫持到黄埔洗脑班。</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">8</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月初，许慧珠被发现在家中已离世多日。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">从1999</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">20</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">日开始至今</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">，前中共党魁江泽民非法发动和维持了对法轮功学员群体灭绝性的迫害，给上亿法轮功修炼者和他们的家人带来巨大苦难。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">2015</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">年</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">月以来，已有</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Calibri;">20</span><span lang="ZH-CN"><span style="color: #000000;">多万法轮功修炼者和家人实名将控告元凶江泽民的刑事控告状邮寄给中国最高检察院，要求最高检察院向最高法院对江泽民提出公诉，将其绳之以法。#</span></span></p>
