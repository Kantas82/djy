<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大马旅美联谊会捞鱼生庆新年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/940c8fb9685c062a30a4e46b8512df24-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>马来西亚旅美联谊会用大马华人过年传统——捞鱼生，庆祝新年，期盼新的一年风生水起。 (林丹/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者林丹纽约报导）<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%9D%A5%E8%A5%BF%E4%BA%9A.md#1">马来西亚</a>（<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E7%BE%8E.md#1">旅美</a>）联谊会年初二（1月29日）晚在法拉盛举行“捞<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E7%94%9F.md#1">鱼生</a>”晚宴，用马来西亚华人<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D.md#1">庆祝</a><ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E5%8E%86%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md#1">黄历新年</a>的传统方式——“捞<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E7%94%9F.md#1">鱼生</a>”，<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E7%A5%9D.md#1">庆祝</a>新年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8759932" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8759932" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/112243-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8759932" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/112243-450x338.jpg" alt="大马旅美联谊会在法拉盛庆鸡年新年。（林丹/大纪元）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8759932" class="wp-caption-text">大马<ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E7%BE%8E.md#1">旅美</a>联谊会在法拉盛庆鸡年新年。（林丹/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>董事局主席邦君雄表示，“捞鱼生”寓意“风生水起”，今年是鸡年，金鸡报喜，祝福大家平安顺利，大吉大利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8759935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8759935" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/112244-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8759935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/112244-450x338.jpg" alt="大马旅美联谊会董事局主席与大马驻美大使祝大马侨胞新年快乐。（林丹/大纪元）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8759935" class="wp-caption-text">大马旅美联谊会董事局主席与大马驻美大使祝大马侨胞新年快乐。（林丹/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/grvkwy343/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E6%9D%A5%E8%A5%BF%E4%BA%9A.md#1">马来西亚</a>驻美大使Zulhasnan Rafique、副大使、纽约总领事馆正副总领事及属下政府机构专员、马来西亚驻联合国代表团正副大使及驻联合国各机构官员等出席，恭祝大家新年快乐。◇</p>
