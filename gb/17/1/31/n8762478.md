<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2017经典汽车展：看车看人看历史</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/1-11-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>现场经典汽车展品。（杨阳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月31日讯】（大纪元记者李子文<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>报导）<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6.md#1">经典汽车</a>以拥有优良设计、高工艺制作标准，区别于现代低成本汽车而受到人们的喜爱。1月27日，<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>首届<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">经典汽车展</a>在市中心开幕，为民众带来复古的独特享受。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8762516" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762516" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/3-9-1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/3-9-1-450x253.jpg" alt="宽敞的会展大厅。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762516" class="wp-caption-text">宽敞的会展大厅。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8762496" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/2-10-1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/2-10-1-450x253.jpg" alt="现场经典汽车展品。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762496" class="wp-caption-text">现场<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6.md#1">经典汽车</a>展品。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8762505" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762505" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/7-1-4-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762505" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/7-1-4-450x253.jpg" alt="现场经典汽车展品。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762505" class="wp-caption-text">现场<ahref="https://github.com/hbjzbb377/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B8%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">经典汽车展</a>品。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-8762507" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/8-1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="8-1" width="450" b="253" /></p>
<p>负责组织本次展览的副主席 Mike Carlucci 介绍说，展览汇集了来自全美的经典汽车收藏单位，“超过55个汽车俱乐部，约三百个展览的商家，共计约有六百辆车展出”。他表示，经典汽车展之前一直在英国举办，此次在洛杉矶举办还是第一回。为期三日的展览，准备阶段长达6个月。大部分展品来自南加，外形闪亮华丽、设计高雅的经典车，让来往的游客目不暇接。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8762530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762530" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5-2-1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/5-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="里根总统的吉普车。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762530" class="wp-caption-text">里根总统的吉普车。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8762534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/6-1-3-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/6-1-3-450x253.jpg" alt="里根总统的吉普车。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762534" class="wp-caption-text">里根总统的吉普车。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于老式汽车爱好者来说，展览还不止这些。Carlucci 表示，现场还有关于经典汽车的讲座，并且还可“与业界名人见面，如Chip、Wayne、Mike Brewer、Dave Kindig、Mike Phillips、 Chris Jacob”，“主要是为了让南加的车迷与业界精英有更多沟通”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8762521" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8762521" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/4-6-1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8762521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/4-6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="洛杉矶经典汽车展副主席 Mike Carlucci。（杨阳／大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8762521" class="wp-caption-text">洛杉矶经典汽车展副主席 Mike Carlucci。（杨阳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展方表示，老式轿车的收藏、投资市场越来越受关注，洛杉矶经典车展就是获取相关资讯的最佳地点。Carlucci说，会展能够“给游客关于车子的知识，比如哪些车越来越火，哪些会是未来趋势，什么样的车可以被翻新，车子从哪里来”。来自Petersen汽车博物馆的讲解员 Peter Homen 解释：“如果你买件儿艺术品，只能挂在墙上欣赏，但车的话你可以使用并乐在其中，我认为这对古董车车主来说，是最大的享受。”◇</p>
