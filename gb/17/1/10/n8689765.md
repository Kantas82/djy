<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>拉丁范儿的西班牙菜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Shrimp-Cocktail-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲜虾鸡尾酒Shrimp Cocktail。（张学慧／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年01月11日讯】（文／ 谢云良）在亚裔群聚的<ahref="https://github.com/wfhser349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>，少有西班牙风味的<ahref="https://github.com/wfhser349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%94%E5%B8%95%E6%96%AF%EF%BC%88tapas%EF%BC%89%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">塔帕斯（Tapas）餐馆</a>，Sangria Tapas &amp; Wine 就是其中之一，老板是西班牙和意大利混血的两兄弟。此家号称是“拉丁范儿的<ahref="https://github.com/wfhser349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西班牙菜</a>”，除西班牙菜之外，揉合了多米尼加、秘鲁和墨西哥等拉丁风味的菜肴。</p>
<p>塔帕斯顾名思义是“小盘餐点”，多为开胃菜，种类丰富，冷热兼有。在西方国家，尤其西班牙，塔帕斯是一种非常流行的就餐形式。有些塔帕斯餐馆里，食客可以站立就餐，或在进食时四处走动，为的是方便聊天。丰富多样的小盘餐点，也方便尝试多种口味。</p>
<h3>拉丁范儿的<ahref="https://github.com/wfhser349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西班牙菜</a></h3>
<p>除了好味的西班牙海鲜炖饭之外（Paella de Marisco， $25），Sangria还有来自拉丁美洲的经典菜肴。不过每道菜推出之前，来自萨尔瓦多的大厨都会略施小技，把传统菜的口味做的更有新意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8693363" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693363" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/9523d6c19c0cd4b44a608f5ccd879028-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/9523d6c19c0cd4b44a608f5ccd879028-600x400.jpg" alt="西班牙海鲜炖饭Paella de Marisco。（张学慧／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693363" class="wp-caption-text">西班牙海鲜炖饭Paella de Marisco。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Pulled pork Tostones （article content begin -->1.95）是一道开胃小吃。金黄的炸大蕉片上垫着满满的手撕猪肉，配有浅绿的鳄梨酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8693358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/57eafd3170f964c9e189e712d75c6613-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693358" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/57eafd3170f964c9e189e712d75c6613-600x900.jpg" alt="Pulled pork Tostones。（张学慧／大纪元）" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693358" class="wp-caption-text">Pulled pork Tostones。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，此“大蕉”（plantain）非彼“香蕉”（banana）。大蕉是香蕉的一种，甜度低，富含纤维，中美洲料理中常用作配菜。多表皮青绿时食用。炸大蕉片则口感外脆内绵，带有些微的甜味和香气。</p>
<p>Shrimp mofonguitos （article content begin -->2.95）也是大蕉为原料的波多黎各经典菜。大蕉去皮、浸盐水，煎炸、磨碎，入蒜头和橄榄油，再加一点酱汁，用手捏压成小碗的形状，内置嫩煎的大虾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8693359" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693359" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/b5e3afbd714377badb3a3cf996dfbf3d-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/b5e3afbd714377badb3a3cf996dfbf3d-600x900.jpg" alt="Shrimp Mofonguitos。（张学慧／大纪元） " width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693359" class="wp-caption-text">Shrimp Mofonguitos。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一道不为中国人熟知的应是Ceviche Mixto（ article content begin -->6.95 ），中文大致可译为柠汁腌鱼生。中美洲多国都有Ceviche，各国有不同版本。这道菜手法独出一格，采用青柠汁和橄榄油将海鲜腌熟。腌鱼生热量低，也能突出食材的原汁原味。虽然看似简单，但用料要均衡，才能衬托出这道菜层次丰富的口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8693364" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693364" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/470baad86aee5a337c59755002acc0bb-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/470baad86aee5a337c59755002acc0bb-600x400.jpg" alt="柠汁腌鱼生 Ceviche Mixto （张学慧／大纪元） " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693364" class="wp-caption-text">柠汁腌鱼生 Ceviche Mixto （张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>下班之后来一杯</h3>
<p>店名“Sangria” 是西班牙流行的果味鸡尾酒。把不同水果切入鸡尾酒中，浸泡过后，水果的香甜味道释入酒中，喝起来清凉、又兼水果香气，如饮料一样容易入口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8693362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693362" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/eaf78f9b35fc2fa99c2547da479be0cc-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/eaf78f9b35fc2fa99c2547da479be0cc-600x400.jpg" alt="Shrimp Cocktail和粉红的Sangria果酒。（张学慧／大纪元） " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693362" class="wp-caption-text">Shrimp Cocktail和粉红的Sangria果酒。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">除此之外，吧台后面整齐摆着五颜六色的酒种。如著名的墨西哥传统鸡尾酒Michelada。常见作法是将西班牙啤酒调入Clamato Juice（混合蕃茄汁和蛤汁）、柠檬汁以及辣酱。还有至少三十种玛格丽塔（Margarita），一种龙舌兰酒为基底的鸡尾酒。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8693360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693360" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f49cf1e489882d07854ad7c7656e39f4-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f49cf1e489882d07854ad7c7656e39f4-600x400.jpg" alt="Caribbean style chimmi sliders。（张学慧／大纪元） " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693360" class="wp-caption-text">Caribbean style chimmi sliders。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8693365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8693365" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/e6ebd790435a916b95a1e648fa949ee7-600x900.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-8693365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/e6ebd790435a916b95a1e648fa949ee7-600x900.jpg" alt="Guacamole。（张学慧／大纪元） " width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8693365" class="wp-caption-text">Guacamole。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
