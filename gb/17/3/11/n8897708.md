<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚洲文化节传统再现 法轮功应邀展功法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/950e6e1d61c8e411775026e98dc2656b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>法轮功学员应邀在亚洲文化节上展示了舒缓优美的五套功法。（李明杰/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年03月11日讯】(大纪元记者李明杰迈阿密报导)一年一度的亚洲文化节于2017年3月4日和5日在迈阿密戴德郡南部的家园市（Homestead）果味公园（Fruit and Spice Park）举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8897830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8897830" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Yuwadee-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8897830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/Yuwadee-450x300.jpg" alt="泰美协会（Thai American Association of Florida） 负责人Yuwadee Wongbundhit女士感谢南佛州法轮大法 跟着练习，她说：“法轮功让我感觉祥和，平静。” （李明杰/大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8897830" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州泰美协会负责人Yuwadee Wongbundhit女士说：“法轮功让人感觉祥和，平静。” （李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚洲文化节由佛州泰美协会（Thai American Association of Florida）主办，戴德郡亚裔顾问委员会协办。不仅有优美的自然田园风景和泰国美食，还有亚洲各国文化摊位和各种娱乐活动，为南佛州游客们带来难得的亚洲风情。泰美协会南佛州负责人Yuwadee Wongbundhit女士感谢法轮功学员为亚洲文化节带来中国传统修炼，法轮功学员在台上展示功法时，她一直跟着练，她说：“法轮功让人感觉祥和，平静。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8897781" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8897781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/ACF06-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8897781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/ACF06-450x300.jpg" alt="亚洲文化节传统再现 法轮功" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8897781" class="wp-caption-text">亚洲文化节上法轮功学员应邀上台展示第五套功法。（李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>亚洲文化表演汇集了各个国家的文化亮点，中国的传统舞狮和功夫表演热闹非凡，激起了观众们的极大热情；泰国传统舞蹈古朴大方，非常具有泰国宫廷特点；日本太鼓则热力十足，鼓声震天；还有印尼民间舞蹈和印度传统舞蹈表演。同时还有法轮功功法展示和太极拳表演等。文化节还设置了亚洲各国文化展位，有泰国，日本，韩国和越南等国以及台湾的文化展示摊位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8898104" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8898104" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/a8c0b672c70781b67e93c8fad64c0db4-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8898104" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/a8c0b672c70781b67e93c8fad64c0db4-450x300.jpg" alt="泰国传统表演。（李明杰/大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8898104" class="wp-caption-text">泰国传统表演。（李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8898105" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8898105" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/3bb44c923b406516914f869ff0d7029d-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8898105" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/3bb44c923b406516914f869ff0d7029d-450x300.jpg" alt="泰国宫廷舞蹈表演。（李明杰/大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8898105" class="wp-caption-text">古朴典雅泰国宫廷舞蹈表演。（李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为代表中国传统修炼气功文化的法轮功也参加了表演，法轮功学员应邀上台展示了舒缓优美的五套功法，其中包括四套动功和一套打坐，对祛病健身有奇效。一些观众在台下学着比划动作，一些向学员索要法轮功资料。功法展示完后获得了观众热烈的掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8897790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8897790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/BOOTH02-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8897790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/BOOTH02-450x300.jpg" alt="游客们饶有兴趣地驻足了解法轮功的情况，学习法轮功功法。（）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8897790" class="wp-caption-text">游客们饶有兴趣地驻足了解法轮功的情况，学习法轮功功法。（李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-8897793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/47672bacbe89f2d0f0531952ce8d6f7e-450x300.jpg" alt="摊位" width="450" b="300" /></p>
<p>在法轮功展位上，很多感兴趣的游客饶有兴趣地驻足了解法轮功的情况，有些游客马上同法轮功学员学习法轮功功法，一些人表示以后每周都去法轮功炼功点炼功。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8898067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8898067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/ACF01-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8898067" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/ACF01-450x300.jpg" alt="南佛州泰美协会负责人Yuwadee Wongbundhit女士和法轮功学员合影。（李明杰/大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8898067" class="wp-caption-text">南佛州泰美协会负责人Yuwadee Wongbundhit女士和法轮功学员合影。（李明杰/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>文化节还有很多亚洲美食摊位，泰国金边炒粉，泰国竹筒饭、越南汤粉、泰国烤猪排，各种口味的春卷等等，游客们品尝各种美食，食欲大开。</p>
