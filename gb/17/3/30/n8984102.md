<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>古早童玩比3C好玩  大湖国小欢庆儿童节</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253966_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大家来滚铁圈。（谢月琴／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年03月30日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）大湖国小于3月30日<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>前夕，发挥创意举办儿童节欢庆活动－<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E6%97%A9.md#1">古早</a><ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E7%8E%A9.md#1">童玩</a>体验，除了让小朋友体验古早童玩的乐趣外，也让阿公阿嬷和爸爸妈妈重温儿时旧梦！小朋友还兴奋的说：古早童玩比3C产品还好玩，叭噗冰淇淋一级棒！</p>
<p>大湖国小邀请传统<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E7%8E%A9.md#1">童玩</a>推广老师林永生老师，带着农业社会的传统童玩玩具，到校指导学童共同游戏与体验。包括滚铁环、铁罐踩高跷、玲珑车、波浪鼓、弹珠台、木枪、竹蜻蜓、竹蝉、小沙包、九连环、拉泡泡、五色抽签筒、拉泡泡等27种自制的<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%A4%E6%97%A9.md#1">古早</a>童玩游戏。林永生老师今年更善用纸杯、宝特瓶、筷子等，做成童玩，也让小朋友学习如何将环保素材再利用。还邀请阿公阿嬷和爸爸妈妈到校一起同乐，重温儿时的童玩游戏，并品尝古早零嘴叭噗冰淇淋、古早麦芽饼等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8984244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8984244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253967_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8984244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253967_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="小朋友开心吃 叭噗冰淇淋。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8984244" class="wp-caption-text">小朋友开心吃 叭噗冰淇淋。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大湖国小林清辉校长表示，为了让学童拥有一个特别的<ahref="https://github.com/bsmkec3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E8%8A%82.md#1">儿童节</a>，也响应县府“走出户外、远离3C”的呼吁，在当日上午上下课时间互换，即每节下课延长为40分钟，上课仅十分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8984245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8984245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253968_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8984245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253968_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="环保素材再利用制成的传统玩具。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8984245" class="wp-caption-text">环保素材再利用制成的传统玩具。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大湖国小已连续第三年举办此活动，因为年代关系，孩子并不熟悉这些古早童玩，他们就熟悉的是3C产品，但这很伤眼睛很容易入迷。而传统的童玩具，得手工自制走出户外，也可亲子同乐，体验古早童玩的乐趣，玩得尽兴，过一个欢乐且特别的儿童节。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8984246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8984246" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253981_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8984246" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253981_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="孩子体验各种古早童玩。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8984246" class="wp-caption-text">孩子体验各种古早童玩。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“好期待呢，我昨天晚上兴奋得差点睡不着觉，早上5点就爬起来了！”小朋友开心的大吃叭噗如此说着。有男同学甚至还说，古早童玩比3C产品还好玩，叭噗冰淇淋一级棒！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8984247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8984247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253982_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8984247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253982_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="孩子边吃边玩真开心。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8984247" class="wp-caption-text">孩子边吃边玩真开心。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8984248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8984248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253983_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-8984248" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/253983_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="林永生老师与小朋互动热络。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8984248" class="wp-caption-text">林永生老师与小朋互动热络。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家长邵美玲说，学校办这样活动很好，可让孩子远离3C产品，体验到古早味童玩的乐趣。</p>
