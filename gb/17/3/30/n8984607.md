<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：令人垂涎的20种世界美食</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/top-fish-chips-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>炸鱼薯条是一道源自英国的热食，鱼裹上面糊油炸，搭配炸薯条，吃的时候还会配上不同口味的调味酱，是著名的英国小吃。(Kariwiil/CC/Pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年04月14日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>永远是我们生活的主旋律之一，许多时候，到一个地方去旅行的原因可能就是因为美食，韩国的烧肉、西班牙<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>饭、越南河粉，不同地方都有令人再三回味的<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>。让我们来看看，到底是怎样的食材，能收服世界最挑剔的味蕾？</p>
<p><strong>1. 日本?章鱼烧（</strong><strong>Takoyaki</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>章鱼烧，直译为“烤章鱼丸子”，是起源于日本关西的料理，作法是在生面团里加入章鱼脚烤成约3至5公分大小的丸子。主要是作为非正餐的点心，在大阪是用牙签叉来吃，而东京是用筷子夹，在早期则是用竹串以三个一串的方式处理的。烤完后再加上柴鱼片、大葱、海苔末或美乃滋，风味绝佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033211" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/20-Takoyaki-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033211 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/20-Takoyaki-450x300.jpg" alt="日本随处可见的章鱼烧。（NICK YEH/ CC / Pixabay )" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033211" class="wp-caption-text">日本随处可见的章鱼烧。（NICK YEH/ CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 韩国?烧肉（</strong><strong>Bulgogi</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>韩国烧烤，是朝鲜族的传统烤肉方式，常用生铁锅、生铁板、铜板、铜锅等材质，通常在吃烤肉时，食材多以腌渍的肉类与<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>为主，有时佐以累积酱料与蔬菜汤汁和在一起烤食，湿烤与干烤都很美味。而肉类又以牛肉为主，此外，牛里脊、牛排、牛舌、牛腰、还有海鲜、生鱼片等都是韩国烧烤的美味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/6-korean-pork-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033245 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/6-korean-pork-450x300.jpg" alt="韩国烧肉。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033245" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E7%83%A7%E8%82%89.md#1">韩国烧肉</a>。（SharonNang / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 泰国?冬荫（</strong><strong>Tom Yum Soup</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>冬荫，也称为泰式酸辣汤，港澳人士多称为冬荫功，是一道著名的泰国酸辣汤，由南姜、酸子、香茅、青葱、酸柠檬、辣椒和鱼露调制而成。泰国是全球产虾最多的地方，所以冬荫功汤的主料便是虾，另外加入大量菌类，较多的是草菇或是金针菇。再加入柠檬草、九层塔、薄荷叶、朝天椒、椰浆、鱼露、咖喱等佐料，令味道更多层次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033270" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033270" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/9-Tom-Yum-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033270 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/9-Tom-Yum-450x300.jpg" alt="泰国冬荫，也称为泰式酸辣汤。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033270" class="wp-caption-text">泰国冬荫，也称为泰式酸辣汤。（Markus Tinner / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 越南?河粉（</strong><strong>Pho</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>越南河粉被视为越南菜的代表菜色。一碗典型的汤河粉可以从四部分：汤底、河粉、牛肉和配菜来评价。其中，汤底的好坏关乎整碗的成败。牛骨头熬成的汤味道浓郁，但颜色却是清浅的褐色，不油腻不厚重，除了牛骨之外，还会加进各种香料、调味品，每家有每家的秘方，所有的美味玄机都深藏在此。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033275" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033275" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/10-pho-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033275 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/10-pho-450x338.jpg" alt="越南河粉。（创用图片）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033275" class="wp-caption-text">越南河粉。（Bill Kasman / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5. 泰国?青木瓜沙拉（</strong><strong>Green Papaya Salad</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>除了冬荫功之外的泰国名菜一定要数青木瓜沙拉。尤其在闷热的天气，品尝这道酸辣中再带点木瓜特有的清甜味，简直是一种享受。整道菜香辣带酸，口感爽脆，入喉那股柠檬的清新果香，酸、辣、甜、咸、香，让味蕾应接不暇，十分开胃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033297" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/16-Green-Papaya-Salad-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033297 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/16-Green-Papaya-Salad-450x300.jpg" alt="泰国青木瓜沙拉。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033297" class="wp-caption-text">泰国青木瓜沙拉。（Wow Phochiangrak / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 马来西亚?叻沙（</strong><strong>Laksa</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>叻沙又称喇沙，是一道起源于南洋的面食料理，为马来西亚和新加坡的代表性料理。叻沙有非常多不同的种类，一般马来西亚和新加坡华人所指的叻沙多为咖喱叻沙（Curry Laksa）或亚参叻沙（Asam Laksa）。其中亚参叻沙在2011年被美国有线电视新闻网络（CNN）旗下的旅游网站CNNGO，选为全球50大美食中的第7名，而咖喱叻沙则被赫芬顿邮报（The Huffington Post）评选为死前必尝的世界十大美食之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033289" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033289" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/15-laksa-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033289 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/15-laksa-450x300.jpg" alt="马来西亚的叻沙。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033289" class="wp-caption-text">马来西亚的叻沙。（Sharon Nang / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7. 北京烤鸭（</strong><strong>Beijing Duck</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>北京烤鸭可谓是中国菜里最有名气的菜肴，最令人难以忘记的一道极品。香脆甜腻的鸭皮、多汁鲜嫩的鸭肉，配以烤香的面饼、爽辣的大葱还有浓浓的甜面酱，鸭肉以果木炭火烤制，色泽红润，肉质肥而不腻，让人一试难忘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033322" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033322" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/17-paking-duck-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033322 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/17-paking-duck-450x300.jpg" alt="北京烤鸭。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033322" class="wp-caption-text">北京烤鸭。（Nipapun Jiranukul / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>8. 新加坡?辣椒螃蟹（</strong><strong>Chili Crab</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>辣椒螃蟹，新加坡著名菜色，在所有蟹类料理中独占鳌头。烹调法是将泥蟹混以番茄及辣椒制成的酱汁一起爆炒，尽管叫做辣椒蟹，它其实并非特别辛辣。此菜色于2011年被美国有线新闻网旗下网站CNNGO评定为全球50大最美味菜色的第35位。新加坡人喜爱以馒头蘸着吃，口感滋味妙不可言。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/19-Chilli-crab-1-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033331 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/19-Chilli-crab-1-450x300.jpg" alt="新加坡辣椒螃蟹。（megawatts86/ CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033331" class="wp-caption-text">新加坡辣椒螃蟹。（megawatts86/ CC / Wikipedia )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>9. 加拿大?起司浇肉汁马铃薯条（</strong><strong>Poutine</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>肉汁干酪薯条是加拿大的特色快餐，源于魁北克，做法是在炸薯条条上加干酪块，然后浇上肉汁或肉卤。有时候还会加入火腿，香肠或者鲑鱼，通常用作配餐。现在加拿大全境和美国北部都有售。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033205" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/4-Poutine-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033205 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/4-Poutine-450x300.jpg" alt="来自加拿大魁北克的肉汁干酪薯条。（创用图片／维基百科）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033205" class="wp-caption-text">来自加拿大魁北克的肉汁干酪薯条。（Yuri Long / CC / Wikipedia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>10. 澳洲?肉派（</strong><strong>Meat Pie</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>犹如美国人爱吃汉堡，日本人爱吃寿司一样，澳洲人最爱的美食及小吃就是肉派。这种源自于英国的便利美食在澳洲随处可见，不管是在餐厅，便利商店，咖啡厅，面包店，超市，肉派专卖店，体育馆，甚至是超商或杂货店都有贩售。由于其便利性，澳洲人在任何时间都吃，包括早餐，早午餐，正餐，下午茶，点心及消夜等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033179" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/1-meatpie-450x269.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033179 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/1-meatpie-450x269.jpg" alt="肉派是澳洲人最爱的小吃。（创用图片）" width="450" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033179" class="wp-caption-text">肉派是澳洲人最爱的小吃。（Brett_Hondow / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>11. 奥地利?</strong><strong>苹果卷</strong><strong>（</strong><strong>Apfelstrudel</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>维也纳苹果卷是一种流行在奥地利、德国和捷克的点心，以苹果和果馅卷为原料。维也纳苹果卷是奥地利最具代表性的点心之一，但却起源于匈牙利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033192" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2-Apfelstrudel-450x346.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033192 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2-Apfelstrudel-450x346.jpg" alt="奥地利苹果卷。（创用图片）" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033192" class="wp-caption-text">奥地利苹果卷。（pixel1 / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>12. 比利时?牡蛎薯条（</strong><strong>Moules-Frites</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>比利时的一道名菜，除了在比利时之外，也在法国和北欧各国流行。牡蛎薯条被认为是比利时的国菜。最早记载有牡蛎薯条的食谱是在1781年。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/3-Moules_Frites-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033203 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/3-Moules_Frites-450x338.jpg" alt="比利时牡蛎薯条。（创用图片／维基百科）" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033203" class="wp-caption-text">比利时牡蛎薯条。（Aimee Cardwell / CC / Wikipedia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>13. 法国?<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%8D%A1%E9%BE%99.md#1">马卡龙</a>（</strong><strong>Macaron</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%8D%A1%E9%BE%99.md#1">马卡龙</a>，又称作玛卡龙、法式小圆饼，是一种用蛋白或糖粉、蔗糖、杏仁粉或杏仁霜以及食用色素制成的法式甜点，通常在两块饼干之间夹有奶油乳酪或果酱等内馅。名称源于意大利语单词macarone、maccarone或maccherone，一种意大利蛋白脆饼。这种甜点的特点是：细腻、方顶、圆形并且圆周有褶边，湿度适中，入口即化。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033234" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033234" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/5-macaron-300x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033234 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/5-macaron-300x300.jpg" alt="色彩缤纷，令人垂涎欲滴的马卡龙。（创用图片）" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033234" class="wp-caption-text">色彩缤纷，令人垂涎欲滴的马卡龙。（Karki / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>14. 法国?可丽饼（</strong><strong>Crepes</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>这种薄饼起源于法国布列塔尼，但现在法国境内外随处可见。他们在大街上制作，在工人和游客之中很受欢迎，通常涂着花生酱、果酱或香蕉、巧克力和奶油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033286" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033286" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/14-crepe-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033286 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/14-crepe-450x253.jpg" alt="法国随处可见的可丽饼。（创用图片）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033286" class="wp-caption-text">法国随处可见的可丽饼。（Peter Douglas / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>15. 西班牙?海鲜饭</strong> <strong>（</strong><strong>Seafood Paella</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>西班牙海鲜饭是和法国蜗牛、意大利面齐名的欧洲名菜。西班牙海鲜饭源于西班牙鱼米之都瓦伦西亚。西班牙海鲜饭卖相绝佳，黄澄澄的饭粒藏住藏红花的尊贵，饭中点缀着无数大虾、螃蟹、黑蚬、青口、鱿鱼等海鲜，热气腾腾，令人垂涎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033246" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7-Seafood-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033246 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7-Seafood-450x299.jpg" alt="西班牙海鲜饭。（创用图片）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033246" class="wp-caption-text">西班牙海鲜饭。（Juan Roman / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>16. 英国?炸鱼薯条（</strong><strong>Fish and Chips</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>炸鱼薯条是英式餐点中极具代表性的食物，将去骨的鱼切成片后裹上湿面团，然后油炸制成炸鱼。配上炸薯条，吃的时候还会配上不同口味的调味酱，是英国最经典的街道小食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033262" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033262" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/8-fish-and-chips-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033262 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/8-fish-and-chips-450x300.jpg" alt="英式餐点炸鱼薯条。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033262" class="wp-caption-text">英式餐点炸鱼薯条。（Tommy pixel / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>17. 墨西哥卷（</strong><strong>Tacos</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>Taco是一种墨西哥风味美食，以其制作简单和多元化口味的特点深受欧美国家的欢迎。可根据自己的喜好加入鸡肉或是牛肉，然后再加入蕃茄、生菜丝、芝士等配料，颜色丰富，令人食欲大增。放入嘴中一咬，外面松脆，而里面却有香、辣、酸、甜各味俱全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033276" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/11-taco-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033276 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/11-taco-450x300.jpg" alt="墨西哥卷饼。（创用图片）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033276" class="wp-caption-text">墨西哥卷饼。（Stina Magnus / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>18. 意大利?拿坡里薄饼（</strong><strong>Neapolitan Pizza</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>Pizza是意大利人最骄傲的美食。而Pizza 的故乡则在拿坡里。拿坡里是意大利南部的第一大城市，在当地最好的餐厅享用正统的拿坡里Pizza，虽然要排队，但总是值得期待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033282" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/12-pizza-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033282 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/12-pizza-450x301.jpg" alt="意大利拿坡里披萨。（创用图片）" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033282" class="wp-caption-text">意大利拿坡里<ahref="https://github.com/wkflyt344/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%AB%E8%90%A8.md#1">披萨</a>。（CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>?19. 意大利?</strong><strong>千层面</strong><strong>（</strong><strong>Lasagna</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>千层面是意大利非常有名面食，特点是用多张宽如手帕的大面皮叠起来，内层夹上多种乳酪及肉酱， 再加入盐、奶油、蕃茄、芝士，尽管每层都是铺进了一样的馅料，可是吃每层的感觉却都不一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9033285" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9033285" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/13-lasagna-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9033285 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/13-lasagna-450x301.jpg" alt="意大利千层面。（创用图片）" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9033285" class="wp-caption-text">意大利千层面。（Kropekk_pl / CC / Pixabay )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>20. 希腊?肉酱茄子（</strong><strong>Moussaka</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p>Moussaka是一道希腊名菜，烹调时将碎羊肉、茄子及蕃茄层层叠上，加上酥皮与奶酪烘烤而成。吃起来有点像意大利式的千层面，但又没有千层味的油腻，是一道令人垂涎的主菜。烤肉端上桌来，希腊人的吃法很特别，必须把这个肉夹在面包里面一起吃。</p>
