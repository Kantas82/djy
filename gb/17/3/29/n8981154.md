<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【折纸】企鹅</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00436-e1490812766418.jpg" />
<h6>(图/ 张芯兰 提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>企鹅在动物园里是最吸引目光的主角之一，不论大人、小孩都喜欢它摇摇摆摆的走路模样。今天，我们就用各种颜色的色纸来折可爱的纸企鹅吧！</p>
<p><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>双面不同色彩花纹的包装纸或是色纸都可以，美工刀、直尺、剪刀、白胶或双面胶带、无墨水的原子笔。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>一、将纸张的对应角相叠合、对折，摊开，留下折线甲，取出纸张的中心线。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981181 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00409-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00409" width="360" b="270" /></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981182 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00410-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00410" width="361" b="271" /></p>
<p>二、将上图中左、右二边的角分别往中央线翻折、对齐，如下图。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981184 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00411-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00411" width="361" b="271" /></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981185 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00412-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00412" width="361" b="271" /></p>
<p>三、再将两角自甲线处分别往外侧翻出约一公分宽后折下。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981186 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00415-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00415" width="361" b="271" /></p>
<p>四、将图6中的上端角，在距顶点约4公分处，将其往下方翻折。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981188 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00417-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00417" width="359" b="270" /></p>
<p>五、将上端的角在距折线约半公分宽处，再反向翻折回去，如下图。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981189 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00418-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00418" width="359" b="270" /></p>
<p>六、将下方的角在距下方顶点约3公分处，往后翻折。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8981191" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8981191" style="width: 359px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00421-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8981191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00421-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00421" width="359" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8981191" class="wp-caption-text">正面图示</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8981192" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8981192" style="width: 359px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00420-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8981192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00420-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00420" width="359" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8981192" class="wp-caption-text">背面图示</figcaption></figure>
<p>七、运用折线甲将企鹅身体两侧往背部（即后方）折叠、放开，这样企鹅就可以站立。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981196 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00422-450x500.jpg" alt="DSC00422" width="295" b="328" /></p>
<p>八、将上端贴着头部折下，成三角状，再打开，从背部依折痕翻折，使企鹅头部往下立体起来。如下图。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981199 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00423-450x370.jpg" alt="DSC00423" width="360" b="296" /></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981200 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00424-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00424" width="361" b="271" /></p>
<p>九、将动作3所翻折出来的小三角形，也就是企鹅的两翅（上肢），分别将外侧的底角再向甲线方向翻折出另一个小小的三角形。</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-8981202 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00425-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00425" width="359" b="270" /></p>
<figure id="attachment_8981203" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8981203" style="width: 359px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00426-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8981203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/DSC00426-450x338.jpg" alt="DSC00426" width="359" b="270" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8981203" class="wp-caption-text">企鹅的两翅的小小三角形特写图</figcaption></figure>
<p>十、企鹅家族有心事要相诉，所以再折几只企鹅家的成员，让其一家团员吧！</p>
