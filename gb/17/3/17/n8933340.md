<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>澳洲现发光波浪 炫目如北极光</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年03月17日讯】（大纪元记者弥亚<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>墨尔本编译报导）很难想像<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1.md#1">摄影</a>师会为了拍海藻在破晓时分起床，但这可不是普通的海藻。</p>
<p>这几日的黎明时分，<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%91%84%E5%BD%B1.md#1">摄影</a>师涌向<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A1%94%E6%96%AF%E9%A9%AC%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A.md#1">塔斯马尼亚</a>（Tasmania）西北部的一个<ahref="https://github.com/ydbzqt347/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E6%BB%A9.md#1">海滩</a>， 因为在那里，海洋呈现一片梦幻的萤光蓝色。</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRpCBp0huun/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A790.3650000000001%7D" width="658" b="722" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>据Yahoo7 News报导，这种如北极光般炫目的蓝色来自Preservation Bay的生物发光藻类（bioluminescent algae）。Instagram用户无须担心是否需要完美的过滤器才能拍到好照片，因为温暖的海洋条件创造了水面上天然的闪烁效果。</p>
<p><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRpB4iXh6uL/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1063.5400000000002%7D" width="658" b="722" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRlIRsgB5AJ/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A396.32500000000005%7D" width="658" b="722" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRf0dEYhkTE/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A409.49%7D" width="658" b="722" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>网友们纷纷表示，梦幻般的蓝色波浪令他们激动不已，流连忘返。＃</p>
