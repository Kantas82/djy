<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马克龙首访德国 与默克尔共推欧盟改革</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/GettyImages-683399382-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>5月15日，法国总统马克龙首次外访选择了德国，与德国总理默克尔会晤 。(Axel Schmidt/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年05月17日讯】刚上任的法国总统<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%85%8B%E9%BE%99.md#1">马克龙</a>首次外访选择了德国。他在与德国总理<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%98%E5%85%8B%E5%B0%94.md#1">默克尔</a>会晤后，两人均表示希望给法德关系注入新的活力，并表示在很多议题上更好地合作。双方强调了<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E6%B3%95%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C.md#1">德法合作</a>的重要性，并准备共同推动<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E6%94%B9%E9%9D%A9.md#1">欧盟改革</a>。</p>
<p>法广报导，上周日（14日）刚宣誓上任的法国新总统<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%85%8B%E9%BE%99.md#1">马克龙</a>，展开忙碌的第一周。周一（15日）马克龙宣布了新总理人选为温和右派新秀、朱佩派菲利普。</p>
<p>周一傍晚，马克龙到访柏林，德国总理<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%98%E5%85%8B%E5%B0%94.md#1">默克尔</a>以军礼表示了欢迎。马克龙对受到的热烈欢迎表示感动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9151057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9151057" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/GettyImages-683390390-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9151057 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/GettyImages-683390390-450x300.jpg" alt="BERLIN, GERMANY - MAY 15: German Chancellor Angela Merkel and newly-elected French President Emmanuel Macron listen to the national anthems in the courtyard of the Chancellery on May 15, 2017 in Berlin, Germany. Macron is visiting Berlin only a day after being sworn in as president in Paris. While Macron and Merkel have both demonstrated an unwavering commitment to the European Union and Merkel strongly applauded Macron's election, they are likely to differ over Macron's desire for E.U.-issued bonds, a measure Merkel has strongly opposed in the past. (Photo by Michele Tantussi/Getty Images)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9151057" class="wp-caption-text">马克龙到访柏林，对受到的热烈欢迎表示感动。(Michele Tantussi/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>会晤之后，默克尔和马克龙举行了联合记者招待会。两人均表示要强化欧盟和欧元区，在双方都关心的议题上共同努力。这些议题包括难民申请庇护权、修订欧洲外籍劳工相关规定、统一贸易政策、加强欧元区的融资能力以便在共同的项目中投资。</p>
<p>默克尔许诺，将和巴黎的新伙伴进行“充满信任、友好和密切的”合作。马克龙回应说，他始终愿意是一位“坦率的、不拐弯抹角且有建设性的伙伴”。面对民粹主义的推进，他期望德法关系更务实、更密切、更有具体成果。</p>
<p>马克龙表示，法、德、英国尽快制定一个共同的路线图以便更好地遏制欧洲的民粹主义。他还说，要给为表达愤怒而投了玛丽娜．勒庞一票的选民一个回应。默克尔也表示，法国的大选表明要进一步加强欧盟合作。</p>
<p>双方会晤没有达成具体协议，但推出了共同推进<ahref="https://github.com/disgyf3173/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E6%94%B9%E9%9D%A9.md#1">欧盟改革</a>“行程表”。为此，法国7月立法大选后，两国将举行政府磋商会议，共商欧盟改革大计。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9151071" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9151071" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/GettyImages-683400202-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9151071 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/GettyImages-683400202-450x300.jpg" alt="BERLIN, GERMANY - MAY 15: German Chancellor Angela Merkel and newly-elected French President Emmanuel Macron attend a press conference at the Chancellery on May 15, 2017 in Berlin, Germany. Macron is visiting Berlin only a day after being sworn in as president in Paris. While Macron and Merkel have both demonstrated an unwavering commitment to the European Union and Merkel strongly applauded Macron's election, they are likely to differ over Macron's desire for E.U.-issued bonds, a measure Merkel has strongly opposed in the past. (Photo by Axel Schmidt/Getty Images)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9151071" class="wp-caption-text">默克尔和马克龙举行了联合记者招待会。两人均表示要强化欧洲和欧元区，在双方都关心的议题上共同努力。 (Axel Schmidt/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>马克龙和默克尔表示，为了强化欧元区，不排除改变已不合时宜的欧盟条约，两国愿意修改现行欧盟协议。马克龙强调说：“对我们来说，不存在任何禁区。”</p>
<p>法、德两国领导人还希望在电子商务、税务、国防、国际政策、教育等领域加强合作。但在欧元区金融改革方案上，马克龙表示，不希望设立欧洲债券，这在德国是一个非常敏感的话题。马克龙不赞成联手共同承担欧洲债务。</p>
<p>默克尔表示，希望在6月11日和18日两轮立法选举中允许马克龙实施他的计划。#</p>
