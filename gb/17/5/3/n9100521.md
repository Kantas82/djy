<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>韩国咖啡王子传技 纽约首创冷饮拉花</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/431c4f09469d52b7c6b5abe9b689af76-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>UJ Lee。 (韩瑞／大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年05月03日讯】（大纪元特约记者柯婷婷纽约报导）在纽约，喝<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>是很多人一天的开始。一位<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>男生，将热<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%95%A1.md#1">咖啡</a>拉花的艺术，成功复制到冷饮上。不仅可以在冷饮上画图，还能写字，目前在纽约为首创。</p>
<figure id="9100532" aria-describedby="caption-9100532" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/c716d1dcc163ee2896cbe0cc4dabe7ed-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/c716d1dcc163ee2896cbe0cc4dabe7ed-450x338.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100532" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(UJ Lee 提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9100531" aria-describedby="caption-9100531" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/30acdceb422e40f27e8fa31d81d5c544-450x322.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/30acdceb422e40f27e8fa31d81d5c544-450x322.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100531" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(UJ Lee 提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9100530" aria-describedby="caption-9100530" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/0310d1c05beb0a14aa2f4bf43cb930b7-450x332.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/0310d1c05beb0a14aa2f4bf43cb930b7-450x332.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100530" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(UJ Lee 提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>热咖啡的拉花很多人都会做，但冷饮拉花（Cream Art）难度可就大了。先用蒸汽冲打牛奶，加温到60～65°c，形成了绵密奶泡后，就可以塑造出各种图案。原本一杯普通的卡布奇诺（Cappuccino），现在也可以变化多种造型，Sweet Moment老板UJ Lee就在上头用冷饮拉花的技巧，绘制了两只小熊图案。</p>
<figure id="9100529" aria-describedby="caption-9100529" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/5a74c6390c0800c6f7b04809b19fd353-450x358.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/5a74c6390c0800c6f7b04809b19fd353-450x358.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100529" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(UJ Lee 提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>UJ Lee介绍说，蒸汽牛奶可以融合进特浓咖啡，所以很容易拉出爱心等图案，“但是，冷饮就不能用蒸汽牛奶，牛奶如果太热，就没办法融入到冷饮里。”不过，他受到的挑战还不只这些，因为<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD.md#1">韩国</a>食材和美国的完全不同，所以刚开始在美国做不出韩国的拉花。于是他花了两年的时间研究，才终于找到最合适冷饮拉花的食材。</p>
<figure id="9100528" aria-describedby="caption-9100528" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cf480ff5540dad782d13704e050cac9f-450x251.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cf480ff5540dad782d13704e050cac9f-450x251.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100528" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(韩瑞／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>说到UJ的一身咖啡绝活，是师承在韩国有“咖啡王子”之称的Kang-Bin Lee，两年前创出冷饮拉花（Cream Art）的技术，将一杯杯冷咖啡变身成一张张的画布。</p>
<figure id="9100527" aria-describedby="caption-9100527" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/86b82e789d100409cecf1cbc33e608cc-450x274.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/86b82e789d100409cecf1cbc33e608cc-450x274.jpg" alt="UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100527" class="wp-caption-text">UJ Lee制作的冷饮拉花。(韩瑞／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>一开始UJ尝试去模仿咖啡王子的技术，可惜做出来的效果并不好。不过幸运的是，这位咖啡王子通过网络找到了UJ。“他想把冷饮拉花发扬到全世界，但他要留在韩国，让我替他完成这件事。噢！这也是我想要做的，所以我一口答应了。”UJ说，现在店里的招牌饮料就是由Kang-Bin Lee所创作。</p>
<figure id="9100526" aria-describedby="caption-9100526" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/62b1c2c358aa180bc089f8fa3771e96f-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/62b1c2c358aa180bc089f8fa3771e96f-450x257.jpg" alt="冷饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100526" class="wp-caption-text">冷饮拉花。(韩瑞／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>UJ Lee一边介绍，一边动作熟练地在桌子上敲着杯子，赶走奶油里的空气。他说，如果有太多泡泡，拉花表面会不平整，就会呈现出像长了痘痘一样的脸蛋，功亏一篑。</p>
<figure id="9100524" aria-describedby="caption-9100524" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/fbe48230708ff9149b839e92fc601c37-450x250.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/fbe48230708ff9149b839e92fc601c37-450x250.jpg" alt="制作拉花前须先用蒸汽冲打牛奶，加温到60-65°c。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100524" class="wp-caption-text">制作拉花前须先用蒸汽冲打牛奶，加温到60-65°c。(韩瑞／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这家位于纽约<ahref="https://github.com/jlgrgx3887/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">华埠</a>的咖啡店，以一杯杯可爱的“拉花冷饮”成功吸引食客们光顾，一探究竟。不过，老板说再怎么爱它都要赶紧喝掉，因为图案在一个小时内就会化掉。◇</p>
<figure id="9100523" aria-describedby="caption-9100523" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cc39e608c78bcc4a3e4bc07c63a14a76-450x254.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/cc39e608c78bcc4a3e4bc07c63a14a76-450x254.jpg" alt="热饮拉花。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9100523" class="wp-caption-text">热饮拉花。(韩瑞／大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
