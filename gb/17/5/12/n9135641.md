<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宜兰县原住民部落大学 开学暨体验活动</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261427_medium-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玛嘎巴嗨文化艺术团表演，左三为宜兰县议员孙汤玉惠。（赖月贵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年05月12日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）宜兰县<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E4%BD%8F%E6%B0%91.md#1">原住民</a>族<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">部落大学</a>于5月12日上午，于冬山河生态绿舟(绿博水原绿境园区)举办106年度宜兰县原住民族部落大学开学典礼暨水原绿境<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%93%E9%AA%8C%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">体验活动</a>。</p>
<p>活动除了<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E4%BD%8F%E6%B0%91.md#1">原住民</a>舞蹈展演（玛嘎巴嗨文化艺术团及门诺漾文化艺术团）、祈福仪式、颁发聘书、一起唱<ahref="https://github.com/urzlpx322/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%A8%E8%90%BD%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">部落大学</a>校歌、还有体验捣麻糬、享用原乡美食等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9135659" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135659" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261428_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135659" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261428_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="门诺漾文化艺术团表演。(中间舞者已84岁高龄)。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135659" class="wp-caption-text">门诺漾文化艺术团表演。(中间舞者已84岁高龄)。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰县原住民族部落大学的成立，是希望在台湾特有的原住民族群历史文化的环境下，提供每一位原住民终身学习的机会。终身学习与社区教育已成为当前世界各国进行教育与社会改革的动力来源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9135660" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135660" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261429_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135660" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261429_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="祈福仪式。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135660" class="wp-caption-text">祈福仪式。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰县副县长朱寿骞表示，台湾是民族大融合，他有泰雅族及噶玛兰族的血统，穿着原民服饰，觉得与有荣焉。今年10月的全国运动会，原民文化将占重要位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9135661" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135661" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261430_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135661" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261430_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="副县长朱寿骞(中)颁发聘书。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135661" class="wp-caption-text">副县长朱寿骞(中)颁发聘书。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>县议员陈杰麟指出，原住民部落大学已迈入第16个年头，主要推动原住民终身学习，包括语言、文化、教育、社区、健康照护等五大类别，第一期有20门课，欢迎大家一起来学习，真正感受原民文化的美好，希望下一代能传承下去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9135662" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135662" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261431_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135662" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261431_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="合唱宜兰原住民部落大学校歌。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135662" class="wp-caption-text">合唱宜兰原住民部落大学校歌。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原住民喜欢用歌声、音乐、舞蹈来表达他们的热情与活力！县议员孙汤玉惠于2002年成立玛嘎巴嗨文化艺术团，玛嘎巴嗨在阿美族有很漂亮美丽之意，她希望原民文化能继续传承下去，能被看见，在国际上发光发热。她表示，91年成立宜兰县原住民族部落大学，原住民事务就要交给原住民来做，族语的振兴，文化的传承，要努力来做，让我们后代子孙来传承原民文化及历史。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9135664" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135664" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261432_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261432_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="宜兰县原住民部落大学 开学 大合照。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135664" class="wp-caption-text">宜兰县原住民部落大学 开学 大合照。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9135665" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9135665" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261433_medium-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9135665" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/261433_medium-450x300.jpg" alt="副县长朱寿骞捣麻糬体验。（赖月贵／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9135665" class="wp-caption-text">副县长朱寿骞捣麻糬体验。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
