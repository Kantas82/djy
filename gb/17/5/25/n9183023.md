<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚裔教师协会年度颁奖 盲人教师感人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01008-AAE-Teacher-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>亚裔教师协会主席Christy Chen与获“AAE年度教师奖”的Richmond小学Harriet Go女士（左）合影。（肖捷/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年05月25日讯】（大纪元记者肖捷费城报导）5月19日(周五)晚，<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E6%95%99%E5%B8%88%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">亚裔教师协会</a>（Asian American Educators Association，AAE）在<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%9F%8E%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md#1">费城华埠</a>聚福金阁举办了第24届年度筹款宴会，支持亚裔学生的教育。宾州州长办公室亚太裔顾问执行主任Tiffany Lawson宣读了州长的贺信，强调教育的重要性。盲人女教师Harriet Go获“AAE年度教师奖”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9183067" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9183067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01018-MargaretChin-450x337.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9183067 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01018-MargaretChin-450x337.jpg" alt="DSC01018-MargaretChin" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9183067" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94%E6%95%99%E5%B8%88%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A.md#1">亚裔教师协会</a>前主席杨美灵（右）和她弟弟为“杨氏奖学金”获得者-来自Academy of Palumbo高中、将进入爵硕大学的Kathrina Cheng颁奖。（肖捷/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9183061" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9183061" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC00999-Tiffany-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9183061 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC00999-Tiffany-450x300.jpg" alt="DSC00999-Tiffany" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9183061" class="wp-caption-text">宾州州长办公室亚太裔顾问执行主任Tiffany Lawson（右）给亚裔教师协会主席Christy Chen颁发了州长对AAE的褒奖。（肖捷/大纪元） DSC00999-Tiffany.jpg</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-9183063 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01001-Tiffany-group-450x243.jpg" alt="DSC01001-Tiffany-group" width="450" b="243" /></p>
<p>旨在促进亚裔学生教育的亚裔教师协会成立已经三十年了，每年都举办筹款活动，以奖学金赞助高中毕业生进入大学学习。2017年“<ahref="https://github.com/occqow360/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/aae%E5%A5%96%E5%AD%A6%E9%87%91.md#1">AAE奖学金</a>”获得者是Central High School的Zhixin Zhang和Nazareth Academy高中的Lin Jia Chen，她们都将进入宾大。“杨氏奖学金”获得者Academy of Palumbo高中的Kathrina Cheng，她将进入爵硕大学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9183064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9183064" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01003-YaLinQian-450x311.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9183064 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01003-YaLinQian-450x311.jpg" alt="DSC01003-YaLinQian" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9183064" class="wp-caption-text">获“AAE写作竞赛奖”、来自Francis ScottKey小学的Ya Lin Qian（左二）与母亲、老师们合影。（肖捷/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9183065" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9183065" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01004-YanhuiWu-450x358.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9183065 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01004-YanhuiWu-450x358.jpg" alt="DSC01004-YanhuiWu" width="450" b="358" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9183065" class="wp-caption-text">获“AAE写作竞赛奖”、来自Kirkbride初中的Yanhui Wu（中）与老师们合影。（肖捷/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9183069" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9183069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01021-ZhixinZhang-450x318.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9183069 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/DSC01021-ZhixinZhang-450x318.jpg" alt="DSC01021-ZhixinZhang" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9183069" class="wp-caption-text">来自Central高中、将进入宾大的Zhixin Zhang（右）获“AAE奖学金”（肖捷/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年“AAE教师奖” 获得者是Richmond小学的Harriet Go女士，表彰她为学生及其家庭、社区的教育所做出的不懈努力。据Delaware County Daily Times报道，特教老师Go女士出生在菲律宾，患有先天性青光眼，婴儿时就被家人带到费城儿童医院治疗。2013年4月11日手持盲人手杖的Go女士在过马路时不幸被汽车撞伤频临死亡：骨盆和尾骨破碎，脑震荡，头上出血包，腿从臀部肿胀到脚踝。但Harriet非常积极乐观，以惊人的意志力，经过艰难的恢复和训练，参加了2014年4月在费城东北部举办的5千米跑步。Harriet毕业于天普大学，一直在Richmond小学教学。她说：“我们这里不论是30年的老教师还是刚进来的新教师，能当上教师非常荣幸，也很重要，我们有责任教育好学生。我们作为亚裔教师不只是提供好的教育，还要教学生们把好的、积极的价值观回馈给社区，使世界更美好。”</p>
<p>晚会还为AAE写作竞赛获奖者颁发了奖状。获奖者分别是小学生组的Ya Lin Qian和初中生组的Yanhui Wu。</p>
