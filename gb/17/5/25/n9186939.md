<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：夏的韵味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705191613462483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏天草甸与五颜六色的鲜花 晴朗的自然，日落。（fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F.md#1">夏</a>有夏的韵味，和别的季节不同。<br />
<ahref="https://github.com/yahntu3246/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F.md#1">夏</a>的韵味是浓烈的。<br />
夏天是丰盛、繁荣、缤纷、豪放、活力充满的季节。<br />
云已不再是薄薄的微云了，通常都是浓浓的一大片乌云，具有吓人的嘴脸，一来便把 大地笼罩在黑暗之中，并且一高兴便下起雨来。那雨可不是毛毛细雨，总是倾盆的滂 沱大雨，大得常常打痛万物的肌肤，伴着轰隆隆的雷声和金蛇般倏现的闪电，令胆小 者被惊吓得以为世界末日已经降临。<br />
天晴的时候，烈日当空，火伞高张，烧烤得大地有如在炉火之中。人们总是挥汗如雨 ，尤其是农人、工人等在烈日下工作的人，不但挥汗如雨，更且在烈日的曝晒之下， 受最严酷的锤炼，被烈日的拳头捶打得皮肤一层层脱去，脱去了仍然不住地锤炼，锤 炼成为赤红黧黑，光滑得闪发钢似的微光。<br />
生命更充沛了。活动更热络了。冬眠的虫、蛇、蛙、鼠诸动物早已在春天时苏醒，此 时正和其他诸种动物到处爬、走、跑、跳、叫，热闹非常。花、草、树木诸植物发荣 滋长，扶疏繁茂，叶子碧绿、釉绿、浓绿，花朵盛放，颜色缤纷，并渐次结果，甚至 连无生物也“热涨冷缩”，一再地澎涨，澎涨，再澎涨……。<br />
夏是丰盛、繁荣、缤纷、豪放、活力充沛的季节。<br />
夏的韵味是浓烈的。<br />
就人来说，这不正是人的盛年？人在盛年，是丰盛、繁荣、缤纷、豪放、活力充沛的 ，韵味是浓烈的。<br />
愿你常时处在盛夏！<br />
2012/9/27人间福报副刊</p>
<p><strong>Graceful Manner of Summer</strong>? ? ?Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>There is the graceful manner of summer, no the same of other seasons.<br />
The graceful manner of summer is rich and bright.<br />
Summer is the season of hearty, flourishing, in riotous profusion, unbridled and full of vitality.<br />
Cloud is no more the weak cloud of feeble, is often a stretch black cloud of dense, has the feature of scare, shrouds whole the world within the darkness as soon as it comes, and rains as it want. The rain is not the drizzle, but often the heavy downpour, often strong to beat all things in the universe into ache, accompany with the booming of thunder and golden snake-like of the lightning, to scare the chicken-hearted to deem it’s the befall of the end of the world.<br />
When it’s the fine day, the raging sun on the sky, the fire-umbrella wide opened, burning the world as if it’s in the stove. All human being often perspiration come down like raindrops, especially the person working under the sun as the farmer, worker etc., not only perspiration come down like raindrops, moreover, scorch under the sun, stand the severest temper, tempered by the fist of scorch sun to peel the skin lay and lay, temper still after peel, temper to red and black, polished to shine the steel like tiny light.<br />
Life is more abundant. Activity is more bustling. The hibernate animals as insect, serpent, frog and mouse etc. revive for a long time of the spring, they are climbing, walking, running, jumping, and crying with the other animals, very bustling. The plants as flower, grass and tree are growing and flourishing, luxuriant and well-spaced, bright green, glazy green, rich and bright green the leave, full open the flower, in riotous profusion the colour, fruiting one after another, even inanimate object also “to expand when hot and to shrink when cold”, expand again and again, expand, expand again…<br />
Summer is the season of hearty, flourishing, in riotous profusion, unbridled and full of vitality.<br />
The graceful manner of summer is rich and bright.<br />
According to human being, is this not the prime of life? Prime of life is of hearty, flourishing, in riotous profusion, unbridled and full of vitality, the graceful manner of summer is rich and bright.<br />
Wishing you always at the height of the summer!@</p>
