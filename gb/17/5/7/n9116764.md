<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>周一早上不太平 轿车撞护栏再遭卡车撞</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年05月08日讯】（大纪元记者宋清宁澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>编译报导）周一（8日）清晨6点前后，在<ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%AB%E7%BA%B3%E4%BB%80.md#1">莫纳什</a><ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F.md#1">高速</a>公路（Monash Freeway）上的同一地点发生两起<ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E7%A5%B8.md#1">车祸</a>，造成三人受伤。</p>
<p>据当地媒体报导，早上5点45分左右，在Dandenong North地区，<ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8E%AB%E7%BA%B3%E4%BB%80.md#1">莫纳什</a><ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F.md#1">高速</a>公路通向Eastlink的驶出匝道附近，一辆小汽车撞到了护栏上。警车和救护车随后赶到。</p>
<p>不到一会儿，一辆货车同时撞上这三辆车，导致警车和救护车严重受损。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9117158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9117158 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/Screen-Shot-2017-05-08-at-9.50.14-am-1-450x291.png" alt="事故现场。（新闻视频截图）" width="450" b="291" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">出事故的卡车。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9117165" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9117165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/Screen-Shot-2017-05-08-at-11.34.56-am-450x280.png" alt="事故现场。（新闻视频截图）" width="450" b="280" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">事故现场。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>货车司机、汽车司机和一位在第一个事故发生后停下来帮忙的好心人都在<ahref="https://github.com/biqbde3726/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E7%A5%B8.md#1">车祸</a>中受伤，被送往Dandenong Hospital医院，情况稳定。</p>
<p>事发后，救护车被拖走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9117163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9117163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/Screen-Shot-2017-05-08-at-11.33.36-am-450x288.png" alt="救护车被拖走。（新闻视频截图）" width="450" b="288" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">救护车被拖走。（新闻视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>所幸，这两次事故没有造成太严重的交通延误。</p>
