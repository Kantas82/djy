<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：超乎想像 美媒眼中的中国疯狂高考</h2>
<a href="https://d348xnvyypk4sr.cloudfront.net/O2xi27K2r"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年05月05日讯】（大纪元记者史轩之报导）6月初，中国数以百万计的学生将迎来一年一度的全国高考。这场“千万人过独木桥”的激烈竞争，给学生造成的压力之大，令美国媒体非常吃惊。</p>
<p>美国“商业内幕”网站5月4日刊文说，<ahref="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E9%AB%98%E8%80%83.md#1">中国高考</a>压力之大，已到疯狂地步。这场考试因其高风险性，而引发严厉批评。它被认为是进入<ahref="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">大学</a>的先决条件，从而令学生承受着不可思议的压力，甚至与学生自杀联系在一起。</p>
<p>高考前，学生们会进行难以置信的大量准备。事实上，一些学校已经因制造出考试“机器人”而被批评。为应对高考，这些学生每天学习长达15小时。下图显示一名学生在进行必要的休息。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110242" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao1.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110242" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao1.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110242" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下图显示，在四川遂宁一家医院，一些学生戴着氧气罩，同时在学习化学。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110257" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao3.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao3.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110257" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早在进入高中前，学生们就开始为高考作准备，初中生就在为高考而学习了。还不止这些，在高考前，学生和教师要参加压力释放活动，如下图所示的信任背摔（trust fall）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110337" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/kaogao12.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/kaogao12.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110337" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，河北省一所学校的学生们还各拿两张5元纸币。这是校方在考前给他们的，希望能带来好运气。</p>
<p>但随着高考临近，气氛变得更加紧张。下图显示，学生们排队报名参加高考，警方试图遏制人潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110277" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110277" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao6.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110277" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao6.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="274" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110277" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高考当天，成千上万的家人和朋友挤满街头，支持考生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110247" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao2.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110247" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao2.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110247" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高考是一场竞争非常激烈的考试，被认为决定着未来的成败。巨大的压力，促使一些学生<ahref="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9C%E5%BC%8A.md#1">作弊</a>，警察甚至都被派到考场抓作弊生。</p>
<p>下图显示，一支钢笔（左起第二个）内含一个隐藏摄像机；一个接收器被伪装成一块橡皮擦。这两样东西都被没收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110309" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110309" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao11.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao11.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="265" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110309" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下图中这副被没收的眼镜，内含一个隐藏摄像机和一个带有微型接收器的硬币。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao10.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao10.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110303" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在四川成都，还发现一个更复杂和新奇的<ahref="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%9C%E5%BC%8A.md#1">作弊</a>装置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110298" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao9.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao9.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110298" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下图是一些被没收的作弊手机和接收器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110284" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110284" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao7.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110284" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao7.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="259" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110284" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高考包含三大科目——英语、数学和语文。下图是考生在广东东莞<ahref="https://github.com/kxcyuk3454/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">大学</a>考场里参加英语考试。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110353" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110353" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/college-exam-1.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110353" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/college-exam-1.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110353" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>遂宁的监考人员在监控考生，以防作弊。在高考前，一些高中在教室里安装安全摄像头，监控学生以防懒惰。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9110268" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9110268" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao4.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-9110268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/gaokao4.jpeg" alt="（视频截图）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9110268" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>高考持续两天。当考生们完成约9小时的考试后，会冲出去，为一切都成过去式而感到如释重负。<br />

<p>外界指，中共教育体制下的高考，是一场全民悲剧。这种“一锤定音”式的考试，令一切从利益出发的学校拚命给学生施压，以各种利益关系鼓动学生为好成绩拼杀，令整个社会陷入了“学习为钱”的泥沼，扼杀了学生的求知欲和创造欲，对知识失去了起码的尊重。</p>
<p>“不上大学没前途，上了大学未必有前途”的教育商业化怪圈侵蚀了中国人的心智，使他们自觉或不自觉地沦为中共教育体制下的受害者。#</p>
