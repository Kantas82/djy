<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哪种厨房风水，能避免夫妻吵架？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/potato-544073_960_720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>小心，厨房风水会影响家人关系。（pixabay.com）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年08月04日讯】俗语说：嫁鸡随鸡，嫁狗随狗。能做夫妻，是百世的缘分，要珍惜。有时候，夫妻两人起争执，有可能是家里的风水不好，下面我们就从风水角度来看看，厨房需要注意哪些要点。</p>
<p><strong>餐桌</strong></p>
<p>许多人喜欢在厨房的一角设置餐桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9495096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9495096" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/apartment-2094650_960_720-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9495096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/apartment-2094650_960_720-600x399.jpg" alt="厨房里的餐桌。（pixabay.com）" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9495096" class="wp-caption-text">厨房里的餐桌。（pixabay.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然感觉方便，但把象征凝聚全家人感情的餐桌，放在火气旺盛的炉火旁，容易使家人暴躁、易生口角。</p>
<p>所以，还是挑选距离炉火远一点的地方来摆设餐桌吧。</p>
<p><strong>锅和铲</strong></p>
<p>锅和铲使用后不要放在一起。</p>
<p>这两种用具平时用来炒菜，吱吱喳喳响已够吵了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9495101" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9495101" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/4349516009_546efc8142_b-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9495101" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/4349516009_546efc8142_b-600x400.jpg" alt="锅铲炒菜时已经很吵了。（Flickr.com)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9495101" class="wp-caption-text">锅铲炒菜时已经很吵了。（Flickr.com)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果使用后不清洗或是清洗后放在一起，都不太好，最好分开放。</p>
<p><strong>刀和砧板</strong></p>
<p>用刀切东西，一刀又一刀都斩在砧板上，不太吉利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9495109" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9495109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/knife-2162020_960_720-600x427.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9495109" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/knife-2162020_960_720-600x427.jpg" alt="刀和砧板不要随便放。（Pixabay.com）" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9495109" class="wp-caption-text">刀和砧板不要随便放。（Pixabay.com）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用过后应该分开放。</p>
<p>如果把刀插在砧板上，很凶很煞。</p>
<p><strong>臼和棒</strong></p>
<p>臼不能够连棒仰着放，这样会让家里大小事情都吵闹一番。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9495114" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9495114" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/White-Mortar-and-Pestle-600x517.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9495114" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/White-Mortar-and-Pestle-600x517.jpg" alt="臼和棒。（Wikimedia Commons）" width="600" b="517" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9495114" class="wp-caption-text">臼和棒。（Wikimedia Commons）</figcaption></figure>
<p>正确的方法是把臼倒盖，然后臼棒置放在上面。</p>
<p>总的来说，一公一母的器皿最好不要一起放，否则夫妻容易动干戈。</p>
