<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>减少幅射伤害 多吃7大健康食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1708111326562483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>有的人容貌平平，但看上去给人的感受就是很自信，很有光彩。似乎心中的平和，内心的喜悦都弥漫在脸上。(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）许多人每天面对电脑，时间一久，<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%90%E5%B0%84.md#1">辐射</a>难免会对身体造成伤害，多吃下列食物可起到预防辐射的作用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>番茄：减少皮肤<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%BE%90%E5%B0%84.md#1">辐射</a>损伤，祛斑美白</strong></span></h2>
<p>科学调查发现，经常食用番茄及番茄制品的人，受辐射损伤较轻，由辐射所引起的死亡率也较低。番茄红素还有祛斑、祛色素的功效，并可促进血液中胶原蛋白和弹性蛋白的结合，使肌肤充满弹性，延缓衰老。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561371" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/tomatoes-600x423.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561371 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/tomatoes-600x423.jpg" alt="番茄。(congerdesign/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561371" class="wp-caption-text">番茄红素有祛斑、祛色素的功效。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>紫菜：抗辐射圣品</strong></span></h2>
<p>紫菜，又称“海苔”。紫菜含碘量很高，能抗辐射、抗突变、抗氧化。碘是种重要的微量元素，能增强机体免疫功能，保护人体健康。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561372" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/7bb3e1ff3c9f3c042769e6b761a6e29e-e1503559095452.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561372 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/7bb3e1ff3c9f3c042769e6b761a6e29e-e1503559095452.jpg" alt="紫菜。(Alice Wiegand/CC/Wikipedia)" width="600" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561372" class="wp-caption-text">紫菜含碘量高。(Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>海带：具有抗辐射作用</strong></span></h2>
<p>海带，别名昆布。吃海带有很多好处，例如：海带含碘，可防甲状腺肿。海带含有丰富的钙，可防止人体缺钙。海带含有大量的膳食纤维，可以增加肥胖者的饱足感，且海带脂肪含量非常低，热量少。但孕妇和乳母不宜多食海带，容易造成胎儿甲状腺功能障碍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/a3789957606a30793b394c042420b5ab-e1503559131966.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561391 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/a3789957606a30793b394c042420b5ab-e1503559131966.jpg" alt="凉拌海带丝。(Amanda/CC/flickr)" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561391" class="wp-caption-text">海带有大量膳食纤维。(Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>绿茶：减轻辐射对人体的不良影响</strong></span></h2>
<p>绿茶中的茶多酚是抗辐射物质，可减轻各种辐射对人体的不良影响。除了减肥瘦身、绿茶中的茶多酚能够溶解脂肪，可防心脑血管疾病，也可防止口臭。在工作的时候一边饮绿茶，可减少电脑辐射对人的伤害，且因绿茶中含有丰富的维生素A和胡萝卜素，对视力也有一定的保护作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561395" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561395" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/green-tea-e1503559163380.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561395 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/green-tea-e1503559163380.jpg" alt="绿茶。(Free-Photos/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561395" class="wp-caption-text">绿茶中的茶多酚可以抗辐射。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>大蒜：减少辐射损伤</strong></span></h2>
<p>大蒜含有蒜素，有很强的抗氧化的功效，因此，可以减少辐射对身体的损伤程度。大蒜也有消炎、杀菌、保护肠道的作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561396" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/garlic-e1503559197543.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561396 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/garlic-e1503559197543.jpg" alt="大蒜。(Adriano Gadini/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561396" class="wp-caption-text">大蒜中的蒜素有抗氧化作用。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>绿豆：有助于排泄体内毒物</strong></span></h2>
<p>民间素有“绿豆汤解百毒”之说。现代研究证明，绿豆可排泄体内毒物，加速新陈代谢的物质，能有效抵抗各种污染，包括电磁污染。但绿豆汤不能空腹食用，过量也不好，也不可和中药一同搭配食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561398" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561398" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/a79a2acdd48e23452ba0d509d23c15aa-e1503559230727.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561398 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/a79a2acdd48e23452ba0d509d23c15aa-e1503559230727.jpg" alt="绿豆。(Mung bean/CC/Pixabay)" width="600" b="382" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561398" class="wp-caption-text">民间有话：绿豆汤解百毒。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>黑木耳：清胃、涤肠、防辐射</strong></span></h2>
<p>黑木耳中的胶质可把残留在人体消化系统内的灰尘、杂质及放射性物质吸附，集中起来排出体外，从而起到清胃、涤肠、防辐射的作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9561399" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9561399" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/25dbcc4c873fcb80efcdaafcac005e5e-e1503559350116.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9561399 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/25dbcc4c873fcb80efcdaafcac005e5e-e1503559350116.jpg" alt="黑木耳。(Phoebe/CC/flickr)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9561399" class="wp-caption-text">黑木耳中的胶质可以吸附体内杂质。(Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
