<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>省长视察山火灾情 研究社区重建</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月29日讯】<span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>省内陆地区的炎热天气令<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E7%81%AB.md#1">山火</a>灾情持续。省长<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E8%B0%A8.md#1">贺谨</a></span><span lang="EN-US">(John Horgan)</span><span lang="ZH-TW">周一到内陆多个城镇视察<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B1%B1%E7%81%AB.md#1">山火</a>灾情。他表示，要协助受影响社区<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E5%BB%BA.md#1">重建</a>，仅有财政援助并不足够。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">省长<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%BA%E8%B0%A8.md#1">贺谨</a>与林木厅长唐诺道（</span><span lang="EN-US">Doug Donaldson</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）视察了</span><span lang="EN-US">Kamloops</span><span lang="ZH-TW">、</span><span lang="EN-US">Cache Creek</span><span lang="ZH-TW">、</span><span lang="EN-US">Ashcroft</span><span lang="ZH-TW">和</span><span lang="EN-US">Kelowna</span><span lang="ZH-TW">的山火灾情。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">贺谨早上到访</span><span lang="EN-US">Kamloops</span><span lang="ZH-TW">紧急事故协调中心时表示，省政府会尽力协助受山火影响的社区，但是提供财政援助只是第一步，当局会了解各个受影响社区以及第一民族原住民的需要，提供切实的援助。 贺谨又赞扬省内各界在山火期间的反应迅速。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>紧急事故管理处的</span><span lang="EN-US">Chris Duffy</span><span lang="ZH-TW">表示，现时全省有</span><span lang="EN-US">37</span><span lang="ZH-TW">个疏散令，</span><span lang="EN-US">2,580</span><span lang="ZH-TW">人需要撤离，</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span lang="ZH-TW">万</span><span lang="EN-US">1000</span><span lang="ZH-TW">人处于疏散预警。他透露，当局已经就<ahref="https://github.com/upgpqd328/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E5%BB%BA.md#1">重建</a>工作展开研究，协助灾民修复由山火造成的创伤和损失。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">目前一共有</span><span lang="EN-US">138</span><span lang="ZH-TW">宗山火正在燃烧，其中</span><span lang="EN-US">7</span><span lang="ZH-TW">宗新增火头。</span><span lang="EN-US">3,800</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名消防员，</span><span lang="EN-US">170</span><span lang="ZH-TW">部消防直升机和飞机正在扑救。由</span><span lang="EN-US">4</span><span lang="ZH-TW">月</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span lang="ZH-TW">日至今，本省一共有</span><span lang="EN-US">1,127</span><span lang="ZH-TW">宗山火，涉及</span><span lang="EN-US">106</span><span lang="ZH-TW">万</span><span lang="EN-US">1000</span><span lang="ZH-TW">公顷土地，扑救费用超过</span><span lang="EN-US">4</span><span lang="ZH-TW">亿元。</span> <span lang="ZH-TW">　</span><span lang="EN-US">◇</span></p>
