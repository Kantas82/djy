<p>作为无数韩国本土人争相追捧的“<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E4%B9%A1%E5%91%B3.md#1">家乡味</a>”，<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E2%80%9C%E5%B1%B1%E2%80%9D%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">“山”餐厅</a>提供各类正宗又健康的的韩国食物，不管是最简单的<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md#1">韩国泡菜</a>，还是经典韩剧出镜率最高的石锅拌饭、烤肉等，在韩国“山”餐厅都能一一奉上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9494727" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9494727" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081928332700-600x402-450x302.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9494727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081928332700-600x402-450x302.jpg" alt="色香味美的石锅拌饭。（商家提供）" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9494727" class="wp-caption-text">色香味美的石锅拌饭。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“山”餐厅的料理不含味精调料和其他任何添加剂，坚持高汤熬制汤底最微调味，让您出门在外也能享受在家的健康温馨美食料理。</p>
<h2>“山”餐厅 暖心<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E5%8F%82%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">人参鸡汤</a></h2>
<p>所以“山”餐厅 的<ahref="https://github.com/fbuetc3721/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BA%BA%E5%8F%82%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">人参鸡汤</a>是不容错过的一道美食。广知韩国盛产食用人参，人参鸡汤里的用料都是来自韩国本土，汤料不加入任何的添鲜剂，只突出肉汤和人参味道的融合，如此养身健康又美味的鸡汤吸引着无数人垂涎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9494702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9494702" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1504171439452700-600x338-450x254.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9494702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1504171439452700-600x338-450x254.jpg" alt="暖心人参鸡汤。（商家提供）" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9494702" class="wp-caption-text">暖心人参鸡汤。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>“山”餐厅 超丰盛鲍鱼粥</h2>
<p>在“山”餐厅不得不提的美食还有韩式鲍鱼粥，香米烹煮足够的时长后鲍鱼才入锅继续缓慢熬制，直到粥里都充盈着鲍鱼的鲜香味，才加其他小葱配料做最后的提香，这样的美味相信没有人可以拒绝。粥类最忌腻口，这里的鲍鱼粥保持了鲍鱼原汁原味的鲜甜，还有米的清香，同样严格的除腥工序，让人流连忘返。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9494719" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9494719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1504171439192700-600x400-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9494719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1504171439192700-600x400-450x300.jpg" alt="超丰盛鲍鱼粥。（商家提供）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9494719" class="wp-caption-text">超丰盛鲍鱼粥。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>泡菜似乎是最能检验一家韩国餐厅如何的基本小食，也是每一顿都少不了的配菜。“山”餐厅的泡菜以鲜辣著称，虽然仅仅只是小菜，但是辣味十足，配合健康独到的“山”餐厅特制紫米饭，更是回味无穷。这家餐厅的泡菜也都是自己腌制，口味独具特色。用来拌饭和配菜都是一流。</p>
<h2>“山”餐厅消暑韩国冷面</h2>
<p>如果觉得洛杉矶艳阳高照，酷暑难耐，不如点一份韩国冷面，也是夏天最推崇的饮食，这里的荞麦面精工细作，爽滑入口，让人吃了还想再吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9494721" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9494721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081923292700-600x400-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9494721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081923292700-600x400-450x300.jpg" alt="韩国冷面。（商家提供）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9494721" class="wp-caption-text">韩国冷面。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“山”餐厅的所有料理都不会放味精，所以入味清新淡雅，没有浓厚的咸味，从真正意义上做到用最纯、最健康的饮食，回馈每一位用餐者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9494730" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9494730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081934432700-600x514-450x386.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9494730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1512081934432700-600x514-450x386.jpg" alt="辛辣牛肉刀切面。（商家提供）" width="450" b="386" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9494730" class="wp-caption-text">辛辣牛肉刀切面。（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>
