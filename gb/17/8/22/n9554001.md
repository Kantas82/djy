<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>哺乳妇女应该吃这些 确保自己与孩子健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/Fotolia_66855345_Subscription_L-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>正在哺乳期间的妇女应该食用鱼、蛋等食物，以确保自己和孩子的健康。(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年08月22日讯】（大纪元记者陈俊村编译报导）<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E4%B9%B3.md#1">母乳</a>对<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF.md#1">婴儿</a>有许多益处，而且女性亲自<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%BA%E4%B9%B3.md#1">哺乳</a>也能增进自己与孩子之间的关系。但女性在哺乳期间应该注意<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>，这样才能确保自己和孩子的健康。以下是每日医学（Medical Daily）网站推荐的5种<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">食物</a>和饮料，提供哺乳妇女参考：</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">1. 豆类和多叶蔬菜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9195689" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9195689" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/shutterstock_151623956-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9195689 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/shutterstock_151623956-450x299.jpg" alt="右为小扁豆。(AN NGUYEN/Shutterstock)" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9195689" class="wp-caption-text">右为小扁豆，蚕豆、扁豆含有丰富的叶酸。(AN NGUYEN/Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_5776966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-5776966" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409140352462153-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-5776966 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1409140352462153-450x338.jpg" alt="波菜(Fotolia)" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-5776966" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜深绿色叶菜类、波菜含有丰富的叶酸。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>叶酸对于孕妇特别重要，因为它能预防新生<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A9%B4%E5%84%BF.md#1">婴儿</a>的天生缺陷，而它对于<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%93%BA%E4%B9%B3.md#1">哺乳</a>妇女也很重要，因为它能帮助女性制造新的细胞。因此，如果你正在哺乳期间的话，应该食用富含叶酸的豆类和多叶蔬菜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">2. 鱼</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_7289340" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-7289340" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/05/1105090231362098-450x301.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-7289340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/05/1105090231362098-450x301.jpg" alt="野生鲑鱼所富含的ω- 3 脂肪酸是大脑所需的基本要素，对于大脑的健康与记忆力的增进非常重要。(图片来源: Photos.com)" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-7289340" class="wp-caption-text">野生鲑鱼所富含的ω- 3 脂肪酸是大脑所需的基本要素，对于大脑的健康与记忆力的增进非常重要。(Photos.com)</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于在哺乳期间的女性而言，像鲑鱼之类富含脂肪的鱼是很好的<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">食物</a>，因为这样的鱼含有一种名叫DHA的脂肪，对婴儿神经系统的发展非常重要。</p>
<p>此外，富含脂肪的鱼也能协助稳定母亲的情绪，这在孩子刚出生后特别重要，因为10%至15%的母亲会经历产后忧郁症（postpartum depression）。</p>
<p>不过，哺乳妇女食用鱼类的量应该适中，因为鱼肉中含有汞的成分，可透过乳汁传输给婴儿。有些鱼类的含汞量较高，例如鲔鱼和箭鱼，最好别吃。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">3. 蛋</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9509919" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9509919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/81a81b1276f7e391e2c39c9d6fbef369-450x307.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9509919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/81a81b1276f7e391e2c39c9d6fbef369-450x307.jpg" alt="白煮蛋。(Pixabay)" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9509919" class="wp-caption-text">白煮蛋。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你不爱吃鱼，你也可以从鸡蛋中取得DHA。含有Omega-3脂肪酸的鸡蛋是吃亚麻籽的母鸡所生的。当这些母鸡吃下亚麻籽时，其部分成分会分解为DHA，然后移转至蛋黄上。所以食用这种鸡蛋的蛋黄，也可摄取DHA。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">4. 水</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_6294232" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-6294232" style="width: 349px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/03/903210949402178.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-6294232 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/03/903210949402178.jpg" alt="多喝水，做个水嫩的女人。(图／大纪元资料室)" width="349" b="548" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-6294232" class="wp-caption-text">哺乳妇女应多喝水，保持水分充足。（大纪元资料室）</figcaption></figure>
<p>哺乳妇女脱水的风险比一般女性高。如果喝水不够的话，哺乳妇女分泌的乳汁可能不足，所以应该保持水分充足。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">5. 牛奶</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9459734" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9459734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/83c72bca443a100d3a6d0f42fec3cbbc-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9459734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/83c72bca443a100d3a6d0f42fec3cbbc-450x300.jpg" alt="牛奶。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9459734" class="wp-caption-text">牛奶。(Couleur/CC/Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛奶是钙的丰富来源之一，而钙正是哺乳妇女所需的<ahref="https://github.com/wtnrng3147/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%90%A5%E5%85%BB.md#1">营养</a>素。美国国立卫生研究院（National Institutes of Health）的资料显示，女性在哺乳期间会流失骨质量的3%至5%，因其体内较多的钙被移转至孩子身上。尽管这些流失的骨质量将来会恢复，但在饮食中摄取多一点的钙会有所帮助。#</p>
