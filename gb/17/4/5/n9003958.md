<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>卧室空间的色彩时尚</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>无论你是早出晚归的上班族，还是是琐事连连的全职妈妈，或者是书本满天飞的用功学生，当太阳下山岗的时候，我们都要在繁忙的一天结束后，回到自己家，自己的卧室，放松心情。休息是生活中一个重要的环节，不能很好的休息，就不能很好的工作和生活。那么卧室这个空间也就变得至关重要了。那么除了摆设和家具，颜色也是尤为重要的一个因素。一起来看看吧！</p>
<p><strong>黄色</strong></p>
<p>这是一种象征健康的颜色，它之所以显得健康明亮，因为它是光谱中最易被吸收的颜色。它可以稳定不良情绪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9013133" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9013133" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_83971242_original-600x520.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9013133 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_83971242_original-600x520.jpg" alt="（poligonchik／depositephotos）" width="600" b="520" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9013133" class="wp-caption-text">（poligonchik／Depositephotos）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>绿色</strong></p>
<p>绿色令人感到稳重和舒适，具有镇静神经、降低眼压、解除眼疲劳、改善肌肉运动能力等作用，自然的绿色还对晕厥、疲劳、恶心与消极情绪有一定的舒缓作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9013219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9013219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_125316968_original-600x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9013219 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_125316968_original-600x400.jpg" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9013219" class="wp-caption-text">(photographee.eu/Depositephotos)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>蓝色</strong></p>
<p>蓝色可以令人产生遐想，它还具有调节神经、镇静安神的作用。但患有精神衰弱、忧郁病的人不宜过多接触蓝色，否则会有加重病情的可能。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9013209" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9013209" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_5019141_original-600x456.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9013209 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_5019141_original-600x456.jpg" alt="（archideaphoto／depositphotos）" width="600" b="456" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9013209" class="wp-caption-text">（archideaphoto／Depositphotos）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>银色</strong></p>
<p>银色这种金属色调会给人奢华但舒缓的感觉。同时，它们可以产生让人意想不到的作用：五分之一拥有银色卧室的受访者表示，他们更有动力在卧室里锻炼身体！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9027641" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9027641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_4739528_l-2015-600x406.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9027641 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_4739528_l-2015-600x406.jpg" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9027641" class="wp-caption-text">（MrHamster／Depositphotos）</figcaption></figure>
<p>橙色</p>
<p>橙色卧室可以增添温暖感，营造轻松的气氛，放松身体的肌肉，获得良好睡眠；还可以帮助消化！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9027658" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9027658" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_2299796_l-2015-600x475.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9027658 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_2299796_l-2015-600x475.jpg" width="600" b="475" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9027658" class="wp-caption-text">（kash76／Depositphotos）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>棕色</strong></p>
<p>浪漫主义者的选择，很感性化，超过任何其他颜色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9027699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9027699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/ikea-relax-and-rejuvenate-in-traditional-style__1364308493672-s5-600x448.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9027699 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/ikea-relax-and-rejuvenate-in-traditional-style__1364308493672-s5-600x448.jpg" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9027699" class="wp-caption-text">（Ikea 官网图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>白色</strong></p>
<p>选择这个色调的人一般喜欢简洁的生活方式，但是别忘了做清洁时的麻烦噢。还有，据调查显示，卧室是白色的人会比其他任何卧室颜色的人更容易把电脑和工作带上床！不想当工作狂？那要慎重考虑噢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9027705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9027705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/ikea-add-order-to-your-bedroom-with-the-brusali-wardrobe-in-white-from-ikea.-classic-style-at-an-afforable-price.-__1364309703765-s5-600x448.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9027705 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/ikea-add-order-to-your-bedroom-with-the-brusali-wardrobe-in-white-from-ikea.-classic-style-at-an-afforable-price.-__1364309703765-s5-600x448.jpg" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9027705" class="wp-caption-text">（Ikea 官网图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>金色</strong></p>
<p>也许很多人都会觉得金色的卧室属于比较拜金的人们的选择，小编觉得未必，金碧辉煌也是一种风情，没必要太拘束于他人的看法，自己喜欢就好！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9027716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9027716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_13130237_l-2015-600x451.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9027716 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Depositphotos_13130237_l-2015-600x451.jpg" width="600" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9027716" class="wp-caption-text">（VictoriaAndrea／Depositphotos）</figcaption></figure>
