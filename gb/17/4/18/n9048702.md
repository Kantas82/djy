<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台湾夜市必吃美食大集合</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年04月19日讯】（大纪元记者罗蓉蓉综合报导）各式各样、口味独特美味又多元的台湾<ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a><ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%90%83.md#1">小吃</a>，经由台湾的历史背景发展熏陶，闻名至国外，纷纷吸引许多外国人特地来台，只为品尝那口感绝佳的台湾地道<ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>。据观光局统计，来台观光客最多人游览地点以“夜市”、“台北101”和“故宫博物院”为前三名，其中又以“逛夜市”为观光客首选最爱。让我们来看看有哪些必吃美食吧！</p>
<p><strong>咸酥鸡</strong></p>
<p>咸酥鸡是台湾相当有代表性的<ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">美食</a>之一，在街上或各大<ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9C%E5%B8%82.md#1">夜市</a>几乎随处可见，已成为台湾经典令人难以忘怀的滋味，更是宵夜的好伙伴。加入蒜头下去油炸的盐酥鸡，加上九层塔调味，迎面扑鼻而来的香味，令人食指大动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048731" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fc8aaea73f5ff54dfc9545e8f1ac7bb5-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fc8aaea73f5ff54dfc9545e8f1ac7bb5-450x338.jpg" alt="咸酥鸡。(创用图片/ Wikipedia )" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048731" class="wp-caption-text">咸酥鸡。（创用图片/Wikipedia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>臭豆腐</strong></p>
<p>台湾著名美食臭豆腐，虽然被很多外国游客认为是怪异食物，但臭豆腐烹调方式有炸、有蒸、有煮，再搭上泡菜、蒜泥和辣油，相当吸引人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048740" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048740" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/b57ee303b53a399b9831865d456ebb19-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048740" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/b57ee303b53a399b9831865d456ebb19-450x338.jpg" alt="酥炸臭豆腐。(Fcuk1203 / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048740" class="wp-caption-text">酥炸臭豆腐。(Fcuk1203/CC/Wikipedia )</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>烤鱿鱼</strong></p>
<p>Q弹的鱿鱼，闽南俗语说得好，好的东西卖一种就够了，新鲜的鱿鱼不管是剪成片还是直接大口吃，都有不一样的感觉，沾上特调酱汁，令人齿颊留香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048747" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/0719973f70814172b47cd104f8543486-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/0719973f70814172b47cd104f8543486-450x300.jpg" alt="烤鱿鱼。(Werasak / CC / Flickr)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048747" class="wp-caption-text">烤鱿鱼。(Werasak/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>烧烤</strong></p>
<p>许多夜市都有串烧摊位，摊位上除了有基本的牛肉、羊肉、猪肉外，还会有海鲜食材，火烤后再淋上酱汁，绝对让饕客意犹未尽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048754" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2c475a174beebe41629b853fa9745c71-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2c475a174beebe41629b853fa9745c71-450x338.jpg" alt="台湾夜市的烧烤摊。(Shih-Han Lin / CC / Flickr)" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048754" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市的烧烤摊。(Shih-Han Lin/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>蚵仔煎</strong></p>
<p>蚵仔煎则是台湾夜市最道地的特色<ahref="https://github.com/kmyhax3748/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%90%83.md#1">小吃</a>。通常店家都会把大铁盘放在店门口，让蚵仔在铁盘上活蹦乱跳、散发香气，吸引客人。作法是先放上蚵仔，接着淋上太白粉勾芡水，稍微煎过后再加上鸡蛋及青菜〈最常见是小白菜及茼蒿〉，最后再淋上甜辣酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048756" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048756" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fd0222379910ad64ee69cc2922adae9b-450x369.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fd0222379910ad64ee69cc2922adae9b-450x369.jpg" alt="蚵仔煎。(LWYang / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="369" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048756" class="wp-caption-text">蚵仔煎。(LWYang/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>生煎包</strong></p>
<p>生煎包原是流行于上海、苏州及其它江南地区的一种传统小吃。也称作水煎包，类似于锅贴。将生煎包放入锅内，边煎边喷水数次，直至底面呈金黄色硬而脆，周边及上部稍软即可出锅，通常配以辣椒酱或辣酱油以增加口感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048757" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048757" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/549cc81c0fadcddbf94675d9cfdba900-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/549cc81c0fadcddbf94675d9cfdba900-450x300.jpg" alt="生煎包。(Popo le Chien / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048757" class="wp-caption-text">生煎包。(Popo le Chien/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>卤味</strong></p>
<p>夜市卤味摊有非常多食物供顾客挑选，想吃什么都可以自己夹，这些食物因为吸收了卤汁的精华，每一样都卤得相当入味，深受台湾人喜爱。也可加入泡面及酸菜，增加饱足感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048765" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/97374babc890b91afa06edbbe38fd5b5-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/97374babc890b91afa06edbbe38fd5b5-450x300.jpg" alt="卤味。(Alien0417/ CC / Flickr)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048765" class="wp-caption-text">卤味。(Alien0417/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>蚵仔面线</strong></p>
<p>蚵仔面线主要原料为蚵仔（小牡蛎）和面线，蚵需先拌太白粉，但市面上也有许多是用大肠面线（卤好的猪大肠和面线）来贩售。食用时，可添加乌醋、蒜末、香菜或辣椒等调味料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048766" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048766" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fb71ef4915e227e9f05b3d98621f4b5d-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fb71ef4915e227e9f05b3d98621f4b5d-450x338.jpg" alt="蚵仔面线。(创用图片/ Wikipedia )" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048766" class="wp-caption-text">蚵仔面线。（创用图片/Wikipedia）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>润饼</strong></p>
<p>润饼，又称润饼卷，是一种比春卷更古老的食品，但不用油炸，流行于福建、台湾。通常台闽一带的家庭，会在清明时食用润饼卷，主要是用来祭祖。之后家族成员齐聚一桌，可根据自己喜爱的菜色，再加入花生粉及砂糖，以润饼皮包裹成长条状食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048768" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048768" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/b7059b7c2b2c1b39fdf992368006b65a-300x400.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9048768 size-medium_vertical" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/b7059b7c2b2c1b39fdf992368006b65a-300x400.jpg" alt="润饼。(Fcuk1203 / CC / Wikipedia )" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048768" class="wp-caption-text">润饼。(Fcuk1203/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>肉圆</strong></p>
<p>肉圆的名称来于它的外观，一般为直径6～8公分左右的半透明扁圆形，以地瓜粉、太白粉、或在来米粉作成，里面包裹猪肉及其它配料。台湾肉圆的调理方式除了油炸还有清蒸，食用时会加上少许蒜泥、香菜，好吃的秘诀在于酱料的制作。清蒸肉圆在吃完后，还会以剩余的酱汁及肉末盛清汤来喝，别有一番风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048774" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fdaa2d5d83feac7f7bac87d01b0ad2ca-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fdaa2d5d83feac7f7bac87d01b0ad2ca-450x338.jpg" alt="肉圆。(Fcuk1203 / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048774" class="wp-caption-text">肉圆。(Fcuk1203/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>大肠包小肠</strong></p>
<p>大肠包小肠，九十年代于夜市开始流行，说穿了很简单，就是将体积较大的糯米肠剪开后，再将体积较小的台式香肠加入其中，现烤完后依客人的口味添加不同的口味，如香菜、九层塔或蒜苗，外观看起来像是台式的“热狗堡”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048776" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048776" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7218c5bc5c1d52b440a358d3f824041d-450x323.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7218c5bc5c1d52b440a358d3f824041d-450x323.jpg" alt="大肠包小肠。(Kaihsu / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048776" class="wp-caption-text">大肠包小肠。(Kaihsu/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>猪血糕</strong></p>
<p>英国旅游网站票选“全球古怪食物”榜首的猪血糕，就是俗称的米血，是来自于中国南方的一种传统米食糕点。通常用新鲜的鸭血加入糯米、盐及其它材料，然后蒸熟成为凝固的块状，通常作为火锅或卤味的食材。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048788" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/95365fc0abfdc3f6395b47aedcb29828-450x284.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/95365fc0abfdc3f6395b47aedcb29828-450x284.jpg" alt="猪血糕。(Richy / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="284" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048788" class="wp-caption-text">猪血糕。(Richy/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>珍珠奶茶</strong></p>
<p>俗称珍奶，将“粉圆”（亦称“珍珠”，Tapioca ball）加入香醇的奶茶中，由于粉圆的口感特殊，加入不同的奶茶、红茶或是绿茶，口味独特，受到广大的欢迎与回响，也成为台湾最具代表性的饮料与小吃之一，多年来，已经由台湾流行至东亚、欧洲、美国甚至中东国家等地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048790" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048790" style="width: 214px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2e9d42e5777c4e96ae7d069db31d7d34-214x400.png" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9048790 size-medium_vertical" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2e9d42e5777c4e96ae7d069db31d7d34-214x400.png" alt="珍珠奶茶。(Howief / CC / Wikipedia )" width="214" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048790" class="wp-caption-text">珍珠奶茶。(Howief/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>锉冰</strong></p>
<p>即shaved ice，作法是将冰块用特殊机器或手工用刨刀、凿刀刨细，在上面添加不同配料，或是容器内先放好配料再切冰洒其上。锉冰要好吃，配料是不可或缺的重点。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048792" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/53c9dcbab420eeca9168810b85445257-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/53c9dcbab420eeca9168810b85445257-450x338.jpg" alt="红豆炼乳锉冰。(Andy Tsai / CC / Flickr) " width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048792" class="wp-caption-text">红豆炼乳锉冰。(Andy Tsai/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9048793" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048793" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7207944e05c79451de472ec1f6dc26f0-450x257.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048793" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/7207944e05c79451de472ec1f6dc26f0-450x257.jpg" alt="锉冰要好吃，配料也不能马虎。(视频截图)" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048793" class="wp-caption-text">锉冰要好吃，配料也不能马虎。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>豆花</strong></p>
<p>各地对其称谓略有不同，中国北方多称豆腐脑，南方多称豆花或豆腐花。豆花是由黄豆浆絮凝后形成的果冻状食品，加上配料与一大匙的黑糖水后，让许多人都赞不绝口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048797" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048797" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/93df2ba7df9034f6f4618c0d22a25da1-450x338.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048797" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/93df2ba7df9034f6f4618c0d22a25da1-450x338.jpeg" alt="豆花。(Outlookxp / CC / Wikipedia )" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048797" class="wp-caption-text">豆花。(Outlookxp/CC/Wikipedia)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>现打果汁</strong></p>
<p>喝腻了珍珠奶茶等饮料，台湾夜市中不乏随处可见的现打果汁摊，商家会将各式水果切成小块，搭配成不同种类，立刻现打成新鲜果汁。有的商家甚至会依不同种类的果汁，命名为有趣的名称吸引顾客上门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048798" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048798" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/15996d82592dca563af9120c95cc18eb-450x299.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048798" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/15996d82592dca563af9120c95cc18eb-450x299.jpg" alt="台湾夜市一处现打果汁摊。(SAM YEH/Getty Images)" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048798" class="wp-caption-text">台湾夜市一处现打果汁摊。(SAM YEH/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>渐层饮料</strong></p>
<p>一杯饮料拥有缤纷丰富色彩，除了味觉之外，视觉也是一场飨宴！渐层饮料首先出现于中台湾，通常以原料水果、气泡水制成，搭配时髦可爱的透明罐子，混搭出视觉上超沁凉拍照也时髦的人气饮品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9048799" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9048799" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/3d9203d53ac536485498a6eb134f88fa-450x258.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9048799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/3d9203d53ac536485498a6eb134f88fa-450x258.jpg" alt="渐层饮料。(视频截图)" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9048799" class="wp-caption-text">渐层饮料。(视频截图)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>芋圆甜品</strong></p>
<p>芋圆是台湾传统甜点，做法是将芋头蒸熟后压成泥，加上地瓜粉及水拌匀成团，搓揉成长条形再切成小块，放入沸水中煮至浮起捞出即成芋圆。煮熟的芋圆可和在冰糖水里食用，或者热食。许多甜点如豆花、刨冰、烧仙草，均可加点芋圆和地瓜圆覆盖其上一起食用。</p>
