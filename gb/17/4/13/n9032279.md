<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>尝加勒比美食 来我家院子作客！</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年04月13日讯】“Back A Yard”是一个加勒比国民，或者是游客通常都很熟悉的说法。这个说法代表着欢迎客人到家里来的生活方式、文化、精神、美食以及欢乐的心情。在加州很多人都知道Back A Yard的生活方式：一个院子致力于满足你对美食渴望，同时给你回家风格的烹饪，让你体验到欢乐的气氛。</p>
<p>我们去了坐落于圣荷西城中的这一家餐馆，中午时分已经人满为患。餐厅看起来很像街边夜市的感觉，但是每个人都吃得津津有味，一副识途老饕的味道，少许有那么些骄傲的感觉，表示会吃才会到此一游。<br />
<figure id="attachment_9032326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/02_BBQ-Pork-Spare-Ribs-Meal.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/02_BBQ-Pork-Spare-Ribs-Meal-450x312.jpg" alt="烧烤猪排餐。（湾区餐馆Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="312" class="size-medium wp-image-9032326" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烧烤猪排餐。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区餐馆</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9032327" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/03_Beff-Oxtails-Meal.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/03_Beff-Oxtails-Meal-450x295.jpg" alt="牛尾饭。（湾区餐馆Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="295" class="size-medium wp-image-9032327" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛尾饭。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区餐馆</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><br />
我们看到菜单上有许多用牙买加辣酱汁（Jamaican Jerk Sauce）做的各式餐点。我们点了几道吃吃看，它的辣味风格十分独特，和我们的四川、湖南的辣完全不可同日而语。就是因为酱汁好吃，所以餐厅也有一瓶瓶的酱汁可买。<br />
<figure id="attachment_9032329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/04_Chicken-Pumpkin-Soup.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/04_Chicken-Pumpkin-Soup-450x312.jpg" alt="南瓜鸡肉汤。（湾区餐馆Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="312" class="size-medium wp-image-9032329" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">南瓜鸡肉汤。（湾区餐馆Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9032331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/05_Jerk-Salmon-Meal.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/05_Jerk-Salmon-Meal-450x300.jpg" alt="牙买加辣酱汁三文鱼餐。（硅谷餐馆Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9032331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牙买加辣酱汁三文鱼餐。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">硅谷餐馆</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9032333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/06_Jerk-Pork-Meal.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/06_Jerk-Pork-Meal-450x300.jpg" alt="牙买加辣酱汁猪肉餐。（硅谷餐馆Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9032333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牙买加辣酱汁猪肉餐。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">硅谷餐馆</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><br />
这家餐厅也提供外烩服务（Catering Service），公司行号、派对及大小宴会，他们都有送餐。但是在网站上写的清清楚楚，至少要两天前预订，生意作得不小！<br />
<figure id="attachment_9032335" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/07_Key-Lime-Pie.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/07_Key-Lime-Pie-450x300.jpg" alt="柠檬派。（加勒比美食Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9032335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柠檬派。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%8B%92%E6%AF%94%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">加勒比美食</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_9032336" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/08_Sweet-Potato-Pudding.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/08_Sweet-Potato-Pudding-450x300.jpg" alt="红薯布丁。（加勒比美食Back A Yard网站）" width="450" b="300" class="size-medium wp-image-9032336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红薯布丁。（<ahref="https://github.com/dwxesj356/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%8B%92%E6%AF%94%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">加勒比美食</a>Back A Yard网站）</figcaption></figure><br />
如果你想轻松吃一顿美食，来点牙买加海边的路边摊美食的味道，不如到此一行，尝尝不同口味的飨宴。</p>
