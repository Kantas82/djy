<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>湾区韩餐：江南豆腐屋 无敌好吃海鲜饼</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年04月12日讯】位在南湾苗必达（Milpitas）的<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%9F%E5%8D%97%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90%E5%B1%8B.md#1">江南豆腐屋</a><ahref="http://kangnamtofu.com/" target="_blank">Kang Nam Tofu House</a>），在旧金山湾区也算是一家老字号韩餐店了，在去年换了新老板后，听说有所新气象，身为爱好韩餐的食客不得不去尝尝！</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9F%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">湾区韩餐</a><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B1%9F%E5%8D%97%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90%E5%B1%8B.md#1">江南豆腐屋</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC.md#1">海鲜煎饼</a></h2>
<p>（Seafood Pancake）article content begin -->3.99：这道<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E7%85%8E%E9%A5%BC.md#1">海鲜煎饼</a>，堪称在<ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9F%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">湾区韩餐</a>馆吃到最好吃的海鲜煎饼了！快要赶上大版烧厚度的海鲜煎饼，虽厚但却被煎得十分酥脆，咬下一口除能尝到浓浓的面香、葱香及鱿鱼的鲜及软Q外，还能感受到口中那厚实酥脆的口感，每次嚼咀都能听到喀滋喀滋声，让人十分满足，大力推荐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9028557" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9028557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/001-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9028557 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/001-5-450x300.jpg" alt="目前吃过湾区韩餐馆，最好吃的海鲜煎饼（Seafood Pancake）。（石岚摄自湾区韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9028557" class="wp-caption-text">目前吃过湾区韩餐馆，最好吃的海鲜煎饼（Seafood Pancake）。（石岚摄自湾区韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9028564" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9028564" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/001-1-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9028564 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/001-1-1-450x300.jpg" alt="海鲜煎饼厚实酥脆的口感。（石岚摄自硅谷韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9028564" class="wp-caption-text">海鲜煎饼厚实酥脆的口感。（石岚摄自硅谷韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>湾区韩餐江南豆腐屋，杂菜粉丝</h2>
<p>（Jap-Chae）article content begin -->3.99：以前吃过的杂菜粉丝，大多是麻油的香及甜甜的口感，没想到江南豆腐屋再加上一点点胡椒，令这道菜的味道提升至另一个层次，胡椒特有的辛香及些许的辣，再加上香甜口感，令人停不下嘴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9028565" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9028565" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/002-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9028565 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/002-5-450x289.jpg" alt="杂菜粉丝（Jap-Chae）一口接一口。（石岚摄自南湾韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）" width="450" b="289" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9028565" class="wp-caption-text">杂菜粉丝（Jap-Chae）一口接一口。（石岚摄自南湾韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%B9%BE%E9%9F%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">南湾韩餐</a>江南豆腐屋，海鲜豆腐汤</h2>
<p>（Seafood Soft Tofu Soup）article content begin -->1.99：海鲜豆腐汤很是鲜香，豆腐软滑、弹口、保持着原形不烂，有着一点点咸和浓浓辣味，的确下饭！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9028566" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9028566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/003-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9028566 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/003-1-450x300.jpg" alt="浓辣的的海鲜豆腐汤（Seafood Soft Tofu Soup）十分下饭。（石岚摄旧金山湾区韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9028566" class="wp-caption-text">浓辣的的海鲜豆腐汤（Seafood Soft Tofu Soup）十分下饭。（石岚摄旧金山湾区韩国料理店江南豆腐屋）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/tqsers3755/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E9%9F%A9%E9%A4%90.md#1">硅谷韩餐</a>江南豆腐屋，烤牛仔骨</h2>
<p>（Galbi）article content begin -->5.99（午餐）、$24.99（晚餐）：这跟一般餐馆的牛仔骨不同，虽然薄却不会干硬，没有太多腻人的肥肉，再搭配随餐附上的紫米饭，及7道小菜，是许多上班族热门的午餐选择。</p>
