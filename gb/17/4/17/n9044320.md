<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>墨尔本高尚购物区原来在这儿啊</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Banner-cca01a1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Camberwell 高尚购物街（Camberwell Shopping Precinct提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年04月17日讯】（澳洲<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%e5%a2%a8%e7%88%be%e6%9c%ac.md#1">墨尔本</a>工商稿）一直只知道Camberwell区的房价很高，不是普通工薪阶层可以承受得了的。最近逛了逛过才发现，Camberwell还有一个更大的秘密，是什么呢？小编这就带您走进Camberwell看一看。</p>
<p>Camberwell 区距离<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>市中心仅10公里，属于墨尔本的高尚富人区，周围顶级学校云集，许多居民都是律师、医生和商人，他们祖祖辈辈都住在这儿。近年来，随着公寓的开发，Camberwell又搬来了很多年轻专业人士。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9044400" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Banner-beauty02a1-450x250.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Banner-beauty02a1-450x250.jpg" alt="（Camberwell shopping precinct提供）" width="450" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044400" class="wp-caption-text">（Camberwell shopping precinct提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>那么小编所发现的秘密是什么呢？原来这些精英阶层都有一个共同点，就是酷爱到Camberwell shopping precinct采购区<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9.md#1">购物</a>，这可是<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>最古老的<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9.md#1">购物</a>区之一。1853年，当墨尔本人口快速增长，农民们开始买地时，在现在的Camberwell Junction交叉口处，先建起了一个酒吧，就叫Camberwell酒吧，这是以伦敦南郊的一个地名命名的。1882年墨尔本铁路线延伸至此，后来就沿着Burke Road打造出一条商业街来。由于到市中心方便，人们就陆陆续续搬来此地居住，商业区和居住区相互依托，共同发展起来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9044392" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044392" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/junction-camberwell-rose-series1-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/junction-camberwell-rose-series1-450x253.jpg" alt="(Camberwell shopping precinct提供）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044392" class="wp-caption-text">(Camberwell shopping precinct提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天，购物区仍保留一些当初的老店，尽管另外一些老店迁到了别处，或换了主人，这些老店风采依旧，比如Ulms Hair、Cavalier Menswear、Dunns Leathergoods、Schwartz &amp;?Angelo、The Chocolate Box、Sainsbury’s Books、Browns Jewellers、The Camberwell Fresh Food Market、Rivoli Cinemas等等。这里是来墨尔本<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%85%E6%B8%B8.md#1">旅游</a>购物、体验纯正西式生活的好去处。</p>
<p>由于Camberwell的服务业非常发达，居民们购物极其方便。那里有验光师、医生、牙医、药剂师、专科儿童诊所、主要银行、会计师行、律师行。此外还有美容服务，如美发沙龙，日间水疗以及健身房，瑜伽等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9044397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044397" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Banner-gift011-450x250.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044397" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/Banner-gift011-450x250.jpg" alt="（Camberwell shopping precinct提供）" width="450" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044397" class="wp-caption-text">（Camberwell shopping precinct提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Camberwell 果蔬市场（Camberwell Fresh Food Market）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_9044374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044374" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/cffm-fresh-deli-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044374" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/cffm-fresh-deli-450x253.jpg" alt="（Camberwell Fresh Food Market）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044374" class="wp-caption-text">（Camberwell Fresh Food Market）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个果蔬市场已经经营了85年，摊主们在这里出售高品质、非常新鲜的水果、蔬菜、各种肉类、海鲜、鲜花等等。1930年代开始经营以前，这里原是一个马车厂，后成为一个食品加工厂。现在，许多摊位主已是第二、第三代家族经营者，他们仍保留着传统经营风格。</p>
<p>Camberwell 果蔬市场位于Camberwell的Riversdale路519-523号，每周二，周四，周五和周六上午7点开放。他们定期举办社区日，开展娱乐和食品展示活动。</p>
<p><strong>扶轮社礼拜天市场（Rotary Sunday Market）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_9044378" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044378" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/dd42835872966164cc9b440aa2c2042a-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/dd42835872966164cc9b440aa2c2042a-450x300.jpg" alt="扶轮社礼拜天市场（Rotary Sunday Market）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044378" class="wp-caption-text">扶轮社礼拜天市场（Rotary Sunday Market）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Rotary Sunday Market 每个周日上午6：30-中午12：30开放，有着种类繁多的小商品摊位，包括艺术品、工艺品、仿古制品、家用品和其他各类宝贝。</p>
<p>主办方为博文区扶轮社（Rotary Club of Balwyn），旨在为慈善机构筹款。自1976年开放以来，已筹集了1千5百万澳元。地点：Station Street</p>
<p><strong>The Well购物中心</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_9044383" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/The-well-camberwell-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/The-well-camberwell-450x253.jpg" alt="The Well购物中心" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044383" class="wp-caption-text">The Well购物中心</figcaption></figure>
<p>The Well?是Camberwell商业区唯一的购物中心，里面有Coles、JB HiFi、Dymocks、Terry White Chemists和 Good Life Fitness Centre。还有<ahref="https://github.com/idzwdk333/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E5%93%81%E5%BA%97.md#1">礼品店</a>、家居用品店、咖啡馆、餐饮店等等。</p>
<p><strong>地标建筑Rivoli 电影院 （The Iconic Rivoli Cinemas）?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_9044386" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/rivoli-camberwell-dusk-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/rivoli-camberwell-dusk-450x253.jpg" alt="地标建筑Rivoli 电影院 （The Iconic Rivoli Cinemas） " width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044386" class="wp-caption-text">地标建筑Rivoli 电影院 （The Iconic Rivoli Cinemas）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9044389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9044389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/rivoli-interior-450x288.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9044389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/rivoli-interior-450x288.jpg" alt="地标建筑Rivoli 电影院 （The Iconic Rivoli Cinemas）" width="450" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9044389" class="wp-caption-text">地标建筑Rivoli 电影院 （The Iconic Rivoli Cinemas）</figcaption></figure>
<p>Camberwell 区的居民们喜欢看电影，<ahref="https://camberwellshopping.com.au/trader/rivoli-cinemas/">Rivoli </a>电影院是他们的历史文化象征。</p>
<p><ahref="https://camberwellshopping.com.au/trader/rivoli-cinemas/">Rivoli </a>电影院开放于1940年，后来收入维州保留遗产名单。2000年做了大翻修，以保护其Art Deco时期的新艺术风格。其现代设计受到墨尔本各界广泛的推崇。有着宏大的台阶、独特的地毯图案以及屋顶花园，深受电影迷和建筑爱好者的喜爱。她不仅设计优雅，装潢富丽，而且舒适惬意，号称墨尔本郊区最后的一座宏伟电影院。</p>
<p><ahref="https://camberwellshopping.com.au/trader/rivoli-cinemas/">Rivoli </a>电影院四通八达的交通也吸引了四面八方的人群。</p>
