<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒸汽火车启航绿博 邀弱势共享绿意时光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257869_medium-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蒸汽火车鸣笛。（郭千华／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年04月20日讯】（大纪元记者郭千华台湾宜兰报导）<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E6%B1%BD%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6.md#1">蒸汽火车</a><ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AF%E8%88%AA.md#1">启航</a>绿色博览会，20日藉由公益与火车结合，邀请弱势朋友共享绿意时光，宜兰县长吴泽成偕同弱势、老人团体等，在头城火车站鸣笛出发沿途停靠礁溪、宜兰、罗东、驶向冬山来到<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%8D%9A.md#1">绿博</a>现场体验，留下美好绿博回忆。</p>
<p>宜兰县长吴泽成表示，以推广民众多多搭乘火车系统工具的方式，用绿色戴具体验绿生活，感受铁路慢活旅行，<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E5%8D%9A.md#1">绿博</a>也配合大众交通工具有优惠打折，目的也是鼓励大家搭乘大众运输工具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9058026" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9058026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257870_medium-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9058026 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257870_medium-600x399.jpg" alt="吴泽成县长鸣笛启航。（郭千华／大纪元）" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9058026" class="wp-caption-text">吴泽成县长鸣笛<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%AF%E8%88%AA.md#1">启航</a>。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>具有时代价值<ahref="https://github.com/ogdnsh3161/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E6%B1%BD%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6.md#1">蒸汽火车</a>由吴县长鸣笛启航，让长辈们回忆起儿时记忆不知不觉哼起宜兰民歌丢丢铜的曲子 。列车行进同时，导览志工解说宜兰铁道旅行及绿博永续理念，了解在地农业与生活带领民众认识土地与环境，开着绿色生活观念的关键火车头，驶进绿博。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9058027" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9058027" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257873_medium-600x399.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9058027 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257873_medium-600x399.jpg" alt="257873_medium" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9058027" class="wp-caption-text">蒸汽火车驶向绿博。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来到绿博会场由现场人员带着导览说明，引领弱势朋友共赏粉彩花海及田园艺术的大地画布，再步行到生态绿洲园区体验环境教育课程，探讨大自然的奥秘，丰富自然绿地希望能给予鲜少出游的弱势朋友走出户外的机会，来到宛如诗画般的绿色艺术殿堂，共同体验绿博及实践绿色生活，享受难得美好绿意时光，留下珍贵的绿色回忆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9058040" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9058040" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257867_medium-1.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9058040" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257867_medium-1.jpg" alt="蒸汽火车启航绿博。（郭千华／大纪元）" width="600" b="901" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9058040" class="wp-caption-text">蒸汽火车启航绿博。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9058057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9058057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257868_medium.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9058057" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/257868_medium.jpg" alt="蒸汽火车启航。（郭千华／大纪元）" width="600" b="901" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9058057" class="wp-caption-text">蒸汽火车启航。（郭千华／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
