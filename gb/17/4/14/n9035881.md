<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>四月踏青 Brookside公园赏多彩郁金香</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-119-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>Brookside公园内栽有五万株郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年04月14日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者石青云美国马里兰州报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">看过了粉色的樱花，现在又到了观赏<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%81%E9%87%91%E9%A6%99.md#1">郁金香</a>的时节。位于马里兰州惠顿的Brookside公园，目前已经盛开了大批的郁金香，喜欢<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B8%8F%E9%9D%92.md#1">踏青</a>或是热爱拍照的朋友们，不妨前去一看。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">地处华盛顿特区北部、坐落在马里兰州惠顿市（Wheaton）的Brookside公园彷若室外桃园，公园的四周环绕着郁郁葱葱的树林，隔绝开了城市的喧嚣，公园内小桥流水，让人心旷神怡。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_9035912" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-122-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9035912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-122-600x400.jpeg" alt="Brookside公园内栽有各种颜色的郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035912" class="wp-caption-text">Brookside公园内栽有各种颜色的<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%81%E9%87%91%E9%A6%99.md#1">郁金香</a>。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9035909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-126-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9035909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-126-600x400.jpeg" alt="公园内小桥流水，让人心旷神怡。（石青云／大纪元） " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035909" class="wp-caption-text">公园内小桥流水，让人心旷神怡。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9035914" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-123-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9035914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-123-600x400.jpeg" alt="在整个四月份，郁金香会轮番开放。（石青云／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035914" class="wp-caption-text">在整个四月份，郁金香会轮番开放。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Brookside公园拥有来自世界各地的植物，在当前时节，正是郁金香花盛开的时候。整个公园内共栽有5万多郁金香，其中Trail Garden里集中栽有2万朵，这里也是欣赏郁金香最好的地点。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_9035918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-128-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9035918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-128-600x400.jpeg" alt="黄色的郁金香在阳光下格外娇嫩。（石青云／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035918" class="wp-caption-text">黄色的郁金香在阳光下格外娇嫩。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9035895" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-120-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-9035895 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-120-600x400.jpeg" alt="Brookside公园内栽有五万株郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035895" class="wp-caption-text">Brookside公园内栽有五万株郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9035916" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9035916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-127-600x400.jpeg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-9035916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/unspecified-127-600x400.jpeg" alt="Trail Garden内载有两万多株郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9035916" class="wp-caption-text">Trail Garden内载有两万多株郁金香。（石青云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">负责栽培和设计Trail Garden内郁金香的园艺家Kelly Heim对记者表示，公园里的郁金香不仅颜色齐全，而且不同种类、不同花期的郁金香将在整个4月的不同时间段内开放，一直持续到4月末。游人不仅有更灵活的时间选择来观<ahref="https://github.com/lhbbsd3124/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E8%8A%B1.md#1">赏花</a>朵，不同时间来访也会欣赏到不同的景色。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Brookside公园在一年四季会举办不同的活动。在即将到来的4月22日地球日上，公园里会有各种游戏、儿童工艺活动和现场音乐表演等。公园活动主管Allison Fuentes介绍，公园还会举行儿童节、蝴蝶展等，到了冬季还有灯展，“游人可以在园内欣赏到由100万盏灯组合成的不同景致。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">公园内还可以被预约举行各类私人活动，比如婚礼、庆祝活动、纪念日等。 Fuentes表示，去年“在公园内举行了83场婚礼。”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Brookside公园全年对公众免费开放。</span></p>
