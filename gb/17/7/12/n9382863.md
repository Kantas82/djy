<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>MLB明星赛  坎诺轰出美联5连胜</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者胡竣智综合报导）<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年大联盟<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%98%9F%E8%B5%9B.md#1">明星赛</a>于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日登场，由国民赛扬奖投手薛泽（<span lang="EN-US">Max Schezer</span>）对决<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E8%81%94.md#1">美联</a>三振王赛尔（<span lang="EN-US">Chris Sale</span>） ，终场靠着<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9D%8E%E8%AF%BA.md#1">坎诺</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Robinson Cano</span>）在延长赛的全垒打，<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E8%81%94.md#1">美联</a>以<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>险胜国联，拿下<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%98%8E%E6%98%9F%E8%B5%9B.md#1">明星赛</a><span lang="EN-US">5</span>连胜，<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9D%8E%E8%AF%BA.md#1">坎诺</a>也拿下大赛<span lang="EN-US">MVP</span>。</p>
<p>一局上，薛泽就大显身手，解决掉奥图维（<span lang="EN-US">Jose Altuve</span>）后，虽然被拉米瑞兹（<span lang="EN-US">Jose Ramirez</span>）拉出一支安打，不过他立刻三振贾吉（<span lang="EN-US">Aaron Judge</span>）、史普林格（<span lang="EN-US">George Springer</span>）。</p>
<p>赛尔也不遑多让，一局下先让布雷克曼（<span lang="EN-US">Charlie Balckmon</span>）飞球出局，接着三振史坦顿（<span lang="EN-US">Giancarlo Stanton</span>） ，下一棒哈波（<span lang="EN-US">Bryce Harper</span>）敲安上垒，但赛尔抓下波西（<span lang="EN-US">Buster Posey</span>），安全下庄。</p>
<p>既然先发投手投得棒，后援投手也不能掉漆，国联红雀<span lang="EN-US">36</span>岁老将尼谢克（<span lang="EN-US">Pat Neshek</span>）投出<span lang="EN-US">3</span>上<span lang="EN-US">3</span>下，接手的红雀王牌马丁尼兹（<span lang="EN-US">Carlos Martinez</span>）表现更猛，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>局<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>无失分。而美联贝坦西斯（<span lang="EN-US">Dellin Betances</span>）虽然一度造成满垒危机，所幸及时抓到第三个出局；接手的老将瓦嘉斯（<span lang="EN-US">Jason Vergas</span>）也投出<span lang="EN-US">3</span>上<span lang="EN-US">3</span>下，四局打完双方依旧<span lang="EN-US">0</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>平手。</p>
<p>五局上由美联率先打破僵局，<span class="st">史库柏</span>（<span lang="EN-US">Jonathan Schoop</span>）、沙诺（<span lang="EN-US">Miguel Sano</span>）连续安打先下一城。六局下，国联随即由捕手莫里纳（<span lang="EN-US">Yadier Molina</span>）阳春炮扳平比数，之后双方再度陷入得分僵局。</p>
<p>国联九局上推出道奇终结者詹森（<span lang="EN-US">Kanley Jansen</span>），连飙<span lang="EN-US">3K</span>；并在九局下守着失分危机进入延长赛。</p>
<p>十局上，坎诺逮中小熊终结者戴维斯（<span lang="EN-US">Wade Davis</span>）的失投球，敲出超前比分的全垒打；十局下，美联靠着精彩守备，加上终结者米勒（<span lang="EN-US">Andrew Miller</span>）及时投出的再见三振结束比赛，终场美联以<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>胜国联。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
