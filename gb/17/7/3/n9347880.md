<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>吴新兴勉励侨界关怀救助会 赠书侨校</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/4b4235105613547cafc58b11e8b88ee9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>侨委会委员长吴新兴半年三次到访纽约，慰问侨胞。 (林丹/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月03日讯】（大纪元记者林丹纽约报导）1日，抵达纽约访问的中华民国<ahref="https://github.com/zghtuf3646/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BE%A8%E5%A7%94%E4%BC%9A.md#1">侨委会</a>委员长<ahref="https://github.com/zghtuf3646/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%96%B0%E5%85%B4.md#1">吴新兴</a>，刚下飞机旋即出席大纽约区侨界关怀救助协会的活动，对该会给予勉励，并向中华公所<ahref="https://github.com/zghtuf3646/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%BE%A8%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">华侨学校</a>赠送华文教材。</p>
<figure id="9347887" aria-describedby="caption-9347887" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/849fb77d723b06eb8b136704af23fde8-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/849fb77d723b06eb8b136704af23fde8-450x338.jpg" alt="吴新兴向中华总商会颁发感谢状。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347887" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/zghtuf3646/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E6%96%B0%E5%85%B4.md#1">吴新兴</a>向中华总商会颁发感谢状。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9347886" aria-describedby="caption-9347886" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/9917bc31b3235888240127875330816c-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/9917bc31b3235888240127875330816c-450x338.jpg" alt="吴新兴向台湾会馆老人中心颁发感谢状。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347886" class="wp-caption-text">吴新兴向台湾会馆老人中心颁发感谢状。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是吴新兴在半年内第三次到访纽约。“侨界关怀救助协会”是他在上任后提议在全球主要城市设立，旨在发挥侨界资源，为旅外的国人、留学生、商务人士、观光客、度假打工青年在海外遇到重大灾难或需要紧急求助时，协助驻外机构提供紧急的避难、救援及关怀帮助。</p>
<figure id="9347885" aria-describedby="caption-9347885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/242749d73575c86083b38ed178511898-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/242749d73575c86083b38ed178511898-450x338.jpg" alt="吴新兴向中华公所华侨学校赠送华文教材。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347885" class="wp-caption-text">吴新兴向中华公所<ahref="https://github.com/zghtuf3646/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%BE%A8%E5%AD%A6%E6%A0%A1.md#1">华侨学校</a>赠送华文教材。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴新兴说，设立该协会甚为必要，他举例，目前在澳洲度假打工的台湾人有3万人，由于澳洲的驾驶座位与台湾的相反，澳洲是右驾，旅澳的台湾人发生车祸的很多，平均每个月都有人因车祸死亡，加上工伤、被雇主克扣工资等事件，所以在澳洲的悉尼、墨尔本、尼斯等地都设立了关怀救助会，对旅澳国人守望相助，他祝愿协会事工“运转顺利”。而在纽约的协会，自5月10日成立后，已为四位旅外乡亲提供救助。</p>
<figure id="9347884" aria-describedby="caption-9347884" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/f2b2a200e11088cc2ad432e21c6e93c8-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/f2b2a200e11088cc2ad432e21c6e93c8-450x338.jpg" alt="吴新兴向美东侨生联谊会颁发感谢状。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347884" class="wp-caption-text">吴新兴向美东侨生联谊会颁发感谢状。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中华公所华侨学校校长王张令瑜表示，该校今年暑期班报名火爆，学生有500多人，比去年增加100多，教材告急，四处找书，电话打到多伦多、加州、波士顿。她感谢吴委员长雪中送炭，解决燃眉之急。</p>
<figure id="9347883" aria-describedby="caption-9347883" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/de5011343bd05fd33ce9cab5a88a16f8-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/de5011343bd05fd33ce9cab5a88a16f8-450x338.jpg" alt="吴新兴向大纽约区台湾同乡会颁发感谢状。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347883" class="wp-caption-text">吴新兴向大纽约区台湾同乡会颁发感谢状。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴新兴还向大纽约区侨界关怀救助协会、大纽约台湾同乡会、台湾会馆老人中心、中华总商会、大纽约台湾社团联合会、美东侨生联谊会等六个侨团颁发感谢状，表彰他们在开展国民外交、帮扶旅外国人、声援台湾加入国际组织上所作的努力。◇</p>
<figure id="9347882" aria-describedby="caption-9347882" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/740bcef96469fc279be41754539cd282-450x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/740bcef96469fc279be41754539cd282-450x338.jpg" alt="台侨务委员长吴新兴访问纽约，向大纽约侨界关怀救助协会等六个团体颁发表扬状。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9347882" class="wp-caption-text">台侨务委员长吴新兴访问纽约，向大纽约侨界关怀救助协会等六个团体颁发表扬状。(林丹/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
