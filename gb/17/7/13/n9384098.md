<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>环保机构反猎象 8月3日将粉碎上吨象牙</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月13日讯】（大纪元记者张谦纽约报导）8月3日上午10点30分，州政府将在<ahref="https://github.com/hcslle3160/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%A4%AE%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">中央公园</a>用粉碎机摧毁一吨以上的<ahref="https://github.com/hcslle3160/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%A1%E7%89%99.md#1">象牙</a>。</p>
<figure id="9384107" aria-describedby="caption-9384107" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/cd050d3bc3d2599f99d13563496765c6-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/cd050d3bc3d2599f99d13563496765c6-450x321.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384107" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的<ahref="https://github.com/hcslle3160/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B1%A1%E7%89%99.md#1">象牙</a>制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9384106" aria-describedby="caption-9384106" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/ff056739c3f83e89b60cc49ee4eb2429-450x630.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/ff056739c3f83e89b60cc49ee4eb2429-450x630.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384106" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9384105" aria-describedby="caption-9384105" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/0646ff4fbb292244893864be169991f6-450x630.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/0646ff4fbb292244893864be169991f6-450x630.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384105" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="9384104" aria-describedby="caption-9384104" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/256791d8619412280ba2f34fcc44cd63-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/256791d8619412280ba2f34fcc44cd63-450x321.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384104" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<p>下个月对全体民众开放的销毁象牙活动，是由野生动植物保护协会（Wildlife Conservation Society）与纽约州环保厅（NYS Department of Environmental Conservation，简称DEC）联合举行，活动筹备方表示，透过销毁NYSDEC和联邦执法行动中没收的象牙，他们要对至今仍持有、买卖<ahref="https://github.com/hcslle3160/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B3%95.md#1">非法</a>象牙的人表达“美国不会容忍猎象行为。”</p>
<figure id="9384103" aria-describedby="caption-9384103" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/28a2af6fc33fee2ec1ec035b84fcaa05-450x630.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/28a2af6fc33fee2ec1ec035b84fcaa05-450x630.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384103" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这些象牙主要来自在纽约对个人或公司<ahref="https://github.com/hcslle3160/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B3%95.md#1">非法</a>买卖查缉行动中的斩获。估计价值超过六百万美金。目前美国的法律对象牙的禁令为有史以来最严厉的时刻，禁止多数形式的象牙交易与跨州买卖，违者买卖双方都会面临民事、刑事上的问责。唯有合法文件证明至少100年前的古董，且含有少于20％的象牙成分，或1975年前有象牙配件的乐器，或学术目的申请通过，或以遗产接收的象牙则不在此列。</p>
<figure id="9384102" aria-describedby="caption-9384102" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/897cf267be46533c92c6694c19b3eb85-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/897cf267be46533c92c6694c19b3eb85-450x321.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384102" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据BBC报导，中国是目前象牙非法市场上最大的买家，输出中国的象牙占目前非法市场的七成，且现有象牙定点加工企业34家，定点销售场所143处，但在2016年底中国宣布，将阶段性的关闭现有的“合法”象牙买卖，在2017年底将完全禁止象牙贸易。◇</p>
<figure id="9384101" aria-describedby="caption-9384101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1f2df2dd361c07b022b492d742e0ad05-450x321.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1f2df2dd361c07b022b492d742e0ad05-450x321.jpg" alt="纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。" width="450" b="auto" /></a><figcaption id="caption-9384101" class="wp-caption-text">纽约州环保厅查获的象牙制品。(NYSDEC)</figcaption></figure>
