<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>MLB柯萧15胜到手　道奇完封白袜</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年07月20日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）洛杉矶<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%93%E5%A5%87.md#1">道奇</a>的王牌投手<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E8%90%A7.md#1">柯萧</a>（<span lang="EN-US">Clayton Kershaw</span>）再度演出惊奇，先发对上跨联盟的对手芝加哥<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%A2%9C.md#1">白袜</a>，担任先发投<span lang="EN-US">7</span>局送出<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次三振，挨<span lang="EN-US">7</span>支安打没有失分，与两位后援投手联手以<span lang="EN-US">1</span>：<span lang="EN-US">0</span>完封<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%A2%9C.md#1">白袜</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%81%93%E5%A5%87.md#1">道奇</a>笑纳<span lang="EN-US">10</span>连胜，<ahref="https://github.com/xejgby3625/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%AF%E8%90%A7.md#1">柯萧</a>则取得<span lang="EN-US">11</span>连胜，本季第<span lang="EN-US">15</span>胜，暂居大联盟胜投王。</p>
<p>道奇强打少年贝林杰（<span lang="EN-US">Cody Bellinger</span>）就适时击出带有一分打点的安打，拿下唯一分数，别的投手可能不够，但对防御率<span lang="EN-US">2.18</span>的柯萧完全没有问题，具有左投优势的他，快速球与变化球一致的出手点，加上他高大的身驱，直球、滑球及曲球交互应用，白袜打者一筹莫展，七局没有失分，防御率降到<span lang="EN-US">2.07</span>。</p>
<p>道奇因为有柯萧和另外<span lang="EN-US">1</span>名年轻左投、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>胜<span lang="EN-US">0</span>败的伍德（<span lang="EN-US">Alex Wood</span>）把关，目前战绩<span lang="EN-US">65</span>胜<span lang="EN-US">29</span>败，傲视全大联盟。<span lang="EN-US">**</span></p>
