<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>新唐人“健康巡展”洛杉矶站图片报导</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/cacbfdc8b1eb9fb649b702212bb388dc-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>亚凯迪亚市长阿曼森（Peter Amundson）给健康展颁发褒奖。（Arthur／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年07月11日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>讯）由<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E5%A4%A7%E7%BA%AA%E5%85%83%E6%97%B6%E6%8A%A5%E3%80%8B.md#1">《大纪元时报》</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E3%80%8A%E6%96%B0%E5%94%90%E4%BA%BA%E7%94%B5%E8%A7%86%E5%8F%B0%E3%80%8B.md#1">《新唐人电视台》</a>联合主办的2017新唐人“<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%81%A5%E5%BA%B7%E5%B7%A1%E5%B1%95.md#1">健康巡展</a>”<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md#1">洛杉矶</a>站7月8日于亚凯迪亚市（Arcadia）社区活动中心（community center）隆重举行。此次健康展围绕民众十分关心的主题“病毒与癌症”展开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9375323" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9375323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/fe8b8443474ad64587906e11408c70f7-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9375323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/fe8b8443474ad64587906e11408c70f7-450x300.jpg" alt="国会议员赵美心为健康展颁发褒奖。（Arthur／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9375323" class="wp-caption-text">国会议员赵美心为健康展颁发褒奖。（Arthur／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9375339" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9375339" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/0af9023ebe7001c303f4f9951dbd7856-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9375339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/0af9023ebe7001c303f4f9951dbd7856-450x300.jpg" alt="亚凯迪亚市长阿曼森（Peter Amundson）与参展商交流。（Arthur／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9375339" class="wp-caption-text">亚凯迪亚市长阿曼森（Peter Amundson）与参展商交流。（Arthur／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现场邀请到多位专家讲解相关知识，其内容涵盖乙肝、丙肝的危害，乙肝和肝癌的关系，HPV和子宫颈癌的关系以及如何有效防预治疗。现场民众对健康讲座十分欢迎，大家或拍照留档，或纸笔记录，且多次与专家提问互动，场内座无虚席，场外人来人往。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9375303" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9375303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/134f6f800608c3578026dcf900ece984-450x300.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-medium wp-image-9375303" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/134f6f800608c3578026dcf900ece984-450x300.jpg" alt="《乙肝和肝癌的关系》主讲者Dr.Laleh Melstrom。（Arthur／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9375303" class="wp-caption-text">《乙肝和肝癌的关系》主讲者Dr.Laleh Melstrom。（Arthur／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除名医讲座外，健康展内还有多名<ahref="https://github.com/ixmzgj302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB%E4%B9%89%E8%AF%8A.md#1">中医义诊</a>，并且现场有免费血压与体脂检测，以及免费乙肝和丙肝检测。同时，抽奖活动更是活跃了场内气氛，民众在获得有效健康资讯的同时，又能带回诸多健康产品，收获颇丰。</p>
