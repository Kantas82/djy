<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阮公子一周美酒推荐（170721）</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月22日讯】<strong>位于<ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>岛</strong><strong>Comox</strong><strong>的</strong><strong>40 Knot Vineyard &amp; Estate Winery</strong><strong>，由</strong><strong>Craigs夫妇在</strong><strong>2014年</strong><strong>购入后，非常积极地建立优质酒庄声誉，成绩辉煌。尤其注意绿化营运，取得观光旅游金牌资格。</strong></p>
<h2><strong><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9449723 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/01-Leve-du-Soleil-Rose-2014-e1500660952504.jpg" alt="01-Leve du Soleil Rose 2014" width="225" b="400" />1) BC</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/pgenre311/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a></strong><strong>Comox</strong><strong>岛露茜酒</strong></h2>
<p><strong>酒目：</strong><strong>40 Knots Leve du Soleil Rose 2014 </strong><strong>。</strong><strong>$22</strong><strong>。</strong></p>
<p>沙当妮葡萄与红皮偌的美妙配合，以经典露茜手法酿造而成，活泼汁润悦喉，桃果与青苹果的香味芳郁，清鲜的橘果韵渐涌。</p>
<p>配肴：海鲜沙律、烧烤品、薰肉拼盆。</p>
<h2><strong><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9449725 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/02-White-Seas-2016_150116-e1500660966568.jpg" alt="02-White Seas 2016_150116" width="225" b="400" />2) BC</strong><strong>温哥华岛</strong><strong>Comox</strong><strong>白混</strong></h2>
<p><strong>酒目：</strong><strong>40 Knots White Seas 2016 </strong><strong>。</strong><strong>$20</strong><strong>。</strong></p>
<p>清悦轻盈，将Salish 海岸的凉快气息奉上。果韵与花情流露在鲜明的酒格中，夹集着隐约的香辣味，生津悦润。</p>
<p>配肴：辣味青豆、肉干、脆口果。</p>
<h2><strong><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9449726 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/03-Ziggy-Siegerrebe-2016_191241-e1500660986526.jpg" alt="03-Ziggy Siegerrebe 2016_191241" width="300" b="400" />3) </strong><strong>BC</strong><strong>温哥华岛</strong><strong>Comox</strong><strong>白酒</strong></h2>
<p><strong>酒目：</strong><strong>40 Knots Ziggy Siegerrebe 2016</strong><strong>。</strong><strong>$23</strong><strong>。</strong></p>
<p>用百分百Siegerrebe葡萄酿成，亚热带果的香气涌现，清凉秀丽，堪称此酒庄的代表作。酒格柔和中有朝阳气息与层次感，在2016 Northwest Wine Summit 中夺得银牌。</p>
<p>配肴：煎蚝、海蜇熏蹄、赛螃蟹。</p>
<h2><strong><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9449727 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/04-Gamay-Noir-2016_191214-e1500660999744.jpg" alt="04-Gamay Noir 2016_191214" width="300" b="400" />4) BC</strong><strong>温哥华岛</strong><strong>Comox</strong><strong>红酒</strong></h2>
<p><strong>酒目：</strong><strong>40 Knots Gamay Noir 2016 </strong><strong>。</strong><strong>$26</strong><strong>。</strong></p>
<p>充满熟透红梅、黑枣与李子的果韵，橡木香气恰到好处。酒格中和舒柔，悠悠在口腔中传送隐约的花香味。</p>
<p>配肴：鲍鱼菇扒菜胆、烧鸡、茄汁意粉。</p>
<p>责任编辑：邓林</p>
