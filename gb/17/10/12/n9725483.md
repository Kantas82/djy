<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>喵星版“白雪公主” 广交异类野生动物朋友</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年10月12日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳综合报导）一位Imgur网友上传了一组家里猫咪和各种野生动物隔窗交流的照片，并写道：“我的猫显然是一位迪士尼公主。现在就在上演呢！”</p>
<p>这只名叫索契（Sochi）的西伯利亚森林猫（Siberian Forest Cat），是主人2014年去俄罗斯索契参加冬运会后带回来的，由此得名。它的主人——Imgur用户“ctrlaltme”透露，索契非常友善和外向，喜欢出去逛，很快就能和邻居及其它动物混得很熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726752" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-659d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-659d98e91b32c5__700-450x663.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="663" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>自从第一次从家里出去散步，索契就爱上了大自然，开始天天坐在窗边向外张望。当主人发现索契的小心思，就着意观察它在家的表现，发现它和各种各样的野生动物都交上了朋友。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726748" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-259d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726748" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-259d98e91b32c5__700-450x595.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="595" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在迪士尼的童话世界里，多数公主都有着和各种动物交流的特殊能力，原来家里这一位就是公猫版的“迪士尼公主”！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726747" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-5-59d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726747" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-5-59d98e91b32c5__700-450x642.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="642" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9726751" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-459d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-459d98e91b32c5__700-450x473.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="473" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>主人看到，野生动物们特意来到窗前找索契，隔窗与它相望、交流，仿佛索契就是公猫版本的“白雪公主”！</p>
<p>兴奋之余，主人在窗前架起了相机，如今已有索契的好几位异类朋友入镜，其中包括一只鹿、约4只松鼠，还有无数的鸟儿。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726750" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-359d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-359d98e91b32c5__700-450x600.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这位猫咪主人的房子变成了白雪公主之家，真是太令人羡慕啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-1-59d98e91b32c5__700.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726746" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/2017-1-59d98e91b32c5__700-450x563.jpg" alt="(ctrlaltme/Imgur)" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/QLrqH" target="_blank">ctrlaltme/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;">猫界“小毛贼”获判决</span></h2>
<p>天下猫咪和人一样，也真是无奇不有。另一位Imgur用户则用照片证明，猫界也有喜欢偷拿东西的“小毛贼”。</p>
<p>这位用户名为“pleasequotemovies”的网友听女朋友说，她的猫总爱偷厨房里的东西，厨房丢东西肯定都是猫咪所为，这种说法让他实在难以相信。为了说服他，女友决定让照片来说话。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9726753" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171012-SULIN-DV556c7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9726753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171012-SULIN-DV556c7-450x600.jpg" alt="(pleasequotemovies/Imgur)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://imgur.com/gallery/vnzpv#DV556cj" target="_blank">pleasequotemovies/Imgur</a>)</figcaption></figure>
