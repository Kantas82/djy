<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>双宋交换婚戒落泪 捧花三度失误新郎笑翻</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年10月31日讯】（大纪元记者卢莉之报导）32岁的韩星<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E4%BB%B2%E5%9F%BA.md#1">宋仲基</a>与35岁的<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%85%A7%E4%B9%94.md#1">宋慧乔</a>（乔妹）于31日下午在首尔新罗饭店迎宾馆举行婚礼，二人十指紧扣步进会场，彼此相互鞠躬后，在300名亲友及来宾见证下交换戒指。宋仲基在宣读誓词时忍不住感动流泪，频频拿面纸拭泪，气氛温馨感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9789279" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9789279" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545461487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9789279" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545461487-600x872.jpg" width="451" b="655" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9789279" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E4%BB%B2%E5%9F%BA.md#1">宋仲基</a>与<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8B%E6%85%A7%E4%B9%94.md#1">宋慧乔</a>在首尔市长春洞的新罗酒店举行婚礼。（（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>典礼没有铺张的排场，以端庄高雅的白色布置为主。由于现场低温15度，新人在听双方父母致词时，宋仲基紧握乔妹的手帮她取暖，还贴心整理头发。他最后捧起乔妹的脸深情一吻，现场嘉宾欢呼庆祝。</p>
<p>有趣是的，新娘丢捧花因力道太轻，丢了3次都没人接到，她跺脚皱眉，一旁的宋仲基笑歪，帮忙把捧花捡回来让新娘重丢。第四次丢时，乔妹索性直接正面扔捧花才成功。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9789280" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9789280" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545381487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9789280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545381487.jpg" width="451" b="668" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9789280" class="wp-caption-text">宋仲基与宋慧乔在首尔市长春洞的新罗酒店举行婚礼。（（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9789283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9789283" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545491487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9789283" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545491487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="569" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9789283" class="wp-caption-text">宋仲基与宋慧乔在首尔市长春洞的新罗酒店举行婚礼。（（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宋仲基的好哥们<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%85%89%E6%B4%99.md#1">李光洙</a>将2人8年来的友情写成信朗读；乔妹的闺蜜玉珠铉担任婚礼歌手，献唱迪士尼动画《阿拉丁》主题曲《A Whole New World》，祝福两人婚后迎接全新的世界。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9789281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9789281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545341487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9789281" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1710310545341487.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9789281" class="wp-caption-text">宋仲基与宋慧乔在首尔市长春洞的新罗酒店举行婚礼，图为婚礼邀请函。（（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国大陆女星<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A0%E5%AD%90%E6%80%A1.md#1">章子怡</a>抢头香到达，新娘姊妹淘崔智友、金喜善随后陆续抵达，宾客还有刘亚仁、朴宝英、<ahref="https://github.com/pgfkuo3662/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E6%AD%A3%E6%B0%91.md#1">黄正民</a>、金玟锡、金智媛、童星金秀安，以及综艺节目《RUNNING MAN》班底金钟国、哈哈、刘在锡 、池锡辰、“Super Junior”东海、“SHINee”珉豪“ZE:A”朴炯植、车太铉、林周焕、李美妍、钱忍和、黄晸玟、朴宝英、赵成夏、高昌锡、朴帅眉，现场星光云集。</p>
