<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>宜兰都会原住民丰年舞祭  感恩祖灵眷顾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/eaa2e06a18417b234076392c8c5f3646-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宜兰县代理县长吴泽成（右三）跟原住民一起跳祈福舞蹈。（谢月琴／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年10月08日讯】（大纪元记者谢月琴台湾宜兰报导）宜兰县政府在10月7日上午在中兴文创园区南榕广场盛大举办宜兰县都会<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E4%BD%8F%E6%B0%91.md#1">原住民</a>ilisin联合<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B0%E5%B9%B4%E8%88%9E.md#1">丰年舞</a>祭，今年以“海啸跳吧！”为主题，超过千名来自各地的都会原住民着传统服饰跳着祈福舞蹈，充分展现其热情与活力，他们对祖灵的眷顾也表达感恩之心。</p>
<p>宜兰<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E4%BD%8F%E6%B0%91.md#1">原住民</a>族有15族，共1万6千多人，其中都会区原住民有6600多人，以泰雅族2300多人及美族1900多人占多数。丰年祭是原住民一年最大节庆，原住民族以谢天谢地谢祖灵方式进行庆典，纵使草地泥泞，族人依旧齐聚一堂，热闹滚滚。</p>
<p>都会原住民ilisin联合<ahref="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B0%E5%B9%B4%E8%88%9E.md#1">丰年舞</a>祭自上午9点开始迎宾，而后举办丰收祭仪，宜兰县长吴泽成着传统服饰，与县议员孙汤玉惠、薛呈懿等人，跟众人一齐手牵手舞动，场面很壮观、热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9711854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9711854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/17/10/8/n9711851.md#1/%e5%8e%9f%e4%bd%8f%e6%b0%91%e8%b7%b3%e7%a5%88%e7%a6%8f%e8%88%9e%e8%b9%88%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-9711854"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9711854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/42079014f830c18d96bf6993b87b6c60-450x300.jpg" alt="原住民跳祈福舞蹈。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9711854" class="wp-caption-text">原住民跳祈福舞蹈。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吴泽成表示，宜兰县政府为落实“幸福宜兰2.0”的执政理念，针对县内原住民族事务规划多项的重大建设，如兴建寒华大桥及武塔横山部落桥梁，其中寒华大桥即将于10月21日举行通车典礼，当地居民从此将不再为水患所苦；修缮原乡关怀站，兴建原住民多功能会馆等，整合长照及幼儿托育功能，提供族人更全面性的综合服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9711853" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9711853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/17/10/8/n9711851.md#1/%e5%8d%83%e4%ba%ba%e7%89%bd%e6%89%8b%e5%a4%a7%e6%9c%83%e8%88%9e%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-9711853"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9711853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/c7d8f32f2cdac701de86413ade09dd7e-450x300.jpg" alt="千人牵手大会舞。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9711853" class="wp-caption-text">千人牵手大会舞。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>值得一提的是丰年祭的奖杯设计十分特别，是请雕刻师专门制作的，有鱼、叶子、刀等造型，让人眼睛为之一亮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9711857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9711857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/17/10/8/n9711851.md#1/%e8%b1%90%e5%b9%b4%e7%a5%ad%e6%9c%89%e7%a8%bb%e7%b1%b3-%e5%b0%8f%e7%b1%b3%e7%ad%89%e7%a5%ad%e5%93%81%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-9711857"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9711857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/89c856bf54966f2d70310c6514c47ca2-450x300.jpg" alt="丰年祭有稻米 小米等祭品。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9711857" class="wp-caption-text">丰年祭有稻米 小米等祭品。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动当天除了有原住民传统歌舞竞赛、精彩大会舞及传统竞技比赛之外，现场还有原住民风味美食及手工艺品的展售，欢迎所有民众一起带着无比的热情，大声欢唱用力跳舞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9711858" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9711858" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/wqsjdc301/djy/blob/master/gb/17/10/8/n9711851.md#1/%e8%b1%90%e5%b9%b4%e7%a5%ad%e7%9a%84%e7%8d%8e%e7%9b%83%e8%a8%ad%e8%a8%88%e5%8d%81%e5%88%86%e7%89%b9%e5%88%a5%e3%80%82" rel="attachment wp-att-9711858"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9711858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/3a3fe330c9a48ce897aa397afa6fd010-450x300.jpg" alt="丰年祭的奖杯设计十分特别。（谢月琴／大纪元）" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9711858" class="wp-caption-text">丰年祭的奖杯设计十分特别。（谢月琴／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
